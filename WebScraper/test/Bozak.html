  • About Bozak

Bozark is a manufacturer established in the United States as a manufacturer specializing in speakers in the 1930s.
In Japan, in the 1960s, the trio formed a technical tie-up and sold Bozark speakers in Japan. At that time, only the speaker units were imported, and Trio manufactured and sold the enclosures.
Then in the 70's Denon imported it.

Currently, we design and sell mixer equipment.
