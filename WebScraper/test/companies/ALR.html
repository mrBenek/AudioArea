  • About ALR

A German manufacturer established in 1989.
The company was founded by Carl Heinz Fink, a well-known European architect of speaker systems and networks.
He learned metal cone technology from E. J. Jordan who is known for Goodmans Axiom80 and Jordan Watts. By asking famous unit manufacturers in Northern Europe to produce original units, he has realized metal cone woofer with all ALR speakers.
