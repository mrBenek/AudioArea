  • About Audio Alchemy

A manufacturer founded in California in 1990 by Mark Schifter, Mel Schilling, and Douglas Goldenberg.
The company produced cost-effective digital equipment, but was dissolved in 1998.
One of the founders, Mark Shifter, later founded Perpetual Technology, and in 2003 he founded Onyx.
