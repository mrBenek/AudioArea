  • About CHARIO

Established in Milan, Italy in 1975, it is one of the oldest speaker manufacturers in Italy.
CHARIO is named after the general manager, Carlo vicenzetto, and the chief engineer, Mario Muracho.
Carlo decides the sound of the speaker system with an emphasis on his sensitivity when he was active as a music player.
