  • About BRAUN

Currently, we do not produce audio equipment, but until around the 1970 s, we actively introduced speaker systems. For the 30 series of HiFi speaker systems, we used units developed jointly with well-known Dr. Kurt Mueller.
According to the catalog, it was Brown that adopted the soft dome type speaker in the system for the first time in the world in the middle of 1960's.
