  • About Ampliton

A French maker established in 1977.
This company was founded by Mr. Herve Thomas, the president of Sofas Co., a French PA company that undertakes audio equipment for theaters and other facilities, and his technical team. They felt that the sound of the transistor amplifier was not enough, so they announced an audio amplifier Ampliton made with vacuum tubes.
