  • ONKYO Integra MT-1000

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1985)

Commentary

A 330 mm wide compact FM/AM tuner.

The bandwidth expansion effect of compressing the IF spectrum signal realizes excellent characteristics.

It is equipped with an RF booster which can select interference rejection performance for neighboring stations and high-sensitivity reception for long-distance stations.
In addition, it is equipped with 2-stage IF bandwidth switching for high sound quality and high selectivity.

Built-in two program Route 3 / reservation timer of Everyday/once/sleep.

Equipped with 8 preset memories for each FM and AM.
