Commentary

A quartz synthesizer tuner with flat characteristics from the ultra-low band to the high band of 5 Hz by DC amplification and DC demodulation including RF system.

The DC peak sample-and-hold MPX circuit is integrated into a single-chip IC. This circuit eliminates phase distortion by using a fast-rising pulse wave for stereo demodulation.

DC amplification, DC detection and DC demodulation, including the RF system, realize flat frequency characteristics from the ultra-low band of 5 Hz.

By installing a pilot signal canceler with auto-level adjuster, wide-band characteristics are realized.
In addition, excellent high-frequency characteristics are obtained by the jitter distortion elimination circuit.

It is equipped with a super narrow IF circuit with a narrow band high selectivity of 25 dB at ± 200 kHz detuning, and a normal IF circuit with a selectivity of 55 dB at ± 400 kHz detuning is automatically switched according to the judgment of a microcomputer, eliminating adjacent station interference.

The RF circuit is also equipped with a normal circuit and a narrow circuit. The microcomputer automatically selects the RF circuit to eliminate spurious waves caused by adjacent station interference and urban noise.

It is equipped with a random preset memory function for a total of 16 FM/AM stations. In addition, an auto memory that can be preset by pressing a button is available.

It is equipped with an auto-scan tuning function that makes manual tuning easy.

Equipped with a 10-dB 4-stage muting for selecting the reception quality.
