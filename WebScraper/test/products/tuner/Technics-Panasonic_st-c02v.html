  • Technics ST-C02V

  • ¥ 38,500 (around 1980)

Commentary

A compact TV sound multiplex tuner that uses an intercarrier system to catch TV sound and images beautifully.

You can enjoy TV sound multiplex broadcasting by connecting your stereo system to a conventional TV.
The TV can be switched by changing the channel of the ST-C02V.

Equipped with a video output port.

Equipped with an automatic program switching circuit, it automatically determines whether it is stereo broadcast transmission or 2-language broadcast, switches the circuit, and displays the mode with an indicator.
