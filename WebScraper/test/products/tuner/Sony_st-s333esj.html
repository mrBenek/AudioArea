Commentary

FM/AM stereo tuner that supports AM stereo.

In addition to the one touch tuning characteristic of the synthesizer system, the tuning knob can be used for tuning.
In addition, we have adopted a pure circuit that stops the operation of the microcomputer when the channel selection is completed, thus suppressing sound quality degradation.

Technology such as radial power supply, which connects the power supply line to each circuit part of the tuner with the shortest distance, and at the same time, the ground is grounded at one point, has been introduced.

Antenna input of A/B2 system is mounted.

RF mode switching, IF band switching, FM mode switching, muting on / off, etc. are available.

Equipped with a multi-process memory that can store various settings, reception band, channel selection frequency, and station name.

Wireless remote control is included.
