  • Marantz Model105

  • ¥ 34,900 (around 1972)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner equipped with Marantz's original gyro-touch tuning.

The dial is a linear scale dial with the same interval between scales, and the orange indicator can be seen on the cobalt blue letters.

Equipped with a high-precision level meter and muting circuit.
