  • SONY ST-S222ESA

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1991)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner that can receive VHF and UHF TV broadcast audio.

By using a high-speed PLL IC developed by Sony, the comparison frequency of the synthesizer tuner is raised to 100 kHz, which is the same as the reception frequency. The direct comparator method is adopted.

WODSD circuits that produce switching signals that are less likely to generate higher harmonics are installed in order to enhance the interference rejection capability of neighboring stations.

It uses a radial power supply in which each block is placed around the power supply.

The TV sound is compatible with sound multiplex.
You can also play programs in two languages such as English by selecting main / sub / main + sub.

Up to 30 preset memories can be registered at random.

Equipped with functions such as digital tuning and preset scanning by using the tuning knob.
