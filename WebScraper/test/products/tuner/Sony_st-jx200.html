  • ¥ 23,000 (released in 1983)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner with complete simplicity of operation.

In order to make channel selection as easy to understand as possible, we have adopted a design in which LED indicators are arranged in a concentrated manner to avoid meters. Channel selection is completed by simply setting the tuning indicator to green. At the same time, you can also check the signal strength and whether it is stereo or monaural.

Equipped with a simultaneous stereo / muting switch that automatically turns muting ON in stereo mode.

Equipped with a 3-point LED signal indicator.
