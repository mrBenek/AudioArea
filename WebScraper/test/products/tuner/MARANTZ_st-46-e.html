  • Marantz ST-46

  • ¥ 28,000 (released in 1995)

Commentary

AM/FM stereo tuner with dual gate MOS FET in front end.

Equipped with station name memory function.

AM stereo is supported.

There were 2 kinds of models, gold and black.

Comes with a card-type remote controller that supports power on / off.
