  • Technics ST-808V

  • ¥ 58,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

A TV sound multiplex tuner using a separate-carrier system.

A separate carrier system has been adopted for IF amplification and detection with a dedicated circuit that separates and separates video and audio signals. This reduces the generation of buzz sound and improves sound quality.

With a built-in RF-out, you can enjoy sound multiplex broadcasting by connecting your stereo system to a conventional TV.

It is equipped with a video connector for monitor television. If a monitor television and a stereo system are combined, sound can be enjoyed as a stereo system and image can be enjoyed as a monitor television.

Equipped with video and audio input / output terminals for home video, it is possible to record TV sound multiplex broadcasting and stereo recording.

It has a built-in RF converter that enables simultaneous channel selection of video and audio.
When the TV antenna is connected to ST-808V and the RF output of ST-808V is connected to the TV receiver, you can switch the image of the TV receiver by channel operation of ST-808V.

Equipped with an antenna output selector switch that sends the signal from the TV antenna directly to the TV without passing it through the ST-808V receiver circuit.

Using an electronic soft touch switch, you can preset a total of 10 VHF/UHF stations. Preset control buttons are built-in in the pocket on the left front panel.

Equipped with a channel lock mechanism, it can be combined with a cassette deck or home video for timer recording.
