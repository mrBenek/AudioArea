Commentary

FM/AM tuner that uses a double motion tuning meter.

In addition to the conventional muting circuit that cancels out inter-station noise during channel selection, we have developed and installed a special muting circuit that catches only the FM stereo broadcast transmission.

The FM front-end uses a newly developed dual-gate MOS FET to obtain high sensitivity.

The FM MPX circuit is integrated into a single IC using a newly developed monolithic IC.
By adopting a switching circuit unique to the IC, separation characteristics in the high frequency range have been improved.

Frequency number line type dial is adopted for accurate channel selection.

There is a headphone out on the front panel, and you can enjoy FM stereo broadcast transmission with this one.

In order to support FM4ch broadcasting, a 4-ch conversion device connection terminal is mounted.

Equipped with two variable and fixed output terminals.
