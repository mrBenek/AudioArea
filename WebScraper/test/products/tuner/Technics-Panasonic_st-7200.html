  • Technics ST-7200

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner developed with sound quality as the first point at a low price.

The IF stage is designed with an emphasis on county delay characteristics.
The MPX circuit is a highly stable circuit using a PLL.

It uses a center tuning method that makes it easy to tune the channel when it comes to the center of the indicator meter.

It is equipped with a soft muting switch to eliminate noise between stations.

There was a wood case sold separately.
