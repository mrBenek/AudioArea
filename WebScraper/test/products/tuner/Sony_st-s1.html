  • SONY ST-S1

  • ¥ 20,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

FM/AM stereo tuner of Sony's compact high-component COMPO series.

AM stereo broadcast transmission can be received.

In addition to manual channel selection, preset memories capable of storing up to 30 stations at random are installed.

In combination with the Component Series, timer recording and operation with the TAE-S1 remote control are possible.
