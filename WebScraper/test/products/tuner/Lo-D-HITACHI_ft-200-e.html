  • HITACHI/Lo-D FT-200

  • ¥ 33,000 (around 1974)

Commentary

AM/FM stereo tuner with low noise design and high cost performance.

By using an FET in the front end of the FM tuner section, noise and interference are reduced.
A ceramic filter is used in the IF section to improve selectivity and distortion characteristics.

It is equipped with a low-pass filter in preparation for carrier leakage, which causes sound quality degradation.

Built-in FM muting circuit reduces inter-station noise.

A stabilized power supply circuit is adopted.
