  • SONY ST-59

  • ¥ 44,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

Tuner for TV sound multiplex broadcasting.

Split carrier system is adopted.

It is possible to preset nine stations from VHF (1 ch ~ 12 ch) / UHF (13 ch ~ 62 ch) by soft-touch selection.

Equipped with a muting switch.

Equipped with an RF output terminal that automatically switches between the TV sound and image with priority to the tuner button by using an empty TV channel (1or2).
