  • ONKYO Integra T-466mkII

  • ¥ 45,000 (Released in November 1974)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner developed based on the basics of a tuner for music playback.

Sensitivity and selectivity have been improved by the front end of FET-based 4-row varicon and 6-element phase-linear-type ceramic filter.

In terms of audio characteristics, we have introduced 4-stage limiter circuits including monolithic ICs and double-balanced multi-demodulators using transistors.

It uses a multi-path detection terminal that can determine the optimum direction of the antenna while listening to the sound and a 2-meter type tuning meter.

The wood case was sold separately.
