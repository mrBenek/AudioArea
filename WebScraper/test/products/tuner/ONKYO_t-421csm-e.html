Commentary

CS-PCM tuner with digital output and audio-only power supply circuit.

Equipped with two digital outputs, optical and coaxial, which can be directly connected to MD etc.

It is equipped with an 11V/15V converter power supply compatible with most CS and CS-PCM antennas.

An audio-only power supply circuit is installed to improve sound quality.

Built-in Core Tech type scramble decoder.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 1bitD/A converter and an 8 x oversampling digital filter.

24-station preset memories are installed.

Antenna reception level can be displayed by C/N value.

Bit stream output terminal is mounted.

Equipped with mini headphone port and headphone volume.

A remote control is included.

* For reception, a CS digital broadcasting receiving antenna with polarization plane voltage switching system or a CS digital broadcasting receiving antenna capable of receiving vertical knitting wave is used.
