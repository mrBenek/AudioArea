Commentary

FM/AM stereo tuner that can be preset by super area call.

Equipped with a Super Area Call function. With this function, frequency data from FM/AM broadcasting stations throughout Japan, including relay stations, is built-in. Simply by selecting the prefecture name, you can check which stations are actually available for reception in the area and automatically preset them.
The station name is also registered and displayed in advance, so you can select not only the frequency but also the station name.

Equipped with a menu entry system, you can replace or delete automatically preset stations.
