  • SONY ST-50

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Tuner for TV sound multiplex broadcasting.

With the split-carrier method, received TV signals are immediately divided into video and audio signals, eliminating unpleasant buzzing noise.

Equipped with one touch channel selection function by electronic tuning.

It uses a system that automatically detects and switches between general broadcasting, stereo broadcasting and bilingual broadcasting.

It is equipped with MPX IN terminal, and if it is a TV with MPX OUT, it is possible to switch image and sound simultaneously.
