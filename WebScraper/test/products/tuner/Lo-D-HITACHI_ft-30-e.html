  • Lo-D FT-30

  • ¥ 33,000 (released in 1979)

Commentary

A4-size FM/AM tuner with improved operability as well as improved basic performance.

The FM front-end section uses a low-noise FET (field-effect transistor) with excellent cross-modulation characteristics, the FM multi-circuit uses a PLL IC, and the FM IF section uses three 2-element ceramic filters with good phase characteristics and a dedicated クオードラチュア detection IC.

Equipped with a tri-state tuning indicator with 3 LEDs, the just-tuning point can be easily confirmed.

A loop antenna is mounted for AM only.

Equipped with a mode / muting switch that integrates the mode switching and muting functions.

It is equipped with a 9 kHz trap coil that eliminates beat interference caused by adjacent stations during AM reception.
