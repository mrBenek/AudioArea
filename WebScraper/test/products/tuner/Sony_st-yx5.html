  • SONY ST-YX5

  • ¥ 28,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

Analog FM/AM tuner using newly developed IC.

It is equipped with a newly developed thin 5-row air varicon and realizes an extremely slim size as an analog type.

The SN ratio has been improved by adopting a newly developed IF stage and MPX IC in the FM tuner section.

Large traverse tuning knob is adopted.

Equipped with a stereo indicator and 5-point signal indicator with red LED, and an FM tuning indicator with green LED.

Equipped with a Stereo/Mute switch.

Uses a die-cast body.
