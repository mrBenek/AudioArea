  • MERIDIAN 204

  • ¥ 185,000 (around 1988)

Commentary

An FM stereo tuner with a unique functional approach that aims to improve musical performance in order to respond to the evolution of FM sources such as multi-channel and high-quality sound.

While adhering to the idea of giving top priority to sound quality in all aspects, thorough consideration has been given to each circuit stage to achieve both excellent reception capability and high sound quality reproduction.

It is equipped with preset functions for 18 stations, timer functions, signal level display, search threshold functions for passing stations below the set signal level, etc.

By connecting a network cable to the 207 mkII CD player or Preamp 201, you can remotely control the entire system and set timers.
The P lead is for connecting to the CD player 207 mkII, and the Q lead is for connecting to the preamp 201.
