Commentary

An FM stereo tuner that pursues the ultimate in receiving and reproducing functions with the main theme of faithful waveform transmission.

Equipped with an automatic IF band switching circuit that detects and operates an interfering signal below the human detection limit.

The pilot signal cancellation circuit achieves flat characteristics over a wide range from 20 Hz to 18 kHz.

It uses a servo tuning circuit that detects and automatically compensates for frequency and tuning deviations caused by temperature changes.

A frequency linear type FM exclusive 8-train varicon has been adopted to realize excellent interference rejection capability.

It has a built-in automatic high blend circuit capable of receiving with less noise.

An and muting switch muting switch system that enables narrow band muting.
