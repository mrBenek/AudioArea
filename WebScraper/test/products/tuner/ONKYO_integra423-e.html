  • ONKYO Integra 423

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

AM / FM tuner with improved bandwidth and selectivity characteristics by using a 6-element mechanical filter.

In order to eliminate image interference and spurious interference, the front end (FET) of high-frequency 2-stage / 4-row variable capacitor specification and 4-stage limiter circuit (IF5-stage) including 3-stage differential limiter are adopted.
