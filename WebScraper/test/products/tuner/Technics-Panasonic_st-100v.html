  • Technics ST-100V

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A TV tuner that can be connected to a stereo system and combined with a TV to enjoy sound multiplex broadcasting.

Stable reception performance is obtained by the circuit configuration which adopted 4-pole MOS FET in the UHF/VHF front end.

PLL MPX IC is installed in the quadrature FM detection and multi-circuit (multi-demodulation circuit).

It uses an electronic tuner that can preset 10 UHF / VHF stations.

Equipped with an automatic program switching circuit, it automatically detects the pilot signal according to the broadcast, automatically determines whether it is stereo broadcast transmission or two language broadcast, and switches it.
In addition, it is equipped with a program manual switching mode switch so that you can select the language you want to listen to in case of 2 languages.
You can enjoy two languages at the same time.

It uses a slim design with a panel height of 1U that matches the Big GM series of Technics system components.
