  • SONY ST-5090

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner with higher cost performance than the ST 5070.

JFET is used for the high-frequency amplifier circuit and FET is also used for the mixer circuit.

The IF section uses two solid-state filters with two elements in one package.

By adopting a sealed coil unit for the MPX circuit, the separation degree is improved and the aging is suppressed.

The AM tuner uses a triple-tune IFT to achieve high selectivity, high signal-to-noise ratio and low distortion factor.

The FM tuner section uses a frequency linear type varicon.

Output level control is mounted on the front panel.

Uses a large tuning meter with back lighting.

Two sets of output jacks, Normal and High, are mounted.

Equipped with a high blend switch.

It is a Lawrence Oak finish.
