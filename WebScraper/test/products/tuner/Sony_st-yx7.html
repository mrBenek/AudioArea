  • SONY ST-YX7

  • ¥ 40,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner with quartz lock synthesizer reception performance and microcomputer control operability.

The s / N ratio has been improved by adopting a newly developed IC in the FM tuner section.

Equipped with 6 preset functions for each FM and AM.

Auto tuning or step tuning can be performed.

Equipped with a 5-point LED signal indicator.

Equipped with a Stereo/Mute switch.

A die-cast body is adopted.
