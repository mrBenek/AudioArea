  • Technics ST-7300II

  • ¥ 30,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner pursuing its original performance as a tuner.

The front-end section is composed of three FM-trains and two AM-trains.

A PLL / MPX demodulation circuit is used in the MPX section.

Three unique ICs (AN 377, AN 363, and AN 217) are used to achieve high reliability, low distortion, and high sensitivity.

Equipped with a Rec level check switch that is convenient for setting the level of the FM air check and a signal meter / tuning meter.

It uses PRC (Printed Resistance Board) with increased reliability of the whole circuit.
