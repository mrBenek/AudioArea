  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

ES-II series FM/AM tuner developed based on technological accumulation since ST-5000F.

In order to improve the accuracy of the varicon, the local oscillator inside the varicon adjusts and manages the rotation angle of the rotary to improve the reception accuracy.

In the front-end section, MOS FET is used for RF section and mixer stage.

Uniphase filter is adopted to achieve both high selectivity and low distortion rate.

A PLL IC is used in the MPX section.

Equipped with LED dial indicators and multi-pass indicators.
