Commentary

FM/AM tuner for multi-channel FM and high-quality broadcasting programs.

The front end uses a twin varactor diode and MOS FET to improve high sensitivity and stable reception performance. The IF circuit uses a ceramic filter with excellent delay characteristics to ensure low distortion and high selectivity.

Uses MPX-PLL and IC.

It has a built-in auto-tuning mechanism that scans automatically by pressing the UP/DOWN keys and stops automatically at the station receiving position.

Up to a total of 20 FM and AM broadcasting stations can be stored in memory at random. If you preset 1 ℃, you can easily call up and tune from 10 keys.
It also has a built-in backup function that does not erase the memory even in case of power failure.

The AM section uses a low-impedance AM loop antenna, and the IF stage is composed of a 3-terminal ceramic filter and coil.
