  • SONY ST-V5

  • ¥ 33,000 (around 1983)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner with compact design.

Up to 8 FM/AM stations can be preset at random.
In addition, the multi-process memory makes it possible to store settings such as FM mode and muting ON/OFF.

Equipped with a memory scanning function, you can call up preset stations one after another at approximately 4-second intervals and check the program contents.

It has adopted a centralized display with FL tube that can tell the setting state at a glance.

Equipped with a 3-point LED signal indicator.
