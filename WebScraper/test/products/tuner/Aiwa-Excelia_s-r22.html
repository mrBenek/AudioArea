Commentary

FM/AM tuner of mini component my Pace5 series.

It uses a digital tuning system and displays the reception frequency accurately in numbers.
It is a method to check the actually received frequency based on the crystal oscillation with excellent frequency accuracy, and prevents the shift between the dial face and the received frequency.

The front-end high-frequency amplifier uses a dual-gate MOS-FET with excellent noise characteristics to improve sensitivity, eliminate interference, and improve selectivity.

PLL-IC is used for the MPX circuit.

Equipped with a 5-point LED bar display signal meter using red LED.

Equipped with a high-blend circuit that cuts out high-frequency noise.

It is equipped with a muting circuit that cuts out pop noise during detuning and unpleasant inter-station noise during tuning.

Equipped with an AFC circuit to prevent reception error.

A 12 VDC power supply can be used.
