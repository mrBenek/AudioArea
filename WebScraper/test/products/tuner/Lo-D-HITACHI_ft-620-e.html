  • Lo-D FT-620

Commentary

AM/FM stereo tuner with sensor auto-lock tuning mechanism.

The front end is equipped with a 4-row frequency number line type varicon and a low-noise dual-gate MOS FET sensitivity.

The IF amplifier circuit uses four stage differential amplification by IC and three ceramic filter elements with excellent phase characteristics to provide high level limiter characteristics and high selectivity.
In addition, wide-band quadrature detection is adopted in the detection stage to realize low distortion and improve the capture ratio.

It is equipped with a sensor, auto-lock and tuning mechanism to accurately maintain the best receiving point.
This auto-lock mechanism catches radio waves at the best reception point, and once radio waves are received, it can always maintain the reception frequency. In addition, it can receive and maintain various characteristics such as distortion and separation in the best condition and stably.
This is due to the automatic control function of the IC. During this automatic lock, the dial indicator lights in red to indicate that the best point has been received and that stability has been maintained.
If the tuning knob is touched during tuning, the auto-lock mechanism will be released and manual tuning can be performed using the center tuning meter.

The MPX circuit uses a PLL (Phase Locked Loop) circuit based on ICHA 1156.

It is equipped with muting circuit by lead relay and IC.

The AM tuner section is designed to reduce noise.

It is equipped with an MPX noise filter, FM multipath terminal and band indicator.

Each AM/FM has a level adjustment mechanism.

Equipped with 4-ch out terminal.

The wood case in the picture was sold as an optional item sold separately.
