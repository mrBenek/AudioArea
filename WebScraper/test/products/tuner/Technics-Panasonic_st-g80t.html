Commentary

FM/AM tuner equipped with functions such as program timer.

A linear switching MPX IC is mounted to eliminate sound quality degradation due to nonlinear distortion and phase shift.
As a result, the switching section is greatly simplified, and the linearity is remarkably improved by constructing the switching section of the dynamic side without nonlinear elements.

A classAA circuit is mounted in the post-amplifier section, and voltage amplification in an ideal A-class state is realized by installing a special current drive amplifier, thereby eliminating adverse effects from connected cables and amplifiers.

Equipped with an auto-IF circuit, a microcomputer automatically switches between a normal circuit that gives the highest priority to sound quality and has high selectivity and a super narrow circuit that has narrow band and high selectivity and is resistant to adjacent station interference, according to disturbance waves and input signal levels, enabling optimum reception at all times.

Equipped with a signal fidelity indicator.
When this indicator lights up, high-quality air check is possible.

It is equipped with a program timer with 2 systems of daily operation and once operation.

Equipped with a quartz trek level check function, it can output a 333 Hz reference signal with an accuracy of 99.999%.

A total of 16 FM/AM random access memories are installed.

Equipped with auto-scan tuning and auto-memory function.
