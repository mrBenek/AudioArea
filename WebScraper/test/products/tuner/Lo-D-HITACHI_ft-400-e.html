  • HITACHI/Lo-D FT-400

  • ¥ 49,000 (around 1974)

Commentary

AM/FM stereo tuner for color-free sound.

A 4-pole MOS FET is used in the front end of the FM tuner to achieve high sensitivity and low noise characteristics.
In addition, mechanical filters and monolithic ICs are used in the IF section to improve various characteristics.

The FM multi-circuit uses a monolithic IC to improve low distortion characteristics and separation.

Equipped with an FM muting circuit, noise between stations can be reduced.

Equipped with an adapter terminal for FM4ch broadcasting.
