  • Technics ST-GT70

  • ¥ 28,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

Concise G Series FM/AM tuner that supports AM stereo.

Quartz synthesizer system is adopted.
Equipped with automatic switching function of auto FM and IF band.

Aluminum panel of 7 mm thickness is adopted for the case.

The AM tuner section supports AM stereo.

Equipped with an everyday timer and sleep timer function.

Up to 12 FM/AM stations can be preset.

You can switch between manual tuning and automatic tuning.
