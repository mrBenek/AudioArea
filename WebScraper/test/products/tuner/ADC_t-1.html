  • Audio Dynamics T-1

  • ¥ 40,000 (around the mid-1980s?)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner of the Eurostyle component series.

Quartz synthesizer system is adopted.

Ten FM/AM stations can be preset / stored.
Also, memory can be retained even when power is turned off by battery backup method.

The channel selection can be done quickly with the 3-speed tuning button.

Equipped with Schutz noise reduction.
This circuit effectively cancels the noise according to the FM signal strength and the high-frequency component level, and realizes clearer stereo reception.
