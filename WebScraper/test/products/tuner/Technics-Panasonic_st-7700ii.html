  • Technics ST-7700II

  • ¥ 37,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner with realistic playback capability even at 300% modulation.

The front end uses FM4-row varicon and high-frequency 1-stage amplification to minimize the effect of Radio noises with high sensitivity.

The newly developed super linear IC has been incorporated to improve the regenerative capacity in case of overmodulation.

The IF stage is designed with an emphasis on group delay characteristics.

The MPX section is equipped with a PLL / MPX demodulation circuit.

Equipped with a deviation / signal meter that can confirm the degree of modulation.
