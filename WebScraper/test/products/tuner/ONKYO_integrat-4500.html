  • ONKYO Integra T-4500

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

Integra GT series FM/AM tuners.

High sensitivity is realized by adopting a low-noise FET in the front end part, and it is strong for large input and sufficiently prevents spurious interference by combining with a 3-row varicon.

The IF stage uses a 4-element linear phase type ceramic filter.

PLL IC is used in the MPX section, and the stability of separation is improved by eliminating the coil.

Low pass filter with high attenuation of up to 65 dB carrier leak reduces noise.

A full-scale power supply circuit and a 2-stage direct-coupled output circuit are used for the output circuit.

It is designed to allow for overmodulation of broadcasting stations, and there is no degradation of sound quality even when overmodulation occurs.

Uses a 2-meter system consisting of a signal meter and a tuning meter.

Equipped with an FM75 Ω antenna terminal.

Equipped with muting and noise filter.

Equipped with an AM bar antenna.
