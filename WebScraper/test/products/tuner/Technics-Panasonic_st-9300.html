Commentary

9000 series FM stereo tuner.

The front-end section is equipped with a 7-row frequency linear variable capacitor dedicated to FM. In addition to three double-tuned circuits with two stages of RF amplification using a dual-gate 4-pole MOS-type FET, a highly stable oscillator circuit, and a full-scale AGC, it achieves excellent performance.

The IF section has a circuit configuration in which the signal system and control system are independent.
Two group delay flat ceramic filters are used for the signal system, and a 6-stage differential configuration including three ICs provides more faithful waveform transmission and high selectivity.

The MPX stereo demodulation circuit uses a PLL system unique to Technics.

The AF part improves the stability while reducing the distortion factor by the stabilized positive and negative two power supply system.

Equipped with 2-level selectable FM muting.

MPX High Blend has an AUTO position.

The selector has 3 positions for stereo only.

An oscilloscope terminal for multi-path detection is mounted.

Equipped with FM4ch MPX out terminal.
