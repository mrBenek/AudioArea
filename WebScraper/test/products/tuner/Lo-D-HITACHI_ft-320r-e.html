Commentary

This FM/AM tuner pursues basic performance and operability.

A wide-band AGC is adopted in the front end section to improve tuning characteristics and interference rejection capability.

The IF section uses a combination of a wide-band 4-element ceramic filter with good phase characteristics and a narrow-band tuning coil to compensate for slight phase waviness.

A PLL circuit is used for the MPX demodulation circuit.
The internal oscillator of the PLL uses an epoxy-coated Styrofoam capacitor, which is stable against temperature and humidity, to achieve stable operation.

The RF coil uses a special mold type that is stable against environmental changes.

The AM IF circuit uses a triple-tuned IF filter to improve selectivity.

Uses a 2-meter system for signal and tuning.

Equipped with an MPX noise filter, it can effectively cut the high-frequency noise of the FM stereo broadcast transmission.

Equipped with a low-pass filter.

Equipped with FM muting and FM indicator.

Ferrite antenna is used for AM antenna.
