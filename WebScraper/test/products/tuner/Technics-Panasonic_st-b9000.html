  • Technics ST-B9000

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1988)

Commentary

A mini-component size BS tuner that can be used for system upgrades with intelligent components.

Equipped with 2 DAC, 8 x oversampling digital filter.

Equipped with two digital output terminals that are coaxial with the optical system.

Equipped with a 2-channel video terminal, bit stream terminal and HDTV terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
