  • SONY ST-A3

  • ¥ 32,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner that uses a uniphase filter to achieve high selectivity and low distortion.

A FET is used for the front end part and it is independent from the substrate to improve stability.
A PLL / IC is used in the MPX circuit to improve separation characteristics and distortion.

Both FM and AM are equipped with a 230 mm long dial scale with equally spaced graduations.
