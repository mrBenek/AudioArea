  • ¥ 55,000 (released in 1991)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner with advanced capabilities as a receiver and enhanced comfort.

Newly developed GIC filter is used for the MPX filter.

Radial power supply is used to connect power supply and signal flow to front-end, IF, DET, MPX and AM circuits with the shortest distance, and ground is also grounded at one point.

Since sound quality is important, the microcomputer is stopped during reception to reduce noise.

It is equipped with a station name display function that shows the station name being received on the display.

Multi-process memory is used and ANT, RF Mode, IF BAND, FM MODE, MUTING, frequency, BAND, station name, etc. can be stored.

There are 2 variations, gold and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
