  • Marantz Model105B

  • ¥ 38,000 (around 1974)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner with low cost design and high quality.

Marantz original gyro-touch tuning is adopted.

An FET is used for the RF amplifier of the FM tuner section, and a wide-band, high-selectivity ceramic filter is used for the IF circuit.

In order to support 4-channel broadcasting, it is equipped with an クォードラジカル out port.

A walnut cabinet and an adapter for rack mounting were sold separately as options.
