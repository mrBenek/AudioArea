  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A synthesizer tuner that realizes faithful waveform transmission up to the direct current region by DC circuit configuration and high operability by microcomputer.

A DC peak sample-and-hold MPX circuit is mounted, and combined with the effect of DC, the separation of 60 dB, which is the same as 1 kHz, is achieved even at 20 Hz.

It is equipped with a pilot signal canceler with an auto level adjuster to realize flat frequency characteristics over a wide band.

It is equipped with a jitter distortion elimination circuit to clear the high frequency.

Our proprietary high-integrated IC sets the reference frequency to 25 kHz, which is outside the audible band, to prevent adverse effects on music signals.
In addition, a unique swallow in counter system is adopted, and by controlling the 1/16 and 1/17 frequency dividers with a prescaler controller, spurious interference within the broadcast band is suppressed.

The IF stage is equipped with a super narrow IF position to obtain excellent adjacent station interference rejection capability.

It is equipped with a 4-bit 2 k microcomputer and features such as 16 FM/AM random access memory, auto-scan tuning, and auto-memory.

Equipped with multi-functional digital FL display.

Equipped with 5 LED signal strength indicator which is also used as multi-pass indicator.

Equipped with 9 kHz AM tuner section.

The gold capacitor enables memory backup without dry batteries.

Other than silver, there was also BlackCar Ra model.
