Commentary

FM/AM tuner with audio IC.

A newly developed junction FET is used in the high-frequency amplifier circuit, and excellent characteristics are obtained by stable local oscillation circuit and FET mixer circuit.

The IF section employs a newly developed 2-element one package solid state filter, Hi-Fi IC, and symmetric diode limiter.
In addition, the FM discriminator uses a non-adjustable solid-state discriminator.

The MPX stereo separation circuit employs a high integration IC and a non-adjusting coil unit exclusively for IC.

The AM tuner section consists of one stage high-frequency amplifier circuit and uses a 3-row variable capacitor, and the IF section uses a high-selectivity triple-tune IFT.

The front panel control on the front panel allows you to adjust the level when switching to another source.

Large dial and indicator with lamp are adopted.

Equipped with a newly developed muting switch.

Equipped with 2 sets of output terminals, fixed and variable.

Equipped with a high blend switch.
