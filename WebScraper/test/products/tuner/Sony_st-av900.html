  • SONY ST-AV900

  • ¥ 50,700 (around 1989)

Commentary

An audio-visual tuner capable of receiving not only FM/AM but also VHF 1-12ch, UHF 13-62ch, and CATV C13ch-C35ch.

When combined with the TA-AV900D, it is possible to perform a TV scan that displays TV programs as still images one after another on a nine part multi-screen.

There are 2 kinds of variations, black and titanium gray.

Equipped with an RF output terminal that can be connected to a TV without video input.

The TV channel number can be displayed on the screen.

A total of 30 TV/FM/AM stations can be preset tuned.

Wireless remote control is included.
