Audio Database

  • SONY ST-JX2

  • ¥ 26,500 (around 1981)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner adopting a slim design with a panel height of 45 mm.

Equipped with a stereo / muting switch that enables simultaneous switching between the FM stereo mode and the muting mechanism that cuts out unpleasant inter-station noise.

Advanced ICs are used in the IF stage and MPX stage of the FM tuner section.

Equipped with FM tuning indicator.

Equipped with a 5-point LED signal indicator that turns on according to the strength of radio waves.

Equipped with FM/AM band indicator.
