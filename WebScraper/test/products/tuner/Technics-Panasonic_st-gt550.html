Commentary

FM/AM tuner for AM stereo.

Equipped with an auto IF selector to quickly respond to changes in radio wave conditions.
This function automatically narrows the IF band to give priority to sensitivity when disturbance is strong, and automatically switches the IF band to give priority to sound quality when there is no disturbance.

A Local/DX switching circuit is used to attenuate radio waves at the antenna input.
This prevents the front end of the tuner from becoming saturated in areas where strong radio waves are received, such as near transmission stations, and ensures stable FM reception.

Class AA circuit is mounted in the audio output amplifier section.

Equipped with 2 dB step FM signal strength display function.

AM stereo is supported.

Wireless remote control is included.
