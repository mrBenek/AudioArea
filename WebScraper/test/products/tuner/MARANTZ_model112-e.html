  • Marantz Model112

  • ¥ 49,900 (released in 1976)

Commentary

An FM/AM tuner that inherits the basic performance of the Model125 and has a compact front panel size to match the pre-main amp Models 1070 and 1040.

The FM front-end uses MOS-FET, and the IF circuit consists of one IC, three transistors, and three 2-element ceramic filters.

PLL is used in the MPX section, and in addition, mono / stereo automatic switching is provided.

The front end, IF stage and MPX circuit are each laid out on a separate board and covered by a completely shielded case.
This also passes the América Futebol Clube C standard for Radio noises.

The wood cabinet in the picture was sold as an option sold separately.
Also, there was an optional rack mount adapter sold separately.
