Commentary

An FM/AM tuner designed with sound quality, design and functions to be combined with the Integra A-550.

Variable output terminal and 4-ch detection output terminal are mounted.

It is equipped with functions to improve sound quality, such as FM muting and SCA filters that are effective in eliminating inter-station noise.
