Audio Database

  • ONKYO Integra MT-1000EX

  • ¥ 33,000 (released in 1987)

Commentary

Multi-functional FM/AM tuner for air check.

It uses a PLL digital synthesizer system.

The built-in quartz timer is within ± 15 seconds per month. Two on/off programs can be used for recording and timer playback.

8 preset stations for FM and AM can be set.
