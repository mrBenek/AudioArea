  • ONKYO Integra T-425AT

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1998)

Commentary

An FM/AM stereo tuner with a front-end developed in-house and improved reception functions.

Quartz synthesizer system is adopted to improve sensitivity, selectivity and stability.

Equipped with an APR system that automatically switches the IF band according to the radio wave condition at the time of FM reception.

AM stereo reception is available.

Up to 40 FM/AM stations can be stored in memory.
Ten stations can be registered for each group from A to D. You can use it to store the stations by genre or by user.

Up to store up to 8 letters of alphabets and numbers in the preset channel.

Equipped with 4 weekly timers : weekday, weekend, once, and sleep, it is possible to play an alarm clock or record your absence.

Devices connected to the AC outlet can also be turned ON/OFF together.

It comes with a remote control for main operation.
