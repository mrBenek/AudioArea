  • SONY ST-S3000

  • ¥ 33,000 (released in 1997)

Commentary

Compact size FM/AM stereo tuner with high quality and ease of use.

Equipped with Super Area Call function for automatic presetting.
With this function, the frequency and station name are stored in advance, and you can operations simply by selecting the prefecture name of the location to be used.
Up to 30 random FM/AM memories are available.

Equipped with a menu entry system, preset stations can be replaced or deleted according to your preference.

Compatible with AM stereo broadcast transmission.

The chassis is a mono-box structure that combines aluminum and steel to form a single box, achieving extremely high rigidity.

An eccentric insulator is used for the leg part to reduce the effect of vibration from the floor.

The connection terminal is gold-plated, making it resistant to aging.
In addition, a very thick AC cable is used for the power cable, which is designed with an emphasis on sound quality.

It is equipped with a detection output terminal, and by connecting the text display unit CTD-S100 (sold separately), you can enjoy the text information of FM text multiplex broadcasting.
