  • Technics ST-9700

  • ¥ 250,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

An FM stereo tuner equipped with a 9-row varicon dedicated to FM.

It achieves input resistance without saturation even at input 140 db (= 10 v).

It is equipped with Technics original servo tuning system which compensates for frequency shift and tuning shift.

The MPX section is equipped with an MPX demodulation circuit using a double PLL system to realize excellent frequency characteristics.

The low-cut filter is designed to operate when the tuning knob is touched to prevent shock sound during detuning.
