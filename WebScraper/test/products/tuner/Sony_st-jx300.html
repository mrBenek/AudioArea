  • SONY ST-JX300

  • ¥ 34,000 (released in 1983)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner with liquid crystal display.

Five FM/AM stations can be preset.

It is equipped with a central LCD display with back-up lighting that displays the reception band, reception frequency, muting, preset memory number, etc. all together.

Equipped with the last station memory, it stores the stations that were received until just before the power was turned off, and when the power is turned on again, the station can be received continuously.
