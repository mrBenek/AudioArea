  • Technics ST-S65V

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

A quartz synthesizer tuner that can receive TV audio in addition to FM/AM broadcasting.

It is equipped with preset functions for a total of 24 stations, 8 each for FM/AM/TV.
The three sources can be selected with the FM, AM and TV selectors, and the select button is push-type.

Equipped with auto-tuning.
Manual tuning is also available.

It is equipped with an auto memory function that enables you to set a preset by pressing the memory button.

Digitally displays the received frequency and TV channel.
