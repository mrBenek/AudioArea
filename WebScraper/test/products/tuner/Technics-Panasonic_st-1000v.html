  • Technics ST-1000V

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A TV sound multiplex tuner equipped with a video connector.

It uses an electronic tuner that can be preset for 10 UHF / VHF stations.

It has a built-in program automatic switching circuit. It automatically detects the pilot signal according to the broadcast, determines whether it is stereo broadcast transmission or 2-language broadcast and automatically switches it.
In addition, it is equipped with a program manual switching mode switch, and if it is 2 languages, you can select the language you want to listen to.
You can enjoy two languages at the same time.
