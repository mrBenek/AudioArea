  • Technics ST-7600

  • ¥ 37,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner of the same size and design as the SU 7600.

In the IF stage, we have adopted a flat delay filter in order to pursue waveform transmission characteristics.
The MPX circuit uses a PLL circuit configuration to supply switching signals that are superior in humidity, temperature, and aging.

A special low-pass filter with a phase delay characteristic of -5.5 μ sec is used for the low-pass filter.

Two FETs are used for the front end part.

An IF band selection switch is mounted so that measures can be taken according to the radio wave condition.
You can switch between Normal and Narrow depending on the situation.

The printed circuit board is divided into two sheets and a vertical rough surface arranged perpendicularly to the bottom plate is adopted.
This prevents the IF stage from becoming unstable due to feedback through the bottom plate. At the same time, high-frequency components such as the front end and IF stage and low-frequency components such as power supply, AF and MPX are separated from each other, eliminating mutual interference.

Uses a 2-meter system consisting of a signal meter and a tuning meter.

Equipped with output level control, muting, high blend switch, etc.

There was a wood case sold separately.
