  • Pioneer T-6000

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

The tape deck is a deluxe version with the same mechanism as the T-5000 and reinforced with the recording / re-amplifier, attached mechanism and attached circuit.

Each channel is equipped with an independent level meter.

Equipped with an auto reverse cancel switch.

Equipped with an automatic reverse reciprocating recording and playback mechanism using sensing foils.
Manual switching is also possible.

Equipped with reverse rotation release switch.

Equipped with an output level regulator.

Equipped with a pinch roller rising mechanism.

Equipped with an auto stopper.

The output level can be adjusted.

Comes with a stand that can be used horizontally and vertically.

Comes with a removable acrylic hood.

No. 7 easy loading reel is included.
