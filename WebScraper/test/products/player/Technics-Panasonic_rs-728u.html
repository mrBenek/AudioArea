  • Technics RS-728U

  • ¥ 69,800 (Released in September 1973)

Commentary

A 4-track multichannel tape deck with 4-ch / 2-ch switching switches.

The 4-ch / 2-ch switch allows you to monitor a recorded track while recording to a different track in a 2-ch stereo.

Uses a combination head with no phase shift.

Equipped with a microphone / line input selector.

Silicon transistors are used in the electrical circuit.

Equipped with auto shut-off function.
