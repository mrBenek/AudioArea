Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC L-F77

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

An automatic music selection player equipped with an electroservo arm controlled by a microcomputer.

You can start up to 10 songs and skip to the next band without touching the arm.

Low mass straight pipe arm and coreless motor are adopted.

MD 1039 e is used for the cartridge.

H Designed for full remote control operation. Remote control unit (sold separately) allows remote operation.
