Audio Database

  • Pioneer PD-X909M

  • ¥ 74,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

A CD changer that can play 6 CDs continuously in a magazine system.

It employs a disk stabilizer that reduces disk resonance and a linear servo system that prevents sound skipping due to scratches or dirt on the disk.

It has a random play function that you can listen to with microcomputer automatic music selection.

It is equipped with a direct programming function that can be programmed immediately while listening to the music.

Equipped with an FL graphic display that shows the disc being played.

6-piece performance magazine and 1-piece performance magazine are included.

There was a wireless remote control unit with 10 keys sold separately.
