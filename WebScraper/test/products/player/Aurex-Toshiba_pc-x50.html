  • Aurex PC-X50

  • ¥ 65,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with recording and remixing mechanism.

The line and microphone level adjustments are independent of each other, so you can mix while recording or play mixing tape and microphone.
Switching between recording and playback mixing is automatic, linked with the operation buttons.

Uses a soft touch mechanism.

Equipped with a memory counter.

Equipped with a + 6 dB peak indicator.

Equipped with an editor volume for both faders.

Equipped with a 3-step switching type tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with soft eject & easy take-out mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

A highly integrated multi-functional linear IC is adopted to achieve low noise and low distortion.

Equipped with back and half lamps, it is easy to check the remaining amount of the tape.
