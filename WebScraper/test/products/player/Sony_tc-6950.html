  • SONY TC-6950

  • ¥ 159,000 (released in 1974)

Commentary

A 3-motor, 6-head tape deck capable of repeat playback in addition to recording / playback Oatley berth functions.

Uses a direct capstan drive system with AC servo motor.
The center capstan system provides a symmetrical mechanism.

It is a 6-head configuration using an F & F head.

Uses direct coupling method by FET amplifier.

A feather touch function switch with logical control is adopted.

Equipped with a tape selector with independent bias and equalizer switching.

Microphone / line mixing is possible.

Equipped with a 20 dB microphone attenuator.

It is equipped with playback volume with center click.
