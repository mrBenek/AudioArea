  • SONY CDP-301V

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

A CD/CDV player capable of playing CDV.

The digital filter uses a 4-times oversampling digital filter. By increasing the sampling frequency of the digital signal to 4 times before D/A conversion, the attenuation characteristics of the analog filter after D/A conversion can be moderated, ensuring good phase characteristics.

The D/A converter uses an independent dual D/A converter on the left and right sides to eliminate the phase shift between channels.
In addition, the analog circuit after D/A conversion is also twin-mono configuration, eliminating mutual interference.

The use of a floating optical mechanism and a large insulator reduces the deterioration of sound quality caused by unwanted vibration.

The pickup section is equipped with a newly developed laser pickup.
The photodetector uses an OE (Optical Electric) IC that integrates the detector and amplifier into a single chip, achieving a horizontal resolution of 410 lines and a luminance signal SN ratio of 46 dB.

Equipped with independent time base collectors (TBCs) for luminance signal and color signal respectively, the two step strict time base correction reduces edge blur due to color shift.

It has a screen message function and can display operation information on the TV screen.

Equipped with program edit function, Delhi and shuffle function, CDV direct button, one touch direct selection with 20 keys, 20-track program (RMS) function, 5-mode repeat of 1 track / all track / part (remote control only) / program / shuffle function.

Equipped with a VHF/AV terminal, it can be connected to various TVs.

Wireless remote control is included.
