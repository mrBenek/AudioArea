  • Technics RS-6

  • ¥ 52,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

A record jacket size stereo cassette deck equipped with the dbx system.

As a noise reduction system, the dbx system is installed in addition to the Dolby NR.

The mechanism uses a 2-motor system with dedicated motors for the capstan and reel, and is controlled by computer logic control.

It supports synchronous recording, and you can synchronize the start of the player's performance with the start of the deck recording.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Equipped with a music selector and auto REC mute.
