Commentary

Micro cassette deck with full logic mechanism.

Equipped with a music scanning mechanism.

The noise reduction system is equipped with ANRS (Dolby B NR).

Equipped with 2 speed switching function, it can be used for long play.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with 2-color LED peak meter.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism.

It has headphone and microphone terminals.
