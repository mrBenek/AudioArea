Commentary

Compact component FILL series MD recorder.

It is equipped with Pioneer's original ARTIST (Advanced Real Time Signal Tuning) system.
Because conventional MD compresses music signals with constant parameters, subtle music components are lost. The ARTIST system performs real-time frequency analysis of input signals, increases the amount of data allocated to bands with a lot of music signals, and makes maximum use of the limited number of data, thus realizing faithful and realistic MD recording with the recording source.

It is equipped with Legato link conversion.
This function realizes more natural MD sound by restoring the music component of 20 kHz or higher based on the recorded signal and assuming the original signal.

Equipped with a jog dial, it realizes speedy MD editing.

Equipped with an easy name list function, you can enter words easily just by selecting words with the jog dial.

Equipped with two optical digital inputs, one optical digital output, and one analog output.

The terminal for recording and entering the dormitory is mounted on the front panel, and MD editing can be done easily from a portable MD.

Equipped with a sampling rate converter.

Wireless remote control is included.
