  • Pioneer PL-M340

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Compact DD full auto player with excellent space factor.

The drive motor is equipped with a high-torque brushless DC servo Hall motor using Pioneer's original Hall element.

The cabinet uses a coaxial suspension cabinet with excellent howling characteristics.

The tone arm adopts a mass concentration system tone arm.

The full auto mechanism is a 2-motor system that uses a dedicated motor for arm drive.

A front-operated type has been adopted to improve usability.
