Commentary

3-head cassette deck.

The mechanism is equipped with a pulse servo DD motor.
Since the motor shaft is the capstan shaft capstan shaft itself that drives the tape, so a large margin is obtained for flutter loss and wow transmission. In addition, the unique pulse servo control ensures high performance against tape load fluctuations, temperature changes, and changes over time.

The mechanism uses a closed-loop dual capstan system to stabilize high tension and improve sound quality such as stable head touch, uneven rotation and noise reduction during rotation.
In addition, the use of a zinc die-cast block head base reduces the influence of external vibration.

Equipped with Dolby HX-PRO, it is possible to expand the dynamic range of high frequency.
The recording bias frequency is set to a high bias of 210 kHz.

The center mechanism is used for the internal layout to achieve weight balance and separation of analog and digital signals.
To further enhance the separation effect, the operation key of the right block and the digital control circuit of the left block are separated. The operation key is an analog voltage that converts the control circuit from A/D to prevent digital noise from entering the analog circuit.

Rounded panel with excellent vibration resistance is used for shearing.

Equipped with REC calibration function and built-in test signal oscillation circuit.

Equipped with CD direct input.

A Dolby B/C is installed as a noise reduction system.

Gold-plated input / output terminal is mounted.

Linear counter is adopted.

Equipped with multi-music scanning function.

An electric cassette door is adopted.

Equipped with cassette shell stabilizer.

Wireless remote control is included.
