Commentary

Double reverse cassette deck with twin Dolby HX PRO.

The Dolby HX PRO is installed on both the I and II decks to support digital sources.
By always detecting and adjusting the appropriate bias amount according to the amount of the high-frequency component contained in the source, the high-frequency characteristics are greatly improved.

It is equipped with a cassette stabilizer that suppresses vibration of the cassette half due to rotational vibration of the motor, vibration from the outside, sound pressure, etc.
Half pressure of special resin is set on the deck body side, and with the cassette holder closed, the stabilizer is designed to firmly hold the cassette half from both sides.
In addition, it uses a double lock mechanism that locks the entire cassette holder from both sides, and suppresses modulation noise caused by vibration by absorbing vibration.

A honeycomb chassis and large insulator are mounted to prevent vibration to the deck body.

The recording / reproducing head uses a laser amorphous head that uses LC-OFC (linear crystalline oxygen-free copper) winding for the coil winding.
In addition, it uses a oxygen-free copper wire power cord with polarity indication that has high conductivity and reduces non-linearity distortion, and also uses a large-capacity electrolytic capacitor for audio.

A large FL display with various indicators is placed in the center of the front panel.

Equipped with an I / II independent Dolby NR switch that allows you to select the type of Dolby NR for each deck I / II.

It is equipped with a parallel recording function that can record one source on two tapes simultaneously.

Equipped with twin recording and playback quick reverse & relay recording capable of continuous 3 hours recording.

Deck I and II are equipped with independent 2-mode electronic counters.

It is equipped with functions such as skip function that automatically detects and fast-forwards unrecorded sections, an auto REC mute function that creates a four second silent section and automatically enters the recording pause state, a double-speed copy function that enables quick and easy dubbing of tapes, and a synchro-start and synchro-stop function.

It is equipped with a skipping selection function of 15 songs before and after which you can easily find the song you want to listen.

It is equipped with a CD-DECK synchro function, and in combination with a Pioneer CD player, it is possible to perform synchronized recording start and auto trackback recording with one touch.

Wireless remote control is included.
