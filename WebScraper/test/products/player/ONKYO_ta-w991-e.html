  • ONKYO TA-W991

  • ¥ 82,800 (around 1984)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with double auto-reverse configuration.

It is a double configuration of Tape 1 (playback) and Tape 2 (recording and playback), both of which are Oatley burstable.
With a C-90 tape, it is possible to dub / record for 90 minutes in a round trip.

Equipped with a reverse relay playback function, it repeats four times in the order of Tape 1 (A → B) → Tape 2 (A → B), so reverse relay playback for 12 hours is possible with two C-90 tapes.

The head part uses a mechanism in which a hard permalloy head and an erasing head are integrated.
In addition, a transparent reader tape sensor is mounted for detecting the inversion position.

Each deck has a 3-motor configuration and all operations are controlled by a computer to achieve stable operation.

Equipped with Dolby C System with excellent noise reduction effect.

Equipped with synchronized dubbing function, microphone mixing recording, full-auto tape selector, auto space rec mute, timer standby mechanism, etc.

Tape 1 is equipped with an automatic music search AMCS that repeats 10-second cue playback and fast forward.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Three types are available : one way / round-trip for recording mode, and one way / round-trip / four time reverse relay for playback mode.
If you set the reverse relay four times when only one side contains a tape, reverse repeat playback will be performed eight times.

Equipped with a synchronous recording function compatible with CP-890R, CP-990R, PL-150, PL-220, PL-330R and PL-350R.
