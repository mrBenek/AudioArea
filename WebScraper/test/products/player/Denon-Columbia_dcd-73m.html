  • ¥ 32,800 (around 1989)

Commentary

A compact CD player with a super linear converter.

The D/A converter uses Denon's original super linear converter to eliminate 0 cross distortion.
The digital filter uses a 4-times oversampling digital filter.

The effect of external vibration is reduced by floating the pickup drive part with special viscoelastic material and supporting it with a mechanical chassis molded with material with excellent impact resistance.

Extruded aluminum material is used for the front panel.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Compatible with 8 cm single CD.

It supports random play and program play by remote control operation.
You can also use the remote control to operate all songs / programs / A-B repeat functions and auto space functions.

Equipped with a display using FL Suga.

Wireless remote control is included.
This remote control is equipped with an auto-edit function that automatically identifies the break of the song closest to one half of the total recording time of the CD and displays the recording time in the first and second half.
