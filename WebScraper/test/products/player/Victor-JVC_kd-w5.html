  • VICTOR/JVC KD-W5

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

Based on the KD-W7 model, this is a double cassette deck in pursuit of usability.

Equipped with synchronized start and synchronized stop functions, dubbing is possible with one button.
The combination of Music-Scan and Auto Rec Mute allows for quick editing.

Equipped with double-speed dubbing function.

It is equipped with a karaoke & mixing simultaneous recording function. If you input sound from a microphone while playing music on A Deck, the contents of A Deck + Microphone are recorded on B Deck by operating dubbing.

A deck is equipped with a pitch control.

The mechanism part adopts ID mechanism and full logic control, and it is equipped with direct change function which can directly shift to any mode.

Equipped with continuous play function, it starts from A tape and automatically plays B → A → B alternately up to 15 times automatically.

Equipped with an auto-Rec Mute function that can automatically set a silent space of approximately 4 seconds by pressing once.

Equipped with ANRS / Dolby B NR.

Equipped with functions such as Music Scan (Deck A), Timer Start (Deck B), and Auto Tape Select (Deck A).
