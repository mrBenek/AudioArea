  • VICTOR/JVC TD-2380

  • ¥ 238,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

The highest quality tape deck is compatible with 38 cm tape and has the same basic specifications as the professional machine.

It uses an electronic logic control mechanism to improve usability and protect the tape.

The mechanism uses a 3-motor system.

The head uses a 4-head system with a 4-track playback function. The head uses an in-house developed Clonios head.

Equipped with a bias adjustment device, recording utilizing the performance of the tape is possible.

Equipped with a high linearity microphone amplifier, it is possible to record mixing of microphone and line.

Equipped with a 2-stage tape selector that supports SH tapes.

Equipped with a locked queuing lever.

Equipped with a one touch reel lock mechanism that does not require a reel cap.

Equipped with a reel size switching switch.

Equipped with left and right independent monitor switches.

Equipped with a recording mode selector.

It has an output volume.

Equipped with an AC outlet.

Equipped with a remote control terminal and can be connected to a remote control.
