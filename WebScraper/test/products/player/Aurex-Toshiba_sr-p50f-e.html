  • Aurex SR-P50F

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

A full auto player that adopts quartz lock DD system.

Quartz lock direct drive system is adopted, and the FG servo motor provides excellent rotational accuracy of quartz crystal accuracy.
In addition, the newly designed servo circuit achieves high-speed response.

Straight pipe arm is used for the tone arm.

The cabinet is made of special material that is resistant to howling. In addition, resonance is suppressed by integrating the turntable center and the arm.

In addition to the full auto function by the front button operation, auto repeat is also possible.

The dust cover was sold separately.
