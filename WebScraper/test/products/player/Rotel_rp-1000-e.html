Audio Database

  • ROTEL RP-1000

  • ¥ 24,500 (around 1973)

Commentary

A record player that pursues high performance at low cost.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used for the drive part.

An S-shaped tone arm is used for the tone arm. In addition, an oil dump type arm lifter is mounted.

A high-compliance type MM cartridge is used.

Equipped with auto-return and auto-cut mechanism.
