Audio Database

  • SONY CDP-33

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

A slim 355 mm wide and 70 mm thick CD player.

It is equipped with the RMS function of up to 16 songs that you can program the songs you want to listen to in your favorite order.

Newly developed compact pickups and high-performance LSIs for CDs are used.

In addition to one or all songs, repeat playback is possible with the RMS program.

It has an AMS function that makes it easy to find the beginning of a song.

Equipped with 2-speed manual search.

Push loading function.

Equipped with a headphone port with variable output.

A remote control is included.
