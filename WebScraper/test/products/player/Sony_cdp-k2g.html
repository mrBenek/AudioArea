  • SONY CDP-K2G

  • ¥ 42,800 (released in 1991)

Commentary

A CD player equipped with CD graphics function and specialized in karaoke function.

It has CD graphics capability and supports CD-G discs.

Equipped with 2 microphone inputs for duet and instrument training.

Equipped with a level adjustment function.

Equipped with a digital echo function.

It has a Karaoke-Pon function, and you can switch ordinary songs to Karaoke.

Equipped with a pitch control function.

The 8fs18-bit D/A conversion system is installed in the D/A conversion part.

The servo circuit is equipped with S Servo III and digital CLV servo.

Equipped with a custom edit function, it has three functions : program edit, variable fade and manual fader.

Program Edit allows you to manually edit your favorite songs in any order.
With the AMS key or direct key, you can program your favorite songs in any order while checking the accumulated time. If you turn on Auto Space at this time, you can edit each song by adding a 3-second blank to the playing time. When you press the pause button, you can program the A side and then the B side together.

The manual fader allows you to fade in and fade out at the desired location.

You can set the fade-in / fade-out time for the variable fade from 2 seconds to 10 seconds in units of 1 second.


It is equipped with 99 music delete shuffle function, and you can leave only your favorite music and play it randomly.

It is equipped with an auto pause function that allows you to pause the performance for each song.

It is equipped with functions such as program performance of up to 24 songs, 5-mode repeat of one song, all songs, program, shuffle / shuffle / delete shuffle, and music search function of fast forward / fast backward while listening to a song.

The display section has a 20-track music calendar.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Wireless remote control is included.

There were a variety microphone ECM-V1 and drum pad DRP-3 as options sold separately.
The ECM-V1 1, you can change the tone of your voice by about one octave or change the voice of your computer or Yodel.
The DRP-3 is equipped with seven drum sounds and sound effects such as human shouts. You can also play your own rhythm in addition to 15 types of rhythm.
