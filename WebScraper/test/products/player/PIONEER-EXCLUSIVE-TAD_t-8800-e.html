Commentary

Top-of-the-line tape deck with 2-motor 6-head function.
A constant voltage power supply and carefully selected transistors are used, the motor arrangement and head shield are carefully selected, and excellent sound quality is achieved through stable bias oscillation, etc.

It is a two motor mechanism using a hysteresis synchronous motor for the drive of the capstan and a special induction motor for tape winding.
This eliminates uneven rotation and always feeds the tape with proper tape tension.

It can be reversed automatically by the sensing tape or by using the one touch push button.
It also comes with a cycle selector that allows you to choose from 4 actions including releasing the Oatley berth.

The use of a 2-motor system avoids excessive fast-forward motion, and does not make the back tension larger than necessary to emphasize tape protection.
In addition, it uses a combination head made by Noteronix Inc. of the United States that combines erasing and recording into one. By shortening the tape travel path (tape path), it reduces the possibility of damaging the tape.

The pinch roller is a unique easy loading mechanism that sinks into the body at Stop and wakes up at Play.
Since there is no obstacle when applying the tape, easy and reliable operation is possible.

Equipped with a tape selector that can select the optimum bias value according to the characteristics of the standard tape and L. H. tape.

Equipped with two line inputs.
The line 2 input is also located on the front panel and automatically disconnects the back circuit when the plug is inserted.
Line input and microphone input can be mixed.

The line output can be switched between fixed and variable levels.
You can also adjust the volume of your headphones freely.

The 3-head system allows simultaneous monitoring of the sound just recorded and the sound of the source by switching the switch.

Sound-on-sound is possible by superimposing sound on sound, with L playback to R recording and R playback to L recording.

Equipped with a track display device, you can see at a glance which track is running in which direction.

Micro switch is used for the operation mechanism, and all operations are possible with feather touch buttons.
The pose lever is a lever type with two steps that can be flipped and locked (stopped).

Since the Head cover can be opened and closed freely, it is easy to perform maintenance such as cleaning and degaussing of the head.

Equipped with a shut-off switch, the pinch roller automatically disengages when the tape is wound onto one reel to prevent accidental deformation caused by the tape remaining in contact with the capstan.

You can freely adjust the height of the reel so that the tape does not hit the reel.
In addition, it comes with a reel clamper that can be fixed by just hanging a reel and twisting a part of the shaft.

Equipped with a meter that can be raised obliquely even when used horizontally.

By using the separately sold remote control unit (PP-210), it can be operated remotely.
