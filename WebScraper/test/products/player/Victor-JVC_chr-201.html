Commentary

An 8-track player that can play cartridge-type 8-track music tapes.

Designed for durability and has passed the 10,000 hour running test.
In addition, it is equipped with a usage time meter up to 10,000 hours.

Designed for playback only, it automatically starts playing just by inserting a cartridge tape.

Uses a capacitor-type outer rotor motor with built-in special pairing.

Wide range is achieved by narrow gap finish head.

Equipped with automatic and manual program switching functions, a repeat unit, and a stereo / mono switch.

Equipped with a program indicator.

It has a headphone port.
