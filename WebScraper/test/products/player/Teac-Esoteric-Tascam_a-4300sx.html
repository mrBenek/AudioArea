  • TEAC A-4300SX

  • ¥ 149,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

This tape deck is the standard model of a four track deck.

4-track 2-channel recording and playback of 19cm/9.5 cm tapes are possible.

Uses a high hardness permalloy head.

Oatley bath regeneration by foil sensing is possible.
Also equipped with counter repeat function, auto repeat can be performed with counter preset.

Equipped with a recording muting mechanism.

Equipped with a timer mechanism that can record absence.

Equipped with a bias, equalizer, and level switching mechanism (3J mechanism).

Uses a quick lock type reel clamper.

There was a remote control mechanism sold separately.
