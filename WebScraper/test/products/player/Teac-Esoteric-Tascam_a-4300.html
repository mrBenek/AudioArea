Commentary

In addition to the 3J mechanism that improves the recording characteristics of the tape, this tape deck has improved operability, functionality, multi-purpose, etc.

It is equipped with a 3J mechanism (Just Bias/Just Equalizer/Just Level), an independent selector switch that provides the optimum bias and equalizer for low-noise and regular tapes, and a level selector switch that raises the recording level by 3 dB to take advantage of the 3-5dB dynamic margin of low-noise tapes.

Equipped with a double-scale expanded VU meter.
This meter is linked with a level selector switch so that the highest level can be selected between + 6 VU (High) and + 3 VU (Normal) depending on the tape.

The head uses a high-hardness permalloy head with improved gap linearity in consideration of core processing accuracy and assembly. Since this head is a hyperbolic type, the head touch is complete, and low-frequency disturbance due to contour effect (shape effect) is suppressed.
It also uses a 3-head system that can monitor the recorded contents as they are.

It is equipped with a pause button. If you press Rec and pause at the same time, recording is on standby, and if you press Play button, recording is started.
You can also make a follow-up recording by switching to the recording mode from the middle during playback.

Equipped with a counter repeat function, the counter can be preset to 0000 at any point where it is repeated, and you can listen automatically and repeatedly between the sensing foils for Oatley verse playback and the set point.

It is equipped with a memory marker, and if the optimum level is preset, there is no need to memorize the position of the volume every time.

It uses a quick lock reel clamper and can set the reel just by turning the shaft to the right.

In order to prevent the tape from being damaged due to the difference in the thickness of the reel, the reel table height adjustment mechanism is mounted.
It can be adjusted by turning the screw of the reel stand with a screwdriver.

It is equipped with a cue out connector, and the sound recorder (A-4070G or A-4070) will Oatley berth depending on the Oatley berth signal of the A-4300 player.

Equipped with two input / output terminals.

It uses an oil dump type tension arm that makes the rise of the tape smooth.

Microphone and line mixing recording is possible.

Stainless steel is used for the front panel.

Equipped with a shut-off arm that stops the mechanism automatically when the tape is finished winding.

It is equipped with a 4-digit index counter which is convenient for storing the position of the program and for starting the head.

There were remote controller and timer control adapter sold separately.
