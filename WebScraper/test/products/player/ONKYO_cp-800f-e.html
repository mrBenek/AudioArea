  • ONKYO CP-800F

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

Equipped with a microcomputer and an arm drive motor, this full-auto player realizes comfortable quick action.

For the tone arm, a straight-type tone arm is adopted in order to measure weight reduction and high rigidity, and low frequency resonance, internal strain and partial resonance such as warping of a record are suppressed.

Equipped with MM type cartridge as standard.

The drive system uses an electronic servo DD motor with stable rotational performance.

A 2-layer cabinet with vibration control structure is adopted to suppress vibration.

Dust cover is sold separately.
