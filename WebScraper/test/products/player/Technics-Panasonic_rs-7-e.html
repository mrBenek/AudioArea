  • Technics RS-7

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

2-motor computer logic concise deck.

The tape drive mechanism uses a highly accurate 2-motor system.
In addition, computer logic is used in the control section to ensure reliable operation.

Equipped with an auto tape selector that detects the tape type and automatically sets the optimum position.

Equipped with a music selector.

Synchronous recording with a record player (SL-6) is possible.

Equipped with a direct connector that can be easily connected to the Concise 77.
