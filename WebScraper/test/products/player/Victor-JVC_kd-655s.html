  • VICTOR/JVC KD-655S

  • ¥ 35,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

This is a stereo cassette deck made of high quality materials with a lean design.

Equipped with a 2-step switching type tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.
The new time constant has been adopted in the equalizer position for chrome tape to bring out the characteristics of chrome tape to the maximum.

There was a dust cover as an option sold separately.
