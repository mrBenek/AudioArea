  • ONKYO CP-890R

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

This is a full auto player with improved operability such as 9 random songs.

In addition to the conventional full auto functions (auto lead in, Oatley turn, auto reject, and Oatley Pete (all songs)), you can select up to eight random songs, including duplicate performances of the same song, or repeat one song.

For the tone arm, a high-precision straight type tone arm is adopted in consideration of light mass and high rigidity.
In addition, the cartridge is equipped with VM type as standard.

An electronic servo DD motor is used for the drive system and a 31 cm aluminum die-cast turntable is used for the turntable.
In addition, a double cabinet with vibration control structure is adopted in order to improve vibration control.

Equipped with synchronized recording terminal with stereo cassette deck TA-44X.

The dust cover was sold separately.

* When the cartridge other than the attached cartridge is attached, you cannot select a random song or repeat one song.
Also, if there are short records between songs or color records, the music selection mechanism may not work.
