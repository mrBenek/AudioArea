  • SONY PS-Q7

  • ¥ 35,000 (around 1983)

Commentary

Full auto player system with built-in FM transmitter and headphone amplifier.

Unlike normal record players, it realizes about 2 / 3 of the size of LP jacket.

Equipped with full auto function by auto start and Oatley turn.

Uses a straight-tone arm with a dynamically balanced needle pressure application method that does not change the needle pressure even when the player is set obliquely.
In addition, a MM type cartridge is installed in the cartridge.

Direct drive system with linear BSL motor is BSL motor.
Also, the flat motor is used as a turntable.

Equipped with a one touch selector that integrates 33 and 45 rotation speed switching and 30 cm and 17 cm tone arm lead-in position switching.

Since it has a built-in headphone amplifier, you can directly enjoy record performance by connecting stereo mini plug type headphones.

It has a built-in FM transmitter that puts the sound of the record on the FM signal and transmits the music by radio wave in a distant place.
