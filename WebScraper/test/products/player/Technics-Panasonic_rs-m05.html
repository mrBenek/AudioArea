  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A 297 mm wide concise cassette deck.

The mechanism is driven by a soft torque drive that uses the rotational force of the motor to hold the head block and to switch the reel drive.
An electronically controlled DC motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with an automatic tape selector that automatically detects the type of tape.
Also, in the case of Fe-Cr tapes without tape detection holes or some metal tapes, manual operation can be used for switching.

As a noise reduction system, Dolby NR circuit is mounted.

Equipped with an output volume.

The level meter has a 2-color FL display with a peak hold function.

It is equipped with functions such as music selector, rewind autoplay and cue & review.

Equipped with auto-stop mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.
