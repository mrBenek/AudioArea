Commentary

This is a two way recorder / re-tape deck with a 3-motor system and center capstan drive mechanism.

We have adopted a 4-8 pole hysteresis synchronous motor for the capstan and a 6-pole eddy current motor for the reel.
It also uses an electromagnetic brake that does not damage the tape to stop.

Oatley Verse and auto repeat are available for both recording and playback.

It uses a center capstan system that is most suitable for a reciprocating recorder and a four head configuration that is arranged symmetrically.

It uses a soft touch electromagnetic push button for operation.

Uses an EL drive system for easy tape mounting.

Equipped with a lock pose button.

Sound on sound is possible.

Equipped with a tape selector for switching between standard and SH.
