Commentary

Flat stereo cassette deck equipped with Super ANRS.

It is equipped with ANRS and Super ANRS as VICTOR/JVC's original noise reduction system.
ANRS improves the signal-to-noise ratio by raising the high-frequency range of the input signal at the time of recording and returning it to the original level at the time of playback to make it flat, thereby relatively reducing only the tape-hiss level. Super ANRS further develops the principle of ANRS by improving the frequency characteristics and distortion factor in addition to the noise reduction effect.

An SA (Sen-alloy) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
Sen-alloy has features such as high maximum magnetic flux density, low Barkhausen noise, high Curie point and stable temperature characteristics, and little degradation of material properties. However, when used in block cores, head loss such as eddy current loss is large, so high-frequency characteristics tend to deteriorate, and it is too rigid for permalloy laminate.
The SA head has a composite structure in which a Sen-Alloy chip is bonded at high temperature to the tape sliding surface of a Permalloy laminated laminate core to realize a Sen-Alloy head, enabling excellent regeneration.

ID mechanism using FG servo DC motor is adopted for the mechanism.
In this system, the drive belt of the capstan is made independent from other drive systems, so that interference from the take-up wheel and other devices is effectively cut off and tape travel is stabilized.

Equipped with a 5-point peak indicator.

There was a dust cover sold separately.
