  • DENON DMD-M33

  • ¥ 38,000 (released in 2005)

Commentary

MD deck of Rapacia series equipped with MDLP mode and group management function.

It is compatible with MDLP mode and has various editing functions.
It is equipped with a group management function that can easily search and play songs with long-duration stereo recording.

Equipped with MD Pick Rec function.
With this function, when your favorite song is played, you can record from the beginning of the song to MD by simply pressing the enter button until the end of the song.
This function does not work if there is no silence at the beginning and end of the song.

Equipped with a digital REC volume function, the level can be adjusted even for digital recording.

Equipped with a jog dial, you can enter the name of music easily.

The design follows the M33 series 3 series and is equipped with aluminum panel and side escutcheon.

In addition to two optical input systems and one optical output system, one analog input / output system is installed.

It is equipped with a sampling rate converter and automatically converts 32 kHz and 48 kHz sampling frequency to MD 44.1 kHz.

Operation linked with RCD-M33 is possible.

A remote control for MD is included.
