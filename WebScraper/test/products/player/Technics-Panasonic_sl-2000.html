  • Technics SL-2000

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A direct drive player with a slim design and high basic performance.

Uses one chip IC (AN620) that integrates the motor drive part and control part in one chip.
In addition, the drive section is equipped with a DC servo motor with low power consumption and high rotational force.

MM type cartridge with shell integrated structure is adopted.

33 1/3, 45 rpm independent rotational speed adjustment is possible.

Equipped with a illuminator that illuminates the strobe and an audio insulator that is resistant to vibration.
