  • KENWOOD KXF-5002

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

The K's series auto-reverse deck achieves high-sound recording with stable tape running.

A front loading system is adopted, and an aluminum die cast 3 flywheel mechanism is adopted for the rotating part to achieve high rigidity and stabilize the tape running.
This ensures stable head touch and excellent recording characteristics.

It is equipped with the CCPS & Auto Bias function that automatically sets the optimum tape recording level and bias amount according to the tape actually used for recording.
CCRS recording is possible when KAF-7002 or KAF-5002, DPF-7002 or DPF-5002 are simultaneously connected via system control.

Equipped with Dolby B/C noise reduction to reduce tape hissing that is a concern during tape playback.
In addition, it is equipped with Dolby HX Pro which improves high-frequency recording characteristics.

It is equipped with Kenwood's original DPSS which enables repeated playback such as repeating one song or repeating all songs, rewind playback, dash and play, jump selection, etc.
