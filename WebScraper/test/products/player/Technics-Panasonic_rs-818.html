  • National/Panasonic RS-818

  • ¥ 79,900 (around 1974)

Commentary

An 8-track recorder with FM/AM tuner and 2 speakers.

In order to realize reliable FM reception, an AFC (automatic frequency adjustment device) switch is mounted.

Equipped with a monitor switch that can check the recording status.
The volume can be either High or Low.

Equipped with an auto-eject switch that enables continuous performance and cutting in the middle.

Volume can be controlled separately on the left and right.

Two level meters are installed for the left and right channels.

Equipped with an FF (2 x speed) button that can be used as the beginning of a song.

The Phono connector allows you to connect a record player using ceramic or crystal cartridges.

Equipped with a microphone terminal.

The program being played is indicated by a light.

It is equipped with an FM stereo eye to indicate stereo reception.

4 ch (speaker matrix) playback is possible by adding a rear speaker.
