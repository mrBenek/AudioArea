  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

This is a tape deck for one way recording and playback with 4-track No. 7 reel and single capstan.

Uses a 3-motor 3-head system.
The capstan uses an electronically controlled DC servo motor, and the recording and reproducing head uses a high-hardness permalloy head.

4-track, 2-channel recording and playback are possible.

Speed switching between 19cm/s and 9.5cm/s is possible.

Equipped with a lock-type timer standby mechanism.

Uses one touch reel clamper.

Microphone and line mixing is possible.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a large VU meter.
