Commentary

A CD player with a CD roulette changer.

The disc tray uses a CD roulette system, and up to 5 discs can be set in the tray.
It also includes a disk exchange feature that allows you to replace non-playing disks without interrupting performance or add disks to an empty tray.

The D/A converter is equipped with a hybrid pulse D/A converter.

The servo circuit is equipped with a high-precision digital servo, and by adjusting the servo amount of the optical pickup for each disc to be played, servocontrol reflecting disc conditions more accurately is realized.

There are 4 groups of music clips, so you can pre-set your favorite songs according to your music genre or mood. You can set up to 4 groups of music clips.

With built-in disc checking, you can play the beginning of the disc one after another when closed (10 seconds, 20 seconds or 30 seconds selectable), and when open, the tray turns so you can see the installed disc.

Equipped with a disc skip function, you can skip the disc you are listening to and move to the next disc with one button.

It is equipped with functions such as shuffle play, delete shuffle which allows one disc / all disc selection, multi-disc program, delete play, delete bank, music scan and 6-mode repeat.

The Custom Edit function allows you to use peak search, time edit, link edit, program edit, time fade, manual fader and variable fade.

Equipped with a control AI terminal that allows you to enjoy playing in conjunction with cassette decks such as TC-WE805S.
You can use this connector to start recording when a CD starts playing, or to hold a recording pause while selecting a CD.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
