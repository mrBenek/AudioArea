  • VICTOR/JVC SRP-B50M

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1972?)

Commentary

A record player that supports 4-channel playback.

A belt drive system using a brushless DC servo motor is dc servo motor.

It is possible to play CD-4 (4 ch) records by exchanging the cartridge and needle.
A low-capacity shield wire is also used for 4-channel playback.

The rotational frequency micromanipulator uses a projection type strobe device.

The cartridge is an IM type.
