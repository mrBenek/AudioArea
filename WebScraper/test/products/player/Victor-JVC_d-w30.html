  • VICTOR/JVC D-W30

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1985)

Commentary

Double cassette deck with 10 songs 16 memory program selection editing function.

The A mechanism is equipped with a random access program that can play up to 10 songs 16 times, so you can easily select songs and edit dubbing to the B Mechanism.

Equipped with Dolby B NR as a noise reduction system.

It is equipped with a program index scan that automatically plays the head of a programmed song continuously for 10 seconds, and multi-song jump selection.

Uses 2-motor full logic mechanism.

Equipped with an auto tape select function.

It is equipped with dubbing time equivalent level recording.

It is equipped with blank skip and blank search.

Equipped with Rewind Autoplay.

Equipped with one touch constant speed / double speed dubbing function.

Equipped with a continuous play function.
