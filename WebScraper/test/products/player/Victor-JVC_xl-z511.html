Commentary

The optical transmission system is a CD player adopting D/A separation two block structure.

In order to eliminate digital noise such as servo current noise of digital circuits and clock pulses of microcomputers, an optica link (optical transmission method) using a photocoupler is adopted.
In addition, by using separate boards for the digital & servo circuit and the analog circuit, the analog circuit is mechanically and electrically separated, eliminating the sound quality degradation caused by noise interference.

It is equipped with two digital outputs which are coaxial with the optical system.

The digital filter uses a newly developed 4-times oversampling digital filter, which realizes 266 order operation, 100 dB out-of-band attenuation characteristics, and ± 0.0001 dB in-band ripple.
As a result, the analog filter at the latter stage can be made loose and simple, reducing phase shift and distortion.

The D/A converter employs a newly developed high-speed fine D/A converter using an independent high-speed I-V amplifier.
The SN ratio and dynamic range have been expanded by greatly increasing the conversion speed to voltage compared to the conventional one.

A lightweight, high-precision 3-beam pickup is used for the pickup part.
In this pickup, the response speed is improved by shortening the distance between the main beam for reading signals and the two sub-beams. In addition, the weight of the moving part is reduced to 1/2 of the conventional weight, and the tracing performance is improved. This reduces excess servo current and greatly reduces servo noise.

The chassis structure uses a newly developed silent base in addition to a large insulated foot.
The SILENT BASE has a 3-layer structure with a special synthetic resin as a core. It has extremely high internal loss and low resonance. Moreover, it has a low center of gravity due to its weight, which reduces vibration structurally.

Non-resonance and non-vibration are achieved by adopting IS mechanism k.
The IS mechanism has a structure in which the disk drive system and the pickup system are set on one base and floating mounted by an insulation system using special rubber. This double anti-vibration structure eliminates disturbance vibration and sound pressure from speakers.

It has an editing function that automatically programs according to the tape time.

Program playback of 32 songs is possible.
Program operations can be performed in the tray eject state, and additional memory is available during performance.

It has a random play function.

It is equipped with an intro-scan function that plays back the head of a song for about 15 seconds each.

Equipped with 5 types of repeat functions.

It is equipped with FL display.

Equipped with a remote control electric volume that can be used as a headphone volume.
The volume is motorized for sound quality, and the LED flashes during operation, making it easy to see from a distance.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Equipped with a synchro terminal, it can be linked with VICTOR/JVC's synchro compatible deck.

Equipped with auto-space function.

Wireless remote control is included.
