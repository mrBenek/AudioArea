Commentary

HMV series cassette deck.

Amorphous magnetic alloys with excellent high-frequency characteristics are used for the head part, and high-purity oxygen-free copper (PC-OCC) is used for the head winding to reduce signal loss and realize recording and playback in a high dynamic range and high-frequency range.

The drive motor is equipped with the well-known pulse servo DD motor, and by directly transmitting the driving force, a large margin is obtained against transmission loss and wow flutter.
In addition, the unique pulse servo control ensures stable performance against tape load fluctuations, temperature changes, and aging.

The mechanism uses a closed-loop dual capstan system to reduce tape tension and modulation noise. Matching with a DD motor stabilizes the head touch and reduces uneven rotation and noise during rotation to reduce sound loss.

An all-discrete high-output equalizer amplifier is used.
It operates stably at a power supply voltage more than twice that of conventional products, and achieves high S/N and high dynamic range with a low-noise FET single-input circuit.

A non-feedback type all-discrete buffer amplifier is also used for the line buffer lamp, resulting in a low distortion and high-speed response design.

The internal structure is designed to be completely separated into five blocks : playback amplifier, recording amplifier, bias control, analog power supply analog power supply section, and system control section, eliminating interference between blocks.
In addition, by adopting the optimum layout of each block and switching relay, the signal path from input to recording, playback and output is minimized.

The chassis structure uses an arc shelf chassis.
It features an integrated front / top panel made of 3.5 mm thick aluminum material, midship layout with heavy items such as mechanisms and power transformers placed in the center, and parts are firmly fixed to a highly rigid chassis made of 3.2 mm thick steel to eliminate unnecessary vibration.

It is equipped with a recording calibration function, and the ability of the tape is brought out by adjusting the calibration.

Equipped with Dolby HX Pro that expands the high-frequency dynamic range.
The 210 kHz high-bias design enables dynamic recording.

Equipped with display ON/OFF function and dimmer function.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

Wireless remote control is included.
