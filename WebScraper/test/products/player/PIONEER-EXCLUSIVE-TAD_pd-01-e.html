  • Pioneer PD-01

  • ¥ 26,800 (released in 1994)

Commentary

A CD player developed with an emphasis on basic performance.

The D/A converter is equipped with a high-speed pulse flow D/A converter developed by Pioneer.
In this DAC, in order to eliminate requantization noise while taking advantage of the features of the 1bitD/A converter, a noise shaper is operated at a high oversampling frequency of 384 fs. By obtaining fine output, the noise in the audible band due to requantization noise is reduced. In addition, the influence of out-of-band noise on sound quality is also reduced by using a low-order noise shaper.
In addition, by operating the master clock at a low frequency of 16.93 MHz, the effect of jitter is reduced.

Equipped with a display off switch to reduce scanning noise when the display is turned on.

A honeycomb chassis with high rigidity and excellent vibration control is used.

It is equipped with functions such as computer program edit, peak search, highlight scan, memory hold and talkback.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.
It also has a digital level control that allows you to adjust the line-out and headphone volume with a remote control.

Power can be turned on/off by remote control.

Equipped with CD-DECK synchro terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
