  • SONY MDP-V70G

  • ¥ 110,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

An LD player for video CD.

Advanced Tri-Digital LSI has been adopted to improve picture quality and sound quality.

High-speed double-sided reproduction mechanism equipped with C quick reverse is adopted.

Equipped with a bookmark playback function, you can mark a scene you want to watch many times and call it up directly.

Equipped with a scene playback function, you can jump to the scene you want to see by the scene number.

The karaoke features include a clapping function that produces a clapping sound, a second reservation function that allows you to reserve music up to the next disc, an auto fade-out function, a vocal assist function, a 17-step key control function, and a digital echo function.

Equipped with two microphone inputs.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.
This jack is not output when playing Video CD.

Wireless remote control is included.
