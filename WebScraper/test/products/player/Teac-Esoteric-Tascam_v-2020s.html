Commentary

This is a stereo cassette deck which is the standard equipment of the V series aiming at the best performance in class.

Equipped with Dolby S NR to support digital sources.
The Dolby S NR is based on the Dolby B/C and incorporates the operation principle of the Dolby SR for professional use. It realizes a significant noise reduction effect of 24 of 24 dB in the high-frequency range and 10 dB in the low-frequency range where noise is conspicuous. In addition, the breathing phenomenon unique to the NR system is suppressed by high-precision signal control technology.
Also equipped with Dolby B/C NR.

The head part uses high hardness permalloy combination 3 head with PCOCC winding.

± 2 power supply DC configuration is adopted for the amplifier circuit, and high frequency characteristics are greatly improved.

The mechanism uses a center mount system with excellent reliability, stability, and durability to realize construction that is less affected by internal and external vibrations.

Aluminum panel is used for the front panel.

Equipped with a triangular cassette support system, it prevents the cassette half from shaking.

Equipped with a tape fine adjustment mechanism with independent bias and level.

Equipped with Dolby HX Pro.

High-bias recording circuit is used.

Equipped with return to 0, auto monitor, auto REC mute, and auto tape selector.

Equipped with timer recording and re-mechanism.

Wireless remote control with display ON/OFF function is included.
