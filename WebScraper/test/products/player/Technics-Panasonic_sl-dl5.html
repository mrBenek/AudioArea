  • Technics SL-DL5

  • ¥ 49,800 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A linear tracking full auto D. D. player with excellent basic performance and operability in a slim body.

A two phase, full-wave, flat-faced, slotless DC motor is used for the motor structure and drive system.

The cartridge is a plug-in connector type MM cartridge with an all-laminate core for the magnetic circuit.

It realizes a simple operation part in which all operations are consolidated into four keys.
In addition, the record size and rotation number are also automatically detected, and auto lead-in is performed.

Equipped with a dial scale for confirming arm travel position.
