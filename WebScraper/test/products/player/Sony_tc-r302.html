  • SONY TC-R302

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1986)

Commentary

Auto reverse deck equipped with Dolby NR / B-C type.

It is equipped with a recording / reproducing Oatley verse function that allows you to choose between two modes : one sided recording / reproducing, two sided recording, and two sided continuous playback, which is ideal for BGM listening.

Equipped with Dolby NR B-C type.

Equipped with a 12-segment x2 LCD peak program meter.

It is equipped with Cue & Review function which is convenient for editing, Rec Mute function with autospace and autoplay function.

Adopts feather touch operation mechanism.

Synchronized recording with Sony record players and CD players is possible.

Equipped with a microphone input terminal for automatic switching of line and microphone.

Timer sold separately allows recording of absence and alarm clock playback.

It has a headphone port.

Wireless remote control (sold separately) allows remote operation.
