Commentary

Cassette deck with GX head mounted.

GX head is used for recording and re-head.
The GX head is made of single-crystal ferrite with excellent durability and high permeability, and is wrapped in super hard glass with high abrasion resistance. This eliminates the influence of dust and dust as well as iron oxides such as magnetic tape.

An O. L. S (Over-Level Suppressor circuit is mounted.
With conventional cassette tapes, the coating layer is thin and the saturation point is low. Therefore, it is difficult to set the recording level high because the distortion increases in response to excessive input. The O. L. S circuit responds to these weak points by simply switching a switch so that the sound is not distorted even when the level is exceeded or when an excessive input is momentarily entered, and the distortion rate is minimized.

A direct function change operation mechanism has been adopted so that operations such as recording, playback, fast forward, rewind and stop can be performed with one touch, and it is not necessary to press the stop button every time the mode is changed.

Equipped with a tape selector switch.
It is compatible with chrome tape and low-noise tape, so you can change the frequency compensation amount to bring out the performance of the tape.

Equipped with an automatic stop mechanism.
