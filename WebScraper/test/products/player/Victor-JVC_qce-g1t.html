  • VICTOR/JVC QCE-G1T

  • ¥ 70,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

8 track deck with built-in head for 4 ch reproduction and pre-main amplifier for 2 ch.

By adding it to a pre-main amplifier or stereo set, you can play 4 channels.

Playback of 2 ch tapes is also possible.

It has a built-in matrix decoder SFCS circuit and can play 2-ch tapes with 4-ch effect.
When connected to a stereo set, the SFCS circuit can also be used to play back records, so that 2-ch records can be played back with the 4-ch effect, or matrix 4-ch records can be played back.

Equipped with a 3 x fast forward mechanism.

Equipped with speaker phase switch and program indicator.
