Commentary

This is a DVD audio / video player with a newly developed PEM-DD converter that supports the DVD audio audio format of 192kHz/24bit. It is equipped with the same IC for all channels and uses a fine clock generator for high sound quality design.

Version2 of the EXTENDED K2 processing, which expands digital audio signals to high-bit and high-sampling and regenerates them, up to four times the frequency expansion level, is installed on the main channel 2.
The 44.1 kHz system is expanded to 176.4kHz/24bit, and the 48 kHz system is expanded to 192kHz/24bit.

It uses Victor's original 3 d natural progressive system which interpolates not only DVD movie software but also conventional image system (interlace system) image by calculation.
This improves the quality of digital image quality.
