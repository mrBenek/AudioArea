  • ¥ 75,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with Pure SenDust Head and dbx.

Pure Sendust low-impedance head is used for recording and reproducing head.
The core material of high-purity SENDUST developed by Yamaha is formed by the high-vacuum centrifugal casting method, and features such as transient characteristics, dynamic range and high-frequency separation are improved by low impedance design with reduced number of windings.

Equipped with dbx NR and Dolby B type noise reduction system.

The mechanism uses a 2-motor separate drive system, with a load-lift DC servo motor for the capstan and a flat-torque DC motor for the reel to ensure highly reliable and stable driving.
The flywheel is a large, highly accurate, dynamic balance type.

The signal system uses a newly developed high-integration 1 package recording and re-amplifier IC, and the recording switch is built in electronically to improve performance and reliability.

Uses a highly integrated logic IC and feather-touch full logic control with a 1-motor, 2-solenoid mechanism.

Equipped with VU meter with peak indicator.

An optional wired remote controller can be connected.
