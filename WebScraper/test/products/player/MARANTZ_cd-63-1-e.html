Audio Database

  • Marantz CD-63

  • ¥ 40,000 (released in 1993)

Commentary

CD player equipped with newly developed full floating mechanism CDM-12.

Equipped with 18bit8 x oversampling digital filter and 1bitD/A converter.

A discrete circuit based on the HDAM concept is used in the audio section.

Volume control of analog / headphone output is possible.

There were 2 kinds of models, gold and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
