Commentary

HMV series CD player.

The digital circuit uses newly developed 20-bit K2 processing.
This circuit expands the CD-16-bit signal by assuming that it is an analog signal of the original sound. It a unique music energy equivalent exchange method to reproduce it with 20-bit accuracy, thereby acquiring sound that could not be expressed by conventional CD-players.

A 20-bit DD converter is used for the D/A converter.
In this method, since 1-bit operation is expressed by a pulse wave with crystal precision, 0 cross distortion is eliminated in principle, and high-precision D/A conversion is realized by a fourth order noise shaper.
In addition, the input mode has been expanded to 20 bit to expand the capability as a DD converter.

The servo circuit is equipped with a newly developed digital servo system.
In this circuit, servo characteristics are optimized by automatic adjustment for each disk, servo error is greatly reduced and stable playability is realized.
To minimize the signal length, the servo circuit is integrated into a small module board.

A symmetrical layout is adopted for the circuit structure, and by making the pattern and part arrangement symmetrical, the left and right deviation of sound quality is eliminated.

An electromagnetic relay is used for the muting circuit of the output stage, and a photocoupler is used to eliminate adverse effects on the audio signal.

Equipped with two digital output terminals that are coaxial with the optical system.
These terminals are set to the terminal farthest from the analog output terminal, and a digital output ON / OFF switch is provided to ensure complete digital / analog separation design.

A 3-pointed base has been adopted for the legs, making it possible to install stably without being affected by the flatness of the place.

The display section has been revised based on the waveform of noise, and the display space and functions that generate noise are minimized in order to reduce noise from the root.
In addition, in order to prevent noise from mixing in from the front display board, the whole board is covered with a metal plate to shut out noise.
It is equipped with a display OFF function to eliminate display noise and the dimmer switch to reduce display light.

Wireless remote control is included.
