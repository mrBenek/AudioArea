Audio Database

  • Marantz CM1040

  • ¥ 76,000 (released in 1997)

Commentary

Slim Series CD/MD combination deck.

Direct digital recording from CD to MD is possible.

Equipped with optical digital input / output terminal and coaxial digital input / output terminal.

Equipped with sampling converter corresponding to 32/44.1/48kHz.

Equipped with synchronized dubbing function.

There are 2 color variations of green and titanium black.

A remote control is included.
