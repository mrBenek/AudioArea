  • ONKYO TA-W890

  • ¥ 72,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

This double-cassette deck was announced as the third high-speed dubbing deck.

It uses 2 hard permalloy heads with strictly uniform sound quality.
In addition, a DC servo motor that automatically controls the rotational speed through an electronic circuit is used in the driving system.

The tape dubbing speed can be switched between 2 x speed and constant speed (normal).
Stereo microphone mixing is also available, and mixing play between Tape 1 and the microphone can be recorded to Tape 2.

Enjoy long listening time with automatic continuous playback from tape 1 to tape 2.
In addition, tapes 1 and 2 can be played simultaneously.

You can adjust the playback speed of Tape 1 within ± 10%.

Equipped with a digital level meter.

Equipped with functions such as timer standby mechanism, Dolby system, tape 1 / 2 independent tape selector, etc.
