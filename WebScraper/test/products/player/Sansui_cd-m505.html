  • SANSUI CD-M505

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

A CD changer with 6 discs + 1 CD.

You can play 6 discs continuously or listen to only the songs you like from 6 discs.
Plus one disc special tray allows you to listen to one CD while swapping it.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 1-bit delta-sigma D/A converter.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

It is equipped with functions such as 36 random program memory with check clear function, random play, repeat play, 3-mode EDIT (AUTO/HALF/HOLD), tape length and tape side switching function, disk check, music scan, 5-mode time display, etc.

Switching between 1-disk and all-disk is possible for each mode.

Equipped with headphone port with level control.

Wireless remote control is included.
