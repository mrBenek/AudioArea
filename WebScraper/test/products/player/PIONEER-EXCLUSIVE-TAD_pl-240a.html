  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

This is a full auto player with high cost performance devoted to simplification.

Equipped with a DC servo motor whose center of gravity is almost aligned with the fulcrum of the turntable, improving rotational stability.

The tone arm uses a mass concentration system static balance S-shaped tone arm.
By reducing the moment of inertia and increasing the trackability, sound quality is improved.

Equipped with MM type cartridge.

The cabinet has a coaxial suspension structure that prevents howling even during high-power playback.

Front operation type that can operate the arm with the dust cover closed.
