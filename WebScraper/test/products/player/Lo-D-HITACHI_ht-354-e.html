Commentary

An auto player that uses a unitorque motor.

The motor is equipped with a unitorque motor based on Lordi's proprietary technology.
The Unitorque Motor is a motor which obtained perfect circular motion from the principle. It consists of a doughnut-shaped rotor magnet magnetized with 8 poles and two sets of star-shaped flat coils offset by 22 ^ 5 degrees. The torque generated by these two coils and magnets is constant at any moment. This principle is called sin2Θ + cos2Θ = 1 ". The Uni-Torque Motor also adopts a simple structure that is brushless, slotless and coreless, and has significantly improved durability.

Equipped with auto return and auto cut auto mechanisms that enable reliable tracing even with light needle pressure cartridges.

Equipped with anti-skating mechanism, strobe with neon lamp, and oil dump type arm lifter.
