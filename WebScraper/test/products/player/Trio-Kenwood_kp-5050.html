Commentary

DD turntable with inertial lock.

Record sound The concept of inertia lock is introduced to prevent smooth rotation from being disturbed by the frictional force generated between the groove and needle tip.
The moment of inertia of the turntable is 550 kg ・ cm.2It uses a 20-pole 30-slot motor with high servo gain and excellent bare characteristics.

We have adopted a 7 φ Center shaft.

The arm base has a large contact area of 90 mm φ (6 mm thickness), holds not only cabinet but also ARCB (anti-resonance compression base) material, and the tone arm is designed as if it is rooted in the cabinet to provide vibration isolation.

Wedge chuck type arm holding is adopted.
By pulling the lock lever toward you, the slide cam is lifted upward, the wedge cam moves to the left, and the arm shaft is pressed against the arm base. The wedge cam is made of high hardness sintered alloy.
The arm shaft is crimped with a contact area of 45 times or more of the fixed screw.

A mass separation type tone arm is used to protect the tone arm from the high level fo resonance among the split vibrations applied to the tone arm.
The arm pipe and main weight are coupled with butyl rubber to suppress the split vibration that occurs in the main weight part.

Uses helicoid type arm height adjustment mechanism.

Static type anti-skating mechanism is adopted.

The cartridge was sold separately.
