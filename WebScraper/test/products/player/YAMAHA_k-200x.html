Commentary

Double reverse deck equipped with Dolby B/C and Dolby HX Pro.

Equipped with Dolby HX Pro in addition to Dolby B/C.
The Dolby HX-PRO greatly reduces changes in frequency characteristics due to changes in sensitivity by keeping the dynamic bias constant. In addition, the left and right operations are completely independent and each has optimal bias servo control. Since it operates only during recording, recorded tapes can be played back on other decks without any problems.

Equipped with a timer function.
In addition to the normal timer function, the K-200X is equipped with a timer every function that repeats timer operation at the same time every day once set.

It has a built-in sleep function that automatically lowers the playback output level by 6 dB when it is set and automatically turns off the power when the tape ends.

Equipped with a 4-digit digital linear electronic counter.

Equipped with a relay playback function that allows continuous playback from Deck A to Deck B.

Two speeds, constant speed and double speed, can be selected for dubbing.

Equipped with a skip dubbing function that stops Deck B when there is a blank for more than 15 seconds on Deck A and starts dubbing again at the beginning of the music.

It is equipped with functions such as auto rec mute, rec return, memory stop, blank screen, music search, etc.
