Commentary

Semi-auto player with direct drive system.

Uses a 20-pole 30-slot DC servo D. D. motor.
The servo circuit eliminates the influence of external factors such as power supply voltage and frequency.

Straight type is used for the tone arm.

Howling is suppressed by the adoption of float suspension.

Equipped with auto return and shut-off mechanism.
