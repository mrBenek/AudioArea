Commentary

A full-auto player focusing on basic performance.

Quartz is used for rotation control, and rotational speed deviation within ± 0.002% is achieved.

A unique integrated structure D. D. turntable is used for the turntable.

The tone arm is equipped with a high-sensitivity gimbal suspension tone arm.

Equipped with a plug-in connector type MM cartridge.

It uses a full auto mechanism that starts playing just by setting the number of revolutions and pressing the start button.

TNRC is used for the cabinet.
