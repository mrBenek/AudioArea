  • Pioneer PD-X333

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in October 1988)

Commentary

A compact 360 mm wide CD player.

2 x oversampling digital filter and left and right independent D/A converter are used.

It uses a magnetic clamp type disk stabilizer that suppresses unnecessary vibration of the disk and does not transmit external vibration to the pickup.
Vibration is also reduced by adopting a direct support mechanism and large insulators.

It is equipped with functions such as auto program edit and random play.
