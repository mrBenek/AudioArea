Commentary

This is a prestige model DVD player that pursues high sound and image quality in every detail.

The audio DAC is equipped with a 96 kHz / 24-bit D/A converter.
To support 96 kHz sampling and 24-bit audio, the shaping noise of the multi-level delta-sigma D/A converter is kept flat to 44 kHz to prevent it from affecting the important audio signal.

A low-impedance power supply is used in the power supply circuit for audio to minimize noise and unwanted signal components.

Equipped with two digital audio output terminals (optical / coaxial) for Dolby Digital and DTS.

Equipped with Dolby Digital 5.1 ch analog output terminal, you can enjoy digital theater sound by combining it with AV amplifier with 5.1 ch input terminal.
It is also equipped with adjustment functions such as speaker mode setting and dynamic range setting of the voice.

It is equipped with anti-vibration measures such as a high-rigidity chassis that absorbs vibration from the inside, a large insulator that suppresses external vibration, and a 20 mm thick side wood that increases vibration control. It realizes high vibration control and quietness.

High-quality parts such as ultra-low impedance organic semiconductor capacitors, audio electrolytic capacitors, and high-quality sound film capacitors are used.
Gold plated terminals are used for the pin plug terminals on the back.

A component video output terminal is mounted to achieve higher precision video playback.

The video DAC is equipped with a 10-bitD D/A converter.
10-bit video D/A conversion of 8-bit component signals prevents data loss.

Equipped with image quality adjustment mode for movies.
You can use the GUI screen to select a cinema image quality suitable for playing movie software. You can also select an image quality suitable for the type of software from four types : normal, soft, fine, and cinema.

With user image quality control, you can adjust contrast, color, sharpness and brightness using the GUI screen.

Equipped with a monitor selector function, you can select the image quality suitable for the type of TV.
You can choose from standard (cathode-ray tube TV), 3-tube projector, LCD projector and projection TV.

It is equipped with a Cinema Loudness function that makes the dialogue easier to hear.

It is equipped with high-speed search and high-speed output (30% higher than in-house).

It consumes only 15W during playback and 1W during standby.
Wireless remote control is included.
