  • NEC CD-430

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1988)

Commentary

A CD player that supports 8 cm CD single.

The pickup section is equipped with a 3-beam laser pickup.

Equipped with an oversampling 16-bit digital filter.

Uses a vibration-resistant floating optical chassis.

It is equipped with functions such as computer editing function (automatic editing), introscan, autospace function, 24 songs random program memory, skip selection, 3-mode repeat between all songs / programs / A and B, timer start, 2-speed music search, etc.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Wireless remote controller with 10 keys is included.
