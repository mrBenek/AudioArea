Commentary

Quick reverse deck equipped with Dolby HX PRO.

The reverse operation is a quick reverse method in which the infrared sensor detects the reader tape at both ends of the cassette tape and reverses it quickly.
The quick-reverse mechanism employs a fine-axis quick-reverse mechanism. In this method, running performance and vibration resistance are greatly improved by maintaining the rotational axis position of the head accurately in order to eliminate the sense of fluctuation in the high-frequency range caused by fluctuations in the frequency characteristics of the outward and homeward paths caused by slight misalignment between the tape and the head.

The mechanism consists of a two motor full logic mechanism consisting of a capstan DD motor and reel motor, and a silent mechanism consisting of a motor for driving the mechanism.

Fine amorphous head is used for the head part.
We are pursuing more faithful recording and playback with a head that combines the characteristics of an amorphous alloy with superior high-frequency characteristics and a sen-alloy alloy with superior magnetic characteristics and abrasion resistance. In addition, the use of PC-OCC for the coil winding of the head significantly reduces signal loss.

The cassette door is equipped with a power eject door to prevent sound from entering the cassette door in order to cut the influence of external sound, and the half shell stabilizer firmly holds the cassette half to protect the cassette body from vibration.

By adopting a high bias system, the deviation from the signal current is almost eliminated.

Equipped with a CD direct input terminal that reduces noise and circuit effects by inputting at the shortest distance.

The display section is equipped with a level meter with 14-level peak hold and a linear counter.
In addition, it is equipped with a digital peak display which can confirm the recording level numerically, making it easier to set the proper recording level.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.
In addition, Dolby HX PRO is mounted.

Equipped with power cord with polarity display.

Equipped with timer start mechanism and can be linked with separately sold timer.

It has a headphone port.

Equipped with a memory button.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Compulink-1 / Synchro terminal is mounted.

Wireless remote control is included.
