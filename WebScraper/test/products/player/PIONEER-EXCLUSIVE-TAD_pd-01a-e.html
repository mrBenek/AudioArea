  • Pioneer PD-01A

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A CD player developed as the simplest component for enjoying CD.

The D/A converter is equipped with a high-speed pulse flow D/A converter.

Uses a honeycomb chassis with excellent vibration damping effect.

Equipped with an optical digital output.

Wireless remote control is included.
