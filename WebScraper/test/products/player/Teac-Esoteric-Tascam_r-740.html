Commentary

3-motor 4-head all-transistor-type commercial tape recorder.

The R-760 consists of a TD-700 type drive mechanism and an AR-740 catalogue sound reproduction amplifier. It can be stored in an optional console cabinet or portable case for use.

Variable resistance attenuator and cannon connector are adopted.

High abrasion resistant ferrite head is used for the head.

Equipped with an operation switch for editing, the take-up side reel motor can be stopped during editing.

Low-noise tape can be used, and Scotch #111 and #206 equivalent can be used for switching.

A plug-in unit system is used for the recording and playback amplifier section for easy maintenance and adjustment.

The format of the R-740 series differs depending on the head configuration. The number after the model number can be used to determine the number of tracks. The N at the end of the format indicates equalizer characteristics. N is NAB standard, B is BTS standard, and C is CCIR standard.
Moreover, it was possible to change functions and specifications by custom-order.
