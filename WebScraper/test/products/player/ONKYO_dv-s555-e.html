  • ONKYO DV-S555

  • ¥ 45,000 (released on July 24, 2001)

Commentary

A DVD video player compatible with CD-R, CD-RW and MP-3.

The video section contains a 27 MHz / 10-bit video D/A converter, and the audio section contains a 192 kHz / 24-bit D/A converter.

It uses a 2-wavelength twin focus pickup compatible with CD-R/RW and MP3.

Equipped with a setup navigator that enables easy and interactive initial setup.

It is equipped with a high-speed resume and last memory function that is convenient for interrupting playback.

Equipped with a Dolby digital and DTS decoder.

The direct digital path circuit realizes a clean and direct digital signal.

It is equipped with a search function that allows you to specify a chapter or time and play it quickly, a repeat play function that allows you to play your favorite tracks or chapters repeatedly, a program play function that allows you to play in your favorite order, and a random play function that allows you to play it out of order.

It is equipped with a condition memory function that stores up to 15 DVD settings.

Equipped with a screen saver function to prevent the display from burning.

Equipped with an RI terminal that enables you to operate the DV-S555 with a remote control such as an Onkyo AV amplifier with the RI mark.

Wireless remote control is included.
