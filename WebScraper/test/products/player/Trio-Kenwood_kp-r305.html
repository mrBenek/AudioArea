Commentary

Auto return player with improved anti-vibration capability.

The player cabinet uses a compression solid base.
The compression solid base is made by connecting materials with different resonance points such as marble stone, glass fiber and natural crushed stone with unsaturated polyester resin, adding calcium carbonate powder and molding it under high pressure.
This eliminates resonance peaks between 20 Hz and 200 Hz, where howling tends to occur, and further improves the high-frequency characteristics by combining with a homogen material to improve sound quality.

A newly designed slim synchronous motor is used as the drive motor.
This motor uses four coils to improve the balance of magnetic flux distribution, and has improved starting torque, reduced leakage magnetic flux, and vibration isolation of the motor.

Burnishing (mirror finish) is applied to the Center shaft shaft and bearing.

A cockpit lever is used for the play lever, a push switch is used for the cut button, and the return speed can be adjusted.

High specific gravity zinc die-cast is used for the bracket part of the tone arm to prevent resonance.
In addition, the main weight shaft of the tone arm and the supporting point are coupled with rubber to suppress the fluctuation of frequency characteristics.
