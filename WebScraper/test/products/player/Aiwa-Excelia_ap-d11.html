  • AIWA AP-D11(R/S)

  • ¥ 25,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A record player from the Mini Component My Pace3 series.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used for the mechanism part.

Static balance J-shaped tone arm is used for the tone arm.
The head shell is detachable and equipped with a MM type cartridge.

Equipped with auto-return and auto-cut mechanisms.

There are two variations on cabinet finishes : the AP-D11R has a rosewood finish and the AP-D11S has a silver-gray finish.
