  • DIATONE TP-81R

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

An 8-track tape deck capable of recording and reproducing cartridge tapes.

Equipped with an output level / input level meter that shows the level during recording and playback.

It has a slide-type recording volume.

It uses an FF mechanism that can fast-forward at 3.5 times the speed of recording and playback, making it possible to start the head in a short time.

Equipped with an auto-eject mechanism to prevent erroneous recording and erasure.

Equipped with an automatic program feed device.
