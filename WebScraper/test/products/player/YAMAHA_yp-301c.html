  • YAMAHA YP-301C

  • ¥ 31,500 (around 1976)

Commentary

A record player with a simple design.

The turntable has a 30 cm aluminum die-cast turntable.
In addition, a belt drive system is adopted for the drive system, and a 4-pole synchronous motor is used for the drive motor.

S-shaped pipe arm is used for the tone arm.
This arm is equipped with a lateral balancer.

The cartridge uses the M75 series of Shure.

Built-in semi-automatic mechanism.

It is finished with silver gray and castol.
