  • AIWA FX-10

  • ¥ 54,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

A stereo cassette deck equipped with Dolby C type.

Equipped with an auto tape selector compatible with LH, CrO2 and Metal.
In addition, the bias can be tuned according to the characteristics of various LH tapes.

A 2-motor full logic mechanism is used for the mechanism part.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Equipped with auto-repeat mechanism.

Equipped with an auto-rec mute mechanism that can make a 4-second blank.

It is equipped with a music sensor that can easily find the beginning of a song.

Equipped with left and right independent 12-point peak meters.

CW-146S is required for connection to the amplifier.

You can record by pressing the red buttons (Phono, Aux, and Tuner). However, if you press Phono, you will stand by in the state of Deck Recording Standby in the pause state.
