  • VICTOR/JVC D-E3

  • ¥ 48,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A thin stereo cassette deck.

Uses full logic control and 4-solenoid ID mechanism.

It adopts a cassette sliding mechanism of motor drive system.

It uses a music scanning mechanism.

ANRS (Dolby B NR) and SuperANRS are installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with 2-color LED peak meter.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism.

Compatible with Victor Compact Component COMPO34EX.
