Commentary

Thin DVD player that best matches Cinema Station S80.

The audio D/A converter is equipped with a 192 kHz / 24-bit DAC, which is equivalent to that for DVD audio players.

Equipped with a D1 / component video output terminal.

It is equipped with functions such as Quick Setup for easy setup and Position Memory (up to 5 discs) for locating the beginning of a partially viewed DVD.
In addition, you can quickly search for the scene you want to see with 200 times faster search function.

The image adjustment menu includes contrast / brightness and can be adjusted according to your preference.

Equipped with advanced surround function which can be enjoyed by 2 speakers.

It is equipped with a cinema image function that reproduces the picture quality of a movie.

It is equipped with a cinema dialog function that makes it easier to hear the lines.
This function is available for analog audio output only.

Equipped with a subwoofer output terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
