  • VICTOR/JVC XL-V1

  • ¥ 189,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

This is Victor's first CD player based on the technology obtained through the development of DAS-90 digital audio system, AHD and VHD system.

An optical pickup system using a semiconductor laser diode is used in the pickup section.

In order to ensure accurate D/A conversion operation, the D/A converter itself is equipped with a calibration function, which reduces the influence of variations in elements and temperature changes, and ensures stable sound quality.

MOS-LSI is used for the digital signal processing circuit.

A low-vibration, high-torque coreless motor is used for the drive motor of the mechanism.

It has a headphone port.

Equipped with two output terminals, fixed and variable.

Equipped with an FL display that can confirm the operation state.
It also has a 5-minute-step location indicator that lets you see where the pickup is.
