  • SANSUI SD-3030

  • ¥ 129,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

Tape deck capable of 4 ch playback.

4-channel playback and 2-channel recording / playback / reverse playback are available.

Hard permalloy head is used for the head part.
In addition, SANSUI's original contourless head with excellent sound and high and low frequency characteristics is adopted.

Measures against modulation noise are taken.

Equipped with a recording bias selector, it is compatible with low noise tapes.

Equipped with a record selector, it is possible to record following.

Equipped with a pause mechanism.

It is equipped with functions such as simultaneous monitoring and microphone / line mixing.
