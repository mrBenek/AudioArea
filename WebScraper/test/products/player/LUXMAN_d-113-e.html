  • ALPINE/LUXMAN D-113

  • ¥ 44,000 (released in June 1988)

Commentary

A CD player compatible with 8 cm CD.

It adopts a high rigid lock mechanism TypeII in which the pickup base and the base of the mechanism body are independent.
By separating the two bases with a special rubber damper and coil spring and floating them, transmission of the vibration of the mechanism to the mechanism is reduced.
We have also adopted optical pickups that use a newly developed die-cast base.

The newly developed high-efficiency pulse transformer is used as the pulse transformer mounted for digital output.

It uses a digital filter and an analog filter with a gentle attenuation curve.

A power line filter is installed to prevent pulse noise from mixing through the power line from other equipment.

By connecting to the separately sold AV surround controller (F-105), ALPINE/LUXMAN devices with system remote control terminals can be controlled by the F-105 remote control.

Equipped with headphone port that can adjust volume.

It is equipped with functions such as direct selection, 16-track program memory, memory check, repeat performance, timer play, and autospace (a function to insert a 4-second space between songs during program performance).

A remote control (RD-113) was included.
