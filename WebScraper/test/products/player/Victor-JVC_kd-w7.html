Commentary

Double cassette deck with full logic control system.

One touch simultaneous start system has been adopted and operability has been improved.
It is also equipped with a constant speed and double speed dubbing mechanism.

It is equipped with mixing simultaneous recording and simultaneous cue weather.

Equipped with a sound-on-sound mechanism, multiple recording is possible.

Equipped with a long-time repeated continuous playback mechanism, continuous play in which both A and B tapes are played alternately is possible.

Full logic centralized mechanical control is adopted.

Equipped with a music scanning mechanism.

Equipped with ANRS (Dolby B NR) as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with slide-type input level control with click for equal level copying.

Equipped with 2-color LED peak meter.

Equipped with auto-stop mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism and by connecting an optional timer, it is possible to record the absence and play the alarm clock.
