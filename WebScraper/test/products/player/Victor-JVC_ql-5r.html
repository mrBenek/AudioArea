Commentary

A quartz rock record player.

The drive motor uses an all-round integral detection FG servo motor with a torque of 800g ・ cm.
In this motor, a pulse for speed detection is directly magnetized to the lower part of the rotor magnet and integrated, and technology such as automation of the winding process of the stator coil is introduced.

The quartz lock servo circuit is entirely integrated into an IC. It uses a high-integration C-MOS IC that includes a crystal oscillator circuit, a divider circuit, and a sample-and-hold phase comparator circuit in a single package, thereby saving hundreds of transistors and improving reliability.

The turntable has a total weight of 2.0 kg including rubber and covering.

Quick stop mechanism using electromagnetic solenoid is adopted.

The tone arm uses a new gimbal system, and the height can be adjusted to match the height of the cartridge.

The cartridge is equipped with the MM type cartridge Z-1EB with an elliptical needle, which has the same performance as the Z-1E sold separately.

QL-7R type know-how is put into the cabinet, and it is designed to prevent howling by applying weights to important parts such as the base of the arm.
In addition, a dome isolator with height adjustment of new structure is adopted for the foot.

Arm sub-weight was sold separately.
