  • ONKYO K-W505

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

A double REC double reverse deck equipped with two 2-motor recording / playback reverse mechanisms.

You can enjoy tape play unique to double REC such as long recording / playback by relaying Deck A / Deck B or simultaneous recording to 2 volumes of tape.

Equipped with an A/B switching 4-digit electronic counter.
In addition, a level meter is installed.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO, which automatically adjusts the bias current to the optimum value according to the high-frequency component of the music signal, and with Dolby B/C noise reduction.

Equipped with 2 speed tape dubbing function of normal / high.

Equipped with auto space REC mute, jump selection function, full auto tape selector and manual REC level control.

It has a headphone port.

Synchronized recording with C-303 etc. is possible.
It can also be controlled by a system remote controller such as A-915R or R-803.
