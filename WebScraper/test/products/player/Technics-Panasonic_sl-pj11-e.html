Commentary

A record jacket size CD player with an FF-1 laser pickup.

An FF-1 laser pickup is used for the pickup part.
This pickup uses a single-beam system with high laser efficiency and greatly improves the S / N ratio of the reproduced signal. Combined with the use of a high-precision digital accu-servo system, it has acquired an excellent signal reading capability that is resistant to scratches on the disc surface and fingerprints.

A high-speed linear access system is adopted in the mechanism part, and high-speed access with an average of 1.0 seconds of pickup movement between program songs is realized.

It is equipped with 15 songs random program selection, and you can play your favorite songs from the disc in your favorite order using skip key and memory key.

It is equipped with functions such as repeating or skipping all songs / program songs.

Equipped with 2-speed manual search, the search speed can be switched from 3 times to 30 times by pressing and holding the key, and speedy search is possible.

It is equipped with a liquid crystal display, and if you press the rememing time key, you can display the number of remaining songs and remaining time on the disc.

Equipped with synchronized recording terminal.
