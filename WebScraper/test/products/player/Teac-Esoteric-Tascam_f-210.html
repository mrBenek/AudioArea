Commentary

This is a cassette deck with a robust design that can be read easily.

The cassette holder uses a soft eject type.
The wider window also improves the visibility of the tape.

FG servo DC motor is used as the drive motor.
We have also achieved stable tape travel by using high-quality materials for the transmission system, such as a high-precision capstan shaft, large flywheels, and precision polishing flat belt.

Equipped with a timer recording and playback mechanism, you can record your absence or play an alarm clock by combining with an audio timer.
In addition, it is equipped with a delay circuit, and it is designed to start up quickly from the beginning of the music after the mechanism becomes stable a few seconds after the power is turned on.

The tape selector is equipped with a two stage switching circuit with independent bias and equalizer, which can be set according to the characteristics of the tape.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

A built-in MPX filter prevents malfunction of the Dolby circuit caused by the stereo pilot signal during FM air check.

Equipped with a microphone / line input selector switch.
This switch allows the line input to be sent directly to the recording amplifier, eliminating distortion and noise when passing through the microphone amplifier. This increases the dynamic range of the line input and gives the full Dolby effect.

It uses an amplifier circuit with a wide dynamic range.

Equipped with a large VU meter.

The rack handle was sold separately as an option.
