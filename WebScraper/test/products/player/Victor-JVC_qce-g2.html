Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC QCE-G2

  • ¥ 27,500 (around 1973)

Commentary

A compact 4ch8 track player that can play cartridge-type 4ch8 music tapes by connecting to a 4ch-playback system.

Playback of 2 ch tapes is also possible.

A DD motor is used.

Equipped with a 3 x fast forward mechanism.

With ESC (Electric Speed Control) system, it is possible to shift from 3 x fast forward to constant speed driving immediately.

It has a 4 ch master volume.

Two sets of 4-ch input terminals are installed to cover the shortage of 4-ch terminals in the system.
