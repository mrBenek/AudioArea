Commentary

Quick reverse deck equipped with 2-motor full logic mechanism and Dolby HX-PRO.

Equipped with a quick reverse function.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO system.
This circuit constantly monitors the variation of the input signal during recording, and adjusts the effective bias to match the amount of the high-frequency signal, so that the high-frequency characteristics can be greatly improved without deteriorating the low-frequency characteristics, and distortion can be reduced over the entire band.

A 2-motor full logic mechanism is used for the mechanism part.

It is equipped with a synchro-edit function and can be easily edited and recorded when combined with a Panasonic / Technics CD player with a synchro-edit terminal.

Equipped with a bias adjustment function, you can adjust the bias according to the tape you use.

It is equipped with 2 types of recording level volume, main and balance.

Equipped with an auto REC mute, you can automatically create an approximately 4-second unrecorded portion when recording.

Equipped with left and right independent microphone terminals.

Equipped with MPX filter that can be turned on / off.

Equipped with an FL electronic counter, functions such as memory stop can be used.

Equipped with Timer Rec & Play function which can select REC, OFF and PLAY.

Wireless remote control is included.
