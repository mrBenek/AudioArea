Commentary

T-3572 Series (T-3572-1, T-3572-2)

Commercial tape players developed for wire-broadcasting and commercial BGM.

The T - 3572 1 was developed for two track monaural program delivery and is designed for high fidelity and high reliability.
Also, the T-3572-2 was developed for the program delivery of 4-track stereo.

High-performance ferrite head with extremely low wear is adopted for the head part.

Designed with consideration for long-term continuous use.

In order to prevent wear and seizure of the motor shaft, it is equipped with a mechanism that stops the rotation of the capstan shaft at the same time when it stops.

Up to 26 types of reel can be used.

Equipped with a tape counter, you can check the tape running fee.

Equipped with semi-automatic heading mechanism by foil sensing method.
In addition, the foil sensing method enables automatic reciprocating regeneration (repeat regeneration).

Remote control of startup and pause is available.

T-3572R Series (T-3572-1R, T-3572-2R)

The T-3572R series is a fully automatic device that incorporates two T - 3572 1 or T - 3572 2 and a T-0101S control unit into the BTS5 rack, enabling continuous broadcasting for 24 hours.
It is equipped with various safety functions required for long-term unmanned broadcasting.

By operating two tape players alternately and continuously, stable performance can be obtained over a long period of time.

Even if the tape player (No. 1) that is being fed stops due to a tape cutting problem, the other player (No. 2) that is waiting will start automatically and broadcasting can continue.

When the No. 1 player finishes playing the tape, the No. 1 player starts playing back and forth again even if the waiting No. 2 player is not on standby.

Even if the power supply is cut off by mistake or a power failure during program sending, the playback machine that is will restart automatically after the power is recovered.


T-0101S

The T-0101S is a mutual control unit designed to be connected to a T - 3572 1 / T - 3572 2 tape player.
