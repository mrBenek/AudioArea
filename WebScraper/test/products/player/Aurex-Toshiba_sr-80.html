  • TOSHIBA SR-80

  • ¥ 71,500 (around 1973)

Commentary

A record player with a condenser cartridge.

A condenser cartridge C-402S is used.

The tone arm uses a shell removable static balanced pipe arm.

A 16-pole synchronous motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with auto-cut and Oatley turn mechanisms.

Equipped with an oil dump type arm elevation lever.

A dedicated equalizer is built in.
