  • Technics RS-1080U

  • ¥ 1,500,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

Tape deck with direct drive system.

Uses a direct drive system with an ultra-low-speed electronic commutator motor.

Equipped with continuously variable tape tension servo mechanism.

4-head configuration including HPF for 2-track is adopted.

You can preset the bias and equalizer in three ways.

Equipped with a precision timer for clock display.

The tape speed can be finely adjusted by ± 2.5%.
