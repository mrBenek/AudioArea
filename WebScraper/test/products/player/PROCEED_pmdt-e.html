Commentary

DVD transport with a modular structure.

A slot-in card type module structure is adopted in the rear panel. By adding an expansion module, it can cope with various audio output and video output formats. In addition to the modularization of the communication board, the built-in control software can be expanded or upgraded by incorporating the computer communication function.
This allows for the playback of dts, 96kHz/24bit high-bit high-sampling software, and DVD audio.

The transport mechanism adopts the latest design mechanism, and the combination of the original design aluminum die-cast high-precision mechanical bench and tray realizes accurate and stable reading.

In the image signal processing section, we have introduced technologies at the level of professional video equipment such as an oversampling video D/A converter and a dual-clock system with high clock accuracy.

Separate power supply units are installed for the drive and servo units of the transport with different operation modes. Separate power supply units are also installed for the video signal system and audio signal system. In addition, the local regulation system is adopted for each circuit to eliminate mutual interference caused by power supply.

When playing DVD software, it has a built-in custom memory function that stores the settings made by the user on the menu screen in the console for each software. This enables you to set the audio, subtitle, screen size, etc. once and then enjoy the same settings at any time by simply pressing the play button.
With this function, it is possible to store the setting information of up to 2,000 discs even if you make full settings such as selecting playback chapters of up to 99 tracks.

Wireless remote control is included.
