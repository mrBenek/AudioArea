  • TEAC T-3400

  • Price unknown (around 1973)

Commentary

3-motor 3-head commercial tape recorder.

The T-3400 consists of a drive mechanism and a recording and playback amplifier. There were two variations of the T-3400-1 with one channel type (single track) and the T-3400-2 with two channel type (two track).

Ferrite head is used for the head.

It is equipped with editing operation mechanism such as EDIT button and CUE lever.

A plug-in unit is used for the recording and playback amplifier section, which is designed for easy maintenance and adjustment.

Canon connector is used.

Full remote control is possible.

It can be stored in a rack mount or portable case for use.

A simulsync recording function can be installed as an option.
