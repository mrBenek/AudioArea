Commentary

This is a twin recording re-reverse W cassette deck equipped with various functions.

For Deck I, you can choose up to 10 songs for each of A side and B side and up to 20 songs in total.
It is also possible to program 2 → 4 → 7 → 8 in the order in which you want to listen to the songs recorded on the tape and play them in that order.

It is equipped with the program selection and constant speed copy function. You can program in the order of songs you want to record and press the sync copy (normal) button to copy at the constant speed as specified. You can enjoy your favorite songs and edit them slowly.
If you press the Synchronous Copy (High Speed) button, the image is copied at double speed.

It is equipped with a parallel recording function that can record one source to two tapes simultaneously.
Each deck is equipped with an auto tape selector.

Quick reverse allows continuous recording of A and B surfaces, and relay recording from Deck I to II allows up to three hours (two 90 minute tapes) of recording.

Relay playback with skip function of up to 17 hours and 15 minutes (90 minute tape) is possible.

The LED sensor is used for the quick reverse, and when the leader tape part is detected, it is reversed.
In addition, in order to prevent degradation of sound quality in the reverse direction of the tape, we have adopted a combination head that integrates a recording / reproducing head and an erasing head with high precision.

Equipped with functions such as Auto I/II Selector, which automatically selects the inserted mechanism when the tape is inserted into Deck 1 or II mechanism, Auto REC Mute, which creates a silent part for 4 seconds when the button is pressed during recording and automatically enters the recording pause state, LED flashing display which shows each operation of pause / REC Mute / program / program start at a glance, which can be specified regardless of the tape running direction, Music Search / Music Skip of 10 songs before and after, and Music Repeat, which repeatedly plays the same song 10 times continuously.

Dolby noise reduction of Dolby B type / C type.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Wireless remote control is included.
