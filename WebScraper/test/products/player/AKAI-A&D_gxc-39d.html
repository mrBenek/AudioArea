  • AKAI GXC-39D

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1975)

Commentary

Cassette deck with GX head.

Equipped with a Dolby NR system as a noise reduction system.

It is equipped with functions such as peak level indicator and limiter, tape selector for Ferrichrome tape, and memory rewind.

The direct function change mechanism is used for shearing.

Equipped with a full-release auto-stop mechanism.

Equipped with a tape running indicator lamp.
