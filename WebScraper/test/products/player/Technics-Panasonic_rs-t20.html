  • Technics RS-T20

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

A double-cassette deck designed with ease of use in mind as a double-deck beginner.

Equipped with synchronized start function, editing can be started with one touch operation from the editing standby state.

Equipped with auto space control, recording stops after making a blank part for about 4 seconds automatically.
Press the Auto Space button again to restart recording.

It is equipped with a series playback function that can play for a long time.

Equipped with Dolby B NR as a noise reduction system.

It is equipped with functions such as double-speed editing and one touch recording.

Equipped with an auto tape selector that automatically selects the type of tape.

Equipped with 6-point x2LED peak level meter.
