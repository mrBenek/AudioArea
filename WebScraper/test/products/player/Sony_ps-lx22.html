  • SONY PS-LX22

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1983)

Commentary

An auto player system equipped with an auto return mechanism.

In order to utilize the light needle pressure / high compliance type cartridge, a low mass / straight tone arm is adopted.
In addition, the cartridge is equipped with MM type cartridge VL-42G with integrated shell arm.

The rotating system is equipped with a linear BSL motor to obtain stable rotational performance.

Uses an insulator filled with a highly viscoelastic material to prevent howling.

Equipped with an arm lifter.

30 Comes with a disk centering guide to make it easy to place the cmLP board.

The dust cover was sold separately.
