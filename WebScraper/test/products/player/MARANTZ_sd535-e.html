  • Marantz SD535

  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1995)

Commentary

Full Logic Oatley Verse Double reverse cassette deck with double mechanism for recording and playback.

Equipped with an FL display tube level indicator that allows visual confirmation of the input / output level.

It is equipped with recording functions such as low-speed / double-speed 2-speed dubbing and CD-synchronized dubbing, and playback functions such as QMS.

Equipped with Dolby HX PRO.

Wireless remote control is included.
