Audio Database

  • DIATONE TP-804

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1972)

Commentary

An 8-track tape deck with 4 discrete channels.

Equipped with 4-channel to 2-channel automatic switching device, both tapes can be enjoyed.
The channel number detection is designed to switch automatically when the tape is set.

It has an independent level adjustment volume for each channel and an output volume that can adjust the volume for 4 channels simultaneously.

Synchronous motor is adopted.
