Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with outer rotor motor.

Uses a newly developed 6-pole hysteresis synchronous outer rotor motor for cassettes.
Since the rotor (rotor) rotates outside the stator, the outer rotor motor has features such as less uneven rotation due to flywheel hardening, less leakage flux and less heat generation, and stronger starting torque.

The operation mechanism is a keyboard type for ease of use.
The Rewind button is a push-lock type, and the Fast button is a non-lock type that makes it easy to start the recording part.

The head is completely sealed with permalloy.

The read / write amplifier uses an all-silicon transistor configuration.
Low-noise 2SC369 is adopted in the first stage and 20 dB NFB is applied to the output stage by the development of a special multi-layer winding transformer.

It is equipped with a large VU meter with a dedicated booth and amplifier so that accurate recording level can be checked.

Equipped with a monitor circuit that can monitor the source during recording.

It has an output volume for playback only.

It is equipped with a bias oscillation circuit using a Pc15W silicon transistor.
Uses a pot core and an oscillation transformer wound with Litz wire.

Equipped with DIN connector.

Equipped with a uni-directional stereo microphone for easy Hi-Fi recording.

Equipped with 3-digit index counter.
