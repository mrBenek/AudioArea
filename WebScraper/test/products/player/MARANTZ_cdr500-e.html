  • Marantz CDR500

  • ¥ 110,000 (around 2000)

Commentary

A commercial CD/CD recorder compatible with CD-R/RW.

The Disc At Once (DAO) feature allows you to create pre-mastered discs.

Dubbing recording at constant speed and double speed is possible.

Equipped with synchronized recording start function without missing head.

It is equipped with an SRC (Sampling Rate Converter) and supports digital sources from 11 kHz to 56 kHz.
It also supports 44.1 kHzSRC bypass recording (guaranteed range : source deviation 44.1 khz ± 100 ppm).

Equipped with an auto-finalize recording function.

It supports the input and display of CD-TEXT character data, and the title can be input to the disc and each track.
Also, CD-TEXT copying is possible during internal dubbing.

Equipped with copy protect selection function, setting free / restricted / prohibited is possible.

Equipped with a digital loop (COAX) output terminal, recording can be performed simultaneously on up to five devices.

Equipped with digital recording volume (+ 6 db ~ -63dB, 1 db step) and balance function.

Equipped with a bit stream D/A converter and a sigma delta A/D converter.

Automatically recognizes CD/MD/DAT track IDs.

Equipped with 2 x finalizing / erasing functions (Track/Disc/TOC).

Compliant with Red Book and Orange Book PartII/PartIII.

Two digital inputs (RCA/OPT (square type)) and two analog inputs (XLR balance / RCA) are installed as input terminals.

Route 3 digital outputs (RCA-OPT / RCA-loop outputs) and one analog outputs (RCA-loop outputs) are installed.

Equipped with 3-pole type AC cord.
