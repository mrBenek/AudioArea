Commentary

Stereo tape recorder with built-in cross-field head and all-silicon transistor amplifier.

Cross-field head is used for the head part.
In this conventional recording method, both a signal current and a bias current are applied to the recording head. Once recorded, the signal is erased or weakened by the influence of a wide bias magnetic field. This phenomenon is particularly noticeable at high frequencies.
In order to solve this problem, the cross-field method applies the signal current to the recording head and the bias current to the external bias head. Since these heads are displaced relative to each other in the specified spatial position, the signal recorded on the tape is recorded without being affected by the bias magnetic field.

Hysteresis synchronous 2-speed motor is used for the mechanism part.

Equipped with a track selector knob for easy switching between stereo and monaural.

Equipped with tone control function and bass switch to emphasize low range.

It has an equalizer for each tape speed.

Equipped with a tape shifter that operates during fast forward and rewind.

Equipped with a 4-digit counter with reset button and two VU meters.

Equipped with a tape cleaner.
This cleaner removes dust and iron oxide powder attached to recording tapes, and eliminates the squeaking noise generated during recording and playback.

Equipped with automatic stop, automatic shut-off, automatic pinch roller release mechanism.
When the tape reaches the fully wound or unwound area, the automatic shut-off lever drops and the tape stops automatically. The automatic shut-off mechanism stops only the operation of the mechanism, and the automatic shut-off mechanism turns off the power supply at the same time. In either case, the pinch roller automatically separates from the capstan shaft, preventing the rubber from wearing out and the shaft from being deformed while the pinch roller remains in close contact with the capstan shaft. This is the automatic pinch roller release.

Equipped with automatic lever release.
With automatic lever release, when the tape reaches the end of the tape, the automatic shut-off lever falls as soon as the tape is fully wound, and the fast-forward / rewind knob automatically and the tape stops.

Equipped with universal power supply voltage switching device.

The speaker unit is equipped with two 10 cm cone speakers.

Both horizontal and vertical specifications are possible.

There were 2 kinds of variations, a leather case and a walnut case.
