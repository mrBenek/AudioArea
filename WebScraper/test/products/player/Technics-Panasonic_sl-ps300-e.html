Commentary

Mash This is a CD player that uses a 1-bit DAC and has extensive editing functions.

The MASH1-bit DAC is adopted for the D/A conversion part, and non-linear distortion such as 0 cross distortion is not generated in principle, and high-precision D/A conversion is realized.

The servo circuit is equipped with a digital servo.
In digital servo systems, the disc status is automatically read by a microcomputer, and the focus, tracking, and traverse servos are adjusted by software processing according to the disc. This greatly improves playability.
In addition, digitization of the servo system has made the servomechanism resistant to changes over time.

A high-precision linear motor is adopted for the traverse mechanism that moves the pickup, and together with the direct 10 key, it realizes high-speed access of about one second until the start of the performance, and it also obtains high durability and quietness.
In addition, the CCF lens of the pickup uses an aspherical integrally molded glass lens with excellent durability.

Equipped with a CD editing function, you can automatically distribute songs to both sides of the tape and set it to a recording standby state just by selecting the length of the tape.
It also has built-in features such as time fade, which automatically fades out at a specified time, and link, which is convenient for editing from multiple CDs.

It is equipped with a synchro-edit function and can be edited and recorded in conjunction with Panasonic cassette decks (RS-BR465, RS-TR355, RS-TR555) equipped with compatible terminals.

Equipped with auto peak search, recording level can be easily set by detecting the peak level.

It is equipped with an auto queue that detects the position where the music actually starts and waits.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
