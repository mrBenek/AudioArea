Commentary

A CD player equipped with a multibit Λ S. L. C.

A multi-bit Λ S. L. C. (Lambda Super Linear Converter) is used for the D/A conversion method.
By shifting the digital data at the 0 cross point during D/A conversion, this method theoretically eliminates the 0 cross distortion at low volume levels by shifting the digital data at the zero cross point, reducing noise and distortion and reproducing the afterglow of music more accurately.

The digital filter is equipped with a 20-bit 8 x oversampling digital filter.
In order to improve sound quality at low levels, the digital filter is of a type with improved emphasis calculation accuracy. In addition to enabling accurate reproduction, the output level is controlled to 12 levels in the digital stage, reducing sound quality degradation at the output level.

Digital servo is used in the servo circuit to detect the disc condition and realize the optimum servocontrol for each disc.

It uses a center mount mechanism and the pickup mechanism is located in the center of the chassis. By optimizing the weight balance of the body, vibration and resonance applied to the chassis when driven by the motor are minimized and the effect on sound quality is eliminated.

High-quality parts with high sound quality are used for power parts such as the coupling part, D/A converter, power decoupling, and servo system.
We are also pursuing high-quality sound reproduction by applying anti-vibration measures everywhere.

Equipped with an optical digital output.

Gold plated terminals are used for the headphone and analog output terminals.

Equipped with auto-edit function.
With this function, the music is divided into the first half and the second half at the beginning of the music number closest to 1/2 of the total playing time of the disc, and the sound can be recorded on the A side and B side of the tape without waste.

Equipped with functions such as direct selection, automatic search, manual search, program of up to 20 songs, program call, repeat of all songs / program / A-B, pause standby, timer play, auto space, display dimmer, etc.

There are 2 kinds of variations, DCD-735-N with gold finish and DCD-735-K with black finish.

Wireless remote control is included.
