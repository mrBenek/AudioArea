  • VICTOR/JVC KD-V6

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

A cassette deck with SA independent 3-head system.

It employs a Senalloy head for the recording head and a Metapalm head for the reproducing head, making it an independent 3-head configuration.
A solid casting head housing has been adopted for the head mount base. The solid block absorbs microvibration to further stabilize running performance, prevent modulation noise, and improve high-frequency characteristics.

The mechanism uses a 2-motor full logic and a silent mechanism with a mechanical drive motor. All modes are moved by detecting the position of the cam linked to the mechanical drive motor with a microcomputer.

DC configuration is adopted for the amplifier section.
It also uses a power supply circuit with a built-in tracking regulator that stabilizes the operation of the amplifier.

Equipped with auto REC mute.
When this function is pressed in the REC mode, a silent space of approximately 4 to 5 seconds is automatically set to the REC standby mode. In addition, Victor's unique circuit design allows for no recording from the stop mode.

It is equipped with an index scan, which allows you to scan the A and B sides one after another while playing the head part of the song for 10 seconds at a time.

Equipped with 2 memory functions of STOP and PLAY, block repeat between 2 points is possible by using both STOP and PLAY memory in addition to the normal STOP memory and PLAY memory.
In combination with the reverse mode, block repeat across A and B faces is possible.

Equipped with a music scan.

Equipped with a 4-digit electronic counter that can be switched to the stopwatch.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.
The DD-VR9 is equipped with an MPX filter to prevent malfunctions.

Equipped with 2-color FL peak meter capable of peak hold.
It is also equipped with a digital peak display function, which enables high-precision recording level setting by always digitally displaying the peak value of the signal.

Equipped with an auto tape select function.

Equipped with an output volume.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism with safety lock.

Remote control terminal is mounted.
