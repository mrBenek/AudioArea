Commentary

A 5-string CD player with a roulette type CD changer.

It uses a five disc playing roulette system. If you press the OPEN/CLOSE button while playing one disc, the tray opens and you can replace the remaining four discs while playing.

The DA converter is equipped with double high-precision S. L. C to eliminate 0 cross distortion and phase difference between left and right channels.
The digital filter uses a 20-bit digital filter.

Equipped with Intelligent Disc Scan (I.D. S) capability, the roulette tray can be rotated in both clockwise and counterclockwise directions for quick disk-to-disk access.

Equipped with three types of random functions : 3-mode random / full random, program random, and disk sequential random.
In the Full Random mode, the microcomputer selects and plays songs randomly from among five discs by pressing the Random button and then the Play button. In the Program Random mode, the microcomputer selects and plays songs randomly from among programmed songs by pressing the Program / Direct button and then pressing the Random button and then the Play button.
With Disk Sequential Random, you press the Random button to specify the disk number and then press the Play button. The microcomputer randomly selects and plays music from the disks in the specified disk order. You can select up to five disks and you can specify the same disk more than twice.

Equipped with 5 types of repeat performance functions, 5-way repeat / 1-track repeat, 1-disc repeat, all repeat, program repeat, and random repeat are possible.

It has a program performance function of 20 songs.

Equipped with a disk-level file function, you can store your desired level of memory on each disk.
Up to 100 disks can be stored and the memory is retained even when the power is turned off.

In addition to two analog systems, one digital coaxial output system is mounted.

Aluminum front panel is used for the front panel.

Wireless remote control is included.
