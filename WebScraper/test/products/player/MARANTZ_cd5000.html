  • Marantz CD5000

  • ¥ 30,000 (released in March 2000)

Commentary

This is a Century Series CD player with high sound quality by introducing high sound quality technology and high sound quality parts.

The D/A converter is equipped with a bit stream DAC.

Equipped with a digital servo.

It is equipped with a digital output terminal that is coaxial with the light.

It is equipped with functions such as edit, peak search, shuffle, program of up to 30 songs, repeat, fade out / fade in, etc.

Equipped with CDTEXT display function.

CD-RW playback is supported.

Wireless remote control is included.

There are 2 kinds of variations, titanium gold and black.
