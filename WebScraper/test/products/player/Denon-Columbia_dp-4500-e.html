  • DENON/COLUMBIA DP-4500

  • ¥ 78,500 (around 1968)

Commentary

A top-of-the-line record player for FM stereo broadcast transmission jointly researched and developed by NHK Technical Research Institute and Nihon Columbia.

MC type cartridge DL 103, exclusive matching transformer DL 103 t, tone arm DA 303 transformer DL - t, tone arm DA -, belt drive type turntable DP-101.

The turntable unit adopts magnetic floating system and belt drive system with polyurethane belt.
