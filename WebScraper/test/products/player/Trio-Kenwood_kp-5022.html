Commentary

A record player equipped with a D. D. (direct drive) motor and full auto mechanism.

D. D. motor is used for the motor part.
A DC motor with ultra-low-speed electronic control is used at the heart of the direct drive to achieve stable rotation.
Once the rotational speed is set, the servo circuit maintains an accurate rotational speed without being affected by power supply voltage fluctuations. In addition, there is no need to replace the capstan depending on the power supply frequency, and there is no reduction mechanism by belt or idler, so initial performance can be maintained semi-permanently.
In addition, there is no trouble caused by the switching mechanism because the speed switching is purely electronic.
33 1/3 and 45 rpm are equipped with independent fine adjustment mechanisms, allowing fine adjustment within ± 3%.

One of the features of the Direct Drive Player is the inertial efficiency of the turntable. However, in consideration of the structural mass distribution in the KP-5022, the inertial efficiency is 200 kg ・ cm.2It reaches constant speed with only 1/2 rotation.

The arm part is designed to prevent resonance over a wide band by tapering the highly sensitive pipe arm of static balance type.
A special damper material is used in the pivot section to improve howling and signal-to-noise ratio.

In order to eliminate variations in resonance points in wooden cabinets, resonance is prevented by low foaming resin molding.
When howling is likely to occur depending on the installation location and acoustic conditions, it can be reduced by floating the panel by the mounted panel floating mechanism.

Equipped with an anti-skating mechanism, it eliminates the inside force that causes distortion during light needle pressure operation. The KP 5022 uses a needle pressure direct reading type that can be adjusted to the needle pressure during performance.

Equipped with pilot lamp combined with needle pressure gauge, and overhang can be adjusted when the arm is fixed to the stand.

Equipped with a full auto mechanism, operations such as auto lead-in, auto cut, Oatley turn and auto repeat can be performed automatically. These operations can be switched to manual operation.
In addition, it is equipped with a record size selector, so that you can start with the record board.

A muting switch shuts out the shock noise generated when the needle tip comes off or lands on the record board.

A unique electromagnetic relay type delay circuit is adopted, and the arm starts operation as soon as the start button is pressed.

Equipped with a pose elevation function and can be paused temporarily.

The plug-in connector adopts a slide lock type so that the shell can be easily and surely replaced.

As a countermeasure for CD-4 regeneration, an ultra-low capacity cord of 32.5pF/m is adopted, and it is designed to limit the distance from the cartridge output terminal to the pin plug to 100 pf or less.

The cartridge is equipped with a stylus horizontal adjuster that can adjust the inclination of the needle tip.
