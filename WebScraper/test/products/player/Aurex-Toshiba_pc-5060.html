  • Aurex PC-5060

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

Cassette deck with editor switch for air check.

It is equipped with an editor switch that turns off the signal during recording without stopping the tape. This allows you to control the interval between songs to the desired length.

In addition to the line input on the rear panel, there is also an AUX connector on the front panel.

Equipped with wide-scale meter of -30dB to + 4 db.

Equipped with a master level control that can adjust both left and right recording levels simultaneously.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise isolation system.

Equipped with chrome tape and auto-selector.

Equipped with a limiter switch.

Equipped with line and microphone input selector switches.
