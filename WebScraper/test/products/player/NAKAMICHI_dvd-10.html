  • Nakamichi DVD-10

  • ¥ 78,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

DVD player with a built-in Dolby digital decoder.

It is equipped with a decoder for Dolby digital reproduction and can output 5.1 ch analog output.
You can also adjust the Center / Surround / Subwoofer settings and the delay time of each Center / Surround speaker.

Equipped with digital output, digital output of DTS sound is possible.

Equipped with an audio D/A converter that supports the 96kHz/24-bit linear PCM format, the highest standard for DVD format.

DVD video data recorded at 8-bit / 13.5 MHz is converted to 10-bit video data and the sampling frequency is stepped up to 27 MHz. This data is then D/A converted by a 10-bit video D/A converter to expand the dynamic range of video.

Component video output is installed, and component video data recorded on DVD can be directly D/A converted and output.

Equipped with a Virtual Surround position, you can virtually play a disc recorded with Dolby Digital 5.1 ch with only two speakers.

The pickup part uses a twin focus type that reads DVD/CD with one pickup.

You can operate the remote control according to the screen display.

Equipped with jog & shuttle search.

Equipped with dimmer control function, you can adjust the brightness of the illumination in 3 stages, and you can also turn off the light.

It is equipped with an intro play function that plays DVD chapters and CD tracks every 5 seconds.

It is equipped with a bookmark function that can store up to 3 disc titles in 3 places each.

Equipped with a headphone port that can adjust the volume level.

Wireless remote control is included.
