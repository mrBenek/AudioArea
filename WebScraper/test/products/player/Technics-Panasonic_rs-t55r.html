  • Technics RS-T55R

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

Equipped with 3 nr, double reverse cassette deck for recording / reproducing and playback.

Non-stop series playback is possible for up to 24 hours (when using 90 minutes tape)
You can repeat playback 8 times consecutively from front to back of Deck A to front and back of Deck B.

Double-speed editing is possible.

Equipped with Synchro Rec Mute.
If you stop the playback deck while you are editing, the recording deck will be in a pause state after making about four seconds of non-recording.

Equipped with synchronized start / synchronized stop function.

Equipped with 3 nr of dbx, Dolby B and C.

Uses a high hardness permalloy head.

It uses a new audio IC with a bias cancel method.

Equipped with a music selector that is convenient for starting a song and an L/R independent rec volume.

Timer Rec & Play function.

2 color FL peak meter.

Auto Tape Selector.
