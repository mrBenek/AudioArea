  • Marantz CD-23Da

  • ¥ 200,000 (released in 1997)

Commentary

A upgraded CD player with digital input based on CD-23.

The mechanism part is equipped with a swing arm mechanical CDM-9.

Equipped with OPT/COAX digital input / output jacks.

A bit stream type D/A converter DAC7 is mounted on the D/A conversion part.

NEW HDAM, a newly developed 4-layer board, has been adopted to improve circuit speed, stability and S/N.

The power supply uses a toroidal transformer with less leakage flux.

An aluminum top remote controller with output level control is included.
