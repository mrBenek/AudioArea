  • Pioneer PL-L5

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

A linear tracking system player designed by utilizing the technology and know-how obtained through the development of PL-L1.

Equipped with electronic full auto mechanism, each operation of auto lead-in, Oatley turn and auto repeat can be performed with high precision.

An SH rotor type motor has been adopted as the drive motor to realize excellent characteristics.
In addition, the combination of a high-torque quartz PLL and a large turntable ensures high stability with no rotational speed fluctuation even under a load of 220g.

Linear DD arm is used for the tone arm part.

A coaxial suspension cabinet has been adopted for the cabinet to improve the howling resistance.

Equipped with quick play and quick stop mechanisms.

Since it is a front operation type, the tone arm can be operated even with the cover closed.
