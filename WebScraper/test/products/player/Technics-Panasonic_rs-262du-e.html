  • Technics RS-262DU

  • ¥ 33,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

This cassette deck can be used for outdoor recording by using batteries.

It uses AC-DC2 power supply system and can be driven by using exclusive battery case RP-097 and 7 single dry cells (sold separately).

LH head is adopted for the head.

In order to take advantage of the characteristics of chrome tape, a 70 μ S equalizer circuit has been adopted to improve characteristics such as SN ratio and dynamic range.

Equipped with a light and battery check switch.

An indicator is displayed when recording CrO2.

Equipped with an auto release mechanism.
