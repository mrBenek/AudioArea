  • Technics RS-M211

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1981)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck for metal tape.

The drive motor is equipped with an electronically controlled DC motor.

An MX head is used for recording and re-recording.

Equipped with a 4-position tape selector with metal position.

Equipped with a twin level meter.

As a noise reduction system, Dolby NR circuit is mounted.

It is equipped with functions such as rewind autoplay and cue & review.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.
