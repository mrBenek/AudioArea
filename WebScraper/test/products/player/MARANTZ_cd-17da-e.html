  • Marantz CD-17Da

  • ¥ 99,000 (released in 1998)

Commentary

This is an upgraded version of the CD player that inherits the basic performance of the CD-17D, such as the digital servo IC CD7 and the bitstream D/A converter TDA1547 (DAC7).

You can play CD-RW discs.

CD text containing character information is supported.

The mechanism part is equipped with Philips VAM1201.

Equipped with D/A converter function corresponding to 32 khz, 44.1 khz and 48 khz.

Comes with an aluminum top wireless remote control.
