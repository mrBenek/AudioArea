Commentary

A CD player with 20bitK2 processing.

The digital circuit is equipped with Victor's own 20-bit K2 processing, and the 20 bit reproduction technology is polished with the sound of the studio master to obtain excellent expressive power.

20-bit digital audio output is available and can be used as a CD transport.
In addition, a 3 fs 20-bit digital input is installed Route 3, and as a 20-bit K2D / A converter, digital outputs of BS, MD, and DAT can be connected.
The 3fs-compatible 20-bit K2 processing function can be used alone to convert 16-bit digital signals into 20-bit output signals.

The mechanism uses an airtight structure, manual clamp and top-loading system, which reduces vibration of the CD disc due to external sound pressure and realizes stable high-precision playback.
To further increase the rigidity of the drive system, we have adopted an in-house high-reliability motor with an extremely thick spindle shaft with a diameter of 6 mm. Thus, we the drive system highly accurate.
By positioning the CD pickup at the center of gravity of the player as a whole, the operation of the pickup is stabilized.

The digital and analog sections combine a newly developed high-speed balanced transmission circuit with a unique K2 interface to greatly reduce mutual interference.

An independent power transformer is set for each of the digital section, analog section and DAC section to suppress interference between circuits via power supply.
In addition, it is separated into three blocks : digital, analog, and mechanism to reduce mutual interference.

The chassis has a 2-layer structure for improved rigidity and shielding performance.

Door open angle of CD can be adjusted by 2 steps.

It has a synchronous input terminal that is effective when used as a CD transport.

Equipped with a display ON/OFF function.

Wireless remote control is included.

There was a large diameter clamper as an option sold separately.
