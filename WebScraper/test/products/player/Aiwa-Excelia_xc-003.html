  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1988)

Commentary

A CD player with quick peak search and optical / coaxial digital output.

Equipped with two digital output terminals that are coaxial with the optical system.

It is equipped with a quick peak search function that detects the peak value in approximately 30 seconds per disc and repeatedly plays that part for approximately 3 seconds, making it easy to adjust gain when recording on a cassette deck.

It employs a 4-times oversampling method that samples at 176.4 kHz, which is 4 times the sampling frequency of 44.1 kHz of kHz. This method, combined with the use of a 16-bit digital filter, improves phase characteristics in all bands.

The mechanism part adopts a floating structure using a unique spring to reduce vibration.

Top lock structure screws are used for the top plate and side plate to reduce vibration.

Equipped with A pause B edit function, editing can be done efficiently when dubbing from a CD to a cassette.

Equipped with 20-key direct music search.

8 cm single disk is supported.

Equipped with 20 songs music calendar.

Wireless remote control is included.
