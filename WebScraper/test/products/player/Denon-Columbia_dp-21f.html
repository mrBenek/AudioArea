Commentary

A full auto player with an electronically controlled tone arm.

The turntable drive is driven by a linear drive motor using DENON's unique magnetic recording detection method. Combined with an electronically controlled tone arm, it realizes a highly reliable full-auto mechanism.
In addition, a newly developed microprocessor is used for the control circuit, and the DENON quartz with bidirectional servo ensures reliable rotational performance.

The tone arm section uses an electronic control tone arm that uses DENON's unique contactless system.
The newly developed microprocessor drives the horizontal motion and lifter motion with two separate dedicated motors. Manual operation of the horizontal motion and lifter motion of the arm can be used even during performance. In addition, the non-contact structure prevents mechanical constraint even during operation of the auto mechanism, ensuring excellent arm sensitivity and operability.
In addition, an electronic servo lifter is used to maintain a constant speed during the up and down movement of the arm without being affected by the needle pressure or ambient temperature, thereby suppressing the shock of the needle tip and record.

The tone arm is equipped with an electronic needle pressure application type dynamic balance type straight arm.
It uses the torque of a vertical drive motor to apply an appropriate needle pressure at all times. The needle pressure can be increased or decreased freely by manipulating the needle pressure volume during performance.

The cartridge is equipped with DL-60 MM type cartridge.

Equipped with quick repeat mechanism as full auto function.
This function allows you to resume playing from the beginning of the record without returning the tone arm to the arm rest when you want to listen repeatedly.

Selected text Self-illuminated buttons are used.
The buttons for record size, rotation number, repeat, and lifter are self-illuminated using LEDs so that the operation status can be checked at a glance.
