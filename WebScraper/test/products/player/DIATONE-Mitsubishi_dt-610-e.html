Commentary

Cassette deck with built-in electronic digital timer.

It has a built-in audio timer that adjusts to the power frequency.
This allows you to record your absence, play an alarm, play sleep, use an audio timer for an external source, or use a digital timer.

It has a built-in Dolby system.

Uses a DC servo motor.

It uses a tape selector with independent bias and equalizer to bring out the performance of normal tapes and special tapes.

Equipped with a full auto-stop mechanism, it automatically releases the pinch roller and head block when the tape is finished, and returns the operation button when recording, playing back, fast forwarding or rewinding.

Soft eject allows for smooth tape loading and unloading.

Equipped with a muting mechanism.
