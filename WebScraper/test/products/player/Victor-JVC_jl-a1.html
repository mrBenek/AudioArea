  • VICTOR/JVC JL-A1

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1974)

Commentary

An automatic player system with a Erm finish.

Built-in auto return and auto cut mechanism.
In addition, the motor is designed to turn off whenever the arm is on the rest.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used as the drive motor.

TH system universal arm is adopted for the tone arm.

MM type cartridge is adopted, and CD-4 playback is possible only by changing the needle.
