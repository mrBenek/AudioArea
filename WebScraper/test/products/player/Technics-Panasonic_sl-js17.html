  • Technics SL-JS17

  • ¥ 42,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

A full auto player developed for Ceder Command.

Equipped with a full auto mechanism, you can start playing just by adjusting the record size and rotation speed and pressing the start button.
Also, the arm returns automatically after the performance ends.

Comes with a stand that fits the Ceder command.

There are 2 color variations, black and grayish white.
