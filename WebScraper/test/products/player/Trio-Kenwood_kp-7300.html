Commentary

A record player developed in pursuit of the theme of picking up the information inscribed on the disc as it is.

The turntable section focuses on transient load fluctuations that cannot be controlled by the servo circuit alone. Diameter 330 mm, Weight 2.6 kg, Finished mass 440 kg / cm2It uses a heavy duty turntable (including rubber sheet). By driving this turntable with a 20-pole 30-slot motor with very low torque ripple, the transient load that occurs when the pickup traces a sound groove with a large dynamic range is also suppressed. Unlike the wow flutter, which is considered to be a periodic load fluctuation, by controlling the non-periodic transient load, the amplitude of the sound groove can be captured more accurately.

In order to suppress torque ripple of the motor, the weight of the cabinet is increased by installing MC material and 1.6 mm thick steel plate on the back of the cabinet using hard chip board.
MC material is a concrete made by adding asbestos and molding it under high pressure. It has very low water absorbency and moisture content and high rigidity. This not only eliminates howling, but also reduces disturbance and provides sound quality in a wide frequency range and low frequency range.

A dual suspension type absorber is used for the insulator.
This absorber has a unique structure with low rigidity in the longitudinal direction and high rigidity in the lateral direction, reducing vibration in both the longitudinal and lateral directions. In particular, it reduces lateral vibration that occurs when a turntable with a large inertial mass is driven by a motor with a large starting torque.

The dust cover is made of acrylic material with a large vibration damping ratio. In addition, the top wall is made thicker and ribs are provided inside each corner to increase rigidity and suppress split vibration.

For the arm pipe part of the tone arm, an aluminum pipe with a diameter of 10 φ and a thickness of 1 mm is treated with hard anodized aluminum. In addition, it is supported by brass with a large vibration damping rate to suppress split vibration.
In addition, the shell finger is made of aluminum die cast that is molded integrally with the head shell. In addition, a rib is provided at the lower part of the finger to increase rigidity and suppress split vibration.

A 6 mm thick rubber sheet is used for the rubber sheet of the turntable, and the split vibration is suppressed by pursuing adhesion.

The cartridge uses DM-11, a DM (dual magnet) cartridge.
By making the mass of the vibration system as small as possible, this cartridge eliminates the resonance frequency of the split vibration from outside the audible band, thereby improving the resolution of the high-frequency range and improving the transient response to a pulsed input.
