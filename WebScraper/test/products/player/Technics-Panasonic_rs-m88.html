  • Technics RS-M88

  • ¥ 145,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with quartz DD motor.

The mechanism uses a 2-motor system. The drive motor is a Quartz Lock Digital FG Servo DD motor, and the reel motor is equipped with a coreless motor.

IC logic control system is adopted.

The new SX sendust head is used for the read / write head, and the double-gap sendust ferrite head is used for the erase head.

Equipped with digital 2-color FL display.

Equipped with a 4-position tape selector with metal position.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Equipped with an output volume.

By connecting the optional wireless remote control unit, you can operate it from a remote location.
