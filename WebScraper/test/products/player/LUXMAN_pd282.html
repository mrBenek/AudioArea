Audio Database

  • LUXMAN PD282

  • ¥ 49,500 (released in January 1976)

Commentary

A direct drive player system descended from the PD121 and PD131.

The drive motor is equipped with an AC servo motor.

A high-sensitivity universal tone arm is used for the tone arm.

A MM type Shure cartridge is used.

Equipped with easy-to-use 1-lever 2-action switch mechanism, etc.
