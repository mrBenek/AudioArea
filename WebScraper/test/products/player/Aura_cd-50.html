  • Aura CD-50

  • ¥ 170,000 (released in August 1992)

Commentary

A CD player created by the best technology and long and thorough listening tests.

The design was done by Kenneth Grange, a famous designer of Pentagram.

A single-beam type low vibration swing arm mechanism is adopted for the mechanism part.

The D/A converter uses the PDM bitstream method.

The loading mechanism and vibration waveform of the entire assembly are analyzed in detail, and vibration is controlled by local braking.

Comes with a multi-functional wireless remote control.
