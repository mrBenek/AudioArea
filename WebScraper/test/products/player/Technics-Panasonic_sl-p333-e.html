Commentary

CD player equipped with 4 dac linear 18 bit conversion system and editing function.

The D/A conversion section uses a 4DAC linear 18-bit conversion system.
This circuit employs a 4 dac configuration using a D/A converter for the positive and negative signals of Lch and Rch, respectively. By combining only the high linearity portion of each signal with a differential amplifier, the 0 cross distortion is eliminated in principle.
In addition, the high-resolution 4 fs digital filter has been adopted to increase the reproduction capability of small-level signals.

In order to reduce the adverse effect of vibration from the floor, four large insulators are adopted, and the optical deck is floated on the chassis by double insulators made of spring and viscoelastic material.
In addition, the BMC base with excellent vibration damping characteristics is adopted in the optical deck section, and high vibration damping effect is obtained.

Equipped with an optical digital terminal.

It is equipped with an edit guide that can be used for recording from CD to tape.
With this function, you can insert a CD and select the length of the tape you want to record (90 minutes, 60 minutes, or 46 minutes). The recorded music is automatically distributed to the A side and B side of the tape, and it is displayed along with the remaining amount of tape after recording.

Equipped with a disk link function, when editing and recording from multiple CDs, after recording from the previous CD, the number of songs that can be recorded from another CD and the remaining time after that are automatically calculated.

Equipped with shuttle search, it is possible to quickly start the head at a wide range of search speed from 1/8 to 76 times in both forward and reverse directions.

Equipped with a 4-mode time display, it is possible to display 4 types of time switching display : elapsed time and remaining time of performance music, elapsed time and remaining time of all music or program music.

Direct access with 10 keys is possible.

Equipped with auto-cue function, it can detect the starting position of performance.

Equipped with a repeat function.

It comes with a wireless remote control that provides random play functionality only.
