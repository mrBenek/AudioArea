  • ONKYO MD-111M

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1994)

Commentary

MC recorder of INTEC275 series.

Equipped with linear single-bit A/D converter and linear phase D/A converter, it enables clear recording and playback.
Digital recording from CD to MD is also possible.

A die-cast mechanism is adopted.

Equipped with optical digital output / input.

It has editing functions such as Divide/Move/Combine/Erase.
It is also equipped with a title input function of up to 100 characters per song and approximately 1700 characters per disc.

Equipped with direct selection / program play / music scan / random play / 3 mode (1 song / all songs / A-B) repeat.

AMS dial is adopted in order to use multi-functions easily.

Equipped with a level meter.

Equipped with microphone input terminal.

SCMS (Serial Copy Management System) allows only one generation of digital recording.
