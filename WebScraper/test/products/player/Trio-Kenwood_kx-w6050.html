Commentary

W quick reverse deck equipped with Dolby HX-PRO, etc.

The Dolby HX-PRO is mounted on the A and B decks, enabling high-quality recording with extended high-pitched sound.

Two tapes can be recorded simultaneously from one source.
Double-speed CCRS (B-deck) and double-speed tape-to-tape dubbing are also available.

Relay Play / Relay Wreck and Index Scan (A deck) are available.

When connected to a Kenwood XS-marked CD player or LD player, the player continuously plays back the loudest part of the sound, and the setting of the optimum level value is completed only by adjusting the recording level in the recording standby state.
