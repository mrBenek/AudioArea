  • Technics RS-8R

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

Reverse deck of record jacket size equipped with dbx system.

Equipped with a playback reverse function, continuous playback from one side to two side or from two side to one side, and switching of tape running direction is possible.

A rotary head system is adopted for the head part, and an AX amorphous head using an amorphous alloy is adopted for the recording / reproducing head.

The rotating system uses a two motor mechanism with motors for the capstan, for the take-up reel and for the rapid feed / rewind. The capstan is designed for forward and reverse independently.

A dbx system is installed as a noise reduction system.

It is equipped with a blank skip, and when an unrecorded portion lasts for 10 seconds during playback, it automatically fast-forwards to the beginning of the next song.

Built-in functions such as Music Repeat, Music Selector and Auto REC mute.

Synchronized recording with record players (SL-6, SL-3, SL-V5) is possible by using a separately sold synchronized recording cord.
