  • TRIO KX-5P

  • ¥ 23,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

A playback-only stereo cassette deck capable of synchronized play.

The synchronized connection code included with KX-5P can be connected to KX-71R/KX-51 for recording start, pause and stop synchronized operation during dubbing.
Turn on the sync switch of KX-5P and press the Play key. KX-71R/KX-51 can be recorded simultaneously.
Next, when you stop or pause, the KX-71R/KX-51 switches from the recording state to the recording pause state with the auto rec mute function.

It is equipped with a headphone output with a high output of 500 mw so that it can drive a small speaker sufficiently.
You can adjust the volume to your preference.

Equipped with a D. P.S. S. mechanism, when you press the rewind or fast-forward key while playing, it automatically detects and plays the next song or the beginning of the currently playing song.

The playback equalizer characteristics are automatically switched depending on whether the cassette half has a detection hole or not.
(Tapes without a detection hole cannot be selected.)
