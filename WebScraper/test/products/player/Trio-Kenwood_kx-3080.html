  • KENWOOD KX-3080

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

This is a cassette deck that simply pursues sound quality.

Uses a power loading mechanism that can open / close the cassette door with one touch operation.

Equipped with a Dolby noise reduction B/C to reduce tape hissing.
In addition, Dolby HX-Pro is installed.

Features include DPSS, Rec Volume / Rec balance control, Auto Tape Selector, System Control and Headphone Jack.
