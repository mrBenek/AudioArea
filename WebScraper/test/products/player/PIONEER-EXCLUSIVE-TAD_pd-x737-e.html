  • Pioneer PD-X737

  • ¥ 57,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

A compact 360 mm CD player with high-precision technology.

It is equipped with a new disk stabilizer that effectively suppresses unnecessary vibration generated during disk rotation and makes signal reading accurate.

Equipped with a high-precision digital filter.

Pioneer's original floating structure, in which the optical mechanism is floated from the chassis, has been further developed into a low-center-of-gravity floating mechanism, which prevents unnecessary lateral vibration and ensures accurate signal reading.

Equipped with auto program edit, program accumulated time display function, pause program function, etc.
It is also equipped with a power on start function that enables timer start.
