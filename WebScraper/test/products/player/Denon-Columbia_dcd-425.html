  • DENON DCD-425

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

A cost-effective CD player.

The D/A converter uses an A. M. N. S. Δ Σ D/A converter to prevent the generation of 0 cross distortion, which is the main cause of sound quality degradation during D/A conversion.

The digital filter uses an 8 x oversampling digital filter.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

It has a large display and displays information such as total number of tracks, total playing time, track playing, index number, and elapsed or remaining playing time.

The mechanism part is located in the center of the chassis to reduce vibration that causes noise.

Equipped with auto-edit function.
This function divides the music into the first half and the second half with the beginning of the number closest to 1/2 of the total playing time of the disc. It can record on the A side and B side of the tape without waste.

It is equipped with functions such as direct selection function, automatic search, manual search, program playback of up to 20 songs, pause standby, repeat function between all songs / one song / A and B, random performance, autospace, introscan, etc.

Wireless remote control is included.
