  • SONY PS-LX300H

  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A manual record player that matches a full-size component.

It is equipped with an aluminum diecast turntable with a large moment of inertia and a mass of approximately 900g. A large type insulator is also used to absorb external vibration.
In addition, an inside four scan seller (anti-skating mechanism) is installed.

Adoption of universal tone arm allows replacement of shell and cartridge.

Equipped with speed adjustment function with strobe.

Two spare replacement needles were included as standard.
