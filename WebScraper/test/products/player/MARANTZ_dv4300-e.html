  • Marantz DV4300

Commentary

A thin design DVD player with progressive function.

Equipped with a 2-3 pull-down progressive circuit.

The PAL/NTSC conversion function allows you to watch DVD-Software recorded in the European PAL format on an NT servo control system.

Equipped with a 192 kHz / 24-bit D/A converter.

Supports DVD-R (video format), video CD (Ver2.0), and S-VCD playback.

Equipped with a 3D surround function that allows you to enjoy a virtual surround effect just by connecting to a TV.

You can operate the top menu by operating the front panel key.

Equipped with front panel lock mechanism to prevent erroneous operation.

Equipped with a dimmer function to darken the display.

You can play MP3 files created on a PC.

It is possible to play CD-R/RW recording images in JPEG format.
