  • VICTOR/JVC KD-25SA-Z

  • Price unknown (around 1979)

Commentary

A cassette deck with built-in ANRS.

An SA (Sen-alloy) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.

The mechanism uses large flywheels to reduce wow and flutter.

Equipped with a 5-point peak indicator.

A gear oil dump cassette door is adopted.

An automatic input switching system is adopted, taking advantage of the input characteristics of MIC, DIN, and LINE.

It is equipped with an original ANRS as a noise reduction system.

Built-in timer standby mechanism enables absence recording and alarm clock playback.

There was a BTS rack handle sold separately.
