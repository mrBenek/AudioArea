  • VICTOR/JVC MTR-15MTD

  • ¥ 88,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A tape deck with 4 ch and 2 ch recording and playback functions.

Uses a 3-stage direct-coupled equalizer amplifier.

Equipped with an automatic stop device that automatically stops the motor when the tape finishes winding.

Each channel level of the 4-ch output can be adjusted.

Equipped with 4-channel independent level meter and level adjustment knob.

Equipped with a pause mechanism.

Equipped with a dust cover made of teak and plastic.
