Commentary

A CD player with high sound quality and high performance.

The D/A converter is equipped with a double integration type D/A converter with a built-in sample-and-hold circuit to obtain sound quality with excellent linearity.
We have also adopted a newly developed high-precision digital filter for digital filters.

Since the digital circuits from the digital signal processing circuit to the D/A converter are synchronized with a single master clock, beat generation due to clock interference of two or more systems does not occur in principle.

The power supply circuit is a 3-winding transformer with independent sections of digital, analog and drive circuits, and each section is stabilized to an independent 6-constant voltage power supply to eliminate interference between circuits.

It employs a 70 mm Φ disk stabilizer developed by Orex Co., Ltd., reducing the natural vibration of the disk and greatly improving the signal reading accuracy of the pickup.

Equipped with a large insulator.

Equipped with a newly developed digital peak search function, it can detect the maximum signal level recorded on the disc and play it back, making it possible to reliably set the level at the time of recording.

It is equipped with functions such as 20-track random memory, 3-mode repeat between all tracks, one track or any two points, 2-speed FF/REV search, and index search.

Equipped with timer play mechanism, automatic performance is possible when the power is turned on.

Equipped with large 3-color FL display with music calendar.

A 31-key wireless remote control is included.
In addition to the main body functions, this remote control allows intro-scan, random play and UP/DOWN of electronic volume.
