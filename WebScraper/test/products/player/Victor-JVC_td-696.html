Commentary

A tape deck with high sound quality by means of a Cronios head and wide range.

The recorder / re-head is equipped with Cronios (hard permalloy) recorder / re-head developed in-house.

The amplifier circuit is designed for a wide range.

Uses an EL drive system for easy tape mounting.

Equipped with an auto shut-off mechanism that works from any mode.

Built-in sound-on-sound recording capability.

Equipped with a 2-stage tape selector that supports SH tapes.

It supports 3 speeds.

Equipped with a one touch reel lock mechanism with a screw for adjusting the height of the reel base.

Built-in casters are included.
