  • Marantz DV8300

  • ¥ 138,000 (released in June 2002)

Commentary

A universal player that can play SACD and DVD audio / video.

The HDAM circuit, an original high-speed voltage amplifier module, is used for all channels in the audio output circuit.

The audio circuit power supply and the video / digital circuit power supply are independent of each other in circuit and structure to isolate the high frequency noise generated by the video / digital circuit from the audio circuit.
A shield plate is also installed between the power supply board and the audio circuit board to prevent noise generated from the entire power supply from interfering with the audio.

The D/A converter uses CS4392 manufactured by Cirrus Logic for all channels.

A copper plate is added to the base of the multi-channel output terminal to realize ground 0 impedance between each terminal. This fixes the ground potential of all 6 ch outputs at one point.

Equipped with video off function.

Uses a double layered chassis structure.

The video section is equipped with a 108 MHz / 12-bit Video D/A converter. High-order oversampling greatly reduces signal degradation and aliasing noise caused by the conventional D/A conversion process, thereby increasing the video level.

Regardless of film material or video material, either 2-3 pull-down processing or 3 d motion adaptive processing is automatically processed to reproduce progressive images.

Built-in image quality adjustment functions such as YNR/CNR / Detail / White Level / Black Level / Gamma / Chroma Delay.

It has a built-in Dolby Digital / DTS decoder.

It has acquired the Lucasfilm THX Select standard.

You can play DVD-RW discs recorded with a DVD recorder.

You can play Audio CD/CD-R/CD-RW Disc and MP3File.

VideoCD (Ver2.0) and SVCD (IEC format) are also supported.

The speaker configuration function is extended to DVD-Audio and SACD multi-channel.

Equipped with D2 video output terminal.

Equipped with GUI with setup navigator.

There were 2 types, gold model and black model.

Comes with a wireless remote control with a light storage key.
