  • SANSUI PC-V100

  • ¥ 74,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

A compact CD player suitable for the midi size system such as Sansui's component "Mighty".

In addition to the 3-beam laser pickup, the optical head is equipped with a newly developed SANSUI Dynamic Servo System, which emits three laser beams to the signal track, detects track deviation at both side spots, and compensates for the deviation with the SANSUI Dynamic Servo System.

A digital filter with excellent frequency and phase characteristics is used to sharply cut unwanted high-frequency components of digital signals other than music signals. The digital filter doubles the sampling frequency to cut the high-frequency components far away from the music signal. A low-pass filter with a wide passband and smooth attenuation characteristics also cuts the high-frequency components. The digital filter has a two stage configuration.

Equipped with a large multi-function display that displays track number, elapsed time, and total playing time.

It is equipped with a 9-song random program performance function with 3-second autospace that allows you to exchange the recorded songs in the order you like.

It is equipped with a function to repeat all songs, program repeat to repeat only programmed songs, repeat only one song, and repeat between two points to repeat a specific section.

It is equipped with an index search function that can specify the middle of a song using the index number and call it up.
