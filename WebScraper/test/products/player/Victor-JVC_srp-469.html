  • VICTOR/JVC SRP-469

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in May 1967)

Commentary

A record player equipped with an IM cartridge.

The drive system uses a condenser advanced phase 4-pole hysteresis synchronous motor and a belt drive system with a polyurethane belt.

Equipped with universal tone arm for both dynamic and static balance with inside force compensation mechanism.

Oil dump type feather touch mechanism is adopted.

The cabinet is made of natural walnut material.
