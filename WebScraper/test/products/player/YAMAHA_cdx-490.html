  • YAMAHA CDX-490

  • ¥ 28,000 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A CD player with a headphone port.

The D/A converter is equipped with Yamaha's original 1-bit DAC.

The servo circuit is equipped with an intelligent digital servo, which reads the state of the disk loaded in the tray and traces it in the optimum state.

It is equipped with a synchronous REC function, and when combined with a Yamaha cassette deck, one touch tape dubbing is possible.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

It has a headphone port and volume.

It is equipped with functions such as a tape program function that can specify the order of songs when dubbing to tape or MD, and an autospace function that automatically inserts space between songs.

Equipped with 3 levels of dimmer control, you can adjust the brightness of the display.

There are 2 color variations, titanium color and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
