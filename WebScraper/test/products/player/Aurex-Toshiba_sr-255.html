Commentary

Direct drive player with 2 types of turntables selectable.

The material of the turntable is different between SR-255 and SR-255D, so you can choose according to your preference.
SR-255 uses AD solid, and SR-255D is made of aluminum die-cast.

A DC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

The tone arm uses a static balance S-shaped tone arm.
Equipped with an anti-skating mechanism needle pressure compatible scale.

The cartridge is equipped with C-230M of MM type cartridge.

33 1/3, 45 Speed fine adjustment is possible according to each rotation speed.
It also has a illuminator with an easy-to-see stroboscopic pattern.

AD solid, a high-density molding material, is used for the player board of the cabinet to suppress resonance.

By installing an insulator, the vibration transmitted from the floor and rack is reduced.
