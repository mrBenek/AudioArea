  • SONY TC-V7000

  • ¥ 56,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

This is a double reverse cassette deck with the most advanced deck technology condensed into a mini component size.

It is possible to dub by programming up to 16 times out of 12 songs on one side of A deck.

Double reverse allows automatic double-sided dubbing.

Equipped with high-speed dubbing mode.

Equipped with AMS, blanking and auto-pause functions.

Continuous repeat playback is possible.

Equipped with Dolby NR B-C type.

The side wood is removable.
