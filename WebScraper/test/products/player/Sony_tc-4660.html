  • SONY TC-4660

  • ¥ 85,800 (released in 1975)

Commentary

1-motor 3-head deck slant type that can use Duad tape.

The track format is 4 tracks and supports 3 speeds of 19cm/s, 9.5cm/s and 4.8cm/s.

Uses the direct coupling method of the F & F head and preamplifier.

Equipped with a 2-stage bias and 3-stage equalizer tape selector that can be used with Duad tapes and SLH tapes.

Equipped with a peak level indicator.

Equipped with a mechanical tension regulator.

Equipped with a mechanical auto shut-off mechanism.

It is equipped with a scrape filter to suppress modulation noise.

Equipped with a 20 dB microphone attenuator.

It uses an escalation drive method.

Microphone and line mixing is possible.

Equipped with line-out volume.

There was a dust protector sold separately.
