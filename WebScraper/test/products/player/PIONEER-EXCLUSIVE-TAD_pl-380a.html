Commentary

Record player equipped with full auto mechanism.

Equipped with an electronically controlled full auto mechanism, auto lead-in, Oatley turn, auto cut and Oatley peat operation can be performed without touching the tone arm.
It can also be used in manual operation.

The drive motor is a quartz PLL DC servo Hall motor using the SH rotor system to realize excellent characteristics.

The tone arm adopts a stability support / mass concentration system tone arm, which realizes excellent initial motion sensitivity and improves traceability.

A MM type cartridge is used.

The cabinet has adopted a coaxial suspension cabinet with excellent howling resistance.

Front operation type is adopted, and arm operation can be performed with the cover closed.
