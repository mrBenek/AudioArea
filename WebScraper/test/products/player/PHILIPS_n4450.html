  • PHILIPS N4450

  • ¥ 330,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

A 3-motor 6-head tape deck with a built-in HiFi amplifier.

The electronic silencer circuit eliminates the click sound at the start of recording.

The electronic memory stores the recording and playback modes.
This allows the memory to reproduce the previous mode with the lamp even after the power is turned on again.

Equipped with a queuing lever that can be used for heading out.

The winding speed is adjustable in 3 stages.

Equipped with an absence recording function, it is possible to record absence by combining with an electronic digital clock.

Recording techniques such as echo and multi-play are available.

Built-in 30W + 30W amplifier as HiFi amplifier.

Equipped with a tape tension comparator and reel size selector, stable tape tension is achieved.

It is compatible with tape number 10.

Remote control unit sold separately allows operation from a remote location.
