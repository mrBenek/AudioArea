Commentary

A 3-head cassette deck equipped with Dolby C NR and 4-digital counter.

Equipped with multi-functional 4-digital counter.
This counter can display the remaining time of the tape in minutes and seconds. The remaining 6 minutes are displayed in minutes, and the remaining 5 minutes and 59 seconds are automatically switched to minutes and seconds. This circuit is structured to detect the rotation speed of the hub on the feeding side of the cassette half by magnet and Hall element. As the remaining amount of the tape decreases, the rotation speed of the hub is detected by pulse, and the remaining time is calculated by microcomputer. In addition, there is a switch for C46 large tape / C46 / C60/C90/C120 to reduce the fluctuation of the remaining time due to the difference in the hub size and tape thickness of the cassette half. The metal tape is different in tape thickness from other tapes, but it can be automatically handled by setting the tape select switch to the metal position.
With this function, it can be used as a 4-digit electronic counter linked with 20-song jump selection, stopwatch and memory switch.

Equipped with Dolby C NR and ANRS (Dolby B NR) as noise reduction systems.
The Dolby C uses a 2-stage sliding band system, which changes the frequency of compression and expansion according to the signal level. The sliding band is effective in reducing bleeding and has excellent S / N ratio and transient characteristics when there is a signal.
The noise reduction band is wider than in the past over the middle and high frequency band to obtain a high noise reduction effect. It also has a function to improve the high-frequency linearity to further improve the high-frequency characteristics.
In addition, by adopting the newly developed Dolby C standard IC, operation is more stable and reliability is improved.

An electronically controlled DC motor is used for the mechanism.

A combination head using a meta-palm head is used for recording and reproducing heads.
Also, a 2-gap ferrite head is used for the erasing head.

Equipped with an output volume.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with full logic control.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism.

Equipped with a peak display type 7-point LED level indicator.

Equipped with headphone port and microphone port.
