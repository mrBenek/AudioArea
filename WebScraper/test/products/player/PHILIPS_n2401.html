  • PHILIPS N2401

  • ¥ 75,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

Cassette deck with changer function.

With N2401, if you use 6 volumes of C-120 tape, you can play 6 volumes of one side for 6 hours continuously.
In addition, by using circulator N6711 (sold separately), it is possible to play both AB and AB repeatedly. However, a cassette of at least four volumes is required for continuous repetition.

With a built-in 5Wx2 stereo amplifier, you can enjoy it simply by connecting the speakers.
