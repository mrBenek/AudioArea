Audio Database

  • Pioneer PD-F25

  • ¥ 26,000 (released in October 1995)

Commentary

A file-type CD player that can store 25 CDs.

It is equipped with functions such as continuous 25-disc performance, random repeat, program play and best selection memory.
It also has a highlight scan function.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

There were 2 types, PD-F25 of black color and PD-F25-S of walkers or rollators for the elderly.

Includes wireless remote control and 25-disc case stand.
