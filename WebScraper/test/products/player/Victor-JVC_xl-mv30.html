  • VICTOR/JVC XL-MV30

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

A video CD player with a changer function.

You can play video CDs, CD graphics, and music CDs.

The mechanism part uses an independent tray 3-game changer, and you can exchange other 2 my discs even while playing.

The playback control function (ver2.0) is supported, and it is possible to play video CD menus and high-definition still images.
In addition, the video and audio of the karaoke intro part can be played continuously for 5 seconds with the video intro search, and the highlight search is also installed to display the highlight scene divided into 6 parts on the television screen.

Equipped with digital key control of ± 6 stages.

15-step digital echo is used.

Route 3 microphone Equipped with a microphone port with volume.

Equipped with a multi-sound switching mode (L/R/L + R) for sound multiplex discs and a voice changer function.

On-screen capability.

Wireless remote control is included.
