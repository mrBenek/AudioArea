  • Panasonic SL-PS30

Commentary

Mash CD player with 1-bit DAC.

Mash 1-bit DAC is used for the D/A conversion part.
High-speed 1-bit operation of the input signal based on the high-precision clock signal generated by crystal oscillation eliminates 0 cross distortion and distortion generated at each bit switching point in principle.
In addition, the Mash uses PWM outputs to eliminate the need for deglitch circuits and noise generation associated with these circuits.

Equipped with A/B double-sided edit guide function.
This function automatically calculates and displays the number of songs that can be recorded on the A side and B side of the tape, the number of songs, and the recording time by simply selecting the recording time (90 minutes, 60 minutes, 46 minutes) of the tape.

When combined with the auto-reverse cassette deck (RS-TR355, RS-TR555) with a synchro-edit terminal, editing from CD to tape is easy.
After editing with the SL-PS30 Edit Guide, set the deck to the recording standby state and press the SL-PS30 Play button to edit and record both sides of the tape.

A high-speed linear access system using a high-precision linear motor is installed in the drive part of the traverse mechanism that moves the pickup, realizing high-speed access.
In addition, the CCF lens of this laser pickup section uses a unique aspherical integrally molded glass lens, providing excellent signal reading ability and high reliability that are resistant to scratches and dirt on the disk surface.

Equipped with auto-cue, it can detect where music actually starts and stand by.

It is equipped with functions such as 20-step random program, all songs / repeat program songs.

Equipped with a 4-mode time display, you can display the elapsed time and remaining time of songs and all songs.
