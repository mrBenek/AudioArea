  • SONY TC-WR800

  • ¥ 54,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

This is a double reverse deck that uses Sony technology, such as a laser amorphous head, to further improve operability.

A laser amorphous head with LC-OFC winding is mounted on the recording and reproducing head of B deck.
In addition to high-speed dubbing, it also uses a super-bias system that improves sound quality at normal speed.

Equipped with a level meter by FL tube.
In addition, a digital counter with a negative counter that can be used for A/B deck is used as a counter to measure the amount of tape running, and memory stop / play with 0.00 as an operating point is possible.

Synchronized dubbing that matches the operation timing of A/B deck is possible at 2 speeds of normal speed and high speed.

Equipped with an auto standby function, if you operate the A deck during normal speed dubbing, the B deck automatically enters the recording pause state. When you release the pause, dubbing starts again, so you can skip unnecessary songs or edit while selecting songs.

It is equipped with an auto pause function. When dubbing at normal speed, every time the B deck records one song, it waits in the recording pause. You can dubbing while selecting songs on the A deck.

It is equipped with a blank skip function that fast-forwards when there is no signal for about 10 seconds or more during playback and plays from the beginning of the next song.
In addition, the AMS function allows you to start the current or next song.

Equipped with a direction mode switch, recording / playback of one side only, recording and playback of one cycle of both sides, and relay playback of A/B deck are possible.

Equipped with Dolby NR B type and C type.

Equipped with a direction indicator that easily displays the tape running condition.

An FL tube is used for the level meter that displays the real-time level.

B deck is equipped with quick auto-reverse function.

Equipped with an autoplay function that starts playing after quick winding.

TypeI/II/IV auto tape selector is mounted on A deck.

Equipped with Rec Mute function with auto space.

It has a headphone port.

Timer sold separately allows recording of absence and alarm clock playback.

It comes with a wireless remote control that can operate both A and B decks.
It can also be combined with Sony's remote-controlled CD player to enable wireless CD synchronized recording using the remote control.
