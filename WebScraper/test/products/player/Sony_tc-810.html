Commentary

An 8-track deck exclusively for playback that allows you to listen to car stereo tapes in your room.

It is an endless system that starts playing when you insert a cartridge and continues playing all day.

Equipped with a program select button.

Equipped with a repeat button to repeatedly play the program you want to listen to.

Equipped with a convenient fast-forward function for heading out.
