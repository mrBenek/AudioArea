  • SONY CDP-CX350

  • ¥ 59,000 (released in 1999)

Commentary

A CD player that can store 300 CDs.

The D/A converter is equipped with a hybrid pulse D/A converter.

Equipped with a Mega CD control function, it is possible to operate the CDP-CX350 using a CDP-CE535 or a PC as the master unit, and to operate 600 disks by connecting two CDP-CX350 units as the master unit and slave unit.
To operate the CDP-CX350 from a PC, a Control A1II-compatible device or a Control A1II-compatible PC and application software (MD Editor2) are required.

Compatible with CD-TEXT and capable of displaying English and numeric characters.
Also, by using the separately sold display unit CTD-S100, Chinese characters can be displayed.

Built-in disk memo function.
The CDP-CX350 allows you to use an IBM PC/AT-compatible keyboard for input.

Equipped with a jog dial dedicated to disk / track for easy selection of music.

Equipped with rich functions such as group files.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

If you use 8 centimeters CD, you need an adapter sold separately.

It comes with a two way remote control with back light that allows you to display album titles while playing at hand.
