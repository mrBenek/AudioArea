Commentary

This is a stereo tape deck with a built-in 3-head recording and playback preamp designed for audio enthusiasts.

Since the recording head has three independent heads for erasing, recording, and playback, you can check the recording status by playing back the sound you are recording with the recording head with the playhead. You can also switch from the sound before recording to check the recording.
A Sony 2-micron head is used as the playhead.

The preamplifier section has an all-silicon solid-state configuration using 18 low-noise, high-gain silicon transistors.

It is equipped with a binaural jack which is convenient for playing and monitoring by headphone.

Equipped with a dedicated volume for sound-on-sound (S. O. S) recording.

The micro switch automatically turns off the power of the mechanism when the tape is wound.

Equipped with DIN standard terminal.

Equipped with a tape counter.

4-track monaural recording is also possible.

Can be used for both vertical and horizontal.
