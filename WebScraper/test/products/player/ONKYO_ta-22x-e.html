Commentary

Stereo cassette deck focusing on basic performance

It is equipped with functions such as a Dolby system, 3-stage tape selector and timer standby.

Equipped with a full auto-stop mechanism to reduce the burden on the tape and mechanism.

Equipped with an independent 7-segment digital level meter on the left and right.
