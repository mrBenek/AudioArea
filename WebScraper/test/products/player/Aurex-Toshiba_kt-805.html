  • TOSHIBA KT-805

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

An 8-track deck capable of recording and rerecording 8-track cartridges.

Equipped with a program indicator that automatically displays the number of the program being played.

A dust-proof cover is provided at the tape insertion port to prevent dust and dust from entering the mechanism.

Equipped with fast-forward mechanism, repeat mechanism, eject mechanism and manual program switching mechanism.
