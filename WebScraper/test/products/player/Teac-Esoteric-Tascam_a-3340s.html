  • ¥ 214,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

Multi-track tape deck capable of simulsync recording.

With the A-3340S, the sound of each part can be divided into tracks and recorded at 0 VU level, and the sound of each part can be balanced and output later using the output volume.
In addition, by using a separately sold mix down panel, you can position the sound of each track to any position, left, center, or right, and complete it as a two channel master tape.

It is equipped with a simul-sync mechanism that is used for business purposes. When one player changes instruments and records, it is possible to record the simul-sync by matching the heads of each performance.
On a normal 3-head deck, if you monitor the performance of a recorded track while recording to another track, the sound will be shifted by the distance between the recording head and the playhead. With simulsync recording, if you set the simulsync switch for the first track to "SIMUL-SYNC" after the first recording, the recording head becomes a temporary playhead and you can record the second track while monitoring the performance. By repeating this process, you can create a multi-track master tape with a one man quartet.

Using tracks 1 and 4, it is possible to listen to two track 38 cm master tapes and old track tapes.

You can play and record 4-track / 4-channel tape, 4-track / 2-channel tape, or 4-track / 2-channel tape.

It is equipped with an independent change-over switch that provides the optimum bias and equalizer for both regular and low-noise tapes.

All control mechanisms are composed of micro switches to achieve reliable operation and soft touch, and a pose button is incorporated into the control mechanism.

The VU meter has excellent transient characteristics and is also linked to the output volume to check the delivery level.

Each channel has an independent input adjustment volume. Each microphone / line has a double knob that allows mixing of the microphone and line.

Each channel has its own output adjustment volume.

It is equipped with a play selector switch. Setting it to the 4 ch side mutes the 4 ch playback, and setting it to the 2 ch side mutes the output of the unnecessary 2nd and 4th tracks and plays the 1st and 3rd tracks.

The first and third tracks are equipped with separate headphone terminals for the front, and the second and fourth tracks are equipped with separate headphone terminals for the rear.

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining the timer control adapter (sold separately) with the timer.

Equipped with a reel size selection switch that can select the torque of the reel motor according to the size of the reel.

Equipped with a 4-digit index counter.

Uses a reel clamp mechanism that allows easy attachment and detachment.

It can be operated from a remote place by using the remote control unit sold separately.

By using the separately sold audio mixer and Dolby unit, it can be used in a wider range of applications.
