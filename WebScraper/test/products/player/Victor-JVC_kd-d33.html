  • VICTOR/JVC KD-D33

  • ¥ 51,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with 4 digital counter.

The Meta-Palm head is used for the recording and reproducing head.

The drive motor is equipped with an FG servo DC motor.

Equipped with a 4 digital counter.
By switching the display mode, this function can be used for direct reading of the remaining time of the tape, selection of 20-track jumps, stopwatch, and memory linked 4-digit electronic counter.

ANRS and SuperANRS are installed as noise reduction systems.

It has a queue and review mechanism.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Soft touch operation button by logic mechanism is adopted.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism and by using an optional timer, it is possible to record absence and play back alarm clock.

Equipped with a large twin VU meter.

Equipped with Rewind Autoplay.
