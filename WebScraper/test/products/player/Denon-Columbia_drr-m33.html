  • DENON DRR-M33

  • ¥ 25,000 (released in 2005)

Commentary

A Lapisia series cassette deck.

Equipped with a horizontal loading mechanism.
The tape can be held in a stable condition by setting it horizontally, and the sound quality during recording and playback is improved by suppressing vibration of the cassette half while the tape is running.

Equipped with Dolby B NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a Oatley berth function, A side / B side can be played repeatedly.

Equipped with an auto tape selector function, it automatically sets the bias and equalizer to match the tape just by setting the tape.

Metal tape playback is supported.

It has the same design as the M33 series, and uses aluminum panel and side escutcheon.
