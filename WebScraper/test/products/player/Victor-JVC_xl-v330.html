  • VICTOR/JVC XL-V330

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

Digifine series CD player.

It has almost the same specifications as the XL-V440 except for 10 keys.

An IS mechanism is adopted for the mechanism part, and the disk drive and optical pickup part are floating mounted on a robust mechanical base to shut off disturbance vibration and sound pressure.

The newly developed High Precision 3-beam pickup is used in the pickup section.
This pickup improves response by narrowing the gap between the servo beam and main beam, and improves traceability.

Equipped with a digital filter.

The digital and analog sections of the power supply circuit are separated to eliminate mutual interference.

Center hold system is adopted for the tray part.

It has a 6-digit LCD display.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Synchronized recording is supported by the DCS jack.

Wireless remote control is included.
