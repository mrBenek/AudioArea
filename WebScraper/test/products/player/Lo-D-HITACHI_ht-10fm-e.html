  • Lo-D HT-10FM

  • ¥ 38,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

An FM Wireless Auto Player System with a built-in FM stereo transmitter that enables you to enjoy recording performance by tuning an FM tuner without connecting a cord.

Microphone mixing is possible with built-in amplifier dedicated to microphone.

Equipped with an auto mechanism capable of playing even with a light needle pressure cartridge.

A belt drive with a DC servo motor is used for the drive system.

It can be enjoyed outdoors with 3 power systems : dry battery (single x6), home power supply (dedicated AC adapter), car battery (car adapter sold separately).

In addition to the black type, there is also a silver type.
