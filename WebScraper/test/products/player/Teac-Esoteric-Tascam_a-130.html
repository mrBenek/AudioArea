Commentary

Standard cassette deck.

A DC motor is used as the drive motor to obtain stable rotation.

Permalloy head is used for the head part.

Equipped with independent left and right VU meters, you can set the appropriate level for recording and playback.

One touch operation is adopted for the operation part such as taking in and out of the tape and playing back the tape, and usability is improved.
In addition, by installing an independent pause button, it is possible to record with less errors than before.

With an auto-eject function, the control button and pinch roller automatically return to the stop position when the tape is finished winding, preventing unnecessary stress on the main body and tape.

My Kujac is installed.

It has a headphone port with a dedicated amplifier.
The volume is linked with the playback volume so that you can enjoy at the desired volume.
