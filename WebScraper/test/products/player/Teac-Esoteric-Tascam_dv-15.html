Commentary

A universal player developed based on the design philosophy of the DV-50 and DV-30.

The D/A conversion part is equipped with CS4392 of DAC chip made by Cirrus Logic.
The analog circuit has a full-discrete configuration, enabling all channels to output same conditions for all channels.

A series power supply using a high-quality transformer is used for the audio part instead of a switching power supply.

High-quality parts with a reputation for sound quality are carefully selected after thorough trial listening.

Equipped with video OFF function.
When playing DVD-Audio, SACD, or CD, you can disable the video circuit to prevent interference with the sound quality of the video signal.

The video section is equipped with a progressive scan circuit.
When connected to a TV or projector that supports progressive input, this function performs either 2-3 pull-down pure cinema progressive scan processing or 3-dimensional motion complement processing depending on the film and video material.

The video DAC is equipped with one manufactured by Analog Devices.
The Noise Shaping Video (NSV) process provides a video signal with excellent S/N and linearity.
In addition, the built-in super sub-alias filter function realizes a flat wide-band characteristic for the required video bandwidth, and the sharp cut-off characteristic that removes aliasing noise in the unnecessary bandwidth enables high-resolution video playback.

It is equipped with image quality adjustment functions such as noise reduction depth and chroma delay.

The mechanism part is firmly fixed with a steel mechanical housing to reduce the effect of vibration.

The front panel has undergone short scratch processing.

There are 2 color variations of silver and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
