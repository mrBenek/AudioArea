Commentary

A wide-range 3-head cassette deck with improved high-pass limits for recording and playback frequency characteristics.

Equipped with a wide-range system that greatly improves the high-pass limit of recording and playback frequency characteristics, the high-pass limit -pass limit is improved to 25 kHz.
In addition to using a high-quality recording / reproducing head for a wider bandwidth, we also increased the bandwidth of the ROCO and playback amplifier, thereby increasing the high-frequency limit.

Equipped with XD FLAT System.
This function is a refinement of the FLAT System, which adjusts recording and playback frequency characteristics to be flat with one touch according to the tape used. It has a high-precision correction capability and a wide dynamic range in the high-frequency range.
The computer checks the high-frequency recording characteristics of the tape used for a high-frequency pilot signal with a level approximately 10 dB higher than that of conventional tapes, and controls the bias of the tape during recording. This makes it possible to record more faithfully in the high-frequency range, especially in normal tapes.

Equipped with Dolby S NR as a noise reduction system, noise reduction of up to 24 dB in the high range and up to 10 dB in the low range is realized, and bleeding noise is also greatly reduced.

The tape guide is equipped with S. R. T. (Smooth Running Tapeguide).
This tape guide pursues shape and dimensional accuracy. It is combined with a high-rigidity and high-accuracy head block, a FS-treated capstan shaft, a heavy-weight flywheel, and a high-rigidity bearing to improve the phase fluctuation characteristics between the left and right channels at high frequencies.

It is equipped with the FLEX (Frequency Level Expander) System, which detects whether recorded tapes are muffled and automatically amplifies the high-frequency level to improve sound quality.

Midship mount structure with Z mechanism placed in the center is adopted.

The 160 kHz high-bias design reduces distortion due to interference between source harmonics and bias current.

It uses a winged chassis and PC-OCC (single-crystal high-purity oxygen-free copper) winding head to release mechanical vibration to the outside.

Equipped with the last memory function, it can store FLAT System data, main switch positions, etc.

Equipped with a CD-DECK synchro function, it can be linked with a Pioneer CD player equipped with a CD-DECK synchro function and automatically linked operation is possible.

Wireless remote control is included.
