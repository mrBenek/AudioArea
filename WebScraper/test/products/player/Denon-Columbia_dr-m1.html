  • DENON DR-M1

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

A cassette deck with auto-fader.

With an auto-fader mechanism, you can fade in and fade out the sound automatically after approximately four illnesses by pressing the buttons.

It uses a computer-controlled silent mechanism.

Non-slip reel drive mechanism is adopted.

A DC amplifier configuration without a condenser is adopted to prevent deterioration of sound quality.

Uses SF (Super Frequency) combination head for recording and reproducing head.

Equipped with Dolby B Type and Dolby C Type as noise reduction system.

Equipped with FL peak meter and 4-digit digital counter.

Equipped with a memory stop mechanism.
