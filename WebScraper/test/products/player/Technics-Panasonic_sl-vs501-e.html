  • Technics SL-VS501

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1997)

Commentary

A video CD player that can play not only CDs but also video CDs (Ver. 2) and CD-G.

Digital servo is used in the servo circuit, and by adjusting the servo amount according to the condition of each disk, stable drive is realized and sound quality is improved.

Equipped with two microphone jacks. You can also record songs with the microphone mixing function.

Equipped with digital microphone echo.

It has a voice mute function that allows you to enjoy non-Karaoke software like Karaoke.

It is equipped with a digital key controller that can adjust the music keys according to the height of your voice.

Program playback, random playback and repeat playback are available.

Wireless remote control is included.
