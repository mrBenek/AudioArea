Commentary

This is a CD player that uses the VRDS mechanism, RDOT, 64 x up-conversion function, etc.
16bit/44.1 khz CD signal is upconverted up to 64 times.

The digital filter section is composed of a FIR digital filter with sharp roll-off characteristics and a Refined Digital Output Technology (RDOT) digital filter, an analog-based interpolation technique based on full entropy theory.
In addition, the linear interpolation technology is interpolated.

In addition to the L/R-independent configuration for the parts after the digital filter, a 4-DAC configuration improves various characteristics.

A quartz timing shaping circuit is installed just before the digital output terminal to improve the quality of the digital output by reducing jitter.
The digital output can be turned ON/OFF independently for TOS and RCA, eliminating mutual interference when transported and used.

A high-speed Schottky barrier diode is used for the rectifier diode of the power supply circuit.
Rectification noise caused by the reverse recovery phenomenon of the diode during normal rectification is shut out in principle and sound field sense is improved.

The parts used in the Esthetic P-70/D-70 are used in the thorough trial listening ↑.

Equipped with a digital servo that adjusts the ideal servo amount for each disk.

It uses a high-rigidity chassis with a heavyweight 3-block configuration, which is also used in ESODIC CD players.
In addition, the front panel is made of 15 mm thick aluminum material to reduce the influence of chassis vibration and external vibration.
