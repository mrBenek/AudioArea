  • AKAI GXC-325D

  • ¥ 110,000 (released in 1975)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with 3-head configuration.

It uses a three head system with specialized recording and playback heads, enabling high-density recording and playback and simultaneous monitoring.

Closed loop double capstan system using AC servo motor is adopted for the mechanism part.

It is equipped with functions such as ADR system, Dolby NR system and mixing recording.

Equipped with peak level lamp.

Equipped with a limiter.

Built-in memory rewind function.
