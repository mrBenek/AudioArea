  • VICTOR/JVC D-E5

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

Metal tape compatible compact cassette deck with 2-motor full logic configuration.

Equipped with a music scan (automatic selection) mechanism.

ANRS/SuperANRS is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with 2-color LED digital level meter.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism and by connecting an optional timer, it is possible to record the absence and play the alarm clock.

Equipped with a 3-step switching type tape selector.

Compatible with the Victor Component Stereo COMPO34.
