  • SANSUI CD - α 205

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

CD player using 1 bit delta-sigma (delta-sigma) system.

A 1-bit delta-sigma D/A converter is used in the D/A conversion section to obtain high-resolution sound in all bands including small signal levels.

It employs a center mechanical layout with the drive part placed in the center, reducing vibration and resonance that adversely affect sound quality.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

It is equipped with functions such as 21 songs random program memory, random play, repeat play, intro skip and 3-mode time display.

Wireless remote control is included.
