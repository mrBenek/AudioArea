  • ¥ 44,800 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A cassette deck that supports metal tapes.

An electronically controlled DC motor is used to achieve quiet operation with less uneven rotation.

A high-precision permalloy head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
In addition, the erase head structure is double-gap to accommodate metal tapes.

A logic control system is used for the operation part.

Equipped with an internal condenser microphone.

Equipped with a 12 dot bar graph meter with two color LEDs.

Equipped with Dolby NRB type.

Equipped with a 3-stage switchable tape selector.

It has an output volume.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism that enables you to record your absence or play an alarm clock.
