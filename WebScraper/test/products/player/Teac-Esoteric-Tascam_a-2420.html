Commentary

Read-only tape deck.

You can play 4-track 4-channel, 4-track 2-channel, 2-track 2-channel, full track, and mono.

It is possible to regenerate the Oatley bath by the foil sensing method.
Continuous playback is also possible by attaching a sensing foil to the beginning of the tape and turning the REPEAT switch ON.

By connecting the cue out terminal of A-2420 and the remote terminal of tape deck A-4070 with Dupree sync cord WR-100 (sold separately), when A-2420 operates in reverse, A-4070 receives cue signal and automatically continues reverse recording simultaneously.

A 4-pole, 8-pole, 2-speed hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the capstan motor in the mechanism section. Combined with the dynamically balanced large capstan flywheel, it realizes stable tape travel and excellent wow and flutter characteristics.
The reel motor uses a 6-pole eddy current type with less hunting and strong starting torque.

Equipped with a bias switching mechanism, recording utilizing the performance of low-noise tape is possible.

Equipped with a height adjustment mechanism for the reel stand.
It can be adjusted by turning the shaft of the reel stand left and right using a Phillips screwdriver.

Durability has been improved by using a 5 mm thick aluminum top panel and a reinforced die-cast frame on the side.

Equipped with Rec. Pause mechanism.
In this mechanism, before recording, you set the control lever to the pause position and press the record button and arrow button to start recording immediately in Play mode. When you pause the control lever during recording, the recording amplifier stays on and the tape stops temporarily. When you switch to Play mode, recording starts again.

Equipped with a tape lifter fixing mechanism.

Equipped with a 4-digit index counter.
