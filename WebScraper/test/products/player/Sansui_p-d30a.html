  • SANSUI P-D30A

  • ¥ 34,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

A full auto player with improved basic performance and operability.

A FG servo DD coreless motor is used as the drive motor.

Straight arm is used for the tone arm.

Equipped with full auto mechanism, auto lead-in, Oatley turn, auto cut and repeat can be operated from the front panel.

It is equipped with a computer selector system that works with SANSUI's pre-main amplifier A-909 and A-707.
With this function, when you press the start button on the player, the selector switch on the amp automatically switches to Phono, or when you press the Phono switch on the amp, the tone arm is led in and the recording starts.
