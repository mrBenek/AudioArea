  • Panasonic SV-E10

Commentary

DAT deck designed for component system imagin (SS-E10).

Mash is used for both the D/A and A/D conversion sections, and 0 cross distortion and nonlinear distortion are eliminated in principle.

Equipped with a variable speed search of up to 400 times speed capable of winding up a 120 minute tape in 27 seconds, the access time is greatly reduced.

Equipped with end search, it is possible to locate the end recorded on the tape at high speed and stop it.

Equipped with auto program number.
With this function, you can record a song number automatically at the point where a sound starts after recording starts or at the point where a sound starts after a silent part lasts for more than 2 seconds.
Manual recording is also possible.

Equipped with a fade in / out function.
By using digital control, it is possible to perform sure fading work at a constant level at all times.

Equipped with auto-rec mute function, silent part for about 4 seconds is created with one touch.

Digital recording / playback is possible in accordance with DAT's S. C. M.S. rerecording standard.
