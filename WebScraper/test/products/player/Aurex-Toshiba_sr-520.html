  • Aurex SR-520

  • ¥ 30,000 (around 1960 s)

Commentary

This is an autochanger type record player that can automatically play seven consecutive records.

You can automatically play up to 7 consecutive records with the same number of revolutions. You can also play repeatedly or manually.

An automatic idler-off system is adopted, and the idler is automatically separated from the motor pulley and turntable when the performance is finished, so there is no rotational unevenness or noise caused by the deformation of the idler rubber.

A double-structured turntable with a diameter of 28 cm is used for the turntable.

The tone arm uses a static balance type pipe arm equipped with a needle pressure direct reading weight.

A MM type cartridge is used.
European style plug-in head is used for mounting to the arm.

The cabinet has a North American natural walnut finish.
