Audio Database

  • Pioneer RT-1020H-A

  • ¥ 149,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

4-track stereo tape deck that supports 2 speeds of 38cm/s and 19cm/s.

Equipped with a tape selector with 3 levels of bias and 2 levels of equalizer.

The drive section uses a 3-motor system that uses a dedicated motor for driving the capstan and reel.

Sound on Sound recording and echo recording are available.

4-track 4-channel playback is possible.
In addition, 2-track 38 cm tapes can be played by using 1-4 tracks.
