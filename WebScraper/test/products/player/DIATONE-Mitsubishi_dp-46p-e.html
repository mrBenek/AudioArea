Audio Database

  • DIATONE DP-46P

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1984)

Commentary

A CD player developed exclusively for DIATONE's system component "Roboty Commander".

Equipped with 3-beam type laser pickup.

Up to 9 program songs can be selected.

* As it is a dedicated model for roboty commander, it cannot operate by itself.
