  • Pioneer T-7050R

Commentary

Easy-to-use and multi-functional auto-reverse cassette deck.

Equipped with a random program selection and playback function that allows you to freely specify the order of songs to be played, up to a total of 20 songs on the A side and B side of the tape.
You can program the order of songs you want to listen by pressing the button in the order of songs recorded on the tape, and then press the play button to enjoy program playback.

FL (fluorescent indicator tube) is used for the display, and the level meter, 4-digit counter, driving direction display, NR position display, auto-reverse mode display, tape selector display, etc. are centrally displayed on a large panel.

It is equipped with skip playback, which automatically fast-forwards the unrecorded portion (8 seconds or more) and quickly plays from the beginning of the next song, and skip reverse, which fast-forwards the unrecorded portion after the last song on one side and plays from the beginning of the first song on the back of the tape.

In addition to the Dolby B/C NR, it is equipped with a dbx system to realize an excellent noise reduction effect.

Equipped with Auto REC Mute, which creates a 4-second silent section (blank) when the switch is pressed during recording and automatically enters the recording pause state.

It is equipped with a music search of 10 songs in front and back that can be specified regardless of the tape running direction.

Equipped with an auto tape selector that automatically sets the equalizer and bias according to the type of tape.
