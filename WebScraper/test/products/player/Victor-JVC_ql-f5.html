Commentary

A full auto player with a variable oil dump arm.

The problem of oil dump arm, which could not be used in full-automatic machine due to its special structure, was solved by the original structural design and mounted.

The rotating system is equipped with a double-servo quartz lock circuit, and the MC combine system integrates a coreless DC motor with less vibration and a servo circuit to improve reliability.

Equipped with a repeat device.

The manual operation priority design is adopted.

The cabinet is made of the cabinet to reduce the influence of vibration and resonance.
