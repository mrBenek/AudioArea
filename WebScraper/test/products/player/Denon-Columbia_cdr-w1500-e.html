  • DENON CDR-W1500

  • ¥ 89,800 (released in August 2000)

Commentary

A double-mechanical unit type CD recorder equipped with a CD recorder and CD player.

The CD recorder and CD player can be used independently.
You can record an external source on the recorder unit and play another source on the player unit at the same time. You can also play multi-zone by connecting the recorder and player to two amplifiers.

It is equipped with a mix mode that you can enjoy like a 2 disc CD changer by using 2 mechanisms of recorder and player.
You can take advantage of playback functions such as full disc repeat playback, random playback, program playback, and relay playback across two discs.

Double-speed dubbing is possible between the CD player and recorder.
It is also equipped with a Scanrec dubbing function that records multiple songs selected during introscan, an all-track dubbing function that records all songs on a disc, a 1-track dubbing function that records only one specified song, and a make CD dubbing function that automatically finalizes after recording.

Equipped with a level sync recording function that automatically starts recording when an audio signal is input by detecting the signal.
The level sync recording supports both external digital and analog signals, and the 1-track sync mode for only one track is also installed (the signal detection level for synchronized recording can be set between -54dB and -30dB).
It also has a fade-in / fade-out function that allows you to set the fade time from 1 second to 15 seconds.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 24bitD/A converter.
In addition, a linear transformer is used for the transformer to achieve clearer sound reproduction.

The player unit section is compatible with HDCD decoding.
Both player and record units can play CD-RW discs (only finalized player units).

A source with a sampling frequency of 44.1 kHz can be recorded directly without passing it through a sampling rate converter.
You can also record faithfully in HDCD format and copy CD text data.

It is equipped with a sampling rate converter for DAT and BS/CS broadcasting.

You can use the front panel jog dial or the numeric keypad on the remote control to enter the album title and the title of each song on the recorded CD-R/CD-RW disc.
You can enter up to 23 characters in a title for a total of 900 characters. You can enter uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. You can also erase all text or individual titles.
You can also display the entered text in a player that supports CD text.

It is equipped with a multitrack erase function that can erase multiple songs simultaneously from the last song recorded on the CD-RW.
You can also delete all songs at once.

Aluminum panel is used for the front panel.

Wireless remote control is included.
