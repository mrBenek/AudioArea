  • YAMAHA CDX-480

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1995)

Commentary

A CD player with an intelligent digital servo that enables optimal tracing.

The servo circuit uses an intelligent digital servo to quickly read the state of the disk and realize tracing in the optimum state at all times.

A 1-bit DAC is used for D/A conversion to achieve delicate and natural music playback.

It is equipped with a synchronized REC function that is system-friendly with Yamaha's KX-580/KX-W382 cassette decks, making it possible to record cassette tapes with simple operation.

It is equipped with play functions such as peak search, 3-mode repeat play, random access, and 25-track program play.

Equipped with 3-position dimmer control, 10-key direct access, autospace, timer play and digital volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
Volume adjustment is possible with this remote control.
