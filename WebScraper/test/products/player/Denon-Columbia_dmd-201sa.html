  • DENON DMD-201SA

  • ¥ 52,000 (Released in September 2001)

Commentary

MDM deck of PRMd 201 sa series which also supports MDLP ESTA.

It is compatible with MDLP mode, and it is possible to record 160 minutes or 320 minutes of stereo on 80 minutes of blank MD.
You can record a mix of MDLP and normal recordings on a single MD disc. Non-MDLP players cannot play the disc.

It is equipped with a group management function, so you can easily search and play songs.
Players that do not support group management function can play only as usual.

Equipped with a digital REC volume, the level can be adjusted even for digital recording.

Equipped with PICK REC function.
In PICK REC mode, you can record your favorite song from the beginning by pressing the Enter button until the end of the song.
This function will not work if there is no silence at the beginning or end of the song.

It is equipped with the basic functions of MD editing such as divide, combine, move and erase.
In addition, editing functions such as program move, undo, disname erase, track name erase, all erase, and autodiscovery are also included.

Equipped with a jog dial, you can enter information such as title name and artist easily with the jog dial.


It is equipped with a sampling rate converter and automatically converts 32kHz/48kHz sampling frequency of BS/CS broadcasting and DAT to 44.1 khz.

ATRAC IC with built-in 24 bit DSP is mounted.

Equipped with system functions that can be utilized in combination with PRESTA201SA series.

Wireless remote control is included.
