  • VICTOR/JVC L-D7Z

  • ¥ 38,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

DD full auto player with slim design.

The drive motor is equipped with a coreless FG servo motor.
In addition, a newly developed IC is used in the servo system to improve the drift characteristics and improve stability.

The tone arm uses a rigid, lightweight straight pipe arm.
The low-mass design takes low-frequency resonance into consideration in conjunction with the highly rigid shell of carbon fiber.

The full-auto mechanism has a layout in which the operation buttons are concentrated on the front panel, and has a design in pursuit of usability such as one button for start and reject.

The cartridge uses Z-1SR type.

The dust cover was sold separately.
