Commentary

This is a cassette deck that enables combined play of memory, AMS and repeat.

Equipped with a linear electronic counter that displays minutes and seconds of tape running time.

The three functions of Memory / AMS / Repeat are controlled by a microcomputer, enabling combined play of three patterns of Memory & Repeat, AMS & Repeat, and Memory & AMS & Repeat.

Equipped with multi-functional peak program meter of double indication system.
16 elements each on the left and right, color-coded based on 0 dB, display the range of -30dB to + 8 dB.

Equipped with a 100 mm wide long stroke slide volume that enables high-precision recording level adjustment.

Uses metal-compatible S & F head for the head.

The mechanism consists of two motors using a BSL green motor and a high torque motor.

Equipped with a 4-stage tape selector.

Uses Dolby NR Complete IC.

Equipped with auto-play function.

Equipped with Rec Mute function with auto space.

Equipped with a timer standby switch.

It is compatible with optional wireless remote control, wired remote control, and remote control unit for enjoying synchronized play.
