Commentary

This is a stereo cassette deck equipped with a microcomputer for improved operability.

It is equipped with a microprocessor auto-scan system using a microcomputer with 20,000 elements. This function automatically detects the no-signal part between songs, and can locate the beginning of a programmed song among 20 songs.

On the upper part of the digital FL display is a music address display using a fluorescent display tube. You can see which song is currently being played and which song will be played next. There is also a blank beep that notifies you between songs with a tone.

An electronically controlled DC motor is used as the drive motor.

An LH head is used for the recording / reproducing head.

Equipped with a 3-stage switchable tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with functions such as REC mute mechanism, microphone / line mixing mechanism, music selector, rewind autoplay, cue & review (lockable after), quick review, autostop, etc.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Equipped with a soft eject & loading mechanism.
