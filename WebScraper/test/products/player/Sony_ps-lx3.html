  • SONY PS-LX3

  • ¥ 35,800 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A full-auto player developed to enjoy disc sound with easy operation.

Equipped with Lummus / Straight Arm and equipped with MM type cartridge of light needle pressure / high compliance type.

Servocontrol is performed by linear BSL motor and magnedisk speed detection.

Equipped with an opto-electronic disc size selector that automatically selects whether the set record is 17 cm or 30 cm and enables accurate lead-in.

Equipped with a muting mechanism that eliminates noise when the needle tip contacts the record board.

The cabinet uses SBMC material with excellent internal loss and strength.

Needle pressure setting guide and disk centering guide are included.

Synchronized play with the cassette deck is possible with the separately sold synchronized unit.
