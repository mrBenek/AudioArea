Commentary

A CD player from the Euro-style component series.

The mechanism uses a tray design that combines an ultra-small pickup and a head amplifier, making it thin and compact. You can also enjoy a CD single without an adapter.

The digital filter uses a 4 x oversampling digital filter.
A dual 16-bit D/A converter is used for the D/A converter.

All functions are designed to be controlled by the included wireless remote control, and the body panel face is simple.
