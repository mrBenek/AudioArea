  • Pioneer PDR-N901

  • ¥ 79,800 (released in 1999)

Commentary

A CD-R/CD-RW recorder that supports two types of media, CD-R and CD-RW.

Equipped with digital sync recording function.
Connect to other digital audio devices for easy recording and editing.

Equipped with a digital volume function, the input level for digital recording can be adjusted.
The adjustment range is 12 dB to -48dB, and can be varied regardless of the sampling frequency.

It is equipped with a sampling rate converter, which converts digital sources with different sampling frequencies such as satellite broadcasting and DVD into CD sampling frequency.

The Legato Link Conversion is installed in the playback part, and it is possible to estimate and reproduce the music component of 20 khz or more which is cut in CD format.

Equipped with two optical digital input terminals and two RCA analog input terminals.
The outputs are equipped with Route 3 analog outputs and two optical digital outputs.

Wireless remote control is included.
