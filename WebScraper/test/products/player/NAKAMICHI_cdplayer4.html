  • Nakamichi CDPlayer4

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1990)

Commentary

A basic single-disc CD player.

The digital filter uses an 8 x oversampling digital filter.
The analog filter is a third order linear phase Bessel type analog filter.

The D/A converter uses a linear 18-bit glitch free D/A converter.

Multi-regulated power supply system is adopted for the power supply section, and power is supplied independently for each circuit section.
In addition, the isolated ground method is adopted to prevent noise contamination between the digital circuit and analog circuit surrounding the ground line.

A floating mechanism has been adopted for the mechanism part to reduce the influence of external vibration.

8 cm CD is supported.

Built-in program memory and repeat play function.

Equipped with a digital output terminal.

Equipped with headphone port with level control.

Wireless remote control is included.
