Commentary

Quick reverse deck equipped with Dolby HX-PRO.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO, which greatly improves the high-frequency dynamic range.
The Dolby HX-PRO is a system that controls the effective bias to be constant according to the fluctuation of the input signal during recording. It can greatly improve the high-frequency dynamic range. Since this system operates only during recording, recorded tapes can be played back with high sound quality on other decks.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

The reverse mechanism employs a fine-axis quick reverse mechanism in which the recording / reproducing head and the erasing head rotate together. In this mechanism, the infrared sensor senses the reader tape part, and quick reverse operation is quickly performed by microcomputer control. This mechanism realizes inversion with almost no loss of sound.

Large 14-point FL meter is adopted.

Equipped with a balance volume, left and right balance control is possible.

The amplifier circuit uses a DC configuration that eliminates capacitors, which adversely affect sound quality, as much as possible, and realizes low distortion and high linearity.

It is equipped with a Compulink-1 / synchro terminal. When connected to a VICTOR/JVC CD player using the included remote wire, it is possible to record synchronously. When connected to an amplifier, the function is automatically switched in conjunction with the play of the deck.

Equipped with a music scan function that allows you to find the beginning of one song before and after.

Equipped with auto REC mute.

Equipped with timer start mechanism, recording of absence and alarm clock playback are possible by combining with optional timer.

Equipped with Auto Tape Select function that automatically detects the type of tape.

Equipped with a 4-digit electronic counter.

Equipped with a gold-plated headphone terminal.

Uses a power cord with polarity display.

Wireless remote control is included.
