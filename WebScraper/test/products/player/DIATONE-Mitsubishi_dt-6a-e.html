  • DIATONE DT-6A

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A metal tape compatible cassette deck with a 4-position tape selector.

It uses a high-precision DC servo motor to achieve stable running with less unevenness in tape speed.

Equipped with a Dolby circuit to reduce the hissing of the tape.

Equipped with VU meter.
