Commentary

Belt drive player capable of auto return and cutting in the middle.

30 cm aluminum die-cast turntable is adopted.

Auto return / intermediate cut is possible by operating the lever.

It uses a low-capacity cord and supports CD-4 playback.

Uses a removable dust cover.
