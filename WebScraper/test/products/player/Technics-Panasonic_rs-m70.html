  • Technics RS-M70

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with digital electronic echo microphone mixing function.

It is equipped with a digital electronic echo microphone mixing function which can mix and play back various tapes by applying echo to the microphone.
It is equipped with a pure electronic full-scale echo device and the echo time can be adjusted.

Equipped with a music selector, it can digitally detect the head of a song and automatically play from that position.

Equipped with a tape pitch control function that can change the tape speed.

The drive motor is a digital FG servo motor.

An HPF head has been adopted for the head.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with MIC/LINE mixing circuit that can perform fade in / out, etc.

It is equipped with a memory autoplay mechanism that can automatically play the tape from the desired position.

Equipped with a transistor-type muting circuit to prevent click noise during operation.

Adopts a timer standby mechanism and can record absence by connecting an optional timer.
