  • DENON DN-1100R

  • ¥ 248,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

A professional MD deck equipped with functions that can be used for DJ play, etc.

Equipped with a hot start function, you can play any song up to 10 songs seamlessly one after another by inputting 10 keys.
The 10-key preset can be used for each track or all tracks can be exchanged. Since it starts up with little time lag, it can be used as a DJ sampler.

Both input and output terminals are equipped with RCA pin terminals and balance terminals, and can be easily selected by switching the input on the front.
A coaxial digital input is also installed as a digital input.

It is equipped with an instant recording function, and recording starts almost simultaneously with the recording start operation.
It is possible to record the part of the target part, etc., and to record at precise timing without cutting off the head.

It is possible to start at the frame unit, and it is possible to start at the preset time with the hot start function and to start at the accurate 1/75 second unit when editing.

Equipped with a pre-U-TOC system, even if the power supply is turned off due to a power failure during recording, the recorded contents and TOC information are retained in the disk to prevent disk errors.

It is equipped with editing functions such as divide, combine, move, erase and so on that are unique to MD.

You can enter information such as disc titles using alphabets, numbers and symbols.
With a maximum of 254 characters per information, about 1700 characters of information can be written on each disc.

Rack mount is supported.
