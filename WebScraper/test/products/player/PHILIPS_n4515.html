  • PHILIPS N4515

  • ¥ 145,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

Tape deck with 3 motors, 3 heads, and 3 speeds.

The capstan motor is equipped with an electronic tacho generator, which works vertically and horizontally by a speed control circuit.

Oil dump type tension arm has been adopted.

A slide control is mounted on the microphone line input.

Equipped with an LED indicator to handle excessive input.

Equipped with pause key and continuous tape skip mechanism.

Equipped with a multi-play mechanism, you can enjoy echo recording and SOS recording.

Equipped with a dedicated output terminal to which MFB speakers can be directly connected.

By using the separately sold remote control unit, it can be operated from a remote place.
