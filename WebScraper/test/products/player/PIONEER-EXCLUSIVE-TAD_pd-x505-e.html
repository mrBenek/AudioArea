  • Pioneer PD-X505

  • ¥ 45,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

360 mm wide compact CD player.

Equipped with a disk stabilizer to reduce disk resonance.

It employs a linear servo system that reduces sound skipping caused by scratches and dirt on the disc.

Both digital and analog sections employ a pure transmission system that pursues high-purity transmission.

It has a program function that allows you to freely select 27 songs out of a maximum of 99 songs.

It is equipped with a track search function and a manual 3-speed search function that enables you to find the track you want to listen to with one touch operation.

There was a wireless remote control with 10 keys sold separately.
