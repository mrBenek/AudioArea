  • Aurex PC-4060S(PC-4060)

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A cassette deck equipped with various functions such as voice recording and autoplay.

The Recording Master Volume has a preset mechanism that allows you to click and stop where you have set the level.

Equipped with an autoplay mechanism, if you press the rewind lever and the play lever at the same time, playback starts automatically after rewinding.

It has a queue review function.

Equipped with an absence recording mechanism, you can record absence by connecting a separately sold timer.

The meter section is a 2-operation switching type, and the level meter can be used as a peak meter.

Equipped with a two step switching type tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.
Also, it has a position for Ferrichrome tape.

Equipped with line and microphone input selectors.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

The model name is PC 4060 s, but PC 4060 was written on the front of the deck.
