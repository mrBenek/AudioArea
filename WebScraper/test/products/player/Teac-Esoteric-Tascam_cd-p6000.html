  • TEAC CD-P6000

  • ¥ 45,000 (released in late May 1999)

Commentary

A CD player with functions such as pitch control.

Equipped with pitch control function that can be adjusted within ± 12% range.
Pitch adjustment can be done in 1% steps.

Center mount mechanism is adopted.

One coaxial system and two optical systems are equipped with digital outputs, of which one optical system is installed on the front in consideration of connection with portable MD.

It is equipped with functions such as program playback and 3-mode repeat.

The headphone port is equipped with volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
