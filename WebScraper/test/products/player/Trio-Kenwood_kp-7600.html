Commentary

A direct-drive player system.

550 kg ・ cm for turntable2The drive motor uses a 20-pole 30-slot DC / DD motor with a starting torque of 1.3 kg / cm.

Uses 10 Φ Center shaft fixed with BSBM material.

Uses MC material weight base that suppresses the reaction and disturbance of motor torque.
When a turntable with a large moment of inertia is driven by a motor with a large starting torque, the inertia energy causes the cabinet to vibrate as a reaction. This vibration shakes the tone arm and distorts the information inscribed on the record.
To suppress this, MC material is placed in the cabinet to increase the total weight and suppress the reaction of the turntable.

Dual suspension absorbers are used to suppress disturbance in both directions.

A pure electronic brake utilizing a starting coil is adopted.

Micro switch control is used for start / stop / rotation number switching and speed control.
A digital control system with a reliable flip-flop circuit is adopted for the rotation number selector switch and start / stop selector.
The speed control mechanism is independent at 33 1/3 and 45 rpm, and can be controlled within ± 3%.

The turntable seat uses an anti-vibration setting table seat that applies the principle of the Helmholtz resonance box.
Also, the table sheet of KP-7600 comes with a strobe for 33 1/3, 45 rpm.

Using BSBM material block cutting arm base and 4 mm double point fixing screw, vibration of the tone arm is suppressed by increasing the fixing strength.

The tone arm employs the TA-51 coupling mass separation type tone arm that simultaneously cancels the arm resonance and the first order split resonance. This increases the howling margin and reduces high-level low-frequency resonance to improve sound quality.
TA-51 has a delicate elastic modulus different from TA-71 adopted in KP 7700.

0 point time Uses a non-contact anti-skating mechanism.
When adjusting the tone arm 0 balance, the tone arm can be completely free by setting the anti-skating scale to 0.
