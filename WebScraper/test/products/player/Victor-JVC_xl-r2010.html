  • VICTOR/JVC XL-R2010

  • ¥ 74,000 (around 2001)

Commentary

A multiple CD recorder that combines a CD player and a CD recorder.

It is equipped with functions such as one touch recording and edit recording.
You can record a whole disc by simply pressing the CDRec button, or you can program your favorite songs from the disc and record them.
Digital dubbing at 4 x speed (2 x speed for CD-RW) is possible for these recording operations.
Also, when recording to a CD-R, it can be played back without being finalized, so it is possible to copy while checking the recording condition.

Equipped with digital input and sampling rate converter, digital sources such as satellite broadcasting and DVD can be directly taken in, automatically converted to CD sampling frequency and digital recording can be performed.
In addition, the 24-bit A / D converter enables digital conversion of analog sources for CD recording.

A brushless DD motor is used in the CD-R recorder section to improve recording accuracy by suppressing noise and vibration.

Regardless of whether digital or analog external devices are connected, automatic recording can be performed by synchronizing recording start / stop.
The digital REC volume makes it possible to adjust the volume of analog and digital signals with different sampling frequencies to the same level for recording.

The D/A converter uses a 24-bit PEM-DD converter.
In addition, it is equipped with Victor's original K2 interface which fundamentally solves jitter (time difference distortion) and ripple (waveform distortion) added to digital signal, and the sound quality during analog playback is improved.

Equipped with a microphone and line input terminal, it is possible to record sounds and musical instruments from outside while mixing them on CD.
There are three possible mixing recording combinations : Microphone + CD, Line + CD, and Microphone + Line.

Equipped with CD pitch control function, you can control the CD performance pitch by ± 12%.
It can be used not only for CD playback but also for recording, so it demonstrates its power for mixing.

Equipped with a fade recording function that smoothly connects the beginning and end of a song.

Equipped with a multi-jog dial, various operations can be easily performed just by turning the knob.

Wireless remote control is included.
