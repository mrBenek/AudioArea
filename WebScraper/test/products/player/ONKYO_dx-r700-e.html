  • ONKYO DX-R700

  • ¥ 55,000 (Available on July 11, 2001)

Commentary

Audio CD recorder with sampling rate converter.

Equipped with a sampling rate converter, you can record digital sources with different sampling frequencies such as Iori, 32 kHz or 48 kHz.

Equipped with a signal-synchronized recording function that starts recording in synchronization with signal input, finalization functions such as double-speed finalization and auto-finalization, 25-track program memory, and CD text input function.

Equipped with digital / analog recording volume.

Two optical digital input / output terminals are mounted.

Wireless remote control is included.
