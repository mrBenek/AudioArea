  • DIATONE DP-72R

  • ¥ 26,000 (released in 1968)

Commentary

A record player developed with a cartridge and precision finish that were born from sound production represented by Mitsubishi monitor speakers.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used as the drive motor.

An aluminum alloy die-cast turntable is used for the turntable.

A static balanced pipe arm is used for the tone arm part.
The head shell of this arm is a European type plug-in type and can be replaced with various cartridges.

Uses a high compliance type MM cartridge.

Equipped with an oil dump type elevation system, the pickup can be operated up and down with a lever.
