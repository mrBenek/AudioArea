  • BSR 1000X

  • ¥ 14,800 (around the early 1970 s)

Commentary

As a mini changer, this compact auto-changer can be placed on a bookshelf or in the corner of a small room.

Its mechanism is the same as that of a full-size autochanger, and it can play up to six 30-cm discs in succession.
Also, when you play a 17 cm record, use the attached 45 rotation adapter.

Repeat play and manual play can be performed with one touch operation.

The BSR's proven ceramic cartridge is included.

Equipped with safety lock mechanism, tone arm can be firmly fixed.

The cabinet is a reinforced plastic cabinet made in the United Kingdom.
Dust cover is included.
