Commentary

A DVD / Video CD/CD changer equipped with a 5-string changer.

The audio DAC uses a 96 kHz / 24-bit D/A converter.
In addition, consideration is given to sound quality such as high sound quality parts and independent power ground.

It is equipped with a 5-string changer that can set up to 5 discs at a time on the tray.
You can enjoy multiple discs for a long time such as continuous playback, program playback, random playback and repeat playback.
It also has a play exchange function that allows you to exchange the other four discs while the disc is playing.

The power supply section uses separate power circuits for video and audio to reduce noise from the digital circuit.

Equipped with a 10-bit video D/A converter.
We are also pursuing high image quality by using high image quality parts and making the power ground of the image circuit independent.

Equipped with a screen saver function, you can prevent the monitor screen from getting burned.

On-screen capability.

Wireless remote control is included.
