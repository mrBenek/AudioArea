  • ONKYO K-W502

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1995)

Commentary

A double-cassette deck capable of simultaneous recording, continuous recording and long-term continuous playback.

The motor is equipped with two recording and re-recording mechanisms that are controlled by a microcomputer.
In addition, a high-hardness permalloy head is used for the read / write head.

Equipped with Dolby noise reduction B type / C type, it reduces the "" hissing which is a concern in cassette tapes.

It is equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO that controls the bias to be optimal according to the high-frequency components contained in the recorded music signal.

You can start dubbing a tape with one touch, and you can choose normal speed with excellent sound quality or high speed speed with quick dubbing.

Equipped with a 4-digit electronic counter, both A and B decks can be displayed by switching.

It is equipped with a skip selection function which is convenient for starting a tape.
By pressing the Fast-forward / Rewind button during playback, you can select up to 15 songs before and after.

Equipped with a level indicator that shows the peak value of the music signal.
A peak hold mechanism is also adopted so that instantaneous peak values can be easily confirmed.

Equipped with a RI remote control terminal, it can be operated with the remote control attached to the amplifier by combining it with an Onkyo amplifier with RI mark.
Also, if you connect a CD player with RI mark, CD synchronized dubbing is also possible.

Uses a power cord with polarity display.
