Commentary

A stereo cassette deck with an F & F head.

Equipped with a tape selector that can be used with a chromicassette.

It uses an auto shut-off mechanism that automatically stops when the tape ends during recording / playback.

Equipped with a detachable 3-step dust cover, it is a 3-step opening / closing type of 30 ° 45 ° 60 °.
