Commentary

CD-R/RW-compatible CD recorder equipped with 24bitD/A converter.

A 24-bit D/A converter is used for the D/A converter, and carefully selected high-quality parts are used for audio circuits and other parts to improve sound quality.
In addition, the power supply section is independent into audio and digital sections to reduce mutual interference.

Equipped with a synchronous recording function, three types of recording are available : disk-synchronous recording for recording one album, track-synchronous recording for recording only one song, and auto-finalized recording for automatically finalizing after disk-synchronous recording.
Manual recording is also operation is also possible.

It is equipped with a Fade In / Fade Out function. If you set Fade Recording before recording, recording stops while fading out when stopped or posed during recording. Also, Fade In Recording is supported in Manual Recording Mode. If you set Fade Recording in the same way, recording starts while fading in from the beginning.
The fade time can be adjusted from 0 to 10 seconds (in 0.05 second steps).

The CD-RW has a track-erase function that enables simultaneous deletion of multiple tracks from the last track recorded on the CD-RW.
You can also delete all songs at once.

Equipped with a digital REC volume, the recording level can be adjusted within a range of -63dB to + 6 dB for analog recording and digital source recording.

Equipped with a text input / display function, you can enter album titles and artist names for recorded CD-R/RW discs and titles and artist names for each song using the front panel jog dial or remote control keys.
You can enter up to 64 characters for each title or artist name, in capital English letters, numbers and symbols. You can also use the Erase mode to erase all text or each title or artist name.
If you finalize after entering text, you can display the text that was entered when played on a CD-Text player.

Equipped with double-speed finalizing function, finalizing process can be done in 2 minutes.

Equipped with a sampling rate converter, the sampling frequency (32kHz/48kHz) of DAT and BS/CS broadcasting can be automatically converted to the sampling frequency of CD (44.1 kHz) for digital recording.
A 44.1 kHz direct recording function allows digital input signals of 44.1 kHz to be recorded directly without passing through a sampling rate converter.

Recording in the HDCD format is supported.

It is equipped with a monitor mode function so that signals from digital sources other than CD (32 kHz or 48 kHz) can be converted to a sampling frequency of 44.1 kHz and output, and analog signals can be converted to a digital signal of 44.1 kHz by passing it through an A/D converter.

The pick-up mechanism is located in the center of the chassis, and a center mount mechanism is used to suppress vibration by optimizing the weight balance of the body.

Aluminum panel is used for the front panel.

Wireless remote control is included.
