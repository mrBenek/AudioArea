  • DIATONE DT-4450

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Front operated cassette deck.

A DC servo motor with a built-in generator is used for the drive motor of the mechanism section, which, together with a stable mechanism mechanism, improves uneven running.

The head uses a ferrite head with mirror surface finish.

A built-in Dolby circuit is used as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a full auto-stop mechanism, the operation button is automatically released when the tape is finished regardless of whether recording, playing or rewinding.

It is equipped with a mechanical pause mechanism be stopped temporarily.

Equipped with a tape selector compatible with chrome and normal tapes.

Equipped with timer recording and re-mechanism.
