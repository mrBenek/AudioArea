Commentary

A DVD player equipped with a Dolby digital decoder for enjoying karaoke and other functions.

The video circuit is equipped with a 10bit/27MHz high-bit high-sampling video DAC.

The audio DAC is equipped with a 96 kHz PEM-DD converter that, together with a fine clock generator, provides a dynamic range of more than 106 dB.

A circuit board composed of two separate boards for video and audio is used.
In addition, by using an independent and separate design, such as supplying power to the analog and digital systems independently, mutual interference that affects image and sound quality is eliminated.

Equipped with Dolby Digital 3D Phonic function.
With this function, video software recorded with 5.1 channels in Dolby Digital can be reproduced by mixing the rear / center signal into 2 channels, so that you can enjoy realistic sound with the built-in TV speaker.

It supports the Karaoke DVD standard.
You can enjoy full-scale karaoke with a single duet function that allows you to enjoy duet songs even when you sing alone, a sound effect function that allows you to enjoy three types of changes, two microphone inputs, digital key con / digital echo, and scoring functions.

Equipped with a graphical user navigator, various settings can be made simply by selecting and executing with the remote control while looking at the on-screen menu.
High-capacity graphics memory makes it easy to perform rich functions.

Wireless remote control is included.
