Commentary

MD deck equipped with sampling rate converter.

It is equipped with a sampling rate converter for digital sources other than CDs. It automatically converts 32 kHz and 48 kHz sources to 44.1 kHz. The conversion center uses the same LSI as the DSRLL installed in the Esotheric D/A converter D-30 to reduce jitters.

Equipped with 1-bit D/A and A/D converter that does not generate 0 cross distortion in principle.

Equipped with digital level control, the level can be adjusted to -6dB to + 6 dB by operating the jog dial during digital recording.

Equipped with PS2 terminal to which 106 Japanese keyboard for DOS/V PC can be connected.

An optical (TOS) input terminal is also provided on the front panel.

Equipped with a digital auto-fader, you can auto-fade in / out at any time during digital recording. The fade-in time can be set to 3/6/9/12/15/18/21/24 seconds, and the fade-out time can be set to 6/9/12/15/18/21/24/27 seconds.

Operability is improved by the multi-functional jog dial.

It is equipped with editing functions such as DIVIDE to divide songs, ERASE to erase unnecessary parts, MOVE to rearrange the order, and COMBINE to connect songs.

It is equipped with a sound-synchronized recording function so that recording can start automatically when the connected CD player or cassette deck starts playing. Recording is also paused when playback ends.

It is equipped with FL display.

Since the date and time can be displayed on the display, it is possible to record the date and time on the disc when recording.

The front panel is made of solid aluminum, and the main chassis is made of steel board with increased rigidity.

Wireless remote control is included.
