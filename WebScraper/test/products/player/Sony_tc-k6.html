  • SONY TC-K6

  • ¥ 74,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with soft touch button by exclusive motor for operation.

It uses a special motor for soft touch operation and realizes almost the same operation feeling as feather touch.

We have adopted a green motor.
This motor has been improved from the frequency servo motor with FG that maintains high-precision rotation to improve reliability and durability.

The head is equipped with an F & F head with excellent head gap accuracy, high frequency characteristics, and abrasion resistance.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism that allows one touch silent recording.

Equipped with a 3-stage peak level indicator that is effective for adjusting the proper recording level.

Equipped with a 3-stage switching tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Equipped with a memory stop mechanism, rewinding stops at counter 999 when the memory switch is turned ON.

Equipped with a Dolby NR to improve the high-frequency dynamic range and an MPX filter to cut off the pilot signal of FM broadcasting.

By using the timer sold separately, it is possible to record your absence and play the alarm clock.

The line and microphone can be mixed.

It has a volume that can be used as both headphone and lineout.

Equipped with auto shut-off mechanism.
