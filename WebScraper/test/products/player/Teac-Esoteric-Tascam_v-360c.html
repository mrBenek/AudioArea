Commentary

This is a cassette deck with a simple structure with an emphasis on quality.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

It is equipped with the Brilliance function in order to ensure sufficient high sound range which tends to be lost in this class of deck, and bright and clear sound quality is achieved by compensating for high sound range when playing back.

Equipped with an operation mode display function by LED.

Uses a Longs scale slide fader.

Equipped with 2-color LED peak level meter.

Equipped with microphone input terminal.

Equipped with headphone output terminal.

Equipped with Rec Mute function.

Metal tapes are supported.
