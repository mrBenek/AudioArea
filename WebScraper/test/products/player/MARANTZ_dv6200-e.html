Commentary

A DVD player equipped with progressive scan video output.

Equipped with progressive scan (2-3 pull-down method) video output function.

Equipped with a 27 mhz / 10-bit video to digital converter.

Progressive video is clocked up and played back by a 54 MHz / 10-bit video to digital converter.

Compatible with D2 input terminal.

Equipped with a zoom function to enlarge part of the image.

Even when connected to a stereo mini-component or TV (with built-in stereo), it is equipped with a 3D surround function that allows you to enjoy virtual surround with 2 ch speakers.

Built-in MP3 decoder enables CD-R/RW playback of MP3 format created by PC, etc.

You can play not only commercially available Video CDs but also Video CDs (Ver. 2.0) created on a PC.

Supports CD/CD-R/RW playback.

A 96kHzD/A converter is installed for audio.

Component / S / Composite video output terminal is mounted.

Gold plated terminals are used for all analog output circuits of video and audio systems.

Wireless remote control is included.
