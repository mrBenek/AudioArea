Commentary

This tape deck is a 2-track model of the A-3300S.

The A-3300S-2T supports 38cm/19cm tapes for high-quality 2-track recording.

The control mechanism is composed entirely of microswitches to provide reliable operation and soft feeling. In addition, the pause button is incorporated into the control mechanism so that you can use the REC and PAUSE buttons to wait for recording and the PLAY button to start recording.

It is equipped with a 2-stage switching mechanism independent of bias and equalizer to cope with low noise tapes.

Equipped with an expanded scale level meter that is independent on the left and right.
This meter has a margin of + 6 VU in full scale, enabling accurate level monitoring during high-level recording. In addition, the level meter is linked to the output volume, enabling accurate delivery level monitoring.

The brake mechanism uses a brake drum made of a combination of aluminum die-cast and special felt, and is designed so that the brake is applied constantly to the drum.

It uses a 6 mm thick panel structure made of a combination of high-precision aluminum and stainless steel, and a reinforced die-cast frame on the side.

A 3-motor configuration is used for the mechanism part.
The use of precision processed parts such as the capstan shaft with a roundness of 0.15 μ mm realizes stable tape travel.

It uses a 3-head system that can monitor recorded content.
In addition, the head uses a hyperbolic type with good tape touch and less contour effect (shape effect).

Newly designed quick-lock type reel clamper is used for the reel clamper.

It uses an oil dump type tension arm that makes the rise of the tape smooth.

Microphone and line mixing recording is possible.

It uses a stabilized power supply circuit that reduces the influence of power supply voltage fluctuations.

Cooling measures have been taken to prevent temperature rise.

Equipped with a shut-off arm that stops the mechanism automatically when the tape finishes winding.

Equipped with a 4-digit index counter.

Uses a dual-purpose mechanism that can be used horizontally and vertically.

There was a remote controller sold separately.

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining the timer control adapter and timer (sold separately).
It has a structure that does not burden all mechanisms including the pinch roller during standby.
