Audio Database

  • Marantz CD-46

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

CD player with digital servo configuration using Philips linear tracking mechanism CDM-12.

The DA converter is equipped with a new CCDAC (TDA1545).

Equipped with a fade in / out function.

Equipped with auto music scan function.

Up to 30 songs can be played either programmatically or randomly.

Comes with a wireless remote control with volume control.
