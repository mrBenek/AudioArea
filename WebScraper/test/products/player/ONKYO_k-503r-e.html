  • ONKYO K-503R

  • ¥ 43,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with quick reverse mechanism.

The head is equipped with a quick reverse hard permalloy rotating head that is firmly held on a highly rigid base.

It uses a three power supply system in which each of the amplifier system, mechanical system and microcomputer system is independent.

It is equipped with a real time counter that measures and displays the remaining amount in minutes and seconds even from the middle of the tape.

Equipped with Dolby HX-PRO that automatically adjusts the bias current to the optimum value according to the high-frequency component.

Equipped with an accubias control that can be adjusted severely to match the tape.

Equipped with timer standby function, recording / playback is possible by external power supply control method timer.

Equipped with 2-mode repeat function, auto-space REC mute function and jump selection function.

A remote control is included.
