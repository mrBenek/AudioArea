Commentary

An 8-track deck that can play 8-track tapes.

Simply insert the cartridge tape to start automatic play.

Equipped with a manual eject switch that can eject a tape at any location.

When one program ends, it automatically switches to the next program. Manual switching is also possible.

Equipped with a program indicator that shows the program being played.

The motor is equipped with an outer rotor motor.
