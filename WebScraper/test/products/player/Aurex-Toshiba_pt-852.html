  • TOSHIBA PT-852

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 4-track 2-channel stereo tape deck capable of bidirectional stereo recording and 4-track monaural recording.

Equipped with a tape changeover switch, it can demonstrate the performance of standard tapes and high-sensitivity tapes (HiFi tape / SH-7).

Equipped with an auto shut-off mechanism that automatically stops the tape running when the tape is finished.

Uses a 4-pole condenser motor with stable performance and low rotational noise and uneven rotation.

The all-silicon transistor circuit reduces the noise level.

It has a headphone port.

Equipped with a 3-digit tape counter.

Equipped with a level meter.
