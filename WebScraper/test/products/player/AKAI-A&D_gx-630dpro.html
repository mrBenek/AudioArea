  • AKAI GX-630Dpro

  • Price unknown (released in 1975)

Commentary

A 3-head tape deck with a GX head for recording and reproducing heads.

Two track recording and playback at 38cm/s and 19cm/s is possible.

Reel 10 can be used.

Equipped with a 2-position tape selector.

The mechanism uses a direct capstan drive with an AC servo motor.
