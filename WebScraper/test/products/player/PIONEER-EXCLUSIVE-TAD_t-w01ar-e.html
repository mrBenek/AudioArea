Commentary

Double reverse deck equipped with newly developed ALCA.

Equipped with Auto Level Control with Tape Analysis (ALCA), which automatically adjusts the recording level according to the capacity of the tape and the volume of the source. The peak and initial recording levels are set by analyzing bias, sensitivity, and high-frequency and high-level recording capability, and the recording level is automatically set according to the input signal.

It is equipped with TDNS (Tape Duplication Noise Suppressor) which reduces noise during tape copying, and realizes the reduction of noise between songs.

Equipped with FLAT System and Dolby HX-PRO.

Equipped with the last memory function, it can store FLAT System data, main switch positions, etc.

Equipped with a CD-DECK synchro function, it can be linked with a Pioneer CD player equipped with a CD-DECK synchro function and automatically linked operation is possible.

Wireless remote control is included.
