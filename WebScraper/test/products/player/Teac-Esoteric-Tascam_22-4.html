  • ¥ 169,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

This 4-channel multitrack recorder was developed as an introduction to multiple recording.

Microphone recording is possible by combining with M-2A or M-09. Music can be created by multiple recording such as changing orientation, equalizing, echo processing, etc.

It is equipped with a headphone selector and can monitor the sound of any channel.

Equipped with ± 6% pitch control that works for both recording and playback.

Equipped with a simulsink mechanism.

It has a queue mechanism.

Equipped with a tape counter with a memory stop.

Dbx unit DX-34 (sold separately) can be connected.
In addition, the separately sold remote control unit RC-70 can be connected.

Rack mounting is possible by using adapter RM-42.
