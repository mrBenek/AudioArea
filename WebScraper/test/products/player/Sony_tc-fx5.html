Commentary

Cassette deck with multi-functional peak program meter.

Equipped with a multi-functional peak program meter of double indication system to accurately capture peak values.

It uses a 100 mm wide long stroke slide volume that realizes highly accurate recording level adjustment.

The head uses an S & F head compatible with metal tape.

The mechanism uses a simple 2-motor configuration mechanism.

The characteristics are improved by direct coupling of the head and reproducing amplifier.

Equipped with a 4-stage tape selector.

Uses Dolby NR / Complete IC.

Equipped with autoplay / memory play function.

Equipped with Rec Mute function with auto space.

Equipped with a timer standby switch.

It is compatible with optional wireless remote control, wired remote control, and remote control unit for enjoying synchronized play.
