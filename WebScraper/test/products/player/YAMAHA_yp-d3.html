  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

A record player with a direct drive system.

A DC servo motor with starting torque of 600g ・ cm is used for the drive system.
In addition, by providing a sufficient margin, the power supply maintains a constant voltage even when a large amount of output current flows, thereby coping with sudden load fluctuations.
The turntable has a diameter of 33 cm and has an inertial mass of 220 kg ・ cm.

The tone arm adopts a static balance system S-shaped pipe arm, and is firmly fixed with a zinc die-cast holder with high rigidity and low squeal.
Needle pressure adjustment is a direct-reading scale with 0.5g steps from 0 to 3.5g. It is a universal type that can be used for cartridges with weight of 4g to 11g.
In addition, it is equipped with various functions such as a spring and lever type inside force canceler that can be directly read by a dial that can adjust the cancellation fee according to the needle pressure, and an oil dump queuing system that allows the arm to move up and down smoothly by the hydraulic pressure of silicon oil is adopted for the arm lifter.
In addition, a dynamic damping system is adopted to suppress resonance of the arm.

The head shell is a plug-in type light shell of EIA standard and can be replaced.
In addition, gold plating on the connector part with the arm reduces sound quality degradation due to poor contact.

Newly designed dual magnet type CG-6600 is used for the cartridge.
The dual magnet type has a feature that noise is limited because the impedance is low.

The cabinet is a solid type with 40 mm thick particleboards of high density and specific gravity, and the insulator is a 50 φ large-size rubber material processed into a special shape to reduce the influence of unwanted resonance and external vibration.

The dust cover is made of thick acrylic styrene to prevent howling, and comes with a free stop hinge so that it can be stopped even at an intermediate angle.
