  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

Full-auto player with synchronized recording function.

Equipped with a synchronous recording mechanism, it is possible to record without error by linking with the designated deck (D-E55, D-E33).

Microcomputer controlled linear tracking system is adopted.

The drive motor is a coreless type.
