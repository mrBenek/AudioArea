  • AIWA AD-L22

  • ¥ 36,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

Mini-size cassette deck that can be used with 12 VDC power supply.

It can also be used outdoors by getting power from a special rechargeable battery.

Equipped with a Dolby system.

Equipped with a tape selector compatible with LH and CrO2.

The mechanism section is equipped with a DC servo motor.

Ultra-hard permalloy head is used for the head.

It uses a full auto-stop mechanism to prevent damage to tapes and heads.

Equipped with 3-color LED optical display.

Equipped with a tape run indicator that blinks red when recording.

The mechanism of the AD-L22 is such that even a recording tape with a folded tab to prevent erroneous erasure is erased when a recording operation is performed.
When recording, the cassette is inserted after pressing the record button.
