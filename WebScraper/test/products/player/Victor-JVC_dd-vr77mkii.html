Commentary

Improved cassette deck of DD-VR77.

OFC material is used for the head winding, the rear Dwyer, and the amplifier substrate, and transmission capability is improved by making the front surface of the signal path OFC.

The power supply circuit uses a ± tracking shunt control power supply that locks the ground potential to 0.
This circuit constantly compares the positive and negative potentials, which vary according to complex music signals, and keeps the midpoint at 0. In addition, stability is improved by combining a constant voltage circuit controlled by a shunt regulator, and the source impedance is reduced to 1 / 100 compared to the conventional one.

The reverse mechanism uses Victor's proprietary Fine Axis Quick Reverse System.
The rotation method of the 3-layer die-cast structure maintains accurate azimuth on the outward and return paths, and long-term reliability is obtained by the unique mechanism and 10 micron order component configuration.
In addition, a microcomputer control is adopted for tape reversal to realize high-speed reversal.

The SA head of the ceramic guard is adopted for the recording and reproducing head.
This head does not magnetize the guard part, and has less surface friction and uneven wear. Moreover, harmful modulation noise is reduced by smooth tape running.

The transport mechanism uses a silent mechanism with 2 motors, full logic + mechanical drive motor.
In this mechanism, detection is performed at the position of an interlocking cam, and the mechanical mode is switched by a microcomputer controlled mechanical drive motor.

All signal paths are DC amplifier configuration and capacitors are eliminated as much as possible to achieve low distortion factor and high linearity.

A pulse servo DD motor is used as the drive motor.
This motor adopts FG servo which is resistant to disturbance and temperature change and multi-pride bridge drive circuit, and has obtained high reliability.

Equipped with 4 digital counter function which can use 4 display functions.
You can use a stopwatch function that can be used for both recording and playback, a リメインタイム function that displays the remaining amount of the tape in minutes and seconds, a 4-digit electronic counter that allows you to specify memory play and repeat sections, and a program number that allows you to select up to 20 songs by skipping over both A and B sides.

Equipped with a digital peak function that shows the maximum value of the signal level by microcomputer.
The hold time is approximately 2 seconds, and the maximum value stored in the microcomputer can be called by pressing the CALL button.

Equipped with multi-editor function, auto fade in / out and after fade / cut can be performed.
When you press the Fade button from the REC pause state, recording starts with an auto fade of approximately 5 seconds. Similarly, when you press the Fade button, the REC pause state is set approximately 2.5 seconds later. The fade time can be set to approximately 10 seconds each.

Equipped with edit control function, half speed / double speed is possible in both forward and reverse directions, and accurate heading is possible.

Uses all-gold-plated input / output terminals with little aging variation.
