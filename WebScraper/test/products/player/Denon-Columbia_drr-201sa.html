Commentary

Cassette deck of PRESTA201SA series.

Equipped with a horizontal loading mechanism.
The tape can be held in a stable condition by setting it horizontally, and the sound quality during recording and playback is improved by suppressing vibration of the cassette half while the tape is running.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.
In addition, it is equipped with Dolby HX Pro to realize high sound quality recording.

Equipped with a Oatley berth function, A side / B side can be played repeatedly.

Equipped with an auto tape selector function, bias and equalizer can be set automatically according to the type of tape.

It is equipped with a music search function that allows you to select songs by jumping 99 songs in front and back.

It is equipped with system functions realized by combination with PRESTA201SA series.
