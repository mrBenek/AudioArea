  • National RS-858U

  • ¥ 78,900 (around 1974)

Commentary

The 4-channel 8-track deck allows recording and playback of 4-channel 8-track tapes and 2-channel 8-track tapes.

The counter uses a minute and second display system.

Equipped with auto-eject mechanism.

The headphone port can be switched between front and rear.

Equipped with a pause button and FF button.

Programs are displayed in a digital format.

Equipped with an AC outlet.

Equipped with a channel indicator lamp.
