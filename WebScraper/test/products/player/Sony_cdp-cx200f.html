  • SONY CDP-CX200F

  • ¥ 59,000 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A changer type CD player that can store 200 discs.

Disks can be added and replaced from the front.

The D/A converter is equipped with a hybrid pulse D/A converter.

The servo circuit is equipped with a high-precision digital servo.
In this method, the servo amount of the high-priced pickup is adjusted for each disc to be played, thereby realizing servocontrol that more accurately reflects the disc condition.

Equipped with a jog dial in the main body, 200 discs can be easily selected.

It supports CD-TEXT containing textual information such as song titles and artist names.
It is alphanumeric on the body, and it is also possible to display Chinese characters by using a text display unit sold separately.

With a built-in disc memo function, you can remember your favorite message such as album title on a disc without CD text.

Built-in group file function allows you to classify your discs into up to 8 groups, such as music genre or artist.

Equipped with multi-mode timer play function, timer play is possible by combining with an optional timer.

It is equipped with a delete bank function that allows you to leave a delete play program in memory for each disk.

It is equipped with functions such as shuffle play, delete play and 6-mode repeat.

Equipped with an optical digital output.

Wireless remote control is included.
