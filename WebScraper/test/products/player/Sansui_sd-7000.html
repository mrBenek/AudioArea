  • SANSUI SD-7000

  • ¥ 168,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

An automatic stereo tape deck.

Auto-rewind, Oatley Piet, and Oatley Verse can be operated with signals (20 Hz) and sensing foils.

Equipped with a tape tension switching mechanism.

Equipped with a pause button and sleeping switch.

Equipped with mixing function by 2-circuit input.

The optional remote controller with pause button can be connected.
