  • Technics RS-4

  • ¥ 34,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

A record jacket size cassette deck with simple operation.

It uses a soft mechanism that uses the rotational force of the motor, making it possible and stable tape travel with light operation.

Equipped with an auto tape selector that automatically detects the type of tape and sets the tape position.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with LED level meter.

Equipped with a synchronous recording function.

Equipped with 1-button timer standby mechanism for recording absence by combining with an optional timer.
