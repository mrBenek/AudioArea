  • Pioneer XL-A700S

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Quartz PLL full auto player which applied high precision of crystal oscillator.

Quartz PLL DC servo Hall motor is used as the drive motor.

Non-contact optical is used for arm return detection to prevent adverse effects on arm sensitivity.

It is equipped with Quick Play that the turntable automatically starts rotating when the arm is moved on the record board.

S-shaped pipe arm is used for the tone arm.

By combining a die-cast frame with a high-density cabinet, we have realized a cabinet that is resistant to howling.
