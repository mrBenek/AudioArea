Commentary

This master recorder combines stable sound quality by 38cm/s and 2 tracks with economical efficiency of 19cm/s.

The capstan motor uses a DC servo motor.

3-motor 3-head configuration is adopted.

2-track 2-channel recording / playback is available.
In addition, the speed can be switched between 38cm/s and 19cm/s.

Equipped with a large VU meter.

Equipped with a lock-type timer standby mechanism, recording of absence is possible.

Uses a reel clamper that allows one touch attachment of the reel.

Microphone and line mixing is possible.

Footswitch type Pose Remote Control can be connected.
