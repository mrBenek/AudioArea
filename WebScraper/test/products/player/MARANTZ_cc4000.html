Audio Database

  • Marantz CC4000

  • ¥ 28,000 (released in March 2000)

Commentary

A CD changer that can hold five discs simultaneously.

CD-RW playback is supported.

Equipped with a digital servo.

The D/A converter uses a bit stream DAC.

Equipped with coaxial digital output terminal.

It is equipped with functions such as edit, peak search, shuffle, program up to 40 songs, fade out and fade in.

Wireless remote control is included.
