  • SONY TC-V910WR

  • ¥ 87,000 (around 1986)

Commentary

This is a liberty size double recording re-reverse deck that can record two volumes of tape simultaneously or continuously.

Recording and playback are possible on both A deck and B deck, enabling simultaneous recording of two volumes of tape, 3-hour continuous recording (using C-90 tape), and up to 15-hour continuous playback (C-90 tape, repeated 5 times).
The double-speed dubbing and auto-pause mechanism make editing operations easier.

Newly developed super bias system and LC-OFC wound laser amorphous head are adopted.
