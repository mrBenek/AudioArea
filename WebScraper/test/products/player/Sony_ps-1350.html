  • ¥ 33,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

Belt drive player system with auto mechanism.

Uses a 4-pole synchronous motor with extremely low vibration developed exclusively for the turntable.
In addition, high-precision urethane belt with both sides polished is used for the belt.

30 cm aluminum die-cast is used for the turntable.
It uses a new rubber sheet that suppresses harmful vibrations of the phonograph record and is designed to fit the phonograph record to the rubber sheet.

Uses a static balanced universal tone arm.
A precision pivot bearing is used for the bearing section to increase sensitivity, and the support section is supported by a damper to provide a vibration-resistant design.
In addition, it is equipped with inside for scan seller.

A MM type cartridge is used.

Equipped with auto-return and auto-cut mechanisms.

The output code is a low-capacity type with less high-frequency loss.

Equipped with a height-adjustable insulator.

Comes with a removable acrylic dust cover.
