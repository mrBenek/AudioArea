  • Pioneer PL-M10

  • ¥ 24,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

A record player with a belt drive system.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used for the drive part, and a double-sided polished polyurethane belt is used for the drive belt. In addition, the accuracy of turntables, rotating shafts, bearings, etc. is improved by an automatic precision machine.

S-shaped pipe arm is used for the tone arm.
Uses plug-in type and anti-skating mechanism for easy cartridge replacement.

Equipped with an arm elevation mechanism.

Howling is suppressed by adopting a structure that supports the whole panel as a flow by a special spring.

The cartridge is equipped with MM type cartridge PC 110.

It uses a low-capacity output code that can handle 4 ch playback.
