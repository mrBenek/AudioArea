Commentary

This CD player incorporates original technology and has improved operation functions.

The mechanism is equipped with a newly developed optical deck.
Two newly developed ICs are used for the focus & tracking servo, and the 3-beam astigmatic system is adopted to improve operational stability.

The digital signal processing circuit incorporates three newly developed LSIs to achieve 100% integration.
In addition, the error correction capability has achieved high performance with the probability that data interpolation is required once in about 10,000 years.

The D/A converter uses AN6806, which was originally developed.
We achieved 16-bit D/A conversion with a non-linear distortion of ± 0.002% or less by pursuing precision with precision trimming of the laser beam.

The low-pass filter is equipped with an 11th-order low-pass filter.

Up to 32 pairs of random programming functions are installed.

Equipped with continuously variable pitch control, it can be freely adjusted within ± 6%.
+ 5.9% and -5.6% are the upper and lower semitones. The gear shift and standard speed can be changed by turning the pitch button on/off.

It is equipped with a music scan key and plays for 10 seconds from the beginning of the music.

It is equipped with FL display.

Equipped with all songs / program repeat function and auto pause function.

Equipped with level-variable headphone jacks, synchronized recording jacks and accessory jacks.

Wireless remote control is included.

There are 2 variations, black and silver.
