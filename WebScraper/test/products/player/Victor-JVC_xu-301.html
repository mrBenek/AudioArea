  • VICTOR/JVC XU-301

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1999)

Commentary

This is an MD-CD combination deck that enables combined play of 3 cd and MD.

An independent tray 3 cd changer and MD deck are integrated, making high-quality digital recording and editing easy.

It is equipped with a one touch recording function, and recording from 3 cd to MD can be done with one touch. Recording starts with one touch of the CD-REC button when recording whole CDs or pre-programmed songs.

Equipped with the best hit recording function, you can select only the first track from 3 CDs of 8cm/12cmCD and record automatically one after another.

It is equipped with a listening edit function. If you press the Listening button when you want to record a song, only that song will be automatically recorded on the MD. This enables efficient editing and recording while listening to the CD.

Equipped with an original recording function, it is possible to record to MD while mixing sound from CD, microphone and external device.

Microphone terminal and analog input / output terminal are mounted.
You can manually adjust the recording level and mix balance.

Equipped with 5-mode (Join/Divide/Move/Erase/All erase) MD editing function, you can edit the recorded contents later.

It is equipped with Mix program performance (program1' 3 + Random) which allows you to freely select songs from 3 CDs and MD and play them in memory.

It is compatible with CD-Text, and you can copy the song name data from the CD to the MD as it is.

Equipped with a CD title memory function, you can enter and display three items (disc title, performer, and genre) on a normal CD. The library function also stores data for 200 CDs.
In addition, the disc title can be copied and used as an MD track title during MD recording.

Equipped with a jog dial to improve operability.

It is equipped with the latest ATRAC and 20-bit A / D&D/A converter.

It is equipped with a 3 fs sampling rate converter that can directly record BS and CS sound.

Optical digital input / output terminal is mounted.

Equipped with timer recording / playback (Daily/Once) function.
Comes with a 2-sided remote control with a system key and a letter key.
With this remote control, you can input Katakana, letters, alphabets, numbers and symbols quickly like a keyboard.
