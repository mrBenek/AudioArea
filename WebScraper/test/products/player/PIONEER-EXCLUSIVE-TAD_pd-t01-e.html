  • Pioneer PD-T01

  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1993)

Commentary

CD player with turntable system.

The mechanism part is equipped with a turntable mechanism.
The disc is rotated by supporting the entire surface of the disc from the bottom on a 12 cm diameter turntable. It is resistant to external vibration and sound pressure, and is also resistant to playback of warped discs, reducing unnecessary disc vibration.
This makes it possible to read data more accurately without being affected by fluctuations or vibrations.

The D/A converter is equipped with a high-speed pulse flow D/A converter.
By operating the Noise Shaper with a high oversampling of 384 fs, the requantization noise is pushed out of band, and by reducing the order of the Noise Shaper, the rise of out-of-band noise is suppressed.
Also, by operating the master clock at a low clock frequency of 16.93 mhz, the effect of jitter is reduced.

In order to realize purer signal transmission, we always use direct construction.
By laying out main circuits such as the power supply, digital / servo circuits, and audio circuits so that the signal flow is at the shortest distance, we are able to reduce the intrusion of noise and interference between circuits caused by the routing of wiring materials.

It is equipped with functions such as computer program edit, auto program edit, peak search, highlight scan, memory hold, and talkback.

Equipped with a display off switch, it is possible to suppress scanning noise when the display is turned on.

Equipped with CD-DECK synchro terminal.
