Commentary

CD player designed with consideration for combination with MDS-S35 and MDS-S37.

The D/A converter is equipped with a hybrid pulse D/A converter.
The hybrid pulse D/A converter has a built-in noise-shaping digital filter for high-precision oversampling operation and is integrated into one chip to simplify the signal path.
In addition, the effect of the direct digital sync circuit formed in the chip has been improved to reduce signal degradation due to jitter.

High-precision digital servo is used in the servo circuit, and by using A/D and D/A converters with high sampling rate, servocontrol which accurately reflects disk condition is realized.

Equipped with a custom edit function, you can use functions such as peak search, program edit, time edit and time fade.

Equipped with functions such as shuffle and 4-mode repeat.

Equipped with timer play mechanism, timer play is possible by combining with separately sold timer.

Equipped with an optical digital output.

It has a headphone port.

Wireless remote control is included.
