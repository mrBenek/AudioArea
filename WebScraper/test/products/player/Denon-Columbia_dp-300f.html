Commentary

Full-auto player with full-scale performance.

It uses a full-auto mechanism. When the record size is selected, the arm moves automatically by simple operation to play the record, and the arm returns after the playback is finished.

Aluminum die-cast turntable has been adopted for the turntable part to realize stable rotation with high inertial mass.
In addition, the turntable sheet with a thickness of approximately 5 mm, which has excellent vibration resistance, has been improved in record holding performance by vibration analysis using hologram vibration analysis.

Straight arm is used for the arm part.

An aluminum die-cast head shell is adopted for the head shell, and a MM type cartridge is included.
In addition, the cartridge can be replaced, and anti-skating and 0 balance adjustment can be performed finely.

Equipped with a manual arm lifter.

It has a built-in phono equalizer and can be directly connected to a mini component or pre-main amplifier without a phono connector.
The Phono equalizer amplifier can be switched ON/OFF.

The cabinet consists of 4 mm thick moulded material and has a glossy paint finish on the exterior.
There are 2 types of exterior finish, premium silver finish and black finish.
