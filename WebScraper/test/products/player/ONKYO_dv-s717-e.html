  • ONKYO DV-S717

Commentary

A DVD player with DTS and component video outputs.

The video section is equipped with a 10-bit D/A video converter.
The audio section is equipped with a 96 kHz / 24-bit audio D/A converter.

It uses a dedicated 2-lens laser pickup that can play DVDs, video CDs, and music CDs.

It is equipped with a jog shuttle and shuttle ring that can be searched and searched.

Equipped with 3-step zoom function and 3D digital noise reduction.

Digital / analog independent power supply circuit is adopted.

Equipped with a digital audio output terminal and component (color difference) video output terminal.
