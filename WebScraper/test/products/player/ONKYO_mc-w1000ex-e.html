  • ONKYO MC-W1000EX

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

REC double reverse deck with two inputs and outputs.

Equipped with a 6-motor and reverse-head for high-quality recording, a twin real-time counter that notifies the elapsed time and remaining time of the tape in units of minutes and seconds has been adopted to improve operability.

Dolby B/C noise reduction circuits are installed independently on Tape 1 and Tape 2.

One can record another source while the other is playing.
