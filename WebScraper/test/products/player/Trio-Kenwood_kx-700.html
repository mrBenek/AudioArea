Commentary

A cassette deck with a built-in Dolby system.

Equipped with a Dolby system that automatically removes hiss noise from the recording and playback units.
The recording unit raises the level of the low level input signal and the playback unit lowers it to the same level as the raised level of the recording unit. Therefore, it is possible to make a difference between the noise component and the signal level, and S/N is improved.

A tape selector is installed so that chrome tapes can be used in the lowest condition.
It is possible to switch between regular / low-noise / chrome and various tapes by one touch switching with push button.

The head uses a super ferrite head sintered by a unique method, and exhibits about five times the hardness of a conventional permalloy head with a thin core piled up.

Synchronous motor with high constant speed stability is used.
