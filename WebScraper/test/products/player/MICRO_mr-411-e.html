Commentary

Belt drive player with stable parts for harmony.

A 4-pole hysteresis synchronous motor, MB 400 s, is used for the drive system, and an aluminum die-cast 31 cm turntable is driven by a belt drive.

The tone arm is equipped with MA-77mkII which is a static balance type tone arm with inside four scan cellar of direct reading system.

The cartridge is equipped with M-2100/5 which is a MM type cartridge.

Equipped with a belt lock mechanism linked to the power switch.

Equipped with an oil dump type arm lifter.

As a countermeasure against howling, 19 cm plywood is used, and a 9 mm back plate using a player base and reinforcement is adopted.

A low-capacity shield wire is adopted to support 4 ch.

Equipped with a parts box that can store accessories.
