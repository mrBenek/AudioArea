Audio Database

  • Nakamichi OMS-20

  • ¥ 70,000 (around 1989)

Commentary

A CD player developed as a device that matches TA-30/TA-20.

A glitch free dual D/A converter is used in the DAC section.
A multi-regulated power supply is used for the power supply to prevent mutual interference.

An isolated ground system is adopted to keep the ground line clean.

System remote control of TA-30/TA-20 is supported.
