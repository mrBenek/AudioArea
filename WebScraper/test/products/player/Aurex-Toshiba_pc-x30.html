  • Aurex PC-X30

  • ¥ 55,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A cassette deck equipped with a recording and remixing function with a vocal mechanism.

Duet recording / playback and microphone mixing are possible.
It is compatible with 2 microphones. If you plug it into My Kujac and turn on the vocal switch, the sound of the microphone will be located in the center.
The volume of the microphone can be controlled by the vocal volume.

The mechanism is a belt drive system in which the capstan and reel stand are independently driven, and a DC servo motor with high rotational accuracy is used for the drive motor.

Equipped with an autoplay mechanism that automatically plays when the tape is rewound.

Uses a soft eject & easy take-out mechanism.

It has a review and queue mechanism.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism, it is possible to record absence and play alarm clock by the timer sold separately.

Equipped with 3-step switching mechanism independent of bias equalizer.
