  • AKAI 1710W

Commentary

A compact type tape recorder that uses a walnut finished cabinet.

It supports 3 speeds of 4.75cm/s, 9.5cm/s and 19cm/s.
In addition, 38cm/s can be handled by using an adapter.

Uses a shield head.

You can easily switch between stereo and monaural using the track selector.

A 2-lever system is used for the operation part.

Equipped with automatic shut-off mechanism.

Equipped with tone control function.

Equipped with a tape shifter.

It has a built-in speaker.
The unit contains two 12x18cm elliptical cone speakers.

Equipped with DIN jack and stereo headphone jack.

Equipped with a 3-digit counter with reset button.

Equipped with a VU meter.

It can be specified in both horizontal and vertical states.

Equipped with a built-in universal power supply voltage switching device, it can be freely changed between 100 VAC ~ 240 VAC.

The speaker system SW-50, which was sold separately, is designed for 1710W. When combined, you can enjoy more powerful playback than the built-in speakers.
