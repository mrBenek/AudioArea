  • VICTOR/JVC XL-Z521

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1988)

Commentary

A CD player with a full-time 18-bit D/A converter.

Full-time 18-bit L/R independent combination 4 dac is mounted on the D/A conversion part.
The XL-Z521 uses a discrete configuration for the lower two bits, which is difficult to achieve accuracy, and a combination of a 16-bit DAC and a 4 DAC configuration for current addition. It realizes high accuracy and high resolution by always operating the 18 bits at full capacity from Chikara Oire to large input.

The digital filter uses an 8 x oversampling digital filter.
By shifting up from 44.1 kHz to 352.8 kHz, which is 8 times higher, and removing unnecessary high frequency components, the load on the low-pass filter after D/A conversion is reduced, and phase characteristics, distortion factor, and SN ratio are improved.

The chassis structure uses a thick triple chassis.
Two bottom bases of different thickness are installed under the chassis base to effectively disperse the resonance mode of the body. In addition, the solid chassis structure realizes a total weight of 8.5 kg, which reduces resonance and eliminates unnecessary vibration.
In addition, a large insulated foot is directly attached to the 3.2 mm thick bottom base to reduce external vibration.

A newly developed stabilizing wheel is mounted on the rotating part to improve rotational stability by equalizing the moment of inertia of disks of different sizes.

The digital circuit, servo circuit and audio circuit are independent on each board, and the logic and analog sections of the audio circuit are electrically separated by an optical link method to eliminate the adverse effects of digital noise.

In order to eliminate the influence of digital noise generated from the FL display, a display ON/OFF switch which can turn off the FL display is mounted.

It is equipped with two digital outputs which are coaxial with the optical system.

Equipped with a Triple A dipping system.
Auto Edit Mode 1 automatically distributes songs that fit within a specified time to the A/B side of the tape according to the order of songs, and Program Edit Mode 2 allows you to program songs that fit within a specified time to the A/B side of the tape according to the order of songs.
In Mode 3 Multi Edit, you can program from any number of discs within the specified time, and even if you change discs, you can display the remaining time for each selected song.

Equipped with an electric volume that supports remote control.

Uses gold-plated output terminal.

It is equipped with a random program selection function of up to 32 songs.

Equipped with 99 track index search function.

It is equipped with an auto space function that allows 4 seconds between songs.

Equipped with 4 types of repeat function between all tracks / one track / program / A and B.

Equipped with a Compulink terminal for synchronized operation.

Wireless remote control is included.
