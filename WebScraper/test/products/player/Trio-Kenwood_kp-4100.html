  • TRIO KP-4100

  • ¥ 38,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

Full auto player.

It adopts a 2-motor system in which the turntable drive system and the arm operation system are separated, and reliability and stability are greatly improved.

Equipped with a muting switch, it can prevent shock noise when the needle tip is detached from the record board.
In addition, the lifting speed of the needle tip can be freely adjusted by the lever operation for the pose elevation.

Uses MM type cartridge V-39mkII.
