  • KENWOOD DM-1001

  • ¥ 90,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

K's series MD recorder.

Front loading mechanism is adopted for the mechanism.
In addition, high rigidity is achieved by 5 mm thick front panel and 1.2 mm steel plate base plate frame.

Equipped with optical digital input / output, digital recording by SCMS method is possible.

Program playback, repeat playback and timer rerecording are available.
In addition, you can insert up to 255 track numbers into recorded songs, and editing functions such as deleting songs in the middle and title keys for assigning titles to each recorded disc or song are enhanced.
