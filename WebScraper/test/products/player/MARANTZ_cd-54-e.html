  • Marantz CD-54

  • ¥ 115,000 (released in 1984)

Commentary

CD player equipped with 4 times over sampling digital filter LSI and swing arm pickup.

It is equipped with a random-access program of up to 24 songs and a delete program function to remove unnecessary songs by a microcomputer controlled control system.
