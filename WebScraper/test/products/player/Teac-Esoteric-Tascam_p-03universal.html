Commentary

Universal Transport, which is a SACD transport, P-03 with video circuitry added.

Starting with the VDDS-NEO, the mechanism system, chassis, exterior finishing, and power drive system are all designed following the P-03.

Both analog and digital systems are equipped with newly designed video output circuits.
ADV7324, a 14bit/216MHz video DAC manufactured by Analog Device Co., Ltd. is used for the analog system, and ABT1018, an LSI manufactured by Anchor Bay Technology Co., Ltd. is used for the digital system.
In order to demonstrate the performance of the newly designed video circuit board, the power transformer and power circuit board are installed exclusively for the video circuit.

The video output system is equipped with a component output terminal in addition to the HDMI terminal for 1080P.
An RS 232 c terminal is also installed as a trigger terminal for device control.

The scaling function supports 480i/576i, 480P/576P, 720P, 1080i, and 1080P.

The mechanism section is equipped with VRDS-NEO mechanism and LED that illuminates the tray top when the tray is opened.

It uses a double block in which the power supply and transport are separate blocks.
This eliminates internal and external vibrations that adversely affect sound quality.

The body structure uses thick aluminum material for the front, side plate, top plate and front corner. It is supported by 3 points with a 5 mm thick steel bottom chassis and Esoteric's unique hardened steel pinpoint foot.

In addition to the PCM signal up-conversion function of CD, it is equipped with DSD conversion function which converts to DSD output signal.

Equipped with a word sync function.

High-grade parts are used everywhere and the highest performance is sought for the internal wiring material.

Wireless remote control is included.
