  • ¥ 188,000 (around 2003)

Commentary

This is the second audio analog CD player.

The mechanism uses a highly reliable photo pick-up drive made by Philips, combined with a servo decoding circuit unique to audio analog.

The D/A converter incorporates a 24-bit real-time resampling circuit and a re-locking circuit to reduce jitter, which is a major factor in sound quality degradation, while expanding the range of the hemorrhoidal cavity mix.

The internal layout makes it less susceptible to external vibration by placing the CD mechanism in the center.
The 3-mm-thick steel sub-chassis unit enhances vibration isolation.

Wireless remote control is included.
