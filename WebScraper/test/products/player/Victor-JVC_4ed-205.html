Commentary

An 8-track deck capable of 4-channel playback and 2-channel recording / playback.

For 4-ch playback, you can adjust the level of each channel.

With a 10-second time counter, you can see the beginning of a tape and the remaining amount of tape.

The motor is a 2-6 pole hysteresis synchronous motor.

Equipped with a 3x-speed fast-forward mechanism, monitoring at -30dB is possible during fast-forward operation.

Equipped with auto-stop mechanism with 3 modes.

The head uses a 3-in-1 head of 0.8 mm permalloy 5-layer laminate.

Equipped with 4-2-ch automatic switching function.

It is equipped with a temporary stop device, microphone terminal and headphone terminal.
