Commentary

A stereo record player equipped with an auto-return mechanism.

The auto-turn mechanism automatically returns the tone arm when the performance is finished and turns off the motor power.

The tone arm is equipped with a sub-weight that reads the needle pressure directly, and an oil dump type arm elevation mechanism.

The MM type cartridge PL-N6 has been adopted.

There was a player unit PU-21 with only mechanism without cabinet.
