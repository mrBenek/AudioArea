  • AVI(AV International) S2000MCII

  • ¥ 160,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

A CD player, the successor to the S2000MC.

The mechanism section uses CDM9 manufactured by Philips.

The D/A converter uses a bit stream method, and the bit stream converter is combined with a proprietary high-precision clock circuit to reduce jitter.
We have also adopted a newly designed analog filter to prevent interference from high-frequency noise.

The remote controller was an option sold separately.
