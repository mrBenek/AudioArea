  • VICTOR/JVC XL-V31

  • ¥ 32,800 (around 1989)

Commentary

36 cm mini component size CD player.

The D/A converter is equipped with a dual D/A converter of independent L/R configuration to suppress crosstalk and improve localization, spaciousness and sense of presence.

Using a stabilizing wheel, the difference in moment of inertia due to the size of the disk is eliminated, and the change of servo current is suppressed.

It uses a large insulated foot.

Equipped with functions such as auto edit, program edit, multiedit, etc.

Equipped with a Compulink terminal, synchronized recording operation is possible when connected to a JVC synchronized cassette deck or mini component.

Equipped with 10-key terminal.

It is equipped with functions such as selecting up to 32 random program songs and all songs / one song / program repeat.

It has an FL display with a program chart of 20 songs.

Wireless remote control is included.
