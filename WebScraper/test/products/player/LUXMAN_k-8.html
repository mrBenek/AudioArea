Audio Database

  • LUXMAN K-8

  • ¥ 79,800 (released in March 1980)

Commentary

Metal tape compatible cassette deck that adopts logic control system.

The recording / reproducing head uses a Sendust head with a 3-layer laminate structure using silver winding.
The use of silver winding improves non-linear distortion characteristics in the middle and high range, especially at high levels.

A DC servo motor is used for the mechanism.

A DC amplifier is used for the recording amplifier.

Equipped with FL display real-time indicator.

Equipped with a 12 dot peak indicator.

There was a dedicated remote control unit sold separately.
