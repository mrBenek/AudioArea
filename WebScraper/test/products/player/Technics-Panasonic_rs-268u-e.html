Commentary

A stereo cassette deck with Dolby NR.

LH head is adopted for the head.

Equipped with a tape selector that also supports chrome tapes.

Equipped with an auto release mechanism.

The lamp lights when recording, Dolby, or Chrome is in operation.

Equipped with a dust cover that can be attached and removed with a 60 ° lock.
