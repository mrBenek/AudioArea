  • ¥ 89,800 (released on November 10, 1975)

Commentary

A cassette deck equipped with various mechanisms based on A-450.

The mechanism section combines the A-450 mechanism with a PLL servo DC motor to further improve driving performance.

Equipped with auto spacer, it is possible to record one after another while making a certain blank part on the tape.
When you finish recording a song and press the Pause button, Mushin-kai recording continues for approximately 3 seconds in the FIXED position (approximately 2 to 8 seconds in the Variable position), and the air is released in the pause state.

Equipped as standard with a Wiyard remote control unit (RC-50) that can be used for both auto spacer and recording / playback pause.

The pose method is used for the pose button, solving the stroke length which was the weak point of the mechanical pose.

Equipped with a timer mechanism that can record absence.

Equipped with a memory counter.

Equipped with a Dolby system that reduces tape hiss.

Equipped with MPX filter, unnecessary pilot signal can be cut during FM air check.

Equipped with a 3-step switching mechanism with independent bias and equalizer.
A dedicated position for Ferrichrome tape is added to this mechanism.

Equipped with VU meter and peak level indicator.

Equipped with a microphone and line mixing mechanism.
