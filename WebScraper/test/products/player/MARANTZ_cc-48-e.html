Audio Database

  • Marantz CC-48

  • ¥ 28,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

A 5-associative CD player that can play 5 CDs continuously / programmatically.

You can also exchange other discs while playing the CD.

It is equipped with editing functions such as an edit function to match the recording time of the tape, a fade in / out function, and a sync recording function.

Equipped with a peak search function that automatically monitors the REC level.

Wireless remote control is included.
