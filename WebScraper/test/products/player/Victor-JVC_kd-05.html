Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with center microphone mixing function.

It is equipped with a microphone mixing circuit using an emitter-follower circuit and is directly connected to an amplifier circuit using a low-noise IC. This makes it possible to perform microphone mixing with a stable center position. The left and right microphone circuits are independent, so the input level can be set individually.

A large flywheel with a diameter of 80 mm and a high-precision capstan shaft with a roundness of 0.1 μ are adopted in the mechanism part to eliminate minute uneven rotation.

It is equipped with a five point peak indicator to monitor the peak level of the input signal that cannot be followed by the VU meter.

Bias equalizer Equipped with independent 3-stage switching tape selector.

The cassette door uses a gear oil dump type cassette door.

By connecting an external timer, it adopts a standby mechanism which can record absence and play an alarm clock.
When the power is turned off after the recording is finished, the mechanism is released at the same time even in the middle of the tape so that the pinch roller does not hurt.

Uses an auto-stop mechanism with reed switches.

3-point support type cassette holder is adopted.
