  • SONY CDP-P79

  • ¥ 22,000 (released in 1989)

Commentary

A small CD player announced as a new 225 mm wide CD player for desktop use.

The digital filter is equipped with a 4 x oversampling digital filter.
The D/A conversion section is equipped with a dual D/A converter with independent left and right channels.

The S-Servo III is installed in the servo circuit to stabilize the power supply by reducing the disturbance of the servo current and to suppress the adverse effect on the audio.

It is equipped with a delete shuffle function corresponding to 99 songs for shuffle play or random play with only favorite songs left.

Equipped with a custom edit function with three functions : time edit, program edit, and time fade.
The Time Edit function uses the Time Set button to input the length of one side of the tape. First, the A side of the tape is programmed in order from the first track. Then, if you encounter a time-over song, the Time Edit function automatically selects the longest song within the time. Press the Edit button again to select the B side of the tape.
Program Edit allows you to freely edit up to 24 songs from the disc and program them in the order you like while checking the accumulated time. Then, you can finish the A side at the point where you pressed the pause button, and continue to create the B side as well.
Time Fade lets you enter the length of the tape and fade out at the specified time.

Equipped with manual faders, you can fade in and fade out wherever you want.

It is equipped with a 4-mode repeat function of one song, all songs, program and shuffle, and a music search function that enables fast forward / fast backward while listening to a song.

The display section has a 12-track music calendar.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
