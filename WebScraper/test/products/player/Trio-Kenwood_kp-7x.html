Commentary

A full auto player that can perform auto-play, Oatley turn, auto-cut and auto-repeat operations without touching the tone arm.

The motor employs a 10-pole, 6-coil uni-torque motor, which reduces the cogging phenomenon (uneven rotation) of direct drive motors and improves the S / N ratio in the sense of hearing.
The rotation control of the motor employs a quartz PLL system that compares the reference signal of the quartz oscillator with the speed detection signal of the motor and controls the phase. The new PLL IC realizes highly stable rotation.
In addition, the AC-DC coupling developed for the L-07D has been incorporated to improve the start-up torque of the turntable and improve the accuracy of steady rotation.

For the arm, the pipe itself is made highly rigid by high-rigid BS materials and ribs, and a straight arm is adopted in which the effective mass of the pickup system is reduced.
In addition, an improved one point support is adopted for the bearing part, taking into account the sensitivity of the one point support type arm.
In addition, the twist of the pipe, which was said to be a defect of the conventional one point support type, is suppressed by the pivot and the bearing made of SK material, and the level of fo (lowest resonance frequency) and near fo can be lowered, and various characteristics such as sensitivity and traceability necessary for the tone arm are improved.

A unique new ARB cabinet with polypropylene, calcium carbonate, etc. as the main component is used for the cabinet. It has excellent vibration isolation characteristics and does not transmit external vibration transmitted from the floor or wall to the tone arm or needle tip.

It uses a dial anti-skating mechanism with step-weight control to counter harmful inside forces (forces that attract the arm to the inside of the turntable).
At the same weight position as the needle pressure, unnecessary inside force is canceled, and faithful tracing is possible without applying unnecessary force to the needle tip.

It uses a MM cartridge equipped with an elliptical diamond needle of 0.3x0.7 mil.
