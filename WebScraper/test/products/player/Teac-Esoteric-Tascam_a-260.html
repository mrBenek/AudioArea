Commentary

Stereo cassette deck that can mix LINE + LINE in addition to MIC + LINE.

Equipped with a review mechanism, the counter and Memory switch allow memory auto-stop, recording auto-review, manual review and recording review.

Equipped with a peak level indicator by light emitting diode.

High hardness permalloy head is used for the head.

It has a built-in Dolby system.

Equipped with a tape selector.

It is equipped with a cue and review mechanism that are convenient for starting a song.

The auto end stop mechanism prevents excessive load on the mechanism.

Equipped with a wide scale level counter.

Equipped with a pause button.

Equipped with stereo headphone jack and DIN jack.
