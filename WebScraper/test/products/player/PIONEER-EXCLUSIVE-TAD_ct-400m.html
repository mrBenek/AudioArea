  • Pioneer CT-400M

  • ¥ 54,800 (released in 1979)

Commentary

A cassette deck corresponding to the metal tape compatible version of CT 400.

The head section features a Sendust recording / reproducing head and a double-gap ferrite erasing head with high erasing performance.

Equipped with a wide range level meter of -20dB to + 8 db.

Equipped with an automatic cue mechanism PMS (Pioneer Music Select System), you can quickly cue the music you want to listen to by detecting between songs on the tape.
In addition, it is equipped with REC mute which can create the interval between songs necessary for PMS detection.

The drive section uses a combination of a high-torque DC servo motor and twin belt system.

Equipped with 3-stage switching type tape selector of metal tape, STD/CrO2, Fe-Cr.

Built-in microphone and line mixing function.

It is equipped with a Dolby system using newly developed IC.

You can also record your absence.
