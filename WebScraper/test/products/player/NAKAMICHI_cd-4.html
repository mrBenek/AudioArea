Commentary

A CD-player of the Selected Component series developed as a Limited Edition limited to 300 units.

The D/A converter is equipped with a dual 18-bit linear glitch free D/A converter. By eliminating the generation of glitch, a kind of digital noise harmful to sound, from the root, resolution at a minute level is greatly enhanced.

A multi-regulated power supply and isolated ground system is adopted to prevent power supply interference and noise contamination to the signal system.

It is equipped with program memory function and remote play function.

Wireless remote control is included.
