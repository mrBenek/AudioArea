Commentary

A record player announced as a top player.

The drive system is equipped with MB 600, an 8-pole hysteresis synchronous motor, and drives a 31 cm aluminum alloy die-cast turntable by a belt drive system with double-side polished belts.

Equipped with a belt lock mechanism that prevents the belt from twisting and improves durability.

The tone arm is equipped with a high sensitivity type MA-101mkII.
The cartridge is protected by the delay motion and arm lifter, and the head shell can be corrected by the adjustment ring.

Equipped with a one touch operation lever for fine adjustment of arm height.

The cartridge is equipped with the VF type cartridge VF 3200.
Uses 25 micron ultra-lightweight cantilever.

Equipped with needle tip protector with magnifying lens.

Equipped with a guide lamp for needle tip illumination that can be operated even in dark places.

The cabinet is a 19mm-thick rosewood finished cabinet with rubber feet as a countermeasure against howling.

A low-capacity shielded cable is used to support 4-channel playback.
