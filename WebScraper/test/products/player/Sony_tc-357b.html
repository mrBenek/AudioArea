  • SONY TC-357B

  • ¥ 66,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

A monaural tape recorder that can also be used as a loudspeaker device.

It is a 2-track monaural machine with a new design utilizing the amplifier part of the long-selling TC 357 a.

You can switch between manual recording and automatic recording.

Equipped with a REC button that allows one touch PA (speaking loudly).

Uses escalation drive for easy tape removal.

Wooden cabinet is adopted.
