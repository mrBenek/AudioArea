Audio Database

  • DENON/COLUMBIA 7D70

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1968)

Commentary

A stereo tape deck with built-in all-transistor recording and playback amplifiers.

High-bias frequencies of 90 kHz enable FM stereo broadcast transmission recording.

Automatic tape stopping device and temporary stopping device are installed.

You can mix left and right recordings separately.

Equipped with DIN connector and level meter.

It supports 3 speeds.
