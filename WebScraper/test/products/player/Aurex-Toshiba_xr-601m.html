  • ¥ 69,000 (around 1988)

Commentary

A CD player equipped with an autochanger.

You can enjoy a total of 7 disks, 6 disks in the magazine and plus 1 tray without replacement.

It is equipped with a 10-key direct music selection function. You can select a song by pressing the No key of the song you want to listen to. You can also select a song by pressing the disc key and the 10 key when more than two discs are loaded.

Equipped with a 2 x oversampling digital filter as a digital filter.
As a result, it is possible to combine an analog filter with a good phase characteristic and a low distortion factor by allowing a margin in the frequency characteristics between the audio component and the unwanted component, resulting in sharper sound.

A newly developed disk stabilizer is used to suppress the natural vibration of the disk and improve the reading accuracy of the pickup.

Equipped with a large insulator.

It is equipped with functions such as 32-track random memory, cumulative memory that displays the total time of stored songs, introscan and random play.

Equipped with a 9-digit FL concentrated display, you can easily see the total time and リメインタイム of all songs, lap time of performance songs, accumulated memory total time, etc.
