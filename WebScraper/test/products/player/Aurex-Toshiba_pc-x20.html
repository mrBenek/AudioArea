  • Aurex PC-X20

  • ¥ 52,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A cassette deck that supports metal tapes.

It is equipped with a bias / equalizer selection switch and is compatible with metal tape.

An AS (All Sendust) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.

It is equipped with a digital meter using LED.
This meter is a peak type with a fast response speed, and can be switched between bar and dot display. When the dot type is -10dB or less, the bar graph is displayed. When the level is -10dB or more, the dot moves. The level can be set with the feeling of a needle meter.

Equipped with auto-repeat mechanism.
If you press the play lever and the REW lever at the same time, the tape repeats from the beginning to the end.

Uses a soft eject & easy take-out mechanism.

It has a review and queue mechanism.

Equipped with an output volume.

Timer standby mechanism is adopted, and by connecting a timer sold separately, it is possible to record absence.

Equipped with a Dolby NR system as a noise reduction system.
