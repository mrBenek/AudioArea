  • VICTOR/JVC TD-W60

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

A double reverse cassette deck with various functions.

A silent mechanism is adopted for both the A mechanism of the reproduction Oatley verse and the B mechanism of the recording and reproduction Oatley verse.
In addition, a half-shell stabilizer is provided to suppress vibration of the cassette.

Uses high hardness Meta Palm head for the head.

Equipped with a microphone mixing function, you can record mixed sound with line input, record mixed sound with A-mecha playback sound and sound mixed with microphone, and record mixed sound with B-mecha by mixing A-mecha playback sound with microphone input.

The A mechanism has a pitch control of about 10%.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.
In addition, it is equipped with a Computer Calibration that adjusts the recording bias and recording sensitivity level for each tape. In addition, once set, settings can be stored for each type of tape (normal / high position / metal).

It is equipped with a Compulink terminal capable of recording synchronized with Victor's CD player.
When connected to the JVC XL-V313 CD player, the DDRP (digitalized Direct Recording Processor) automatically adjusts the recording level to match the peak level of CDs.

Equipped with timer switch for timer recording / playback.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with Dolby HX-PRO.

It is equipped with synchronized dubbing, high-speed dubbing and multi-music scan up to 99 songs.

Equipped with auto REC mute.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Equipped with a large FL meter and double electronic counter.

It comes with a wireless remote controller equipped with operation keys for A mechanism and B mechanism.
