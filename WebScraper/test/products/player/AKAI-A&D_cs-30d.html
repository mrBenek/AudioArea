Commentary

A cassette deck with a direct function change mechanism.

A hard permalloy head is used for the read / write head.

The drive motor of the mechanism section is equipped with an electronic governor motor.
Equipped with a tape changeover switch.

Equipped with an automatic stop mechanism.
