  • AIWA AD-F70M

  • ¥ 105,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

A metal-compatible cassette deck that pursues perfection in basic performance and operability.

In addition to the 3-step tape selector with independent bias and equalizer, you can fine-tune the bias of each of LH, FeCr and CrO2 tapes.

Uses Sendust head for the head.

The linearity of the recording amplifier is 20 dB (0 VU) and is compatible with metal tapes.

A dual motor drive system is used for the mechanism.

In addition to the VU meter, it is equipped with a 9-point multi-peak indicator.

Equipped with auto repeat and memory repeat mechanism.

It has a queue review function.

Equipped with a tape level meter.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with a pure electronic full autostop mechanism.

Equipped with a Dolby system.

Equipped with a synchro operation mechanism.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism, and by connecting an external timer, absence recording and alarm clock playback are possible.

There was a remote control unit and a handle for rack mounting sold separately.
