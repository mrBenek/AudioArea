  • VICTOR/JVC KD-65SA

  • ¥ 75,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

X-cutSA head is adopted.

Uses an X-cut SA (Senalloy) head for recording and re-recording.
In the X-cut SA head, the Sendust tip of the tape sliding surface is processed into a special shape and bonded to the permalloy laminated core at high temperature, so that the waviness (contour) of the low-frequency characteristics is greatly improved without changing the high frequency characteristics.

Double-gap ferrite head is used for the erasing head.

Equipped with a spectropeak indicator, it is possible to record a wide dynamic range with less distortion over the entire frequency band.

The 5-step recording equalizer allows you to adjust the high-frequency characteristics.

It is equipped with ANRS and Super ANRS which are original noise reduction systems.
The Super ANRS is an addition of a function to raise the high-frequency clipping level at high-level to the ANRS, which has a high-frequency noise reduction function at low-level, and expands the recording / reproducing dynamic range.

Equipped with input 3-stage switching of Mic, DIN, Line.

A gear oil dump cassette door is adopted.

There was a BTS rack handle sold separately.
