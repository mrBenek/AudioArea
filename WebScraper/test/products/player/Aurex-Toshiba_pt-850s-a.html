  • TOSHIBA PT-850S-A

  • ¥ 37,000 (around 1960 s)

Commentary

4-track 2-channel tape deck.

There are 2 tracks for one way, and it is a 4-track system that can record both ways.
In addition to stereo recording / playback, 4-track monaural recording / playback is possible by switching the recording volume.

The amplifier section contains a 10-chip recording / playback amplifier including two low-noise transistors.

Equipped with an auto shut-off mechanism, the power is automatically turned off when the tape finishes winding and the tape stops.

Equipped with a level meter.

A 4-pole condenser motor is used as the drive motor.

It is equipped with a compensation circuit for speed switching. The compensation circuit linked to the tape speed switching shaft operates at both 19cm/s and 9.5cm/s.

Can be used in both vertical and horizontal positions.

Equipped with a DIN jack.
