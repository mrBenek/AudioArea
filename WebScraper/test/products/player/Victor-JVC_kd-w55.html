  • VICTOR/JVC KD-W55

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

Double cassette deck with 10 songs 16 memory program selection editing function.

There are 2 color variations, silver and black.

The A mechanism is equipped with a random access program that can play up to 10 songs 16 times, so you can easily select songs and edit dubbing to the B Mechanism.

Equipped with Dolby B NR as a noise reduction system.

It is equipped with a program index scan that automatically plays the head of a programmed song continuously for 10 seconds, and multi-song jump selection.

Equipped with a computer shift control function that changes the function of mechanical operation with one shift button.
If you press Play while holding down the Shift button, you can use the Blank Scan function. If there is a blank for more than 10 seconds during playback, this function will fast-forward with the FF scan, stop at the beginning of the next song, and start playback.
Press the Shift button while pressing the pause (B-mechanism) to perform a blank search. If you find a 14-second blank in FF mode (equivalent to 2-6 minutes of playback), the program returns to the end of the previous song and stops after making a 4-second blank in regenerating condition.
If you press REW while holding down the shift button, the rewind / autoplay function is activated, and playback starts from the beginning of the tape after rewinding.

Uses 2-motor full logic mechanism.

Equipped with an auto tape select function.

It is equipped with a continuous play function. When you play with a tape inserted in both A and B mechanisms, both A and B mechanisms play back alternately, enabling long-time continuous playback.

Equipped with auto / synchro rec mute function.

The A mechanism has a ± 10% pitch control function.

Equipped with a microphone mixing function.

Equipped with an independent microphone volume.

At maximum volume during dubbing, equal-level recording is possible.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism.

Equipped with a music scan function for 10 songs before and after.

Equipped with a joy-full menu function, you can play music roulette and introquiz.
