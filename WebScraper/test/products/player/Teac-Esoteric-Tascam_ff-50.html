  • TEAC ff-50

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A 2-motor cassette deck equipped with a teac-style operation mechanism.

Micro-touch is used for the operation system.

The mechanism section is equipped with dedicated motors for constant speed running and quick winding.
This allows fast forward and rewind and improves durability.

Equipped with a peak level meter.

Equipped with a REC Mute mechanism for one touch, zero signal recording.

Equipped with a timer mechanism that can record absence.

Equipped with a switching mechanism in which bias and equalizer are independent.

Equipped with mono / stereo switching mechanism linked to My Kujac.

Equipped with a memory counter.

Microphone / line input switching is adopted to improve the dynamic range of the line input.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

HD ferrite head is used for the head part.

A gear dump type is adopted for the ejection mechanism to realize soft opening and closing.

Equipped with a removable cassette holder cover.

It can be used from a remote location by connecting a separately sold remote control unit.
