  • Pioneer PL-A20

  • ¥ 26,500 (around 1972)

Commentary

A stereo record player equipped with a full auto mechanism.

By operating the lever, selection of record size, cut from start, and repeat can all be operated automatically.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used to drive the turntable and a dedicated gear motor is used to operate the arm to ensure smooth operation.

A static balance type pipe arm is used for the tone arm.

The lightweight head shell is equipped with a MM type cartridge as standard. By adopting an international standard connector, the cartridge can be freely replaced.

Equipped with a free stop hinge that can stop the dust cover at any position.
