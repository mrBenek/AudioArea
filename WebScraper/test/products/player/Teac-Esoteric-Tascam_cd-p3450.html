  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1995)

Commentary

CD player with 1-bit DAC and center mount system.

Uses a center mount system in which the mechanism is located in the center of the body.
The center arrangement keeps the overall balance and suppresses the vibration transmitted from the legs and other parts that is harmful to sound quality.

The D/A converter is equipped with a stable, high-resolution 1-bit D/A converter and an 8 x oversampling digital filter to achieve high-precision digital / analog conversion.

It is equipped with functions such as 32-track random memory program, shuffle play, music search / skip and 2-mode repeat play.

Equipped with an optical digital output.

Equipped with 4-mode time counter.

Wireless remote control is included.
