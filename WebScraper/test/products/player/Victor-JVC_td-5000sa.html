Commentary

Stereo tape deck with SA head.

SA (Sen-alloy) heads are used for recording and playback heads.
This head is an extension of the SF head supplied for commercial use. By depositing a high-hardness Sendust alloy on the tape sliding surface of the permalloy core, it achieves both magnetic characteristics and abrasion resistance, which have been considered extremely difficult until now.

A high linearity design has been adopted in which all circuits have sufficient allowance for the dynamic range of the tape, including a microphone amplifier circuit that can withstand a large input of + 55 dB of the reference input.
The 38cm/sec recording equalizer uses an IEC curve with an excellent signal-to-noise ratio, which is widely used in European commercial equipment. The playback equalizer is a two step switching type between IEC and NAB.

It is equipped with an automatic tension control system to improve the stability of tape running by keeping the back tension constant.
In this circuit, the rotational speed of the supply reel is counted by a photoelectric pulse detection mechanism, and the drive current of the motor is controlled according to the remaining amount of the tape. As a result, the tension, which normally fluctuates nearly three times in the case of No. 7 reel, is almost equalized, and tape damage caused by excessive tension is prevented.

In addition to a purely electronic IC logic control circuit, it also incorporates a motion sensor circuit that allows for an immediate transition from FF/REW to PLAY. By using the pulse detector of the automatic tension control, the circuit operates as soon as the supply reel stops, allowing for a transition to PLAY without a time lag.

A one way dump tension arm is used for the supply side, and a double action tension arm is used for the take-up side. Both tension arms have locks for easy tape mounting and editing.

Upper and lower double support capstan is adopted to improve stability.

It is equipped with a normal / variable switching type bias adjustment system with a built-in reference oscillator.

Mic/Line addition type OP amplifier / mixer circuit without level interference is adopted.

Equipped with a VU meter with a wide-scale mirror of -30dB to + 10 db.

Equipped with a new No. 10 reel adapter that does not require height correction.
