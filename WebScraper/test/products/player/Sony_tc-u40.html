Commentary

Metal tape compatible cassette deck with an emphasis on basic performance.

Uses Sendust head for the head.
A magne-Focus 4-Gap erasing head is used for the erasing head.

We have adopted a green motor.

Equipped with peak program meter of single ch16 segment.

Uses Dolby NR system.

Equipped with auto-play function.

Equipped with a timer standby switch.

Equipped with 4-stage tape selector for simultaneous switching of bias and equalizer.

It uses a soft eject mechanism.

Equipped with a Line/Mic input selector.

Uses an auto shut-off mechanism.
