  • ¥ 400,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

CD transport using a mechanism for high-precision CD-ROM drives.

A 3.2 mm thin tray is used for the tray part.

Jitter is eliminated as much as possible by combining a FIFO buffer memory with a high-precision crystal oscillator.

It can be operated with other proceed products.

It is equipped with a wide range of digital output terminals including XLR type.

A dedicated remote controller is included.
