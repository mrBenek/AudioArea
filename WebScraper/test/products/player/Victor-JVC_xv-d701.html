Commentary

DVD player equipped with a Dolby digital decoder.

The video circuit is equipped with a VFP (video fine processor).
This circuit uses a newly developed wide-band digital filter and four independent 10-bit / 27-MHz video DACs to realize high-definition video with more than 500 horizontal resolutions. In addition, an independent and multiple analog filter with excellent group delay characteristics eliminates shooting and ringing, which degrade images, and realizes accurate color reproduction up to the edge.

Equipped with a Dolby Digital (AC-3) decoder as an audio decoder.
This allows for Dolby Digital 5.1 channel surround sound.

The audio circuit is equipped with a K2 interface and a 96 kHz / 24-bit PEM-DD converter with a dynamic range of over 108 dB. The audio transformer, power supply circuit and fine clock generator are also used for high quality sound reproduction.

Component (Color Difference) Equipped with a video output terminal and a digital output terminal that supports DTS, the next-generation digital audio format.
You can play a DTS-compatible disc by connecting a DTS-compatible decoder or amplifier.

Equipped with a graphical user navigator.
You can easily set various functions while watching the graphic display, and it is equipped with flash playback of up to 9 still images and a digest function to search for the scene you want to see.
Wireless remote control is included.
