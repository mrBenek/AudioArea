Commentary

A full-auto player released by BSR as the only player that is not an auto changer.

Equipped with a full-auto mechanism, you can perform auto-start, Oatley turn, auto-stop, start of music with oil dump queuing, etc. with one touch lever operation. The power is automatically turned off when the performance ends and the arm is automatically locked.
In addition, a jam-proof design has been adopted to prevent failures due to operational errors, thereby suppressing internal failures.

An idler drive is used as the drive system.
Our 4-pole synchronous motor is used as the drive motor, and the turntable is rotated by a highly reliable idler also manufactured by BSR.

A die-cast turntable is used for the turntable.

The tone arm is a highly sensitive square arm with tapered bearings, which is resistant to torsion and has low resonance frequencies, enabling clear sound and Yutaka Fukuda bass reproduction.
In addition, by adopting a sliding head shell, it is possible to fix the cartridge exactly horizontally while suppressing contact failure and rattling.

Equipped with double scale anti-skating mechanism.
In this mechanism, the canceling amount of the inside force can be adjusted with one touch according to both types of conical needle (round needle) and elliptic needle.

It uses M75-6S, a MM type cartridge made of Shure.

Cabinets specially designed and managed by BSR are manufactured and used in Japan.
Rosewood finished cabinet with howling protection, acrylic dust cover is thick enough to suppress resonance, and free stop hinge makes it easy to put on and take off.
A space is provided on the arm side of the cabinet as a hand protector, and it can be adjusted accurately with your hand when changing the needle or fine-adjusting the arm.
