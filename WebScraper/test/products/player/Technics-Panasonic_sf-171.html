Commentary

A record player with a static balanced tone arm.

The turntable uses a rim drive system using a 4-pole hysteresis synchronous motor.

The tone arm uses a pipe arm equipped with a lateral balancer and an inside for scan seller.
The lateral balancer balances the weight of the left and right sides so that no unnecessary force is generated between the needle tip and the record. In addition, the lateral pressure applied to the record is reduced by the inside for scan seller.
In addition, it is equipped with an oil dump mechanism to enable stable pickup operation.

The head shell has adopted a universal type corresponding to international standards, and various cartridges can be used.
