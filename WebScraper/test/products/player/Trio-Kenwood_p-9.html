Commentary

In addition to the full auto mechanism, this linear tracking full auto player is equipped with a repeat function and a tone arm free selection function.

In addition to the conventional full repeat operation, a variety of repeat playback is possible, such as repeat from desired song to end, repeat one song, repeat two or three songs from any song to any song, repeat from any phrase to any phrase in a song, etc.
For full repeat or repeat from desired song to end, press the repeat switch in the lead groove or the groove at the head of the song you want to listen to. For repeat one song, repeat two or three songs in a row, or repeat from phrase to phrase in a song, press the repeat switch at the start and end of repeat.
The Repeat indicator lights up during any repeat operation, and you can cancel the repeat operation with the Play/Cut switch.
After setting the repeat start point, you can also set the repeat end point by moving the arm with the free selection mechanism.

It has a depth of 187 mm and uses a front loading type that does not have difficulty in placing it.
This front loading type has the advantage that the vertical floor vibration, which causes howling, is not easily transmitted to the needle tip.

By connecting to the X-9 cassette deck of the trio, it is possible to operate the recording mechanism of the deck in synchronization with the playback start of the player.
When you press the Synchro button to lead in the arm, the cassette deck enters the recording state and the tape starts as soon as the needle touches the record needle.
Also, if you press the Synchro button during playback, as soon as the needle tip leaves the record, the deck stops recording and enters a Rec / Pause state.

The arm uses a linear tracking arm that moves in the same way as the cutting machine.
This reduces the tracking error angle and enables accurate pickup.
In addition, a unique tracking servo mechanism using a DC motor and wire drive achieves quiet arm travel.

When automatically playing transparent or color records, use the supplied malfunction prevention sheet to prevent malfunction of the Auto Size Selector or Auto Speed Selector.
