  • VICTOR/JVC TD-664

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

In addition to the 3-head system, this tape deck is equipped with high quality design and abundant auxiliary circuits.

A 4-8 pole 2-speed condenser phase advance type induction motor is used for the drive system.

Equipped with auto-stop and auto-start mechanisms.

The fast-forward and rewind speed can be changed in two stages.

Uses an EL drive system for easy taping.

It is equipped with a tape recorder to demonstrate the characteristics of SH tape.

3-head system allows simultaneous recording and monitoring.
In addition, it has a switch for switching between the source monitor and the monitor.

Echo recording, sound-on-sound recording, and microphone mixing recording are available.
