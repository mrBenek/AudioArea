Commentary

A record player equipped with an auto mechanism.

Equipped with an auto mechanism, Oatley turn and auto cut are possible.
The arm automatically returns to its original position after the record is played, or if you want to stop playing in the middle, the arm returns to its original position by auto-cutting.

A belt drive system using a 4-pole AC servo motor is used for the drive part.
A strobe light is attached to the outer circumference of the turntable and a neon lamp is built-in, so that the rotation speed can be adjusted correctly.

TH system universal arm with good tracing ability is adopted for the arm.

A MM type cartridge is used.

A low-capacity cord for CD-4 is used for the output cord of the arm, and it is compatible with 4 ch just by changing the needle tip for CD-4.

Free hinge type acrylic case is included.
