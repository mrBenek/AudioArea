Commentary

A record player with a belt drive system.

Ultra-precision processing of stainless steel with low friction coefficient is used for the Center shaft and shaft receiver used for the drive part.
The shaft has less contact area, less friction, 4 mm thick polyurethane embedded in the bottom of the bearing to absorb vibration, and is completely sealed and free from foreign matter.
The surface of the shaft is provided with a spiral groove in the order of microns, and molybdenum disulfide, which is used as a lubricant, is designed to travel through the groove and always reach the surface of the shaft evenly.

A 4-pole hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the drive part.

A large 60 mm diameter idler that has been processed to be soft within the range of rubber hardness that does not deform is used to absorb the vibration from the motor. In addition, by increasing the diameter of the idler, the circuit speed conversion ratio becomes smaller and the occurrence of wow becomes less.

The turntable is made of aluminum alloy die-cast 32 cm in diameter and 3.0 kg in weight. The weight balance is finely adjusted and stable rotation is possible.
Also, the outside of the turntable is slightly bent for the use of 30 cmLP records.

The tone arm uses a static balance type.

A MM type cartridge is used.

Developed and adopted special oil for idler, and clay does not change even if temperature changes.

It is designed for easy refueling.

The mechanism is connected with lever operation.

The pressed part inside has been made thicker and stronger than before.
