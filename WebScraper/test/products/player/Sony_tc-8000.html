Audio Database

  • SONY TC-8000

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1971)

Commentary

An 8-track deck capable of recording and playing 8-track tapes.

Equipped with an auto shut-off mechanism in which the cartridge pops out at the end of recording and playback and the power is turned off after that.

Equipped with an auto-eject device that automatically stops when the tape ends and ejects the cartridge.

Equipped with VU meter, fast-forward button and headphone port.
