Commentary

"Full-scale high fidelity utilizing a wide range of tracks and sufficient tape speed" is a favorite of open-reel, and master tape deck which demonstrates its advantages based on advantageous conditions of open-reel.

The mechanism is an orthodox 3-motor system, and the capstan motor adopts a hysteresis synchronous type with 4-pole / 8-pole switching.
Two tape speeds, 38cm/s and 19cm/s, can be selected by changing the number of poles of the motor.
By using large flywheels and a wide and high precision flat belt, excellent wow and flutter characteristics are achieved.
The reel motor uses a 6-pole eddy current pole eddy current type motor with excellent drooping characteristics, and the quick winding speed is appropriately adjusted so as not to damage the tape. The tape tension can be switched according to the reel size.

The control buttons are completely independent of each other, and you can play directly from any button without stopping. When you press the control button, the indicator lamp lights up.
Remote control is also possible, and the pose button can be locked.

It has a four head structure consisting of a 2-track 2-ch erasing / recording / reproducing head and a 4-track 2-ch reproducing head.
The erasing head is a sintered ferrite type, and all others use HPF head.

The HPF head has excellent frequency characteristics due to its high resistivity and low overcurrent, as well as high efficiency in the high frequency range and high frequency compensation for recording.
In addition, the hardness is about five times that of permalloy, and since it is molded with a single core, the gap accuracy is high, and in-line performance is excellent, and phase characteristics are also improved.
In addition, the contour effect and head touch are improved by adopting the hyperbolic type, and the surface is mirror finished.
The life estimated from the abrasion test is approximately 200,000 hours and has excellent durability.

The head part is a block including the tape guide, and it is mounted on a die-cast head mount with consideration of vibration absorption by plug-in method.

The electrical circuit section has a structure using silicon transistors with 15 stones per ch. A 3-stage direct-coupled circuit is adopted, with 1-3 stages for reproduction and 2-4 stages for recording.

Mixing recording is also possible because it uses an input level controller independent of the line and microphone.
The microphone can be either a high-impedance type or a low-impedance type depending on the switch.

The clamper for Reel 10 is a push-lock type that can be clamped just by pressing it.

Equipped with an EDIT button. If you press this button at the time of stop, the shifter moves backward and it is possible to start the head manually.

During playback, you can switch from playback to recording without stopping the tape by pressing the REC and FWD buttons simultaneously.

The LH tape is switched using the orthodox method of changing the bias current.

Large VU meter is used.

It is equipped with an output level adjuster that can also adjust the volume of headphones.

Power frequency can be changed with one touch operation.
