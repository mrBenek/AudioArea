  • Marantz SD-62

  • ¥ 45,000 (released in 1991)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with combination 3 heads.

Stable running performance is realized by high rigidity die cast head base, large precision flywheel and cassette half stabilizer.

Equipped with digital peak level display function to digitally display peak value.

Equipped with Dolby HX POR.

It is equipped with performance functions such as QMS and AMS.

Wireless remote control is included.
