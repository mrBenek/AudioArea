  • Pioneer PL-12

  • ¥ 21,500 (around 1972)

Commentary

A record player with low cost but simple and excellent characteristics.

A belt drive system is adopted, and a 4-pole synchronous motor is adopted for the motor.
In addition, the belt uses a polyurethane belt with both sides polished to micron precision.

The tone arm uses a static balanced pipe arm, and the head shell is a standard plug-in type to facilitate cartridge replacement.

Equipped with an oil dump type arm lifter and a counter weight with a needle pressure direct reading scale.

The cartridge is equipped with PC-11 which is a MM type cartridge.
