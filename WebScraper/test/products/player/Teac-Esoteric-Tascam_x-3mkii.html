  • ¥ 105,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

Tape deck that also supports EE tapes.

The capstan uses an electronically controlled DC servo motor.

The recording and reproducing head uses a high-hardness permalloy head with excellent wear resistance.

Uses one touch reel clamper.

Microphone and line mixing is possible.

Equipped with a large VU meter.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.
