Audio Database

  • TOSHIBA PT-820S

  • ¥ 46,000 (around 1968)

Commentary

A tape deck that can play four track stereo tapes.

It is an all-transistor design that operates as soon as the switch is turned on. The amplifier section uses NAB standards and consists of nine transistors.

It supports 3 speeds.

It is possible to record and play back 6 hours round trip.
Also, you can close the wooden lid with the No. 7 tape on.

Can be used both vertically and horizontally.

Wooden cabinet is adopted.

Each channel can be recorded and played back separately.

Equipped with a tape counter.

Equipped with DIN connector.

Number 7 trial listening tape is included.
