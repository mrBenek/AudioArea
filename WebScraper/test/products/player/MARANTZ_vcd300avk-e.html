Audio Database

  • Marantz VCD300AVK

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1997)

Commentary

A Video CD player that supports Video CD version 2.0.

It has a karaoke function which can adjust echo level and key control.

On-screen display capability.

Equipped with an S-video output and two video outputs.

NTSC/PAL switching is possible.

Wireless remote control is included.
