Commentary

A CD player with the same technology as high-end models.

It is equipped with a digital filter that was previously used only in high-end machines.
We have also achieved high performance, high reliability and simplicity by integrating it into an LSI together with a digital signal processing circuit.

An IS mechanism is adopted for the mechanism part. In addition to strengthening the chassis with a strong mechanical base, a large insulator is used to float the rotating system and pickup part from the body.

A new Y servo system composed of two types of servo circuits is used for the servo circuit.
A high-traceability servo circuit with frequency characteristics, phase characteristics, and gain that make full use of the high-sensitivity pickup always provides the best operating conditions. A second high-balance servo circuit prevents trace errors caused by disc scratches and dirt. This circuit uses the time difference between the leading and trailing beams of the three spot beams. It delays the flaw waveform detected by the leading beam and cancels it by overlapping it with the trailing beam.

The disk tray uses a disk center hold system, and only the center of the disk is supported by a spring mechanism to prevent damage to the disk by touching the signal recording surface.

The power supply circuit controls beat disturbance and interference distortion by separating analog system and digital / servo system independently.

It is equipped with a 6-digit 3-mode FL display.

It is equipped with a random program function of all 15 songs.

Equipped with 2 speed search function which automatically speeds up when the button is pressed and held.

It has a headphone port.
