  • YAMAHA KX-493

  • ¥ 38,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with play trim function.

The recording / reproducing head is equipped with a hard permalloy recording / reproducing head.

The dolby B/C noise reduction system is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with Dolby HX Pro.

Equipped with an auto tape tuning function, you can automatically set the recording bias and sensitivity optimum for the tape to be used.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Equipped with peak level meter with peak hold function.

Equipped with auto rec mute function and rec return function.

It is equipped with a play trim control function which plays back the high sound range clearly.

It is equipped with functions such as front-and-back one track cue function, intro-scan function, memory function and repeat function.

Equipped with a wireless remote control.
