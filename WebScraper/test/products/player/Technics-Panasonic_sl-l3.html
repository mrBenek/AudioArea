Commentary

Quartz D. D. full auto player with 8-track program mechanism.

Equipped with an 8-track program performance function.
In addition, with an electronically controlled full auto mechanism, the performance starts with a single start button.

Equipped with a skip function, it is possible to find the beginning of the previous or next song.

Linear tracking arm is used for the arm.

Quartz D. D. turntable is used for the turntable part.

Equipped with a plug-in connector type MM cartridge.
