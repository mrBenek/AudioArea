Commentary

A high-cost performance model with low distortion and high S / N ratio achieved by unique super-clean digital filter and thorough vibration isolation design.

Equipped with a super clean digital filter to remove noise components.
This is composed of an audio circuit combined with a 96th order digital filter and an analog low-pass filter with high calculation accuracy. This filter removes unnecessary noise components without damaging audio characteristics and improves sound quality.

In order to prevent adverse effects caused by vibration from the floor, four insulators block the vibration transmitted to the body.

It is equipped with a preset editing function which is convenient for editing a CD according to the specified time.
If you store the recording time of your tape in memory, you can automatically calculate the remaining time of the tape and display it on the display when you press the TIME MODE key during the program.

It is equipped with a unique high-speed linear access system that employs a high-precision linear motor in the drive part of the traverse mechanism that moves the pickup.
The newly developed microcomputer servo system achieves an average access time of 1.0 seconds.
In addition, there is no reduction mechanism such as gear belt, and quietness and durability are also improved.

The display section is equipped with an easy-to-see large FL display.
In addition, the track number of songs recorded or programmed on the disc is displayed at one time up to the 20th song, so that you can see the progress of the performance at a glance.

Equipped with a remote control, you can perform basic operations such as play, stop, and pose, as well as direct music selection and 20-piece random program with 10 keys at hand.
It is also equipped with a digital Attenuator function that can adjust the volume in increments of 2 dB from 0 dB to -12dB by operating the level key.

Equipped with high-precision 1-beam type laser pickup.

Equipped with 2 speed manual search and headphone volume.
