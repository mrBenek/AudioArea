Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with Senalloy head.

An SA (Sen-alloy) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
Senalloy is a permalloy core with a high-hardness Sendust alloy bonded to the front surface of the permalloy core. It has abrasion resistance equivalent to ferrite, and has excellent magnetic properties of permalloy with less risk of cracks and chips.

Equipped with an ANRS automatic noise reduction device integrated into an IC, various characteristics are improved by all-stage direct connection circuit and distortion correction circuit, and reliability is improved by using a non-contact electronic switch inside the recording / re-switching IC.

The amplifier circuit is designed with an emphasis on linearity in all stages, including the 3-stage amplified CR type equalizer. The line amplifier uses an IC circuit that can withstand a large input of + 18 dB above the reference level.
In addition, the amplifier circuit on a unit board has many variable points and is carefully adjusted one by one. The use of wires for communication between boards is avoided as much as possible, and printed resistance is used for the line board to reduce the number of soldering points. Reliability and uniform quality are emphasized.

The line input can be adjusted by an independent volume without passing through a microphone amplifier.

The VU meter is driven by an IC circuit with low output impedance, and by using a meter with good accuracy and damping characteristics, the indication at 20 kHz is within -2dB.
Also, by switching the switch, it can be operated as a peak check type which can follow the pulse input.

Equipped with a tape selector that can switch the bias and equalizer independently.
The chrome-biased position has a recording sensitivity compensation circuit and is designed for use with Ferrichrome tapes. The chrome-equalizer has a time constant of 3180 μ S-70 μ S to improve linearity not only in the high-frequency range but also in the low-frequency range.

The mechanism part is a slant mechanism that is tilted 30 degrees to make the tape easier to see, and a DC servo motor with a built-in frequency generator is used for the motor.
In addition, the combination of a capstan, a large flywheel, and a neoprene rubber belt made by Schmerg of Germany, which boasts a high roundness of 0.1 micron, realizes low wow, flutter and stable driving.

Equipped with photoelectric full auto-stop mechanism using CDs photocell.

The cassette door adopts an air dump mechanism to realize soft opening and closing.

Equipped with a timer recording mechanism combining a pause mechanism and electromagnetic solenoid.

SK steel is used for the mechanism operation lever.

The power supply circuit is stabilized by using a stabilized power source, and the transformer uses an Orient Core type.

Equipped with a pure electronic tape run indicator that does not prevent tape running.

Equipped with a memory counter.

It has a read-only volume.

Equipped with AC outlet.

There was a special wood case sold separately.
