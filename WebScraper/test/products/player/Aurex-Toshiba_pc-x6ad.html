  • Aurex PC-X6AD

  • ¥ 118,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

A cassette deck corresponding to the address.

It is equipped with an address originally developed by Orex as a noise reduction system.
By compressing and expanding the signal, the hissing is reduced to an inaudible level. Moreover, by greatly expanding the dynamic range, excellent playback sound is realized.

An AS (All Sendust) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
A ferrite head is used for the erasing head.

Soft touch operation mechanism is adopted in the operation part.
