Commentary

DAT deck with AA converter.

It is equipped with newly developed AA (analog-accurate) converter.
This circuit is an A/D conversion application of the D/A conversion technology of 1-bit pulse-width modulation. It also performs fourth order noise shaping to increase the linearity at a minute level, and realizes excellent S/N and distortion factor.

A 1-bit dual D/A converter is used for the D/A converter.
In this circuit, the digital signal is first upsampled by a factor of 256 through an oversampling digital filter, then passed through a noise shaper to greatly shift the requantization noise, which adversely affects the audio band, out of the audible band. The resulting digital code is then converted an analog quantity by pulse density modulation.
Since this method uses 1-bit data, there is no need for precision in the amplitude direction like the integration method. Since this method can be controlled only by a precise quartz clock in the time axis direction, linearity problems caused by amplitude errors and 0 cross distortion do not occur in principle, enabling D/A conversion with excellent resolution of minute signals.

The drive train uses a fine tracking mechanism to ensure the highest possible accuracy and stability. In addition, Victor's unique double-scan method allows the recorded signal to be read twice during playback to minimize errors.
As a result, a high-performance 32k-LP mode comparable to the 48k mode has been realized, enabling up to four hours of recording (when using R-120) while covering the dynamic range and frequency characteristics of the satellite broadcast A mode.

It has a 5-block structure in which both A/D and D/A converters, mechanical part, power supply part, signal processing part and display part such as display are clearly separated, and the transformer eliminates interference and adverse effect on the audio circuit as an analog / digital separate winding.
Furthermore, these constructions are installed on a 1 mm thick steel plate base incorporating rib structure, and vibration - and interference-free design is also realized to improve total sound quality.

By using the shortest signal path design, input selectors and and volume are placed close to the input terminal, the signal line is as short as possible, and the board pattern is not wasted, which reduces loss and interference during transmission.

Equipped with a digital mixing function, you can enjoy mixing the digital sound being played on the XD-Z707 with another sound. The input signal to be mixed is both analog and digital.

It is equipped with a digital fade system in which smooth fade operations are memorized in a computer.
5-second fade-in and 10-second fade-out can be done automatically with a single switch. Digital input can also be faded.

When editing a CD, it is possible to automatically read the Q-code information of the CD and write the start ID just before the start of the song.
In addition, it is equipped with Auto ID Edit which tells the position where the start ID is written by a computer, and it can be easily rewritten manually.

Equipped with a sampling monitor, the digital input is also monitored by D/A conversion immediately before recording so that sound quality and level can be easily checked.
It can also be used as an A/D / D/A converter unit because it can output after A/D / D/A conversion even without a tape.

Cassette tray can be opened / closed by remote control.

Equipped with a 20-dot digital meter with peak hold function.

It has a direct selection function with 10 keys.

Equipped with intro-scan.

It is equipped with a repeat playback function for all songs / one song.

It has a Queue & Review function.

Equipped with P-time display function.

Equipped with digital input / output of 2 optical / coaxial systems.

Equipped with a digital / analog source selector.

Equipped with Compulink-1 / synchro terminal, recording operation linked with VICTOR/JVC's synchro compatible CD player is possible.

Equipped with timer recording and replay function.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Gold-plated input / output terminal is mounted.

Wireless remote control is included.
