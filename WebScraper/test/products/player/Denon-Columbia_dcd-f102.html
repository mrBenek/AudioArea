  • DENON DCD-F102

  • ¥ 36,000 (around 2006)

Commentary

F102 series CD player with 192kHz/24bitD/A converter.

The D/A converter is equipped with a Bar-Brown D/A converter, which is also used in DENON's pure audio components, and audio characteristics such as dynamic range, S/N ratio and distortion factor are improved.

The digital circuit is equipped with an 8 x oversampling digital filter.
By increasing the sampling frequency by 8 times, high-frequency noise generated during D/A conversion can be effectively reduced.

You can play MP3 or WMA audio files written to CD-R/RW.
The file name, artist name, title name, etc. can be displayed on the display (single-byte alphanumeric only).

It is equipped with a repeat play function and a program playback function in which you can choose 2 types of playback patterns.

Equipped with two optical digital output terminals.

Equipped with system functions that can be combined with the F102 series.
