  • SONY TC-9040

  • ¥ 320,000 (around 1971)

Commentary

A four channel tape deck with a synchronized track mechanism.

38cm/s, 4-channel rerecording and 2-channel rerecording are possible with No. 10 reel.

Closed loop dual capstan system with AC servo motor is adopted.

Equipped with a synchronized track mechanism, the sound recorded on channel 1 can be monitored for playback while recording on channel 2, and both channels are completely synchronized during playback.

Equipped with a speed tuning mechanism that can vary the tape speed by ± 5% during playback.

It uses a system control method that allows you to press any operation button at any time.
It is also designed to reduce click noise.

Equipped with automatic head-out (APS) function and automatic stop (ATS) function.

Equipped with a tape selector that supports SLH tapes.

Built-in reel in reel lock mechanism.
