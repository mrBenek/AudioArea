  • DENON DMD-7.5L

  • ¥ 70,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

MD-deck of PRESTA series equipped with Λ S. L. C.

The D/A converter is equipped with a multi-bit Λ S. L. C (Lambda Super Linear Converter), which eliminates the 0 cross distortion that occurs in principle during D/A conversion, and realizes excellent D/A conversion.

Equipped with a sampling rate converter, it supports not only MD and CD at 44.1 kHz, but also BS and DAT at 32kHz/48kHz, enabling digital recording with less sound quality degradation.

Katakana character input is supported, and disc name and song name can be entered.
The display can display up to 14 characters at a time, and the rest are scrolled.

You can edit the contents of a recorded disc, such as splitting one song or joining two songs.

Built-in functions such as program playback, random playback, and repeat playback of all songs / one song.

Equipped with two optical digital input terminals and one optical output terminal.

Digital servo is adopted.

Equipped with system functions, easy operation is possible by connection with PRESTA-7.5L/6.5L series.

Wireless remote control is included.
