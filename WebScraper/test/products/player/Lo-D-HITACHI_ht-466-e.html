Commentary

A full auto player that uses a quartz lock unitorque motor.

The motor is equipped with a unitorque motor based on Lordi's proprietary technology.
The Unitorque Motor is a motor which obtained perfect circular motion from the principle. It consists of a doughnut-shaped rotor magnet magnetized with 8 poles and two sets of star-shaped flat coils offset by 22 ^ 5 degrees. The torque generated by these two coils and magnets is constant at any moment. This principle is called sin2Θ + cos2Θ = 1 ". The Uni-Torque Motor also adopts a simple structure that is brushless, slotless and coreless, and has significantly improved durability.
Stable rotation is obtained by applying crystal control (quartz-locked PLL servo) to this motor, which is hardly affected by voltage fluctuation, aging and temperature change.

Equipped with a full auto mechanism that adopts a motor exclusively for arm drive.
It is designed so that the full automatic mechanism does not interfere with manual operation.

An opto-electronic auto-return detection mechanism has been adopted in which no side pressure is applied by return detection and no extra distortion is generated during record performance.

It uses a gimbal support tone arm with excellent tracking performance for light needle pressure performance.
In addition, the head shell uses a head shell containing glass fiber.
