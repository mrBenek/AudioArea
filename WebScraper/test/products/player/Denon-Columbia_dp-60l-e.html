  • DENON DP-60L

  • ¥ 90,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

Auto lift player equipped with auto lift mechanism with non-contact type end sensor.

The up / down speed of the arm is optimally controlled by the electronic servo lifter, protecting the record board and needle.

An arm pipe replaceable type is adopted for the tone arm part, and it is equipped with 2 of light mass straight tone arm and universal S-shaped tone arm.
It is compatible with all types of cartridges including high compliance type cartridges by exchanging the arms.

DENON quartz and AC servo motor are used for the rotating system to realize low wow flutter and high S / N ratio.
