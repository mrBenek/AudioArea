  • ONKYO MD-124

  • ¥ 49,800 (Released in September 1998)

Commentary

MD deck with 20 bit processing.

All digital data processing, from recording to playback, is done in 20-bit to achieve higher sound quality.

24-bit arithmetic processing ATRAC is adopted.

It has two digital inputs and a sampling rate converter (32kHz/44.1kHz/48kHz).

Vibration control insulator is adopted.

Equipped with timer recording / playback function by external timer.

Equipped with a signal sync recording function convenient for MD dubbing and a digital sync recording function from a CD player with the Onkyo RI mark.

Wireless remote control is included.
