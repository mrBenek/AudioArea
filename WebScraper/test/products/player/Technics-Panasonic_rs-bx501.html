  • Technics RS-BX501

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1994)

Commentary

Stereo reverse deck equipped with ATC (auto-tape calibration).

It is equipped with ATC in order to maximize the performance of the tape.
This function records a test signal generated inside the set and detects its characteristics at three points of 400 Hz, 5 kHz and 10 kHz. The bias adjustment, recording equalizer and recording level adjustment are each adjusted in 64 high-precision steps of 0.2 dB. This function enables recording with flat frequency characteristics.

High-speed FF/REW has been adopted, and the time for fast forward and rewind has been reduced to about half of the conventional time.
And TPS is also faster.

A power assist cassette holder has been adopted to ensure stable tape travel by securely holding the holder in place.

A permalloy head is used for the recording / reproducing head.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO system, the effective bias during recording is automatically adjusted to greatly improve the high-frequency recording characteristics.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with an MPX filter ON/OFF switch.

Equipped with auto REC mute and memory stop functions.

You can adjust the left / right balance, recording level, and headphone level.

Wireless remote control is included.
