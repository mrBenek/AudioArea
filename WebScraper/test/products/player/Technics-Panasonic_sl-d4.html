Commentary

A jacket size D. D. player with full automatic operation.

All operations such as arm up/down, forward / backward control, etc. can be performed only with the front panel buttons with the dust cover closed.

The upper cabinet is equipped with a linear tracking arm, and the tracking error angle is within ± 0.1% and the inside force is almost 0, realizing high-precision arm travel by microcomputer control.

The arm bearing structure of the gimbal suspension system is adopted, and the same high precision in all directions is obtained by the 4-point support by the pivot and ball bearing, and the stable tracing performance is realized by the dynamic balance system arm.

Highly accurate rotational performance is achieved by adopting a unique integrated structure D. D. that integrates the motor rotor and turntable, and an all-around detection F. G.

Equipped with a plug-in connector type MM cartridge.

The lower cabinet and bottom base are all composed of TNRC to improve vibration isolation performance.
