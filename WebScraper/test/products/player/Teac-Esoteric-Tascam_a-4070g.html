Commentary

Stereo tape deck with HD ferrite head.

By attaching a sensing foil at the end of the tape, it is possible to record and play back in a reciprocating manner and to perform Oatley verse without changing the reel. In addition, there are two sensing posts, and if you attach a foil at the beginning and end of the tape, you can repeat playback continuously.

The head uses HD ferrite head with excellent wear resistance. The HD ferrite head uses high density ferrite with excellent wear resistance for the core material, and the base part of the head is made of permalloy alloy with high saturation point and excellent electromagnetic conversion characteristics.
The 6-head 4-unit configuration, including a combination head for erasing and recording, shortens the tape path and improves wow flutter characteristics.

A center capstan system is used to realize a symmetrical mechanism and to shorten the tape path. A delay circuit is inserted in the pinch roller mechanism to improve the start-up and protect the tape when reversing.
When the capstan rotates in the reverse direction and reaches constant speed, the pinch roller instantaneously compresses and moves the tape.

The pause lever is designed to be linked with the recording master volume, allowing for fade-in and fade-out.
The pause is turned on when the recording input is closed. The pose is a Klix/Klukš top type. The pause start is one touch and the volume is automatically adjusted by the spring.
You can freely control the speed of fade-in / fade-out with your fingers.

In order to demonstrate the performance of low-noise tape, a bias current selection switch is mounted.
There is also an equalizer selector switch that allows you to set the optimum equalizer for the tape by setting it to High for low-noise tapes and Normal for standard tapes.

Newly developed reel clamp mechanism is adopted.
The reel can be fixed securely by simply screwing the shaft to the right, and the reel can be removed by simply turning the shaft to the left.

In order to prevent the tape from being damaged by contact with the edge of the reel due to the difference in the height of the reel, a reel table height adjustment mechanism is mounted.
It can be adjusted by simply turning the screw attached to the reel stand left and right using a Phillips screwdriver.

Equipped with a full-scale VU meter. Since the scale is expanded type close to a straight line, the sense of the scale at low level and high level is wide, and it can monitor up to 6 VU reliably.
In addition, it is equipped with an output volume linked to the VU meter, and the delivery level can be freely adjusted.

The mechanism section employs a 3-motor mechanism consisting of a 4-pole 8-pole capstan motor and a high-torque eddy current induction motor.

Soft touch is realized by combining micro switches and relays.

The microphone input and line input volumes are independent and can be mixed with each other.

Equipped with a tape tension switch, it can be switched to the optimum tension between thin tape and regular tape.

It is equipped with a 4-unit dedicated amplifier with 2 systems each for recording and playback, and a 3-stage directly connected equalizer.
In addition, it is designed so that all adjustment of the recording and playback amplifier can be performed simply by removing the lid of the bottom of the deck.

We have adopted a Oatley sink mechanism that enables the recording side to Oatley broadcast by the Dupree broadcast signal of the playback unit (A-2420).

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining the timer control adapter sold separately with the timer.
