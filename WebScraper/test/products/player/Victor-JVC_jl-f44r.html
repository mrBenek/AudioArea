  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A direct drive type full auto player with light operability.

The drive motor is a coreless motor that eliminates the core of the stator, which causes harmful vibration. Accurate rotation is obtained by combining this motor with a precision DC servo system with FG detection.

The full-auto mechanism is designed to give priority to manual operation so that manual performance can be performed freely without changing the switch.

It is equipped with a memory repeat device, and you can select from 1 to 6 times or infinite number of times repeat.

The tone arm uses a new gimbal system with high sensitivity and excellent stability.

NR head shell is used for head shell, and MM type cartridge Z-1S is used for cartridge.
