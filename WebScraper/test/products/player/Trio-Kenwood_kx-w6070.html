Commentary

W reverse deck with Dolby HX-PRO mounted on both A and B decks.

The Dolby HX-PRO is a circuit that detects the high-frequency signal flowing in the recording head during recording, controls the recording bias current according to the increase or decrease of the high-frequency signal, and improves the high-frequency characteristics for recording.
The Dolby HX-PRO is installed on both A and B decks. (Simultaneous recording on A and B decks is not possible.)

B deck is equipped with auto bias / manual bias function.
With auto-bias, it is possible to record by changing the bias level in 15 steps using the signal of 400 Hz and 12.5 kHz, and at the same time record at the proper bias value according to the output level.
In addition, manual adjustment with 7-point bias adjustment is also installed.

It is equipped with double-speed tape-to-tape dubbing that enables tape dubbing in about half the time, relay play / relay rec functions that enable long-time playback / recording, and MPX-Filter that reduces noise during FM air checks.
