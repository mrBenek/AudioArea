Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with ANRS.

Equipped with an automatic noise reduction device ANRS as a noise reduction system.
This circuit is composed of newly developed IC and realizes reduction of distortion factor by distortion correction path and variable output impedance design, improvement of transient characteristics, phase characteristics and noise modulation characteristics by all stages direct connection structure, and improvement of reliability by electronic switching of recording and reproduction switching.

Equipped with 5-point multi-peak indicator controlled by TTL IC using LED.
Five LEDs light up one after another at each stage of -10dB, -5dB, 0 dB, + 3 dB and + 6 dB to indicate an instantaneous pulse input that cannot be followed by the VU meter.

The recording amplifier has a linearity of + 48 dB from the reference level before the volume and + 18 dB at the final stage.
In addition, the playback amplifier also secures over + 20 dB, and combined with the 5-point multi-peak indicator and ANRS IC circuit, clear sound quality with less distortion can be obtained.
We have also improved usability and operability by using an electronic muting circuit that prevents click noise at the start and a method that enables level adjustment simply by locking the recording button.

The line input is input to the recording circuit without passing through the microphone amplifier by switching the switch to improve the SN ratio and linearity.

The tape selector switches the bias and equalizer separately with a single switch.
The Chrome equalizer has a time constant of 3180 μ S-70 μ S, which improves the linearity not only in the high range but also in the low range.

The recording / reproducing head uses a hard permalloy (Cronios) head with a narrow gap precision finish. The excellent magnetic properties of permalloy are achieved while being compatible with hard chrome tapes. In addition, the shape of the gap surface reduces the bumpiness in the low frequency range.
The erasing head uses high density ferrite material that is resistant to wear.

In the power supply section, a ripple filter and a constant voltage power supply are adopted for all circuits to realize a stable power supply with little fluctuation.
In addition, bias oscillation circuit with less distortion and precise adjustment bias trap circuit using variable coil improve bias efficiency and suppress adverse effect of bias leakage.

The mechanism part adopts an upright direct-viewing type, and the capstan belt is an ID (Independent Drive) type that is independent of other drive systems to prevent interference from reel stands, etc. and reduce wow and flutter.
Capstan itself is also used with a roundness of 0.1 micron.

Equipped with a full auto-stop mechanism using CDs photocell and automatically returns the mechanism to the stop state.

The cassette door opens and closes smoothly with an air dump mechanism.
In addition, the cassette holder has a free hinge structure so that the tape can be easily removed and the door can be easily removed for head cleaning or degaussing.

It is equipped with a timer recording mechanism combining a pause mechanism and an electromagnetic solenoid, and timer recording can be performed by using a commercially available timer.
Since the pinch roller is separated from the capstan during standby, there is no need to worry about deformation of the pinch roller or damage to the tape.

There was a special wood case sold separately.
