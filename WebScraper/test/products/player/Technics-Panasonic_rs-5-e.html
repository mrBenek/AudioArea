  • Technics RS-5

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

31.5 cm wide LP jacket size cassette deck.

An MX head is used for recording and re-recording.

An electronically controlled DC motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a direct connector that can be easily connected to the Concise 55.
