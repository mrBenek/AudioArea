  • Technics RS-1W

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

Double cassette deck of record jacket size.

Songs played on Tape 1 can be edited on Tape 2. In addition, auto-level recording eliminates the need for troublesome level setting.
When editing a tape, you can press the tape speed button to edit the tape at twice the speed.

Tape 1 and microphone can be mixed for recording. The microphone volume can be adjusted with a dedicated volume.

AX amorphous head is used for the head.

Equipped with an auto tape selector that detects the type of tape and automatically sets the position.
In addition, Tape 2 is equipped with an indicator so that you can confirm the set type.

Equipped with a music selector.

Equipped with an auto input selector, the input is automatically switched to the microphone when the microphone is connected.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Synchronized recording with record players (SL-6, SL-3, SL-V5) is possible by using a separately sold synchronized recording cord.
