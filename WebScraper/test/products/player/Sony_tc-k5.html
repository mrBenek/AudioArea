Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with green motor.

The motor is equipped with a green motor with improved reliability and durability based on a frequency servo motor with FG that boasts high precision rotation.

The head is equipped with an all-ferrite F & F (ferrite & ferrite) head that uses ferrite, which has excellent wear resistance and high-frequency characteristics, not only for the core part but also for the guard part.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism that allows silent recording with one touch, CM can be cut at the time of FM air check and adjustment between songs can be performed.

Equipped with a 3-stage peak level indicator that is effective for adjusting the proper recording level.
Since the peak is accurately indicated, it is possible to adjust the recording level finely.

Equipped with a 3-stage switching tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Equipped with a memory stop / autoplay mechanism.
If you press the rewind button and play button simultaneously, playback starts automatically from the position of the tape counter 999.

We invested in a Dolby NR/MPX filter switch equipped with a position where you can turn on / off the MPX filter while keeping the Dolby NR ON.

By using the timer sold separately, it is possible to record your absence and play the alarm clock.

Equipped with volume for both lineout and headphone.

Equipped with auto shut-off mechanism.

An optional audio handle is attached and rack-mountable.
