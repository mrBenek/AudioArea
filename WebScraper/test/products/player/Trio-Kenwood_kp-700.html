Commentary

Based on Trio's unique inertial lock and quartz lock, this is a player system with stability transfer in higher dimension.

In order to ensure that the center of gravity of the cabinet coincides with the rotation axis of the motor, we have adopted a two layer high rigidity cabinet made of two materials with different resonance frequencies. A particle board and a 6 mm thick steel plate are bonded together.
Since the shape of the iron material is determined taking into consideration the balance of the entire cabinet, we succeeded in bringing the center of gravity of the cabinet onto the rotating shaft of the motor and obtained stable rotation.
In addition, in order to suppress the adverse effect on the cabinet caused by the reaction to the direction of rotation due to mechanical torque change (ripple) called cogging, which is inherent in DC motors, we have improved the rotational accuracy by using insulators that do not move in the horizontal direction.

The motor is a converter that converts current into torque. We consider it necessary to increase the conversion efficiency of the motor in order to handle heavy duty turntables, so we have adopted a 3-phase switching plane drive motor with an 8-pole 6-coil structure.
Since all installed coils and all installed rotors are used all the time, torque ripple is small and high efficiency is realized. This reduces the gain of the amplifier in the feedback loop and reduces the influence of drift.
The Center shaft is made of hard stainless steel with a diameter of 10 and is 450 kg ・ cm.2It drives the large moment of inertia turntable stably.

450 kg / cm2It uses a large inertia turntable, which locks by inertia and locks by quartz at the same time to prevent uneven rotation during actual performance where the record needle is traced by the needle tip.

A quartz PLL system is used to control the phase difference by comparing the reference signal of the quartz oscillator with the speed detection signal of the motor.
An FG coil that generates power by changing the magnetic flux density of the magnet rotor is used for rotation speed detection, and a newly developed IC with a digital memory inside the IC is used for speed control system.
Unlike the conventional sample-and-hold method, temperature characteristics and stability are improved by eliminating the use of coils and resistors.

It uses an auto-up mechanism that detects angular velocity by projecting light from an LED toward a mirror attached to the tone arm axis and receiving the returned light with a photo reflector.
Since it does not use mechanical contact points or magnets, it is not affected by internal transformers, etc. and performs accurate and quiet auto-up operation.
In addition, the turntable keeps rotating for approximately 4 seconds after auto-up to protect the needle tip.

Cartridge is not included.
