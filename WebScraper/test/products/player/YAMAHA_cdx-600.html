  • YAMAHA CDX-600

  • ¥ 54,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

A CD player with a glitch less high-speed twin D/A converter.

The digital filter uses an 8 fs digital filter and the D/A converter uses a new type of glitch less high-speed twin D/A converter to eliminate glitch noise.

Equipped with a high-bit direct out switch that can output directly from the DAC without passing through a low-pass filter.

In order to prevent unwanted vibration from being transmitted to the disk, an anti-vibration floating suspension using a 2-layer structure damper is adopted.
Also, the tray supports 8 cm CD single.

Using a current isolation method, the digital noise coming in from the digital section is dropped to the ground side of the V/I conversion amplifier to eliminate adverse effects on the analog signal.

The optical pickup has a built-in head amplifier to improve S/N ratio when reading signals.
In addition, the built-in 2-way computer servo minimizes reading errors due to disc dirt and scratches and eliminates noise generation.

Equipped with a 75 Ω coaxial pin jack digital out terminal.

It has a headphone port equipped with a volume for exclusive use of headphones.

2 speed monitor search is adopted, and fast forward / quick return is possible while playing.
At first, it is low speed, and if you keep pressing it, it switches to high speed to search.

Index search and direct music selection are available.

Equipped with 3-mode program function of manual / delete / auto.

4-mode repeat playback of one track / all tracks / A-B / program is available.

Equipped with a timer play function with a program backup function. When combined with an external timer, an alarm clock can be played back.

Equipped with 24 songs music calendar.

It is equipped with an 8 digit FL display with a reproduction display.

There are 2 color variations of titanium and black.
It also comes with a wireless remote control for each color.
