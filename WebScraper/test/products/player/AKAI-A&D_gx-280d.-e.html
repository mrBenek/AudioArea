Commentary

This 3-motor, 3-head stereo tape deck adopts a direct capstan drive motor with servo circuit and an GX head.

We have adopted a newly designed direct capstan drive motor with servo circuit for capstan drive in pursuit of higher performance.
It is designed to detect and amplify the motor speed purely electrically and compensate for changes in the rotational speed by means of a servo circuit, by integrating an Eddy Current Outer Rotor Motor, a control winding and a pulse generator for speed detection into an integrated structure. It is able to maintain a constant rotational speed at all times and achieves very stable tape running.
In addition, belts, idlers, flywheels, etc. are layered, and the wow and flutter are held down by a simple mechanism.
We have adopted two Eddy Current Outer Rotor Motors for driving both supply and take-up reels.

Uses GX (Glass & X' Tal Ferrite) head.
By applying crystal ferrite (single crystal ferrite) developed for VTR and various ultra-precision processing technologies for VTR head to audio head, this head uses single crystal ferrite with superior characteristics to conventionally used permalloy as core material for the first time in the world. In addition, the periphery including gap is hardened with glass.
The surface of this head is made of glass and crystal ferrite, so that dust does not adhere to it and it does not wear. Moreover, the "focused field" provides excellent frequency characteristics.
In addition, there is no need to replace or clean the head, and it has a Longraima which is 100 times larger than the conventional permalloy head.

Equipped with auto stop / auto shut-off function.
When the tape is fully wound and reaches the end, the automatic shut-off lever will stop the tape from running.
When the auto shut-off switch is pressed, the power of the whole unit is turned off when the shut-off lever is returned.

You can use S. R. T. tape (Super Range Tape).
By pressing the S. R. T. button, you can demonstrate the performance of a low-noise tape.

Sound-on-sound recording is possible, and it is possible to record one person duet making use of language, practice of musical instrument performance and double recording.
In addition, since the microphone and line inputs are independent, mixing recording is possible.

Equipped with a direct function change mechanism that automatically enters the selected mode simply by pressing the Play or Reverse button in the fast-forward / rewind state.
