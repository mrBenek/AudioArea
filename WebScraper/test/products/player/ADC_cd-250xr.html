Commentary

CD player with full remote control and multi-display.

A three beam system is adopted for the pickup section to ensure stable tracing capability.
The 3-beam system uses two sub-beams in addition to the main beam to detect the track shift of the main beam in real time and controls it with a servomechanism, which reduces the effects of disc warpage, eccentricity, dirt and scratches, and makes stable reading possible.

The D/A converter is equipped with a linear D/A converter with greatly reduced conversion error and less distortion.

Analog circuits are designed with an emphasis on sound quality, such as the adoption of filters with excellent sound quality and simple and straight signal transmission.

It has a program memory function of up to 16 songs.
To make a reservation, use the SKIP key to specify the track number of the song you want to listen to, and then press the Eneter/Set key.

It is equipped with a repeat function of 1 song / All.
When you repeat All, you can repeat the music you reserved with the program memory function.

Equipped with a skip search function.

The display is equipped with a large multi-display.
You can view the track number of the song you are listening to and the playing time independently, press the Remaining key to see the remaining time on the disc, and the total number of songs and playing time when the disc is set.

Wireless remote control is included.
This remote control allows not only normal operations such as Play, Pause, and Stop, but also all operations except power ON/OFF such as repeat, program memory reservation, open / close of disk tray, etc.
