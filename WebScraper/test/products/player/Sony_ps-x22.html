Commentary

An auto player that emphasizes usability and basic performance.

The front operation auto mechanism is adopted, and the tone arm can be up / down and rejected (cut in the middle) even with the dust cover closed, and the turntable speed can be switched and the pitch can be controlled.
In addition, it is equipped with an auto return mechanism that automatically returns the tone arm when the performance finishes and stops the turntable.

The turntable is driven by a direct drive system in which a linear BSL motor is servocontrol by a magnesium disk.

The cabinet uses an acoustic material, SBMC, which is less likely to resonate than metals and has high precision and strength. Partial resonance is suppressed by considering the thickness and structure in a comprehensive manner and reinforcing the structure at important points.

Uses XL-15, which is a MM type cartridge.
