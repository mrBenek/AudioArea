Audio Database

  • SONY MDS-302

  • ¥ 65,000 (released on December 1, 1994)

Commentary

Full component size MD deck.

It is equipped with a smart space function that automatically adjusts the silence to approximately 3 seconds when there is a gap of more than 3 seconds between songs when digitally recorded from a CD, etc.

Simply press the Record button to automatically find the unrecorded part and start recording. You can also record from the beginning or middle of any song.

Equipped with a TOC EDIT function.

Equipped with a multi-jog dial.

Equipped with a disk-in auto power function that turns the power on just by inserting a disk.

Equipped with timer standby function, external timer can be connected.

Optical digital input / output terminal is mounted.
Digital signals are available only at a sampling frequency of 44.1 kHz.

It is equipped with track marking function and 15-segment digital peak meter.

It comes with a wireless remote controller that allows one touch direct music selection using 25 keys.
It can also be used with a Sony CD player for wireless CD sync recording.

Mono format discs are play-only.

It is equipped with a 4-megabit DRAM that can store music signals for up to about 8 seconds to prevent skipping sound.
In addition, taking advantage of memory storage, Memory Start playback, in which the sound starts immediately after the pause is released, is made possible.

Serial Copy Management System (SCMS) allows only one generation of digital recording and no more than two generations of digital recording.
