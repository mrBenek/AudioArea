  • MICRO MR-606U

  • ¥ 37,500 (around 1973)

Commentary

A custom player that does not have an arm and can be equipped with various tone arms by an armboard.

The drive part is equipped with MB 600 which adopts belt drive system with 8-pole hysteresis synchronous motor.

Both sides of the belt have been polished to reduce motor noise.
Also equipped with a belt lock mechanism.

Equipped with a shell holder.

It uses a large cabinet that can mount arms up to 40 cm.

It has a front pocket that can store accessories and Tools.

Arm board with mounting II and MA-101mkII come with an arm board with mounting holes and a spare that can be used immediately.
