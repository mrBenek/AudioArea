Commentary

A full auto player with an orthophone cartridge.

If you set the record size and rotation speed, all automatic operations such as Auto Lead In, Auto Cut, Oatley Turn, and Auto Repeat will be performed automatically without touching the tone arm. If you return the arm to rest by hand during a performance, all automatic operations will stop instantaneously.
During manual operation, the turntable starts rotating simply by removing the arm from the rest, and the auto return mechanism always operates even in manual operation.

FG (frequency generator) control motor is adopted for the turntable drive part.
Equipped with 33 1/3rpm and 45 rpm independent volume for fine adjustment of the rotation speed, accurate rotation speed adjustment is possible while monitoring the strobe of the 2 thread type.
Urethane belt is used for driving belt.

The cabinet has a unique composite structure, and the upper and lower cabinets are integrally molded with vibration resistant mold.

The tone arm uses a static balance S-type universal tone arm. In addition, the needle pressure can be adjusted in 0.2 mg steps from 0g to 3g using a needle pressure direct reading type weight counter.
Non-resonant aluminum die-cast bracket is used for the arm support part, and EIA standard plug-in head shell is used for the head shell.

The cartridge is a VM type manufactured by Orthophone.

A dial-type dynamic anti-skating mechanism is used in the inside four scan cellar that cancels out harmful inside forces.

There was a dust cover sold separately.
