  • VICTOR/JVC L-E500

  • ¥ 43,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

A full-auto player with linear tracking system.

It uses a top-side linear system with the arm and its drive part built into the top cover of the cabinet, and an elaborate foolproof design for easy operation.
The L-E500 is designed so that the arm does not move unless the top cover is completely closed, and the arm will automatically return to the rest position when the top cover is opened or the power switch is turned off during play.

Easy operation is adopted. After setting the record and adjusting the size and rotation speed, it can be moved only by the key operation of the front side. All operations such as arm feed, arm position fine adjustment and arm Up down are automatically performed by the function of the logic circuit.

Newly developed IM type cartridge is adopted.

Equipped with an LED illuminated pointer that can tell the movement of the arm at a glance.

Adopts belt drive system using FG servo motor with high rotational accuracy.

High-density cabinet is adopted.
