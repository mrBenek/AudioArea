  • Technics RS-2630U

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with peak check meter.

A 1-motor belt drive system is adopted for the mechanism part.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a tape selector.

Built-in memory rewind function.

Equipped with a 3-digit tape counter.

Equipped with a lock type pose button.

Uses a slide-type level controller.

Equipped with My Kujac.
