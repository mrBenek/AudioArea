Commentary

This is a stereo cassette deck with improved basic functions, inheriting the technology of high-end machines.

A high-hardness permalloy head is used for the read / write head, and a double-gap ferrite head is used for the erase head.

In addition to the capstan motor, a full-logic 2-motor system with a dedicated DC reel motor has been adopted to realize highly stable tape travel and to improve operability by accurately controlling the operation with a microcomputer.

Equipped with dbx system and Dolby B / C NR as noise reduction system.

Equipped with 3-color wide-range FL meter and electronic counter.

Equipped with independent round input volume and balance volume.

Equipped with automatic input switching function of microphone and line.
