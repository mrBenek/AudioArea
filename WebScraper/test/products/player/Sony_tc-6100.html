  • SONY TC-6100

  • ¥ 32,800 (around 1971)

Commentary

Tape deck with relatively compact design.

The head uses a narrow gap 2 micron head with excellent wear resistance and high frequency characteristics.

Equipped with a tape selector that supports SLH tapes.

Low-noise silicon transistors are used in the amplifier section.

Equipped with electron spectroscopy for chemica analysis rate drive for one touch tape application.

It uses an anti-vibration roller that suppresses the nigori of sound and an anti-vibration motor that suppresses vibration and noise.
