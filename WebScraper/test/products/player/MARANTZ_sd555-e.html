  • Marantz SD555

  • ¥ 30,000 (released in 1997)

Commentary

W reverse cassette deck with bias fine function for optimum recording.

Equipped with 2-speed dubbing function.

Timer recording and playback are supported.

The digital counter is equipped with a memory stop function.

Equipped with synchronized recording function with Marantz CD compatible models.

It is equipped with a dimmer function that can set the display to 7 levels of brightness.

It comes with a wireless remote control equipped with an indicator for reception confirmation.
