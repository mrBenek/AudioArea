Commentary

This direct drive auto-changer was developed by taking advantage of many years of overseas auto-changer technology as well as development technology of direct drive.

By using a special spindle, up to 6 pieces of LP, EP, or doughnut can be played continuously.
If the manual spindle is used, full auto player operation such as auto start / Oatley turn / memo repeat / intermediate cut is possible.

The auto-start mechanism allows the needle tip to be gently lowered into the groove for the record of the set size only by operating the lever. You can enjoy playing the record without worrying about the arm operation at the start of playing the record.
In addition, SL-1350 is equipped with a memo repeat mechanism of 1 to 5 times.

The motor uses a unique D. D motor with an integrated structure. In addition, the rotating part of the motor is integrated with the turntable and the fixed part is integrated with the case. There is no need to stack the case, motor, and turntable, thus improving performance and reliability.
