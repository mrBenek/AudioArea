Commentary

A CD player developed by focusing on the basics of playing music.

The circuit configuration and newly developed printed circuit board pattern design technology are used to ensure a stable ground potential and eliminate the influence of digital noise on analog circuits.

The D/A converter is equipped with a linear single-bit accu-pulse D/A converter with excellent reproducibility of small signals.
The crystal oscillator that generates the clock signal is a small crystal oscillator that is resistant to external vibration. Moreover, it is firmly fixed to the substrate to provide a stable supply of the clock signal.

In order to prevent noise from the microcomputer system from flowing into the signal circuit through the power transformer, we have adopted a power transformer with separate windings for the signal system and the microcomputer system.

Equipped with two optical digital output terminals.

Equipped with next selection function, peak search function, time edit function, memory / memory reverse performance function.

Wireless remote control is included.
