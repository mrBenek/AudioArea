  • NEC CD-610

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

A CD player equipped with various editing functions.

It is equipped with an auto-edit function that automatically programs how many songs can be recorded within a specified time, and a manual edit function that allows you to record while checking the specified remaining time.
In addition, it is equipped with an auto space function that automatically sets a space of at least 4 seconds between songs.

It is equipped with functions such as intro-scan function, 3 types of repeat function, 24 songs random program memory, and index search.

Equipped with two series analog output terminals, variable and fixed.

Wireless remote controller with 10 keys is included.
