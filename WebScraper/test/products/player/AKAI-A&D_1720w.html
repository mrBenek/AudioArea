  • AKAI 1720W

  • ¥ 60,000 (around the late 1960 s?)

Commentary

Standard type tape recorder for easy stereo sound enjoyment.

The SN ratio has been improved by shielding all heads.

Equipped with a tape lifter to prevent wear of the head when the tape is fast-forwarded or rewound.

The amplifier circuit uses a solid-state Hi-Fi amplifier with 14 silicon transistors.

It can be used both horizontally and vertically.
