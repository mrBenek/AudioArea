Commentary

A stereo tape recorder that uses a cross field head.

Cross-field head is used for the head part.
In this head, the signal current is applied to the recording head, and the bias current is applied to the external bias head. These heads are positioned at predetermined spatial positions relative to each other so that the signal recorded on the tape is not adversely affected by the bias field.

A hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the capstan in the mechanism section, and an induction motor is used for rapid feed and rewind.

An all-transistor solid-state amplifier is used for the amplifier section.
In addition, an amplifier arrangement by a card system is adopted.

Equipped with automatic reverse function of dial and pin system.

It is equipped with functions such as automatic repeat, automatic stop and automatic shutoff.

Sound on sound, sound with sound, sound over sound, and so on.

It is equipped with an echo device.

Recording of FM multiplex broadcasting is supported.

Equipped with a recording monitor function.

Remote control is possible.

Wow flutter is removed by double stabilizer.

Equipped with a tape lifter.

It can be horizontal or vertical.

There were 2 kinds of variations, X-355 and X-355D.
The X-355D is configured without the main amplifier block and built-in speakers from the X-355.
