  • Pioneer PD-M500

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

A magazine-style CD player capable of playing six games in a row.

It is possible to play continuously just by putting 6 CDs in the performance magazine and playing it.
Also, you can play up to 32 songs from 6 CDs.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 4-times oversampling digital filter to obtain low-distortion audio characteristics with little phase deviation up to high frequencies.
It is also equipped with a twin-linear envelope D/A converter system to reduce the distortion factor by adjusting the error generated at the MSB switching section to an appropriate value.

It employs an Accu-Focus system that reduces pit signal reading errors, a honeycomb chassis that reduces vibration from outside, and a large insulator.

It is equipped with a time fade edit function. If you specify the desired time with the 10 key, it will fade out automatically at that time and enter a pause state.

All 6 discs can be played at random.

It has a direct programming function that allows you to program while listening to music.

Equipped with a pause program function, it is also possible to temporarily stop in front of a specified song during program performance.

Equipped with digital level control that allows volume adjustment by remote control at hand.

You can share carrozzeria car audio and magazine.

Equipped with a power on start function that enables timer start.

By using the 8 cm CD magazine sold separately, it is also possible to play 6 discs of 8 cm CD.
