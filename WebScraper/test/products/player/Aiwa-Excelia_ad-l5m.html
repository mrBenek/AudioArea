  • AIWA AD-L5M

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

Compact size cassette deck equipped with metal position.

Ultra-hard permalloy head is used for the head.

Excellent rotational accuracy is achieved with DC servo motor and double flywheel.

Equipped with 5-point 3-color LED optical display.

Equipped with a Dolby system.

Uses slot-in type tape mounting.

Equipped with an LED blinking tape run indicator that can confirm the rerecording status.

Equipped with a bias equalizer switching mechanism corresponding to LH, CrO2, and metal tape.

Equipped with a pure electronic full auto-stop mechanism.

When recording, the cassette is inserted after pressing the record button. In addition, because of the mechanism, even a tape with a folded tab to prevent accidental erasure will be erased when recording.
