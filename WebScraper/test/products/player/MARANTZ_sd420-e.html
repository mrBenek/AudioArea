Audio Database

  • Marantz SD420

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

This is a stereo cassette deck that allows you to jump up to 30 songs before and after you want to listen to and automatically select songs once.

A Dolby B/C is installed as a noise reduction system.

There are 2 types, gold and silver.

There was a wired remote control sold separately as an option.
