Commentary

3-motor 3-head commercial tape recorder.

The T-3600 series consists of a drive mechanism and a recording and playback amplifier, and there was a wide variation.
The T-3600-1, T-3600-1P and T-3600-1C are single-channel (single-track) systems using 6.3 mm wide tapes, while the T-3600-2, T-3600-2P and T-3600-2C are two channel (two track) systems, while the T-3600-4C is four channel (four track) and the T-3600-XC is four channel, four track system using 12.7 mm wide tapes.
The models with numbers at the end of the model number are rack mount type, P is portable case storage type, and C is console cabinet type.

The head housing is equipped with a scrape filter to eliminate modulation noise generated during tape travel.

It is equipped with an EDIT switch.
When this switch is set to ON, the power of the reel motor on the take-up side is turned off and the brake is released in the play state so that the player is in a free state for easy editing. In addition, the shaft-off switch linked to the safety arm does not operate so that playback can be performed regardless of the arm position.

Equipped with a CUE lever.
Normally, the tape moves away from the head surface in the fast-forward or rewind state, but when the lever is pushed toward the head assembly, the tape touches the head and the recorded sound is played.

Equipped with a recording selector, 4-channel SYNC recording is possible.

Equipped with VU meter with LED peak indication.
The LED is lit at + 8 VU.

A 1-dB-step attenuator is used to adjust the playback and recording levels.

There were console cabinets and portable cases for each variation.
In addition, there was a remote control unit, monitor speaker and microphone input unit as an option sold separately.
