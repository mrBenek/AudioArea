  • Aurex PC-3110

  • ¥ 32,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A compact stereo cassette deck.

It uses a mechanical auto-stop mechanism, and the operation lever returns automatically when the tape is wound to the end during recording or playback.

Equipped with a tape selector compatible with normal tapes and chrome tapes.

Equipped with a recording indicator.

A DC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

A permalloy head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
