Commentary

This CD player was developed based on non-resonance and non-vibration technology and know-how from commercial digital recording systems.

The D/A converter uses a 16-bit D/A converter, and the digital filter uses a double oversampling digital filter.
By shifting up the sampling frequency by two times with the digital filter, unwanted signals are suppressed to a low level before D/A conversion, a simple analog filter can be used to improve sound quality, and reliability is enhanced by integrating the digital filter into the digital signal processing LSI.

The IS mechanism is adopted for the mechanism part.
In the IS mechanism, the chassis is reinforced with a strong mechanical base, and the rotating system and pickup section are floated from the main body with a large insulator. Together with the increased electric servo stabilizer effect, stable sound quality is obtained.

New Y servo system is used for the servo circuit.
The New Y Servo consists of two types of servo circuits. First of all, a high traceability servo circuit with frequency characteristics, phase characteristics, and gain that make full use of the high sensitivity pickup is used to ensure the best operating conditions at all times. Second, a high balance servo circuit is used to prevent trace errors caused by disc scratches and dirt. This circuit uses the time difference between the leading and trailing beams of the three spot beams. The flaw waveform detected by the leading beam is delayed, and the waveform is superimposed on the trailing beam and cancelled.

The disk tray uses a disk center hold system, and only the center of the disk is supported by a spring mechanism to prevent damage to the disk by touching the signal recording surface.

The power supply circuit controls beat disturbance and interference distortion by separating analog system and digital / servo system independently.

The analog stage is composed of many discrete parts with good sound, and the printed circuit board is composed of a symmetrical isometric pattern of left and right channels to reduce crosstalk, noise and phase distortion.

It uses high-quality parts such as electrolytic capacitors for audio, fast recovery diodes, low-noise low-distortion OP amplifiers, and copper screws.

A large FL multi-display is used for the display.

Uses a large insulator.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

It comes with a wireless remote control that allows you to turn the power ON/OFF and operate the buttons except for the disk tray OPEN/CLOSE.
