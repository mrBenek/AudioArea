  • DENON DMD-M7

  • ¥ 60,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

MD deck of the Lapisia series.

It has built-in editing functions such as divide, move, combine, erase and so on that are unique to MD.
In addition, it is equipped with CD-SRS function, and one touch recording from CD is possible by connecting the system with High Component Mini D-M7.

Equipped with a sampling rate converter, it can automatically convert 32kHz/48kHz sampling frequency of BS/CS broadcasting and DAT to 44.1 khz of MD.

Equipped with two optical digital input terminals and one digital output.

You can enter up to 100 characters in katakana, alphabet (upper / lower case), numbers and symbols for the title name, song name, artist name, etc.
Data can be written smoothly by the jog dial.

Up to 14 characters can be displayed on the display, and more than 14 characters are scrolled.

Wireless remote control is included.
