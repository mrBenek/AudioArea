  • Panasonic RS-TR515

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1991)

Commentary

Quick double reverse deck for rerecording & rerecording.

Since both deck 1 and deck 2 can record and play back, it is possible to record a series of up to 3 hours (when 90 minutes tape is used) or to record two tapes simultaneously.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO system, which greatly improves high-frequency recording characteristics.

Equipped with 2 nr of Dolby B/C as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a synchroedit function, it can be combined with Panasonic CD players (SL-PS300, SL-PS700, etc.) for easy CD editing and recording.

Equipped with a double electronic counter that can be used for timing adjustment during dubbing, etc.

Only deck 2 can adjust the bias.

The main / balance recording level can be adjusted.

Equipped with double-speed dubbing function and timer trek & play function.

Wireless remote control is included.
