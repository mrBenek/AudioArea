  • DENON DP-11F

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A microprocessor controlled compact player.

A non-contact tone arm controlled by a microprocessor is mounted on the tone arm drive system.
In addition, an electronic servo lifter is adopted to realize smooth arm operation.

The tone arm is equipped with a light weight straight arm.

A quick repeat system is adopted, and the record is lead-in without returning to the armrest.

The cartridge is equipped with DL-60 MM type cartridge.

DENON quartz is used for the drive part to improve rotational accuracy.

Uses a dust cover with opaque coating on the back of the ceiling.

The operation part uses a soft type push button, and by eliminating the unevenness of the button, the design quality is improved.
An insulator is equipped as a howling countermeasure.
