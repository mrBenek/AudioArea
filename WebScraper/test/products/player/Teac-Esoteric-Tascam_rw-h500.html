Commentary

CD recorder of EARNEST series.

The DA/AD conversion section is equipped with a 20-bit AD/DA converter.

It is equipped with a sampling rate converter that automatically converts each sampling frequency to 44.11 kHz when a digital signal with pitch control or a source with a sampling frequency different from that of a CD such as CD/BS or DAT is input.

Equipped with 4 x speed finalization function to reduce the finalization time.
The same processing is also possible for ERASE and REFRESH.

Equipped with digital REC volume.
In this volume, the recording level is adjusted after the analog signal is converted to digital signal, so the digital area can be adjusted even with analog input.
The adjustment range is - ∞～+ 18 dB.

Built-in number-of-songs mode allows you to end recording automatically when you record the specified number of songs.
You can specify up to 99 songs in total.

Equipped with 3 types of track mark functions that can be used depending on the source.
Autotrack Increment (digital) automatically assigns a track number when a change of track is detected by a digital signal.
With Autotrack Increment (sound level), the number of songs is automatically changed when the next song starts after the analog input has been silent for more than two seconds (below the level set for sound level). The sound level can be changed from -24dB to -72dB in 9-step increments of 6 dB.
Manual Track Increment allows you to assign track numbers wherever you want during recording, even during autotrack recording.

Equipped with a monitor function, it is possible to monitor the recording source by operating the remote control.

Equipped with a fade-in / fade-out recording function that allows you to freely set the fade time.
Can be set in 1-second intervals from 1 to 24 seconds.

Equipped with Rec Mute function.

Three playback modes (Normal / Shuffle / Program) and one or all songs can be played repeatedly.

It is equipped with synchronous recording function for both analog and digital.
Synchronized recording with analog sound sources such as cassette decks and analog players, CD players and MD decks is possible.

Wireless remote control is included.
