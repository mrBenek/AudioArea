Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with DNL circuit.

Equipped with DNL (Dynamic Noise Limiter) circuit to reduce the tape hissing.
This circuit reduces noise of hissing components of 5 kHz or higher at playback levels of approximately 40 dB or lower than 0 VU, and improves SN of 10 dB or higher at 10 kHz. Playback levels of 40 dB or higher and frequencies of 5 kHz or lower pass through flatly.

Equipped with a tape selector switch, it can demonstrate the characteristics of general tapes (Hi-Fi tapes) and chrome tapes.

Equipped with a full auto shut-off mechanism in which the operation button returns when the tape is wound in any operation state.

Equipped with a playback volume that allows you to set and match the playback level without touching the main amplifier.

Synchronous motor with less vibration and noise is adopted.

It has a headphone port.

Equipped with a 3-digit tape counter.

The level meter has a built-in lighting lamp.
