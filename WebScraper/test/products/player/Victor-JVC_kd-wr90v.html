Commentary

Twin recording re-quick reverse cassette deck that can record continuously for 3 hours.

The reverse mechanism is equipped with a rotary head type fine axis reverse system.
This system is a 3-layer die-cast structure, and the head is not fixed directly to the head holder, but the head height can be adjusted by inserting a head clamp in between. This allows the head to be accurately positioned relative to the tape, so that the frequency characteristics in both forward and reverse directions, as well as the high-frequency characteristics, which tend to fluctuate, are always kept the same.
In addition, a high-precision mechanism has been realized by a mechanism that maintains initial performance, a highly durable molybdenum (baked) rotating part, a 2-point holding structure that minimizes head tip shake, and precision of 10 micron order.

The tape is inverted by an infrared sensor embedded in the guide pin of the tape. When the light emitted from the light-emitting element hits the splicing tape between the magnetic tape and the reader tape, the light-receiving element detects the change in the light and instructs the tape to be inverted.

The mechanism uses a silent mechanism with a 2-motor full logic + mechanical drive motor. The microcomputer detects the position of the cam linked to the mechanical drive motor to move all modes.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.
In addition, by adopting Dolby standard IC, operation is further stabilized and reliability is improved.

I mechanism and II mechanism alternate continuous recording is possible by microcomputer control.
By combining a program tuner and a timer and setting two C-90 tapes, it is possible to record continuously for up to three hours. Also, one source can be recorded simultaneously to the I and II mechanisms.

You can play back one mechanism while playing back the other.

Equipped with auto REC mute.
When this function is pressed in the REC mode, a silent space of approximately 4 to 5 seconds is automatically set to the REC standby mode. In addition, Victor's unique circuit design allows for no recording from the stop mode.

Equipped with auto tape select mechanism.

Equipped with Music Scan (1 song before and after I mechanism).

Equipped with a running indicator that shows the tape running mode.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism.
