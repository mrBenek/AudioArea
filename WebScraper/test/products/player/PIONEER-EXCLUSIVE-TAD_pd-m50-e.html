  • Pioneer PD-M50

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1987)

Commentary

A CD changer that can play 6 CDs with a special magazine.
The newly developed compact pickup and loading mechanism make it compact and slim.

You can play 32 songs of your favorite songs from 6 CDs.
In addition, random playback from 6 images is also possible.

Equipped with a pause program function convenient for tape editing.

It uses a direct program that can be programmed while listening to the music.

Digital filters are used in the audio section.

As a vibration countermeasure, we have adopted a honeycombed chassis and innovation actuators with excellent vibration resistance.

Uses a linear servo system to prevent skipping sound.

Playback starts automatically when the power is turned on.

In case you want to enjoy with only 1 CD, 1 magazine for performance is also included.

Remote controller with 10 keys is included.
