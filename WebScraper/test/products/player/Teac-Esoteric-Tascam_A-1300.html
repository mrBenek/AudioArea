Commentary

One motor type tape deck.

The head is a combination head combining an erasing head and a recording head.

Equipped with the bias switching mechanism adopted in A-2300.
Setting the Record Bias Switch to High matches the characteristics of low-noise tapes, and setting it to Normal matches the characteristics of standard tapes. When the Record Bias Switch is set to Normal, a bias current suitable for standard tapes flows to the recording head.

Equipped with an output volume, it is possible to match the specifications of the amplifier.

Equipped with the height adjustment mechanism of the reel stand to protect the tape from damage.

The reel clamp mechanism has a newly designed quick lock that eliminates the hassle of tape replacement.
In addition, by simply twisting the tip of the shaft, a click is inserted every 60 ° rotation.

It uses a full-scale electronic control system, and when the tape is finished, the operation lever automatically returns and the pinch roller also separates.
Also, when the power supply is cut off due to an accident or the like, the mechanism completely returns to the stopped state.

Since the diameter of the supply side (left side) tape is different at the beginning and end of the tape winding, the back tension increases and the tape speed slows down.
In order to prevent this speed deviation, it is equipped with a tension servo control that provides constant back tension at all times, overcoming the weak point of the one motor deck.

The head assembly is plug-in type, making it easy to maintain and replace the head.

Equipped with feather touch operation lever and independent pose button.

Microphone and line-input can be mixed.
