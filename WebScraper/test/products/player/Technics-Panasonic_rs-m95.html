Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with tension control by microcomputer.

The Quartz DD, which boasts high rotational accuracy, has been equipped with tension control by microcomputer. By adding appropriate back tension, precise head touch has been obtained.
The running mechanism adopts a two motor system using a flat-faced quartz DD motor, and a highly complete mechanism has been realized by applying highly reliable quartz oscillation to the reference frequency of the FG servo system.

The recording and reproducing heads use a new HPF head that has been improved for metal tape, resulting in a more vertical combination head.
In addition, a new double-gap Sendust ferrite head is used for erasing.

Equipped with 2 memory rewind mechanism.
The microcomputer displays the tape count digitally and allows two memory plays.

Equipped with a digital 2-color FL display with peak hold capable of displaying in the range of -42dB to + 8 dB.

Equipped with a 4-position tape selector with added metal position.

Equipped with built-in oscillator calibration function.

By connecting the optional wireless remote control unit, you can operate it from a remote location.
