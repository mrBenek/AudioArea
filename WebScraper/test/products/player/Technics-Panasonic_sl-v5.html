Commentary

Vertical type D. D. full auto player that can be installed even in narrow spaces.

Equipped with a linear tracking arm, a unique dynamic balance system realizes stable tracing performance.

High-precision rotational performance has been achieved by the use of an integral structure D. D. and an all-around detection F. G.

All operations such as arm up/down, forward / backward control can be performed with the dust cover closed.

Equipped with a plug-in connector type MM cartridge.
