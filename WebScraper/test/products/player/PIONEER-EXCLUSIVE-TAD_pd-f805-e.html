  • Pioneer PD-F805

  • ¥ 49,800 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A file-type CD player that can be used as a 50 + 1 CD library with 2 racks for 25 CDs and plus 1 slot.

Equipped with an additional 1 slot for immediate performance just by inserting it.

For higher sound quality, it is equipped with a legato link conversion and a high-speed pulse flow D/A converter.

Equipped with 3 types of file CD playback modes.
In All mode, you can play back all the files sequentially in sequence. In Single mode, you can select only one file to play. In Custom mode, you can register and play discs classified by genre or artist. With the PD-F805, you can set one genre.

With the Best Selection Memory function, you can automatically store your favorite songs by pressing the Best button while playing them. With the PD-F805, you can store up to 15 songs.

You can program play up to 24 songs.

Equipped with a rental CD function, it is possible to play only the first track of the disc arranged in the file section one after another.

Equipped with a scanning function, you can play part of a song one after another for 10 seconds and search for the song or disc you want to listen to in the file.
Previa Disk Scan plays back the most recently listened discs in order from the previous one, and starts normal playback when you press the Play button while playing your favorite disc.
In the disk scan, the first track of the filed disk is played in order, and the storage disk can be checked.
Track Scan plays all songs on a filed disc in sequence.
You can also play programs, scan custom files, and scan best selection memory.

It is equipped with functions such as memory backup and memory hold, direct disk access, direct track access, random play and repeat play.

Compatible with the CD-DECK synchro system.

Wireless remote control is included.
