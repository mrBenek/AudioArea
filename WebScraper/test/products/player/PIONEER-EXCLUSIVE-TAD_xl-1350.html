  • Pioneer XL-1350

  • ¥ 52,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A quartz PLL player that combines the high precision of a crystal oscillator with a highly reliable speed detection mechanism.

Quartz PLL DC servo hole motor is used as the drive motor.

The tone arm is equipped with a mass-concentrated tone arm to reduce the moment of inertia of the arm.

A coaxial suspension cabinet with greatly improved howling margin is used for the cabinet.

It uses an optical electronic detection auto-return mechanism to realize smooth operation without adversely affecting the sensitivity of the arm.
