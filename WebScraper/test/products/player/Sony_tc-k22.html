  • SONY TC-K22

  • ¥ 33,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

A metal-compatible cassette deck with a slim design.

A newly developed SD head made of high-density magnetic material is used for the head to cope with metal tape.
The erasing head is a MagneFocus 4-Gap erasing head in consideration of the nature of metal tape.

The motor is equipped with a green motor that is a specially polished FG servo motor for use in audio equipment.

It has a built-in Dolby NR system that reduces the tape hissing.

Equipped with wide-scale twin level meter of -20 to + 5 VU.

Equipped with 4-stage tape selector for simultaneous switching of bias and equalizer.

Equipped with a timer standby switch that can record absence and play back alarm clock.

Mic/Line input selector is mounted.

Uses a soft eject mechanism.

Equipped with auto shut-off mechanism.

Equipped with a stereo headphone jack.
