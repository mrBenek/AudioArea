Commentary

Reverse deck with thrust hold rotary head.

The head part uses a thrust hold rotary head.
The rotary head is supported by a 4-point support system in which four high-hardness bearings are arranged on the head base and ring part on the disk, and smooth head rotation is achieved.

An electronically controlled DC motor with high rotational accuracy for the capstan and a 2 + 1 motor system with separate motors for reel and mechanism drive. Combined with the thrust hold rotary head, this system enables highly accurate reverse reproduction in both forward and reverse directions.

Equipped with 3 nr of dbx system and Dolby B / C as noise reduction system.

Equipped with a wide range 3-color FL display of -40dB + 18 dB (when dbx operates).

It is equipped with functions such as music selector, auto rec mute and auto tape selector.

The left and right independent microphone input terminals are used.
It is also equipped with an automatic input switching function between microphone and line.
