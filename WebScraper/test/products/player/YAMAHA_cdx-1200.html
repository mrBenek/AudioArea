Audio Database

  • YAMAHA CDX-1200

  • ¥ 98,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

A CD player with improved sound quality by focusing on analog precision and purity.

It uses a unique I-PDM 1-bit DAC that does not generate any distortion in principle.
In addition, high-quality D/A conversion is achieved by the adoption of secondary noise shaping that reduces noise from 100 to 500 Hz while maintaining a high S/N ratio.

All the analog audio parts are designed as high quality class A operation.

The chassis and body are designed to be vibration-proof, and weight is increased by double construction.

Features include a program file that stores up to 100 pages of programs and automatically plays programs on each disk, 10-key direct access, index search, 5-mode repeat, random program play, tape edit, 25-track program play, delete program play, and autospace.

Equipped with an optical / coaxial digital output terminal.

It came with a remote control. There was also a black model.
