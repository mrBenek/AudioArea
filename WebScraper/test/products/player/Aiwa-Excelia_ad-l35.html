  • AIWA AD-L35

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A compact stereo cassette deck equipped with a tape level meter.

It is equipped with a tape level meter that is convenient for checking the remaining time of the tape. Press the tape level switch to display the remaining tape level on the R channel of the optical display.

Ultra-hard SH head is used for the head.

A DC servo motor and flywheel are used for the rotating system.

Equipped with 2-color 5-point LED optical display.

Equipped with a 3-position tape selector compatible with Metal, CrO2 and LH tapes.

Equipped with a Dolby system.

A slot-in method is used for mounting the tape.

When recording, the cassette is inserted after pressing the record button.
Because of the mechanism, even a recording tape with a folded tab to prevent erroneous erasure will be erased when the recording operation is performed.
