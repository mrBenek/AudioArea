Commentary

Digital output and feature-rich CD player.

It is equipped with two digital output terminals, optical and coaxial.

The digital filter uses a newly developed four times oversampling digital filter.
By shifting up the sampling frequency from 44.1 kHz to 176.4 kHz, interference in the audio band is eliminated and the load on the low-pass filter at the later stage is reduced.

The D/A converter uses a 2 dac configuration with independent left and right channels. Crosstalk, separation and S/N are improved by eliminating interference between channels.

Optica Link (optical transmission method) is adopted, and digital noise is eliminated by converting digital signals into light and transmitting them.

A triple suspension floating mechanism is adopted for the mechanism part.
In this mechanism, the pickup base is placed on the mechanical base via an insulator, and the mechanical base is also placed on the chassis base via an insulator.
By floating mounting with 3-layer structure suspension combined with insulation foot, the effect of resonance and vibration is eliminated.

The chassis structure uses a W dimple chassis that combines two different direct dents.
The dents with different resonance points effectively cancel out vibrations, shutting out external vibrations, sound pressure, and vibrations generated from inside.

Equipped with a multi-disk editing function.
This function is a further development of the conventional editing function. You can program songs that fit within a specified time from up to six discs.

With a built-in CD disc memory function, the CD player can store information for each disc or song and display text on the display when playing. You can store combinations of up to 10 characters including letters, numbers and symbols.

Equipped with a sync terminal, you can record and edit in conjunction with Victor's sync-compatible cassette deck.

Up to 32 songs can be programmed while the tray is ejected.
Additional memory is also available during program performance.

There are functions such as random play, introscan and 5 kinds of repeat in this city.

Equipped with a large FL display.

Equipped with a remote control electric volume that can be used as a headphone volume.
This volume uses a motor drive to ensure sound quality, and the operation status can be checked by flashing lights.

It is equipped with a timer play function that the microcomputer plays while randomly selecting songs.

Uses a tray that supports 8 cm disks.

Wireless remote control is included.
