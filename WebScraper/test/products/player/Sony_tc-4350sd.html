  • SONY TC-4350SD

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1974)

Commentary

A cassette deck with all front operation.

It has a built-in Dolby system.
In addition, an F & F head is used for the head.

Equipped with a 3-stage switching tape selector that is also compatible with the newly developed Duadferi chrome cassette.

The Sony Limiter recording method is adopted.

Equipped with auto shut-off mechanism.

Equipped with a memory counter.

Microphone / line mixing is possible.

Equipped with line-out volume.

Equipped with peak level indicator.
