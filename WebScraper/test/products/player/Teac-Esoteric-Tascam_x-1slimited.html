Commentary

This CD player was released to commemorate the 5th anniversary of the birth of Eso Telik and the 10th anniversary of the birth of CDs. Based on X-1s, this CD player is made with Japanese traditional craft technology.

The X-1sLimited was carefully finished over 40 processes using the traditional Japanese lacquer, and the front panel was finished in three types : "kinko dried sweet potato Furuki", "oxidized silver" and "Rikyu Kanaji".
Gold Furuki is coated with black lacquer, covered with silver foil, and coated with wood-ground lacquer to bring out the taste and depth of amber Furuki.
The oxidized silver fabric is coated with black lacquer and then coated with silver foil oxidized with sulfur etc. at random. The hard coat gives a soft texture even though it is metal.
The Rikyu Kanaji is painted black with gold powder and then painted with wood-ground lacquer to create a delicate shade and three dimensional beauty.
All letters on each finish are gold lacquer finish, and top plate and side plate are black octal-gloss lacquer finish.

The basic structure of the X-1s is the same as that of the X-1s. However, the X-1s is equipped with an additional AT&T ST connector for digital output in consideration of combination with a variety of high-performance D/A converters. This makes it possible to use short wavelength beams in phase with minimal jitter.

The New VRDS mechanism has been adopted for the timing mechanism, and by crimping the CD onto a double turntable made of machined brass + aluminum, the disc surface blur has been eliminated.

A 1 kg zinc die-cast mechanical base is used for the mechanical base supporting the mechanism.

It is equipped with pickup balance drive and new silent servo circuit to promote ultra-low noise vibration system.

Mechanism construction uses a center mount method.

The D/A / A conversion part, and a newly developed 1/16 shift 20-bit 4 dac is mounted. ZDII

The clock oscillation circuit is equipped with a high-precision clock within ± 50 ppm of average absolute frequency deviation.

A 24k gold-plated substrate is used.

It adopts balanced transmission.
