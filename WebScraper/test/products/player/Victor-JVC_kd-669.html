  • VICTOR/JVC KD-669

  • ¥ 95,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with circuits such as ANRS based on 2-motor ID mechanism.

The ID mechanism with 2-motor system improves driving performance.

ANRS is installed as a noise reduction system, and a calibration mechanism is also installed.

It is equipped with a tape selector that switches bias and equalizer independently, and a new time constant equalizer is used for chrome tape.

All-transistor non-contact logic control circuit is used for operation.

Equipped with memory counter of auto-stop and auto-play switching type.

The recording / reproducing head uses a super chronios head made of ferrite material.

I/O independent volume is installed.

Equipped with input switching of microphone and line.

Equipped with peak level indicator.

You can also record messages by adding a timer.

ANRS automatic detection is possible by the sensing method.

There was a remote control unit sold separately.
