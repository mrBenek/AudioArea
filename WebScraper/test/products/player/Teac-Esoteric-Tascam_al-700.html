  • TEAC AL-700

  • ¥ 198,000 (around 1978)

Commentary

A cassette deck that achieves sound quality that matches the open-reel.

A high-performance drive system similar to a measuring instrument incorporating data recorder technology is adopted for the drive system. Four new technologies promote the advantages of the closed-loop dual capstan stable tape drive system.
The four new technologies are a twin-belt closed-loop dual capstan, a semi-fixed head with high mounting accuracy, a self-aligning pinch roller, and a magne-float capstan.

High hardness permalloy head is used for the head.

Equipped with automatic switching mechanism for bias and equalizer.

Equipped with photoelectric auto end stop mechanism.

Equipped with Rec Mute, one touch, signalless recording is possible.

Equipped with a timer mechanism.

Equipped with memory auto-stop / auto-play mechanism.

Equipped with a Dolby Copy Switch, it can be used to copy from Dolby Encoded Tape.

Equipped with mono / stereo switching mechanism linked to My Kujac.

Separately sold dbx unit RX-10 can be connected.

Remote Control Unit RC-90 (sold separately) can be used.
