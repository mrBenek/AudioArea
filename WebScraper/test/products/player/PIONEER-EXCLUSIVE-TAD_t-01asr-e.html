  • Pioneer T-01ASR

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1996)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with FLAT System and Dolby S.

It is equipped with the FLEX (Frequency Level Expander) System, which detects whether recorded tapes are muffled and automatically amplifies the high-frequency level to improve sound quality.

Equipped with Dolby S NR as a noise reduction system, noise reduction of up to 24 dB in the high range and up to 10 dB in the low range is realized, and bleeding noise is also greatly reduced.

It is equipped with the FLAT System and Dolby HX PRO that correct the frequency characteristics of the tape to be flat.

Equipped with the last memory function, it can store FLAT System data, main switch positions, etc.

Equipped with a CD-DECK synchro function, it can be linked with a Pioneer CD player equipped with a CD-DECK synchro function and automatically linked operation is possible.

Wireless remote control is included.
