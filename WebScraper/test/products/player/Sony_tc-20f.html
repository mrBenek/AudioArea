Commentary

At a depth of 14 cm, this compact cassette deck is packed with a mechanism that responds to the ease of use and reliability required for the deck.

By using the 3 switches of counter memory, autoplay and Oatley wind, you can perform up to 12 kinds of multi-play such as headband performance and repeat performance.

Logical control allows quick button operation without burdening the tape.

We have adopted a two motor mechanism using a green motor for the capstan and a high torque motor for the reel drive.

Equipped with LED peak program meter.

F & F head is used for the head.

Equipped with a 3-step tape selector independent of bias / equalizer.

Equipped with Dolby NR system.

Equipped with Rec Mute.

It is compatible with the Sony Micro Stereo "Falcon".

There are 2 types, TC-20F with silver finish and TC-20FB with black finish.

Separately sold wired remote control allows remote operation.
