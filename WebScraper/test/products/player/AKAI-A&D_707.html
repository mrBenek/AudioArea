  • AKAI 707

  • ¥ 49,500 (around 1965?)

Commentary

Tape recorder with monaural design.

The tape speed can be switched by moving the slide capstan up and down with one touch.

It uses a new mechanism that automatically turns off the power when the tape is finished.
