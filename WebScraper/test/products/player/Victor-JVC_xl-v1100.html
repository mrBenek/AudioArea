Commentary

A reference CD player of the Digifine series aiming at the ultimate in digital audio.

The internal structure adopts a D/A2 box design in which the digital part and analog audio part are completely independent.
In order to separate the audio section from the digital servo system and power supply section, it is housed in a shield box using a thick non-magnetic chassis.
In addition, all chassis other than the audio section are made of a non-magnetic structure consisting of extruded aluminum and copper-plated steel plates to eliminate magnetic distortion. In addition, by placing damping materials at important points, harmful resonance is suppressed.

The newly developed High Precision 3-beam pickup is used in the pickup section.
In this pickup, the distance between the main beam and the servo beam has been shortened from the conventional 22 μ m to 16 μ m. This accelerates the response to scratches and dirt on the disk, increases the amount of information from the servo beam, and improves the stability of tracing.
This enhances the effect of the Y servo, which superimposes and cancels out the abnormal waveform of the preceding and succeeding beams.

A V servo has been newly added to the servo circuit.
In the V servo system, a highly sensitive vibration detection element V sensor senses external vibration and sound pressure from speakers, and controls servo gain quickly.

It adopts IS (Independent Suspension) mechanism which is a large insulation support structure.
The IS mechanism has a structure in which a disk drive and an optical pickup are floating mounted with a large insulator on a mechanical base made of damping material, and shuts off disturbance vibration and sound pressure.
In addition, rotational accuracy is improved by a chucking center clamp mechanism that reduces disc eccentricity and surface runout and by a thick shaft motor.

The digital filter uses a 4-fold oversampling digital filter to increase the sampling frequency from 44.1 kHz to 176.4 kHz before D/A conversion to reduce interference in the audio band.
As a result, the structure of the analog filter at the latter stage can be simplified, and sound quality with less phase shift and distortion can be obtained.

The D/A converter uses an independent D/A converter for the left and right channels, and the output signal of the D/A converter uses a current transmission method instead of a conventional voltage transmission method to improve the ability to eliminate digital noise.

Newly developed digital filters and D/A converters are used to improve phase and crosstalk characteristics.

In the audio section, the printed foil on the upper part of the double-sided printed circuit board is grounded to prevent a slight induction noise, and the L/R symmetric isometric pattern is used for the signal line to eliminate mutual interference.

We use high-quality parts such as high-speed diodes with low pulse noise and wide-band low-noise electrolytic capacitors for audio. We also select the best products for signal processors and other digital ICs.

The power supply unit is composed of three independent power, servo system, digital system and audio system, eliminating interference between each block.

Equipped with two gold-plated output terminals, fixed and variable.

Subcode output is installed.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.
The volume uses a motor drive system that emphasizes sound quality.

Equipped with a large FL multi-display.

It comes with a wireless remote control that allows you to play a program of 15 songs and open / close the disc tray.
