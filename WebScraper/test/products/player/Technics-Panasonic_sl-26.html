  • Technics SL-26/SL-26MK

  • ¥ 33,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Auto return player that adopts FG servo DC motor.

A FG servo DC motor with large starting torque and low load fluctuation is used for the drive motor.

33 Each 1/3 and 45 rpm has an independent rotation speed adjustment mechanism.
It is also equipped with a strobe illuminator, which is essential for fine adjustment.

The cartridge contains a MM type cartridge built into the shell.

Equipped with an auto return mechanism, the detection mechanism works at the end of the performance, and the tone arm automatically returns to the rest and the player's power is turned off.

Besides SL-26, there was a Shiraki cabinet type SL-26MK.
