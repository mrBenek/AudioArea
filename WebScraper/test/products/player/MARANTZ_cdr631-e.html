  • Marantz CDR631

  • ¥ 85,000 (around 2000)

Commentary

A commercial CD recorder compatible with CD-R/RW.

It is equipped with a synchronous recording start function for digital / analog without missing head.

It supports the input and display of CD-TEXT character data, and the title can be input to the disc and each track.

With a built-in digital loop (COAX) output terminal, it is possible to record multiple copies simultaneously.

Equipped with digital recording volume (+ 6 db ~ -63dB, 1 db step) and balance function.

Equipped with an auto-finalize recording function, it is possible to complete lead-in / lead-out writing with one operation.

It has a sample rate converter (SRC) and supports digital sources from 11 kHz to 56 kHz.
Also supports 44.1 kHz SRC bypass recording (guaranteed source frequency deviation 44.1 kHz ± 100 ppm)

Equipped with copy protect selection function, setting free / restricted / prohibited is possible.

24-bit D/A converter and 20-bit A/D converter are installed.

Automatically recognizes CD/MD/DAT track IDs.

Equipped with 2 x finalizing / erasing functions (Track/Disc/TOC).

The Route 3 digital input jacks (AES/EBU, RCA-pin, OPT (square)) and XLR/RCA-analog inputs are used as input jacks.

Route 3 digital outputs (RCA, OPT and RCA-loop) and one analog outputs are provided.

Fully compliant with Red Book and Orange Book PartII/PartIII.

Equipped with 3-pole type AC cord.
