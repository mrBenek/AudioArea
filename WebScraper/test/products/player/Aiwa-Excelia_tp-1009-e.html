Commentary

A stereo cassette deck using a pop-up system.

It uses a cassette pop-up system and the cassette pops out just by pressing the button.

Compatible head is used for the head.
A 4-pole hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the motor.

All push button system is adopted for operation.

A built-in stereo preamplifier using silicon transistors.

It is equipped with a level meter, 3-digit tape counter, temporary stop device, headphone jack, etc.

Comes with a single directional dynamic microphone for stereo recording.
