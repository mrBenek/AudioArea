  • McIntosh MCD7005

  • ¥ 450,000 (around 1987)

Commentary

A CD player developed with the aim of becoming a high-quality player by putting Macintosh technology into it.

The D/A converter consists of a full 16-bit 4 x oversampling D/A converter, a double digital filter and a special error correction circuit.

In addition to improving the mechanical accuracy of the optical system, the effect of external vibration is eliminated by incorporating it into a structural material made of aluminum die cast.
In addition, the self-centering support spindle system improves the centering accuracy of the CD, and along with the servo system of the optical system, the reading accuracy is also improved.

It is equipped with a headphone port and a ladder attenuator electronic volume.

It is equipped with a wireless remote control and can be controlled with the remote control of the control amplifier C31V.
