Commentary

This is a cassette deck where you can enjoy cassettes easily.

Low-noise silicon transistors are used in the amplifier section.

The head uses a narrow gap 1 micron head.

Equipped with AGC recording and AGC stereo meter that prevents recording failure.

It has a pilot lamp that can check the running condition of the tape.

Newly developed DC motor is adopted, and cycle change is not required.
