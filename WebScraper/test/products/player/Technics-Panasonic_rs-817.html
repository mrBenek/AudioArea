Audio Database

  • National RS-817

  • ¥ 53,900 (around 1974)

Commentary

An 8-track recorder equipped with an FM/AM tuner.

Equipped with FM/AM radio, you can record while listening to it.
It also has an external input terminal so that it can be connected to a record player or other device.

Mixing play is possible by using the included microphone.

By connecting four speakers, you can play four channels (speaker matrix).
