Commentary

A tape deck based on A-7030GSL and equipped with new mechanism.

The head consists of four heads, two track erase-record-playback heads, plus a four track playback head, enabling you to play four track tapes.

A high-hardness permalloy head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
Careful consideration is given to the machining accuracy and assembly of each head core, and the accuracy of gap width is also improved, resulting in flat characteristics.
In addition, the head shape is hyperbolic and the head touch is stable.

It is equipped with a peak level meter that can confirm the peak of the signal.
The VU meter, which has been used to monitor the recording level, is designed to indicate the average value of the signal waveform in order to grasp the sense of volume, whereas the peak level meter is designed to indicate the peak value of the signal waveform and can monitor the pulse component contained in the signal used in raw recording.
In general, even if a pulse signal with an attack time shorter than 10 ms is clipped and distorted, it is difficult to perceive it as distortion in the sense of hearing. Therefore, the peak level meter of A-7030mkIV uses an attack time of 10 ms. The recovery time of 750 ms is long, making it easier to read the high-speed response of the indicator. In addition, in order to monitor the dynamic range, the scale instructions range is expanded from -40dB to + 5 dB with a logarithmic amplifier. Moreover, the interval of the scale around 0 dB is widened, making it easier to read.

Equipped with a bias equalizer independent 2-stage switching mechanism to cope with low-noise tapes.

A 3-motor mechanism is used for the mechanism part.
In addition, the mechanism is firmly supported by the 6 mm thick panel chassis configuration that combines aluminum and stainless steel.

The combination of a smoothly rotating impedance roller that absorbs the wow flutter and a tension arm that absorbs the rough motion of the tape at the start ensures stable tape travel.

It uses a new reel clamper that does not need a correction sheet and can easily attach the No. 10 reel.

It has a cue button.
If you press the Q button in the fast-forward state, you can monitor the sound and find the beginning of the song or the position you want to edit.

The microphone input and line input volumes are designed to be independent, making it possible to mix the microphone and line.

Simultaneous monitoring is possible.

It uses a tension changeover switch that can be used with No. 10 / No. 7 reels.
By combining the separately sold remote control unit, it can be operated from a remote location.

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining the timer control adapter sold separately with the timer.
