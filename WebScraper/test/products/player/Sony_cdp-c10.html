Commentary

A CD player that can store 10 CDs.

It uses a magazine system that can store 10 CDs, and allows one touch direct music selection with independent disc selection keys and music selection keys.
This magazine is compatible with Sonny's in-car CD-player CDjuke, so you can enjoy both.

There are three play modes available : 10-disc continuous play, up to 20 songs programmed across multiple discs, and 10-disc shuffle play.

Equipped with a digital filter, distortion factor characteristics and phase characteristics are improved.

A CD LSI is used to increase the integration density and reduce the number of peripheral parts by half, thereby realizing a simple and reliable circuit.

The power supply section uses seven independent stabilized power supplies that separate the digital and audio power supplies.

Equipped with a centralized display for switching between disc No / music No and music No / minutes / seconds, and for displaying the progress of the program.

You can check the program while playing or add or change it.

Repeat is compatible with all three performance modes.

Equipped with 2 systems of fixed and variable gold-plated output.

Equipped with auto pause function.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Equipped with a timer play function.

It comes with a wireless remote control with volume control.
