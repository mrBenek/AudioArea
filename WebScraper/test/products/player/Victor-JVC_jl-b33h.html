  • VICTOR/JVC JL-B33H

  • ¥ 32,500 (around 1973)

Commentary

A record player based on the SRP-B33M with no resonance and vibration.

The drive part uses a belt drive system, and the drive motor uses a 6-pole hysteresis synchronous outer rotor motor to achieve stable rotation. In addition, carefully selected polyurethane belt is used for the belt.

Turntable is made of high precision aluminum alloy die-cast.

The tone arm uses the TH (tracing and hold) method used in the JL-B55 series.
In the TH method, the tracing ability of the arm is improved by lowering the center of gravity of the arm moving part. In addition, the force that tries to return to the correct position acts, so that even warped records can be traced stably.
In addition, it is equipped with a pressurization mechanism that can directly read up to 3g with 0.1g scale, an oil dump-type arm lifter, and a string-suspended inside four scan seller.

In order to prevent resonance of the tone arm, damping material is packed inside the arm pipe, and a strong impact press head shell is adopted.

The output code uses a low-capacity code that also supports CD-4.

A MM type cartridge is used.
This cartridge is a high compliance type with a 0.5 mil diamond needle and can also be used for CD-4 by replacing the needle tip with 4DT-1X.

The S / N ratio is improved by applying a special anti-vibration NR (Non-Resonance) coating surface of the motor board made of thick metal.
In addition, external vibration is suppressed by adopting a 25 mm thick assembly structure for the cabinet part.

In order to prevent howling, a special shape is used for the rubber feet.

A push-button type is used for changing the speed.

In order to confirm the correct needle tip position when the cartridge is replaced, there is an overhang indication point which is also used as a sign lamp.

An oil dump-type arm lifter is attached to the arm part.

Two shell stands are provided so that you can stand the shell while the cartridge is attached.
It also has an accessory space that can store accessories such as a record cleaner.

Comes with an acrylic dust cover.
It can be used at any angle with a free hinge, and can be removed when not needed.
