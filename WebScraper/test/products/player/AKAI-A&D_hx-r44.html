  • AKAI HX-R44

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1984)

Commentary

A quick reverse deck that pursues the sound quality of reverse in the same way as GX-R66/R55.

The BSP mechanism, dual-wide tape guide system, and accurate reverse head system are used in the mechanism section to improve stability and sound quality during reverse operation.

HD head is used for the head part.

Equipped with a REC cancel mechanism.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

An electronic Rec Level volume is used.

Equipped with an IPLS mechanism that can be used to start a song.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Equipped with auto-mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism.

Equipped with a reverse selector function.

A 2-color FL display is used for the display part.

Equipped with a remote control function and can be operated from a remote location by using a separately sold remote control unit.
