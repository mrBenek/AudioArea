Commentary

In response to the needs of the cassette era, this stereo cassette deck is equipped with functions such as the automatic noise reduction system ANRS, ID mechanism and tape selector.

Equipped with ANRS (Automatic Noise Reduction System) as an automatic noise reduction device.
In this method, hissing is also reduced by increasing the recording level at a constant rate when recording at a low volume, which is easily heard by the tape-hiss. During playback, only the raised level portion of the recording is restored.
In addition, in order to maintain compatibility with other noise reduction methods, the recording and playback characteristics curve is adjusted to other formats, and it is effective even for Dolby tapes.

It is equipped with a tape selector out the characteristics of normal tape, SH tape, and chrome tape.
For SH tapes, the equalizer circuit is switched. For chrome tapes, the bias current and equalizer circuit are switched simultaneously.

The recording and re-head uses a Super Chronios head with a narrow gap made of ferrite material.

The drive section is equipped with an ID (Independent Drive) mechanism to ensure ideal driving performance.
Unlike general drive systems, this ID mechanism is an independent drive mechanism that separates the drive of the capstan that drives the tape and the drive of the reel stand that winds the tape. Since the rotation from the motor is divided into two drives, other rotating parts do not affect the tape running.
In addition, a 4-pole hysteresis synchronous motor with large torque developed for this system is used for the motor, and stable speed and characteristics are obtained by a back tension mechanism that always applies constant force.

Equipped with photoelectric auto-stop / eject mechanism by Cbs.

Built-in tape heading function by memory counter.

Equipped with a peak level indicator that indicates a transient large input signal when the lamp is lit.
In addition, you can easily record at the optimum level by selecting the level at which the lamp does not turn on.

A constant voltage circuit is used to stabilize the bias current.

In consideration of maintenance such as head cleaning, a removable Head cover and holder are adopted.

Equipped with 2-step switching function of headphone output.
