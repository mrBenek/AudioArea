Commentary

A record player with a combination of direct drive and heavyweight cabinet.

The drive system uses a direct drive system, and the drive motor uses an 8-pole 24-slot DC servo motor to achieve smooth low-speed rotation.

The JL-B-B77 series turntable plate is used as it is. This turntable plate has a detailed finish with dynamic balance adjusted one by one to achieve excellent wow flutter characteristics.
In addition, the high-grade rubber covering is used as it is. Not only does it hide the metal background, it also adheres to the turntable plate to suppress the squeal and hold the record firmly.

The tone arm uses the TH method. By lowering the position of the main weight, the center of gravity is designed to be lower than the fulcrum of rotation, which realizes excellent tracing.
In addition, in order to eliminate resonance, anti-vibration material is packed inside the pipe, and an excellent impact press head shell is also used.

A MM type cartridge is used.
The characteristics of this cartridge are improved by the use of a sandwich damper and wire suspension, and CD-4 playback is possible by changing the needle tip to one for CD-4.

Uses a low-capacity output cord.

Similar to the JL-B77 series, the cabinet is made of luxurious beech wood laminate with a thickness of 60 mm. High-density beech wood is stacked in 41 layers, and only the motor and arm mounting parts are hollowed out.
As a result, even slight internal vibrations are controlled, and vibrations from outside are also suppressed.

A specially-shaped rubber isolator with height adjustment mechanism is used to suppress external vibration.

Built-in reflective and easy-to-see strobe.

The dust cover has a free hinge. It is also removable.

It is equipped with an oil dump type arm lifter and a thread suspension type inside four scan seller.
