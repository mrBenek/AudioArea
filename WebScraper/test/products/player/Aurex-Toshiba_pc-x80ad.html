Commentary

An address compatible cassette deck that supports metal tapes.

It is equipped with an address originally developed by Orex as a noise reduction system.
By compressing and expanding the signal, the hissing is reduced to an inaudible level. Moreover, by greatly expanding the dynamic range, excellent playback sound is realized.

The PC-X80AD also has a Dolby noise reduction system.
Address and cannot be used simultaneously.

Equipped with a dedicated position corresponding to metal tape.

The AS head has excellent abrasion resistance and magnetic characteristics, and achieves high sound quality.
In addition, a ferrite head with a high erasure rate is adopted for erasure.

The meter uses a 201 segment plasma type bar graph meter.
By setting it to 201 segments / ch, it is possible to read finely like an analog type even from a remote place.

A 2-motor IC logic control system is adopted for the operation part to improve stability and operability.

It uses a special hold mechanism and holds tapes securely with 4 chucks.

The mechanism is integrated with an aluminum die-cast panel.

Equipped with an autoplay mechanism.

Equipped with a memory counter.

By combining with the separately sold remote control unit RM-15, it can be operated from a remote location.
