  • Pioneer CT-300

  • ¥ 44,800 (released in 1979)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with FL meter.

Uses a high-precision 2-color FL meter that covers a wide range of -20dB to + 8 dB, and responds to rapidly changing music signals.

The drive system combines a high-torque DC servo motor with a twin belt system to reduce uneven rotation.

Equipped with a Dolby system.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.
