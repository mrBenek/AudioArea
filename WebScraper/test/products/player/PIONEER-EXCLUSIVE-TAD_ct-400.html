Commentary

This is a stereo cassette deck equipped with functions such as an automatic head-out mechanism, absence recording function and Dolby based on high basic performance.

The drive section is equipped with a highly stable high-torque DC servo motor.
Approx. 1,400kg-cm2Excellent rotational performance is obtained by the synergistic effect of large flywheels with 80 mm outer diameter obtain inertial energy, capstan with precision processing, and newly developed twin belt system.

Equipped with Pioneer Music Select System (PMS), you can quickly start the current song or start the next song.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

It uses a timer start button, and by combining with a timer sold separately, it is possible to record absence and play an alarm clock.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a 3-stage tape selector of STD, CrO2, Fe-Cr, recording and playback utilizing the characteristics of various tapes are possible.

The L/R facing vertical type is used for the level meter.

It has a queue and review mechanism.
