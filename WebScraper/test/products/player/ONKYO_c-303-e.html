  • ONKYO C-303

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1994)

Commentary

A CD player with improved reproduction capability of music signals.

The D/A converter is equipped with a Accupulse DA converter.
In order to improve the accuracy of the linear single-bit D/A converter, it is equipped with an accupulse quartz system using a new small crystal oscillation element with thorough anti-vibration measures to improve the reproducibility of music signals.

It is equipped with a music file function which can memorize up to 204 pieces of the order of performance of CD.

Equipped with an optical digital output.

It is equipped with a time edit function. When you specify the tape size, you can specify the music to be recorded on the A side and wait at a pause after the A side is finished.
It is also equipped with a peak search function that searches and checks the peak level of the CD at high speed in order to set the proper recording level, and plays the part of the maximum level for 6 seconds.

Equipped with 5-mode repeat function (all tracks / one track / A-B / memory / shuffle).

Equipped with 3-stage dimmer control function.

A remote control is included.
