Commentary

This CD player was born in pursuit of higher purity sound reproduction and comfortable functions.

The D/A converter is an 8 fs real 18 bit direct linear conversion with one chip real 18 bitLSI on each left and right. Furthermore, linear envelope technology to reduce 0 cross distortion has been added to achieve high precision conversion.

The AcFOCUS system is used to improve the phase difference that occurs when pit signals are read by laser light and converted into RF signals.

It uses a magnet clamp type stabilizer, and since the stabilizer and clamper arm rotate the disk in contact with the sun, unwanted vibration is prevented from being transmitted to the pickup, etc.

The chassis is a honeycomb chassis that reduces low-frequency external vibration, and a hybrid honeycomb chassis with superfine pile electrostatic flocking.
In addition, a side wood is mounted to increase the vibration damping effect of the entire bonnet.

Equipped with a computer program editing function, the microcomputer automatically selects music according to the tape time.

It is equipped with digital level control which can adjust the volume with remote control while keeping the volume of the amplifier as it is.

Equipped with an auto space function that automatically puts a space of 3 seconds between songs.

Equipped with deck sync function for one touch tape editing (available on Pioneer's cassette deck with CD-DECK sync mark).
