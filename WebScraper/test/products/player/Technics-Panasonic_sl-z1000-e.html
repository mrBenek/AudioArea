Commentary

This CD player unit uses a vibration control structure to achieve high-fidelity CD playback.

In order to realize a highly rigid and robust chassis structure, we have adopted a 2 mm thick frame for the outer chassis, 2 mm thick vibration control steel plate for the bottom plate, 3 mm thick aluminum plate with vibration control treatment for the top plate, and 8 mm thick extruded aluminum for the front panel.
We have also adopted TNRC (Technics Non-Resonance Compound) insulators.

The internal construction uses a symmetric structure with the mechanism at the center, and vibration resistance is improved by achieving the optimum weight balance.

The power transformer is filled with special resin in a special case, floated with rubber, and mounted on a chassis to reduce vibration.

The mechanism section employs a magnet suction type vibration resistant linear traverse mechanism to eliminate mechanical rattling and prevent vibration of the optical pickup due to external vibration. The optical deck section has a structure in which a load is equally applied to four floating support points, and vibration resistance is improved by keeping it in a horizontal state without twisting.
In addition, by adopting aluminum die-casting, dimensional stability and high rigidity are realized.

It uses microcomputer controlled speed control software loading to realize quiet and smooth movement. It also responds to load fluctuations due to variations in frictional force of the mechanism and temperature changes with constant movement.

The spindle acceleration control for disk has been adopted to shorten the time from disk stop to playback.

Uses high-torque brushless D. D. spindle motor and high-precision glass press lens.

Equipped with two optical digital outputs.

Wireless remote control is included.
With this remote control, you can perform fast manual search / slow manual search, 20-key direct play and 32-step program.
