  • VICTOR/JVC KD-667mkII

  • ¥ 51,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with ANRS and new chrome time constant equalizer.

ANRS is installed as a noise reduction system to reduce tape hiss.
You can also play Dolby B music tapes.

Equipped with an equalizer and a tape selector with independent bias switching.
In addition, a new time constant equalizer has been adopted for chrome tape.

The recording and re-head uses a ferrite material narrow gap super Cronios head.

A 3-stage direct-coupled E-E feedback amplifier is used recording amplifier.

Equipped with photoelectric auto-stop mechanism.

Input / output independent volume is adopted.

It uses a detachable Head cover and cassette holder.
