  • SANSUI D-W10

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1984)

Commentary

Double reverse cassette deck for double recording, series recording and reverse recording.

It is equipped with a double recording system that allows simultaneous recording on the a deck and b deck, a series recording system that starts the b deck round trip recording after the a deck round trip recording is finished, and synchronized dubbing / double-speed dubbing that makes the a deck play standby and the b deck record standby when the dubbing switch is turned on.

It is equipped with functions such as Computer Edit Dubbing, which allows you to edit and record by changing the order of songs by repeatedly recording and re-muting, while program playback of 15 randomly selected songs in RMPS memory for 15 songs on deck a, and editing and recording by repeating recording and re-muting on deck b.
In addition, it is equipped with a RCPS (Rec Cancel Program Skip) mechanism that cancels any song you do not like during Computer Edit / Dubbing and automatically starts Computer Edit / Dubbing from the next reserved song.

After the round-trip playback of the a deck is finished, you can enjoy the long play of 2 full tapes by the relay playback to the A side of the b deck.
If you set it to repeat mode, it plays continuously from deck a to deck b and from deck b to deck a.

Equipped with an auto-fader for fade-in and fade-out.
After fading out, a 4-second rec-mute is performed and the recording is standby, so you can create an appropriate interval between songs.

It is equipped with a mixing dubbing function that enables playback mixing during playback, mixing recording between source and microphone, and mixing recording between the playback sound of deck a and microphone.

By utilizing full logic control of microcomputer control and making unique one key control, simple operability is realized.

It is equipped with a blank search to find the unrecorded part of the tape.
If the silence continues for more than 10 seconds, it returns to the end of the last song, re-mutes for 4 seconds, and becomes recording standby.

It is equipped with an ALC that automatically adjusts the recording level according to the input signal to prevent clipping at excessive peak input, which often occurs in digital sources. The recording level can also be manually adjusted.

Equipped with a Dolby B/C type noise reduction system.
In addition, in order to prevent double working of the Dolby during dubbing, it is designed to be Dolby OFF.

Equipped with a time-sharing rewind (TSR) function that allows you to rewind and fast forward one deck while the other deck is playing.

It is equipped with an intro skip mechanism that plays the intro part of the song one after another for about 10 seconds.

It is equipped with an AMPS function that allows you to find the beginning of the previous or next song.

Equipped with an auto tape selector that automatically detects metal / high bias (CrO2) / normal.

Timer recording and playback is possible by connecting an optional audio timer.

Equipped with a 5-point peak level indicator.

It uses an electronic buzzer that sounds when an operation command is received.

Equipped with a Rec Mute function that allows you to make 4-second intervals between songs with one touch.
