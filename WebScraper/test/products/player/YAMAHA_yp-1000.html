Commentary

A record player with a direct drive system.

The turntable has a diameter of 310 mm and a weight of 2.4 kg.
This turntable was machined from aluminum plank by precision machining of micron order, and the moment of inertia is 340 kg ・ cm.2In addition, the resonance of the turntable itself is taken into consideration, and the resonance of the turntable is eliminated by adopting the surface contact method at the joint with the direct drive motor.
In addition, the rubber sheet has been replaced with a rubber ring that is designed to fit the material and shape of the contact surface with the record.

The drive system uses a direct drive system, and the motor uses a 20 pole 60-slot brushless DC servo motor developed especially for phono motors.
This motor uses a non-contact electronic control system with an electronic commutator circuit consisting of a position sensing coil, a position sensing iron core, a transistor and a diode, eliminating mechanical reduction mechanisms such as rim drives and belt drives.

For 33 1/3 and 45 rpm speed switching, an electronic speed switching method via a one touch operation relay is adopted so that the specified rotation speed can be selected reliably. In addition, a ± 6% variable pitch control mechanism is installed independently at 33 1/3 and 45 rpm.
In addition, the use of a stabilized power supply reduces the influence of fluctuations in external power supply.

For the tone arm, the UA-7 type tone arm of ST ax company is adopted.
The UA-7 has a one point support structure with a pivot with a stabilizer in the bearing part, and realizes a high initial motion sensitivity of 5 mg. In addition, to improve tracing ability, a pivot roller type cam is adopted in the inside for scan seller. In addition, usability is pursued by setting a canceler lock clutch to accurately balance the needle pressure.
In addition, the head shell made of light alloy die-cast with tapered rib that is strong against twist and resonance and the height adjustment of the tone arm are adopted.

The cartridge uses V-15 Type III, a SHURE MM cartridge.
The V-15 Type III uses a newly developed laminated magnetic core and stylus assembly with 25% reduced effective mass.

The player board is made of aluminum die-cast with rib processing, and the punching metal of the bottom plate is also rib processed to provide thorough anti-vibration measures.
The acrylic cover is made of thick plates, and the insulator is equipped with a height adjustment mechanism.
