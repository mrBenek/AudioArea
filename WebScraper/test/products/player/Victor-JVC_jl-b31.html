Commentary

Direct drive player using AC servo motor.

A newly developed AC servo motor with less cocking is used as the drive motor.
The rotation control circuit is a system using 148 detection coils, and the start-up is quick, and there is almost no overshoot or undershoot when entering constant speed.
In addition, since it is a transformer-less system, there is no degradation of SN ratio due to magnetic vibration or leakage flux of the transformer.

Strobe, we have adopted a direct illumination system and installed a high-brightness neon lamp close to the turntable to prevent light diffusion and improve visibility.
In addition, 33 1/3rpm and 45 rpm can be independently adjusted.

The tone arm uses the TH method with a low center of gravity to improve the tracing capability by improving the adhesion to the sound groove. In addition, the arm pipe is filled with anti-vibration material to reduce resonance.
In addition, the height can be changed by loosening the screw of the arm base.

The anti-skating mechanism uses a direct reading type with a click every 0.5g from 0 to 3g, eliminating the side-slipping of the needle tip.

The head shell uses a highly rigid and lightweight head shell that has undergone cold processing.

A MM type cartridge is used.
This cartridge uses a laminated core for the core of the power generation coil and can reproduce CD-4 by changing the needle.

The motorboat is firmly mounted on an aluminum die-cast board similar to JL-B41 and fixed to the cabinet, which increases the strength of the cabinet and reduces the adverse effects of internal vibration.
