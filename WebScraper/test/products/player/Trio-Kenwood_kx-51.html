  • TRIO KX-51

  • ¥ 65,000 (released in 1982)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with D. P.S. S. (automatic music selection device).

Equipped with a D. P. S. S. that makes deck operation as simple as possible by TRIO's original microcomputer.
This makes it easy to perform such functions as skip play (fast-forward key during Play), head-up play (rewind key during Play), single repeat play (play key during Play), single repeat play (play key during Play), one side full repeat play (fast-forward and rewind key pressed simultaneously), and single rerecording (rewind key during recording).

The head uses an amorphous head with high permeability and high magnetic flux density with a small magnetic field.

Equipped with a 7-point LED level meter for each left and right that can be set level.

It has a dedicated microphone amplifier circuit independent of the line amplifier.

The tape selector uses a push type that allows selection of 3 positions.
The playback equalizer, recording equalizer, and recording bias are switched simultaneously.

Equipped with a sync terminal that is convenient for tape-to-tape dubbing editing when combined with the KX-5P read-only deck.

Equipped with a noise reduction system, Dolby B type which is well known for reducing hissing.

Equipped with an LED display D. P.S. Indicator that allows D. P.S. operation to be recognized at a glance.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.
