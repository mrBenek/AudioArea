  • Aurex XR-V34R

  • ¥ 44,800 (released in 1987)

Commentary

A mini component size CD player.

A high-performance D/A converter and an independent 4-constant voltage power supply are used to achieve higher quality sound.

It has a 16-track random memory function.

A 6-digit LCD display is used to display various information such as track number and lap time.

Equipped with 2-speed FF/REV search.

Equipped with timer play mechanism, automatic playback is possible when the power is turned on.

Wireless remote control is included.
With this remote control, you can control the volume, play, pause, stop, and other basic operations and various functions.
