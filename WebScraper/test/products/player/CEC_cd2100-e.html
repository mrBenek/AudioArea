Commentary

CD player equipped with 3rd overtone oscillation circuit.

The 3rd overtone oscillation circuit uses a specially cut crystal oscillator that reduces the impedance of the circuit to less than 1/10 of the fundamental wave impedance, making it possible to selectively oscillate only stable 3rd overtone waves.
In addition, the clock generator allows the duty ratio to be set at 1 : 1 so that the duty ratio becomes more accurate by increasing the stability of power supply and voltage, the oscillation mechanism of the 3rd overtone wave is improved and the jitter value is greatly reduced.

The D/A converter uses a 1-bit D/A converter without 0 cross distortion.

The front panel is made of 4mm-thick aluminum extruding material, and the top cover of the enclosure and the chassis under the pickup are also made of special anti-vibration material.

Plug-in type power cord is used.

Wireless remote control is included.
