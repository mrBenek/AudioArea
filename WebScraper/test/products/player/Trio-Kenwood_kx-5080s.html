  • ¥ 37,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with Dolby S NR.

Equipped with Dolby S NR, which has approximately 40% noise reduction effect compared to Dolby C NR, it reduces the unnatural appearance that tends to occur when a sound suddenly starts.
Also equipped with Dolby C/B NR, you can enjoy optimum playback according to the recorded tape.

It is equipped with an ATCS function that automatically adjusts the bias value and recording sensitivity in order to draw out the characteristics of the tape you want to record. When used with Dolby HX-Pro, you can record excellent high-frequency characteristics.

It is equipped with functions such as 0 stop and repeat between A and B in addition to DPSS which enables operations such as jump selection, rewind playback, single track repeat, and dash and play.

Equipped with a power loading mechanism.

Equipped with Rec volume and Rec balance control.
