  • ONKYO TA-44X

  • ¥ 56,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

This is a multi-functional stereo cassette deck equipped with a multi-program system.

With the multi-program system, you can select a song by jumping from the current position to the 9th song before and after, play one song 10 times repeatedly with the repeat button, play between the counter 000 position and the tape end / tape start 10 times repeatedly, and search only the intro part of a song continuously with automatic music search AMSS.

Equipped with Autospace Rec Mute that allows you to create 5 seconds of unrecorded space.

It uses a 3-motor system and is centrally controlled by a computer to improve operability and driving stability.

The head uses a hard permalloy head with a hard guard.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Synchronized recording with Record Player CP-890R and PL-220 is possible.
