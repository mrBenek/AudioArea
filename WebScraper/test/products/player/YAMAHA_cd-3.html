Commentary

CD player with digital filter built-in type LSI.

We have adopted a newly designed LSI that is finer than before and has increased the circuit integration by adopting the 2 μ m rule.
The signal processing LSI with built-in digital filter is an N-MOS type, and has a structure that integrates various digital signal processing including interleave demodulation, error detection and error correction.
The servocontrol LSI is a C-MOS type that enables high-speed processing and low power consumption. It performs signal processing such as demodulation of EFM signals and sub-codes as well as various servocontrol including optical heads, and eliminates the influence of disturbance and provides highly reliable disk control.

Equipped with a digital filter with 2 x oversampling, the CD signal sampled at 44.1 kHz is doubled to 88.2 kHz, reducing the load on the analog low-pass filter.
In addition, the analog low-pass filter is composed of a third order active filter abrupt changes in phase characteristics and acquires simple characteristics. The analog low-pass filter also uses a carefully selected high-quality capacitor.

The optical head uses a three beam method, in which three beams are irradiated obliquely, and errors between the leading and trailing beams are detected to provide precise high-speed servo.
The new aLPC circuit automatically controls the amount of light reflected from the disk, enabling stable reading.

The use of a large, low-impedance, large-capacity power supply improves sound quality.

It is equipped with a 9-track random program function, a repeat function between all tracks / programs / A and B, an index search, and a 2-speed search capable of monitoring.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
