  • Technics RS-678U

  • ¥ 96,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with horizontal drive 2-motor system.

FG servo DC motor is used for constant speed running.

An HPF head has been adopted for the head.

Transistor is used for the switch element to prevent noise generation due to contact and improve reliability.

Equipped with 2 types of muting circuits, noise generation at power ON/OFF, recording / reswitching, and STOP is suppressed.

Equipped with a 2-stage switching tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Equipped with Dolby NR circuit.

Equipped with a memory rewind and play mechanism.

Equipped with a meter capable of peak checking by switching the switch.

The line and microphone can be mixed.

Recording of absence is possible by combining with an optional timer.
