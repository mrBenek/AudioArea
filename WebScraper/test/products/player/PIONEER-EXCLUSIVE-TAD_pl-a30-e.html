  • Pioneer PL-A30

  • ¥ 34,500 (around 1972)

Commentary

A full auto player with remote control function.

Equipped with a remote control unit, it can be operated from a position away from the player.

With full auto function, auto lead-in, Oatley turn, Oatley peat and auto cut are possible.

It uses a belt drive system with a 4-pole synchronous motor in the drive system.
Uses a polyurethane belt.

S-shaped pipe arm with lateral balancer is adopted.

Equipped with pilot lamp for operation confirmation.
