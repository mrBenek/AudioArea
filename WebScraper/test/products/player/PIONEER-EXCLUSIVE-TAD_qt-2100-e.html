  • Pioneer QT-2100

  • ¥ 48,500 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 2ch/4ch8-track cartridge tape deck with a large flywheel.

8-track cartridge tape enables discrete 4-channel playback.
Stereo playback is also possible with an 8-track 2-channel cartridge.

A hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the motor, and a large flywheel is used to obtain stable operation.

An amplifier section using silicon transistors is adopted.

Track switching can be done automatically with sensing tapes or manually with one touch pushbuttons.
You can also listen to the same program repeatedly with the repeat switch.

It is equipped with an illuminated indicator that can tell whether it is 2 channel or 4 channel playback.

The exterior and design are prepared with Pioneer UA series amplifier.
