  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1970)

Commentary

This is a stereo tape deck with Oatley berth.

Equipped with an auto stopper for pinch roller OFF.

0.1 mm permalloy laminate head with 2 micron gap is used.

Band brake is adopted.

Equipped with a hysteresis synchronous motor.
