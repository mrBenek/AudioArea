  • DENON DMD-F10G

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1996)

Commentary

MD deck equipped with rich functions.

Equipped with a sampling rate converter, it detects and automatically responds to sampling frequencies of 44.1 kHz, 32 kHz and 48 kHz and supports a wide range of digital sources.

Equipped with a jog dial, it is possible to specify a music number and input text data such as a title.

Equipped with a sound-synchronized recording function, recording can be started on the automatic t-meter simultaneously with the start of playback of the connected external device.

Equipped with 2 times longer monaural mode.

Katakana character input is supported.
You can enter up to 100 characters for each song and display up to 12 characters on the display.

Built-in digital clock timer with date function enables ON/OFF once a day.
If you use the DMD-F06 timer function in combination with a single component other than other than the [ef] series, you can record a timer using an air check.

It is equipped with functions such as 20-track program memory, random play and repeat.

Equipped with MD editing functions such as divide, combine, move, erase, etc.

Wireless remote control is included.
