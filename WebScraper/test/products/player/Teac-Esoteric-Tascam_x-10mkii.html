Commentary

Tape deck equipped with mechanisms such as closed loop dual capstan and EE tape support.

The mechanism employs a dual capstan system in which two sets of capstan and pinch rollers securely hold and drive the tape. Moreover, by placing the erasing head outside each capstan shaft, we have succeeded in shortening the span while keeping the tension in the loop constant.
As a result, tape running is stabilized and modulation noise and flutter components are greatly reduced.

An EE position has been added to the tape selector to take advantage of the performance of the EE tape.

A DC servo motor is used to drive the capstan, and a magnet-float type flywheel with high rotational accuracy is used for the flywheel.

The head is composed of three independent recording, playback and erasing heads, and a high-hardness permalloy head is used for the recording and reproducing heads.

Equipped with a playback pause mechanism that enables accurate head positioning.

Uses direct function for smooth transition from quick winding to play operation.

Equipped with a microphone and line mixing mechanism.

Equipped with a pitch control function that works for both recording and playback.

Equipped with a VU meter.

Equipped with a left-right linked recording mode switch.

Equipped with a guide roller that absorbs uneven running of the tape and realizes smooth running characteristics.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

You can connect a separately sold dbx system.
