  • DENON DMD-F102

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 2006)

Commentary

F102 series MD deck that also supports MDLP.

It is compatible with MDLP mode, and it is possible to record 160 minutes or 320 minutes of stereo on 80 minutes of blank MD.
You can record a mix of MDLP and normal recordings on a single MD disc. Non-MDLP players cannot play the disc.

Equipped with a group management function, many songs recorded on one MD can be divided into groups and managed.
You can assign a group name to a group of songs and edit them individually.
Players that do not support group management function can play only as usual.

Equipped with CDSRS (synchronized recording) function, one touch recording from CD to MD is possible.
There are ALL TRACK SRS, which records all tracks sequentially, and 1TRACK SRS, which records only one track.

Equipped with MD PICK REC function.
If you are listening to music on CD or FM and press the PICK REC button, you can record your favorite song from the beginning by pressing the enter button until the end of the song.
This function will not work if there is no silence at the beginning or end of the song.

Equipped with two optical digital inputs and one optical digital output terminal.
It is also equipped with a sampling rate converter that automatically converts the 32kHz/48kHz sampling frequency of BS/CS broadcasting and DAT to 44.1 khz.

Equipped with a digital REC volume function, it can be used to record multiple single CDs with different recording levels.

Equipped with system functions that can be utilized in combination with F102 series.

Wireless remote control is included.
