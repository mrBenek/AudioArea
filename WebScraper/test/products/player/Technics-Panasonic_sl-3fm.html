  • Technics SL-3FM

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

Jacket size full auto player with built-in FM stereo transmitter.

Record signals can be transmitted on FM radio waves, and record playback can be enjoyed by setting the dial of the radio cassette recorder or FM tuner to 78 MHz.
A line output terminal is also mounted on the rear panel.

Equipped with an electronically controlled full automatic mechanism, it automatically detects the record size and starts playing just by pressing the start button.

Equipped with a linear tracking arm controlled by a microcomputer, precision running is realized.

Equipped with a plug-in connector type MM cartridge.
