  • VICTOR/JVC TD-551

  • ¥ 139,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

Auto-reverse deck with center capstan drive system.

Equipped with a Oatley berth mechanism, it can be automatically reversed at the tape end and continue recording.
Auto repeat playback is also available.

A symmetric mechanism has been adopted for the mechanism part, and stable running has been achieved by arranging one recording / reproducing head and one erasing head symmetrically around the capstan.

The motor section uses a 3-motor system. The capstan motor is equipped with a 4-8 pole hysteresis synchronous motor, and the reel motor is equipped with a 6-pole eddy current motor.

Uses precision capstan and automatic balance type pinch roller, and realizes ultra-long life mechanism that withstands overuse.

The hard permalloy Clonios head developed in-house is used for the recording / reproducing head.

Safety stop mechanism with electromagnetic brake is adopted.

Uses a one touch reel lock mechanism.

Uses an aluminum die-cast head cover.

Uses an EL drive system for easy tape mounting.

Sound on sound is possible.

Uses a pose button with lock.
