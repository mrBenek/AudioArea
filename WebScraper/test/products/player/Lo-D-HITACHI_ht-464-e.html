Commentary

A DD full auto player developed from the same design philosophy as the high-performance player of Lordi following HT 860 and HT 660.

The motor is equipped with a unitorque motor based on Lordi's proprietary technology.
The Unitorque Motor is a motor which obtained perfect circular motion from the principle. It consists of a doughnut-shaped rotor magnet magnetized with 8 poles and two sets of star-shaped flat coils offset by 22 ^ 5 degrees. The torque generated by these two coils and magnets is constant at any moment. This principle is called sin2Θ + cos2Θ = 1 ". The Uni-Torque Motor also adopts a simple structure that is brushless, slotless and coreless, and has significantly improved durability.

A dedicated DC motor is used to drive the arm, and equipped with a full auto mechanism that reduces the effect on the turntable.
It is designed so that this full automatic mechanism does not interfere with manual operation.

Uses a highly electronic auto return detection mechanism that can detect returns without contact using a light source (LED), shutter, and photo sensor (Cds).

Uses a gimbal support tone arm.

The head shell uses a head shell containing glass fiber.
