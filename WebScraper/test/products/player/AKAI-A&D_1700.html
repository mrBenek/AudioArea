  • AKAI 1700

  • ¥ 53,500 (released in 1965)

Commentary

This tape recorder is compact, lightweight and high-performance.

4-track stereo / mono playback is supported.
It also supports 3 speeds.

The speaker is equipped with 2 18x12cm cone units.

A 2-speed induction motor is used for the mechanism.

Recording of FM multiplex broadcasting is supported.

The head part uses a recording head with a 100 μ inch gap.

Equipped with automatic stop lever (automatic stop lever) and instantaneous stop lever.

Equipped with a VU meter and 3-digit tape counter.

Equipped with a heat control system, it maintains stable performance over a long period of time.

It can be used either horizontally or vertically.

38cm/s is also supported by using an adapter.
