  • Technics SL-PJ30

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

A record jacket size CD player with 10 keys in the body.

High-precision super clean digital filter is adopted, and unnecessary noise components are removed by 96th order digital filter.

It employs a high-speed linear access system that can start the head in an average of 1.0 seconds.

Both the main unit and remote control are equipped with 10 keys for direct music selection.

Equipped with a preset editing function.

You can play a 20-piece random program or repeat one or program song.

It is equipped with a time mode display which can display in 4 ways.

Comes with a wireless remote control with volume control.
