  • VICTOR/JVC KD-35

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

This cassette deck is based on KD-31 and has high reliability in both mechanical and electrical systems.

An ID mechanism is adopted for the mechanism part.

Equipped with a 5-point peak indicator, recording level monitoring is possible.

The recording and playback amplifier uses a high linearity design.

Equipped with ANRS-IC circuit.

It has an output volume.
