  • Marantz SD285

  • ¥ 41,200 (around 1989)

Commentary

This double cassette deck inherits the design philosophy of SD565.

For synchronized start, the playback of A is started by one touch operation at the same time as the recording start of B deck.
The output of A deck is not affected by the recording level of B deck, and recording is possible by the shortest path from tape to tape.
It is also equipped with various functions such as double-speed dubbing and long-time playback.

Dolby B/C NR is built-in.

The head is equipped with a hyperbolic metal alloy head with an emphasis on sound quality.
