This is a player system that is the highest level of Technics Player, using the best parts carefully selected from the needle tip to the base.

In addition to turntable SP-10mk3 and turntable base SH-10B5, dedicated straight pipe tone arm straight pipe tone arm and MM type cartridge EPC-P100Cmk4.
