Commentary

A CD player with digital filters.

The digital circuit is equipped with a high-precision 4 x oversampling digital filter to digitally cut off unwanted components in the signal read from the disk.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 16-bit linear D/A converter.

A 3-beam system is used for the pickup part.
In the 3-beam system, the track shift of the main beam for reading music signals is detected in real time by two sub-beams, and is controlled by a powerful servomechanism. This enables stable tracing with little influence of disc warpage, eccentricity, scratches and dirt.

It has a program memory function of up to 16 songs.
It is also equipped with functions such as a 3-type repeat function (one song / all songs / program) and a skip search function that can send songs with one touch.

Equipped with a large multi-display.

Wireless remote control is included.
