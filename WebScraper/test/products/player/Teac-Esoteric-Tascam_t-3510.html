  • TEAC T-3510

  • Price unknown (around 1973)

Commentary

Commercial tape recorder that adopts 3-motor 3-head system.

The T-3510 consists of a drive mechanism and a recording and playback amplifier. There were two variations, the T-3510-1, a single-channel (single-track) type, and the T-3510-2, a two channel (two track) type.

Ferrite head is used for the head part.

It is equipped with editing operation mechanism such as EDIT button and CUE lever.

A plug-in unit is used for the recording and reproducing amplifier section, and it is designed for easy maintenance and adjustment.

Canon connector is used.

Full remote control is possible.

It is equipped with a scrape filter.

It can be used in a rack-mount, portable case or console cabinet.

A simulsync recording function can be installed as an option.
