  • TRIO KX-4000

  • ¥ 43,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with bias equalizer switch and noise filter.

Hard permalloy head is used for the head.

The mechanism uses a precision finished capstan with a roundness error of 0.1 μ and achieves stable rotation.

It is equipped with a noise filter to reduce tape hiss and tape noise added during recording.

You can switch between two levels of bias (normal / chrome) and three levels of equalizer (normal / chrome / extra) in order to record data using the bias and equalizer values that are most suitable for the tape you are using.

When the tape has finished running, the full auto shut-off mechanism is activated to prevent stress on the tape and mechanism.

It is equipped with a VU meter with a recording magnetic flux density of 160 pwb of the tape as the recording standard level of 0 VU.

Equipped with a mirror that makes it easy to check the running state of the tape and the remaining amount of the tape.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism that can be used to record messages and play alarm clock by connecting a commercially available timer.

There was a carrying handle as an option sold separately.
