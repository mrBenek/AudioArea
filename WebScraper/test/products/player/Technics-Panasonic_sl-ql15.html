Commentary

A compo-size (430 mm width) quartz D. D full auto player with a 10-track program mechanism that allows you to enjoy automatic music selection performance.

You can program up to 10 songs freely, and you can enjoy the songs you want to listen to in the order you want to listen to them.
In addition, various performance patterns are possible during program performance. You can repeat the programmed sequence of songs, jump from the scheduled song to be played, start out, start out between songs, search between specified songs, and release the program.

Quartz D. D. turntable is adopted and excellent rotational performance is obtained.

It is equipped with a unique dynamic balance linear tracking arm and achieves high-precision arm travel by microcomputer control.

The arm gimbal suspension system is adopted for the arm bearing structure, and the same high initial motion sensitivity is realized in all directions.

Equipped with plug-in connector type MM cartridge.

The upper and lower cabinets are all made of TNRC, a unique acoustic material with excellent vibration absorption.
