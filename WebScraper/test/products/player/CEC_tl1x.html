Commentary

Belt-drive-type CD transport with a unique vibration control mechanism and further improved perfection.

In the past, the mechanism chassis was floated with a suspension composed of cushion rubber and coil spring. However, it integrating it with cushion rubber and making the material more flexible, damping characteristics have been improved.
In addition, the drive motor fixed to the base chassis of the housing is used as a belt drive to separate the mechanism from the drive part, effectively absorbing and blocking vibrations generated by the motor, servo noise, and micro vibrations caused by inside and outside. In addition, the drive motor fixed to the base chassis is floated via a rubber cushion for complete vibration control.

We have newly adopted resin-based Corean material for the stabilizer.
Although the mass of this material is small, it is excellent in vibration damping characteristics and high wheel performance that the material itself possesses.

The turntable is equipped with a new non-slip sheet that accurately transmits the rotation of the spindle shaft to the disk to reduce the signal reading jitter value related to the rotation accuracy.

Master clock oscillation circuit is mounted.
This circuit is located on the PCB board of the digital output circuit, and the signal sent from the servo circuit is oscillated in perfect synchronization with the master clock by high-speed memory, which eliminates the jitter value of the signal caused by external interference such as the servo circuit.

Wireless remote control is included.
