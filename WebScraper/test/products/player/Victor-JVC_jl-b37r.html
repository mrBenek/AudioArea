Commentary

A record player with a high-torque motor.

The drive motor uses the same DC-type 12-pole 24-slot all-round integral detection FG servo motor used for the turntable TT-71/TT-81. By adding a spare drive circuit and power supply circuit to this motor, quick start-up characteristics and torque of 1 kg ・ cm or more are obtained.
The FG velocity detector is built into the motor and consists of a magnetic disk with 180 detection grooves and 180 printed coils facing each other. With this, 180 pulses are generated in the coil in one rotation, and the total detection output appears in the form of integrating the output of the entire coil corresponding to 180. Thus, high detection accuracy is obtained so that errors in the detection output can be practically ignored.

A turntable of 2.15 kg including rubber covering is adopted, and inertial mass is 350 kg ・ cm.2We have achieved.
33 1/3rpm flash is engraved on the outer circumference. The 45 rpm speed is adjusted to 33 1/3rpm in advance so that it will automatically fit. However, it is designed so that it can be directly checked with the center plate type flash.

New Gimbal Support System has been adopted for the bearing of the tone arm. This system has a structure in which micron-precision radial bearings are symmetrically arranged at equal distances from the center point of the arm shaft. It has the advantages of both the gimbal system and the one point system by making the operation equivalent to the one point support at the center point.
The inside of the arm pipe is filled with anti-vibration material to reduce cavity resonance.

The anti-skating mechanism adopts a click-equipped system, and the operability is improved by adopting a large knob.
The cartridge connector uses a new chucking lock type connector that prevents looseness by tightening a tapered cylinder with a plug-in nut.

The head shell forged head shell is used for the head shell part.
This product is made by forging molten pure aluminum. The density is increased by eliminating the internal cavity and resonance is eliminated.

The cartridge is equipped with the Z-1S cartridge which inherits the design content of the highest quality cartridge X-1.

A large howling margin is obtained by adopting a double structure with less internal space for the cabinet.
In addition, the exterior is finished in Rosewood-style.

Arm sub-weight was available as an option sold separately.
