  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1990)

Commentary

A CD-player using Mash.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 1-bit Mash D/A converter.

Equipped with a digital output terminal with ON/OFF switch.

It is equipped with functions such as music scan, auto spacing, timer play, and random play.

8 cm CD is supported.

Wireless remote control is included.
