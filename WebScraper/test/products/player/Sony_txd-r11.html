Commentary

A CD / cassette deck that combines a CD player and an auto reverse cassette deck.

When the disc and tape are loaded and the CD sync button is pressed, the disc is checked, the appropriate recording level is automatically set and recording starts.

It is equipped with a time-edit function that automatically selects the longest remaining song to fit when the next song does not fit in the remaining time of the tape, and a sound-focus function that detects the peak of the source and automatically sets the recording level.
Also equipped with a fader button that allows you to fade in and fade out at the touch of a button when recording.

Equipped with a Dolby NR B/C type noise reduction system.

It is equipped with a multi-AMS function that enables quick cue of up to 30 songs before and after.

The CD player and deck can be independently connected to other components such as amplifiers.

Wireless remote control is included.
