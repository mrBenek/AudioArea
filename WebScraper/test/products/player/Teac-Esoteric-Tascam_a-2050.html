Commentary

A tape deck equipped with various functions in a one motor deck.

Equipped with 4-head and foil-sensing Oatley bath function.
Automatic continuous recording and playback is possible by attaching a sensing foil at the end of the tape, and manual reverse is also possible.
It has a built-in protection mechanism such as reversing the motor after the mechanism is completely stopped when reversing.

Symmetrical control is adopted in the operation part, and rapid forward movement in both forward and reverse directions can be freely performed by one lever operation. In addition, operability is improved by making the operation of the lever ergonomically symmetrical.

The newly developed seesaw type shifter allows the tape to touch only the head in the direction of travel. Unused heads do not touch the tape, thus extending the life of the head.
In addition, the balanced light pressure pad, which operates parallel to the head, presses the tape uniformly on the head surface to obtain an ideal head touch.

The drive motor uses a newly designed 4-pole hysteresis synchronous outer rotor motor.

It uses a synchronous band brake with the same mechanism as a 3-motor machine, and the brake mechanism operates at precise timing.

Polyurethane with excellent durability is used for the idler.

Equipped with a full-scale VU meter of the same type as the A-6010.
In addition, the A-2050 is equipped with a dedicated playback volume linked to the VU meter.

Cut core transformer is used and the motor is tightly shielded to shut out hum from the power supply.

A push-pull circuit using 2SC491 is adopted for the bias oscillation circuit, and a sufficient oscillation output of 100 kHz is obtained.

Equipped with an equalizer volume that allows you to freely change the characteristics of the treble section.

It has a headphone amplifier.

Stainless hairline finish is applied to the panel.

Equipped with a 4-digit index counter.
