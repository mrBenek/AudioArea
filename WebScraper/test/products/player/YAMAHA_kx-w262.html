  • YAMAHA KX-W262

  • ¥ 49,800 (released in 1992)

Commentary

Double cassette deck for continuous play.

A Dolby B/C is installed as a noise reduction system.

Hard permalloy head is used for the head.

Auto-reverse mode enables various relay playback between decks A and B. Two tapes can be played repeatedly on both sides up to eight times.
The Quick Auto-reverse Deck A realizes quick switching by optically detecting the leader tape portion.

Equipped with Auto-Rec Mute, which automatically creates the unrecorded portion of a song that is needed to start a song.
It is also equipped with a Rec Return function that returns to the recording start position or the checked position during recording.

For dubbing, you can choose 2 speeds, normal and high.

It can be operated by the system remote controller attached to an amplifier with Yamaha RS mark.

Equipped with an auto tape selector that automatically selects the position of the tape.

Equipped with REC pause function.

Equipped with a deck selector that allows you to choose the playback deck with one touch.

Equipped with a direction switch that changes the direction of tape travel.

It is possible to start one song before and after.

Equipped with a tape counter.
