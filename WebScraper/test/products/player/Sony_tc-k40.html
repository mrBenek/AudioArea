  • SONY TC-K40

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

A cassette deck with an electronic echo circuit and center microphone input.

With built-in electronic (B. B. D.) echo, it is possible to add thickness and spread to sound.
It also has a center microphone input that can adjust the volume and echo effect.

Microphone mixing is available for both recording and playback.

The motor is a green motor with excellent durability and quietness.

An F & F head with excellent wear resistance and high-frequency characteristics is used for the head, and characteristics are improved by direct coupling with the amplifier.

Equipped with a Dolby NR system that reduces hissing specific to tapes.

Equipped with a 3-stage tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

It is equipped with a cue / review function that can be used to start a song.

Equipped with a one button recording function.

Equipped with Rec Mute function.

Equipped with peak indicator.

Equipped with a timer standby function.
