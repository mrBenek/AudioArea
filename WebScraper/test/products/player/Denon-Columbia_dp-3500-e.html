  • DENON DP-3500

  • ¥ 62,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

An armless player system combining the turntable DP 3000 with a dedicated cabinet.

The drive unit is equipped with DP 3000, a direct drive type turntable.
A solid rotor type AC2-phase torque motor is used for the motor, and a frequency detection servo control by a magnetic recording / reproducing system is installed as a servo mechanism to achieve stable rotation.

Up to 14-type arm can be attached to the pickup arm.

Equipped with a removable arm board.

The dust cover is finger mounted and removable.

Equipped with an insulator.
