  • SONY TC-9000

  • ¥ 193,000 (around 1971)

Commentary

Tape deck capable of 38cm2 track stereo recording / playback by using No. 10 reel.

An AC servo motor is used for the motor and a closed-loop dual capstan system is used.

Equipped with an automatic heading and stopping device.

Designed to reduce click noise.
