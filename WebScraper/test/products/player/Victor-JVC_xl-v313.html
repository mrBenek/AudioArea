  • VICTOR/JVC XL-V313

  • ¥ 24,800 (around 1999)

Commentary

A CD player that achieves high sound quality and optimum CD recording with easy operation.

The D/A converter is equipped with Victor's original 1-bit DD converter. In principle, no 0 cross distortion occurs, and high sound quality is obtained even at low levels.

The digital filter uses an 8 x oversampling digital filter.

The Digitalized Direct Recording Processor (DDRP) automatically sets the level settings that cause problems when recording CDs. This function quickly searches for the peak level of CDs with a wide dynamic range and variation, and the microcomputer automatically sets the optimum recording level and outputs it, making it easy to record high-quality sound.
In addition, the DDRP does not require setting the level of the deck side from the magnetic field once recording is done, so high quality recording can be performed easily.

Equipped with 2-mode (auto / program) edit function which is convenient for recording.

Center tray mechanism is adopted for the layout of mechanism.
As a result, the weight balance is optimized and the traceability of the pickup is improved without being affected by vibration.

The front panel has a layout with a recording button on the left and an operation button on the right to improve usability.

Equipped with Auto Power ON/OFF function, tray can be opened / closed by pressing by pressing the eject button even in the standby state, and the tray is automatically closed and turned off by pressing the power button after removing the disk from the tray (power ON state).

It is equipped with a function of continuous program performance of 32 songs and 3-mode repeat performance of all songs / one song / program songs.

Comes with a wireless remote control that can be turned on / off.
