Audio Database

  • AKAI GX-286DB

  • ¥ 210,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

A 3-motor tape deck with a 3-head / 4-head function configuration using a GX head for recording and reproducing heads.

4-track recording and playback at 19cm/s and 9.5cm/s is possible.

Equipped with Oatley verse playback function.

Uses a non-stroke touch button with an all-transistor circuit.

D-D system with AC servo motor is adopted.

Equipped with a Dolby system.
