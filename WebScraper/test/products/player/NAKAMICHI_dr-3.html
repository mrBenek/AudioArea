Commentary

This is a cassette deck of the Selected Component series developed as a Limited Edition limited to 300 units.

The head is equipped with a 1 μ - gap recording and reproducing head using a Sendust laminate core.

A DC servo capstan motor is used for the mechanism.

Dual monaural construction is used for the amplifier circuit, and high quality parts are used to bring out the capability of the head.

Multi-regulated power supply system has been adopted to reduce interference between circuits.

Equipped with a Dolby B/C type noise reduction system.

A microprocessor is used for the mechanism operation to realize silent operation.

Equipped with a tape slack prevention mechanism.

Equipped with a 4-digit tape counter.

Equipped with bi-directional counter search.

Equipped with a tape selector.
It also has a bias fine adjustment volume.

Equipped with timer recording / playback mechanism.
