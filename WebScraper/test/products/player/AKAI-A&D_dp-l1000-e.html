Commentary

Compatible player capable of playing LD, CDV and CD

By adopting a newly designed compact pickup, we have achieved a horizontal resolution of more than 420 lines and an image SN ratio of more than 46 dB.

Enjoy digital sound quality with a 4 x oversampling digital filter and twin D/A converter.

The chassis is made of a special resin with high rigidity and high specific gravity, and features a honeycomb structure underbase, a non-contact magnet clamp mechanism that reduces vibration caused by disk rotation, and a honeycomb structure clamper arm.

With its high-quality 8-bit digital memory, various trick plays can be controlled freely using the jog / shuttle dial of the wireless remote control, and it is equipped with an optical digital output that can be connected directly to a digital amplifier.
