  • YAMAHA KX-W592

  • ¥ 39,000 (released in 1997)

Commentary

Dual REC auto-reverse deck equipped with Yamaha's original DSS (digital surround system).

Equipped with 4-mode DSS of hall, disco, church and jazz, you can add surround effect.
DSS can be used for recording as well as for playback.

Equipped with New Play Trim, high-frequency characteristics can be corrected.
This function can be used during playback and dubbing.

It is equipped with Dolby HX-PRO that automatically adjusts the bias amount according to the high-frequency strength of the recording signal and Yamaha's original bias adjustment, making it possible to record at the lowest possible level on various tapes.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with synchronized REC and one touch dubbing function.

Wireless remote control is included.
