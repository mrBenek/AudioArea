  • VICTOR/JVC D-E50

  • ¥ 52,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

Recording and re-auto reverse deck corresponding to Victor's mini component Creation RX.

A rotary head type Fine Axis Reverse System is used for the reverse mechanism.

A 2 + 1 motor full logic and silent mechanism is adopted.

You can skip 9 songs that you want to listen to in both A and B.

Equipped with a player / synchro terminal.

Equipped with auto REC mute.

Equipped with Dolby B NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with 2-color LED peak meter.

Equipped with a timer start mechanism.
