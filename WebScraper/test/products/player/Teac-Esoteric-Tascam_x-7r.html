Commentary

This tape deck has been developed as a compact version of the X-10R and can record and play back the No. 7 reel back and forth.

A dual capstan system is adopted for the mechanism part.
In this system, two sets of capstan and pinch rollers are used to sandwich the tape and drive it. This structure greatly reduces modulation noise. In addition, since tape tension is given by the slight rotational difference between capstan and capstan, even minute tape vibrations (flutter components) that do not appear in catalog data are shielded.
In addition, due to the symmetrical design, even in reverse operation, the same conditions as in forward operation are obtained, and uniform tape travel is achieved in both directions.

A magne-float type flywheel is adopted.
It is designed so that there is no play in the axial direction of the capstan by pressing it against the bearing using the repulsion of the magnet, thereby realizing stable rotation of the flywheel.

The head section is composed of 6 heads, and each head for erasing, recording and playback is independent of each other.
A high-hardness Permalloy head is used for the head, and an erasing head, which does not affect the sound quality, is placed outside the closed loop to shorten the tape span.

You can set timer recording in either direction by setting a timer switch in the center of the front panel and a commercially available audio timer.

Uses an electromagnetic brake.

Equipped with a motion sensing mechanism, if you press the play button directly from the quick turn, the electromagnetic brake acts on the reel, it stops softly and moves to play action instantly.

When the Oatley bar knob is set and the counter is set to 0000 and the tape is played back, it can be automatically reversed from the reverse direction to the forward direction at that position. Only by combining this function with the Oatley bar, you can play it endlessly all day.

Equipped with Rec Mute function.

It is equipped with a real-time pause function that makes it easy to rise from a recorded pause.

Microphone and line mixing is possible.

Equipped with a pitch control function that works for both recording and playback.

Equipped with an L/R linked recording mode switch.

Equipped with independent bias and equalizer switching functions.

Equipped with a large VU meter.
