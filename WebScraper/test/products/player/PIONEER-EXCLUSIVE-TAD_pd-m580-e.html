Commentary

A magazine-type CD player with talkback input.
As it is a magazine type that can store 6 CDs, you can enjoy up to 6 CDs continuously without changing disks.

Equipped with an ADLC (Automatic Digital Level Controller), the microcomputer samples digital data at any time, and controls the digital attenuator in real time so that the volume becomes appropriate.
This allows you to listen to any disc or song at the appropriate volume, and even when playing randomly, the volume level is controlled for each song, making it easier to listen.

Equipped with a talkback input, a set of programs, delete programs, edits, etc. can be input while viewing the text information on the FL display.

Equipped with a magazine highlight scan that shows the contents of the magazine.
A highlight scan plays a song for 10 seconds, starting one minute after the start of the song, and provides a smoother review than an intro scan.
In addition, the magazine highlight scan allows you to highlight scan the first track of each disc sequentially.

The D/A converter is equipped with a high-speed pulse flow 1bitD/A converter originally developed by Pioneer.

Mechanisms such as pickups are positioned in the center to measure the shortest signal path.

Quiet and smooth loading is realized by adopting a silent multi-mechanism and a monocoque structure with high rigidity and dimensional accuracy for the chassis.

It is equipped with a memory hold function which also memorizes the magazine information such as program, delete and volume level for power supply after turning off.
Also, with the last disc memory, you can start from the same disc again by memorizing the disc that was playing in the straight line of turning off the power.

Built-in computer program edit and time fade edit functions.

Equipped with an auto power on function, the power turns on automatically when the magazine is inserted.
In addition, it is equipped with a power down eject function, and even when the power is off, you can eject the magazine by pressing the eject button.

Equipped with a power on start function that enables timer start.
