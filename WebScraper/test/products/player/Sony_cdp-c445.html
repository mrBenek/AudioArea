Commentary

A 5-disc CD changer that uses the CD roulette system.

The disc tray uses a CD roulette system, and up to 5 discs can be set in the tray.
And with disk exchange, you can pull out the tray to replace another disk without interrupting the performance.

The D/A converter is equipped with a hybrid pulse D/A converter, and the SN ratio is improved by incorporating a noise-shaping digital filter in one chip that performs high-precision oversampling operations.
In addition, the signal path is simplified by using a single chip, and the effect of the direct digital sync circuit formed in the chip is also improved, reducing jitter.

The servo circuit is equipped with a high-precision digital servo, and the adoption of a high-sampling rate A/D and D/A converter realizes servocontrol that accurately reflects disk conditions.

Equipped with a custom edit function, you can control functions such as peak search, time edit, link edit, program edit, multi-disk program, time fade, manual fader and variable fade.

It is equipped with the custom file function and can use the delete bank function.

It is equipped with functions such as delete play, 3 types of shuffle of 1 disc, all disc and select disc, 3 way music scan and 6 mode repeat.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
