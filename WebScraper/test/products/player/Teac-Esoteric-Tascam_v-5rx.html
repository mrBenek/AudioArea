  • TEAC V-5RX

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A cassette deck with a dbx system.

As a noise reduction system, the dbx system is installed in addition to the Dolby system.
The dbx system has a good noise reduction effect, reducing the noise level by 30-40 dB over the entire band.

The mechanism part uses a 3-motor 3S (soft, smooth, silent) mechanism, and the effect of the dbx system is demonstrated by the reliable tape running.

Vertical design peak level meter is used.

An input volume of the master volume type is mounted.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism, it can be used with an audio timer (sold separately) to record your absence or play an alarm clock.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a self-illuminated indicator that can be checked at a glance by Rec/Rec Mute/Pause.

Equipped with a tape selector that supports metal tapes.
