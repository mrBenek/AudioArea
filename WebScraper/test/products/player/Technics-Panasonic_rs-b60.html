  • Technics RS-B60

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

A two motor cassette deck equipped with dbx.

For recording digital sources such as CDs, it is equipped with dbx, which has a noise reduction effect of 30 dB or more.
In addition, it is designed for 3 nr with Dolby B and CNR.

The AX amorphous head made of amorphous alloy is used for the recording / reproducing head, thereby realizing excellent high-frequency characteristics and distortion characteristics.

The mechanism uses a 2-motor computer logic system to achieve stable tape travel and reliable operation.

Equipped with an auto input selector and an auto tape selector.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.
