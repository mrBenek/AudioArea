Commentary

DVD player powered by Premium Digital technology.

Equipped with Dolby digital and DTS digital audio output jacks.

Component (color difference) video output terminal is mounted.

Equipped with a 10-bit video D/A converter.

Equipped with a 96kHz/24bit audio D/A converter.

A high-precision coupling capacitor for audio is mounted on the analog audio output.

Equipped with 3-step zoom function.

You can set the functions using the Japanese on-screen display.

Wireless remote control is included.
