Audio Database

  • DENON DN-C615

  • ¥ 50,400 (tax included, around 2006)

Commentary

A low-cost rack-mount CD player.

Equipped with a sleep mode that reduces the burden on the CD mechanism when power is turned on for a long time.

Equipped with an instant start mechanism that enables instantaneous sound output.

Equipped with a frame search function that enables frame-by-frame search and head-out search.

Pitch control of ± 12% is possible.

Up to 99 songs can be played.

It has a shock proof memory of about 10 seconds.

There was an infrared remote controller sold separately.
