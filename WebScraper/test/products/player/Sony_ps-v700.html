  • SONY PS-V700

  • ¥ 12,400 (around 1995)

Commentary

A full-automatic record player with a built-in phono equalizer amplifier.

The PS-V700 has a built-in phono equalizer amplifier that can be connected to an amplifier or radio cassette that does not have a phono (MM) input to enjoy analog recording.
The phono equalizer amplifier can be turned ON/OFF. If the amplifier has a phono jack, it can be connected in the same way as a normal player.

Equipped with a full auto mechanism, the size of the record is determined by one touch of the start button and the performance starts.
When the play is finished, the arm automatically returns to the rest and stops, or you can stop the play in the middle with one button.

It supports 2 speeds of 33.1/3 rotation for LP and 45 rotation for EP.
