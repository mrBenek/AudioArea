Commentary

A full-auto player system that employs a direct drive system.

The tone arm is equipped with a counter-weight resonance prevention mechanism to prevent sharp resonance that occurs in the pipe and head shell. In addition, the counter-weight has a double structure and a rubber damper is installed between the counter-weights to improve sound resolution in the low frequency range.

A 12-pole 24-slot brushless servo motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with auto mechanism, auto start, Oatley turn, auto cut, auto repeat are possible.
We have also adopted a mechanism that automatically determines the number of turns of the turntable when the size of the record to play is set.

All control levers are centrally located in the front of the cabinet and can be operated with the dust cover closed.

The cabinet is molded with iron powder and a large insulator is used to suppress howling.

Equipped with anti-skating mechanism and oil dump-type arm lifter.
