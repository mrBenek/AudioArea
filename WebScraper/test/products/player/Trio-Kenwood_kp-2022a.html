  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

This is an auto return player that pays close attention to stability of rotation, raising and lowering of the needle, needle pressure adjustment, prevention of howling, etc.

The arm uses a total lateral balance type resonance prevention tapered pipe arm.
A plug-in method is adopted for cartridge connection, and various cartridges can be used.

Equipped with auto mechanisms such as auto return and pose elevation, you can use it without worrying about operation error.
