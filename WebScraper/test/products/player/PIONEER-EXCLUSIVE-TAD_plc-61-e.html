Commentary

This is an armless player system with a hall motor that can be attached to any tone arm.

The phono motor uses MU-61, a low-speed DC servo hole motor.
S/N and durability have been improved by adopting a newly developed Hall element brushless DC servo motor with low speed rotation 10 times that of a turntable.

Equipped with a pure electronic speed switching mechanism, and in addition, speed fine adjustment of ± 2% is possible.

Comes with a gauge that can easily determine the mounting position of the arm.

By removing the dust cover, a 16-type arm can be mounted.

An insulator made of special rubber is included to prevent howling.

There was arm sub board sold separately.
