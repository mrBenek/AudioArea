  • ONKYO PL-330R

  • ¥ 55,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

DD Full Auto Player with multiple functions realized by computer.

It is equipped with a mechanism in which the computer detects the playing time of the record and displays it in units of minutes and seconds. You can call up all the songs that have been played by pressing the music selection key, so you can display the playing time of the song you want to know with a single stroke.
You can also change the switch to display the total playing time and the elapsed time of the item being played in real time.

It is equipped with a random selection system with a repeat of one or all songs, which is the same as the CP 890 r.
Up to 8 songs can be selected freely, including duplicate songs, and can be canceled or added during performance.

The playing time display and music selection display can be switched with the switch.

The drive system is equipped with a direct drive motor whose servo circuit controls the rotational speed to be stable.
In addition, frontal pivot type tone arm is adopted.

Equipped with synchronized recording terminal with cassette deck TA-44X.
