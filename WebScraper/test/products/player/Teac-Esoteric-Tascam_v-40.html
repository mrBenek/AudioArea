Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with simple operability and mechanism.

The mechanism uses a single capstan drive system with a simple design.
The head uses an electronically controlled DC motor that is quiet and does not rotate unevenly. The head uses a high-precision Permalloy recording / reproducing head.

Equipped with a Dolby NR system as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a tape selector whose bias / equalizer can be switched.

Equipped with a 2-color (green and red) 12-dot LED bar graph meter.
Also, 3-digit mechanical counter.

Input selector and input volume

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism convenient for narration and CM cutting.

Uses a direct function mechanism that can be operated directly from any mode.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism convenient for voice recording and alarm clock playback.
When the Record/Play button is locked, the time of the timer standby (standby) or timer (sold separately) is set. Recording / playback starts when the time comes.
