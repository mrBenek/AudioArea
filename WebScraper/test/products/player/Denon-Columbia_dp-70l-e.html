  • DENON DP-70L

  • ¥ 120,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

An auto lift player equipped with an auto lift mechanism using a contact-free end sensor.

Equipped with lifter speed control function, the lowering speed of the arm can be freely adjusted.

Uses a double-structured turntable.

An out Rocester type AC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

The tone arm is equipped with a high-sensitivity S-shaped tone arm with dynamic damping mechanism that is not affected by external vibration.

Equipped with a helicoidal arm elevation mechanism, the height of the tone arm can be adjusted according to the cartridge.

A 70 mm thick heavyweight cabinet has been adopted.
