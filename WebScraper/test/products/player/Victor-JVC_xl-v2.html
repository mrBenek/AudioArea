  • VICTOR/JVC XL-V2

  • ¥ 145,000 (released in 1983)

Commentary

A compact CD player, equivalent to Victor's second CD player.

The pick-up section is equipped with a unique servo system with accurate separation of detected signals and loop characteristic switching to improve signal reading accuracy and stability.

A digital filter is installed in the digital circuit and the dual D/A configuration improves the separation.

The analog circuit uses a newly developed discrete low-pass filter, and an isometric substrate pattern design reduces interference, cross-modulation crosstalk, and left-right crosstalk.

Up to 12 songs can be played in memory.
In addition, it is equipped with functions such as manual search of two speed forward and backward, direct search by track number instruction, Oatley Pete, and A-B interval repeat.

Equipped with a large FL display.

By using the sync play cable (sold separately), it is possible to play in sync with the twin recorder re-reverse deck KD-WR90.
