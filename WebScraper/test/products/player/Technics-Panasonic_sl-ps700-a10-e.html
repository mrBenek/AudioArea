Commentary

A CD player that uses Mash 1 bit DAC and original swing arm mechanism.

A Mash 1-bit DAC is adopted for the D/A conversion section, and non-linear distortion such as 0 cross distortion does not occur in principle, making it possible to achieve high-linearity reproduction during small signal reproduction.

Digital servo is used to adjust the servo amount for each disk.
In digital servo systems, the focus, tracking, and traverse servo amounts for each CD are adjusted by software processing. Stable servo characteristics reduce fluctuations in the time axis of the digital signal and ripples in the servo current, improving sound quality. Playability is also improved by strengthening the ability to cope with disc warpage, scratches, and eccentricity.
In addition, access accuracy, access speed and reliability have been improved by digitization of the circuit.

In order to reduce vibration from the outside, TNRC base made of multi-layer composite of metal and BMC and four large insulators are used, and optical deck insulator composed of spring and viscoelastic material is adopted to suppress resonance of the mechanism.
In addition, a newly developed swing arm mechanism has been adopted for the drive mechanism of the tray to realize silent operation.

Equipped with a function manager, you can store the 5 most frequently used functions from the built-in 17 functions as main functions and select them directly.

Equipped with a total of 17 functions including tape length setting, side A/B display switching, convenient link for editing from several CDs, time fade to fade out at specified time, just-time edit to minimize remaining time on tape, arrangement, auto-peak search, repeat, A-B repeat, random play, forward skip, reverse skip, forward index, reverse index, fader, auto-cue and auto-space.

High-speed linear access system is adopted, and high-speed access is enabled by high-precision linear motor.

Uses an optical digital output terminal.

The use of Class AA circuits and audio parts improves sound quality.

Wireless remote control is included.
