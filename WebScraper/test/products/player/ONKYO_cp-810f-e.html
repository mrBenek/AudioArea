  • ONKYO CP-810F

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

A full auto player equipped with repeat and search functions.

It is equipped with a microcomputer and a motor for arm drive, and in addition to the normal full auto function, it is equipped with repeat function and search function.

For the tone arm, a straight type tone arm is adopted in consideration of weight reduction and high rigidity.
In addition, a MM type cartridge is installed in the cartridge.

The drive system is equipped with an electronic servo DD motor, and the cabinet is a two layer cabinet with vibration control structure.

Dust cover is sold separately.
