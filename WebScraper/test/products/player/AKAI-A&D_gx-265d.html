Audio Database

  • AKAI GX-265D

  • ¥ 139,000 (released in 1975)

Commentary

A 4-head / 6-head functional tape deck with 2 recording GX heads / erasing heads and 2 playback GX heads.

You can record and play in reverse.

The mechanism uses a direct capstan drive with an AC servo motor.
