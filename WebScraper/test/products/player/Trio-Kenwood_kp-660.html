Commentary

An electronic full auto player with a high-output MC cartridge.

The tone arm uses a high-rigidity straight tone arm that suppresses the resonance of the tone arm itself by devising the inside of the pipe.
The arm bracket is made of aluminum die cast to prevent external vibration and at the same time, the supporting point of operation is made clear by metal weight. In addition, the lateral counter-weight has been newly developed to make the structure strong against the torsional moment of the arm, enabling more faithful tracing.

The tone arm drive system uses an electronic drive system with a non-contact type position sensor and horizontal linear motor. It realizes smooth arm movement because there is no mechanical sound or external addition during tracing, which cannot be avoided with the mechanical drive system.
In addition, speed feedback control in the horizontal direction during performance prevents arm vibration due to low-frequency resonance, enabling more accurate tracing of the needle tip.

Equipped with a high output MC cartridge that can be directly connected to the MM position of the amplifier without using a step-up transformer or head amplifier.
It is also designed to facilitate needle exchange, which was not possible with conventional MC cartridges.

Gold plated terminal is used for aluminum die-cast shell.
