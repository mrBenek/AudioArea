Commentary

A player system developed to allow users to easily enjoy advanced disc playback technology.
CP-701u is a model without a case of CP-701.

An 8-pole induction motor is used motor.

It is equipped with a lateral effect by unbalanced main weight and arm elevation mechanism to prevent damage to the disc.

The CP 701 is equipped with a speed micromanipulator so that it can play accurately.

The cartridge uses a variable flux (VF) type cartridge with a diamond elliptical needle of 0.25x0.7 mil.
Unlike the MM type, the VF type cartridge does not have a magnet directly connected to the cantilever, so the movement of the needle is similar to that of the IM type or MC type.

Two speeds of 45 rpm and 33 rpm can be obtained by lever switch.

Uses an electrostatically treated rubber sheet.

It uses a European standard 4-terminal plug-in head and is compatible with foreign cartridges.
