  • YAMAHA KX-T950

  • ¥ 85,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

Two quick-auto-reverse decks are condensed into a single case, making simultaneous recording and playback possible. This twin deck is completely independent.

Equipped with an independent display, you can enjoy 2 decks at the same time.
For example, DECK 1 and DECK 2 can be played back alternately, and up to 24 hours of relay playback using Oatley bus and 3 hours of relay recording can be supported.

It uses a high bias of 210 kHz, twice that of conventional products, and prevents beat distortion.

Bias adjustment and play-trim improve recording and playback quality in the high-frequency range.

8-mode dubbing of variable level by auto, manual, skip, program, normal, high speed, cross dolby, dubbing VR is possible.

It is equipped with functions such as introscan, front-and-back cue, 10-key direct selection, REC return, auto-REC mute, blank skip, etc.

Wireless remote control is included.
