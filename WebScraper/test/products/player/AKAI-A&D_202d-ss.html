  • AKAI 202D-SS

  • ¥ 135,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

3-head 4-channel tape deck.

The tape speed is 19cm/s and 9.5cm/s, and 4ch/2ch recording and 2 ch Oatley verse playback are possible.

Equipped with a tape selector and automatic stop mechanism.

Adopts a 3-motor mechanism using an oil circulation type oil-free motor.
