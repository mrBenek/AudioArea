Commentary

CD player with 4 x digital filter.

X oversampling digital filter and twin DAC are adopted.

Newly developed disk stabilizer is adopted.

Equipped with digital peak research function, it is easy to set the level when recording.

The display section is equipped with an FL display with music calendar.

Timer is supported.

Wireless remote control is included.
