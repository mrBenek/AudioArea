  • VICTOR/JVC XL-Z211

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1988)

Commentary

CD player with digital output.

Equipped with coaxial digital output.

The mechanism part adopts a W suspension floating mechanism structure, and the pickup system and drive system are floating mounted on the mechanism base through an insulator. The W suspension structure combined with an insulation foot eliminates adverse effects caused by resonance and vibration.

The digital circuit uses a newly developed high-order four times oversampling digital filter.

Equipped with an editing function, the microcomputer automatically selects songs that can be recorded just by setting the recording time.

Up to 32 songs can be programmed with the tray ejected or with additional memory while the program is playing.

It has an intro-scan function that allows you to listen to the head of a song for 15 seconds each.

Equipped with 5 types of repeat functions.

Equipped with a large FL display.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Equipped with a timer play function.

8 cm disk compatible tray is adopted.

Wireless remote control is included.
