  • VICTOR/JVC TD-695

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

Although it is a 2-head system, the tape deck is designed for high quality such as wide range and stability.

Uses an easy-loading type that allows easy tape mounting.

Equipped with a tape selector switch to support SH tapes.

Equipped with an automatic stop device that automatically stops the tape running when the tape has finished winding.

Sound on sound is possible.

4.8cm/s is also supported.

It comes with a reclining stand that can also be used in an inclined style.

There was a dust cover sold separately.
