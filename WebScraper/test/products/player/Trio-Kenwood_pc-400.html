Commentary

A record player with a belt, idler and double drive system.

A belt, idler and double drive system is used as the drive system. This is a combination of the belt drive system and drive system and the idler drive system. It combines the newly developed 80 mm diameter large idler, 32 cm diameter turntable, and 8 mm width silicon belt with precision polishing on both sides. This system realizes the performance taking advantage of the advantages of both systems.

The Center shaft is made of stainless steel with very low friction. By applying precision polishing treatment to the surfaces, the contact surface between the shaft and bearing is reduced and friction is further prevented. In addition, 4-mm-thick polyurethane is embedded in the bottom of the bearing to absorb the vibration of the motor.
The bearings are completely sealed and free from foreign matter. In addition, the surface of the shaft is provided with a spiral groove in the order of microns. When the shaft begins to rotate, molybdenum disulfide, a lubricant, travels through the groove, and the surface of the shaft is always evenly covered.
Because molybdenum disulfide has negative electrical characteristics, it is easily adsorbed on metal surfaces with positive characteristics. By using molybdenum disulfide as a lubricant, friction is reduced and smooth rotation without uneven rotation is achieved.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used for the drive part. In addition, the power supply voltage is slightly lowered to ease the motor vibration during rotation.

An aluminum die-cast turntable with a diameter of 32 cm is used for the turntable.

The tone arm uses a static balance type.
All audio codes and head shell plug-ins conform to EIA standards.

A MM type cartridge is used.

In consideration of the relationship between the friction of the idler shaft and oil, we have developed and adopted a special oil for the idler.
As a result, the clay does not change even if the temperature changes.

A lever is used for switching between 50 Hz and 60 Hz.
This lever can be operated from the top of the player simply by removing the turntable. At the same time, the capacity of the condenser can be changed.

Equipped with an arm elevation mechanism.
The arm lowering speed can be adjusted by the speed adjustment knob.

In order to prevent very small vibration from the motor from being transmitted to the die-cast board, a new material for vibration absorbing rubber is used. The part where the vibration absorbing rubber touches the board is made into point contact to reduce the degree of vibration transmission to the board.
