Commentary

4-ch 8-track deck with built-in 4-ch pre-main amplifier.

It has a built-in pre-main amplifier for 4 ch and an independent output volume control for each channel.
It is equipped with a master volume, and 4-channel simultaneous control is possible.

It is equipped with tone control that works on 4 ch.

Equipped with 4ch/2ch automatic switching function by sensing slot.

Equipped with 4 VU meters.

Equipped with a fast-forward device.

Pre-output terminal is mounted.
