  • ONKYO DV-L5

  • ¥ 40,000 (released on September 10, 2001)

Commentary

As a DVD player player, it can be connected directly to the MD deck.

Equipped with a 27 MHz / 10-bit video D/A converter for video and a 192 kHz / 24-bit D/A converter for audio.

Uses a 2-wavelength twin focus pickup compatible with CD-R/CD-RW/MP3.

Equipped with a setup navigator that enables easy and interactive initial setup.

Equipped with two digital output terminals for direct connection with MD deck.

Equipped with a Dolby digital and DTS decoder.

Direct digital signal is realized by the direct digital path circuit.

Equipped with a high-speed resume function and last memory function that are convenient for interrupting during playback.

It is equipped with a search function that allows you to specify a chapter or time and play it quickly, and a repeat play function that allows you to repeatedly play your favorite track or chapter.

It is equipped with a condition memory function that can store up to 15 DVD settings you often watch.

Equipped with a screen saver function to prevent the display from burning.

Equipped with an RI terminal that enables the DV-L5 to be operated by the remote control of an Onkyo AV amplifier with the RI mark.

Wireless remote control is included.
