  • SONY CF-2700D

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A portable stereo cassette recorder with a tuner.

It uses 4 power sources for 4 types of batteries : 100 VAC, dry batteries, rechargeable batteries, and car batteries.

It is equipped with a tape selector corresponding to Duad tape.

Equipped with full auto shut-off mechanism.

Equipped with a variable monitor.

Equipped with FM/AM2 band radio.

Equipped with a sleep mechanism.

Servo motor is used for the motor.

The carrying handle was sold separately.
In addition, there were carrying case and pre-main amplifier of unified design sold separately.
