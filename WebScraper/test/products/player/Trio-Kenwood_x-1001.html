  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

This is a K's series cassette deck.

A front loading system is adopted, and the mechanism is equipped with an aluminum die cast 3 flywheel mechanism.

Equipped with Dolby B/C that reduces tape hissing.

It is equipped with Dolby HX Pro which improves the high-frequency recording characteristics.

When dubbing from a CD to a tape, it is equipped with a CCRS & auto-bias function that automatically sets the tape recording level and bias amount that are optimum for the tape to be used, and enables recording of higher quality sound.

It is equipped with DPSS that can perform operations such as repeat playback of one song or repeat all songs, dash and play, and jump selection.

The remote control included with the K's series amplifier KA-1001G allows you to play and stop.
