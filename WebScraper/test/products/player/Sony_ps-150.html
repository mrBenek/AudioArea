Commentary

An auto player system with a built-in headphone amplifier.

With a built-in phono equalizer amplifier, it can be easily connected to a radio cassette or component.

Low-mass straight arm is used for the tone arm.

It is equipped with linear BSL motor and magnedisk servo.

Equipped with auto return and reject mechanism.

30 It comes with a disc centering guide that can fit the center hole of the record board when setting the cmLP board.
