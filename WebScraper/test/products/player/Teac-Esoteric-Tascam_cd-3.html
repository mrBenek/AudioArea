Commentary

CD player using bit stream 1 bit DAC.

The D/A conversion section uses the Philips D/A converter bit stream "SAA7350" and has a 1-bit 4-DAC configuration, thereby eliminating non-linear distortion and 0 cross distortion in principle.
The digital filter uses a 20-bit 8-times oversampling digital filter.

By placing the CD mechanism in the center position, low center of gravity and good weight balance are obtained, and vibration resistance is improved.

The CN servo is mounted to reduce the adverse effect of current disturbance caused by servo current on analog system.
In this circuit, the driving voltage to the actuator is filtered by a high-precision low-pass filter to reduce unnecessary noise components other than servo current.

It is equipped with the TFHS (Track Forcus Holding System) mechanism that maintains stable pickup performance without audio skipping.
When there is a scratch or dirt on the disk surface, the tracking error signal (up / down) and the focus error signal (left / right) are accurately read, and the trace signal immediately before the scratch or dirt is retained by the pickup. After passing through the scratch or dirt area, it immediately starts reading the pit of the same track, so there is no unpleasant audio skipping.

It uses carefully selected audio parts such as electrolytic capacitors and power transformers.

It has a random program memory of up to 20 songs.

It is equipped with a music skip function that you can start the current song and the next song with one touch.

Equipped with a cue lock function that enables music search without holding down the fast forward / fast return buttons.

Equipped with 4-mode repeat function between 1 song / all songs / program / A and B.

Equipped with a 3-mode time counter that can display elapsed time, remaining time of one song, and total remaining time.

It is equipped with functions such as auto space, index search, music search and timer play.

The exterior of the front panel consists of rough scratch treatment and metallic paint stripe design.
In addition, the black model uses a rubber-coated border design.

Wireless remote control is included.
