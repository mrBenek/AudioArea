Commentary

CD player equipped with advanced 1-bit system DAC.

The D/A converter is equipped with an advanced 1-bit DAC that supports 8 times oversampling.
By taking advantage of the advantages of both 1-bit and multi-bit DACs and compensating for their disadvantages, this method is superior in noise and jitter resistance, as well as small signal conversion.

It adopts a center mechanical layout with the mechanism placed in the center, and by the housing design with the optimum weight balance, deterioration of sound quality due to vibration and resonance is suppressed.

Wireless remote control is included.
