  • DENON DMD-F07

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1996)

Commentary

Ef series MD recorder.

The D/A converter is equipped with a multi-bit system Λ S. L. C. (Lambda Super Linear Converter).
In this method, by shifting the digital data at the 0 cross point during D/A conversion, the 0 cross distortion at low volume is eliminated in principle, reducing noise and distortion.

It is equipped with a character input function, and you can enter up to 100 characters of the title name, song name and artist name in katakana, alphabet (upper and lower case), numbers and symbols for one song.
Up to 14 characters can be displayed on the display.

3 mm thick gold aluminum panel is used for the front panel.

It has built-in editing functions such as divide, move, combine, erase, etc. that are unique to MD.

It is equipped with performance functions such as 25-track program, random and repeat.

Ef series D-F07 and system operation such as Auto-ON, auto function, CD sync recording, timer recording and playback are available.

Wireless remote control is included.
