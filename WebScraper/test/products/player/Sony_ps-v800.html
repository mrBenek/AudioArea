  • SONY PS-V800

  • ¥ 12,400 (released in 1998)

Commentary

A full auto player with a phono equalizer amplifier.

With a full auto system, you can start playing with just one start button. When you finish playing, the arm automatically returns to rest.

It has a built-in phono equalizer amplifier and can be connected to audio equipment that does not have a Phono MM input.
The built-in phono equalizer amplifier can be turned ON/OFF and connected to the Phono MM input of the amplifier in the same way as a normal record player.

It supports 2 speeds of 33.1/3 rotation and 45 rotation.

A spare replacement needle is included.
