Commentary

This CD player is based on the professional player DN-3000F with added usability for consumer use.

A PIN terminal is provided on the lineout for general components.

The time from the start queue to sound output (sound rise time) is always constant at 0 seconds regardless of disc rotation.
When the attenuator is started, a delay start is possible.

The short access time allows for efficient playback.

Track number, index and time can be tracked automatically.
It can also automatically detect and cue the actual starting point of the sound in the track.

Equipped with an operating time integrator to measure operating time.

Audio can be monitored by speakers on the panel or by headphones.
