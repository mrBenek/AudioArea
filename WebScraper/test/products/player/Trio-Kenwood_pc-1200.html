Commentary

A record player equipped with auto mechanisms such as triode start, auto stop, and full stop.

It uses a tri-auto start system. The record is placed on the turntable, the arm is set on the start point, and by pulling the lever, the automatic mechanism operates and the motor guides, the idler enters the drive position and the arm goes down to the record and the performance starts.

Equipped with auto-stop function of auto-off and idler system.
When the performance is finished, the power of the motor is automatically turned off, the idler returns to the rest position and the arm quietly ascends.

It uses a full stop system. Just by setting the lever to the Full Stop position, when the playing of a record is finished, the d-lever of the whole device is automatically cut off and the device goes into a resting state.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used for the drive part.

A static balance type pipe arm is used for the tone arm.
It is a plug-in type head of JIS and EIA standards so that you can use your favorite cartridge.

A MM type cartridge is used.

A plastic cover is included as a dust cover.
