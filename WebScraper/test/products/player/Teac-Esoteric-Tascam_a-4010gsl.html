Commentary

This tape deck was developed by putting the latest technology into the A-4010 which became a best seller of 200000 units.

HD ferrite head is used for the head.
The front face of this head is completely shielded with ferrite and molded with hard glass to prevent track crosstalk. The sensitivity and saturation level of ferrite are improved by combining a permalloy core with good magnetic performance inside the head.

In order to bring out the performance of low-noise tape, a bias current changeover switch is mounted.

Equipped with an equalizer / level switch, you can adjust the amount of compensation in the high-frequency range when recording between a low-noise tape and a standard tape. Set the switch to High when recording with a low-noise tape, or to Normal when recording with a standard tape.
Low-noise tapes have a wider dynamic range than conventional standard tapes and can be recorded at higher levels. On the A-4010GSL, when the switch is set to High, the level indication of the VU meter is lowered by 3 VU and you can record up to + 6 VU while monitoring the meter.

Equipped with a double scale VU meter.
Since the scale of this meter is an expanded type close to a straight line, the interval between low and high levels is wide, and it can monitor up to + 3 vu, and the delivery level can be adjusted by the output volume linked to the VU meter.
The double scale consists of a normal scale and a 3 vu offset scale. When the equalizer / level selector switch is set to "High", the upper scale is read ; when set to "Normal", the lower scale is read.

Equipped with a pause mechanism that can be locked in either recording / playback / stop mode. Press the pause button to set the tape deck to record mode for preparation, and press the pause button again for recording without clicking noise.

A new type of reel clamp mechanism is adopted. With this mechanism, the shaft can be fixed securely only by screwing it to the right, and the reel can be removed only by turning the shaft to the left.

A height adjustment mechanism is provided to prevent the tape from being damaged by contact with the edge of the reel due to the difference in the height of the reel.
It can be adjusted by turning the screw attached to the reel stand left and right with a Phillips screwdriver.

It uses a soft touch mechanism combining a micro switch and relay.

Simultaneous monitoring during recording is possible by switching the switch.

By attaching a sensing foil to the back of the tape, reverse playback can be performed automatically at the desired position.

It is equipped with a dedicated amplifier of 4 units, 2 systems for recording and playback respectively. The equalizer stage of this amplifier is a 3-stage direct connection type.

A delay circuit is inserted in the pinch roller mechanism to improve the rise in reverse and to protect the tape.
When the capstan rotates in the reverse direction and the constant speed rotation is achieved, the pinch roller instantaneously compresses the tape and drives the tape.

Since the volume of microphone input and line input are independent, mixing is possible.

It has a headphone port.

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining the timer control adapter sold separately with the timer.
