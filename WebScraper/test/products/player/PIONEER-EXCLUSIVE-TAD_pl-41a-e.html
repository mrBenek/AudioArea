Commentary

Belt drive player with excellent accuracy as a finished product player.

A hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the motor, providing a structure that is resistant to voltage and load fluctuations.
It also uses a belt drive system with a polyurethane belt.

There was also a player unit PU-41A with only mechanism without cabinet.
