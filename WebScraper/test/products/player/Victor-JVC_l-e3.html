  • VICTOR/JVC L-E3

  • ¥ 51,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

A full-auto player with linear tracking system.

The record size can be automatically detected by the microcomputer controlled full auto mechanism, and the number of revolutions is also automatically selected.
In addition, manual feed of the arm by soft touch and optional switching of rotation speed are also possible.

The tone arm uses a low-mass, straight high-rigidity arm that is integrated with the cartridge.
This arm is a plug-in type, and an MC cartridge with an arm pipe sold separately can be used simply by replacing the pipe without adjusting the needle pressure, etc.

Coreless DD a coreless DD motor that eliminates the vibration characteristic of a DC motor.
In combination with high-precision FG velocity detection, accurate rotation is realized, and ± 2.5% pitch control is also equipped.

Equipped with an infinite repeat device.

It is equipped with an electronic arm lifter.

Uses a direct flash strobe.
