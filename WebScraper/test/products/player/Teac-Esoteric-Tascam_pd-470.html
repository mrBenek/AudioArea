  • TEAC PD-470

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1988)

Commentary

A CD player with twin D/A converter and 4 x oversampling.

A 16-bit D/A converter is used in the D/A converter section. Independent L/R and twin D/A converters are used to achieve higher sound quality.
The digital filter uses a 4 x oversampling digital filter.

The accumulated time is digitally displayed for each program of songs you want to record.
It also has a "pose program" function that allows you to put a pose into a program.
It is convenient to switch between A side and B side when recording with a cassette.

Features include a delete program function that cuts out unnecessary songs and plays only the songs you want to listen to, an index search function, a 20-track random memory program, direct random play, music skip, music search, cue lock, 4-mode repeat, and 3-mode time counter.
In addition, timer play is also possible by the combination with auto space which can set the music interval of about 4 seconds and timer sold separately.

CD single is available.

Full-function wireless remote control with 10 keys is equipped as standard.
