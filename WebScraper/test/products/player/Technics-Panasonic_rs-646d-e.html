  • Technics RS-646D(D-46)

  • ¥ 65,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Portable stereo cassette deck that is portable.

An electronically controlled DC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

It is equipped with a full auto-stop mechanism. When the tape reaches the end of the tape in all operations of REC/PLAY/FF/REW, the mechanism is automatically released and the STOP state is set.

Equipped with a silent stop mechanism to prevent the release sound of the mechanism.

Equipped with a level lock mechanism convenient for fade-in and fade-out.

As a noise reduction system, Dolby NR circuit is mounted.

The recording input can be switched by the selector to a 20 dB microphone Attenuator in addition to the line and microphone.

It has a built-in limiter circuit that enables sound recording with less distortion even when input is excessive.

Equipped with a 2-stage tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Uses LH head with excellent recording and re-recording characteristics and long life.
In addition, the erasing head has a ferrite structure that does not cause slippage, and uses a double-gap type with high erasing efficiency.

The amplifier section pursues high linearity and low noise, and measures such as the adoption of IC are taken.

Equipped with a battery check switch to indicate the depleted state of the battery.

Uses a meter light switch that turns on only when necessary.

It uses 3 power source system which can be driven by electric power line, battery and car battery.

It contains a 10 cm aperture monitor speaker and a 0.8W output monitor amplifier.
