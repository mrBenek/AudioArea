Commentary

This is a four channel deck with a complete three head system that can monitor playback even when recording four channels.

It is equipped with a pan pot mechanism that can locate the input of the back amplifier to the desired position on the front for 2-channel recording.

Direct coupling method of head and preamplifier is adopted.

Uses a mechanical tension regulator that maintains tape tension appropriately.

Equipped with a pause / muting function that is convenient for cutting short recording.

Equipped with a 20 dB microphone attenuator.

Microphone / line mixing is possible.

It is equipped with a scrape filter to suppress modulation noise.

It uses an escalation drive method.

Equipped with mechanical auto shut-off mechanism.

Equipped with a tape selector that can use SLH tapes.

Equipped with line-out volume.

There was a special dust protector sold separately.
