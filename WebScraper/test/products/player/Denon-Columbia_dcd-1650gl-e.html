Commentary

A special limited model CD player developed by refining the DCD-1650G.

The DAC is a specially selected product for DENON (AD1862N-Z version).
We also carefully select materials for capacitors, resistors, and substrates to ensure high quality.

The D/A converter uses the Λ S. L. C (Super Linear Converter) 4D/A conversion method to improve the reproduction capability of small signals. In addition, the output of one side of the Λ processor is delayed by a half period of 8 fs and combined at the output stage to obtain an operation equivalent to 16 fs, shifting noise to a higher frequency band, improving phase characteristics and resolution.

The chassis has a double structure, and the insulator is a sintered alloy covered with an aluminum cover.
In addition, the pickup part is made to float by low-repulsion rubber and spring, and vibration isolation measures such as separating the mechanism, power supply part and circuit system are taken.

The chassis has a grace gold finish and comes standard with sidewoods and trap doors.
In addition, the disc tray has been given a special and amount of suede to improve the texture.

Uses AC cord with polarity display, large power transformer and large capacity smoothing capacitor.

It is equipped with balanced output terminal which was equipped with DCD-3500RG, etc.

It is equipped with functions such as peak search, time / auto-edit, fader function, up to 20 songs program, 4-level display dimmer, digital pitch commander, etc.

It has a remote control volume that can also be used for headphones.

Wireless remote control is included.
