  • TEAC f-200VA Synchro Boy

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

A cassette deck that can be used in a wide range of applications by installing a new mechanism in addition to the functions of the conventional tape deck.

f-200VA is equipped with new functions such as simulsync recording, blend microphone and cross feed.

In a simulsync recording, the left channel is recorded first. If you rewind and then press the simulsync button to start recording, the left channel becomes regenerating condition and only the right channel becomes recording. This allows you to record to the right channel while monitoring the left channel with your headphones. In this case, the meter for the left channel turns off and the simulsync recording is displayed. Unlike mixing lines and microphones, this function does not erase the sound of the left channel.
For example, you can use it to record a guitar duet alone.

The cross-feed allows sound to be spread appropriately.
Simulsync Recorded sound can be played separately on the left and right side as it is. Crossfeed can be improved by making the left and right sound cross to the center, so that it can be played back as a natural stereo.

With the Blend Microphone function, you can record mixed sound at the center.
First, set the Input select button to Line and plug the microphone into the left channel. Then you can monitor and record line inputs such as records with headphones while simultaneously recording the sound from the blend microphone to the center.
If you use a blend microphone during stereo playback, you can mix in the center position.

Equipped with a memory switch.
If you set the counter to 000 at the beginning of the song and press the memory switch, it will stop automatically at 999 when rewinding.

By using the green VU meter, the recording level can be confirmed more clearly.

High hardness permalloy head is used for the head.

FG servo DC motor is used as the drive motor.
This motor uses a built-in frequency generator (FG) to suppress changes in motor rotation caused by voltage and frequency fluctuations of the power supply.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a 2-stage bias / equalizer switching mechanism, recording and playback can be performed according to the characteristics of the tape.

Equipped with timer mechanism, timer recording / playback is possible by combining with an audio timer sold separately.
In addition, the f-200VA is equipped with a delay circuit so that it starts after the mechanism becomes stable a few seconds after the power is turned on.

Equipped with line / microphone input selection switch.
This switch allows the line input to be sent directly to the recording amplifier without passing through the microphone amplifier, eliminating distortion and noise when passing through the microphone amplifier and improving the dynamic range of the line input.
