Commentary

Mini component size twin recording re-reverse deck that can record two volumes simultaneously and play continuously.

Both decks use a twin Rec reverse configuration for recording and playback.

The head uses a laser amorphous head with LC-OFC for the head winding.

Super bias is adopted.

Equipped with Dolby NR B-C type.

Equipped with linear electronic counter with subtraction function of A/B deck independent count.

Equipped with AMS function and blank skip function.
Equipped with an auto tape selector.

By using the timer sold separately, it is possible to record the absence and play the alarm clock.

Equipped with Rec Mute function with auto space.

It comes with a wireless remote controller that can also record CD sync.

The side wood is removable.
