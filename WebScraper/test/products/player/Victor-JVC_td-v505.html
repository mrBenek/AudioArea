Commentary

A 3-head cassette deck that achieves both sound quality and cost performance.

Combination 3 heads are mounted on the head part.
High-hardness MetaPalm with excellent wear resistance is used for the head, and PCOCC is used for the coil winding and lead wire of the head.

An arc base is installed in the lower part of the chassis to cope with vibration and resonance caused by vibration and external sound when the mechanism is activated.
The arc base is a compression molded board, and by mounting the mechanism and transformer directly on it, each weight is increased equivalently, and operating vibration and external vibration are absorbed.
In addition, the cassette door is equipped with a newly developed stabilized power ejector door to prevent the resonance of the lid, and the vibration of the cassette half itself is held by the half shell stabilizer, greatly reducing acoustic modulation noise.

The mechanism section is equipped with a closed-loop dual capstan mechanism.
In combination with a DD motor, it greatly reduces sound loss such as stability of head touch, uneven rotation, noise reduction during rotation, etc.

A zinc die-cast block is used in the head base to suppress the influence of external vibration on the head and reduce total modulation noise.

Carefully selected parts are also used for internal parts such as transformers and condensers.

The bias frequency is set at a high frequency of 210 kHz to suppress interference of the bias frequency.

In addition to Dolby B/C NR, Dolby HX PRO is installed.

Linear counter is adopted.

Equipped with built-in oscillator re-calibration.

Equipped with an auto monitor function, the recorded tape sound is automatically switched during recording, and the source sound is automatically switched during pause standby.

Equipped with an automatic music selection function.

Power cord with polarity display is used.

Equipped with timer start mechanism and can be combined with separately sold timer.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Equipped with a memory button.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Wireless remote control is included.
