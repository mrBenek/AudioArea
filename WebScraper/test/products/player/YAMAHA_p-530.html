  • YAMAHA P-530

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1987)

Commentary

A record player with a full auto function.

A direct drive system is adopted for the turntable drive part.
A DC motor is used for the motor, and the FG quartz servo system is used to compare the FG (frequency generator) signal with the quartz signal to achieve stable rotation.

MM type cartridge of T4P plug-in standard is adopted.

Uses a front operation method that allows all operations with the dust cover closed.

Play/Stop can be controlled from the system remote controller installed on the AVC-30/AVC-50.
