Audio Database

  • Marantz CD-72a

  • ¥ 75,000 (released in 1993)

Commentary

A CD player with a pickup mechanism equipped with CDM-4.

DAC7 is used for the D/A converter.

The audio part uses a discrete circuit based on HDAM concept.

High-frequency noise is suppressed by copper plated chassis / copper plated shield case.

There are 2 variations, gold and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
