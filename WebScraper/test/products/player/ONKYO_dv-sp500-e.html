  • ONKYO DV-SP500

  • ¥ 45,000 (Available on July 10, 2002)

Commentary

This DVD player is equipped with progressive scan circuit and newly developed VLSC circuit.

It has a built-in progressive scan circuit for smoother images.

It is equipped with a 27 mhz / 10-bit video D/A converter that realizes a horizontal resolution of approximately 540 lines for video.

For audio, a 192 kHz / 24-bitD / A converter and newly developed VLSC circuits provide excellent 2-channel playback.

It has a built-in direct digital path circuit that enables clean and direct digital signal transmission.

Uses a 2-wavelength twin focus pickup compatible with CD-R/CD-RW/MP3.

Equipped with a setup navigator that enables easy and interactive initial setup.

Equipped with a Dolby Digital & DTS digital audio output.

You can switch between 96 kHz and 48 kHz PCM digital output.

It is equipped with a high-speed resume function and a last memory function which are convenient for interrupting during playback.

It is equipped with a search function that specifies the chapter and time and plays it instantly.

It is equipped with a repeat play function that repeatedly plays your favorite tracks and chapters.

It is equipped with a program playback function that plays back up to 24 steps in the desired order.

It is equipped with a random playback function that plays in random order.

It is equipped with a condition memory function that can store up to 15 DVD settings you often watch.

Equipped with a screen saver function to prevent the display from burning.

Equipped with an RI terminal that enables operation of the DV-SP500 with a remote controller such as an Onkyo AV amplifier with the RI mark.

Wireless remote control is included.
