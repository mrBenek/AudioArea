Commentary

CD player with non-resonance and non-vibration technology.

The mechanism part uses an independent suspension mechanism pursuing non-resonance and non-vibration.
In this mechanism, the rotating system is fixed to a sturdy mechanical base mixed with glass. Together with the pickup section, it is floated from the chassis by a special shape of insulation rubber. By this, disturbance vibrations and sound pressure of speakers are shielded, and traceability is improved.

The Y servo system is used for the servo circuit.
The Y servo minimizes the operation of the error correction function and increases the traceability of the pickup body. When an error occurs, the servo loop characteristics are automatically adjusted so that the pickup can accurately capture the signal pit.

Equipped with 2 x oversampling digital filter.
The sampling frequency is doubled to 88.2 kHz, and unwanted signals located just above the audible band are removed by a digital filter and then attenuated by a slow analog filter with good phase characteristics.

For analog circuits, filters are composed of discrete parts with good sound quality to reduce sound quality degradation.

It is equipped with functions such as random program function of up to 8 songs, heading by program / song number and index, skip / 2-speed manual search, all repeat, program repeat, pause, etc.
