  • YAMAHA K-100X

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

Double reverse cassette deck with program function.

You can play a random program that allows you to specify the order of up to 15 out of 30 songs, or you can dub a random program that allows you to pick up and edit only your favorite songs.
In addition, by utilizing the program mode, it is possible to skip 30 songs before and after.

There are three types of dubbing modes. In either of Deck A or Deck B, the side that reaches the reverse position first waits and automatically aligns the head of the other side for recording. In addition, there are two types of dubbing modes : Shake Dubbing, which enables recording with the head of the other side automatically aligned, and Skip Dubbing, which enables blank skipping when the non-recording portion of the playback side continues for more than 15 seconds, while the recording side waits for the, and Random Program Dubbing.
In all dubbing modes, two speeds are possible : constant speed and double speed.

A Dolby B/C is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a relay playback function capable of switching playback from deck A to deck B up to 8 times.

Equipped with a REC return function that returns to the recording start point and stops when the rewind switch is pressed directly during recording.

Equipped with Auto-Rec Mute, which makes a 4-second blank with one touch and then enters a rec pause state.

Equipped with Music Search, Blank Search and 3 reverse mode.
