Commentary

Double cassette deck with double-speed dubbing mechanism

Equipped with double-speed dubbing function.

It is equipped with a music sensor specify the beginning of the music.

Equipped with bias fine adjustment knob corresponding to normal tape and CrO2 tape.

A Dolby B/C is installed as a noise reduction system.

Auto Rec Mute is installed.

Equipped with an auto tape selector compatible with normal / CrO2 / metal.

Equipped with a one push recording mechanism that allows one touch recording.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism, and by connecting a timer sold separately, it is possible to record absence and play alarm clock.
