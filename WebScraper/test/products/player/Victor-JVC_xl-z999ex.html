Commentary

A CD player developed as a culmination of three K2 processors.

In addition to the K2 interface, 20bitK2 super coding and 20bitK2 processing, which have improved sound quality by the cooperation of software and hardware of JVC, it is equipped with Extended K2 processing which has advanced further by the flow of high bit high sampling.
In this technology, the input digital signal is assumed to be the music signal before band limitation, and is expanded to twice the sampling frequency and reproduced. This makes it possible to recover natural harmonic components close to the original music signal, reproducing subtle differences in sound quality, afterglow, and the atmosphere of the performance venue.

The 4-power transformer 22 independent power supply system, 2-layer chassis and 3-block structure for mechanical arrangement to the center of gravity point, and the shortest signal path eliminate adverse effects due to mutual interference.
In addition, we aim to reproduce high-purity music signals by connecting digital and analog boards with a high-speed balanced transmission circuit, and by adopting a K2 interface for high-speed balanced transmission that removes extraneous components such as jitter and ripple added to digital waveforms from the root.

Equipped with 3fs-capable 20-bit K2 digital outputs and Route 3 inputs, you can not only play CDs, but also combine them with BS, MD, DAT and external processors.

The CD transport section uses a top-loading system.
In addition to the highly airtight air-tight structure, the top cover uses a metal-evaporated glass door to minimize adverse effects of electromagnetic waves and infrared rays.
In addition, the drive system is equipped with a linear torque motor with an extremely thick spindle shaft with a diameter of 6 mm and a center-of-gravity point holding mechanism that stabilizes the operation of the pickup. This ensures high rigidity and high accuracy.

Wireless remote control is included.

There was a large diameter clamper as an option sold separately.
