  • VICTOR/JVC L-E5

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A full-auto player with linear tracking system.

It uses a linear tracking method in which the arm moves horizontally straight in parallel with the sound groove of the record. Since tracking error is less than that of the rotary arm, tracing distortion is reduced. In addition, the inside force is 0 in principle, and separation is improved and harmful intermodulation distortion is reduced.
The L-E5 is equipped with a non-contact arm horizontal angle detection device using a photo sensor in the arm travel mechanism. It detects an extremely small deviation in parallelism and drives the arm base with a high-speed servocontrol. This is an application of the electronic control arm technology developed with the QL-Y series player, and realizes accurate and stable tracking without requiring high stability.

Low-mass straight short arm is used for the arm part.
In addition, a new gimbal support system for virtual one point support with high sensitivity and excellent stability is adopted in the bearing section.

The cartridge uses a dual magnet type low-mass integrated cartridge developed for the L-E5.

The auto mechanism uses an electronic servo full auto mechanism controlled by a micro computer. This mechanism uses the drive system of the linear tracking arm as it is.
All you have to do is set the size and number of revolutions by putting the record on the turntable. It is controlled by a microcomputer by pressing the start button. You can also play from the middle of the record by moving the arm to any position with the arm feed button.
If you press the repeat button, you can play repeatedly.

The turntable uses a direct drive system using a coreless DC type FG servo motor.

The cabinet has a compact design and a high-density design made of materials that do not transmit vibration to limit the effects of resonance and vibration.
In addition, measures against howling are taken by installing insulators.

Side hinge system has been adopted for the dust cover so that it does not protrude backward even when opened.
