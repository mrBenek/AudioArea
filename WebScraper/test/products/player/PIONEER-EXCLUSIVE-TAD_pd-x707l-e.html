  • Pioneer PD-X707L

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

A 360 mm wide compact CD player with LV digital sound decoding.

Equipped with LV Digital Sound Decode capability to enable digital playback of Laser Vision Disc, sound can be extracted from Laser Vision Disc with digital sound.

It uses a disk stabilizer to prevent disk resonance and a linear servo system to prevent sound skipping.

It is possible to play a random program in which you can listen to your favorite songs in the order of your choice.
You can also play up to 27 songs in the program.

Equipped with a repeat playback function.

Equipped with track search and manual 3-speed search that enables fast forward / fast reverse while listening to sound.

Subcode output terminal is mounted.
