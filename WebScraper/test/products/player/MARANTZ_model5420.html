  • Marantz Model5420

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

The cassette deck is the top-of-the-line machine that pursues operability close to that of the open deck.

Ferrite head is used for the head.

The input terminal is composed of four separate circuits.
Each channel has its own level control for mixing, fade-in, fade-out, and cross-fading.

Equipped with a recording pan pot system, you can move the stereo image of an audio source from right to left or left to right.

Equipped with 2-color illumination VU meter and peak indicator.

Built-in memory rewind function.

Equipped with MPX filter.

Equipped with a recording limiter switch.

Equipped with a pause mechanism with electronic muting.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.
