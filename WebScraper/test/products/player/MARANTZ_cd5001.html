  • Marantz CD5001

  • ¥ 28,000 (Released in September 2005)

Commentary

A CD player with a design that matches PM4001.

The D/A converter uses CS4392, a 192 kHz / 24-bit product made by Cirrus Logic.

It has a headphone circuit with an input buffer circuit.
The input buffer circuit reduces the interaction with the audio output circuit and provides stable sound without fluctuation.

It is equipped with a quick replay function and plays back from 10 seconds before with one touch.

Equipped with a pitch control function.
The CD5001 can be adjusted up to ± 12% in 1% steps.

The front panel is made of extruded aluminum material and is designed to match PM4001.
We have also obtained a significant effect in damping the microvibrations generated by CD drives.

Equipped with a dot matrix display that supports CD text.

Gold plated terminals are used for analog output and digital coaxial output.

Equipped with a remote control terminal, Marantz devices can work together and control the system.

It is equipped with functions such as program of up to 30 songs, delete program, random, all songs / one song / A-B repeat, AMS and edit.

Equipped with a display off function.

CD-R/RW playback is supported.

Equipped with peak search.

Equipped with 20 songs music calendar.

There are 2 color variations of gold and black.
