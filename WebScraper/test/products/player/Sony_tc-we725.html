  • SONY TC-WE725

  • ¥ 34,000 (released in 1998)

Commentary

Twin recording re-reverse deck capable of recording on either A or B deck.

Both decks possible on both decks, and external sources can be simultaneously recorded on two tapes.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

The A deck has a wide-range pitch control function that allows you to adjust the tape speed within ± 30% during playback.

Equipped with RMS function for 28 songs (14 songs on one side) convenient for editing from tape to tape.
With this, you can make programs in your favorite order of songs on the A deck and record on the B deck.

In addition to normal speed, high-speed dubbing that copies tapes at double speed is also possible.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO, the bias current is controlled in units of one thousandth of a second to keep high linearity in the high frequency range.

Both decks are equipped with automatic calibration that automatically checks the characteristics of the tape.

Equipped with a sound auto-focus that controls the recording level setting with a microcomputer. This function can be switched between auto and manual with a switch.

FL linear counter with subtraction function is installed independently on each A/B deck.

Equipped with a fader button, you can fade in and fade out with a single button when recording.

It is equipped with a multi-AMS function that allows quick cue by specifying the number of up to 30 tracks before and after the track being played.

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining with the separately sold timer.

Wireless remote control is included.
