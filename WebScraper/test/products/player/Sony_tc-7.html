Audio Database

  • SONY TC-7

  • ¥ 19,800 (around 1972)

Commentary

An 8-track tape player that can play 8-track tapes by connecting to your stereo.

When you insert the tape, the music starts automatically and plays continuously.

It has a channel selector button.

Equipped with an auto-eject function that automatically stops when the tape ends and ejects the cartridge.
