  • Technics SL-D5

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A D. D. auto-changer that allows you to enjoy the continuous performance of up to records.

The B. FG method is adopted for motor speed detection, and excellent load-speed fluctuation characteristics are obtained.
The motor is an integrated D. D. motor.

The cabinet and base have a double vibration isolation structure using TNRC, an acoustic material unique to Technics.

The tone arm uses a gimbal suspension system to achieve a high initial motion sensitivity of 7 mg.

All front operation system is adopted.
