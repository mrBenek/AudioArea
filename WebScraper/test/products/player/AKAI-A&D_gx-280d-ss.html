Audio Database

  • AKAI GX-280D-SS

  • ¥ 199,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

A 4-channel tape deck with a GX head for recording and reproducing heads.

It supports tape speeds of 19cm/s and 9.5cm/s, and 4-track, 4-ch / 2-ch recording is possible.

Equipped with an auto-repeat circuit capable of 4-channel continuous playback.

2 ch Oatley Verse playback is possible.

Uses a direct capstan drive system with servo motors.
