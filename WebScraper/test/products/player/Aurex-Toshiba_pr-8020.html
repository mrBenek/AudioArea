  • Aurex PR-8020

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Stereo tape deck with real-time pause switch.

A push switch type is adopted, and temporary stop and start can be operated instantaneously.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism, you can record your absence using a timer sold separately.

It is equipped with a two step tape selector for the bias and equalizer.

A 3-head system is used for the head part, and a high-hardness permalloy head is used for the read / write head.

Microphone and line mixing recording is possible.

Equipped with auto shut-off mechanism.
