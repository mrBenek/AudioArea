Commentary

A double Oatley berth cassette deck capable of relay recording.
Relay recording and playback is possible using A and B decks.

In order to bring out the capabilities of cassette tapes, it is equipped with a bias adjustment function that can be used either automatically or manually.

It is equipped with a dual counter using a large fluorescent display tube.

Setting the Recording Level of a CD Automatically I see through the C. C. R. S.

In addition to the Dolby B/C noise reduction system, the Dolby HX-PRO is also installed.

Equipped with functions such as D. P.S. S., auto tape selector and high-speed dubbing.
