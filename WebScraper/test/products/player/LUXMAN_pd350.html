  • LUXMAN PD350

  • ¥ 240,000 (Released in September 1983)

Commentary

Turntable adopting adsorption system.

A belt drive system is adopted for the drive system. A turntable with a weight of 9.5 kg and an inertial mass of 1.2t is driven by a uniquely designed super aramid belt.

The chassis has a high rigidity integrated structure with the turntable bearing and arm base mounting part, and is combined with chassis insulation.

Designed to use vacuum unit VS300, sold separately.
The VS300 has a built-in power supply for the PD350.

The arm base is sold separately and you can choose the one that matches the tone arm.
