Commentary

An AV cassette deck with a stereo audio track and a cue track for projector control.
It is useful for multi-image programming of video and audio.

System control using a microprocessor is adopted.

Tape speeds are available in 9.5 cm (High) and 4.8 cm (STD).

Simulsync recording is possible.

With the built-in OSC Cue Pulse, auto present (Oatley Peat) is possible at any tape position.

The meter section consists of three meters, two VU meters and one peak meter.

Equipped with a headphone port that can be controlled independently.

Equipped with Dolby NR system.

It is equipped with a for connecting an external noise reduction system (such as dbx).

Equipped with a switch that can change the output level in 2 steps of -10dB/0dBV.

Equipped with meter and output level linked volume.

The tape is compatible with Co (CrO2) tape.

A remote controller capable of Cue Pulse recording was sold separately.

The rack handle in the picture is optional and sold separately.
