  • Panasonic RS-DCM1

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1994)

Commentary

The newly developed Mash 1-bit type is used for both the AD converter and DA converter, and the newly developed LSI is also used for the signal processor of the PASC to pursue high sound quality.

You can enter characters such as album name and artist name on the tape. Scroll display function is supported for 12 characters or more.

High-speed search function that changes the tape speed and accurately reads marker information.

All digital sources such as CD, DAT, BS and CS-PCM can be recorded.

High-speed search, high-speed search is possible at up to 60 times speed.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR for analog cassette playback.
