  • Technics RS-263DU

  • ¥ 43,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck using AC-DC2 power source system.

Uses an AC-DC2 power supply system that allows outdoor recording by connecting an optional battery set.

LH head is adopted for the head.

A dolby circuit is installed as a noise reduction system.

The new 70 μ S equalizer circuit improves sound quality.

Equipped with a memory rewind mechanism.

Equipped with a light switch convenient for DC drive.
