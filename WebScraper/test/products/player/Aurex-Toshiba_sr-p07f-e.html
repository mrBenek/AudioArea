  • Aurex SR-P07F

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

A full auto player with an electronically controlled tone arm.

The tone arm is driven electronically by a non-contact position sensor and a DD horizontal linear motor. This eliminates mechanical sounds and external addition during tracing, which were generated in conventional mechanical types, and prevents arm vibration due to low-frequency resonance.

An electronic anti-skating mechanism is also adopted, and the control amount can be changed according to the needle pressure.

The DD motor is driven by a quartz lock PLL circuit with excellent rotational accuracy.

The cabinet is made of phenol-calcium carbonate glass fiber as the main component and is resistant to howling and has excellent acoustic characteristics.

Equipped with MC cartridge as standard.

Gold plating is used for the head shell terminal.

Equipped with a dust cover.
