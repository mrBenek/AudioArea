  • Technics SL-55

  • ¥ 42,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

A record player that pursues cost-performance.

The drive section uses a direct drive system using an ultra-low speed electronic commutator motor.

The speed change can be adjusted by 33 1/3 or 45 rpm independently by 10%.

The needle pressure application mechanism is 0 balanced, and the needle pressure adjustment is a simple rack opinion type.

The tone arm uses a die-cast high-sensitivity S-shaped pipe arm.
Equipped with an oil dump type arm lifter.

We have adopted a MM type cartridge using a newly developed magnet.

The legs have built-in audio insulators.

You can add a plus arm.
