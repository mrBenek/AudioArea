  • SONY TC-K4

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

This cassette deck was created by inheriting the technology of the high-grade TC-K7.

It is an upright see-through type cassette loading with all front operation, and the remaining amount of the tape can be by the see-through lamp.

F & F head is used for the head.

The motor is equipped with a frequency control type servo motor with FG.

Equipped with a Dolby NR system as a noise reduction system.
It also has an MPX filter switch.

Equipped with a 3-step switching type tape selector with independent equalizer and bias.

By connecting a timer (sold separately), you can record your absence or play an alarm clock.

Equipped with a RecMute mechanism that enables one touch silent recording.

Uses a soft eject that opens the cassette lid smoothly with an air damper.

Equipped with a large level meter and peak level indicator.

Equipped with auto shut-off mechanism.

Equipped with volume for both headphone and line out.

An optional audio handle is attached and rack-mountable.
