  • Pioneer CT-800

  • ¥ 92,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A stereo cassette deck with a new swing lever.

A new electronic swing lever is adopted as the operation method.
By using an electronic system, direct change is possible, so quick operation is possible.

A 2-motor system is used for the drive part.
A PLL servo motor is used for constant-speed drive to ensure high rotational accuracy by controlling the rotational speed with phase difference based on a reference signal from a highly accurate oscillator, and a DC torque motor is used for rapid feed.

Equipped with CrO2 automatic switching mechanism.

It uses a bias adjustment mechanism and display meter to select recording bias suitable for tape characteristics.

Equipped with an auto-repeat mechanism, repeated playback is possible.

Equipped with a wide-range meter that can switch between peak and VU.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with an MPX filter release function.

It can be mounted on an EIA-standard rack by using a separately sold rack-mount adapter.
