Commentary

A cassette deck with a direct function change mechanism.

Equipped with Dolby NR system.

Equipped with a tape selector for chrome tapes.

Equipped with a limiter function.

Hard permalloy head is used for the head.

Equipped with full-release auto-stop mechanism and lock type pause mechanism.

Equipped with a tape running indicator lamp.
