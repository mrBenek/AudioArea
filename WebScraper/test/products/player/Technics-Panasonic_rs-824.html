  • National RS-824

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

An 8-track karaoke deck where you can enjoy karaoke by connecting to the audio system.

Equipped with a microphone mixing function.

It supports sound multiplex tapes as well as normal 8-track, 4-track / 8-track tapes.

It is equipped with a fast-forward function of approximately 4.5 times speed that can quickly start a song.

Equipped with 2045 stage BBD electronic echo.

Equipped with tape speed control function of ± 12%.

Equipped with a microphone tone color selection switch, you can choose from 3 types : soft, standard, or fine.

Two microphone terminals are installed.

Equipped with a large program button, you can select songs with one push.
