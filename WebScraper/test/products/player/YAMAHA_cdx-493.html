  • YAMAHA CDX-493

  • ¥ 28,000 (released in 1998)

Commentary

A CD player with a 1-bit D/A converter.

Stable reading performance is obtained by adopting an intelligent digital servo in the mechanism part.

Equipped with synchronized recording / tape program / autospace function.

It is equipped with a dimmer function to select the brightness of the display.

Wireless remote control is included.

There are 2 color variations, titanium color and black.
