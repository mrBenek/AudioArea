  • SONY CDP-CX100F

  • ¥ 59,000 (released in 1995)

Commentary

A 100-disc CD changer that can store 100 discs together.

Discs can be grouped into up to 10 groups, such as music genre or artist, and the same disc can be in more than one group.

Operability is improved by operation using the jog dial.

The D/A converter is equipped with a hybrid pulse D/A converter, and the SN ratio is improved by incorporating a noise-shaping digital filter in one chip that performs high-precision oversampling operations.
In addition, the signal path is simplified by using a single chip, and the effect of the direct digital sync circuit formed in the chip is also improved, reducing jitter.

The servo circuit is equipped with a high-precision digital servo, and the adoption of a high-sampling rate A/D and D/A converter realizes servocontrol that accurately reflects disk conditions.

It has a custom file function and can store disk memos, delete banks, group files, etc.

It is equipped with functions such as delete play, delete shuffle, multi mode timer, and 6 mode repeat.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

It is equipped with a play function in cooperation with the AV amplifier AVU-700, and data of disk memo can be transferred using the control A terminal.
This allows you to enjoy a CD while watching a menu on the TV screen with the one button remote control of the AVU-700.

Wireless remote control is included.
