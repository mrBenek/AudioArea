Audio Database

  • YAMAHA K-300B

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with IC full logic control and Dolby C.

A hard permalloy head is used for the read / write head.

Equipped with an auto tape selector compatible with metal, chrome and normal.

A 2-motor separate drive system is adopted for the mechanism part.

Equipped with 2-color 7-dot LED peak level meter.

Equipped with timer REC/PLAY mechanism.
