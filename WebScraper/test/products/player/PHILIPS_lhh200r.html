Commentary

This CD player inherits the concept and technology of the high-end LHH800R and 500R.

The bit stream D/A converter DAC7 is used for the D/A converter, and the performance of DAC7 is using the single differential mode.

A high-speed discrete amplifier is installed in the differential amplifier in the latter stage. By eliminating the operational amplifier, the simplification and low NFB are carried out thoroughly, and the high through rate is obtained, and the sharp rise of sound and the deep sound which is unique to the reference series are obtained.

It uses the non-feedback high-speed power supply and the third passive low-pass filter used in LHH800R and LHH500R.

The drive uses CDM-9, a swing arm pickup mechanism.

Using a layout with a mechanism in the center, interference between the digital and analog sections is greatly reduced.
In addition, the chassis is equipped with a 2 mm thick base chassis and a 1.6 mm thick mechanical base with a diaphragm to increase the strength. The brackets are shielded by copper plating to ensure stability and strength.

3-point support type sintered alloy insulator is used for the leg part.
For this insulator, you can choose either a point grounding or an interview site.

Uses high-precision parts common to the reference series such as metal film resistance with an error of ± 1% or less, dip mica capacitor, high voltage electrolytic capacitor, etc.

Wireless remote control is included.

As an option sold separately, there was an aluminum side panel (refer to the picture above) and a brass point base.
