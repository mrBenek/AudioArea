Commentary

Double reverse deck with PC-OCC winding amorphous head.

An amorphous head with PC-OCC winding is used for the head.
This head is made of amorphous material with high magnetic flux density, and PC-OCC is used for coil winding to improve sound quality.

As a noise reduction system, dbx is installed in addition to Dolby B and C types.

Equipped with Dolby HX Pro, which improves the dynamic range and frequency characteristics of the high-frequency range.
To prevent over-biasing of the high-frequency component that acts as a bias current during recording, the Dolby HX Pro adjusts the bias amount according to the high-frequency signal.
The Dolby HX Pro works only during recording, so it also works well when played on an unequipped deck.

Both decks 1 and 2 use a quick reverse mechanism with a sound cut off time of 0.3 seconds.

It is equipped with a bias fine adjustment mechanism in order to bring out the performance of the tape, and the bias value which changes for each tape can be adjusted to be optimum.

Equipped with a digital tape counter.

Equipped with constant speed / double speed one push dubbing buttons.

Equipped with a continuous play function capable of playing two tapes continuously for 24 hours.

Equipped with a blank grip function.

Equipped with a music sensor.

Equipped with a tracking recording and tracking mute mechanism.

Equipped with auto-rec mute function.

Cabin back lighting is installed on both decks.

Timer Rec/Play is available.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

The headphone jack has a gold-plated terminal.

Equipped with a wireless remote control that can operate both decks 1 and 2.
