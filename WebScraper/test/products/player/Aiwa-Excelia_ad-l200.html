  • AIWA AD-L200

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck for mixing play.

Microphone and line-in level controls allow for microphone recording mixing and playback mixing to match recorded tapes.

Equipped with a 2-color 12-point FL optical display. The FL is a fluorescent display tube, which has a high response speed (5 μ s) to signals and is visually clear.
On the optical display, the VU and peak can be displayed by switching.

Ultra-hard permalloy head is used for the head.

Equipped with bias fine adjustment mechanism for LH tape.

LED is used for each display.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with a quick review queue mechanism.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism, and by connecting a timer sold separately, it is possible to record absence and play alarm clock.

Equipped with a Dolby system with built-in MPX filter.

Adopts a mechanical full auto-stop mechanism.

Equipped with an oil dump eject.

Equipped with an end mark that makes it easy to check the remaining amount of tape.
