  • SANSUI QD-5500S

  • ¥ 169,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 4-channel tape deck capable of 2-channel and 4-channel recording / playback and 2-channel reverse playback.

Hard permalloy heads are used for recording and reproducing heads.

The use of vibration isolation rollers and impedance rollers reduces modulation noise.

An optional remote control unit can be connected.
