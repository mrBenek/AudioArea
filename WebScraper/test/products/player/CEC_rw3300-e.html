  • CEC RW3300

Commentary

CD recorder capable of 4 x speed dubbing.

This dual-deck type CD recorder combines the player and recorder sections into a single case. It can dub music CDs (including HDCD) in their entirety with one touch.
The dubbing mode can be selected from constant speed, 2 x speed, and 4 x speed. When the whole dubbing is finished at 2 x speed or 4 x speed, the system automatically finalizes the dubbing.
In addition, it is possible to dub by programming part of CD or favorite music.

It can be used as a CD changer because it can repeat playback, program playback and random playback across two discs.

Equipped with an AD converter, recording from an analog device connected to the external input terminal is also possible.
It is also equipped with a sampling rate converter (32 to 48 kHz) for digital recording from MD/DAT/CS/BS etc. connected to the digital input terminal.

The recording level can be adjusted by digital volume (± 18 dB, approximately 1 dB step).

When recording from digital input, if the auto-track function is selected, it is possible to detect the change of music and assign the number automatically (only when recording a signal with track information such as a CD).
For analog input, the song number is automatically updated at the start of the next song after a silence period of 3 seconds or more (below the preset increment level). The increment level can be selected from -60dB, -50dB, and -40dB.
If you select a manual track, you can assign a track number to the desired location.
