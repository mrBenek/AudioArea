Audio Database

  • Toshiba SD-1500

  • Discretionary price (released February 1, 2001)

Commentary

DVD player compatible with a variety of optical disks.

Video part

In addition to the DVD-Video disc, video CD, and music CD that were playable in previous models, the new CD-R/RW format is also supported.
CD-R/RW can only play media recorded in digital audio format.

Equipped with an image adjustment function, you can select an image quality mode suitable for the image you are viewing from 5 image quality settings.
The Standard mode produces images with standard quality and contrast, while the Bright mode produces natural and bright images with reduced contrast.
Movie 1 is a dark movie mode that improves the reproduction of dark areas with bright images, Movie 2 is a bright movie mode that produces moist and glossy images, and Animation is an animation movie mode that produces vivid, dynamic, and tight images with vivid colors.

Equipped with a 10-bit / 27-MHz video processing circuit, the DVD video signal expressed in 8-bit data is converted into 10-bit data, and then the arithmetic processing is carried out to make it analog to prevent information loss.

D1 video output terminal and component video output terminal are mounted.

Auto pause mode is set, and still image display can be controlled automatically according to the condition.
The CPU automatically controls the image so that it becomes a field still image when there is a flicker, and becomes a frame still image when there is no flicker, and the still image without unnatural feeling is reproduced.

In order to take full advantage of the characteristics of the video encoder, it is equipped with a video amplifier with an integrated LPF, which improves the matching with the video encoder and greatly improves the linearity of the video signal.

It is equipped with an optimal servomechanism that realizes stable playback by adapting to the disc condition.

Equipped with a bit rate indicator display function.
When video data is compressed and recorded on a DVD using MPEG2, a variable transfer rate technology is used to allocate the amount of data according to changes in the state of the image. SD 1500 uses numbers and level bars to indicate the amount of data transferred for each image during playback of DVD software.

It is equipped with letterbox conversion circuit which can enjoy beautiful images even on 4 : 3 TV.

Equipped with an S video terminal.

Audio part

Equipped with a sound field effect function, you can select the sound mode that matches the situation of the image you are viewing from among three sound settings.
The standard mode reproduces the original sound without any special effects, and the 3D (N-2-2) provides stereo sound with only two front speakers.
In Movie Voice Mode, you can play back software recorded on 5.1 ch by adjusting the volume so that you can hear the lines easily even at low volume.

The D/A converter uses an audio DAC that supports 192 kHz / 24-bit linear PCM.
A dynamic range of 108 dB or more and a total harmonic distortion rate of 0.002% or less at 24-bit input are achieved.

It also supports DTS, an optional audio format for DVD video. By connecting an amplifier with a built-in DTS decoder from the digital audio output and playing DTS-compliant discs, sound reproduction closer to the original is possible on a 5.1 ch system.

Equipped with the Specializer N-2-2 Digital Virtual Surround System, which reproduces stereo sound images with two speakers.
This function virtually creates a rear surround speaker with only two speakers at the front of a TV or mini-component, and creates a stereo sound image with speakers at the front, left, and right of the room. All processing is done within a single processor, eliminating the sound quality degradation associated with effects.

Audio parts are used.

Other

Equipped with 4-step zoom function that enables 3-step (approx. 1.5 x / approx. 2.5 x / approx. 3.5 x) zoom up and reduction of the screen.

Space saving has been realized by shortening the depth by about 80 mm compared with the conventional model SD 1200.

It supports CD text.

New GUI functions have been adopted to improve operability such as function setting by adopting icon display.

It is equipped with a screen saver function to prevent screen burning in still image state.

It has an energy-saving auto power-off function that automatically turns the power off.

Wireless remote control is included.
