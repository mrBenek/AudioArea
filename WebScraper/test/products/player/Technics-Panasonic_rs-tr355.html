Commentary

Double reverse deck equipped with Dolby HX-PRO.

A full logic mechanism has been adopted, and playback reverse can be performed on Deck 1, and recording reverse can be performed on Deck 2.
In addition, it is equipped with a series play function so that you can play continuously from Deck 1 to Deck 2 (or vice versa).

It is equipped with Dolby HX-PRO to support high-level and high-frequency sources such as CD.
The Dolby HX-PRO automatically adjusts the effective bias to match the input signal during recording. This greatly improves the high-frequency characteristics without deteriorating the low-frequency characteristics, and reduces distortion over the entire band.

It is equipped with a synchroedit function and can be edited and recorded in cooperation with CD players (SL-PS30, SL-PS70).

Equipped with 2 nr of Dolby B/C as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with timer rec & play function.

Uses a large insulator.

Wireless remote control is included.
