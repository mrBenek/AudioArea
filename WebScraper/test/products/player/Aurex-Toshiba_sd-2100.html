  • Toshiba SD-2100

  • ¥ 75,000 (released on November 16, 1997)

Commentary

DVD player with a compact design of 370 mm wide.

It adopts a new body chassis that pursues compactness and simplicity, and achieves a reduction of approximately 60 mm compared to conventional models.
The front panel has a simple design with only the frequently used control buttons laid out.

It is equipped with a specializer which is a 3D sound function and can reproduce a three dimensional sound image of 6 speaker level with 2 speaker sound. This function is only available for analog output.
(The specializer is manufactured under license from Despper Products, Inc.)

It is equipped with a highlight zoom function that magnifies the part you want to see by 4 times.
If you press the zoom button during playback, you can zoom up approximately 4 times the center of the screen, and you can move the part you want to see up, down, left or right with the remote control.

Equipped with color difference output terminals (Y, Cr, Cb), the video signal can be output in the form of luminance signal (Y) and two independent color signals (Cr, Cb).

Play DVDs, video CDs, and music CDs.

Linear PCM audio corresponding to 96kHz/20bit is adopted.

Equipped with headphone output terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
