  • SONY MDS-102

  • ¥ 86,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

The MD deck features a compact size of 225 mm in width.

Equipped with a multi-jog dial that allows you to select songs, enter programs and titles, improving operability.

Equipped with a disk-in auto power function that turns the power on just by inserting a disk.

Equipped with microphone input terminal.

Equipped with an audio bus connector that can be used with Sony's personal components.

Mono format discs are not supported.

Wireless remote control is included.
