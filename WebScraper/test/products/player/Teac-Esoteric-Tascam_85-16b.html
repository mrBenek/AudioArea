Commentary

A 16-channel multi-track recorder developed as the highest model of the TASCAM series.

Sync recording The first priority control system and performance-oriented 38cm/s single speed design ensure smooth, high-quality and overdubbing recording.

Dbx system DX-16B is equipped as standard.

It is equipped with group punch-in / out and preload sync mechanism by system control LSI (equipped with Input/Sync switch capable of sync monitoring by rear timing).
