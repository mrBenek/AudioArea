Commentary

This auto-reverse tape deck adopts closed-loop dual capstan system and 6-head system.

In order to make the most of the performance of EE tape, it is equipped with EE tape selector position.

It uses a 6-head configuration in which recording, playback, and erasing heads are separated into forward and reverse heads.

Equipped with a playback pause mechanism that enables accurate head positioning.

Equipped with a direct function that makes the transition from quick roll to play operation smooth.

Equipped with a microphone and line mixing mechanism.

BIAS/EQ independent switching method is adopted.

Equipped with a pitch control function that works for both recording and playback.

Equipped with a VU meter.

Equipped with a left-right linked recording mode switch.

Uses a Magne-float type flywheel with high rotational accuracy.

Equipped with a guide roller that absorbs uneven running of the tape and realizes smooth running characteristics.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.
