Commentary

A cassette deck equipped with a Oatley berth playback mechanism.

Equipped with a Oatley verse playback mechanism that can automatically play back and forth the tape.
It is also equipped with Oatley Pete (automatic repeat) playback, so you can play your tapes endlessly all day long.

It is equipped with an accurate head system that precisely moves the head back and forth so that it can be played back with the same sound quality for both going and returning.

A bi-directional seesaw capstan system is adopted in which a dedicated capstan is provided for each round trip.

It uses a micro-touch direction button, so you can easily change from forward to reverse.

Equipped with 3-step switching type reverse selector.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a memory counter.

Uses a Sendust head with excellent wear resistance.

Equipped with timer recording mechanism.

Equipped with microphone and line mixing mechanism.

(0, 15 db, 30 db) Equipped with microphone ATT.
