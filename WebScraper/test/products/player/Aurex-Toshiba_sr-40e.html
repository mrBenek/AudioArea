Audio Database

  • TOSHIBA SR-40E

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

A record player with an IC cartridge.

The tone arm uses a slim-type static balanced pipe arm.

A dedicated equalizer is built in.

Uses belt drive, auto-cut, Oatley turn system.

A 16-pole synchronous motor is used as the drive motor.
