  • PHILIPS N2506

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck with built-in DNL.

Equipped with DNL (dynamic noise limiter), it is possible to effectively remove tape hiss while suppressing the effect of original sound.
This function works only during playback, so it works for all existing cassette tapes.

It has a compact design with an emphasis on ease of use.
