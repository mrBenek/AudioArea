  • AIWA AD-L60

  • ¥ 47,600 (around 1981)

Commentary

A compact size cassette deck equipped with an auto-loading mechanism and amount display mechanism.

Operability is improved by IC logic control.

Automatic loading system with motor is used for tape mounting.

Equipped with a tape time indicator that makes it easy to check the remaining amount of tape.
The right channel of the optical display shows the remaining amount of tape to prevent recording errors.

Equipped with a music sensor function that is convenient for selecting music.

Equipped with a 3-step switching tape selector compatible with metal, CrO2, and LH tapes.

The head uses a DX head with a core laminated with a hard composite material.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Equipped with a Dolby system.

Equipped with a 5-point 3-color optical display for recording level checking.

It has a player sync terminal.

Uses a pure electronic full auto-stop mechanism.
