  • Marantz DV-17

  • ¥ 125,000 (released in October 2000)

Commentary

DVD player that has acquired THX Ultra standard.

It has acquired the THX Ultra Standard, the playback quality level required by Lucasfilm.

Separate power supply circuits for audio and video / digital circuits are included.

Equipped with a 10-bit / 27-MHz video D/A converter.

Equipped with a DC coupling video amplifier that realizes a high slew rate and high S/N ratio.

Equipped with a 96kHz/24bit audio D/A converter.

The audio circuit is equipped with an HDAM circuit. In addition, the D/A converter and HDAM are arranged in the shortest position, and the direct twin mono construction is adopted to improve the sound quality.

Equipped with a video-off function, you can shut down the video circuit to prevent degradation of the music signal when you play a CD.

Uses a double layered chassis that reduces vibration of the mechanism and eliminates vibration from the outside.

Playback of CD-R/CD-RW discs is supported.
Non-finalized disks are also supported.

Component video output terminal is mounted.

Comes with an aluminum top remote control with a joystick.
