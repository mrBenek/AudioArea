  • VICTOR/JVC KD-E18

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

An 8-track cassette karaoke deck that combines an 8-track player with a stereo cassette deck.

The 8-track section is equipped with a 4-track / 8-track tape switching and a 3.5 x speed fast forward mechanism.
Also equipped with fast-forward / playback indicators.

The cassette section is equipped with a 9-track skip selection mechanism and a full auto-stop mechanism.
It is also equipped with a timer start mechanism and recording peak meter.

Equipped with a sound multiplex function.

Equipped with a BBD electronic echo function.

Equipped with ± 10% playback tape speed control function.

Equipped with microphone terminal and headphone terminal.
