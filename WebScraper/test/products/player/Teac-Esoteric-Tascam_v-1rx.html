Commentary

Cassette deck with DD motor equipped with dbx by IC circuit.

As a noise reduction system, db using a new IC circuit has been adopted to improve temperature characteristics.
In addition, the dynamic range has been improved by adopting a high-headroom pre-amplifier to fully support the new dbx circuit.

Equipped with Dolby NRB type.

A full-scale 3-head system has been adopted for the head section, and a newly developed head has been adopted for recording and playback. As a result, crimping between the head and the tape has been improved and stable tape travel has been achieved.

For the mechanism part, a 3-motor type is adopted in which the capstan, reel, and mechanical drive are independent.
In addition, a DC servo DD (direct drive) motor is used exclusively for the capstan, and driving characteristics are greatly improved by improving rotational accuracy.

Equipped with a block repeat mechanism that allows you to repeat the part you want to listen to as many times as you want.

Equipped with a memory stop mechanism that is convenient for starting a song.

Equipped with a green 4-digit electronic display LED counter, which can display minus.

Equipped with a peak meter with improved drive capacity.
The meter reading range is -30dB to + 10 dB, and an edge-light system is used for scale illumination.

Equipped with a bias switching switch (± 20%) that allows you to fine-adjust the bias by yourself.

An input volume of the master volume type is mounted.

Equipped with a REC MUTE mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism that can be used for absence recording and alarm clock playback by combining a separately sold timer.

Equipped with an output volume.
