  • SONY TC-8040

  • ¥ 32,800 (around 1971)

Commentary

An 8-track player with 4-channel playback on 8-track tapes.

The tape automatically switches between channel 4 and channel 2.

Equipped with an auto-eject device that automatically stops when the tape ends and ejects the cartridge.

Equipped with equipped with an AC shut off switch. If it is turned ON, the power is automatically turned off when the tape ends.
