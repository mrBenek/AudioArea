Commentary

This cassette deck has excellent basic performance and enables a variety of tape play.

The mechanism uses a microcomputer controlled 3 DD motor system.
Operations such as index scanning, skip playback / skip reverse, and quick music search are available for enhanced convenience.

As a noise reduction system, Dolby C type is installed in addition to Dolby B type.

A ribbon sendust head is used for the recording and reproducing head.

An illumination indicator is installed in the center of the panel so that the operation status can be determined at a glance.
Various operation buttons are concentrated on the right side of the panel.

Equipped with 2-color LED level meter.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Equipped with a REC mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

By combining the separately sold remote control unit, it can be operated from a remote location.
