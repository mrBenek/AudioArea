  • Pioneer PL-X50

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A front loading type full auto player with a compact design of 21 cm depth.

It uses a front-loading system so that it can be stored in a bookshelf slim, and amplifiers and tuners can be stacked on top.

Easy Operation Full Auto is adopted, and even the record size is automatically detected just by setting a record and turning ON the START switch, and playing is possible without touching the tone arm.

SH rotor type motor is used for the drive motor.

The cartridge uses a high-output MC cartridge that does not require a transformer or head amplifier.

All operation switches are concentrated on the upper left of the front surface to improve operability.
