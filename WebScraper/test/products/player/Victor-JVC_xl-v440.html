  • VICTOR/JVC XL-V440

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

Digifine series CD player.

It employs an IS mechanism to shut off the disturbance vibration, and by floating the disk drive and optical pickup on the robust mechanism base, it shuts off the disturbance vibration and sound pressure.
It also uses a center hold method for disk loading that does not damage the CD.

The newly developed high-precision 3-beam pickup is used in the pickup section.
In the High Precision 3-Beam Pickup, the response is improved by shortening the distance between the beams. This improves the effect of the High Balance Y-Servo System, which cancels out the abnormal waveform detected by the preceding and succeeding beams in opposite phases.

The digital filter uses a 2 x oversampling digital filter and is integrated with a digital signal processing circuit into a one chip LSI to improve stability, reliability and cost performance.

The power supply circuit separates digital and analog circuits to eliminate mutual interference.

It is equipped with a program function for a total of 15 songs and functions such as program and repeat all songs.

It has a 6-digit LCD display.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Synchronized recording is supported by the DCS jack.

Wireless remote control is included.
