Commentary

Record player designed for high stability

By adopting a specially designed synchronous motor and a 30 cm turntable to increase the flywheel effect by concentrating the weight on the outer part, stable rotation is obtained which is resistant to fluctuation of power supply voltage and fluctuation of mechanical load when the cartridge is mounted.

Howling and external vibration are reduced by the combination of the heavy motor board and cabinet, special rubber incorporated into the arm base, etc., and insulator of the legs.

The arm adopts a simple S-shaped structure, and achieves high sensitivity and follow-up performance by reducing mass and precision machining.
In addition, there is no need to worry about damaging the needle tip or record by adopting the utility model oil dump type arm lift.
