Commentary

A record player with improved rotational accuracy, stability, and howling function as a player system, while pursuing cost performance.

Uses 20 pole, 30 slot DC brushless servo motor.

High-sensitivity tone arm that eliminates unnecessary resonance.

Uses cabinet and new insulator to cope with howling.

Turntable seat with large contact area.
