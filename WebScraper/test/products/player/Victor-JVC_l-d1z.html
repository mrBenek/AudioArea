  • VICTOR/JVC L-D1Z

  • ¥ 24,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

This is a belt drive auto return player with a slim design that uses low mass arms.

A synchronous motor with high torque and excellent constant speed is used for the motor.

It has a semi-auto mechanism that allows for auto return and rejection during the performance.

The tone arm uses a straight pipe arm with a low-mass design that minimizes the effect of low-frequency resonance.
A semi-integrated design with a Z-1SR cartridge mounted in a rigid plug-in shell provides excellent tracing performance and clear sound quality.

The dust cover was sold separately.
