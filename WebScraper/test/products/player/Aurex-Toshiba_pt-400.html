Commentary

A cassette deck designed with an emphasis on usability and compactness.

Equipped with an auto-level control circuit that automatically adjusts the recording level, stereo recording can be performed by simply pressing the push button.

Uses an all push button type that is easy to operate.

It has a headphone port.

Equipped with an electronic governor motor with less vibration and noise.
