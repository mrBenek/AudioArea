  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1980)

Commentary

This auto reverse deck can run in three modes : one way recording and playback, two way recording and playback, and endless playback.

The direction of travel can be changed manually by one touch operation with a large direction bar.

The head uses a fixed 4-channel hard permalloy recording and reproducing head and a special alloy erasing head for small windows with high erasing efficiency.
