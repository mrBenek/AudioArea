Commentary

This CD player was developed based on the technology and know-how accumulated through analog players.

Large rigidity is obtained by hollowing out and attaching solid aluminum high-speed steel of 15 mm thickness for the front panel and 10 mm thickness for the side panel to the minimum necessary outside of the aluminum die-cast chassis.
A lead sheet is inserted between the bottom plate and bottom plate and the chassis to ensure sufficient mass for eliminating resonance. In addition, the tray mechanism and the top cover are provided with a ferrite vibration control sheet to suppress resonance and obtain a large effect.

To minimize electromagnetic vibration from the transformer itself, the power supply section uses a short ring, a ham proof belt, and a copper-plated shield case to prevent magnetic flux leakage that may adversely affect other circuits.
The secondary winding has three independent circuits for the digital circuit, analog circuit and display circuit to prevent interference between the circuits.

Both the digital circuit board and the analog circuit board are completely shielded with a large copper plated cover to prevent the high frequency of the digital circuit from interfering with the analog circuit due to unnecessary radiation.

A simple, all-IC balanced out circuit uses capacitors such as ERO and Telefunken, and PCOCC thick wires to achieve higher quality playback.

The CFC panel is finished with satin, hairline treatment and titanium color.
The side and top panels have a black rabbi paint.

The legs have a highly stable 3-point layout, and a chrome plating finish is used for the pure brass removal.
In addition, we have adopted a two way foot that can be used for both rigid type and pallet type depending on the condition of the place.
