Commentary

CD player using super 1-chip LSI.

The signal processing circuit is equipped with a super 1-chip LSI, and by integrating optical servo (focus, tracking, spindle), signal processing, and D/A conversion part into one chip, the signal path is shortened and noise is reduced.
In addition, a Mash 1-bit DAC is used for the D/A conversion section, and non-linear distortion such as 0 cross distortion is not generated in principle, thereby realizing high-fidelity D/A conversion.

It is equipped with a digital servo circuit and realizes stable drive by adjusting the servo amount according to the condition of each disk.

Equipped with a time edit function.
With this function, the tape can be divided into A side and B side according to the length of the tape.

It is equipped with a disk link function that can edit multiple disks.

Equipped with a peak search function, the recording level of the cassette deck can be easily adjusted.

Equipped with a synchroedit function, it can be combined with a cassette deck with a dedicated terminal to enable one button recording and simultaneous start.

Wireless remote control is included.
