Commentary

V Series high cost performance cassette deck.

The head part is equipped with a recording / recombining 3 head using a high hardness permalloy head.
In addition, in order to take advantage of the high characteristics of the head, the amplifier section has a ± 2 power supply DC configuration and acquires linear characteristics up to high frequencies.

It uses a center mount mechanism to ensure stable driving that is less affected by vibration.
In addition, aluminum front panel is adopted for the front panel, and stability around the tape running part is improved by high mass.

Equipped with Teac's original triangle cassette support to eliminate micro vibration caused by looseness of the half.
Combined with the highly rigid cassette holder, it achieves reliable mounting.

Equipped with a bias fine function that allows fine adjustment of the bias.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with Dolby HX Pro Recording System.

High-bias recording circuit is used.

Equipped with 2-way counter for tape running time and index display.

Equipped with return to 0, auto monitor, auto REC mute, and auto tape selector.

Equipped with timer recording and re-mechanism.

Wireless remote control with display ON/OFF function is included.
