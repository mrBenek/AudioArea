  • KENWOOD DP-2080

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

Basic CD player with 8 x oversampling digital filter and D. P.A. C. circuit.

Uses an 8 x oversampling digital filter for the digital filter.
By increasing the sampling frequency by eight times, high-frequency noise is kept away from audible frequencies and noise components are easily removed by a low-pass filter.

A D. P.A. C. (Digital Pulse Axis Control) circuit is mounted immediately before the D/A converter to prevent jitter.

It is equipped with editing functions such as time editing, fade editing, program editing, multi-disc editing and recording.

CCRS is mounted.
