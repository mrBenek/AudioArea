Commentary

Duplicator for CD-ROM and CD-DA.

Our own CD-ROM drive (MASTER) and CD-R drive (SLAVE) are mounted in a 2U size chassis.

Choose from 1 x, 2 x or 4 x write speeds.
In addition, it is equipped with a Test Write function that can confirm whether or not duplication is possible at the set write speed.

It is equipped with a Compare function (comparison confirmation function) when duplicating CD-ROM.

Both CD-ROM and CD-R drives can play Audio CDs.
