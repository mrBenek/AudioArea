  • YAMAHA CD-X2

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1984)

Commentary

A compact CD player that uses an optical head with ALPC circuit.

The optical head uses a three beam system in which three laser beams are applied to the signal track, and track deviation is read by two lasers at both ends. In addition, the control and drive circuit of the optical head uses a new ALPC (Auto Laser Power Control) circuit to achieve high tracking accuracy.

Yamaha uses original LSIs such as signal LSIs with built-in digital filters and servocontrol LSIs, and has succeeded in converting more than 90% of digital circuits to LSIs.

A digital filter and a third active filter are used in combination.

It is equipped with 9 songs random program playback, 2 mode repeat of all songs / programs, index search, timer playback, and headphone port with volume.
