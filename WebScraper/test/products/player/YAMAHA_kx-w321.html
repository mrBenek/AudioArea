  • YAMAHA KX-W321

  • ¥ 32,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

This is a double cassette deck that achieves both multi-function and high sound quality.

Both Decks A and B are equipped with hard permalloy heads.

The dolby B/C noise reduction system is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Equipped with peak level meter with peak hold function.

Equipped with auto-rec mute function.

Equipped with a REC return function.

Equipped with a dubbing function that allows you to choose between two types of dubbing speeds.

Deck A/B continuous relay playback and front and rear single track cue function are installed.

Equipped with a wireless remote control.
