Commentary

This tape deck combines the long-time recording and playback functions of the 4-track No. 10 reel.

Equipped with a Oatley berth regeneration mechanism.
If you apply a sensing foil to the position where you want to reverse the tape, it automatically reverses from forward to reverse. In reverse, a delay circuit operates, and the tape is designed to run after the capstan motor reaches a constant speed. This ensures quick start-up and protects the tape.

Equipped with counter repeat playback mechanism and can be repeated many times by combining with Oatley verse mechanism.
If you set the counter to 0000 at the point you want to repeat, it automatically returns from reverse to forward.

Equipped with an auto spacer mechanism.
This is an extension of Rec Mute. With the Auto Spacer ON, pressing the pause button during recording automatically creates a constant interval of silence and waits for the next recording to resume. It is useful for timing between songs and cutting commercials and narration.
The length of the white space can be set arbitrarily from approximately 3 to 8 seconds.

Equipped with a timer mechanism with a delay circuit, it is possible to record absence by combining with an audio timer sold separately.
In addition, a delay circuit is installed, and the deck starts after waiting for a few seconds until the mechanism and amplifier become stable after the power is turned on. Therefore, stable recording and playback are possible from the start.

Equipped with a switching mechanism with independent bias and equalizer, it can be set to the optimum position for high-performance low-noise tapes and general low-noise tapes.

Equipped with a recording mode standby lamp.
When the Recording Mode switch is turned ON, the red REC lamp flashes. When recording starts, the lamp lights to indicate that recording is in progress.

The head is equipped with a high hardness permalloy head.
The shape of the head is hyperbolic to obtain a stable head touch, little contour effect and clear low range.

In the mechanism section, a compliance arm with appropriate viscous resistance and a guide roller that reduces running friction are combined to upgrade the running characteristics of the reverse.
In addition, the large motor and flywheel are firmly fixed to the thick aluminum panel, realizing durability that is resistant to shock during long-term continuous use and transportation.

Equipped with an input / output volume with a memory marker that can be used for fade-in and fade-out.

It uses expanded scale VU meters that are easy to read at low levels.

It comes with a reel clamper that is easy to mount the No. 10 reel.

Equipped with a separately sold remote control unit, it can be operated from a remote location.
