Commentary

Double reverse deck equipped with Dolby HX-PRO.

Equipped with a high-precision mechanism that employs a logic mechanism and quick reverse, it can record a maximum of 3 hours of series recording (when using a 90 minute tape) or 2 tapes simultaneously.

Equipped with Dolby HX-PRO, the effective bias is adjusted according to the input signal at the time of recording, so that the high-frequency characteristics can be greatly improved without deteriorating the low-frequency characteristics, and distortion can be reduced over the entire band.

Equipped with a synchro-edit function, you can easily edit CDs by connecting to Technics CD players (SL-PS300, SL-PS30, SL-PS70).

It is equipped with an FL-type peak level meter that can be selected in two modes, and dedicated electronic counters are installed on Deck 1 and Deck 2 respectively.

Equipped with a music selector.
With this function, if you press the FF/REW button during a performance, the FF button starts the next song, and the REW button starts the current song.

As a noise reduction system, in addition to Dolby B/C, dbx, which has a high noise reduction effect, is installed.

Equipped with auto REC mute and double-speed editing functions.

Equipped with timer rec & play function.

Wireless remote control is included.
