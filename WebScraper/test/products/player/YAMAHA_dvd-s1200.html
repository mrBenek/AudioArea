Commentary

A DVD audio / video player that also supports DVD audio.

Equipped with a DVD audio playback function, you can enjoy multi-channel audio by connecting to the 5.1 ch analog audio input terminal of the AV amplifier.
It is also equipped with a video off function that reduces the influence of the video system by turning off the power of the video circuit when the music disc is played.

3-2 pull-down type progressive circuit is mounted.
This circuit directly performs 3-2 pull-down to progressive processing, enabling more natural image reproduction than conventional DVD player.
In addition, by directly converting video sources such as motion picture films that have been progressively recorded in 24 frames into 3-2 pulldown 60-frame progressive signals, it is possible to enjoy images with reduced shaggy noise while maintaining the atmosphere of motion picture films even in fast-moving scenes that were difficult to reproduce with conventional DVD player systems.

The video DAC is equipped with a 54 MHz / 12-bit video DAC, enabling better image reproduction with high-precision conversion by oversampling.

The audio DAC is equipped with 192kHz/24bit (front 2 ch) and 96kHz/24bit (other ch) DAC.

Built-in DTS / Dolby digital recorder and headphone surround circuit.

The mechanism part adopts a large diameter stabilizer of 12 cm in diameter, and realizes a more stable pickup by effectively suppressing unnecessary vibration of the disk surface.

Equipped with a block noise reduction function, block noise specific to MPEG compression can be reduced.
It also includes mosquito noise reduction, which removes noise around edges.
In addition, 3 d noise reduction eliminates random noise in the time axis direction while comparing the previous and next screens.

In addition to the D1/D2 video output terminal, a component (color difference) video output terminal is mounted.
In addition, various output terminals such as S image output and composite image output are mounted.

Equipped with a cinema position function.
With this function, you can get natural coloring and resolution like a movie in a movie theater by effectively drawing out the moist texture of the image and details of the dark part.

Equipped with a Cinema Loudness function, you can easily listen to the dialogue by increasing the sound level of the center channel when the volume is low such as at night.
This function is available for analog audio output only.

Wireless remote control is included.
