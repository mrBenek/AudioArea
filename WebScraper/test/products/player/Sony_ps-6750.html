Commentary

A direct-drive player system that employs a magnedisk servo.

A magne-disk servo is used for the detection part of the servo motor.
An 8-element multi-gap head (MGH) is used to detect the average value of 512 detection signals, which are magnetized over a wide range around the periphery of the turntable. This improves servo detection accuracy.

The turntable uses die-cast aluminum of precision processing with deadning to reduce the resonance sharpness.

Acoustic material SBMC is used in the cabinet.
This SBMC is a thermosetting reinforcing resin that combines polyester resin with calcium carbonate and glass fiber. It is 1.5 to 2 times stronger than wood (lauan plywood) and has a resonance sharpness (Q) of less than one third that of aluminum die-cast. This reduces howling and improves the S / N ratio.

Oil dump type insulation mat is used for the turntable seat.
This is a special liquid with high viscous resistance enclosed in soft rubber, which suppresses the vibration and resonance of the phonograph record.

33 equipped with independent pitch control for each of 2 speeds of 33 1/2 and 45 rpm, there is no need to adjust the speed every time the speed is changed.

The tone arm employs a static balanced universal tone arm that reduces resonance by using carbon fiber in the arm pipe.
In addition, it is equipped with a lateral balancer, inside for scan cellar, gold plated connector, etc.
The arm lifter is a remote type.

Head y cell is made of aluminum die-cast.

A special insulator with a high vibration absorption rate is used for the leg of the player case, and a lock mechanism and a height adjustment mechanism are mounted.

Dust cover with anti-static treatment is included.
