Commentary

A stereo cassette deck with Dolby NR.

The head uses an F & F (ferrite & ferrite) head with an all-ferrite structure that achieves abrasion resistance approximately 200 times that of permalloy and excellent characteristics.

Equipped with Dolby NR system that can be turned on / off to reduce the hissing of the tape.
In addition, it is equipped with a 3-type tape selector.

Uses a DC servo motor with FG for the motor.

Equipped with a ON/OFF Sony Limiter Recording switch.
In this recording method, manual recording is performed when an appropriate input is input, and a limiter circuit is activated when an unexpected large input is input to prevent distortion.

Built-in auto shut-off mechanism, lock type pause button, MPX filter, etc.
