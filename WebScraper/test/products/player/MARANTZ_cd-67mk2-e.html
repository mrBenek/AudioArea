Audio Database

  • Marantz CD-67mk2

  • ¥ 35,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

CD player using 18-bit DAC.

The mechanism part is equipped with Philips CDM 12.1.

The HDAM circuit is mounted.

It is equipped with two digital outputs which are coaxial with the optical system.

There are 2 variations, gold and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
