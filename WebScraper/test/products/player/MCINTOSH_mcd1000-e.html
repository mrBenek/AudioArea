Commentary

A CD transport that was developed by pouring all the technology and know-how of Macintosh into only CD playback.

The mechanism uses a high-precision cutting aluminum material loader that boasts high durability and stable operation.
A 3-beam pickup is used for the reader.

It has coaxial, optical and balanced (AES/EBU) digital outputs.

The fluorescent display mounted on the jet-black glass panel is an illumination structure using light diffusion fiber with improved visibility without losing the external design.

The top cover and side panels are made of machined aluminum to reduce the impact of vibration.

Wireless remote control is included.
