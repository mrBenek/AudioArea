Commentary

This is a player system with a DL motor system and DS tone arm for more ideal playback.

The rotating system is equipped with a DL motor system unique to TRIO.
In this system, oil circulates as the spindle rotates by a special oil slit and oil hole cut in the outer diameter surface of the spindle, and the whole spindle is covered with ultra-high-pressure thin film to form a rigid thrust bearing part. At the same time, an inverted V-shaped oil slit called a ring bone fixes the spindle on the rotating shaft by a powerful force using the pressure of fluid.
In addition to achieving a wow flutter of 0.006%, this new bearing eliminates vibration and noise from the bearings, and does not have any parts that wear out, so there is no deterioration in performance even after long use.

DS tone arm of 1-knife and 2-pivot system is adopted for the tone arm part.
A unique knife edge is installed to prevent the tone arm from moving forward, and the fulcrum of the tone arm is fixed so that the fulcrum of the arm will not be pulled into the swing even if the needle tip traces any large signal groove.
The high-rigidity design that eliminates the rattling of the bearing reduces crosstalk in the low frequency range and chattering in the high frequency range, and realizes higher sound quality.
In addition, the arm body uses a high-rigidity straight pipe arm with aluminum as the base material.
Five ribs are provided inside the arm to increase rigidity in shape. Furthermore, by laminating hard alumina, the lowest resonance frequency is lowered and high rigidity is realized.

A quartz PLL system is used to control the phase difference by comparing the reference signal of the quartz oscillator with the speed detection signal.
A magnet and an FG coil that generates power by changing the magnetic flux density of the motor rotor are used to detect the rotational speed, and a newly developed IC with a digital memory inside the IC is used for the speed control system.
Unlike the sample-and-hold method, no coil or resistor is used, so temperature characteristics and stability are improved.

450 kg / cm2Inertia lock is used for dynamic uneven rotation when the needle tip traces a record board, and quartz lock is used for static uneven rotation.

A mechanical short structure is adopted to clarify the motor support point (turntable rotation support point) and the arm support point.
By fixing the arm support and motor shaft with ARCB material and at the same time fixing the DL motor system directly, transfer error (conversion loss) is greatly reduced.
