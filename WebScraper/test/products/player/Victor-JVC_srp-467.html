  • VICTOR/JVC SRP-467

  • ¥ 26,500 (around the mid 1960 s)

Commentary

A record player with a perfect magnetic pickup.

It uses a perfect magnetic pickup with a diamond needle to maintain balance in all directions, up, down, left and right, to enable stereo playback in a wide band, and to extend the life of the record needle by three times.
Diamond needle with higher durability than sapphire needle is used for needle tip.

The drive motor is a 4-pole synchronous condenser motor that is not affected by fluctuations in the power supply voltage, and the turntable is a 28-cm double turntable.

An arm elevation mechanism by oil dump system is adopted.
