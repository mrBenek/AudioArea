  • DENON DMD-7.5E

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1996)

Commentary

MD-deck of PRESTA series equipped with Λ S. L. C.

The D/A converter is equipped with a Λ S. L. C (Lambda Super Linear Converter), combined with a 20-bit 8 x oversampling digital filter to reproduce more natural sound.

The A/D converter is equipped with a 4th-order ∑ Δ type AD converter.

It is equipped with a character input function, and you can enter up to 100 characters for each song such as the title name in alphabet or symbol.
The display can display up to 14 characters at a time, and the rest are scrolled.

Equipped with MD editing functions such as divide, combine, move, erase, etc.

It is equipped with functions such as 25-track program memory, random play, and repeat all tracks / one track / between A and B.

The display section is equipped with left and right segment level meters to confirm the recording level, etc.

Built-in Sound Skip Guard Memory.

Synchronized recording with the DCD 7.5E is possible.

Wireless remote control is included.
