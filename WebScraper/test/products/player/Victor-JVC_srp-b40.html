Commentary

A record player that uses an isolated belt drive system.

In the isolated belt drive system, in addition to the general belt drive system in which the rotation of the motor is transmitted to the turntable by the belt, the outer rotor motor is attached to an isolation board made of aluminum die-cast to prevent the transmission of vibration and improve S/N.
A hysteresis / synchronous outer rotor motor is used for the motor, a polyurethane belt is used for the belt, and a 31 cm aluminum die-cast turntable is used.

The tone arm is equipped with a double clutch inside for scan cellar.
In addition, the needle pressure application method is a dynamic balance type, and a lateral balancer is also installed.

An IM-type cartridge is used.

Uses newly developed 0.8g light needle pressure auto mechanism.

The cabinet has a walnut matte finish.
