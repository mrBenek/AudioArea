  • VICTOR/JVC XL-G55

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1990)

Commentary

A CD graphics player that allows you to enjoy pictures and letters by connecting to a TV.

CD-G (CD CD Graphics), you can record lyrics, animation and information as CG (Computer Graphics) still images in the empty space of a normal CD and play them back on the TV screen.

It is equipped with a karaoke function such as 2 microphone inputs.
In addition to having two microphone inputs, it is equipped with functions for producing karaoke such as microphone mixing which can adjust the two systems independently, sound multiplex support, and electronic echo.

It is a compact size of 36 cm wide, and can be matched with mini components.

Equipped with 10 keys on the main body, you can directly select the music you want to listen to.

Equipped with CD direct output.

Equipped with CD-G channel select (1 ~ 15 ch) function.

The sound multiplex can be switched so that the balance between the accompaniment and the song can be changed.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
