Commentary

DD turntable equipped with electronically controlled full automatic mechanism.

The operation position of the tone arm, such as auto-in position, return position and armrest position, is detected by the optical detection mechanism which combines photodiode and LED, and the operation detection accuracy is improved.
In addition, since the auto mechanism does not contact the tone arm, even if harmful vibration is generated by the auto mechanism, it is not transmitted to the destination.

A dedicated motor is used to drive the arm and a dedicated plunger is used to operate the arm lifter. By providing a dedicated drive source for the turntable drive, arm drive, arm lifter drive and each drive part, mutual interference is suppressed.

It uses a feather touch operation switch with embedded LED, and the LED of the touched switch turns on to make the mechanism during operation easy to understand.

The electronic control mechanism is designed to give the highest priority to the cut operation among all auto operations. Even when the play button is pressed by mistake or while the tone arm is descending, the return operation starts when the cut switch is pressed.
When the arm is returned to the rest position, all automatic actions are cancelled automatically, even during the transition of the arm or while tracing records.

The high-precision core-and-slotless DC-servo motor realizes steady rotation with a wow flutter of 0.04%.
In addition, the turntable improves transient load fluctuations that cannot be controlled by the servo circuit alone.

The cabinet is a composite structure using NewARCB material with excellent housing margin, and vibration isolation characteristics are improved by combining two physical properties with different resonance characteristics.

The cartridge (F150MKII) made by Orthophone was adopted.

By using the separately sold remote controller (RC-500), operations such as Play, Cut and Size Select can be controlled remotely.
In addition, if you turn the remote control to Power Off during performance, the memory trace status will be set and the performance will continue.
When the player finishes playing, the arm returns and returns to the armrest, the player's power is automatically turned off.

The dust cover was sold separately.
