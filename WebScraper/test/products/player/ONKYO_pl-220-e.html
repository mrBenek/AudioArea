  • ONKYO PL-220

  • ¥ 45,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

A full auto player that uses frontal pivot arm.

We have developed a frontal pivot arm with a separate horizontal and vertical pivot bearing in order to pursue a short size and tracking performance that is not inferior to that of the normal type, and we are improving the reproducibility on the disk.
The material is a straight aluminum pipe arm with high rigidity and light mass.

It uses a microcomputer control system and can control auto lead-in, auto cut, Oatley turn, auto repeat, and all operations without a turner by operating the operation key.

Equipped with computer search function, arm lift-up, cue down and search operation can be controlled by operation of the front key.

The drive system is equipped with a direct drive motor whose servo circuit controls the rotational speed to be stable.

The cabinet adopts a 2-layer vibration control structure, and vibration is reduced by combining it with a spring / rubber compound insulator.

It is equipped with a dedicated output terminal that enables synchronized recording in conjunction with the TA-44X cassette deck or CX 110 deck receiver.

There was also a black type PL-220B.
