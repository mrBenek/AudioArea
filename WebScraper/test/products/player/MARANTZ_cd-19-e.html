Audio Database

  • Marantz CD-19

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

A CD player equipped with Philips CDM 12.1 linear tracking mechanism.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 1-bit double-differential D/A converter.

Low-noise, low-impedance design is used for the power supply.

The HDAM configuration is used for the audio output.

There are 2 color variations, gold and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
