  • AKAI CD-M515

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

350 mm wide CD player with the performance and functions of CD-A30

It is a 350 mm size that fits Akai's compact component "Property" series.

The CD-A30 uses a vibration control design that is also used for the CD-A30. A new material, antiresonance special rubber, is used as an insulator. In addition, this special rubber is used for a double floating mechanism in which a mechanical block including the pickup and drive system is inserted from the main body. This reduces vibration and resonance and realizes a stable trace.

We have adopted a servo control system that has reinforced the power supply and eliminated adverse effects on sound quality.

It is equipped with a random program play function of up to 36 songs, repeat all songs / program / one song / section (A ⇔ B), search function, etc.

There are 2 color variations, silver and black.
