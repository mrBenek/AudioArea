Commentary

A PLL servo type D. D player that pursues the basics of the player system such as accurate rotational speed and excellent wow / flutter characteristics.

A PLL servo system is adopted which combines accurate pulses of a high-precision photoelectric generator of a motor rotating shaft with a high-stability oscillation circuit.

Mass concentration system tone arm with improved tone quality by strengthening the arm supporting part.

Heavy cabinet with high class piano finish.
