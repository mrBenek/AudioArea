Commentary

An auto player with limited basic performance and complete ease of use.

A linear BSL motor with high basic performance as a motor for acoustic equipment is servocontrol by a magnedisk velocity detection applying a measurement technology in the micron unit.

In order to eliminate resonance as a system, SBMC material with excellent internal loss and strength is used for cabinet material.

In order to utilize the light needle pressure / high compliance type cartridge, it is equipped with a low mass / straight arm.
In addition, the MM type cartridge XL-150 is mounted in the cartridge.

Equipped with an opto-electronic disc size selector that automatically selects whether the set record is a 30 cmLP or a 17 cm EP and accurately leads in the tone arm.

Equipped with a muting mechanism that eliminates noise when the needle tip contacts the record board.

Equipped with an auto return mechanism, the performance ends automatically.

It comes with a needle pressure setting guide to adjust the proper needle pressure of the standard equipped cartridge and a disk centering guide to make it easy to place the 30 cmLP board.
