  • Pioneer RPD-500

Commentary

This professional CD-R recorder realizes high-quality sound recording with simple operation.

The RPD 500 conforms to the unified CD-R standard. Recorded CD-R discs can be played on ordinary CD players, and music can be recorded on CD-R discs for personal computers.

It is equipped with a sampling rate converter that can convert 48 kHz and 32 kHz digital sources to 44.1 kHz for digital recording.

The mechanism section uses Pioneer's original turntable mechanism.

Equipped with a digital sync recording function that automatically starts / stops when recording digitally.

Equipped with digital faders, you can fade in and fade out with one touch.

It has a finalizing function.

Compatible with 3U size EIA standard rack.
Wireless remote control is included.
