  • TEAC VRDS-25xs

  • ¥ 230,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

A CD-player with full-bodied construction and DSRLL circuits.

A turntable mechanism VRDS is adopted for the mechanism.

Equipped with the DSRLL (Digital Servo-Ratio-Locked Loop) circuit used in the Eso Teric D-3, it reduces the audible bandwidth of all digital signals by 40 dB.

The digital circuit uses a 1 / 16 shift method (digital offset) and a 20-bit DAC to achieve a significant improvement in linearity.

Equipped with Distortion Shaper ZDII that realizes an ideal conversion step.

Equipped with a digital servo that adjusts the servo amount for each disk.

Newly developed full-bodied construction is used for the chassis structure.
The full-body construction eliminates any floating structure, and the chassis consists of an 8 mm thick 8 kg steel board bottom chassis and an ultra-rigid chassis with solid aluminum blocks. The legs are made of heavy-weight cast iron pinpoint insulators similar to Esotheric to create an ultra-mass with a total weight of 23 kg and eliminates external vibration and resonance.

Equipped with digital input, D/A conversion of external devices such as MD is also possible.

Equipped with digital output ON/OFF switch.

Equipped with analog balance output.

Equipped with 1 dB step digital volume.

Equipped with an auto fader.

Includes a wireless remote control that enables you to turn the display on and off.
