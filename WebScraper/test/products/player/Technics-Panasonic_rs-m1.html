  • Technics Co-deck RS-M1

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

This is a read-only cassette deck that is compact enough to hold the functions of a cassette deck.

Softtom mechanism is used for the mechanism part.
A DC governor motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with a 2-position tape selector with metal position.

Equipped with a line-out terminal convenient for dubbing.

Equipped with a monitor mechanism, you can listen to external sound with the built-in microphone when you pose.

It is equipped with functions such as rewind autoplay, queue & review and autostop.

Dry-cell battery is used for the power supply.
Can be driven by AC or car battery with separately sold adapter.

It comes with headphones.

There are 2 color variations of black and silver.

There was a special carrying case sold separately.
