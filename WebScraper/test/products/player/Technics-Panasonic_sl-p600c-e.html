Commentary

A multi-CD player equipped with a magazine that can play up to 6 CD dances continuously.

Equipped with a 6-disc continuous play function, you can play up to 6 discs in the order of disc number loaded in the magazine.
The number of recorded CDs appears on the display when they are loaded into the CD player.

Equipped with 36 step random program performance function.
With this function, you can program up to 36 steps in the desired sequence, one step for one song or all songs on a disc (in the recording order), and you can check the program contents sequentially on the display by pressing the recall key.

It is equipped with a random performance function, and you can play by setting the order of music at random by the function of microcomputer.

It is equipped with a 2-color LED disk select key. When the magazine is set, the green lamp lights up to show the number of disks. When you press the number of disks you want to listen to, the color of the selected key changes from green to yellow.

Equipped with a music matrix display, you can check which song is currently being played by the No display (1-6) and the song number display (1-20) of the disc. When you check the program, you can switch the program song for each disc.

Equipped with a 4-mode time display, you can switch and display the elapsed time and remaining time of playing songs, and the elapsed time and remaining time of all songs (program songs).

The digital filter uses a 4-times over-sampling digital filter that increases the sampling frequency from 44.1 kHz to 176.4 kHz, resulting in high resolution and excellent noise reduction effects. The low-pass filter also reduces group delay characteristics.

Newly developed optical deck is adopted for the mechanism part.
This deck adopts a unique high-speed linear access system, which realizes high-speed access as well as high durability and quietness.

Equipped with 2-speed manual search and all songs / program songs / 1-song repeat.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
