  • YAMAHA CDC-805

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1990)

Commentary

A five disc CD changer that can play five discs.

It uses a carousel type in which five discs are set in a large tray. There are two playback modes available : SINGLE mode in which one disc is selected for playback, and ALL mode in which five CDs can be played as one large CD.
In addition, the Play Exchange feature allows you to open the tray and replace one disc while playing (not during programming).

Equipped with DEQ (digital equalizer), you can enjoy your favorite sound field feeling from 4 modes of rock, vocal, jazz and classical.

Equipped with a program file function, it is possible to store up to 100 discs (when 10 songs are programmed) with the program contents and DEQ mode of the disc.

When two CDC 805 s are connected using an interface cord, they can function as a 10-string CD changer.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 3rd-order noise-shaping 1-bit DAC that, in principle, produces no 0 cross distortion or glitch distortion and provides stable and high sound quality.

Since the tray opens and closes during play with the play exchange function, we use rubber, spring and damping metal to ensure vibration resistance equivalent to car stereo.

Wireless remote control is included.
