  • DIATONE DP-73B

  • ¥ 28,000 (released in April 1969)

Commentary

A player system with an auto-return / auto-cut mechanism.

The turntable has a urethane belt around it.

The tone arm uses a static balance type. This arm is equipped with a lateral balancer, inside for scan seller, and sub weight.
The arm has an S-shaped universal arm and the needle pressure can be varied.
The lateral balancer is also used as an SME type needle pressure weight and comes with a lever type inside force canceler.

The arm lifter is operated by a lever that is also used as a power switch and can be switched to 3 stages of power OFF, power ON, and PLAY.

Equipped with an auto / manual changeover switch, manual operation can be performed by releasing the auto mechanism.

Equipped with arm elevation and Oatley turn functions.
