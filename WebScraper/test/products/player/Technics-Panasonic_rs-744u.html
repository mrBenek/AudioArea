  • Technics RS-744U

  • ¥ 105,000 (around 1974)

Commentary

An orthodox 3-head tape deck with 4ch/2ch recording / playback heads.

The monitor for 4-channel recording can be operated by switching only one switch, and the head is equipped with 8 amplifiers.

The I/O meter display can be switched by one operation of the monitor switch.

Center float type motor holding.

The equalizer can be switched according to the speed, and optimum recording / playback is possible at 19cm/s and 9.5cm/s.
