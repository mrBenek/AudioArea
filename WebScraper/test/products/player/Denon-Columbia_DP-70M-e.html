  • DENON DP-70M

  • ¥ 98,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

A manual record player with a double structure turntable.

A double structure is adopted for the turntable part.
Structurally, it consists of a high-cut filter that connects the upper and lower turntables with springs and dampers, and is fixed to the motor shaft, thereby blocking external vibrations such as acoustic feedback from speakers.

An out Rocester type AC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

The tone arm is equipped with a high-sensitivity S-shaped tone arm with dynamic damping mechanism.

Equipped with a helicoid arm elevation mechanism that can adjust the height of the tone arm according to the cartridge.

Uses a 70 mm thick cabinet made of particleboards with a Bubinga natural wood finish.

The tone arm can be replaced with the light mass tone arm DA 401, which is compatible with high compliance type cartridges.
