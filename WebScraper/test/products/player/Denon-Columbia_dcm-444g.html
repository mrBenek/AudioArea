  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1990)

Commentary

A CD player with a magazine-type changer function.

Six CDs can be played continuously using a special magazine, and up to 32 songs can be played automatically by selecting and programming the songs you want to listen to from among the six CDs.

In Random Play, you can enjoy Random Play, where you play songs randomly from each disc, or Disk Sequential Random Play, where you specify the disc and play the songs in each disc. This function is designed so that you don't have to play the same song twice (once played).
If you press the play button, you can play all songs from the first song of the first album to the last song of the sixth album in succession.

The digital filter is equipped with a 20-bit 8 x oversampling digital filter.
In addition, it is equipped with a noise shaper that can reduce requantization noise by feeding back the calculation error of the 20-bit output of the digital filter.

The D/A conversion section is equipped with a double D/A converter type W super linear converter to suppress 0 cross distortion.

Equipped with analog fixed output and coaxial digital output.

Wireless remote control is included.
