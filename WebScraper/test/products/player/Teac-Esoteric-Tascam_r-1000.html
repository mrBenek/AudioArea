Commentary

This is a popular home-use stereo tape recorder that takes full advantage of the performance and usability of high-end machines.
The R-1000 is based on the R-1000 with two speakers and a main amplifier in one case for the R-1100.

In order to automatically play back and forth 4-track stereo tapes, a 4-head configuration has been added with a head exclusively for reverse feed.
The head uses a hyperbolic shape, and the frequency range and crosstalk are improved by 3 micron (regeneration) gap, complete front shield, precision processing, etc.

It is a 3-motor system that uses independent motors for tape drive, rewinding, and winding.
Each motor is equipped with a hysteresis torque motor, enabling the tape to run stably.
In addition, as a capstan drive system, a 4-pole 8-pole 2-speed motor is adopted to avoid mechanical switching.

It is equipped with a Safety switch. If you press this switch, you will not erase the tape even if you record by mistake.

Equipped with an automatic reverse playback function, if the end of the tape is fixed to the reel, it can be played back and forth without changing the reel.
When rewinding or reverse regeneration is finished, the switch linked to the tension arm works and the machine automatically stops.

By operating the ADD switch, you can record the sound from one track to the other with a new input.

Equipped with a stereo co-switch, an electrical echo effect can be obtained.

With built-in 4-unit amplifiers for 2 recording systems and 2 playback systems, stereo recording and simultaneous playback monitoring is possible.

You can adjust the recording level of microphone input and line input independently.

Equipped with index counter and dual type meter.
