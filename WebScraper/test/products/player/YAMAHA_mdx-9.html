  • YAMAHA MDX-9

  • ¥ 75,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

Compact MD recorder with performance and functions equivalent to MDX 793 in toP-ART size (280 mm width).

ATRAC-IC with built-in DSP of 24-bit precision is installed in the audio compression part.

Equipped with a sampling rate converter corresponding to 48/44.1/32kHz.

Equipped with music sync recording and timeless cue recording functions.

Monaural long-time mode of up to 148 minutes is supported.

Alphanumeric kana characters Wireless remote controller with direct input key is included.
