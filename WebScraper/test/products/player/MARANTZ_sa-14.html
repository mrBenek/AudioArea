  • Marantz SA-14

  • ¥ 250,000 (released in December 2000)

Commentary

Premium series SACD player.

Single-layer, dual-layer and hybrid SACD disks are supported.

It has also acquired a high level of musical performance for CD and CD-R disc playback.

SACD DAC is mounted is mounted on the differential to realize low distortion and high quality sound.

The output amplifier section uses a current feedback system using HDAM, a high-speed module originally developed by Marantz.

The power supply uses a super-ring toroidal transformer and a high-quality condenser.

High reliability design with die-cast mechanism and all-aluminum cabinet is adopted.

Wireless remote control is included.
