Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC SRP-V7

  • ¥ 27,000 (around 1972?)

Commentary

A record player unit developed for the all-mighty 4-channel system V7 series.

CD-4 records can be reproduced by exchanging the cartridge and needle tip.

Low capacity shielded wire is used for the cable.

Equipped with auto return and auto cut mechanism.

Can be stored in VICTOR/JVC's component rack (LCU-V7 type).
