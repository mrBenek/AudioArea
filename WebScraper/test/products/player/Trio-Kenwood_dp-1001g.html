  • KENWOOD DP-1001G

  • ¥ 48,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

K's series CD player with DAC7 in the D/A converter.

The D/A converter uses DAC7 to improve the low-frequency power sense that is often lacking in 1-bit DAC.

A discrete buffer amplifier is added to the final stage of the audio circuit to enable low-impedance output.
In addition, we carefully select the parts that affect the sound quality through a thorough hearing.

The front panel is made of 5 mm thick extruded material, the chassis is 1.6 mm hot, and the rear panel is 1.0 mm thick to increase the strength.

Program playback, repeat play, program selection and skip selection are available.
The A-1001 and X 1001 amplifiers and system control enable easy CCRS recording of CDs.
