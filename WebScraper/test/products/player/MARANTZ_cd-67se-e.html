Commentary

This CD player is a special edition of CD-67.

A large power transformer using OFC winding is adopted to improve regulation.

The center of gravity is lowered by adding the anchor base to the bottom chassis.
We've also enhanced our thin chassis.

Philips CDM-12.1 is used for the mechanism part.

18-bit DAC is used for the DA conversion part.

The HDAM circuit is mounted.

Equipped with two digital output terminals that are coaxial with the optical system.

Wireless remote control is included.
