  • ¥ 248,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

The first DIATONE CD player.

In order to utilize digital signals recorded on CDs, it is equipped with an error correction circuit and a low-distortion D/A converter. In addition, it focuses on analog circuits and uses parts carefully selected with an emphasis on sound quality, up to one screw that secures the chassis.

The servo circuit uses a quartz-controlled phase-locked circuit with AFC and an additional instantaneous pull-in circuit for powerful rotation control.

Two 8-bit microcomputers are installed in the operation control center to realize various operations such as automatic performance and program performance.

Equipped with disk loading mechanism with auto-up function.
Turn on the power and press the open / close switch to open the disc holder and slide up to make it easier to load the CD. Press this button to close the disc holder and slide down to close the disc holder and the CD starts rotating automatically.

Up to 30 songs can be played in the program.

You can specify the start / end position of the performance by the number of songs, the elapsed time in one song, and the index number in one song.

It is equipped with a repeat performance function for one or all songs.

Wireless remote control is included.
