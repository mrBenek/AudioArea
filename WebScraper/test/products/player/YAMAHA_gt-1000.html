Commentary

A record player with high C/P by inheriting the technology obtained in GT-2000.

A 36 cm diameter, 3 kg weight, high precision aluminum die cast turntable is used for the turntable. Combined with a 400g SBR sheet with good adsorption, inertial mass 600kg/cm2For the first time.

The drive uses a direct drive system using a DC coreless Hall motor with a starting torque of 1.2 kg ・ cm and a quartz PLL system to achieve rotational accuracy of 20 ppm or less compared to the crystal oscillation level.
A 10 mm diameter motor shaft is used to support the turntable.

A virtual one point support gimbal support arm is used to support the arm. This system has a structure in which the pivot is received by an angular contact bearing and the bearing is supported by a rubber bush. It eliminates play and achieves a horizontal and vertical initial motion sensitivity of 7 mg.
The arm is an S-shaped light heavy arm made of aluminum. By making it a long arm with an effective length of 262 mm, the S-shaped wake can be made small and the horizontal tracking error is kept within a narrow range.
A thread suspension type is adopted for the anti-skating mechanism.

Zinc die-cast is used for the arm base.

The cabinet is a 115 mm thick cabinet with a weight of 13.4 kg using a five layer structure of high-density particle board including one 20 mm thick bottom plate.


As a separately sold option, the tone arm YSA-1 adopted in the GT-2000X and the straight arm YSA-2 produced in limited quantity can be equipped.

Non-contact optical auto lifter YAL-1 can be used.

Anchor block YAB-1 can be used.
YAB-1 is a set of 2 dumbbell weights connected by a horizontal bar, and 1 set is 32 kg in weight.
In addition, it comes with a spring-rubber compound foot with height adjustment mechanism that can be used by players other than GT series players.

Disk stabilizer YDS-3 can be used.
The YDS-3 has a double structure consisting of a brass ground metal part and an African paddock wood part. You can enjoy the change of sound by using both parts separately.

74 mm φ x34mm aluminum / brass composite disk stabilizer YDS-8 can be used.

Pinpoint block YPB-1 can be used.
YPB-1 is a brass machined pinpoint block that can support GT players, speakers and amplifiers with points.

Disk stabilizer YDS-1 can be used.
The YDS-1 is an electric pump type record adsorption system using a brass milling machine. In principle, it can exhibit the power equivalent to increasing the record weight to 4.5 kg.
