  • SONY TC-W7R

  • ¥ 75,000 (around 1986)

Commentary

Both A deck and B deck are double cassette decks equipped with Oatley berth function.

Constant speed and double speed dubbing from A deck to B deck can be performed with one touch, and dubbing editing on both sides of the tape can be performed.
In addition, continuous reverse playback of A deck and B deck can be performed up to the maximum, and continuous playback for 15 hours is possible with C-90 tape.

Laser amorphous head is used for the head.

There are 2 types of finish, black and silver.
