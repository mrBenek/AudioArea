  • Pioneer PL-1050B

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A record player with a belt drive system.

The drive motor uses a FG servo motor with a large rotational torque and is controlled by an electronic circuit to maintain stable rotation.

Equipped with auto return mechanism.

Rotational speed fine adjustment is possible.

Equipped with a strobe light.

40 mm thick particleboards are used for the cabinet.
