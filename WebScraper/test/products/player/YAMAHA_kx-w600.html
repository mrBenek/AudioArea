  • ¥ 60,000 (released in 1989)

Commentary

Double reverse deck with amorphous head.

An amorphous head is used for the read / write head.
Since this head is non-crystalline, it has high resistivity and low magnetic loss in the high frequency range. It also has high permeability and saturation magnetic flux density, sufficiently low strain and excellent abrasion resistance. Moreover, the 12-sheet laminated structure contributes to the reduction of eddy current loss.
LC-OFC is used for the head winding to minimize signal loss.

The Dolby HX-PRO is built into Deck A.
This circuit automatically adjusts the bias amount according to the strength of the high-frequency level of the recording signal, and realizes smooth tape recording with f-characteristics up to the high-frequency range regardless of the type of tape or ON/OFF of noise reduction.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

A non-resonant structure using floating resin is adopted for each of the mechanism, substrate, and anode.
In addition, a power transformer with independent windings for the mechanism system and the amplifier system is installed to reduce interference between circuits.

It is equipped with dual rec function which can record CD to 2 tapes simultaneously.
It is also equipped with a tape-to-tape 4x2 mode dubbing function (two speeds, constant speed and double speed).

Equipped with full auto-dubbing function.
This function allows you to copy the same content by aligning the heads of the A side and B side of the tape.

Equipped with a skip dubbing function, you can dub by reducing the unrecorded portion for more than 15 seconds.

Equipped with 9-track random program dubbing function.
You can dub your favorite songs in your favorite order.

Equipped with a manual dubbing function, you can dubbing while editing using rec pause and mute.

Equipped with equipped with an LCD display.
This display includes a 13-segment peak level meter, a linear counter for changing the tape length that can display the remaining time of the tape, a REC indicator, and the direction of tape travel.

Equipped with Auto-Rec Mute which makes a 4-second blank with one touch.

Equipped with Rec Return.
If you rewind directly during recording, this function returns to the recording start position and stops automatically.

Equipped with 3-hour relay recording (when using C90 tape), 24-hour relay playback (when using C-90 tape), 9-track random program playback, intro-scan, 99-track jump selection, auto tape selector, etc.

There are 2 color variations, titanium color and black.

A 31-key remote included.
With this remote control, you can record CD, TV/FM, tape dub, program playback, etc. from your hand.
