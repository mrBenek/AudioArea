Commentary

ALPHA processor-based PRCD 201 sa series ESTA player.

It is equipped with ALPHA processor which is analog waveform reproduction technology.
This circuit reproduces 16-bit data with 20-bit quality by compensating for signals lost during digital recording of CDs and other media. This makes it closer to the smooth analog waveform that existed in the natural world, and enhances the reproduction of fine nuances such as the moment when the afterglow disappears.

Real 20-bit Lambda S. L. C. is mounted on the D/A conversion part.
In the Real 20-bit Lambda S. L. C., the 0 cross distortion, which is the cause of sound quality degradation, is solved in principle, and excellent reproducibility is obtained by combination with a 20-bit D / A converter.

You can play finalized CD-R and CD-RW discs.

Equipped with two optical digital output terminals.

Equipped with functions such as direct selection, automatic search, manual search, program of up to 30 songs, program call, repeat function of one song / all song / between A and B / program song, random function, auto space, display dimmer, etc.

When used in combination with the PRESTA201SA series, you can perform synchronized recording, timer playback, auto function, auto power on and other operations.

Wireless remote control is included.
