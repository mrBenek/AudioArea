  • YAMAHA KX-W382

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1995)

Commentary

This double-cassette deck pursues high sound quality and operability for CD recording.

Equipped with a unique play trim function, you can correct the high-frequency characteristics degraded by the azimuth deviation.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with Dolby HX-PRO.

The main body and remote control are arranged with keys for one operation, so there is no worry of misoperation.

It is equipped with a synchronous REC function that can be linked with CDX 580 and CDX 480 CD players, and CD recording can be controlled from the CD player's remote control.
In addition, normal, high-speed, and 2-mode tape-to-tape dubbing can be done easily with a single key operation, making dubbing operation simple.

It is equipped with functions such as Auto-Rec Mute, Rec Return and Rec Pause.

Wireless remote control is included.
