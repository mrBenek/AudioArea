Commentary

This is a full-auto player that pursues sound quality equivalent to that of manual machines.

For the turntable, diameter 35 cm, weight 2.9 kg (including mat), inertial mass 560kg-cm2As a result, the inertia mass is increased by more than 30% compared to a 30 cm diameter turntable with the same weight and shape, and basic rotational performance is improved.

A magnetic support DD motor is used to reduce the bearing load and friction caused by the heavy turntable, as well as the downward force caused by the rotor magnet.
In the magnetic support system, the rotor is located below the rotor yoke and the suction force is applied upward. As a result, the bearing load is reduced to about 1/10 of that of the conventional system, to only about 500g, thus reducing noise and wear caused by bearing friction.

An ED (electro dynamic) servo arm is used for the tone arm.
The ED servo tone arm is an electronic Q damping arm that detects vibration of the arm by two pairs of coreless linear motors directly connected to horizontal and vertical bearings, and cancels the vibration by generating inverse torque by feedback control of a microcomputer. This suppresses needle skipping and intermodulation distortion induced by low frequency resonance. In addition, full automatic operation is possible without touching the arm body by volume and button operation.
In addition, manual operation is also supported and the arm can be moved by hand.

An up pipe replacement system is adopted, and straight and S-shaped arm pipes can be replaced freely.
The height of the arm can be adjusted. In addition, since the dynamic balance system is adopted, the movement of the arm is not affected even if the player leans.

The cabinet uses a rose-like all-wood cabinet with mirror finish.

The servo circuit uses a double-servo quartz circuit with 30 times higher quartz lock stability than conventional ones.

Equipped with a turntable independent start / stop switch that can be used for manual operation, etc.
