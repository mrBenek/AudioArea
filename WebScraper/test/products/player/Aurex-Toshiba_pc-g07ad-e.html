  • Aurex PC-G07AD

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with 3 noise reduction systems.

The capstan uses a direct-drive FG motor to achieve stable driving with less flutter.
The reel drive and the mechanism drive are each equipped with a dedicated motor to create a silent mechanism with quiet operation sound.

The head uses an amorphous head.

Three noise reduction systems, address and Dolby B/C, are installed.

The play function has been improved by adopting NEW MQJS controlled by microcomputer.
