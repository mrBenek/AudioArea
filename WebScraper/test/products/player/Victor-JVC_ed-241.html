Commentary

An 8-track deck capable of recording and playing 8-track tapes.

High-precision, ultra-long life capstan with oilless metal bearing is adopted, and a mechanism strong for continuous use is realized.

Equipped with a fast-forward device with automatic muting.

Equipped with a pause button and left / right independent VU meter.

You can set the level just by pressing the record button without attaching the tape.

It uses a 3-in-1 head for recording, playback, and erasing in one case.
Head touch is also improved by the parallel head shift mechanism.

Equipped with 3-mode auto-eject mechanism.

Equipped with microphone and headphone jacks.
