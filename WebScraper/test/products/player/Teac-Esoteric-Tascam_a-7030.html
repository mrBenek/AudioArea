Commentary

This is a stereo tape deck that thoroughly pursues performance to meet the needs of professionals and audiophiles.

A 3-motor system is used for the mechanism.
A 4-pole, 8-pole, 2-speed hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the capstan motor, and a 6-pole eddy current motor with bearings is used for the reel motor. Together with a full-scale impedance roller, it enables stable tape travel and reduces wow flutter.

In addition to 19cm2 track recording and playback, 19cm4 track playback, and 38cm2 track 10 reel recording and playback functions are installed.

The head section uses a 4-head system, with two independent heads for erasing, recording and playback and four independent heads for playback, enabling tape monitoring.
The recording and playback heads are TEAC's unique hyperbolic type, enabling stable recording and playback, and a permalloy head is used for the playback head.

Soft touch operation using micro switches is adopted, and playback, quick winding and stop operations can be performed with 4 push switches.
The operation switch can be operated from playback to fast forward or rewind, or from fast forward to rewind. However, fast forward and rewind are designed not to directly enter playback. Combined with a full-fledged brake mechanism, this switch allows smooth operation without damaging the tape.

Equipped with a foil sensing stop function, it can be auto-stopped or auto-rewound at the desired position by attaching a conductive foil sensing tape to the back of the tape. This function is performed by incorporating a sensing post into the tension arm. The switch on the back of the deck switches the stop and rewinding.

Tape tension switching system is adopted, and the tape tension can be switched between two stages depending on the size of the reel.
This prevents damage to valuable tapes and can also be used with 150 type thin tapes.

Equipped with a cue switch, you can find the part of the recording by touching the tape to the head surface during fast forward.

The amplifier section consists of two dedicated amplifiers for recording and playback, each with a total of four units.
The amplifier section is a 3-stage direct connection type using a new silicon transistor.

Recording level adjustment of microphone input and line input is independent, and mixing is possible.

Equipped with a large VU meter.

It uses a push-pull oscillator with a bias oscillation frequency as high as 100 kHz and with less even harmonic distortion and a margin of output, so that the frequency range is wide and recording noise is reduced.

Source and tape can be switched and monitored by the monitor switch.

Equipped with 8 Ω headphone terminal.

Equipped with DIN connector.

With the separately sold remote control unit, playback, stop, quick winding and recording can be performed from a remote location.
The pause control unit (sold separately) allows you to pause during recording and playback.

Microphone transformer and line transformer adapter are sold separately and can be used for 600 Ω balanced microphone input and 600 Ω balanced line output (-20dB).
