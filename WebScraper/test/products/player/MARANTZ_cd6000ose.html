Commentary

A CD player developed by bringing together high-quality sound technology, construction and parts that were put into equipment such as CD-7.

Marantz's original high-end device HDAM (Sonic voltage amplification module) with high shield structure is used for all analog stages.

The D/A converter uses two SM 5872 which are 1-bit differential output D/A cotractors. Low noise is realized by using double differential.

The mechanism section uses VAM1201, a 3-beam linear tracking mechanism developed by Philips, and the servo decoder IC is equipped with CD7 for excellent performance.

High rigidity has been realized by the adoption of steel reinforcement beam and double bottom cover which have been proven in CD-67MK2SE.

CD-RW playback is supported.

Equipped with CD-TEXT display function.

Equipped with various edit and search functions.

Gold finish aluminum panel is used for front panel.
An optical digital output terminal is mounted on the front panel.

Wireless remote control is included.
