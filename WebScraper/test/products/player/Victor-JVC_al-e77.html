Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC AL-E77

  • ¥ 42,000 (around 1986)

Commentary

Full auto player with linear tracking system.

Fully automatic function by microcomputer control is adopted.

It is equipped with a random program selection function of 8 songs and 15 times so that you can listen to your favorite songs in your favorite order.
You can add programs in the middle of the performance or repeat the performance.

It is equipped with an index function that automatically plays the intro part of each song for approximately 15 seconds.

Synchronized edit recording is possible by connecting to TD-W77 or TD-W33.
