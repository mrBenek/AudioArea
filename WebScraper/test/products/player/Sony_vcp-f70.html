  • SONY VCP-F70

  • ¥ 49,000 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A video CD player with high image quality using the Picture Control function.

It is equipped with two types of picture control functions, and high picture quality is achieved by digital noise reduction and sharpness circuit.
Digital Noise Reduction combines Sony's original noise filter with a circular noise reduction technology that uses field memory to effectively cut out noise components by comparing the video between the previous and next fields. This greatly reduces background background noise that is noticeable in static areas during video playback.
The sharpening circuit emphasizes the edges of the image so that it can be enjoyed with a clear image quality.

It supports video CD software with playback control function.

Equipped with a jog dial to improve operability.

Equipped with a help guide function, you can display the mode you are operating and the next operation procedure on the TV screen in Japanese.

It is equipped with an autoplay function, and if a disc is set, playback can be started just by turning the power on.

Equipped with 3 stages of slow playback function, slow motion playback is possible.

Equipped with a digest function, you can use a disk digest that displays the video at the beginning of each track on a 9-split screen and a track digest that shows the development of one track on a 9-split screen.

It is equipped with a continuous function. Even if you stop playing in the middle or turn off the power, you can watch from the next scene by pressing the play button. This function is disabled when the tray is opened.

It is equipped with functions such as program play and shuffle play of up to 24 songs and repeat one song / all songs / A-B (part).

Equipped with S video output.

Equipped with an optical digital output that can only be output when playing music CDs.

It comes with a multi-functional wireless remote control which can also operate basic Sony TV.
