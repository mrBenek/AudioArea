  • DENON DCD-770

  • ¥ 32,800 (around 1991)

Commentary

CD player with 20-bit super linear converter.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 20-bit S. L. C (super linear converter) to eliminate 0 cross distortion at low volume.
The digital filter uses an 8-times oversampling digital filter.

The output level can be adjusted in 12 levels using the digital filter.
This function can be operated with a remote control.

Equipped with a digital output.

It is equipped with functions such as peak search, fader and time edit.

The pickup part is floating with a special material, and is supported by a mechanical chassis molded with a material with excellent shock resistance. Moreover, vibration resistance is improved by using a magnet chucking system.

Aluminum is used for the front panel.

Wireless remote control is included.
