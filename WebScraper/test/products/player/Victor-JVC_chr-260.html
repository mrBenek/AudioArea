  • VICTOR/JVC CHR-260

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

An 8-track deck with a recording function.

Two independent level meters for easy recording level adjustment.

It is equipped with a condenser type hysteresis synchronous motor.

Uses a 3-in-1 head that combines recording, playback, and erasing in one case.

Equipped with an auto-stop mechanism with 3 modes.

Equipped with a pause button.

The parallel shift mechanism ensures reliable head touch.

Equipped with 3 x fast forward mechanism, microphone terminal and headphone terminal.
