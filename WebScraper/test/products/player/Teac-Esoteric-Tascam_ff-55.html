Commentary

This is a low-cost stereo cassette deck with improved mechanisms and functions for higher sound quality.

It adopts a 2-motor 2-head configuration. The motor is equipped with a servo DC motor for the capstan and a DC motor for the reel. The head is equipped with a high-hardness permalloy head.

The built-in Dolby NR system eliminates high-frequency noise (tape hiss) throughout recording and playback.

In order to bring out the characteristics of metal tape, cobalt (chromium) tape, and normal tape, the bias / equalizer can be switched to 3 independent states.

It uses specially programmed IC and can be controlled by soft touch operation.

If you press and hold the rec mute button for more than 3 seconds, the auto spacer operates as a continuous rec mute. After about 3 seconds of silent recording, the auto spacer waits in pause state.

It is equipped with memory auto-rewind. When the memory switch is set to play, auto-rewind is performed at the tape end, and auto-play is performed at counter "999".

Equipped with a peak meter.

Equipped with an output volume.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism, recording of absence and alarm clock playback are possible by setting a timer sold separately.

By connecting a separately sold remote control unit, it can be operated freely from a remote place.
