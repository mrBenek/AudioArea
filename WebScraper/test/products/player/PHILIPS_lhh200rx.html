Commentary

A CD player developed by inheriting LHH series technology such as LHH900R.

In order to eliminate the influence of high-frequency noise drawn by NFB circuits present in various parts such as D/A converters, we are making every effort to eliminate NFB from D/A converters, amplifiers, and power supplies.

In order to achieve more stable sound reproduction, a double-sided copper foil substrate is used, which minimizes ground impedance and achieves a high shielding effect.

Center mechanical arrangement is adopted for the layout, and interference between circuits is eliminated by separating the digital part and analog part on left and right.

In addition to the adoption of a chassis with high rigidity, the effect of vibration is reduced by the three point grounding sintered alloy insulator.

DAC7 is used for the D/A conversion part.
It also employs devices such as the SAA7310 decoder and the SAA7350 noise shaper.

The mechanism uses CDM-9, a swing arm pickup mechanism unique to Philips.

The front panel is treated with Eurogray alumite.

Wireless remote control is included.
