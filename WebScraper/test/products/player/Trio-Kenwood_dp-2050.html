  • KENWOOD DP-2050

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1993)

Commentary

A basic CD player.

A crystal oscillator is used immediately before the 1-bit D/A converter, and a DPAC (Digital Pulse Axis Control) is mounted for time adjustment including the calculation speed of the digital filter.
As a result, jitter is absorbed immediately before the D/A converter, the signal level is read with an accurate clock, and a more faithful analog signal is obtained.

Compatible with double-speed CCRS.

Wireless remote control is included.
