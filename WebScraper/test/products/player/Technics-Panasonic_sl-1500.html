  • Technics SL-1500

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A record player with a direct drive system.

The drive unit uses a direct drive system. The rotating part of the motor is integrated with the turntable, and the fixed part is integrated with the case.

The turntable uses a tapered edge for easy record attachment and detachment.

33 Each 1/3 and 45 rpm has an independent rotation speed adjustment mechanism.
It is also equipped with a stroboscopic illuminator using a prism.

A MM type cartridge is mounted in the cartridge part.

The tone arm uses a gimbal suspension system.

The legs are equipped with an audio insulator to prevent external vibration and acoustic feedback.
