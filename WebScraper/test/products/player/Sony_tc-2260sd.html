  • SONY TC-2260SD

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1974)

Commentary

A stereo cassette deck employing the closed-loop dual capstan system.

The closed-loop dual capstan system reduces modulation noise.

Equipped with a Dolby system.

F & F head is used for the head.
It also uses a direct coupling method in which an FET amplifier with ± 2 power supply is directly connected to the head amplifier.

Equipped with a 3-stage switching tape selector corresponding to Duadferi Chrome Tape.

It is equipped with a Sony Limiter recording system, a line and microphone selector switch, an auto shut-off mechanism and a memory counter.

There was a special dust protector sold separately.
