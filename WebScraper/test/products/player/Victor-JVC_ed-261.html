  • VICTOR/JVC ED-261

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

An 8-track deck equipped with a recording mechanism.

Two independent level meters are installed to adjust the recording level.

It uses a condenser type hysteresis synchronous motor.
It also uses a 3-in-1 head that combines recording, playback, and erasing in one case.

Equipped with an auto-stop mechanism with 3 modes.

Equipped with a pause button.

Head touch has been improved by parallel shift mechanism.

Equipped with a lock-type 3 x fast forward mechanism.

Equipped with microphone and headphone jacks.
