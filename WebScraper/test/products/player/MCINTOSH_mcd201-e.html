  • McIntosh MCD201

Commentary

SACD/CD player for 2 channels only.

Equipped with a multi-bit D/A converter.

It employs a CD transport section that is an improved version of the digital servo circuit, enabling smooth playback with almost no operating sound.
In addition, Super Audio CD2 x and CD4 x high-speed reading processing realizes excellent error collection.

Equipped with one unbalanced / balanced analog output that can be switched between fixed and variable.
The variable output allows level control of up to 6 v (RMS).

One coaxial and one optical system is mounted for digital output.

Wireless remote control is included.
