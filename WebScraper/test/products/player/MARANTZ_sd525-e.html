Commentary

Full Logic Oatley A double reverse cassette deck that employs a double mechanism for recording and reproducing.

Equipped with a level indicator by FL display tube.

Equipped with 2 speed dubbing function of constant speed / double speed and CD synchronized dubbing function.

It is equipped with regeneration functions such as QMS.

Equipped with Dolby HX PRO.

Wireless remote control is included.
