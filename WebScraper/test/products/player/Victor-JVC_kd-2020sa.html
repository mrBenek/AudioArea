  • VICTOR/JVC KD-2020SA

  • ¥ 77,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

A cassette deck equipped with a rack handle that was an option sold separately and has the same specifications as the KD-75SA.
It is compatible with 2020 series which is the system component of VICTOR/JVC.

An SA (Sen-alloy) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
Sen-alloy has features such as high maximum magnetic flux density, low Barkhausen noise, high Curie point and stable temperature characteristics, and little degradation of material properties. However, when used in block cores, head loss such as eddy current loss is large, so high-frequency characteristics tend to deteriorate, and it is too rigid for permalloy laminate.
The SA head has a composite structure in which a Sen-Alloy chip is bonded at high temperature to the tape sliding surface of a Permalloy laminated laminate core to realize a Sen-Alloy head, enabling excellent regeneration.

Double-gap ferrite head is used for the erasing head.

Equipped with a 5-point peak indicator, it is also possible to monitor pulse input signals with sharp rise.

Equipped with a 5-step recording equalizer switch, you can correct high-frequency characteristics that are impossible with sensitivity adjustment.

An ID mechanism using a FG servo DC motor is adopted for the mechanism part.

ANRS and Super ANRS are installed as noise reduction systems.

Equipped with dual ball cassette holder and kangaroo type air dump cassette door.
