Commentary

In addition to the full-auto mechanism, this full-auto player uses resonance prevention design such as tapered arm and low foaming cabinet.

Equipped with full auto mechanism, auto lead-in, auto cut, Oatley turn and auto repeat are possible.
A muting switch shuts out the shock noise generated when the needle tip moves to or from the record board.

It uses a unique electromagnetic relay delay circuit, and the arm starts operation as soon as the start button is pressed.
In addition, it is equipped with a pose Up down mechanism, and the arm can be raised by switching the pose lever between ON and OFF.

A 4-pole synchronous motor is used to drive the turntable, and a 24-pole synchronous geared motor is used to drive the arm operation to achieve rotational stability.
For the Center shaft, the mirror finished shaft is roll burnished, and the bearing is ball burnished with ultra-hard steel balls to achieve accurate rotation.

The arm uses an elliptical tapered arm that prevents resonance over a wide band by tapering the highly sensitive pipe arm of the static balance type. In addition, a special damper material is used for the pivot part to improve howling and SN ratio.
This tone arm is designed to achieve lateral balance across the entire arm, so there is no need to attach a balancer to the arm, thus reducing the effective mass of the tone arm.
In addition, it is equipped with an anti-skating mechanism to cancel the inside force that causes the needle to jump during light needle pressure operation.

The overhang can be adjusted while the arm is fixed to the stand.

Sliding lock type plug-in connector is adopted, and reliable shell replacement is possible.

The cartridge is equipped with an adjuster that can adjust the inclination of the needle tip.

By eliminating variations in the resonance point in wooden cabinets and using low-foaming resin molding, resonance is suppressed.
In addition, a panel floating mechanism is installed. Depending on the installation location and acoustic conditions, when howling is likely to occur, a large effect can be obtained by floating the panel board from the cabinet.

As a countermeasure for CD-4 regeneration, a low-capacity cord of 32.5pF/m is adopted, and the distance from the output terminal to the pin plug of the cartridge is limited to 100 pf or less.
