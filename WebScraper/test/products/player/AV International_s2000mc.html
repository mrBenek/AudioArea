  • AVI(AV International) S2000MC

  • ¥ 200,000 (released in March 1993)

Commentary

A multi-bit CD player.

The mechanism part adopts the mechanism of Philips company.

The D/A converter is multi-bit, equipped with a 20-bit D/A converter, and uses an 8 x oversampling digital filter.

The remote controller was an option sold separately.
