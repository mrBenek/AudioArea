  • Technics RS-B22W

  • ¥ 64,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

This is a double cassette deck that can be used for tape editing in one unit.

AX amorphous heads are used for Tape 1 read-only head and Tape 2 read-write head.

It is equipped with a high-speed tape editing function. If you press the tape speed button when editing, the speed becomes about twice as fast as usual, and editing time can be shortened.

Equipped with a synchronized start function that synchronizes the start of playback of Tape 1 with the start of recording of Tape 2.

It is equipped with a series playback function that starts the playback of Tape 1 as soon as the playback of Tape 2 is finished.

It is equipped with a 5-song skip selection function.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.
