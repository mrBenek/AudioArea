Commentary

A CD player with Optica Link.

An Optica link using an optical transmission photocoupler is adopted.
Optica Link eliminates the digital noise associated with the signal before it passes by transmitting the music signal data from the digital portion to the audio portion by optical transmission.

The digital filter is a 2 x over-sampling digital filter. Before the D/A conversion, the sampling frequency is shifted up to 2 x and the frequency is attenuated to 44.1 kHz. This allows unwanted signals in the very high band to be removed from the audio band with a simple analog filter.

The newly designed high-precision 3-beam type is used for the pickup part.
Because this pickup is lightweight and operates with a small servo current, servo noise is reduced. Stable tracing is achieved by combining it with a unique high-balance Y servo system that cancels out abnormal signals caused by disc scratches.

An IS mechanism is adopted to achieve no resonance and no vibration.

Equipped with an editing function, the computer can find songs that can fit in the recording time of the tape efficiently.

It is equipped with an introscan function or a random introscan function that continuously plays the head of a song for about 15 seconds each.

Equipped with an 8-digit large FL display.

Equipped with 5-mode repeat function.

Equipped with synchronous terminal.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

Wireless remote control with 10 keys is included.
