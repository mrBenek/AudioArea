  • KENWOOD DPF-3010

  • ¥ 26,000 (around 2000)

Commentary

CD player with 1-bit DAC and D. R. I. V. E. system.

Equipped with the D. R. I. V. E. system, which is Kenwood's original digital processing technology, it improves waveform changes in the digital signal processing process, which have been generated from small-level signals to very quiet small signals.

The D/A converter uses a 1-bit D/A converter to bring out the features of the D. R. I. V. E. system.
In addition, it is equipped with an 8 x over-sampling digital filter. By increasing the sampling frequency by 8 x, high-frequency noise is moved away from the audible frequency band, and noise components are easily removed with a low-pass filter.

It is equipped with functions such as CCRS, 20-track memory, 20-track music calendar, program tuning, skip and repeat.

Wireless remote control is included.
