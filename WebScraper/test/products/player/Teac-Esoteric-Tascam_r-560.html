  • TEAC R-560

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1995)

Commentary

Reverse deck equipped with reverse function.

A center mount system is adopted, and the mechanism is designed to be placed in the center with the best stability.

All DC configuration amplifier with ± 2 power supply system is used for the amplifier section.

It is equipped with a reverse mode selector which can select repeat playback of one way or reverse (5 consecutive round trips).

It is equipped with an intro check function that plays the head of the music one after another.

Equipped with a C. P.S. function that allows you to skip a song by simply pressing the FF or REW key while playing the tape.
With this function, you can skip up to 20 songs before and after.

Equipped with Auto-Rec Mute which can create a silent part of about 4 seconds.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

It has a headphone port.

It is equipped with a CD sync function, and synchronous recording is possible by connecting to a CD player compatible with CD sync of Teac with a sync cord.
