  • TEAC A-6700DX

  • ¥ 315,000 (around 1978)

Commentary

This is a 2-track 38 cm master recorder that is based on the A-6700 master recorder and can be connected to the dbx system.

Connection of separately sold dbx unit RX-15 is possible.
Dbx system type I can be used by connecting RX-15, and high SN ratio can be obtained by expanding dynamic range.
The dbx system is a noise reduction system developed by dbx Co., Ltd., which compresses the dynamic range of the input signal to 1/2 in decibels during recording and expands it to 2 times during playback to reproduce the dynamic range of the original sound.

Equipped with tension servo and impedance roller, it enables stable running of the tape.
The tension servo keeps the back tension generated by the change in the winding diameter of the feed reel electrically constant, while the impedance roller absorbs uneven running due to the flywheel effect.

A direct function logic circuit is used in the operation section. The logic circuit allows direct switching from all modes to other modes without using the stop button.
In addition, if you press the Play button from the quick-winding state, the motion sensing mechanism will enable quick Play operation.

It uses an open / close head housing that is easy to edit the tape.

It uses a 4-track playback head and can be used in combination with a 4-track deck to print or edit between four tracks.

It uses a full-track erasing head that conforms to the specifications of commercial decks. With its high erasing efficiency, it can erase even 4-track recorded tapes with the head.
Also, in order to focus on stereo recording, the L/R type recording mode switch is adopted.

To accommodate all commercially available tapes, the bias is 6-step switching type and the equalizer is continuously variable type.
In addition, if you set it to either 19cm/s or 38cm/s, you don't need to fine-tune it for the other speed.

Equipped with NAB and BTS standard switching mechanism.

Equipped with peak level indicator.

Equipped with a left and right channel independent volume fine adjustment mechanism.
It is also equipped with a left / right independent fine adjustment volume and a master volume for playback.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch with CAL position.

Equipped with a two stage switching type microphone attenuator of 0 db and 20 db.

With a carrying handle attached, it is easy to carry.

In the A-6700DX, the top panel of the transport section is changed from black to gray, and the front panel of the amplifier section is changed from silver to coffee brown.

The Console Rack CS-600 was sold separately as an option.
