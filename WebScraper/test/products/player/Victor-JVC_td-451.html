Commentary

A 3-head tape deck capable of multi-microphone mixing recording.

Uses a 3-head system, allowing simultaneous recording and monitoring.

The head uses the Clonios head developed in-house.

It is equipped with a blend microphone terminal that can freely determine the orientation. It can record 3 multi-microphones by combining the left and right microphones.

A stable basic mechanism with a long track record is adopted.

We have adopted a wire-saving and highly reliable circuit board.

The amplifier circuit uses a PNP-NPN hybrid 3-stage direct-coupled E-E feedback circuit.

A floating 4-pole induction motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with a VU meter.

Equipped with a 2-stage switchable tape selector for SH tapes and normal tapes.

Equipped with one touch reel lock mechanism.

Uses an EL drive system for easy tape mounting.

Equipped with an auto shut-off mechanism that works from any mode.

Uses an aluminum die-cast head cover.
