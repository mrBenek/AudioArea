Commentary

Quartz D. D. full auto player with emphasis on basic performance and operation performance.

Quartz is used for rotation control, and by making full use of unique all-around detection F. G. and electronic circuit technology, rotation accuracy within ± 0.002% rotational speed deviation has been achieved.
In addition, a unique integrated structure D. D. turntable is adopted.

Straight pipe tone arm is adopted, and high-sensitivity gimbal suspension system is adopted for the bearing structure.

Equipped with a plug-in connector type MM cartridge.

Equipped with a full automatic mechanism, it starts playing just by setting the number of revolutions and pressing the start button.
All operation buttons are laid out on the front of the cabinet so that all operations can be performed while the dust cover is closed.

The upper and lower cabinets and bottom base are all made of TNRC with excellent vibration absorption.
