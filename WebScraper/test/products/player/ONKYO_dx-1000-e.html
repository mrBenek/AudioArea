  • ONKYO DX-1000

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

A compact CD player with the latest technology and operability.

The D/A converter is a 16-bit D/A converter, and the low-pass filter is equipped with a 9th-order low-pass filter whose constants are set by listening.
An operational amplifier with a distortion factor of 0.002% or less (1 kHz) is used as a buffer amplifier.

The power supply section is equipped with a dedicated power supply for digital and analog systems to reduce distortion.

We have designed the left and right independent patterns of the audio circuit and improved the suspension of the laser pick-up section to achieve higher sound quality.

It is equipped with an 8-track random memory that allows you to freely set the order of performance, UP/DOWN direct selection function, FF/FR, repeat function, etc.

The display is a 3-step switching type, and shows track number, playing time and remaining time.

It is designed as a CD player compatible with the INTEC series and can record in sync with the INTEC series deck.
