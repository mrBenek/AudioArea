Commentary

A 2-speed record player made of carefully selected precision processed materials.

CP-301 is a model with a cabinet and CP-301u is a model without a cabinet.

A large hysteresis synchronous motor with 4 poles and 16 coils is used for the mechanism part.

A 30 cm static balance pipe arm is used for the tone arm.
Sapphire pivot and ball bearing are used for this arm.
In addition, a plug-in type is adopted for cartridge mounting, and JIS / EIA standard cartridges can be mounted.

A newly designed MM cartridge is attached to the cartridge.
