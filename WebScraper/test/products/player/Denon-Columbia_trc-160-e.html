Commentary

A stereo and cassette coder that won the Good Design Award.

Can be connected to an external amplifier with DIN cord.

Solid-state amplifier is used in the amplifier section.

Equipped with a level meter for recording and playback.

Equipped with a stereo headphone jack.

Monitor terminal is mounted.
