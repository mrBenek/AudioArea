  • SONY CDP-CE535

  • ¥ 36,000 (released in 1999)

Commentary

A CD player with a CD roulette type changer.

The disc tray uses a CD roulette system and can hold up to 5 CDs.
It also includes a disk exchange feature that allows you to replace an unheard disk without interrupting your performance or to load new disks into an empty tray.

The D/A converter is equipped with a hybrid pulse D/A converter.

Equipped with a Mega CD control function, you can connect a CDP-CX350 or another CDP-CE535 through the control A1II terminal and operate it from the main unit and remote control.

Compatible with CD-TEXT and capable of displaying English and numeric characters.
Also, by using the separately sold display unit CTD-S100, Chinese characters can be displayed.

Features include custom edits, multidisk programs, delete play, delete bank, music scan, and 6-mode repeat.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
