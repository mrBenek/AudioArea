  • VICTOR/JVC L-E30

  • ¥ 42,000 (around 1986)

Commentary

Front key operated microcomputer controlled record player.

You can program and play your favorite songs up to 8 songs and 15 times in your favorite order.

It is also possible to perform an introscan and an index performance in the order of program.
