  • Technics RS-TR979

Commentary

Equipped with A. T. C. (Auto Tape Calibration) that realizes high-quality recording according to the sensitivity and frequency characteristics of the tape. It detects the characteristics of the tape at a frequency of 3 points (400 Hz, 5 kHz, 10 kHz) and adjusts the bias, equalizer, and level according to the results. It is effective not only for low-speed recording but also for double-speed recording.

Series recording for continuous recording from Deck 1 to Deck 2. Simultaneous recording of 2 tapes and double-speed dubbing are also available.

The Dolby HX-PRO greatly improves high-frequency recording characteristics by automatically adjusting the execution bias during recording.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR.

Power assist cassette loading to prevent vibration of the half itself.

T. N. R. C. (Technics non-resonance compound) base that protects high sound quality by suppressing the effect of external vibration.

Synchro edit function.

Recording level adjustment (main / balance)

Timer Rec & Play function (REC/OFF/PLAY)
