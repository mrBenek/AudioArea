Commentary

Black model sold as a limited version of tape deck X-10R.

It is a reciprocating recording and playback machine of Reel 10, so it can record 3 hours at 19cm/s and 6 hours at 9.5 cm speed.

The mechanism uses a dual capstan system. By driving the tape with two pairs of capstan and pinch rollers sandwiching the tape, the modulation noise is greatly reduced. In addition, due to the symmetrical design, the same conditions are obtained even in reverse as in forward, and uniform tape travel is achieved in both directions.

The head section uses a 6-head configuration in which each head for erasing, recording and playback is independent in both reciprocating and reciprocating operations.
A high-hardness Permalloy head is used for the head, and an erasing head, which does not affect the sound quality, is placed outside the closed loop to shorten the tape span.

You can set timer recording in either direction by setting a timer switch in the center of the front panel and a commercially available audio timer.

Equipped with a 1-3/2-4 track selection queue mechanism, you can queue the necessary songs without switching tapes.
When the driving indicator lamp for either the forward (1-3 track) or reverse (2-4 track) track is on, queuing can be used to start the track.

It is equipped with a playback pause mechanism. If you press the pause button after getting a rough idea with queuing, the tape touches the head and you can turn the reel with both hands to get an accurate cue.

Equipped with a motion sensing mechanism, if you press the play button directly from the quick winding, the electromagnetic brake acts on the reel, it stops softly and moves to play action instantly.

When the Oatley bar knob is set and the counter is set to 0000 and the tape is played back, it can be automatically reversed from the reverse direction to the forward direction at that position. Only by combining this function with the Oatley bar, you can play it endlessly all day.

Equipped with Rec Mute function.

Equipped with a pitch control function that works for both recording and playback.

Equipped with an L/R linked recording mode switch.

Equipped with independent bias and equalizer switching functions.

Rack mounting is possible by using the separately sold kit.

Dbx system can be connected. By connecting the dbx system DX-2B (sold separately), the hissing of hearing can be reduced.
