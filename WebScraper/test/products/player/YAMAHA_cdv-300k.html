  • YAMAHA CDV-300K

  • ¥ 108,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

A CD/CDV/LD player where you can enjoy karaoke.

Both the video and audio sections are equipped with unique 1-bit D/A converters.

It is equipped with functions such as eighteen file, next reservation, one smore, and one touch karaoke.

Wireless remote control is included.
