  • TEAC MD-H500

  • ¥ 75,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

Mini-compact MD deck

Equipped with 1-bit D/A and A/D converter that does not generate 0 cross distortion in principle.

Operability is improved by the multi-functional jog dial.

It is equipped with editing functions such as DIVIDE to divide songs, ERASE to erase unnecessary parts, MOVE to rearrange the order, and COMBINE to connect songs.

It is equipped with a sound-synchronized recording function so that recording can start automatically when the connected CD player or cassette deck starts playing. Recording is also paused when playback ends.

It is equipped with FL display.

Since the date and time can be displayed on the display, it is possible to record the date and time on the disc when recording.

The front panel is made of solid aluminum, and the main chassis is made of steel board with increased rigidity.

Wireless remote control is included.
It can also be connected to the remote control terminal of the "Ernest Series" Diac component for system control.
