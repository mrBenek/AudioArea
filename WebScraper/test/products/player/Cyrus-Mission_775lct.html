Commentary

A record player with Tone Arm 774 lc and cartridge Solitaire.

A belt drive system is adopted for the turntable part.
A motor made in Switzerland is used as the drive motor.

The tone arm is equipped with the Mission Tone Arm 774 lc.
The 774 lc is a descendant of the first generation Tone Arm 774, and is manufactured according to the principles of the first generation Mission Arm.

The cartridge is equipped with a Solitaire MM-type cartridge.

MDF is used for the cabinet part, and the adverse effect due to vibration is eliminated by using a shock absorption mechanism using a sol button (equivalent to sol ossein?).
