  • Pioneer CT-205

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A simple stereo cassette deck.

The drive section is equipped with a high-torque DC servo motor. In addition, a twin belt system is adopted, and the rotation of the take-up reel and the rotation of the capstan are obtained by separate transmission belts to improve stability.

A newly developed permalloy head is used for the recording / reproducing head.

Equipped with a 3-stage tape selector.

Equipped with a timer start button.
