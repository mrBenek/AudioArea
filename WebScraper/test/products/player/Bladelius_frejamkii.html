  • Bladelius Freja markII

Commentary

SACD/DVD audio player specialized in pure audio while supporting various types of media.

Freja markII specializes in pure audio playback by eliminating the video and multi-channel playback capabilities from the universal player.

The twin-wave pickup enables playback of CD, SACD, CD-R, CD-RW, and DVD audio.

By synchronizing the operation clocks of the transport section and digital filter with the clock of the D/A converter, jitter, which interferes with sound quality, is reduced to the limit.

The sampling rate converter uses the Bar Brown SRC4192 for 192 kHz upsampling.

The D/A converter uses two DSD1792 D/A converters made by Bar Brown for each left and right channel in a differential balance configuration, and the I/V converter uses a discrete circuit with an emphasis on sound quality.

A large-capacity toroidal transformer is used for the power supply.

Equipped with XLR balanced analog output.
The Konek Tour uses the Swiss ノイトリック XLR connector.

Equipped with unbalanced analog output of 2 high / low systems.
The system remote controller ミョルナー is included.
