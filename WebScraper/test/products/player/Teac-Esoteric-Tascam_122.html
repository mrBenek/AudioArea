  • TEAC TASCAM 122

  • ¥ 125,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A master cassette deck for master recording of creative sound from an audio mixer using instruments, vocals, and synthesizers.

3-head, 2-motor configuration with mechanical tension servo.

IC logic control is used for various functions to realize accurate and smooth operation.

Equipped with double speed (9.5 cm) function.
* Double speed installed in 122 is not compatible with other cassette or cassette recorder.

Line in terminal is also mounted on the front.

It has a special volume for headphones.

Equipped with the Dolby HX System, it improves high-level high-pass characteristics.

Equipped with VU meter with built-in peak LED.

Equipped with a memory mechanism that makes it easy to find the beginning of the song.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a bias adjustment system.

Separately sold dbx unit can be connected.

Equipped with a remote control unit connection terminal, a separately sold remote control unit can be used.

The rack handle is optional and sold separately.
