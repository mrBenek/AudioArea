Commentary

This is a compact CD player with higher sound quality by improving the precision of the pickup section and eliminating vibration and resonance.

Equipped with a newly designed GIC-type low-pass filter, the processing of digital signals becomes more precise and simpler.

The optical pick-up cap is made of a material that is less susceptible to unwanted vibration. The optical block and disk table are separated into a floating structure to reduce the influence of external vibration and improve the accuracy and stability of tracing.

The optical block, circuit system and main circuit board are arranged in the chassis completely separated from each other, and structural rigidity is improved by repeatedly examining the position of the screw.
This eliminates the effect of circuit system vibration on audio and digital circuits and eliminates resonance.

The analog section has a simple and straight design. In addition, the digital section, analog circuit and power supply circuit are all arranged on a single printed circuit board to achieve a high-purity circuit design.
A microcomputer for system control is also integrated into the display section.

The analog section uses a carbon resistor for audio with a non-magnetic cap, a large-capacity power transformer for digital / analog independent power circuits, and a oxygen-free copper power cord.

The board is fixed in the middle between the top and bottom of the chassis, which is less affected by magnetic distortion. In addition, a non-magnetic oxygen-free copper heat sink and mounting screws are also made of copper-based screws to eliminate adverse effects on sound quality.

Equipped with a music Karenna Gore Schiff that displays up to 20 songs at once.

It is equipped with functions such as RMS function which can register up to 20 songs, original shuffle play, auto space play which automatically makes between songs 3 seconds, 5-mode repeat of 1 song / all songs / between A and B / RMS / shuffle, music search and index search.

Control S terminal, sync terminal and subcode output terminal are mounted.

A 20-key wireless remote control is included.
