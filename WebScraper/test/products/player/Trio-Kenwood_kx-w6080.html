  • KENWOOD KX-W6080

  • ¥ 32,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

A double Oatley berth cassette deck capable of relay recording of A/B decks.

After the recording of A deck is finished, B deck is activated in a moment, and it is possible to record music that can not fit on a single tape such as long-time live music.

Equipped with auto-bias function, bias can be easily adjusted.
Manual bias is also possible.

Equipped with C. C. R. S, Dolby B/C noise reduction, Dolby HX-PRO, D. P.S. S., auto tape selector, high speed dubbing, etc.
