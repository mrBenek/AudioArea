Commentary

This is a linear tracking full auto player that allows you to instantly select one song.

You can instantly select up to 9 songs on one side.
If you set a record board and want to listen to the third song, for example, you can press the Program switch three times and the arm will lead in to the groove at the head of the song and play the desired song.
A 7-segment indicator shows the number of times the Program switch has been pressed.

When you place a phonograph record on a turntable seat, it automatically selects the sizes for the 30 cm and 17 cm discs, and sets the speed to 33 rpm for the 30 cm disc and 45 rpm for the 17 cm disc.
Use the speed selector switch to play 45 rpm on a 30 cm disc.

It is equipped with free selection switches (Forward, Cueing, Reverse) that can move the arm forward and backward without touching the tone arm at all. The arm can be moved anywhere without opening the dust cover when the arm is at rest or during a performance.
Use the Forward or Reverse switch to move the arm to the desired position, and press the Cueing switch to move the needle down and start playback.

The arm uses a linear tracking arm that moves in the same way as the cutting machine.
As a result, the tracking error angle is small and no inside force occurs in principle.
In addition, the tracking servo mechanism using a DC motor and wire drive achieves quiet arm travel.

When automatically playing transparent or color records, use the supplied malfunction prevention sheet to prevent malfunction of the Auto Size Selector or Auto Speed Selector.
