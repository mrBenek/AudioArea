  • SONY TC-6364

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1971)

Commentary

A tape deck capable of recording and replaying both 4-channel and 2-channel.

It uses a 3-head type that can monitor playback even for 4-channel recording.

Equipped with a tape selector that supports SLH tapes.

Equipped with mechanisms such as mechanical shut-off, mechanical tension servo, and escalation drive.
