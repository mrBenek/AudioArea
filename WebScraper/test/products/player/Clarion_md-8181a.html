  • Clarion MD-8181A

  • ¥ 87,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

This is a double cassette deck that combines two cassette decks into one.

Both Tape1 and Tape2 support independent recording and mutual dubbing.
When the relay switch is turned on, recording on Tape 2 automatically starts when recording ends at the end of Tape 1.

Five indicators show the status of continuous recording, playback and dubbing.
Orange and green LEDs are used as indicators.

Tape selectors with independent bias and equalizer are installed on both Tape1 and 2.

Equipped with a monitor switch, the source signal and recorded signal can be switched and checked with one touch operation during recording.

Each level adjustment incorporates a slide-type marker knob.
By marking the optimal level, you can change the level frequently without losing track of the optimal level.

Route 3 mixing of Line, Mic, and Tape is possible.

The left channel of My Kujac can be used for both channels (L + R), and a single microphone can record mono on both channels.

By combining the timer sold separately, it is possible to record your absence.

Equipped with a memory stop mechanism.

The cassette holding mechanism adopts the full perspective style, and the remaining amount of tape and cassette half can be seen from the transparent cassette door.

Equipped with an electronic full auto-stop mechanism, all operations are canceled at the end of the tape.

Equipped with an accessory terminal, accessories such as Dolby NR unit and mixer can be connected.
