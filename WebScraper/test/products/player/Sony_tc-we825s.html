  • SONY TC-WE825S

Commentary

Twin re-reverse deck with Dolby S NR independently mounted on each A/B deck.

Dolby S NR, which reduces tape noise to about 1/16, is installed on both A and B decks.
Since both decks are capable of recording, it is possible to record two tapes simultaneously from an external source.

The A-deck has a wide range pitch control function that allows you to adjust the tape speed within ± 30% during playback.

Equipped with RMS function for 28 songs (14 songs on one side) convenient for editing from tape to tape.
You can program in your favorite order of songs on the A deck and record on the B deck.

In addition to normal speed, it is equipped with a high-speed dubbing function that copies tapes at double speed.

The Dolby HX PRO, which controls the bias current in units of one thousandth of a second, is installed independently on both decks.

Both decks are equipped with an auto-calibration function that automatically checks the characteristics of tapes.

Equipped with a sound auto-focus that controls the recording level setting with a microcomputer.
Switch allows switching between auto and manual operation.

Each of the A and B decks is equipped with an FL linear counter with a subtraction function that makes it easy to know the remaining amount of tape.

Equipped with a fader button that allows you to fade in and fade out at the touch of a button when recording.

It is equipped with a multi-AMS function that allows you to specify the number of up to 30 songs before and after the number of songs being played.

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining with the separately sold timer.

Wireless remote control is included.
