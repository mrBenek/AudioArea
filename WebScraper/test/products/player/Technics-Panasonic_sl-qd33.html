Commentary

This full-auto player system focuses on basic performance.

Quartz synthesizer system is adopted for rotation control, and excellent rotational performance with rotational speed deviation within 0.002% is achieved.

A unique integrated structure DD turntable is adopted for the turntable part, and excellent characteristics are realized by integrating the motor rotor and turntable with high precision.

The tone arm is equipped with a high-sensitivity gimbal suspension tone arm, which is identical in all directions and has a high initial motion sensitivity of 7 mg.

Equipped with T4PMM type cartridge as standard.
The T4P cartridge can be mounted with high precision simply by directly inserting it and fixing it with screws.

It uses a full automatic mechanism, and just set the number of revolutions and press the start button to start playing.

The cabinet uses TNRC, a unique material with excellent vibration resistance.
In addition, the small number of large insulators installed in this class completely eliminates vibration that adversely affects sound quality.
