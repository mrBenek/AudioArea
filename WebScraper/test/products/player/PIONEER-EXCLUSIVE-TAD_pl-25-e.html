  • Pioneer PL-25

  • ¥ 26,000 (around the late 1960 s?)

Commentary

A stereo record player equipped with an auto-return mechanism or auto-cut mechanism.

The auto-return mechanism automatically returns the arm at the end of the performance, and the auto-cut mechanism allows the same operation during the performance.

Uses PN-10, a MM type cartridge.

Equipped with oil dump type lever type arm lifter.
