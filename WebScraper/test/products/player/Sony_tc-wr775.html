Commentary

Twin record re-reverse deck with enhanced editing functions.

Twin FL counter with subtraction function independent of A/B deck is equipped.
This allows you to select the playback time for the A deck and the total recording time for the B deck.

The A/B deck is independent and equipped with Dolby HX Pro. When recording, the effective bias amount is controlled to the optimum value in units of 1/1000 second to prevent over-bias in the high frequency range and improve linearity in the high frequency range.

As a noise reduction system, it is equipped with Dolby NR B type and C type.

Wireless remote control is included.
This remote control is also equipped with a wireless CD sync recording function that allows users to enjoy synchronized recording without connecting a sync cord to a Sony remote-controlled CD player.

It is equipped with a peak level meter with real time peak display and peak hold function.

It uses a twin recording re-reverse mechanism that enables simultaneous recording of 2 tapes, relay playback of up to 5 round trips, and continuous recording of 180 minutes.

Equipped with AMS (Automatic Music Sensor) function that can quickly cue the beginning of the previous or next music.

It is equipped with a blank skip function, and when there is a silent part of more than 10 seconds, it fast-forwards to the beginning of the next song and restarts the performance.

Equipped with auto pause function.
With this function, when dubbing at normal speed, the A deck is put into a pause state every time a song is played, and the B deck waits in a recording pause. This enables synchronized dubbing while selecting a song.

Equipped with an auto tape selector, it automatically detects type I (normal), II (high position), or IV (metal).

Equipped with an autoplay function that automatically starts playing after rewinding, and a memory play function that starts playing from the position reset to counter 0.

By combining the timer sold separately, it is possible to record your absence and play the alarm clock.

Equipped with an autospace Rec Mute function that automatically records muting for approximately 4 seconds by pressing a button and temporarily stops it.
