Commentary

An ink-line type cassette deck equipped with an operation system on the inclined surface.

By adopting an ink-line type that incorporates the advantages of top and front operation, we have taken into consideration the balance of performance, operability and durability.

Wow and flutter characteristics dc-servo motor with built-in generators and a stable mechanism mechanism for the mechanism part, wow and characteristics are improved.

It uses a tape selector with independent bias and equalizer switching.

Equipped with an end stop mechanism, the operation button is automatically released at the end of the tape in both recording and playback.

It is equipped with a mechanical pause mechanism be stopped temporarily.
