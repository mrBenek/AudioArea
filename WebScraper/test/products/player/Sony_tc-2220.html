  • SONY TC-2220

  • ¥ 46,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Compact and lightweight portable stereo cassette recorder for beginners.

It is a 4-power supply system that supports 4 types of batteries such as 100 VAC for ordinary homes, dry batteries, rechargeable batteries, and car batteries.

Uses a DC servo motor with FG for the motor.

It is equipped with a tape selector corresponding to Duad tape.

Uses newly developed super permalloy head.

Equipped with a variable monitor.

Uses an auto shut-off mechanism.

Equipped with a memory light.

Comes with a shoulder belt.
Also, there was a carrying case sold separately.
