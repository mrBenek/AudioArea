Commentary

A full auto player with a quartz PLL servo DC motor.

In addition to the full-auto mechanism, it uses a photo-sensor system that detects the return position. This system is a completely contactless type that uses a high-sensitivity IC made of Cds and does not adversely affect the arm sensitivity.
In addition, an oil dump type arm lifter is mounted.

Adopts the feather touch type front operation which is also adopted in the DD-8200.

Straight type is used for the tone arm.
Excellent mass distribution is obtained by the uniform lateral balance of the straight pipe arm, and combined with the reduction of effective mass by the adoption of the lightweight carbon fiber head shell, it is compatible with high compliance cartridges.

In order to reduce the adverse effects of vibration caused by sound pressure from speakers and external vibration, we have adopted a suspension system with independent tone arms.
