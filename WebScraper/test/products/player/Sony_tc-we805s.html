Commentary

Twin recording re-reverse deck with Dolby S NR.

Both A and B decks are equipped with Dolby S NR, so you can record with less noise on either deck.
Simultaneous recording to two tapes is also possible.

The A deck is equipped with a wide-range pitch control function that can adjust the playback tape speed within ± 30%.

Equipped with RMS function for 28 songs (14 songs on one side) convenient for tape editing.

Normal / high speed dubbing is possible.

Dolby HX-PRO is installed independently on both decks.

Both decks are equipped with auto-calibration independently, making it possible to record with the full capacity of the tape.

It is equipped with sound auto focus which controls the setting of recording level by microcomputer.
Switching between auto and manual is possible with the switch.

A and B decks are each independent, and FL linear counter with subtraction function is adopted.

Equipped with a fader button that allows you to fade in and fade out at the touch of a button when recording.

Equipped with a multi-AMS function that allows you to start up to 30 tracks before and after the track being played.

Wireless remote control is included.
