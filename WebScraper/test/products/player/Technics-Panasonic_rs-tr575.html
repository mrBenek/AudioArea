Commentary

Double reverse deck equipped with ATC (auto tape calibration).

Equipped with ATC in order to bring out the maximum performance of the tape used.
This function records the test signal generated inside the set and detects the characteristics at three points of 400 Hz, 5 kHz and 10 kHz, and automatically adjusts the bias, recording equalizer and recording level in 64 steps of 0.2 dB each.
This makes it possible to record data with flat frequency characteristics.

Since you can record on both Deck 1 and Deck 2, it is possible to record in series or two simultaneously.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO system, the effective bias during recording is automatically adjusted to greatly improve the high-frequency recording characteristics.

As a noise reduction system, Dolby B/C NR is installed on both deck 1 and 2.

Equipped with a power assist cassette holder that securely holds the holder in place to ensure stable tape travel.

Equipped with auto REC mute, you can make a 4-second non-recording part with one touch when recording.

It uses high-speed FF/REW that reduces the fast-forward and rewind time to about half that of conventional models. This also makes TPS faster.

Equipped with a double electronic counter.

You can adjust the recording level and left / right balance.

Wireless remote control is included.
