Commentary

Mini component size CD player with edit guide function.

Equipped with an edit guide function that can be used when recording from a CD to a tape.
By simply selecting the length of the tape you want to record (90 minutes, 60 minutes, 46 minutes), EDIT automatically distributes the recorded music to the A and B sides of the tape and displays it along with the remaining time on the tape after recording.
With WARP, you can search for songs that can be recorded in the remaining time of the edited tape, and rearrange the program so that the amount of remaining tape on that side is minimized by moving or swapping songs.
When editing and recording from multiple disks to a single tape, LINK allows you to display the number of songs that can be recorded from the next CD and the amount of time left in the Time left recorded from the previous CD.
With SCAN, you can play the intro of songs in order for 15 seconds each, and you can program while listening to your favorite songs.

Equipped with auto-peak search, it automatically detects the peak portion of the CD that you want to record, and accurate recording level setting on the deck side is possible.

The D/A converter employs a 2 dac configuration with dedicated D/A converters for both L and R channels, eliminating the phase shift between the channels. In addition, a 4 x oversampling digital filter reduces sampling noise and improves the S / N ratio.

A linear motor is used for the traverse mechanism that drives the pickup to improve durability and reliability.

Equipped with a 4-mode time display function that allows you to switch between displaying the elapsed time and remaining time of a performance or all songs (program songs).

Equipped with auto-space function.

Wireless remote control is included.
