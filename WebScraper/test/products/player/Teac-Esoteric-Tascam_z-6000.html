  • TEAC Z-6000

  • ¥ 238,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck consisting of 3 heads, 3 DD motor, 3 calibration, 3 nr system.

By adopting die-cast body realizing high reliability and preventing generation of fine vibration, more smooth driving is realized.

The 3 dd (direct drive) DC-motor is used for the drive mechanism, and the wow flutter is greatly improved by direct drive of the capstan and reel motors. The brushless DC-servo motor is used for the capstan, and the flat coreless DC-motor is used for the left and right reel motors.
In addition, the Z-6000 uses a dual capstan system in which two sets of capstan and pinch rollers firmly hold the tape and drive it to stabilize the tape running. In addition, by changing the diameter of the left and right pinch rollers, the waw flutter cycle is dispersed. The oil-impregnated plastic used in the pinch roller bearing absorbs minute vibrations. In addition, by using a titania-based precision processed ceramic tape guide with excellent conductivity, static electricity (the cause of noise) generated on the tape is released, and characteristic degradation due to long use is suppressed.

The head base, which slides the head, uses a die-cast base in which the recording / reproducing head and the erasing head are mounted in one unit to maintain accuracy even when used for a long time.

Specially programmed LSI is used for the control of the mechanism.

Almost all of the circuit has a DC configuration with ± 2 power supplies for a significant improvement in characteristics. In addition, a direct coupling method without a condenser is used for connecting the playhead to the equalizer amplifier to improve sound transparency and dynamic range.

The head uses a contourless type with a unique design of TEAC, and the surface of the reproducing head is specially shaped to obtain excellent characteristics.

Gold-plated terminals are used for the line input / output terminals and the supplied input / output cords are also gold-plated.

In order to bring out the performance of each tape such as normal, chrome, metal, etc., bias / level / equalizer fully independent adjustment mechanism which can be fully adjusted independently of L/R is adopted.
The Z-6000 uses a method that first sets the optimum bias value (minimum distortion point) and then flattens the frequency response by adjusting the equalizer, thus achieving both the optimum bias value setting and the flatness of the frequency response.
In addition to this adjustment mechanism, the Z-6000 is equipped with a fully adjusted reference position with standard tape at the factory.

As a noise reduction system, a total of 3 types of Dolby B, C and dbx are installed.

Equipped with a large FL (fluorescent tube) display controlled by a full-scale microprocessor.

Uses a power eject that opens the cassette holder just by lightly touching it.
When the power is turned off, press the button again to open it smoothly.

Two types of adjustment mechanisms are provided : a recording preset volume and a master fader.
The master fader is a high-end type used in commercial mixers.

It is equipped with a SYNC monitor, which automatically switches to TAPE when running at constant speed and to SOURCE when running in other modes.

It is equipped with an introcheck mechanism that searches only the head part of the song one after another and plays it for approximately 10 seconds.

It is equipped with CPS (Computomatic Program System), and you can skip over other songs and play the songs you want to listen in your constituency.

It is equipped with a block repeat mechanism. When the position of the tape to be rewound is memorized, it is repeatedly played back between counter 0000 and it.
