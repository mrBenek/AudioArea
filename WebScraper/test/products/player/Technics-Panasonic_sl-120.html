Commentary

Armless record player announced as an armless version of SL 1200.

Comes with 2 types of arm panels to which you can attach your favorite short arm.
In addition, the same arm panel as the attached arm panel was sold as a separately sold option.

The drive section employs an electronically controlled ultra-low-speed DC motor of the same specifications as the SL-1100. This eliminates the influence of power supply voltage and frequency fluctuations and provides stable rotation.
310 kg ・ cm due to large starting torque2The turntable is rotated at a constant speed of 1/2 rotation, and quick start is also possible by stopping the turntable by hand and starting it instantly.

Excellent performance is obtained by direct drive system without any reduction mechanism such as belt and idler.

A strobe is engraved on the edge of 45 ° inclination, and the rotation speed can be finely adjusted accurately by illumination of the illuminator.
Speed switching is electronic, and fine adjustment is possible within 10% range by 33 1/3 and 45 rpm alone.

A large dynamically balanced turntable 33 cm in diameter and 1.75 kg in weight has been adopted.

Equipped with an insulator to prevent external vibration.

Dust cover is included.
