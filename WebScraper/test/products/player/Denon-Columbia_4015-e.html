  • DENON/COLUMBIA 4015

  • ¥ 22,800 (around 1968)

Commentary

A 4-speed record player that uses a static balanced pipe arm with an auto lifter.

High-compliance MM type cartridges are used.

Equipped with an auto stopper that stops rotating as soon as the performance ends.
