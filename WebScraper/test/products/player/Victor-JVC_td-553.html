  • VICTOR/JVC TD-553

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

Tape deck with 3 motor mechanism and 3 head system.

A 4-8 pole hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the capstan motor, and two 6-pole eddy current motors are used for the reel.
An electromagnetic brake that does not damage the tape is also used to stop the machine.

Equipped with a bias adjustment circuit that can adjust the bias current according to the tape used.

It is equipped with a tape selector which adjusts the equalizer circuit according to the standard tape and SH tape respectively.

Simultaneous recording and monitoring is possible.

Microphone mixing recording, echo recording and follow-up recording are possible.

Equipped with all electromagnetic push buttons.
