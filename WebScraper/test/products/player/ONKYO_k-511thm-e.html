Commentary

INTEC275 series 3-head dual capstan cassette deck.

The closed-loop dual capstan realizes stable tape travel.

Equipped with a manual recording volume and a level meter.

Equipped with a CD sync recording function.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch.

Equipped with a real-time counter with tape size detection function.

Equipped with AEI transformer and power cord with polarity display.

Dolby B / C NR/HX-PRO is built-in.

A-911M/A-911MLTD/R-811M can be linked by the system remote controller attached.
