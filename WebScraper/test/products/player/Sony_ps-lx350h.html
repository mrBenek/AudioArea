  • SONY PS-LX350H

  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1999)

Commentary

A 450 mm wide record player.

Uses a turntable with a mass of approximately 900g with a high moment of inertia to reduce uneven rotation.

Universal S-shaped tone arm is used for the tone arm, and the shell and cartridge can be replaced.
In addition, it is equipped with inside for scan seller, and stable tracing is possible.

It supports 2 speeds of 33.1/3 rotation and 45 rotation.

Equipped with speed adjustment function with strobe.

Uses a large insulator to reduce vibration from the legs.

Comes with 2 replacement needles.

  • ・ PS-LX350H listed on Sony's website
