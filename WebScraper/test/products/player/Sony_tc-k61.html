  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A cassette deck announced as a standard machine corresponding to metal.

The S & F head combines the features of Sendust with high saturation magnetic flux density and ferrite with good high-frequency characteristics.

The mechanism uses a BSL green motor that achieves excellent rotational accuracy, stability, quietness, and durability by eliminating the cause of torque unevenness, and a 2-motor simple mechanism that uses a high-torque motor.

Uses a logic control feather touch operation that also supports direct change.

Equipped with multi-functional peak program meter.

Uses Dolby NR Complete IC.

Equipped with Rec Mute function with auto space.

Equipped with a 4-stage tape selector.

Equipped with autoplay / memory play function.

Equipped with a timer standby switch.

The flywheel is deadened to eliminate resonance.

Equipped with an output level attenuator.

Remote control unit sold separately allows operation from a remote location.
Also, by using the synchronized remote control unit, it is possible to play synchronized with the player and the playback deck.
