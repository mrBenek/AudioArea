  • ONKYO Integra C-701XG

  • ¥ 57,000 (around 1989)

Commentary

An optical transmission type CD player equipped with ACCU BIT and super quartz system.

The Super Quartz System is designed to improve the accuracy of the crystal oscillator. We are reviewing the electronic circuit, anti-vibration measures, and selection of elements.

An 18-bit D/A converter is used for the D/A converter. To further improve accuracy, the "ACCU BIT" adjustment is performed to adjust the slight error of resistance value for the upper bits of the left and right sides.

Two block construction.

Linear slide motor.

A hybrid die-cast tray with high damping effect is used for the tray.

Equipped with 2 digital outputs (optical / coaxial) and 2 analog outputs (fixed / variable).

The power supply section is equipped with dedicated power supply for digital and analog respectively.
