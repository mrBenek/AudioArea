Commentary

2-motor type full auto player.

A 2-motor system is adopted in which the motor for driving the turntable and the motor for automatic operation are separated. Lead-in and return operation can be performed without applying load to the rotation of the turntable. In addition, the motor for automatic operation is also separated and independent, so accurate automatic operation is achieved.

In order to reduce vibration of the tone arm, zinc die-cast is used for the bracket part and aluminum die-cast is used for the arm base. In addition, the tone arm and main weight shaft are coupled with rubber to suppress low-frequency resonance.

The drive motor is equipped with a newly developed slim 4-coil 4-pole motor.
Simply making the motor thin reduces the torque and causes problems such as magnetic flux leakage, vibration, and heat generation. However, in the case of the new slim type strong motor, the distribution of magnetic flux is averaged by installing four motor coils.

A polyurethane belt is used for the belt that transmits the rotation of the motor to the turntable, and absorbs weak vibrations.

The turntable shaft and bearings have a burnished finish in which cemented carbide is pressed under a constant pressure to improve machining accuracy, eliminating the wow flutter and improving S/N.
