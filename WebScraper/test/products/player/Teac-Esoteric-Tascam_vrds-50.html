Commentary

CD player with newly developed D/A converter RDOT and VRDS mechanism.

Uses newly developed D/A converter RDOT (Refined Digital Output Technology) with excellent waveform reproducibility.
This D/A converter generates data in excess of 20 kHz by analogizing the data read from a CD to music information that seems to have existed between the data.

A unique non-floating turntable mechanism VRDS is adopted for the mechanism.
The VRDS-50 has a new spindle drive circuit equivalent to the P-0s.

In addition to the 8 mm thick (8 kg) steel board bottom chassis and aluminum solid block, a new steel structure with a diameter of 17 mm that connects the mechanical and bottom chassis directly is added to the high-rigidity chassis.

By mounting a timing shaping circuit with quartz accuracy just before the digital output terminal, jitter in the digital output is reduced.

Built-in DAC ON/OFF switch is mounted.

It is equipped with supporting pinpoint foot and insulator made of heavy grade cast iron.

Equipped with a digital servo that adjusts the servo amount for each disk.

We use high-quality audio-only parts such as operational amplifiers and capacitors, which are also used in Eso Telic products.

Uses a tray with Teflon coating that does not damage CDs.

Wireless remote control is included.
