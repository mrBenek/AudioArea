Commentary

Cassette recorder "Cassette Denske Type III" with built-in Dolby system.

F & F head is used for the head.

It is compatible with 4 types of power sources such as 100 VAC, dry batteries, rechargeable batteries and car batteries for general households.

It is equipped with a stabilizing power supply and DC-DC converter to stabilize the power supply voltage of batteries.

Uses the DC servo motor used in professional machines.

Equipped with a 20 dB microphone attenuator.

It uses the Sony Limiter recording method, which is safe even for sudden large input.

Equipped with a tape selector that can be used for chromi-cassettes.

Equipped with line / microphone selector switch.

It has a built-in amplifier and speaker for monitor.
