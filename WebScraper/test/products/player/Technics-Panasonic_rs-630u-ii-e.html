  • Technics RS-630U-II

  • ¥ 55,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with large double scale peak check meter.

Equipped with a level meter that enables peak checking by switching the switch.

Equipped with Dolby NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a 2-stage switchable tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

LH head is adopted for the head.

Equipped with a microphone and line input selector, line recording / playback with excellent SN ratio and linearity is possible.

Equipped with input level adjustment volume and output adjustment volume for L/R2-axis double friction drive.

A lateral sliding cassette door is mounted on the front of the mechanism.
In addition, it is equipped with a double mirror for driving monitoring.

Recording of absence is possible by combining with an optional timer.
