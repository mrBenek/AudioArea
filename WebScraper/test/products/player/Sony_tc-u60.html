  • SONY TC-U60

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

A metal tape-compatible cassette deck with S & F heads and AMS.

An S & F head is used for the recording / reproducing head, and a MagneFocus 4 Gap F & F erasing head is used for the erasing head, in order to cope with metal tape.

The motor is equipped with a BSL green motor.

Uses feather-touch logical control.

Equipped with multi-functional peak program meter.

Equipped with AMS (Automatic Music Sensor) for free selection of music.

Equipped with Rec Mute function with auto space.

Uses Dolby NR Complete IC.

Equipped with a memory play function.

Equipped with a timer standby switch.

Remote wireless control is possible by using PT-79R sold separately.
