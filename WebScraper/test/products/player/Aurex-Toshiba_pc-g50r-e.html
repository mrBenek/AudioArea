  • Aurex PC-G50R

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

A cassette deck equipped with quick auto reverse mechanism and NEW MQJS system.

Equipped with an IC logic auto-reverse mechanism that enables long recording and playback with simple operation.

The Oatley Verse is a quick type that detects the leader tape and quickly reverses from the A-plane to the B-plane, so it realizes seamless recording and playback.

The head uses a rotary head system with less azimuth displacement.

Equipped with the NEW MQJS system, you can skip and select songs, repeat one song, rewind play, full repeat, blank search, index scan, auto rec mute and rewind rec.
