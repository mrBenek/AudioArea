  • ¥ 152,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

Tape deck A-3300SX 2-track type.

It is compatible with 38cm/19cm and can record and play 2 tracks and 2 channels.

High hardness permalloy head is used for the head.

Equipped with a recording muting mechanism.

Equipped with a timer mechanism that can record absence.

Equipped with independent switching mechanism for bias and equalizer.

It is equipped with a cue mechanism that can make accurate head positioning.

Equipped with expanded scale VU meter which is easy to read at low level.

It uses a click-lock type reel clamper.

There were remote control unit and portable case sold separately.
