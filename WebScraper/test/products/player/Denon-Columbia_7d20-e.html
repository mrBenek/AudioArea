  • DENON/COLUMBIA 7D20

  • ¥ 36,800 (around 1968)

Commentary

Compact size tape deck.

It uses a chassis floating system that can be directly connected to an external transless amplifier or stereo set.

An all-transistor preamplifier is built-in.

It is equipped with automatic tape stopping device and temporary stopping device.

Equipped with DIN connector and level meter.

Can be used in horizontal, vertical and inclined directions.

Equipped with a one touch reset type tape counter.

It supports 3 speeds.

Equipped with a sliding roller.
