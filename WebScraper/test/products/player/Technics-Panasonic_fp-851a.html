  • National FP-851A

  • ¥ 27,800 (around the late 1960 s?)

Commentary

A 4-speed record player equipped with an autochanger.

If the number of revolutions is the same, it is equipped with full intermix system which can play even records of different sizes.

Equipped with an auto-cut system that can stop a record in the middle.
