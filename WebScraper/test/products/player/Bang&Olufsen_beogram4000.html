Audio Database

  • Bang & Olufsen BEOGRAM 4000

  • ¥ 215,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A record player that employs a linear tracking system.

The turntable is driven by a belt drive system, and an electronically controlled DC motor is used as the drive motor.

A linear tracking system is used for the tone arm section, and the tone arm always slides while maintaining its level with respect to the center of the board.

SP15 cartridges are used.
