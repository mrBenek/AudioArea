Commentary

A top-of-the-line stereo tape recorder with professional-grade performance.

In order to automatically play back and forth 4-track stereo tapes, a 4-head system has been adopted in which a head exclusively for reverse feed playback is added.
In addition, a plug-in type head assembly is adopted, and changes such as 4 tracks / 2 tracks can be freely made by replacing the head assembly.
The head has a hyperbolly-shaped surface, a 3-micron (regenerative) gap, a complete front shield and precision machining to improve the frequency range and crosstalk.

The motor is a 3-motor type with independent motors for tape drive, rewinding, and winding. All motors are hysteresis motors.
Instead of mechanical switching, the capstan is driven by a 4-pole 8-pole 2-speed motor. It also adjusts the equalizer in parallel with the tape speed.

The control system uses a unique symmetrical control system, and you can freely play forward / backward, fast forward in both directions, and stop with 3 buttons and 1 lever.

If you fix the end of the tape to the reel, the tape is automatically reversed and played in the reverse direction when the forward direction of the tape is finished.

When rewinding or reverse regeneration is finished, the switch linked to the tension arm works and the machine automatically stops.
If the tape runs out, it will stop automatically to prevent further damage to the tape.

Built-in amplifier of total 4 units of 2 systems for recording and 2 systems for playback, stereo recording and simultaneous playback monitor is possible.
Mixing recording is also possible because the recording level adjustment of microphone input and line input is independent.

Since the bias oscillation frequency is as high as 100 kHz, the frequency range of the recording is wide, preventing beat noise from FM broadcasting from being picked up.

Each channel has an independent VU meter.

Equipped with a monitor selection switch, the recorded state can be checked simultaneously with the recording.

By using the plug-in microphone transformer (sold separately), it is possible to use microphones of various impedances.

Equipped with a 3-digit digital index calendar.
