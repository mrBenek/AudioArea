Commentary

A tape deck with full-fledged live recording and editing functions.

In addition to the VU meter that indicates the average value of the signal, it is equipped with a peak level indicator that monitors the maximum value (peak value) of the signal.
The A-6100 peak level indicator responds to a short pulse signal by means of a light-emitting diode. In addition, a memory holding circuit lights up at a constant illumination for 70 ms (7/100 sec), making it easier to detect.
In addition, since the peak margin of the tape varies depending on the type of tape and the tape speed, the lighting level is switched to four levels in conjunction with the speed switching mechanism and the meter range switching mechanism.

Equipped with a 3-stage switching type (0, 15, 30 dB) microphone attenuator, which can attenuate excessive input to an appropriate level and send it to the microphone amplifier, enabling recording with less distortion.

You can white-mark the microphone volume.
If the VU meter indicates near 0 VU even when the microphone volume is reduced to the white-marked position, the input is overstated and the microphone attenuator must be switched to IN.

A FET with excellent noise characteristics is carefully selected for the first stage of the microphone amplifier and noise is suggested.

Equipped with a cue mechanism that can be locked. Sound can be monitored by simply pushing up the cue lever in the fast forward mode (FF, REW).
When the lever is pushed up and locked, the tape touches the head. At this time, if you turn the reel by hand, you can find the head part and the character position you want to edit.

It uses a head housing that can be opened and closed.
This makes it easy to mark edit points on the Dermatograph and to clean the head easily.

Equipped with a memory auto-stop mechanism that stops at the preset counter position.
If you preset the counter to 0000 at the position you want to edit, and turn on the memory auto-stop, the tape automatically stops at the preset position when rewinding.

With the slide switch on the back of the deck, the rated output can be switched between 0.3 v and 0.775 v.

High hardness permalloy head is used for the head.

It is equipped with 3J mechanism (Just Bias/Just Equalizer/Just Level) in order to utilize the performance of low noise tape.
With this mechanism, you can set the bias, equalizer, and level switch independently to the optimum position for the tape.

Equipped with a 4-track playhead.

Equipped with a memory marker with a click stop convenient for level setting.

Equipped with an Output 0 VU marker that displays the correct 0 VU level.

Timer recording / playback is possible by combining the timer control adapter sold separately.

By using the separately sold remote control unit, it can be operated from a remote location.

There was a portable case sold separately.
