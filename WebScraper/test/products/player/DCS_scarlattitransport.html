Commentary

Scarlatti Series SACD/CD transport.

The mechanism part is equipped with Ecotheric's VRDS-Neo, which has an order of magnitude higher fidelity than currently available mechanisms.
In addition, in order to bring out the capability of the VDDS-Neo mechanism to the maximum, the signal processing circuit was made completely new, and the FPDA and DSP used at that time were also newly designed.

The basic control board uses an 8-layer board called V7, and the transport ideal for DCs is realized with a completely new power supply.

In addition to IEEE1394 transmission of DSD data, DSD conversion of PCM data of PCM data and selection of readout layer of hybrid disk.

Word clock input / output is mounted.
In combination with DAC and clock of the same series, the best sound quality can be obtained, and it is also compatible with other DCs devices and lockable DAC of other companies.
