  • National/Panasonic RS-847U

  • ¥ 32,000 (around the early 1970 s)

Commentary

A 4-channel 8-track player that also plays 4-channel 8-track tapes.

You can play 4-channel and 2-channel 8-track tapes.
During playback, it automatically identifies the type of tape inserted into the slot, switches the circuit and automatically displays it.

Equipped with an auto-eject switch, the cartridge pops out automatically when the performance ends.
You can also switch to automatic continuous performance.

The digital light allows you to view the program being played.

A hysteresis synchronous motor is used as the drive motor.

It uses a specially designed new head with 4 head slits aligned in a straight line. In addition, it uses a super vertical system with less error for vertical movement of the head.

IC is used for the amplifier circuit.

Equipped with an AC outlet.
