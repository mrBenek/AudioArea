  • DIATONE DT-4200D

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

A cassette deck with Dolby circuit mounted on DT 4200.

Quick recording monitor system is adopted.
If you want to monitor the recording after it has been recorded, press the REVIEW button and release your finger at the desired location to see the regenerating condition. You can also do the same during playback.

A dolby circuit is installed as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with a chrome / normal tape selector which can switch both bias and equalizer.

Equipped with a muting circuit.

Equipped with end stopper.
