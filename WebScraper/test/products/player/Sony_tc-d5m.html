Commentary

Portable Cassette Recorder "Cassette Denske D5M" for Metal Tape

An original disk drive capstan servo is used in the mechanism section.
It consists of three components : a capstan servo control that has the same effect as a direct drive, a disk drive system that directly connects the motor shaft and the flywheel, and a small, low-power, high-torque coreless motor. Even with a small mechanism, it achieves high anti-rolling effect and high torque.

The head uses an S & F head compatible with metal tape.
We have also adopted a DC-DC converter with improved efficiency to stably supply a large bias current.

Equipped with a 4-stage tape selector that can be switched with a single switch.

Equipped with peak level indicator.

Equipped with a Sony Limiter to suppress excessive input.

Equipped with Dolby NR system.

Equipped with a microphone attenuator.

Equipped with MPX filter.

Built-in monitor speaker.

It uses 3 power sources compatible with 100 VAC, dry batteries and car batteries.

There was a carrying case sold separately.
