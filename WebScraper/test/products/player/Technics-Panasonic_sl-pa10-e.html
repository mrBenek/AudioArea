Commentary

This CD player unit was developed to be combined with a DAC built-in amplifier such as SU-MA10.

In order to reduce sound pressure and vibration transmitted from the speakers, a multi-layer structure using a metal chassis base and a TNRC weight base with a thickness of approximately 11 mm and a weight of approximately 3 kg is adopted. In addition, vibration isolation measures using special visco-elastic damping material are applied to the top plate to greatly improve the dispersion of resonance mode and the damping characteristics of vibration.
In addition, four large TNRC insulators are used to block vibrations transmitted to the player body, and the optical deck is double-floated on top of the four large TNRC insulators to achieve significant vibration damping.

A center mechanism has been adopted in which the optical deck is placed in the center of the main body with good weight balance.

The power supply circuit is equipped with a large transformer and two audio electrolytic capacitors (3,300 μ F) with anti-vibration measures to ensure a stable power supply.
A cord with polarity indication is used for the power cable.

The display can be selected from two levels of brightness or can be turned off.

Equipped with a 4-mode time display function, you can switch between the elapsed time and the remaining time of playing music or all music (program music).

Equipped with two optical digital outputs.

There are only the minimum necessary keys in the main body, and most functions are done by the included wireless remote control.
This remote control allows direct access with 20 keys, 20-track random program, index skip, random play, repeat, two mode search of slow (approx. 8 x) and fast (approx. 76 x), and auto-queue.
