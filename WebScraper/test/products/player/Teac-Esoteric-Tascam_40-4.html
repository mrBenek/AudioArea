Commentary

4-track 4-channel tape deck.

It uses an operation system that gives top priority to simultaneous sync recording and improves usability.

Ping-pong recording between tracks is possible.

It is equipped with a cue mechanism which is convenient for starting a song.

Equipped with memory auto-stop mechanism.

A dedicated dbx unit can be connected.

Equipped with a reel clamper that allows one touch attachment of the reel.

It uses a printed circuit board convenient for maintenance.
