Commentary

This is a cassette deck that has inherited the design philosophy that emphasizes the operation sense and sound quality of high-end machines.

Electronic control is used for the mechanism control, and feather touch operation with light operation feeling is adopted. In addition, the music search mechanism and auto tape selector mechanism are more important for operability.

As a noise reduction system, Dolby C Type NR is adopted in addition to Dolby B Type NR.

A hard permalloy head is used for the recording / reproducing head, and a ferrite head is used for erasing.

A DC servo motor is used in the mechanism, and the combination of a capstan with precision processing in micron order and a flywheel with excellent dynamic balance realizes stable tape travel.

Equipped with LED peak level meter.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

REC Mute

Equipped with one touch recording function.
