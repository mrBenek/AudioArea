  • Aurex PC-4030D

  • ¥ 55,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A stereo cassette deck, an improved version of the PC 4030.

* The inside of PC-4030D has been changed, but the description on the front panel is still PC-4030. Therefore, there are actually two versions of PC-4030.

In the PC 4030 d, an AS head is newly adopted to realize improved characteristics.

The panel surface is designed to be inclined at 30 °, and the visibility of the level meter is improved.

In addition to the level control of the left and right channels, it is equipped with a fader that can control the level of the left and right channels.

Equipped with an autoplay mechanism that allows automatic playback from rewinding.

It has a review and queue mechanism.

Equipped with a two step switching type tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Equipped with line and microphone input selectors.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.
