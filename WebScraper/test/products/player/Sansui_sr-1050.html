Commentary

Belt drive player without auto mechanism from SR-2050.

The tone arm is equipped with a lateral balancer and an inside for scan seller.

Equipped with a newly designed slow-acting oil elevation mechanism that is resistant to temperature changes.

Equipped with speed switching mechanism by push button operation.

Equipped with an accessory base made of zinc die-cast.

Gold plated terminal is used for the connection terminal.

Universal type light shell is adopted, and any cartridge can be used.

Equipped with a feather touch and easy-to-use start lever device.

Equipped with a power supply checking lamp.

Equipped with an insulator to prevent howling.

The cabinet has a walnut and open pore finish.
In addition, a removable smoke dust cover is used for the dust cover.
