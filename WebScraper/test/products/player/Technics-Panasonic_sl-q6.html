Commentary

This is a jacket size quartz D. D. player with an 8-track program that allows for automatic music selection.

Equipped with 1 to 8 program buttons, you can automatically play your favorite songs in the LP in any order you like.

Equipped with an electronically controlled full automatic mechanism, it automatically detects the size and number of revolutions of the record and starts playing just by pressing the start button on the front panel.

The upper cabinet is equipped with a linear tracking arm, and the tracking error angle is within ± 0.1% and the inside force is almost 0, realizing highly accurate arm travel by microcomputer control.

The gimbal suspension type arm bearing structure provides the same high sensitivity in all directions, and the dynamic balance type arm eliminates the need for precise horizontal adjustment of the cabinet.

By introducing quartz for rotational control, rotational performance within ± 0.002% rotational speed deviation has been obtained.

Plug-in connector type MM cartridge is equipped as standard.

All lower cabinet and bottom base are composed of TNRC, and vibration isolation measures are taken thoroughly.
