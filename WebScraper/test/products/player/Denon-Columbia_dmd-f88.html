  • DENON DMD-F88

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1998)

Commentary

Ef series MD deck with Λ S. L. C.

The D/A converter is equipped with a multi-bit Λ S. L. C (Lambda Super Linear Converter).
In the S. L. C., 0 cross distortion is eliminated by adding a unique conversion error correction to the D/A converter. By combining this system with a technology called the Λ method, superior original sound reproduction is realized.

You can enter up to 100 characters in katakana, alphabet (upper / lower case), numbers and symbols for the title name, song name, artist name, etc.
The display can display up to 14 characters at a time, and the rest are scrolled.

It has built-in editing functions such as divide, move, combine, erase, etc. which are unique to MD.

It is equipped with functions such as 25-track program, random playback and repeat playback.

It is equipped with a system function. When you upgrade the system with the D-F88 series CD player or tuner, you can play AUTO-ON, auto-function, CD synchronized recording, timer playback and recording, etc.

Wireless remote control is included.
