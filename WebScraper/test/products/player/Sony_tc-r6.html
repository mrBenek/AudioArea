Commentary

The mechanism of TC-R7-2 is left as it is, and only the head block is a tape deck with four tracks.

F & F head is used for the head.
In addition, the SN ratio, distortion factor characteristics and frequency characteristics have been improved by the direct coupling method in which the head and the reproducing amplifier are directly connected.

The capstan uses a servo motor that incorporates an FG frequency detection system servo to achieve stable rotation.
In addition, the reel motor is equipped with a dedicated motor for both feeding and winding, and the stable tape running characteristic of three motors is realized.

The tape drive system uses the well-established closed-loop dual capstan system, which improves driving accuracy and reduces modulation noise.

The tape travel button uses a feather-touch logical control system directly connected to an electromagnetic solenoid.
In addition, any position can be moved to the next position without stopping, and there is no wasted waiting time.

Equipped with a 3-step switching tape selector independent of bias equalizer.

By using the separately sold timer, continuous absence recording and alarm clock playback are possible.

Equipped with a follow-up recording function.

Recording and playback independent monitor is available.

Equipped with a recording monitor muting switch.

The line and microphone can be mixed.

Equipped with left and right independent recording mode switches.

The optional wired remote control in the picture allows remote control and REC-MUTE (silent recording).

Equipped with a 15dB/30dB microphone attenuator.

Equipped with a reel size switching switch.

There was a dust protector sold separately.
