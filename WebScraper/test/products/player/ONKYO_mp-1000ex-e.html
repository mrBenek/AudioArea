  • ONKYO MP-1000EX

  • ¥ 53,000 (around 1987)

Commentary

A full auto player with quartz lock control system.

It features a direct drive motor with quartz lock control, a tri-balance tone arm with lightweight and highly rigid Rahmen structure, and an MC type cartridge with LC-OFC coil.

Non-repulsion rubber with an elastic value of 1/10 or less of general rubber is adopted to eliminate the effect of unnecessary vibration.

It uses an electronic brake with smooth start and stop, and synchronized recording and repeat performance are possible by linking with a cassette deck.
