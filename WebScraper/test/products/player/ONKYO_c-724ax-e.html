Commentary

A CD player capable of reproducing minute audio signals by stabilizing the ground potential.

When a digital signal, servo current, or clock signal is mixed into the ground of an analog circuit, the reference potential fluctuates, leading to sound quality degradation.
To solve this problem, the C-0. 724 ax has been repeatedly verified through theoretical analysis and hearings. We have developed and adopted a circuit configuration that does not change the potential even if digital signals are mixed into the ground and a printed circuit board pattern, ensuring a stable ground potential.

The D/A converter employs a linear single-bit D/A converter with excellent reproducibility of minute audio signals.
In addition, the crystal oscillator that oscillates the clock signal that controls the signal processing of the D/A converter is small and resistant to external vibration. It is fixed even more firmly to obtain a stable clock signal.

Equipped with two optical digital output terminals.

It is equipped with a next selection function that allows you to specify the next track during performance, a peak search function, a time edit function, and a memory function / memory reverse function.

Wireless remote control is included.
