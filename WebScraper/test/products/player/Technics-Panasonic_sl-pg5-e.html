Commentary

CD player equipped with super 1-chip LSI.

The SL-PG5 is equipped with a Super 1-chip LSI. By integrating the functions of optical servo (focus, tracking, and traverse), signal processing, and D/A conversion into a single chip, the signal path is greatly shortened and noise is reduced.

The Mash 1-bit DAC is used for the D/A conversion section, and non-linear distortion such as 0 cross distortion does not occur in principle, so more faithful DA conversion is possible.

The audio circuit uses an electrolytic capacitor with a bamboo mixing separator and a THCB for mechanical vibration to improve sound quality.

Digital servo is used to achieve stable drive by adjusting the appropriate and stable servo amount in response to different disc conditions for each disc.

Equipped with digital optical terminal.

Equipped with a peak search function.

The main body and remote controller are equipped with 10 keys for direct access.

Playback is supported for music CD-R and CD-RW recorded in CD-DA format and finalized after recording.

Wireless remote control is included.
