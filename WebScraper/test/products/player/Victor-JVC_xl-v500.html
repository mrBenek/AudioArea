  • VICTOR/JVC XL-V500

Commentary

A CD player that integrates Victor's original technology.

The mechanism part uses an independent suspension mechanism pursuing non-resonance and non-vibration.
In this mechanism, the rotating system is fixed to a sturdy mechanical base mixed with glass. Together with the pickup section, it is floated from the chassis by a special shape of insulation rubber. By this, disturbance vibrations and sound pressure of speakers are shielded, and traceability is improved.

The disk clamper uses a magnet catch method.
When the arm of the disk clamper directly touches the disk, the sound quality deteriorates due to vibration transmitted from the outside and resonance of the arm. To prevent this, the magnetic catch method disk clamper adopts a structure in which the clamper is completely floated from the arm during performance. The disk is pressed against the turntable by magnetic force.

The Y servo system is used for the servo circuit.
The Y servo minimizes the operation of the error correction function and increases the traceability of the pickup body. When an error occurs, the servo loop characteristics are automatically adjusted so that the pickup can accurately capture the signal pit.

Equipped with 2 x oversampling digital filter.
The sampling frequency is doubled to 88.2 kHz, and unwanted signals located just above the audible band are removed by a digital filter and then attenuated by a slow analog filter with good phase characteristics.

The analog circuit uses an isotropic substrate pattern with symmetrical left and right channels to minimize crosstalk up to the high frequency range.
In addition, the filter is composed of fine discrete parts to reduce the deterioration of sound quality.

The power supply section employs a double power transformer system, and the analog power supply is independent of the transformer itself, which fundamentally cuts off digital noise and beat interference.

Along with vibration isolation measures everywhere, distortion and noise are reduced by adopting thick earth lines and non-magnetic parts.

Equipped with timer play function and auto pause function.

It comes with a wireless remote control that can perform most operations such as 15-track program with 10 keys and direct selection of music.
