Commentary

A direct drive player that adopts quartz synthesizer continuous pitch control system.

The continuous pitch control system of the quartz synthesizer allows the pitch to be changed continuously within ± 8% with the quartz lock on.

An all-around detection FG is used to detect the entire circumference of the rotating shaft, so more accurate detection signals are obtained than the single-point detection method.
The motor is an integrated D. D. motor.

It uses a gimbal suspension system and realizes an excellent initial motion sensitivity of 7 mg for both horizontal and vertical motion.

It is equipped with an arm height adjustment mechanism with a lock mechanism, and fine adjustment within 6 mm is possible with the height adjustment cylinder of precision helicoid finish.

The upper part of the cabinet is made of aluminum die-cast, and the lower part is integrally molded with heavy special rubber which has a large vibration damping rate and excellent vibration damping characteristics. Excellent vibration damping performance is obtained.
In addition, the D. D. motor, tone arm and dust cover are connected with special rubber until the mounting part, and each part is isolated separately. In addition, vibration resistance of the turntable is further improved by rubber sheet and デッドニングゴム attached to the back of the turntable.

A slide lever system is used for pitch adjustment.
The display board reads the pitch change directly in increments of 1%, and the green LED lights up at 0 points (rated speed).

Equipped with a pure electronic brake mechanism, it can be braked quickly with one touch.

Equipped with a pop-up stylus illuminator, it can be stored in a cabinet when it is not necessary.

Equipped with a strobe illuminator integrated with the power ON/OFF switch.
