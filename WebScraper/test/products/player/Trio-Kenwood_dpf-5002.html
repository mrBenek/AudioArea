  • KENWOOD DPF-5002

  • ¥ 34,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

CD player with 1-bit DAC and D. R. I. V. E.

Equipped with a D. R. I. V. E. system with 8 fs 20-bit resolution using a 1-chip D. R. I. V. E. IC, the reproducibility of small levels and small signals is improved.

In order to read information on the disk more accurately and stably, a digital servo is installed.

Equipped with 1-bit D/A converter.

The chassis has a thickness of 1.6 mm to increase the strength and prevent harmful vibration.

It is equipped with functions such as editing functions which are convenient for tape dubbing, program playback, program selection, and repeat all songs / program / one song.

You can do most of the operation with the remote control attached to KAF-5002 and KAF-7002, and there was a dedicated remote control sold separately when you use the CD player by itself.
