  • SONY CDP-C100

  • ¥ 79,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

A 10-disc CD changer, the successor to the CDP-C10.

A magazine system is adopted, and 10 CDs can be set together.
In addition, it is equipped with various functions such as 10-piece continuous performance, 10-piece program performance and 10-piece shuffle play.

Equipped with 4 x oversampling digital filter and dual D/A converter.
The servo circuit is equipped with S Servo II.

With built-in disk memo capability, you can record messages on each CD for up to 240 titles in 24 magazines.

Equipped with a program bank function that can store programs in units of magazines.
Up to 90 banks are available for each magazine.

Equipped with multi-mode timer function.

Equipped with an auto pause function, you can pause each song.

Equipped with 4-mode repeat function of one disc full music / all discs full music / all disc program music / one disc shuffle.

The display section has a 10-disc / 20-track music calendar.

Equipped with an optical digital output.

Equipped with a headphone port with volume linked to lineout.
The volume of the CDP-C100 can be adjusted by remote control.

Includes a 10-key + 10-key wireless remote control.
