Commentary

A record player in which the weight of to vibration are reduced by increasing the weight of important parts.

The motor is equipped with an 8-pole 24-slot DC servo motor with a large torque.
In addition, improvements such as faster response of the servo circuit have made it possible to maintain accurate rotational speed even against load fluctuations.

The turntable uses a 1.6 kg aluminum die-cast turntable that is dynamically balanced from the mold by precision metal processing technology.
This turntable has an inertial mass of 220 kg ・ cm to increase the actual weight when rotating by shifting the weight distribution to the outer circumference.2Value.
In addition, the material and shape of the turntable rubber sheet with good sound are determined by a strict hearing test.

A lead-alloy die-cast arm base of 1.3 kg is used for the arm base. By fixing the foot of the arm firmly, harmful unnecessary vibration is prevented and the adverse effect on the reproduced sound is suppressed.

The arm uses a twist resistant light alloy pipe filled with special fillers to suppress the resonance of the pipe itself.
In addition, the main shaft of the rotary bearing section and the weight shaft with weight for applying needle pressure are connected through a rubber damper. In addition, the bearing case of the bearing and the main shaft are also connected through a rubber damper. In addition, the main shaft is also equipped with thorough anti-vibration measures such as eliminating the aluminum pipe that is usually used in many cases and reducing resonance by cutting out brass.

Specially selected angular contact bearing is used for the rotating part of the arm so that it can receive both horizontal and vertical loads equally.

The inside force canceler, which cancels the inside force (force toward the inner circumference of the record), is a dial direct reading type that can be adjusted according to the needle pressure.
In addition, the arm lifter uses oil dump queuing that allows the arm to move up and down smoothly with the hydraulic pressure of silicon oil.

As a MM type cartridge, SHURE M75BM is equipped as standard.

The cabinet is a solid type with 48 mm thick high density particleboards and is finished with an American Walnut Dap makeup finish.

Dust cover with free stop hinge is adopted.

Equipped with speed control and strobe, both 33 1/3 and 45 rotations can be independently adjusted to 6% fine speed.
