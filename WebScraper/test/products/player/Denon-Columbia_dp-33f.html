  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A full auto player that uses DENON quartz.

Equipped with non-contact type, electronically controlled servo tone arm.
In addition, by adopting an electronic servo lifter, the needle tip has a soft Up down and there is no impact.

Equipped with a position selector, you can select any position on the record board with the locate knob.

The drive section uses DENON quartz and AC servo motor to achieve stable driving.

The cabinet uses an integrally molded cabinet made of a new material, H. D.C. (High Density Compound).
H. D. C. is a mixture of special materials and heat compression molded. It has acquired a specific gravity of approximately 2 and realizes excellent howling characteristics and high strength.
In addition, since high heat treatment has been applied to processing and molding, there is no deformation even in long-term use.

The cartridge is equipped with DL-8A, MM type cartridge.

Equipped with auto-repeat function.
