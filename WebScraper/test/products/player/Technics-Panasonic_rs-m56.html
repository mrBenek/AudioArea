Commentary

This is a stereo cassette deck equipped with a microcomputer for improved operability.

It is equipped with a microprocessor auto-scan system using a microcomputer with 20,000 elements. This function automatically detects the no-signal part between songs, and can locate the beginning of a programmed song among 20 songs.

On the upper part of the digital 2-color FL display, a music address display using a fluorescent display tube is mounted. This display allows you to see which song is currently being played and which song is to be played next. A blank beep is also mounted to let you know between songs with a tone.

The drive motor is a digital FG servo motor.

An HPF head is mounted on the read / write head.

Equipped with a 3-stage switchable tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.
You can also fine-tune the bias.
