  • ONKYO TA-440

  • ¥ 41,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

Metal-compatible cassette deck with an emphasis on basic performance.

A DC servo motor and belt drive mechanism are used for the drive system.

Hard permalloy head is used for the head.

It is equipped with Dolby system, 3-stage tape selector, slide-type input balance control, timer standby mechanism, etc.

Equipped with a full auto-stop mechanism to reduce the burden on the mechanism and tape.

Equipped with an input auto-selector that automatically switches between line input and microphone input.
