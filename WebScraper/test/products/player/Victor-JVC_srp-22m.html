  • VICTOR/JVC SRP-22M

  • ¥ 25,500 (around 1972?)

Commentary

This record player unit is compatible with CD-4 playback by changing the needle.
It corresponds to SRP-B22M model without cabinet.

The tone arm uses a TH-type high-sensitivity arm.

The cable uses a low-capacity (low-capacity) special shield wire, and is also suitable for playing CD-4 records.

Equipped with functions such as push-button type speed switching, overhang indication indicator, and arm elevator.

Can be stored in Victor Component Rack (LCU-V50, V5 type).
