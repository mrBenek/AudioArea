  • Technics RS-M220

  • ¥ 42,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with music selector.

Equipped with a music selector, you can start the next song or repeat the same song with one button.

The mechanism section employs a soft tom drive that uses the rotational force of the motor to switch between head hold and reel drive.
An electronically controlled DC motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with a large level meter.

An MX head is used for recording and re-recording.

Equipped with a 4-position tape selector with metal position.

Equipped with an input selector.

Equipped with Dolby NR circuit.

Equipped with 1-button timer standby mechanism.

It is equipped with functions such as rewind autoplay, queue & review and autostop.
