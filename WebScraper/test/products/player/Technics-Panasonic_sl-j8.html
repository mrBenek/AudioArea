Commentary

A record player with a built-in phono equalizer amplifier.

The built-in phono equalizer amplifier allows direct connection to devices with external input terminals for RCA pin plugs such as Aux.

Equipped with an autosize selector that automatically detects the record size.

The equalizer can be turned ON/OFF according to the system.
