  • SONY TC-FX33

  • ¥ 37,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

Cassette deck equipped with AMS and auto tape selector.

With the easy-to-operate logic control, the mechanism operates correctly and reliability is improved.
In addition, the operation buttons are fine feather touch operation.

Equipped with AMS (Automatic Music Sensor) function, you can easily start a song.

Equipped with an auto tape selector, the type of tape is automatically detected by the detection hole of the cassette half and the position is automatically switched. Also equipped with a manual selector when using TypeIII (Fe-Cr) or metal tape without detection hole.

SD head is used for the head.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism, and by using a timer sold separately, it is possible to record absence and play an alarm clock.

Equipped with peak level meter of -20dB to + 8 dB.

Equipped with Dolby NR / B type.

Equipped with Rec mute function with auto space.

Equipped with long stroke recording slide volume.

Equipped with a high-output headphone port.

Uses a soft eject mechanism.
