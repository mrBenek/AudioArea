Commentary

A record player from the HMV series.

The drive motor is equipped with a high-torque quartz DD motor, originally designed by JVC, and achieves 0.147N · m (1.5 kgf · cm).
In addition, the use of ± servo with quick response and φ 10 mm thick shaft makes the basics of analog playback unshakable.

The newly designed TH (Tracing Hold) type S-shaped tone arm is used for the tone arm to stabilize the tracing performance and pursue non-resonance.
This arm realizes both high sensitivity and high stability by utilizing ultra-precision machining technology such as micro bearing. Moreover, OFC (oxygen-free copper) is used for the internal lead wire, and it is directly connected to the signal cable to achieve both high sound quality and high stability.

The motor and the tone arm are mounted on the frame with a highly rigid 3.2 mm thick steel plate. This increases the coupling rigidity of both and realizes accurate tracing and unification of vibration mode.
In addition, the cork sandwich structure provides a large vibration margin.

A newly designed R-core transformer is used for the power transformer.
In addition to reducing vibration level and leakage flux, this transformer is also compact.

A universal head shell made of magnesium is adopted for the head shell, making cartridge replacement easy.
In addition, we aim to unify the arm and the shell by double pin fastening with the arm.

The turntable uses a 3.3 kg aluminum die-cast turntable.
The top surface has an extremely gentle conical taper, and the bottom surface has a shape that does not have parallel surfaces to improve the adhesion of the record board. We are pursuing the reduction of the standing wave of the turntable. In addition, the thick shape reduces the influence of harmful noise components generated by the high torque motor on the cartridge.

The cabinet is made of pure mahogany change over time due to resin impregnation.
The dust cover is also made of acrylic resin with high internal loss and has a resonance dispersion structure by changing the thickness of the top plate and side plate.

A special gel-enclosed foot is used for the leg part, making it a low-resonance structure resistant to howling.

Uses a gold plated pin plug.

There were weight for the heavyweight cartridge and record stabilizer as an option sold separately.
