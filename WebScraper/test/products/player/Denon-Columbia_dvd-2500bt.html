Commentary

A BD transport dedicated solely to the transport function.

It supports the bitstream output of Dolby TrueHD, Dolby Digital Plus, DTS-HD Master Audio, and DTS-HD High Resolution Audio, lossless audio formats for Blu-ray Disc.

1080/24p HDMI output is possible.
When combined with the 1080/24p compatible display, high-quality video playback is possible.

HDMI terminal (Ver1.3a) is mounted as an output terminal.

Equipped with high-definition I/P conversion and scaling functions up to 1080p.

The housing structure is the same as that of the DVD 3800 bd.
It is arranged in 3-block configuration, and by separating the video circuit and power supply circuit, signal degradation due to interference between each circuit is completely eliminated.
In addition, the structure of 2 layers of the top cover and 3 layers of the bottom chassis provides a thorough vibration isolation structure against external vibration.

The mechanism part is equipped with Denon's original mechanism developed for BD player.
The entire mechanism unit is covered with upper and lower shield steel plates to improve magnetic protection, dust protection and sound insulation. In addition, the S. V. H. (Suppress Vibration Hybrid) loader, a hybrid structure combining different materials, realizes stable disk rotation and improves disk reading accuracy.

PC format is also supported, so you can play MP3, WMA, and JPEG files written on CD-R/RW.

Supports video compression format DivX (ver6).

You can play MP3, WMA, and JPG files saved in SD card.

Wireless remote control is included.
