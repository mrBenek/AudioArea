  • PHILIPS GA212

  • ¥ 58,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A record player with electronic touch control.

The turntable and arm are suspended integrally with a spring to reduce the influence of howling.
In addition, wow flutter is minimized by servocontrol.

Needle pressure can be read directly from the scale of the arm.

Equipped with an inside four scan seller.
The elliptical needle and round needle can be individually adjusted.

The cartridge uses GP400 of the MM type cartridge.
