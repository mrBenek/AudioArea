Commentary

CD player equipped with super linear converter.

The D/A converter is equipped with an S. L. C (Super Linear Converter). The 0 cross distortion is eliminated by adding a correction signal from a conversion error correction circuit to the D/A converter. The D/A converter uses an IC manufactured by Bar Brown of the United States.
In addition, the double-D/A converter system eliminates the time difference between the left and right channels and prevents sound quality degradation.

The digital filter uses a 4 x oversampling digital filter, and the analog filter uses an LC-OFC analog filter.

It is equipped with a newly developed floating mechanism, and the optical pickup drive section floats double with a spring and low-resilience rubber with special viscoelasticity. Moreover, it is supported by a mechanism chassis molded from a material with excellent impact resistance, so that the influence of external vibration is completely eliminated.

Extruded aluminum is used for the front panel.

It is equipped with 2 types of digital output which are coaxial with optical output.

8 cm single CD is also supported.

Equipped with an auto-edit function that shows the length of the tape corresponding to the recorded disc.

There are two analog outputs, fixed and variable.
The volume of variable output can be adjusted by remote control.

Random playback and program playback of up to 20 songs are also available.

Equipped with various repeat functions.

It is equipped with functions such as index search and autospace.

Wireless remote control is included.
