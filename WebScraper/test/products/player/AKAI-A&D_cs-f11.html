  • AKAI CS-F11

  • ¥ 47,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

This is a cassette deck with advanced mechanism and technology.

A 2-motor mechanism with excellent durability is adopted for the mechanism part to realize stable tape travel.

Uses Sendust head for the head part.

Equipped with a digital bar meter.

Equipped with a Dolby NR system as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with MPX filter.

Equipped with timer standby mechanism, recording of absence and alarm clock playback are possible by combining the timer.

Equipped with a 3-position tape selector, compatible with metal tapes.

Equipped with a recording mute mechanism.

Equipped with mono-microphone mechanism.

Equipped with remote control terminal.
