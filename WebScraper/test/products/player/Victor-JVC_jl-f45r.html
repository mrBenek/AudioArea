Commentary

A full-auto player capable of both full-auto and manual operation without switching the switch.

The full-auto mechanism has a structure that minimizes operating noise. In addition, the arm is equipped with a unique safety mechanism that does not require excessive force even when manually operated.
In addition, it is equipped with a repeat device that allows you to choose between one to six repeat plays and permanent repeat plays.

A 12-pole 24-slot DC servo motor is used for the drive motor, and a torque of 700g ・ cm is obtained.
In addition, a turntable with a weight of 1.85 kg including rubber covering is used.

New Gimbal Support Arm is used for the tone arm.
In addition, Z1-S cartridges are used.

Equipped with a turntable rotation OFF mechanism convenient for cartridge replacement.

The cabinet comes with a steel bottom board with a special anti-vibration coating to prevent external vibration.

There are two types of exterior finishes. JL-F45R has a rosewood-like finish and JL-F45K has a Brilliant-wood finish.
Also, JL-F45M had a metallic finish.

Arm sub-weight was available as an option sold separately.
