Commentary

An 8-track stereo deck for recording and playback.

It can be connected to a stereo device for recording, or can be used for live recording using an optional microphone.

Equipped with an AC outlet auto-off device that turns off the power of the device connected to the main unit and the AC outlet auto-off device as soon as recording and playback are finished.

Equipped with an auto-eject device.

Recording is done manually.
