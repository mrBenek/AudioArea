  • Technics RS-686D(D-86)

  • ¥ 129,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

Portable stereo cassette deck that is portable.

It uses an anti-rolling mechanism that is strong against vibration and minimizes uneven rotation even during moving recording.

FG servo DC motor is used as the drive motor.

We have introduced a large number of stainless steel materials with excellent abrasion resistance in order to pursue precision of components.

The head section is composed of three heads : an erasing head, a recording / reproducing head, and a monaural monitor head.

Built-in head amplifier with a wide dynamic range for microphone recording.

The REC/PLAY buttons that are frequently used are arranged to improve operability.

Equipped with an end-eye to announce the end of the tape and a silent stop function.

Each level meter has its own peak indicator on the dial face.
The level meter can also be switched to check the meter light and battery.

It has a built-in limiter circuit to enable low-distortion recording in live performances.

Equipped with a low-cut filter that effectively eliminates the pops of on-microphone recording from the recorded signal.

Equipped with a 5 cm aperture monitor speaker with a maximum output of 200 mW, the volume can be adjusted with the speaker volume.

Equipped with a tape selector that can switch between two stages of bias (High/Low) and two stages of equalizer (70 μ sec/120 μ sec).
