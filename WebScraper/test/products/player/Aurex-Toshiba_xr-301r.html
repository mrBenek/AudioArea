Commentary

A CD player equipped with a 10-key remote control for sound quality and multi-functional rating.

Equipped with a digital filter.

Equipped with a digital output terminal.

It is equipped with functions such as 32-track random memory, repeat function of all songs / memory songs, and intro-scan memory.

Equipped with timer play mechanism, automatic playback is possible when the power is turned on.

Wireless remote control is included.
