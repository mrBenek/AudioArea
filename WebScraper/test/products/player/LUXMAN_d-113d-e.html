  • ALPINE/LUXMAN D-113D

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in June 1988)

Commentary

This model corresponds to the CD transport released as a player exclusively for digital output.
It has a digital output that is coaxial with the light.

It was compatible with 8 cm CD.

In order to protect the pickup from external vibration, a high rigid lock mechanism TypeII is adopted.
The pick-up base and the mechanism base are separated by a special rubber damper and coil spring, so that even if the mechanism base vibrates, it is not transmitted to the pick-up.
We have also adopted a newly developed optical pickup that uses a die-cast base.

The newly developed high-efficiency pulse transformer is used as the pulse transformer mounted for digital output.

A power line filter is installed to prevent pulse noise from mixing through the power line from other equipment.

By connecting to the separately sold AV surround controller (F-105), it is possible to control ALPINE/LUXMAN audio equipment with the system remote control with one F-105 remote control.

Built-in functions such as 16-track program memory, memory check, repeat performance, timer play, and disc mark which notifies the existence of the disc when the disc tray is closed.

A remote control (RD-113) with Direct 10 keys was included.
