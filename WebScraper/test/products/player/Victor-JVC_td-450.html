  • VICTOR/JVC TD-450

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

Tape deck with 3-head system.

A center floating type condenser phase-advancing induction motor is used for the motor.

Equipped with mechanical auto-stop.

The equalizer circuit uses an E-E feedback type IC.

Tape s erector is mounted to support SH tape.

Easy tape removal is possible by EL drive system.

3-head system allows simultaneous recording and monitoring.

It comes with a reclining stand that can be used by tilting and roller casters.

There was a dust cover sold separately.
