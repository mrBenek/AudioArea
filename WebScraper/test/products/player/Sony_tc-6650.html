  • SONY TC-6650

  • ¥ 118,000 (released in 1975)

Commentary

Compact tape deck composed of 3 motors and 3 heads.

By directly coupling the F & F head and the FET amplifier, the characteristics are improved.

By connecting the timer sold separately, it is possible to record absence.

Equipped with a scrape filter to prevent modulation noise.

It has a separate tape selector for bias and equalizer.

Equipped with equipped with a mechanical tension regulator.

It uses an escalation drive method.

Microphone and line mixing is possible.

Equipped with a constant voltage power supply circuit.

Equipped with line-out volume.

There was a dust protector sold separately.
