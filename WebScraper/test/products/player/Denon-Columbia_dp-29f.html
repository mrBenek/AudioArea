  • DENON DP-29F

  • ¥ 14,500 (released in March 2002)

Commentary

This is a full auto player with built-in phono equalizer that allows you to enjoy analog sound easily.

It has a built-in phono equalizer and can be directly connected to a mini component or pre-main amplifier without a phono jack.

Aluminum die-cast turntable is used for the turntable part.

A full auto system has been adopted for operation, so you can enjoy with simple operation.

The cartridge comes with a MM type cartridge.

Available in 2 color variations, black and silver.
