Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC SRP-B20

  • ¥ 32,000 (released in June 1968)

Commentary

Record player with solid state cartridge

An outer rotor 4-pole condenser motor is used for the drive system.

Uses a double idler system.

It is equipped with functions such as feather touch lead-in, idler shut-off system, Oatley turn, auto-cut, etc.

Walnut natural material is used for the cabinet.
