  • SONY TC-2300

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1971)

Commentary

This cassette deck was the top-of-the-line model at the time when the Oatley Berth Rerecording and closed loop dual capstan system were adopted.

Closed-loop dual capstan system is adopted.

You can select the Sony Limiter recording method that does not distort even for large inputs.

Equipped with a tape selector switch that can be used with the Sony Chromicassette.

Oatley Verse, Oatley Peet, auto-stop and reverse recording are available.

Equipped with mechanical shut-off mechanism.
