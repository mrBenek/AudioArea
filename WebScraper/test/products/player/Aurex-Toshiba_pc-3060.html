  • Aurex PC-3060

  • ¥ 52,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A stereo cassette deck with various functions.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism, and by connecting a timer sold separately, it is possible to record absence and play an alarm.
In this mechanism, since the pinch roller is separated from the capstan until the power is turned on, there is no excessive burden on the mechanism and no trouble such as deformation of the pinch roller.

Equipped with an autoplay mechanism. If you press the rewind and play lever simultaneously, playback will start automatically after the rewind is finished.

Includes queue and review capabilities.

It is equipped with a two step switching type tape selector for bias and equalizer.

Equipped with line and microphone input selectors.

L/R sliding type recording volume is adopted, and the level can be adjusted simultaneously or separately.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.
