Commentary

Compact 8-track player.

You only need to insert the tape to start the automatic play.

The motor is equipped with an AC hysteresis synchronous motor.

Manual eject is installed.

Equipped with 3-mode auto-eject mechanism.
With this function, you can select three modes : eject for each program, eject after finishing the fourth program, and endless play.

Manual program selection is also possible.

Equipped with a program indicator.

Equipped with a parallel head shift mechanism to stabilize the head.
