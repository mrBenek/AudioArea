Commentary

An improved cartridgeless stereo player system based on the best-selling PL-41.

The tone arm is equipped with a newly designed S-shape, magnetic inside force canceling mechanism, and lateral balancer.

There was also a player unit PU-41C with only mechanism without cabinet.
