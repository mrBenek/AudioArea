Commentary

A high-end record player designed for professional use as well.

The drive motor uses a capacitor advanced phase 4-pole hysteresis synchronous motor.
Since this motor rotates in synchronization with the power supply frequency, it is able to rotate stably without being affected by fluctuations in the power supply voltage supplied to ordinary homes.

The tone arm is simplified to reduce weight, and is designed to follow only the record groove.

MC type cartridge is used for the cartridge part.
This cartridge is a universal type with a large output voltage. It is generally a low-power MC type and achieves a high output of 5 mv without a step-up transformer.

An aluminum die-cast turntable is used for the turntable. The shape distortion is eliminated by ultra-precision processing, and the effect of magnetic induction is eliminated by using aluminum.

The player board is also made of aluminum die-cast, which eliminates motor rotational vibration and external vibration.

It uses an open cabinet, and the attached transparent plastic case prevents dust.
