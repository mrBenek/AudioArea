  • ONKYO C-702

  • ¥ 35,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

A CD player equipped with FPCS which is an original digital filter of Onkyo.

Uses FPCS (Fine Pulse Conversion System) for the digital filter, and realizes smooth waveform reproduction by applying computer image processing technology.

The D/A converter uses a single-bit method that reduces distortion at a very small level.
In addition, the Accupulse system with anti-vibration measures is adopted for the master clock part, which is the standard of operation.

It is equipped with a peak search function that searches for the recording level of the CD at high speed and repeatedly reproduces the peak part.
This feature allows you to easily adjust the optimal recording level for dubbing.

When you enter the tape size, it is equipped with a time edit function to display the recorded music of A side and B side.
It also has a function to wait in a pose after finishing dubbing on the A side.

It is equipped with various play modes such as 36-track random memory, shuffle play and 5-mode repeat.

Equipped with a digital output.

There are two analog outputs, fixed and variable.

Equipped with RI remote control terminal.

A power cord with polarity indication is used.
