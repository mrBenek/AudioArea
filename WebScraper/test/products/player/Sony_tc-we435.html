Commentary

Twin reverse cassette deck equipped with Dolby B/C NR.

It is twin reverse deck equipped with A deck for playback and B deck for recording / playback, and double speed dubbing is also possible.

The A deck is equipped with a wide range pitch control function that allows you to adjust the playback speed within ± 30%.

In combination with a Sony CD player, it is possible to record CD synchronously with the start of recording of the deck and the start of performance of the CD player by the instruction from the included remote control.
In combination with a Sony-manufactured CD player equipped with a control A1 (A1II) connector, a connection code (sold separately) can be used to record synchronized CDs programmed by the CD player.

Equipped with a sound auto-focus that controls the recording level setting with a microcomputer.

Equipped with the Dolby HX-PRO, which improves high-frequency characteristics during recording.

It is equipped with multi-AMS that can start up to 30 songs before and after.

Wireless remote control is included.
