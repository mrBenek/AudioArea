  • YAMAHA KX-R700

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

Auto-reverse deck equipped with Dolby B/C NR, Dolby HX Pro and dbx NR.

As a noise reduction system, dbx NR is also installed in addition to Dolby B/C NR.
Dbx NR compresses and expands the entire audio band, reducing high-frequency noise and greatly improving S/N in the mid-low range.

The Dolby HX-PRO is installed to improve the high-frequency characteristics during recording.
The Dolby HX-PRO automatically adjusts the bias amount according to the high-frequency level of the recording signal, enabling optimum tape recording regardless of the type of tape or the ON/OFF of noise reduction.

The bias frequency is 210 kHz high bias, which is twice the conventional 105 kHz.
As a result, the bias is sufficiently separated from the audio signal to reduce the beat distortion and intermodulation noise caused by the interference between the signals.
In addition, the recording amplifier uses a constant current drive method, enabling the head to be driven independently of the impedance change of the head due to the frequency of the signal being recorded, greatly improving current distortion.

An electronic volume with UP/DOWN keys is used to adjust the recording level. In addition, a digital dB indicator is also equipped to accurately read the set recording level.
It is also equipped with an auto fader that uses electronic volume.

Equipped with programmable timer and timer synchronous AC outlet.
The timer can be programmed either once a day (ONCE) or every day at the same time (EVERY). This timer is a 24-hour clock based on the power frequency synchronization method and functions as a clock in normal times. It also has a maintenance monitor function that displays the total duration of use of the head.

The recording / reproducing head uses an amorphous 12-sheet laminated head with low magnetic loss, and by increasing current efficiency, clear sound quality up to high frequencies is obtained.
The head winding uses LC-OFC (linear crystalline oxygen-free copper) with very low signal loss.

Equipped with a power eject mechanism using a dedicated motor, the cassette lid can be opened and closed smoothly.

You can skip up to 99 songs before and after by using 10 keys.

9 songs can be played in random program.

It is possible to perform an intro-scan playback in which the beginning of a song is played one after another for approximately 14 seconds.

If the unrecorded portion continues for more than 15 seconds, you can automatically send and play the next song.

Equipped with a linear counter with a tape length switching function, the running time can be displayed with high precision according to the length of the tape to be used, and the reproducing time can also be displayed when recording.

A headphone jack with volume for adjusting the line output level is included.

It has a sleep timer function that can set up to 2 hours every 15 minutes.

There are 2 color variations of titanium and black.

It comes with a 39-key remote control that can operate most of the deck.
