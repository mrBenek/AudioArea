  • DENON DMD-F10G

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1996)

Commentary

MD deck equipped with Λ S. L. C.

The D/A converter is equipped with Λ S. L. C (Lambda Super Linear Converter).

The A/D converter is equipped with a ∑ Δ type AD converter.

It is equipped with a title function, and the title name, etc. is displayed on the FL display with 14-digit characters in alphabet and numbers.

Equipped with MD editing functions such as divide, combine, move, erase, etc.

It is equipped with functions such as 30-track program memory, random play, and repeat all tracks / one track / between A and B.

Wireless remote control is included.
