  • McIntosh MCD7007

  • ¥ 390,000 (released in 1988)

Commentary

The third generation Macintosh CD player.

The mechanism uses an aluminum die-cast base.

The D/A converter is equipped with a real 16-bit D/A converter.

It comes with a wireless remote control and can be controlled centrally with the remote control of the C35V control amplifier.
