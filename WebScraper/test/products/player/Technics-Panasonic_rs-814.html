Audio Database

  • National RS-814

  • ¥ 39,900 (around 1974)

Commentary

An 8-track player with speaker connections.

An AC motor is used in the mechanism part, and it is designed to withstand continuous 3,000 hours of overuse.

Comes with a mixing volume that allows you to adjust the microphone volume freely.

By connecting four speakers, you can play four channels (speaker matrix).

It comes with a remote control box so you can to select programs from a remote location.
