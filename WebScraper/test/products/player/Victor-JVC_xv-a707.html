Commentary

A DVD player that supports DVD-Audio.

In addition to DVD-Video and DVD-Audio, DVD-RW can play a variety of discs, including DVD-RAM/-RW/-R discs. DVD-RW supports both video and VR modes.

To support DVD audio, the D/A converter has a 192 kHz / 24-bit DAC.
The XV-A707 supports 192 kHz / 24-bit 2-ch playback and 96 kHz / 24-bit multi-channel playback.

It is equipped with Victor's original digital direct progressive circuit and super high speed MPEG decoder.

The video DAC is equipped with an independently developed 12-bit / 108 mhz video DAC.
This DAC greatly reduces the aliasing noise that occurs with video signals, and realizes smoother, higher-definition images.

Wireless remote control is included.
