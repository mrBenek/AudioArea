Commentary

A CD transport equipped with a VRDS mechanism that employs a large diameter crimping tapered turntable.

The mechanism part uses a VRDS mechanism (Vibration-Free Rigid Disc-Clamping System) that uses Teac's unique large diameter crimping tapered turntable.
In the VRDS mechanism, the magnetic floating clamper head, which utilizes the repulsive force of the strong magnetic force of cobalt magnet, presses the disk against the turntable, which has a delicate angle so that the disk can be brought into close contact with it, thereby correcting the warpage and eccentricity of the disk and eliminating unnecessary vibration. In addition, by using the turntable and the pick-up with an extremely precise angle adjustment, it is possible to read the pit at the center of the optical axis at all times. This minimizes the occurrence of jitter caused by vibration and disk warpage, etc., and realizes accurate and stable original signal processing. In addition, by reading the pit more accurately, fluctuations in servo current are reduced, and noise generated by vibration of the lens and drive unit as a whole during servo is minimized.
In addition, the mechanism structure is such that the disk drive motor of the turntable integrated structure is fixed to the upper part of the aluminum die cast chassis, and vibration is eliminated by separating the pickup.

It features a twin-bottom chassis with a High Legh girder structure that is tightly integrated with a thick aluminum front panel and a large insulator and compact body to reduce the impact of external vibration and sound pressure.

The newly developed Hi-Cera base is used for the base of the drive part. This Hi-Cera base is a combination of metal and ceramic powder, and realizes high vibration control and high precision.
In addition, the entire drive is supported by a floating suspension to protect the optical system mechanism and drive section from internal resonance and external vibration.

A 3-beam pickup is used for the pickup section, and a newly developed linear motor is used for the pickup section motor.

Wireless remote control is included.
