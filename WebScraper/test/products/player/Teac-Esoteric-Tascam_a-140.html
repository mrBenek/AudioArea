  • ¥ 35,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

A stereo cassette deck that thoroughly pursues basic performance.

In order to maximize the performance of chrome tape, a tape selector is equipped to switch the bias and equalizer into two stages independently.

High hardness permalloy head is used for the head.

It is equipped with a queuing function, and you can queue by pressing the play button and pressing the fast forward or rewind button.

Equipped with an auto end stop, the operation button, head and pinch roller automatically return to the stop position when the tape finishes winding during recording and playback.

It is equipped with a level meter that can monitor the level up to approximately + 6 VU as a left and right independent type.

Equipped with a tape running indicator lamp.

Equipped with a pause button.

Equipped with a tape counter.

Equipped with a stereo headphone jack.

Equipped with a DIN terminal.
