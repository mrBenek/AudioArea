Commentary

This quick-reverse cassette deck is designed to improve sound quality by refining basic technology and thoroughly reviewing sound quality.

In order to improve the fluctuation of the frequency characteristics of the outward and return path caused by the slight gap between the tape and the head, the relative position of the head and the tape can be adjusted, and the rotation speed (axis) position of the head with respect to the tape is accurately maintained. By adopting a fine axis quick reverse mechanism, we have succeeded in always maintaining the same high-frequency characteristics.
In addition, a 2-point holding structure is adopted to minimize head tip vibration during rotation. In addition, an aluminum die-cast base is adopted at the heart of the mechanism to cut unnecessary vibration. This greatly improves running performance and vibration resistance.

The mechanism uses a twin belt drive system that uses two belts to provide a stable driving force.
In addition, by adopting a silent mechanism consisting of a two motor full logic and three motors for driving the mechanism, the tape reversal is judged at high speed by a microcomputer, and smooth head reversal has been achieved.

It is equipped with the Dolby HX PRO, which produces flat frequency characteristics by varying the bias amount according to the change of the music signal during recording. This reduces the level change and distortion of the high-frequency signal and realizes more natural sound.
In addition, Dolby B/C NR is also installed, and by using it together, more faithful recording and playback become possible.

A heavy solid base is used for the lower part of the body to reduce the vibration of the body.
In addition, the cassette itself is equipped with half-shell stabilizers to prevent vibration, and a thick aluminum cassette lid is used for the entire surface to ensure thorough earthquake-resistant structure.
The internal structure consists of a two box layout with separate amplifier and control sections, a remote bar with a short signal path to eliminate noise contamination, and a CD direct input terminal, all of which are designed with an emphasis on sound quality.

The head is equipped with a fine amorphous head with excellent high-frequency characteristics, excellent magnetic balance, and long life. The head winding uses PC-OCC (single-crystal high-purity oxygen-free copper material) for pure transmission.

It is equipped with functions such as 9-track jump selection, remaining time display, memory stop, blank skip, block repeat, timer start, bias adjustment, etc.

Wireless remote control is included.

In addition to the black model TD-R631, we also have the gun metallic finish TD-R631GM.
