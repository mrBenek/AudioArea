  • DENON DMD-1500

  • ¥ 85,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

MD deck equipped with sampling rate converter.

It has a sampling rate converter and supports sampling frequencies of 44.1 kHz, 32 kHz and 48 kHz.

The D/A converter is equipped with Λ S. L. C. (Lambda Super Linear Converter) which combines the S. L. C. and Λ (Lambda : Ladder-from Multiple Bias) system.
In S. L. C., 0 cross distortion is eliminated by adding original conversion error correction. In the Λ method, digital data is divided into two systems and shifted by applying digital bias in + and - directions near the 0 cross point to prevent 0 cross distortion in principle. By combining these two systems with a 20-bit 8 x oversampling digital filter, more natural sound quality is reproduced.

The A/D converter uses a 4th-order ∑ Δ - type AD converter with 64 times oversampling.

Equipped with a name input function, you can enter information such as title name, song name and artist name in alphabet (upper case and lower case), numbers, symbols and katakana. You can also manage data up to 100 characters per song.

Equipped with MD editing functions such as divide, combine, move, erase, etc.

The display section is equipped with a left and right segment type level meter that can be used to check the recording level.

It is equipped with a sound skip guard memory, and the sound skip caused by vibration is eliminated by storing the music signal for approximately 10 seconds at the start of playback, enabling seamless playback.

It is equipped with functions such as program playback of 25 songs, random play playback, and repeat playback of one song / all songs / between A and B.

There are 2 color variations, black and champagne gold.
Also, there was a side wood as an option sold separately.

Wireless remote control is included.
