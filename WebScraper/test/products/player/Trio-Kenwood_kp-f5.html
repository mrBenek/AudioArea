Commentary

A full auto player with a quartz PLL direct drive motor.

The motor uses a high-torque 6-coil 10-pole structure.
By suppressing the cogging phenomenon that occurs in conventional motors due to the core and slot, smooth rotational torque is realized.
A newly developed PLL IC is used for motor control, and the motor drive system is an AC-DC coupling to obtain excellent wow and flutter characteristics.

A high-synthesis tone arm has been adopted to improve various characteristics such as sensitivity and traceability.
