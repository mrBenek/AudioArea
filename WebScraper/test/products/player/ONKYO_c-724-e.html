  • ¥ 36,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

A CD player that analyzes the effects between digital and analog, and elaborates the circuit design through careful cut-and-try while confirming through trial listening.

The D/A converter employs a linear single-bit D/A converter that is strong in reproducing very small signals.
We have also selected a compact crystal oscillator that is strong against external vibration and is firmly fixed to the substrate.
The digital filter is equipped with an 8 x oversampling type.

Equipped with optical digital output for digital connection to MD deck, etc.

Peak search and time edit are possible with the attached remote control.
In addition, it can be operated with the A-924 remote control by the attached RI code.

It has 36 songs random memory function.

Uses a power cord with polarity display.
