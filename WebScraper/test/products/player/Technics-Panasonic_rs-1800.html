  • Technics RS-1800(U-76)

  • ¥ 950,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

A 2-track 76 cm tape deck developed with the aim of ultimate tape audio.

SX Sendust heads are used for recording and reproducing heads, and double-gap ferrite heads are used for erasing heads.

A SEPP circuit is used in the output stage of the independent amplifier section to ensure high linearity that allows faithful reproduction of open sound with a wide dynamic range. Moreover, a constant voltage power supply is used to prevent degradation of characteristics due to changes in the power supply voltage.

Uses auto-bias and auto-equalizer circuit.

Equipped with ± 9.9% quartz synthesizer pitch control.

It uses a digital computer display to display timer count, tape speed / speed deviation, and auto-locator memory.

Equipped with autoplay mechanism of light detection system.

Equipped with an autolocator.
