Commentary

This is a full-auto player with non-resonance and non-vibration technology and computer technology.

Uses a full auto mechanism with non-contact electronic control.
Equipped with an auto-sensor for record size, it detects the presence or absence and size of a record with light, and the microcomputer automatically selects the number of revolutions and lowers the arm to the specified position. In addition, the number of revolutions can be manually switched as it supports records with a special number of revolutions.
If you want to start playing from the middle of a record, you can move the arm freely with your hand.

The tone arm uses an electroservo straight arm.
In this arm, a linear motor is directly connected to the horizontal rotating shaft, and the microcomputer accurately controls full automatic operation. Therefore, there is no mechanical noise from the arm drive, and the sensitivity of the bearing is not impaired or resonance is not induced.
In addition, it is equipped with an electronic Q-dump system, and the linear motor has a function to suppress the peak of ultra-low frequency resonance (fo) generated in the horizontal direction by microcomputer control, and the anti-skating is also electronically controlled.

The cartridge is an MC type cartridge that uses the newly developed 4-magnet direct flux system.
This cartridge can be easily replaced by inserting or removing the clamp just like MM type.

The head shell is a plug-in type and other cartridges can be used.

A high-torque low-vibration coreless DD motor is used as the drive motor, and a double-servo quartz lock circuit is used as the servo circuit with improved reliability due to the use of IC.

Wood cabinet is used for the cabinet to reduce vibration and resonance.
The exterior is finished with a rose-like mirror finish.
