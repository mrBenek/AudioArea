  • Technics RS-620U

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A cassette deck with a Dolby NR circuit.

LH head is adopted for the head.

As a noise reduction system, it is equipped with a Dolby NR circuit that can reduce the hissing to approximately 1/3 in the middle and high frequency range.

It is equipped with separate volume for microphone input and line input, and mixing is possible.

Equipped with a tape selector that adjusts sensitivity in addition to switching bias and recording / playback equalizer.

It has an output volume.

The amplifier section is designed to make great use of a printed circuit board, and reliability is improved by embedding components directly into the board.
