  • DIATONE DP-77B

  • ¥ 34,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

A full auto record player equipped with an auto mechanism.
The size is selected according to the size of the disk, and all operations are done automatically just by pulling the lever.

The belt uses a polyurethane endless belt with precision polishing.
In addition, a low-vibration 4-pole synchronous motor has been adopted to obtain stable rotation.

The arm part adopts an S-shaped arm with an inside four scan cellar and a lateral balancer.

MM type cartridge is equipped as standard.
CD-4 playback is also possible simply by attaching a CD-4 replacement needle (MD-1) to this cartridge.
