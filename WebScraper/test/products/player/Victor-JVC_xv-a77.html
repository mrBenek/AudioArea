Commentary

A DVD player that supports DVD-Audio.

It supports DVD audio in addition to DVD video.
It also supports DVD-Video format discs recorded on DVD-R/-RW, VR (video recording) format discs recorded on DVD-RAM/-RW, CDDA, Video CD, and Super Video CD formats, as well as JPEG still images, MP3, and WMA recorded CD-R/-RW discs.
The D/A converter is equipped with a PEM-DD converter capable of handling 192kHz/24bit with less harmonic noise in the D/A converter for all 6 channels.
In addition, when playing music CDs, the audio signal is upsampled by a factor of 2 to achieve higher quality sound.

The video circuit is equipped with an ultra high-speed MPEG decoder equipped with digital direct progressive.
Digital Direct Progressive makes it possible to process images as they are without performing 2-3 pull-down by field processing by converting them to interlacing as in the conventional progressive method, so that higher quality images can be obtained.
In addition, the ultra-high-speed MPEG decoder is equipped with functions such as 4 : 4 : 4 chroma mapping to improve the information content of the luminance signal and a video fine processor (VFP) that enables delicate picture quality control according to software, realizing picture quality closer to that of film images.

The newly developed 8 x over-sampling type 12bit/108MHz image DAC is installed in the image DAC, which greatly reduces the aliasing noise that occurs with the image signal and realizes smoother high-definition video.

A graphical user navigator is included as a GUI function, and functions such as speaker configuration can be set on the on-screen screen.

Equipped with a disk resume function, the extracted disk can also be played from the interrupted scene.
This function has a memory capacity of 30 pages.

Wireless remote control is included.
