Commentary

This is a double cassette deck with a wide range of tape play and double reverse.

The W-760R can record and re-reverse only Deck II, and Deck I can record and reverse only.

TEAC's unique high-precision rebus technology realizes uniform and stable travel in both directions.

I → II and II → I deck It is equipped with the continuous reverse playback function and can play for a long time.
Repeat playback is possible up to 5 consecutive times.

Two modes (constant speed and double speed), dubbing speed can be selected.

Equipped with a sync reverse that can align the head of the B side even when dubbing tapes of different lengths.

Equipped with an auto-rec mute function that allows you to create approximately 4 seconds of silence.

Equipped with microphone input terminal.

It has a headphone jack.

Built-in CD sync function.
Synchronized recording can be performed by connecting to a CD player compatible with TEAC's CD sync with a sync cord.
