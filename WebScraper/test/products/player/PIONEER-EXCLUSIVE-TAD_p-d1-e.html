Audio Database

  • Pioneer P-D1

  • ¥ 198,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

The first CD player released by Pioneer.

Pioneer's precision technology has been applied to the three servos of focus, tracking and spindle.

Audio parts are used for the analog section after the D/A conversion section.

Functions such as random search, phrase specification, skip play, index scan, repeat play, program function and total key display are provided.

Uses a dedicated eject motor.
