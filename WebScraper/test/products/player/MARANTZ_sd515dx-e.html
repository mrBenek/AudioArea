  • Marantz SD515DX

  • ¥ 45,000 (released in 1995)

Commentary

Double reverse cassette deck compatible with W LEC & Quick Reverse.

Equipped with Twin Dolby HX PRO.

It is equipped with functions such as parallel recording and 2-speed synchronized dubbing.

It is equipped with QMS, continuous play and other playback functions.

There were 2 kinds of models, gold and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
