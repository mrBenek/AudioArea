  • Pioneer PL-6100A

  • ¥ 38,500 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A full auto player that uses a high-output MC cartridge, SH rotor system and coreless motor.

The cartridge uses a high-output MC cartridge and can be used in the MM position without using a dedicated transformer or head amplifier.

A straight arm using carbon graphite is adopted for the tone arm.

By adopting a coreless structure and SH rotor system for the drive motor, excellent characteristics are obtained, and load resistance characteristics are improved by DC servo.

The cabinet uses a coaxial suspension system to prevent howling.

Adopts a full auto mechanism.
