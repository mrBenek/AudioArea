Commentary

A tape deck capable of recording and rerecording 4 ch and 2 ch.

The motor is a center floating type condenser phase-advancing induction motor.

In-line 4-ch Cronios (hard permalloy) recording and re-head developed in-house is used for recording and re-head.

Equipped with a mechanical autostop that works from any mode.

Tape mounting is made easy by EL drive system.

Equipped with 2-stage switching type tape selector in order to utilize SH tape.

Equipped with 3-stage direct connection E-E feedback circuit.

Automatic tape cleaner, reel lock mechanism (with reel stand height adjustment screw), die-cast head cover, built-in casters are adopted.
