  • VICTOR/JVC 4ED-207

  • ¥ 35,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

An 8-track player with both 4-channel and 2-channel support.

Simply insert the cartridge to start automatic play. 2 ch and 4 ch can be switched automatically.
In addition, 4-ch tapes recorded by other recording / rerecording devices can be played back by manual switching.

Equipped with 4 ch and 2 ch indicators.

An AC hysteresis synchronous motor is used as the drive motor.

Equipped with 3-mode auto-eject mechanism.
This function allows you to choose between eject for each program, eject after finishing the fourth program, and endless play.

Manual ejection is also possible.

Equipped with automatic program switching mechanism.
It also includes a manual program selector and program indicator.

Head touch is improved by the parallel head shift mechanism.
