  • Pioneer CT-405

  • ¥ 49,500 (around 1979)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with PMS mechanism.

It is equipped with a PMS mechanism that can quickly find the music you want to listen to.
It also has a REC mute function that allows you to create the space between songs required for PMS operation.

A high-torque DC servo motor and twin belt system are used for the drive part.

The Dolby System is installed as a noise reduction system.

Timer standby mechanism is adopted, and absence recording is possible.

Equipped with a 3-stage tape selector of STD, CrO2, Fe-Cr.

You can queue and review.
