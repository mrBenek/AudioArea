Commentary

Player system with DP-75.

The Turntable DP-75, which is sold separately, is mounted on the turntable part.
This turntable has a dual structure and an external rotor type AC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

Uses an electronic brake.

Two types of arm pipes, straight and universal S-shaped, are attached to the tone arm part, and can be exchanged for a wide range of cartridges.
In addition, the straight arm comes with a lightweight cartridge counterweight.

Equipped with non-contact anti-skating mechanism and oil dump lifter.

Equipped with a helicoid type arm height adjustment mechanism.

The cabinet has a laminated structure of 90 mm thick particle board.
The exterior is mirror finished with Bubinga natural wood.
