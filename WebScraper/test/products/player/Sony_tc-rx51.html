  • SONY TC-RX51

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

Reverse deck with wireless remote control for CD sync recording.

The recording / reproducing head uses an amorphous alloy with excellent magnetic properties for the core material and an LC-OFC wound laser amorphous head that uses LC-OFC with low transmission loss for the head winding.

By adopting a super-bias that greatly increases the bias frequency, beat noise and intermodulation distortion are reduced, and resolution in the high-frequency range is improved.

It is equipped with a level meter by FL tube which displays real-time peak value.
In addition, it is equipped with a digital counter of FL tube display.

Equipped with a 2-position direction mode switch that allows you to select either single-side recording / playback or double-side recording / playback.

Continuous playback of 5 round trips is possible.

Equipped with Dolby NR / B-C type.

It has a queue review function.

Auto Play and Memory Stop / Play are available.

Equipped with Rec Mute function with auto space.

Soft eject is used.

Equipped with a microphone input terminal with an automatic line / microphone switching mechanism.

By connecting a timer (sold separately), it is possible to record your absence or play an alarm clock.

It has a headphone port.

In addition to allowing tape operation from the listening position, it comes with a remote control that allows wireless synchronous recording with a Sony remote-controlled CD player.
