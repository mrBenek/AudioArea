  • VICTOR/JVC KD-A3

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck for metal tape.

It is compatible with Create 303 series of VICTOR/JVC system components.

An SA (Sen-alloy) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.
The SA head has a bonded structure in which a Senalloy chip is bonded at high temperature to the tape sliding surface of a Permalloy 6-layer laminate core, and has many advantages such as core loss, gap accuracy, wear resistance, and corrosion resistance.

An SA (Sen Alloy) erase head is used for the erase head.

The amplifier section uses a metal tape with a wide dynamic range and a high linearity amplifier with a margin of 0 VU + 20 dB to take out the output without losing the characteristics of the SA head with a high maximum magnetic flux density.

Equipped with a 4-stage tape selector with metal tape position.

Equipped with ANRS.

Equipped with 5-point multi-peak indicator.

Equipped with a rec mute mechanism.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism and by connecting an optional timer, it is possible to record the absence and play the alarm clock.

There was a rack handle sold separately.
