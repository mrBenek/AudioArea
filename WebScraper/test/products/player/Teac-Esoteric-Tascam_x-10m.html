  • TEAC X-10M

  • ¥ 179,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

Two track master recorder.

A closed-loop dual-capstan system has been adopted for the running system, greatly reducing modulation noise and tape vibration, which cause sound turbidity and distortion.

High hardness permalloy head is used for the head.

The front panel uses a 5 mm thick panel that firmly holds heavy class motors and flywheels.

It has a queue mechanism.

Equipped with a motion sensing mechanism for smooth transition from quick winding to play.

It is equipped with a real-time pause function that makes it easy to rise from a recorded pause.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism.

Equipped with a BIAS/EQ independent switching function that takes advantage of tape characteristics.

Equipped with a pitch control function that operates for both recording and playback.

Equipped with a left-right linked recording mode switch.

Equipped with a timer standby mechanism.

NAB/BTS equalizer can be switched.

A separately sold dbx system can be connected and a separately sold remote control unit can be connected.
