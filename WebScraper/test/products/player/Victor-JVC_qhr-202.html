Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC QHR-202

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 4ch8-track player that can play cartridge-type 4-ch music tapes by connecting to a 4-ch playback system.

Playback of 2 ch tapes is also possible.

Equipped with manual program switching button.

Equipped with a program indicator.

Synchronous outer rotor motors and low-noise silicon transistors are used.

The level of each 4 ch channel can be adjusted.
