  • SANSUI SR-717

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1974)

Commentary

A record player with a direct drive system.

The drive motor is equipped with an independently developed 20-pole brushless DC servo motor.

Uses a self-position detection system that enables accurate rotation.

High-durability precision special alloy is used for the bearing part.

Knife edge method (vertical direction) is adopted for the arm support part, and one point support method is adopted for the horizontal direction.

A solid board is used for the cabinet.
