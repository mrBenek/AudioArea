  • Aurex SR-L70R

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

A full-auto player capable of synchronized recording.

Equipped with an autosize / autospeed selector that detects the size of the record board and sets the number of revolutions.

Arm with linear tracking system is adopted.

The motor is equipped with a quartz lock PLL coreless DD motor with excellent rotational accuracy.

Equipped with a 2-step speed manual search function that is convenient for starting a song.

Synchronized recording with the PC-G50R cassette deck can be performed simply by pressing the accompanying dubbing button.

Equipped with a microcomputer controlled function to select 7 random songs.

The dust cover was sold separately.
