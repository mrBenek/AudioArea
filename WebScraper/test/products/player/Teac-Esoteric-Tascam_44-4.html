  • TEAC 44-4

  • ¥ 400,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

4-channel multitrack recorder.

It uses an operation system that gives top priority to simultaneous sync recording.

Uses DC servo capstan motor.

Equipped with a function selector.

It has a queue mechanism.

Equipped with pitch control, tape speed can be varied within ± 6% range.

Ping-pong recording between tracks is possible.

Equipped with a tape counter with a memory stop.

Printed circuit board design is adopted.
