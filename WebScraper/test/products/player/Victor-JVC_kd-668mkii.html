  • VICTOR/JVC KD-668mkII

  • ¥ 62,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

Stereo cassette deck equipped with ID mechanism and ANRS.

ANRS is installed as a noise reduction system, and noise components such as tape hiss can be reduced by up to 10 dB.
Also, Dolby B tapes can be played.

The mechanism uses an ID mechanism that separates the reel drive and capstan drive.

It has a tape selector with independent switching of bias and equalizer.
A new time constant equalizer has been adopted for the chrome tape.

The recording and re-head uses a ferrite material narrow gap finish Super Chronios head.

Equipped with a peak level indicator, memory counter and rechargeable auto-stop mechanism.

The amplifier section uses a 2-stage direct-coupled CR type amplifier.

Linear slide type volume with independent input / output is adopted.

Input switching between microphone and line is possible.

The Head cover and holder are detachable.
