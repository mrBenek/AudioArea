Commentary

A quick reverse deck with a fine axis reverse mechanism.

The reverse mechanism employs a fine axis quick reverse mechanism in which the recording / reproducing / erasing head rotates in one unit, enabling switching with almost no sound interruption. In addition, the tape reversal detection by microcomputer control, and quiet quick reverse operation by 2-motor full logic + mechanical drive motor.

The amplifier circuit has a DC configuration without capacitors, which adversely affect sound quality.

It is equipped with Compulink-1 / sync terminal which can be linked with CD player and amplifier.

It is equipped with a music scan that can start one song before and after.

Equipped with auto REC mute.

Equipped with a timer recording / playback function. By connecting an optional timer, you can record your absence or play an alarm clock.

Equipped with Dolby B/C NR as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with an automatic tape select function that automatically detects the type of tape and matches characteristics.

Gold plated headphone terminal and power cord with polarity display are adopted.

It comes with a wireless remote control for recording and playback, changing the driving direction, and music scanning.
