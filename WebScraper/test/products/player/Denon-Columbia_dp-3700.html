  • DENON DP-3700

  • ¥ 80,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A direct drive player system using the DP 3000.

DP-3000 is mounted on the turntable part.

The tone arm is equipped with DA 305 which is a static balance S-shaped tone arm.

Equipped with an oil dump type arm lifter.
