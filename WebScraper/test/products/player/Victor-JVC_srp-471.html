  • VICTOR/JVC SRP-471

  • ¥ 32,400 (around 1966)

Commentary

A record player released as a top-of-the-line model.

The front panel can be removed and a pre-main amplifier (MCA 103 h) can be installed.

It uses a perfect pickup with long lasting records and needles.
In addition, a universal type is used for the cartridge part so that high-ranking officials can use it.

The cartridge is equipped with MM type cartridge.

An outer rotor type 6-pole condenser motor is used as the drive motor.

Uses a double idler system.

Feather touch and lead-in mechanism is used to prevent the record and needle tip from hurting. In addition, it is equipped with a reject mechanism that can be easily stopped by operating the lever.
It is also equipped with an auto-return system that automatically returns the pickup when the performance is finished, and an idler shut-off system.
