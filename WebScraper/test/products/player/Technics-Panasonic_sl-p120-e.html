Commentary

A simple CD player based on high-sound quality design using Technics CD player technology such as high-precision digital filter.

The combination of a 96th order digital filter with high calculation accuracy and a high-precision analog low-pass filter eliminates unnecessary noise components and realizes excellent audio characteristics.

Equipped with a preset editing function that enables efficient CD editing while monitoring the remaining amount of tape.
Set the recording time of the tape to be used and program the music you want to edit. Press the TIME MODE key in the middle to calculate the remaining time of the tape and display it.

A high-precision linear motor is adopted in the drive part of the traverse mechanism, and a servo system using a microcomputer realizes high-speed access with an average of 1.0 seconds until the start of a song.

It comes with a remote control with functions such as basic operation of play, stop and pose, search, skip, direct selection with 10 keys, program of 20 songs and volume level adjustment.

Equipped with a music matrix display, you can check the progress of performance at a glance.

Equipped with a time display, you can display the elapsed time and remaining time of all songs and playing songs in 4 modes.

Equipped with a high-precision single-beam laser pickup.

Vibration resistant design using large insulators

Equipped with functions such as 20-track random program, one track / all track / program repeat, 2-speed manual search, headphone volume, etc.
