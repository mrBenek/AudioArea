  • Technics RS-630U

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

This is a horizontally driven cassette deck that adopts a front operation system.

Equipped with a Dolby NR circuit as a noise reduction system, the hissing is greatly reduced.

In order to easily adjust the input level, we have adopted a 2-axis double-friction drive volume on the left and right.
It also has an output adjustment volume.

Equipped with a tape selector.

Equipped with a microphone and line input selector, it enables line recording and reproduction with excellent linearity and S / N ratio.

A horizontal sliding dust cover and double mirror are mounted on the front of the mechanism part.

Equipped with a large double-scale peak check meter.

There was a wood case sold separately.
