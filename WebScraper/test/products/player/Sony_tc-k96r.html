Commentary

Oatley this is a cassette deck with a removable wired remote control that can be used for re-recording and repeat playback of Verse Records.

The Oatley Verse function allows you to record continuously for a long period of time and repeat playback up to 3 times from A to B and B to A of the cassette.

The head adopts a rotobilateral head system in which the recording / reproducing head rotates 180 ° in accordance with the direction of travel of the tape, and recording / reproducing characteristics are uniform in both directions.

A microprocessor is used for logical control of the mechanism, and accuracy and high reliability are obtained by logically controlling 8 kinds of button operation.

The operation button part is detachable and can be used as a wired remote control.

It is a 2-motor configuration using a BSL green motor with a simple structure and smooth rotation for capstan and quick winding.

The head is equipped with an F & F head with excellent head gap accuracy, high frequency characteristics, and abrasion resistance.

Equipped with a 3-step switching tape selector with independent bias and equalizer.

Equipped with a 3-stage peak level indicator that is effective for adjusting the proper recording level.

Continuous recording is possible by using the program timer (sold separately).

Equipped with a memory stop / play mechanism that enables repeated playback of program source.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism that allows one touch silent recording.

It is equipped with a Dolby NR system that improves the high-frequency dynamic range and an MPX filter that cuts the pilot signal of FM broadcasting.

Follow-up recording is possible.

The line and microphone can be mixed.

Equipped with volume for both line-out and headphone.
