  • National RS-855U

  • ¥ 59,600 (around 1974)

Commentary

A 4-channel 8-track deck capable of playing 4-channel 8-track tapes and recording / replaying 2-channel 8-track tapes.

You can play 4-channel music tapes by connecting to a 4-channel stereo.

Equipped with a time counter that can be used for editing tapes.

Equipped with an auto-eject mechanism with a sleep function.

Equipped with a rotary selector that allows you to select the performance method.

Equipped with a pause / FF button that can be used for recording and playback.

Equipped with a digital program display lamp.

Equipped with a 4-channel display lamp.
