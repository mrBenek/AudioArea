  • MICRO MR-211

  • ¥ 31,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A stereo record player that supports 4 ch.

A four pole outer rotor motor equipped with a die-cast radiator is used as the drive system, and a 30 cm aluminum die-cast turntable is driven by a belt drive system.

Equipped with an arm rest function linked to the power switch.

Equipped with a spring type inside four scan seller.

Aluminum hairline finish is used for the motor board.
In addition, shock-absorbing rubber legs are used to suppress howling.

A low-capacity output code is adopted to support 4 ch.

The cartridge is equipped with M-2100/6.
