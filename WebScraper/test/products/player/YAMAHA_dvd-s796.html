Commentary

DVD player equipped with cinema position and cinema loudness cinema loudness functions.

It is equipped with an optical digital output terminal for DTS / Dolby digital, and you can enjoy 5.1 channel digital surround by combining it with an AV amplifier with a built-in DTS / Dolby digital recorder.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 96 kHz / 24-bit audio D/A converter.
The video D/A converter is equipped with a 27 MHz / 10-bit video D/A converter.

Component coaxial video output terminal is mounted.

Equipped with a cinema position.
This function realizes natural coloring and resolution as seen in a movie theater by effectively drawing out the moist texture of the image and details of the dark part.

Equipped with a Cinema Loudness function, you can easily listen to your lines at low volume such as at night.
This function is available for analog audio output only.

Wireless remote control is included.
