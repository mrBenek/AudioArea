  • SONY TC-TX9

  • ¥ 24,800 (released in 1996)

Commentary

This is a single reverse cassette deck with the same design as Sony's MD Pixy DHC-MD9/MD7.

In conjunction with MD Pixie, CD sync recording and timer recording are possible.

Horizontal loading mechanism is adopted for the mechanism.

Equipped with Dolby NR B/C type which can record with less noise.

Metal tape is also supported.

Equipped with FL display tube.

You can control playback and CD-synchronized recording with the wireless remote control included with DHC-MD9/MD7.
