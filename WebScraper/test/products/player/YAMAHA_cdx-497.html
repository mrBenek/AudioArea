Commentary

A CD player capable of playing MP3 and WMA.

In addition to CD-R/RW discs recorded with music CDs and CD recorders, MP3 and WMA files recorded on CD-R/RW discs can be played.

Equipped with an intelligent digital servo, it uses a microcomputer to monitor the signal and adjusts the spindle motor speed, pickup tracking and focus to maintain optimum conditions.

You can play 40 songs in the order of your choice, program play or random play, or A-B play, in which you can select one song, all songs, or between two songs stored on the disc and play them repeatedly.

Built-in functions include an auto display function that automatically turns off the display during playback, an auto space function that automatically inserts 4 seconds of free time between songs, a peak level search function that is convenient for setting the recording level during tape recording, and a synchronous recording function.

Equipped with headphone output.
