Audio Database

  • DENON DP-55M

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

A record player corresponding to the manual machine of DP-55L.

It has built-in bi-directional servo that quickly responds to both increasing and decreasing speed changes and smooth electronic brake.
An AC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

An S-shaped tone arm is used for the tone arm.
In addition, the use of 60 mm thick large arm board reduces howling.

The cabinet has a wood grain finish.
