  • SONY 6F-20 Solid State 10

  • ¥ 9,900 (around 1968)

Commentary

This is a two band portable radio developed as a younger model of Solid State 11 (TFM-110F).

FM and AM can be received.

Equipped with a dial light.

Equipped with an FM stereo connector, FM stereo playback is possible by connecting to a stereo adapter (sold separately).

Equipped with external power supply terminal, it can be used at outlet by using AC adapter sold separately.
