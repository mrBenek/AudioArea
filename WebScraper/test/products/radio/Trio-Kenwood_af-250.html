  • TRIO AF-250

  • ¥ 7,950 (around 1960)

Commentary

This is a 3-band high-fi radio that supports FM, Nakanami and shortwave.

In addition to FM and AM broadcasting, it supports shortwave broadcasting and terrestrial TV analog broadcasting (1-2ch).

It is designed to be small and light for home use.

The FM tuner section is equipped with an automatic stabilizing device (AFC), which automatically tunes to the FM broadcast frequency, allowing you to enjoy broadcasting in a stable state for a long time.

The external output terminal is mounted on the back, and high-fi speakers can be connected.

Crystal earphones are included.

It has an FM antenna.
