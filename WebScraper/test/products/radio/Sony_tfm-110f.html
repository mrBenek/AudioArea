  • SONY TFM-110F Solid State 11

  • ¥ 13,800 (around 1968)

Commentary

The portable radio became a best seller.

It can receive FM, Nakanami and short wave.

It is designed with high sensitivity and high selectivity using silicon transistors, ceramic filters, and triple chain IFT.

It is equipped with functions such as tuning meter, AFC switch and tone control.

By combining with the separately sold stereo adapter STA-110F, you can support FM stereo broadcast transmission.
It can also be used as a loudspeaker device along with a wireless microphone.
