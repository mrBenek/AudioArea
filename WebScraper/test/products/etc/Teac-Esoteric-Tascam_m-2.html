  • TEAC M-2

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A multi-channel mixer with compact mixing functions.

You can select 6-channel inputs, freely change their tone and image, and output 4-channel outputs.

It can be upgraded to a 12-channel mixer by cascading another M-2.

It is possible to combine with the separately sold 4-channel meter bridge MB-20.
Connected to the line output of the M-2, you can monitor the level with the VU meter and the peak level indicator (lit at + 10 dB). You can also use the BUSS/TAPE button to switch between the output of the M-2 and the input from the tape input, enabling overtab recording and mixdown from a multitrack recorder.
