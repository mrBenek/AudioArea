Commentary

Tone arm exclusively for light needle pressure developed by improving FR-24.

It uses a gyro-stat type rotating part that pursues high accuracy.

As an attached mechanism, it is equipped with a needle pressure application mechanism of 0.1g interval, inside four scan cellar, lateral balancer for trace stability by lowering the center of gravity, arm horizontal adjustment screw, lever type arm height lock mechanism, etc.
