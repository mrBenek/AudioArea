Commentary

AN MC type stereo cartridge that uses a diamond cantilever with an integrated needle tip and a figure-8 coil.

The cantilever is made of diamond, which is the hardest material on the earth, and the tip of the cantilever is used as the tip of the needle. We have adopted an integrated needle tip type diamond cantilever.
This greatly improves the fidelity of vibration transmission of the cantilever itself, and moreover, there is no deterioration of sound quality due to looseness and stiffness of the attachment of the needle tip.

In order to improve the small output of the MC-type cartridge, which is said to be a weak point of the cartridge, based on the idea of push-pull power generation that efficiently utilizes the movement of the armature, we have adopted an 8-shaped coil that reduces the generating power of a coil that moves in a magnetic field to two by twisting a conventional round coil once.
As a result, sufficient output is obtained without increasing the number of coil turns and without using an iron core.

In order to give perfect symmetry to the push-pull operation of the figure-8 coil, Permendur is used for the magnetic circuit.
This is a magnetic alloy with a high magnetic flux density, which prevents magnetic saturation of the yoke material and more perfectly cancels the strain by the push-pull operation.

In order to obtain the straight-line braking required for the damper, we examined it from the angle by returning to the origin, clarified the direction of motion and the fulcrum of motion, and adopted a special shape that eliminates the interaction between channels due to lateral motion.
This greatly improves the distortion and channel separation in the middle and high range.
