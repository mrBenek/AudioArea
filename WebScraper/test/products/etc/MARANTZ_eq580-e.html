Audio Database

  • Marantz EQ580

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1998)

Commentary

A graphic equalizer that integrates a 10-band spectrum analyzer and a 10-band independent left and right Gray.

The included microphone can be connected to correct the sound field accurately.

There are 2 color variations, gold and black.
