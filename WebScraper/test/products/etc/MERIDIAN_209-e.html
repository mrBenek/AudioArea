Audio Database

  • MERIDIAN 209

  • ¥ 30,000 (around 1988)

Commentary

Remote master control which can control the audio system "200 series" such as CD player 207 mkII, pre-amplifier 201, FM tuner 204 etc. from one unit.

You can switch 8 preamp inputs, select FM channels, operate a CD player including 10 keys, and control all functions such as volume control and absolute phase switching.
