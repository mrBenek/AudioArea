Commentary

This player cabinet was developed by reviewing its value as a case.

By stacking lauan materials to a thickness of 70 mm, resonance of the cabinet itself is suppressed.
In addition, we have adopted a newly developed insulator to stabilize the player. This insulator is adjustable in height.

Arm board replaceable type is adopted for replacement of the arm.
The arm board is 20 mm thick and is compatible with the tone arm up to 350 mm in total length.

It uses a 4 mm thick acrylic cover. This cover is equipped with a finger button that is convenient for opening and closing, and it can be removed with one touch if necessary.

The cabinet is finished with Tamo material.
