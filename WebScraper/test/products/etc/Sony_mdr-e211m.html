  • SONY MDR-E211M/MDR-E212M

  • ¥ 2,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

High-performance monaural ear receiver.

Samarium-cobalt magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and CCAW is used for the voice coil.

The cord is a neck chain system and uses OFC litz wire.

The MDR-E211M is an L-type super mini plug specification and the MDR-E212M is an L-type mini plug specification.
