  • TEAC AV-500

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

An AV controller equipped with a Dolby surround system and a surround dedicated power amplifier.

In addition to the main two channel sound, Dolby Surround simultaneously encodes and records the AND channel sound, which is lost in opposite phases, in addition to the main two channel sound during the production process of the movie. This creates a more realistic sound field space.
The AV 500 is equipped with a decoder for Dolby Surround, and Dolby Surround is available.

It has a built-in power amplifier for surround rear speakers.

The surround control function allows you to adjust the effect volume for controlling the delay time and the balance of the front and rear speakers.

Line-out terminals for center and rear are mounted.

In order to enjoy the surround effect with all kinds of software, it is equipped with a switch for changing between Dolby surround and matrix surround.

It has two VCR channels, one VCR channel, and two aux channels.

The fader function allows simultaneous fade-in / fade-out of sound and image.
It is also equipped with a mute function that can mute sound and image with one touch, and an enhancer function that makes the outline of the image clear.

Synthetic Stereo adds stereo realism and spaciousness to mono audio.

Equipped with an RF input / output terminal with a selector, 2 video decks can be connected to a TV with only antenna input, and selection can be made freely.
