Commentary

Carbon fiber semi-integrated type tone arm.

Strong and light carbon fiber helical wound taper pipe is adopted.
Carbon fiber, which has eight times the tensile strength compared to aluminum alloy, is crossed at an angle to obtain the highest strength, wound spirally, and processed into a taper pipe, making it resistant to bending and twisting and lighter.

The shell also uses lightweight, highly rigid carbon-fiber reinforced resin.

Uses a high-sensitivity design gimbal suspension bearing structure suitable for light needle pressure cartridges.
