  • SONY CTD-S100

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A text display unit that can display CD text in Chinese characters.

By connecting this unit to a CD player that supports CD text, you can display Katakana, Hiragana and Kanji in addition to alphanumeric characters.

CTD-S100 can be connected to MD Pixie DHC-MD99 or Tuner ST-S3000 to display text information of FM text multiplex broadcasting.
* It does not fully support Level 1 of FM character multiplex broadcasting. It will display 15 characters x2 lines.
