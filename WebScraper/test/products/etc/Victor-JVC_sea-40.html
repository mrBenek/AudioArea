  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-40

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

7-element graphic equalizer with independent left and right channels.

Equipped with an SEA character switch that can invert the ± characteristics of the SEA.
By using SEA to raise the high-frequency range for recording and pressing the SEA character switch during playback, it is possible to reduce the hissing without changing the program source's frequency-characteristics.

By switching the SEA REC switch, SEA compensation is possible both during recording and playback.

The illuminated function indicator in the center of the front panel shows the operation mode.
The SEA character display is turned off at the time of SEA defeat.

A semiconductor inductor circuit is used in the SEA resonance circuit to eliminate coils that tend to induce noise and distortion.
