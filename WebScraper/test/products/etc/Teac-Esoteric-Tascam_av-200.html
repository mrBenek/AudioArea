Commentary

AV controller with motor drive volume and wireless remote control.

Equipped with eight AV input / output terminals (four video decks, two video disks, and two auxes), up to a maximum of six simultaneous dubbing g red-brainers.

Equipped with a motor drive volume to achieve both sound quality and operability.
It is equipped with a muting switch that temporarily narrows down the voice.

It is equipped with a video gain volume that accurately matches the video impedance to all eight inputs and compensates for variations in the playback screen.

The input / output terminal of Aux2 is mounted on the front.

Gold-plated input / output terminals are mounted to improve transmission performance.

With a wireless remote control, you can control the selector switch, master volume and muting from your listening position.
