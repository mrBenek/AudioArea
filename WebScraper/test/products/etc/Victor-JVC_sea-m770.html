  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-M770

  • ¥ 49,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

An SEA graphic equalizer that can store 12 patterns and remotely control volume and power supply.

The 7-band audible band can be controlled within ± 12 dB.
In addition, the independent left and right design allows fine adjustment for each stereo channel.

There are 6 preprogrammed patterns that are most suitable for each music genre.
You can also store up to 6 types of original patterns adjusted according to your preference.

Equipped with a multi-functional remote controller, you can perform Gray co-control of 7 elements on left and right, 12 memorized patterns, volume and power ON/OFF in the listening position.

On the display, you can switch between the correction pattern co correction pattern and a spectrum analyzer that can check the input level of each range in real time. Gray
