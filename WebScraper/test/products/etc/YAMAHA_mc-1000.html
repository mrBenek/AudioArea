  • YAMAHA MC-1000

  • ¥ 52,000 (released in 1983)

Commentary

MC type cartridge with light weight and high rigidity.

The cantilever is made of a new material with a 35 μ m thick beryllium tapered pipe coated with diamond (0.5 to 1 μ m thick) to achieve high rigidity and weight reduction.

By adopting a 0.105 mg lightweight vibration system and LTD damper, the movement of the needle tip is linearly transmitted to the coil.

Cross power generation system is adopted.
