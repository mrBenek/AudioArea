Commentary

Quad riser amplifier for 4-channel conversion from 2-channel stereo system.

4-2-4 method, 2-4 matrix circuit クォドライザー is mounted.
It is also possible to adjust the individual level of four channels or the total volume of all channels to accommodate the circuit configuration of a 2-channel stereo system.

You can use the tape monitor switch of a stereo system or amplifier, or the pre-out / main in jacks for connection.

There is a built-in 50W power amplifier for the rear amplifier. This amplifier can be used independently as a sub amplifier.

4-channel effects such as Hall and Surround, and a Discrete position for playing 4-channel tapes.

There are two channel and four channel tape monitor switches with a built-in high-cut filter.
