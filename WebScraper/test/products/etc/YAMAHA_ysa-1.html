Commentary

Fully integrated straight arm adopted in GT-2000X.

To eliminate magnetic elements from signal paths, brass is used for signal paths such as arm pipes and pin jacks.

Non-magnetic, aluminum cut head shell is used.

The inside four scan cellar uses a thread suspension system.

By adopting OFC lead wire of 1.2 mm φ and ITK #3000, the reciprocating resistance value of 0.15 Ω is realized.

Yamaha GT series auto lifter YAL-1 can be mounted.
