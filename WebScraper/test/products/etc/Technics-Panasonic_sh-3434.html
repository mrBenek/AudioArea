  • Technics SH-3434

  • ¥ 62,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

In addition to the discrete AFD control function on the SH 3400, it also incorporates an AFD scope that allows you to visually check the 4 ch sound field. This is a full-scale control center for the 4 ch era.

It uses a unique AFD scope that allows you to visually confirm the sound field of the four channels. This allows you to make adjustments such as phase switching, volume, and front-back balance necessary for precise four channel playback.
It can be used not only for 4-channel sound fields, but also for waveform check of each channel and for oscilloscope for observation.

The discrete sound field can also be controlled freely.

Equipped with a phase selector switch.

It is equipped with recording and reterminal for 4 ch tape deck.
