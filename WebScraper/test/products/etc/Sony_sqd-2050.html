  • SONY SQD-2050

  • ¥ 21,800 (released in 1973)

Commentary

A 4-channel SQ decoder that supports all SQ, RM and discrete methods.

With five system push buttons, you can play all four channels.
In the SQ position, the built-in SQ logic decoder is used to extract 4-channel information, and in the R-MTX position, the built-in decoder conforming to the built-in RM method is used to play RM records in the best condition.
The 2-4 position allows you to play back stereo records with a 4-channel holetone effect, and the 2 ch position allows you to play back stereo records as they are on channel 2.
In the DISCRETE position, you can play 4 ch tape decks or discrete records by connecting a demodulator.

Uses a front back logic circuit.
The SQ method is originally superior in front left / right and rear left / right separation, and is theoretically infinite as in conventional two channel recording, but crosstalk occurs between the front center and the rear center.
To solve this problem, a front-back logic circuit has been employed to reduce crosstalk and achieve excellent separation and localization of SQ records.

A 4-channel master volume is installed.
By adjusting the level of each channel in advance, you can adjust the volume with one volume.

The back channel can be adjusted with the level adjustment volume on the back side, and a power amplifier can be connected directly.

Equipped with front back balance, front and back level balance can be easily adjusted.
