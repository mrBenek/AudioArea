  • Pioneer PD-AP1

  • ¥ 24,800 (around 1995)

Commentary

A CD controller that can connect up to three PD-F100 file CD players and can control up to 300 CDs.

File Type You can connect up to three CD-Player PD-F100 and control them together.
You can search for the initial letter of the pre-registered disc title with the jog dial and play it immediately by pressing the play key. You can find and play the CD you want to play from a maximum of 300 CDs.

Each disc can be titled with up to 10 letters of the alphabet, numbers and 12 special characters.
Input can be done simply by specifying characters with the jog dial and specifying the position with the cursor feed key. It is easy to input and can manage CDs collectively.

With 10 custom modes, you can register and manage discs for various purposes such as genre, instrument, artist, etc.
At the time of shipment, the custom modes of "CLASSIC", "JAZZ", "POPS", "ROCK", "DANCE", "COUNTRY", "NEW AGE", "P AND B", "OTHER1" and "OTHER2" are registered.
Random play and repeat play are available in each custom mode.

* The disc title entered in the remote controller attached to the PD-F100 cannot be sent to the PD-AP1. In addition, the PD-AP1 cannot control the PD-F51.
