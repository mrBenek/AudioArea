  • TEAC AV-100

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

AV controller equipped with DNR as a noise reduction system.

In dynamic noise reduction (DNR), a variable low-pass filter is controlled by the magnitude and frequency of the input signal, resulting in an NR effect of approximately 10 dB against high-frequency noise during playback.

It has two VCR channels, one VCR channel, and two aux channels.
Also, the Aux1 input can be simultaneously dubbed into five VCR units, and the other inputs can be simultaneously dubbed into four VCR units.

The fader function allows simultaneous fade-in / fade-out of sound and image.
It is also equipped with a mute function that can mute sound and image with one touch, and an enhancer function that makes the outline of the image clear.

Synthetic Stereo adds stereo realism and spaciousness to mono audio.

The Insert function allows you to record audio input from the Aux1 to your VCR.

Equipped with an RF input / output terminal with a selector, 2 video decks can be connected to a TV with only antenna input, and selection can be made freely.
