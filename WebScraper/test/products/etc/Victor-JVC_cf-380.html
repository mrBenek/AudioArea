Commentary

A crossover network developed for 3D systems with a center-channel subwoofer.

The low-pass filter has a continuously variable cut-off frequency and output level, and is applicable to any type of speaker.

The new FET input circuit, carefully selected elements and wide-band low-impedance power supply improve sound quality.
