Commentary

Dolby Pro Logic Processor with Yamaha DSP interface function.

The Dolby Pro Logic is equipped with a directional enhancement circuit that effectively adjusts the volume of the main and surround sound so that you can enjoy the surround sound of a Dolby movie at home. This directional enhancement circuit improves the separation between the main and surround sound to 25 to 40 dB, which was only about 3 dB for Dolby surround sound.
Yamaha's original digital delay LSI realizes an S/N ratio of 90 dB and a dynamic range of 100 dB.

Dolby Pro Logic can improve center orientation by adding one or two center speakers around the screen.
In addition, the Dolby Pro Logic outputs the same signal from the center speaker on the left and right channels, and outputs different signals from the speakers or surround speakers on the respective channels, so that only sounds that should be localized to the screen, such as speech, are output from the center speaker.

Equipped with an interface function to the Yamaha DSP system, it mixes the Dolby Pro Logic effect with the sound field of the Yamaha DSP, and controls the overall volume with the 7-channel VCA master volume.

Tape monitor switch and test mode switch are mounted.

Equipped with an LED level meter that can check the output level of each channel.

There are 2 color variations of titanium and black.
It also comes with a wireless remote control for each color.
