  • SANSUI SE-88

  • ¥ 59,400 (around 1989)

Commentary

A graphic equalizer with a detachable remote control on the front.

The band is divided into 14 bands and can be adjusted independently or simultaneously.
In addition, it is equipped with a real-time spectrum analyzer that displays the frequency component of the music source being played and also has a peak-hold function.

It is equipped with 5 memories of memory backup for about 5 years, and by storing the set equalizer curve in memory, it can be called up anytime with one touch.

It is equipped with a detachable remote control and can be operated from the listening position.
