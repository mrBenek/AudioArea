Commentary

Recording amplifier dedicated to stereo tape deck TC-263D.

Mixing recording is possible because the recording volume of the microphone input (magnetic cartridge input) and auxiliary input is possible.

Equipped with a VU meter.

The built-in RIAA equalizer allows recording directly from the magnetic cartridge.

In order to match the tape speed of 9.5cm/s and 19cm/s, a changeover switch of recording compensation characteristics is mounted.

It is equipped with a mode selection switch to enable monaural recording.

It is equipped with a safety button erroneous erasure.
