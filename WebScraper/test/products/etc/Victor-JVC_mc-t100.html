  • VICTOR/JVC MC-T100

  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

MC booster transformer developed for direct-coupled MC cartridges.

It is designed with an emphasis on sound quality such as left and right independent configuration, double floating, copper contact switch, non-magnetic metal case, etc.

Matches with MC type cartridges of other manufacturers.
