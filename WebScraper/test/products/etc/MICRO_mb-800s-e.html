  • MICRO MB-800S

  • ¥ 33,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

A turntable with a unique double voltage mechanism that starts at 100 V and rotates the motor at 80 V.

An 8-pole hysteresis synchronous motor is used for the motor.
A high-mass 32 cm zinc alloy turntable is used for the turntable.
