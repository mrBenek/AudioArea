  • YAMAHA MC-3

  • ¥ 37,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

MC cartridge with 0.145 mg lightweight vibration system.

The MC-3 employs a cross-power generation system in which the coil is replaced with an air-core type using a high-strength plastic cross-shaped core, thereby solving the problem of magnetic strain generated by the metal core.

Samarium-cobalt magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

The housing is molded with terminals made of high-strength plastic to reduce unwanted resonance.

The damper uses a unique stepped damper made of butyl rubber.
