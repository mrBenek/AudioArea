Audio Database

  • SONY MDR-CD6

  • ¥ 15,000 (around 1987)

Commentary

Promonitor stereo headphones that use an electron beam evaporated gold diaphragm.

CCAW voice coil and samarium-cobalt magnetic circuit are adopted.

LC-OFC litz wire is used as wiring material.

The ear pad uses an elliptical soft fit ear pad that fits perfectly to the shape of the ear.
In addition, it has a cross-point center-off mechanism with equal lateral pressure to the ear.

Uses a double-click slider.

One side cord type.
