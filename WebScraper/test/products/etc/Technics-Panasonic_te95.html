  • National TE95

  • ¥ 23,900 (around 1978)

Commentary

A full-week programmable audio timer.

High precision and functionality are realized by microcomputer and quartz.

ON → OFF can be set 4 times a week (timer type is 1 week type, 8 operations), up to 28 times at the same time every day.

Can be set in minutes.

Equipped with crystal oscillation circuit and temperature compensation circuit.

Second adjustment is possible.

Continuous ON/OFF is possible.

Equipped with a second counting function, you can check the recording time and remaining tape amount.
Since it is an independent circuit, it does not affect the current time.

Equipped with a sleep timer.

The set time can be recalled and confirmed with one touch operation.

Comes with a second blink display sign.

It has a power outage display function that flashes to let you know.

Equipped with a power indicator lamp that shows the power status.

There was a special power failure compensation unit TE95101 as an option sold separately.
By connecting the TE95101, the battery can be automatically switched to the built-in battery in case of power failure. In case of power failure up to about 3 hours, the time display disappears but the battery operates normally and the set time remains stored.
