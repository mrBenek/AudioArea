  • Marantz HP-1DB

  • ¥ 14,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

These surround sound headphones were developed to allow users to enjoy surround sound playback such as Dolby surround sound through headphones.

It is a 4-unit configuration with 2 main units and 2 subunits, so you can accurately reproduce the realistic sensation of Dolby Surround.
