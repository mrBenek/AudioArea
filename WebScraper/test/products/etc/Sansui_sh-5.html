  • SANSUI SH-5

  • ¥ 5,500 (around 1974)

Commentary

Open air type stereo headphones.

An ultra-thin 25 μ m thick polyester film is used for the diaphragm of the unit.
The bobbin uses a bobbinless voice coil made of copper coated aluminum wire (CCAW) to improve electrical conductivity.
Copper plating is applied to the magnetic circuit of the unit to improve the high frequency characteristics.

Foamed urethane is used for the ear pad.

Slide type position adjustment is adopted and can be freely adjusted.

Uses a 2m long cord.
