  • ¥ 15,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

MM cartridge developed under the concept of "Lomas, High Compliance".

A carbon-clad cantilever with carbon fiber clad aluminum pipe is used.
It also uses a cross-type magnetic circuit with a good balance of left and right channels and low loss.

By adopting a pole piece block mixed with carbon fiber, electrostatic noise is prevented and resonance of the cartridge is prevented.
