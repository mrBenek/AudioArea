  • Pioneer VS-100

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1985)

Commentary

Video selector for AV source.

Equipped with a recording selector that can select the VTR output separately from the function source, allowing mutual dubbing.

It is equipped with a simulated stereo so that you can enjoy a realistic sound even from a monaural source of TV or video.

Equipped with a DNR (Dynamic Noise Reduction) switch that cuts the hissing of the VTR tape to reproduce a clear reproduction sound.

It is equipped with an audio input terminal that enables recording of Hi-Fi audio on video tracks for Hi-Fi VTRs.
