Commentary

An electronic crossover network developed for two - or three way speakers.

Equipped with continuously variable level control with attenuation characteristics of 12cm/oct.

The input is unbalanced high impedance, and the output is unbalanced low impedance.

The crossover frequency can be freely selected by inserting an optional crossover card. There were five crossover frequencies and blank cards were available for other frequencies.
