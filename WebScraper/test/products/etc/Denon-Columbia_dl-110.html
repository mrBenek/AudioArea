Commentary

An easy-to-use MC cartridge that has been loved by many users.

The DL-110 was released in 1983 (1983) at a price that allows you to enjoy the sound of the MC cartridge easily. It has been loved by many users. Production of the DL-102 has been discontinued since then. In 1996, to commemorate the 35th anniversary of the birth of the MC cartridge, it was rereleased together with the DL -, and it is still on sale.

It has a high output design and can be directly connected to the Phono MM terminal.
