Audio Database

  • TEAC AX-300

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

Audio mixer with 4-channel input and 4-channel output

The input of 4 channels can be switched to the line using the switch.

All channel inputs are equipped with a 20/40dB 2-stage attenuator that prevents clipping.

Equipped with a low-cut filter.
