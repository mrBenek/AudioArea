  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1971)

Commentary

Belt drive turntable with DC servo motor.

A low-speed rotation DC servo motor is used.
Low speed reduces vibration and noise, and servo control prevents uneven rotation. Also, a polyurethane belt is used to drive the turntable.

A dedicated IC is used for the servo circuit to greatly improve reliability.

31 cm aluminum die-cast is adopted for the turntable, and oilless metal is used for the bearing so that no lubrication is required.

Strobe with large neon tubes is adopted.
