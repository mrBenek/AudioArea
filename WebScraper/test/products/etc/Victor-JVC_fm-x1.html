  • VICTOR/JVC FM-X1

  • ¥ 11,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

A compound type small indoor antenna exclusively for FM using a comb-shaped dipole antenna and a low-loss ferrite core.

It has a high selectivity design that is resistant to external noise and has a rotation angle scale that is easy to adjust.

AM loop antenna can also be mounted.

* The picture shows the AM loop antenna attached to the tuner.
