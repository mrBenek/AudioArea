  • ¥ 680,000 (around 1987)

Commentary

An automatic microphone mixer developed using JBL's original electronic technology.

Equipped with 4-channel microphone input, it can be expanded up to 24 channels in 4-channel unit by adding separately sold modular unit 7510-02.
