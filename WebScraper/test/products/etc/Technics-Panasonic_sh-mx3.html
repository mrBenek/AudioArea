  • Technics SH-MX3

  • ¥ 15,800 (released in October 1982)

Commentary

Compact 315 mm wide microphone mixing amplifier.

Equipped with 2 microphone terminals, and with electronic echo using BBD element, natural echo is obtained.
You can adjust the echo amount to your preference by operating the knob.

The tape terminal is mounted on the rear panel and the tape deck can be connected.

Equipped with a microphone mixing knob, you can adjust the mixing balance between the source and microphone.
