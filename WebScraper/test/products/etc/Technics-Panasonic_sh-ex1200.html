  • Technics SH-EX1200

  • ¥ 50,000 (around 2001)

Commentary

DMC official mixer with optical cross-fader.

Equipped with a newly developed optical cross-fader that maintains initial performance for a long period of time.
Equipped with a cross-fader reverse switch.

The fader controller using VCA circuit reduces the residual noise.

It uses a connector connection method that allows easy replacement of faders.
In addition, a spare fader is attached to the body (one channel)
There are 2 color variations of silver and black.
