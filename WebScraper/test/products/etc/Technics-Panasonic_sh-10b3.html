Commentary

Turntable base for SP-10mkII and SP-20.

With the triple structure of natural obsidian with large specific gravity and excellent vibration characteristics, viscoelastic material and wood base, excellent vibration isolation characteristics are obtained.

Uses a large insulator with improved vibration isolation characteristics.

Uses an acrylic integrally molded dust cover.

Plus arm can be mounted.
