Commentary

An enclosure designed by Sansui and jointly developed with JBL to maximize the power of JBL's speaker units.

The EC-11 has a structure in which the EC-10 is installed horizontally on the EC-10, and the driver / horn and tweeter for 2 - to 4-way are mounted.
For example, you can mount a combination of LE85 and radial horn 2345, 375 and HL89 or wood horn 2397, or a horn tweeter 075 or 077.

When using EC-10 and EC-11 in combination with 136A at the center, a 2-way system can be constituted by LE85 + horn lens HL92, and a 3-way system can be constituted by 375 + HL89 and 077.
In addition, by using sub-baffle plates 11-21 (sold separately), a sealed box for 24L Midbus 2121 can be made and a 4-way system can be assembled.

There was a net grill as an option sold separately.
In addition, a special baffle plate corresponding to the combination of speaker units was sold separately.

When mounting HL89, 2397 and 2345, the net grill cannot be mounted.
