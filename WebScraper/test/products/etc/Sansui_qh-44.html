  • SANSUI QH-44

  • ¥ 11,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

4-channel / 2-channel switchable headphones.

It is an open air type.

Ultra-thin 25 μ m polyester film is used for the vibration plate of the unit.
The voice coil is a bobbinless type made of CCAW (copper coated aluminum wire), and the high frequency characteristics are improved by copper plating on the magnetic circuit.

Uses a 4/2 channel switching switch.

With level adjustment (4-channel back).

Uses a 2.5m long cord.
