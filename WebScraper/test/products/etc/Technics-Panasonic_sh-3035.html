  • Technics SH-3035

  • ¥ 18,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

Mixing amplifier for microphone / synthesizer.

You can mix a stereo system with a microphone, guitar or synthesizer.

The echo volume affects two microphones simultaneously.

The microphone mixing knob allows you to adjust the amount of mixing between the source and microphone.

A tape deck terminal is mounted on the rear panel.
