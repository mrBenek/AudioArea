Commentary

Step-up transformer for MC cartridge.

Uses high rigidity, non-magnetic aluminum machined body.
In addition, the inside is a hollow structure with independent left and right channels to eliminate mutual interference.

Uses a large 78 permalloy core.

Compatible with 3 ~ 40 Ω cartridges.
