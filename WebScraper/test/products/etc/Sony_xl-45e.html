  • SONY XL-45E

  • ¥ 23,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

MM type cartridge with elliptical needle with emphasis on 2-ch follow-up performance

It uses a carbon-clad cantilever that combines and integrates excellent carbon fiber and aluminum as acoustic materials.

The pole piece part uses resin mixed with carbon fiber.

In order to improve the S / N ratio, a double shield case structure is adopted.

Cross type magnetic circuit with excellent left / right balance characteristics is adopted.
Also, rare-earth magnet is used for the magnet.

It uses a cup type damper with tension wire.
