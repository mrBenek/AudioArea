  • ¥ 9,800 (around 1972)

Commentary

This is a tone arm that uses Micro's original spring type inside four scan cellar after all the problems of the offset type tone arm have been cleaned.

The side pressure can be changed according to the needle pressure of the cartridge by the spring system inside four scan seller.

By using a hard gold plated connector and a 55pF/m low-capacity output cord, it is compatible with discrete 4 ch, etc.
