  • SONY XL-15

  • ¥ 11,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

MM type cartridge with cone type damper.

Uses resin mixed with carbon fiber.

The magnetic circuit uses a cross type magnetic circuit with excellent left-right balance characteristics.

A cantilever with a special thermosetting treatment is used for the hard aluminum alloy pipe.

Comes with a quick touch needle cover.
