Commentary

The Laboratory series Super Direct Coupled MC cartridge developed with VICTOR/JVC technology.

Micro power generation coil applying VLSI manufacturing technology is adopted.
The size of this coil is 0.9x1.8 mm square, and the thickness is only 25 μ m. It is even smaller than the conventional Victor Microcoil, and the power generation efficiency is improved by the double-sided structure.
In the MC-L1000, we have succeeded in connecting the micro mass coil directly to the top end of the diamond needle. The needle tip and the generator coil are directly connected. The coil moves in exactly the same way as the needle tip and literally converts vibrations into electrical signals directly. It generates high speed wide-band power without being affected by the cantilever or damper. In addition, since it has an air-core structure, there is no distortion caused by the magnetic core.

In order to take advantage of the super direct coupling system, it has been designed with emphasis on sound quality such as precision magnetic circuit of high magnetic material Semendur York(permendur york?) and duralumin machined body.
