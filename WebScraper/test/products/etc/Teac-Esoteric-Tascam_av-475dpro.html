Commentary

Surround processor with Dolby Pro Logic.

Dolby Surround performs surround playback by applying a 15-30 ms delay time to surround components (L/R 2-ch stereo signal and L-R phase difference signal). It is compatible with the Dolby Pro Logic system, which further enhances the realism of the normal surround system (Passive Dolby Surround) with the help of the directional enhancement circuit.

The Dolby Pro Logic drives five speakers with four output channels : stereo 2 ch (L/R), center (C) and surround (S).
Here, by the function of the directional enhancement circuit, various components contained in the stereo 2 ch signal are analyzed by the pro-logic circuit, and the L/R components, the S component which is the phase difference signal of L-R, and the C component which is the in-phase signal read by the high-precision level sensor are controlled. In other words, by strengthening the C relative to the L/R and emphasizing the level difference of the S relative to the L/C/R, stable center orientation and front-rear and left-right separation are ensured.

In Dolby Pro Logic, a decoder (Active Dolby Decoder) reads the strength and weakness of the in-phase components of the L and R signals. If the strength is high, the C component is output from the center channel as the C component. At the same time, by rejecting the L and R signals, the C component is positioned at the front center.
The center channel output can be selected from two modes, normal or phantom, depending on the system you are using and the source you want to see.

It is equipped with 3 surround modes which you can choose according to the software you want to play and the room.
With matrix surround, you can enjoy outdoor concerts, stadiums, BGV, etc. with a sense of scale that envelops you from the front, back, left and right sides. The rear speaker is delayed to reproduce the expansion.
Hall surround is designed to reproduce the sound of concert halls and live performances such as classical concerts. The rear speaker is delayed and echoed to reproduce the echo sound.
Synthetic surround gives a stereo sound effect to a source of mono sound, with the front speakers playing pseudo-stereo and the rear speakers playing with delay.

With built-in test tone circuit, the test tone is reproduced while moving sequentially from L → C → R → S, and various settings can be adjusted.

Equipped with a 5-channel surround indicator that allows you to check the current output level of each channel at a glance.

It is equipped with a digital delay terminal to which you can connect a digital sound effector AV-D50, and a surround amplifier connection terminal.

It has a built-in power amplifier for surround sound.

Equipped with a one touch selector, input calibration mechanism, master volume, surround on/off switch, delay continuous variable knob, etc.
