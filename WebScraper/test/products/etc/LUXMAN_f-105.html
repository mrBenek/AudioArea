  • ALPINE/LUXMAN F-105

  • ¥ 65,000 (released in October 1986)

Commentary

AV surround controller with surround function and system control function.

Equipped with 4-mode surround function, you can choose from Dolby Surround, Time Delay Surround, Matrix Surround and Simulated Stereo.
In the Time Delay Surround mode, the delay time can be changed in 5 msec steps within a range of 10 to 35 msec. The circuit is composed of BBD elements.

Equipped with one AV / video input Route 3, one monitor output and one dubbing output.
The video section has a built-in video buffer amplifier.

Power amplifier a power amplifier for the surround rear speaker.
An IC drive circuit is used for the circuit, a power transistor is used for the output stage, and the heat radiation effect is enhanced by the large fin.

System remote control is included.
With this remote control, you can set the mode selector for each surround, the overall volume, the volume of the rear speaker, and the delay time.
In addition, ALPINE/LUXMAN's Brid series is equipped with system remote control terminals, and total control is possible with the F-105 remote control.
