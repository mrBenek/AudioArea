Commentary

MM type cartridge developed as a cartridge with high cost performance.

There were 3 types, 0.6 mil round diamond needle M-2100/6, 0.5 mil round diamond needle M-2100/5, and 0.3x0.8 mil oval diamond needle M-2100/e.
