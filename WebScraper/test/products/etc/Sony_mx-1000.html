  • SONY MX-1000

  • ¥ 48,000 (released on December 1, 1980)

Commentary

Microphone mixing amplifier developed as a microphone amplifier compatible with TC-K777/TC-K555.

It consists of a two stage differential head amplifier with dual FET and a DC amplifier in all stages. All input channels are equipped with a pan-pot function.

Input 4 ch is Line/ATT/MIC, with 3-step switching of input.

Equipped with microphone clipping warning peak indicator (clip level -20dB or more).

1ch/2ch is equipped with 50Hz/150Hz, 6dB/oct low-cut filter.

Uses high-performance audio parts and gold-plated output terminals.

It uses through output which can directly take out Line input 3ch/4ch even when power is OFF.
