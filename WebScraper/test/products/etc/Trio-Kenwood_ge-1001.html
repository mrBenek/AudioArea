  • KENWOOD GE-1001

  • ¥ 38,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

This is a parametric equalizer of the K's series that allows fine sound quality correction.

The frequency, level, and Q curve can be adjusted in each of the high, middle, and low frequency bands.
The Q curve is a unit that represents the sharpness of the equalization curve. In the Wide direction, the equalization curve can vary widely, while in the Narrow direction, the equalization curve changes sharply.

Equipped with two input / output terminals, Line and Tape.

You can turn the equalizer ON/OFF with the KA 1001 g remote control.
