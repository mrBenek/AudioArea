  • AKAI DT-125

  • ¥ 11,200 (around 1982)

Commentary

An audio timer with built-in quartz.

The ON/OFF time set can be selected in increments of one minute.

Equipped with an auto-dimmer sensor, the display illumination is automatically adjusted according to the ambient brightness.

Equipped with a sleep timer mechanism that can be set arbitrarily in 1 minute increments from 1 minute to 1 hour and 59 minutes.
