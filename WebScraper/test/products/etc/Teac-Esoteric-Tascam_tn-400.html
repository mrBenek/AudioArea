Commentary

Direct drive type turntable equipped with magne-float system and electro-touch operation mechanism.

The TN 400 does not use mechanical switches such as microswitches or electromagnetic relays. Instead, it uses an operation method using a touch plate and transistor, which eliminates click noise.
Each button is a self-illuminated lamp.

A magne-float system is adopted to reduce the load on the bearing by utilizing the repulsive force of the magnet of the heavy turntable.
Weight : 2.4 kg Inertial mass : 325 kg ・ cm2Turntables have been adopted.
In addition, this turntable is strictly dynamic balanced for each one.

The motor is equipped with a 20-pole, 60-slot DC servo motor.
This motor is an electronic commutator motor that detects the position of the rotor by three position detectors attached to the stator with a sense of 120 ° and a position detection magnet that rotates together with the rotor, and rotates by switching the drive current of the motor electronically.
Electronic commutator motors have no brushes and are switched electronically, so there is no arcing and no electrical noise.

The voltage generated by the back electromotive force of the coil wound in parallel with the motor-driven coil is applied to the feedback circuit of the servo circuit, and the motor is controlled to maintain a constant rotational speed.

In addition to selecting highly efficient materials for the core material, a current of 8 times that of constant speed rotation is supplied at the start, and a large torque is obtained at the start, enabling quick and stable constant speed rotation.

The speed can be adjusted independently at 33 and 45 rpm.
The number of revolutions can be checked with a self-illuminated strobe mirror.

In order to obtain excellent regulation, it is equipped with a high-efficiency large cut core transformer and a stabilized power supply circuit, so that even if the power supply voltage is changed by 10%, the regulation of 0.5% fluctuation is obtained.
In addition, in order to prevent the deterioration of the SN ratio due to electromagnetic vibration of the power transformer, the power transformer is mounted in a shield case with a pitch fit. In addition, the entire transformer is mounted in a raised state with a double rubber cushion.

Equipped with a large level of 20 mm diameter.

There is a concentric groove of 10 mm and 15 mm in the center of the turntable for checking the overhang.
