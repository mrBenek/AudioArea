  • LUXMAN FL15

  • ¥ 19,500 (around the mid 1960 s)

Commentary

Channel divider developed for 2-channel multi-amplifier system and 3D system.

It is designed for a 2-channel multi-amplifier system that uses separate amplifiers for bass and mid-and-high sound. Four types of crossover points can be selected by the changeover switch.

It is also equipped with a connector for the 3D system in which the bass of both left and right channels is covered by a single speaker.
