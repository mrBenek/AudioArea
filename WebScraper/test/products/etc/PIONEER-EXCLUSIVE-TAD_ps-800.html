  • Pioneer PS-800

  • ¥ 23,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

The MM type cartridge adopts a evaporated beryllium cantilever.

A cantilever with beryllium vapor deposition processing using Pioneer's original technology is used.

The super permalloy-laminated power generation unit realizes wide-band reproduction.

One point support system with 7-strand strand wire suppresses harmful distortion and realizes clear reproduction.

Magnesium shell is included.
