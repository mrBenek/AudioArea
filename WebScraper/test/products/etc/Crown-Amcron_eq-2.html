  • AMCRON/CROWN EQ-2

  • ¥ 551,000 (around the late 1970 s)

Commentary

Graphic equalizer equipped with tone control.

The range from 20 Hz to 20 kHz can be divided into 11 bands in 1 octave steps, and each band can be boosted or cut by ± 15 dB.
When used in mono, it can be divided into 22 parts.
