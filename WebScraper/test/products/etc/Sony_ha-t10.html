Commentary

Step-up transformer for pin jack type MC type cartridge.

By making it an ultra-small pin jack type, it can be easily connected to the MM terminal of various amplifiers for use.

We have adopted a U-core and double-coil system to cope with sound quality and noise caused by external induction, which were problems with conventional small transformers.
The newly developed U-shaped core with double coils ensures sufficient low-frequency characteristics and high S/N ratio.

It is compatible with various MC type cartridges by designing it to fit 3 Ω ~ 40 Ω.

Although small in size, sufficient gain is secured by setting the boost ratio to approximately 20 times.
