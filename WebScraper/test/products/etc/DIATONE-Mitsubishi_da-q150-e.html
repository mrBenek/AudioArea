  • DIATONE DA-Q150

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1972)

Commentary

A 4 ch SE decoder developed for components and stereo sets that incorporates SE logic circuits in a 4 ch decoder.

Use the Tape Rec connector for connection, and input the output of the DA-Q150 to four source inputs.

The SE logic circuit eliminates crosstalk and improves resolution.

The decoder has built-in RM, SQ and QM-4 matrix and is adaptable enough to play 4 ch of most commercial records.
