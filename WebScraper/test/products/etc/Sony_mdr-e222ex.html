  • SONY MDR-E222EX

  • ¥ 2,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

This is a stereo ear receiver that uses the Sony acoustic turbo circuit system.

The unit is equipped with a 13.5 mm driver unit.

Gold-plated L-type plug is used for the plug, and OFC litz wire is used for the cord.

Uses a neck chain system.

Comes with a coat winding type compact case.

There are 2 color variations, black and white.
