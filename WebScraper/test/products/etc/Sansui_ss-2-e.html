Commentary

Stereo headphones with enhanced ability to reproduce deep bass sound.

By using a moving coil type with excellent transient characteristics and special cone paper with extremely high radiation efficiency, low-frequency reproduction capability is enhanced.

Balanced stereo playback is achieved by minimizing the difference between the left and right output sound pressure levels.

By adopting a special soft vinyl pad, it reduces discomfort and pressure peculiar to headphones, and fatigue when used for a long time.
Also, this ear pad is removable and can be washed with soapy water or alcohol.

You can adjust the length of the band.
