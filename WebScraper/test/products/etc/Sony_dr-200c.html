Audio Database

  • SONY DR-200C

  • ¥ 9,800 (around 1988)

Commentary

A headphone with microphone equipped with a drip-proof small high-sensitivity dynamic microphone.

Uses CCAW voice coil.

In addition to the flexible arm that can freely adjust the direction and position of the microphone, it is equipped with an up / down 120 degree rotation mechanism.
It also has a microphone muting switch that turns the microphone off when the arm is raised.

It is equipped with rubber head pad and windscreen.
