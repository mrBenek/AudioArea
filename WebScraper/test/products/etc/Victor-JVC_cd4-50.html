Commentary

A demodulator for CD-4 playback that improves sound quality by taking advantage of professional machine technology.

The separation can be finely adjusted according to the output voltage and phase characteristics of the cartridge.

4-ch master volume and Aux input terminal are mounted, and 2-ch system can be converted to 4-ch.
