  • VICTOR/JVC QTH-V7

  • ¥ 10,000 (around 1971)
    ¥ 11,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

High-end headphones with 2 channel and 4 channel playback.

The QTH-V7 is equipped with phase selection switches for the front and rear units to prevent the unnatural feeling of sound gathering in the center of the head.

The unit uses a dome-shaped diaphragm with non-directional radiation structure.
Tangential corrugation edge is used for the edge part to realize a sound with a good start up.

Uses artificial leather.
