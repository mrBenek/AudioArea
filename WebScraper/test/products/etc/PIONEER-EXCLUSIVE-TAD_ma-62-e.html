  • Pioneer MA-62

  • ¥ 48,500 (around 1975)

Commentary

Mixing amplifier equipped with 6 ch input.

There is a Route 6 input for six microphones. In addition, four line inputs and two phono inputs can be used depending on the switch. The microphone input for each channel is equipped with a 20-dB attenuator and a low-cut filter.
The CH-3 and CH-4 are equipped with a panpot function that allows you to move the sound image orientation freely. The other channels are equipped with L/L & R/R channel selection switches.

The Phono input is a monaural input. By combining two channels, you can mix a stereo pair.

It has a marking space where you can write letters and marks directly with a water-soluble felt-tip pen.

Equipped with a memory marker so that the set level position can be checked at any time.

Equipped with fade-in / fade-out function.

It has a headphone port for monitor and two output ports.

Equipped with large handle and vinyl dust cover.
