Commentary

The D/A converter with the latest digital technology applied to the No30L to further improve the sound quality.

It uses a 4-layer board with separate lines for the music signal, plus / minus power supply, and ground. It reduces the power supply impedance and also suppresses the generation of time axis distortion by improving the transient characteristics.

Custom-made Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillators (VCXOs) reduce jitters to less than 20 ps.

Full 20-bit D/A conversion is achieved using a 24-bit digital filter and a custom-made 20-bit dual D/A converter.

Equipped with two digital inputs via RCA terminal.

You can enter the source name from the front panel.

Upgrade from No30L is possible.
