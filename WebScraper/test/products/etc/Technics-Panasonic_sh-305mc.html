Commentary

MC step-up transformer with amorphous core.

We have developed and adopted amorphous magnetic alloys with high permeability and low magnetic loss.

The multi-spiral toroidal core and four shield structure realize a high S / N ratio and reduced inductive hum.

Equipped with 4 types of input impedance switching switches : Pass, Low, Middle and High.
