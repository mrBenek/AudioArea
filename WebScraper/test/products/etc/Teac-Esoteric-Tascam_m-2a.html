  • TEAC M-2A

  • ¥ 63,800 (around 1978)

Commentary

A 6-input 4-output audio mixer with a compact body and the essence of mixing functions.

In the picture, it is combined with MB-20 which is a dedicated meter bridge.

The M-2A can be used for recording with two or more microphones on a single track, for adjusting tone quality with an equalizer, or for echo processing by connecting an echo machine.

Equipped with a pan pot function, you can position the sound of each track to any position on the left or right.

Functions and applications can be expanded by adding MB-20 meter bridge or M-1 mix down unit.
