  • TEAC AP-300

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

A peak level meter that can be connected to a deck for accurate peak level display.

The AP 300 is a high-end model with a low-level input terminal and a look-up indicator, and was especially recommended for those using open reel decks.

High-speed response type with scale range of -40dB to + 5 db.

An electronic circuit with a response time of 10 ms catches even short pulse sounds, detects peak levels and drives the meter.
You can also use the low-level input terminal (Line input A) to monitor the state of noise such as hum and rumble.

You can monitor the music power by connecting it to the speaker output of the power amplifier.
