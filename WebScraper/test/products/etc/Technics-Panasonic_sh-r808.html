  • Technics SH-R808

  • ¥ 35,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

An infrared remote control system consisting of a receiver and transmitter.
In combination with Technics Space audio "The Space", you can do the main stereo operations right at hand.

In combination with "The Space"
Switching the input program and turning on the system power.
Channel switching of FM (8 stations) / AM (8 stations) / TV (10 stations)
Player start / stop, arm up / down
Deck operation (with RS-M45 : record, play, fast forward, rewind, stop, pause, silent record, with RS-M24 : silent record, pause)
Volume adjustment (up ⇔ down32 steps)
Muting
You can turn off the power.

The transmitter is compact in size and uses a soft-touch switch.
