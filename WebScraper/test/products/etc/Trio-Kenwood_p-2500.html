  • TRIO P-2500

Commentary

Phono motor supporting 4 speeds.

A 30 cm aluminum die-cast turntable is used for the turntable part.

The drive section uses a four pole synchronous motor with a capacitor advanced phase, and the bearing section uses special steel bearings polished with precision technology.

A new design has been adopted for the groove of the rubber sheet so that records of each size can be handled easily.
