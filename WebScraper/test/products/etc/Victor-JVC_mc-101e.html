  • ¥ 48,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

MC cartridge with high output achieved by 3-layer microcoil.

A multi-layer microcoil has been realized by developing the direct-coupled system technology. The MC-101E has a three layer structure but uses a microcoil with the same mass as that of a single-layer structure to realize higher output.
This 3-layer microcoil has a resonance suppression effect because the coil itself has an appropriate internal loss. In addition, since the output lead wire is printed by the same manufacturing method as the coil, reliability is increased and the mass is reduced, and the vibration mode of the coil is improved.
In addition, improvements in the coil pattern and magnetic circuit have reduced nonlinear distortion.
