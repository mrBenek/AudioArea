Commentary

Dolby unit which can freely combine 4 channels.

It consists of four units and four meters, and each channel can be combined freely.

We have adopted a double-wave rectification system that can obtain the same control voltage even for vertically asymmetric waveforms, and we have obtained the same performance as commercial systems.

Equipped with an input switch, FM reception and copying can be performed without changing the code.

The built-in B-type Dolby tone oscillator with alternating repetition of 400 Hz and 420 Hz makes identification and level setting much more convenient.

To prevent malfunction, a filter circuit is incorporated that rapidly attenuates frequencies above 20 kHz, which are unnecessary for recording and playing music, at an octave of 36 dB.

Equipped with a mode switch that can change encoding and decoding.

When combined with the AX-300, master recording of excellent quality is possible.
