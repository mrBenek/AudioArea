  • SONY SAU-450CC

  • ¥ 15,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

A closed caption decoder that allows you to enjoy English subtitles on CS programs such as LD, VHS and CATV.

Equipped with a subtitle transfer function, you can move the position of English subtitles to 3 places.

It has a function to stop subtitles, so you can stop only English subtitles and check the spelling.

It is equipped with an auto caption hold function, and when you pose LD or VHS, the English subtitles automatically stop.

You can adjust the brightness of English subtitles in 5 levels.

* English subtitles may not be played depending on the type of video deck.

Wireless remote control is included.
