  • Technics SV-50

  • ¥ 36,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

A component TV that can be connected to an amplifier and enjoy TV sound with the power of components.

The screen uses a black-and-white 5-inch CRT with an AVR circuit.

A picture ON/OFF switch that can turn the image OFF is installed for enjoying only sound.

There are 2 color variations, black and silver.
