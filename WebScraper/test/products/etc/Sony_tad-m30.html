Commentary

A compact Dolby digital processor with a Dolby digital decoder and 3 ch power amplifier.

It can be used in combination with the DVD player DVP-M30 and DHC-MD919/MD717 of the Pixy series.

The power amplifier section is equipped with power amplifiers for three channels. These circuits are equipped with a dynamic three amplifier system.
This amplifier can be used as front (L/R) + center when used alone or as rear (L/R) + center when used with the Pixie series or other audio systems.
When used alone, a virtual enhanced surround effect that virtually creates a rear speaker can be added, and surround playback can be enjoyed with three speakers.

In combination with MD Pixy DHC-MD919/MD717, you can use the TAD-M30 to automatically switch functions during DVD playback and to adjust the volume of the Pixy front speakers.

It has a built-in Dolby digital processor with 24-bit DSP and can be connected to the digital output of a DVD player to decode 5.1 ch Dolby digital.

When combined with Pixie series and other audio systems, you can enjoy surround effects such as Cinema Studio mode and virtual 3D mode.

Equipped with a base boost button.

It is equipped with 2 systems of digital input which is coaxial with optical system.

Wireless remote control is included.
