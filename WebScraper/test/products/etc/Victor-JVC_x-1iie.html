Commentary

The MM type cartridge is designed with a wide bandwidth.

The clear one point support structure of the vibration system by tapered pipe cantilever made of titanium material, center hold bushing, magnetic circuit of laminated core, etc. suppresses the disturbance of the mid-range response and realizes almost flat frequency characteristics up to the ultra-high frequency of 40 kHz.

There were 2 types of variations, X-1IIE for cartridge only and X-1IIE/D for shell mount type.
