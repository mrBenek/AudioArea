  • VICTOR/JVC HP-D550

  • ¥ 5,600 (around 1987)

Commentary

Digital pro-monitor headphones pursuing high input resistance, high efficiency, and wide-band reproduction.

Equipped with a dedicated driver unit with an aperture of 30 mm.

The shape of the headphones is sealed to eliminate the influence of external sound and to reduce sound leakage.

Uses a smooth slider adjustment mechanism with a click.
In addition, the head pad and ear pad move back and forth and left and right, so they fit ears more securely.

It is a 2-way specification that can be connected to a standard jack or a mini jack.
