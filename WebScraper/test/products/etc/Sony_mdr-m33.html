Audio Database

  • SONY MDR-M33

  • ¥ 5,500 (around 1987)

Commentary

Semi-open air type stereo headphones equipped with a 30 mm diameter driver unit.

Uses CCAW voice coil and samarium-cobalt magnet.

The cord is an OFC litz wire (one side cord).
In addition, since it uses a cone shield wire type, the cord is not visible and it has a smart design.

The 3-curve wide headband and ear pad are elliptical soft ear pads for a good holding feeling.
