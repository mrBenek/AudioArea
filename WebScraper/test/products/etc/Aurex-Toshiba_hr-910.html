  • Aurex HR-910

  • ¥ 19,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

This is an electret condenser headphone with independent configuration of the main body and adapter.

Uses back electret (back electrode electret) system.
In this method, since the diaphragm and the electret are separated, the efficiency of the electret itself can be increased. Moreover, by making the diaphragm thin, the vibration mass can be sufficiently reduced.
In the HR-910, a newly developed high-voltage electret (a special polymer film bonded to a metal plate) is electrically connected to the diaphragm through a high resistance, realizing stable operation of the diaphragm.

The diaphragm is made of polyester film with evaporated aluminum and has a diameter of 51 mm, a thickness of 2.5 microns and a mass of 7 mg.

The matching impedance of the Adapter part is a 2-stage switching type, and the output can be taken from the speaker terminal of the amplifier and can be set to a through state.

The ear pad is removable and comes in 2 types, ear pad type and ear cover type.
