  • SONY TA-100MX

  • ¥ 50,000 (released in 1983)

Commentary

Vocal amplifier with 2 types of sound effects.

Equipped with a record, tuner, tape and CD input terminal.

Microphone 1 is equipped with a high-tone / low-tone switch.

Equipped with BBD echo. In addition, depth control is possible.

It is equipped with a rhythm accent function that emphasizes the sharpness of the rhythm and makes it easy to take the trigger and tempo of the start of singing.

It is equipped with a space sound function to give the sound spread.
