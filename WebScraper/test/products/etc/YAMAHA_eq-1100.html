  • YAMAHA EQ-1100

  • ¥ 74,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

A graphic equalizer equipped with an automatic room EQ that can automatically adjust room acoustic characteristics.

It has a built-in pink noise generator and can measure indoor sound using the attached microphone.
In addition, it is equipped with an automatic room EQ that automatically adjusts the room acoustic characteristics of L and R to flat by computer control.

Up to four patterns of the adjusted original equalizer characteristics can be stored.

Built-in 3-mode preset EQ for vocals, fusion and disco.

2 db step, ± 12 db electronic volume, 10-band spectrum analyzer is adopted.

It comes with a full 18-key remote control that allows 10-band equalizer control, user memory recall, preset EQ selection, input switching and display switching.
