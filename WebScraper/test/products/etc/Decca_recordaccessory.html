  • Accessories for record players

　
  • DECCA Record Brush

    ¥ 3,500 (around 1975)
    ¥ 3,800 (around 1977)



    A record brush that uses one million artificial fine hairs (8/9 micron diameter) developed by DECCA.
    Since about 1000 hairs can be used for one record groove, it is possible to remove fine dust and dust which tend to be left behind until now.
    In addition, since the thin hair itself is conductive, static electricity can be minimized and unpleasant noise can be reduced.

  • DECCA Record Cleaner

    ¥ 4,300 (around 1975)
    ¥ 4,500 (around 1977)



    Arm-type record cleaner.
    As with the record brush, artificial fine hairs can be used to clean the record groove. The conductive brush and specially processed arm picks up static electricity charged on the record and drops it to earth.
    It can be installed and used in most manual type record players.
