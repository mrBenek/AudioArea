  • Technics SH-8055

  • ¥ 54,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

Stereo graphic equalizer with 12 independent elements on the left and right and equipped with spectrum analyzer.

It is equipped with a spectrum analyzer using a high-precision FL bar graph, and can display each frequency level and total level of 12 points.

With a built-in pink noise generator and microphone amplifier, you can easily correct the sound field by using a separately sold microphone for sound field measurement.

Equipped with a semiconductor bandpass filter.
