  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

MC type cartridge integrated with E aluminum die-cast shell.
There were walkers or rollators for the elderly LX-44 and BlackCar LX-44B.

Magnetic distortion is reduced by the 8 shape power generation coil.

A circular armature with no iron core reduces the weight of the vibration system and reduces strain.
