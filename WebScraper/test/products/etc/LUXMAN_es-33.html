  • LUXMAN ES-33

  • ¥ 50,000 (released in October 1999)

Commentary

A passive large-capacity clean energy filter that blocks the effects of power supply noise and electromagnetic waves.

The main body of the ES-33 consists of a choke transformer and a capacitor to eliminate various noise components including electromagnetic waves.
The inside is separated into two systems, so that mutual interference can be suppressed when connecting to both digital devices that generate high-frequency noise and analog devices that are susceptible to high-frequency noise.

There are 4 power outlets of 2 systems, and digital equipment system and analog equipment system can be separated.
The AC outlet uses a 3P hospital grade AC outlet with a thick pure copper plug contact to minimize contact resistance and improve sound quality.

LUXMAN JPP-10000 power cable is used for the power cable.

It is a large capacity specification of 1,200W.
* The total power consumption of connected devices should be 1,200W or less.
