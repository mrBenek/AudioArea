Commentary

MC cartridge with cross power generation system.

A cross power generation system with a cross-shaped vibration system is adopted.
In normal cartridges, the vibration system and electric system are integrated power generation system. Therefore, as long as the vibration system does not always move in the ideal range of 45 ° - 45 °, defects in the vibration system directly affect the output. However, in the cross power generation system, even if the movement of the vibration system becomes larger than the stereo cutting of 45 ° - 45 °, the output can always be accurate at 45 ° - 45 ° by compensating by reducing the electric system to less than 45 ° - 45 °.

The core material of the armature is high-purity Sendust with high saturation magnetic flux and minimal quantization noise.
