  • ¥ 26,500 (around 1972)

Commentary

This is a phono motor that uses the rotating parts of Pioneer record players PL-41D and PL-41W.

The motor uses an 8-pole hysteresis synchronous motor in which the rotation speed is reduced to 1/2 and the rotation ratio is reduced to reduce vibration and uneven rotation.
The turntable is made of 2.2 kg, 31 cm aluminum alloy die-cast to improve the processing accuracy of the pulley and reduce uneven rotation.

The suspension of the motor has a double structure of rubber and spring.

A polyurethane belt is used for the belt, and thickness unevenness is reduced to less than 5/1000mm by unique processing.

Comes with an arm mounting gauge for easy mounting of various tone arms with effective length of 195 ~ 365 mm.
