  • Technics SH-3060

  • ¥ 19,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

The Ambience controller expands and localizes the stereo sound field from the lateral direction to the oblique rear.

With the SH 3060, the playback sound field can be changed not only between the left and right speakers but also to the sound field of the whole room.
