  • Pioneer CU-AV200

  • ¥ 18,000 (Released in November 1989)

Commentary

AV programmable remote controller with built-in learning function equipped with LCD display.

Multiple remote control operations such as TV, VTR and audio equipment can be controlled in a single unit.
In addition to Pioneer products, we also have built-in TV and VCR commands of 10 leading AV equipment companies, and a learning function to memorize the operation methods of other companies' products.

The number of keys that can be learned is 427 (61 keys x7 mode).

It is equipped with a writable LDC display that allows you to confirm at a glance which key corresponds to which AV device.

It is equipped with a multi-command function that can store multiple one key.
