Commentary

Quartz lock servo DD turntable with advanced machine technology.

The drive motor uses the same DC brushless motor as the TT-81 type. Smooth and accurate rotation is achieved by the combination of a strong torque of 1kg-cm or more and a high-precision all-round integral detection FG servo system.
The phase comparator circuit uses a sample-and-hold type circuit with good response to improve the accuracy of the speed control signal.

350kg-cm in order to deal with short period rotation unevenness and instantaneous load fluctuation which cannot be covered by quartz lock.2Heavy duty turntable with inertial mass is adopted.

A touch sensor system is used for speed switching and stop.

Equipped with an electromagnetic quick brake mechanism.
