  • SANSUI AV-77

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

A full-featured audio and video processor.

It is equipped with 3 patterns of video art function which can make a fantastic screen.

Equipped with horizontal wipe and vertical wipe.

Equipped with an AV fader.

It has an enhancer.

Equipped with a Route 6 selector that can also connect a camera.

Equipped with dbx noise reduction system to expand dynamic range.

It is equipped with an audio insert function that allows you to add sound effects to tapes taken by the camera.

It is equipped with an RF converter that can be connected to a TV without a monitor input terminal.
