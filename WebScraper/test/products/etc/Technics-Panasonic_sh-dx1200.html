  • Technics SH-DX1200

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 2001)

Commentary

An audio mixer certified by "World DJ Championship" sponsored by DMC.

Uses a new 45 mm stroke version tough & long life fader with dustproof structure.

Sound leakage between the A-B channels has been reduced by more than 10 dB.

You can select hip-hop and techno fade curve characteristics by switching the switch.

It uses a connector connection method that allows easy replacement of faders.
There is also a spare fader (one cross and one channel) inside the unit.
