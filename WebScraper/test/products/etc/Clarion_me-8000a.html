  • Clarion ME-8000A

  • ¥ 32,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

Dolby noise reduction unit.

By connecting to a deck without Dolby, you can record and play back with Dolby NR.

Two independent circuits for recording and playback are built in, enabling simultaneous Dolby recording and playback.
For level adjustment, recording / playback / CAL Tone can be switched with one touch.

Built-in 400 Hz reference signal oscillator.
In addition, calibration tape necessary for adjustment is included.

An IC is used for the circuit.
