  • Technics SH-8045

  • ¥ 26,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

Stereo graphic equalizer with 12 independent left and right sections.

Continuous control within ± 12 dB is possible at 12 center frequencies for each channel.
For more subtle control, the variable range switch allows the variation to be selected to ± 3 dB.

Low distortion and high SN are achieved by band-pass filter using semiconductor inductor.

Equipped with an equalizer position switch that enables equalization during tape recording.

Equipped with an equalizer on/off switch to easily check the equalization effect.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch.
