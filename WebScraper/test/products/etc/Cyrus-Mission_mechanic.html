Commentary

A tone arm born from research and development unique to the mission.

From the mounting hardware of the main body balance weights to the structure of the fixed head shell, it is made by precision Uchide processing from single billets of pure aluminum alloys.

The bearings are made of ceramics equivalent to those used in high-end aircraft and spacecraft.
In addition, it is extremely finely tuned in the manufacturing process to withstand long-term continuous use.
