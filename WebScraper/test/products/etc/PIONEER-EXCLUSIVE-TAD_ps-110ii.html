  • Pioneer PS-110/II

  • ¥ 8,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

MM type cartridge with aluminum light alloy pipe cantilever.

The vibration system consists of a light alloy pipe cantilever and a special diamond.
Furthermore, in order to prevent resonance, the stylus tip and the press-fit fixed magnet are arranged in a straight line to realize reproduction with less distortion.

It is designed to adjust the damping amount of the vibration system to improve the high-frequency characteristics.

Aluminum shell is included.
