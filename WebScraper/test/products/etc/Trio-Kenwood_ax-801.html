Commentary

A system selector that can easily switch between 135 combinations.

Three amplifiers, three players, three tape decks, and five pairs of speakers (10 pcs) can be connected.

All critical connection points can be switched between positive and negative, and are designed so that components do not interfere with each other and do not induce hum or buzzing noise when all terminals are fully used.

The timing and type of switches are examined to prevent shock noise during switching.

It is devised so that the signal does not flow in parallel to the two systems even if the two switches are pressed simultaneously, and safe switching is possible without overload.

Pilot lights for speakers A pilot light change-over switch is incorporated.
When a power supply of approximately 10 v is applied externally, it is possible to determine at a glance which speaker is being driven.

Equipped with left and right independent terminals for VU meter connection, the output level of each amplifier can be level of each amplifier.

DIN terminal for recording is mounted.

The MIC terminal is mounted on the front side and the output terminal is mounted on the top side. You can use the microphone freely by connecting it to the MIC terminal of the amplifier.

A blank sheet has been inserted over the switch on the front panel so that you can write the model name of the model you are using.
White paper can be replaced by removing one side plate.

The width is designed according to the ATC series.
