  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-50B

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

2-channel interlocking 10-element SEA graphic equalizer using semiconductor inductor circuit.

The SEA is composed of 10 elements at intervals of one octave, and the center frequency of the highest range can be changed from 12k/16k/20kHz.

Two tape circuits are mounted, and one of them has an input / output terminal on the front panel to improve operability.
It is also possible to record SEA and copy from Tape-1 to Tape-2 only by switching the front panel switch.

Equipped with SEA defeat switch.

There was a special rack handle sold separately.
