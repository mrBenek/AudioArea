Commentary

Noise reduction unit equipped with dbx system.

The dbx system records music signals compressed by half and expands them twice during playback, so that even music signals with a dynamic range of 110 dB can be recorded on cassette tapes with a dynamic range of 55 dB.
The dbx system provides a noise reduction of more than 30 dB over the entire audio band and more faithful recording at a recording saturation level of + 20 dB.

We have solved the problem of cassette tapes in which the higher the input level, the lower the high-frequency characteristics.

It is capable of simultaneous recording and monitoring as possible with a 3-head deck, and is equipped with dbx disc position.

There are 2 variations, silver and black.
