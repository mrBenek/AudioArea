  • Marantz AD-5

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Two way multi-channel electronic crossover network.

The LPF and HPF sides are independent of the cutoff frequency.
Plus, you can choose from 11 points : 70 Hz, 90 Hz, 180 Hz, 300 Hz, 500 Hz, 800 Hz, 1.2 kHz, 3.2 kHz, 5 kHz, 7 kHz and 10 kHz.

The slope selector is a 2-point slope selector that allows you to select either 6dB/oct. or 12dB/oct. In addition, the LPF and HPF are independent knobs.

The level control uses high-precision conductive plastic volume with less error with the panel display. In addition, the L and R independent double-row type allows you to adjust the subtle volume of the left and right speakers.

By setting the panel switch to INTERNAL, the HPF and LPF are connected in series and can operate as a band-pass filter. It can be upgraded to 3 way or 4 way by adding AD-5.

Equipped with a center channel for sub-woofers, cutoff frequencies of 70 Hz, 100 Hz and 150 Hz, and slope characteristics of 24dB/oct.
