Commentary

A D/A converter developed using Victor's K2 technology.

It uses 20-bit K2 processing to convert 16-bit music data recorded on CDs into 20-bit data.
In addition to the advanced signal processing technology, the Lugorhythm has been polished with the listening sense of professionals such as engineers and directors of the music software division to realize the quality which is close to studio master.

It is equipped with a left-right independent dual D/A converter capable of 96 khz sampling of DVD-Video for the first time as a consumer gray fabric.
In addition, we aim to reproduce a purer music signal by adopting a K2 interface and a high-speed balanced transmission circuit that eliminate out-of-code components such as jitter and ripple added to digital waveforms.

Extended K2 processing is included.
This technology analyzes the input digital signal are analyzed and expanded to 24 bits based on the analyzed digital signals. At the same time, music signals before being subjected to bandwidth limitation by the sampling frequency are newly expanded to twice the sampling frequency and reproduced.
As a result, natural harmonic components close to the original music signal are generated, and changes in the tone and presence of instruments due to differences in players are restored.

The 5-power transformer 27 independent power supply system and 5-block structure eliminate adverse effects caused by mutual interference.

It also functions as a digital selector because it is equipped with various digital input / output terminals and matrix control of all connected devices is possible.

A remote control is included.
