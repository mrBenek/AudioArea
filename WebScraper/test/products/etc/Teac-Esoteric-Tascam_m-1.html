Audio Database

  • TEAC M-1

  • ¥ 38,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

8-input 2-output line-level mixer for various applications.

It is used as a mixdown unit for producing two channel master tapes from a multi-channel multi-recorder.
It can also be used as a sub-mixer for an audio mixer, as an echo feed, as a cue feed to a player, or as a monitor mix.

Comes with an adapter that can be mounted on a 19-inch standard rack.
