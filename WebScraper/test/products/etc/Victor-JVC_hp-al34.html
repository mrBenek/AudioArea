  • VICTOR/JVC HP-AL34

  • Discretionary price (around ¥ 2,000, released in February 2005)

Commentary

Armless mini headphones with improved comfort and sound quality when worn.

New shapes have been adopted for ear hooks and protectors to improve comfort when worn.

By adopting the Neodymium magnet for the driver unit, the compact body ensures comfortable sound.

New resilient material is used for the cord, making it hard to get tangled.

Uses a 0.5m short cord that is convenient for connecting to a portable audio remote controller.

There are 3 color variations of blue, black and pink.
