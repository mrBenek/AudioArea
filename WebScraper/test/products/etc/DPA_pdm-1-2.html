  • DPA PDM-1 Series2

  • Price unknown (around the early 1990 s)

Commentary

D/A converter announced as an upgraded model of PDM-1.

The input format is SONY/Philips Biphase Format TYPE1 Standard.

The chip is a bit stream type 7350 chip.
Fully differential balanced mode with a sampling rate of 384 x.

The digital filter uses an 8-times oversampling 18-bit filter.

For the analog circuit, OH-OA32 which is Hybrid OP-Amp of Dell Tech is adopted.

There is an LED indicator on the front panel that shows power on/off and digital lock.

The power supply consists of two power supplies with independent regulators for digital and analog power supplies.

Dark green aluminum die-cast case is used for the case.

Gold-plated terminals are used for input / output terminals.
