  • TEAC M-09

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

This 4-channel audio mixer was developed using TEAC's mixer development technology cultivated in the TASCAM series.

It is designed with an emphasis on sound quality so that it can be used for future applications.
Equipped with 4 Cue out in addition to 4in-2out.

Equipped with a VU meter.

Equipped with a parametric equalizer.

4-channel multi-recording by connecting to 22-4 or 2-channel mixdown by connecting to 22-2.
