  • SONY TB-2000

  • ¥ 58,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

A turntable base developed as a cabinet utilizing TTS-8000 and TTS-6000.
There were TB-1000 for single arm and TB-2000 for twin arm.

It has a two layer structure separated into a first cabinet and a second cabinet, and a high viscoelastic gel-like insulator is inserted between the upper and lower cabinets to further insulate the entire structure.
This increases the howling margin and allows for more faithful record playback.

Artificial marble has been adopted for the 1st cabinet to keep the resonance sharpness of the whole cabinet low.

10.0m/m arm base of m / m thickness.

Comes with an acrylic dust cover.
