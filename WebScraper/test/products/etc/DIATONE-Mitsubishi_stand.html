  • DK-1

    ¥ 19,000 (a set of two cars, around 1979)



    Stand with adjustable width and height according to the speaker.
    You can adjust the width by sliding it left or right according to the size of the speaker.
    In addition, since the legs are composed of two wooden cylinders connected vertically with screws, the height can be adjusted in two stages by attaching or removing the lower cylinder.
    The combination of a wooden cylinder and a die-cast base reduces resonance, and a special rubber cushion is placed at the top of the base to securely hold the speakers.

    Maximum External Dimensions : 402 mm W x 320 mm H x 352 mm D
