  • JBL 5308

  • ¥ 337,100 (around 1973)

Commentary

Additional mixer with 8 microphone input channels.

The 5308 is connected to the mixer preamp 5306.
The circuit configuration is equivalent to the microphone mixer section of 5306, and a 14-channel mixer / preamp can be configured by adding 5308.

The input connector uses a Canon plug.

Each channel is equipped with an indicator for over-input monitoring.

Power is supplied from 5306.
