  • Marantz EQ-10

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

This is a graphic equalizer that keeps the contents of the EQ-20 as they are, and the control knob is linked to the left and right to improve usability and cost performance.

Equipped with a defeat switch.
