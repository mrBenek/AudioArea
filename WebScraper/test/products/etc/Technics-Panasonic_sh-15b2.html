Commentary

Turntable base suitable for SP-15 and SP-25.

The cabinet is integrally molded with a viscoelastic material (special rubber) that has a large vibration damping ratio and excellent vibration isolation characteristics to provide vibration isolation.

The turntable, arm panel, and dust cover are also directly connected with special viscoelastic material, and anti-vibration measures are thoroughly taken.

The resonance frequency and resonance Q of the entire cabinet are kept to extremely low values.

The top of the cabinet has a rosewood grain finish.
