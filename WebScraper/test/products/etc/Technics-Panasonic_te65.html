  • National TE65(TE656/TE655)

  • ¥ 8,900 (around 1978)

Commentary

Mechanical audio timer.

There are two types of TE65, TE655 for 50 Hz and TE656 for 60 Hz.

It is a multi-action type that can record up to 96 actions and 48 messages in a day.

The clock face is equipped with lighting, so you can know the time even in the dark.

Timer setting is performed by inserting the attached set pin into the groove of 15 minute interval.
With a pin holder, unused pins can be stored.

It is a 24 hour type.

Equipped with an on-offset switch that can be switched ON/OFF.

Equipped with a select switch that can be continuously energized.

Equipped with a power indicator lamp.
