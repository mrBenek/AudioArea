Commentary

Channel divider for 2-way to 3-way multi-amplifier systems.

The crossover frequency is set to a highly practical frequency. Especially in the case of 2-way, 8-point high and low frequencies can be used.
The band pass characteristic is fixed at 18dB/oct in consideration of sound continuity and sound quality, and a phase selector switch is provided for each channel.

The level control can be adjusted in 1 dB steps.

A gold-plated RCA pin jack is used for the input / output terminal.
