Commentary

An IM cartridge developed by fully integrating ADC technology.

The ADC-25 uses a unique stylus interchangeability system. In addition to the standard 0. 3x0. 7 mil elliptical needle (red), the ADC-25 comes with a 0. 3x0. 9 mil elliptical needle (blue) and a 0.6 mil conical needle (white) for high-quality playback and old records playback.
The 0.6 mil conical needle is best suited for playing Hi-Fi records cut using tracing simulators such as RCA's Dynagroove, Toshiba Angel's PTS, and Nippon Columbia's ND Records. These cutting systems are produced with the least distortion when played with a 0.6 mil conical needle.

By adopting the newly developed magnetic circuit, wide-band reproduction, light needle pressure and racing ability have been acquired.

The ADC-25, ADC-26, ADC-27 and ADC-X series are all compatible with the stylus and can be upgraded by exchanging the stylus.
