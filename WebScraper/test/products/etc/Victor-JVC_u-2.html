  • VICTOR/JVC U-2

  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

MM type cartridge with shell integrated structure developed based on long-selling Z-1 series.

The structure of the vibration system is different from that of U-2E.

Since the head plug slides back and forth, the overhang adjustment can be performed accurately.

The magnetic circuit uses pole pieces with a 3-layer laminate structure to prevent degradation of the high-frequency response due to magnetic loss. Combined with the low-impedance design, flat frequency characteristics are obtained up to the ultra-high-frequency range.
