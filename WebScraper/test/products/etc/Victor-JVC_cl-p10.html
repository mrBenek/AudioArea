Commentary

Cabinet with beech laminated compression structure.

Beech wood, which has an extremely high vibration damping rate, is made into a special laminated structure and further compressed by 30%. This realizes superior resonance - and vibration-free characteristics compared with the conventional laminated type.

This item has a rosewood natural wood finish.

The dust cover is made of 4 mm thick acrylic resin by hand pasting.

Suitable for TT 801 and TT series turntables.
