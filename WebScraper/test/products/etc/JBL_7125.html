Commentary

Safety matrix required for parallel use of two power amplifiers.

If one of the amplifiers fails or its performance deteriorates, only the normal amplifier is selected. In this case, the load impedance remains unchanged and the output loss remains at 3 dB.
A faulty amplifier is automatically disconnected from the circuit and can be removed for repair without interrupting the operation of the whole system. When a faulty amplifier returns to normal, it automatically returns to the circuit and can be checked by the indicator.

Equipped with a 0 synchronous meter on the front panel, you can easily adjust the output balance of two power amplifiers.
It also includes two circuit relay contacts for installing remote alarm signals such as bells, buzzers, and lamps.
