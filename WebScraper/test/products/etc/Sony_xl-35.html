  • SONY XL-35

  • ¥ 16,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

MM type cartridge with elliptical needle

It uses a carbon-clad cantilever that combines and integrates excellent carbon fiber and aluminum as acoustic materials.

The pole piece part uses resin mixed with carbon fiber.

Uses a magnetic circuit with excellent left / right balance characteristics.

In order to improve the S / N ratio, a double shield case structure is adopted.

Cone type damper is adopted.
