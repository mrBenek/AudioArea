Commentary

200 Series Total Performance Indicators (TPI).

For more accurate FM tuning, connect the AZ 200 to the TPI jack of the FM/AM tuner AT 200 s.
At the same time, multipath distorts the waveform, so the antenna direction can be adjusted to eliminate multipath, enabling FM reception under better conditions.

You can draw a Lissajous waveform based on the input from the FM tuner and check the stereo separation.

Can be used as a peak meter.
Since 0 dB and 6 dB are displayed in concentric circles on the CRT, it can be used as a high-precision peak meter by matching the VU meter on the tape deck with 0 dB in advance.
In the case of TPI, since it is an electrical display, the attack time is extremely fast, and it has excellent conditions as a peak meter.

Can be used for phase check during recording and playback.
It can be used to check the phase and L/R by connecting to the output circuit of the tape deck or the output circuit of the amplifier.

In order to minimize the drift of the spot due to the effect of temperature, a differential amplifier circuit directly connected to all stages is adopted, and the drift is suppressed within 2 mm maximum.

High impedance input circuit with FET in input stage is adopted.
