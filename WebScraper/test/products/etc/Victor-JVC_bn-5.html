  • VICTOR/JVC BN-5

Commentary

Binaural A biphonic processor that plays a recorded source through speakers.

The combination of a multistage direct-coupled transistor and a delay circuit can reproduce a sound field space that is completely different from the conventional one.

Equipped with a listening angle selector switch to clearly locate sound sources such as sense of distance, direction, and depth.

You can also get the expansion effect of a standard stereo tape.
