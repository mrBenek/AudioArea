Audio Database

  • Pioneer PP-215

  • ¥ 7,500 (around 1975)

Commentary

An audio timer that can be used for recording messages and alarm clock playback when combined with a tape deck with a built-in recording standby mechanism.

It is a 2-action type that automatically turns off the power after a certain period of time after starting (fixed with minimum 70 minutes guarantee).

Equipped with timer control operation lamp and release switch.

Built-in sleep timer that can be set up to 3 hours.

Two AC outlets are installed.
