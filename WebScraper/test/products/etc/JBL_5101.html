  • JBL 5101

  • ¥ 95,700 (around 1973)

Commentary

Compact solid-state microphone pre-amplifier with high S/N ratio and low distortion.

The input is high-Z unbalanced with built-in RF1 filter, and the output load impedance is unbalanced at 600 Ω.
With the optional 5195, it can be converted to 600 Ω balanced.

By using 5195 as the output terminal, the 600 Ω unbalanced terminal can be changed to the 600 Ω unbalanced bridge output.
