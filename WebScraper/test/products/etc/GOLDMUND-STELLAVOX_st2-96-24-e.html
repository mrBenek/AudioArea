Commentary

Based on the D/A converter ST2 released by ステラヴォックス, a professional audio brand of Gold Mundo, the DA converter module DA converter module has been updated to the newly developed 96kHz/24bit compatible DA96.

The DA96 is designed to minimize the emission of high-frequency noise, minimize the signal path and increase the circuit speed by modularizing the four sections of the DIR, digital filter, D/A and analog filter with a jittering absorption function.

It was also possible to upgrade from the normal ST2 to 96kHz/24bit compatible version.
