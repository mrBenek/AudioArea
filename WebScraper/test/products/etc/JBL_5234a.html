  • JBL 5234A

  • ¥ 140,000 (around 1983)

Commentary

A dual-channel electric crossover network developed for studio monitors and sound reinforcement.

The 5234A allows two way (stereo) or three way (mono) multi-amplifiers.

Equipped with a continuously variable high-level control.

The crossover frequency is determined by inserting a filter board (sold separately). There are 8 types of 12dB/oct filter boards and 3 types of 18dB/oct filter boards. For other frequencies, a blank board corresponding to the cutoff characteristics is used.
For blank circuit boards, any frequency can be set by installing capacitors or resistors. The recommended parts to be used are a Mylar capacitor with less than 5% error and a resistor with 1/4 watts and less than 5% error.
