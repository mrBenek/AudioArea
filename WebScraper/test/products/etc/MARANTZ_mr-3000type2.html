  • Marantz MR-3000typeII

  • ¥ 19,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

Professional 19-inch wide steel rack.

It is 19 inches wide (482 mm, the same width as BTS5 rack) and can be mounted on four sides if it is 5.25 inches (133 mm) or three sides if it is 7 inches (178 mm).
In the case of the Marantz line-up at that time, it is possible to attach with adapter RA-10 or RA-2.

The top plate is for the player base, and the bottom is for the storage space of record or cassette deck.

The mounting surface is slanted to improve operability and visibility.

Equipped with casters, it can be moved freely.

It has a black finish.
