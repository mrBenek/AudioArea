Commentary

Turntable adopting direct drive system.

In the direct drive system, since the motor shaft is a turntable as it is, there is no part that rotates faster than the rotation of the record, and vibration generation can be reduced.
In addition, a frequency-generator (FG) directly connected to both the motor and turntable constantly monitors the rotating condition, and a servomechanism that maintains the specified number of revolutions reduces wow and flutter.

The motor is equipped with the same AC servo motor as TTS 4000.
By applying the principle of eddy current (overcurrent), no discontinuous torque is generated in principle, and smooth rotation is achieved.

Servo circuit is installed to achieve smoother rotation.
A dedicated IC is used for this circuit to improve reliability.

It is equipped with a large neon-tube strobe.
