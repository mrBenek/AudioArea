  • AUDIO CRAFT TS-200

  • ¥ 160,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

MC transformer with Hi - μ core.

By taking advantage of the excellent characteristics of Hi - μ PC material, the DC resistance of the winding is reduced, and stray capacity between the wire and the case is greatly improved to realize low loss.

Internal connection is equipped with a floating input circuit to achieve high S/N ratio.
There is also a floating ON/OFF switch for some cartridges, tone arms and connection cables that cannot be floating.
