Commentary

This monaural MC cartridge is the predecessor model of DENON's representative MC cartridge DL-103.

In 1961 (1961), DENON's first monaural MC cartridge for broadcasting stations was delivered to a number of broadcasting stations before the DL-102 was marketed.
It was reprinted in 1996 to commemorate the 35th anniversary of the birth of the MC cartridge and has been sold to date.

It has a high output design and can be directly connected to the Phono MM terminal.
