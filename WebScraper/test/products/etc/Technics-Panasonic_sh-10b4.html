Commentary

Turntable base for SP-10mkII and SP-20.

The base is designed to prevent resonance by adopting a laminated boat and make full use of the player's performance.

An insulator with excellent vibration isolation characteristics is used.

Thick wooden arm panel with sufficient consideration for mounting accuracy is adopted.

Dust cover is removable and can be stopped in the middle.

Plus arm can be mounted.
