Commentary

Quartz phase-locked control DD turntable realized high performance by utilizing the development know-how of SP-10mkII.

Starting torque 1.5 kg ・ cm, dead weight 2.5 kg (inertial mass 345 kg ・ cm)2) at a constant speed of 1/4 rotation from the start.

With a strong magnetic circuit and large starting torque, there is no change in rotational speed even if 150 tone arms with 2g needle pressure are used. You can also use a cleaner while playing.

The pure electronic brake realizes a smooth stop of the turntable.

It uses an LED lighting strobe with one row of stripes.

The finish is wrinkle finish with special surface treatment of aluminum die-cast to prevent surface changes.
