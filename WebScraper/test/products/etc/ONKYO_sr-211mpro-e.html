Commentary

INTEC275 series surround processor.

Equipped with a Dolby pro-logic circuit with a built-in center / rear amplifier.

Equipped with Route 3 AV selector with S-terminal.

Built-in 3-band tone control.

You can control the delay time and center / rear level.

It can be operated by the remote control of A-911M/A-911MLTD/R-811M.
