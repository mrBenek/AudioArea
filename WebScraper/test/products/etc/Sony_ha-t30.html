  • SONY HA-T30

  • ¥ 15,000 (2-piece set, sold in 1983)

Commentary

A step-up transformer that allows you to enjoy an MC cartridge by simply attaching it to the phono MM input terminal of the amplifier.

Uses a magnetic core mixed with 78% permalloy and amorphous magnetic alloy.
Oxygen-free copper wire is used for the coil.

Matches 3 Ω ~ 40 Ω cartridges.
