  • DENON DK-2300

  • ¥ 70,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

Universal cabinet designed for twin arms.

The cabinet uses Inschlight, which has a specific gravity approximately twice that of plywood, and has a 70 mm layered structure.

Arm board is designed to be replaceable.
The DK 2300 comes with 4 arm boards including a perforated board for the DA 308.
The arm board is 30 mm thick.

Dust cover was sold as an option sold separately. Dust cover AS-5 is made of 4 mm thick acrylic and is resistant to howling.
