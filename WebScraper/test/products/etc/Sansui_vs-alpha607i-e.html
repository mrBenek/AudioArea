Commentary

A/V selector that allows one touch selection of monitor select function and dubbing function.
Audio input Route 6, Out Route 6, Video Input Line 5 and Out Line 5 can be connected.

In order to maintain the quality of both audio and video, the music signal system and video signal system are designed to be completely independent.
Each circuit is separated and tightly shielded, and the terminals on the rear panel are also separated and laid out to eliminate A/V interference from the input to the output.
Each video input is equipped with a discrete broadband video amplifier.

Up to 5 devices such as Video and VDP can be connected.
The monitor select function allows you to freely select and output the video being dubbed, and the input selector allows you to freely select and output the source or dubbed video.

It is equipped with an audio insert function that can insert an external audio signal into the sound of a video device.
It is also equipped with an A/V independent processor selection switch that can be connected to an A/V processor or graphic equalizer for editing.

Equipped with a direct position switch, it is possible to connect input and output in the shortest distance without passing through the processor circuit.

Equipped with an AC line noise filter that greatly cuts high frequency noise of 500 kHz ~ 20 MHz mixed from the AC line.
It also has four AC outlets.

Uses a very thick power cord with polarity display.
