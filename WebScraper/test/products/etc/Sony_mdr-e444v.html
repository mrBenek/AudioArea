  • SONY MDR-E444V

  • ¥ 3,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

Stereo ear receiver with small volume.

Equipped with twin turbo mechanism.

Samarium-cobalt magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and CCAW is used for the voice coil.

The plug uses a gold-plated L-type mini plug.

The cord uses a neck chain method.

Comes with a cord winding type compact case.

There are 8 color variations of white, black, light pink, light blue, pink, blue, red and dark blue.
