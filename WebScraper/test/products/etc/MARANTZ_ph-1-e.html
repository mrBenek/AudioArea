  • ¥ 180,000 (released in 1991)

Commentary

This is a phono equalizer amplifier of the Music Link Series, which was developed purely for music reproduction capability, not for mechanism.

Equipped with three types of SP equalizers, all analog discs including SP records are supported.
We have also adopted an all-stage discrete NF-CR-type equalizer to achieve rich musical performance.

It is equipped with an MC transformer developed exclusively for it.

Each MC/MM has its own dedicated input terminal.
In addition, as a function of the front, 2 MC and 2 MM for a total of 4 positions can be selected, and it is compatible with various cartridges.

A toroidal transformer, copper-plated chassis, and side die-cast are used to prevent vibration.
