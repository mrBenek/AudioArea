  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-200

  • ¥ 37,000 (around the late 1960 s)

Commentary

Micro component series SEA system controller (sound quality control device).

It can be used between the preamp and main amp or in a separate stereo with a premain connection terminal to control sound quality.

The entire band is divided into 5 bands of 60 Hz, 250 Hz, 1 kHz, 5 kHz and 15 kHz, and can be adjusted up to ± 10 dB with a 2-dB-step selector switch.
