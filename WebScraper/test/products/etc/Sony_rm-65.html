  • SONY RM-65

  • ¥ 3,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

A remote control unit for synchronized play between a record player and a cassette deck.

On a player and deck with a remote control terminal, the player's lead-in, the start of the performance and the start of the recording on the deck are matched, and the deck is automatically moved from Rec-Mute to the recording pose as soon as the performance ends.
