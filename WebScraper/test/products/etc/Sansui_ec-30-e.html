  • SANSUI/JBL EC-30

  • ¥ 42,000 (1 unit, around 1978)

Commentary

An enclosure designed by Sansui and jointly developed with JBL to maximize the power of JBL's speaker units.

The EC-30 can mount an JBL 20 cm speaker unit. There are 3 types of speaker units, LE8T, D208 and 2110.
You can also add a cone type tweeter LE20.

It comes with a pipe duct, a sub baffle plate for the tweeter and a grill depending on the characteristics of the mounted speaker unit.
