Commentary

2-way to 3-way switchable multi-channel filter unit.

The crossover frequency of the AF 200 is 9 points from 125 Hz to 8 kHz. By lowering the minimum crossover frequency of the Low/Mid to 125 Hz, it is compatible with large-diameter woofer.

The AF-200 Low/Mid and Mid/High crossover switches have a Flat position.
When it is in the Flat position, the circuit becomes through and can be used as 2-way circuit.

Equipped with low boost circuit of turnover frequency switching type.
You can choose from five levels of turnover frequency and four levels of boost, so you can set it to match your speakers.

The NF circuit configuration provides accurate attenuation at 12dB/oct.

Each band and left and right channels are equipped with independent volumes for fine adjustment.
