  • Technics SH-C02

  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

Compact size microphone / synthesizer mixing amplifier.

By connecting to the tape terminal of the preamplifier, you can enjoy mixing signals from records and FM with external signals from two microphones and a synthesizer into one system.

You can echo both of the two microphones.

The rear panel has a tape terminal that allows you to record mixed signals on tape and to mix signals from a microphone or synthesizer with signals from tape for playback from speakers.
