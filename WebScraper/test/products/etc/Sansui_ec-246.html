  • SANSUI EC-246

  • ¥ 160,000 (1 unit, around 1983)

Commentary

The largest enclosure in the EC series.

The EC 246 is a symmetrical bass enclosure that can mount both 46 cm and 30 cm mid-woofers.

It has an internal volume of 227 liters and a 63-liter back cavity for use as a four way mid-woofer.

It is composed of Scandinavian birch isobaric plywood common to the EC series and high-density particle board. In addition, unnecessary vibrations such as standing waves are suppressed by the asymmetric arrangement of Yonematsu reinforcement.
