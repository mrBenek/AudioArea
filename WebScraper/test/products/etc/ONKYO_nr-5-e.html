  • ONKYO NR-5

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

An address unit that enables simultaneous monitoring of address recording with excellent noise reduction effect.

By connecting NR-5 between the amplifier and the deck, noise can be reduced.
In addition, since it is an independent circuit for recording and playback, simultaneous address recording monitoring is possible with a 3-head deck.

Improves the SN ratio and dynamic range, and reduces harmonic distortion, cross-modulation distortion, and bleeding (noise breathing).

You can also play back address records.

Up to 3 tape decks can be connected at the same time, and you can directly enjoy the mutual dubbing of decks 1 and 2.

Equipped with -40dB to + 12 db wide range peak meter.

It has an output volume.

It is equipped with functions such as fine adjustment of address reference signal output level.
