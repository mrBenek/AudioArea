  • NEC AV-251

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

This AV surround processor was released as an entry-level machine for enjoying surround sound reproduction.

Equipped with 3 types of surround mode : Dolby surround, hall surround and matrix surround.
It also has a surround indicator that lets you see the level of each channel at a glance.

The delay circuit is equipped with NEC's original digital delay.

A 2-channel power amplifier is installed to drive the rear or front speakers.

Input jacks for four video channels, an audio Line 5, and a built-in amplifier are provided.
Outputs include two video channels and one monitor channel, audio Route 3, pre-out, two monaural channels, and speaker channels.

Equipped with master volume, front / rear volume, right (front / rear) / left (front / rear) volume, sound field control is possible.

It comes with a remote control that enables you to change the surround mode, adjust the volume and adjust the balance.
The remote control can store up to three types of volume settings.
