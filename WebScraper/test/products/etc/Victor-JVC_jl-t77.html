  • VICTOR/JVC JL-T77

  • ¥ 53,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

Turntable adopted for JL-B77/JL-77.

The drive motor is a 20-pole, 60-slot, 6-phase DC direct drive motor.
Quiet rotation is realized by a non-contact Hall element servo and Victor's original sine wave drive system.

The turntable is made of die-cast aluminum alloy and is finished with careful dynamic balance one by one. For rubber covering, attention is paid to adhesion with the turntable and holding of records.

Equipped with reflective strobe, rotation speed can be adjusted.
