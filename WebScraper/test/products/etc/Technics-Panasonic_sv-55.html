  • Technics SV-55

  • ¥ 52,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

A component TV that matches the design and size with the System Component Technics GM1.

When connected to an amplifier, you can enjoy the video with a monitor CRT and the audio with the sound of components.
It is also equipped with an audioscope function that displays the output waveform of an amplifier or TV on a cathode ray tube.

It is equipped with a picture ON/OFF switch that allows you to enjoy only the sound by erasing the image.

Equipped with 4-stage scope range switch and Rec level check function.
