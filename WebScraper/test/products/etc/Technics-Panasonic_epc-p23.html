  • Technics EPC-P23

  • ¥ 13,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

MM type cartridge adopting plug-in connector type.

A laminated core with very low high-frequency attenuation is used for the magnetic circuit, and weight reduction is achieved by the adoption of samarium-cobalt magnet.

By adopting a one point suspension with a clearly defined vibration supporting point, generation of cross modulation distortion is suppressed.

Adapter for conventional head shell is included.
