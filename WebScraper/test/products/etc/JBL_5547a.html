Commentary

Graphic equalizer.

The audio frequency range from 25 Hz to 20 kHz can be divided into 30 bands, each of which can be boosted or cut by ± 12 dB.

Equipped with high pass and low pass filters.

Equipped with an equalizer function bypass switch.
