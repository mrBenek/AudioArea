Commentary

AV selector with A/V independent circuit.

The audio and video circuits are completely independent, the circuits and circuit boards are shielded and separated, and the terminal layout of the rear panel is divided into music and video according to signal characteristics.

With a direct position, both audio and video circuits can be output without going through a processor circuit.

Equipped with a monitor select function, you can freely select the video being dubbed or the source / dubbed video with the Input Selector.

Equipped with an audio insert function and a processor switch, editing is possible by connecting an AV processor or graphic equalizer.

A built-in line noise filter prevents noise from the AC line.
In particular, the line noise filter significantly reduces high-frequency noise from 500 kHz to 20 MHz.

It is equipped with 4 AC outlets, 2 power switch linked systems and 2 non-linked systems.

Each video input has a discrete video amplifier.

Gold plating is applied to the main terminal.

Uses a power cord with polarity display.

Newly developed large insulator with excellent vibration resistance is adopted.
