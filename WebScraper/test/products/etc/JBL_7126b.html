  • JBL 7126B

  • ¥ 214,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

Compressor / line amplifier to prevent excessive input.

The switch allows you to select line mode and 2 : 1 or 4 : 1 compressor level to control the dynamic range of the PA system.

Two types of recovery time, 0.5 seconds and 1.5 seconds can be selected.

The microphone input is for high impedance, and can be used for low impedance by adding the optional 5901 (sold separately).
It can also be changed to the line input level by switching inside.
