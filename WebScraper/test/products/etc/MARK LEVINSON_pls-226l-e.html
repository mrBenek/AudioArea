  • ¥ 280,000 (released in 1988)

Commentary

Power supply unit developed for preamplifier No26L/No26SL and equalizer amplifier No25L/No25SL.

This power supply unit is required when the No25L is used in combination with a preamplifier other than the No26L or No26SL.
