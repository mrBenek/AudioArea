  • SONY SQA-2030

Commentary

A 4-channel decoder with 3 SQ / ICs and a built-in back amplifier.

Uses F/B (front / back) logic that reduces front-center and rear-center crosstalk and W/M (waveform-matching) logic that significantly reduces corner crosstalk.
In addition, the use of SQ-IC enables stable 4-channel regeneration.

It has a built-in power amplifier for the back channel using hybrid power IC developed for audio.

It has built-in SQ and RM decoders, and discrete 4 ch is possible by using a demodulator for CD-4.

It is equipped with a 4-channel master volume. If the level of each channel is adjusted in advance, the volume can be adjusted with the master volume.

It is equipped with a mode switch and can be switched between 4 channels and monaural, so the level and phase of each channel can be adjusted.

Equipped with NF type tone control for back channel.

The front and back channels are equipped with effective loudness controls, enabling you to compensate for the high and low frequencies when listening at low volume.

Equipped with 2 sets of input level switching switches, 2 ch input can be switched between Low-Normal-High.
In addition, the tape input can be switched between Low and Normal.

Equipped with 2 ch tape monitor switch, recording and re-monitoring of 2 ch tape recorder is possible.

Equipped with 2 → 4 position, you can listen to the conventional 2 ch source with 4 ch hole cone effect.
