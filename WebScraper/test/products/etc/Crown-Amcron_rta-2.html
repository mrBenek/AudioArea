  • AMCRON/CROWN RTA-2

  • ¥ 1,199,000 (around the late 1970 s)

Commentary

A real-time analyzer equipped with a CRT display that displays sound field characteristics.

When used in conjunction with a graphic equalizer such as the AMCRON EQ-2, the sound field can be corrected visually.

It can also be used as an oscilloscope by using an external input terminal.

It is equipped with a vernier and a pink noise oscillator whose output can be attenuated by a 0 db or -40dB changeover switch.

32-channel 1/3-octave or 1-octave displays the amplitude-frequency of the input signal on a 5-inch CRT in real time. You can switch between 1/3-octave and 1-octave on the front panel.
