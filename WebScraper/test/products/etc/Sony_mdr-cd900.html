Commentary

Digital monitor stereo headphones equipped with a large diameter driver unit.

Uses a long-life design 40 mm driver unit.
CCAW voice CCAW voice coil and amorphous diamond diaphragm make it possible to realize a sound with a good release.

LC-OFC litz wire is used as wiring material.

Uses an oval type special urethane skin ear pad.

Uses a single release curl cord.

Can be folded.
