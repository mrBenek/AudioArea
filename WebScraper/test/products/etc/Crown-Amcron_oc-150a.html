Commentary

Output Control Center with meter developed by gathering all know-how of recording studio in response to the demand of recording engineers.

In addition to the peak meter, VU meter and output selector, it is also equipped with AC line monitoring function.

Equipped with 2 units of 90 mm 90 mm meter with extremely high accuracy for professional use.

Equipped with peak hold function that can change hold time.
