Commentary

Player cabinet suitable for DP-1000.

When DP-1000 is mounted on DK-70, most of commercially available arms with total length of 330 mm or less and effective length of 245 mm or less can be mounted.

24 mm thick laminated plywood is used for the plate material.
In addition, the surface has a real wood finish of plug material.

Various considerations have been taken to prevent howling.

The dust cover is a fleece top type and can be stopped at any opening angle between 30 ° and 60 °.

High-grade insulators with adjustable height are used for the legs.
