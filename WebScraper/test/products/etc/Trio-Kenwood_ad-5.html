  • TRIO AD-5

  • ¥ 11,200 (around the mid 1960 s?)

Commentary

An FM multi-stereo adapter that can receive FM multi-stereo stereo broadcast transmission from devices that cannot receive stereo reception.

The matrix circuit uses the CCM system developed by Trio.

It is equipped with a stereo monitor that can easily find the FM stereo station.

Equipped with a noise filter.

Power supply is built-in.

When recording to a tape recorder, noise due to mutual interference with the adapter is not generated.

It supports stereo tri-amp W-39 and W-50, stereo component ST-810 and ST-720, etc.
