  • VICTOR/JVC HP-D500

  • ¥ 5,600 (around 1990)

Commentary

Stereo headphones with an aero port installed in the center of the magnetic circuit to improve the ability to reproduce heavy bass sound.

By focusing on wearing feeling, it is considered that you won't get tired even after long listening time.
