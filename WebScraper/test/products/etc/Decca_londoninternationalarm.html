  • DECCA London International Arm

  • ¥ 32,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

Oil dump type tone arm with one point support system.

The arm is supported by a highly sensitive one point support system using a jewel with less bearing friction.

The Inside Four Scan Seller uses the repulsive force of the magnet and ideally cancels the inside force which changes depending on the needle pressure and the position of the cartridge on the record board.

The arm support part contains 3 magnets, and the arm itself is lifted from the board by the repulsive action of the magnets, reducing vibration to the arm.

With a built-in level in the head shell, it is possible to more accurately adjust the balance of the arm in the horizontal and vertical directions, which was previously done by eye measurement.

It is designed so that the adjustment of lateral balance and needle pressure can be done more easily than the old type.
