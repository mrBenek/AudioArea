  • SONY MDR-E444

  • ¥ 3,300 (around 1987)

Commentary

Stereo ear receiver equipped with twin turbo mechanism.

Samarium-cobalt magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and CCAW is used for the voice coil.

The cord uses a neck chain method.

Uses gold-plated L-type mini plug.

Comes with a cord winding type compact case.

There are 12 color variations available : light pink, light green, violet, yellow, white, light blue, pink, green, blue, red, dark green and black.
