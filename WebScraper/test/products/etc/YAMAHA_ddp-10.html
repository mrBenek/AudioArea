  • YAMAHA DDP-10

  • ¥ 50,000 (around 1997)

    　
Commentary

A compact Dolby Digital / Cinema DSP processor with which you can enjoy 3 sound field cinema DSPs of Dolby Digital.

It uses newly developed Dolby Digital LSI and realizes 24 bit high-precision decoding.

Equipped with 3 sound field cinema DSP processor.

Equipped with 3 types of sound field programs.

Equipped with interactive functions unique to Dolby Digital, you can set the speaker, center delay and dynamic range of each channel.
The D/A converter is equipped with an 8 x oversampling delta-sigma 20bitD/A converter for all channels.
A switched capacitor filter and a continuous filter are used for the post-filter, providing excellent jitter resistance and reproducibility of very small signals.

There are two digital inputs, each with an optical and coaxial input.

By using APD-1 which is sold separately, it is also possible to play 5.1 ch of LD software corresponding to Dolby digital.
