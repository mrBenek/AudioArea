Audio Database

  • Audio Technica AT-15E

  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

A VM-type cartridge designed for a wide dynamic range.

The structure is based on AT-15Sa technology.

Uses an elliptical needle with ultra-fine particle polishing for the needle tip.

The body is made of silver plated die-cast metal.
