Commentary

MM cartridge with improved characteristics by using amorphous magnetic alloy developed by triple roll method and ⊿ -type core for magnetic circuit.

Nearly 20 thin amorphous magnetic alloy films of 40 μ m thickness are laminated, and ⊿ -type structure is adopted to shorten magnetic path length in order to bring out the excellent magnetoelectric conversion efficiency, high magnetic linearity, and low magnetoelectric conversion noise of this material to the maximum. It is adopted as magnetic core.
This realizes high-frequency and noise characteristics that cannot be obtained with conventional 78% Permalloy.

The cantilever is made of sapphire processed into a pipe shape.
Sapphire is extremely hard and has a vibration transmission speed of approximately 11,000m/s, which is more than twice that of aluminum. This reduces the degradation of mid - and high-frequency characteristics due to the deflection and torsion of the cantilever and transmits vibrations more faithfully to the magnetic circuit.

The needle holder is fixed directly to the body of the cartridge with a screw, preventing mounting looseness between the needle holder and the body, and achieving clear playback sound.
