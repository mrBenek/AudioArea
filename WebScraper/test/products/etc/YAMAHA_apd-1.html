Commentary

RF Demodulator for Dolby Digital LD Player

Converts the Dolby digital RF signal output from a Dolby digital LD player into a digital signal.

Equipped with an optical output terminal and a coaxial output terminal that can output to two amplifiers, etc.

The Demodulate indicator is mounted. It lights when the Dolby Digital RF signal is input and normal operation is performed.
