Commentary

An AV controller equipped with various functions for enjoying surround sound.

By using the AV 550 d surround decoder, you can play Dolby surround sound stored on video software or video disc, enabling realistic surround sound playback. Also, by connecting the AV-D50 digital sound effector, you can create Dolby surround sound with digital delay.

It is also equipped with matrix surround, hall surround and synthetic surround, so you can choose the sound field according to the source.

Built-in power amplifier for surround and rear speakers.

Equipped with a delay control that varies the delay time.

Rear line output terminal is mounted.

It is equipped with an input calibration control system that can adjust the front and rear separation, a delay volume that can change the delay time, and a rear off switch that can cut only the rear output.

In addition to the input and output terminals for three video decks, a video disk input terminal, an audio input terminal, and a monitor output terminal are installed.
You can also use the input selector to select the monitor source and edit / record mode simultaneously.

Equipped with fade-in / fade-out function, enhancer function, B / G / V function.
