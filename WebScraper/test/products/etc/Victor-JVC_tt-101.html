  • VICTOR/JVC TT-101

  • ¥ 150,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

Turntable of the laboratory series which introduced the concept of pitch control for the first time in the world.

Based on the standard A sound of 440 Hz, ± 6 Hz can be changed in precise 1 Hz steps with the quartz lock on.

The servo circuit employs a high-precision all-round integral detection FG servo, which realizes extremely high-precision speed detection integrated with the motor.

The technology is fully applied to every part such as coreless motor without cocking, toroidal power transformer with high efficiency and good S / N ratio, measuring instrument grade digital speed display with 4-digit LED counter, plus / minus bidirectional servo with good response and quick stop with electronic brake.
