Commentary

Based on the XL-88D, this MC cartridge inherits the design policy and sound quality while achieving a more affordable price.

In order to improve the small output, which is a problem of the MC type, a figure-8 generator coil has been adopted.
This uses a push-pull power generation system that efficiently utilizes the movement of the armature and provides sufficient output without using an iron core, thus reducing magnetic distortion and loss.

In order to make the push-pull operation of the figure-8 coil more complete, special magnetic alloy called Permendur is used for the magnetic circuit.
By having a high maximum magnetic flux density and preventing magnetic saturation of the yoke material, almost perfect symmetry can be obtained and efficiency is also improved.

The cantilever is composed of three layers of beryllium / aluminum / carbon fiber and is designed for higher rigidity and lighter weight.

In order to achieve linear braking, we have adopted a newly developed damper that has been reconsidered from an angle.
