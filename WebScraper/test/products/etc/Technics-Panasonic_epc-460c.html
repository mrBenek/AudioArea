Commentary

A semiconductor 4 ch cartridge with high cost performance.

Separation of ultrasonic wave region is improved by vibrator support structure of micro coupler system.

By using a special 4-channel diamond needle, it is possible to play back CD-4 records and stereo records.

Head shell is included.
