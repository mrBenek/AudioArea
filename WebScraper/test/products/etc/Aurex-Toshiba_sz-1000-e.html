  • Aurex SZ-1000

  • ¥ 30,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

This is an equalizer amplifier developed with the goal of creating an equalizer that fully draws out the performance and attractiveness of a capacitor-type cartridge.

It has a simple circuit structure without tone control circuit.
