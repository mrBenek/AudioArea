  • VICTOR/JVC CD4-20

  • ¥ 52,000 (around 1972?)

Commentary

A disk demodulator that demodulates a CD-4 signal extracted with a 4-ch cartridge into four channels.

It has built-in rear volume control, rear balance control and rear tone control (bass and treble) that control ch2 and ch4 of the 4 ch output.

Equipped with an auto noise reduction circuit, it can cut harsh noise.

It is equipped with 4-channel radar and tape monitor switch.

Two 4-channel sources other than CD-4 can be connected.
