  • SONY SB-5335

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

A system selector with which you can enjoy various combinations.

You can operate any combination of 3 players, 3 tuners, auxiliary input Route 3, 3 amplifiers, and 5 speakers.
You can make an independent tape copy while listening to a tuner or a record or recording this sound.

You can use the external adapter jack to further expand functions such as playing and recording input programs and tape copying.

One series of Input and Output terminals are provided on the front for easy replacement of connected devices.

Equipped with a master volume, muting switch and headphone port.
