Commentary

This lightweight and compact MC cartridge further reduces the size of the original air core 8-shaped coil power generation mechanism.

It uses an air core 8-shaped coil, a power generation mechanism originally developed by Sony.
In addition to the high output achieved by the push-pull operation, this system does not use an iron core, so there is little time strain and the vibrator itself can be made lightweight. In addition, the circular armature provides a good balance against electrical and mechanical interference, which greatly contributes to improved sound quality and high compliance.

A high energy samarium-cobalt ring magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.
As a result, we have succeeded in creating a magnetic field (symmetrical flux) with high efficiency and left-right channel symmetry without using a conventional magnetic core, and have achieved ultra-miniaturization of the cartridge.

A replaceable needle unit system is adopted for needle replacement, overcoming the disadvantages of conventional MC-type cartridges.
In this method, the power generation part including the vibrator can be easily replaced.

The cantilever uses a tapered cantilever made of hard aluminum.
In addition, an elliptical needle is used for the needle tip.
