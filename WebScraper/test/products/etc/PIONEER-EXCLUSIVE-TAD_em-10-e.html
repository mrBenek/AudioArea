Commentary

D. D phono motor adopted in Exclusive P10.

By adopting a coreless and slotless structure linear torque hole motor that does not cause cocking, strong starting torque is obtained.

In order to obtain stable rotation, ヤジロベータイプ's SH rotor (Stable Hanging Rotor) system has been adopted to reduce lateral pressure applied to the bearings and to lower the center of gravity of the rotating part to improve rotational performance.
