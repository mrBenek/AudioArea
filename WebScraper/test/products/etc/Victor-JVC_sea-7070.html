Commentary

Graphic equalizer of the Laboratory 7070 series.

The main controller consists of 10 elements, each element being capable of changing the center frequency by ± 1/3 octave.
In addition, it is equipped with an 11-point additional controller that steps between the elements of one main controller, and you can select the center frequency of 649,539 single channels.

There are two levels of variation : ± 12 dB and ± 6 dB. At the ± 6 dB position, one click of the slide volume is equivalent to 1 dB.

Equipped with two tape circuits, two decks can be connected at all times and all modes can be processed only by switching the front panel switch. SEA recording / playback, SEA dubbing, simultaneous SEA recording for two systems, etc. are possible.
You can also change the gain using the master level control and the 3-stage input attenuator.

The SEA resonant circuit uses a semiconductor inductor circuit that completely eliminates coils that tend to cause induction noise and magnetic distortion.

There was a rack handle sold separately.
