Commentary

Turntable adopting direct drive system.

An AC servo motor is used for the drive part.
The AC servo motor applies the principle of eddy current (eddy current). In principle, discontinuous torque is not generated, so smooth rotation can be obtained.

A servo amplifier is mounted to achieve smoother rotation.
A specially developed IC is used in the servo circuit. A frequency generator (FG), which is directly connected to both the motor and the turntable, constantly feeds back the rotational status to maintain the specified rotational speed.

The turntable is made of 32 cm aluminum alloy and weighs 1.75 kg.
An accurate strobe is engraved on the outer circumference, and the correct number of revolutions can be checked by a large neon tube.

Since a servo motor is used, speed switching and speed adjustment are electronic.
The control box has a separate structure so that it can be placed in the desired location.

A disk frame is adopted, and an arm set can be set at any position around it.

The rubber sheet is made of an acoustically excellent material, and is charged to prevent dust from sticking.
