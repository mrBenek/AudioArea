Commentary

A graphic equalizer that can also correct sound fields using pink noise.

Equipped with a pink noise generation function.
By using this function and picking up the pink noise output from the speakers with the attached microphone, you can adjust the sound according to the sound characteristics while watching the display.

Left and right independent 10 bands can be adjusted within ± 15 db range.

Spectrum distribution can be displayed by the high-precision RTA (real-time analyzer) FL display.

Equipped with two tape outputs.
