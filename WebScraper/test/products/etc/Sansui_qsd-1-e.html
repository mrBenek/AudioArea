  • SANSUI QSD-1

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

The technology of QS Barrio Matrix which grew up in the professional world is integrated into IC, and the 4 channel synthesizer / decoder of high quality sound quality is built into the QSD-1.

Equipped with a unique New QS synthesizer function that converts general 2-channel sources such as records and FM stereo broadcast transmission into 4-channel.

The synthesizer has 2 positions of surround and hole.

Equipped with a decoder function for good sound image localization.

Equipped with 4-channel input terminal.
