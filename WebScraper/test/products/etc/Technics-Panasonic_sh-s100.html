  • Technics SH-S100

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1988)

Commentary

AV selector with volume and wireless remote control.

The audio input is equipped with a Line 5 of CD, aux, tape, DAT and ext. The AV input is also equipped with a Line 5 of TV/tuner, BS, VDP, VTR1 and VTR2. Up to five tape decks including a VCR can be connected.

For the video system, dedicated S-VHS terminals with more than 400 horizontal resolutions are installed in the input Route 3 and the output Route 3.

Audio and AV input / recording selectors are independent.

The built-in volume allows you to control the volume of each device in the system in a centralized manner.

Equipped with a BGV function that allows you to monitor the video while the audio source is playing.

Equipped with a muting function.

Equipped with a balance volume.

You can switch between mono and stereo modes.

Wireless remote control is included.
This remote control includes power ON/OFF, muting, volume UP/DOWN, REC selector switch, BGV selector switch, audio selector, video selector, etc.

Since the SH-S100 is not a preamp, the volume may be insufficient when directly connected to a power amplifier.
