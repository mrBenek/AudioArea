Commentary

A crossover network employing a composite amplitude characteristic flat system.

The composite amplitude characteristic flattening method uses a filter array method different from the conventional method, and at the same time, the phase change characteristics of all channels are made equal by inserting a phase shooter circuit, thereby realizing the amplitude characteristic (frequency characteristic) after the composite.
Since the filter characteristics themselves are not different, this method can be changed to a conventional method by simply changing the arrangement of each block, and the best result can be selected according to the characteristics of the speaker system.

The circuit uses an all-stage discrete ICL circuit including a high-Gm low-noise FET.
Sound quality is also improved by using carefully selected circuit elements and large-capacity, low-impedance, wide-band power supplies.

The low-pass and high-pass independent 11-point crossover allows the slope characteristics to be switched between 6dB/oct. and 12dB/oct.
When the slope selector is in the OFF position, the signal of all channels is cut off is cut off, and when it is in the PASS position, a flat signal that does not pass the filter is sent out.
The Q selector switches the crossover depth between -3dB (Q = 0.5) and -3dB (Q = 0.7) depending on the characteristics of the speakers.

If you need a frequency other than the preset crossover frequency, you can add a filter capacitor to the internal AUX circuit board and switch the AUX switch.
In this case, the low-pass and high-pass frequencies can be selected independently.

The rear panel is equipped with a filter mode switch that can select three positions : HPF-LPF, BPF, and 3D.
The HPF-LPF provides both low and high sound output, and allows 2-way configuration with one unit. The BPF is a band-pass filter that provides the medium sound output, and the other unit can be combined with the HPF-LPF to make 3-way configuration. Using this method, you can freely increase the number of channels by combining three or more units.
In the 3D position, the 3D-type center woofer output can be obtained, and three slope characteristics of 24, 12 and 6dB/oct. can be selected.

The input mode switch can connect or disconnect the input terminals of the low-pass and high-pass sides internally, and is used at the SEPATED position when the composite amplitude characteristic flat method is used.

There was a rack handle as an option sold separately.
