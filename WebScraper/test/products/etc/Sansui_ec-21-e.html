Commentary

An enclosure designed by Sansui and jointly developed with JBL to maximize the power of JBL's speaker units.

The EC-21 has a structure in which the EC-20 is vertically mounted and installed on it, and a 2 - to 3-way driver, horn / lens, and tweeter are mounted.
For example, a 2-way system can be configured by mounting LE14A on EC-20 and LE85 + HL91 on EC-21, 3-way system with 124A, LE85 + HL92, 077, or all-cone 2-way system with LE10A and LE20. 175

There was a net grill as an option sold separately.
Special baffle plates for mounting LE20, HL87, HL91 and HL93 were also sold separately.
