  • VICTOR/JVC MC-5E

  • ¥ 28,000 (released in December 1981)

Commentary

MC type cartridge that uses a direct couple system.

A direct-coupled system is adopted, and transmission loss due to the cantilever is suppressed by installing the coil near the needle tip.
The MC-5E uses a micro-coil that was developed using an LSI manufacturing method. This coil is only 1/100 of the weight of the wound coil, making it possible to connect the needle tip directly to the coil.

In the conventional direct-coupled MC-type cartridge, the distance from the needle tip to the coil was 1.5 mm, which is about 1/3 of the distance of the normal cartridge, but in the MC-5E, the distance is reduced by 2/3 to 1.0 mm.
In addition, the entire vibration system has been redesigned to prevent deterioration of characteristics and trace performance due to an increase in effective mass by moving the coil closer.

The cantilever is made of beryllium material. The weight of the cantilever is reduced by more than 10% compared to the conventional direct-coupled MC type cantilever. The weight of the cantilever is increased while the strength is increased by the strong taper processing.

The newly designed high-efficiency magnetic circuit realizes a low mass body.
