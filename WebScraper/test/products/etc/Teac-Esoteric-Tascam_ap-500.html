  • TEAC AP-500

  • ¥ 70,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

Peak programmed meter unit according to IEC standard.

Four tape decks can be connected to the system, and the recording / playback level of each deck can be displayed on the meter with one touch and the decks can be dubbed to each other while watching the meter.

Equipped with a peak memory mechanism.

Equipped with a sine wave oscillator of 400 hz.
