  • SONY MDR-M66

  • ¥ 8,000 (around 1987)

Commentary

Semi-open air type stereo headphones equipped with a 30 mm diameter driver unit.

CCAW voice coil and samarium-cobalt magnet are adopted, and the cord is OFC litz wire (one side cord).

It is a 3 curve free-setting headband with a smart cone shield wire type with no visible cord and good holding feeling. The ear pad is an elliptical soft ear pad.
