  • SANSUI PC-X1

  • ¥ 168,000 (released in March 1983)

Commentary

Tricode PCM audio processor for digital recording and playback.
It consisted of the main unit PC-X1 and AC adapter AC-X1.

Equipped with Tricode PCM circuit, a unique digital technology, it realizes high-precision reading of digital signals.

It enables digital recording / playback for up to 8 hours on VHS and 5 hours on β III.

The compact and lightweight design of 2.5 kg makes it portable.

In addition to the attached AC adapter, you can use rechargeable batteries and car batteries sold separately.
