  • Technics SH-F102

  • ¥ 16,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

Compared with SH-F100 and SH-F101, this is a 2-element FM wing antenna with an electronic tuning circuit that improves the gain by 3 dB.

For single frequency tuning, a signal is received from the antenna terminal of the Technics Quartz Synthesizer Tuner or an optional FM booster (SH-4070) for automatic tuning.

As it has sharp selectivity characteristics and high suppression capability outside the FM band, it can cope with beat interference between broadcasting signals and urban noise.

End-Fire Bi-directional beam pattern is realized, and multipath in the left / right and up / down directions is eliminated.
