Commentary

An 8-input, 4-output audio mixer ideal for studio 8-track recording.

The M-35 has M-35T with talkback module and M-35EX 12-input expander.

A plug-in module is used.

Equipped with eight monitor control functions.

Equipped with a parametric equalizer.

Submix circuit is used.

High-slew rate IC is adopted.
