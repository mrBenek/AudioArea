Commentary

Aiming for the ultimate MM type, this is a wide range, top-of-the-line MM type cartridge combining advanced technologies since the development of the CD-4.

Carefully selected materials such as Shibatar needle MKII stylus and magnesium alloy body are introduced.

There were 2 types of variations, Z-1E for cartridge only and Z-1E/D for shell mount type.
