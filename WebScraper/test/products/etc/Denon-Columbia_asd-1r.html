  • DENON ASD-1R

  • Discretionary price (around ¥ 13,000, around March 2006)

Commentary

A control dock for iPod that connects iPod with DENON audio equipment.

Only DENON with a system connector can be connected to a system component, or only DENON with a dedicated Dock control terminal can be connected to a system component, so that you can enjoy connecting and playing back your iPod.
You can also control basic iPod functions (play, stop, skip, etc.) using the remote control provided with the connected audio device.

You can charge your iPod.

When connected to a DENON product with a dedicated Dock control terminal, iPod text information (menu, track name, etc.) can be displayed on the display screen or on the front display of a connected DENON audio device.
This function only accepts half-pitch alphanumeric characters and does not support Japanese display.

It also supports playback of iPod photos and videos.

There are 2 color variations, white and black.

* Compatible with 3rd generation or later iPod equipped with Dock connector. Some functions may not work depending on iPod generation or software version.
