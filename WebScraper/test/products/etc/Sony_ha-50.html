  • SONY HA-50

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

A low-cost head amplifier based on the design concept of the HA-55.

The direct coupling method is used for input, and we pursue design ideas that emphasize sound quality, such as searching for wires from the signal system.

It has a built-in muting circuit to prevent pop noise that occurs when switching positions.
