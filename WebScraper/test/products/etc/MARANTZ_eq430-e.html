Commentary

A Gray co-mixer equipped with a graphic equalizer, echo and chorus functions, and a mixer.

If you connect an electric guitar to the guitar / keyboard connector and set the selector to the guitar, you can use your own amplifier as a guitar or keyboard amplifier.

The input jacks include microphones 1 and 2, a guitar / keyboard, and tapes 1 and 2 / lines for mixing in various combinations.
You can also adjust the output level collectively on the master volume.

Microphones 1 and 2 each have a pan pot function, so you can move the vocals left and right and change the sense of distance between the two microphones.

The graphic equalizer can be adjusted by ± 12 20 Hz to 20 kHz, each of which can be adjusted by ± 12 dB.

Equipped with an echo / chorus function, you can enjoy the echo effect or chorus by yourself.
