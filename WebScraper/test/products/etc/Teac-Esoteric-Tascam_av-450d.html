Commentary

A full-featured surround processor for enjoying surround sound.

By using the AV 450 d surround decoder, you can play Dolby surround sound stored on video software or video disc, enabling realistic surround sound playback. Also, by connecting the AV-D50 digital sound effector, you can create Dolby surround sound with digital delay.

It is also equipped with matrix surround, hall surround and synthetic surround, so you can choose the sound field according to the source.

It is equipped with functions such as an input calibration control system that can adjust the front and rear separation to your discretion, a delay volume that can change the delay time, and a rear off switch that can cut only the rear output.

A dedicated power amplifier for the surround rear speaker is built in.
In addition, in order to realize high quality round, amplifier connection terminal for rear is mounted.

Equipped with a cross-type 4-way level meter that matches the basic position of the speaker.

It is equipped with a center-out terminal for compensating for sound loss on large screens such as projectors, and for super woofer.

Equipped with a one touch selector for switching between two systems of video deck and video disc.

Equipped with a beat cancel filter selector switch that removes beats generated by connected TVs or tuners.

Equipped with surround on/off switch.
