Commentary

This is a parametric equalizer for the System-20 series.

The System-20 is a system mixer consisting of a main mixer MM-20, a meter unit MU-20, an expander EX-20 and a parametric equalizer PE20.
The input / output is patch-type can be easily connected according to the purpose.
