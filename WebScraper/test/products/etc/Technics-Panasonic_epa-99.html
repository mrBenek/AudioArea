Commentary

Universal tone arm equipped with inside force canceling mechanism of special structure combining 2 cams.

With a special structure combining two cams, cancellation corresponding to the geometrical position of the arm can be performed. Inside force cancellation amount can be adjusted according to needle pressure.

Four sub-weights can be used, and cartridges with weight weight of 4g ~ 35g can be used.

The needle pressure scale is 0.25g increments, and the desired needle pressure of 0 ~ 3g can be obtained just by turning the balance weight.

Equipped with an oil dump type arm lifter.

An S-shaped pipe arm is used.
