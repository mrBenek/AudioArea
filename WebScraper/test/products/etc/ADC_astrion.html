Commentary

IM type cartridge made by one skilled craftsman from inspection and selection of parts one by one, assembly to completion.

In the IM type cartridge, the needle does not have anything other than the needle.
The MM cartridge has a heavy magnet behind the cantilever that moves through the pickup coil to generate the signal, and the MC cartridge has a coil behind the cantilever that reduces the weight but the output.
In the IM-type cartridge, a heavy and large permanent magnet is mounted floating above the cantilever. The cantilever, which is partly made of MU metal with high magnetic permeability, is magnetized by electromagnetic induction by the permanent magnet. This allows the cantilever to perform the same function as the MM-type cartridge. In addition, because the restoring force is exerted on the cantilever by electromagnetic induction, the fulcrum of gravity and the fulcrum of vibration coincide. The moment of inertia is minimized and the needle becomes neutral except when it is moved by the record groove. This structure is called CED (Controlled Electromagnetic Damping).

It uses a laser-shaved pure sapphire shaft to minimize the distortion and resonance caused by the cantilever.

It uses Orbital pivot system which is an advanced version of Omni pivot which boasts ultra-precision machining.
The Omni pivot is designed to fit snugly into the suspension block by ultra-precision machining of the armature to less than 1/4 of a human hair. The polished Orbital pivot takes full advantage of the characteristics of the IM type.
