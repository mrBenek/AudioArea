  • YAMAHA AD-100

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 2001)

    　
Commentary

An AAC decoder that supports 5.1 ch AAC digital audio of BS digital broadcasting.

By connecting an AV amplifier with a 5.1 ch external input terminal and a BS digital tuner, you can convert 5.1 ch AAC digital audio signals into 5.1 ch analog audio signals.
When the digital input is PCM, it can be decoded and played back by the AV amplifier by letting it pass through to the digital output.

Equipped with two optical digital input terminals.
In addition, one through terminal for 5.1 ch analog audio input is mounted.

Equipped with a digital through output function.

The front panel has a 435 mm wide silky gold finish that matches Yamaha AV amplifiers.
