  • MICRO MTA-41

  • ¥ 7,500 (around 1972)

Commentary

This is a Grand Base Circuit head amplifier developed with the aim of high-quality reproduction without blurring the sound of the MC cartridge.

Can be used for cartridges with output impedance of 1 Ω ~ 50 Ω.
