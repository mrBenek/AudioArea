  • Lo-D MT-101

Commentary

The MM cartridge has the same structure as the MT-202mkII and inherits its basic performance.

Uses a pivot support system using jewelry.
In addition, tracing capability has been improved by a lightweight vibration system.

The magnetic circuit uses a C-shaped yoke with high power generation efficiency.
