  • SONY MDR-E141/MDR-E142

  • ¥ 1,500 (around 1987)

Commentary

Monaural ear receiver with 16 mm diameter driver unit.

Samarium-cobalt is used for the magnetic circuit, achieving a sound pressure sensitivity of 108dB/mW.

The MDR-E141 was an L-type super mini plug specification, and the MDR-E142 was an L-type mini plug specification.
