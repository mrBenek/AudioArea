  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-100

  • ¥ 19,900 (around the late 1960 s)

Commentary

SEA (Sound Effect Amplifier) controller that divides the audible frequency band into five and can adjust each band.

The SEA-100 divides the audible frequency band into five bands : 60 Hz, 250 Hz, 1 kHz, 5 kHz and 1.5 kHz, each of which can be adjusted within ± 10 dB.
It is also equipped with an SEA defeat switch that can compare the sound before and after correction.
