Audio Database

  • YAMAHA MVS-1

  • ¥ 19,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

A master volume controller / selector that can be added to the DSP-1 system to adjust the volume of the entire system and become an input selector.

The input level to the DSP-1 can be increased, and digital noise can be reduced by making maximum use of 16 bits.
This greatly improves the S/N ratio in actual use.
