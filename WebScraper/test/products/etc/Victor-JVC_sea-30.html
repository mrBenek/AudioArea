  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-30

  • ¥ 30,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

A compact graphic equalizer using a semiconductor inductor circuit without using a coil.

It is composed of 7 elements of left-right linkage.

Equipped with -6dB input attenuator and defeat switch.

It has a tape input.
