  • AKAI AS-P302

  • ¥ 44,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

An AV surround processor that supports Dolby surround sound.

It has a built-in Dolby Surround Decoder and supports video sources recorded in Dolby Surround sound.
It also includes a matrix position and supports non-Dolby encoded sources.

Equipped with tone control, you can adjust the impact of low range surround.

A built-in amplifier for the rear speaker is used by connecting the tape terminal of your amplifier to the source terminal of the AS-P302.

Four video terminals are installed.
It also has a built-in re-hold function that allows you to dub the source of a video disc with Video1 while monitoring the software of Video2.

Equipped with a 3-mode display that displays volume, sound field balance of L and R, and memory contents.

Equipped with a mute button.

Equipped with an input balance adjustment knob that can adjust the L/R imbalance of the input source.

Monaural input is possible using the L side of the video audio terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.
