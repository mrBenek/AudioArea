Commentary

Specially designed cartridge developed as the top model of VM type.

The AT-20SLa is designed and manufactured with the aim of a perfect transducer with no color = distortion, by taking the best of technical theory and eliminating all sound making and compromises.

This production method is exclusive to this model, and the parts are assembled one by-one by selected engineers, and changes over time are minimized by special environmental testing after assembly adjustment, aging, and repeated fine-tuning.

Uses a die-cast metal body.

Measured data is attached to all models.
