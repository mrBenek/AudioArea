  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

Left and right independent 33 elements, stereo graphic equalizer with fine adjustment at 1/3 octave.

The 16 Hz-25 kHz band is divided into 33 left and 33 right bands, and the center frequency of each band is designed according to the 1/3 octave step, which is the basic of audio.
By adding 16 Hz and 25 kHz to the 31 bands of an advanced spectrum analyzer, the array of 33 sliders draws a frequency curve as it is.

Semiconductor inductors are used for each band filter.

It is equipped with a variable range switch that can be selectively used : ± 12 dB for large changes in frequency characteristics, and ± 3 dB for delicate characteristics.

Equipped with an equalizer position switch for easy equalization recording.

Equipped with a characteristic switch that can obtain inverse characteristics of the set frequency.

In addition to the black model, there is also a silver model.
