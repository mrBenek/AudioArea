Commentary

Stabilizer unit with built-in exhaust unit for 2-stage turbo fan.

When the suction pipe and the remote cable are connected to TT 801 and the TT 801 type is started, the operation starts simultaneously.
It is a constant suction system that continues to operate while the turntable is rotating, so that the record and mat always adhere to the turntable with a constant force and stable sound quality is maintained.

The displacement is 70 mmaq (equivalent to 4 kg).

The cabinet has a rosewood natural wood finish.
