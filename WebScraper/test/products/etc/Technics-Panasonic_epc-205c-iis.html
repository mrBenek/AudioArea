  • Technics EPC-205C-IIS

  • ¥ 21,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

MM type cartridge which is the standard model of the 205C-II series and can be used with interchangeable needle and body.

The power generation structure is common with EPC-205C-IIH.

Uses a one point suspension system with samarium-cobalt magnets.
