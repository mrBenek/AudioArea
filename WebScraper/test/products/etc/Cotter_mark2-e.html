Commentary

Four types of step-up transformer are available : Type P/Type PP/Type S and Type L with special specifications.

Type P is for low impedance type such as Ortofon, Type PP is for EMT, and Type S is for high impedance type such as Denon.
These three types are imported as a common Type EX and shipped in one of the three sets at the dealer according to the user's request. Also, it is possible to convert between each type after purchase.

TypeL, a special specification product, is only for cartridges with output impedance of 1 Ω ~ 6 Ω and cannot be converted to other types.

It comes with Cotter's original silver wire triaxial cable (which was also sold separately) that provides excellent lossless transmission and external noise rejection.
