Audio Database

  • AKAI DT-128

  • ¥ 11,200 (around 1983)

Commentary

For the first time in its class, an audio timer that allows two programs to be reserved per day.

It is equipped with 12 hour current time display which simultaneously displays up to the second unit.

Quartz is used as the time base.

Equipped with a sleep timer mechanism that can be set freely every 1 minute up to 1 hour and 59 minutes.

Equipped with an auto-dimmer that automatically adjusts the display brightness according to the surrounding brightness.
