Commentary

A spectrum analyzer that can measure the frequency characteristics of the entire audio system in a listening room.

You can check the frequency characteristics of the system including the whole room by connecting it to a pre-amplifier or pre-main amplifier and turning on the pink noise switch with the attached microphone set.

By connecting to the input / output terminals of the amplifier or deck, the frequency characteristics of each device can be measured.

The SA-1 display can display 10-band and average-band LEDs and also has a peak-hold function to hold instantaneous frequency characteristics.
The display range is 1-12dB/2-24dB/3-36dB, 3-step switching type, and one LED has a range width of 1dB/2dB/3dB.

The display of Lch/Rch/Both can be changed by switching the switch.
