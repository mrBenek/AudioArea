  • JBL 5233

  • ¥ 100,000 (around 1978)

Commentary

Channel divider used for 2-way division of single channel (monaural).

The cutoff characteristic is 12dB/oct. and is equipped with a continuously variable high-level control.

The crossover frequency can be determined by inserting an optional filter board.
There is a board for each of the seven commonly used frequencies and a 52 to 5120 blank card is used for the other frequencies.
For the 52-5120 blank card, any frequency can be set by installing capacitors and resistors as shown in the attached table. The recommended parts to be used are a Mylar capacitor with less than 5% error and a resistor with 1/4 watt and less than 5% error.

Can be stored in 1 EIA standard rack.
