Commentary

MM type cartridge with MM type structure pursuing MC type sound quality.

The FR-5/FR-5E uses a ring-shaped toroidal core to eliminate harmful distortion of magnetic materials.

Conical solid diamond needle is adopted for FR-5 and elliptical solid diamond needle is adopted for FR-5E.
