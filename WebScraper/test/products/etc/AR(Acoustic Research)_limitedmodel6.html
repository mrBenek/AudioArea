  • AR(Acoustic Research) Limited Model 6

  • ¥ 650,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

A limited series of spectral equalizers designed to vary the input signal.

Equipped with 6 frequency adjustment points that can freely vary the input signal and the necessary equalization curve, the characteristics can be adjusted according to the listening room environment.
