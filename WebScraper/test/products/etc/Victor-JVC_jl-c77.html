Commentary

Cabinet adopted for JL-B77/JL-77.

High-density beech wood is piled up to a thickness of 60 mm with a special vinyl adhesive, and only the motor and arm parts are hollowed out. This structure controls slight internal vibration and also suppresses external vibration.

7 mm thick aluminum alloy plate is adopted for the arm board, and the arm can be firmly fixed.
In addition, the arm board can be attached and detached, and the arm can be replaced with an optional spare.

The legs are equipped with a special rubber isolator with adjustable height.

Equipped with a thick transparent acrylic dust cover.
This dust cover can be fixed at the self-weight angle with a free stop hinge, and can be put on and off.
