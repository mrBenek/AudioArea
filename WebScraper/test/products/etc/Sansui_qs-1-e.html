Commentary

This is a 4-channel synthesizer equipped with a unique phase modulation circuit and phase shifter based on the Sansui QS method.

By adding a QS-1 and a rear power amplifier or pre-main amplifier, you can play four channels.

In addition to Matrix 4-channel records and FM broadcasts, you can enjoy 4-channel playback of 2-channel sources from records, tape, or FM stereo broadcast transmission, as well as discrete 4-channel source playback control.

Equipped with a function switch, you can select a 4-channel sound field effect and reproduction method according to the number of songs, composition and recording method.

There are two types of front and rear output circuits, Low and High, so you can choose your favorite amplifier whether it is a main amplifier or a pre-main amplifier.

SANSUI's original phase modulation circuit allows the dynamic range to be expanded.

A specially developed IC-based quasi-electronic circuit is used to obtain a natural sound field.

4-channel recording of 2-channel source is possible with 4-channel tape deck.

Left / right balance of front and rear can be independently adjusted.
In addition, the balance adjustment of front and rear is a slide type.

Equipped with 4 VU meters to check the balance.
