  • TRIO MF-5010

  • ¥ 26,500 (around 1970)

Commentary

A crossover network developed by inheriting the performance and quality of F-6000.

Four levels of low / mid and four levels of mid / high crossover frequencies can be selected, and the FLAT position is also equipped.

2-stage switching of crossover / slope is possible.

With a direct output terminal, the input signal can be directly output.

It is equipped with independent level control for the left and right channels and the high and middle ranges.
