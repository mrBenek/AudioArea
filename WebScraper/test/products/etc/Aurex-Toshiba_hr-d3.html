Audio Database

  • Aurex HR-D3

  • ¥ 5,500 (around 1976)

Commentary

This headphone was developed by applying the know-how obtained with the condenser type to the dynamic type.

The diaphragm is a cone-type speaker using a polyester film with a diameter of 5.7 cm and a thickness of 50 microns.

The case is semi-open air to eliminate the sense of sealing.
