Commentary

The MC cartridge was developed as a cartridge that matches the lightweight arm without sacrificing strength by promoting miniaturization by strengthening the magnetic circuit, examination of body materials, and weight reduction of the vibration system.

Based on the concept of push-pull power generation that efficiently utilizes the movement of the arm tube, we have adopted a figure-8-shaped coil that doubles the power generation of a coil that moves in a magnetic field by twisting a conventional round coil once.

In order to reduce the weight and size of the body, we are trying to reduce the size by improving the efficiency of the magnetic circuit.

Uses a tapered cantilever made of heat-cured aluminum alloy.
