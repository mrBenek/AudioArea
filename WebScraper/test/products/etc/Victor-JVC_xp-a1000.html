Commentary

A digital acoustic processor equipped with data programs and other functions to reproduce the scale, shape and atmosphere of the concert hall.

Using the symmetric 6-point sound field analysis method that employs a total of six microphones, we have actually measured the sound field space at 15 locations around the world, and it is equipped with a computer analysis program for its characteristics.
In addition, it is equipped with a total of 20 kinds of preset patterns including the data of the movie theater by simulation. In addition, the preset program can be changed according to preference, and up to 20 new patterns can be registered.

Equipped with a left and right independent signal processing system that integrates sound field measurement, analysis and reproduction.
The A/D converter, which affects the sound quality, uses a twin ADC with 64 x oversampling digital filter.

With parameters that can be adjusted according to the reverberation time and space of the listening room, you can set natural characteristics even in a dead or line-biased room or a small room.

6ch/4ch switching is possible.

Digital input / output terminals for optical and coaxial Route 3 are mounted.

It uses FL display that can display the original name in characters.

A remote control is included.
