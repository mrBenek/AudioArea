  • Technics EPC-300MC

  • ¥ 15,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

MC-type stereo cartridge with coreless twin ring coil system.

A coreless twin coil system with a pure coil structure without an iron core is adopted, which reduces magnetic loss and distortion and realizes an extremely small effective mass.
In addition, excellent channel separation is achieved by independently oscillating two ultra-small ring-shaped coils corresponding to each channel.

Titanium nitride (TiN) cantilever is used.

The support structure adopts a one point suspension system.
