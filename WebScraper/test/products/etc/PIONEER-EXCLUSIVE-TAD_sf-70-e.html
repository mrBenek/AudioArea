  • Pioneer SF-70

  • ¥ 33,000 (around 1969)

Commentary

An electronic crossover network in which crossover points and slopes can be freely selected.

2-way and 3-way can be used by switching the switch.

The crossover frequency can be adjusted to 5 levels of high and low, and can be freely selected according to the speaker.
In addition, the slope can be selected in 3 stages at 4 positions.

It is equipped with a center channel output jack that mixes low range L and R, allowing 3D playback.
