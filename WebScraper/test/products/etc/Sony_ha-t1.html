Commentary

Booster transformer developed for MC cartridge.

The newly developed amorphous ribbon is used for the core material to reduce strain in the low frequency range and eliminate magnetic loss in the high frequency range.

Oxygen-free copper wire is used for the coil, and the capacitance between wires is reduced by hyperfractionation winding.

A solid silver sealed switch with little change over time is used for the signal system selector switch, and the input terminal is gold-plated.
