  • Technics SH-MX1200

  • ¥ 115,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

A multi-channel high sound quality Pro DJ specification audio mixer focusing on analog.

Equipped with microphone input and 4-channel input.

Low/Mid/High cut switches are provided for the master and fader respectively.

Equipped with an effect send and return switch.

High/Mid/Low equalizer is mounted.

Input channel assignment to cross-faders and ON/OFF functions are provided.
