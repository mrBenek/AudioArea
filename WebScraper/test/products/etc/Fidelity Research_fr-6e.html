Commentary

An improved MM cartridge based on the FR-5 series.

The newly designed toroidal magnetic circuit is used.
By reexamining everything from the shape of the core to the circuit design and body structure, we aim to expand expressive power.

The FR-6SE is devised to increase the momentum of the magnet of the vibration system. By generating a bias effect in the magnetic circuit, the low-level non-linear part is out of the usable range.
