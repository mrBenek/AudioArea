  • Pioneer MU-31D

  • ¥ 18,500 (around 1972)

Commentary

Belt drive phono motor with hysteresis synchronous outer rotor motor.

Uses a 4-pole hysteresis synchronous outer rotor type motor with large torque and enhanced durability.
An aluminum alloy die-cast turntable is used for the turntable.

A Center shaft side pressure prevention mechanism is adopted. A pulley is placed in the opposite direction of the capstan, and a belt is also placed on it to prevent side pressure to the shaft. This is a unique mechanism.

Equipped with a button lock function, it has a safety design so that the rotation speed cannot be changed when the motor stops.
This prevents the belt from hurting.
