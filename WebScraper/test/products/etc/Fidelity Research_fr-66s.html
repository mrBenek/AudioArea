Commentary

A tone arm developed to bring out the capabilities of the cartridge.

The body of the 40 cm type is made of high strength stainless steel, and the arm outlet adopts a collet chuck method without looseness.
Also, a dynamic balance type is used for needle pressure application.

FR-66S has adopted Elevation Base B-60 for the arm base.
The B-60 uses a multi-thread screw in the helicoid, which is driven by a gear.

The FR-66 has fixed specifications for the arm base of FR-66S, and the body part has the same structure.
