Commentary

An AGC amplifier that automatically adjusts the volume of the sound system against ambient noise.

Suitable for systems installed in environments with variable ambient noise (such as airports, bus terminals, stadiums, factories, and outdoor PA systems).

The circuit configuration consists of a signal / control amplifier and a detection amplifier. The volume is automatically changed by the function of a control circuit using FET.

The input terminal is equipped with a high-level input terminal in addition to a microphone input.
