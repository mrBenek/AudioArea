  • SONY DR-A90C

  • ¥ 9,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

A headphone with microphone equipped with a small and highly sensitive back electret condenser microphone mounted on the left and right units.

It uses a binaural recording method that enables sound recording with a rich sense of realism by capturing sound at the position of the ear.

It uses a vertical in-phase system and a two way power system that does not require a battery.

It can be folded, and the volume of the headphones can be adjusted and the microphone can be turned on / off at the chest.
