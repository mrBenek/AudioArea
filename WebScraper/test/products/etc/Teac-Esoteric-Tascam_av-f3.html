Commentary

A digital video still unit that allows users to enjoy digital still or strobe operation simply by connecting it to an AV device such as a video player or video disk player.

It is equipped with a digital memory still function to freeze the video and video disc player screen.
In addition, it is equipped with a digital strobe function that projects the still image of the image with a strobe while the sound is played back as usual. The image interval can be varied continuously from approximately 0.1 seconds to approximately 3 seconds.

With the built-in memory read function, even if the image is restored to the normal image after still operation or strobe operation, the last screen of still or strobe image can be displayed by pressing the memory read switch.

The soft switch applies software to the apply software to the screen when the still or strobe is operating, and adjust the image quality to make it easier to see the rough screen when playing back.

When a color level indicator is installed, the indicator lamp flashes or goes out when the hue is not reproduced properly or the input signal level is not normal.
