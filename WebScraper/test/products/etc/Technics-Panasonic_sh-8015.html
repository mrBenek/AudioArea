Commentary

A stereo frequency equalizer that can divide the audio band into five and continuously control the level of each band within ± 12 dB.

Equipped with an equalizer on/off switch.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch.

Rec out/source Equipped with an equalizer position switch.

Equipped with a variable range indicator.
