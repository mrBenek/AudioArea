  • VICTOR/JVC NR-20

  • ¥ 53,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

Noise reduction unit which can add noise reduction effect by ANRS circuit to the tape deck.

By using the NR-20 for recording and playback, the effect of ANRS can reduce the harsh low-level mid-to-high-frequency noise by up to 10 dB.

Equipped with a calibration mechanism.

Standard tapes for cassette and open-reel are mounted.
