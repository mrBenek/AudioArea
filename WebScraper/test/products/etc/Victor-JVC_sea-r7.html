  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-R7

  • ¥ 65,000 (released in 1982)

Commentary

A graphic equalizer that can control not only the transmission frequency characteristics but also the frequency characteristics of the reverberation time.

The left and right frequency equalizer (F. EQ) consists of 12 elements, and the control range is ± 12 dB and ± 6 dB.

The SEA-R7 is equipped with a delay time equalizer (D. T. EQ) consisting of five independent elements on the left and right sides. This equalizer uses an electronic delay circuit to generate a delay signal and a five band bandpass filter to add frequency-specific reverb to the main signal. The level and decay time of the delay signal can be controlled continuously. The level can be up to 0 dB relative to the main signal and the decay time can be selected from 0.04 to 1.2 seconds.
In addition, the Depth indicator changes continuously from green to red depending on the position of the volume, showing the approximate reverberation time.
When the cross switch is pressed, the phase of the delayed signal can be inverted and the left and right can be switched.

Equipped with two tape circuits, SEA recording / playback, SEA dubbing, two simultaneous SEA recording, etc. are possible.

Equipped with a character switch that can instantly reverse the SEA correction characteristics.

Equipped with a large FL (fluorescent display tube) function indicator.
