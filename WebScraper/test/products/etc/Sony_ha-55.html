  • SONY HA-55

  • ¥ 47,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

Head amplifier for MC cartridge.

The head amplifier consists of a differential circuit that employs an ultra-low noise LEC transistor at the first stage.

The power supply section uses an FET buffer power supply to stabilize the amplifier and improve low-frequency separation.
It also has a built-in muting circuit to prevent pop noise when the power is ON / OFF.

Equipped with an input impedance selector that can select the proper load resistance of the MC cartridge.
In addition, it is equipped with a Pass position that allows direct connection of MM type cartridges.
