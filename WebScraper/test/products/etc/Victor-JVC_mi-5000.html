  • VICTOR/JVC MI-5000

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A multi-functional 6-channel mixer with a phono input.

It has an all-channel independent pan-pot circuit that can freely control the localization of sound image.

All channels are equipped with echo devices.

Equipped with two independent systems and two channel output.

An addition type mixing circuit is adopted to realize high S/N and high linearity.

Equipped with a monitor select switch.

Equipped with a microphone input circuit with built-in overload indicator.

Equipped with an AC outlet.

Portable belt can be attached.
