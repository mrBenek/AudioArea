Audio Database

  • Audio Research EC-5

  • Price unknown (around 1978)

Commentary

Electronic crossover with 2-way stereo configuration.

The slope can be switched to a special one.

The crossover frequency is user-specified and can be changed to the desired frequency by replacing the optional network part card.

Eight analog modules Type4 are used.
