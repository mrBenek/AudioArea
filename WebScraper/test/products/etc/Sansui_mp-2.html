Commentary

FM multi-adapter required to receive FM stereo broadcast transmission with mono tuner.

It uses a circuit method with good separation, and you can adjust the spread of sound on left and right with the dimension control.

High efficiency is achieved by printed wiring.
In addition, an SCA filter is built in to improve stability.

With a built-in filter circuit, you can receive signals comfortably by cutting out noise, especially in areas with weak radio waves or areas with a lot of noise.

Equipped with a stereo indicator for easy confirmation of stereo / monaural.

Power supply circuit is built-in.
