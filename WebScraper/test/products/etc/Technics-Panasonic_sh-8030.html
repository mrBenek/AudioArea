Commentary

The Space Dimension Controller integrates the dimension controller and microphone mixing function, and further improves the play function.

Equipped with a unique space dimension controller.
The ambience that provides a sound field of up to 240 ° and the additional BBD Electronic Echo can be adjusted independently by the coaxial knob.

Equipped with a display for peak level meter.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit, both Microphone 1 and Microphone 2 are equipped with BBD electronic echo, and the echo volume can be adjusted.
Microphone 1 is also equipped with a pan-pot function that localizes the sound image to any position between the left and right speakers.

It has phono and aux jacks, and the mixing knob allows continuous mixing with the line connected to the amplifier.

Equipped with a frequency equalizer capable of level control of ± 12 dB at five positions.
It is possible to flatten the frequency characteristics of the system, eliminate booming, and create desired sound quality.

Equipped with an equalizer on/off switch.

Equipped with an effect position switch.
