Commentary

MM type cartridge developed for Hi-Fi regeneration.

Both the 244 / 17 and 244 / C are equipped with a 0.7 mil spherical finished diamond needle tip, and the 244 / C is specifically designed to fit older players or changers with a needle pressure of 2.5g to 5g.

244-65 comes with SP disc sapphire needle and can also be used to play 78 revolution shellac disc.
