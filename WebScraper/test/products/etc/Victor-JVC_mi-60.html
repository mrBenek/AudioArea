  • VICTOR/JVC MI-60

  • ¥ 42,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

A 6-channel microphone mixer with built-in echo.

It is equipped with a pan-pot function that localizes sound image to left, center and right and an echo function.

Built-in -20dB microphone attenuator.

It is equipped with functions such as memory marker, master volume and marking space.
