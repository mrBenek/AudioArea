  • Technics SH-3433

  • ¥ 47,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 4-channel audioscope that reflects the 4-channel playback sound field.

In addition to visualization of the sound field, waveform observation and phase check of each channel are also possible.

It is also possible to select the optimum direction of the FM antenna by observing multipath waveform.
