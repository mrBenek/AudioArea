Commentary

ES series MC cartridge.
In order to pursue not only the performance expressed by characteristic tables and numerical values, but also the audibility, we developed it after conducting many trial experiments and research.

The output voltage is 4 mv, which is equivalent to the general MM type.
In addition, in order to eliminate the hassle of replacing the needle of the conventional MC cartridge, we have adopted a structure that allows easy replacement.
