  • LUXMAN G-11

  • ¥ 68,000 (released in January 1980)

Commentary

10-band graphic equalizer with independent left and right adjustment.

The audio frequency band is divided into 10 bands with one octave intervals around the A sound (440 Hz), which is the reference sound of the instrument.

The left and right channels are independently designed to improve operability and various characteristics.
You can also change the maximum amount of change.

A bypass switch is installed.
