  • Technics SH-3280(D)

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

A demodulator developed for discrete four channel record (CD-4 record) playback.

A PLL circuit is used to eliminate noise caused by dust or worn records.

It is equipped with a carrier level adjustment knob that can adjust any cartridge output to the optimum output.

The separation / carrier level adjustment knob is mounted on the bottom plate to prevent malfunction.

Compatible with 2 types of cartridges for CD-4, semiconductor type and MM type.

It is equipped with a Direct terminal which can utilize the equalizer circuit of the pre-main amplifier.

Equipped with a phono output jack that takes out the equalized signal.

The 4-ch, stereo and direct can be switched by operating the function switch.

It is equipped with a 4-channel radar that informs 4-channel playback.

It has an AC outlet of up to 300W.
