Commentary

Step-up transformer for MC cartridge.

Can switch between 2 types of input impedance and 2 cartridges.

The transformer is housed in an iron case on top of a triple shield to reduce the influence of SN ratio due to external induction noise.

Low-capacity code is used for output code.

Gold plated terminals are used for input / output terminals.
