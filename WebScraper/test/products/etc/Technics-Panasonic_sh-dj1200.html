  • Technics SH-DJ1200

  • ¥ 50,000 (released in 1996)

Commentary

An audio mixer developed exclusively for DJ play.

Equipped with tough & long life fader using high-precision resistor.
This fader has achieved a nominal wear resistance of 100,000 times compared to 15000 times for ordinary Technics.

In order to respond to accidents, a set of spare channel faders and cross faders is built into the rear of the body.

Equipped with a 3-bar level meter exclusively for DJ play.

Built-in tone control (-24dB / + 12 db), trim, monitoring balance, and booth monitor output.

There are 2 color variations of black and silver.
