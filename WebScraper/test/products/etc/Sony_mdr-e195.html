Audio Database

  • SONY MDR-E195

  • ¥ 1,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

Waterproof type monaural ear receiver conforming to JIS waterproof type.

It is waterproof design and can withstand water pressure of 2m depth.

Samarium-cobalt is used for the magnetic circuit, and CCAW is used for the voice coil.

OFC litz wire is used for the cord.

3 types of ear pieces (L/M/S) were included.
