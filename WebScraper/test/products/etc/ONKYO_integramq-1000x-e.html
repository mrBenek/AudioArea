  • ONKYO Integra MQ-1000X

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

An improved 330 mm wide compact graphic equalizer based on the Integra MQ-1000.

The left and right independent 10-element graphic equalizer adjusts the sound field according to the listening conditions.
The maximum variation of each element is continuously variable from ± 6 dB to ± 12 dB.

The change characteristics can be checked at a glance by the controller LED.
