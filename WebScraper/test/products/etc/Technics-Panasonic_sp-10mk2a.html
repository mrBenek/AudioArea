  • ¥ 150,000 (released in 1983)

Commentary

Quartz D. D. turntable upgraded based on famous SP-10mk2.

All electric circuits of SP-10mk2 are IC.
In addition, quartz control has been introduced to the integrated D. D. and all-around detection F. G. to achieve rotational speed deviation within ± 0.001%.

By using both the electronic brake and the mechanical brake together, there is almost no shock sound and it stops instantaneously in 0.3 seconds.
