  • Technics EPC-P202C

  • ¥ 20,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

Plug-in connector type MM cartridge that uses pure boron pipe cantilever.

The effective mass of a light oscillator of 0.29 mg is obtained by a pure boron pipe cantilever, and high resonance frequencies are excluded from the range of audibility.
In addition, flat transmission characteristics have been achieved by adopting a multi-layer all-laminate core with extremely low high-frequency attenuation.

Adapter for conventional head shell is included.
