Audio Database

  • SONY MDR-E464

  • ¥ 4,400 (around 1987)

Commentary

Stereo ear receiver equipped with a 16 mm diameter driver unit.

Sapphire diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

Equipped with twin turbo mechanism.

It has an ergonomic design that doesn't feel pressure on your ears even when you listen for a long time.

A coat winding type compact case is included.

There are 4 color variations of blue, pink, white and black.
