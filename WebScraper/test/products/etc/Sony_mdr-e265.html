Commentary

Stereo ear receiver using Sony acoustic turbo circuit system

It uses a drip-proof 4-layer structure protector that prevents water and sweat from entering.

OFC litz wire is used for the cord. In addition, it is a neck chain system.

Comes with a cord winding type compact case.
