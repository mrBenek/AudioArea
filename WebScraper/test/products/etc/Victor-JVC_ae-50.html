  • VICTOR/JVC AE-50

  • ¥ 300,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

An acoustic measuring device capable of accurately and easily measuring the frequency characteristics of an audio system, transmission characteristics of a listening room, reverberation time, sound insulation characteristics, noise level, etc..

In particular, the reverberation time can be automatically measured and digitally displayed only by adjusting the level.

Comes with a convenient trunk case for storage and transportation.

Microphone stand is sold separately.
