  • JBL 5101B

  • ¥ 95,700 (around 1975)

Commentary

A microphone preamplifier with excellent S/N and low distortion.

The input is a high-impedance type. When using a low-impedance microphone, add a microphone transformer 5901.

The maximum output level is + 10 dBm unbalanced and can be balanced by using a 5195 matching / bridging transformer.
