  • Pioneer MU-1200

  • ¥ 32,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

A direct drive phono motor using a Hall element.

A brushless DC servo motor is used as the drive motor.

Constant speed rotation is maintained by automatic control by electronic circuit.
Fine adjustment within ± 2% is also possible.

Equipped with a neon lamp for strobe.

A DC stabilized power supply is used for the power supply.
