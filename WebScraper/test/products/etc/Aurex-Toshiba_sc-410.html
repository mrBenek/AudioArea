  • TOSHIBA SC-410

  • ¥ 36,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 4-channel QM (RM) decoder that upgrades 2-channel stereo to 4-channel system.

Built-in 4-channel matrix circuit with dual differential circuit.

Equipped with QM (RM) effect switch which can obtain 4 effects.

It has a power amplifier for the rear.
