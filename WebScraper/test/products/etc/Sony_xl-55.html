Commentary

An MC cartridge that employs a newly developed non-iron core circular armature and an 8-shaped generator coil.

A unique 8-shaped generator coil is used for power generation.
In this system, a ring-shaped space with high magnetic flux density is provided, and a figure-8 coil with orthogonal left and right channels is wound around this space.

The armature has an empty core instead of an iron core, and moreover, has a round shape to realize an ideal vibration system both electrically and mechanically.

The cantilever uses the carbon clad cantilever that is used in the XL series. In addition, it has a 3-layer structure combined with special light alloy, so it has higher rigidity and lower Q.

Tension wire and flat armature clarify the vibration supporting point.

The magnetic circuit consists of a rare-earth magnet and a ring-like air gap, so the sensitivity is 0.2 mV, which is easy to handle despite the air core.

Uses an elliptical solid diamond needle.
