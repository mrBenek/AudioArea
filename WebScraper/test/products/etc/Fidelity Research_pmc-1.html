Commentary

Standard type MC type cartridge.

The PMC-1 is designed to provide high-quality sound even when played by the MC head amplifier built into the pre-main amplifier.

The has the same basic structure as the PMC-3, such as a specially hardened aluminum alloy cantilever, lightweight air-core coil, double tapered damper, samarium-cobalt magnet, new yoke, and metal housing.

There were two types of PMC-1 : a cartridge single model and a model with a head shell RS-10.
