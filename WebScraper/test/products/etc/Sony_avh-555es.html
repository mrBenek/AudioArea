Commentary

The Hi-Fi audio / video controller connects the Line 5 of audio equipment and the Line 5 of video equipment in a centralized manner. In addition, the combination of audio and video sources can be freely adjusted.

Simply press the function switch of the source you want to watch or listen to, and the necessary equipment (for example, if you press Video1, the connected amplifier or monitor TV) will be turned on simultaneously.
You can also combine the desired video from Video1-4 with the desired sound from the audio source Line 5 by simply pressing the function switch, making BGV easy to operate.

It comes with a wireless remote control that allows you to turn the power on / off, switch functions, and up / down of the master volume from a remote location.

The AC outlet is separated from the main unit in consideration of the effect on sound and image quality.

It is equipped with a graphic equalizer switch that can equalize the sound of all connected devices.

Connect the PCM processor once and you can record and play PCM on three video decks without changing the connection (Sony PCM processors only)

Uses a mechanical volume controller to minimize sound quality degradation.

A centralized display with LED is used so that the source during playback or recording can be identified at a glance.

It is equipped with an outlet control switch which cuts the power of unnecessary equipment.

Line Out can be switched between variable and fixed.
