  • ¥ 19,800 (around 1969?)

Commentary

This phono motor is an independent motor of the Record Player PC 400.

It has the same structure as the mechanism of PC 400 except that the motor board is shortened by the length of the arm.

A belt, idler and double drive system is adopted as the drive system.
