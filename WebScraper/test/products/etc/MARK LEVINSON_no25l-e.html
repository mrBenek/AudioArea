Commentary

A phono equalizer amplifier with separate phono amplifiers for No26L and No26SL in a housing.

Power supply is not included.
Power supply from the power supply PLS-226L attached to the No26L/No26SL is required for use. In addition, by using the separately sold PLS-226L, operation by itself is possible.

For those who already have a MM or MC phono card, there were models without a phono card.
