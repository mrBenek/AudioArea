Commentary

Dolby NR / C type processor that reduces hissing by 20 db (5 khz).

The noise reduction of the Dolby NR / C type is 3 dB at 100 Hz, 18 dB at 1 kHz, and 20 dB at 5 kHz, so it can be reduced to a region where hissing is hardly felt.

It uses a sliding band method that effectively reduces noise by suppressing breathing.

Equipped with an anti saturation circuit to improve the high saturation level of the tape.

Supports a wide range of 2-head and 3-head decks.
Recording simultaneous monitor is not available.

Equipped with a level check indicator.

An all-front control type playback CAL volume is mounted independently on L/R.

A cassette tape for the reference level is included for easy adjustment of the playback level.
