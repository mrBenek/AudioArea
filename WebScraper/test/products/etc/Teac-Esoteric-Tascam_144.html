  • TEAC Sound cookee 144

  • ¥ 175,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

Cassette 4-channel multi-track recorder.

The audio mixer section consists of four inputs and two outputs.

It is equipped with an equalizer, a pan pot, an AUX SEND circuit for echo processing, and an independent tape cue for multiple recording and mixdown.

Equipped with independent line out and aux in, it can also be used as an independent mixer.

The cassette deck consists of four tracks, four channels, one way tracks at a speed of 9.5cm/s and is designed exclusively for cobalt (chromium) tapes.
In addition, the mechanism part is designed with an emphasis on the durability of the 2-motor system, and soft touch logic control is adopted.

Equipped with a pitch control function.

Equipped with counter memory function.

Equipped with a Dolby NR system as a noise duction system.
In 144, it is always ON.

Equipped with a VU meter.
