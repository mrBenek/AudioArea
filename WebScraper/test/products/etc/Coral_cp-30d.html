  • CORAL CP-30D

  • Price unknown (around 1960 s)

Commentary

MM type cartridge used for Record Player CP-301/CP-301U.

A vibration system with excellent follow-up performance is realized by careful selection of dampers.

Uses the same ideal vertical angle of 15 ° as record cutting.

The entire cartridge has been reduced in size and weight.

The mounting dimensions conform to JIS and EIA standards and are compatible.

0.65 mil diamond needle is used for needle tip.
