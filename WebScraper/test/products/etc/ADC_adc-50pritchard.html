Commentary

A tone arm designed to maximize the performance of the cartridge.

Equipped with an inside four scan seller.

By 4 precision miniature ball bearings, frictional resistance of the supporting point is kept low, and smooth arm operation is realized.

The body of the arm is made of walnut, and the resonance frequency is limited to 6 hz or less.

The total length is 27 cm, and the rear counter-weight part is 4.5 cm maximum.
