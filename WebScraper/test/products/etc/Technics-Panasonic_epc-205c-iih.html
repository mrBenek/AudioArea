  • Technics EPC-205C-IIH

  • ¥ 23,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

MM type cartridge corresponding to the high output model of the 205C-II series in which the needle and body can be used interchangeably.

The power generation part is common with EPC-205C-IIS and the needle is common with EPC-205C-IIL.

Uses ultra-thin titanium taper pipe.

Uses a one point suspension system with samarium-cobalt magnets.
