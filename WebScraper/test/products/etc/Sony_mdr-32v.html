Audio Database

  • SONY MDR-32V

  • ¥ 4,200 (around 1988)

Commentary

Stereo headphones with volume control for volume adjustment and stereo / monaural switching.

It supports sound multiplex TV broadcasting.

It uses 3.5m OFC litz wire cord, so you can listen to it away from the TV screen.

Other functions are the same as the MDR-32.
