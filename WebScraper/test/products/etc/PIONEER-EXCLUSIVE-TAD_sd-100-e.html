  • Pioneer SD-100

  • ¥ 108,000 (around 1971)

Commentary

A stereo display that can observe various waveforms of equipment, check and measure characteristics, and comprehensively observe acoustic characteristics such as sound field characteristics of a listening room.

You can easily determine the antenna direction by connecting a tuner with a multi-path terminal.

In addition to being used as a VU level meter, the large level meter can also be used as an output meter or a voltage meter.

An input terminal for 4 ch is also provided.
