  • National TE97

  • ¥ 11,200 (around 1978)

Commentary

Electronic audio timer.

It is a 2-action type that can set both ON time and OFF time in minutes.

The recording time of the absence is from 1 minute to 23 hours and 59 minutes, and long recording is supported.

Equipped with digital reverse (return feed), even if you go too far in the set of desired time, you can set it accurately if you go back a little.

It is a 24-hour type that turns on and off at the fixed time every day.

Second adjustment is possible.
