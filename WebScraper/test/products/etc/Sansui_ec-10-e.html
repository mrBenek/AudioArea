Commentary

An enclosure designed by Sansui and jointly developed with JBL to maximize the power of JBL's speaker units.

The EC-10 has a structure that can mount 38 cm diameter woofer and wide range speaker including JBL professional unit.
A total of 10 models can be mounted : 7 models of woofer such as 136A, 130A and LE15A, and 3 models of wide-range speakers such as D130.

It comes with four pipe ducts that can be replaced according to the characteristics of the mounted speaker unit and room conditions with four screws.

Carefully selected materials are used for the enclosure, including baffle plates made of equal-thickness plywood made of high-quality Northern European birch and retaining structures, and Yonematsu and Yellow Yang as reinforcement materials.

There was a net grill as an option sold separately.
