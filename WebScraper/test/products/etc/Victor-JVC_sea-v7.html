  • VICTOR/JVC SEA-V7

  • ¥ 54,000 (around 1971)

Commentary

A 4-channel SEA controller developed for 4-channel systems.

7-element SEA system is adopted.

The switch allows three types of interlocking operation : 1-3/2-4 channels, 1-2/3-4 channels, and 1-4/2-3 channels.
When used for two channels, the left and right can be controlled independently.

Equipped with an output terminal for recording.
