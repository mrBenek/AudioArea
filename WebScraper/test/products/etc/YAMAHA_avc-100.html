Audio Database

  • YAMAHA AVC-100

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1990)

Commentary

An AV selector equipped with a video Line 5 (four S terminals) and multi-input terminals for ten audio systems.

It can be operated from the learning system remote controller in combination with the DSP 107 ast.
