Commentary

Static tone arm integrating MA-77 and MA-101 technology and experience.

In addition to the arm lifter and lever that can easily adjust the arm height, the head shell and tension wire for inside four scan seller have been improved by adding protectors.

An ultra-precision angular type bearing and Micro's original pivot are used for the bearing of the rotating part, realizing vertical sensitivity of 5 mg or less and horizontal sensitivity of 10 mg or less.

Precision is sought in the material and finish of the pipe, and low-frequency resonance and local resonance are reduced by using damping material.
