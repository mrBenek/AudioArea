  • SONY MX-22

  • ¥ 9,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

Sound mixer with echo for karaoke play

It is equipped with an input selector that enables mixing with line inputs such as records and 8 tracks through amplifiers and with tape inputs from cassettes.

Equipped with an echo volume that can be adjusted according to your preference.

External DC power is required and can be supplied from the TA-AX22.
