  • AMCRON/CROWN VFX-2A

  • ¥ 160,000 (around the late 1970 s)

Commentary

A channel divider that can be used in multichannel systems.

All filters are continuously variable from 20 Hz to 20 kHz, allowing you to set the optimum crossover frequency while emitting sound from the speakers.

When using stereo, it is used as a 2-way divider.
When monaural is used, it can be used as a 3-way divider or as a 2-way divider + bandpass filter.
