Commentary

High-end address unit equipped with meter.

The address is a noise reduction system originally developed by Orex.
This method compresses and expands the signal so that the hissing can be reduced to an almost inaudible level, and the dynamic range can be greatly expanded, resulting in better playback sound.
The address noise reduction level at 10 kHz is approximately -60dB at address-out and -90dB at address-in. This improves the S/N ratio by approximately 30 dB. In terms of the dynamic range at 1 kHz, the maximum recording level at address-in is increased by 7 dB to + 15 dB, and the noise level is also reduced to -85dB. In addition, since the maximum recording level is increased, the distortion is significantly reduced by 1 / 6.

Equipped with a peak meter with an attack time of 10 msec.
