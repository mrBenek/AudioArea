  • SANSUI SDM-1

  • ¥ 8,200 (around 1973)

Commentary

A single-directional dynamic microphone that cuts ambient noise and faithfully records the sound you want to record.

Switching between low impedance and high impedance is possible.

It can be used as stand type or hand type depending on the attached desk stand.

Made of metal die-cast.
