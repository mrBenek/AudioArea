Commentary

A channel divider designed with technology developed in professional studios.

The basic settings are 3-way mono, 2-way stereo, or 2-way stereo + sub-stereo mono.

You can vary the crossover frequency and curve settings.

XLR3 balanced terminals are used for the input / output terminals and the output impedance is lowered as much as possible.

There are 2 color variations of black and silver.

The option was to select a slope curve with a sharp cutoff.
In this case, 24dB/oct or 36dB/oct can be selected, but since it is a fixed frequency, it was necessary to specify it at the time of order.
