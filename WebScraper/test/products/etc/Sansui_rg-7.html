  • SANSUI RG-7

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A graphic equalizer with a pan pot function.

Equipped with 2 microphone inputs, mixing with music sources is possible.
Also, Mike CH-2 can connect electronic musical instruments such as electric guitars and synthesizers.

Equipped with a pan pot function that allows you to move the sound image freely.

It is equipped with a reverb circuit using a BBD element, and an echo effect can be obtained.

It has a 10-band graphic equalizer function.
The resonance circuit is a semiconductor inductor circuit composed only of transistors, resistors, and capacitors to prevent deterioration of sound quality caused by coils.
