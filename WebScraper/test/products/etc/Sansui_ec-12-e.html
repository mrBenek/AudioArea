Commentary

An enclosure designed by Sansui and jointly developed with JBL to maximize the power of JBL's speaker units.

The EC-11 has a structure in which the EC-10 is vertically mounted and installed on it, and a 2 - to 3-way driver / horn and tweeter are mounted.
For example, LE85 and HL92, 375 and HL89, and Tweeter 077 can be combined.

When EC-10 and EC-12 are used in combination with LE15A at the center, LE85 + HL92 2-way system or 3-way system by adding horn tweeter 075 to it can be constituted.

There was a net grill as an option sold separately.
A special baffle plate suitable for the diameter of the horn to be mounted was also sold separately.

Net grill cannot be mounted when HL89 is mounted.
