Commentary

IM (Induced Magnet) type cartridge.

The damper mechanism of the ADC-10/EmkII has been improved to prevent problems during playback.

The armature, which is tapered by processing a special aluminum alloy, has high strength with a thickness of 1/1000 inch, and transmits the delicate vibration of the needle tip to the pole piece more accurately.
