Commentary

A channel divider capable of dividing the band.

To match any speaker, the crossover frequency is divided into a wide range of frequencies from 200 Hz to 8 kHz using a √ 2 geometric progression, and the slope of the crossover cutoff can be changed to 6dB/oct, 12dB/oct or 18dB/oct.

The cutoff frequency can be varied independently for each band.

With a defeat switch, it can be divided into two parts, middle and high range and low range.
In addition, by using two CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CHD-CH 300

It is equipped with a channel phase inversion function for high and low frequencies.
