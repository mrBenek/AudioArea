Commentary

MM type cartridge developed by inheriting the X-1 series technology.

We have inherited X-1 series technologies such as beryllium cantilever, laminated core, samarium-cobalt magnet, etc.
We also improve the completeness of the program through theoretical analysis using computers and rigorous interviews.

There were 2 types of variations, Z-1 for cartridge only and Z-1/D for shell mount type.
