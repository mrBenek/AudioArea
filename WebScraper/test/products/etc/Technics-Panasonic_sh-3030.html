Audio Database

  • Technics SH-3030

  • ¥ 7,500 (around 1975)

Commentary

A microphone mixing adapter that can be connected to the tape monitor terminal of the amplifier to mix and play the source signal and microphone (guitar).

Equipped with a microphone mixing control, you can fade in and fade out.

Equipped with microphone position control and tape monitor terminal.
