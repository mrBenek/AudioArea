  • AX-20

    ¥ 6,500 (around 1978)



    A mixdown unit that converts 4-channel signals recorded on a 4-channel tape deck into 2-channel stereo signals (mixdown).
    Each sound recorded in four channels can be positioned arbitrarily by the AX-20 to either the center of two speakers or to three points left or right.
