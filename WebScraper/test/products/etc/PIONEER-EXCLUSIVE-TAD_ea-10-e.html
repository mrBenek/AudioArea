  • ¥ 130,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

Twin pipe tone arm of the arm exchange system adopted in Exclusive P10.

A variable level oil dump mechanism using highly reliable silicon oil has been adopted to improve traceability and greatly reduce cross-modulation distortion that occurs during large amplitude reproduction in the low frequency range.
In addition, by rotating the adjustment ring, the damping is off, and the horizontal balance can be accurately taken.

It uses a straight tapered pipe made of carbon fiber with a head shell that flattens the mechanical impedance of the tone arm.
It is also equipped with an S-shaped universal arm pipe to enable the use of commercially available head shells.

There was a straight pipe arm sold separately.
