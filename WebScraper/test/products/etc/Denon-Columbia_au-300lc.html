  • DENON AU-300LC

  • ¥ 10,000 (around 1992)

Commentary

Step-up transformer for MC cartridge.

Uses LC-OFC (linear crystalline oxygen-free copper) winding and cord (0.4m).

Compatible with 3 ~ 40 Ω cartridges.

A high S/N ratio has been realized by the left and right independent Permalloy double shield.
