  • ONKYO ED-205

  • ¥ 35,000 (released on April 20, 1999)

Commentary

Surround processor capable of 5.1 channel surround playback by connecting to INTEC 205 series etc.

Equipped with 5.1 ch input jacks, surround playback is possible by connecting to a DVD player with a decoder.
It is also equipped with an AV selector function that can operate video signals together with audio signals.

In addition to Dolby Pro Logic Surround, surround modes such as HALL, LIVE and ARENA are equipped.

It has a built-in center / surround amplifier.

It is equipped with delay time adjustment function, center / surround (rear) level control function and auto input balance function.

It comes with a system control remote control which can mainly operate the 205 series units except CDR-205TX.
