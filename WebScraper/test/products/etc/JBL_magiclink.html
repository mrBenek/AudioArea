Commentary

A cordless transmission and reception system for surround sound of the Sound Effect Series.

The Sound Effect series was developed as a sound system for multimedia applications such as audio, TV, PC and game.

Uses IR (infrared) method.

Power pack for transmitter is included.
Also, the power pack for receiver was sold separately.
