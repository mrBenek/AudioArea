  • Technics SH-AV44

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

AV surround processor with built-in AV surround and Dolby surround.

Equipped with a total of five surround modes : Dolby surround, movie, music, manual and monaural.
In the manual, you can adjust the surround effect to your preference.

Equipped with Route 6 input terminals.

It has a power amplifier for the rear.

Equipped with BGV function to enjoy video and sound from different sources.

Equipped with a bass boost switch that can increase the bass range.

Center speaker output terminal is mounted.
