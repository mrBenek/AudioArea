  • Cyrus/Mission Cyrus PSX-R

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

A dedicated DC power supply unit to upgrade the performance of Cyrus III and other Cyrus systems.

It employs a 300 VA strong toroidal transformer and a 30,000 μ F condenser with slot foils.

It employs an intelligent design that automatically sets the necessary output parameters according to the equipment being used.
