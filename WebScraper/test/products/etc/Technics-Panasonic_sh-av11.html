  • Technics SH-AV11

  • ¥ 24,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

31.5 cm wide record jacket surround processor.

It is equipped with tape/TV, VTR and VHD input terminals that can easily increase the input system to the amplifier.

It has a built-in amplifier for the rear speaker.

It has a slide-type master volume.

Equipped with an effect volume that allows you to freely adjust the amount of surround sound.

Equipped with surround on/off switch.

Equipped with a stereo / monaural changeover switch.
