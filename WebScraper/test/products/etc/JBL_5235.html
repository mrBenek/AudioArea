  • JBL/UREI 5235

  • ¥ 150,000 (around 1988)

Commentary

Dual channel electronic debinding network developed for studio monitor system and SR.

By dividing the audio spectrum before the power is amplified, interference between the high and low frequencies is prevented, and a high purity signal can be sent directly from the amplifier to the speakers.
It also more efficient use of amplifier power is possible.

The plug-in circuit board allows cross-over frequency washing.

The subsonic low-frequency Q characteristic can be switched.
