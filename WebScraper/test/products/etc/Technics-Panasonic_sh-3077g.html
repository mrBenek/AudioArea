  • Technics SH-3077G

  • ¥ 27,700 (Released in November 1978)

Commentary

A microphone / synthesizer mixing amplifier with two microphone / guitar terminals and one synthesizer / aux terminal.

With built-in electronic echo using BBD / IC, you can freely adjust the echo amount according to your preference.

You can record and play the mixed signal.

It is equipped with a pan pot mechanism that can move the sound image freely.
