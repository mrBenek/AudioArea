Commentary

An audio monitor system developed in 1964 (1964) by incorporating an all-transistor-type amplifier into the well-established 2S 305 type speaker system for broadcasting monitor speakers.

Its excellent quality control and simple configuration provide stable stereo playback for monitoring stereo broadcast transmission sound quality.

Casters are attached, and the height of the speaker is adjusted to the position of the ear sitting on the chair.

At that time, it was fully adopted by NHK Broadcasting Center.

Canon connector is standard for the tip of input cord.

The standard cabinet color is BTS 0001 Association color (gray), Size 2 (75B5/0.5).
