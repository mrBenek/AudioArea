Commentary

A compact monitor system for the digital age that brings together the best of diatone and monitor technology.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses aramid cloth and cone. This diaphragm uses aromatic polyamide aramid fiber with high rigidity, light weight and excellent vibration damping characteristics. In addition, the 2-layer structure eliminates the binder and eliminates the resin (resin content), making it lighter.
In addition, an alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

A 4 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
In this unit, an aramid cross cone is used for the cone part and a B center cap is used.4By installing C, wide-band reproduction with less natural sound is realized.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system, making it possible to play back low-frequency sound that is voluminous despite its small size.
In addition, by damping control of the large bass reflex port, the group delay time of the low sound range is shortened, and the response of the low sound is quickened, and the reproduced sound with clear pitch is obtained.
A rounded baffle is used for the front baffle in order to achieve the reproducibility of the sound field and the flattening of the characteristics, and the Canadian maple with the beautiful sound is arranged on the cabinet joint part contributes to the beauty of the music.

Sold separately, stress-free 6N oxygen-free copper 2 mm.2There was a speaker cable with star quad structure and para-finish at both ends using x4 cores.
It is also compatible with the DK-A3 speaker stand for DS-A3.
