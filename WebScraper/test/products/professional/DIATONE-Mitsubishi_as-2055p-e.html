Commentary

A two way portable speaker system with an internal monitor and amplifier.

The AS 2055 p has a high output sound pressure level of 104dB/W/m for signals of 200 Hz or higher, which enables more dynamic playback.

It has a 20 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure is a trunk case type with a removable front cover.

The monitor amplifier has two inputs, balanced and unbalanced, and is equipped with 5-step switching tone control.
