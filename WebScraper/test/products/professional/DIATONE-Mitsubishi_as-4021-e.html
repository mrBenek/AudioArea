Commentary

This large-size audio monitor system incorporates an all-transistor amplifier into a 3-way high-fidelity speaker that combines a newly developed 12 cm mid-range speaker with a 40 cm low-sound speaker.

Similar to AS-4001, it was completed in 1966 (1966) in technical cooperation with NHK Technical Research Institute.

Each unit is fully adjusted in an anechoic room.

Since it was developed for stereo, it has a symmetrical unit arrangement.

It is designed to be used as a large trial listening room, recording monitor speakers for music, and audition speakers.

The input code is based on 21P3B of BTS-4101.
