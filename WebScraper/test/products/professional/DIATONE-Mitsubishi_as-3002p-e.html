Commentary

A monitor speaker system with built-in amplifier developed in 1977 (1977).

It has a 30 cm cone woofer for the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter for the high range.

The enclosure is designed with a moderate roundness to prevent degradation caused by mid-frequency diffraction.

A single push-pull system is adopted for the output stage of the amplifier section to realize clear reproduction with less notching distortion, and a constant current load system is adopted for the driver stage to improve performance by increasing driving effect.

It is equipped with a function to directly monitor the delivery from the mixing console.

Comes with a dedicated cart.
