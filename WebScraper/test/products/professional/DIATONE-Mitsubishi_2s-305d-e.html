Commentary

A speaker system with a redesigned 2S 305 for home use.
It was released at the same time as the second DIATONE DS series speaker.

2S-305D uses the same cabinet and unit as 2S-305.
The main changes include a walnut finish on the exterior, a removable front back RUNNET, and a network-based tone adjustment mechanism that changes input terminals and smoothly changes high-frequency slopes.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The vibration system of the PW 125 is a mechanical filter that attenuates the high-frequency range at a crossover frequency of 1500 Hz or more, so there is no unnecessary high-frequency radiation even without using a network.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm of this unit has an effective vibration radius of 2 cm, and the magnetic circuit is made of iron-cobalt alloy, which is considered to have a higher saturation magnetic flux than other magnetic materials, to achieve higher efficiency.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method. In addition, thin plates of hippopotamus and lauan are bonded one by one to make the exterior angle round, preventing the influence of the diffracted phenomenon on the middle and high frequency range and increasing the mechanical strength.
