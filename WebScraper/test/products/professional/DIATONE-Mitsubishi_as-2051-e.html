Commentary

This speaker system was developed in 1964 (1964) for portable monitors for business use.

The 20 cm class uses a completely enclosed cabinet with a minimum volume and is configured with an all-transistor amplifier with an output of 10W.

It was widely used by NHK, commercial broadcasting stations, and movie-related industries as broadcast vehicles and relay stations.

Some connectors for input and AC power came with a cannon connector (AS-2051D type)
