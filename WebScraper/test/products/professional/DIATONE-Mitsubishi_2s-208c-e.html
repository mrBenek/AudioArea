Commentary

A speaker system in which the 2S 208 type can be mounted on the wall.
It was used for a wide range of applications such as a speaker for monitoring in a sub-adjustment room with a small floor space, talkback, and audition.

The performance is the same as that of the 2S-208 type, and it is a semi-fixed type so that the angle can be adjusted by listening position.

To monitor the sound quality of the NHK-designated name T-205, it was used in the coordination rooms of NHK, commercial broadcasting stations and major theaters.

Standard enclosure color is BTS 0001 Association Color No. 2 Gray (7.5B5/0.5).

There was also one with 600 Ω matching transformer.
