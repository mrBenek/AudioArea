  • SANSUI CP-A4

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1990 s)

Commentary

This is a CD radio cassette recorder designed with the concept of music notebook.

The CP-A4 is designed to be an A4 size when the speaker is closed, making it possible to carry and install freely.
There are 3 color variations of the body color : gray, white and red.

Body Part
The switches are concentrated on the top of the body so that it is easy to use and can be operated while being carried around.

It is equipped with a Karaoke Boyscan Cellar. When the Karaoke switch on the back is turned on, only the vocal range from CD, tape, or FM broadcast music is lowered. This allows you to enjoy Karaoke easily by using an optional Karaoke microphone.
This function only affects stereo audio.

Built-in digital clock with 2-way timer. If ON/OFF timer is set, alarm playback is possible. If sleep timer is set, power is automatically turned off when specified time elapses.
This feature does not work on CDs, and the pinch roller may deform if the PLAY or REC button is pressed down for a long time during timer playback or recording of tapes.

CD player part
The CD player is equipped with functions such as program performance of up to 20 songs, music scan to play songs on the disc for 10 seconds in order, and 3-mode repeat (one song, all songs, between songs).

It is equipped with an auto space function that automatically opens the interval between songs for 4 seconds when recording CD.

It has a random performance function that you can listen to the music on the disc at random.

8 cm CD single is supported.

Cassette deck section
Equipped with 2-way reverse mode of 1 round trip regeneration and continuous regeneration.

Equipped with an automatic recording function to eliminate recording errors.

Tuner section
It uses a 3-band PLL synthesizer tuner of analog TV, FM and AM.

It is equipped with a preset function that can read out the desired broadcasting station.
Presets can be set for 10 TV stations, 20 FM stations and 10 AM stations.

Equipped with 2-mode tuning function of manual and auto.

Speaker part
A neodymium magnet with a strong magnetic force is used in the magnetic circuit of the speaker unit to achieve both thinness and sound quality.

It is a magnetic resistant design.
