  • AIWA CS-72

  • ¥ 56,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

3-band compatible radio cassette.

Equipped with a playback microphone mixing mechanism. With microphone volume, volume can be adjusted.

A 2-way speaker with a 12 cm cone woofer and a 4 cm cone tweeter is used for the speaker section.

In addition to FM/AM broadcasting, shortwave broadcasting (SW) is also available.

It has a music sensor function of up to 9 songs.
The number of specified songs is displayed with 9-point LED and can be checked.

Equipped with a Rec Mute mechanism that is convenient for unrecorded parts.

An ITL/OTL circuit is used for the amplifier.
In addition, the external speaker terminal is mounted so that external speakers can be connected.

Equipped with an FM muting mechanism.

Equipped with a 2-stage switching type tape selector corresponding to CrO2 tape.

It uses an oil dump mechanism and is a soft cassette eject.

It has queue / review capability.

Equipped with a full auto-stop mechanism, the button returns when the tape is finished and the mechanism stops.

It is a 3-power system and supports battery / AC power supply / car battery (with adapter).

It is equipped with a loudness switch.

With built-in tone control, the bass / treble can be adjusted independently.

Equipped with an AFC switch, stable FM reception is possible.

It has a large 2-meter with 4 functions.

Equipped with a mechanical pause mechanism.

Equipped with a tape sleep mechanism.
