Commentary

This pre-main amplifier has been improved based on excellent circuit technology and audition results in pursuit of high musical satisfaction.

The equalizer amplifier uses a push-pull circuit to achieve an allowable input of 650 mV, and the phono gain is a 3-step switching type that can be set according to the cartridge.

Equipped with a protection circuit.
