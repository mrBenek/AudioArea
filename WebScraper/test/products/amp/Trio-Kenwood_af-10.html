  • TRIO AF-10

  • ¥ 22,900 (released in 1958)

Commentary

This tri-amplifier was announced as the final version of home Hi-Fi amplifier which can enjoy FM, BC, SW and LP.

The FM tuner is equipped with an AFC (automatic stabilizer) and can receive stable signals for a long period of time with a 1 ℃ dial.
In addition, a limiter (noise elimination circuit) is mounted to eliminate whistle noise, interference noise, and fluorescent light noise.

By installing a high-frequency amplifier, it can be used in areas where radio waves are weak.

It is equipped with an amplifier of output 10W.
