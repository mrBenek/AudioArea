  • AR(Acoustic Research) Limited Model 2

  • ¥ 650,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

Stereo power amplifier of Limited series.

The fully balanced design allows stable signal amplification while minimizing distortion in response to constantly changing load impedance.

It can also be used as a monaural power amplifier by bridge connection.
