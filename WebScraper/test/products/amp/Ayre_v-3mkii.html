  • Price unknown (around 1998)

Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier that refined the V-3 and thoroughly pursued low-level reproducibility.

A non-NFB non-feedback circuit is constructed by carefully selecting a MOS-FET with excellent linearity for the output stage.

A fully balanced circuit is constructed from input to output.

A toroidal choke coil is used to reduce the noise component of the power supply and eliminate the effect of the return signal.
