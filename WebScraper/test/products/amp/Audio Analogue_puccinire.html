  • Audio Analogue PUCCINI RE

  • ¥ 168,000 (around 2003)

Commentary

As the successor model of Puccini, this integrated amplifier is compatible with remote control.

The circuit board has been renewed to minimize the signal path.
In addition, a signal switching relay is placed near the input terminals for brush-up.

A phono equalizer circuit corresponding to MM and MC is mounted.

The power supply is equipped with a toroidal transformer.

Equipped with a pre-out terminal, it can be developed into a bi-amplifier system.

Wireless remote control is included.
The remote control allows input selection, volume adjustment, and mute operation.
