  • YAMAHA MX-55

  • ¥ 98,000 (released in 1987)

Commentary

A 4-ch / 2-ch switchable power amplifier developed for DSP systems.

Four presence speakers can be driven by a 2-amp independent power supply system with a symmetrical design (left / right for 2 ch, front / back for 4 ch).

It can also be used as a stereo power amplifier with a 4-ch / 2-ch selector switch.

Equipped with a level control, you can adjust the volume level of each of the front and rear speakers for 4 ch, and each of the left and right speakers for 2 ch.
