  • YAMAHA RX-V10mk2

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1998)

    　
Commentary

DSP AV amplifier which is the successor of RX-V10.

Equipped with 5.1 ch external input terminals, you can enjoy Dolby Digital by connecting to a DVD player with a built-in Dolby Digital Decoder.

Equipped with on-screen function, you can operate while watching the monitor screen.

Equipped with 6 types of surround programs that can be enjoyed even in movies, music videos and CDs.

Discrete configuration is used for the amplifier circuit.

Built-in FM/AM tuner for AM stereo.
Equipped with FM auto-preset function and 40-station preset function.

You can connect three AM devices and three audio devices.

Built-in functions such as sleep timer.

By combining the separately sold Dolby digital / cinema DSP processor DDP-10, you can enjoy 3 sound field cinema DSPs.

It comes with a wireless remote controller that can operate Yamaha components.
