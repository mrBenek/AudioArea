  • SONY SBF-77

  • ¥ 49,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

A stereo receiver with video input.

It has two video inputs and can be copied from video 1 to video 2.

It uses a direct access tuning method with 10 keys, and you can directly input the frequency of the broadcasting station you want to receive.

FM/AM random 10-station memory is installed.

It is equipped with a memory scan function that allows you to check preset stations one after another.

Equipped with a centralized display by FL tube.
