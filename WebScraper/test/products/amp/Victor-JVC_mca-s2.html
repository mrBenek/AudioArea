Commentary

Pre-main amplifier that adopts pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages.

All stages are directly connected to pure complimentary service OCL circuit, and coupling capacitors and output capacitors are completely eliminated. As a result, sufficient NF is applied from ultra-low band to ultra-high band, reducing distortion and improving low-band characteristics.

Equipped with the Sound Field Composer System, you can enjoy 2-channel sources such as 2-channel records, tapes and FM broadcasting with 4-channel realism. You can also use the selector to switch between Hall tone and Matrix.

Equipped with a subsonic filter.

The tone control uses the Klix/Klukš top volume, and the left and right channels can be adjusted independently.

Equipped with a muting switch that reduces the volume to 1/10 with one touch.

Equipped with a mode switch for changing between stereo and mono.

It is equipped with a protective circuit that uses relays to protect speakers and transistors.
