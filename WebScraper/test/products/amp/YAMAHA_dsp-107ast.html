  • YAMAHA DSP-107AST

  • ¥ 139,800 (around 1990)

Commentary

Digital sound field processor with power amplifiers for all 7 channels.

Equipped with all power amplifiers for the main, effect and center channels, it is possible to play sound fields of 11 programs and 20 patterns.
In addition, 6 channels, except for the center, are compatible with Yamaha active servo speakers.

A learning remote controller is included.
