  • Marantz PM-44SE

  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1995)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier of special edition series.

The power amplifier section has a symmetrical independent monoblock configuration.

The chassis is copper plated to cut high-frequency noise.

A noise killer is installed in the power supply circuit to cut off rectification noise.

Equipped with a source direct switch.

Equipped with a banana plug speaker jack for bi-wiring connection.
