Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier with improved cost performance while applying traditional Marantz technology.

Fairchild P for the output transistorD200W is used.
In addition to the VU meter, it is equipped with peak indicators for the left and right channels independently, and it warns with red lighting of current limiting circuit operation in case of instantaneous clip or abnormal low impedance.

There were Walnut wood cabinet and rack mount adapter as an option sold separately.
