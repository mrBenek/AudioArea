  • MARK LEVINSON ML-12L

  • ¥ 720,000 (released in 1982)

Commentary

A control amplifier developed for use only as a pair with the ML-11L.

The ML-11L is designed to be powered by two DC cables from the ML-11L without a power supply unit, thereby reducing costs while maintaining performance.

The basic circuit and parts used are equivalent to the ML-10L. The gain and impedance of the MC/MM cartridge can be changed with the internal switch and loading socket.

The left and right volumes are separated to increase sound purity.

When the ML-11L is used as a bridge, it is supplied with power from two ML-11L units and more stable operation is possible.
