  • Technics SU-8044G

  • ¥ 44,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier improved from SU-8044.

A single-package dual transistor with uniform characteristics is adopted at the first stage of the power amplifier. It is operated by a current mirror load which can select an operation current with low distortion, and is fed to the voltage amplifier stage by an emitter follower.

A wired microphone and a wireless microphone can be used at the same time. In addition, a mixing recording terminal is installed to record mixed sounds and music as they are.
In addition, the microphone / echo independent volume allows adjustment of the depth of sound and echo from the microphone.

Equipped with an independent Rec/Input selector.
