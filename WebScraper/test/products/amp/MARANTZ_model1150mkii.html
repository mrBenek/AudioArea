  • Marantz Model1150mkII

  • ¥ 129,000 (around 1977)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier which is an improved version of Model1150.

A low-row-cage type coupling capacitor is used in the pre-amplifier section to pursue linearity.

The control stage is equipped with a variety of functions to cope with the diversification of program sources and environmental conditions.

The power supply section uses a 56,000 μ F large-capacity filter condenser.

We had two wood cabinets, Walnut and Mahogany, and an adapter for rack mounting as an option sold separately.
