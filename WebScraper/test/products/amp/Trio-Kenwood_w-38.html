  • TRIO W-38

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1961)

Commentary

An integrated stereo tri-amplifier capable of receiving FM/AM/SW (short wave) broadcasting.

The vacuum tube of the equalizer circuit is fully DC ignited by the cathode current of the output tube to prevent hum noise.

Equipped with an FM tuner circuit for the FM stereo broadcast transmission.

It is equipped with functions such as a loudness control and a rumble filter eliminate unpleasant low-frequency noise such as motor gobos and howling.

It is equipped with a one connection tape recorder terminal agreed with manufacturers such as Sony and Akai.

The station selection of the radio is equipped with Magic Eye, and accurate station selection is possible.
