  • SONY TA-AX22

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with built-in sound effect with display.

It is equipped with a sound effect that can control tone with a single switch.
In addition, fine manual control is also possible.

Equipped with two tape terminals that can be copied from tape 1 to tape 2.

Power can be supplied to the sound mixer MX-22.
