Commentary

Control amplifier equipped with VGCA.

Uses a newly developed low-noise amplifier VGCA (Variable Gain Control Amplifier).
In VGCA, we have adopted a method in which the gain of the amplifier itself is changed instead of the amplifier configuration in which the signal is reduced by the input volume and then amplified, which was used in conventional amplifiers. This reduces noise and deterioration of dynamic range even when the volume is reduced.

A Ni-cd battery is used as the power supply for the audio circuit to further improve sound quality.

Equipped with a 6-channel input jack that supports multi-channel DVD audio.
There is also a 6-channel output jack that allows you to control the levels of all channels with the main volume.

The power supply is equipped with an R-core transformer that minimizes magnetic flux leakage.

The power supply section is equipped with a TAKEII electrolytic capacitor.

The relay is equipped with a silver-palladium / gold-clad contact relay.

Equipped with two pre-out terminals for bi-amping.

Equipped with a wired remote function that enables you to turn the power of the power amplifier (SE-A1010) on / off.

Uses a high-capacity OFC power cord with polarity display.

System remote control is included.
