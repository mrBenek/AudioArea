  • Technics SU-4100

  • ¥ 68,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

2ch/4ch pre-main amplifier developed based on SU-3000.

The main amplifier section consists of all stages of directly connected OCL circuits.
The first stage consists of a low-noise PNP transistor with uniform characteristics, and the output terminal is stabilized together with a d-circle reduction circuit using a zener diode.
This main amplifier section can also be used as a 2-channel high-power amplifier with BTL connection by switching the switch.

The equalizer stage consists of a PNP-NPN2-stage direct connection circuit using specially selected low-noise transistors.

The tone control circuit is composed of the main part.

Equipped with an AFD circuit that can adjust the matrix 4 ch.
It also has a 0 ° / 90 ° phase shifter.

Equipped with rotary type low / high filter.

Equipped with 2 pairs of front and rear headphone jacks.

Equipped with a microphone terminal.

Equipped with a preset volume and four sub-volumes with independent adjustment for each channel.

The indicator lights up when playing 4 ch.

A 4 ch balancer can be connected.

There was a wood case sold separately.
