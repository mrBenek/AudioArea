Commentary

A stereo power amplifier with an independent power amplifier section of the PMA 300.

The circuit configuration employs an OCL circuit that connects all the circuits directly and drives the speakers directly without passing through a capacitor.
In order to improve the reliability and stability of this direct circuit, we have adopted a monolithic IC that is stable against temperature and voltage fluctuations and introduced a differential amplifier circuit.
