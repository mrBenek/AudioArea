Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with side terminal.

All connection terminals are provided on the side panel for easy connection even when installed on a shelf or rack.
The input / output terminal is on the right side of the connector and the speaker terminal is on the left side.

All stages are directly connected OCL circuit.

Equipped with a panel handle.
