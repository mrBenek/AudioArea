Commentary

The NII series pre-main amplifier has improved sound quality by analyzing the dynamic characteristics of all stages of the amplifier and enhancing the power supply and ground line.

In order to improve the separation of the pre-main amplifier in the NII series, the correlation between the vector analysis of the separation characteristics and the audibility is thoroughly clarified. The NII series is designed so that the absolute value of the separation is kept to a value that is sufficiently negligible, and the phase is around which the most natural audibility can be obtained.

The power supply section uses a 1.6 mm thick bare earth line made of pure copper and a 5.5 mm copper wire equivalent to five 50-core wires, and two 15,000 μ F electrolytic capacitors dedicated to the power supply with low earth impedance.
As a result, the rise and fall of the middle and low frequencies, which are important for the excitement of the basic rhythm of music, are improved.

The power amplifier circuit employs a single-stage differential constant-current drive OTL circuit that has been developed to reduce the rise characteristics and distortion, especially to improve linearity.
Linearity (h), especially for the driver stageFEAnd high frequency characteristics (CobF)TThe power band width characteristic, which greatly affects audibility, has been extended up to 100 kHz.

The tone amplifier circuit is an operational amplifier type circuit with a differential 1-stage 3-chip configuration.
By selecting an optimum time constant, ensuring a dynamic range in an open loop, and improving the SN ratio, we have achieved a low-distortion design with excellent response characteristics regardless of tone ON / OFF.

The equalizer circuit uses an operational amplifier with a single 3-chip configuration at the final stage.

The power supply of the preamplifier uses a feedback-stabilized ± 2-conductor Minamoto no Sadamu voltage circuit with a fluctuation rate of 1.7%. This circuit is carefully designed to avoid the influence of power supply fluctuations.

The volume control and tone control use a precision trimming Attenuator volume with an interlocking error of ± 0.5 dB.
The volume can be freely set in the middle position with 32 contacts, and the tone can be adjusted in 10 2 db steps.
