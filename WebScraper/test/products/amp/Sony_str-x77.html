Commentary

A stereo receiver with extensive preset functions.

You can preset the desired program source to the 12 buttons with the best sound quality.

It uses all-push control that can operate 39 functions with 33 buttons.

The amplifier section uses a legato linear system.

Pulse power supply is used.

The tuner section uses a quartz lock synthesizer with a high-speed PLL IC.
