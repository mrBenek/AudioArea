  • ¥ 49,800 (released in December 1984)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier of Brid series which adopted Duo β circuit.

The Duo β circuit is a technology that first enhances the bare characteristics of the amplifier by simplifying the amplification stage, and then tunes the amplifier by applying an appropriate amount of NFB. It is a system that is simple and easy to bring out the goodness of the material.

NF type is used for tone control.

Built-in equalizer amplifier for MM and MC types.
