Commentary

Stereo pre-main amplifier using V-FET.

The power amplifier section is composed of 8 Nch/Pch V-FETs, pure complimentary service and parallel push-pull DC-amplifier.

The equalizer stage employs an ultra-low noise LEC transistor and a small-signal V-FET with good linearity.

We have adopted direct coupling and DC unit amplifier in order to achieve "faithful waveform transmission" which is the design policy of the ES-II series.
