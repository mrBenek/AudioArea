  • DENON PMA-930V

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with a non-feedback power amplifier.

The power amplifier section is a non-feedback 0 db amplifier in which the voltage amplifier stage and the power amplifier stage are completely electrically separated to reduce dynamic distortion such as TIM distortion and all kinds of distortion. Moreover, the effect of the back electromotive force from the speaker on the predriver stage is suppressed.
In addition, Pc100W power transistor is adopted to obtain high power.

A multi-shielded power transformer and -shielded power transformer and a 16,400 μ F block capacitor.

By using a super equalizer in the equalizer amplifier section, a wide bandwidth is achieved.
In addition, the MC/MM is switched by the in-flight remote control to prevent deterioration of the SN ratio due to wiring.

It is equipped with wide-band real-time tone control with limited quality degradation and subsonic filter.

Equipped with high-speed protection circuit.
