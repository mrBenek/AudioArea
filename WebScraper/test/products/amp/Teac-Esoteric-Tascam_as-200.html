Commentary

This pre-main amplifier was developed with the aim of becoming an amplifier for the tape deck by examining the function which can be integrated with the tape deck.

The mode selection switch is also linked to the recording output circuit, and it is possible to change the stereo tape to a mono tape and record by switching the switch.
Also, if you have 3 tape decks, you can record 3 tapes at a time.

The power amplifier section employs a DC amplifier (differential amplifier) to improve various characteristics.

The power supply unit uses a cut core double power supply (+ 40 v ~ 0 v ~ -40V).
As a result, two output integrated capacitors are removed, and movement and fluctuation of DC operating point caused by excessive input, fluctuation of power supply voltage, and deterioration of transistor are suppressed.

As a protective circuit, a thermo breaker is mounted on the output terminal side in addition to a general protective circuit.
This breaker is delayed with a time constant so that it does not operate with only a transient pulse-like sound.

The preamp section contains an FET input circuit that matches any external device and solves the problem of low dynamic margin that was the fate of conventional transistor amplifiers.

The Phono input uses a 3-stage NPN-PNP-NPN direct-coupled circuit with silicon transistors.
This makes it possible to apply a large amount of NF, reducing distortion and expanding dynamic range.

Equipped with a push-button input selector for one touch selection, a tone diff switch, and two speaker outputs, main / remote.

Only switches that are frequently used are placed on the front panel, and those that are not frequently used are placed in the lower panel.
