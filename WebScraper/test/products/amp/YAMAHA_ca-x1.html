  • YAMAHA CA-X1

  • ¥ 49,500 (around 1976)

Commentary

This pre-main amplifier was developed with the theme of "Silent and clear sound that expresses the reality of stage sound alive and hot".

For the CA-X1, a new measuring instrument has been introduced under the theme of reducing the absolute value of the distortion factor by one order of magnitude, realizing a distortion factor that is one order of magnitude lower than the conventional one.

The power amplifier section uses a pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages of differential-input power supplies with a current mirror circuit.

The equalizer amplifier uses a 4-stage differential input direct-coupled circuit that combines a current mirror circuit.

Equipped with a Rec out selector, you can record FM broadcasts while listening to a record.
