  • SANSUI AU - α 305R

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier of the α -07 series with the technology polished by the α -05 series.

The well-balanced layout of the transmission and amplification circuits reduces the adverse effect on sound quality.

Equipped with CD direct function to transmit CD input directly to power amplifier section.

There are 2 color variations of gold and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
This remote control can operate Sansui CDs, Tuners, and Decks.
