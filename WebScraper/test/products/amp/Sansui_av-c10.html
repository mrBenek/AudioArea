  • SANSUI AV-C10

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

An AV center equipped with surround and equalizer functions.

The Surround feature lets you choose between Theater Surround mode for movies and QS Surround mode for live, as well as Simulated Stereo mode for stereo mono sources.

Equipped with 2-channel amplifier.
This amp can be used either as a front or rear amp.

Equipped with a 5-band graphic equalizer.

Built-in RF converter.

Equipped with an audio insert switch, you can enjoy BGV (background visual), etc.

It has an external processor switch and can be connected to video processors, graphic equalizers, dbx units, PCM processors, etc.

Surround output terminal is mounted.
