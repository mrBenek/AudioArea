Commentary

A stereo AV amplifier developed using Marantz's pure audio technology.

Equipped with 6.1 es and CS (circle surround) decoder in addition to Dolby Digital and DTS.

It has the same twin monaural construction configuration as the Hi-Fi stereo amplifier, and is equipped with 2 channels of full-scale current feedback type power amplifier equipped with Marantz original HDAM.

It has a 4-box chassis structure in which the audio amplifier, DSP, video amplifier, microcomputer and display are separated. It also has a rigid case with vibration control structure made of luxurious aluminum material.

Flexible configurations are available, from single virtual amplifiers to full-channel systems with combined power amplifiers.

Parts such as super ring core and toroidal power transformer, speaker terminal made by WBT and warm-up meter are adopted.

The decoder / processor section adopts a high-spec 3 dsp configuration.

This is the world's first AV amplifier to incorporate Dolby headphones, which provide superior extra-head positioning.

It is the first in the world to incorporate SRS Ultra Surround and CS-Virtual technology.

LCD display learning remote controller with macro function is included.
