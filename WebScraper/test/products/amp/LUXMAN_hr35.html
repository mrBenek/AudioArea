Audio Database

  • LUXMAN HR35

  • ¥ 33,000 (released in January 1963)

Commentary

A small-power tri-amplifier developed for low cost but high quality amplifier.

A preamplifier compatible with magnetic type cartridge is adopted.

Equipped with a scratch filter and a rumble filter.

Equipped with a universal terminal to which a reverb amplifier, etc. can be connected.

Equipped with a terminal for tape recording.
