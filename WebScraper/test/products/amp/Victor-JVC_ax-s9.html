Commentary

Integrated amplifier with all-stage differential signal transmission.

We focus on differential signal transmission, which can cancel noise components generated in digital devices and amplifiers and amplify only music signals, and we have adopted this method in all stages.
The ground line, through which signal current and noise current used to flow in common, is separated into a dedicated music signal line and a dedicated noise line, and the music signal line is independent from the input terminal to the power amplifier section, including the volume control. This makes it possible to transmit music signals with higher purity.
In addition, the high-power transistor is a parallel push-pull type with four single-channel high-power transistors, while reducing the rated output to give priority to quality.

The Advanced Super A is a further evolution of Victor's original Super A to bring out the characteristics of a transistor, which is a current amplifying element.
The effect of secondary distortion of the power supply current is suppressed.

In order to eliminate vibration of the transformer itself, we have developed and adopted a transformer base made of high carbon cast iron with high damping capacity.
The transformer base not only functions as a base for the transformer, but also penetrates through the base plate and serves as a foot for the amplifier body.
In addition, the transformer itself is polished as the path of the music signal, and a core bobbin transformer is adopted. There is no gap in the coil and vibration inside the transformer is eliminated.

In order to solve the problem at once, we have adopted the all-input relay switching source selector in order to solve the problem of the source selector, which has caused the complexity of signal lines and the deterioration of sound quality due to contact points due to the concentration of various input signals.
The relay uses a gold-clad silver-palladium contact and has a completely sealed structure, eliminating the deterioration of sound quality and aging due to contact.

Power amplifier section with L/R separation configuration up to the power amplifier board to completely eliminate mutual interference.

Equipped with a CD differential input terminal (with a selector button) that eliminates noise upstream from the CD player.

Equipped with a phono equalizer amplifier for MM/MC.

Equipped with speaker terminal for gold plated banana plug.
