Commentary

High-speed DC integrated amplifier.

A newly developed high-speed circuit has been adopted to improve the waveform collapse and turbulence that are likely to occur at high speed.

A straight DC amplifier configuration is adopted without a low-frequency cut capacitor in the signal path from the Tuner (Aux) terminal to the SP terminal or in the NF loop.

The MC head amplifier section adopts a complimentary service push-pull circuit for all stages using a low-noise transistor for the first stage.

The power supply section uses a large power transformer and four 6,800 μ F high-capacity electrolytic capacitors, and two power supplies on the left and right sides to reduce mutual interference.
