Commentary

MFB stereo integrated amplifier with FM multi-circuit.

MFB system is adopted.

6BQ5 is used for the output tube.

Full DC ignition is adopted for the preamplifier section.

It supports 3 bands.

Ferrite antenna is built-in.

Equipped with AFC, loudness control, low / high-cut filter, mode switch and stereo eye.
