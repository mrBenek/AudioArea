Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with further reduced distortion by adding a pure NFB circuit to Super A.

The A-X7D has a simple two amp configuration with only an equalizer and a power amp. One amp configuration with full DC between the Tuner, Aux, Tape and SP. out.

Super A circuit is used for the power amplifier section.
This circuit achieves both A-class sound quality and B-class power efficiency and high power by automatically controlling the current flowing through the power device according to the input signal, and improves bare characteristics by minimizing phase correction by using a wide-band element.
In addition, the A-X7D is equipped with a pure NFB circuit in the NFB circuit. In the pure NFB circuit, the capacitor for phase compensation of the signal circuit, which had been absolutely necessary to stably apply NFB, B, is removed. Taking advantage of the excellent bare characteristics of Super A, the NFB circuit performs two stage phase compensation. As a result, even NFB can be stably applied to ultra-high frequencies, which was impossible with the conventional method, and the flat range of distortion factor and damping factor is greatly expanded.

The equalizer amplifier uses an ICL circuit using an extra-low noise pair EL-FET unique to JVC.
It is a high-gain A-class circuit that can be used for MC cartridges only by switching the switch.

The power supply section is equipped with a high-capacity transformer and a high-density capacitor for audio. Combined with a direct power supply system in which the power supply is directly connected to each circuit, the power supply impedance is reduced over a wide band.
In addition, copper bus bars with sufficient current capacity are used for power supply lines to prevent magnetic distortion caused by large currents.

The A-X Series unique non-clearance volume knob has a new illuminated pointer.
This pointer also serves as a protection indicator and blinks when the power supply is turned on or when the protection circuit is operating due to an output short circuit.

Self-illuminated indicators are used for the function switches.

It is equipped with tone control that minimizes unnecessary changes in the ultra-low and ultra-high ranges.

The Tape2 connector is installed not only in the rear panel but also in the sealing pocket of the front panel to improve operability.

Equipped with a subsonic filter that prevents sound quality degradation by eliminating a large-capacity condenser.
