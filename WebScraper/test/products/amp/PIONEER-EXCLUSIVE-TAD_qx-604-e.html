Commentary

A 4-channel receiver equipped with 3-format (CD-4/RM/SQ) 4-channel playback function.

With both RM and SQ matrix decoders and built-in demodulator for reproducing CD-4 discrete 4 ch, all 4 ch sources can be reproduced. In addition, 2 ch sources can be effectively reproduced by passing through the matrix decoder.

The CD-4 demodulator uses PLLs and FETs to give sufficient consideration to frequency characteristics and separation. It also incorporates an ASC circuit that automatically compensates for noise and improves S/N even for frequently used records.

The tone control uses a double volume, and both Bass and Treble can control the front and rear separately.

By using the balance control composed of 3 volumes, you can control the volume adjustment of front left and right, rear left and right, front and back.

The FM front-end is equipped with an FET, and the IF section is equipped with an IC and ceramic filter to improve the interference rejection capability. In addition, it is equipped with an on/off switching type FM muting to cut unnecessary long-distance stations.

4 ch CD-4, RM, SQ and 2 ch are displayed on the dial by switching the mode switch and lighting the lamp simultaneously.

Equipped with a loudness contour and headphone terminal.
