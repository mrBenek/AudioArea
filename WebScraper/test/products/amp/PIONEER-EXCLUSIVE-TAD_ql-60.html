  • Pioneer QL-60

Commentary

You can enjoy 4-channel stereo by connecting to 2-channel system with a quadrariser amplifier.

With a built-in クォドラライザー, you can enjoy 4-channel matrix recording.
You can also enjoy 4-channel sound fields using conventional 2-channel stereo sources, FM, or your own tape records.

A pre-main amplifier for the rear channel is built in.
A quasi complimentary service circuit is used and a tone control circuit is mounted.

You can adjust the balance of each channel with the level meter and preset level control for each channel.
You can adjust the volume of all channels simultaneously using the master volume.

It includes discrete 4-channel circuitry and connects 4-channel open reel decks and 8-track cartridge tape decks.
A discrete 4-ch disk can also be played using an adapter.

QL-60 seems to have early and late models.
QL-60 is also listed in the catalog of the 1971 general stereo amplifier (printed in May / June, not owned by us). The one listed here seems to be the early model. Among the listed specifications, preamplifier output, input sensitivity, impedance, number of semiconductors, etc. are different. This seems to be because it was revised for improvement in the later stage.
