Commentary

Compact size control amplifier.

IC is used for all input changeover switches.
By turning the circuit ON/OFF with a minute DC voltage, we have succeeded in suppressing switching noise without affecting frequency characteristics.

± 12 db adjustment is possible for each frequency of 30 Hz, 750 Hz and 10 kHz, and the sound quality adjustment is free.

The loudness control can be controlled continuously with the "LOUD" knob.
The FLAT position provides maximum volume and a flat frequency response, while turning it to the right reduces volume and increases the loudness effect.
The Loudness effect is a system that controls the balance between volume and quality by increasing the low-frequency range to match the characteristics of the human ear when listening at a lower volume.
