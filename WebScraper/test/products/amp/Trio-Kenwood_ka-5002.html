  • TRIO KA-5002

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1971)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with common basic circuit with KA-7002.

Pure complimentary service circuit directly connected to all stages is adopted for the circuit structure.

Equipped with a head amplifier for low output cartridge.

Equipped with input / output jacks and control volume for 4D stereo.

Load impedance selection switch for MM is mounted.
