Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier that realizes as good open-loop characteristics as possible and applies extremely low NFB.

The circuit structure and power supply that guarantee perfectly symmetrical operation are enhanced, and the dynamic characteristics are improved with an emphasis on overall balance.

It can be used as a BTL connected monaural power amplifier.

Equipped with an LED peak indicator that can be used as a pilot lamp.
