  • Technics ST-K806

  • ¥ 62,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner amplifier developed as a sister unit of the ST-K808.
The quartz microcomputer timer is omitted.

As with the ST-K808, you can precisely control the stereo system based on infrared and manual commands.

It is equipped with a quartz synthesizer tuner that realizes 8 preset FM/AM stations and accurate tuning.

Equipped with FM/AM muting and microphone level volume.

Equipped with connection terminal for FM wing antenna SH-F100.
