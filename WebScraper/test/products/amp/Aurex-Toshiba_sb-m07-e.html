  • Aurex SB-M07

  • ¥ 64,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with two power amplifiers.

Two types of amplifiers, low power and high power, are used for the output section, allowing for a wide dynamic range source.

A gain-change-type equalizer amplifier is used.

The structure is designed to minimize interference between each unit, and the signal path is minimized to ensure sound quality.
