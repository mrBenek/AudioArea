  • Discretionary price (around ¥ 20,000, released at the end of March 2006)

Commentary

An integrated amplifier developed to easily enjoy music data from DAP or PC.

By rationally arranging the signal path from the signal input stage to the selector, volume, and amplifier stage, more faithful signal amplification is achieved.
The power supply section uses a power transformer with a copper short band to reduce leakage flux and a large-capacity capacitor.

It is equipped with an N. B. circuit that can adjust the amplification amount and frequency band of the low sound according to the installation environment and the size of the speaker. It can be adjusted by selecting three stages of amplification circuits with different center frequencies to be amplified.

The front, rear, left and right panels are made of 3.5 mm thick aluminum extruding material to increase the rigidity of the case and reduce the influence of external vibration.

The speaker terminal is equipped with a screw terminal that also supports banana plugs.

Vertical installation is also possible by the attached stand.
