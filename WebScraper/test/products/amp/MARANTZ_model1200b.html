  • Marantz Model 1200B

  • ¥ 325,000 (around 1974)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier that combines pre-amplifier Model3300 and power amplifier Model240 into one.

Equipped with a high / low filter with two cutoff frequencies.

Marantz's unique variable overlap drive is adopted.

Walnut cabinet and rack mount adapter were sold separately as an option.
