Commentary

Integrated amplifier with perfect balance design.

The amplifier has a fully balanced configuration using four independent amplifier circuits, which eliminates the effect of transmission noise and enables stable namp operation and high power output.

A 1,800 VA large-capacity toroidal power transformer is used for the power supply.
We have also adopted power filter condensers for audio equipment.

In addition to two XLR balanced inputs, the input / output terminals are equipped with Line 5 RCA unbalanced inputs, tape out, pre-out, etc.
The processor loop also connects surround processors for your home theater.

The display section is equipped with a dot matrix display.

Solid aluminum is used for the front panel.

Speaker terminal made by WBT is used.

Wireless remote control is included.
