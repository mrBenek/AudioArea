Commentary

The Laboratory series monaural power amplifier was developed based on the idea of Isolated Power Train = non-interference energy amplification.

In pursuit of the ideal material for the amplifier chassis, and in pursuit of high damping and high rigidity, the 33 kg heavy cast iron chassis is adopted.
Using this chassis as a mechanical earth, the four major mechanical parts of the power amplifier, power transformer, power condenser, heat sink and foot are directly attached.
In addition, connecting rigidity of heavy parts such as power transformer and heat sink is increased by through bolt.
As a result, we succeeded in suppressing the vibration of the part, and it becomes a base of non-interference amplification as a powerful mechanical earth.

The conventional variable bias type output stage, Super A, has been reconsidered, and the Advanced Super A has been adopted. It not only achieves high power and high quality, but also eliminates crossover distortion, switching distortion, and nonlinear distortion of the output transistor, and realizes wide bandwidth and stability of output impedance.
This enables non-feedback operation of the output circuit.
In addition, high power of 1000W/2 Ω can be obtained with high stability by 4 parallel push-pull of bipolar transistor by advanced output circuit design.

Equipped with a new Gm circuit with an emphasis on dynamic characteristics.
This makes it possible to achieve both superior performance and higher linearity than the conventional major loop feedback type.
In addition, the balance input is operated in the current mode for both negative and positive input signals, and a balance input circuit with a high CMRR is realized without adjustment, enabling fast music playback.

A real-time thermotracer is used.
The temperature of the output stage is detected, and based on this temperature, the temperature of the modularized advanced super A circuit is controlled to realize remote and high-precision temperature compensation.

2 box construction Equipped with AC primary isolated power supply.
In order to avoid interference with the secondary side, the AC primary side has a box structure made of cast iron that is molded independently and integrally with the front lower part of the cast iron chassis.
In addition, the AC100V cable is 3.5m3Power cable made of 6N material is used.

Uses ultra-high-speed, large-capacity, and rectifier diodes to handle large instantaneous power with plenty of margin.

Strong electric parts are used across the board to minimize energy loss.
