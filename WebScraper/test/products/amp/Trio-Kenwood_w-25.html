  • TRIO W-25

  • ¥ 25,900 (around 1960?)

Commentary

A stereo tri-amplifier that combines a 10W stereo amplifier with an AM/SW tuner.

It supports monaural / stereo AM broadcasting, monaural / stereo recording and shortwave broadcasting.

You can adjust the sound by adjusting the volume of 2 pairs of amplifiers with the clutch type knob.
It is also possible to adjust the left and right separately.

Pickup is compatible with small output magnetic pickup and crystal pickup.

Equipped with an external input terminal, FM tuner and tape recorder can be connected.

It can be attached to wooden cabinet or furniture by removing the case.
