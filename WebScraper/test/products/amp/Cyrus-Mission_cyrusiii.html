  • Cyrus/Mission Cyrus III

  • ¥ 110,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

An integrated amplifier based on CYRUS I and CYRUS II with further improvements.

A 180 VA toroidal transformer is used for the power supply.
By adopting a unique configuration for the core of this transformer, mechanical noise is suppressed to 0.

By using a patented slit foil condenser with small eddy currents and a star earth system that concentrates the earth point at one point, we have obtained accurate localization and stable sound images.

It is designed to adjust volume and balance through an 8-bit microprocessor.

Magnesium / aluminum die-cast chassis is adopted for the chassis, which is resistant to disturbance vibration and magnetostriction and has improved heat radiation efficiency.

DC power supply unit PSX-R is available.
