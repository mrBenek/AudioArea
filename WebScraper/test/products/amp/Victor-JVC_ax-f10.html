Commentary

A digital AV amplifier equipped with DEUS, a unique technology of JVC.

The amplifier section is equipped with DEUS which is Victor's original D-class amplifier.
DEUS uses Victor's unique hybrid feedback technology, which combines digital feedback and analog feedback.
The digital feedback constantly monitors the PWM modulation and the analog feedback eliminates the effects of noise and distortion introduced during the digital amplification process and the back-emf of the connected speakers.
In addition, a newly developed power module with greatly improved switching capability is installed in the output stage, and the multi-pole compensation technology has been introduced to further improve sound quality. As a result, it achieves high sound quality while consuming less than half the power consumption of a same class analog amplifier and achieving a compact size.

With Smart Surround Setup Ver2.0, you can easily set up your multi-channel speakers.
With this function, you just need to clap your hands at the listening point and you can adjust 6 speakers.

It is equipped with Dolby Digital EX and DTS-ES compatible with 6.1 ch DVD, DTS 96/24 capable of 96kHz/24bit multi-channel playback, and MPEG-2 AAC decoder used for BS / terrestrial digital broadcasting.
DTS NEO : 6 expands 2 ch signal to 6.1 ch and Dolby Pro Logic II expands to 5.1 ch.

Equipped with a D4 input Route 3 for high-definition and progressive high-definition video signals.

Comes with a luminous multi-brand remote controller.
