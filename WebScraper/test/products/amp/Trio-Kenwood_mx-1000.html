  • TRIO MX-1000

  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1970)

Commentary

A multi-channel IDS amplifier that integrates a stereo main amplifier and a crossover network.

2-way multi-amplifier configuration is possible by adding to the amplifier you have.

An NF-type 12dB/oct. crossover network is used.

Equipped with power ON/OFF, loudness control and tone control.

It has a headphone port.
