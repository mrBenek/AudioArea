  • Technics SA-R80

  • ¥ 167,200 (released in 1982)

Commentary

Cassette receiver with integrated deck, tuner and amplifier.

By direct operation, the input selector is automatically switched by simply performing the play operation of the deck and the start operation of the player (when combined with SL-DL5).

A new class A amplifier configuration with linear feedback.

The tuner section uses a quartz synthesizer system with seven preset FM and AM stations.

IC logic deck is adopted.

The mixing mechanism that can be used for wireless microphones is equipped with BBD electronic echo.

By combining with the remote control unit SH-R801, it can be operated from a remote location.
