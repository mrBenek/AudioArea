  • VICTOR/JVC JA-S35

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

35 series pre-main amplifier.

The volume is a 21-point click volume.

A differential amplifier circuit with a current mirror load is adopted at the first stage of the power amplifier section.

There is a Tape2 terminal on the front panel for easy connection of deck, SEA, microphone and mixing adapter, etc.

Uses a relay-type protection circuit.

Equipped with 2-system speaker selector.
