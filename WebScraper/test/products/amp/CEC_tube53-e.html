Commentary

Integrated amplifier announced as the first vacuum tube amplifier by CEC.

6L6GC is used for the output tube. The 6L6GC is a vacuum tube with improved stability and reliability based on the 6L6 released by RCA in the US in the 1930 s. The 6L6GC is a vacuum tube with improved stability and reliability.

The power amplifier section forms an inverted signal with a Mulard inversion circuit. The four 6L6GCs use two push-pull circuits for each channel. The self-bias circuit also extends the life of the output tube.
By keeping the NFB as a whole to the minimum necessary, we can obtain a denser music playback.

The output transformer is specially designed and hand-finished by experienced engineers using an Orient Core material. Phase rotation is extremely small and straight sound quality is obtained.

The power transformer uses a toroidal type with excellent start-up characteristics.
As a countermeasure against noise, the transformer body is fixed to a strong steel case with a filler to completely eliminate leakage flux.

Low-noise parts are carefully selected for capacitors and resistors as well as low-distortion type volume.

It uses a solid aluminum material and a curved glass shield made by a glassmaker's technique.
