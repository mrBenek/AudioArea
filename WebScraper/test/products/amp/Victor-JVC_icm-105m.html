Commentary

105 series 4-channel power amplifier developed for multi-channel playback.

This is the first power amplifier that uses four power ICs.
The power IC used in the ICM 105 m has a built-in semi-heat circuit, and is a hybrid type (hybrid integrated circuit), so it is compact with high power and no worries about complimentary service.

It uses a 4-channel source system that can be connected to a multi-source tape deck such as MTR-10M to drive.

Equipped with 4 VU meters.
The meter is equipped with a sensitivity change-over switch, and 3-switching is possible according to the input.

Equipped with 4 input adjustment volumes.
