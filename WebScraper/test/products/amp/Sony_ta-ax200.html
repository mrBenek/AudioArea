  • SONY TA-AX200

  • ¥ 29,800 (released in 1983)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with built-in sound effects.

Equipped with a sound effect that allows you to select tone control according to the source with one button.
Also, manual control is possible.

A simple and straight transmission method is used to minimize the routing of unnecessary wiring materials.

Equipped with two tape terminals capable of tape copying.
