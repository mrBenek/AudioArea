  • ONKYO Integra 225

  • ¥ 62,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

This receiver has been developed with an emphasis on sound quality. For example, the main amplifier section is directly connected to all stages and the FM-MPX circuit is a monolithic IC.

The tuner section is designed with 4-row varicon, and the IF section is designed with 6-element mechanical filter, 4-stage limiter including differential amplifier, etc.

The amplifier section takes advantage of the Integra701 technology and uses a direct-coupled differential amplifier circuit for all stages.
