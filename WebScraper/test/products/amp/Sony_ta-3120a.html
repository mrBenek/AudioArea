Audio Database

  • SONY TA-3120A

  • ¥ 52,000 (released in April 1967)

Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier that is manufactured by taking out only the main amplifier of the TA 1120 a to support multi-channel systems.
The performance and standard are the same.

A quasi complimentary service SEPPP OTL circuit is used for the circuit system.
