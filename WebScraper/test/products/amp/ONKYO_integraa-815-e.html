  • ONKYO Integra A-815

  • ¥ 56,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

Cost-performance machine that adopts W / Super Servo system, dual direct amplifier system, etc.

The W / Super Servo system is adopted in which all components caused by the inside of the amplifier are canceled by the feedback loop of the servo operational amplifier separately provided.
As a result, harmful components such as ultra-low frequency components below 1 to 2 Hz and noise electromotive force due to impedance caused by power supply capacitors, ground patterns, wiring, etc. are canceled, and the purity of the input music signal is increased.

The A-815 uses a linear switching method for amplification.
Although the basic operating characteristics of this system are high efficiency class B, the amplification part of the two transistors is made smooth, and harmful crossover distortion hardly occurs. Thus, the system has operating characteristics equivalent to class A.

Compatible with MC cartridges. In addition to the head amplifier and step-up transformer, it is equipped with a straight equalizer amplifier designed mainly for MC.
In addition, the tone circuit and buffer amplifier are omitted, and a 2-amplifier configuration is used for direct input to a high-gain power amplifier, giving priority to sound quality.

It is equipped with accessory circuits such as a 5-stage mode selector, a subsonic filter that cuts low frequencies below 15 kHz, a high-cut filter connected directly to the Treble control, a loudness switch, and a 2-channel speaker selector switch.

It uses a direct tone system composed only of passive elements.

In order to avoid high-order harmonic distortion, we use non-ferrous metals instead of magnetic materials such as iron for mechanical parts.
