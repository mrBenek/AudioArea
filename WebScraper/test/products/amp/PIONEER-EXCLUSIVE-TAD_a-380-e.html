  • Pioneer A-380

  • ¥ 44,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with non-switching circuit.

Pioneer's proprietary non-switching circuit is used in the power amplifier to eliminate switching distortion, which adversely affects sound quality.
In addition, multiple differential feedback is also employed to achieve a lower distortion factor.

A DC servo circuit is used for the equalizer to realize a non-coupling capacitor.

A line-straight-switch is also installed to simplify the signal path and achieve higher purity sound.
