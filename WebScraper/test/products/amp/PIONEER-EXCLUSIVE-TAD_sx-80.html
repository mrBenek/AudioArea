Commentary

AM/FM multi integrated stereo amplifier equipped with stereo / mono automatic switching mechanism.

The FM tuner uses a front end of the cascode system combined with a 3-row varicon using a Newby star. This part is also thoroughly shielded to minimize unwanted radiation (spurious).
The IF amplifier consists of four stages, and the FM detector uses a wide-band ratio-detection method suitable for FM stereo broadcast transmission reception. The AFC circuit ensures stable broadcast reception at all times.
In addition, it is equipped with 3 stages of limiter circuit to remove noise, and it always promises beautiful broadcast reception.

It is equipped with a muting circuit, and noise between stations can be removed.

Switching system is adopted for FM multi-circuit.
The stereo indicator lights during FM stereo reception.

It is equipped with an automatic mono-stereo switching mechanism. If the selector knob is set to FM-AUTO, it automatically switches to stereo when an FM stereo broadcast transmission is received.

The AM tuner uses a 3-row varicon with excellent selectivity, and a compact bar antenna with ferrite core is used for the antenna. In areas near broadcasting stations and with strong radio waves, you can receive signals without using an external antenna.
The intermediate frequency section is equipped with a Sharp-Broad switch that allows you to change the sound quality according to your preference.

With a built-in equalizer for NAB characteristics, you can easily play 4-track recorded tapes by directly connecting the head output of the tape deck (dedicated playback machine).

For the output stage of the main amplifier section, high efficiency tube 7189A with good sound quality is adopted by fixed bias system push-pull.
A large transformer with good characteristics is used for the output transformer.

It has a headphone port.

It features a speaker ON/OFF switch, low / high filter, loudness contour, muting circuit, etc.
