  • CLASSE CAV-150

  • ¥ 800,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

A 6-channel power amplifier that can adjust the channel output according to the speaker configuration.

The CAV 150 can be used for straight 6 ch and 5.1 ch surround sound, as well as for 4 ch + 1 ch, 2 ch + 2 ch or 3 ch configuration by bridge connection.

The input terminals are equipped with two balanced systems in addition to an unbalanced Route 6.
