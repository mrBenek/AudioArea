Commentary

Audio and video master amplifier for both audio and visual.

A total of 10 systems of video Line 5 and audio Line 5 can be connected.

Equipped with a surround system in which you can enjoy 4 modes.
You can enjoy the atmosphere of movies and concert live in Theater mode, and open air space like sports and outdoor concert is expanded in Stadium.
With Simulated Stereo, you can enjoy extended surround sound in mono sources.
Expanded Stereo is effective for stereo sources, amplifying the sense of left-right spread even in two channel stereo playback.

The 2 ch and 4 ch compatible power amplifier system is adopted for the amplifier section, and stereo mode output and surround mode output can be switched.

Two video outputs are installed : a video signal output and an RF output.
It is also equipped with one video and one audio adapter that can be connected to computers, audio effectors, video effectors, character multiplexing adapters, etc.

Equipped with a recording selector, you can record and dub while watching your favorite video or listening to audio.

Equipped with a full remote commander that is integrated into the amplifier and can be removed with one touch.
