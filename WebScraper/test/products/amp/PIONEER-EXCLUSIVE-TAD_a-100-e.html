Commentary

Pre-main amplifier equipped with dynamic non-switching amplifier.

Pioneer's original dynamic power supply system is used to supply power to CDs and other sources with a wide dynamic range.
In this system, an input signal is detected by a level detector, and a low voltage section (VLWhen the output exceeds a certain level, the high-voltage section (VHIn addition, the power section is equipped with Pioneer's original non-switching circuit that completely eliminates switching distortion, thereby realizing high-quality drive.

In addition to a simple two amplifier configuration from the MC input to the speaker output, the high-level signal is directly input to the power amplifier to improve sound purity.
In addition, by adopting DC servo, the equalizer amplifier and power amplifier are completely non-coupling capacitors.

The equalizer section employs a DC servo high-gain equalizer with low-noise, high-gain dual FET in the first stage to improve the signal-to-noise ratio.
In addition, the RIAA element, which actually affects sound quality, is composed of a polypropylene condenser and a brass cap non-magnetic resistor for high sound quality. In addition, a resin case condenser is used for the DC servo circuit. This structure emphasizes sound quality.

Equipped with a line straight function, it passes through the tone circuit and loudness circuit by pressing the switch, and you can enjoy more pure sound quality.

The power cord uses a double-shielded cord.
