  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier developed by inheriting the technology of F group, a high-end machine of Sony.

The power amplifier section uses a differential amplifier, two power supplies directly connected to all stages, and a pure complimentary service and parallel push-pull circuit.

The equalizer amplifier uses a newly developed dual FET, which is a package of two FETs with high input impedance, and combines a FET differential amplifier with a low-noise IC developed for audio.
In addition, a constant voltage circuit is installed to improve sound quality.

The flat amplifier uses a high-voltage low-noise IC.

The volume knob is designed to be a 4-channel master volume.

It uses tone control with turnover frequency switching.
The circuit uses the NFB method with good characteristics and low distortion.

All-push button type input selector is adopted.

Center channel terminal is mounted.

Built-in speaker protection circuit.

The preamp and power amp can be connected / disconnected.
