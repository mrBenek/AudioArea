  • Pioneer VSA-30

  • ¥ 54,800 (around 1985)

Commentary

AV surround pre-main amplifier for audio and visual.

It is equipped with a surround circuit to reproduce the reality of movie theaters and concert halls.
It also has a built-in simulated stereo circuit that converts mono sound into expanded sound.

With built-in 45W + 45W power amplifier on the front side and 17W + 17W power amplifier on the surround side, you can reproduce sound field space just by connecting 4 speakers.

It comes with a wired remote control that can adjust the volume of the front and surround independently.

It is equipped with four audio input terminals and two video input terminals, two sy power terminals, and two video and audio input switching circuits.

Equipped with monitor television outputs.
