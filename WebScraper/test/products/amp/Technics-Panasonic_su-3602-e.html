  • Technics SU-3602

  • ¥ 79,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with further improved performance and functionality based on the SU-3600.

All stages of the power amplifier section are directly connected OCL circuits, and a differential amplifier circuit using a constant current circuit is adopted at the first stage to improve DC stability.
In addition, the complimentary service stage is composed of all stages combined with PNP NPN transistors to suppress harmonic distortion and IM distortion and improve high-frequency characteristics.

Low noise and dynamic range are improved by an equalizer circuit composed of three stages of PNP-NPN-PNP directly connected with a low-noise PNP transistor at the first stage.

The volume is a preset type, so it is easy to return to the original position even when the volume is reduced too much.
Unlike conventional audio muting, the volume can be changed smoothly.

Equipped with two pairs of tape monitor circuits, two devices can record simultaneously and dubbing from one side to the other.

The tone control uses the Technics tone control.
At the midpoint of the knob, a completely flat characteristic can be obtained, and the characteristic change point can be changed to three levels of low and high sound.

It is equipped with a speaker connection terminal that uses 2 sets of easy connectors, main and remote.

Equipped with low / high filter and loudness switch.
