  • SONY XO-3

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1982)

Commentary

A deck receiver equipped with a tuner section capable of receiving short wave in addition to FM/AM.

It uses an automatic level recording mechanism that always sets the correction level.

Soft touch operation is adopted for deck operation.

Equipped with a 3-position auto tape selector.

Equipped with high-boost and low-boost circuits.
