  • SONY TA-N220

  • ¥ 40,000 (released in 1988)

Commentary

Multi-purpose 4-channel power amplifier.

Equipped with a 4-channel power amplifier, it can be used for a wide range of applications such as AV system configuration.

The output stage employs a super legato linear system that reduces switching distortion and crossover distortion, improving the sense of orientation as well as the sense of auditory distortion.

2ch/3ch/4ch switching is possible.

It can be used as a high-power stereo amplifier with a BTL connection.
