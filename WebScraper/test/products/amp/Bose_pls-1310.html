  • BOSE PLS-1310 WestBorough

  • ¥ 119,000 (released in 1996)

Commentary

A CD amplifier with built-in CD player and stereo FM/AM tuner.

It has an active equalizer circuit that maximizes the performance of the 363 WestBorough speaker.

Equipped with a PAP circuit that produces balanced sound even at low volume.

It uses a 5 mm thick panel on the front. In addition, the side panel is finished with bird's eye maple style.

Wireless remote control is included.
