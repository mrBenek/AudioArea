  • ONKYO A-601V

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1989)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with improved operability for digital and AV systems.

In order to increase the digital reproduction capability, the signal transmission circuit has been thoroughly simplified, various switches and tone amplifiers have been removed from the signal path, and a source direct which sends the input directly to the power amplifier has been incorporated.

Power amplifier section is High fTDiscrete output stage with power transistor is used.

Equipped with twin turbo control, it is possible to increase the power mainly at 50 Hz when it is ON.

In addition to the voice input Line 5, two A/V input / output terminals are equipped.
It is also equipped with a simulated stereo switch that converts the monaural input of a VTR into stereo.
The VCR-2 is equipped with a mono / stereo input selector switch and can be used according to the purpose.

Equipped with FL tube display for easy viewing and centralized management of various operation states.

Two banana type speaker terminals are installed.

A gold-plated input terminal is mounted on the VCR-1/VDP.

Uses a power supply cone with polarity display.

Gold plated headphone terminal is used.

It comes with a remote controller that enables volume adjustment, power ON/OFF, muting ON/OFF, source direct ON/OFF, and input selector switching.
