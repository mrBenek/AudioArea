  • SONY TA-YX7F

  • ¥ 60,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

Compact pre-main amplifier.

Equipped with a newly developed universal pulse power supply and high-efficiency power amplifier, a 50W + 50W output is achieved despite its thin design.

Hi-ft power transistor is used for the output stage.

The main parts including the volume are feather-touch operated.

Equipped with base boost and loudness functions.

Terminal for TSS (tuner signal sink) is mounted.

The chassis uses a die-cast body.
