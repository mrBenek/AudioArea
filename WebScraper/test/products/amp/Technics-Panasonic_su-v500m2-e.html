Commentary

Bamboo mixing Integrated amplifier using electrolytic capacitor.

Equipped with a new class A circuit to suppress switching distortion and crossover distortion.

Equipped with two series A and B speaker terminals that can be driven simultaneously.

Equipped with phono inputs connected to an analog player and two tape inputs / outputs.
