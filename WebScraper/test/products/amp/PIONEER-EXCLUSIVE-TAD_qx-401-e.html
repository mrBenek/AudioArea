  • Pioneer QX-401

  • ¥ 68,000 (released in 1972)

Commentary

This is a 4 ch receiver that can play a variety of 4 channels.

Discrete 4-channel tape playback, regular / SQ matrix 4-channel, and CD-4 disc playback with a demodulator.

FET is used for the FM front end, and IC and ceramic filter are used for the IF part to improve selectivity.
In addition, the linear type dial scale and large meter are adopted, and accurate tuning point catch is possible.

A muting switch is installed to prevent unpleasant inter-station noise.

Each front and rear has its own tone control.
In addition, it is equipped with 3 balance control which can control the balance of front left and right, rear left and right, front and rear.

Two pairs of rear speakers can be connected simultaneously.
