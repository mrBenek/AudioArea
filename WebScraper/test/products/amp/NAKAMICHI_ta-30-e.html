  • Nakamichi TA-30

  • ¥ 128,000 (around 1989)

Commentary

A tuner amplifier developed as a high-end home entertainment center for enjoying high-quality sound and images.

Uses a STASIS power amplifier based on a license from Threshold.
In addition, it has an audio section similar to a single component, such as an equalizer with dual FET differential inputs and a quartz PLL synthesizer tuner.

Equipped with video milk input / output terminal with built-in wide-band video buffer amplifier.

Equipped with a system remote controller that can operate Nakamichi decks and CDs.
