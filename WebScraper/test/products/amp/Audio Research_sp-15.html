Audio Database

  • Audio Research SP-15

  • ¥ 1,250,000 (around 1990)

Commentary

Control amplifier with separate structure.

The main body and power supply are designed separately.

Hybrid design. The SP-15 uses FET for most circuits, but the phono equalizer stage uses three twin triode 6DJ8 tubes to achieve high input resistance and high sound quality.

A large toroidal transformer is used for the power supply section.

Balance output is possible using the Canon terminal.
