  • Technics SA-4500

  • ¥ 58,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

This is the first FM/AM stereo receiver in this class that uses a fully connected differential amplifier OCL circuit in the audio section.

Low-noise PNP silicon transistors are used for all stages of the preamplifier.

A low-noise FET is used for the FM tuner section.

Electronic transistor protection circuit is adopted.

There are two sets of speaker jacks, Main and Remote.

NF type tone control is adopted.
