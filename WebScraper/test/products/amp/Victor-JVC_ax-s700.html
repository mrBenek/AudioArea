Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier with improved speaker drive power using Victor's original voltage-to-current conversion amplification Gm driver and huge power supply.

The Gm driver is a low-impedance drive circuit that sends the energy of complex music signals to the speakers correctly. It drives the power stage stably even if the impedance of the speakers drops or the back electromotive force occurs due to changes in the signal level.
In addition, the power stage employs a dynamic super A circuit that does not generate harmful switching distortion.

The power supply section uses a huge transformer of 4.8 kg, nearly half the weight of the whole amplifier. In addition to blocking by high-speed diodes, etc., a more stable power supply section is constructed.

The signal circuit is a 2-amp configuration that focuses on simplicity. The CD and other high-level inputs are directly connected to a high-gain power amplifier, and the MC input is simply connected to an EL-FET low-noise ICL equalizer. This reduces noise and distortion caused by excessive routing.

Equipped with a speaker selector that can drive two systems simultaneously.
