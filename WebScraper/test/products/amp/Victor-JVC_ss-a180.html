  • VICTOR/JVC SS-A180

  • ¥ 270,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

Professional stereo power amplifier with wide bandwidth, low distortion rate and high durability.

It is equipped with four protection circuits, and its reliability is enhanced by heat detection circuit, DC voltage detection circuit, ASO limiter and muting circuit.

An electronic balanced input circuit using a high-slew rate BI-FET operational amplifier is adopted.

The level meter uses a 12-point 3-color power peak display for easy level monitoring.

It can also be used as a 360W monaural power amplifier with the BTL operation selector switch.
