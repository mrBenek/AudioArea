Commentary

Integrated amplifier with constant current drive system.

The driver stage of the main amplifier is driven by constant current (high-impedance drive) to completely eliminate crossover distortion and distortion caused by Oire force.

By adopting a constant-current drive system with excellent low-distortion characteristics instead of a direct-coupled system, an excellent linear drive is achieved. In addition, an electronic protection circuit is mounted to provide a safe circuit design.

Uses carefully selected silicon transistors for Hi-Fi.

The equalizer uses a high-gain, low-noise hybrid IC (FA-6001T) to achieve uniform characteristics and a wide dynamic range.

The tone control uses a switch-over type BAX tone circuit.

Equipped with center channel output terminal, it can be developed into center woofer system.

Built-in amplifier for microphone allows mixing with other program sources.

Built-in pre-main isolation terminal allows the pre-amplifier and main amplifier to operate separately.

The preamp section has a built-in amplifier for VU meter. Power check and balance check can be performed by changing the meter range.

Built-in head amplifier for MC type cartridge.
