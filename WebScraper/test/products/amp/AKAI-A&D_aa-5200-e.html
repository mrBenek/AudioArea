  • AKAI AA-5200

  • ¥ 43,500 (around 1973?)

Commentary

The power amplifier section consists of a SEPP-ITL-OTL system circuit directly connected to all stages, and the phono amplifier in the pre-section uses a two stage IC directly connected circuit.

Two tape decks can be connected at the same time, or the pre-main can be used alone.
Also equipped with bus treble control, balance control, high / low cut filter, loudness switch and muting switch.
