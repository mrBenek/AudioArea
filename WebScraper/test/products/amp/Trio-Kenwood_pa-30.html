  • TRIO PA-30

  • ¥ 19,900 (around 1960 s)

Commentary

A PA amplifier suitable for public relations activities such as school broadcasting and in-house broadcasting.

It can be used for both AC and DC. Not only AC power supply but also 6 v and 12 v battery by adding converter.
Switching between AC and DC is performed by replacing the rear socket.

The microphone is compatible with both crystal and dynamic types.

Can mix 2 microphones and 3 pickups simultaneously.
