Commentary

A compact DSP AV amplifier equipped with Dolby Pro Logic and Yamaha's original CINEMA DSP.

Equipped with 6 CINEMA DSP surround programs.
In addition, it is equipped with a 5 ch audio input terminal, and you can enjoy Dolby Digital by connecting a DVD player with a built-in Dolby Digital Decoder.

Add the Dolby Digital / Cinema DSP processor DDP-10 (sold separately) to enjoy a full-fledged three field CINEMA DSP.

Two AV devices and three audio devices can be connected.

Equipped with an FM/AM stereo tuner.

Equipped with a sleep timer that automatically turns off after 60 minutes.

It comes with a system remote control that can control Yamaha components such as CDs and cassette decks.
