Commentary

An AV control amplifier equipped with video audio input and remote control.

Remote control for audio and video input / output.
When an MCD7005 or MR7082 is connected, these devices can also be operated.

It is equipped with functions such as a 5-band program equalizer common to both L and R, continuously variable loudness control and low and high filter.

The top of the front panel is glass (black) with gold / green illumination, the bottom of the front panel is aluminum panel with black anodized finish, and the chassis is black finished.
