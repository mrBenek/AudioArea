  • SANSUI SAX-100

  • ¥ 36,700 (around 1964)

Commentary

FM/AM receiver compatible with FM stereo broadcast transmission.

A nominal power tube 6BQ5 is used for the outputs, and low distortion and high damping factor are realized by combining current positive feedback and voltage negative feedback.

Four transistors are used in the head amplifier section.

Equipped with FM multiplex circuit, compatible with FM stereo broadcast transmission.

Uses a friction tone control that can be adjusted separately on the left and right.

Terminal for capacitor tweeter is mounted.
