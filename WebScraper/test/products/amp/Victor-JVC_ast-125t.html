Commentary

An FM/AM receiver equipped with a slide-type graphic control volume, etc.

The power amplifier section employs quasi-frequency ITL and OTL circuits. A filter that cuts frequencies below 30 Hz removes distortion due to low-frequency noise while reducing distortion within audible complimentary service with sufficient NFB.

The FM front-end uses a field-effect transistor (FET) to improve the selectivity.
It is also equipped with a muting switch that can eliminate unpleasant inter-station noise during channel selection.

It is equipped with an SEA connector that can be connected to an SEA controller that can correct the sound characteristics of the listening room and adjust the sound quality. This SEA connector can be developed into a multi-channel system and can be used independently for pre-main and main use.
