  • TRIO KA-3004

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1973)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed by inheriting the design philosophy of the Hi-ATC series high-grade equipment.

All stages are directly connected + pure complimentary service circuit is adopted for the power amplifier section.
The equalizer uses a two stage, two power supply system.

The tone control also has a tone defeat switch.
