  • SONY XO-5

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

A deck receiver equipped with a tuner section capable of receiving short wave in addition to FM/AM.

The deck section has a 2-motor full logic configuration and can be used with metal tapes.

Equipped with an automatic level recording mechanism that can always record at an appropriate level.

Equipped with a Rec Mute function with autospace that allows you to create a certain interval between songs.

Equipped with a 3-position auto tape selector.

It is possible to play synchronized between the player and deck part.
