  • ¥ 220,000 (around 1989)

Commentary

An AV control amplifier announced as the control amplifier part of the separate AV amplifier AV1000.

The signal flow is organized by dividing the circuit board into video, audio, digital, and power supply blocks. In addition, the power transformer uses an independent winding video, audio, and digital systems.

It is equipped with two independent copy functions that can copy video and audio between connected devices, and two monitor outputs that can switch between main and sub monitor television images during copying.
The input / output status of video and audio can be checked on the front panel display.

Equipped with ASP function, you can create 8 preset sound fields and 5 sound fields of your own.
It also has a surround processor that supports Dolby Pro Logic.

The D/A converter uses the bit stream method.
Independent D/A converters are installed for D/A conversion of front and surround sound signals generated by ASP.

Equipped with a master volume controller, you can adjust the volume of the whole surround sound.

You can connect a video processor that can correct and process images.

Wireless remote control AV1003 with learning function is included.
