  • Technics SU-3100

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that uses the all-stage direct connection ITL-OTL-OCL circuit used in the SU-50A and SU-3600.

A monolithic IC is used in the drive stage of the main amplifier to improve the SN ratio and characteristics.

The equalizer circuit is a combination of PNP-NPN transistors.
The first stage uses specially selected PNP transistors and has succeeded in reducing noise.

Separate left and right level meters are used on the front panel to monitor the output level of the left and right channels.

Equipped with a microphone terminal.

It has a built-in matrix circuit that takes out 4 ch output from 2 ch source. By connecting 4 speakers, you can enjoy matrix 4 ch playback.
