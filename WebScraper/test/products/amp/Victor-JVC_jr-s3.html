Commentary

A stereo receiver with a full-scale circuit.

A PureCon OCL circuit is used for the amplifier section.

The tuner section is equipped with a 2-meter system to enable more accurate tuning.
It is also equipped with a stereo radar that automatically notifies you of stereo when stereo reception is received.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit.

It is equipped with a terminal to connect an adapter for future FM discrete 4 ch broadcasting.

Built-in functions include an FM muting switch, a stereo / mono mode switch, loudness control, and a tape monitor circuit.
