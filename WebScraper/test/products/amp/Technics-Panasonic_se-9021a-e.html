  • Technics SE-9021A

  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A stereo DC power amplifier that can be paired with the SU-9011A that adopts DC amplifier design.

In addition to the input / output coupling capacitor, the interstage capacitor has been removed to realize flat frequency characteristics and excellent phase characteristics up to ultra-low frequencies.

The FET used in a DC amplifier is not used, but a dual transistor is used in the first stage to achieve excellent DC stability.

Equipped with a high-precision peak meter.
