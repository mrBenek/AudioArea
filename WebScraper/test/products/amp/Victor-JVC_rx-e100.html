  • VICTOR/JVC RX-E100

  • ¥ 45,000 (around 2001)

Commentary

Equipped with an AAC decoder, this AV amplifier is compatible with the audio format of digital BS broadcasting.

It is also equipped with a decoder compatible with DTS and Dolby Digital which are audio standards for DVDs, and you can enjoy multi-channel playback by digital connection with DVD player.

A 5 ch power amplifier for 5.1 ch surround sound is installed in a discrete configuration.

It is equipped with Victor's original DAP (Digital Acoustic Processor), which is computer-processed based on sound data of the hall sound field based on actual measurement. By reproducing sound field patterns of various concert halls and live houses, the sound of 2 ch stereo such as CD can be enjoyed with multi-channel sound field effect with realistic feeling.

It is equipped with multi-brand remote control which can control TV, video and DVD of major manufacturers.
