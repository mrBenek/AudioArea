  • Lo-D HTA-3000

Commentary

A tuner amplifier that combines an amplifier and a tuner.

It is designed with an emphasis on operability and feeling of operation, and each switch is considered.

Equipped with LED signal indicator.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit and a loudness switch.
