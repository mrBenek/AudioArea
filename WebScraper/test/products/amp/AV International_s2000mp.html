  • AVI(AV International) S2000MP

  • ¥ 120,000 (released in March 1993)

Commentary

A low-distortion and compact control amplifier developed by Martin Greenrod, who was engaged in the development and design of defense electronics as an engineer of British aerospace engineering.

Both the total harmonic distortion factor and the intermodulation distortion factor are -120dB.
This value is comparable to the thermal noise generated by copper wires at room temperature and is 40 dB better than most preamps.

The input has a line-level Route 6 and a recording selector.

The remote controller was an option sold separately.
