  • YAMAHA A-200X

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed as a 340 mm width compact component series.

The ZDR circuit is mounted on the class A turbo system which shifts to class A operation at small output and class AB operation at large output.

It is equipped with Route 3 (VD, VTR1, front VTR2) for video input, and also with a CD-direct switch.
