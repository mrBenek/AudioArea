  • Marantz SM-5ver2

  • ¥ 520,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

Based on SM-5, this is an upgraded model that further pursued low noise in the peripheral part.

Low-impedance drive design enhances speaker drive capability.

DC clean drive by bb-5 is possible for the first stage of buffer amplifier and power amplifier.

New HDAM (gold-plated high-speed voltage amplifier module) of newly developed 4-layer substrate structure which realizes high S/N is adopted.

The output stage is a parallel push-pull configuration using a high-frequency large power transistor.
