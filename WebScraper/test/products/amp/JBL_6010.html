Commentary

Commercial monaural power amplifier with effective output of 100W.

All-silicon transistor configuration is adopted.

Equipped with forced-air cooling fan, effective heat dissipation is possible.
Even if the fan fails, the thermal switch operates when the temperature of the heat sink reaches 93 ° C or higher.

The high impedance input terminal has a socket to which a balanced line can be connected, and a matching input and a bridge input can be used.
Screw-type terminals are for balanced line input and high impedance unbalanced input.
