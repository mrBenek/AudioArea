Audio Database

  • LUXMAN SQ38FD/II

  • ¥ 168,000 (Released in September 1974)

Commentary

38 series tube type pre-main amplifier which is an improved version of SQ38FD.

The coupling capacitor has been changed to a film type.

The for the appearance, the panel is changed to bronze and the knob has been changed to be slightly flat.
