Commentary

FM/AM receiver with built-in SEA equipment.

It is equipped with 5-division SEA equipment.
The SEA circuit is composed of a series resonance circuit consisting of a coil, a resistor, and a capacitor, and is structured to take advantage of the fact that it has the minimum impedance with respect to the resonance frequency. In the SEA, for example, when both the 60 Hz and 150 Hz knobs are moved, the overall characteristic at the intermediate frequency is the sum of both SEA characteristics at that frequency.
It also has a defeat switch.

Semi-output ITL and OTL circuits are used for the complimentary service circuit.
In addition, a pre-main independent terminal is mounted so that it can be used separately.

It is equipped with functions such as a speaker selector, low filter, high filter, tape monitor, mode switch and loudness switch.

FET is used in the FM tuner section to increase sensitivity.
Equipped with muting and AFC switch.

There was a wood case as an option sold separately.
