  • SANSUI SM-35

  • ¥ 55,500 (released in 1963)

Commentary

FM/AM receiver equipped with FM multiplex circuit.

Built-in FM multi-stereo adapter allows you to enjoy FM stereo broadcast transmission (FCC method).

Built-in power amplifier with output of 32W.
It achieves almost flat characteristics from 30 hz to 70 khz.

The preamplifier section uses a transistor configuration with four transistors to reduce hum and noise.

A silicon diode is used for the power supply rectification circuit.

Built-in functions include a scratch filter, loudness circuit, tone control, AFC circuit, and tape monitor circuit.
