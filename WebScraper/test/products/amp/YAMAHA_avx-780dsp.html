  • YAMAHA AVX-780DSP

  • ¥ 89,800 (released in 1994)

Commentary

5chCINE-DSP AV pre-main amplifier with DSP mounted.

This is the first CINE-DSP AV amplifier to support 5-channel surround sound.
As with conventional Cine-DSPs, this is achieved by processing 7 channels of signal and then combining the signal for the front effect speaker with the signal for the main speaker. This makes it possible to enjoy Cine-DSPs more easily.
In addition, by regenerating the center as a phantom, it is possible to regenerate at 4 ch.

16 types of CINE-DSP programs are installed.

All discrete configuration is adopted for the power amplifier section.

You can perform operations such as input switching, power on/off, program selection, and volume adjustment while watching the superimposed image on the screen.

There were 2 types, titanium color and BlackCar color.
