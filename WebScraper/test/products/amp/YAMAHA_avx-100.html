  • YAMAHA AVX-100

  • ¥ 110,000 (around 1990)

Commentary

AV pre-main amplifier with 10 audio input systems and video input Route 6.

Audio and video are configured independently and each can be selected independently.
Equipped with two S-video input systems.

The power amplifier section uses a discrete 4-ch power amplifier and 3-ch switching is possible by the BTL method.

Yamaha's proprietary digital delay LSI enables you to control four delays simultaneously.

Equipped with a video enhancer for image quality correction, you can adjust the シャープエンス, detail and video level.
It also uses a video amplifier capable of handling 700 horizontal resolutions.

Uses A/V2 hierarchical construction.

A learning remote controller is included.

There are 2 color variations of titanium and black.
