  • TRIO W-41

  • ¥ 25,500 (around 1964)

Commentary

This is a stereo pre-main amplifier that utilizes the technology of the trio in the circuit and mechanism.

DC ignition of the vacuum tube is used for the equalizer circuit to prevent hum noise.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch terminal that allows easy recording and playback by connecting a tape recorder.
By using a 3-head tape recorder, recording and playback can be performed simultaneously.

Equipped with left and right independent tone control.

It is equipped with functions such as loudness control and stereo headphone jack.
