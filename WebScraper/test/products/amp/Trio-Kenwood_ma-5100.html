Commentary

A multi-channel IDS stereo amplifier that combines four power amplifiers and a crossover network into one.

You can build a 3-way multi-amplifier system by adding it to the amplifier you have.

An NF-type 12dB/oct. crossover network is used.

2-way or 3-way configuration can be selected by switching the switch.

With a direct output terminal, the input signal can be taken out as it is.

It has a headphone port.
