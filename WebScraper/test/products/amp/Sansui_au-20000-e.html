Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier with built-in high power and substantial basic performance as well as maniac attached functions.

Triple push-pull complimentary service stage is adopted, and all stages are directly connected with pure output OCL system enabling high power and low distortion factor.

Equipped with 2-amp CR + NF type equalizer.

Equipped with a triple tone control function.

A Tone defeat with flat characteristics.

Large power supply unit using high-performance toroidal transformer and stabilization circuit.

Switchable input sensitivity / impedance.
