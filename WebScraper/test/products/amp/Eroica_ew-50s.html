Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier developed with the ultimate aim of amplifier.

The transformer developed jointly with tamura Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is used as the output transformer.
This transformer employs a new method for coupling a tertiary winding (tamura Patent) exclusively for NFB with a circuit unique to ELOICA.

KT-66 is used for the output tube.

The circuit design employs a two stage low-frequency time constant, a high gain in the loop using a cathode-coupled phase inversion circuit with a good balance, and a stable method of returning NFB from the secondary side of the output transformer to the cathode of the first stage tube.
The NFB is 24 dB.
