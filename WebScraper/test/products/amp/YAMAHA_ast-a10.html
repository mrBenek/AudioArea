  • YAMAHA AST-A10

  • ¥ 50,000 (around 1989)

Commentary

A power amplifier for active servo speakers.

The input can be connected from a pre-main amplifier as it can be connected in three ways : an accessory terminal, a pre-out terminal and a speaker terminal.

Conventional speakers can also sound by replacing the attached general-purpose cartridge.
