Commentary

An AV amplifier with 5.1 channel input.

5.1 channel input jacks and 3-1 stereo 1 stereo (Hi-Vision) HD input jacks are installed. You can enjoy surround sound playback by connecting to a DVD player with a built-in Dolby digital decoder.

Equipped with a decoder compatible with Dolby Pro Logic, surround playback with compatible software is possible.
In addition, a cinema studio and a simulated (stereo) mode are installed as surround modes.

A dynamic 5-amp system is used to provide the same output for all channels.

It is a thin design.

The remote control is equipped with a base boost button that can increase the bass sound with one touch and a muting button that cuts the sound temporarily.

A wireless remote controller with Japanese display is included.
