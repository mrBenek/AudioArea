  • Marantz PM4000

  • Discretionary price (released in November 1999)

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier of the C series which has reverified "reproducibility of music" by going back to the idea of original Marantz.

The design also takes into consideration matching with the B & W speaker system.

Equipped with tone defeat circuit and loudness control.

Equipped with a dedicated CD-R/MD terminal.

Equipped with two types of speaker terminals for banana plugs.

Equipped with Phono MM input.

Hairline finish is applied to the front panel.

There are 2 color variations, titanium gold and black.

Comes with a system remote controller that allows simultaneous operation of Marantz products.
