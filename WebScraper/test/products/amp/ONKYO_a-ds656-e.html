Commentary

This AV amplifier is compatible with Dolby digital and new format with 5.1 ch input.

The DSP processor uses Motorola's 24 bitDSP.

The built-in Cinema Re-EQ (Cinema Re-EQ) circuit allows you to re-correct the recorded sound signal with high frequency emphasis, assuming that the speaker is placed behind the screen.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 20-bit delta-sigma A/D and D/A converter.

A low-feedback circuit that minimizes the amount of NFB has been adopted, inheriting the technology of Integra's non-feedback circuit.

Equipped with a 5.1 ch input, it can be developed into new formats such as DTS and MPEG multi-channel by receiving the analog output audio signal through the processor.

Surround mode is equipped with all 6 modes including original mode.
In addition, you can create your favorite surround sound by fine-tuning the parameters.

With 3-D Bass, you can hear the deep bass from the front / center speakers more clearly.

The Midnight Theater feature compresses the dynamic range to make it easier to listen to small sounds during low-volume playback (Dolby Digital Only)

It features a smart scan controller and a rotary dial controller on the front panel for easy system setup and parameter settings.

Equipped with I. P. M. (Intelligent Power Management) function, when the TV is turned on / off, the power of the amplifier is automatically turned on / off in conjunction with the sound signal of the TV.

On-screen capability.

Equipped with 5.1 ch pre-out.

It comes with a remote controller with a learning function that can memorize the main operations of video devices such as TV and DVD, and audio devices such as CD player.
