  • ONKYO Integra 701

  • ¥ 156,000 (around 1969)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with a fully connected circuit in the main section.
Differential amplifier circuits are used in all drive stages by a special combination.

The pre-section uses a complimentary service equalization circuit to achieve a maximum allowable input of 230 mV or more.
