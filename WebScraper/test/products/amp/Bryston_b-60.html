Commentary

This is a simple pre-main amplifier with the performance of a professional amplifier in the design size for private use.

It is composed of a thin 1U size with left and right independent power transformers.

Both the preamplifier and power amplifier have a fully discrete circuit configuration.

The front panel is available in 2 colors, black and silver.
