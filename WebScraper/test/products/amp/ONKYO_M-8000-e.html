  • ONKYO M-8000

  • ¥ 62,000 (around 1978)

Commentary

A power amplifier with improved dynamic characteristics by using a dual channel system in which all circuits are composed of two monaural amplifiers.

The circuit system is all-stage direct connection semi - complimentary service SEPP / OTL circuit.
0.01 ~ 100W direct reading type LR power meter and -24 ~ 0dB5 point peak indicator are mounted.

Equipped with 2 AC inputs.
