Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with video input and AV support.

Equipped with dual super non-feedback power amplifier.
In addition, a full DC configuration is adopted for all signal paths between the MC input and the speaker output terminals without using a coupling capacitor, and a new current mirror circuit using a high-speed transistor with excellent high-frequency characteristics is adopted for the voltage amplification stage to realize an excellent slew rate.
In addition, f equivalent to Pc200WTA large pellet power transistor with a 50 MHz multi-emitter structure is provided with sufficient margin to expand the full operating range.

By setting the floating circuit with clear ground potential, generation of low-frequency distortion is reduced.
In addition, by adopting a "pure dynamic power supply circuit" using a proprietary 2-4 block capacitor, dynamic IM distortion (intermodulation distortion) caused by ripple current is suppressed and low-frequency reproduction is improved.

In order to prevent noise from the power supply due to an increase in various digital devices, we have adopted a newly developed power transformer with a special electrostatic shield. We have also adopted a structure in which the power supply and main signal system are separated via a heat sink to improve the interference characteristics.

It is equipped with a DC servo type super equalizer using a low-noise FET differential amplifier input and a high-speed operational amplifier, and the RIAA deviation of 20 Hz to 100 kHz and ± 0.3 dB is obtained when using an MC cartridge.

It is equipped with a processor loop mechanism so that graphic equalizer can be used.

It uses an input selector with an electronic switch and reduces the number of wiring wires to form a simple signal path.

Equipped with Phono dedicated subsonic filter.

Built-in high-speed protection circuit.
