Commentary

An improved control amplifier based on the CA-50 and incorporating the technology obtained in the development of the CA-70.

The MC head amplifier uses a parallel push-pull input configuration with super Hi-gm FET to reduce noise level.
In addition, the resistance value of the signal system is minimized to ensure a good S/N ratio.

The equalizer amplifier and line amplifier have a two stage differential amplifier configuration with a dual FET differential input at the first stage and a bipolar transistor differential input at the second stage. The final buffer has a sufficient current supply capability as an emitter follower with a current bootstrap to maintain high linearity against impedance drops in the high-frequency range.
The emitter follower with current bootstrap has a simple structure and high current supply capability. Moreover, it maintains full Class-A operation. This circuit ensures high linearity in actual operation against the stray capacitance in the later stage and stable current supply against load impedance fluctuations.

It is equipped with tone control that takes into consideration the effect on sound quality by minimizing the amount of change.

A multi-regulated power supply is adopted for the power supply section, and mutual interference due to voltage fluctuations between amplifiers is eliminated by making the power supply voltage constant in two stages. In addition, ground interference is eliminated by an isolated ground system that incorporates an FET buffer into the power supply line.

In order to make the signal flow as simple and straight as possible, we have adopted a crossbar twin-type relay that is directly connected to the input terminal for switching the program source. The audio circuit has an L/R twin-mono structure to ensure excellent channel separation.
In addition, the front / bottom chassis is made of copper-plated chassis, and the structure of the aluminum chassis has been made more rigid to improve vibration resistance, preventing adverse effects of magnetic flux and external vibration.

Carbon resistors and film capacitors developed exclusively for audio are actively used, and gold contacts are used for the cartridge selector and MC gain / MM load capacity selector switches.
In addition, we use many parts with excellent characteristics and audibility, such as a toroidal transformer for the power supply, a oxygen-free copper wire with low transmission loss for the wiring material for the signal system, and a oxygen-free copper pattern bonded to an epoxy board for the printed board.

Load capacity in MM position (100pF/200pF/300pF) and gain in MC position (36dB/30dB/24dB) can be selected.
In addition, the load resistance at the MC position can be adjusted from 100 Ω to 50 Ω / 30 Ω / 20 Ω / 10 Ω / 3 Ω (by service center or dealer).

Equipped with mutual dubbing function of the tape deck.

Uses gold-plated input / output terminals.

The power cord uses a very thick power cord with polarity indication.

Remote power ON/OFF control terminal is mounted, and power operation of combined equipment can be linked with CA-50II by using SPC-1J sold separately.
