  • ¥ 66,800 (around 1970)

Commentary

Mania-standard FM/AM receiver.

It is equipped with sound adjustment functions such as tone control, low filter, high filter, and loudness control.

Equipped with a tape monitor function.

The FM tuner section is equipped with an FM muting mechanism.
