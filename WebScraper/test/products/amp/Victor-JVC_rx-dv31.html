Commentary

A DVD receiver with integrated progressive DVD player for DVD-Audio.

In order to reproduce DVD movie software with film quality, Victor's original digital direct progressive system is adopted.
In addition, it is equipped with DVD audio support and PEM-DD converter supporting 192kHz/24bit.

Equipped with a 100Wx5ch power amplifier for 5.1 ch surround sound.

The Quick Speaker Setup allows you to set the surround sound field according to your environment simply by setting the number of satellite speakers, the availability of sub-woofers, and the size of your room.

You can use the remote control to select and determine the functions of your DVD or amp while viewing the on-screen menu, or change the settings while playing.

Equipped with "Audio Position" that can easily adjust the low band balance according to the music source.

Includes mp3 playback.

Equipped with JPEG still image playback function.

It is equipped with an MPEG-2 AAC decoder for enjoying 5.1 channel BS / terrestrial digital broadcasting, a decoder compatible with DTS, and Dolby Pro Logic II for expanding 2 ch source to 5.1 ch.
