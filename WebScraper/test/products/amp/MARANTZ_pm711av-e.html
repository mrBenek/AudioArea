  • Marantz PM711AV

  • ¥ 60,000 (released in 1995)

Commentary

An AV amplifier with Dolby Pro Logic.

A 4-amp configuration with front 2 ch, rear 1 ch and center 1 ch is adopted.

Pre-out terminals for front 2 ch, rear 1 ch and center 1 ch are mounted.

The surround mode of matrix and home is equipped.

Built-in sleep timer that can be set between 10 and 90 minutes.

There were 2 kinds of models, gold and black.

Pre-coated remote control is included.
