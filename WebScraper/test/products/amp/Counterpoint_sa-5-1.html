  • COUNTERPOINT SA-5.1

  • ¥ 630,000 (around 1984)

Commentary

This tube type preamplifier was improved as a minor change model of SA-5.

The SA-5.1 uses 6DJ8, a vacuum tube for high-frequency amplification with extremely low internal resistance. It was designed with the aim of producing sound that takes full advantage of the excellent linearity of 6DJ8.
The equalizer stage consists of a CR type equalizer, designed to leave amplifier performance to the bare characteristics of 6DJ8 by focusing on non-NFB.

The power supply is designed separately.
The power supply unit contains a rectifier tube 6CA4.

There are 2 color variations of black and silver.

The wood case was sold separately as an option.
