  • Technics SA-5150

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

FM/AM stereo receiver with FM/AM tuner.

The main amplifier section uses a direct-coupled differential amplifier OCL circuit IC.

The tone control circuit reduces distortion by using the NF circuit of the main amplifier.

It is equipped with a loudness switch.
