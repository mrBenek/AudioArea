  • Pioneer A-2050

  • ¥ 58,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier using class AB operation system.

A DC configuration is adopted in which the condenser is removed from the NFB circuit.

Uses a 3-transformer 3-power supply system with a dedicated transformer for small signal on the left and right sides of the power section.

Uses a 32-step attenuator type volume.
