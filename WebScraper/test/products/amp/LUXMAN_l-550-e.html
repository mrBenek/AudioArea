Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with DUO β circuit / S.

We have developed the DUO β Circuit / S, which combines an "appropriate amount NFB" and a "DC-servo", and the DUO β Circuit / S, which adds a "single-stage amplifier" in addition to the control feedback power supply plusX, which is superior in sound quality. These circuits are installed in the equalizer and power section.
The first stage amplifier uses an FET for the MC head amplifier in the first stage. Originally, this FET has high gm and low noise, and it has a structure in which a large current of 7 mA per 1 stone is applied to obtain the necessary gain.
At the same time, the output stage is completely independent so as not to affect the first stage, and the second stage is removed. This realizes an ideal circuit with only one stage for voltage amplification.

The output section has an intrinsic class A operation with fixed bias current, and realizes a large output of 50W/ch.
The large amount of heat generated by class-A operation is handled by two large heat pipes, which are symmetrical in shape to eliminate heat radiation noise.

A single-stage amplifier is also used for the head amplifier.
The PNP transistor with very low input equivalent resistance at the first stage is made to be single differential, and low noise is realized while obtaining sufficient gain.

It is equipped with a phono straight function that separates the input selector, filters, monaural switch, balance control, etc., and connects the equalizer and power amplifier directly.

In some cases, the recording frequency of a disc, etc., rises gradually toward the high frequency range, and in other cases, it falls. Therefore, a linear equalizer is incorporated to correct this tendency and return the recording characteristics to a flat level.

It is equipped with a tone control that allows you to select nine turnover frequencies for both high and low frequencies.
It is designed with emphasis on the frequency at which the change starts rather than the amount of change, and uses the NF type Lux-style tone control that can obtain straightforward change characteristics.

The power supply section uses a special EI core with a capacitance of 400 VA, and a power transformer with the inside hardened by 哇砂 to suppress temperature rise.
Two electrolytic capacitors with an aluminum purity of 99.99% and a capacity of 30,000 μ F with sound quality measures are used.

Equipped with a pre-heating function, it can be heated by passing a small amount of current.

Equipped with a warm-up indicator, it is possible to confirm that stable operation has started.

Equipped with a line phase sensor, it can control sound quality by detecting the polarity of the AC power supply in the process.
