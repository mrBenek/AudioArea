Audio Database

  • Pioneer SA-50A

  • ¥ 25,900 (around 1971)

Commentary

A stereo pre-main amplifier developed to enjoy component stereo at an affordable price.

The power amplifier section uses a quasi complimentary service SEPP circuit.

Equipped with two Phono and two Tape Rec/Mon connectors.
There are also two speaker outputs.

Tone control is an 11-step quick-stop type.
