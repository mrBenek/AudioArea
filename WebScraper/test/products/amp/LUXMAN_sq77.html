  • LUXMAN SQ77

  • ¥ 27,800 (released in April 1964)

Commentary

Tube type pre-main amplifier.

Uses 6BQ5 push-pull circuit and high-fidelity output transformer with dedicated NF coil.

Miniaturization has been achieved by the adoption of silicon rectification, etc.

The tone control circuit is a separate type in which the left and right are independent, and a special NF type is used for the circuit.

Equipped with tape monitor function, scratch filter and rumble filter.

It has a headphone port.

The panel design seems to be different between the early stage and the later stage. Also, some of the listed specifications were different.
