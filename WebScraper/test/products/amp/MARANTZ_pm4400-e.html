Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that uses Marantz's original high-speed circuit technology "Absolute SA Technology".

Absolute SA Technology, a circuit speed-up technology developed for high-end models, is used as much as possible.
Special emphasis was placed on shortening, smoothing and reducing noise in the signal / power lines and eliminating signal path loss by adding two relays to the speaker output switch.
In addition, we are reviewing the thickness and route of the wires used and the arrangement to reduce the effect of transformer leakage.

Equipped with Phono input terminal for MM cartridge.

Equipped with two speaker output terminals for by-wire connection.

Features such as tone control, loudness and full REC selector are included.

Marantz Equipped with remote control terminal.

It comes with a wireless remote control that can switch the PM4400 power on/off, volume up/down, and input.
This remote control also allows basic operation of DVD/CD players, tuners and cassette decks.

There are 2 variations, gold and black.
