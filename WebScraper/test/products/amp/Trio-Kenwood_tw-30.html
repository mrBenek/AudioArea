  • TRIO TW-30

  • ¥ 36,500 (around 1965?)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with all-transistor configuration.

The all-transistor configuration realizes the OTL system.

It is equipped with an output stabilizing circuit that prevents transistor breakage, output equalization and over damping.

The control unit is equipped with a dual volume for volume and balance adjustment, various filters and loudness control.
