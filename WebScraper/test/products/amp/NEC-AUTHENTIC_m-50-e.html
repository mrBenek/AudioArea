  • NEC M-50

  • ¥ 50,000 (around 1986)

Commentary

A single channel amplifier with volume.

It is equipped with 3 input jacks including the CD direct position, and even though it is small, one M-50 can connect 2 channels of speakers.

Equipped with a high-cut / low-cut filter, it can be used as a dedicated amplifier for super woofer or noise cut for Dolby surround sound by combination.

A 2-ton steel plate is used for the bottom plate, and the sintered alloy used in A-10II and A-10III is used for the insulator.
