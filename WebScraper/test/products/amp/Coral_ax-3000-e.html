  • CORAL AX-3000

  • ¥ 88,000 (around 1965)

Commentary

AM/FM multi-stereo integrated amplifier with high sensitivity.

Equipped with FM stereo / monaural automatic switching function.

All-silicon transistor configuration is adopted.

The power transistor is protected by a new E / P / C device (New Electronics Portection Circuit, all-electronic protective device).

Tape head can be used directly.
