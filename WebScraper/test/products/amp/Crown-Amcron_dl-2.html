Commentary

Digilogic control center equipped with various functions.

The DL-2 consists of three cases : the main body (switching module), power supply, and phono module.
Each was sold separately, and there was a phono module for MC as a separately sold option.

The level control by the digital LED display has a precision of + / - 0.2 dB, Up down every 0.5 dB step, and each channel can be controlled by 63.5 dB. Up down is achieved by the feather touch switch.

Feather touch system is also adopted for the function switch, and power ON/OFF, muting and 8 input systems can be selected.

The external processor switch can be connected to a graphic equalizer, a Dolby or an expander to operate either before or after recording.

Audio imaging works as a mini-mixer.

Can be exchanged with the phono module of control amplifier SL-1.
