  • Lo-D HA-310

  • ¥ 35,800 (released in 1975)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that utilizes semiconductor technology to maintain high performance while reducing costs.

All stages of the power amplifier are directly connected pure complimentary service emitter-grounded inverted Darlington circuit configuration.

The Phono input can be switched between Low and High.

Equipped with electronic protection circuit.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit, a loudness switch and a tape monitor switch.
