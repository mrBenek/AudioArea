Commentary

Pre-main amplifier that uses direct energy MOS FET.

Direct Energy MOS FET is used for the output stage.
This device has low power loss, which adversely affects sound quality, good power linearity, and low load drive performance that enhances speaker drive performance. In addition, the high input impedance of the circuit allows the driver stage and output stage to be electrically separated, reducing interference with each other.
By combining this element with a wide-range linear circuit, we have achieved a simple circuit configuration that eliminates the emitter resistor and the choke coil at the speaker output terminal.

Wide range linear circuit is used for the circuit structure.
This circuit employs a simple structure in which the required gain is obtained by one stage amplification using a current feedback circuit. This circuit has excellent current linearity, flat output impedance from low to high band, and enables stable drive of speakers over a wide band.

Super-ring toroidal transformer is used for the power supply.
This transformer has the advantage of low loss and small leakage flux, making possible a cleaner power supply.

Copper plated steel is used for the chassis and rear panel to increase conductivity and reduce high-frequency impedance.

The power cord uses a grounded 3-core extra-thick type.

Speaker terminal compatible with banana plug is adopted.

Wireless remote control is included.
