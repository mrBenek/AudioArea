  • YAMAHA DSP-105AST

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1990)

Commentary

Digital sound field processor with a 5-ch power amplifier supporting active servo technology.

It is equipped with Dolby Pro Logic and DSP program of 11 programs and 20 patterns.

Wireless remote control is included.
