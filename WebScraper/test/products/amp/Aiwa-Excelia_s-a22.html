  • AIWA S-A22

  • ¥ 21,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier of mini component my Pace3 series.

A 2-stage differential SEPP circuit is used.

Cascade and current mirror circuits are used in the preamplifier section.

The final stage reduces the second harmonic distortion with the SEPP complimentary service output.

Equipped with a 5-point LED power indicator that displays the total power of both channels.

The tone control circuit uses NF type.

Equipped with a loudness control.
