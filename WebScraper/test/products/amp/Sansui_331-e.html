Commentary

This is an easy-to-use FM/AM receiver that combines a high-sensitivity FM/AM tuner section and an audio amplifier focusing on sound quality into a compact design.

The power amplifier section uses a high-performance hybrid IC with sufficient power and low distortion characteristics.

Equipped with tone control with stable operation and straightforward characteristics.

The FM front-end uses a high-sensitivity dual FET to catch weak radio waves with excellent high-frequency characteristics.

Equipped with tape monitor switch and loudness switch.

Equipped with speaker switching switch.
