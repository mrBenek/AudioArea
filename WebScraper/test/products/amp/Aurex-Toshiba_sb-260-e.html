  • Aurex SB-260

  • ¥ 37,800 (around 1970 s)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that adopts a pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages.

A low-noise transistor and resistor improve the SN ratio of the preamplifier section.

Equipped with a loudness switch.

Equipped with a wide range power meter.

Mixing recording is possible by using the separately sold microphone mixing amplifier MX-1.
