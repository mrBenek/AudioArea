  • McIntosh C34V

  • ¥ 680,000 (released in October 1986)

Commentary

A control amplifier that reduces interference by using an optional external interlocking switching method for video signal selection.

Equipped with a 5-band equalizer and compensator, it can also be inserted into a recording system.

As an accessory circuit, there is a video selector MVS-1, which can be connected to a C34V to switch the video signal without affecting the audio circuit.
The speaker control relay SCR-2 allows switching between two AC-systems and two speaker systems.
