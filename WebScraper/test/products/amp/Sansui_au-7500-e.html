Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that uses the latest circuit technology and selected parts to pursue a high level of invisible perfection.

All stages are directly connected to the OCL pure complimentary service circuit for the power amplifier.
Stabilization is also achieved by a + - 2 power supply circuit with a large transformer and a 6,800 μ Fx2 large-capacity capacitor.

The equalizer uses a 3-stage PNP-NPN-PNP direct-coupled circuit to achieve low noise and wide dynamic range.

The protective circuit is a combination of a transistor, a diode and a relay, and four short-cut fuses are used in combination.

Uses triple tone control for low, medium, and high tones.

Equipped with two Phono inputs, and Phono2 can switch impedance.

Equipped with a 4-channel adapter terminal.

The pre-part and main part can be used independently.

Equipped with a 5-position mode selector, it can be switched between Stereo/Reverse/Normal/Mono L + R/L/R.

Equipped with muting, loudness and low / high filters.
