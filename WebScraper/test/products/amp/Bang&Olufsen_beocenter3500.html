Audio Database

  • Bang & Olufsen BEOMASTER 3500

  • ¥ 249,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A receiver / player set that combines BEOGRAM1202 and BEOMASTER 3000 2.

Equipped with an automatic tuning function for FM preset.

A belt idler drive is used for the record player part.
