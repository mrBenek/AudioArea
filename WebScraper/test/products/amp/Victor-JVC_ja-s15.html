  • VICTOR/JVC JA-S15

  • ¥ 35,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed as a sister unit of the JA-S35 direct series.

The circuit employs a pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages using a current mirror load differential input circuit to reduce distortion.

Equipped with a large power meter.

The front panel is equipped with recording and re-terminal, making it easy to add a deck and upgrade the system such as SEA and microphone mixing adapter.
