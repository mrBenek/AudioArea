  • Marantz SM-17SA ver.2

  • ¥ 100,000 (released in 2002)

Commentary

A stereo power amplifier with high sound quality by introducing Absolute SA Technology based on SM-17SA.

We have introduced Absolute SA Technology, a circuit speedup technology unique to Marantz that reexamines power supply, parts and wiring.

Equipped with a -6dB attenuator that is convenient for BTL (MONO) connection of SM-17SA ver. 2 via bi-amplifier connection with the PM-17SA.

Uses speaker terminal made by WBT in Germany.

The warm-up meter allows you to check the heat up condition.

An inlet type AC cable is used.
