Commentary

Professional preamp for various applications such as editing and mastering studios, recording systems, and power amp drive amplifiers.

AF01 was designed by Satoshi Suzuki, the founder of Soul Note.

P &G's high-precision faders are used for volume control.

The internal circuit is a fully balanced Non-NFB discrete amplifier with frequency characteristics exceeding 600 kHz and excellent high-speed response.

With its wide current supply capability and low impedance output, it can be used to connect and drive multiple power amplifiers.

Equipped with a Porality switch that matches the signal polarity of the system.
