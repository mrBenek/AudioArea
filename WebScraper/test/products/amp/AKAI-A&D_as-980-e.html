Commentary

The highest quality receiver announced as 4chFM/AM total stereo amplifier.
It is designed so that you can enjoy 2 ch stereo and the output is doubled when you switch from 4 ch.

The SEPP-ITL-OTL circuit is used for the power amplifier.
In addition, power supply stabilization circuit and power transistor protection circuit are mounted.

All stages of the preamp section are directly connected, and an NF tone control is built in.
It also incorporates four channel decoder amplifiers for discrete and CD-4 systems, SQ and regular matrix (RM) systems.
In addition to the remote control of surround balance, it is equipped with a 4-channel dubbing jack and an impedance level meter can be installed.

The front end of the FM tuner section is a 2-stage RF amplifier circuit using an FET and a frequency linear 4 series variable capacitor, and excellent interference rejection capability is obtained.
In addition, a ceramic filter and 4-stage direct-coupled IC are used for the IF stage, and an IC and a 15 khz low-pass filter are used for the MPX circuit.

The AM tuner uses a 3-row varicon for the RF stage and a ceramic filter for the IF stage.

Built-in preset volume.

A 2-meter system is used for the tuning instruction.
