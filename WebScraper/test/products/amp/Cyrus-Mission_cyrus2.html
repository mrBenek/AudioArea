  • Cyrus/Mission Cyrus 2

  • ¥ 105,000 (around 1987)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier designed based on the same concept as Cyrus1.

The Cyrus2 has improved current supply capability in addition to increased output.
It also has an MC input designed with an emphasis on noise and hysteresis elements.

There was an external power unit Cyrus PSX sold separately.
It is possible to expand the power supply capacity by combining with Cyrus PSX.

Since protection circuit is not mounted, it is necessary to pay attention to short circuit, etc.
