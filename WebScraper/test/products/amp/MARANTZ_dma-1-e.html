  • Marantz DMA-1

  • ¥ 125,000 (released in 1989)

Commentary

A monaural power amplifier developed as a power amplifier to be paired with DAC-1 under the theme of "The best technology for the best music playback".

The output stage is of parallel push-pull configuration.

The power transformer uses a toroidal type with low loss and low leakage flux.

Noise is reduced by copper plated chassis.
