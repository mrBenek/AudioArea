  • DENON PMA-6.5L

  • ¥ 40,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier of the point component series that was developed with the aim of high-purity music reproduction in spite of its compact size.

The left and right independent structure is adopted, and interference between the left and right channels is suppressed by the left and right independent amplifier circuit and heat sink layout.

Equipped with a large power transformer, it improves the speaker drive performance.

It comes with a remote control that can turn the power on/off, adjust the volume, and switch the source.
The ease of use is improved by keeping the infrequently used keys in the sliding door.
