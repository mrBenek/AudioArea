  • Marantz SM-50

  • ¥ 52,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

A stereo power amplifier of the Marantz slim type component component 50 series.

The amplifier circuit realizes a Low-TIM design by the DC amplifier configuration with DC servo IC.

The power supply section uses a newly developed EI transformer with superior high-frequency characteristics and sufficient collector loss.

Equipped with a logarithmic compression type power meter that can read peak power of 4 Ω and 8 Ω without switching the range.

Equipped with two types of speaker jacks, you can sound simultaneously or separately.
