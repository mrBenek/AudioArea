  • VICTOR/JVC JR-X10

  • ¥ 124,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 4 ch receiver that combines 4 ch amplifier, tuner, SEA controller, CD-4 demodulator and matrix circuit into one unit.

Equipped with a CD-4 demodulator that allows you to enjoy a discrete 4-channel system. The built-in demodulator is a high compatible type.
The JR-X10 also has a built-in circuit for matrix 4 ch playback. This matrix circuit is a two position switching type of matrix 1 and matrix 2, and all matrix 4 ch modes can be played back by two matrix circuits.

Four power amplifiers are built in for 4-ch playback. This power amplifier can be used as a high-power 2-ch amplifier by connecting a BTL circuit during 2-ch playback.

A 5-element SEA system is incorporated in place of the normal tone control. The SEA system divides the audible range into five parts and allows for ± 12 dB variation at each point.
There is also a normal tone control for the rear channel.

Equipped with an FM/AM tuner.
The tuner section uses a 2-meter system for more accurate tuning.

Equipped with low-cut / high-cut filter and roundness control.

The optional 4-ch remote controller terminal and 4-ch headphone terminal are installed.
