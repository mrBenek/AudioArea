  • BRYSTON 14B-ST

  • ¥ 920,000 (around 2000)

Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier corresponding to the flagship model.

The circuit configuration uses a Bryson's unique quad complimentary service circuit to achieve low distortion, high linearity, and high speed that cannot be achieved with a general Darlington connection.

The 14B-ST takes a closer look at the circuitry and components to achieve higher levels of distortion, noise, and speed.
It also provides a powerful driving force that does not saturate even with a 1 Ω ultra-low impedance speaker.

There are 2 color variations of black and silver.
