Commentary

A control amplifier developed with 105 improvements based on the ML-7L.

The body (ML-7AL) and power supply (PLS-154L) are designed to be separated and independent, and three bus lines of plus, minus, and earth realize low impedance and suppress mutual interference.

All parts, including the volume and tape switching relay, are made from carefully selected materials such as MIL-standard for cosmic rays and aircraft. In particular, the heaviest RIAA path on the Phono stage uses MIL-standard vibration-resistant Teflon capacitors.

The connection terminals of the cards that make up each stage use an 8-PIN type with minimal contact resistance and little change in sound quality due to insertion and removal. A heat sink is installed on each card to stabilize the elements and improve the strength against external vibration.

Lead wires are minimized by thorough simplification such as the use of a four layer board for the I/O board.

The phono amp has two optional high-gain L3 and L3A plug-in cards that can be connected directly to the MC cartridge.
