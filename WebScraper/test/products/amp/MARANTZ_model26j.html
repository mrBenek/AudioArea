  • Marantz Model 26J

  • ¥ 75,000 (around 1970)

Commentary

FM/AM tuner with gyro-touch tuning.

The amplifier section consists of all-silicon transistors.

Marantz's unique gyro-touch tuning is used in the tuner section, and smooth tuning can be performed by directly rotating the flywheel.

It is equipped with an inter-station muting circuit to suppress noise during FM channel selection.

Equipped with a tuning meter.

The front headphone jack can be used with the speakers on or off.

An automatic circuit breaker is incorporated into the power amplifier section as a protective circuit.
It prevents damage to output transistors and speakers caused by overload conditions.

It is equipped with functions such as main and sub speaker switching, tape monitor, loudness control and high-cut filter.

The wood cabinet was sold separately as an option.
