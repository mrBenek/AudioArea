  • Technics SU-8055

  • ¥ 51,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

A DC pre-main amplifier that eliminates the capacitor with a time constant between the high-level input and the speaker output.

In the first stage of the power amplifier section, a single-package dual transistor with uniform characteristics is adopted. This transistor is operated by a current mirror load capable of selecting an operating current with low distortion, and is fed to the voltage amplifier stage through an emitter follower.

Power Pack ICs composed of 2-stage Darlington connection pure complimentary service circuit are mounted on the output-stage.

It uses a newly developed recording and playback selector that makes the Rec selector and Input selector independent.

Equipped with an FL peak power meter that lights up in a bar graph style from 0.1W to 100W to display output.
