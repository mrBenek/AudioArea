Commentary

A surround amplifier equipped with four surround modes including Dolby surround.
With the switch, you can choose different realism such as Dolby Surround, Theater, Stadium, Simulated Stereo, etc.

Built-in 40W + 40W power amplifier for surround sound.

It is equipped with a dynamic expander to enhance the low and high frequencies and to enable sharp sound field reproduction.
