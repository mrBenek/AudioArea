Commentary

A 4-channel receiver with built-in SE decoder in addition to RM (regular matrix) and SQ matrix circuits.

The amplifier section is equipped with a two source simultaneous play mechanism, so that two sources such as disc and FM broadcast or disc and tape can be played simultaneously in two channels.

Pure complimentary service OTL circuit is adopted in the power amplifier section, and noise and distortion are reduced by eliminating the condenser between stages.

SE logic circuit is adopted in the decoder section.
This eliminates crosstalk, which is said to be a defect of the conventional matrix 4 channel, and improves separation.

The FM tuner uses FET and RF1-stage amplification.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit.

4-channel recording and playback is possible.

Equipped with 4 level meters.

Die-cast frame is used for the front panel.
