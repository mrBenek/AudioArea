  • SONY TA-YX5F

  • ¥ 40,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with TSS (tuner / signal sync) terminal.

Uses high-efficiency FET buffer power supply and Hi-ft power transistor with excellent high-speed response.

Equipped with a TSS (Tuner Signal Sync) terminal that automatically sets the recording level of the deck (TC-YX7) in conjunction with the tuner function.

It is equipped with a base boost function to compensate for the low range of small speakers.

It uses a sliding volume.

Uses a die-cast body.
