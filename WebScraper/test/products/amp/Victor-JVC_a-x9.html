Commentary

Pre-main amplifier that adopts super A circuit.

The A-X9 uses a simple two amplifier configuration with only an equalizer and a power amplifier. It was developed with the aim of becoming a "straight wire with amplification degree" which is the ideal amplifier.

Ultra-low distortion and high efficiency Super A circuit is used for the power amplifier section.
Super A has A-class operation with no switching distortion, yet has power efficiency and high power comparable to B-class amplifiers, and achieves a lower distortion rate than conventional A-class amplifiers.
In addition, the high level input is directly connected without passing through the preamp because of the high amplification degree, which realizes a complete DC single-amplifier configuration with no coupling capacitor from the tuner, tape and aux inputs to the speaker output.

The output stage has the latest high f.TIt uses a power transistor. Together with the Super A driver circuit with fast response speed, the measured value of TIM strain is 0.

The equalizer section is a high-gain DC configuration using JVC's unique pair EL-FET. The servo system cuts the ultra-low frequency band that is unnecessary for music playback and improves DC stability, while utilizing the circuit technology of Super A to reduce distortion.
The equalizer is designed so that the MC cartridge can also be connected directly. Switching between MC and MM is done by the remote switch located in the front panel ceiling door, and the gain changes automatically.

A 380 VA toroidal transformer is used for the power supply section. Mutual interference is suppressed by separating the secondary side of the transformer for the voltage amplifier stage and the power amplifier stage. In addition, all power supplies are directly connected to each circuit by the direct power supply system for all stages, and wide-band low impedance is achieved. Especially, copper bus bars are used for the power supply line for the power stage to reduce the power supply impedance.

Newly developed tone control for audio band is adopted.
Since this tone control circuit is incorporated in the high-gain power amplifier section, the number of amplification stages does not change even if it is turned ON, the deterioration of sound quality is minimized.

The relay type speaker selector and copper plate output line provide low impedance from the power transistor to the speaker output terminal.

Equipped with air dump type ceiling door and illuminated function indicator.

Uses a newly developed subsonic filter.
In the conventional subsonic filter circuit, the signal current flows through a large-capacity electrolytic capacitor. However, in the newly developed circuit of A-X9, the signal current almost flows through the capacitor, and a small-capacity one can be used. Therefore, by using a high-quality film capacitor, distortion is reduced and sound quality is improved.

A gold-plated terminal is used for the phono input.
