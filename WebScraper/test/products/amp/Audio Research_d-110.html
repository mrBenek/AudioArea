  • Audio Research D-110

  • ¥ 720,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

A stereo power amplifier with the same chassis structure as the D-350.

It uses the same chassis structure and power supply as the D-350, and only output is limited to 100Wx2.

It can also be used as a high-power monaural power amplifier by bridge connection.

Fully 2-monaural configuration is adopted.
