Commentary

This pre-main amplifier realizes low impedance load drive capability of up to 2 Ω by mounting a strong power stage and power supply.

The power supply section is equipped with a heavyweight power transformer and a dedicated audio capacitor.

Equipped with a source direct function, all input sources can be transmitted to the power amplifier in the shortest distance after passing the accessory circuit.

Equipped with tone control equipped with defeat function.

The chassis is a solid chassis with sufficient consideration for vibration resistance and shielding characteristics, eliminating the influence of unnecessary noise and electromagnetic wave noise.

The REC selector allows you to disconnect the recording input / output circuit from the main signal path when the recording or dubbing function is not used.

Equipped with a MM/MC switching phono equalizer.

Uses a power cord with polarity display.

Equipped with a subsonic filter.
