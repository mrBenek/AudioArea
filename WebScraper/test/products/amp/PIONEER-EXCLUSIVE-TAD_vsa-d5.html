Commentary

An AV amplifier equipped with a Dolby digital decoder.

The surround format is Dolby Digital.
The decoder is equipped with a Dolby Digital 1-chip IC-based decoder. By reducing the size of the digital circuit, noise is eliminated as much as possible quality.

The power amplifier section is equipped with a 5-channel power amplifier in the same condition, enabling surround sound reproduction with a rich sense of presence.

Equipped with 6-mode basic sound field programs (Theater1, Theater2, Hall1, Hall2, JAZZ, and Dance) using original DSP technology.
This program allows you to adjust the effect level so that you can create your own surround space to suit your room preferences.
Equipped with Midnight Listening mode.
In this mode, you can compensate for the power and sound that tend to be lacking during low-volume playback at night. Various parameters such as frequency characteristics, inter-channel balance, inter-channel delay, and dynamic range are changed in conjunction with volume changes, so that compensation can be made according to the characteristics of human hearing.

It is equipped with Route 3 digital input and one digital output.

The audio system has four inputs and one output, the video system has four inputs and two outputs, and the S video terminal has four inputs and two outputs.

An energy-saving design that reduces power consumption during standby is adopted.

A multi-control remote controller with some self-illuminated keys is included.
