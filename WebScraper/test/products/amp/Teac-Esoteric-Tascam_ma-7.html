  • TEAC MA-7

  • ¥ 180,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

An Armonia series power amplifier jointly developed by TEAC and TANNY under the theme of "Reproducing high-quality sound from TANNY speakers using high-quality MC cartridges".

Mr. Norman J Crocker, then president of TANNOY, evaluated this amplifier as an important listening test system for TANNOY products.

The MA-7 has a fully DC power amplifier configuration with a newly developed protective circuit built in, and uses a double monaural configuration to prevent interference between the left and right channels.

Equipped with a newly developed protective circuit that sharply cuts frequencies below 5 Hz.

A feedforward circuit is adopted.

Equipped with load short-circuit protection circuit.
