Commentary

Integrated amplifier with the concept of non-interference energy amplification from input to output.

The signal flow is thoroughly verified, and construction according to the energy level of the music signal and the energy level of the interference signal is pursued, and independent 3-block construction is adopted.
This structure clearly separates the preamplifier section, control section and power amplifier section, and the signal length inside the chassis is reduced to 1/3 compared with the conventional Victor model.
In addition, the volume and function by remote control are used to achieve a thorough non-interference design.

In order to eliminate the effect of the chassis on sound quality, high carbon cast iron, which is the most acoustically excellent cast iron, is used in the chassis base to achieve excellent vibration damping characteristics.
Parts that are sources of vibration such as power transformers and heat sinks are firmly fixed with through bolts.

We pay attention to the fact that the transistor is a current amplifier, and all necessary voltage amplification is done in the preamplifier section.
As a result, the preamp itself acts as a volume, so more the volume is reduced, the less residual noise.

It consists of a power amplifier with a "voltage amplification factor of 0 dB" that feeds back the current generated by the difference between the input signal and the output signal to the predriver.

Victor's original Super A has been improved to Advanced Super A.
The output device is narrowed down to 3 para PP, which can be called single PP, and 200W/3 Ω is realized.

Equipped with a phono amplifier.
It is equipped with a high gain equalizer of 1 kHz and 60 dB. Moreover, high output is realized by increasing the power supply voltage by about three times that of the conventional one. Various cartridges can be connected directly, from MC cartridge with 0.2 mV sensitivity to MM cartridge with 5 mV sensitivity.
In addition, when any input other than the phono input is selected, the power supply of the phono circuit is automatically cut off, and we are particular about improving the sound quality of the line system such as CD.
