  • KENWOOD KA-3020

  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1993)

Commentary

This pre-main amplifier was highly evaluated in Europe, especially in the United Kingdom, and later released in Japan.

It inherits the design philosophy of advanced components by adopting a pure signal ground line that enables pure amplification and logical flow construction of internal layout along the signal path.
