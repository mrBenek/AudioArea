  • ¥ 45,000 (around 1983)

Commentary

This pre-main amplifier is designed to be compatible with CD players.

The power amplifier section uses a 2-stage differential circuit to achieve low distortion and low noise and stability.

An electronic one touch operated input selector is used for the operation part.
