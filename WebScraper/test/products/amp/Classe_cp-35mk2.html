  • CLASSE CP-35mk2

  • ¥ 280,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

A preamplifier designed to reproduce music details with high-speed response characteristics.

By using a large number of sealed toroidal transformers and high-quality, small-capacity capacitors in the power supply section, high-speed response characteristics are realized, and reproducibility of music details is enhanced.

The input jacks are equipped with Line 5 inputs including tape inputs, and the output jacks are equipped with one unbalanced output.
There is also one balanced input / output system.

Wireless remote control is included.

There was an optional MM/MC phono input card sold separately.
