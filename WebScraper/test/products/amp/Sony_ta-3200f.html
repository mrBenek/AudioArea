  • SONY TA-3200F

  • ¥ 80,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

An ES series power amplifier that pursues not only power supremacy but also ultra-low distortion.

In addition to the output coupling capacitor, the coupling capacitor between each stage is removed, and a direct coupling method using a pair of transistors with excellent characteristics is adopted.

Uses a protection circuit that detects the output current and controls the current of the output transistor.

Equipped with a power limiter switch that limits the maximum output to 1/2 or 1/4.

Equipped with two inputs and two outputs, each of which can be switched with a switch.

Equipped with a knob for adjusting the input level.

It is equipped with a special circuit to protect the speaker during abnormal operation.
