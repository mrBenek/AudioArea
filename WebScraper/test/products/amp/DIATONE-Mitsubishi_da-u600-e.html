  • ¥ 46,800 (around 1972)

Commentary

Solid state pre-main amplifier with all stages directly connected.

The power amplifier section uses a differential amplifier and an all-stage direct connection OCL circuit without coupling and condenser. This circuit reduces the distortion factor and expands the power band with compared with the output circuit using conventional condenser. The output circuit is not affected by the output condenser and prevents the deterioration of sound quality.

It uses an equalizer amplifier with a high allowable input, enabling playback with less distortion.

A 3-layer protection circuit is provided. In the event of an error, a protection circuit consisting of four transistor protection fuses, two speaker protection fuses, four transistors fuses, four transistors, and four diodes.

Two types of tapes can be played and dubbed without changing the connection.

Built-in microphone mixing circuit.
