  • AKAI AA-6000

  • ¥ 53,000 (around the early 1970 s)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with improved operability.

The power switch and volume control are placed in the large space in the center of the front panel, and the left and right tone control with the balance control at the center is placed in the lower part for ease of use.

A low-noise transistor is used in the preamplifier section.
