  • Marantz AV500

  • ¥ 75,000 (released in 1993)

Commentary

An AV surround preamplifier equipped with Dolby Pro Logic, 3-channel logic and 4 AV sound field programs (Movie, Matrix, Hall1 and Hall2).

Equipped with a picture-in-picture for the first time in an AV amplifier.

It has an on-screen display that shows the configuration status.

Four audio channels, AV (S) Line 5 input / output jacks are mounted.

Intelligent remote control is included.
