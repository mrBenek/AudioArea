  • ¥ 88,000 (around 2001)

Commentary

5chx100W discrete configuration, AV amplifier with Victor's original advanced super A in main 2 ch.

The Z-chassis eliminates unnecessary vibration, the shortest layout of the signal path, and the wrap-packed EI core transformer are all measures taken to enhance sound quality.

Equipped with Victor's unique K2 interface that eliminates distortion in digital signal transmission. This digital signal is converted into analog by a PEM-DD converter with a dynamic range of over 108 dB.

Equipped with a digital acoustic processor (DAP) jointly developed with Tokyu Construction Co., Ltd., which is engaged in the acoustic design of Shibuya-ku Orchard Hall and other buildings, this system enables the extraction of various acoustic patterns, which were difficult to obtain by measuring reflected sound using conventional impulse responses.
