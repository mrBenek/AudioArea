  • Pioneer QX-704

  • ¥ 148,000 (released in 1973)

Commentary

A 4-channel receiver equipped with 3-way 4-channel playback function.

4 ch playback of 3 systems (CD-4, RM/SQ) is possible.
The CD-4 Demodulator is designed with consideration for frequency characteristics and separation by using PLL and FET. It also has an ASC circuit that automatically compensates for noise even in frequently used records to improve S/N. Separation and carrier level can be adjusted using the attached test record.
RM/SQ4ch playback has built-in decoders for efficient playback of RM/SQ records. In addition, 2 ch records, tapes, broadcasts, etc. can also be played through the matrix decoder to get a sense of presence.

The power amplifier section uses a pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages.

A 15,000 μ Fx2 power Fx2 power supply capacitor.

Equipped with a power boosting circuit, it is possible to enhance the output during 2 ch playback by switching the switch on the rear panel.
The ordinary BTL circuits, the circuit used in the QX 704 can be driven by a 4 Ω speaker.

The equalizer amplifier section is a 3-stage direct connection type.

The control amplifier is an NF type with a 3-chip configuration and has excellent control characteristics.
Front and rear can be adjusted independently of each other.

Equipped with a protective circuit that combines a power relay and an electronic circuit.
This circuit also functions as a power muting, and reduces shock noise when the power is turned on.

The front-end section of the FM tuner uses a frequency-linear 4-row varistor and a MOS FET. In addition, high-integration ICs for FM and the IF section of a 5-stage limiter that employs a phase-linear ceramic filter, a highly linear quadrature detection circuit, and ICs for FM MPX are used to improve the performance.
It also has an FM muting switch and a 4 ch MPX output terminal.

Equipped with a 4-channel level indicator that allows you to see the output level at a glance and allows you to accurately adjust the level during 4-channel playback.
The indicator sensitivity can be switched in 4 stages according to the volume level.
