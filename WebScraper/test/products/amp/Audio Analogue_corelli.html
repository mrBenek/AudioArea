  • Audio Analogue CORELLI

  • ¥ 220,000 (around 2003)

Commentary

Stereo power amplifier with dual monaural configuration.

The power supply section is composed of two large toroidal transformers and a large capacity capacitor arranged on both sides.

The power stage adopts a parallel push-pull configuration.
