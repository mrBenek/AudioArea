  • YAMAHA M-35

  • ¥ 39,800 (released in 1987)

Commentary

Multi-use power amplifier capable of 2ch/4ch switching.
Switching between 2 ch and 4 ch is possible with the selector switch.

Equipped with speaker level adjustment function that can adjust the volume of F/R (front and rear) speakers for 4-amp, and L/R (left and right) speakers for 2-amp.

Equipped with AC outlet and built-in DC detection protection circuit.
