Commentary

A stereo power amplifier incorporating the basic design of the famous monaural power amplifier Mark-III as it is for stereo.

The output transformer is a custom-made output transformer made by Acrosound Inc., which was established by David Hafler, the founder of DYNACO.

By using spare parts throughout the entire circuit, aging due to long-term use is reduced.
In particular, the output tube is limited to 65% of the rated output, and the filter condenser is operated at less than 85% of the rated voltage.
