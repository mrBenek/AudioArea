  • DENON/COLUMBIA MAT-4

  • ¥ 56,800 (around 1968)

Commentary

Solid-state premain amplifier with all-silicon transistor configuration.

A single diffused mesa type power transistor is used for the output circuit, and SEPP-OTL system with Darlington connection is adopted.

Equipped with a purely electronic automatic recovery protection circuit.

Equipped with tone control, defeat switch and rumble filter.

Equipped with a latch filter.

Equipped with a loudness control.

Equipped with a level meter.

Uses DSC method.
