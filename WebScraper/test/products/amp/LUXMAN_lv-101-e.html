Commentary

Pre-main amplifier of the Brid series.

Equipped with CD straight function.
When this function is turned ON, part of the signal path is bypassed, so that sound quality degradation due to switch contacts and wiring can be prevented.

Equipped with two video input systems.

MOS-FET is used in the driver stage of the power amplifier section.
In addition, S. T. A. R. Saiki has been adopted. Sharing of power supply lines and ground lines has been completely abolished. All lines go directly to circuit blocks and signal paths, improving the S / N ratio in terms of audibility.

It is equipped with signal processing jacks for surround processors and graphic equalizers.
