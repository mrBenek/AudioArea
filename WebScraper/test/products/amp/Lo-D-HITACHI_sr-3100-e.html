Commentary

AM/FM stereo / FM stereo receiver that integrates Lordi's receiver's receiver technology.

The FM tuner uses a dual-gate MOS FET and a 3-row varicon AGC on the front end.
In addition, a high integration IC, the IF part of a 4-element phase linear ceramic filter, and highly reliable quadrature detection have realized good distortion factor and S/N characteristics.

The multi-demodulation circuit uses a double-balanced differential switching circuit to obtain stable separation.

The power amplifier section uses an inverted Darlington type OCL circuit directly connected to all stages using NPN and PNP silicon transistors with good linearity.

In the equalizer stage of the preamp section, a circular transistor designed for low noise is used in the first stage to reduce input conversion noise. In addition, the 3 stages of ± 2 power supply system 2 power supply system, it has a large bare characteristics. Since a large amount of NF is applied, low distortion is realized over the whole band.

Large-capacity transistors and zener diodes are used for the tuner, equalizer and driver stages. Each stage is supplied by a separate rectifier circuit and constant voltage power supply, thereby suppressing voltage fluctuation of the AC line and interference between circuit blocks and channels.

The AM tuner section uses a 4-element phase linear filter and a high-integration IC balanced mixer.

Equipped with microphone mixing circuit and FM muting mechanism.
