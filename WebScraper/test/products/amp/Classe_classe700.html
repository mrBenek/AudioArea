Commentary

A monaural power amplifier developed as a subordinate of the Classe1000.

The Circuit Design Of The Classe700 is a continuation of the advanced model, and the case size and power supply unit are reduced by one.

The signal system has a fully balanced design consisting of differential amplifiers from the input stage to the output stage.

Equipped with balanced input terminal.
