Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with classAA technology of SE-A100/SU-A200.

ClassAA has a VC-4 amplifier configuration with two types of amplifiers for each L and R. One is a pure class A voltage-controlled amplifier that is free from switching distortion and crossover distortion, and the other is a current-driven amplifier that drives speakers dynamically.
As a result, the voltage-controlled amplifier is freed from the burden of current supply, and the voltage can be controlled faithfully to the input signal. The current-driven amplifier supplies the current required to drive the speaker and eliminates adverse effects such as impedance fluctuations of the speaker.

The power supply section is equipped with a large transformer and a large capacitor with an instantaneous current supply capacity of 300A and excellent linearity. In addition, the output stage and the power supply section are integrated with an ingenious configuration by a unique concentrated power block, eliminating high-frequency distortion caused by electromagnetic radiation.

Equipped with a REC mode selector that allows you to select the recording source independently.

Equipped with the CD direct function, it is possible to connect directly to a volume without using the input selector.

The phono equalizer is a high-gain type with MC basic design. It is an ICL configuration combining a first stage amplifier with ultra-low-noise FET and a high-voltage and high-slew rate operational amplifier.

Large metal legs are used to suppress adverse effects caused by external vibration.
