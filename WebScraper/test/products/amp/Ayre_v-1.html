  • Price unknown (around 1998)

Commentary

A stereo power amplifier developed as the top-end model of Ayre.

A selected MOS-FET with excellent linearity is used for the output stage, constituting a non-NFB non-feedback circuit.

A fully balanced circuit is used from input to output.

The toroidal choke coil reduces the noise component of the power supply and eliminates the effect of the return signal.

Equipped with a remote control function, it can be linked with a K-1 preamplifier.
