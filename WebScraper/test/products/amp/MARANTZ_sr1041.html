  • Marantz SR-1041

  • ¥ 52,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

Slim style stereo receiver.

Built-in FM/AM tuner.
The tuner section is equipped with 30 random preset memories.

Equipped with 4 types of timers including sleep mode.

There are 2 color variations of titanium black and green.

System remote control is included.
