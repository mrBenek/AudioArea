  • Lo-D HA-630

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier that uses Dynaharmony (E-class drive).

The power amplifier uses the Dynaharmony (E-class drive) system to achieve high power efficiency.
The class A drive circuit adopts a current mirror circuit and performs push-pull drive of the power stage.
A four power supply system of ± 67 v high voltage and ± 31.5 v is adopted for the power supply. High-speed power switching is performed for the instantaneous level of the music program, and dynamic margin for the dynamic range of the music program is improved to achieve high efficiency.
We also use high-speed switching elements such as high-voltage high-speed switching diodes and high-current high-speed switching diodes that have been developed especially for the Dynaharmony system.

The pre-equalizer stage has a 3-stage direct connection structure using a PNP transistor for the first stage differential.

The tone control section combines a high-gain and high-stability ICHA 1456 with an NF-type circuit to achieve a low distortion rate.
It is also equipped with a tone defeat switch. When the tone switch for adjusting the sound quality is switched to defeat mode, flat frequency characteristics can be obtained instantly regardless of the position of the tone control.

A 32-contact attenuator is used for volume control, enabling precise level control.

Equipped with a 3-stage gain selector, the volume can be adjusted by attenuator to 0 db, -15dB or -30dB depending on the efficiency of the speaker.

Equipped with a subsonic filter, high filter and loudness control.

Built-in electronic protection circuit.

The wood case in the picture was sold as an optional item sold separately.
