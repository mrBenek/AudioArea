Commentary

Multi-function control amplifier with built-in surround processor.

With a built-in surround function, you can choose from three positions : Dolby surround, Matrix surround, or Simulated Stereo which adds breadth and depth to mono source.
Also, the Dolby input can be calibrated with the rear panel knob.
The surround function can be bypassed.

It has an external surround processor input and can be connected to an external surround processor.

Equipped with 3-band split tone control.

Audio and video signals are completely isolated to eliminate mutual interference.
Each of the phono equalizer, tone controller and surround processor is equipped with a power regulator to eliminate interference between circuits.

Each video circuit is equipped with a buffer amplifier to reduce video buzz and pop noise.
