Commentary

A preamplifier developed exclusively for analog records.

The 838mk2 is designed to transfer the output signal through an independent passive circuit when connected to a power amplifier, so that line-level music playback operations are not affected even when the power supply is turned off. This not only eliminates the convenience of requiring no connection changes, but also eliminates the effect of degraded signals from other preamps on the phono stage.
It is also possible to use MM/MC preferred cartridges by adding 838mk2.

Only for MC or MM type. Two types of cartridges cannot be connected at the same time.
The standard input terminal is an unbalanced type, and the balanced type is optional.

Six pairs of adapter plugs are included as standard to optimize the impedance of the cartridge and preamplifier.
The impedance range is from 3 to 1,000 Ω.

Equipped with a DIP switch with eight different sensitivity settings, the input sensitivity can be adjusted, which is important for optimum S/N ratio and over-load margin. This improves the S/N ratio and protects the cartridge from over-loading.

By setting the linear stage before RIAA equalization, the dynamic range which stands out in the high frequency region is obtained.
By preserving the minimum HF signal even after the equalization process, the sound quality is transparent, stretchy and high-pitched sound, and three dimensional and stable stereo images are reproduced.

The RIAA deviation is less than 1% of the characteristic curve in the 30 hz to 20 khz band.
The hum noise is at least 20 dB below the noise level typical of analog records, and the distortion is less than the measurement limit.

It is a dual monaural design including the transformer in the independent power supply box, and there is no intermodulation between each channel.

A step-type attenuator type level controller using a low-capacity register and an effective range doubling independent switch is adopted.

The power amplier or active speaker can be remotely turned on / off by the DC signal of the independent power box.
