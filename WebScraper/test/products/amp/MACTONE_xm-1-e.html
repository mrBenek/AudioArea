  • MACTONE XM-1

  • ¥ 390,000 (around the 1990 s?)

Commentary

A bulb-type pre-main amplifier equipped with a McTon's original direct linear system.

It is equipped with a direct linear system and consists of a highly sensitive output circuit to eliminate redundant amplifier circuits. In addition, by minimizing the number of tubes, the number of components through which signals pass is reduced.

6550A is adopted for the output tube, and it is a push-pull configuration.
The output transformer is specially designed for XM-1.
