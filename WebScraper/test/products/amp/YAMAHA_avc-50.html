Commentary

A video enhancer with 4 video input channels and 10 audio channels, and an AV pre-main amplifier with built-in surround sound.
It is equipped with functions such as playing back any combination of video and audio sources.

It is equipped with a full Rec Out selector which is independent of image and sound and can freely edit AV bidirectional dubbing.
Equipped with two video Rec Out channels and four audio Rec Out channels, it can record two devices simultaneously and four devices simultaneously.

A 3-mode video enhancer is provided to correct image conditions.
Detail mode repairs the details of the image by adjusting the video frequency.
In Sharpness mode, the edges of the image are emphasized by making the boundaries between bright and dark areas more distinct.
Video Level mode controls the magnitude of the video signal level and adjusts the subtle brightness of the screen.
By operating these three modes, the image condition can be adjusted.
In particular, the Sharpness is equipped with a Picture Clean circuit that predicts the brightness signal, and predicts bright and dark areas in the screen to make the edges of the image clearly visible.
In addition, it is equipped with a video enhancer on / off switch that enables quick comparison of images before and after correction.

Equipped with 6-mode surround sound which enables sound field effect reproduction.
In the Simulated Stereo mode, the mono source is divided into two channels using a comb filter to create a pseudo stereo.
In Simulated Round mode, surround playback is performed with a delay applied only to the rear side.
In defeat mode, a mono or stereo source is played through to four speakers.
In Natural Surround mode, a comb filter is used to distribute the reverberation from the stereo source and play it from the rear.
In Whole Surround mode, a further delay is applied to play back the surround sound with a deeper sense of spaciousness.
The Dolby Surround mode combines a Dolby Surround decoder with a delay unit to play Dolby Surround credited video.
The delay time is continuously variable from 10 ms to 30 ms. It is equipped with a bus extension switch to increase the volume of the low band and a high filter switch to cut the high band noise.

Comes with a remote control that can operate Yamaha components with the RS mark.
