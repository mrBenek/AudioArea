  • Technics SU-V500

  • ¥ 32,800 (released in 1996)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with new class A circuit.

By adopting a new class A circuit, switching distortion and crossover distortion are suppressed, and transparent sound quality is realized.

Equipped with two speaker terminals that can be driven simultaneously (8 Ω + 8 Ω).

It is equipped with a phono input to which an analog player can be connected and 2 channels of tape input / output.

It is equipped with a system remote control which can operate Technics CD player, tuner and cassette deck.
