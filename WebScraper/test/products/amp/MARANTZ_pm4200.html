  • Marantz PM4200

  • Discretionary price - around ¥ 25,000 (released in June 2001)

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier of the Century series that is based on PM4000 and has a sound quality tune-up.

Equipped with a phono equalizer amplifier.

Equipped with a tone defeat circuit.

It is equipped with various input / output terminals such as CD-R dedicated input / output.

Equipped with a Rec-out selector.

Equipped with two speaker outputs.
The terminal uses a screw-type speaker terminal that is compatible with a banana plug.

Hairline finish is applied to the front panel.

There are 2 color variations, black and gold.

Comes with a system remote controller that allows simultaneous operation of Marantz products.
