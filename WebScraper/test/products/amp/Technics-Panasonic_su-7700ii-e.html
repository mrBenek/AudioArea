  • Technics SU-7700II

  • ¥ 52,800 (around 1977)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that has been improved based on the SU 7700.

A constant voltage power supply of ± 2 power supply using transistor and zener diode is adopted for the preamplifier section.

The common emitter power supply of the first stage differential amplifier of the main amplifier is constantly electrified by a transistor, and is also received by a current mirror.
