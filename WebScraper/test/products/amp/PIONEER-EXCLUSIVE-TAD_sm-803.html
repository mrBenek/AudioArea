  • Pioneer SM-803

  • ¥ 36,500 (around 1965)

Commentary

A bulb-type pre-main amplifier equipped with a full-scale equalizer amplifier.

Equipped with an equalizer amplifier, you can directly receive the output from a crystal, magnetic or tape head.

For the output stage, a new type power tube 7189A was adopted as a push-pull fixed vise system.

The power supply uses a silicon diode for voltage doubler rectification, and the head uses a selenium rectifier for DC ignition.

CR type is used for the tone control part.

Equipped with low / high filter and loudness control.
