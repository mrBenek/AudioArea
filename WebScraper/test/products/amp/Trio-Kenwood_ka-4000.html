  • TRIO KA-4000

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1968)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed based on Trio's excellent amplifier results.
High-quality silicon transistors are used from the preamp to the main amplifier, and both frequency and output band characteristics have improved high-frequency characteristics.

The preamplifier section is directly connected 3-stage amplification, and a large amount of NF is applied to improve the dynamic range.

The tone control for both Bass and Treble uses an 11-contact rotary switch that can change the tone in increments of 2 dB per step.
It also has a muting circuit so that the volume can be reduced by -20dB with a single switch.

The preamp and main amp can be used independently.

To protect the power transistor in case of emergency, it is equipped with an automatic return type pure electronic protection circuit.
