  • Audio Research SP-6

  • Price unknown (released in 1978)

Commentary

A preamplifier developed based on SP-3.

The latest technology advances the direction of several improvements made at the time of sale of the SP-3 and thorough enhancements to the power supply.

The circuit consists of six ECC83/12AX7 cards.
