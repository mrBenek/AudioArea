  • SANSUI BA-202

  • ¥ 42,200 (around 1970)

Commentary

A tube type power amplifier released by SANSUI.
For a long time after BA-202 and BA-303, tube type amplifiers were not released.

The 3-pole output tube 6R-A8 developed by Shin-NEC is used.
