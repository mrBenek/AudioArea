Commentary

This is a stereo control amplifier developed to be paired with a power amplifier of the dynamic harmony system.

The equalizer circuit section adopts a 3-stage connected 7-chip configuration of first stage FET differential-PNP differential-constant current drive NPN.
The coupling capacitor uses a capacitorless (ICL) system for the input and a non-inductive metallized polyester film capacitor with superior high-frequency characteristics for the output to improve the frequency and phase characteristics.
In addition, low-noise FETs and high-voltage low-noise transistors are used to provide sufficient feedback for stability over a wide band to improve various characteristics.

The tone control circuit adopts a 3-stage direct connection 6-chip configuration of PNP push-pull with a first stage FET differential low-current drive, and realizes low distortion factor by using carefully selected low-noise FETs and high-voltage low-noise transistors.
In addition, the tone volume is lost at the center, and a method is adopted in which the tone quality can be continuously changed without waviness.
In addition, the volume can be adjusted separately on the left and right by the 2-axis volume, and the sound quality can be corrected by changing the turnover frequency or by the tone defeat switch.

The HCA 8300 employs a 32-contact detent volume with a new mask to improve the conventional trimming type detent volume, which has a drawback that the attenuation characteristic deviates from the standard value at every one step.
The attenuation characteristics can be adjusted in 2 dB steps from 0 to -30dB and in 3 dB steps from -30dB to -70dB. In addition, a four string gang volume is adopted to improve S/N when listening at a low volume.

Equipped with low filter circuit.
In addition, it is equipped with a 3-stage gain selector that expands the fine adjustment range.

The level of Phono1 can be finely adjusted.

The headphone amplifier circuit is built-in.

Muting is activated by the reed relay when power is turned on.

The rack handle used in the photo was sold as a separately sold option.
