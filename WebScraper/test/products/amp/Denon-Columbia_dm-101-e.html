  • DENON/COLUMBIA DM-101

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1968)

Commentary

This tube type pre-main amplifier was designed to withstand professional use and was the top model at that time.

Equipped with a tone control defeat switch.

Wooden cabinet is adopted.
