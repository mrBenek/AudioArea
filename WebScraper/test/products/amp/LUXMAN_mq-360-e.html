  • LUXMAN MQ-360

  • ¥ 319,400 (released in October 1985)

Commentary

Bulb-tube type power amplifier.

In order to make full use of the attractiveness and real power of vacuum tubes, it operates in the class A range.
MQ-360 uses a self-bias method.

The driver circuit that drives the power stage employs a bi-triode 6CG7 that provides drive power with low impedance, and provides ample operation within the push-pull configuration of all stages.

The power supply is equipped with a high-capacity transformer to ensure high regulation even in the event of overcurrent, while also giving careful consideration to polarity control.
The output transformer uses an ultra-linear connection dedicated transformer that is ideal for a self-bias circuit. The high-frequency characteristics smoothly extending to 100 kHz and the distortion factor realize the sound quality unique to the tube bulb type.

The S. T. A. R. circuit is adopted with the idea that each circuit block does not interfere.
