  • ONKYO Integra 714

  • ¥ 48,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier equipped with preset tone control of 3-circuit switching.
The circuit configuration is almost the same as that of Integra 713.

In addition to a multi-step-up system that allows for multi-function, top panel and new chuck type speaker terminals are adopted.
