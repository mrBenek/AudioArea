  • ¥ 85,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

AV control center equipped with 5.1 ch input terminal and 5 ch power amplifier.
Equipped with Dolby Pro Logic.

It features a digital surround processor that decodes Dolby Digital (AC-3) and a discrete 6-channel input that can be connected to the analog outputs of a DVD player with 5.1 channel decode outputs.

It is compatible with discrete 4-ch Hi-Vision for Hi-Vision broadcasting.

Even when playing non-Dolby encoded CDs or cassette tapes, the SFC (Sound Field Control) is equipped so that you can enjoy 7 kinds of sound effects by DSP signal processing in a hall or live house.

It comes with a system remote controller that enables centralized control of Panasonic / Technics AV equipment.

Equipped with a power meter that can turn lighting on / off.
