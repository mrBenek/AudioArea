Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with a side terminal structure to realize a straight circuit structure.

A side terminal structure is adopted. All input signal terminals are laid out on the right side, and the speaker terminals are laid out on the left side. This realizes a circuit configuration according to the signal flow.

The equalizer circuit uses an IC of the first stage differential amplifier configuration with current mirror load.

In the pre-drive stage of the main amplifier section, a differential amplifier of the current mirror load is configured in the first stage to realize a low distortion factor. In addition, since tone control is performed using the NF circuit of the main amplifier, the circuit block has a simple structure consisting of an equalizer section and a power amplifier section.

The power supply circuit is equipped with a high-density winding transformer and 6,800 μ Fx2 electrolytic capacitor.

Equipped with a power meter that can switch the range between 0 dB and -10dB.
Germanium diode is used for rectification to improve linearity at small output power.

With independent tape monitor and recording mode selectors, you can switch the tape monitor switch to a different source while dubbing.

Equipped with a low filter and loudness switch.
