Commentary

This is a hybrid monaural power amplifier positioned at the top of the classic series.

The output stage is a tube bulb type, and a large output of 150W is achieved by using eight 6550 s. In addition, the output stage employs a balanced drive system that drives the plus side and minus side of the signal independently.

The input level can be adjusted in 5 levels.
