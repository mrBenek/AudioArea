  • Technics SU-3050

  • ¥ 36,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with microphone circuit for enjoying microphone mixing.

The main amplifier section consists of two ICs for all stages of direct-coupled differential amplifier OCL circuit.
Because the OCL circuit does not degrade the damping factor in the lower range, the speakers can be adequately damped to provide a tighter bass playback. The differential amplifier also eliminates disturbances (temperature fluctuations, power supply changes, noise, etc.) in the form of in-phase inputs.

The equalizer circuit uses a 2-stage direct-coupled IC to increase reliability and provide a wide dynamic range to minimize clip distortion.

Equipped with two tape monitor circuits.

Equipped with a high filter.

Equipped with two speaker outputs.
