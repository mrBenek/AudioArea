  • DENON PMA-380V

  • ¥ 36,400 (released in 1989)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier equipped with video input / output and remote control.

In addition to audio input / output, video input / output terminals and switching functions are installed in the Route 3, making it possible to connect a video deck, etc.

The motherboard chassis layout is designed to simplify the circuitry and optimize the layout of parts and wiring.

It comes with a remote control that allows power on/off, input switching, volume and muting operation.
