  • AIWA S-C7

  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

Stereo preamplifier with built-in MC head amplifier of mini component my Pace series.

Built-in head amplifier for MC type cartridge.
Switching between MM and MC is done from the front panel.

The low-noise transistor in the first stage of the equalizer circuit is combined with a high-gain IC to reduce noise and improve stability. The RIAA device uses carefully selected polypropylene capacitors and high-precision metal film resistors to achieve a frequency response of 20 Hz to 20 kHz ± 0.2 dB or less.

The operation part is operated by push keys.

Equipped with a muting relay to prevent pop noise coming out of the speaker.

Equipped with tone control.
S-C7 is designed to be lost at the center of the knob.

Equipped with a low-cut filter, muting switch and loudness control.
