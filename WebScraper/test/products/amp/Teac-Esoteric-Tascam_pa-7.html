  • TEAC PA-7

  • ¥ 180,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

An Armonia series control amplifier jointly developed by TEAC and TANNY under the theme of "Reproducing high-quality sound from TANNY speakers using a high-quality MC cartridge".

Mr. Norman J Crocker, then president of TANNOY, evaluated this amplifier as an important listening test system for TANNOY products.

The PA-7 is a DC control amplifier with a built-in double integration servo circuit. It is intended to improve the sound quality of the MC head amplifier assuming the use of the MC cartridge.

It has a built-in amplifier for headphones only.

You can adjust the recorded sound quality using the tone control.

Protection circuit and click countermeasure are provided.

Equipped with a subsonic filter.
