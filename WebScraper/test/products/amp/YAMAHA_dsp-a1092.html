Commentary

DSP AV amplifier for 7-ch full-mode surround playback.

Equipped with the YSS 245 f as a key device for sound field processing.
The YSS140-F has built-in functions required for Yamaha's original cinema DSP processing, and realizes a Dolby Digital Prologic Decoder and a hard DSP sound field processing in one chip for the YSS140-F. 245.
As a result, 5.1 channels of Dolby digital signals can be fully decoded with 24-bit high precision, and combined with a 3-field cinema DSP LSI, it realizes realistic theater sound.

The Yamaha Sound Field Program has 13 programs.
Sound field data exclusively for Dolby Digital is used for Enhanced and Movie Theater, sound field programs for Dolby Digital, drawing out the potential of Dolby Digital.

Power amplifiers of the same design are used for the front 3 channels and the rear 2 channels.
In addition, it is equipped with Yamaha's original 2-channel amplifier for front effect, and by installing it, the sound field expands and the sense of depth is enriched.
A 4 Ω speaker drive is available except for the front effect.

It is equipped with nine input terminals, and DVD/LD and TV/DBS are equipped with digital input coaxial with optical.

The Rec out selector on the front panel allows smooth recording without changing the connection on the rear panel.
It also has a built-in BGV function that combines AV source video with audio from other audio sources.

Equipped with wood-grain side panels.
This panel is made of carbon black foam resin and has excellent vibration absorption effect. The surface is mirror finished with acrylic resin by a new manufacturing method.

On-screen capability.
* The output of on-screen signal is output to both composite / S video if there is no input to composite / S video. Also, if there is input to composite and there is input to S video, it is output only to composite output.
If both composite and S-image have input, S-image output will be used.

By using the separately sold APD-1, it is possible to play 5.1 channels with a Dolby digital compatible LD player.

There are 3 color variations of titanium color, gold and black.

A learning remote a learning remote controller that allows you to select the sound field and adjust various parameters.
