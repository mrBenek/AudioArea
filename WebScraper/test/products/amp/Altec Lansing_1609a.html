Commentary

Bi-amplifier that combines two power amplifiers and a crossover network.

A 100-W low-frequency amplifier, a 50-W middle-high amplifier, and an electronic crossover network are integrated.

Three frequency points can be selected for the crossover frequency so that the Altec speaker can be driven under ideal conditions.

Professional 19-inch rack-mount type.
