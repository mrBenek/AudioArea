  • Technics SU-A700MK2

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1996)

Commentary

Integrated amplifier with MOS class AA circuit.

We have adopted a Bamboo Mixing Electrolytic Capacitor that suppresses unnecessary incidental sounds and further enhances the sound field.

An R-core power transformer is used in the power supply section to reduce noise generation due to magnetic flux leakage.

The multi-layer vibration isolation structure using THCB reduces the adverse effect of vibration on sound quality.

High reliability has been obtained by adopting input / output switching directly connected to terminal by gold-plated contact relay.

Speaker terminal compatible with banana plug is adopted.

Simultaneous drive of A/B of speaker output (8 Ω + 8 Ω) is also possible.

It comes with a system remote controller that can operate a Technics CD player, tuner and cassette deck.
