Commentary

This is an upgraded model that further pursued the thorough low-noise detail.

Battery drive by exclusive power supply is adopted for all stages, and interference of AC power supply is eliminated.

New HDAM (gold-plated high-speed voltage amplifier module) with newly developed 4-layer board structure is adopted to further improve circuit speed and stability.

In order to improve S/N in actual use, a preamplifier and a gain controller are separately equipped.

Equipped with a phono equalizer for MM/MC.

The bb-5ver2 uses a lead-sealed battery with superior vibration control and stability.
A microcomputer monitors the power supply status of the battery to ensure a stable power supply at all times.

Equipped with a direct-view meter that can check battery voltage at a glance.

The total Route 3 of SC-5x1 and SM-5x2 DC-outputs is secured.
