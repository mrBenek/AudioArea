Commentary

DSP AV amplifier for Dolby Digital.

Equipped with the YSS 245 f as a key device for sound field processing.
The YSS40-F has built-in functions required for Yamaha's original cinema DSP processing, and realizes a Dolby Digital Prologic Decoder and a hard DSP sound field processing on a single chip with the YSS40-F 245.
As a result, 5.1 channels of Dolby digital signals can be fully decoded with 24-bit high precision, and combined with a 3-field cinema DSP LSI, it realizes realistic theater sound.

The Yamaha Sound Field Program has 13 programs.
Digital Enhanced and Digital Movie Theater, sound field programs for Dolby Digital, use sound field data exclusively for Dolby Digital.

The amplifier section is equipped with a 5-channel discrete amplifier of the same design.
Main ch is also compatible with 4 Ω drive.

Built-in FM/AM tuner.
The tuner is equipped with preset functions for 40 stations, auto-preset functions for FM stations, and edit functions for preset stations.

On-screen capability.
You can display the actual operating conditions on the monitor screen, such as program names, parameter settings, speaker level settings, etc.

Audio Route 3 and AV4 input terminals are provided. Digital input is coaxial with optical input for DVD/LD, and coaxial with optical input for TV/DBS.

There are 2 color variations of titanium color and gold.

By using the separately sold APD-1, it is possible to play 5.1 channels with a Dolby digital compatible LD player.

A learning remote a learning remote controller that allows you to select the sound field and adjust various parameters.
This remote control is pre-set with Yamaha's centralized operation control function and is equipped with a macro function that allows multiple operations to be performed by pressing a single key.
