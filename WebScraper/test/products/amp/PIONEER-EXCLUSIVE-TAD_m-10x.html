Commentary

Stereo power amplifier equipped with direct energy MOS-FET.

Uses Direct Energy MOS-FET with low loss and high linearity.

The chassis structure employs a newly developed sound stabilizer.
This system uses a thick steel plate stabilizer at the mounting part of the transformer and at the bottom of the chassis to increase the chassis rigidity.
