  • Technics SU-9600

  • ¥ 175,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

A stereo control amplifier that uses a constant voltage power supply for all circuits.

The two Phono inputs can switch impedance in three stages.
The input sensitivity of Phono2 can also be continuously variable in the range from 1 mv to 3 mv.

NF type tone control circuit of differential amplifier configuration is mounted.

Equipped with Acti-Blow high filter.
