  • Marantz SC-50

  • ¥ 42,800 (around 1980)

Commentary

A stereo control amplifier of the Marantz slim type component component 50 series.

Equipped with a Rec out selector, it is possible to record FM broadcasts to Tape1 while playing back records.

In addition to the Low-TIM design, the phono amplifier uses specially selected elements such as transistors to improve the sound quality.
In addition, it is compatible with MC cartridge by installing MC position, and in addition, it is compatible with various cartridges by installing 3-position cartridge selector in Phono1.

The tone control has an OUT position that only removes the tone element from the circuit, and a tone switch that can bypass the tone amplifier itself.

Equipped with a 2-position loudness circuit and subsonic circuit.
