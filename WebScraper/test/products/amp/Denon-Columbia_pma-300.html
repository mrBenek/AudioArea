Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that achieves both high performance and excellent cost performance.

The circuit structure adopts a two power supply type differential-amplification all-stage direct connection pure complimentary service OCL circuit.

The equalizer amplifier section is designed with special emphasis on the dynamic range and equalizer characteristics. The circuit configuration is a 3-chip E-E feedback type using specially selected silicon transistors. The power supply voltage is approximately 40 v.

The tone amplifier section has a 3-chip configuration with an FET in the middle. The collector current of the in the first stage is reduced to operate in a region with low noise level. In addition, it is designed with a power supply voltage of 40 v like the preamplifier to obtain a wide dynamic range and excellent linearity.
Also, in consideration of connecting with a multi-amplifier circuit, an emitter follower is used to extract the tone control characteristics so that external loads do not affect them.
In addition, the tone control knob has an 11-position click stop.
