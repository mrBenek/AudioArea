  • KENWOOD KR-V770

Commentary

Multi AV center equipped with FM/AM tuner and Dolby Pro Logic Surround.

Equipped with Dolby Pro Logic Surround, you can enjoy surround sound playback.
The amplifier section is composed of 5-ch amplifiers and a large transformer is used for the power supply section.

By adding steering logic (directional enhancement circuit) to the DSP mode of two movie theaters, you can enjoy the sound field and sound effects of a movie theater.
By adding delay and echo to Dolby Pro Logic, you can enjoy a variety of software in the atmosphere of a movie theater.

It is equipped with DSP which can create the same condition as the atmosphere played in the listening room according to the music software. You can choose from 3 modes of arena / jazz club / stadium.

Equipped with an FM/AM tuner that supports AM stereo.
You can preset 40 channels at random, and you can set it freely from the station you want to listen to and call it up with one touch.

A preset universal remote control is included.
