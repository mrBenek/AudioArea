  • Marantz Model-66

  • ¥ 600,000 (released in 1998)

Commentary

This is a bulb-type pre-main amplifier that pursues not only the classical image of the bulb-type but also the ability to play back modern audio.

Switching between UL connection and Triode connection is possible.
Four 6CA7/EL-34 output tubes are used.

Transformers and CRs are manufactured in the USA.

The phono equalizer amplifier section uses a 2-stage NF type.

Uses CR type tone control.
