Commentary

Pre-main amplifier equipped with newly developed Hyper α -X balance circuit.

In order to eliminate distortion caused by element asymmetry, we have developed and adopted a Hyper α -X balanced circuit based on our original X balanced amplifier technology.
In addition to the conventional balance circuit, this circuit has newly introduced a high CMRR type constant current circuit at the first stage of the power amplifier and pre-amplifier, which determine the dynamic characteristics of the amplifier. This circuit achieves a theoretical CMRR improvement of 140 dB. This reduces current variations due to DC offset caused by element asymmetry and reduces dynamic noise generated in the circuit.
In addition, all stages are balanced so that music signals can be transmitted and amplified simultaneously, and the amplification circuit and ground circuit are completely separated to eliminate adverse effects caused by back electromotive currents and external noise from speakers.
In addition, dedicated amplifiers are placed on the + side and - side of the output respectively, and a push-pull system is adopted in which a total of four amplifiers drive the speakers under the same conditions.

The power stage is equipped with an audio-only device, NM-LAPT, which has excellent high-frequency characteristics and current limit value.

DC amplifier configuration is used for all stages from input to output.
In addition, the performance of the DC amplifier is improved by adopting the NEW diamond differential circuit which can take out the output of the - side without phase inversion, and by improving the response to the instantaneous signal and suppressing the generation of TIM distortion.
In addition, in order to make the circuit wide range and high speed, the first stage FET is cascade-connected to eliminate the sound quality degradation that occurs when connecting to external devices with different impedances.

In order to prevent unbalanced power supply, a closed loop configuration is adopted in which the power supply is separated from the ground. The power stage and voltage amplification stage are completely separated. This provides a stable large current and protects the signal from the natural vibration of the power supply and noise generated during charge and discharge.

Reinforced twin monaural construction is used to prevent vibration from the power and signal systems.
First, large transformers and electrolytic capacitors are installed in the center, and the power unit, which consists of power transistors, radiators and other signal systems, is placed symmetrically to ensure an optimal weight balance.
In addition, the transformer, volume, and NM-LAPT are isolated by pure copper plates, and a newly developed insulator with a special structure disperses and absorbs external vibrations.

It is equipped with a power amp direct input that can directly input the digital source music signal to the power amp section, and a balance input terminal.

A 6-row detent volume is used.

Equipped with source power direct, REC selector, AC outlet, etc.

Also, there was a real side wood sold separately.
