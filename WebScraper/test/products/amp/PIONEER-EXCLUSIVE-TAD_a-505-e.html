Commentary

This is a stereo pre-main amplifier that combines convenience and digital sound quality.

The power supply section uses a large 230 VA power transformer and a 12,000 μ Fx2 electrolytic capacitor.

To bring out the quality of digital sound, it is equipped with a CD direct switch that connects the CD output directly to the power amplifier.
In addition, by adopting the REC selector with OFF position, deterioration factors can be forcibly eliminated.

It employs a non-switching circuit TypeII to further expand the 0 switching distortion range and further increase the bandwidth. At the same time, thermal distortion caused by the drift of idle current and non-linear distortion of the output stage are greatly improved, and sound quality degradation is prevented and distortion free sound is pursued.

It is designed to be completely non-magnetic and vibration-proof, and uses an extra-thick power cord with polarity display that improves conductivity and reduces nonlinear distortion.
In addition, nickel phosphorus is used for the electrodes and the end of the power plug is made non-magnetic. In addition, parts such as oxygen-free copper wires, copper-plated screws that are effective in absorbing vibrations, and non-magnetic cap audio fuses are used for the speaker terminals of the vise type, large polycarbonate insulators with high rigidity and high vibration damping rate, gold-plated input terminals, and all cords for the signal and power systems.
