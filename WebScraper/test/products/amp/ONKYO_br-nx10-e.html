  • ONKYO BR-NX10

  • ¥ 94,500 (tax included, sold on July 5, 2007)

Commentary

CD/HDD tuner amplifier with HDD and interface for connecting various media.

Equipped with a CD player that can play not only music CDs but also linear PCM and MP3 type CD-R/RW (finalized) discs. Up to 8 x speed dubbing from music CDs or linear PCM type CD-R/RW to HDD is possible (when audio monitor is OFF and ATRAC 132/105/66kbps mode).

The built-in FM/AM tuner can record to the HDD, and Any Music can be used to display FM broadcast program / music information.

Equipped with a USB port on the front panel, you can transfer USB storage, MP3 player and MP3 files to each other.
Transfer to ATRAC players is also supported for transfer of ATRAC (ATRAC3, ATRAC3plus) files in addition to MP3.

With a built-in LAN port, it is possible to record MP3 files from a PC shared folder to the BR-NX10 via a network (LAN).

It adopts 80 gb HDD and can record in ATRAC (ATRAC3, ATRAC3plus), MP3 and linear PCM.

Equipped with two voice input / output terminals.
In addition, since it is compatible with the system interlock "RI" unique to Onkyo, it can be operated from the attached remote controller by connecting to MD deck made by Onkyo.

When recording to the HDD, title information such as album name, artist name, and song name can be automatically registered from the database of approximately 350000 albums recorded on the BR-NX10. Also, by connecting to the Internet, title information of the latest songs can always be registered.
It is also possible to register title information by automatically recognizing audio waveform data even when dubbing from an analog sound source such as a cassette deck that does not originally contain title information.

It supports Any Music. You can download singles and albums, book and buy CDs at online CD shops, and display the names of songs and artists in FM on-air.

Equipped with 4.3 "color display.

You can enjoy repeat / random playback or playback mode to enjoy songs on the HDD (Artist / Album / Title (50 syllables / alphabet display) / Playlist playback of your favorite songs / 50 frequently listened songs / 50 recently listened songs / 50 recently added songs / 50 dug songs etc.).

Equipped with FM20/AM10 preset tuner (auto-preset compatible).

Equipped with tone control, superbus circuit and 14-mode program timer.

The amplifier circuit has a discrete configuration, and the parts are carefully selected one by one for precise tuning. In addition, it is equipped with a unique technology WRAT (Wide Range Amplifier Technology) for wideband / high dynamic range reproduction.

The VLSC (Vector Linear Shaping Circuitry) is a unique sound circuit that prevents the mixing of pulse noise generated in the main body to improve sound quality.

Equipped with an optimum gain volume, the SN ratio in the sense of hearing is greatly improved.

It is equipped with a normalization function that automatically adjusts the volume level of the music when the music in the HDD is played back. It can be played back with irregular peak values for each music.

Wireless remote control with BGM button is included.
