  • MARK LEVINSON ML-10L

  • ¥ 850,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

A control amplifier designed with added flexibility while incorporating the know-how achieved with the ML-7L.

In order to support MC cartridge, switching system by internal switch is adopted.
The input impedance of the phono amplifier can be adjusted to the optimum value of the cartridge used by replacing the optional load component.

The tape monitor function is enhanced and the Rec out defeat switch is mounted on the front panel.

Comes with a wooden case.
