  • Technics SU-C03

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

Compact size integrated DC amplifier.

The main amplifier section employs six dedicated ICs for each of the voltage amplifier stage and power amplifier stage, and has a DC amplifier configuration that eliminates capacitors with time constants from the signal path.

The power supply circuit uses a small cut-core transformer with less leakage flux to ensure a low-noise design.
In addition, two 8,200 μ F electrolytic capacitors are used.

Approximately 1/3 of the total internal volume is secured as heat radiation space, and a high-efficiency large heat sink is adopted.

Uses precision die-cast panels.

Equipped with a tone control that is dimmed at the midpoint.
