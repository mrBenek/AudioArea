  • SONY TA-FE400R

  • ¥ 30,000 (around 1998)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that is slim but equipped with a variety of input / output terminals.

It is equipped with one Phono MM input system and line Line 5 input terminals.

It is equipped with a source direct switch and can bypass the tone circuit when it is unnecessary.

It is equipped with a loudness switch.

Wireless remote control is included.
In addition to input switching and volume control, basic operation of other Sony components is also possible.
