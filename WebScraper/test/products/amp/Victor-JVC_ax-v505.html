Commentary

This is an AV amplifier for Dolby Pro Logic that has inherited the design of high-end machines.

The AX-V505 supports Dolby Pro Logic Surround.
It is equipped with 4 types of pro-logic mode, and in addition, it is equipped with 3 types of surround sound which can be enjoyed with sources other than Dolby surround sound and 3 types of delay time.
Dolby Pro Logic mode allows you to choose between normal, wide, phantom and 3-channel logic. Normal mode is selected when a small-size speaker is used for the center channel. Frequencies below 100 Hz are distributed to the front L/R and output. Wide mode is used when the same speaker is used for the center channel. Phantom mode does not have a speaker for the center channel. The center channel signal is distributed to the front L/R and output. 3-channel logic mode mixes the rear surround signal to the front L/R.
You can choose between Surround mode : Hall, Live, or Synthetic. Synthetic creates stereo-like spaciousness on a mono source.
The delay time can be selected from 15 msec, 20 msec and 30 msec.

The power amplifier section contains a 5-channel discrete power amplifier.
All channels are designed with emphasis on sound quality using carefully selected power transistors. In addition, construction to protect the power amplifier section from noise, adoption of vibration control base for power transformer, oxygen-free copper AC cord, and carefully selected power capacitor are used to ensure high sound quality.

Equipped with the CD direct function, the number of contacts can be reduced by shortening the signal path of the CD source, and you can enjoy a simple signal line.

A total of 16 input terminals are provided.
A video input terminal for the Route 6 and an S-video input terminal for the Route 3, one system for video movies is installed on the front panel, and an input terminal for the Route 6 is installed for audio equipment.

The front panel is equipped with independent REC selectors for Tape1 and VTR1.
The Tape1 can record audio and the VTR1 can record visual audio and video independently of each other. While listening to a CD, you can edit from tape to tape and record from behind or behind, such as dubbing a video. It also has a REC monitor that allows you to check the situation.
You can also use the Sound Selector to add audio sources to visual sources.

Y/C separation circuit is mounted on the S image terminal, and composite connected images can be dubbed with the S image terminal.

Equipped with on-screen function, operation status can be displayed in text on TV screen.

Equipped with a video enhancer, the contour of the image can be corrected continuously from soft to sharp.

A full remote control is included.
With this remote control, the remote control signals of VICTOR/JVC CD, deck, TV and VTR are already input, and it can be operated together.
