  • Lo-D HA-30

  • ¥ 43,000 (released in 1979)

Commentary

A4 size pre-main amplifier for space factor and multi-function.

The circuit configuration adopts the pure complimentary service OCL method.

Equipped with an LED function indicator that clearly shows the changed position.
There is also a built-in initialization circuit that always sets the position of the tuner when the power is turned on.

Equipped with microphone mixing circuit, speaker selector switch, low filter and loudness switch.
