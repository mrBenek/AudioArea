  • ONKYO A-915R

  • ¥ 59,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with 4 Ω low-impedance drive capability aiming at class-best speaker drive power.

The power amplifier section adopts twin mono layout configuration.

We have introduced high-quality parts common to the Integra series such as blue condenser.
In addition, a power transformer with low leakage flux is installed.

Direct Tone is used for tone control.

It uses a motor drive selector and can be switched by remote control.
In addition, T-403, C-303, K-503R, K-W505, etc. can be operated with the attached remote control.

Equipped with tone / direct switching function.

Compatible with MM/MC cartridges.
