Commentary

A monaural power amplifier boasting a large output of 1 kw.

The basic circuit is based on the conventional model, and the drive power is improved by adopting the newly developed ultra high current transfer circuit for the power supply.

The signal system has a fully balanced design consisting of differential amplifiers from the input stage to the output stage.

Equipped with balanced input terminal.
