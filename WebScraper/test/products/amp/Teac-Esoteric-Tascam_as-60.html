Commentary

This receiver uses a low-noise silicon transistor and is designed with an emphasis on sound quality.

There are two dedicated tape deck terminals that can be switched at any time during recording and playback. In addition, there are separate terminals dedicated to playback so that you can print from tape to tape using two tape decks.

The amplifier section is designed so that the preamplifier section and the power amplifier section can be used independently. Other control amplifiers can also be inserted between the preamplifier and the main amplifier.
We use carefully selected transistors, resistors, and capacitors.

Two pairs of speakers can be connected.
