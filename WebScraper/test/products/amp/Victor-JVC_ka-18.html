  • VICTOR/JVC KA-18

  • ¥ 85,000 (around 1979)

Commentary

Cassette stereo amplifier with built-in mixer.

It has a built-in amplifier so you can enjoy karaoke just by connecting the speaker.

An SA (Senalloy) head is used for the recording and re-recording head of the cassette deck.

It is equipped with karaoke functions such as an automatic two track selection device, an echo device and tape speed control.

Equipped with a Line 5 input mixer.

Equipped with a record player connection terminal and a spare input terminal.

Equipped with 20 multipeak level indicators on the left and right.

Japanese character display has been adopted to improve operability.
