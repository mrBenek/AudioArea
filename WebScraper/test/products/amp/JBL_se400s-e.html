  • JBL SE400S

  • ¥ 178,000 (around 1970)

Commentary

Silicon transistor power amplifier with 40Wx2 output.
The internal specifications are almost the same as those of the SE408S.
In the SE408S, the front panel is replaced by the rear panel, and the entire panel is covered. The equalizer board can be seen from the acrylic center of the front panel.
The rear panel is also designed as a radiator for the transistor.

A method called Energizer is adopted.
In this method, there is a printed circuit board inside. This board is provided with several types of circuits depending on the JBL speaker system. It is also used as an equalizer to control the frequency characteristics (including the damping factor depending on the model number) of the power amplifier and speakers to the ideal characteristics.

Some JBL speakers have a notch on the back of the enclosure to accommodate the SE408.
