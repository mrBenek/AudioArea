Commentary

Control center equipped with panorama mechanism.

It is equipped with a panorama mechanism, and in combination with a balance control, not only can the stereo / mono / reverse be changed continuously, but also can freely locate unnatural sound images depending on the source and sound field environment.

Eight input systems are installed, and the phono input can vary the gain by 30 to 50 dB.
