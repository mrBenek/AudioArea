Audio Database

  • SONY TA-3120

  • ¥ 52,000 (released in 1965)

Commentary

This power amplifier was developed at the same time as Japan's first all-silicon transistor stereo premain amplifier TA-1120.
The internal structure is the same as that of the main amplifier section of the TA-1120.

Equipped with SCR protection circuit.
