  • TRIO KR-7070

  • ¥ 150,000 (around 1970)

Commentary

FM/AM receiver equipped with auto-tuning mechanism.

FET is used for the front end of the FM tuner section and crystal filter and IC are used for the IF amplifier section to improve the image ratio, intermodulation distortion factor and S/N ratio.

Equipped with electronic FM mono / stereo switching mechanism.
If you set the selector to FM-Auto, stereo broadcast transmission reception will automatically switch to stereo reception and the indicator lamp on the dial will light.

Equipped with SCA filter.

The KR-7070 is designed so that the MC cartridge can be connected as it is without using a booster transformer.

Equipped with a purely electronic protection circuit to protect the power transistor.
