Commentary

Stereo preamplifier with built-in pre-preamp for MC cartridge.

The first stage of the MC pre-preamp consists of a low-noise transistor with complimentary service push-pull to achieve low noise, low distortion and low output impedance.

A newly developed low-noise transistor is used in the first stage of the phono equalizer section, and the SN ratio is improved by using a current mirror load differential amplifier that can select an operating current that minimizes the distortion level.
In addition, the equalizer element uses metal film resistance within ± 1% deviation and polypropylene capacitor within ± 2% to obtain excellent characteristics.

In this mode, the frequency response is flat and the circuit is composed of a low-noise IC.

The power transformer is housed in an iron case with excellent magnetic shielding effect, and a low-noise Zener diode is also used in the constant voltage circuit to minimize the noise generated in the power supply line and ensure a low noise design.

It comes with a one touch connection cord that directly connects the power amplifier, tuner and SU-C01 via the shortest path.

Precision finish die-cast cabinet is adopted.

Equipped with a subsonic filter.
