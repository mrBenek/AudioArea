  • SONY TA-4650

  • ¥ 84,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Stereo pre-main amplifier using V-FET.

All stages are directly connected pure complimentary service SEPPOCL circuit and DC-amplifier configuration using four Nch and Pch V-FETs.

An equalizer stage with two LEC transistor and V-FET for small signal is adopted.

The flat amplifier uses a DC amplifier that extends the band to DC in order to achieve faithful waveform transmission.

Equipped with a muting switch.
