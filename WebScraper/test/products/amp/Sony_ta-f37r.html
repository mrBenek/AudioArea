  • SONY TA-F37R

  • ¥ 25,000 (around 1997)

Commentary

Compact size pre-main amplifier with front input terminal.

Equipped with a source direct switch, the signal purity can be maintained and amplified by bypassing the circuit when tone control is not required.

The front panel is equipped with a stereo mini-jack input terminal for easy connection of portable devices.

Equipped with a -20dB muting switch that temporarily reduces the volume.

Wireless remote control is included.
With this remote control, you can turn the TA-F37R on / off, source select and volume control, as well as basic operation of Sony CD player, MD deck and tuner.
