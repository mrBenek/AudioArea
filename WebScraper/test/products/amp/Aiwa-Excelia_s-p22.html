Commentary

Stereo power amplifier of mini component my Pace5 series.

The circuit has a DC configuration and no capacitor is used in the negative feedback loop to solve the phase distortion problem.
Moreover, in order to suppress DC drift, a dual FET with good thermal coupling is used to realize stable operation even in a wide range of temperature changes.

The final stage adopts a pure complimentary service SEPP-OCL circuit of RET (ring emitter transistor) with good linearity.

The power supply uses a well-regulated toroidal transformer and a large capacitor.

It can also be used as a monaural power amplifier via BTL connection by switching the rear panel.

The protection circuit uses a newly developed IC and relay protection circuit.

The total power of both channels is the total power of both channels.
