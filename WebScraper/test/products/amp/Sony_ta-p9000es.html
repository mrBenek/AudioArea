  • SONY TA-P9000ES

  • ¥ 90,000 (released in 2000)

Commentary

An ES series multi-channel preamplifier developed to focus on the diversification of 5.1 channel sources.

It has two 5.1 channel inputs and one bypass 5.1 channel input that does not pass through the volume.
By connecting the 5.1 channel output, such as the TA-E9000ES, to the TA-N9000ES via the TA-P9000ES, you can add a 5.1 channel input system.

5.1 Channel inputs 1 and 2 provide independent level control for the center, rear left and right, and woofer.

It also has a bypass 2-channel input for pure audio.

Wireless remote control is included.
