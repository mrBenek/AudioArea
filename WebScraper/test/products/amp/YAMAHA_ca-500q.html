  • YAMAHA CA-500Q

  • Price unknown (around the early 1970 s)

Commentary

Stereo pre-main amplifier with 4-channel quad riser.

In addition to the circuit configuration as a pre-main amplifier, the CA 500 q has a built-in matrix type quadlizer. By connecting an additional stereo amplifier, you can play four channels.

It is equipped with a separation mechanism for the preamp and main amplifier sections and a level selection switch (Rear Gain + 12 dB) mechanism. The main amplifier section can be used for both front and rear applications.

The function switch uses a push button.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch, high / low filter and loudness switch.
