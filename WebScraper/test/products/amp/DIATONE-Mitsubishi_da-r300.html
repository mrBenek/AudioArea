  • DIATONE DA-R300

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1972)

Commentary

Solid-state integrated amplifier with all stages directly connected.

The power amplifier section employs an all-stage direct-coupled OCL circuit that uses a differential amplifier and removes the coupling capacitor from the output circuit, thereby greatly improving the frequency characteristics and damping factor.

A loudness control circuit is adopted so that it can be enjoyed even at low levels.

It has a microphone mixing circuit.

In the tuner section, a ceramic mechanical filter and IC are used in the FM intermediate frequency stage to improve selectivity characteristics.
A mechanical filter is also used in the AM circuit, and a ferrite bar antenna and filter realize high sensitivity reception.

A muting switch is installed to suppress unpleasant noise during channel selection.
