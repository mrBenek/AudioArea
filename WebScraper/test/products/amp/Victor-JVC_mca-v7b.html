Commentary

A 4-channel play main amplifier designed for 4-channel playback.

The power amplifier section employs a pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages. Negative feedback is applied coupling capacitor between amplification stages, negative feedback is applied in the band from almost direct current to several hundred kHz.
In addition, by operating with ± 2 power supply system, the speaker coupling capacitor in the last stage is removed, and by operating the transistor in each half cycle of the input signal up and down, the low-frequency distortion is reduced and the pulse-like switching distortion is eliminated.

When the switch on the rear panel is switched to 2 sp, the two power amplifiers become BTL connected and can be used as a high power stereo amplifier.

Equipped with 4 level meters.

It is equipped with S. F. C. S (Sound Field Composer System) to play back four channels of stereo records.
This circuit uses an electronic Matrix circuit to extract the reverberation from a 2-channel stereo record and plays it back on the rear speaker.

It contains a matrix decoder circuit that can play back matrix records.
For playback, set the Function Selector to SFCS (MATRIX).

The equalizer amplifier section employs a PNP-NPN hybrid two stage direct-coupled C-E feedback circuit using a low-noise silicon PNP transistor at the first stage. By applying a large amount of negative feedback from DC to high frequency, high stability, low noise and high dynamic range are obtained.

NF-type tone control is used, and precise adjustment is possible by use of 4-string volume with click position.

Dedicated input terminal for discrete 4 ch is mounted.
In addition, it is equipped with a 4-channel recording and playback terminal so that a 4-channel tape deck can be connected.

It is equipped with two pairs of headphone jacks so that you can use 4-ch headphones. ch-1/ch-3 are front jacks, and ch-2/ch-4 are rear jacks.

It is equipped with two rear speaker terminals and is compatible with 2-2SD system and 2-2FT system.

By connecting the 4-ch Balance Remote Controller (RM-1) (sold separately), you can adjust the volume balance of 4 ch from a remote location.
