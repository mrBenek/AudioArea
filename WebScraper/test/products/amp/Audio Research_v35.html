  • Audio Research V35

  • ¥ 560,000 (around 1992)

Commentary

A stereo power amplifier that inherits the technology of the high-end V70.

The output stage uses two KT90s per channel, and has a single push-pull configuration with 3-pole tube connection.
A total of two 6CG7 units are used for the driver stage.
