Commentary

Stereo pre-main amplifier with high power design.

The power amplifier consists of an all-stage direct-coupled circuit from the first stage to the last stage without the interstage coupling capacitor, a pure complimentary service circuit using the Darlington method of PNP-NPN for the output stage, and a ± 2 power supply system using the first stage differential-type.
As a result, various characteristics in the low frequency range have been thoroughly improved, and stable output has been obtained over the entire audible frequency range.

TRIO's unique ASO detection type limiter protection is adopted.

DLOA (dual low-noise operational amplifier) is used for the equalizer stage and tone control circuit.
The DLOA consists of a first stage differential amplifier, a buffer, a third stage Class A drive and a pure complimentary service. This realizes a high dynamic range, high input impedance, high power utilization factor, low impedance and low distortion factor.
Shock noise is also suppressed because the DC current at the output terminal is 0 V.

Equipped with a low filter, high filter and -20dB muting circuit.

Equipped with a mode selector that allows you to choose between stereo and mono.

Equipped with 4 ch input / output terminal.
