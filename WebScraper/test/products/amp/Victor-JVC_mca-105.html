Commentary

Pre-main amplifier equipped with 105 series SEA system.

This is the first pre-main amplifier equipped with a 7-element SEA system.
The SEA system makes it possible to adjust the frequency within a fine range and adjust the sound field, program source and component balance according to the listening room. It is also equipped with a center frequency switching function of 40 Hz and 60 Hz to increase the bass sound.
The the SEA linear volume, a center stopper type is adopted so that the SEA knob becomes flat at the center, and the SEA circuit is a 2-stage direct-coupled NF type to realize low distortion factor.

This is the first audio amplifier equipped with a pink noise generator.
You can use this function to check the phase of connected speakers, adjust the level of multi-channel playback, and adjust the level of multi-amplifier playback.

Darlington complimentary service SEPP-OTL circuit is used for the power amplifier section.
An electronic automatic recovery circuit is mounted as a protection circuit to protect the power transistor.

The equalizer is a 3-stage direct connection type using a selected low-noise silicon transistor 2SC458LGC.
In the 3-stage type, the gain of the circuit can be large, and since the NF can be taken deep even in the high frequency range, the distortion can be suppressed over the whole range, and the dynamic range can be wide.

The Aux1 connector has a level control at the input.
The pre-out1 and power amplifier input terminals also have a level adjustment volume. This volume can be used to improve the S/N ratio when the efficiency of the combined speaker system is high and residual noise is reduced.

It is equipped with functions such as audio muting, mode selector and SEA ON-OFF switch.

There was a wood case as an option sold separately.
