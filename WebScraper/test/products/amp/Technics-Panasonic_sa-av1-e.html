  • Technics SA-AV1

  • ¥ 139,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

An AV control center that integrates FM/AM/TV sound multiplex tuner, amplifier and AV selector functions.

It is equipped with connection terminals of audio Route 6 and image Route 3, and input to MSX PC is also possible.

Equipped with an auto-preset function to easily preset 12 FM/AM stations and 12 TV stations.

Equipped with a power amplifier.

Comes with a multi-functional remote controller.
With this remote control, you can turn the console on/off, switch the input source, adjust the volume, direct channel selection of FM/AM/TV, and perform basic operations on connected video and video disc.
