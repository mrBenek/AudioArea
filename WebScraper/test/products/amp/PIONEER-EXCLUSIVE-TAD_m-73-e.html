  • ¥ 75,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

Stereo power amplifier with 2 power transformers.

A ± 2 power supply, a 2-stage differential amplifier current mirror circuit, and a final stage parallel push-pull pure complimentary service circuit are used.

The power supply section employs a two power transformer to enhance dynamic characteristics, eliminating mutual interference between channels.
In addition, a 12,000 μ Fx4 condenser can be used to reproduce high-amplitude low-frequency continuous sound with sufficient margin.

Equipped with a large peak power meter with a logarithmic compression circuit that can directly read the output.

It uses a protective circuit composed of highly reliable power relays and quick response electronic circuits to protect speakers and transistors from accidents.

Equipped with two speaker output jacks, A + B playback is also possible.

A panel design that can be mounted on an EIA-standard rack.
