  • YAMAHA YPC-1

  • ¥ 200,000 (Released in November 2003)

Commentary

A passive stereo control center with no amplifier circuit.

The volume part uses a continuously variable attenuator with a brass case.
This volume guarantees a gang error of less than 1 dB when the volume is reduced to -100dB.

The rotary selector switch uses a high-precision product with a contact resistance of 5m Ω or less.

Low-loss design pin jack is adopted.

The case is finished with an aluminum piano finish.
The front / side panel has a maximum thickness of 12 mm and the inner chassis made of copper plated steel plate. The design emphasizes sound quality from the selection of parts to the manufacturing process.
