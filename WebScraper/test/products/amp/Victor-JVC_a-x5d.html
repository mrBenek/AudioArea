Commentary

A pre-main amplifier designed to further reduce distortion by using a pure NFB circuit.

It has a simple two amp configuration with only an equalizer and a power amp. There is a one amp configuration with full DC between Tuner, Aux, Tape and SP. out, eliminating the coupling capacitor.

Super A circuit is used for the power amplifier section.
This circuit achieves both A-class sound quality and B-class power efficiency and high power by automatically controlling the current flowing through the power device according to the input signal, and improves bare characteristics by minimizing phase correction by using a wide-band element.
In order to further reduce distortion, the A-X7D is equipped with a pure NFB circuit. In the pure NFB circuit, the capacitor for phase compensation of the signal circuit, which was absolutely necessary to stably apply NFB, is almost removed. Taking advantage of the excellent bare characteristics of Super A, the NFB circuit performs two stage phase compensation. As a result, even NFB can be stably applied to ultra-high frequencies, which was impossible with conventional methods, and the flat range of distortion factor and damping factor is greatly expanded.

JVC's original ICL circuit using EL-FET is used in the equalizer amplifier section. By constructing a high-gain A-class circuit, it is possible to use MC cartridges simply by switching the switch.

The power supply section uses two independent low-impedance direct supply type power supplies. The secondary side of the large-capacity transformer is divided into a power amplifier stage and a voltage amplifier stage, and is directly connected to each circuit to reduce the power supply impedance over a wide band.
The power amplifier stage is equipped with a new high-density capacitor dedicated for audio and a copper bus bar feeding line to further reduce power source impedance. In addition, consideration has been given to reducing magnetic strain by making important parts non-magnetic and arranging parts.

An illuminant pointer has been newly added to the A-X series unique non-clearance volume knob.
This pointer also serves as a protection indicator and blinks when the power supply is turned on or when the protection circuit is operating due to an output short circuit.

Self-illuminated indicators are used for the function switches.

It is equipped with tone control that minimizes unnecessary changes in the ultra-low and ultra-high ranges.

The Tape2 connector is installed not only in the rear panel but also in the sealing pocket of the front panel to improve operability.

Equipped with an equalizer subsonic filter.
