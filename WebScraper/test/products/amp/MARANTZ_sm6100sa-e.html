  • Marantz SM6100SA

  • ¥ 36,800 (released in 2000)

Commentary

Similar to the SM-17SA, this stereo power amplifier was developed based on the "simple & flexible power amplifier concept".

A current feedback type is used for the power amplifier.

In addition to the direct input, there is also a variable input, the maximum gain of which is set to 29 dB, which is the same as a general AV amplifier.
The direct input has the same gain as the power section of the PM6100SA, so it can be used as a bi-amplifier drive when using a combination.

It is equipped with through output terminal which can be driven simultaneously by stacking multiple units of SM6100SA.

The speaker terminal is made by WBC of Germany.

An inlet type AC cable is used.
