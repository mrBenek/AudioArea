Commentary

FM/AM multi-stereo tri-amplifier capable of receiving FM multi-stereo.

The FM multi-stereo circuit uses the CCM system.
It is also equipped with a monitor circuit to distinguish between stereo and monaural.

An SCA filter is installed to prevent interference with FM broadcasts and reception interference when SCA broadcasts used in the United States begin in Japan.

It is equipped with a tape monitor device and also with a tape head terminal for direct playback.

It is equipped with a loudness control circuit to adjust the sound balance at low volume.

It is equipped with a low filter to remove low-frequency noise such as motor rumbling sound, and a high filter to remove high-frequency noise such as scratch noise of records.

The AM section contains a ferrite bar antenna.

Equipped with FM AFC (Automatic Frequency Stabilizer), FM broadcasting once tuned does not shift the tuning point or distort the sound by the AFC function.
