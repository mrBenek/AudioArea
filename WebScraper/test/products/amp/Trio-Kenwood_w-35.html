  • TRIO W-35

  • ¥ 35,300 (around 1960?)

Commentary

A hi-fi tri-amplifier that allows you to enjoy stereo and monaural records, normal broadcasting, stereo broadcast transmission and FM broadcasting.

It uses a special circuit that can use 3 speakers so that it can be enjoyed even in a large room.

A high-end indicator meter is used for tuning the tuner section.

FM stereo broadcast transmission can be received by connecting the FM stereo adapter AD-1 of TRIO.

A tape recorder can be connected.

There are 2 variations of the exterior, silver and gold.
It was also possible to remove the case and attach it to a wooden cabinet or furniture.
