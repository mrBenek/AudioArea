  • TRIO W-10

  • ¥ 24,700 (around 1960?)

Commentary

Stereo tri-amplifier for FM broadcasting.

The tuner section is compatible with three bands, FM, AM and SW, and the TV is compatible with channels 1 to 3 of terrestrial analog broadcasting.

Equipped with tone control.

It is equipped with multiplex terminals to support the FM stereo broadcast transmission.

Compatible with both 100 V and 117 V.
