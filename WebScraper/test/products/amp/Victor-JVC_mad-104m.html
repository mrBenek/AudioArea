  • VICTOR/JVC MAD-104M

  • Price unknown (around the late 1960 s?)

Commentary

An amplifier with a built-in channel divider for multi-amplifier.

It is equipped with a channel filter with a crossover frequency of 6 points.
The cutoff characteristic is 12dB/oct. and an NF type filter is used.

The Darlington complimentary service SEPP-OTL circuit is used for the power amplifier.

Equipped with level control.

By adding JVC 4-channel tape decks (MTR-10M, MTR-100MS) to the program source, it can also support the 4-channel system.

It is most suitable for combination with MCA-104 type.
