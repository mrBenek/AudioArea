  • Aurex SB-420

  • ¥ 56,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with various functions.

The RIAA element of the equalizer amplifier section uses a high-precision metal film resistor and capacitor to keep the equalizer curve within ± 0.3 dB. The circuit structure is a 3-stage direct-coupled circuit including one differential stage. By applying a high voltage of ± 40 V, an input resistance of 350 mV is obtained.

The main amplifier section is equipped with a highly stable pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages including one differential amplifier.
The output stage is of parallel push-pull configuration.

A 10,000 μ Fx2 electrolytic capacitor is used for the power supply.

Equipped with microphone mixing circuit with independent volume.

Equipped with a tape duplicate switch, you can copy between two decks at the same time.

The tone control section allows 2-step switching of both high and low turnover frequency.
It also has a defeat position.

Equipped with low filter and high filter.

The volume uses a 22-point click type high precision attenuator.

Equipped with 3-stage switching audio muting of 0, -10 and -20dB.

Actually measured data sheet is included.
This data sheet contains five items : input sensitivity (Phono, Aux), continuous output, harmonic distortion factor, SN ratio, and Phono maximum allowable input.
