  • Technics SU-3000

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

An excellent pre-main amplifier of CP that challenged the limit of cost reduction while maintaining excellent characteristics.

The power amplifier stage is composed of all stages directly connected OCL circuits with a differential amplifier circuit at the first stage.
Also, a PNP pair transistor developed for a differential amplifier is used.

The equalizer circuit is composed of two stages connected together with specially selected low-noise PNP-NPN transistors.
The power supply voltage of the equalizer stage is set high by the + -2 power supply, and the allowable input is set large.

It is equipped with a microphone amplifier, and the microphone volume and source volume can be adjusted separately for level adjustment.

Two pairs of tape monitor circuits are installed.

Equipped with tone control.
