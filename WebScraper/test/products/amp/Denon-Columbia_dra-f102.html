  • DENON DRA-F102

Commentary

F102 series tuner amplifier.

It is designed for wide-range reproduction and high sound quality, and frequency characteristics up to 80 kHz are secured by careful tuning in each circuit.
In addition, in order to achieve clear stereo separation and noise reduction, the output stage is composed of discrete elements and HC (High Current) transistors are used.

The power supply section uses a toroidal transformer with high magnetic efficiency, low distortion and low loss, and a high-tone block capacitor.

Equipped with a Speaker Optimise circuit that ideally drives the SC-F102SG and SC 103 sg.
Bass reflex speakers allow for energy-rich low-frequency playback. However, sources cause the diaphragm to move unnecessarily in the ultra-low-frequency range, causing distortion. In consideration of this phenomenon, the SC-F102SG is equipped with a Speaker Optimise circuit that can be driven ideally according to the characteristics of the SC-F 400 SG to control the low-frequency range and achieve playback without sound loss.
This circuit also works well for common bass reflex speakers.

There is a mini-jack analog input on the front panel for connection with portable players such as digital audio players.

Equipped with phono input terminal for MM cartridge.

Equipped with Dock Contorol terminal for enjoying iPod and portable player.

The tuner section can be preset up to 40 FM/AM stations and can be called up by the system remote control.
It also has an auto-preset memory function.

Equipped with a timer function.
The Every Day Timer turns ON/OFF once every day, and the Sleep Timer allows you to set the OFF time in 10-minute increments between 10 and 60 minutes.

By connecting the optional Control Dock for iPod (ASD-1R) to the dedicated Dock control terminal and audio input terminal, you can enjoy connecting and playing back your iPod.
When connected, the remote control included with the DRA-F102 can be used to control and charge the battery. The DRA-F102 also displays the iPod text information (song titles, artist names) on the display.
This function is supported on 3rd generation or later iPods with a Dock connector. Some functions may not work depending on the iPod generation or software version. Also, the name of the holder or file name of the iPod is not supported in Japanese.

In combination with the F102 series, you can easily perform synchronized recording, timer playback, auto function, auto power on, and other operations.
You can also perform major operations with the system remote control included with DRA-F102.

It has a dimmer function, and the brightness of the display can be changed to 4 levels.

Equipped with a pre-out terminal for a subwoofer.

Wireless remote control is included.
