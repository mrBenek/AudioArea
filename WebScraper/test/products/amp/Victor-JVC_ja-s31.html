Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that takes advantage of the merits of its components while reducing costs by streamlining the production process.

A power pack is used for the output stage, in which four transistors and peripheral parts of the output circuit are enclosed in a single package to achieve higher output and lower cost.
All stages are directly connected Purecon OCL circuit.

The preamplifier section uses a constant voltage ± 2 power supply system and is designed with an emphasis on class A operation and transient response.

The power supply section uses a 2.6 kg power transformer and 10,000 μ Fx2 electrolytic capacitor.

A design with as few shield wires as possible is adopted, and by using almost no shield wire except for the wire going to the tape terminal of the front panel, deterioration of the high frequency characteristics is suppressed.

Equipped with a 5-position mode selector, you can check not only Stereo and Mono, but also L and R reverse and one channel.

The master volume has 19-point clicks.

Equipped with tone control and loudness.

Equipped with a relay-type 3 Oshige point protection circuit also serves as muting when the power is turned on/off.
