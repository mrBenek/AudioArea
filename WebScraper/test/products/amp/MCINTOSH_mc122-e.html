Commentary

A stereo power amplifier with high sound quality while maintaining high reliability through various guard systems.

The power guard function constantly compares the input and output signal waveforms. When a difference of 0.3% or more is detected, the gain is automatically lowered to prevent abnormal operation. A safe overdrive of 14 dB is guaranteed until the output distortion exceeds 2%.
In addition, reliability is enhanced by circuits such as the Sentry Monitor, which protects the output stage, and temperature protection, which prevents overheating and realizes a long life.

High speed is achieved by cascade connection of driver stages.

The input level control on the rear panel allows fine adjustment of each channel.
