  • ONKYO A-812XG

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1989)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed as a full-scale introduction to digital / AV.
Power supply circuit of the integra direct system or High fTDiscrete output stage using power transistor is adopted.
Equipped with a CD direct switch, it bypasses the input selector switches and tone control, and can amplify the pure music signal from the CD player by sending it to the power amplifier section. The CD terminal is gold-plated.

Equipped with Route 6 input terminals.
The Phono input is equipped with an MC/MM change-over switch so that an MC cartridge can also be used.

Equipped with Twin Turbo tone control, it is possible to enhance the ultra-low band around 50 hz, which is even lower than the normal Bass band of 100 hz.

Linear switching method is adopted to improve linearity.
Equipped with two speaker outputs, A, B, and A + B.

Uses a power cord with polarity display.

Equipped with delta turbo power supply to eliminate noise from AC line.
