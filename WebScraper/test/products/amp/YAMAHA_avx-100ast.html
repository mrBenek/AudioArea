  • YAMAHA AVX-100AST

  • ¥ 84,800 (around 1990)

Commentary

An AV amplifier with Dolby Pro Logic and active servo technology.

Equipped with Dolby Pro Logic and 3-mode digital surround sound.
It is also compatible with Yamaha Active Servo Speakers.

The power amplifier section is equipped with a 5-channel power amplifier.

A learning remote controller is included.

There were 2 types, titanium color and BlackCar color.
