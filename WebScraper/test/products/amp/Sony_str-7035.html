Commentary

An AM/FM stereo receiver developed by putting in the technology obtained in STR-7065, etc.

A newly developed junction FET has been adopted for the FM front-end to improve reception capability.
In addition, by using a solid-state filter in the IF section, the separation of adjacent channels is improved, and the ability to receive weak radio waves adjacent to powerful stations is also improved.

By adopting a frequency number line type varicon, it is possible to synchronize by dialing at equal intervals.

Solid state filter is used for AM part.

The power amplifier uses a hybrid power IC2 power source direct coupling system.

The tone control circuit uses NF type.

Built-in microphone amplifier for mixing.

In addition to a filter switch for removing scratched noise and ヒズノイズ, a loudness switch and a speaker switch for using two sets of speakers simultaneously or independently are incorporated.
