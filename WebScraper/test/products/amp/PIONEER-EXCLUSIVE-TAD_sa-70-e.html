  • Pioneer SA-70

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

Solid state pre-main amplifier using silicon transistor.

The head amplifier uses 3-stage direct-coupled E-E feedback using low-noise silicon transistors.
A low-noise FET is used in the first stage of the preamplifier to improve S/N.

The preamp and power amplifier can be used separately, and the center channel output terminal is mounted on the output stage.

It uses a 3-dB-step switch for tone control and features such as audio muting.

Equipped with two Phono inputs, one of which can be MM type and the other can be MM/MC switching.
