Commentary

Automatic AM-FM multi-stereo tri-amplifier developed for stereo.

The output is 6BM3P. P., with consideration given to power consumption, external heat generation, economy and durability.

Equipped with FM mono - stereo broadcast transmission automatic switching device, it is automatically switched to stereo broadcast transmission reception.
In addition, an FM stereo display lamp is installed so that it can be checked at the time of FM stereo broadcast transmission.

It is equipped with a newly developed MPX noise filter.
Conventional filters cut high frequencies, but a newly developed method cuts only noise without compromising sound quality.

DC ignition is used for the equalizer amplifier to eliminate hum noise.

You can record while listening to stereo.
Input impedance is also taken into consideration so that no matter what type of tape recorder is connected, the sound quality will not be affected.
Also, when receiving and recording FM stereo, measures are taken to prevent interference beat interference.

An SCA filter is built in to prevent interference from SCA broadcasts.

It is equipped with functions such as headphone jack, loudness control and FM AFC (FM Automatic Frequency Stabilizer).
