Commentary

AV amplifier equipped with Dolby digital and DTS decoder.

Multi-channel playback is possible when combined with a DVD player with digital outputs.

Equipped with an integrated 500W power amplifier that supports multi-channel surround sound.

Equipped with DAP (Digital Acoustic Processor) that reproduces natural hall sound field, it is possible to enjoy 5-mode sound field effect according to playback software and preference.
By using this function, you can of 2 ch stereo such as CD can also be reproduced at 5.1 ch.
