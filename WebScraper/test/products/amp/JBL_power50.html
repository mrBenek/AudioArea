  • JBL power50

  • ¥ 34,000 (around 1993)

Commentary

Stereo / monaural power amplifier of Sound Effect series.

The Sound Effect series was developed as a sound system for multimedia applications such as audio, TV, PC and game.

The power50 can be used as a monaural power amplifier via bridge connection.

Equipped with power output for cordless receiver.
