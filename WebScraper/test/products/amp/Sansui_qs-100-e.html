Commentary

A 4-channel rear amplifier that incorporates a main amplifier for the rear in a Sansui QS synthesizer decoder.

By connecting to an amplifier with a tape monitor switch, you can enjoy 4-channel playback.

The function switch allows you to select the sound field effect according to your preference, as well as the recording method of the source, the number of songs and the composition.

It is equipped with a phase modulation circuit, and from the rear, phase difference signals (indirect sound and reverberation components) that are transmitted in the hall changing from moment to moment are reproduced. By combining it with the sound from the front, a sound field like that of a concert hall can be reproduced.

In order to obtain a natural sound field, an IC-based pure electronic circuit system is adopted.

The rear amplifier uses the quasi complimentary service ITL-OTL system.

It is equipped with a protective circuit using a quick disconnect fuse.

The rear speaker can be arranged in two ways (2-2 system, 2-2 system in front) depending on the number of songs and composition.
Two methods can be compared by switching the switch.
