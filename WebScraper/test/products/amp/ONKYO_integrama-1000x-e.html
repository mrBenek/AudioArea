  • ONKYO Integra MA-1000X

  • ¥ 69,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that condenses the basic design of the Integra A-817RXII into a compact size of 330 mm wide.

An in-phase transformer is added to the signal system and power supply circuit. This in-phase transformer cuts off the loop with the connected equipment to prevent external interference to the amplifier.

The output is equivalent to that of the A-815RXII.

It is equipped with a direct tone with a Contrabass with a booth in the ultra low range.
