  • Marantz Model 1040

  • ¥ 51,900 (around 1974)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that uses the Malantz traditional inverting darlington circuit.

The tone control section has a mid-control that allows you to adjust the high, middle, and low ranges.

Equipped with output transistor protection function by PEP circuit.

A walnut cabinet and an adapter for rack mounting were sold separately as options.
