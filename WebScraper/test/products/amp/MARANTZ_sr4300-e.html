  • Marantz SR4300

  • ¥ 45,000 (Released in November 2002)

Commentary

An AV amplifier equipped with a decoder that supports rich surround sound.

Supports surround sound formats such as Dolby Digital EX, Dolby Pro Logic II, DTS-ES, and DTS-Neo : 6.
It is also equipped with Circle Surround II, which can convert up to 6.1 channels of monaural source, TruSurround, which can play surround sound with two speakers, multi-channel stereo (6 ch), and HT-EQ (home theater equalizer), which realizes natural speech reproduction.

All 6 channels are equipped with a discrete power amplifier. The power amplifier realizes a playback frequency of 100 khz and is compatible with super audio.

Equipped with AV selector, Euterpe oleracea slave, and digital input jacks.

Equipped with a digital output terminal.

Equipped with 6.1 ch pre-out and 6.1 ch direct input terminal.

All channels have screw-type speaker terminals.

System remote control is included.
