  • Bladelius Thor mkII

  • ¥ 590,000 (released in April 2006)

Commentary

Fully balanced integrated amplifier.

The power amplifier circuit consists of a single-ended J-FET input stage and two parallel transistor output stages. High power is achieved by a simple bridge amplifier circuit and 16 high-power, ultra-fast output devices.
In addition to the DC coupling design, the full balance across the entire signal path minimizes noise and distortion.

It has a preamp output, so you can connect an external power amplifier and use the Thor mkII as a preamp.

Input sensitivity can be adjusted for each input.
This ensures that the output level remains constant even when the input is switched.

Equipped with home theater input, multi-channel setting for home theater is possible with one button.

The power supply section is equipped with a custom-designed 1,800 VA toroidal transformer and a high-capacity 80,000 μ F filter capacitor, enabling stable power supply to any load.
The display section uses a dot matrix display with excellent visibility.

System remote controller "ミョルナー" is included.
