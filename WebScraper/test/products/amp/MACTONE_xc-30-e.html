Commentary

This is a high-grade control amplifier that has been developed based on Marantz's R & D model and after a series of comparative studies on audibility.

The K ・ K ・ NF method is adopted for the first time in Japan, and excellent high-frequency characteristics are realized.

The tone control circuit is equipped with a rising point moving type circuit by frequency switching using K ・ K ・ NF method.

Various output circuits such as the main output circuit and the output circuit for tape recorder recording are reduced to 200 Ω or less by the cathode follower.

DC ignition is used for all stages.

Multi-channel output is possible with the separately sold split frequency unit.
