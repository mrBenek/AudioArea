  • VICTOR/JVC MCA-103

  • ¥ 27,000 (around 1966)

Commentary

Small pre-main amplifier with all-transistor configuration.

It uses an OTL circuit with good frequency characteristics and low distortion, and four UL-type power transistors for high power.

By adopting a 3-stage configuration in which two silicon transistors with a cutoff frequency of 140 transistors with a cutoff frequency of MHz are directly connected.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch.

Equipped with DIN standard stereo recording and playback terminal.

Supports magnetic, crystal, ceramic, etc. cartridges.

It is equipped with tone control in which high and low frequencies are independent.

Equipped with a stereo headphone jack.
