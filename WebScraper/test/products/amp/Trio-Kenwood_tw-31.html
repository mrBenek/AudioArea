  • TRIO TW-31

  • ¥ 22,000 (around 1967)

Commentary

This small pre-main amplifier was developed with the aim of making it as small as possible while maintaining performance taking advantage of the features of solid state.

The output circuit uses the OTL method of ITL-SEPP with silicon transistors.
Therefore, the output transformer is removed.
It also has a purely electronic protection circuit to protect the power transistors.

It is equipped with functions such as stereo headphone switch, tape monitor, CR tone control and loudness.
