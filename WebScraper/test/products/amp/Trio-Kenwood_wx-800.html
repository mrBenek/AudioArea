Commentary

A top-of-the-line stereo tri-amplifier using New Vista for FM high-frequency section.
The output section is 7189P. P to achieve 30W + 30W output.

The equalizer amplifier is fully DC ignited to prevent hum induction.

Equipped with a tape head direct input terminal, recording and playback terminal, and a tape monitor device for easy recording and playback.

The FM tuner section uses a 3-row varicon with a 2-stage limiter. The high-efficiency ratio detection system also functions as a limiter.
New Vista is used in the high-frequency section to achieve high sensitivity.
Furthermore, noise is reduced by cascode amplification.

Equipped with FM AFC (Automatic Frequency Stabilizer), the strength of AFC can be continuously variable according to the strength of radio waves.

In order to be able to determine whether FM multi stereo broadcast transmission is being received, it is equipped with an automatic indication device, which indicates that reception is being performed.

The AM tuner section uses a high-frequency amplified 3-row varicon and a ferrite bar antenna for stable reception.

It is equipped with functions such as loudness control, high / low filter, headphone jack and operation indicator pilot lamp.

An SCA filter was installed in order to respond to the start of SCA broadcasting.
