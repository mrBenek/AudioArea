Audio Database

  • YAMAHA AVR-1000

  • ¥ 159,800 (around 1987)

Commentary

AV amplifier equipped with FM/AM tuner.

Not only audio input but also 2 video input systems are equipped.
In addition, it is equipped with speaker outputs in the Route 3 so that it can be used freely.

It is equipped with a 3-band tone control that allows fine adjustment of sound quality and a simulated switch.

It comes with a remote control so that switching operation can be performed at hand.
