  • SANSUI AU-6500

  • ¥ 59,900 (around 1973)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with substantial basic performance and practical functions.

The power amplifier section is a pure complimentary service circuit directly connected to all stages by symmetrically combining PNP-NPN power transistors, and realizes low distortion.
The power supply is equipped with a large transformer and a 6,800 μ Fx2 capacitor.

The equalizer circuit uses a combination of low-noise transistors and PNP-NPN-PNP to achieve low noise and wide dynamic range.

The protective circuit is a combination of a transistor, a diode and a relay, and is also equipped with a short-cut fuse to ensure safety.

Equipped with tone control, loudness, muting circuit and low / high filter.

Equipped with a 4-channel adapter terminal.
