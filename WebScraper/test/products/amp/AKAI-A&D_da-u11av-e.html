Commentary

The AV center is equipped with a total of seven input terminals, four audio terminals and a video Route 3, including the S terminal.

Sound quality improvement technology such as super dynamic delay line and multi-feedback servo has been put into the highly rigid cabinet.

The built-in surround processor offers four positions : Dolby Surround, High Presence, Theater and Simulated Stereo.

It comes with a wireless remote control with volume control and most other controls.
