Commentary

A stereo power amplifier in which only the power amplifier section of the System909 is independent.

A balanced input terminal is used.
Unbalanced connection is also possible with the supplied XLR adapter.

The power supply section has a spacious configuration of 160,000 μ F.

Equipped with a microprocessor protection circuit that monitors overheating from outside the signal path.

Equipped with a soft start function to reduce inrush current.

It can also be used as a high-power monaural amplifier for bridge connections.
