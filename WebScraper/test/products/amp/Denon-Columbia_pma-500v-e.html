  • ¥ 59,800 (around 1986)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with video input and AV support.

It uses a unique non-feedback 0 db power amplifier that pursues high-purity and high-speed amplification.
The power stage electrically separates the voltage amplifier stage from the power amplifier stage to eliminate the influence of the back electromotive force from the speaker and to eliminate dynamic distortion caused by signal return to improve the dynamic characteristics.

The use of a pure dynamic power supply circuit with a 2-2 block capacitor suppresses the occurrence of dynamic IM distortion (intermodulation distortion) and improves the acoustic quality.

In order to eliminate the intrusion of external noise components, the power supply and main signal system are separated through a radiator in a chassis structure.
In addition, an input selector with an electronic switch has been adopted to provide an ideal layout for signal lines and to form a simple signal path by reducing the amount of wiring and shortening the wiring wires.

Equipped with a processor loop mechanism.

Equipped with two video inputs by complimentary service and push-pull circuit.

Equipped with Phono dedicated subsonic filter.

Built-in high-speed protection circuit.

Equipped with real-time tone control.
