  • DIATONE DA-U650

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1974)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with 2 power supply system for all circuits.

The power amplifier section employs a pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages without a capacitor. Combined with a high-power transistor, various characteristics are improved.
It is also designed to reliably drive all of the speakers on the market with a power ratio of 2.8.

The preamp section also uses two power supply systems, plus and minus. The input side base potential and output side collector potential of each unit amplifier can be set close to 0 potential, reducing click noise.

The preamp section and power amplifier section can be used independently with a slide switch.

It is equipped with functions such as duplicate function and microphone mixing circuit.

Equipped with a muting circuit to suppress noise when power is turned on.

There was a wood case as an option sold separately.
