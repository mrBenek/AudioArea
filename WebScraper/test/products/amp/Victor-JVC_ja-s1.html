  • VICTOR/JVC JA-S1

  • ¥ 39,500 (around 1973)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that inherits the circuit technology of the S series and is developed with the aim of reproducing high-quality music.

All stages are directly connected to pure complimentary service OCL circuit for the power amplifier.
In addition, the equalizer section uses carefully selected elements to ensure flat characteristics, while the A-class operation emphasizes sound quality.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit.

Equipped with a subsonic filter, high-cut filter and loudness control.

It is possible to use the Pre and Main separately.
