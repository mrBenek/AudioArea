  • ¥ 65,000 (released in 1999)

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier of the C series that reexamines the thought and technology that Marantz has advanced for half a century and thoroughly implements the original policy of "the highest priority is the reproducibility of music".

Pure class A operation is adopted, and twin monaural construction is adopted.

Equipped with a 4-row active volume, it improves the SN feeling in the auditory sense.

It is equipped with Marantz original HDAM.

Equipped with a source direct switch and an active filter NFB-type tone control circuit.

High-quality sound condenser and other parts are used.

The front panel uses a gold finish and hairline finish aluminum panel.

Marantz Comes with a system remote control that enables simultaneous operation of products.
