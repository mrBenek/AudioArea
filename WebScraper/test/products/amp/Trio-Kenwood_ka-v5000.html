Commentary

A 5-channel AV amplifier with a built-in monaural power amplifier for a subwoofer.

It has a built-in mono amplifier for a sub-woofer that performs surround playback of deep bass sound, enabling deep bass playback.

The surround circuit is equipped with the surround function of Dolby, Theater and Stadium Route 3.
Dolby Surround is effective for Dolby-compatible software that reduces the influence of noise and increases the signal-to-noise ratio, while Theater Surround plays back stereo-recorded movies and live music with appropriate reverberation.
Stadium Surround emphasizes the spread of the sound field, and plays baseball and other events and sports in a wide space with sound with a sense of scale.

It uses 16-bit digital delay and 3 types of delay time can be switched.

Equipped with Gray CO, and equipped with 3 recommended Gray CO patterns of rock, fusion / jazz and vocal.
In addition, the Gray co-pattern set by yourself can be stored up to the Route 3.

Equipped with a remote controller, you can operate many functions such as power ON/OFF, surround mode switching / OFF, delay time switching, Gray ON/OFF, etc.
