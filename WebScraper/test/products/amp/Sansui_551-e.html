  • SANSUI 551

  • ¥ 65,000 (released in 1974)

Commentary

FM/AM receiver with improved basic performance in both the audio and tuner sections.

The power stage uses a SEPP quasi complimentary service circuit.

A high-gain IC is used for the equalizer.

Various characteristics have been improved by adopting a dual gate MOS FET in the FM front-end section.

Equipped with a loudness, high filter and FM muting switch.
