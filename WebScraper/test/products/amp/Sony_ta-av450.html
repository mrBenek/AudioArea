Commentary

An AV amplifier with Dolby Pro Logic and 4-amp configuration.

Equipped with a 4-channel power amplifier of front L/R and rear L/R.

Equipped with Dolby Pro-Logic Surround you can choose from four modes depending on the placement of the speaker : normal, wide, phantom, or 3 ch logic.
In addition, hall mode and simulation mode are also installed.

The delay time can be adjusted in 5 steps in the range of 15 ms to 35 ms in 5 ms increments.
(35 ms cannot be selected in Dolby Surround)

The selector section is installed in the Line 5 for audio equipment and in the Line 5 for video source. In addition, S-video terminals are installed in the Route 3 for input and two outputs.

Equipped with center speaker output (x2).

Equipped with a mix mode, you can select a video source and an audio source separately.

We have adopted newly developed chassis, large power transformer and large insulator with an emphasis on sound quality.

Equipped with a test tone function that makes it easy to adjust the level balance of each speaker.

Equipped with a sleep function that can turn off power in approximately one hour.

Equipped with a muting switch.

Wireless remote control is included.
