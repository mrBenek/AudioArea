  • McIntosh MC207

  • ¥ 650,000 (Released in September 1991)

Commentary

A 7-channel power amplifier with DPM (Dynamic Power Manager) circuit.

By switching the rear switch, either 4 Ω or 8 Ω speakers can deliver 200Wx7ch.

The front panel is equipped with a blue-collar power meter Route 3.
The meter body is housed in a glass housing, and the 3-row meter can be switched between front / rear, center / subwoofer, etc., and the lighting of the meter can be turned off.

Equipped with balanced input terminal.
