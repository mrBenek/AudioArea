  • ¥ 1,080,000 (a set of two cars, around 1993)

Commentary

A monaural power amplifier that eliminates electrical and mechanical interference between L and R by making it completely monaural.

The basic design is a compact condensation of the design method and sound quality for high-end aircraft performance.
The power supply uses a choke input method.

The elements used have been completely reconsidered, and the internal wiring design has also been changed.

The input is mainly balanced by the Fisher connector, and unbalanced connection is also possible by using the Fisher RCA converter.
In addition to the standard input, a reversed phase input is also provided, and bridge connection is also possible by using a special cable kit.
