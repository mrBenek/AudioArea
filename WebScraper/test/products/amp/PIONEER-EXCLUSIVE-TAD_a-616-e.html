Commentary

This is a stereo pre-main amplifier designed to minimize signal paths, eliminate vibration and resonance, and improve speaker drive performance at low load.

The input block is designed to minimize signal routing, and the volume and power amplifier are directly connected when the source / direct switch is turned ON, so you can enjoy more pure sound.
The power amplifier block is laid out along the signal flow to minimize the distance from the power supply.
In the speaker selector block, the relay board and the speaker terminal are connected directly to reduce unnecessary wiring.

A honeycomb shape with high rigidity and excellent vibration damping is adopted everywhere, and vibration is dispersed and damped in many directions, while vibration distortion noise is kept low by high rigidity.
In addition, the honeycomb heat sink of the chimney type has an excellent heat radiation effect.
In addition, we have adopted a honeycomb structure for the insulator to reduce vibration from outside.

The volume has a balanced structure that reduces the mechanical stress of the volume body and uses a floating structure to minimize the effect of vibration.

Non-switching circuit TypeII which further expands the region of 0 switching distortion is adopted.
This greatly improves thermal distortion and non-linearity of the output stage.

NF-CR type is used for the Phono equalizer.

An extremely thick oxygen-free copper power cord with polarity display is used to prevent power line noise from entering.

A vise type speaker terminal is used to supply current with low loss.

It uses a fuse for audio with an emphasis on sound quality and a rotary knob made of solid aluminum machined.
