  • TRIO TW-61

  • ¥ 31,600 (around 1967)

Commentary

Solid state pre-main amplifier with rich functions.

The complimentary service circuit eliminates the transformer, which eliminates problems associated with transformer coupling such as low-range clipping distortion and high-range distortion.
In addition, the power transistor is protected by a protection circuit with repeated operation to check the safety of the circuit during operation.

Even when the selector switch is switched, a harsh click sound is not generated.

It is equipped with circuits such as low / high filter, mono output terminal, headphone terminal and loudness.
