  • CLASSE CA-301

  • ¥ 1,080,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier that inherits the technology developed in the OMEGA series.

The amplifier circuit uses a balanced amplifier configuration.

The power supply section uses a large sealed toroidal transformer and a high-voltage / high-current power supply that uses a high-quality, small-capacity capacitor to provide the required power for all stages of the amplifier at high speed.

It can also be used as a monaural power amplifier by bridge connection.
