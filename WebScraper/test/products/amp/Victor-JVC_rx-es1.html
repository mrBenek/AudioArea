Commentary

A digital AV amplifier equipped with DEUS, a unique technology of JVC.

The amplifier circuit is equipped with DEUS which is a digital amplifier of VICTOR/JVC.
This system uses hybrid feedback technology to monitor digital modulation accurately and to eliminate noise generated in the digital amplification process as well as the influence of connected speakers to improve sound quality.
The digital amplifier DEUS uses a high-precision floating triamble PWM (digital) modulation circuit. This circuit realizes digital conversion without noise in the audio band by introducing digital feedback. The generated digital signal is amplified by a high-speed switching element and passed through an LPF to remove the high-frequency signal and return it to the music signal. This adds switching noise and magnetostriction of the ferrite core used for the LPF. It is also affected by the back electromotive force of the connected speaker system.
In order to solve these problems, the DEUS employs analog feedback. Compared with the input signal, the DEUS and influence from the speaker are greatly reduced, and the sound quality is improved compared with the conventional digital amplifier.

Built-in Smart Surround Setup makes it easy to set up your annoying speakers.
With this function, the speaker is regarded as a microphone, and the sound of hand tapping is detected as electromotive force, and the level and distance are calculated and the setting is completed automatically.

In addition to the MPEG-2 AAC decoder compatible with Dolby Digital, DTS and BS digital broadcasting audio formats, various decoders such as Dolby Pro Logic II, which can be reproduced by expanding 2 ch source to 5.1 ch, are installed.
It also has an audio input terminal that supports analog 5.1 channels such as DVD audio.

It is equipped with D4 input / output jacks for high-definition video signals such as HDTV and progressive.

Comes with a luminous multi-brand remote control.
