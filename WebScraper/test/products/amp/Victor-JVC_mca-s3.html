  • VICTOR/JVC MCA-S3

  • ¥ 50,000 (around the early 1970 s)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier that adopts pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages.

All stages are directly connected to pure complimentary service OCL circuit, and coupling capacitors and output capacitors are completely eliminated. As a result, sufficient NF is applied from ultra-low band to ultra-high band, reducing distortion and improving low-band characteristics.

Equipped with 5-element SEA as tone control function.

Equipped with a subsonic filter to cut out unwanted low-frequency components.

With SFCS circuit, you can enjoy matrix 4 ch with stereo source.

The Phono input is equipped with two systems, high and low, according to the output of the cartridge.

Center channel output terminal is mounted.

Equipped with high-cut filter, low-cut filter and muting circuit.
