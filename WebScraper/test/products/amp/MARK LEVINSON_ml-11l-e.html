  • MARK LEVINSON ML-11L

  • ¥ 720,000 (released in 1982)

Commentary

Stereo power amplifier with the same basic configuration and components as ML-3 and ML-9L.

Class AB operation is adopted for the circuit.

Only the toroidal transformer of the power supply section is common to the left and right sides, and the bridge rectifier and capacitor after that are configured separately to the left and right sides.

The peak voltage (output) can be controlled and especially for speaker drives that require power limits such as electrostatic speakers.

High power amplifier by bridge connection is possible.
