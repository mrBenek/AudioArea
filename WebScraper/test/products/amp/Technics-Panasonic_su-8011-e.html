  • Technics SU-8011

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with high response speed LED power indicator and microphone mixing circuit.

It is equipped with an LED power indicator with a response speed of 200 μ sec that displays music signals with 5 LEDs arranged symmetrically.

Uses a low distortion and low noise design that inherits the concept of 99A (SU-8099) Concentrated Power Block.
