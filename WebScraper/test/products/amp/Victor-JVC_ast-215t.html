Commentary

FM/AM receiver equipped with SEA system.

It employs the SEA system, a sound quality control device unique to JVC, which makes it possible to adjust sound quality according to the characteristics of the room.
In the SEA system, even within the audio frequency range, it is divided like tone control, and each frequency can be freely increased or decreased.

The power section is based on the AST 140 t model, and uses a quasi complimentary service ITL and OTL circuit.

The equalizer amplifier section employs a direct-coupled amplifier circuit, eliminating capacitors and resistors that couple ordinary amplifier stages, enabling more faithful playback even at lower frequencies.
In addition, distortion is reduced by adopting NF type.

Equipped with pre-main independent circuit, it can also be used for multi-amplifier systems.

It has a built-in filter that cuts 30 hz or less and 30 khz or more.
In addition, circuits such as a tape monitor, loudness control, and mode switch are used.

The FM tuner section is equipped with a one touch AFC switch that can receive highly sensitive and stable signals.
In addition, the use of a cross-tuning dial using a tuning needle exclusively for FM/AM has been improved to improve usability.
