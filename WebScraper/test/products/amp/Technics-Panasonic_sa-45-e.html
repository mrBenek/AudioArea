Commentary

MFB stereo integrated amplifier with FM multi-circuit.

MFB system is adopted.

6BQ5 is used for the output tube.

The FM tuner is equipped with an FM multi-circuit and is compatible with stereo broadcast transmission.

Equipped with an MFB switch, it is also compatible with ordinary speakers.

It is equipped with a loudness control, AFC, FM feeder antenna, stereo eye and mode switch.
