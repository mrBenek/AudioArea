  • CORAL A-707

  • ¥ 39,200 (around 1965)

Commentary

A solid-state pre-main amplifier using a mesa silicon power transistor.

The circuit configuration uses the SEPP full OTL circuit system.

The power transistor is protected by an all-electronic protective device (E. P. C.).

A tape head direct input terminal, a headphone jack, and various filter circuits are also mounted.

The tone control is installed separately on the left and right.
