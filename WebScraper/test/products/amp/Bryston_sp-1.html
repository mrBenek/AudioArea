Commentary

An AV surround processor preamplifier that combines an analog pure amplifier with a digital surround processor.

Dolby Digital, Dolby Pro Logic Dolby Pro Logic and DTS.
It is also THX compliant.

The digital circuit is equipped with the latest 24-bit D/A converter and multi-DSP chips.

The analog circuit is separated from the digital circuit.
The circuit configuration is discrete.

The analog surround output has two systems, XLR3 balanced and RCA unbalanced.

Equipped with 12 v power ON/OFF, programmable remote trigger input terminal.

The volume part uses a motor drive system.

There are 2 color variations of black and silver.

Infrared remote controller is included.
