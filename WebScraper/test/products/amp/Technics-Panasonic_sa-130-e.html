  • Technics SA-130

  • ¥ 66,600 (around 1979)

Commentary

Multi-function receiver with built-in microphone mixing circuit with an upright see-through deck and microphone echo.

The deck is equipped with TPS (Automatic Tape Selection Mechanism), which is convenient when you want to listen to the song you just listened to again, or when you skip a song and listen to the next song.
