Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that has received a high reputation around the world.

Careful attention is paid to circuit design to reduce the number of components in the signal path.

The power transistor, which corresponds to the military standard, was specially manufactured for Silas Electronics and is a high-speed linear device.
The driver stage transistors are manufactured at a factory that supplies semiconductors for the United Kingdom military.

It uses passive elements made in West Germany including polypropylene capacitors, polystyrene capacitors, metal film resistors, etc.

The case uses a non-magnetic, non-conductive metal substitute resin manufactured by the Space Development Department of General Electric in the United States and is manufactured by precision injection molding.

In order to simplify the amplifier, circuits such as tone controls, filters, headphone jacks, speaker ON/OFF switches, protective circuits and balance control have been eliminated.
