Commentary

A 1U size stereo DC control amplifier with A-class operation in all stages, ICL configuration in all stages, and a presettable electronic selector.

The equalizer circuit is based on the MC high-gain basic design by using a low-noise FET in parallel and combining a cascade connection input for differential amplification with a high-performance operational amplifier.

The tone circuit is also fully DC-integrated flat / tone amplifier, which increases the fidelity of waveform transmission.

Switching distortion and crossover distortion are eliminated by the class A operation of all stages.
In addition, all basic circuits are ICL, forming a straight DC amplifier in which the phono input passes through only one coupling capacitor and the high-gain input does not pass through any capacitor.

An electronic selector is used for the input selector.
The phono and high-level selectors are 2-wei-switchable electronic selectors (MM ⇔ MC, tuner ⇔ aux) that can be used to preset the buttons by pressing down firmly.

It is equipped with sliding volume and touch fader which can fade in and fade out the volume.

Two tape decks can be connected.
