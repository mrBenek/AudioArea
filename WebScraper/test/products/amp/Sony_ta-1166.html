Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that uses a direct coupling type power amplifier section.

The power amplifier section uses a direct coupling method. All stages are directly coupled without using a coupling or capacitor.
Compared with conventional output circuits using capacitors, this allows power to be taken even at a lower frequency and prevents a decrease in damping factor.
In principle, this direct-coupling method amplifies up to direct current, so it protects the speaker by cutting out the very low frequencies below the music component.

The preamp section is equipped with input circuits such as two phono inputs and an AUX input Route 3, left and right independent tone control, low / high filter, and sliding balance control.
In addition, a large dynamic range is obtained by using a high voltage of 50 v for the pre-part power supply and increasing the allowable level of each input.

Equipped with a quick access switch, the phono 1 and tuner can be switched quickly with this lever.
This eliminates the need to switch the rotary switch in many steps and suppresses inadvertent noise.

The switches on the back allow you to use the preamp and power amp independently.

Equipped with a speaker selector switch, two sets of speakers can be switched for use.

The tone control is 2 dB step NF type.

Equipped with a tape monitor / source selection switch.

It is equipped with a loudness switch.
