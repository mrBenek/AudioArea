  • KRELL KSL

  • ¥ 420,000 (around 1991)

Commentary

A high-level preamplifier designed for pairing with the KST-100.

It is equipped with a total of 4 input systems and a tape I/O system.
In addition, two parallel outputs can be used to drive two independent power amplifiers.

Two input channels and one output channel are balanced input / output via XLR connector, so balanced transmission is possible.

It has a simple structure with only balance control and high-precision level control.

There was a special plug-in phono amplifier sold separately. With this phono amplifier, you can build in a phono equalizer amplifier.
The switching and impedance adjustment of MM cartridge and MC cartridge are possible by the adjustable switch mounted on the board of the fo section.
