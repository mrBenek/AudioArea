  • ¥ 160,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in March 1993)

Commentary

High-speed monaural power amplifier.

It follows instantaneous load fluctuations and is compatible with low-impedance and condenser speakers that are difficult to drive.

Uses the same low distortion design as the control amplifier S2000MP.
