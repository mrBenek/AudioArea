Commentary

The TW-80A -80A, this is a top-of-the-line stereo pre-main amplifier designed for more ideal original sound reproduction.

Mesa silicon transistors are used from the equalizer amplifier to the preamplifier.
We have improved various characteristics such as frequency characteristics, S / N ratio and damping factor by adopting a mesa silicon transistor that is resistant to heat and can be used semipermanently.

NF-type tone control is used for the tone control.
In addition, this tone control board is made into a block and can be removed with one touch.

The equalizer amplifier section uses a direct-coupled amplifier without capacitors or resistors to prevent the sound quality degradation that occurs when capacitors or resistors are inserted between stages.

It is equipped with a protection circuit that protects the power transistor and also repeatedly checks the safety of the circuit during its operation.
There is no need to worry about failure as it is a pure electronic type without using relays, etc.

There was a connection adapter with a DIN standard connector sold separately.
