  • Technics SU-9011A

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Stereo preamplifier that is paired with power amplifier SE 9021 a.

The low-noise transistor M47LP is used for the first stage differential amplifier to reduce distortion.

Equipped with MC pre-preamp to which MC cartridge can be directly connected.

By using an equalizer element with a capacity deviation of 2% and a resistance deviation of 1% or less, accurate frequency characteristics are achieved.

Built-in microphone mixing circuit.
The mixing recording output terminal is mounted on the rear panel.
