Commentary

This is a stereo pre-main amplifier equipped with left and right independent separate power supplies to improve dynamic crosstalk.

When a large transient signal with low-frequency energy is input to one channel of an amplifier, such as a musical tone signal that is a continuous collection of powerful pulses and whose energy is concentrated in the low-frequency band, such as a Japanese drums, the power supply voltage is fluctuated and dynamic crosstalk occurs in other channels, causing distortion.
To solve this problem, independent power transformers are mounted on the left and right channels, and four 10,000 μ F electrolytic capacitors are used to suppress dynamic crosstalk in principle.

In the tone control section, the Bass and Treble circuits are separated and independent to prevent mutual interference. In addition, the newly designed logic AND circuit allows both the Bass and Treble controls to perform complete tone jump.
Bass/Treble tone defeat and turnover frequency can be switched. In addition, the flat amplifier + Bass control + Treble control with the first stage differential amplifier 2 stage connected ± 2 power supply system eliminates the problem of phase inversion when tone jumps.

The equalizer stage employs the ICL method in which an FET is used for the first stage amplification and input capacitors are eliminated.
Since the signal from the cartridge can be directly transmitted to the amplifier element, characteristics such as phase characteristics and transient response are improved. Moreover, the amplifier circuit is FET differential amplifier 3-stage direct connection system, and excellent characteristics are obtained by driving this circuit with ± 2 power supply.
A direct connection between the input terminal and the equalizer is used to improve the SN ratio and sound quality by eliminating the influence of low-level signals from the player on the stray capacitance of the wiring.

The power amplifier section uses an all-stage direct-coupled OCL circuit with first stage differential-amplification + constant current drive class-A amplification + pure complimentary service and power Darlington block.
In particular, the Power Darlington block has an original device with built-in final, drive stage, transistors and limiter protection.

In order to compensate for the sense of hearing depending on listening conditions and sources, a 3-stage switching loudness control is adopted.

The volume includes a -20dB active muting for controlling the NFB volume of the flat amplifier in the tone control section and a 32-contact attenuator volume.

It is equipped with a subsonic filter to cut out harmful ultra-low frequency noise caused by warping of records.
Also equipped with a low filter / high filter.

Equipped with a through circuit, tape dubbing, and tape monitor for enjoying other sources such as FM and records during tape dubbing.

There was a carrying handle sold separately.
