  • Marantz PM-17

  • ¥ 100,000 (released in 1997)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with newly designed adjustable HDAM.

It has four active volumes based on the new HDAM.

Current feedback system is adopted in the power amplifier section.

Equipped with a thermal monitor meter that can check the heat up state.

It is equipped with an active filter type NFB tone control circuit.

The power supply section uses a super-ring core toroidal transformer.

There are 2 color variations, gold and black.

Aluminum top system remote controller is included.
