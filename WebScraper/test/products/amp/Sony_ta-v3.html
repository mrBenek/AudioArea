Commentary

This pre-main amplifier matches the size of the Liberty Series.

Equipped with a microphone mixing mechanism with volume.

The function display allows you to display the selected music source with illumination.

A one touch stereo cable is available to facilitate connection with Liberty Series components.

It is equipped with a loudness switch.

Equipped with headphone jack with auto speaker OFF function.
