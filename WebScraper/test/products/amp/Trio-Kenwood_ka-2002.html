  • ¥ 26,800 (around 1971)

Commentary

ATC2000 series solid state pre-main amplifier.

It has been developed with emphasis on basic performance by cutting off accessory circuits such as tone control defeat switches.

It can be upgraded to a multi-system by using a pre-main jumper circuit.
