Commentary

This is Ayre's debut stereo power amplifier.

The circuit is composed of a balanced circuit for all stages, and a single push-pull FET output stage is arranged on the plus side and minus side.
By adopting FET element with excellent linearity, sufficient characteristics can be realized with non-NFB non-feedback circuit.

Two double-layered filtering coils are installed to reduce noise components mixed into the power supply.

Uses simple internal construction.

An optional discrete phono amplifier can be set up in the enclosure.
