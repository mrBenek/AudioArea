  • SONY TA-AV590

  • ¥ 49,000 (released in 1995)

    　
Commentary

AV amplifier with DSP for effect and rear signal.

The effect component and rear signal are digitally processed by DSP to realize realistic sound field reproduction.

Surround mode includes Dolby Pro-Logic Surround with Auto Input Balance, Theater, Hall and Simulated Surround as well as fine tuning of the effect level.

The power amplifier section is equipped with a 5-ch amplifier, and the power supply section uses an S. T. D. power supply.

For system expansion, it is equipped with a monaural output, center output, and front L/R pre-output, which are ideal for connecting devices such as a subwoofer.

There are 2 variations, black and titanium gray.

It comes with a wireless remote control with a learning function. By storing the signal in this remote control, it is possible to operate other AV equipment with infrared remote control.
