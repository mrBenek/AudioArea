Commentary

A stereo power amplifier that pursues balance theory.

The Model878 supports balanced connections and provides better reproducibility than conventional unbalanced connections.

The interior is completely independent on the left and right.

By replacing the plug-in type circuit board, you can use it in three different ways : stereo amplifier, monaural amplifier, or active speaker.
When using a stereo amplifier, the two monaural amplifiers in the main unit amplify each of the L and R channels. When using a monaural power amplifier, a bridge connection allows two monaural amplifiers to be driven in one block for high power output.

The active speaker configuration uses an optional crossover module (sold separately).
A crossover circuit board allows you to switch between two monaural amplifiers, one with an integrated high-pass filter and the other with an integrated low-pass filter. The circuit board has a 6 dB per octave attenuation and allows you to specify the crossover frequency.
