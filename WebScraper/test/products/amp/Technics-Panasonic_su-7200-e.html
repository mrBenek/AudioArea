Audio Database

  • Technics SU-7200

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1975)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with all stages directly connected circuit.

The power amplifier section uses a fully connected differential amplifier OCL circuit.

The equalizer circuit uses a 3-stage direct-coupled low-noise IC with high-precision components.

Equipped with tone control.

Two types of speakers can be connected.

There was a wood case sold separately.
