  • DENON PMA-7.5S

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1993)

Commentary

PRESTA series pre-main amplifier.

The power supply section uses a toroidal transformer with little leakage flux and excellent performance in vibration prevention.

Low-impedance volume is used to improve the SN ratio at general listening levels.

Equipped with a source direct switch, it is possible to bypass the tone control.

It comes with a remote control so you can control not only the main body but also the CD player, cassette deck and tuner in a centralized manner.
