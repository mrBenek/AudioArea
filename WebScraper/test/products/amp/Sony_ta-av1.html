  • SONY TA-AV1

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1988)

    　
Commentary

AV1 series AV amplifier commercialized with AV formation as a design concept.

With the digital AV master TA-AV1 at the center, it is designed to be combined with AV tuner ST-AV1, multi-disc player MDP-AV1, speaker system SS-AV1, and cassette deck TC-AV1.

It is equipped with 8 input / output terminals mix function for switching between audio and video sources.

Equipped with a digital surround processor that supports 3 modes of Dolby, Hall and Simulated.

It has a built-in amplifier for a total of 4 channels of front and rear.

Equipped with digital picture functions such as picture-in-picture, 4/9/16 multi-screen split, flash motion, stop motion, picture art, mosaic, zoom and TV scan.

Equipped with a center output terminal for a subwoofer or center positioning speaker.

It comes with a programmable remote controller and can control Sony AV equipment.
As for other products, if they are compatible with infrared remote control, they can be operated by reading and storing the signal.

Built-in timer that can turn ON/OFF the system power at set time.
