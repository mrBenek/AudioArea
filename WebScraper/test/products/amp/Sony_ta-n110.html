  • SONY TA-N110

  • ¥ 32,800 (released in 1988)

Commentary

A stereo power amplifier that can also be used as a monaural power amplifier.

Equipped with Route 3 stereo input selector and volume.

It can also be used as a high-power monaural power amplifier by switching the switch.
It also has a 3D input terminal that allows only the low-frequency component to pass and amplify when monaural is used.
