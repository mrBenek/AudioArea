Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with AB / LR independent power supply system.

An AB and LR independent power source system is adopted to integrate VICTOR/JVC's power-oriented design, reducing dynamic interference distortion caused by power supply voltage fluctuations.
JA-S-S75 uses separate power transformers for Class B and Class A stages, which have large current changes. This prevents voltage fluctuations from becoming a type of input signal in the Class A amplifier circuit before the pre-driver stage and being amplified as distortion components.
In addition, in JA-S75, the power transformer for the class B amplifier stage is dedicated to the left and right, thereby eliminating interference between the left and right channels.

The equalizer circuit of the pre-amplifier section is equipped with a low-noise FET and employs a 3-stage cascode-connected ICL configuration that eliminates the input capacitor. In addition to eliminating the effect of the input capacitor, the ICL-FET eliminates the pop noise and dynamic range at the time of input selector switching.

The tone control circuit uses a 2-stage NF type direct-coupled high-gain amplifier to obtain high linearity and low distortion factor.

The volume uses a High Legh near-volume with a special structure. This volume uses a resistor that improves distortion and high-frequency characteristics at low volume levels by changing the area of the uniform material. In addition, the S/N ratio at low volume levels is greatly improved by using a twin volume system that is placed at the front and back of the tone control circuit.
In addition, the latest low-distortion condenser is used for the main signal system to improve sound quality.

The front panel is equipped with cartridge load switching function, and load resistance and capacity can be selected in 3 stages respectively.

The wood case was sold separately as an option.
