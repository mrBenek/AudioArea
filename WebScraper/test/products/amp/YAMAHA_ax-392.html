  • YAMAHA AX-392

  • ¥ 35,000 (released in 1998)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed based on the design philosophy of high-end machines.

Equipped with a CD direct amplifier that reduces noise in actual use to 1/3.

Equipped with linear damping circuit.

It features a phono equalizer amplifier, tone control, continuous loudness and a 60-minute sleep timer.

Equipped with 2 system speaker terminals corresponding to banana plug.

Wireless remote control is included.

There are 2 color variations, titanium color and black.
