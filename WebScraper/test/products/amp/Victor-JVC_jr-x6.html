  • VICTOR/JVC JR-X6

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

A 4 ch receiver that combines 4 ch amplifier, FM/AM tuner, CD-4 demodulator and matrix circuit into one.

Equipped with a high compatible type CD-4 demodulator, it can play discrete 4-channel CD-4 records.
In addition, it has a built-in matrix circuit equipped with a 2-position switching circuit, and is compatible with matrix 4 ch.

Four power amplifiers for 4 ch playback are built in.

It is equipped with a level meter for each channel and its own volume.
It also has a master volume that can adjust the overall volume.

Equipped with a tone control that can adjust all channels.

Equipped with an FM/AM tuner.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit.

It is equipped with a headphone port for 4 ch headphones.
