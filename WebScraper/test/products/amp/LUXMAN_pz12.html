Audio Database

  • LUXMAN PZ12

  • ¥ 17,000 (Released in September 1964)

Commentary

This all-transistor stereo preamplifier is an improved version of PZ11.

Built-in AC power supply supports both AC and DC.

NF type is used for tone control.

Metal fittings for mounting in the player are included.
