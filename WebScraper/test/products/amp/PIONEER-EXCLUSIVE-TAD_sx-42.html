  • Pioneer SX-42

  • ¥ 49,900 (around 1965)

Commentary

AM/FM multi-integrated stereo amplifier with equalizer for 4-track tape.

The tuner section is an RF tuning type using a 3-row varicon, equipped with a self-excited tetrode front end, four stages of intermediate frequency including two stages of limiter, wide-band ratio detection, and AFC.

A new type of tuning meter is used for tuning instructions.

A push-pull circuit using 6BQ5 is used for the amplifier.

Equipped with tone control, loudness contour and low / high filter.

The power supply employs a voltage doubler rectification system using a silicon diode.

Equipped with a stereo indicator.
