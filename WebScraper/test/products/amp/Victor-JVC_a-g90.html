  • VICTOR/JVC A-G90

  • ¥ 69,000 (around 1985)

Commentary

This compact pre-main amplifier incorporates technologies such as Dynamic Super A and Gm circuits used in high-end machines.

In order to support digital sources with a wide dynamic range, we have adopted a Gm circuit that increases the SN ratio and speaker drive power during actual use.

Seven input terminals are installed.
