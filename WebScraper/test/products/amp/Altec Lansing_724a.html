Commentary

FM/AM tuner preamplifier with all-transistor configuration.

It is designed for maximum performance when combined with an electronic crossover bi-amplifier 770A.

High sensitivity and stability have been obtained by using FET for the tuner section.

Plug-in modular circuit design is employed.
