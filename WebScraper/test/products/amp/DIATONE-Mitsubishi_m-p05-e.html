Commentary

In addition to its thin design, this preamplifier is equipped with a mixing amplifier with a reverb function.

Mix with other sources using a wired microphone, or mix two microphones simultaneously using an FM wireless microphone.
The mixing amplifier section also has a reverb mechanism that allows you to add reverb to only two microphones, separate from other sources.

The equalizer amplifier uses a 3-stage direct-coupled class-A SEPP circuit with differential amplification with dynamic load at the first stage, high voltage and low noise IC. Low distortion is achieved by applying stable NF to a circuit with high gain and excellent bare characteristics.

A tone defeat switch is installed to prevent undulation of the frequency response at the tone control flat position.
The tone control amplifier uses a high voltage and low noise IC similar to the equalizer amplifier.

Two tape decks can be connected for tape duplication.

Equipped with a subsonic filter.
