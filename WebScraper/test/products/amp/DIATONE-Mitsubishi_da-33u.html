  • MITSUBISHI/DIATONE DA-33U

  • ¥ 39,500 (released in 1970)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with all-silicon transistor configuration.

A quasi complimentary service SEPPP OTL circuit is used for the outputs.

A 3-stage direct connection amplifier is used for the preamplifier section.
In addition, a 3-stage direct-coupled equalizer amplifier is used to achieve low distortion by applying sufficient negative feedback.

There are two types of speaker terminals and they are switch-over type.

There are two Phono inputs.

The left and right channels are equipped with independent tone control.
