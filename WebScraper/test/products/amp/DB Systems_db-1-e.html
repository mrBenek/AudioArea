Commentary

A control amplifier designed by David Hadaway and named "The Essential Preamp".

The control amplifier DB-1 has a structure in which the power supply part is separated, and the control body DB-1 (¥ 157,000) and the power supply part DB-2 (¥ 41,000) are combined.

The basic functions of the preamp are thoroughly pursued, and the circuit is composed of a cascode amplifier without using any differential amplifier or complimentary service method.

A wood case is attached to the control body DB-1.

DB-3 Crossover, DB-4 Head Amplifier and DB-5 Tone Control are sold separately. These three models are designed to share the DB-2 power supply.
