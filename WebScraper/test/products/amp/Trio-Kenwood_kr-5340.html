  • TRIO KR-5340

  • ¥ 99,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

A high-end receiver with four main amplifiers and two BTL connections to each left and right.

The FMMPX section adopts TRIO's unique double-switching and de-modulator method, and the separation is greatly improved over a wide band from 50 Hz to 8 kHz.
In addition, the FM DET terminal is also installed in preparation for FM discrete broadcasting, which was expected to begin at that time.

With built-in SQ decoder and RM circuit, it is also possible to form a 4-channel system by adding 2 rear speakers (CD-4 demodulator and CD-4 cartridge are required for CD-4 playback).
