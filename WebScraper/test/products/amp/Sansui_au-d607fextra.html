  • SANSUI AU-D607F Extra

  • ¥ 78,000 (released in 1981)

Commentary

An improved integrated amplifier based on the AU-D607F.

Super FF and diamond differential super FF and a diamond differential circuit. In addition, the AU-D607F Extra uses the same circuit as the AU-D707F Extra for one notch higher performance.
The first stage is equipped with a constant-current differential circuit using a dual FET capable of conducting a large current with low noise. The second stage is equipped with a diamond differential circuit using a dual complimentary service with greatly improved dynamic characteristics. The power stage is equipped with a more complete Super FF. The output transistor is equipped with a high-speed NM-LAPT.

The power supply consists of two independent power supply systems on the left and right sides. A large transformer with excellent regulation is used for the transformer, and a total of 32,800 μ F is used for the capacitor.

The EQ amplifier uses a newly developed Hi PrecisionMC / MM compatible type EQ that achieves an RIAA deviation of 20 hz to 300 khz ± 0.2 db.
A low-noise, high-gm dual FET is used for the first stage, and the push-pull drive is a pure complimentary service SEPP output with two stage differentials and current differentials with a current mirror, and all the signal capacitors from the input to the speaker out are eliminated.
The power supply section for the equalizer is equipped with a linear servo regulator, which allows a constant bias current to flow in the circuit to cope with transient loads, enabling a clean and stable power supply at all times.

The signal path is simplified and straightened by the adoption of heat pipes and three dimensional wiring.
In addition, NM transistors and NM resistors are used to reduce magnetic distortion that pollutes sound.

Relays are used for both A and B speaker terminals, and the influence of magnetic flux is reduced by adopting a remote control type that eliminates the routing of the output to the speaker selector switch.

Equipped with two independent source recording selectors and a two circuit tape monitor / copy function.

Equipped with a subsonic filter.

It uses a detent type volume.

Large speaker terminal is used.

Equipped with audio muting function of -20dB.

Uses a real wood side panel and top panel.

There are 2 color variations of black and silver.
