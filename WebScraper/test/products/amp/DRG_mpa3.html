Commentary

KT88 Push-pull monaural power amplifier.

The circuit is KT88 UL connection configuration and dual fixed bias is adopted.

The power supply unit is designed to withstand twice the supply of full power, ensuring high stability.

It is hand-made.

The chassis has a 24-carat gold coating.
