Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that is the highest grade model of Sansui's PB series and an upgraded version of AU 777 d.
Aiming for more faithful sound reproduction, it was developed by accumulating thorough hearing tests.

The main amplifier section uses a 2-stage differential amplifier circuit and a fully connected system with two power supplies (plus and minus) without an output capacitor. This makes NFB uniform from the ultra-high band to the ultra-low band and improves various characteristics.

The equalizer stage uses a 3-stage E-E NF type PNP silicon transistor with low noise, and achieves low noise, low distortion, wide dynamic range and high stability.

A direct-coupled circuit is used as much as possible for the amplifier circuit, and a Mylar capacitor is used as much as possible for the necessary part of the coupling capacitor. Electrolytic capacitors with large phase distortion in the high frequency range and poor transmission characteristics are eliminated.

The tone control part uses SANSUI's original triple tone control, and not only Bass and Treble but also Midrange can be adjusted.
It uses a tone selector that switches between two frequencies for both the defeat point and the rising / falling point, and a click-step method with 2 db steps for low and high tones and 1 db steps for middle tones for precise adjustment.

Can be used independently by separating the pre-main part.

Two tape decks can be used simultaneously and a TapetoTape reprint switch is mounted.
It also includes a stereo balance check circuit, level adjuster, NF low / high filter, tuner selector, microphone input jack, muting switch, program indicator, headphone jack and DIN connector.

The wood case was sold separately.
