  • Pioneer SX-90

  • ¥ 80,000 (around 1970)

Commentary

A total stereo amplifier developed in pursuit of higher quality by IC and best-in-class power.

The FM front-end section is composed of a low-noise FET and a four link varicon.
The IF and MPX circuits are integrated into highly reliable ICs.

The tuner meters are equipped with a signal strength meter and a center tuning meter to enable more accurate tuning.

Equipped with a Low/High filter.

Equipped with Route 3 speaker outputs and center channel outputs.

It has a mixing circuit that can mix a microphone with other sources.

In consideration of upgrading to a multi-amplifier system, the pre-part and main part can be separated.
