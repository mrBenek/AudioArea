Commentary

This flat pre-amplifier has a simple configuration with a flat amplifier in the equalizer circuit to eliminate coloring and improve basic performance.

The combination of 1% metal coating resistance and 2% polypropylene capacitor realizes excellent characteristics.

The first stage employs a differential amplifier circuit with a current mirror load to achieve a high gain of 60 dB and low noise.
The next stage consists of a voltage amplification stage with a pure resistance load. The distortion factor is 0.26% at a gain of 90 and 20 V output.

A subsonic filter switch is mounted on the equalizer stage.

In order to eliminate coloring due to changes in frequency characteristics, a flat amplifier without any accessory circuit is adopted.

Uses a 26-point high-precision attenuator type master level controller.

A subsonic filter switch is mounted on the equalizer stage.
