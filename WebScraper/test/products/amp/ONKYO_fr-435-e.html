  • ONKYO FR-435

  • ¥ 85,000 (released on June 30, 1999)

Commentary

CD/MD tuner amplifier that integrates CD, MD, tuner and amplifier.

A 4 Ω discrete amplifier is used for the power amplifier.

The power supply is composed of digital and analog double transformers.

The digital signal processing section of the MD is equipped with a 20-bit processing circuit.

Operability is improved by the Multijog dial.

Equipped with CD → MD one touch dubbing function.

Equipped with signal sync recording function.

It is equipped with a sampling rate converter and an optical digital input / output terminal.

Power cable with polarity display is used.

The speaker terminal connector is used for the speaker terminal.
