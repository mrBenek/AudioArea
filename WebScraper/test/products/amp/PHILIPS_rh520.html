  • PHILIPS RH520

  • ¥ 76,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with design and performance conforming to RH521.

The power amplifier section is designed to increase the bias current, so that notching and crossover distortion are reduced even when the signal is small.

Equipped with a speaker matrix circuit, 4-channel playback is possible.
