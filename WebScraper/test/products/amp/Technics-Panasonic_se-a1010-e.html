Commentary

Stereo power amplifier equipped with MOS class AA amplifier.

A MOS class AA amplifier is adopted. The pure class A voltage control amplifier and current supply amplifier are combined with a class AA bridge to improve sound quality.

A newly developed Take II electrolytic capacitor has been adopted.
This condenser is an upgraded version of an electrolytic condenser that uses a bamboo fiber mixed separator. The basic structure of the electrolytic condenser has been revised to improve sound quality.

The input level volume is mounted on the back panel, and fine adjustment is possible when bi-amplifier drive or multi-amplifier drive is performed.

Equipped with a power meter that can turn on / off the lighting.

Uses a high-capacity OFC power cord with polarity display.

Gold-plated input terminal is used.

OCC wire material is used for internal wiring.

Speaker terminal compatible with banana plug is adopted.
