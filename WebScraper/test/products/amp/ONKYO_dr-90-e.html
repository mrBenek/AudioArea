Commentary

A DVD player component that integrates a DVD and an AV amplifier.

Equipped with Dolby Digital / DTS-compatible 24 bitDSP decoder for DVD-Video surround playback, up to 5.1 channels of surround playback are possible.
Also, 4-channel analog surround playback is possible with Dolby Pro Logic compatible sources.

The D/A converter is equipped with a 96 kHz / 24-bit D/A converter.

Equipped with an FM/AM tuner.
Up to 30 stations can be preset in the tuner section.

It offers five original surround modes for CD and analog input audio, including Orchestra, Unplugged, Studio-mix, 5 ch Stereo and TV Logic.

It has a Japanese on-screen display function.

Equipped with a late night function for low-volume playback.

The video circuit contains a 10-bit video D/A converter.

Equipped with 3-step zoom function.

Equipped with a cinema re-equalizer.

Wireless remote control is included.
This remote control is equipped with a learning function and can memorize key TV operations.
