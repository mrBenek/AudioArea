Commentary

A highly stable and reliable mono power amplifier for business use.

All-silicon transistor configuration is adopted.

It is equipped with a protective circuit to prevent failure due to unstable load conditions or mismounting.
A DC fuse is used to protect the power supply, and a special circuit is used to protect the output transistor.

The input / output terminals are designed with flexibility. Unbalanced 50k Ω input terminals can be changed to balanced lines by installing a 5195 transformer. All output terminals are of balanced type. 8 Ω, 25 v, and 70.7 v screw-type terminals are available for Class 2 wiring.
The bridge output is unbalanced.
