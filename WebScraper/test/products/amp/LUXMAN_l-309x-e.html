Commentary

It is an improved version of L-309V and a pure DC configuration integrated amplifier with completely new contents.

In the power amplifier section using a DC configuration, elements with particularly short switching times, such as a ring emitter transistor (RET) in the output stage and an emitter ballast transistor (EBT) in the driver stage, are used.
In addition, notch distortion is reduced by taking various circuit measures such as setting the output stage to AB-class operation and setting the operation point at which as much current as possible flows over the entire power amplifier section.

The equalizer stage and flat amplifier stage of the preamp section are DC. Moreover, instead of lowering the output impedance by applying NFB deeply, the current as much as possible is applied to make the impedance low to improve bare characteristics and high-frequency stability.

A large toroidal power transformer and large toroidal power transformer and a block capacitor (15,000 μ Fx2) with sound quality.

It uses excellent parts such as newly developed sound quality improvement film and condenser.
In order to improve the S / N ratio during actual use, a 4-channel volume with a preset mechanism is installed to return the volume to the optimum volume level position.

It is equipped with a linear equalizer that compensates for subtle differences in characteristics across the entire spectrum of all program sources, as well as the tone control required for sound quality compensation.
