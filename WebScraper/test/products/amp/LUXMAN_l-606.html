Commentary

This pre-main amplifier takes a step forward from the conventional rack type and incorporates a new concept into the circuit structure.

The circuit configuration is designed to eliminate coupling capacitors as much as possible. The signal is fed from the equalizer to the main amplifier only via the buffer stage.

A signal indicator with a circuit block diagram is mounted on the top of the panel.
On this panel, arrows indicate changes in signal paths such as tape dubbing, tone controls, and filters.

Equipped with high and low filters, low boosters, and tape-dubbing circuits.
