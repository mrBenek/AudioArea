  • ¥ 125,000 (Released in September 1977)

Commentary

DC control amplifier with thin design.

All amplifier circuits are DC-converted into a DC amplifier configuration.
The equalizer amplifier section uses a DML-IC that blocks a differential amplifier circuit with an FET at the first stage, a cascode circuit, a current mirror circuit and a constant-current circuit to assist the differential amplifier circuit, and a cascode circuit with a constant-current circuit and an output circuit using SEPP of class A amplification.
The flat amplifier section consists of a DC amplifier that combines the same DML-IC as the equalizer amplifier with an emitter follower output circuit with a constant current circuit.

By adopting a unique DML-IC, the generation of DC drift due to the change of external conditions such as temperature is coped with.

The chassis layout considered from all angles is adopted, and the input circuit is wired in the shortest path with almost no shielding of signal path.
In addition, all the basic amplifier circuits, such as the equalizer and flat amplifier, are placed on a one my-board, and the left and right channels are completely separated. As a result, excellent frequency characteristics, S/N ratio, stability, and crosstalk characteristics are achieved.

Although the volume is a continuously variable type, it is extremely precise. There is almost no linkage error between the left and right channels, and a new volume with improved sound quality is adopted.
In addition, various parts such as resistors, semi-fixed resistors, capacitors, and so on have been well considered.

Equipped with a twin T-type filter to effectively remove ultra-low frequency noise.
This filter is composed of capacitors and resistors without any active elements, and effectively filters out only very low frequency noise from 5 hz to 10 hz.

Equipped with a linear equalizer, you can adjust the recording characteristics of program sources such as disks and tapes.

Equipped with an attenuator that can be preset in the range from -15dB to -40dB.

It is equipped with functions such as a two line tape monitor circuit and a tape dubbing circuit.
