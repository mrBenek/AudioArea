  • SANSUI SAX-250

  • ¥ 39,900 (released in July 1969)

Commentary

FM/AM receiver targeted for personal use.

The front end of the FM tuner section is equipped with an FET and a 3-row varicon.
In addition, a 5-stage IF amplifier achieves low distortion rate and high sensitivity.

The power amplifier section uses a quasi complimentary service circuit using silicon transistors.

It is equipped with FM stereo indicator, MPX noise canceler, automatic FM stereo switching function, tape monitor and loudness control.
