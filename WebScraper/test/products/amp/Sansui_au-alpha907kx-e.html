Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with advanced α -X balance circuit.

The advanced α -X balanced circuit has a balanced configuration in which signals are transmitted and amplified simultaneously. The amplifier circuit and the ground circuit of the amplifier are completely separated. Therefore, the circuit itself blocks the in-phase distortion caused by counter electromotive current and external noise generated at the speaker side.
In addition, a push-pull system in which a total of four dedicated amplifiers on the + side and - side of the output drive the speakers under the same conditions from both + and - sides realizes a low distortion factor, and dynamic frequency characteristics are greatly improved by lowering the circuit impedance.

The power stage is equipped with the NM-LAPT developed exclusively for audio. The bonding wires are completely made of pure copper and non-magnetic. Excellent high-frequency characteristics and current limit values are obtained.

All stages from input to output are configured as a wide-range DC amplifier, and NFB is minimized by expanding frequency characteristics in bare characteristics, improving phase characteristics, and reducing distortion factor.

The power stage uses the NEW diamond differential circuit that can take out the - side output without phase inversion, and the generation of TIM distortion is suppressed by the quick response to the instantaneous input signal.
In addition, by cascading the first stage FET, the circuit is made wide range and high speed.

NEW α -X balanced power supply is used for the power supply part.
As a closed loop isolated from ground, the unbalanced power supply, which causes sound quality degradation, is minimized.
In addition, the power stage and voltage amplification stage are completely separated, and electrolytic capacitors for power supply and fast recovery diodes for high sound quality are used.
The power transformer uses a large power transformer with a total weight of 13 kg and micro-leakage flux, which is packed with high-density filler inside a four layered structure.

The power supply unit is placed in the center of the amplifier, and the power units are symmetrically arranged on the left and right sides of the power supply unit using twin monaural construction to achieve the optimum weight balance as a structure.

In order to control the vibration and resonance of the amplifier, an integrated mechanical feedback is introduced.
Vibration control isolation parts made of pure copper are used in the vibration transmission path between the master volume and the chassis and between the power transistor and the radiator.
In addition, a Teflon sheet with high internal loss and excellent vibration absorption capacity is placed between the insulator and the bottom plate, and a Teflon tape is placed between the radiator, bonnet and chassis to isolate vibration.

In addition, Teflon materials and damping parts made of pure copper such as rolled copper base, copper-plated rear panel, pure copper insulator, specially painted bonnet and pure copper base are used.

This product is selected carefully from the high-quality parts obtained from the development of the Vintage series of SANSUI.
In particular, gold-plated lead resistors are used for important parts of the circuit where signals directly pass through and affect sound quality. Gold plated leads are also used for the film capacitor of the power supply part in order to pursue pure sound.
The AU - α 907 kx uses ARS, ARSA, AWF, gold-plated lead GUG, SAG, GSG, film condenser, special styrene condenser, coil with copper case, RMG gold plated resistor, etc.
In addition, copper-plated screws are used for front panel mounting screws.

Equipped with a power amplifier with balanced / normal inputs and a direct input.

Uses source power direct, REC selector, all-gold plated input terminal 11 systems, 6-row detent volume, real side wood, power cord with polarity display, etc.
