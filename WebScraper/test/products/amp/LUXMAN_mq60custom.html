  • LUXMAN MQ60Custom

  • ¥ 89,000 (around 1974)

Commentary

Tube type stereo power amplifier with non-feedback system.

The MQ60 is a negative feedback amplifier with approximately 17 dB negative feedback, but the MQ60Custom is a non-feedback amplifier without negative feedback.
The MQ60 with negative feedback has superior characteristics such as effective output, distortion factor characteristics, and frequency characteristics. However, by adopting a no-feedback system, the MQ60 realizes tones that cannot be obtained without no-feedback.

NEC's 3-pole output tube 50CA10 is used for the circuit in a push-pull system.
Equipped with an input level set function.
