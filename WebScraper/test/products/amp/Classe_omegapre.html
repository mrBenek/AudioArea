  • CLASSE OMEGA PRE

  • ¥ 1,950,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

OMEGA series preamp.

A balanced amplifier configuration is used from input to output.
In addition, a symmetrical discrete circuit is used for the circuit. A low-noise, low-distortion J-FET is used for the input stage, a high-gain bipolar is used for the voltage amplification stage, and a high-power MOS-FET with high drivability is used for the output stage.

The power supply consists of independent left and right channels, two toroidal transformers and a large capacitor with a total capacity of more than 62,000 μ F.

The volume part adopts 12-bit logic control method and uses 48 high-precision metal film resistors, and realizes high-quality analog signal transmission despite digital control.

The chassis structure adopts a separate chassis configuration with the power supply part as a separate case, eliminating interference between circuits.

Wireless remote control is included.
