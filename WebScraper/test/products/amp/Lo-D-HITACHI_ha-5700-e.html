  • Lo-D HA-5700

  • ¥ 69,800 (released in 1979)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier using power MOS FET.

The power amplifier circuit is basically the same as the HMA 9500 and uses a simple 3-stage direct connection DC power circuit.

Newly developed low-noise integrated circuits are used for the equalizer amplifier and tone amplifier. The circuit configuration is a differential input 3-stage directly connected push-pull output.
The use of high-gain integrated circuits makes it possible to connect MC cartridges.

The power supply section uses two power transformers and a smoothing capacitor (1000 μ F + 1000 μ F) to provide a sufficient power supply section for large amplitude.

Equipped with a low filter, tone defeat switch, monitor switch, loudness and speaker selector switch.
