Commentary

Compact pre-main amplifier.

A-M1H is a horizontal type with a silver finish, and A-M1V is a vertical type with a black finish.

Uses D-class power supply utilizing digital technology.
In addition, the entire signal path has been integrated into an IC and a high-efficiency heat sink designed by a computer has been used for miniaturization.

The normal function is a feather-touch push-button operated type, and the sub function is stored in the ceiling pocket.
