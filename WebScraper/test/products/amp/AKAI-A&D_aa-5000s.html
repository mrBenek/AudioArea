  • AKAI AA-5000/AA-5000S

  • ¥ 42,000 (around 1967)

Commentary

A solid-state pre-main amplifier using transistors.

A silicon power transistor is used at the final stage of the power amplifier section.
In addition, an EPC circuit is mounted as a protection circuit to protect the power transistor in case of an accident such as output terminal short circuit.

OTL system is adopted.

A built-in equalizer for the tape head allows direct playback from the head.

Equipped with DIN jack and stereo headphone jack.

Equipped with stand-alone high / low filters and tone control.

* The difference between AA-5000 and AA-5000S is unknown.
