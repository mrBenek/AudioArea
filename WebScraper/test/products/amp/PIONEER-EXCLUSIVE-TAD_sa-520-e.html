  • Pioneer SA-520

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

An authentic pre-main amplifier designed for ease of use.

All stages of the power amplifier are connected to a semi-power complimentary service system. The first stage is equipped with an operation amplifier and an input bias compensation circuit to achieve high DC stability.

The equalizer amplifier is a 2-stage direct-coupled system that combines a low-noise transistor with a low-noise resistor and a styrofoam capacitor to improve S/N and stability.

CR-type tone control has been adopted, and in pursuit of component accuracy, the tone control knob has obtained characteristics with less waviness in the frequency characteristics in the flat position.
