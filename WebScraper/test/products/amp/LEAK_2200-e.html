Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that has inherited the tradition since Point One.

The power amplifier employs a pure complimentary service OCL stage and a constant current drive class A driver stage. By applying a deep and stable DC NFB, it is designed to operate stably without being affected by temperature changes, load fluctuations, shock noise, etc.

A protective circuit is provided to protect the speakers and output transistors in the event of an accident such as output terminal short-circuit or excessive input.

A circuit with differential inputs is used for the equalizer stage, and the DC NFB is applied to the overall stage so that it is shock-resistant and stable.

It is equipped with functions such as a BTL circuit that can produce monaural output with a single switch, and a speaker matrix switch that can produce a hall tone effect.
