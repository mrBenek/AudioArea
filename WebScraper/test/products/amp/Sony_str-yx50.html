Commentary

Stereo receiver with 8 FM / AM random presets.

The use of a synthesizer tuner enables random presetting of 8 FM / AM stations.

With the 10-key input method, you can directly input the frequency of the broadcasting station you want to receive.

Digital display is adopted.
