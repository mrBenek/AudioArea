  • SONY TA-2650

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

This pre-main amplifier is based on the technologies developed in the ES-II series and Sony's semiconductors.

All stages are directly connected to the power amplifier section with pure complimentary service symmetrical OCL circuit.
In addition, differential amplification by dual transistors is adopted.

It also functions as a control amplifier, such as two way tape copy function and Tape2 input / output jack.
