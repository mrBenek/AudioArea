Commentary

This pre-main amplifier is a low-cost amplifier for beginners, with LAX technology.

The power amplifier section uses a quasi complimentary service system circuit.
The output stage uses the SEPP-OTL is adopted for the output stage, and the protection circuit is omitted by the system unique to LUX.

The preamp section consists of an NF-type equalizer and an NF-type tone control.
These circuits use carefully selected low-noise transistors.

Equipped with two types of speaker terminals.

Equipped with an attenuator of -18dB.

Equipped with left and right independent tone control.

Equipped with a tape monitor switch.

The front panel is an aluminum hairline finish finished with white gold alumite dyeing.
The knob is also finished in the same way.

Heat-resistant high-impact styrol is used for the hood, and the surface is processed with leather for a unique shape.

A newly developed LUXMAN original terminal is used for the speaker terminal.

The internal structure uses a rational structure without using a chassis.

There was a special wood case sold separately.
