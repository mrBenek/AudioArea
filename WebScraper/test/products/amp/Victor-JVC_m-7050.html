Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier of the Laboratory series that adopts Super A circuit.

It is equipped with a super A circuit developed to improve the efficiency of A class amplifier without switching distortion.
In the Super A, the bias voltage of the power stage is not fixed and is automatically controlled according to the input signal. This allows power to be utilized according to the magnitude of the signal while the power transistor is always kept ON, achieving efficiency close to that of Class B.
In addition, this Super A bias circuit realizes more excellent linearity and lower distortion factor than the conventional A-class power stage. In addition, it is easy to obtain the theoretical output characteristic which is inversely proportional to the load impedance with Super A. Therefore, it is possible to send a high-fidelity signal to the addition of a speaker with many undulations in the impedance characteristic.

In the Super A, a unique concept is incorporated not only in the power stage but also in the driver stage, and an excellent distortion factor is realized in the overall.
Class A operation is often used in general driver stages because they do not use as much power as the power stage. However, even class A operation cannot cover the nonlinearity of the semiconductor itself. Super A is reconsidering the use of semiconductors. By making the operation locus of the static characteristics of the common-emitter circuit constant, which changed according to the input signal level, a low distortion factor of less than 1/10 compared to the conventional driver stage is realized.

The power supply section uses a special structure transformer with a capacity of 500 VA and a weight of 15 kg, an electrolytic capacitor that maintains low impedance and low distortion rate up to ultra-high frequencies, and also takes into consideration dynamic interference distortion between the power stage and driver stage.

Aluminum is used for the chassis base to suppress distortion caused by non-magnetic materials such as iron, and magnetic materials are eliminated from signal circuit parts to eliminate magnetic distortion.

Equipped with a relay-type speaker selector that maintains the same low impedance as the Direct Out.

Equipped with a color tone switchable illuminate scale large power meter.
This meter can confirm the operation of the protection circuit.

High reliability glass epoxy board is adopted.

The input terminal is equipped with two systems, direct (DC) and subsonic (AC).

Equipped with level control.
