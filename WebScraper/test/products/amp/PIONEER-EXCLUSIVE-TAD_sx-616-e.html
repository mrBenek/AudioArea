Commentary

A fully functional and versatile stereo receiver.

The FM tuner consists of an FM front-end with an FET and four variable capacitor, and an IF with a ceramic filter and IC to improve sensitivity, image ratio, and selectivity.
The AM tuner uses a 3-row varicon and a ceramic filter.

The dial scale is a wide type that covers the entire panel surface, and the FM is a linear scale with regular intervals of 250 kHz, making it easy to read frequencies and to select neighboring stations.

FM muting circuit using FET is adopted, and unpleasant inter-station noise is effectively cut off.

Equipped with Klix/Klukš top tone control and FET in the first stage of control amplifier, input impedance is high and control characteristics with high stability are obtained.

It is equipped with two pairs of tape monitor terminals and two pairs of phono terminals for tape-to-tape duplication.
The speaker outputs have a Route 3 and can be switched to five positions : A, B, C, A + B and A + C.
