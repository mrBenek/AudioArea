  • PHILIPS RH720

  • ¥ 190,000 (around 1972)

Commentary

A stereo receiver that integrates a pre-main amplifier RH521 and a tuner RH621.

Equipped with FM preset touch control mechanism using FET, 6 stations can be easily selected.

It meets the German Industrial Standard (DIN) HiFi specification 45500.
