  • Audio Dynamics A-1

  • ¥ 80,000 (around the mid-1980s?)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier of Euro-style component series.

By arranging independent power supply for each stage, mutual influence is suppressed, and stable operation with low distortion rate and good start-up is enabled.

Equipped with tone control.
It also has an infrasonic filter that cuts out harmful ultra-low frequency signals.

Equipped with a CD direct position, CD signal can be directly input to the power amplifier section.

System remote control is included.
This remote control supports not only A1 operation but also various components of the Euro-Style series, enabling centralized control of tuner tuning, CD selection and programs.
