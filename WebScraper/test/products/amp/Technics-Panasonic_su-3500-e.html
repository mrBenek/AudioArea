  • ¥ 86,800 (released in 1973)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that improves circuit stability with a constant voltage power supply of ± 40 v.

The EQ circuit is a 3-stage direct-coupled circuit configuration using a differential-amplified 2-power supply system with a pure complimentary service SEPP output stage.
In addition, the input impedance can be switched in 3 levels of 25k Ω / 50k Ω / 100k Ω.

The main amplifier section is composed of a pure complimentary service OCL circuit connected to all stages. In addition, the power supply voltage is lowered and two 10,000 μ F electrolytic capacitors are used for the output stage to pursue low distortion rate and high stability.

The tone control circuit is NF-type and equipped with a tone defeat switch that instantly restores the characteristics.

Uses a preset volume that can also be used as an audio mute.

Two tape decks can be connected.

Equipped with two Phono terminals.
