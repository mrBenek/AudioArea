  • JBL 6233

  • ¥ 700,000 (around 1978)

Commentary

Dual channel power amplifier for professionals requiring high output and low distortion.

It can also be used as a 700W (8 Ω) monaural power amplifier by using a bridge connection.
An optional transformer 5195 is required for balanced input and bridge connection.

A unique power supply circuit using a switching circuit is adopted.

The output can be seen by a lamp on the front of the panel.

Can be stored in 3 EIA standard rack.
