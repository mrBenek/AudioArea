  • Technics SU-HT500

Commentary

An AV amplifier with 4 ch power amplifier and Dolby Pro Logic.

It is equipped with an independent 4-ch power amplifier with front 2 ch, center 1 ch and surround 1 ch.

Equipped with Dolby Pro Logic to maximize the sound effects of software recorded with Dolby Surround sound.
In addition, it is equipped with SFC (Sound Field Control) to support music software and games, and you can enjoy three surround modes : game mode, hall mode and simulated mode.

Four video and two audio connections are provided.

It comes with a system remote controller that enables centralized control of Panasonic / Technics AV equipment.
