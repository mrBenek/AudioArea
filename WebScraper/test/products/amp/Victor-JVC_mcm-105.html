  • VICTOR/JVC MCM-105

  • ¥ 52,600 (around 1970)

Commentary

105 series stereo power amplifier.

It is designed to be combined with the MCP 105 and can be used in multi-amplifier systems using the #105 series.

A quasi-output circuit is used for the complimentary service circuit, and 85 v is applied for the B voltage to improve the distortion characteristics and transients.

It has a built-in subsonic filter to cut the noise in the ultra low and high frequency range.

It is equipped with an electronic automatic recovery type protection circuit to protect the power transistor.

A volume for level adjustment is mounted on the back and can be adjusted when using a multi-amplifier.

Equipped with a VU meter with sensitivity selector switch.

Equipped with two channels of speaker jacks, System1/2/1 + 2 can be selected by the selector.

Equipped with 3-step switching function of damping factor.
You can use 50 if you prefer a crisp sound and 5 or 0.5 if you like a vacuum tube sound.
