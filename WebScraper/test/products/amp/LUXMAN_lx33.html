  • LUXMAN LX33

  • ¥ 138,000 (released in December 1979)

Commentary

Tube type integrated amplifier with simple configuration.

It is designed with the aim of reducing various kinds of distortion as evidence of sound quality improvement.

6CA7 is used for the output tube and 15-inch output transformer, a new type developed for the LX33.
Ultra-linear connection is used for the output stage.

The preamplifier is equipped with a LUX type NF tone control circuit.
