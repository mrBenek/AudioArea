Commentary

AV amplifier equipped with Dolby Pro Logic II and MPEG-2 AAC decoder.

It is equipped with Dolby Pro Logic II which realizes 5.1 ch playback by further developing the conventional Dolby Pro Logic which can only enjoy 2 ch source in 4 ch surround sound. You can choose between Dolby Pro Logic II Movie for movies and Dolby Pro Logic II Music for music.
It is also equipped with an MPEG-2 AAC decoder that is used in BS digital TV broadcasting.

For initial settings, tuner control and input source switching, the Smart Scan Navigator makes simple operation possible. Custom settings are also available. The listening mode can be memorized in each input selector, and the signal level can be adjusted slightly for each input device and source by the intelligent volume function. Settings can be made according to each use state.

The amplifier section is equipped with a newly designed discrete 5-ch power amplifier.
It also features a 24-bit crystal DSP processor for audio, a 96 kHz / 24-bit D / A converter for high-precision analog conversion, and a large extruded aluminum heat sink.

Wide Range Amplifier Technology WRAT for DVD-Audio and SACD is introduced.

Equipped with an optimal gain volume circuit that improves S/N with optimal attenuation control.

Equipped with a non-scaling configuration to prevent dynamic range and S/N degradation caused by downmix.

It is equipped with a Cinema Re Equalizer (Re-EQ), which adjusts the sound field to obtain the sound field of a movie theater in the home environment.

Equipped with a speaker impedance selector that can use 4 Ω speakers.

It uses an audio / video separation board that protects the signal purity by preventing noise contamination.

All channels have banana plug-compatible speaker terminals.

Equipped with digital / analog automatic input switching function.

Equipped with power on volume function that can set the level when the power is ON.

Built-in 40-station preset FM/AM tuner.

A learning remote controller with pre-set operation codes for AV equipment of major manufacturers is included.
This remote control also has a macro function that combines continuous remote control operations into one button.
