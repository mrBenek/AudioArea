  • PHILIPS AH380

  • ¥ 90,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

Stereo power amplifier of the slimline and component series.

A DC amplifier configuration is adopted in which all capacitors are removed from the signal path.

Parallel push-pull configuration is adopted for the output stage to obtain high power.

Equipped with 3 protection circuits.

As an option sold separately, there was a special wood case with mahogany finish.
