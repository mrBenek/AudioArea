  • CLASSE CP-47.5

  • ¥ 450,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

A preamplifier that pursues sound quality thoroughly.

Careful design from the board to device placement eliminates magnetic distortion and noise that pollute delicate music signals.

A sealed toroidal transformer is used for the power supply section.
It is also designed with high sound quality and a small capacitor.

The input jacks are equipped with two balanced inputs and a Line 5 unbalanced input.

The output terminal is equipped with one balanced output and two unbalanced outputs.

Wireless remote control is included.

There was an optional MM/MC phono input card sold separately.
