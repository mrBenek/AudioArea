  • SANSUI PM-900

  • ¥ 24,400 (around 1960)

Commentary

3-band monaural receiver.

In addition to FM and BC (AM), you can receive shortwave broadcasting.

Compatible with crystal type and magnetic type cartridges.

A microphone can be connected.

Equipped with a connection terminal for tape recorder.

The FM tuner uses the Foster Seeley method as a detection circuit with less distortion.
A limiter circuit and an AFC circuit are also installed.

Equipped with a loudness control.
