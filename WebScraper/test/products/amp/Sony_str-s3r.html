Commentary

Stereo receiver compatible with total system remote control.

By using RM-S5 (¥ 35,000) (sold separately), it is compatible with a total system remote control that can operate connected video and audio devices wirelessly.

It is equipped with a sound effect which can choose 3 sound qualities with one touch.

The tuner section uses a digital synthesizer that can preset seven FM/AM stations.

Equipped with a microphone mixing mechanism with reverb.
