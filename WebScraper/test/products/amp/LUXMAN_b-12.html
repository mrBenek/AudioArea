  • LUXMAN B-12

  • ¥ 125,000 (1 unit, released in December 1977)

Commentary

A DC-configuration monaural power amplifier designed for the ideal form of a power amplifier.

The output stage uses a triple push-pull connection to take out a large output with a margin.

It is designed with a heat sink as part of the front design.

A large toroidal transformer is used for the power supply section.
