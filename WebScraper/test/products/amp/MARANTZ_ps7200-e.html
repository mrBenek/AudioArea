Commentary

As a direct model of the PS-17SA, this AV amplifier realizes Marantz's original AV concept of "pure surround".

Built-in 6.1 ch mode and 6 ch power amplifier for Dolby Surround EX and DTS-ES software.
It is also equipped with an MPEG2 AAC decoder for BS digital broadcasting.

Matrix format "Dolby Pro Logic II" (movie / music) is installed.

Equipped with Circle Surround CS5.1 adopted in PS-17SA.

Supports virtual surround sound.

Equipped with 6-channel stereo.

All channels are equipped with the same power and response discrete power amplifier.

HT-EQ, which corrects lines for home use, and TV Auto / On, which automatically turns the power on/off with a video signal.

Equipped with AV selector and Euterpe oleracea slave digital inputs, digital outputs, pre-out jacks for all channels, and SACD 6.1 ch direct inputs.
All channels are equipped with a large screw-type speaker terminal.

Equipped with LCD learning remote controller with macro function.
