  • BEVERIDGE RM-1/RM-2

  • ¥ 980,000 (released in 1980)

Commentary

A bulb-type control amplifier that Vivaridge announced as a single component for the first time.

Control amplifier RM-1 consists of an amplifier body RM-1 and a power supply RM-2.

A semiconductor circuit is used for the power supply and control system supporting the amplifier system, and a vacuum tube is used for the amplifier element.

The main circuit configuration consists of a DC amplifier configuration using a servo loop, a CR phono equalizer, an absolute phase control, and a touch-sensing mute mechanism.

The audio booster consists of 12 6DJ8 (E88CC) balls.

Equipped with a super sonic filter and a sub sonic filter.

Mutual interference is suppressed by using a separate chassis for the power supply.
