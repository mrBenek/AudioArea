  • Pioneer SC-70

  • ¥ 19,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

Compact stereo preamplifier.

The equalizer amplifier is a 3-stage direct connection EE feedback type using silicon transistors.
In addition, an FET is used for the tone control circuit, so that there is no fluctuation in the mid-range level due to tone adjustment and the tone is stable.

Equipped with two Phono inputs and AUX inputs.

Accessories such as audio muting switch and headphone output are mounted.
