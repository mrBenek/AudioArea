  • SONY TA-AX4

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier using legato linear system.

By adopting the Legato linear system, Hi-ft power transistor, pulse power supply, heat pipe, etc., simple and straight transmission is ensured.

Uses a high-gain equalizer amplifier compatible with MC cartridges.

Equipped with 2-speed feather touch volume.

Equipped with 2 tape monitor / independent tape copy function.

Equipped with self-defeat tone control.

Built-in functions such as audio muting, subsonic filters, and loudness control.

The phono terminal is gold-plated.
