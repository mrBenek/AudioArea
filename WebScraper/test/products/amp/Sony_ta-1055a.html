Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that inherits the design philosophy of the ES series, eliminates coloring, and aims to extract program source faithfully.

The power amplifier section uses a direct-coupling type differential-amplified two power supply and semi- complimentary service circuit.
In the preamplifier section, a high-voltage low-noise IC specially developed for audio is used for the equalizer amplifier to improve linearity and distortion factor characteristics.

It has a built-in PNP-NPN2-stage direct amplifier dedicated for microphone mixing, so that the appropriate level can always be set by the microphone level control.
In the case of a single microphone, if it is plugged into the left side, even in monaural, only the sound of the microphone can be transmitted from both channels.

The tone control uses a slide type and comes with a center click so that flat position can be easily obtained.
