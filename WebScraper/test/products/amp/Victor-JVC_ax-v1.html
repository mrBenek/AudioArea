Commentary

Integrated amplifier of HMV series.

The sound quality of the ME-1000 and AX-900 has been improved by applying the technology cultivated in the development of the ME-1000 and AX - 400, and through more thorough hearing.
In addition, it is equipped with non-interference energy amplification and symmetrical design, inheriting the concept of the Laboratory Series.

The amplifier section uses Advanced Super A, an advanced version of Super A, a unique high-efficiency A-class circuit.
The output transistor has a push-pull configuration.

The phono equalizer amplifier section uses an all-discrete configuration.
It operates at more than twice the power supply voltage of conventional products, and realizes high S/N and high dynamic range by the single input circuit of low-noise FET.

The power supply section uses a Phillips / Slotted independent twin power transformer with matching power power supply characteristics of both Phillips and Slotted. The power transformer uses a low-Bm EI type power transformer.
2 mm for the power cable2It uses an extra-thick OFC power cord, and by inserting the power cord from the bottom, the cord is directly connected to the power transformer, eliminating interference caused by internal routing.

The front and top panels have an arc shell form and is an integrated 3.5 mm thick L-type aluminum panel. This is not only the beauty of the form but also the chassis strength.
In addition, a 3.2 mm thick steel plate weight chassis is adopted, and main parts such as resonance dispersion tapered heat sink and power transformer are firmly fixed to the chassis base by through bolts to unify vibration mode.

The internal layout uses independent 3-block construction.
The three blocks of the power amplifier, preamplifier and control are clearly separated to eliminate mutual interference between circuits.
To minimize the signal path, we have adopted an extension shaft from the volume knob on the front panel to the preamplifier section located close to the rear input terminal. We have also adopted an input switching relay to prevent signal degradation due to contact.

The volume section uses a high-density resin volume knob to improve the operation and feel with high sound quality.

Equipped with 3-position presence control, you can adjust the low range for room conditions, speaker characteristics and soft recording.

The speaker terminal uses a brass machined gold plated terminal.

System control remote control is included.
This remote control allows basic operation of the CD player XL-V1 and cassette deck TD-V1.
