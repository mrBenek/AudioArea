  • SANSUI PR-333

  • ¥ 15,400 (around the 1950 s?)

Commentary

A mono tuner preamplifier with a built-in tuner.

The tuner section uses a 3-stage variable band system. It can be switched according to the application, either for separation in areas with high interference or for sound quality when receiving broadband broadcasting.

It supports LP/SP record playback by crystal and magnetic pickups, Hi-Fi tape playback by tape deck, and announcement by microphone.

A cathode follower system is adopted.

Equipped with a loudness control.
