  • YAMAHA AVX-590DSP

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1996)

Commentary

DSP AV amplifier equipped with Yamaha's original CINEMA DSP.

A total of 8 surround programs are installed.

All 5-channel power amplifiers are installed in discrete configuration.
In addition, Yamaha's unique linear damping circuit is incorporated to ensure the optimum drive for any speaker at all times.

Eight voice channels and four video channels (two S-terminal channels) are installed, including the Phono terminal.
In addition, a 5 ch pre-out terminal is mounted.

Equipped with sleep timer (120/90/60/30 minutes).

There are 2 color variations, titanium color and black.

Wireless remote control is included.
