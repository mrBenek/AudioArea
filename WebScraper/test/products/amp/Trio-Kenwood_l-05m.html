  • TRIO L-05M

  • ¥ 60,000 (1 unit, around 1979)

Commentary

This high-speed power amplifier was completed with the theme of improving the transient response in the high-frequency range that matches the low-frequency characteristics improved by the DC amplifier.

The combination of a newly designed high-speed circuit and a newly developed high-speed element achieves broadband characteristics from DC to 600 khz.
