  • SANSUI Q-55

  • ¥ 25,160 (1 unit, released in 1958)

Commentary

A professional-type monaural power amplifier designed for high-fidelity broadcasting facilities such as schools, theaters, coffee shops, halls and studios.

High output and low distortion factor are realized by fixed bias system of GT tube 6CA7PP and large transformer.
In addition to its excellent electrical characteristics, it also has high stability, making it possible to play continuously for a long time.

Equipped with a high impedance terminal and monitor terminal, there is little loss even if the speaker is extended far away. Moreover, the output can be heard at the monitor terminal.

The NF amount of 19 dB (V-NFB) is applied and a damped sound can be obtained.

It is designed with punching metal and 2 tone color metallic paint.
