  • AVI(AV International) S2000MI

  • ¥ 160,000 (around 1995)

Commentary

AV International's first integrated amplifier.

The peak power that enables stable control of low-impedance speakers and condenser speakers is inherited from the separate amplifier.

It is designed with less distortion regardless of the volume level.

The remote controller was an option sold separately.
This remote control allows you to turn the power on / off, switch the input, adjust the volume and mute.
