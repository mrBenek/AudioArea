  • SANSUI QR-500

  • ¥ 55,000 (around 1973?)

Commentary

A 4-channel receiver equipped with Sansui QS synthesizer and decoder functions.

The 2-2-4 method allows you to enjoy four channels of the conventional 2-channel source.
You can also play 4-2-4 matrix records, 4-channel FM broadcasts, and 4-4-4 discrete 4-channel tapes.

The IC developed by SANSUI is used in the synthesizer and decoder section.

A slide type is used for adjusting the volume of front and rear.

Semi-output ITL-OTL system is used for the complimentary service circuit.

Equipped with an FM/AM tuner.
