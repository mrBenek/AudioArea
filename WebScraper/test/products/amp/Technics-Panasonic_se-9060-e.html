Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier that uses the best circuit technology to achieve the ultimate level of high performance.

We have achieved 0.27% bare characteristics at 50W output power, and further applied NF to improve the characteristics to the extent that measurement cannot be performed.

The first stage consists of a current mirror load differential-amplifier circuit with low noise and high gain, the voltage amplifier stage consists of a pure resistance load with distortion factor priority, and the output-stage consists of a 3-stage Darlington full complimentary service SEPP circuit with sufficient current amplification.

A ± 6 power supply configuration with excellent dynamic characteristics is adopted, and two power transformers are used in a left-right independent two power supply system.

Equipped with a protection circuit that also serves as a muting to prevent shock noise.
