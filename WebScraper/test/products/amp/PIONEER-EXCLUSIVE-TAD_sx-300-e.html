  • Pioneer SX-300

  • ¥ 33,900 (released in 1973)

Commentary

A stereo receiver that embodies the charm of its components in a simple form.

The front-end of the FM tuner uses carefully selected FETs.

The equalizer amplifier uses a 2-stage direct connection NF type circuit.

Equipped with a loudness contour and a mono-stereo changeover switch.

Two auxiliary power outlets are installed.
