  • ONKYO A-911MLTD

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1994)

Commentary

A limited version model with improved speaker drive based on A-911M.
It is compatible with 4 Ω drive of the speaker.

Blue condenser developed in the Integra series is adopted.

Uses a fully discrete configuration.

Equipped with straight CD position exclusively for CD.

It uses a DuPontCORIAN insulator with both rigidity and damping, an extra-thick power cord with polarity display, and a large AEI transformer.

Equipped with 3-band tone control.

It is equipped with processor input / output and mono output for super woofer.

System control remote control is included.
