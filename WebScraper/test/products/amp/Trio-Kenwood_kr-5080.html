Commentary

This is a multi-amplifier receiver making full use of the technology built up with Suprim 1.

The KR 5080 consists of an FM/AM tuner, preamp, three channel divider, and six power amplifiers.

Equipped with 6 independent level controls on the left and right.
Two high, mid, and low frequencies are available, and the crossover frequency can be set so that the characteristics of each speaker can be extracted.

The crossover frequency can be switched to 3 levels for both low and medium range and high and high range. It can be combined with a full-range speaker 2-way and 3-way speakers.

Functions such as DIN connector, tape monitor and SCA filter are included.
