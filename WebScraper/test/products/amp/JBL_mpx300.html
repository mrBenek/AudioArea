Commentary

A power amplifier developed to bring out the performance of JBL's SR-speaker system.

Equipped with automatic recovery protection circuit and multi-speed forced cooling fan.

The input terminal uses a combination type connector that can be used for both XLR and 6.3 mm standard plugs.
It also comes with a Euro-style barrier strap that allows cables to be inserted and removed together with terminals for installation and maintenance.

A built-in high-pass filter protects the low-frequency unit by cutting low frequency signals of 30 Hz or less.

Equipped with a mode switch, it can be used as a bridge drive in addition to stereo / parallel ones.
