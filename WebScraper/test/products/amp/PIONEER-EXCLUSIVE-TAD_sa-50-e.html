Audio Database

  • Pioneer SA-50

  • ¥ 23,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

Stereo pre-main amplifier of the UA (unit amplifier) series.

The power amplifier section uses a quasi complimentary service SEPP-OTL circuit using silicon transistors.

A low-noise transistor is used in the head amplifier section to reduce distortion and improve S/N.
