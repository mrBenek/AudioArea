Commentary

A tube type preamp built by Dynaco based on a consistent basic policy.

Distortion is reduced by applying negative feedback across the entire circuit.

A special volume is used for the tone control, and when the knob is placed in the middle point, the tone control circuit is detached and becomes a flat amplifier.
Also, both Bass and Treble adjust the left and right channels independently.

With a special input circuit, it is possible to use a microphone or phono.

There was a wooden case sold separately.
