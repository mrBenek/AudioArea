Commentary

The Point Component Series compact is a power amplifier.

A 50W output is achieved by using a discrete circuit and a power transistor with a sufficient capacity in the final stage.

Equipped with a bus booth and circuit, it is possible to increase the bass range.

Power ON/OFF, volume adjustment, speaker ON/OFF, muting operation are possible by the optional remote control.
