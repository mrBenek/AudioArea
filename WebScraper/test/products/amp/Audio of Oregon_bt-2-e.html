  • Audio of Oregon BT-2

  • ¥ 178,000 (around 1978)

Commentary

A preamplifier designed for Audio of Oregon by Barry Thornton, the designer of the Quintessence PRE-1, using an all-stage pure Class A operation circuit.

It consists of three integral modules.

Equipped with a signal / output ON/OFF switch.

Power supply switch is not installed, and continuous power supply is recommended.
It also requires approximately 12 hours of warm-up to reach the best operating condition.
