Commentary

The FM front-end section uses a high-end dual-gate MOS FET and a frequency-linear four series varicon to improve various characteristics. The IF amplifier stage uses an IC and three ceramic filters to improve selectivity and capture ratio.

The AM tuner section uses a 3-row varicon and ceramic filter.

An FET is used in the first stage of the preamplifier section to eliminate distortion caused by impedance deviation, and a low-noise transistor is used in the equalizer section to improve the dynamic range.
The tone control uses an NF type that takes advantage of the linear characteristics of the B-type variable resistor.

The power amplifier section uses a SEPP-ITL-OTL-OCL direct connection circuit.

In addition to two types of dubbing jacks, a microphone jack is installed.

Comes with a preset volume and can be used independently for pre-main and main.

Equipped with a microphone jack.
