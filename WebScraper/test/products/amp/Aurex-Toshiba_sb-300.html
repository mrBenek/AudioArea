Audio Database

  • TOSHIBA SB-300

  • ¥ 35,800 (around 1973)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that uses a fully complimentary service all-stage direct connection OTL circuit.

Built-in speaker matrix 4-ch circuit.

Equipped with a microphone terminal.

The preamp section and main amplifier section can be separated.

Two types of speakers can be connected.
