  • McIntosh MA6200

  • ¥ 478,000 (released in February 1979)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed by integrating the Macintosh technology into one unit.

It is equipped with a 5-band independent program equalizer, two tape circuits capable of mutual dubbing, a pre-main separation terminal, and a continuously variable loudness compensator.

As a protective circuit, it is equipped with Power Guard System which is unique to Macintosh. Clipping distortion is suppressed automatically, and the speaker is protected from accident.
