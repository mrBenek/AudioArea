  • ¥ 398,000 (released in 1980)

Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier from Grand Ramray Inc. in England, which started under the close relationship with The M-O Valve Company which is famous as a vacuum tube manufacturer.

KT-77 of "Gold Lion" brand made by M-O Valve Company is used for the output tube.

Class AB1 push-pull configuration with ultra-linear connection and fixed bias system.
