  • LUXMAN HQ555

  • ¥ 85,000 (released in August 1968)

Commentary

Stereo receiver with built-in AM/FM tuner.

Equipped with CR type tone control.

An FET cascode circuit is used for the FM tuner.
The AM tuner uses high frequency 1-stage and intermediate frequency 1-stage amplification circuits.
