  • Audio Analogue PUCCINI SE RE

  • ¥ 248,000 (around 2003)

Commentary

An integrated amplifier with special specifications developed as a special model of Puccini RE.

The power stage has been changed to parallel push-pull configuration.
In addition, the power supply section has been enhanced to a dual monaural specification with a left and right independent toroidal transformer.
In addition, two speaker outputs allow for bi-wiring.

The features of Puccini RE are inherited as they are.
The circuit board has been renewed to minimize the signal path, and the signal switching relay has been placed near the input terminals for brush-up.

Equipped with a standby function that cuts off the speaker output at the minimum volume position.
A phono equalizer circuit corresponding to MM and MC is mounted.

The power supply is equipped with a toroidal transformer.

Equipped with a pre-out terminal, it can be developed into a bi-amplifier system.

Wireless remote control is included.
The remote control allows input selection, volume adjustment, and mute operation.
