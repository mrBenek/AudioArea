  • TRIO W-50

  • ¥ 56,300 (around the early 1960 s?)

Commentary

High-grade tri-amp developed as a super deluxe type.

The output power is 60W monaural and 30Wx2 stereo.

It is equipped with a rumble filter that eliminates low-frequency noise and a scratch filter that eliminates high-frequency noise.
In addition, it is equipped with a loudness control to compensate for the low and high sound when the sound is reduced.

Equipped with hetero filter to eliminate unpleasant whistle sound.

It is equipped with an AFC circuit, enabling stable FM broadcast reception.

Uses full DC ignition and eliminates hum.

Both magnetic and crystal pick-up jacks are available for stereo and mono.
Equipped with a dynamic or crystal microphone jack.

Equipped with a tape recording and playback terminal.

Equipped with FM stereo jacks.

Equipped with a rod antenna for FM broadcast reception.
