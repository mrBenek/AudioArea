Commentary

The control center was developed for the top-of-the-line audio system by LUX's unique technology such as duo-beta circuit.

In order to improve sound quality, we have adopted LUX's own duo-beta circuit.
In this circuit, the minimum necessary amount of NFB is applied to all bands, and a DC servo circuit is combined.
This eliminates TIM distortion by reducing the amount of NFB by keeping the gain of the amplifier low. In addition, the DC servo circuit not only cuts ultra-low frequency noise, which causes intermodulation distortion, but also uses a low-noise FET amplifier and a high-quality film capacitor in the low-pass filter circuit low-pass reproduction.

In order to realize an amplifier with low gain and good characteristics suitable for a duo-beta circuit, we have adopted a combination of a cascode differential input bootstrap circuit and a transistor dynamic resistance circuit at the input stage.
In the output stage, a four transistor SEPP circuit is used to keep the output impedance low.

In order to minimize wiring, all switches are remotely connected to the board so that the circuit on the board can be directly switched by the knob on the panel. In addition, multi-circuit switches are used in parallel to prevent noise degradation caused by contacts.
In the circuit configuration, the left and right channels are divided into blocks, and each board is separated independently to eliminate mutual interference.
Highly reliable connectors used in communications equipment and other devices are carefully selected employment to the connecting parts of the circuit boards, and gold plating is applied to the terminals on the circuit boards to increase the reliability of the contacts.

The power supply section is equipped with a high-capacity toroidal transformer and a twin monaural high-speed constant-voltage power supply.
For the transformer, a toroidal transformer with low leakage flux and high magnetic efficiency is adopted, and the core size is large enough to be used for a power amplifier of about 25Wx2, which is a common size, so that extra space is provided.

In order to prevent electromagnetic distortion, metal shielding is removed from the opposing surface of the substrate.
In addition, to prevent noise, we use double-sided boards to block the boards and use remote switches to avoid adverse effects caused by wiring. We also use specially ordered switches made of non-magnetic materials for switches.

It uses carefully selected parts with an emphasis on sound quality.
Considering that non-polar film capacitors also have polarity, we have determined the polarity and consciously applied a bias to solve this non-linearity problem. This DC bias generates a Coulomb force between the polarities of the foils inside the film capacitor, and simultaneously prevents mechanical vibration.
The volume is made of conductive plastic in consideration of durability, sound quality and usability.
In addition, the equalizer stage and flat stage, which are essential parts of the pre-amplifier, are made of tantalum nitride, which is the most excellent in performance, after confirmation by trial listening.

For the tone control, it uses the NF type Lux System with a curved point switching.

Equipped with high filter and subsonic filter.
Also, the filter circuit can be bypassed when not in use.

Equipped with socket corresponding to 8000 series booster transformer for MC type cartridge sold separately.
This transformer uses the best materials for the core material and is a toroidal transformer using silver wire and Litz wire for the winding. It has excellent high-frequency characteristics.
