  • Panasonic SU-AV100

Commentary

5-channel AV amplifier equipped with Dolby Pro Logic.

5-channel configuration with built-in power amplifiers of 100W each for front L/R and center, and 50W each for surround L/R.
By making the front three channels have the same output, Dolby Pro Logic's ideal surround playback conditions are satisfied and a more realistic sound field is realized.
In addition, the center amplifier can be switched between Dolby and Subwoofer.

In order to maximize the sound effects of software recorded in Dolby Surround, it is equipped with Dolby Pro-Logic circuit.
The SU-AV100 is designed to perform Dolby surround sound playback in three modes. You can choose from two options : Normal, which is low cut in the center on the premise of using a wide and small speaker that outputs the three front channels at the same level, and Phantom, which plays back by dividing the center output into L and R when there is no center speaker.
In addition, you can create four surround sound fields : simulated, hall, jazz club, and live concert just by selecting from the display.

It is equipped with a bus synthesizer that enables powerful deep bass reproduction.
With this function, you can sample a signal of 160 Hz or less from the input audio signal, create a 1/2 frequency signal based on it, and add it to the original signal at the desired level.

The VCR1 has an independent recording selector and video monitor output for editing and recording.

The S video input / output terminal is installed in all video systems.

It is equipped with a total of 13 input / output systems, AV8 system and audio Line 5 system.
In addition to the input / output of the 13 systems, the video and audio adapter input / output terminals are mounted.

Equipped with a subwoofer drive terminal.

Equipped with a BGV function capable of combining video and audio independently.

It is equipped with a display with dimmer function which can adjust the brightness according to preference.

Equipped with a monitor selector that enables recording monitoring.

Learning remote controller equipped with macro and direct function is included.
This remote control can be programmed with up to eight keys per key, and is equipped with a learning function that allows you to operate other devices with one remote control.
