Commentary

Stereo pre-main amplifier with audio IC.

The amplifier section uses a direct-coupling type differential-amplified 2 power supply and quasi complimentary service circuit.

The equalizer amplifier uses a low-noise IC developed specifically for audio applications.

Equipped with a microphone mixing mechanism with built-in PNP-NPN2-stage direct connection amplifier for microphone mixing.

Equipped with slide-type tone control.
