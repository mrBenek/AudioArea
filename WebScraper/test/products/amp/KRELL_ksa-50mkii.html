Commentary

Stereo power amplifier with pure A-class operation.

The circuit configuration employs a pure complimentary service circuit of perfect symmetry with carefully selected elements, and NFB is suppressed to 15 dB with an emphasis on dynamic characteristics.
The Power Block eliminates heat generation by combining a heat sink and an extremely quiet cooling fan, and thermal distortion is suppressed by strictly controlling the temperature of the Power Block to 75 to 80 degrees Celsius at all times.

Both sides of the power supply section are completely independent, and each channel is equipped with a 80,000 μ F condenser and a 800W class large toroidal transformer. This structure is resistant to load fluctuations during music playback and enables stable and comfortable playback even when the speaker impedance drops to nearly 1 Ω.

The internal construction is a fully twin-monaural configuration with sufficient space between each block to prevent mutual interference due to signal jumps and to improve thermal stability.

Equipped with a switch for BTL connection, it can also be used as a high-power monaural power amplifier with BTL connection.
