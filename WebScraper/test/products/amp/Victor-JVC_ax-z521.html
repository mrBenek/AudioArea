Commentary

This pre-main amplifier is equipped with a video terminal and D/A converter, and is also compatible with AV.

The D/A converter is equipped with a newly developed full-time 18-bit noise-shaping DAC.
From the digital code computed by the digital filter, the requantization noise that adversely affects the audio band is largely shifted out of the audible band by the noise shaper, and D/A converted by the full-time 18-bit DAC.
As a result, it has excellent linearity and in principle does not generate 0 cross distortion, thus improving the ability to reproduce music at a minute level.
It is also designed to automatically select three types of sampling frequency.

In order to eliminate the adverse effect of digital noise on the analog section, the transformer winding is completely separated into digital and analog sections and is used as a dedicated digital power supply circuit.
In addition, in order to prevent adverse effects on sound quality caused by vibration of the power transformer, the power transformer is mounted on a plate with a W dimple structure with excellent vibration damping characteristics to suppress resonance and vibration.

Dynamic Super A circuit is used in the power amplifier section to obtain stable power against the impedance fluctuation and back electromotive force of the speaker.

Video and audio inputs are installed in the Route 3. Two of these systems are equipped with S-video input terminals. This enables dubbing of S-video and cross-playing of sound and video using the sound selector.

In order to prevent degradation of sound quality due to transmission loss, it is equipped with a DAC direct function that can directly connect the D/A converted signal to the power amplifier.
In addition, it is equipped with a CD direct switch which can shorten the analog signal line of CD and reduce the contact point.

In order to prevent adverse effects due to digital noise, the digital clock operation is automatically stopped at analog input.

It has a Compulink terminal that can be operated in conjunction with VICTOR/JVC CD players and cassette decks.

Two types of speaker terminals are installed for biwiring.

The volume uses a 2-point support system with excellent SN ratio.

A phono equalizer amplifier is installed for MM cartridges.

Wireless remote control is included.
