Audio Database

  • Audio Research SP-14

  • ¥ 650,000 (around 1990)

Commentary

A control amplifier corresponding to the upper model of SP-9mkII.

The flat amplifier which used to be a vacuum tube in SP-9mkII is changed to FET in SP-14.

In addition to the SP-9sp-9mkII, functions such as a Rec out selector, a 6-dB step 5-position attenuator, mutual copying of two types of tapes, and an auxiliary circuit bypass switch are provided.

The black panel and knob were provided as optional parts.
