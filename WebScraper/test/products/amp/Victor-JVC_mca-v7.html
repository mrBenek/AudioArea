Commentary

A 4 ch pre-main amplifier equipped with Sound Field Composer.

Equipped with a Sound Field Composer circuit that allows you to enjoy a 4-channel atmosphere with 2-channel source.
This circuit is a device that reproduces a pseudo 4-channel sound. Only the reverberation component contained in the 2-channel source is output from another speaker to reproduce the realistic sensation.

The MCA-V7 uses a BTL switching 2-4-channel complimentary service circuit. Four pairs of SEPP circuits drive each speaker for 4-channel playback, while BTL connection provides high power for 2-channel playback.

A quasi complimentary service ITL / OTL circuit is used for the circuit structure.
A low-noise silicon transistor is also used to reduce noise.

The equalizer circuit consists of two stages. The PNP transistor is used at the first stage to suppress transistor noise.

Equipped with two 4-channel inputs and 4-channel recording and playback terminals.

Equipped with 4 ch pre-out / main-in terminals, pre-amplifier and main amplifier can be used independently.
