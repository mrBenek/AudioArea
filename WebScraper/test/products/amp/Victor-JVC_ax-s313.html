Commentary

Pre-main amplifier equipped with technologies such as Z chassis and advanced super A.

Uses Z chassis that completely eliminates unnecessary vibration from the amplifier.
Ribs for increasing chassis rigidity are arranged on the entire surface, and the side of the chassis base is L-shaped, and the strength is increased by raising up to the top plate.
In addition, we have adopted a stay bracket that reduces distortion by connecting the front and rear of the chassis, and we have also incorporated other ideas such as attaching a hatch directly to the chassis.

In order to realize the sound of class A operation efficiently, it is equipped with Advanced Super A which is further improved from Super A of variable bias system which changes drive current according to the magnitude of input signal.
In the past, excess current flowed in the driver current that drives the most numerous power transistors. By eliminating this excess current, we have achieved amplification with lower distortion and better linearity.

The shape of the heat sink has been improved to take vibration into consideration, and the noise emitted from the transistor is prevented by a shield.

The power supply is equipped with an EI transformer with a weight of 3.1 kg.
In addition, the current circuits of the voltage amplifier stage and the current amplifier stage are independent to reduce mutual interference.
