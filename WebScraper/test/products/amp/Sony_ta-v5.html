Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with slim design and seat key.

A self-illuminated sheet key has been adopted for the function switch and the tape monitor switch to improve operability.

Equipped with a loudness and base boost switch.

A driver IC ensures high power while maintaining a thin design.

Graphic equalizer Equipped with an illuminated indicator that indicates when in use.

A semiconductor switch with low contact resistance is used for the function and tape monitor switch.

Weight reduction is achieved by adoption of high-efficiency power supply part.
