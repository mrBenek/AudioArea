Commentary

A 7 ch power amplifier developed in consideration of combination with AVC-3000DSP.

The AVM-3000 uses 5-block independent all-discrete configuration of L, C, R, F (front effect) and S (surround), and is designed with an orthodox sense of stability in mind.

Four Pc150W power transistors are used for each main L, R, and C.
In addition, mutual interference is suppressed by separating it from the transformer winding of the power supply and feeding power to each block amplifier independently.

Thick aluminum heat sink and high rigidity chassis are adopted.

A dedicated interface is provided for the pair link with the AVC 3000 DSP.
In this connection, the audio signal of each channel is balanced by positive and negative polarity. Especially, the L, C, and R channels are balanced symmetrically with respect to the ground up to the power amplifier, so the configuration is advantageous in sound quality.

Each of the five amplifier blocks has an input selector switch so that they can be used independently.
The amplifier block side is equipped with RCA pin jack and cannon terminal, and also equipped with phase switching and level volume.
