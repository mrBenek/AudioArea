  • TRIO AF-22

  • ¥ 18,000 (around 1960?)

Commentary

Tri-amp supporting 3 bands.

It can be used for FM, Nakanami, and shortwave broadcasting. It also has an analog input and can play LP.

The FM tuner section is equipped with high-frequency amplification to achieve high sensitivity and enable reception with less noise even in areas with weak radio waves.

Equipped with tone control.
