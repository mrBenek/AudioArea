Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier with low distortion and low noise by using transistors based on Hitachi's semiconductor technology.

Uses carefully selected Hi-Fi dedicated silicon transistors.

It is equipped with two tape monitors and two speaker outputs.

Microphone mixing is possible.
