Commentary

AV amplifier with built-in Dolby Pro Logic Surround and 5 ch amplifier.

In addition to the Dolby Pro Logic with Auto Input Balance, it is equipped with three surround modes : Hall, Matrix and Simulate.

Equipped with 5 ch power amplifier of front L/R, center and rear L/R.

For input / output, two video systems are installed with an input Line 5, and one audio system is installed with an input Line 5.
The video system is equipped with an S video input / output terminal.

The Input Selector is a simple up / down method that takes into account the operation interval of the video equipment. It also uses a large function display to confirm the selected input source and surround mode.

It is equipped with a mix mode that can output any combination of video and audio sources.

Two pre-out terminals out terminals for center output.

System remote control is included.

Center output is available in Dolby mode.

There are 2 color variations of titanium gray and black.
