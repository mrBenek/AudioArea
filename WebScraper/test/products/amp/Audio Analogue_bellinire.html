  • Audio Analogue Bellini RE

  • ¥ 198,000 (around 2003)

Commentary

A preamplifier that is ideal for combination with power amplifiers such as Donizetti and Corelli.

With the addition of the remote control function, the circuit board of RE has been completely renewed and the signal path has been minimized.
The signal circuit has a DC configuration without using a coupling capacitor.

It is equipped with a phono equalizer amplifier for both MM and MC.

Equipped with an independent recording selector.

Two output terminals are provided for bi-amplifier systems, etc.

A 10 mm thick silver alloy aluminum front panel is used for the front panel, and the knob is machined to finish.

Wireless remote control is included.
The remote control allows input selection, volume adjustment, and mute operation.
