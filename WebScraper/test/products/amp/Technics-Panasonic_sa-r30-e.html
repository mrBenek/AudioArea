Commentary

An air check amplifier that integrates an FM/AM tuner, cassette deck and pre-main amplifier.

The deck mechanism uses an electronically controlled DC motor to drive the capstan, a DC motor to drive the reel, and an IC logic control.

A unique SP (Solid Permalloy) head is used for the recording / reproducing head.

Metal tape is supported and the tape selector is equipped with 3 positions.

It has built-in Dolby NR circuit.

Equipped with an automatic music selection mechanism (TPS) that is convenient for starting a song, you can fast forward / rewind a song with one operation.

Equipped with rec mute, recording / playback timer standby, and 2-color LED level meter.

The FM front-end section of the tuner uses a junction FET and a high-precision 3-row varicon in the RF stage, and two ceramic filters in the IF stage to increase selectivity.

A PLL-IC is used in the MPX demodulation circuit to improve reliability against temperature and humidity changes.

Equipped with a 5-point 2-LED signal strength meter that shows the electric field strength.

The main amplifier section has a simple circuit configuration with two high-integration ICs and one transistor.
The input stage IC is a differential amplifier with a current mirror load capable of selecting an operating current with low distortion, and is connected to the pre-drive transistor of the next stage by an emitter follower.
Moreover, the power ICs of the complimentary service stage consist of a two stage Darlington semi-output circuit.

The phono equalizer uses a first stage differential amplifier current mirror load.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit.

Equipped with tone control and Rec out jacks.
