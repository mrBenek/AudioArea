  • VICTOR/JVC A-E5

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1981)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier incorporating Victor's amplifier technology such as Super A Circuit.

Switching distortion and TIM distortion are eliminated by super A circuit.

The power supply section is equipped with a toroidal transformer and a large-capacity capacitor.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit and two types of tape terminals.
