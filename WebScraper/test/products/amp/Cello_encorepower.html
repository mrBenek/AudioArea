  • Cello Encore Power Amplifier

  • ¥ 1,198,000 (around September 1989)

Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier that condenses the design method and sound flavor of the high-end performance into a compact body.

The power supply section uses a choke input method, providing a stable supply from small current to large current to obtain excellent sound quality.

Class AB is used for the output stage.

Balance input by Fisher terminal is used for input terminal.
The Fisher connector is also used for unbalanced inputs.
