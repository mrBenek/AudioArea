  • Aurex SB-320

  • ¥ 39,800 (around 1976)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier that uses a discrete power amplifier.

The circuit system of the main amplifier section uses a pure complimentary service OCL circuit directly connected to all stages. The power transistor is driven by a single push-pull operation, so that there is room for maximum output.

The tone control amplifier section is CR type and consists of two stages directly connected.

The power supply section uses a one pack capacitor with two power supplies of 10,000 μ Fx2.

The volume control uses a volume with a 41-point mechanical click.

Built-in audio muting circuit.

It has a duplicate switch that can copy between two decks.

Equipped with a panel handle.
