Commentary

This pre-main amplifier pursues operability while pursuing pure sound quality by adopting a large toroidal transformer and a new structure SLDC.

A toroidal transformer is used for the power supply section.
Unlike conventional transformers, toroidal transformers consist of a toroidal ring core with a coil wound around it, and have excellent characteristics such as stable supply of current, leakage flux, and vibration prevention.

A new structure S. L. D. C. (Signal Level Divided Construction) is adopted in order to suppress the mutual interference of each circuit.
By dividing the signal handling into low-level blocks, high-level blocks, and microcomputer blocks that handle digital signals, electrical interference between each block is suppressed.

The low-impedance volume reduces the impedance of the volume control circuit, preventing the S/N ratio and frequency characteristics from deteriorating at general listening levels.

It is equipped with an equalizer amplifier that can be used with either MC or MM cartridge by switching operation.

It is equipped with a source direct switch and can bypass control circuits such as Bass, Treble, Balance and Loudness by turning it on.

The linked side of the AC outlet is linked to the remote power on/off.

It comes with a system remote control that supports DENON CD players, cassette decks and tuners.
In addition, the muting switch on the remote control body can be used to cancel the sound from the speaker.
