  • Technics SA-C06

  • ¥ 97,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

FM/AM stereo cassette tuner amplifier with tuner, deck and amplifier condensed into LP jacket size.

The tuner section can receive 1 to 3 channels of TV in addition to FM/AM.

The amplifier has a built-in microphone mixing circuit with BBD electronic echo.

The cassette deck is an IC-logic metal deck with an electronically controlled DC motor.
Equipped with TPS (Automatic Music Selection Mechanism).
