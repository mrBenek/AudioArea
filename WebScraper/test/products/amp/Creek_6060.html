Commentary

It is an integrated amplifier that eliminates excessive decoration and unnecessary functions and thoroughly implements Clique's company philosophy.

For the circuit configuration, the drive stage is class A operation, and the output stage is composed of a bipolar output element with a grounded emitter Darlington connection.
In addition, NFB is eliminated to improve sound quality.

CAD system is used for circuit design.

Monaural bridge connection is possible.

The power supply uses a toroidal transformer and a high-grade capacitor.

In addition to the glass-epoxy double-sided printed circuit board, we also use parts that pursue sound quality, such as a pre-amplifier power supply with a high-precision voltage regulator and a power supply with a built-in filter.
