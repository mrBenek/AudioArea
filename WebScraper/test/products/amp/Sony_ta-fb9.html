Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with linear phase circuit.

The power amplifier section is equipped with a newly developed linear phase circuit for pre-main amplifier, and has acquired excellent high-frequency and phase characteristics.
Power MOS-FET with excellent acoustic characteristics is adopted in the final stage of the amplification stage.

The power supply section is equipped with a large torus and toroidal transformer.
The advanced s. T. D. power supply system, which supplies power to the voltage amplifier stage and power amplifier stage independently from the transformer winding stage, has been adopted to constitute a powerful power supply circuit.

Equipped with phono input compatible with MM cartridge.

Equipped with a source direct switch.

It is equipped with a loudness switch.

Wireless remote control is included.
