  • SANSUI MX-10

  • ¥ 28,100 (around 1960 s)

Commentary

A professional mixer amplifier that can mix inputs of 6 circuits simultaneously.

You can mix three microphones, a player, a tape, or other inputs simultaneously.

DC ignition is adopted for the head amplifier part to prevent hum and noise.
In addition, a cathode follower system is used for the output circuit to prevent the inclusion of hum due to the extension of the cord.

Equipped with high-cut filter and low-cut filter.

Equipped with a VU meter.

Since the specified matching transformer (A-603, 50k Ω : 600k Ω) with MT9P type pin can be attached and detached freely, 2 types of microphones of high (50k Ω) and low (600 Ω) impedance types can be used.
