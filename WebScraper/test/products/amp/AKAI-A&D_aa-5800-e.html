  • AKAI AA-5800

  • ¥ 79,800 (around 1973?)

Commentary

All stages are directly connected ITL-OTL-OCL system circuit is adopted for the power amplifier section, and PNP-NPN-NPN3-stage directly connected circuit is adopted for the equalizer of the preamplifier section.
In addition, a two stage IC directly connected circuit is used for the microphone amplifier.

Transistor and speaker protection circuit are mounted.

Four tape decks can be connected at the same time, a tape recording selector switch is installed, and mixing by microphone level control is also possible.
