  • CORAL A-7

  • ¥ 33,800 (around 1965)

Commentary

A solid-state pre-main amplifier using transistors.

The circuit configuration is mainly based on the SEPP / OTL circuit method of 2SB425.

The power transistor is protected by a double protection circuit consisting of a positive star (heat-sensitive element) and a quick disconnect DC / FUSE.

Features such as tone control, scratch filter, rumble filter, and loudness control are included.
