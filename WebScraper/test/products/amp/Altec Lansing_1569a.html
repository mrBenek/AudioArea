Commentary

This tube type monaural power amplifier is designed with an emphasis on stability for professional use.

EL-34 (6CA7) is used for the output tube in parallel push-pull operation.

A peerless output transformer with 3rd winding for NF is adopted, and stability against load fluctuation is obtained.

Can be mounted in standard 19-inch racks
