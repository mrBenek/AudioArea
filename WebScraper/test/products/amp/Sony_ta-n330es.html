  • SONY TA-N330ES

  • ¥ 44,800 (released in 1989)

Commentary

ES series stereo power amplifier that uses G chassis.

Uses G chassis, which was born from the design concept of no resonance and no vibration.

The power supply section is equipped with a large toroidal transformer.
The S. T. D. power supply system is adopted in which the rectifier circuits for the A-class stage and the power stage are separated and independent.

We apply velvet-like electrostatic hair transplantation to the vibration proof platform to fix the power capacitor.

Equipped with a volume and two input selectors.

It can also be used as a monaural amplifier by switching the switch.
