  • SONY TA-F3

  • ¥ 48,000 (around 1976)

Commentary

While the circuit technology of the amplifier stage is becoming more complete, the power supply is the key to the improvement of sound quality.

A new FET buffer power supply is adopted to improve crosstalk between channels.

Pure complimentary service all-stage direct-coupled circuit using dual transistors with uniform characteristics for differential amplifier stage is adopted.

Equipped with a power meter that can directly read from 0.01W to 100W.

It is equipped with self-defeat tone control and muting switch that can obtain flat frequency characteristics in the center position.
