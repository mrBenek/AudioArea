Commentary

FM/AM receiver with a 4-channel decoder.

This 4-channel amplifier can play four channels of regular / SQ matrix, four channels of discrete tape, and even CD-4 discs by adding a demodulator.
You can also enjoy Matrix 4 channel playback through FM broadcasting.

The FM tuner uses an FET in the front end and an IC and ceramic filter in the IF section.
The dial has a linear scale and is a 2-meter system with a signal meter and a center tuning meter so that precise tuning points can be caught.
It is also equipped with a muting switch to cut out unpleasant inter-station noise.

Equipped with tone control, you can control the sound quality of front and rear separately.
Equipped with front left and right, rear left and right, front and rear balance control, it can be adjusted according to the structure of the room and listening position.

The preamplifier section and power amplifier section can be used separately.
