  • CLASSE CAV-500

  • ¥ 1,750,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

A 5-channel power amplifier that pursues surround playback with pure audio quality.

A large sealed toroidal transformer is used for the power supply part, and it is a high voltage / high current design.

All channels are equipped with balanced and unbalanced terminals.
