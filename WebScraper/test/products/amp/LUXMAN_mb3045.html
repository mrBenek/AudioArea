Commentary

Tube type monaural power amplifier using 8045G.

8045G of LAX brand is used for the output tube, AB160W output is obtained by the class push-pull operation.

The transformer is equipped with GX100-3.6 developed for 8045G. This transformer uses a special winding method called Quadra filer.
