Commentary

AV amplifier with space-saving and slim design.

It is equipped with a decoder compatible with Dolby Digital and DTS which are adopted as the standard audio system of DVD video.
It is also compatible with MPEG-2 AAC, which realizes 5.1 channel surround sound digital BS broadcasting.

Equipped with room setting function.
This function allows you to complete speaker settings that are difficult for beginners by simply pressing the dedicated button on the remote control. You can easily set the sound field suitable for speakers by simply selecting S : 6 tatami mats, M : 8 tatami mats, or L : 12 tatami mats.

It is equipped with an input Line 5, one output system, and one video output system for TV for AV.
One of the input terminals is installed on the front panel and has optical digital terminals for connection with games and portable DVD player.

As surround mode, it is equipped with advanced theater mode, 5-D theater mode and Midnight listening mode.
In addition, it is equipped with TV surround mode, which converts monaural and stereo TV sound into 5.1 channel surround sound, game mode for games, virtual mode using SRS TruSurround technology, and headphone surround mode.

It is equipped with manner mode.
In this mode, the volume of the serif band remains the same, suppressing sudden explosions in action movies, etc. and preventing sound from leaking into the next room.

An AV preset remote controller is included.
