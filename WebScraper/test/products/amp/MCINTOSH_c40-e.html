Commentary

Audio control center with 8 unbalanced inputs and processor loops including Phono, main outputs of Route 3, and balanced input / output jacks.

All signal switches use a read relay system with digital logic control.
Sealed in glass tubes, these relays are highly reliable for long periods of time because they have little distortion and are insulated from the atmosphere.

The 5-band equalizer can be freely boosted or cut by up to ± 12 dB at 30 Hz, 150 Hz, 500 Hz, 1.5 kHz and 10 kHz.
At the center click position, the entire equalizer circuit is bypassed so that accurate flat characteristics can be obtained.
The compander system has expansion and compression modes, and you can optionally compensate for the dynamic range of the source.
It also has an active loudness control that can be adjusted independently of the master volume.

The equalizer for analog disks is powerful enough to match the output performance of a mini power amplifier of 100 mW or higher, and the low-impedance RIAA element uses a precision resistor of 1% equalization deviation, distortion and noise.

Built-in monitor power amplifier with power guard.
In addition to being able to monitor switching between the listening system and REC system, it is also equipped with an external input terminal so that it can be used for surround amplifier, etc.
Power Guard is a Macintosh unique protection circuit that constantly compares the input and output waveforms and automatically lowers the input level when a difference (distortion) is detected.

With the SCR-3 relay unit (sold separately), you can switch between two speakers and control the power supply of the power amplifier using the front panel switch.

A 7-position full mode selector for mono and bilingual sources.

Equipped with a processor switch on the front panel that enables ON/OFF signals for external equalizers, surround processors, etc.

Front panel is glass with gold / teal blue lighting.
