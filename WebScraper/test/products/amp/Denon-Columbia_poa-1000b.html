Commentary

Tube bulb type stereo power amplifier.

The output stage combines a newly developed output transformer with an extremely wide transmission band and a beam 5-pole tube 6GB8 to stabilize the B voltage of the second power tube grid and driver stage.

Equipped with a large level meter.

Equipped with a DC balance check meter.
