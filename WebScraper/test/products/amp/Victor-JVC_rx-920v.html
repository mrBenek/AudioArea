Commentary

An AV receiver that supports Dolby Digital (AC-3) 5.1 ch.

It supports the 5.1 channel analog outputs of DVD player or Dolby Digital (AC-3) decoders.
By connecting to the DVD multi-channel input jack of the RX 920 v, you can enjoy a 5.1 channel input source.

The power amplifier section has built-in discrete power amplifiers for front L/R, rear L/R, and center 5 ch.
The stage of the main channel is equipped with Advanced Super A, an audio technology unique to JVC, which enables speaker drive and high-quality and high-density playback at a minute level.

Equipped with DAP mode, sound fields of famous concert halls and live houses can be created and reproduced by digital processing.

Equipped with SEA mode, it can be adjusted according to the software and preference such as emphasizing high, middle and low frequencies and enhancing the sense of presence.

Equipped with a one touch operation function that can store the optimum sound setting for each software.
You can store the optimal settings for seven items, including volume level, speaker balance, DAP mode, and SEA mode.

Dolby Pro Logic Surround is supported.
Digital processing allows faithful reproduction while maintaining high sound quality.

Equipped with a wideband FM/AM tuner.
40 ch random preset function allows one touch selection of music.

Equipped with AV Compute function, it can be combined with VICTOR/JVC TV, video and audio equipment for one touch collaborative play.

It is equipped with an on-screen function so that you can check the playback sound field and perform operations such as surround sound settings while watching the screen.

Multi-brand remote control is included.
With this remote control, you can control the power and volume of not only JVC but also TVs and videos of 32 companies in Japan and overseas.
