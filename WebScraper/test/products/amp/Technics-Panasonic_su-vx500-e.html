Commentary

Class AA integrated amplifier.

Panasonic / Technics Class AA system has been adopted.
In this circuit, voltage amplification and current drive, which were conventionally performed by a single amplifier, are each performed by a dedicated amplifier, so that phase shift of current / voltage due to fluctuation of signal level and load impedance is suppressed, and stable transmission and amplification are maintained.

A high-purity OFC winding is used for the power transformer, and an X-Pro twin capacitor is installed in the electrolytic capacitor to suppress mechanical vibration and magnetic radiation.

Equipped with Power Amp Direct, signal degradation can be minimized by shortcutting the signal path such as tone control.
