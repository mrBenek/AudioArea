Commentary

This is a high-end pre-main amplifier with Sony's audio technology and transistor technology without any compromises to improve performance.

The power amplifier section is directly coupled except for the coupling capacitor of each stage from the conventional OTL amplifier, and a direct coupling method is adopted in which the speaker is directly driven.
As a result, power, distortion characteristics, damping factor, etc. do not deteriorate even in the ultra low frequency range, and excellent characteristics are realized.

The preamp section uses a differential amplifier (differential-amplifier) that uses an FET at the first stage of each input, which gives good results in terms of characteristics and sound quality.
In addition, the phono amplifier and microphone amplifier are each independent amplifier, and the equalizer is switched to obtain ideal characteristics.

Uses a quick access lever that allows one touch selection of the three frequently used inputs.

Equipped with 2 dB step tone control.
It is a variable turnover type in which the turnover frequency changes depending on the compensation amount.
In addition, a cancel switch is installed that allows the tone control circuit to be skipped regardless of the position of the knob to obtain a flat characteristic.

Equipped with 12dB/oct. high and low filters.

It is equipped with a pre / main connection / disconnection switch that can be used independently for the pre-amplifier and main amplifier.

Equipped with Check-L and Check-R switches for easy checking of each channel.

The tuner and AUX1 are equipped with a volume for adjusting the input level so that you can adjust the level of the photo and.

The microphone and AUX2 are connected to the front panel with jacks for easy connection.

Equipped with a speaker selector switch that can be used for 2 pairs of speaker systems.

It is equipped with a protection circuit to protect the speaker even in abnormal operation.
