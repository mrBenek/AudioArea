  • Marantz PM-62

  • ¥ 65,000 (released in 1993)

Commentary

This is a stereo pre-main amplifier with wireless remote control using the included remote control.

The power block uses an independent heat sink on the left and right sides with little crosstalk.

It is equipped with a large power transformer and a noise killer circuit to cut off current noise.

Speaker terminal for banana plug is equipped.

Available in 2 color variations, gold and black.
