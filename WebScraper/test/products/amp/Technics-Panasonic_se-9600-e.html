  • Technics SE-9600

  • ¥ 225,000 (around 1975)

Commentary

A stereo power amplifier that employs a constant voltage power supply drive for all circuits including the output stage.

Equipped with 3 types of protection circuits : power supply, current, and temperature.

Equipped with an output impedance 4-stage switching switch, an appropriate damping factor can be set according to the speaker.

Equipped with a peak output direct reading power meter equipped with 3-step range switching.

A radiator fin with a label is also used to inform the temperature change.
