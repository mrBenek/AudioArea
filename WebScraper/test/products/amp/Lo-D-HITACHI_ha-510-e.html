  • Lo-D HA-510

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier with low distortion and high output.

The power amplifier section uses carefully selected Hi-Fi pair transistors and is made of emitter-grounded inverted Darlington pure complimentary service.
In addition, by adopting a stable differential amplification constant current drive system in the driver stage, crossover distortion at the time of very small output is suppressed and high sound quality is achieved.

The equalizer stage of the preamp section has a 3-stage configuration consisting of first stage differential input, FET and bipolar transistor. By driving this circuit with ± 2 power supply, a dynamic margin of 280 mv is obtained.
A high S/N ratio has also been achieved by using carefully selected Hitachi LTP transistors.
The flat amplifier section is a 2-stage direct connection type by the combination of FET-PNP, and low distortion and stability are achieved by applying NFB simultaneously with AC and DC.
By driving the tone control circuit with low impedance, ideal tone control without undulation of frequency characteristics is realized.

The volume uses a 22-contact switch type attenuator.
An FET with high input impedance is used in the first stage of the control amplifier, and it is combined with a switch-type attenuator to achieve accurate level attenuation. Moreover, by using an FET, the change of NF due to the variation of signal source impedance is small, and there is no degradation of SN ratio due to volume position.

The level of the speaker jack B can be adjusted up to -15dB.
This makes it possible to adjust the level of the speakers at the time of comparative listening.

The Phono2 terminal is equipped with an input sensitivity selector switch that can be used according to the output of the cartridge.

The protective circuit contains Lordi's proprietary electronic protective circuit.

Built-in features such as tone defeat, low filter, mode switch, loudness and microphone jack.

The wood case was sold separately as an option.
