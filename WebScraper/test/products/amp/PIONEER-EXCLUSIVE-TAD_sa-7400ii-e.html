  • Pioneer SA-7400II

  • ¥ 34,800 (around 1979)

Commentary

Pre-main amplifier with independent power meter on left and right.

The large power meter can be read directly from 0.01W to maximum output without switching by the logarithmic compression circuit.

Uses a push-switch type speaker selector.
