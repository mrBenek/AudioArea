  • TOSHIBA SA-400C

  • ¥ 49,000 (around 1973)

Commentary

A two channel stereo receiver that employs an OCL circuit using the IC Power Pack TH-9015P.

The preamp section and main amplifier section can be separated.

You can switch between two speakers.
