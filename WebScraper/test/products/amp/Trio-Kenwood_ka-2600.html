  • TRIO KA-2600

  • ¥ 37,500 (around 1969)

Commentary

A pre-main amplifier developed by inheriting the technology of KA-6000 and KA-4000.

An all-silicon transistor configuration is adopted, resulting in a low distortion factor design.

Equipped with tone control, both Bass and Treble can be changed by ± 10 dB.
It also has a cancellation circuit to eliminate waviness when the tone control is set to the "0" position.

Equipped with a muting circuit.

A preamplifier and a power amplifier can be used separately.
