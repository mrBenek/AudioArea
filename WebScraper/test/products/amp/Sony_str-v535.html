Commentary

An AV receiver equipped with digital cinema sand technology.

Equipped with digital cinema sound, it is equipped with cinema studio A/B/C mode which adds the reverberation of editing theater, virtual multi-dimensional mode and multisemi multi-dimensional mode.
It is also equipped with sound modes such as hall, jazz club and game.

Dolby Digital and DTS decoding is supported.
A 24-bit DSP is used to decode these signals.

The power amplifier section uses a dynamic five amplifier system in which the same power transistor is used for all five channel amplifiers.

Equipped with FM stereo / AM tuner.
In addition to direct tuning, in which the frequency of the broadcasting station is entered using the numeric buttons on the remote control, automatic reception, which searches for a receivable station, and preset tuning, in which up to 30 FM/AM stations can be stored at random, are installed in the tuner section.

It is equipped with a sound field link function. When you select a program source, the sound field that was previously selected in that program source is automatically set. You can also use the tuner to store sound fields for each registered station.

The sound field can be fine-tuned to suit your preferences, including the effect level.

Equipped with tone effect buttons, you can turn on / off tone effects adjusted with cursor buttons or jog dial with one touch operation.

Equipped with base boost button, muting button and dimmer button.

Equipped with a sleep timer function.

It comes with a wireless remote control that preset the remote control mode of AV equipment of major manufacturers such as Sony.
