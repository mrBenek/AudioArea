Commentary

A compact monaural power amplifier that is easy to use not only for 2-channel playback but also for multi-channel playback.

The power supply section is equipped with a large 350 VA toroidal transformer and super Ultra-high-speed, massive capacities filter condenser.

It uses 2-layer fiber glass printed circuit board and wide-band transistor.

Safety is enhanced by incorporating the latest microchip management system into the protection circuit.
It also has an automatic standby function based on input signal detection.
