Audio Database

  • CLASSE CAV-75

  • ¥ 500,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

A 6-channel power amplifier that can adjust the channel output according to the speaker configuration.

In addition to straight 6 ch and 5.1 ch surround, the output can also be set to 4 ch + 1 ch, 2 ch + 2 ch or 3 ch by bridge connection.

The input jacks have an unbalanced Route 6.
