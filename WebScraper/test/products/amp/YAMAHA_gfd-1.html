Commentary

A dedicated drive amplifier designed and tuned only to make the ground floor speaker GF-1 sound completely.

With a 6-position line input and a 23-position Attenuator with a rotary switch, you can create a simple system by simply adding player equipment.

The GFD-1 GFD-1 adopts a power amplifier configuration to realize high voltage and low impedance transmission.
This can also be used as a pre-main amplifier.
