Commentary

This is an integrated amplifier that eliminates ground line noise and pursues higher quality playback.

In conventional signal transmission, the noise component on the ground line is amplified as it is, but by installing a differential amplification transmission circuit, the differential signal transmission line is set separately from the ground line, and pure reproduction without noise is realized.

The variable bias type output circuit Super A originally developed by JVC is improved, and the Advanced Super A system is installed, which eliminates crossover distortion, switching distortion, and nonlinear distortion of the output transistor.

We consider the power supply circuit as part of the signal path and focus on the mechanism of the transformer. We have adopted a core bobbin transformer that eliminates the internal space of the transformer.
As a result, we succeeded in eliminating the air gap that vibrates and adversely affects the signal system.

In the power amplifier section, all stages from input to output are separated into left and right, and mutual interference is suppressed.

It is equipped with a CD differential input terminal that eliminates digital noise and power supply noise flowing in the cable between the CD player and amplifier.
This eliminates noise back to the output line of the CD player.

Equipped with a phono equalizer amplifier for MM/MC.

All source input / output terminals are gold-plated in order to suppress sound quality degradation caused by contacts, and speaker terminals compatible with banana plug for linear connection of speaker cord are used.
