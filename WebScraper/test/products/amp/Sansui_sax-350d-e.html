  • SANSUI SAX-350D

  • ¥ 54,900 (around 1971)

Commentary

This stereo receiver was developed for the full-scale stereo era and for enjoying Hi-Fi with ease.

The FM front-end uses an FET and a frequency linear 3-row variable capacitor to improve stability.
The 4-stage IF amplifier and 3-stage limiter circuit provide sufficient limiter effect.

The output circuit uses the quasi complimentary service SEPPP OTL system.

A low-noise silicon transistor is used in the preamplifier section.

Equipped with two FM antenna input terminals of 300 Ω and 75 Ω, and an FM input selector switch.

The FM tuner section is equipped with a new type noise canceller.

Equipped with a tape monitor, loudness control, headphone jack, FM stereo indicator, MPX noise canceler, AM ferrite bar antenna, tuning meter, etc.
