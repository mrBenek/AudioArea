  • Pioneer SMT-804

  • ¥ 57,500 (around 1965)

Commentary

Stereo pre-main amplifier with all-transistor configuration.

A SEPP-OTL circuit is used for the circuit system, and a mesa silicon transistor is used for the output stage.

It is equipped with functions such as tone control, low / high filter and loudness control.

An electronic protective circuit using a relay is used for the protective circuit.

The power supply employs a bridge rectification system using a silicon diode.
