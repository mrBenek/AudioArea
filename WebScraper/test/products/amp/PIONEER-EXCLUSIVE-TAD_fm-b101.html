  • Pioneer FM-B101

  • ¥ 19,900 (around the early 1960 s)

Commentary

A general monophonic amplifier for AM/FM/SW broadcasting.

The FM Tuner section consists of Foster Seeley detection, one high frequency amplification stage, three intermediate frequency amplification stages, and one limiter stage.

6BM8 push-pull circuit is adopted for the amplifier section, and self-bias system is adopted.

Equipped with tone control and loudness control.
