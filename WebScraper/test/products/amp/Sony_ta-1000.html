  • SONY TA-1000

  • ¥ 32,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

A stereo pre-main amplifier developed as an easy-to-use universal type.

ITL and OTL circuits are fully adopted in the circuit structure.

Equipped with a speaker selector switch that can use 2 pairs of speakers.

It is equipped with a loudness control for adjusting the sound balance at low volume, a high filter for eliminating scratching noise, a lever-type balance control knob, and a headphone jack.

Equipped with two pairs of phono input jacks for switching between two cartridges.
