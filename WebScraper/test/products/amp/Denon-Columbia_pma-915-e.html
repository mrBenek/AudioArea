Audio Database

  • DENON PMA-915

  • ¥ 33,000 (around 1988)

Commentary

Thin pre-main amplifier with simple straight configuration.

50W + 50W (6 Ω) output is achieved by adopting a large capacity power transistor in the final stage.

A tone control circuit is used.

It has Route 6 (including Phono MM) audio inputs and two speaker outputs.
