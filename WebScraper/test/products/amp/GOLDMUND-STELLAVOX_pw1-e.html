Commentary

A monaural power amplifier released by ステラヴォックス, a professional audio brand of Gold Mundo.

Equipped with a JOB circuit, an amplification module unique to Gold Mundo, it realizes a power of 200W in spite of its small size.

A 1-dB step 10-position input attenuator is available for balanced and unbalanced input.

Aluminum heat sink is used for the front panel.
