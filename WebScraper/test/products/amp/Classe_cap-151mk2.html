  • CLASSE CAP-151mk2

  • ¥ 450,000 (around 1999)

Commentary

An integrated amplifier that combines the high quality of a separate amplifier into a single unit.

The power supply section is equipped with a large sealed toroidal transformer.

Preamp section and power amplifier section can be used independently.
It can be switched by pressing the buttons on the front panel.

Equipped with Route 6 line input including one balanced input system.

Wireless remote control is included.
This remote and input switching are possible with this remote control.

There was an optional MM/MC phono input card sold separately.
