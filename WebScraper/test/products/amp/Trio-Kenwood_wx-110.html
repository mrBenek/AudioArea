  • TRIO WX-110

  • ¥ 34,900 (around 1964?)

Commentary

AM/FM multi-stereo tri-amplifier for FM multi-stereo.

CCM system is adopted for FM multi-circuit.
It is also equipped with a monitoring device that reliably selects FM multi stereo broadcast transmission.

In order to prevent interference to FM broadcasting reception when SCA broadcasting is conducted in Japan, an SCA filter is installed.

It is possible to record and play FM broadcasting by using a tape recorder together.

The equalizer amplifier section uses full DC ignition to suppress hum noise.

Equipped with FM AFC (Automatic Frequency Stabilizer), FM broadcasting once tuned does not shift the tuning point or distort the sound by the function of AFC.

It is equipped with a high filter in order to remove the scratch noise of the record and the high-frequency noise that occurs when the broadcast is received.
