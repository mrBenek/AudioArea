Commentary

This is an upgraded stereo power amplifier based on the power amplifier part of Integra 725.

Distortion measures are taken for all frequencies, including those outside the audible band.

It is equipped with left and right independent volume, and the input sensitivity is two step switching between 1 V and 0.5 V.
