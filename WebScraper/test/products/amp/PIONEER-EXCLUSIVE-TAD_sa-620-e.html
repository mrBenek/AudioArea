Commentary

In line with the original purpose of the pre-main amplifier, this model is designed with full functionality and sound quality as the goal.

The equalizer amplifier uses a 2-stage direct-coupled circuit with transistors that further select the low-noise type to improve S/N and stability.
In addition, high-precision parts (resistors and capacitors) are used in order to reproduce the recording characteristics of the record with fidelity.

It is equipped with two Phono terminals and two pairs of terminals for tape decks so that two tape decks can be connected at the same time.

With a built-in microphone mixing amplifier, you can mix and play the program source and the sound from the microphone.
The mixed signal can be taken out from the Tape Rec jack (DIN jack), so you can also record by connecting a tape deck.
It can also be used as a simple microphone loudspeaker device.

The tone circuit is a 2-stage direct connection NFB type with three carefully selected transistors.
By adopting the emitter follower circuit, the flat position of the tone control knob provides a simple characteristic with less waviness.

All stages of the power amplifier are directly connected with pure complimentary service system.
A differential amplifier is adopted in the first stage and a high DC stability is obtained by using an input bias compensation circuit in combination with the amplifier. In addition, a large radiator and a power transistor with high breakdown strength are used to provide ample room even at high power.

Equipped with two types of speaker terminals, each can be driven separately or simultaneously.

It is fully equipped with functions such as muting switch, high filter, loudness contour, pre-main separation switch, etc.
