  • NEC V-111

  • ¥ 89,800 (around 1982)

Commentary

A stereo cassette receiver that combines a cassette deck, FM/AM tuner and amplifier into one.

The tuner section uses a microcomputer synthesizer system and preset memories, and can store up to six FM and AM stations each.

Equipped with an auto-Rec mute function by microcomputer, the interval setting between songs is fully automated.

Equipped with a sound make-up that gives 3 sound effects with one touch.
Natural provides flat characteristics, Dynamic provides resistance sound enhancement, and Close-Up provides mid-range enhancement.

Equipped with an automatic music selection mechanism (NSPS), you can select 9 songs before and after.

Equipped with an auto tape selector, it is also compatible with metal tapes.

Equipped with an automatic recording mechanism, there is no need to adjust the recording level.

Equipped with a microphone mixing mechanism, you can easily adjust the volume balance between the microphone and the music source.
