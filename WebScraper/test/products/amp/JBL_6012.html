Commentary

A monaural power amplifier with reliability and integrity to withstand harsh conditions.

The 6012 is a direct type without output transformer.
Other specifications remain the same as 6011.

The output circuit is a single module, and maintenance is simplified by making removal easy.

5195 transformer is used for balanced input.
