  • SANSUI AU - α 507

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1986)

Commentary

Integrated amplifier with heavy-duty power supply.

The dynamic power of 350W + 350W (2 Ω) increases the speaker drive capability under low impedance loads, and realizes excellent transient response characteristics, reducing TIM distortion, harmonic distortion, and intermodulation distortion.

The power supply section uses a 10,000 μ Fx2 Great Supply power supply condenser specially developed for audio use. In addition, the use of anti-vibration material reduces the deterioration of sound quality caused by unnecessary vibration of the transformer itself.

The concept of high rigidity is thoroughly applied to the whole amplifier in order to firmly suppress unnecessary resonance.
In the AU - α 507, not only the rigidity of the external housing is increased, but also the power transistor is installed in a position where vibration and noise generated does not adversely affect other parts.

It is equipped with source direct, and input can be made without passing through an accessory circuit by simply switching the switch from the normal input state.

It is equipped with tone control with defeat function.

Uses a power cord with polarity display.

It is equipped with a REC selector with an OFF position to prevent degradation of sound quality due to excess circuit.

The MM/MC phono equalizer uses a high-gain IC.

The front panel has a glossy and hairline finish made of aluminum material.
