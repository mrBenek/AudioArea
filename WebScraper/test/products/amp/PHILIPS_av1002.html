  • PHILIPS AV1002

  • ¥ 160,000 (around 1989)

Commentary

This 6-channel power amplifier was announced as the power amplifier part of the separate AV amplifier AV1000.

All channels use a discrete method.

You can configure the BTL connection with two channels as a single unit. If you use the BTL connection, the output is doubled to 100W and can be used as a high-power 3-channel amplifier.

There are independent tone controls for the front channel, center channel and surround channel.
It can also be bypassed by a separate defeat switch.

The front channel, center channel, and surround channel have independent input level controls.

Copper plated chassis is used on the entire surface.

The power supply section is equipped with a large 550 VA power transformer and a 27,000 μ Fx2 electrolytic capacitor.
