  • LUXMAN C-1010

  • ¥ 180,000 (released in December 1975)

Commentary

Transistor control amplifier, the successor to C-1000.

The basic circuit configuration and function style are almost the same.
The C-1010 employs an operational amplifier circuit configuration similar to a DC amplifier in all stages to improve the characteristics of the high-frequency range, which tends to be problematic.

LUX method is used for tone control.
