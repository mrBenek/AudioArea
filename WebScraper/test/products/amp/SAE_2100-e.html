  • SAE 2100

  • ¥ 460,000 (around 1981)

Commentary

A control amplifier that can handle any music source and has been developed to make all components work flexibly.

As a control function, it is equipped with SAE unique parametric equalizer which can continuously change frequency, level and band width.

Equipped with two tape copy circuits and 30Hz/100Hz switching low filter.
