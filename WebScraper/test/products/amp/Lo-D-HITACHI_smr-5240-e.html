  • Lo-D SMR-5240

  • ¥ 89,800 (released in 1974)

Commentary

A 4-channel stereo receiver developed using semiconductor and circuit technologies.

Built-in regular matrix (RM) and SQ matrix circuits.

The 4-channel power amplifier section consists of a quasi complimentary service OCL circuit.
The BTL connection allows the power amplifier to play back a sound with a margin when two channels are used.

The FM tuner uses a MOS-FET in the front end to achieve stable reception with low noise and reduced interference. The intermediate frequency amplifier uses a mechanical filter and IC with sharp performance.
A switching system is adopted for the MPX circuit.

Equipped with a pair microphone mixing circuit.
