  • Lo-D SR-5100

  • ¥ 59,800 (released in 1974)

Commentary

AM/FM receiver combining 25W + 25W audio section and 1.9 μ V high sensitivity tuner.

We have achieved low distortion and high S/N by using Hitachi's proprietary transistors and ICs for the power amplifier and by adopting a grounded emitter-grounded inverted Darlington pure complimentary service OCL circuit.

The equalizer stage of the nf is applied to the equalizer stage of the preamplifier section by ± 2 power supply and 3-stage directly connected monolithic IC, and low distortion rate characteristics are realized over the whole band.

The FM tuner uses a 3-row varicon and a dual-gate MOS-FET front-end, and a full-scale circuit configuration that combines a high-integration IC, an IF circuit with a 4-element phase linear ceramic filter, and quadrature detection.
By adopting double-balanced differential switching for the MPX demodulation circuit, excellent separation characteristics are realized.

Equipped with an FM muting switch.

Linear scale FM memory is adopted.

It is equipped with 2 types of tape input and can be dubbed.

Equipped with a microphone mixing circuit.
