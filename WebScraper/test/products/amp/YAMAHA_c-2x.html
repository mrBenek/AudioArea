Commentary

This is a preamplifier that has been created in pursuit of the ideal image of an amplifier without compromise in performance and sound quality while maintaining the minimum necessary functions.

Equipped with an MC head amplifier composed of eight low-noise transistors in single-channel parallel push-pull configuration, and with the adoption of Yamaha's original ZDR circuit, it has a high characteristic of passing a sharp square wave as signal input.

The equalizer amplifier has a parallel configuration using High-gm low-noise dual FET.
It has a ZDR circuit as well as the MC head amplifier, and also adopts a current noise suppressor circuit that reduces the noise when the cartridge is mounted by approximately 3 dB.
In addition, two Phono inputs are installed. Phono2 is fixed at 47k Ω and 220 pf, and Phono1 is selectable at 100 Ω and 1k Ω at MC position and at 100, 200 and 300 pf at 47k Ω at MM position.
In addition, the gain of the equalizer amplifier can be switched on the rear panel, making it easy to adjust with other inputs.

NF-type tone control circuit using high-quality film capacitor. High-precision 21-point click special volume and center defeat mechanism.

Equipped with 4-connected volume to improve S/N at low volume.

It uses a 2-transformer independent power supply that independently feeds the equalizer amplifier section and the flat amplifier section, and eight low-magnification chemi-cons of 4700 μ F are specified.

Equipped with a muting circuit that employs a semiconductor switch to prevent shock noise during power on / off.

The output amplifier is equipped with a 0 dB buffer amplifier that enables low-impedance signal transmission.

Uses a composite printed circuit board with excellent high-frequency characteristics.

It is a unit amplifier in which each amplifier section is housed individually to prevent the influence of disturbance.

The front and upper panels are made of extruded aluminum, and the volume, balance, tone control and switches are made of machined solid aluminum.

Equipped with a subsonic filter switch, stereo / mono mode selection switch, and audio muting switch.

Input Selector Equipped with an independent Rec Out selector.

Uses a gold-plated input / output terminal made by scraping out non-magnetic pure brass.

An OFC power cord with polarity indication is used.
