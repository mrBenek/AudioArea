  • ¥ 27,800 (released in 1973)

Commentary

This is a pre-main amplifier that inherits the design philosophy of the ES series, draws out the program source faithfully, and aims at no coloring.

The power amplifier section uses a direct-coupling type differential-amplified 2 power supply and pure complimentary service circuit.
In the preamplifier section, a transistor developed for low noise like the TA 1140 is used as an equalizer amplifier.

Equipped with NF-type tone control, the sound quality can be adjusted according to the program source and sound characteristics of the listening room.
