  • Price unknown (around 1983)

Commentary

Stereo power amplifier using FET for all stages.

All stages from the first stage to the output stage are composed of FETs.

It uses the patented TRANS-NOVA circuit and realizes excellent rise time, slew rate and bandwidth.
In addition, the new feedback method brings the output stage distortion and output impedance close to 0, resulting in a high damping factor.
