Commentary

A control amplifier that uses a newly designed circuit for transistors and carefully selected materials such as transistors, resistors and switches.

The equalizer stage, tone control stage, and output amplifier stage are directly connected with a differential circuit at the first stage.

The power supply is designed as a constant voltage circuit with a power transformer that also serves as a ripple filter to cope with the asymmetrical and intense waveform of real music, and the silicon diode is separated into left and right at the exit to improve crosstalk in the low-frequency range.
In addition, for the transformer, cut core is used and short ring is wound, and the transformer itself uses the one with the minimum flux, and sufficient mounting space is taken to suppress the effect.
