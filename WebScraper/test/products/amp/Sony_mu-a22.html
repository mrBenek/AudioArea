  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1983)

Commentary

This is a small stereo power amplifier of the Studio Lab series.

By adopting a strong power supply, high power and weight reduction are realized.

It has stacking connection with 2 / 3 units, and has stacking terminal for power up.

Equipped with an input level control that can adjust the L/R input level.
