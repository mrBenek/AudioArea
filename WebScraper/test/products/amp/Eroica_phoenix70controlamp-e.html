  • Eroica Phoenix70 control amp

  • Price unknown (around the mid 1960 s?)

Commentary

Separate amplifier Phoenix70 control amplifier.

It is composed of a 2-stage NF amplifier so that the output voltage can be large.

It is equipped with the ascending point switching type tone control which Eroica commercialized for the first time in the world in the EW-50, and the turnover frequency can be switched between high four stages and low four stages.

A CR type equalizer is used.

The power transformer used is a completely sealed and completely shielded transformer manufactured by tamura Manufacturing Co., Ltd., and all vacuum tubes are DC ignited by bridge rectification using silicon diodes.

Aluminum chassis is used for the chassis.
