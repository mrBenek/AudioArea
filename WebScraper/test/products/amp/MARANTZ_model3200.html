  • Marantz Model3200

  • ¥ 64,000 (released in 1976)

Commentary

An economical preamplifier that inherits traditional circuit technology.

Equipped with 5 types of mode switches.

Equipped with a bass, treble and mid-tone control.

Equipped with a low filter.

The rear panel has terminals for the main and remote speakers, which can be switched by the control amplifier.

There were Walnut wood cabinet and rack mount adapter as an option sold separately.
