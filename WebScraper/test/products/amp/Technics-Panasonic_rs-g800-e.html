  • Technics RS-G800

  • ¥ 108,000 (around 1982)

Commentary

A micro receiver deck integrating a micro cassette deck, tuner and amplifier.

A quartz synthesizer is used for the tuner section, and six FM/AM presets and auto-scan functions are installed.

SX Sendust head is adopted for the micro cassette deck part.

Equipped with an auto reset peak hold 2-color FL display.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.
