  • TRIO W-45A

  • ¥ 35,300 (around the early 1960 s)

Commentary

Deluxe type pre-main amplifier.

The output is 36W for monaural and 18Wx2 for stereo.

Uses a full DC ignition system to eliminate hum.

Equipped with a rumble filter.

It has a headphone port.

Equipped with a new type balance meter, you can accurately balance the left and right channel amplifier outputs while watching.

You can adjust the phase of the speakers with the switch on the back. You can also adjust the phase of the speakers after they are connected.

Equipped with a loudness control.

There was a tuner AF 270 which was sold separately and matched in performance and design.
