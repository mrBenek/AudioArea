Commentary

A home cinema compact system where you can enjoy CD and FM/AM tuner.

The AV-1 consists of TCD-AV1, a preamplifier unit with a CD player and tuner, SW-AV1, a subwoofer unit with a 6-channel power amplifier, and NX-AV1, five center / front / rear satellite speakers.

Equipped with 8 types of surround programs, you can enjoy movies and music with realistic realism.

It is equipped with four input terminals for video Route 3 and audio.
The CD has an optical digital output, which can be used for MD recording.

The CD has a 1-bit DAC in the D/A converter.

Equipped with an FM/AM tuner.
This tuner is compatible with wide-band FM and AM stereo, and has 40 preset functions.

Equipped with Yamaha's original YST system subwoofer.

On-screen capability allows you to display programs, volume and test tones on your TV screen.

Built-in timer allows CD playback and FM/AM tuner to be turned and FM/AM tuner at any time.

It comes with a light storage type remote controller with illuminated operation buttons.
You can adjust the volume of the subwoofer with this remote control.

There were TV side bracket and speaker stand as options sold separately.
