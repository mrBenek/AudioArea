  • ONKYO PHC-5

  • ¥ 39,000 (released on November 1, 1998)

Commentary

This is a home theater system equipped with virtual Dolby surround sound that is wrapped in 360 degree surround sound with only 2 built-in speakers.

Virtual surround processing generates virtual surround (rear) speakers, and the surround sound field is reproduced with only two built-in speakers.
The PHC-5 is equipped with Theater-Dimensional, a virtual technology developed in-house by Onkyo, and creates surround feeling by digital signal processing using DSP (Digital Signal Processor).

The unit is equipped with two 10 cm cone type units that employ silk OMF diaphragms.

Connect to an optional subwoofer for more immersive surround playback.

Equipped with an IPM function that turns the power switch ON/OFF in conjunction with the audio output signal of the TV.

Virtual / stereo mode switching is possible.

Equipped with a surround sound field adjustment function that allows you to adjust the DISTANCE/EXPAND / surround sound volume according to the viewing conditions.
You can also adjust the volume of the connected subwoofer.

Equipped with a tilt function that can adjust the angle of 20 degrees upward and 10 degrees downward.

Display can be turned ON/OFF.

It comes with a learning remote controller that allows basic operation of TV and video.
