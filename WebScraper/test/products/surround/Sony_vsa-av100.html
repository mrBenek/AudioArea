Commentary

A set of AV center amplifier TA-VE100, center speaker SS-CN15 and rear speaker SS-SR15.

You can enjoy surround playback simply by adding a TV and front speaker.

AV Center Amplifier TA-VE100

Dolby Pro Logic Surround is supported.
In addition, 2 kinds of surround functions of theater and simulated are installed.

Equipped with HD input for 3-1 stereo, you can enjoy Hi-Vision TV and Hi-Vision LD sound.
* A TV with built-in MUSE decoder or MUSE decoder is required.

Wireless remote control is included.

Center Speaker SS-CN15

The unit contains two 8 cm cone type full-range speakers.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

Rear Speaker SS-SR15

The unit is equipped with an 8 cm cone type full range speaker.
Equipped with 60 mm pitch mounting screw holes on the back.
