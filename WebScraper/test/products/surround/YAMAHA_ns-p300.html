  • YAMAHA NS-P300

  • ¥ 39,800 (one set of six, around 1999)

Commentary

A set of 6 speakers including 2 main speakers, 2 rear speakers, center speakers and YST subwoofer.

The main speaker is a two way speaker system with a 12 cm cone woofer and a 2.2 cm dome tweeter.

The center speaker consists of two 8 cm full-range cone units and a 2.2 cm dome tweeter.

The rear speaker uses a thin 10 cm cone unit.

The subwoofer has a built-in 16 cm woofer unit and a power amplifier with a maximum output of 40W for a compact yet powerful bass reproduction.
