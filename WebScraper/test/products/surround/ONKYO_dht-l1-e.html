Commentary

This digital home theater system is a set of 5 satellite speakers D-L1, 6 ch amplifier built-in subwoofer SWA-L1 and AV surround center ED-L1.

The AV Surround Center ED-L1 is equipped with three types of decoders : Dolby Digital, DTS, and Dolby Pro Logic.
It is equipped with a 24 bit DSP chip made by Crystal and a 96kHz/24bit D/A converter.

The D-L1 uses an 8 cm full-range speaker with an OMF diaphragm and an S-line edge that reduces the reflection of propagating waves at the diaphragm and edge junction.
The enclosure is made of MDF material with 60-mm-pitch screw holes for brackets at the bottom and wall mounting holes at the back.

The SWA-L1 is a subwoofer with a built-in power amplifier that drives the D-L1.
The enclosure uses the J' DRIVE system and the unit is equipped with a 20 cm cone woofer.
A dedicated 6-channel audio RCA pin cable is included for easy connection.

Wireless remote control is included.
