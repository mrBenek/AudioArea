Commentary

An AV system that combines a VCR TN-AV5, an AV amplifier SU-AV5 and an intelligent remote control.

The TN-AV5 uses the S-VHS system and achieves a horizontal resolution of more than 400 lines.
A dual-mode loading mechanism is adopted in the mechanism part, and high-speed image output of about 2 seconds from the stop state until the screen appears is realized.

With the adoption of Sprint FF and REW, the fast-forward and rewind speed is approximately twice that of conventional products.
In addition, it is equipped with Sprint Search, and high-speed search of 21 times speed is possible.

The SU-AV5 is equipped with four types of surround sound field playback modes (Dolby, Movie, Music, AV Mono) and a deep bass equalizer to create an effective sound field that matches the playback software and is controlled by a DSP.

Equipped with front and rear 4-channel power amplifiers.

An intelligent remote controller equipped with macro function is included.
This remote control can memorize up to seven key operations and aggregate them into one key operation. The built-in optional mode allows you to learn and memorize remote control commands for your AV equipment.
