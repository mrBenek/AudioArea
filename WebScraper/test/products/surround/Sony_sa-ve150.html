  • SONY SA-VE100

  • ¥ 35,000 (1 set, around 1997)

Commentary

A speaker package that combines a compact speaker and a super woofer.

A newly developed 5x9cm track-type unit is used for the front and rear satellite speakers.

The center speaker uses a dual unit system that uses two of the same units as the front and rear speakers.
In addition, the cabinet has a structure that is divided into two parts inside, and the interference between units is cut off to prevent the sound from becoming muddy.

The super woofer is equipped with a 16 cm diameter cone type woofer.
The cabinet also adopts the advanced SAW system, which achieves both expansion of low-frequency playback range and quick response.

Front and rear speakers are equipped with 60 mm pitch M5 nuts and hook holes for wall mounting.

All speakers are anti-magnetic design.

It was sold separately with stand and bracket, so it could be installed in a wide range.
