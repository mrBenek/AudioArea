Commentary

A system set with an AV amplifier for Dolby Pro Logic and speakers for 5.1 channels.

The AV center amplifier has a thin design and fits neatly into a TV rack. It has a 5.1 channel input and can be combined with a DVD player with a built-in Dolby Digital or DTS decoder.
A dynamic five amplifier system using the same power transistor for all five channels is adopted.

Wireless remote control is included.

Separately sold floor stand and bracket can be used.
