Commentary

This home theater system combines an AV amplifier equipped with digital cinema sound technology and surround speakers.

The AV amplifier is equipped with a Dolby Digital and DTS decoder, enabling surround playback of 5.1 channels.
Also equipped with digital cinema sound technology, it is equipped with a cinema studio mode A/B/C that reproduces the sound characteristics of a movie mixing studio, and a virtual multi-dimensional mode that virtually localizes sound sources from multiple rear speakers.

A socket-type speaker terminal with color-coded outputs is used for the output terminal.

Wireless remote control is included.

Separately sold floor stand and bracket can be used.
