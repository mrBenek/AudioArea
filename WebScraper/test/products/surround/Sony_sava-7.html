Commentary

An active AV speaker system that allows you to enjoy surround sound simply by setting it on the side of your TV.

The SAVA-7 has four built-in speakers, including a main front, a center where the left and right units are in charge of sound directly in front, a rear on the top of the top, and a super woofer unit on the bottom.

It has a built-in Dolby Pro Logic Decoder.
Other surround modes include news, live, stadium, and games.

It is a compact design suitable for 21 inch TV.

It is equipped with two input terminals, and the sound of two inputs can be mixed.
You can adjust the mixing ratio.

It has an output terminal and can be connected by inserting it between existing devices.

A tone control function is provided for the front channel.

Wireless remote control is included.
