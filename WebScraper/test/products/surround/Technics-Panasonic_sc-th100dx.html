Commentary

Home THX audio system.

The SC-TH100DX consists of three SH-TX100DX control amplifiers, three SE-TX100D power amplifiers, three SB-TF100D front speakers, two SB-TW100D sub-woofers, and two SB-TS100D rear speakers. 200

Except for power amplifier SE-TX100D, home THX license has been obtained.

The SH-TX200DX can connect up to 12 sources including audio and video, and is equipped with S-VHS terminal and optical digital input / output terminal.

The audio circuit is equipped with a Mash 1-bit DAC, high-performance pro-logic circuit, and THX's original signal-processing circuit to achieve more faithful playback.
In addition to the THX Cinema, which reproduces the original sound of movies as it is, the Academy Filter, which brings back the atmosphere of old monaural movies, Dolby Pro Logic, and various modes such as simulated surround sound, it can be reproduced according to the genre of the source.

It also has a mixing microphone terminal with digital echo.

The SE-TX100D is equipped with a unique class AA circuit that combines high-quality sound with powerful speaker drive.
High power of 140W + 140W (4 Ω) with independent L/R-high capacity transformer provides strong drive power to each speaker.
The SC-TH100DX comes with three of these amplifiers.
