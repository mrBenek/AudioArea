Commentary

This speaker package is a set of satellite speakers that can play back very high frequencies.

Satellite speaker

Two 5 cm cone type speakers are installed for the low range.
A repartion magnetic circuit is adopted for the magnetic circuit. By mounting two neodymium magnets with a plate sandwiched between them so that the magnetic flux faces each other, the area of strong magnetic flux acting on the voice coil is expanded, and the braking force of the diaphragm is improved. In addition, the driving force is increased by increasing the diameter of the voice coil.
In addition, this unit uses a plastic butterfly damper to improve the linearity of the amplitude against the input and to obtain a sharp rising sound.

A 1.9 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
A carbon-graphite composite diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, enabling ultra-high frequency reproduction up to 70 kHz.

An aluminum die cast oval enclosure is used for the enclosure.

It comes with 2 types of net grills, silver and light blue.

Subwoofer

The unit is equipped with 20 cm cone type woofer.

The high-speed MFB circuit expands the low-frequency reproduction capability, and a large power transformer and high-sound condenser are used.

The advanced SAW system is used for the enclosure.
In the advanced SAW system, an appropriate radiator is installed in the front baffle to prevent delay of the emitted sound and to achieve both good response and heavy bass reproduction.

Equipped with a mode selection switch for movies and music.
Also equipped with auto power ON/OFF function.

Other

There were floor stand, TV bracket, wall mounting bracket and so on sold separately.
These accessories are available in two color variations : black and silver.
