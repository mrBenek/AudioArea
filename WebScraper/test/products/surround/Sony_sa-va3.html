Commentary

This active speaker system is a set of 5 channels including an AV amplifier equipped with Dolby Pro Logic Surround, front L/R, center, rear and subwoofer.

Equipped with an auto power switch function that automatically turns the power on/off depending on the presence or absence of an audio signal.

Wireless remote control is included.
