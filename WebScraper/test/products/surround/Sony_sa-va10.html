Commentary

An AV active speaker system developed based on the concept of replacing TV speakers with powerful ones.

Surround mode includes Dolby Pro Logic, "Hall" for concerts, and "Game" for video games.

The SA-VA10 consists of two front and two rear bookshelf speakers.
Center speakers are installed on the left and right sides of the front speaker for easy installation. In addition, an independent center speaker terminal is installed for use with an optional center speaker.
In addition, the front right speaker has a built-in superwoofer using the SAW method, enabling heavy bass playback.

The rear speaker is a compact design and can be installed freely.

Equipped with an auto power switch function that automatically turns on when an audio signal is input.

It has a built-in amplifier with total output of 100W for front, center and rear.

Wireless remote control is included.

There was a speaker stand sold separately for the front speaker.
