  • YAMAHA CinemaStation AV-S7

  • ¥ 65,000 (1 set of 6, released in 1998)

    　
Commentary

A home cinema sound system that lets you enjoy theater sound simply by adding it to your TV.

The AV-S7 consists of a total of six speakers : a center speaker NX-AVS7 with a control section, a subwoofer SW-AVS7 with a DSP circuit and a 6-channel power amplifier, and four front / rear speakers NX-AV1.

A through terminal is mounted.
This connector automatically switches between the AV-S7 side and the TV side in conjunction with the power ON/OFF. Therefore, the TV and video can be used as they are even when the power of the AV-S7 is OFF.

Five surround programs are installed.

The NX-AV1 and NX-AVS7 are equipped with an 8 cm cone type full range unit, and the SW-AVS7 is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer.

The amplifier section built into the subwoofer realizes a total output power of 200W (30Wx5 + 50W).

The subwoofer uses Yamaha's unique YST YST system to achieve powerful low-frequency playback.

The second audio input jack lets you play TV games and CD/MD movies with theater sound.

Comes with a light storage type remote controller.

There were speaker stand for satellite speaker and TV side bracket as an option sold separately.
