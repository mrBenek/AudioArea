Commentary

A speaker package with two satellite speakers for improved drive power.

Satellite speaker

The satellite speakers have two full-range units with 5 cm balanced drives.
This unit damper uses a butterfly damper made of resin, and it is characterized by high linearity of amplitude against input and sharp rise of sound.
In addition, two neodymium magnets are used for the magnetic circuit to expand the strong magnetic flux area, and the voice coil is larger in diameter to increase the driving force.

Center speaker stand with angle adjustment mechanism is included.

Subwoofer

The unit is equipped with 20 cm cone type woofer.

The advanced SAW system is used for the enclosure.

Equipped with a mode selection switch for movies and music.

Other

There were floor stand, TV bracket, wall mounting bracket and so on sold separately.
These accessories are available in two color variations : black and silver.
