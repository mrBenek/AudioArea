Commentary

A surround set developed as an all-in-one home theater system.

Center unit section
A DVD/CD player and an FM/AM tuner are integrated into the center unit.

Built-in DTS / Dolby Digital Recorder as decoder.
In addition, it is equipped with 6 Adobanced Theater modes unique to Pioneer, and the sound field can be reproduced according to the content of the software.

It is equipped with 7 DSP modes which make sound field optimum for the reproduction software.

Equipped with Midnight listening mode that reproduces dynamic sound even at low volume.

10-bit / 27-MHz processing and 10-bit video DAC are used for video processing.

It is equipped with input / output terminals for 2 systems each of AV and audio capable of digital input / output and S video input / output.


Center / Satellite speakers
The unit uses a 8.7 cm cone type full-range speaker.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.


Powered subwoofer section
The compact center unit is realized by incorporating the amplifier part, which is usually independent, into the subwoofer.

The unit is equipped with 16 cm cone type woofer.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.


Other
It uses a separate display with a free layout.
The display has a 5-stage dimmer function.

It comes with a TV preset and a multi-control remote control with learning function.
