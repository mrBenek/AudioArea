Commentary

A speaker package with a compact design that allows free installation.

Satellite speaker

The unit uses a 5x10cm track type unit.
In this unit, a track-type magnet is used for the magnetic circuit without wasting space behind the diaphragm, and a high magnetic force is obtained. In addition, a plastic butterfly damper with high amplitude linearity against input and sharp sound is used for the damper.

The center speaker is equipped with the same two units as the satellite speaker, and by reducing the load per unit, the sense of distortion is further reduced.

The front has a metal grill net finish.

Subwoofer

The unit is equipped with 16 cm cone type woofer.

The advanced SAW system is used for the enclosure.
In the advanced SAW system, an appropriate radiator is installed in the front baffle to prevent delay of the emitted sound and to achieve both good response and heavy bass reproduction.

Equipped with a mode selection switch for movies and music.

Other

There were floor stand, TV bracket, wall mounting bracket and so on sold separately.
These accessories are available in two color variations : black and silver.
