Commentary

A speaker package that uses satellite speakers capable of playback up to 40 kHz.

Satellite speaker

Two 5 cm cone type units are installed for the low range, and a 1.9 cm dome type tweeter is installed for the high range.
A plastic butterfly damper is used for the damper of the cone type unit, and it has the advantages of high linearity of amplitude against input and sharp rise of sound.

A neodymium magnet is used for the magnetic circuit of each unit to realize a compact and powerful magnetic circuit.
The 5 cm cone unit uses a repulsive magnetic field circuit with two magnets to strengthen the magnetic circuit and improve sound quality. The voice coil also has a larger diameter to improve driving force.

Aluminum die-cast is used for the enclosure to reduce unnecessary resonance.
In addition, the rounded design prevents the occurrence of internal standing waves and realizes a clear sound.

Comes with a net grill of 2 colors and can be switched according to your preference.

Desktop stand is included. Center stand is equipped with angle adjustment mechanism.

Subwoofer

The unit is equipped with 20 cm cone type woofer.

The advanced SAW system is used for the enclosure.

Equipped with a mode selection switch for movies and music.

Other

There were floor stand, TV bracket, wall mounting bracket and so on sold separately.
These accessories are available in two color variations : black and silver.
