Commentary

This satellite speaker system is a set of NS-10MM and YST Super Woofer YST-SW40.
The stand in the picture was sold separately.

Since the NS-10MM and YST-SW40 are designed as a pair, a well-balanced playback is possible only by setting the volume and high-cut frequency of the YST-SW40 to the recommended level.

Various installations are possible by combining the separately sold stand and bracket.
