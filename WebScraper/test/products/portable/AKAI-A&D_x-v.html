  • AKAI X-V

  • Body only : ¥ 85,000 (around 1967?)
    No. 7 reel with adapter : ¥ 87,900 (around 1967?)

Commentary

Stereo tape recorder with portable design.

Cross-field head is used for the head part.

A brushless servo motor is used as the drive motor.

The amplifier section has a solid-state configuration.

With a built-in storage battery, it can be used for both AC and DC.

Automatic recording is possible.

8-hour Hi-Fi recording is possible using a 5-inch 180m tape.

Equipped with a 3-digit tape counter.
