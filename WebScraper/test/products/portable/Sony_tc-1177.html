  • SONY TC-1177 MAGAZINE-MATIC EM GO

  • ¥ 19,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

A cassette recorder that uses IC as a circuit for the space age.

IC is adopted at the heart of the circuit to obtain high reliability and stability.

It incorporates an electret condenser microphone that succeeded in making the condenser microphone for professional use ultra-compact.

It has a review button as a one touch function.
You can listen to the same sound as many times as you want by simply pressing the review button during playback.

Equipped with an automatic recording function, there is no need to adjust the volume when recording.

It is equipped with an end alarm, and when the tape ends during recording, an alarm sound is emitted from the speaker to notify the end of the tape.
(Sony Magazine tape with auto sensor is used)

The power supply unit is a 4-power source type and can be powered by home power supply, car battery or rechargeable battery in addition to battery power.

Equipped with a remote control jack, you can start and stop the tape at hand using a microphone with a remote control switch.

Equipped with tone control.

The speakers are 10x7cm elliptical cone type speakers.
