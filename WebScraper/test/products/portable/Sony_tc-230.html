Audio Database

  • SONY TC-230

  • ¥ 67,500 (around 1969)

Commentary

A complete system stereo tape recorder that can also be used as a stereo amplifier.

By connecting an FM tuner and player, it can be used as a full-fledged high-fidelity amplifier.

It is a solid-state design using silicon transistors.
