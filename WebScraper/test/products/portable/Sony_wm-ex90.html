  • SONY WM-EX90

  • ¥ 32,000 (around 1992)

Commentary

With its thin body and plenty of functions, this Walkman is perfect for learning English conversation.

It has a section repeat function, and you can repeat the section many times if you indicate any 2 points.
The interval can be set arbitrarily from 2 seconds to the end of the playback tape surface, and can be operated with a remote control.

Equipped with a removable battery case (AAA 3x2), it can be used with a rechargeable battery for 17.5 hours long play.

Equipped with a large liquid crystal display.
It also has a 3-digit LCD tape counter, clock display and battery voltage drop display.

It has a Queue & Review function.

An EX amorphous head is used for the head part.
It uses an amorphous material that has better magnetic characteristics and durability than conventional permalloy materials. In addition, it uses an EX mechanism with a special tape guide that keeps the angle between the head and the tape constant to obtain excellent sound quality.

Equipped with Dolby B as a noise reduction system.

Equipped with an auto tape selector.

The earphones use headphones that reduce sound leakage.
These earphones use a teardrop shape to reduce sound leakage through the space between the headphone and the ear, and a silent cap to eliminate unwanted sound leakage by leaving only the ear hole for sound.

Equipped with an EXD. B. B. circuit, it is possible to play back more powerful bass sound.
The Dynamic Base Boost circuit can enhance 50 Hz bass by up to 16 times. This function is not just a boost, it automatically adjusts the emphasis according to the current volume position.
In the EXD. B. B. circuit, the amplification amount is also changed linearly according to the volume change of the original sound to prevent the delicate tone of the weak sound from being lost.

It is equipped with a 10 minute quick charge function, and it can be charged up to 50% of full charge in 10 minutes, and full charge in 30 minutes.
It automatically switches between fast charging and normal charging according to the remaining amount of the rechargeable battery.

There are 2 color variations, black and white.
