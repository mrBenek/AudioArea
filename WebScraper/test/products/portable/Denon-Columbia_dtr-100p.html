  • DENON DTR-100P

  • ¥ 99,000 (around 1992)

Commentary

Portable DAT of B5 book size which can be used indoor and outdoor.

The low-power design enables continuous two hour recording / playback.
Also equipped with an auto power off function, the power is automatically turned off after approximately 5 to 6 minutes.

It is equipped with an A/D converter, microphone amplifier and standard microphone jack for digital live recording.

The exterior has a suede-like finish so that dirt does not stand out and it does not slip.

The head uses Sendust sputter head with high output and high abrasion resistance.

The mechanism part uses a unique small portable mechanism that is resistant to external vibration.

It is equipped with skip play function and ID writing function.

The LCD panel uses a multi-display.
This display shows the elapsed time from the beginning of the tape (ABS : absolute time display), the remaining time on the tape (REM : remaining time display), and the elapsed time for each song (PRG : program time display).

All terminals except the microphone jack are gold-plated.

It supports SCMS and allows digital copying directly from CD or DAT only for the first recording to tape.
However, you cannot record (digitally copy) a tape that you have recorded digitally.

Comes with an AC adapter.

There were carrying case and rechargeable battery as optional items sold separately.
