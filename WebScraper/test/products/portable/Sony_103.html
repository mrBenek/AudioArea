Commentary

A tape recorder equipped with transistor radio.

It is equipped with a radio tuner using Sony's high-frequency transistors.
Ferrite bar antenna is built in.

A microdrive system is used for channel selection.

If you record a tape while receiving the radio, you can record the broadcast as it is.
You can also record while listening to the radio by switching the switch.

A transistor is used in the first stage of the circuit of the tape recorder.
The amplifier uses printed wiring to improve the stability of the circuit.

The AC bias method is used for recording.

You can record up to 2 hours with tape number 7.

Two types of tape speeds are available, with switching between 19cm/s and 9.5cm/s.

A single control system unique to Sony is used for shearing, and the tape can be operated with one knob.

Equipped with a recording meter.

It is possible to record while listening through the speaker.

By using the included 8 mm sync device and Sony Movie Sync, you can make talkie movies easily.
