  • DENON DMP-P33

  • ¥ 37,000 (around 1996)

Commentary

Portable MD player with small, light and thin design.

It has a compact design that fits neatly in the pocket.
Even with a built-in lithium ion rechargeable battery, the weight is reduced to approximately 150g.

Approximately 4 hours of continuous playback is possible.
In addition, the remaining charge of the rechargeable battery can be displayed in 4 levels with the remaining battery indicator.

It is equipped with a sound skip guard memory and stores the read signal in the memory during playback. Even if the signal cannot be read, the stored information is sent and played back. This allows uninterrupted playback.

It comes with an earphone with LCD remote control which can display Katakana.
The LCD of the remote control can display not only alphabets, numbers and symbols but also katakana characters. In addition, the main operations are concentrated in one button, making it possible to operate quickly with the touch of a fingertip.

Random playback and repeat playback are available.

Equipped with a bus boost circuit, you can enjoy 3 levels of powerful playback sound with one touch.
