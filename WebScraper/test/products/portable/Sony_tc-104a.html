  • SONY TC-104A

  • ¥ 29,800 (around 1969)

Commentary

A tape recorder equipped with a one touch tape device.

It uses a electron spectroscopy for chemica analysis rate drive system that can be applied with one touch of tape.

Manual recording is also possible by canceling the automatic recording mode Auto.

The motor is designed to stop automatically when the tape is wound.

3-speed system with knob switching is adopted.
