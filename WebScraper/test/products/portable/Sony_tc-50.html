  • SONY TC-50

  • ¥ 36,000 (around 1969)

Commentary

This cassette tape recorder became the world's smallest model at that time.

It became a hot topic because it was also used by the crew of the American manned spacecraft Apollo 7.

The automatic recording ソニオマチック system is used for recording.

It has a built-in microphone.
It also comes with an external microphone with a remote control, so it can be used for different purposes.
