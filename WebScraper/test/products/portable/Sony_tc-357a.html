Commentary

A tape recorder developed as a high-end machine for recording music.

The TC-357 is a 2-track monaural system, and the TC-105 is a 4-track monaural system as an international model.

Operations are automated and simplified, and an automatic recording ソニオマチック system and an automatic device escalation drive system are adopted.
