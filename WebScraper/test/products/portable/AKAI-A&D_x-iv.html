  • AKAI X-V

  • ¥ 85,000 (around 1965?)

Commentary

A portable stereo tape recorder with a cross field head.

The drive section is equipped with a Devi action motor that enables stable operation even after 1,000 hours of continuous use.

It has a built-in storage battery and an automatic charger.

4-speed, 4-track stereo / mono recording / playback is possible.

12-hour recording / playback is possible using 5-inch tape.

Equipped with an all-transistor amplifier with output of 4W.

All-push button type is adopted.

Uses a metal case.

The unit uses a 15x8cm cone type speaker.

There was a shoulder hanging leather carrying case sold separately.
