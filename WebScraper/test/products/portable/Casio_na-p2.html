  • CASIO NA-P2

  • ¥ 7,800 (around 1991)

Commentary

The CBBS circuit makes it possible to play back powerful deep bass sound in a headphone stereo.

Equipped with a Oatley berth function, it can play continuously for a long time.

It uses an anti-rolling system, enabling stable playback with less sound vibration.

It is compatible with 3 power sources, and can be used with batteries, AC power sources, or in cars by combining with separately sold AC adapter or car adapter.
