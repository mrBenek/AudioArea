Commentary

8 Ω specification dividing network to drive one TL-1601a.
It is adopted in Exclusive model2402.

The design philosophy of low loss and low leakage is thoroughly carried out by introducing OFC (oxygen-free copper) into all wiring cords and choke coil winding, press-joint type connection using OFC sleeve, enlargement and gold plating of terminal, development and adoption of high-quality condenser, etc.

By making the LF circuit a balanced circuit, we have succeeded in eliminating the leakage caused by the ground line.
In addition, it is designed to fully separate the HF and LF circuits and eliminate noise turbidity near the crossover frequency to extract the performance of each combined unit.
