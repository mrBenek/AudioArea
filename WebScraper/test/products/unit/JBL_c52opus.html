  • JBL C52 Opus

  • Released in 1962
    Not sold in Japan

Commentary

A moderately sized enclosure with features of Olympus and Apollo.

It can be used either horizontally or vertically.

The high-frequency sound lens can be installed in either horizontal or vertical position, depending on the installation condition of the enclosure.

The exterior is finished on four sides.
Tauny Walnut, Oiled Walnut, Mahogany, Danish teak mahogany, Danish teak and Ebony.

There was also a model called C52SM for studio monitor.
The basic design of C52SM is the same as C52, the exterior is finished in gray, and the net grill is made of cloth.
