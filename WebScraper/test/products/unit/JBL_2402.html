Commentary

Horn type tweeter with high efficiency design designed for sound reinforcement.

The 2402 can be used from a low band of 2.5 kHz or higher, and the directional characteristic becomes a 40 ° cone at 10 kHz.

Baffle mounting holes are 8.1 cm in diameter.
