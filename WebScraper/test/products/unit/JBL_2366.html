Commentary

JBL bi-radial horn with a unique shape.

This series is a constant directional horn developed for sound reinforcement.
The 2366 is designed for long-distance use with 47 ° x27 ° directivity, and the sound pressure is 3 dB higher than that of the 2365, and extremely uniform high sound pressure can be obtained from 500 Hz to 16 kHz within the horizontal 47 ° range.

The 2360/2365/2366 has a 79.5 cm square horn mouse.

The driver unit to be connected is 2441 or 2482, but by using throat adapter 2327, 2.5 cm diameter drivers such as 2410, 2425J, 2461 etc. can be used.
