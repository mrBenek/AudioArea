Commentary

20 cm double cone type speaker unit.

A unique equalizer ball (vertical equalizer) is mounted on the front of the high-frequency cone. It uses the diffraction of sound waves to align the wave surface and flatten the high-frequency characteristics.

A unique UL edge is used for the edge to achieve high compliance.

By using high-purity aluminum wire for the voice coil, the high-frequency reproduction limit is extended.
