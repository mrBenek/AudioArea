Commentary

A special horn tweeter limited to 300 pieces with a professional driver equipped with a wood horn.

The driver unit uses a diaphragm made of precisely molded hard aluminum alloy and a φ 110 mm ferrite magnet.

Newly designed conical type wood horn has been adopted to realize excellent pointing characteristics with flat 30 ° C characteristics.

Can be used from low cross-bar frequency and can be combined with various types of woofer from 20 cm class to 30 ~ 40 cm class.

The horn part has a dark blue finish.
