Commentary

A high-frequency power pack designed to extend the high-frequency range of JBL instrument speakers.

Used when the reach distance is short.

The 2901 consists of a horn / lens driver 2461, a horn 2301, and a crossover 3 kHz network that can be connected to systems with 4, 8, or 16 Ω inputs of less than 300W.

Directional shows a 90 ° cone pattern.
