Commentary

A tweeter that uses a ribbon shape.

It uses a ribbon-type structure that vibrates by supplying current directly to a vibrating foil placed in a magnetic flux.
The entire 9 μ m ultra-thin vibrating foil is driven to achieve flat and smooth frequency characteristics up to 100 kHz.
