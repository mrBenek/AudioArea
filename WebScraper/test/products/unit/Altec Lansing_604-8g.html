Commentary

604 series 2-way 38 cm coaxial type speaker unit combined with multi-seller horn.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.

A horn type unit using a multi-seller horn is mounted in the high frequency range.
In the 604-8G series, the transient response characteristics are improved by reducing the weight of the vibration mass of the diaphragm, and at the same time, linearity for dynamic margin is also improved.
