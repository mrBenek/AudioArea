Commentary

Thin 2-way separate type in-car speaker.

A 20 cm cone type woofer using PP cone is mounted in the low range.

The high-frequency band is equipped with an RP full-surface drive type tweeter.

It is applicable to high power output by adopting the heat resistant voice coil bobbin.
It also uses a magnet with excellent magnetic properties.
