Commentary

Speaker baffle with mounted TW-501, PW-201, network condenser.
It has the same configuration as 2S-208.

Unlike the coaxial two way system, by mounting the woofer and the tweeter on the same plane, interference distortion is reduced.

Since each speaker is measured and adjusted in an anechoic chamber, it can be handled in the same way as a single cone speaker.
