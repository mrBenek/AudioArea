Commentary

A 30 cm cone type wide-range speaker unit designed to deliver performance even when mounted in a 57L enclosure.

In order to obtain a flat response, the split vibration is reduced by making the cone paper shallow and applying optimum corrugation. In addition, an aluminum dome is used for the center dome.

The voice coil uses a precision and high-precision aluminum ribbon wire edgewise winding.
