Commentary

A horn developed to demonstrate the broadband characteristics of the driver unit TD-4001.
Used in Exclusive model2401twin and model2402.

Painted maple It is a stabilized dispersion type made of plywood, and it is possible to improve the directivity at 10 kHz or higher, which is difficult with conventional large horns.

We pursue a directional model pattern obtained from an actual sound field analysis to obtain a stable listening area and improve the sense of orientation.
