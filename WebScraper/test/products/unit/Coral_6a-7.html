Commentary

Double cone type 16 cm full range unit.

It is a combination of a 16 cm diameter woofer and a high-frequency subcone.

Around 1965, 8 Ω and 16 Ω normal models and 200 Ω and 400 Ω high impedance models were sold.
In the late 1970 s, only 8 Ω was sold.
