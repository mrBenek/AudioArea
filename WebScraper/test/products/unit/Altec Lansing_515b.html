Commentary

The most powerful 38 cm cone shaped woofer in the lineup of Altech's woofer at that time.

The structure of the magnetic circuit is designed for the horn load. It can be installed especially in the front load type 825B (A7) cabinet to enable large-scale and deep bass reproduction.
