Commentary

Wide range driver unit with linear phase equalizer.

Using precision machining technology, the linear phase equalizer is constructed so that each of the five slits draws a calculated curved section, thereby equalizing the length of the sound path from the diaphragm to the horn throat and eliminating phase disturbances.
Moreover, by making the aperture ratio of the slit linear, we have achieved broadband and flat frequency characteristics.

Linear rolling edge is adopted.
