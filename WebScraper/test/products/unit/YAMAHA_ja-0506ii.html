  • YAMAHA JA-0506II

  • ¥ 27,000 (1 piece, around 1980)

Commentary

Horn type tweeter, an improved version of JA-0506.

A V-shaped ring diaphragm made of hard duralumin with a thickness of 30 microns is used for the diaphragm, and tangential edges are integrally molded on both inside and outside.

Alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

A precision horn hollowed out from an aluminum round from an aluminum round bar.
