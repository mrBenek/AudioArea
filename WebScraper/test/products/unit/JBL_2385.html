  • ¥ 75,000 (1 unit, around 1985)

Commentary

Bi-radial horn designed for large to medium-sized SR.

The 2385 is a constant directional horn that has a slightly narrower horizontal directional angle than the 2380 and raised the sound pressure level by approximately 2 dB.
The throat diameter, horn length and opening size are the same as 2380.

The complex flare by computer analysis not only makes it easy to predict and set the service area with an accurate directional pattern, but also greatly improves frequency response and distortion factor by reducing mutual interference when using many horns.
