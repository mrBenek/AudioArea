Commentary

An exponential horn with an elliptical opening.

It is an aluminum die-cast horn with an emphasis on horizontal directivity. It can obtain a nearly uniform diffusion pattern within a range of 60 ° x30 °. It is compact, has a high sound pressure level, and is strong enough to be suitable for portable SR systems.

The recommended crossover frequency is 800 Hz.

Driver of 2.5 cm diameter throat can be installed by using separately sold throat adapter 2327 together.
