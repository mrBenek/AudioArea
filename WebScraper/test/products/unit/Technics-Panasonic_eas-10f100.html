Commentary

F100 series 10 cm cone type full range unit capable of high power regeneration.

The voice coil uses an edgewise wound voice coil with copper-coated aluminum ribbon wire, and has a highly heat-resistant design that can withstand temperatures of 200 ℃.

By thoroughly implementing anti-vibration measures at the edge, we aim to enhance the vocal band.
