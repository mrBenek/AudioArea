Commentary

Professional series 38 cm cone type woofer.

The difference between JA 3882 and JA 3882 b is the impedance, JA 3882 is 8 Ω and JA 3882 b is 16 Ω.

Designed for professional use such as musical instruments with high input resistance and high efficiency.
It can be used in a wide variety of enclosures, including large enclosed, bass, back-load, and front-load enclosures.
