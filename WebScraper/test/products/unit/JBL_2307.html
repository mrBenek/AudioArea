Commentary

An exponential horn used in combination with a 2308 lens.

It has the same performance except for the difference between the 2308 and 2311 combination and the compatible driver, the mounting dimensions to the baffle and the crossover frequency are the same.

Throat diameter is 2.5 cm.

The combination of this horn and 2308 lens has excellent frequency and directional characteristics and is suitable for studio monitors.
It is mounted on a baffle plate for use.
