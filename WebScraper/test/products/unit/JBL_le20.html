Commentary

Wide range type cone type tweeter unit.

It can be used as a high-frequency correction for the full-range units such as D123-3 and D208, or as a 2-way system by combining with woofer units such as LE14A and LE10A.

Good performance can be obtained by selecting a network with a crossover frequency of 2 kHz or 2.5 kHz in accordance with the speaker unit to be combined.
