Commentary

A 20 cm cone type woofer that uses a double voice coil system.

It has a double-voice structure with two independent 8 Ω voice coils, so that one of the two voice coils can be used as a normal 8 Ω woofer, two in parallel can be used as an efficient 4 Ω woofer, and two in series can be used as a 16 Ω woofer.
In addition, if two voice coils are connected to Lch and Rch respectively, it can be used efficiently as a 3D woofer.

You can also use one voice coil as a pickup coil for motional feedback.

A repulse type magnetic circuit with extremely low leakage magnetic field is adopted for the magnetic circuit, making it possible to manufacture AV speaker systems.
