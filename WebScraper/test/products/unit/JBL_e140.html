Commentary

E-series 38 cm cone type woofer unit developed for musical instrument performance which boasts high efficiency, large allowable input and wide playback band.

The E140 is used for bass guitar and organ.

The adoption of an aluminum center dome improves the high-frequency characteristics.

There were 2 kinds of variations of 8 Ω and 16 Ω in the early period, but it seems to be only 8 Ω in the later period.
