  • ¥ 53,000 (1 unit, around 1983)

Commentary

Horn type tweeter with high efficiency design designed for business use such as SR system.

The magnet used for the magnetic circuit has been changed since 2402.

The combination of JBL's unique ring diaphragm and aluminum machined horn realizes high efficiency, input resistance, and broadband response.

By mounting it on a baffle form a line array configuration, handling power and directivity can be further improved.
