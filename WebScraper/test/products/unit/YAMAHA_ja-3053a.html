  • YAMAHA JA-3053A

  • ¥ 11,000 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

A 30 cm cone type speaker unit developed for mid-range use.

The cone paper made from carefully selected materials is blended with wool to attenuate the high range, and has a special cross-sectional shape to reproduce the rich middle range.

The 66 φ voice coil adopts edgewise winding of aluminum ribbon wire, and strong magnetic circuit is constituted by the adoption of strong magnet YM-5S.

It can also be configured as a 2-way speaker system with a tweeter.
