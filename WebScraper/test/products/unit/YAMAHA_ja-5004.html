Commentary

This NS type woofer unit has a large 420x540mm diaphragm and is designed to be used in two way system.

In order to make the diaphragm lighter and more rigid, a molding method is adopted in which expanded polystyrene is molded with an epoxy binder and then finished by special processing.

Yamaha's proud YM-5S magnet is used.

Because of the wide range design, it can also be used as a 2-way unit.
