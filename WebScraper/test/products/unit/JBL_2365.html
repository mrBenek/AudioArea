Commentary

Bi-radial horn with unique shape.

This series is a constant directional horn developed for sound reinforcement. It spreads with a constant directivity in a wide band from 500 Hz to 16 kHz, so that uniform on-axis and off-axis responses can be obtained.

The 2365 is designed for medium distance with 66 ° x46 ° directivity.
The aperture size is the same as 2360, but the sound pressure is raised by approximately 2 dB by controlling the directional beam slightly narrower.

The 2360/2365/2366 has a 79.5 cm square horn mouse and the 2360 and 2365 horn lengths have the same dimensions.

The driver unit to be connected is 2441 or 2482, but by using throat adapter 2327, 2.5 cm diameter drivers such as 2410, 2425J, 2461 etc. can be used.
