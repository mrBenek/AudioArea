Commentary

20 cm diameter cone type full range unit.

It has uniform characteristics over 8 octaves.

Aluminum die-cast frame and Alnico V magnet are adopted, and by storing in the return circuit of cast iron, leakage magnetic flux is reduced and the highest efficiency is demonstrated.

The ダンプド duralumin dome directly bonded to the voice coil bobbin enhances the high-frequency reproduction capability.

In the early stage, it was only 2115 with 16 Ω impedance, but after the latter half of 1970's, it was changed to 2 types of variation, 2115A with 8 Ω specification and 2115B with 16 Ω specification.
