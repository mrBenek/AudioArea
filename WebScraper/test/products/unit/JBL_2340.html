Commentary

A radial horn with a special shape developed for spaces with small depth.

The depth is reduced by the design that the horn throat is bent at a right angle.

It has excellent high-pass diffusion capability and is used for systems with a crossover frequency of 1.2 kHz.

Aluminum die-cast is used for the material, and the outside is coated with a special damping agent Lancer Plus to prevent resonance.

The mount is mounted directly on the baffle plate.
The mounting flange is attached as part of the body.
