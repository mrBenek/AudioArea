  • JBL 2213

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 unit, around 1978)

Commentary

A 30 cm cone type woofer with excellent transient response, high efficiency and high power reproduction.

The voice coil uses copper ribbon edge width winding.

A large Alnico V magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and the gap is narrowed to concentrate the magnetic flux to improve efficiency.

The 2213 is suitable for monitoring applications using a bass reflex enclosure.
