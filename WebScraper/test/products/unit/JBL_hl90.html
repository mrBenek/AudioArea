Commentary

A horn lens composed of a 91.4 cm wide slant plate and an elliptical exponential horn that is also used in Paragon.

The plates are designed based on careful calculation and are placed at regular intervals to enhance the diffraction effect of the lens to achieve a wide directivity of 140 ° horizontal and 45 ° vertical.

Can be used in combination with Driver Unit 375 for Middle Range.

Use the attached stand for installation.
