  • Pioneer PAX-20H

  • ¥ 5,650 (around 1974)

Commentary

20 cm coaxial 2-way speaker unit.

The woofer section uses high-compliance cone paper with roll edges and ceramic magnets.

The tweeter section is combined with a multi-cellular horn type tweeter.
