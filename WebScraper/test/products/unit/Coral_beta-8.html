Commentary

BETA series 20 cm double cone type full-range speaker unit.

The diaphragm is made of pulp, the main raw material, mixed with chemical fibers to reduce the unevenness of thickness and density seen in conventional speakers. In addition, carefully selected diaphragm is used in white color to avoid coloring.
Strong and high-density pulp is specially treated for the subcone for the high-frequency band.

The voice coil uses copper-coated aluminum wire that combines the advantages of copper and aluminum wires.

It uses Coral's unique skybar edge and star-shaped diffuser that expands directivity.

Uses a prominent frame and allows flat mounting on the baffle surface.
