Commentary

This is a 30 cm cone shaped woofer that inherits 136A features such as durability and reliability.

Equipped with a mass control ring on the back of the center dome, the playback band is smoothly extended to the ultra-low band while preventing deformation of the voice coil and cone paper.

It uses a large diameter voice coil of 10.2 cm diameter and a strong magnetic circuit of 5.4 kg weight and 12,000 gauss magnetic flux density using an Alnico V magnet, and the main parts are the same as 136A.
