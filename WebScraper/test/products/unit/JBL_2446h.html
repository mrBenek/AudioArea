Commentary

High frequency compression driver unit with throat diameter of 49 mm.
2446H is 8 Ω model and 2446J is 16 Ω model.

A phase plug with a coherent wave structure is adopted.

The diaphragm is made of titanium, and the edge of the unique diamond surround structure reduces metal fatigue and improves durability.

Ferrite magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.
