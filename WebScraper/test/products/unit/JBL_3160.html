  • ¥ 74,000 (1 unit, around 1986)

Commentary

A dividing network with a crossover frequency of 500 Hz.

The 3160 is a high-power type designed for SR.
In addition, the low-frequency impedance is set to 4 Ω to cope with the double woofer.

An impedance correction circuit is built into the low-frequency side, so that you can accurately cut the high-frequency range of the woofer.
In addition to the strapping bar type attenuation, a high-frequency boost circuit is provided to prevent the attenuation of the high-frequency range.

After careful testing, we employ high-voltage, non-inductive, non-polar capacitors developed specifically for networks, finely tuned low-loss inductors, resistors that withstand harsh use, and switches for high currents.
