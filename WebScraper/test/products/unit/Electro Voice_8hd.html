Commentary

A medium-range dilution horn made of zinc die-cast.

Directional characteristics are improved by special flare shape.
The radiation angle is horizontal 120 degrees in the vertical position and 90 degrees in the horizontal position over the whole band.

It can be used in combination with driver unit T25A or 1823M or crossover network X8.

As a rule, it is fixed to the baffle.
