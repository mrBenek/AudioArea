Commentary

The Wolverine series 38cm3-way coaxial speaker unit.

The LT15 has a 3-way structure that combines a double-cone woofer and a horn tweeter.

Excellent stability and linearity have been achieved by adopting a newly designed three sided suspension in the woofer part.

The tweeter section is equipped with a sonoface tweeter.
