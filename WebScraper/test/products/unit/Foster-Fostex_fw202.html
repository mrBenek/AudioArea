Commentary

20 cm diameter cone type woofer unit.

The FW202 is designed to provide high-quality bass playback even in a small enclosure and is suitable for multiple drives.

At the time of sale, there were 2 types of variations, 8 Ω and 16 Ω, but around 1976, only 8 Ω was available.
