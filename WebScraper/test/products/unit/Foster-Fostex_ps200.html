Commentary

A 20 cm double-cone type full-range speaker designed for professional use in consideration of sound quality, high efficiency, and high durability.

It can be used as a bounce speaker, mobile monitor speaker, or wall speaker.
It is also approved by the Emergency Broadcast Facility Committee.
