  • JBL 2370

  • ¥ 50,000 (1 unit, around 1983)

Commentary

Compact FRP bi-radial horn developed by JBL's new technology.

The horn is designed to have a smaller vertical dimension so that it becomes narrower as the directivity angle in the vertical direction becomes higher frequency, so that uniform response can be obtained in 90 degree range from 630 Hz to over 16 kHz in the horizontal direction.

The throat diameter is 2.5 cm.
