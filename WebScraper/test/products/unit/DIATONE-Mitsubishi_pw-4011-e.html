  • DIATONE PW-4011(B)

  • ¥ 80,000 (1 piece, around 1970)

Commentary

40 cm cone type woofer unit adopted for AS-4001, AS-4021, etc.
It has been developed for high-fidelity large speaker systems, especially for bass speakers with high undistorted maximum output sound pressure level.

The only difference between PW 4011 and PW 4011 b is the impedance.
