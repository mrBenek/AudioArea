  • CORAL 12SA-7

  • ¥ 29,000 (1 piece, around 1980)

Commentary

3-way speaker system kit.

It uses a 30 cm cone woofer for the low range, a semi-hard dome squaker for the middle range, and a semi-hard dome tweeter for the high range.

A set of level controls, network, cords, screws, etc. can be easily assembled into a speaker system by combining an enclosure kit (sold separately) with a baffle plate.
