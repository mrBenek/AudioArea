Commentary

An in-vehicle 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter unit that achieves both high efficiency and high sound quality despite its small size.

A strong neodymium magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

With the attached bracket and spacer, the direction of the tweeter can be set to the optimum direction, and with the attached cable with built-in low-cut filter, 2-way system can be assembled easily.
