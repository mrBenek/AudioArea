Commentary

A diffuse horn with wide directivity of 140 ° x60 °.

Sound waves pass through six exponential horns and are spread out through a common aperture.

The throat is square, and a 5 cm throat driver is mounted using a separately sold 2328 adapter.
You can also use a 2329 adapter for twin drive.

This horn was originally developed and designed for concert halls. It is made of high-density wood.
