Commentary

E series 25 cm cone type wide range unit developed for musical instrument performance which boasts high efficiency, large allowable input and wide playback band.

E110 is perfect for playing lead guitar, rhythm guitar, organ and vocals.

An SFG magnetic circuit is adopted for the magnetic circuit to reduce distortion.

There were 2 kinds of variations of 8 Ω and 16 Ω in the early period, but it seems to be only 8 Ω in the later period.
