Commentary

A horn lens consisting of a square exponential horn and an acoustic lens with a hyperbolic plate.

The plate is bent into a 45 ° waveform to shorten the lens depth. It is also called Gold Wing because of its golden finish.

Directional characteristics are horizontal 100 ° and vertical 45 °.

It is recommended to mount it on the enclosure and use it with baffle plate.
