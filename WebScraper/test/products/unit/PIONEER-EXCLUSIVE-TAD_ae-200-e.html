  • Pioneer AE-200

  • ¥ 21,400 (a set of two cars, around 1975)

Commentary

An enclosure kit with an internal volume of 30 liters that can be used for various configurations by using a separately sold dedicated baffle plate.

A stronger structure is realized by making the joint part a structure in which the groove firmly bites into it.

The exterior has a wood finish on the plug. Also comes with a grill net.

There were 2 types of exclusive baffle plates of EB-200 and EB-202 separately sold for AE-200.
The EB 200 is a special baffle plate to which the commercially available units PW-A21 and PT-20 can be attached, and the EB 202 is a special baffle plate to which the 2-way speaker system kit AS 202 can be attached.
