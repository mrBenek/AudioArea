Commentary

An equipment cabinet that can be used as a stereo set by combining with Subline I or Subline II.

The structure is exactly the same as the C58 Delphi, and you can mount a record player, JBL SG 520, etc. inside.

There were country oak finish and golden oak finish on the exterior.
