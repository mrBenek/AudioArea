Commentary

A round acoustic lens with a set of plates with numerous holes attached to a horn at the exit.

The perforated screen is ring-shaped, hyperbolic when viewed from the side of the assembled plate opening, and conical overall.
The directional characteristic describes a conical pattern.

It is used for high-frequency playback above 1.2 kHz, and is especially designed for high-frequency playback in 2 - to 3-way systems.

The driver is compatible with throat diameter of 2.5 cm.

When used, it is mounted on the baffle plate by the attached clamp ring.
