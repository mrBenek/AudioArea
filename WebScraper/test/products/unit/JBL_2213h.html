Commentary

A 30 cm cone woofer developed for professionals.

2213H is mounted on 4311B.

The cone uses JBL original cone paper with Lancer Plus coating. The voice coil uses edge-wise rolled copper ribbon voice coil.

An SFG magnetic circuit is adopted for the magnetic circuit to realize a low distortion factor.

Rigidity is increased by adopting an aluminum die-cast frame with ribs.

Mounted and used in a bass reflex enclosure.
