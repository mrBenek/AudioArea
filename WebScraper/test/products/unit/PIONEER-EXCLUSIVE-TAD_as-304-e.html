  • Pioneer AS-304

  • ¥ 20,000 (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system kit with a combination of 30 cm woofer, cone mid-range and horn type tweeter.

A 30 cm cone type woofer with carbon fiber cone is used for the low range.

10 cm cone type mid-range is used for the mid-range.
The diaphragm uses a dry pre-cone in consideration of the connection with the woofer, and the back cavity reduces interference from the woofer.

A multi-cellar horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.

A network with level control for middle and high frequencies is included.

The AS 304 can be used as a speaker system by combining the Enclosure Kit AE 300 and the dedicated baffle EB 304.
