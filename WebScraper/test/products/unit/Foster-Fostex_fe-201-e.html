Commentary

A 20 cm full-range speaker unit with improved high-frequency characteristics by adopting a double cone system.

In the double-cone system, the main cone is in charge of the low and middle sound range, and the subcone in the center works in the high sound range to expand the frequency characteristics and improve the directional characteristics.

Kapok is used for the corn.

We recommended about 140L when putting it in an airtight box.
