Commentary

2-way speaker system kit.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
FB cone is used for the diaphragm.

A soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.
Animal fiber is used for the diaphragm to obtain a soft high frequency.

There was a special enclosure LE-B20 sold separately.
