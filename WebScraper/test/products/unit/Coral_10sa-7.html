  • CORAL 10SA-7

  • ¥ 19,500 (1 piece, around 1980)

Commentary

3-way speaker system kit.

It uses a 25 cm cone woofer for the low range, a 4 cm dome squaker for the middle range, and a 2 cm dome tweeter for the high range.

A set of level controls, network, cords, screws, etc. can be easily assembled into a speaker system by combining an enclosure kit (sold separately) with a baffle plate.
