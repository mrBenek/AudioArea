Commentary

A 20 cm cone type full-range speaker unit that utilizes the basic design of the D130 with a small aperture as it is.

Uses 5.1 cm aluminum ribbon voice coil and strong magnetic circuit.

By directly attaching an ultra-thin aluminum center dome to the voice coil bobbin, the regenerative capacity in middle and high areas is enhanced.
