Commentary

Front load horn type enclosure which is an improved version of 4550.

In 4550 bka, it has been changed to a bass reflex structure.

Equipped with 2 38 cm diameter woofers.

It can be used up to 800 Hz and play back up to 40 Hz. The frequency response below 100 Hz is compensated by the phase inversion operation.

4550 BKS is a domestic type.
