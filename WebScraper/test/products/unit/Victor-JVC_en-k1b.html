Commentary

CW horn type enclosure kit designed based on the theory of backload horn.

38 cm unit can be mounted.

It is processed using NC router which boasts precision of 1/100mm unit by computer linkage.

There was an adapter kit which can attach a squaker and a tweeter separately.
