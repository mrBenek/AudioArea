Commentary

A driver unit for mid-range applications with improved durability and reliability while maintaining the highest performance standards for Hi-Fi applications.

It is used in speaker systems such as the SENTRY III and SENTRY IVB.

A non-metal dome unique to Electrovoice is used for the diaphragm.

Magnetic efficiency is improved by adopting bottom yoke made of sintered steel, and temperature rise is reduced by more than 20 ℃ by cooling fin effect.
