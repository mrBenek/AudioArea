Commentary

Speaker System Kit Enclosure kit designed for the AS-252.

A stronger structure is realized by making the joint part a structure in which the groove firmly bites into it.

The exterior has a wood finish on the plug. Also comes with a grill net.

The AE 300 has a speaker baffle EB 252 sold separately.
The EB 252 is a special baffle for mounting the Speaker System Kit AS 252. It is processed to mount the speaker unit.
