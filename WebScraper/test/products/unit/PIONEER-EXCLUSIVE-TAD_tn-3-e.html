  • TAD TN-3

  • ¥ 65,000 (around 1991)

Commentary

Interface network for bi-amplifier drive of driver unit TD 4001 equipped with Woofer TL 1601 a and horn TH 4001.

The HF circuit contains an HP filter to protect the HF driver from the DC leakage accident of the power amplifier, and a compensation circuit to flatten the impedance and sound pressure characteristics, so that efficient matching with the power amplifier can be achieved.

In addition to the enlargement of the LF and HF dedicated input terminals, it is designed with low loss and low leakage common to the TN series.
