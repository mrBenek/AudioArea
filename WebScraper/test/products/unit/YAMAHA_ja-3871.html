  • YAMAHA JA-3871

  • ¥ 60,000 (1 piece, around 1980)

Commentary

38 cm cone-shaped woofer designed with low fo and low distortion factor as the theme.

JA-3870 uses an Alnico magnet for the magnetic circuit, but JA-3871 uses a ferrite magnet.
The output sound pressure level is higher than JA-3870.

It is designed for use in a bass reflex enclosure of approximately 150L to 200L.
