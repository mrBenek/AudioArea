Commentary

Large radial horn developed for professional use.

It can be used in a wide range of applications such as high-power outdoor PA systems, theaters and halls.

A crossover frequency of 500 Hz or higher is appropriate, but it can also be used as a speech system with a phenol diaphragm driver and a moderate low-frequency cut.

Can also be used by vertically stacking.
For example, when four 2350 s are stacked, the effective vertical diffusion angle is from 40 ° to 15 °, which is several dB more sensitive than a single horn / driver.

Throat Adapter 2328 and 2329 sold separately are required for use.
