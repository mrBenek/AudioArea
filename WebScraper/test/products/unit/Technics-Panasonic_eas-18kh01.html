Commentary

18 cm diameter dome type tweeter unit with wide range.

By analyzing the radiation theory of a dome-shaped diaphragm and making the baffle plate surrounding the diaphragm spherical, ideal directivity has been obtained.
In addition, the use of a double damper completely prevents rolling at large amplitude, and the use of a back cavity system with a wider rear cavity keeps the lowest resonance frequency low to enhance the mid-range.

Titanium diaphragm is used for diaphragm.
