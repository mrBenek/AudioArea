Commentary

Dome type tweeter which adopted beryllium diaphragm.

Newly developed beryllium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.
Beryllium is a material that is extremely light and highly rigid. By using beryllium in the diaphragm, split vibration is suppressed, and flat characteristics up to 55 kHz, which greatly exceeds the audible band, and excellent transient characteristics are realized.
