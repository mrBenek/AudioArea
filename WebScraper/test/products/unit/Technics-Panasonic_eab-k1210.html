  • Technics EAB-K1210

  • ¥ 18,800 (1 unit, around 1979)

Commentary

Two way speaker system kit with 12 cm passive radiator and 12 cm cone type full range unit.

A 5 cm horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.

A 12dB/oct. network with an impedance correction circuit is used.
The positions of the parts are printed on the board for easy assembly.
