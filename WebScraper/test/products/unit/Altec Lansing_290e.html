Commentary

Driver unit for large acoustic PA system that can be used outdoors.
At the Japan World Exposition, 1803B was used in combination with the speaker tower for the water stage in front of the square.

Waterproof type with a completely sealed structure.

When used in combination with a dedicated throat and a multi-seller horn or 311-90 or 311-60, sound pressure distribution characteristics of an ideal directional pattern can be obtained.
