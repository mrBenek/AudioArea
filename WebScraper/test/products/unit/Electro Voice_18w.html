Commentary

A 46 cm cone woofer designed for enclosed and bass-reflex enclosures.

The 18 wk unit is designed specifically for the Klepush Horn and is suitable for the woofer of various horn-type speaker enclosures. The 18 wk unit is designed to be used as a 4 ohm unit in general enclosures such as enclosed or bass reflex enclosures, and as a 16 ohm unit in horn-type enclosures.
