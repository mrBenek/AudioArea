Commentary

A 38 cm cone type woofer developed for large monitor speakers.

It is used in 4435 studio monitor.

The 2234H has been developed to provide the best balance between conversion efficiency and low-frequency response and can provide excellent low-frequency response in a relatively small enclosure.

By adopting a unique SFG magnetic circuit and reinforced suspension, low distortion rate and high input resistance characteristics are realized.
