Commentary

A 20 cm cone type woofer equipped with a low distortion magnetic circuit (Lo-D Actuator) in addition to a ギャザードエッジ.

A ギャザードエッジ is used at the edge part to lower resonance frequencies and greatly improve the mechanical straightness of piston motion.

Lo-D Actuator is used for the magnetic circuit.
The Lo-D Actuator uses an aluminum short ring and a copper short cap to control the current flowing through the voice coil, greatly reducing the second and third harmonic distortion. As a result, the distortion rate in the mid-frequency range reaches approximately 0.3% at an input power of 1W.

Aluminum is used for the bobbin of the voice coil to improve input resistance.
In addition, a long travel type with a voice coil wound widely is adopted in order to withstand large amplitude and realize low distortion.

Special latex is applied on the back side of the cone paper to suppress the split vibration of the cone.
