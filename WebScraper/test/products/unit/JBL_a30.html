  • JBL A30

  • ¥ 66,000 (a set of two cars, around 1983)

Commentary

Coaxial 2-way type in-vehicle speaker unit.

An elliptical woofer (15.2cmx22.9 cm) is used for the low range, and an original piezoelectric high-range element is used for the high range.
Excellent sound pressure level and sound quality are obtained by adopting a strong magnetic circuit with low distortion rate, a precision processed long voice coil with greatly improved input resistance characteristics, and a half roll edge with reduced distortion.

Resonance is reduced by adopting an aluminum die-cast frame, and a metal mesh grill with good sound dissipation is adopted on the front.

There was a mount adapter as an option sold separately.
