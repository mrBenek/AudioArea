Commentary

A 20 cm cone type full range unit corresponding to the magnetic circuit modification type of LE8T.

The diaphragm uses cone paper coated with white damping material, and the center dome uses a 0.05 mm ultra-thin processed aluminum dome.

The voice coil of aluminum wire edgewise winding is used for the magnetic circuit.
