Commentary

The highest class driver unit that represents JBL.

High efficiency is achieved by a 10.2 cm diameter large diaphragm, a ribbon wire voice coil and a 10.8 kg weight class magnetic circuit with a 20,500 gauss magnetic flux density using an Alnico V magnet.

Medium range playback from 500 Hz to 10 kHz is possible.

Can be used in combination with HL88, HL89, HL90, HL93 with throat diameter of 5 cm.
