Commentary

A multi-cellar horn designed to ensure wide sound pressure distribution characteristics required in theater halls, etc.
The 1803B was used in combination with the 290E as a speaker tower for the water stage in front of the square at the Japan World Exposition.

The driver unit can be mounted from one to a maximum of four by changing the throat.
