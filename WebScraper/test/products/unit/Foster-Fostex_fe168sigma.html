  • FOSTEX FE168 Σ

  • ¥ 11,800 (1 piece, around 1992)

Commentary

Σ series 10 cm double cone type full range unit that adopts high rigidity round frame.

Uses newly designed high-rigidity round frame. In addition, FE168 Σ has been changed to mount with 8 stops.

It uses a strong and highly rigid magnetic circuit with 2 layers of large magnets, and has high efficiency, low frequency characteristics with strong damping, and excellent transient characteristics.

In consideration of compatibility with the conventional Σ Series, the mounting hole dimensions and main standards have been designed to match as much as possible, and can be replaced with FE106 Σ.

Designed for backload horn enclosures, but can also be used with bass reflex enclosures.
