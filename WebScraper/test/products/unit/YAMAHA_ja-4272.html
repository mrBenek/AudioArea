Commentary

Horn type tweeter with beryllium ring diaphragm.

It consists of a 200 mg lightweight V-shaped ring diaphragm of 50 mm outer diameter and 30 mm inner diameter made of high-purity beryllium with a thickness of 50 microns, and a short-cut, low-cutoff exponential spherical horn that takes sound quality and directional characteristics into consideration.

An internal magnetic circuit with an Alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.
