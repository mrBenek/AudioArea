Commentary

A 20 cm double cone type full-range unit that is also adopted by GEORGIAN.

In the 70's catalog, it was written as 16 Ω specification, but in the 72's catalog, it was 8 Ω specification.

SP8B is compatible with TweetBlock Kit BB1 (sold separately).
BB1 is a set kit of T35 driver unit, X36 crossover network, AT37 attenuator and wiring material.
