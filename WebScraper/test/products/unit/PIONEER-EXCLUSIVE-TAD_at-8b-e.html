Audio Database

  • Pioneer AT-8B

  • ¥ 1,100 (around 1974)

Commentary

A level control for the network part of a speaker.

Unlike general volumes, there is no change in impedance seen from the input side.

Attenuation can be read directly on the dB scale.

Fine adjustment is possible with the knob rotating 300 degrees.
