Commentary

This 38 cm corn type woofer unit has been improved while inheriting the tradition.

Pure pulp cone is used for the diaphragm.
This product is made from carefully selected natural pulp with long fibers. The bonds between fibers are strengthened by natural drying. In addition, the front surface of the cone is coated with corrugation rib, and the back surface of the cone is coated with JBL's original Aqua Plus coating. It has a high rigidity that does not cause breakup even at high output.

Lightweight and low-loss EPDM foam rubber with high durability and weather resistance is used for the edge. In addition, NOMEX double damper with symmetric structure which cancels even order harmonic distortion is used for the damper.
As a result, a highly linear cone operation with less distortion is realized from the minute output to the large output.

The magnetic circuit is equipped with an Alnico 5 dg magnet.
In this circuit, a large-capacity aluminum short ring and a copper-steel laminated inner gap ring are provided under the voice coil gap so as to surround the magnet, to stabilize the local magnetic field and suppress magnetic modulation to overcome the problem of demagnetization associated with high-power drive.

The frame has a bent frame structure to rectify the unit back pressure.
It is also equipped with a bent-gap cooling mechanism and three air tunnels from the pole piece to the bucket cover effectively dissipate heat generated by the voice coil.

Uses the same frame size as JBL15 inch woofer.
