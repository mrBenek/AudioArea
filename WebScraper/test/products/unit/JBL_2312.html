Commentary

An exponential horn used in combination with a 2308 lens.

2312 has a throat diameter of 2.5 cm.
Since the horn length is long, it can be used at a low crossover frequency.

This combination of horn and lens has excellent frequency and directional characteristics, making it suitable for studio monitors.
It is mounted on a baffle plate for use.
