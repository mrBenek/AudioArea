  • Pioneer PIM-25A/PIM-10

  • ¥ 4,800 (around the mid 1960 s?)

Commentary

25 cm speaker unit with mechanical 2-way configuration.

There is only one voice coil, and the cone paper has a structure in which a tweeter cone is joined to the center of the woofer with a corrugation edge.

When it was changed from inches to centimeters, the model number was also changed from PIM-10 to PIM-25A.
