Commentary

8000 series 20 cm coaxial 2-way speaker.

The 8140H is a 20 cm cone shaped woofer with a Piezo Ceramic tweeter.

The 8000 Series is a high-efficiency speaker series developed for broadcasting, BGM and other distributed sound systems. It features high intelligibility, wide directivity and high input resistance.
