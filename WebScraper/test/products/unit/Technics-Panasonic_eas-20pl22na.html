Commentary

20 cm diameter cone type woofer unit.

At the time of sale, there were 2 types of variations, 8 Ω and 16 Ω.
Since the EAS-20PL22NA was an 8 ohm specification, the 16 ohm specification was probably the model number EAS-20PL22NB. The 16 ohm specification model ended relatively early and the EAS-20PL22NA was sold as EAS-20PL22.
