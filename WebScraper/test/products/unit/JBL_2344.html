Commentary

Bi-radial horn with unique shape.

The unique composite flare shape of computer analysis realizes a wide constant directivity of 100 ゜ x100 ゜.
In addition, uniform diffusivity is obtained from 1.25 kHz to 16 kHz in both horizontal and vertical directions.

Since the depth dimension of the 2344 is short, phase matching with the woofer can be easily achieved even when mounting the baffle.

To be used in combination with a 2425J driver.
