Commentary

This is a horn driver unit for mid-range applications with low cost by changing the magnetic circuit of JA-4270.

It uses a special aluminum alloy diaphragm integrally molded with tangential edges, and a pure aluminum edgewise wound lightweight voice coil.
The equalizer and throat are machined from pure zinc.
