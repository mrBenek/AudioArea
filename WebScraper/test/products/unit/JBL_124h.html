Commentary

A 30 cm cone type woofer unit with the same characteristics and sound quality as JBL studio monitor woofer.

10.2 cm diameter voice coil is used.

Equipped with a unique mass control ring, it extends the playback band to the ultra-low frequency range and improves characteristics such as low distortion.
