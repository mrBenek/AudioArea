Commentary

A horn type tweeter unit based on the SG-16TT and incorporating the know-how obtained in the development of the SG-370BL/DX.

The SG 160 combines the titanium dome diaphragm used in the SG-16TT with a 130 mm φ magnetic circuit.

SG-160 can be upgraded to SG-160BL in which the diaphragm is changed to beryllium dome.
