Commentary

3-way 20 cm coaxial speaker unit.

A medium-sound cone is mounted on a low-fo woofer with excellent transient characteristics and directional characteristics, and a horn type tweeter with light mass and high efficiency is added.

A high-frequency level attenuator and an L/C-type 12dB/oct network are included.
