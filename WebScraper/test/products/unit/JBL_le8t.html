Commentary

20 cm single cone type full range unit.

The cone paper is coated with a special white dump material to obtain optimum mass and rigidity. The center dome uses an aluminum center dome with an ultra-thin 0.05 mm finish.

Transient is improved by 3 kg magnetic circuit using Alnico V magnet and aluminum wire edgewise wound voice coil.

Aluminum die-cast frame is used for the frame.
