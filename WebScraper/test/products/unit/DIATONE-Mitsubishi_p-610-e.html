Commentary

Diatone's most representative 16 cm full-range speaker unit.
The P-610 was the first model, and after detailed model changes, it was sold almost as it was when it was released until 1996.

The P-610 is the first in the industry to pass the BTS standard established by NHK.

The initial model P-610 is equipped with a matching transformer and can be used with 2 impedance types of 6 Ω and 600 Ω.
For this reason, the mounting hole of the transformer remains in the P-610 series which was released later.
In addition, only impedance is different from P-610A/P-610B.

A pure iron is used for the magnetic circuit to increase effective flux density, and excellent damping and high efficiency are obtained for a 16 cm class.

The cone section adopts a matched resonance form designed by the direct attack method, and achieves broadband characteristics with a single cone by corrugation and free edge.
