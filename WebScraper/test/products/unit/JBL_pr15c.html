Commentary

38 cm cone type passive radiator to be used with 38 cm cone type woofer 136A.

It uses cone paper and frame with adjusted mass and compliance.

The dynamic range and response in the low frequency range can be improved by installing it in an enclosure with an internal volume of 114L to 169L.
