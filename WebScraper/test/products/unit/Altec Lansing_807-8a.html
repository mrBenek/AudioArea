  • ALTEC LANSING 807-8A

  • ¥ 49,800 (1 unit, released in 1970)

Commentary

Driver unit adopted for A7-8 etc.

Wide-band playback is possible when combined with a 811B sectoral horn.

The newly developed Symbiotik (Sympbiotic) diaphragm is used.
A new assembly combining a polyimide film and an aluminum alloy diaphragm is used for this diaphragm, and the allowable input is approximately twice that of the conventional model.

The voice coil uses an edge-wise wound voice coil using a flat aluminum ribbon.
