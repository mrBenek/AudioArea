Commentary

20 cm diameter cone type full range unit.

It has uniform characteristics over 8 octaves.

It can be used in a wide range of applications such as PA by line array or single monitor mounted in bass reflex type.

2115H has two types of impedance, 2115H has 8 Ω and 2115J (2115 hj) has 16 Ω.
