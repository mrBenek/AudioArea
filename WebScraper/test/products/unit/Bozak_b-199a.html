  • BOZAK B-199A

  • ¥ 29,700 (1 unit, around 1966)

Commentary

This 30 cm cone type woofer unit was used for the low range of the ボザーク speaker system at that time.

Felted paper cone with wool embedded in pulp is used for the diaphragm to obtain light weight, hard and high compliance as well as low distortion bass.

An Alnico V magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and an aluminum ribbon coil with a diameter of 4 cm is used for the voice coil.
