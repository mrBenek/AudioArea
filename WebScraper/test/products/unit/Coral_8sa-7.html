  • CORAL 8SA-7

  • ¥ 12,000 (1 piece, around 1980)

Commentary

2-way speaker system kit.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range and a 4 cm dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

A set of level controls, network, cords, screws, etc. can be easily assembled into a speaker system by combining an enclosure kit (sold separately) with a baffle plate.
