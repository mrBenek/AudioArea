  • CORAL 8CX-30

  • ¥ 3,600 (1 unit, around 1965)

Commentary

This 20cm2-way coaxial speaker unit was developed as a sister unit of the 8CX-50.

The 20 cm cone type woofer used for the low range has a skybar edge.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.

It has a built-in network.
