Commentary

This is a 30 cm cone type woofer that was adopted in 4310.

Uses precision processed 7.5 cm edgewise rolled copper ribbon voice coil, elaborate ultra-precision voice coil gap, Alnico V magnet, etc.

The magnet is housed in a return circuit made of cast iron to reduce the leakage speed per hour.
