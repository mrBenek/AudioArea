Commentary

Driver unit used for A7-8 and Late Valencia.

The magnetic circuit is different between 806-8A and 802-8D.

Broadband playback is possible when combined with 511B and 811B horns.

High performance is secured by the equalizer using aluminum alloy diaphragm and special double slit structure with precision processing.
