Commentary

Professional series 38 cm cone type woofer developed with the concept which is completely common with JA 3882.

The difference between JA 3881 and JA 3881 b is the impedance, JA 3881 is 8 Ω and JA 3881 b is 16 Ω.

Designed for professional use such as musical instruments with high input resistance and high efficiency.
