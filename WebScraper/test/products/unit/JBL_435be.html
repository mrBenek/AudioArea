Commentary

Driver unit employing forged pure beryllium diaphragm.

A 3-inch (75 mm) diameter diaphragm, the first of its kind as a JBL compression driver, is made by forging beryllium, which is difficult to process, using new technology.
The diaphragm is coated with JBL's original Aqua Plus coating to eliminate split vibration. This ensures a wide range of piston motion over 15 kHz and provides excellent regeneration capability that can also be used as a - and high-frequency driver for 2-way systems.

A high flux neodymium magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and a lightweight aluminum ribbon wire wrapped around a highly heat-resistant bobbin is used for the voice coil.

The phase plug uses the rapid flare type used by JBL Professional Unit to reduce the generation of second harmonic distortion.

The housing uses an aluminum die-cast back cover with a rib-type heat sink structure.

By using separately sold Hon Slow and adapter, it is possible to combine 2-inch opening / 4-inch driver horn.
