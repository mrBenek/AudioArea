Commentary

This unit has undergone a model change of the P-610.

There were 3 kinds of patterns such as A, AT and AJ for P-610A in the initial stage.
A is B. T. S standard product without transformer and 16 Ω, P-610AT is B. T. S standard product and 600 Ω (16 Ω without transformer), P-610AJ is JIS standard product and 16 Ω.

It seems that there was actually no difference in quality between the B. T. S standard product and the JIS standard product, and it seems that the B. T. S standard product was the one that was picked out at random from the JIS standard product and actually measured it and attached the data.

In addition, P-610B is a JIS standard product with 8 Ω impedance.
