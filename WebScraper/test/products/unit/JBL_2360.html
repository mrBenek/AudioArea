Commentary

JBL bi-radial horn with a unique shape.

This series is a constant directional horn developed for sound reinforcement. It spreads with a constant directivity in a wide band from 500 Hz to 16 kHz, so that uniform on-axis and off-axis responses can be obtained.

The 2360 is designed based on computer analysis for short-range applications with a 93 ° x46 ° directivity. It spreads uniform sound pressure over a wide area with a minimum number of units, and has a square aperture for ease of stack use.

The horn body is made of fiberglass reinforced resin, which is robust and has low resonance, and the aluminum throat section has a tag at a position that takes into consideration the center of gravity when the driver is mounted.

The 2360/2365/2366 has a 79.5 cm square horn mouse and the 2360 and 2365 horn lengths have the same dimensions.

The driver unit to be connected is 2441 or 2482, but by using throat adapter 2327, 2.5 cm diameter drivers such as 2410, 2425J, 2461 etc. can be used.
