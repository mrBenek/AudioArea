  • JBL 2231A

  • ¥ 56,000 (1 unit, around 1978)

Commentary

A 38 cm cone shaped woofer with excellent transient response and high efficiency for high power regeneration.

The voice coil uses copper ribbon edge width winding.

A large Alnico V magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and the gap is narrowed to concentrate the magnetic flux to improve efficiency.

The 2231A is suitable for monitoring applications using a bass reflex enclosure.
