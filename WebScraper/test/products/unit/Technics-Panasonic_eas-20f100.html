Commentary

F100 series 20 cm cone type full range unit capable of high power regeneration.

The voice coil uses an edgewise wound voice coil with copper-coated aluminum ribbon wire, and has a highly heat-resistant design that can withstand temperatures of 200 ℃.

By adopting a pure copper short ring for the pole piece, distortion due to eddy current is reduced, and high-frequency characteristics are further improved.
