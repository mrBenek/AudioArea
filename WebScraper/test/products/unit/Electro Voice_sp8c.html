Commentary

A 20 cm full range unit that adopts a 2-way mechanical system.

The high-frequency characteristics are extended by the free edge subcone.

A voice coil of 5 cm in diameter is adopted in the vibration system, and high efficiency and maximum sound pressure of 107 db can be output.
Also, aluminum die-cast frame is used for the frame.

A 34-liter enclosure provides good low-frequency performance.
