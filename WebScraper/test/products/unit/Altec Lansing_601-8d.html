Commentary

A 30 cm coaxial composite speaker unit developed for a monitor.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range and a multi-seller horn made of zinc die-cast is used for the high range.

The 601-8D speaker was developed in consideration of limited installation space, and can be smaller than 38 cm coaxial speakers.
In addition, the directional characteristics are good up to the ultra-high range, and the frequency characteristics are up to 20 kHz, but the measured value of the high range limit is extended to 22 kHz or more.
