Commentary

A 30 cm coaxial speaker unit that combines a 30 cm cone woofer and a 5 cm cone tweeter.

The woofer uses a 7.6 cm voice coil.

With a depth of 11.1 cm, it is thin and can be used for embedding on walls.

Built-in network with crossover frequency of 3 kHz.
