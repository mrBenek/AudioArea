Commentary

VGC series 38 cm VGC series.
2226H is 8 Ω model and 2226J is 16 Ω model.

The VGC series realizes smooth characteristics without distortion with extremely low power compression (decrease in effective sensitivity) despite high output.
In order to achieve both high sound quality and high output, it uses the Ventilated Gap Cooling technology. By introducing air directly into the voice coil, heat radiation is remarkably improved. In addition, by polishing the Symmetrical Field Geometry magnetic circuit and suspension design, performance improvements such as low distortion rate, weight reduction and increased displacement have been achieved.
