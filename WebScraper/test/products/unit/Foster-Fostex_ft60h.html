Commentary

Horn type tweeter using 5-layer structure diaphragm.

A newly developed special 5-layer structure is used for the diaphragm to realize a lightweight and highly rigid diaphragm.
This diaphragm is made of a sandwich structure consisting of a thin glass fiber, a thermosetting resin, and an ultrathin thermoplastic resin film with a special metal vapor deposition on the surface. It is light, has a large elastic coefficient, and is made of very thin glass fiber. This structure eliminates the rattling sound that is often seen in metal diaphragms, and provides clear tone and excellent transient characteristics.

The horn part uses a hypervolic type with aluminum alloy cut all over.

Alnico magnet is used for the magnet.

It comes with a dedicated speaker base and can be mounted on top of an enclosure, etc.
When mounting on the baffle surface, make a mounting hole 71 mm in diameter in the baffle and fix it using the supplied mounting brackets (3 pieces).
