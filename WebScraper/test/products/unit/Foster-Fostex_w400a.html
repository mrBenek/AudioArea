Commentary

A 40 cm cone type woofer unit using an alnico magnet.

Fine ceramic coated multi-layer cone is used for the diaphragm.
A double damper is used for the support system.

3.1 kg extra-large alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

The standard enclosure is a 150 liter bass-reflex type, and even a double woofer can be used at around 300 liters.
