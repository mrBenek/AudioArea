Commentary

13 cm cone type full range unit of FE series.

Roll edge method is adopted.

In consideration of mounting in a small-capacity cabinet, a ferrite magnet is used so that the total height of the speaker is low.

There was a model change around 1976 and it was described as FE133New (FE133N) in the catalog.
Also, in the picture at that time, the magnetic circuit was changed, and there were 2 kinds of variations of 4 Ω and 8 Ω.
