Commentary

A radial horn designed for long throw that spreads sound far away.

This is the first full-size horn to use the radial expansion principle.

Since a desirable direct / indirect sound ratio can be obtained even at distances of 150m or more, it can be used in stadiums and airports.

To reduce weight and suppress resonance, a non-metal composite structure is used to reduce weight.
