Commentary

Horn type tweeter of bi-radial horn type.

By adopting a newly designed bi-radial horn, constant directivity of 100 ° in both horizontal and vertical directions over a wide range of 3 kHz to 20 kHz is realized.
It also has the advantage that second harmonic distortion is extremely small due to the rapid expansion of horn flare.
