Commentary

The front load horn enclosure was also used as a monitor system for the theatre.

A 2-way system can be configured by combining with a horn box B-301 of a different specification.

There are 2 variations of the appearance, walnut finish and black finish.

Major Specifications for System Examples Using B-300 and B-301

For High Range : Horn Type (M-100 + AH-501)
