Commentary

A high-efficiency, high input-resistant 40 cm cone type woofer unit with characteristics and sound quality suitable for speakers for musical instruments such as electric guitar and electric bass.

In addition to speakers for electric musical instruments, it can also be used for making bounce speakers.
