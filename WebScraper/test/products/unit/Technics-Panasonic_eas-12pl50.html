Commentary

12 cm cone type woofer unit.

Cone paper with high stiffness and moderate loss is used for the diaphragm. The unique manufacturing method reduces the deformation of the cone at the time of large input. In addition, the characteristic unevenness in the middle and high range is reduced.

By adopting a large damper, UL edge, and long stroke voice coil, distortion is reduced.
