  • ¥ 82,000 (1 unit, around 1983)

Commentary

Wide range driver unit with flat and low-distortion design over a wide range.

Suitable for studio monitor and SR.

A pure titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm. In addition, a diamond suspension developed from an original idea is used to extend the high range and improve the linearity.

2425J is 16 Ω type and 2425H is 8 Ω type.
