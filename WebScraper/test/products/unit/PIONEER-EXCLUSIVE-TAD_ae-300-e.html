  • Pioneer AE-300

  • ¥ 29,600 (a set of two cars, around 1975)

Commentary

Enclosure kit with an internal volume of 50 liters that can be used for various configurations by using a separately sold special baffle plate.

A stronger structure is realized by making the joint part a structure in which the groove firmly bites into it.

The exterior has a wood finish on the plug. Also comes with a grill net.

There were 3 kinds of exclusive baffle plates of EB-300, EB-302 and EB-304 separately sold for AE-300.
The EB 300 can be installed with the commercial units PW-A31, PM-50 and PT-50. The EB 302 is for the Speaker System Kit AS 302 only, and the EB 304 is for the Speaker System Kit AS 304 only.
