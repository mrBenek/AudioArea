  • JBL TM630

  • ¥ 56,000 (1 unit, around 1990)

Commentary

A 3-way onboard speaker system.

Uses low-distortion SFG magnetic circuit and edge-wise wound voice coil.

Polypropylene cone is used for the low range, and pure titanium diaphragm is used for the high range.
