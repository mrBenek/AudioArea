Commentary

High-pass compression driver unit with throat diameter 38 mm.
2447H is an 8 Ω model and 2447J is a 16 Ω model.

The phase plug has a coherent wave structure and is designed so that the phase plug can be directly connected to the horn throat.

The diaphragm is made of titanium and the edge of the unique diamond surround structure reduces metal fatigue and improves durability.

Ferrite magnet is used for the magnetic circuit of 2447.
