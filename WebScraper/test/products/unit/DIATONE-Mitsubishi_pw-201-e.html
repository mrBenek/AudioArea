Commentary

A 20 cm cone type woofer unit used in the 2S 208.

In addition to preventing low-frequency distortion by using reverse-curved cone paper, we have designed Qo to be 0.5 so that it can be mounted in a phase inversion cabinet and exhibits its true potential. We take special consideration for distortion.
