  • FOSTEX CSF10

  • ¥ 25,000 (1 pair, about 1992)

Commentary

A 10 cm aperture cone type full-range speaker for car use.

Natural pulp corn is used for the diaphragm.

It is equipped with a magnetic circuit using a large ferrite magnet and a high rigidity die cast round frame.

It can also be used as a mid-range 3-way multi-system.

Comes with a a dedicated grill and can be used for door embedding or rear board mounting.
