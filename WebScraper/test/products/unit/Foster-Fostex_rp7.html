Commentary

A 3-way automotive speaker using a full-drive unit.

For low range, it is equipped with rear panel embedded CAM cone 16 cm woofer.

The mid-range is equipped with an RP full-drive scoker, and the high-range is equipped with a tweeter of the same system.

Uses a tough frame structure.
