Commentary

Bi-radial horn which is a minor change model of 2344.

The unique shape realizes wide directivity of 100 ° x100 ° over a wide band.

Since the depth dimension of the 2344A is short, phase matching with the woofer can be easily achieved even when mounting the baffle.
