Commentary

Driver unit used in L101 and other speaker systems.

JBL technologies such as a 3.4 kg magnetic circuit using an Alnico V magnet, an ultra-thin diaphragm, and an edgewise wound voice coil with an aluminum ribbon wire of 4.4 cm in diameter.

Can be used in combination with horns HL91 and HL92 of throat diameter 2.5 cm.

LE175DLH is a horn type unit combining LE175 with 1217-1290 (HL87).
