Commentary

Horn type tweeter that employs an elliptical horn.

The 2403 has an elliptical opening to improve directional characteristics. The design also takes into consideration the phase characteristics when installing the baffle.

The mounting hole is 8.1 cm common to 2402, 2403 and 2405.
