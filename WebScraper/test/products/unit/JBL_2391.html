Commentary

A slant-plate acoustic lens designed for 2 - to 3-way systems where the listening position does not exceed 9m.

Each plate is calculated to be hyperbolic and is spaced at equal intervals to enhance the diffraction effect of the lens.

The 2391 is intended to be used at frequencies above 800 Hz, but it is more effective when used at frequencies above 1.2 kHz.
