Commentary

Coaxial composite speaker unit for PA designed for high efficiency and large sound output.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.

A horn-type unit with a water-drop equalizer called the Meat Bonner is used for the high-frequency band to achieve excellent directional characteristics.
The driver is equipped with a Phenolic diaphragm for improved input resistance.

The crossover network is attached to the speaker frame.
