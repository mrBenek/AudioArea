  • CORAL 8L-50

  • ¥ 4,200 (1 unit, around 1965)

Commentary

20 cm cone type woofer unit of Coral L50 series (Pervection series).

The L50 series uses a skybar edge to achieve low fo.

Alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

The プロミネントリング is included.

It seems that it was 16 Ω around 1965, but 8 Ω was sold around 1970 which is the latter period.
