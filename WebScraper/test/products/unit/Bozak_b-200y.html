  • BOZAK B-200Y

  • ¥ 18,100 (a set of two cars, around 1966)

Commentary

A 5 cm cone type tweeter unit using the same manufacturing method as B-209B.

The ultra-hard aluminum alloy cone is dumped with rubber from the front to ensure smooth characteristics.
In addition, the adhesion of rubber is improved by making holes in important parts of the aluminum cone.

Alnico V magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.
