Commentary

Radial horn with 1 inch throat.

The high-density laminated material of natural wood Itaya amicorum maple, which has good acoustic performance, is processed by high-precision machining. By making the material thick enough, it eliminates partition plates that tend to affect sound quality and characteristics.

It comes with a 1-inch throat and supports 1-inch horn drivers such as D100A.
