Commentary

A horn lens, nicknamed "bee's nest".

An acoustic lens consisting of 14 layers of porous ring-shaped punching metal with different inner diameters and an exponential horn is used. This unique acoustic lens provides a uniform pattern of 70 ° directivity in a conical shape.

Can be used in combination with driver 375 for mid-range of throat diameter 5 cm.

The attached stand is used for installation.
