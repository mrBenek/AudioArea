Commentary

Driver unit ideal for high-grade monitor system and indoor / outdoor loudspeaker systems

The diaphragm is made of 0.05 mm duralumin alloy by the pneumatic drawing method.

Aluminum ribbon wire edgewise wound voice coil, Alnico V magnet, pure silver impedance control ring, etc. are adopted for high performance.
