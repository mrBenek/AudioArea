Commentary

The tweeter unit used in the 2S 305 monitor speaker.

A small カーブドコーン with an effective radius of 2 cm is used in the cone section. The magnetic circuit that drives this vibration system is made of iron-cobalt and vanadium alloys, and is as strong as 18000 Gauss. This realizes a wide range of 1500 Hz to 35000 Hz.

In addition, it has excellent directional characteristics, such as -3dB at 30 ° and -6dB at 60 ° at 15 kHz.
