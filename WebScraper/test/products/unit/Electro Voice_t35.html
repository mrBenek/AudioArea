Commentary

Horn type tweeter that can be used from 3.5 kHz.

Uses Electrovoice's unique differential horn and Sonophase throat equalizer.

The voice coil is free-standing without a bobbin, and is firmly connected to a phenol-impregnated non-metallic diaphragm.
