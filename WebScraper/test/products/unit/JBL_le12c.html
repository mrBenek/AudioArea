Commentary

A 30 cm composite speaker unit with low and high frequency speakers and a network mounted on the same diecast frame.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is used in the low range. The woofer is driven by a magnetic circuit using a 7.6 cm diameter ribbon wire edgewise wound voice coil and an Alnico V magnet.
Low frequency characteristics are improved by special control cone suspension.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is used for the high range. The structure of the tweeter is equivalent to that of the LE20. By fixing it coaxially with the woofer, the sense of orientation is improved.
