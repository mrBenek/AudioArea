Commentary

Double cone type 38 cm full range unit.

The SP15 is used in speaker systems such as REGENCY and CARLTON.

Built-in LC circuit for high-frequency impedance correction.
In addition, it is equipped with level control for adjustment.

It was 16 Ω specification around 1970, but it became 8 Ω specification in 1972.

Compatible with separately sold Midrange Kit BB4 and TweetKit BB1.
