Commentary

A 46 cm cone type woofer of the K Series of speakers for musical instruments.

By using cone paper with the lowest combination of hardness, density and size, it is possible to make a powerful reproduction.

It is mounted on a horn load or a bass reflex enclosure for use.

Suitable for playing bass and organ.
