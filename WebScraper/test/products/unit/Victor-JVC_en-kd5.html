Commentary

Enclosure kit with 38 cm passive radiator

It is equipped with a 38 cm cone type passive radiator, and with 20 cm speakers you can get the same low sound as a large system.

It is processed using NC router which boasts precision of 1/100mm unit by computer linkage.

The cabinet is made of carefully selected Yonematsu for the baffle board, and the side, rear and top panels are made of special single-layer particleboards that do not make box squeals due to sound pressure.

Equipped with 270 ° rotating wing, baffle effect can be controlled according to listening conditions.
