Commentary

This horn-type tweeter unit is mass-produced without deteriorating the characteristics of the H-70HD.

By taking into consideration the clamp of the diaphragm and suppressing the non-linear movement of the vibration system, the distortion is reduced.

To improve the heat resistance of the voice coil, we use carefully selected heat-resistant materials.
In addition, a special lightweight wire is used for the voice coil to lighten the vibration system.

The diffusion effect is improved by adopting an acoustic lens with a special shape.
This acoustic lens is designed with an entrance to exit ratio of 10 : 7 and a thickness of 15 mm.

Die-cast is used for the horn part.
