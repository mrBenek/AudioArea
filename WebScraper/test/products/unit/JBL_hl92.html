Commentary

Horn lens designed for LE85 and LE175.

Since the effective length of the exponential horn is longer, the connection with the woofer becomes smoother.

It is designed to be most suitable for spreading in the middle and high frequency range above 800 Hz.

Directional characteristics are 90 ° horizontal and 45 ° vertical.

The mounting method is the same as HL91. It can be mounted in an enclosure or a baffle plate is used.
