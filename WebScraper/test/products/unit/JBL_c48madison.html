Commentary

Bookshelf enclosure designed for LE8 and S5 systems.

The unit is compatible with LE8, LE8T, and S5 systems. For LE8/LE8T, it is a bass reflex system, and for S5 system, it is a sealed system.

The exterior is finished on 4 sides.
There are five variations of finishes : Mahogany, Tawny Walnut, Oiled Walnut, Danish Teak and Ebony.
