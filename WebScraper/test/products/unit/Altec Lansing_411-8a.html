Commentary

38 cm cone type woofer unit for high compliance chair and high power input.

A large voice coil of 10 cm diameter and a 8.2 kg magnet are used for the magnetic circuit.

Combined with a large volume sealed cabinet, it is possible to play back low 16 Hz sound.
