Commentary

Dedicated enclosure for BETA-10 which is a beta series unit of Coral.

The speaker is mounted using a prominent method to suppress cavity resonance between the baffle plate and diaphragm and to prevent reflection interference between the baffle plate and diaphragm.

Acoustic plywood is used for the plate material.

Glass wool for sound absorption is pasted inside.
