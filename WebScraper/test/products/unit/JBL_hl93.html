Commentary

A horn lens that combines a short 11.7 cm exponential horn and a 25 cm wide slant plate.

The slant plate has the same specifications as HL91 and HL92.

Together with driver 375 of 5 cm throat diameter, it reproduces the middle range of 500 Hz or more.

Baffle plate You can mount a 375 in an enclosure with a small area.
