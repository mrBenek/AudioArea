Commentary

Auto transformer with large core volume.

15067 has a wide range and can usually be used with 70 or 140 v lines.
Almost all impedance matching can be performed by changing the connection, and the frequency range is 30 Hz to 15 kHz.

Insertion loss is also minimized.
