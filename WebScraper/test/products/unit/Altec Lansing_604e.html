Commentary

A hybrid speaker unit used in recording studios around the world.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range and a coaxial combination structure with the woofer is achieved by using a multi-seller horn and driver for the high range.

There were 2 types of variations, 8 Ω and 16 Ω.
