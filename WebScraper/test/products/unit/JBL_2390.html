Commentary

Horn lens composed of a combination of horn throat and lens.

It uses a hyperbolic plate (slant plate) designed based on precise calculation and is used at 800 Hz or higher.

The lens part has a plate bent into a 45 ° wave shape to shorten the depth.

The horn to be connected is a square exponential horn and the driver is 5 cm in diameter.

The lens is mounted on the baffle surface.
If it is placed outside the enclosure, use a baffle plate between the lens and horn, with a minimum width of 51 cm on either side of the lens.
