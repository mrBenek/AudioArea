Commentary

20 cm cone type full range unit.

The design is optimized for line arrays and PA equipment.

It consists of a magnetic circuit using an Alnico V magnet, an edgewise wound voice coil, and a cone paper with a shallow special shape.
The high-frequency characteristics are also improved by using an aluminum center dome.
