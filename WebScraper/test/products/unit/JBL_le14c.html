Commentary

36 cm aperture 2-way coaxial speaker unit.

A 36 cm cone type woofer with the same design as LE14A is used for the low range part.
Uses a 10 cm diameter voice coil and Alnico V-type magnet with copper ribbon wire edgewise winding.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter, equivalent to the LE20 direct radiator, is used for the high-frequency part.

The crossover frequency is divided by the attached dedicated network LX2-1.
The LX2-1 uses a Mylar condenser and an Orient Core coil, and the level of the tweeter can be adjusted according to the acoustic characteristics of the room.
