Commentary

Double cone type 30 cm full range unit that can also be used as a woofer.

SP12 is used for the mid and low band of Patrician800.

There are ones with specifications of 8 Ω and 16 Ω. It seems that the former period was 16 Ω and the latter period was 8 Ω.

Compatible with TweetBlock Kit BB1 and Midrange Kit BB4 (sold separately).
