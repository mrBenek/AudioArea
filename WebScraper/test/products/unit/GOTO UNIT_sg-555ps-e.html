Commentary

Driver unit for middle range of SG 555 series.

Hard duralumin diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and Mylar (FRP) is used for the edge.

The magnetic circuit uses a 130 mm φ x60mm alnico ring magnet using Permendur.
