  • VICTOR/JVC EN-K3

  • ¥ 47,000 (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

CW horn type enclosure kit designed based on the theory of backload horn.

30 cm units and tweeters can be mounted.

It is processed using NC router which boasts precision of 1/100mm unit by computer linkage.

The EN-K3 has a round hole for the tweeter, and there are two types depending on the position of the round hole for the tweeter : one for the right side and the other for the left side.
