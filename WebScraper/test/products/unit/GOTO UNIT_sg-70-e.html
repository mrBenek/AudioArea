Commentary

Horn type tweeter unit corresponding to the horn modification type of SG-7.

The horn section uses a new type horn with a cutoff of 2.5 kHz attached to the board.

The magnetic circuit is an internal magnet type.

Uses Mylar (FRP) edge.
