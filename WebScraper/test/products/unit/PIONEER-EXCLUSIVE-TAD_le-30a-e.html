  • Pioneer LE-30A

  • ¥ 85,800 (around 1974)

Commentary

A 25cm/30cm enclosure designed to take advantage of A-Series speaker units.

Mid-range and tweeter can be installed.

It can be freely applied to both bass reflex type and closed type.

By using an adapter (metal sub-baffle), it is compatible with a slightly smaller speaker unit.

It is made of hard particle board and plywood of sufficient thickness. It is reinforced in front and back by passing between the baffle and the back plate.

It has a walnut finish.
