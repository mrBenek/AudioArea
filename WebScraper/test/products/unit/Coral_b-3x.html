  • CORAL B-3X

  • ¥ 9,800 (1 unit, around 1980)

Commentary

This is a super tweeter system that can improve the quality of the entire system simply by connecting it to your own speaker system.

The unit is equipped with H-60 horn type tweeter.

A network and a continuously variable level controller are built in.

It can be mounted on the front baffle or equipped with an acoustic lens AL-602 on the front.
