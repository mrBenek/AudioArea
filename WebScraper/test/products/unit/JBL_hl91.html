Commentary

A horn lens combining a 25 cm wide slant plate and a die-cast exponential horn.

The slant plate is inclined to enhance the diffraction effect.

Directional characteristics are 90 ° horizontal and 45 ° vertical.

Used in combination with driver LE175 or LE85.

It can be mounted in an enclosure or a baffle plate can be used.
