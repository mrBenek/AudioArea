Commentary

76 cm super large woofer unit.

By using a magnet assembly that accounts for more than 2 / 3 of the total weight, a high input resistant edge wound copper ribbon voice coil, and a foamed polystyrene cone, excellent low-frequency playback is achieved.

This unit is installed in the Patrician 800, a typical Electrovoice speaker system.
