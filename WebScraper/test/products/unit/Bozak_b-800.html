  • BOZAK B-800

  • ¥ 25,700 (1 unit, around 1966)

Commentary

20 cm diameter single cone full range unit.

It is used as an extension speaker or auxiliary, and it is designed as an all-weather type for outdoor use.
It can be used multiple times as a high-fidelity speaker system in concert halls, and is also suitable for mid-range multi-way speakers.

The cone is made of BOZAK patented hard metal with special treatment.

Alnico V magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and aluminum ribbon voice coil is used for the voice coil.
