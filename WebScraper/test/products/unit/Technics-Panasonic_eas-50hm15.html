Commentary

Horn type skoker unit

It employs a full-scale cellular phone and has acquired excellent directional characteristics.

The diaphragm uses rigid duralumin with high rigidity. This diaphragm has a special design of molding method and shape so that there is no split resonance in the dome-shaped part or antiresonance in the edge part in the operating frequency band.

EAS-50HM15NA is an 8 Ω model and EAS-50HM15NB is a 16 Ω model.
