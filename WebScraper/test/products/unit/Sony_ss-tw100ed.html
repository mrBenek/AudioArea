Commentary

A super tweeter that can be combined with existing speakers to enable ultrahigh-frequency playback.

The unit is equipped with a 2.5 cm hard dome type super tweeter.
This unit uses a light-weight and highly rigid carbon-graphite composite hard dome diaphragm combined with an electromagnetic inductive magnetic circuit to realize ultra-high frequency reproduction up to 100 kHz. In addition, the unit is housed in a spherical housing that reduces the effect of sound commentary, thereby reducing sound turbidity and improving low-level reproducibility.

In order to facilitate the setting of the super tweeter with sharp directivity, it is equipped with a high-precision angle adjustment function of the grade used for optical equipment, and the angle of the unit can be finely adjusted within 20 degrees left and right.

The network section is designed so that it can be used by switching the cut-off frequency for ease of combination with speakers.
The connection terminal is changed to a simple specification.
