Commentary

Hard dome type super tweeter.

The diaphragm uses an ultra-thin film of 18 micron thickness super duralumin that has undergone precision heat treatment.

The voice coil uses a ribbon wire of high-conductivity aluminum alloy to reduce weight, and is edgewise wound to allow a large space occupancy in the magnetic gap.

The equalizer is a wide-spread equalizer.

Resonance is prevented by adopting a square rigid duralumin panel.
In addition, the exterior is treated with special treatment after hairline treatment to bring out the beauty of the material.
