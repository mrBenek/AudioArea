Commentary

A horn type mid-range unit developed for mid-range use.

The horn section consists of a metal throat section and an 8-part wooden multi-cellar horn. By adopting an 8-part multi-cellar horn, almost uniform characteristics are obtained over a wide angle of 135 °.

Phenol resin is used for the diaphragm, and polymer adhesive is used for the voice coil.
