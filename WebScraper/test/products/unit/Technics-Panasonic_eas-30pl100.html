Commentary

A 30 cm cone woofer with reduced distortion and improved characteristics.

The distortion has been greatly reduced by 1/5 to 1/100 in the low band (200 Hz or less) and the middle band (500 Hz to 2 kHz), which were highly distorted by conventional units, and by 1/3 to 1/5 in the middle low band (200 Hz to 500 Hz).
