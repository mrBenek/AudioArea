Commentary

A 38 cm cone shaped woofer that can be attached to an infinite baffle or a bass reflex type and used in a large theater or hall.

The distance between the coil and pole piece is designed to be a minimum of 0.36 mm. In addition, the long cone and label system reduces mechanical failure and enables continuous use at high volume.

There were 3 variations of impedance.
