  • CORAL H-50

  • ¥ 4,050 (1 unit, around 1965)

Commentary

A horn-type tweeter designed to reproduce ultra-high frequencies.

Ultra-hard lightweight super duralumin diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and aluminum ribbon wire edgewise voice coil is used for the voice coil.

It was sold at the same time as the H-1, but the H-50 has adopted a square design in consideration of easy installation.

H-50 seems to have 2 kinds of variations, 8 Ω and 16 Ω.
There were also two variations of the output shown.
