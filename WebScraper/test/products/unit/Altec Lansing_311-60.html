Commentary

A centrifugal horn developed for large sound reproduction.
It is designed to achieve maximum effect when wide sound pressure distribution characteristics are required in a wide listening room in a home, a hall, outdoors, etc.

Driver units such as 291-16A, 290E and 288D are used in combination.

The difference between 311 ° - 60 and 311 ° - 90 is the directional which is 60 ° and 90 ° in the horizontal direction respectively.
