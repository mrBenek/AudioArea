Commentary

A 36 cm cone woofer unit designed for linear playback even when mounted in a small enclosure.

The cone uses an original cone paper coated with a special white dump material to improve rigidity and obtain an appropriate mass.

It uses a cooperative magnetic circuit using an Alnico V magnet, and a voice coil with ribbon-like copper wire precisely edgewise wound.
