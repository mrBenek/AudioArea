A typical aluminum die-cast Celtal horn of Altec.

It is used in many speaker systems such as the A7-8.

With uniform sound pressure distribution in 90 ° horizontal and 40 ° vertical, and wide diffusion characteristics, it is also effective for sound reproduction in theaters and halls.
