Commentary

30 cm full range unit that adopts a mechanical 2-way system.

The high-frequency characteristics are extended by the free edge subcone.

It has a high-efficiency design and can extract more than 4 times the acoustic power in the low range and more than 2 times the acoustic power in the middle range compared to SP8C.

A bass reflex enclosure with an internal volume of 160 liters has a low-frequency response of 43 Hz at a 3 dB downpoint.
