  • Technics EAS-30PL40NA/NB

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, around 1966)

Commentary

30 cm cone shaped woofer unit.

The cone paper is made of a material with high Young's modulus and high internal loss. This reduces the unevenness of the frequency characteristics and provides ideal sound pressure reduction at 1 kHz or higher.

The difference between EAS-30PL40NA and EAS-30PL40NB is that the V. C impedance is 8 Ω for NA and 16 Ω for NB.
