Commentary

Large manta ray Rehhorn.

MR94 and MR64 have different sizes.

Unlike conventional horns, the manta ray Lehorn has excellent uniformity in directional characteristics in the operating frequency band, and has a feature that there is very little difference in frequency characteristics between the on-axis and off-axis listening points of the horn.
The manta ray Rayhorn is designed so that the diffusion pattern does not become beam-like as the frequency characteristics become higher.
