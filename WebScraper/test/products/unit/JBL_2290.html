Commentary

38 cm diameter passive radiator.

It consists of a cone paper and frame with carefully adjusted mass and compliance.

The 2290 is a sealed 142 to 227 liter enclosure designed for use with 2205 or 2215 woofers to enhance the dynamic range and enhance the impact of the lower 150 Hz range.
