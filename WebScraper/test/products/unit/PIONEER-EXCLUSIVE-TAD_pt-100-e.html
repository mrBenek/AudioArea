Commentary

Multi-cellular horn type tweeter.

Aluminum light alloy diaphragm and strong magnetic circuit are adopted.

The horn section is a 10-aperture multi-cellular horn. Semicircular diffusers installed above and below the horn aperture prevent interference and disturbance of sound.
