Commentary

A 3-way 25 cm coaxial speaker unit.

It consists of a mechanical 2-way in which a medium-range cone is mounted on a low-fo woofer with excellent transient characteristics and directional characteristics, and a light mass and high-efficiency horn type tweeter added.

A die-cast prominent frame is used for the frame.

A high-frequency level attenuator and an L/C-type 12dB/oct network are included.
