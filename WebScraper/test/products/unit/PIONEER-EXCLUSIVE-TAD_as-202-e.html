  • Pioneer AS-202

  • ¥ 9,000 (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

2-way speaker system kit with 20 cm woofer and tweeter.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
The cloth roll edge and long voice coil lower fo and improve linearity.

The PT-20, a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter, is used for the high range.
The diaphragm of PT-20 is made of silk base impregnated with special resin.

A network with high-frequency level control is included.

Speaker system can be constructed by the combination of exclusive enclosure AE-200 and baffle plate EB-202.
