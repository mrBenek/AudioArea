Commentary

A horn made of fiber glass with uniform radiation characteristics over a wide range of 100 degrees horizontally and 40 degrees vertically.

It is designed with a combination of a hyperbolic exponential throat and a conical flare.

Used in combination with Driver Unit DH-1012 for Middle Range.
