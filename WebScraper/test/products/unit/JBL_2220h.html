Commentary

38 cm cone type woofer developed for professionals such as SR system.

A lightweight and rigid curved cone is used for the diaphragm.

The voice coil uses an edgewise wound copper ribbon voice coil, and the magnetic circuit uses JBL's unique SFG magnetic circuit.

Mounted in a horn-type enclosure, such as 4560 bka.

2220H has two types of impedance, 2220H has 8 Ω and 2220J has 16 Ω.
