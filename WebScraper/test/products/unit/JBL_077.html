Commentary

Super Tweeter developed using JBL's professional technology.

The 077 is used in the L300 and provides clear and flat sound over 6.5 kHz.

A ring-shaped aluminum diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

In order to improve directional characteristics, we have adopted a horn with a unique shape that combines the characteristics of an exponential horn and a diffuse horn. By combining an acrylic internal diffusion element, we have obtained directional characteristics of 130 ° horizontal and 40 ° vertical at 15 kHz when the horn mouse is vertically oriented.
