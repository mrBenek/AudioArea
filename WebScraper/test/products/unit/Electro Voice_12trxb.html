Commentary

A 30cm3-way coaxial type unit combining a double-cone type speaker SP12B and a horn type tweeter T35.

Built-in special high-pass filter and level control.

It was 16 Ω specification around 1970 and 8 Ω specification around 1972.

You can combine it with the separately sold Midrange Kit BB4.
Note that if you combine BB4 with 12 trxb, you must also use an X36 network.
