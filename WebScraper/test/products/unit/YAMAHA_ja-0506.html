  • YAMAHA JA-0506

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, around 1970)

Commentary

Horn type tweeter developed as a part of NS series.

Special hard duralumin foil of 30 μ thickness is used for the diaphragm.
In addition, tangential corrugation edge is used for the edge.

The voice coil is 23 mm in diameter and is edgewise wound with aluminum ribbon wire.
In addition, a 40 φ diameter magnet is used.

An aluminum hollowed horn is used for the horn part.
In addition, it is equipped with an equalizer with the same aluminum precision processing finish, and realizes a tweeter with excellent frequency characteristics and wide directivity.

Actual measurement data measured in the measurement room was attached to each product.
