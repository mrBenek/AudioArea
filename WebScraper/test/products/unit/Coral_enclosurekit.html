Commentary

Enclosure kit with high density particle board.

The EK-55, EK-45, and EK-35 each have several kinds of baffle plates. By combining baffle plates suitable for the speakers used, we were able to configure a wide variety of systems.
It is also possible to combine commercially available speaker units by machining a non-perforated baffle.

The external appearance was a stopper thrust plate finish.

Adhesive, sound absorbing material, speaker cord, and speaker RUNNET are included.

The EK-8BL is a backload horn kit and does not come with a sub RUNNET.
