Commentary

The network uses excellent parts such as high-voltage non-polar capacitors and low-loss Orient Core coils.

LX5 is compatible with 8 Ω, but with a special compensation circuit, it is possible to match 375 (16 Ω) without changing the crossover characteristics.

When the enclosure is mounted, a plastic tube packing is used for the frame to maintain airtightness.
