Commentary

Horn-type tweeter units also used by Olympus and Paragon.

A ring-shaped diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and solid aluminum is used for the horn part.

In combination with a wide-range speaker, it can be used for high-frequency compensation above 2.5 kHz. It can also be used as a tweeter for 3-way systems at frequencies above 7 kHz.
