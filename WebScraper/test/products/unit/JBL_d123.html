Commentary

This 30-cm cone type full-range speaker unit is designed to be able to demonstrate its performance even in a 100-liter enclosure.

Large diameter ribbon voice coil of 8 cm diameter and large magnet are adopted.

It is designed to be thin with a depth of 10 cm, and can be used in various ways such as attaching it to a wall.
