Commentary

Driver unit to be used in combination with horn.

High hardness titanium foil is used for the dome part of the diaphragm, and polyester film with good linearity is used for the edge part.

By adopting the edgewise wound voice coil and large magnet, efficiency and transient characteristics are improved.
