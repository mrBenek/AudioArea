Commentary

30cm3-way coaxial speaker unit of Coral Golden 50 series.

The 30 cm cone type woofer and the cone for mid-range use are connected coaxially by the same magnetic circuit in a mechanical 2-way structure.
The center is equipped with a horn type tweeter for high frequencies.

The mid-range cone has a braking hole for reflection resonance control, and has excellent characteristics over the entire range.

The edge part of the woofer uses a skybar edge.
This is a variant of the free edge and is used overseas as roll surround edge.

The magnetic circuit uses a pole yoke made of NKS magnet and high-purity iron material to improve efficiency.
The voice coil uses a long travel type to prevent the voice coil from coming off even at a large amplitude from the next time.

Duralumin diaphragm of 20 micron thickness is used for the vibration plate of the tweeter.
We have also adopted an aluminum edge-wise voice coil for the voice coil and a newly combined magnetic circuit for improving the level of the tweeter.

A rectangular diecast horn is used for the horn section.
A tweeter equalizer adjusts the phase shift of the sound from the diaphragm.

A prominent frame has been adopted for the frame part.
This frame structure reduces unnecessary distortion when mounted in a cabinet.

It has a built-in network.
