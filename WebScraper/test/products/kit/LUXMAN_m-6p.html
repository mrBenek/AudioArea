Commentary

Power supply kit for measuring instrument series.

The M-6P is a compact power supply that can be used for driving the measuring instrument series and various circuit experiments. Although the measuring instrument series is a built-in dry battery type, it can provide a stable power supply by using the fixed output of M-6P.

The fixed output is directly wired (screwed) inside and taken out. However, it is separated into a measuring instrument connected to the input side and a measuring instrument connected to the output side. When more measuring instruments are used together or more advanced measurement is performed, consideration must be given to the mutual connection, especially the connection related to earth.
To cope with such a situation, the power supply is separated into two systems to prevent mutual interference.

The variable output can be taken out from the terminal installed on the panel surface, and power can be supplied in the variable range of ± 3 v to ± 15 v.

It has a built-in overcurrent protection circuit to protect the power supply from the operation during the experiment and the abnormality of the experiment circuit.
