Commentary

Tube type pre-main amplifier kit of 7591 pp configuration.

Equipped with tape and phono equalizer switches and two low-level inputs, it can be used with two magnetic cartridges, two tape heads, or one cartridge and one tape head connected.

Equipped with a D' Arsonval calibration meter, you can enjoy the best performance.

The connector for the center speaker is mounted, and the level control and OFF switch of this output are mounted.

Terminal for tape monitor and switch are mounted.

Features include a high-cut filter, loudness, stereo dimension control, and tone control.
