Commentary

Battery adapter kit developed for voltage amplification stage of A902-M.

The structure is the same sealed type as the one mounted on A901-C, and free grounding is possible.
The battery is a maintenance-free type and consists of 4 12 V AH (Amp-hour) batteries for ± 24 V power supply.

When connected to A902-M, it operates in conjunction with A902-M. Charging starts with the power switch OFF of A 0 - M. When charging is completed, it automatically turns OFF. It can be used for 7 ~ 10 hours with one charge.
Also, if you turn on the charging switch of A902-M, you can use it while charging.
