  • LUXKIT A832/II

  • ¥ 9,800 (1 unit, released in 1982)

Commentary

A small monitor speaker system developed as a successor model of A832.

In the A832/II, sound grouping is improved by improving the network.

A 10 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Special rubber is used for the edge, and ferrite magnet of 70 mm diameter / 15 mm thickness is used for the magnet, which realizes a low-temperature range with effective damping.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Special woven fabric is used for the diaphragm.

A tandem circuit is used in the network section, and a capacitor for the network is used to strengthen the mid-range.

Aluminum die-cast is used for the enclosure to eliminate unwanted resonance, which is likely to occur during Dainyu forces, and to improve airtightness.

There was an optional stand sold separately.
The speaker stand arm OP832 can be used for a car stereo, or in a room on a bookshelf or desk. The speaker stand arm can be used to place the OP-10 on a desk or floor.
