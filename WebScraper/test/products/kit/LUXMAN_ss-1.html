  • LUXKIT SS-1

  • ¥ 3,900 (1 unit, around 1980)

Commentary

A driver unit system for use with A800 series enclosures A834E and A835E.

A 10 cm cone type woofer with a large magnetic circuit of 7 cm magnet diameter is used for the low range.
In addition, a 5.5 cm cone type tweeter with a 3 cm magnet is used for the high range. By making it the same cone type as the low range, a smooth sound connection is obtained.
