Commentary

This is a stereo power amplifier kit of Lux Kit A802 series developed as an upgraded model of A800.

Duo-beta circuit is newly adopted for A802/II.
After improving various characteristics before applying NFB, an appropriate amount of NFB is applied to all bands. Furthermore, by applying DC servo, the sound image is tightened from the ultra-low band, and the sound quality full of dynamic feeling is finished.

The input sensitivity can be switched and an FM tuner or deck can be connected without a preamplifier.
