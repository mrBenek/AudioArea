Commentary

Control amplifier kit with CL34 kit.

Although it is a tube type, it has a duo-beta circuit.

The equalizer circuit is a 3-stage direct connection structure without using an interstage coupling capacitor, and S. R. P. P. is used for the output stage.
The S. R. P. P. operates to compensate for non-linear operation and has various advantages such as excellent linearity, high output voltage, and extremely low output impedance.
To this amplifier circuit, we have added an appropriate amount of NFB to improve the middle and high frequency range and DC servo to improve the low frequency range (damping) to create an excellent equalizer amplifier.

The flat amplifier section is a 2-stage differential amplifier circuit that theoretically does not generate even order distortion, has a simple configuration with tone control in the NFB loop, and achieves excellent frequency characteristics of 3 Hz to 70 kHz by eliminating the interstage coupling capacitor.
The NFB amount is set to an optimal value.

Equipped with a DC configuration headphone amplifier.

It is equipped with functions such as tone control, subsonic filter, low-cut filter, attenuator, signal off switch, tone defeat switch and muting.
