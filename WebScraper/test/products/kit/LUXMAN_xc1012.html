Commentary

Digital electronic clock kit.

The XC1012Quartz is a crystal oscillator with high precision as a clock, and the XC1012 is a synchronous power source with similar precision.

It can be used for audio.

The power synchronous type can be changed to the quartz type as an option sold separately.
