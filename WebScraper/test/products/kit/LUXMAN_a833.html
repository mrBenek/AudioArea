  • LUXKIT A833

  • ¥ 16,000 (1 unit, around 1980)

Commentary

This 3-way monitor speaker has a maximum input power comparable to that of a normal bookshelf type.

It has a 13 cm cone woofer in the low range and a dome unit in the middle and high range.

Aluminum die-cast is used for the enclosure to eliminate unnecessary resonance.
