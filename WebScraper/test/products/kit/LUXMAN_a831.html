Commentary

A woofer system kit specifically designed for 3D systems.

The unit is equipped with 25 cm cone type woofer.
Beading Oil Board (BOB) sheet is used as a cone for the diaphragm of this unit. BOB has improved Young's modulus by approximately 2 times and internal loss by approximately 1.5 times compared with conventional papermaking types. It also has strong aging characteristics.

The enclosure uses a unique linear acoustic load method, in which a front board is placed 20 mm in front of the unit mounting surface to apply moderate pressure and cut off unnecessary high frequencies.
High-density chip board is used for the plate material.
