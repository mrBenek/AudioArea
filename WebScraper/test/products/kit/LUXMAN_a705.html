Commentary

Sound field reproduction type speaker system kit.

Equipped with 10 cm cone type full range unit for all band.
Also, a 2.5 cm matrix dome type tweeter is mounted on the upper part for sound field reproduction.

Can be attached to wall surfaces or shelves with grips, etc.
