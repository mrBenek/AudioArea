Commentary

A 800 series enclosure to be used with the SS-1 driver unit system.

The A835E has adopted a shape suitable for multi-use, and it is installed horizontally.
Metal fittings and long screws are also included, and the speaker terminal is a one touch type that can be attached and detached.

The enclosure is entirely made of aluminum die-cast, including the front panel, eliminating resonance.
In addition, the front panel is not made of punching metal, but is made of a net structure of lath material to ensure an area that allows sound to pass through.

The glass wool is treated with a sandwich process so that it can be touched without gloves.

It comes with an orange sticker that says 835 MONITOR.
