Commentary

A vacuum disk stabilizer type turntable kit developed as the 6th series of the LUX KIT A500 series.

The turntable uses a unique vacuum disk stabilizer system developed by Lux. In this system, the turntable is attached to the disk and integrated to achieve accurate stabilization.

A quartz lock direct drive system is adopted for the drive system to realize more accurate rotation.
It also uses a powerful motor to start up the turntable in 2.5 seconds.

High-sensitivity J-type universal arm is used for the tone arm part.
This arm was newly developed for the adsorption system and employs a double system of high-precision pivot bearings and miniature bearings.
It is also equipped with functions such as a lateral balancer, an oil dump-type arm lifter, and a lever-type inside four scan seller.

Equipped with an acrylic dust cover.

Double insulation structure is used for the legs.
