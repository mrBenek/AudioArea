Commentary

A 800 series small enclosure to be used with the SS-1 driver unit system.

The enclosure is entirely made of aluminum die-cast, including the front panel, eliminating resonance.

The front panel is not made of punching metal but made of lath material with a net structure to ensure an area through which sound can pass.

The glass wool is treated with a sandwich process so that it can be touched without gloves.
