Commentary

A800 series DC-configuration stereo preamplifier kit.

All circuits are discrete to ensure high sound quality.

The configuration is simple with only an equalizer amplifier and a flat amplifier, and the tone control circuit is omitted.
This eliminates as much as possible the adverse effect on sound quality caused by the complicated circuit.
