Commentary

This tube type preamplifier kit was announced simultaneously with the launch of the LUX KIT brand.

The equalizer circuit is similar to the circuit used in the C-22 Macintosh, and consists of a combination of a P-K / NF circuit (feedback from the second stage plate to the first stage cathode) and a cathode follower circuit.

An orthodox circuit is used to adjust sound quality.
The low-cut filter is CR1-stage type, the high-cut filter is NF-type, and the tone control is Lux-type NF-type with less waviness at the mid-point. The tone control can be adjusted more precisely because the bending point frequency can be switched.

Equipped with an attenuator that can attenuate the gain by 20 dB.

It is equipped with a pure complimentary service type headphone amplifier composed of 4 transistors.
This circuit automatically switches the output of the pre-section to the headphone amplifier when the headphone is inserted.

A3300 does not have a built-in power supply.
For this reason, a special power supply or a power amplifier such as A3500/A2500 is required.
