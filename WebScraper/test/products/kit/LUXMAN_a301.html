Commentary

A speaker with a multi-functional amplifier for mixing play.

It is equipped with 3 channels and 5 inputs such as microphone, guitar and keyboard.
You can create your favorite sound with the level control, reverb and distortion functions for each channel.

The unit is equipped with a 16 cm cone type unit.

Can be used not only with 100 VAC but also with car power source and battery.
