Commentary

A500 series tube type preamplifier kit.

Since it is a tube bulb type, a non-feedback amplifier is realized, and NFB is 0 dB over all stages, which brings out the taste of the vacuum tube itself.

Equalization, tone control and subsonic filter are CR-type.

All stages of the circuit are push-pull configuration, from the input stage of the equalizer to the output stage of the flat amplifier. The circuit is S. R. P. P. (Shunt-Regulated Push-Pull) suitable for vacuum tubes. This circuit automatically compensates for non-linear operation, eliminates distortion, keeps output impedance low, and allows output voltage to be high.

Equipped with a dedicated booster transformer to support MC cartridge.
This transformer was newly developed for A505 and is a compact type using high-performance permalloy core with a boost ratio of 20 dB (10 times / 0.2 mV) and designed for ease of use.

Equipped with tone bypass function and monaural output function.
