Commentary

A800 series FM/AM digital tuner kit.

Digital lead-out is adopted, and C-MOS LSI and other technologies are invested in the IC.

The inside has a sandwiched substrate structure and is the highest density in the 800 series.

Measuring instruments for adjustment can be done only by a tester.
