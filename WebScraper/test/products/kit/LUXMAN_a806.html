  • ¥ 18,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

A800 series TV sound multiplex adapter kit.

This adapter allows you to enjoy the sound of analog broadcasting TV in stereo and two languages. It is only for TV with multiple terminals.

The built-in amplifier has DC configuration and is designed using LSI.

Some TV manufacturers required additional parts.
