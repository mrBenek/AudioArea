  • ¥ 24,000 (1 unit, released in 1982)

Commentary

This is a mono power amplifier kit of Lux Kit A801 series developed as an upgraded model of A800.

Duo-beta circuit is newly adopted for A801/II.
It is also designed to be made as a class A power amplifier.

The circuit is a fully discrete design.
