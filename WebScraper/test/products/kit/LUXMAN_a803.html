  • ¥ 24,000 (around 1980)

Commentary

DC channel divider kit specialized for A800 series 3D system.

A803 has 11 points of low-pass switching, and the slope can be adjusted in 3 steps.
As for highpass, there are 11 points and 3 levels, which is the same as lowpass, and various combinations are possible.
