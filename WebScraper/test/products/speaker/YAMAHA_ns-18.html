  • YAMAHA NS-18

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 piece, around 1970)

Commentary

A speaker system that uses a unique unit for the low range.

Unlike the high-end models NS-30 and NS-20, it is a 2-way configuration. The woofer is equipped with JA 5004 and the tweeter is equipped with a horn with an equalizer.

The enclosure is an open rear type and uses 16 mm lauan plywood as the material.
The front net is not removable.

Equipped with a high-frequency level control.
