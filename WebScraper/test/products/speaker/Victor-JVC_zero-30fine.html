Commentary

This speaker system was developed as a speaker capable of reproducing not only digital source sound but also AV sound field space.

A 23 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The combination of a fine ceramic diaphragm, die-cast frame, and a flat damper that is strong against large amplitudes enhances the fundamental sound band, which is important for music playback.

It is equipped with a 7.0 cm compound type unit for the middle range and a 2.5 cm dome type unit for the high range.
Fine ceramic is used for the diaphragm.

Each of the three units has a double magnet type magnetic circuit to suppress the leakage of magnetic flux.
In addition, it is carefully designed by computer simulation so that it does not have any adverse effects even if it is close to a TV.

The enclosure is built using Victor's proprietary high pressure flash consolidation method.
