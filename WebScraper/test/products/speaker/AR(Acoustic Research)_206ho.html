Audio Database

  • AR(Acoustic Research) 206HO

  • ¥ 60,000 (a set of two cars, around 1996)

Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system of the HO series.

A 16.25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.

A 1.9 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.
In addition, there were 2 kinds of variations for the finish of the exterior, black and maple.
