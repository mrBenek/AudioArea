Commentary

A floor-type speaker system using Coral's Professional Series units.

A 38 cm cone type woofer 15L-100 is mounted in the low range.

The middle area is equipped with a horn type squaker that combines a driver M-100, an acoustic lens AL 603, and a straight horn AH 503.
The M-100 uses an originally developed ultra-hard and lightweight diaphragm composed of 9 kinds of chemical components such as Al (aluminum), Ti (titanium), and Mg (magnesium).

A horn type Super Tweeter H-100 is mounted in the high range.
Coral's unique ultra-thin super duralumin diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Multi-amplifier input terminal is mounted.
