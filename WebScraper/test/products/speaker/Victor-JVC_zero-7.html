Commentary

This 4-way speaker system was developed with the aim of W & D (wide & dynamic) playback through full band pistic motion.

The low-range is equipped with a 32 cm flat woofer made of foam resin.
Foam resin has a specific gravity of about 1/20 of that of paper and has an appropriate internal loss just like paper. This material is used for the diaphragm and the front of the diaphragm is made flat to solve the problem of cone.

A 12 cm flat skoker is mounted in the middle area.
This foam resin unit was developed after thorough study and was developed from an experiment in which the front of the diaphragm was buried flat using a 10-cm Scoker of the Zero-5 type.
The foam resin was changed and the surface material was changed to pursue all possibilities, and the diameter was also increased to 12 cm from the middle.

A 4-cm flat tweeter is installed in the high range.
The sound pressure frequency characteristics and directional characteristics have been improved by adopting a plane diaphragm made of foamed resin similar to Woofer.

A ribbon tweeter with a full-surface drive light quantity flat plate diaphragm is adopted for the ultra-high frequency range.
The combination of this diaphragm and a powerful push-pull magnetic circuit expands the response to a band of 100 kHz.

The network uses carefully selected materials such as wiring materials for oxygen-free copper wires, coils, printed circuit boards, and step-switching attenuators for copper contacts, resulting in a design with less distortion.

The enclosure arranges units in millimeters to maximize flat response.
Also, it is finished with natural rose wood.
