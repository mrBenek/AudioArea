  • YAMAHA NS-C105

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, around 1999)

Commentary

Center speaker with virtual coaxial arrangement.

Two 10 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range. The virtual coaxial arrangement of the tweeter sandwiched between two woofers improves sound image localization.
