Commentary

A two way bookshelf speaker system that employs a passive radiator system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In order to stabilize the operation of the cone paper and reduce the load on the frame, this unit uses a newly developed material and a free edge developed using a special molding technology. In addition, a newly designed long voice coil improves sound quality.

It is equipped with a 20 cm cone type passive radiator in order to increase the bass reproduction capability.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.
By using a special molding resin that is light and has high rigidity, the weight of the vibration part is reduced.

The network is selected through listening tests conducted by audio consultants and the general public.
In addition, the speakers are acoustically isolated to suppress interference distortion between them.

Equipped with 2-step switching type level control on the rear terminal plate.
