  • CORAL BX-60

  • ¥ 35,200 (1 unit, around 1965)

Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system using a large-diameter woofer.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 16 cm cone type squaker is installed in the middle range, and two horn type tweeters are installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a walnut oil finish on the exterior.
