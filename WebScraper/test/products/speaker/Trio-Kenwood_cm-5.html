  • ¥ 14,000 (a set of two cars, around 1987)

Commentary

A small speaker system that can be installed freely.

10 cm full range cone type unit is adopted for the unit.
In order to realize a wide range and flat characteristics, a light cone is used. In addition, the high frequency peak is removed by making the shape カーブドコーン. In addition, a Mylar is used in the center chamber to extend the high frequency.
A urethane roll edge is adopted for the edge to improve non-linearity and to support a large input in a low range.

The enclosure is made of special polypropylene to ensure adequate internal loss while thoroughly suppressing resonance.

The back has vertical and horizontal wall mounting holes, and the bottom has female screws that can be used for various types of commercially available mounts.
