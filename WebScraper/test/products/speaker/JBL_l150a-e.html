Commentary

This is a tall boy type speaker system using JBL's accumulated know-how.

In the low range, 30 cm cone type woofer 128H and 30 cm cone type passive radiator PR300 are mounted.
The 128H uses cone paper coated with white damping agent, a low-distortion SFG magnetic circuit, and changes in the air pressure inside the enclosure caused by the movement of the woofer drive the passive radiator, thereby increasing the bass range.

The 13 cm cone type Scorker LE5-12 is mounted in the middle area.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter 044 is installed in the high range.

The enclosure has an inline design in which units are arranged in a vertical line to improve sound image localization.

There are 3 variations of grill color, brown, camel and red.
