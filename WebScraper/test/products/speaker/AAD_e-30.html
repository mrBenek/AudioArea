Features

Speaker system of cube cinema series.

The 9cm cone type full range speaker is carried as a full range.
This unit uses an aluminum cone diaphragm. Furthermore, the special electric field processing is given to the surface of the diaphragm. This is suppressing distortion.

The high pass filter is carried. This protects the driver from extremely low frequency input.

The enclosure is a closed box type.
Maple finish is given to the exterior.

Speaker stand and bracket were sold as an option.
