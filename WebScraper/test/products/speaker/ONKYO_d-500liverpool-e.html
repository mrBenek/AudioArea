Commentary

Based on the concept of acoustic ナチュラリティ (natural sound field expression), this speaker system aims to create natural sound without emphasizing resolution and wide range.

The Woofer uses pure cross carbon and the Tweeter uses ultra-hard titanium diaphragm materials. By analyzing the diaphragm structure, we removed peaks that cause unnatural sound and ensured smooth characteristics.
In addition, by attaching a center cap made of cloth to the woofer and a cork material to the frame of the tweeter, the cause of the stimulating sound is completely removed.

The cabinet has a real wood finish with a planing walnut board, and the enclosure structure has a unique method of sandwiching the top and bottom plates and front and rear baffles with high-density custom wood side plates. This realizes a beautiful sound without discomfort.

Quality parts such as copper core choke coils and high-quality film capacitors are used for the network, and interference between networks is eliminated by between networks.
