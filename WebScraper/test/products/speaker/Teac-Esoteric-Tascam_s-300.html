  • TEAC S-300

  • ¥ 29,800 (a set of two cars, around 1989)

Commentary

A compact speaker system with an anti-magnetic design that employs a coaxial 2-way unit.

The unit uses a 13 cm cone type woofer made of laminated pulp cone and a coaxial two way unit made of soft dome speaker.
In addition, a double magnet cancels the magnetic leakage, and in addition, a shield cap is attached to the double anti-magnetic specification.

The enclosure uses a high-density particle board and a rear-duct bass reflex system.
The exterior is finished with Tasman-Maple.
