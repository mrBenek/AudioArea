Commentary

Speaker system with slim tower enclosure.

It is equipped with an 18 cm cone woofer for the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter for the high range.
By thoroughly implementing anti-magnetic measures using a magnetic shield, the screen will not be affected even on the side of the TV.

The enclosure has a bass-reflex design.
