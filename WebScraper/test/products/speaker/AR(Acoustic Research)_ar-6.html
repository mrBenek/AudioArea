Commentary

A 2-way speaker system using a newly developed speaker unit.

The low-range 20 cm cone woofer unit has a low resonance of 25 Hz, coupled with an acoustic suspension enclosure for low-distortion bass playback.

A 3.8 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range. By making the shape of the diaphragm unique and close to the dome type, excellent directional characteristics and natural response are obtained.

It is equipped with level control for high sound.

The enclosure uses an acoustic suspension system and the exterior is finished with an oil finish using a walnut.
