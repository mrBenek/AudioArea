Commentary

Compact active speaker system with built-in amplifier.

The unit uses an 8 cm cone type full range unit.

It is equipped with a superbus control circuit to improve the bass sound.

The input terminal of the stereo mini-jack is mounted, and it is designed with high sensitivity to line level.

Equipped with a speaker direct function.
