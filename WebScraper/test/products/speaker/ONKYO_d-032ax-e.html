  • ONKYO D-032AX

  • ¥ 17,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

Compact speaker system with wooden cabinet.

It uses a 12 cm cone type woofer with a bio black cone for the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter for the high range.
Each unit has an anti-magnetic design and does not adversely affect the TV screen.

MDF material is used for the enclosure.
