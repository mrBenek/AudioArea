Commentary

CLASSIC series tall boy type speaker system.

Two 14 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
The diaphragm uses a new metal cone with a surface coating to enhance musical expression.

Equipped with a high frequency 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter.

The network section employs a fourth stage LinkWitz-Riley network.

MDF material is used for the enclosure and a bass reflex system is adopted.
In addition, a virtual coaxial layout has been adopted for the unit layout to improve the sense of sound image localization. It is finished with natural alder material as exterior finish.

Bi-wired connection is supported.

Cleats are included.
