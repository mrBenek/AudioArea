Commentary

A small AV speaker system designed to fit a 19 - to 20-inch TV.

The magnetic shielding design prevents adverse effects on cathode ray tubes, etc.

The unit is equipped with 2 10 cm cone type full range units.

The enclosure has a light black finish.
