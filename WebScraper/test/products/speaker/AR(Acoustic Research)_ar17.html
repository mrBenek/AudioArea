  • AR(Acoustic Research) AR17

  • ¥ 60,000 (a set of two cars, around 2000)

Commentary

High Resolution Series bookshelf type speaker system.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 2.5 cm hard-domed tweeter is installed in the high range.
Diamond-coated titanium dome is used for the diaphragm.

Anti-magnetic design is applied.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
There were 2 kinds of variations exterior finish, black wood grain finish and cherry.
