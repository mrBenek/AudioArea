  • DALI DALI-2

  • ¥ 80,000 (a set of two cars, around 1986)

Commentary

This is the most compact speaker system in the lineup at that time.

A 16.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Synthetic laminated pulp polyvinyl cone is used for the diaphragm to pursue tight and accurate bass characteristics.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter using a polypropylene dome is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
In addition, the exterior has a Danish beach finish.
