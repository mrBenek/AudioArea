Commentary

A subwoofer system developed to realize the excitement of live performance.

The unit is equipped with a 30 cm cone shaped woofer.
The diaphragm is produced by an oven manufacturing method using SP pulp as the main component. This diaphragm is driven by a magnetic circuit exceeding 1,000 mT and a special voice coil with 5 layers structure.

The cabinet uses a sealed system.
30 mm MDF is used for the front baffle, 25 mm particleboards are used on both sides of the top and bottom, 25 mm MDF is used on the rear side, and the front and top are reinforced.
The exterior is finished with a glossy coating of maple veneer.

It is equipped with a velocity feedback type MFB servo in order to prevent the sound from becoming heavy and dull while reproducing up to the heavy and low range.
In this circuit, the motion of the diaphragm is directly detected by the sensor attached to the voice coil, so that it strongly follows the music signal. This realizes low-frequency reproduction with excellent transient and faithful music source.

D-class amplifier is used in the amplifier section.
The latest Power MOS-FET has been carefully selected, and a specially designed PWM Class-D power amplifier circuit has been constructed to achieve a maximum output of 600W.
Furthermore, we have succeeded in forcing the diaphragm to drive by the newly developed low-noise, high-efficiency switching power supply and MFB velocity feedback function.

It is designed to continuously control the crossover of the high-cut filter in the range of 40 Hz to 120 Hz for matching with a wide range of speaker systems.

It is equipped with an adaptive control circuit that maintains stable operation for any input signal.
