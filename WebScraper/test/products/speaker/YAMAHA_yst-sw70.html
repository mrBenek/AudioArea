Commentary

This is a super woofer system that uses the Yamaha Active Servo Technology (YST) system.
The unit is driven linearly by a built-in amplifier, and its ultra-low frequency vibration is radiated as sound pressure from the port.

The built-in amplifier has an all-discrete configuration with a power of 60W.
In addition, the system can be upgraded regardless of the type of amplifier because it is equipped with 2 input terminals of speaker and pin.

Since the cut-off frequency is continuously variable from 50 Hz to 200 Hz, it can be set at the lowest point of the speaker and the sense of hearing.
The slope of the high-cut frequency is -24dB.

Equipped with a MUSIC/MOVIE sound quality switching switch which can switch the characteristics by software.

It is equipped with a phase switch that can be switched according to the type of speaker to be paired, the setting condition of the listening room and the preference of sound.
This eliminates the phase difference between the main speaker and the super-woofer.

Comes with a remote control that can control power ON/OFF, volume UP/DOWN, high-cut frequency UP/DOWN, and MUSIC/MOVIE switching.
