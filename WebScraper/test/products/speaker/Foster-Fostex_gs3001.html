Commentary

A front-load type speaker system designed for theaters, cinemas, stage PA, etc.

The low range is equipped with L470 which is 38 cm cone type woofer.

A horn type unit combining driver D252, H351, and HA21 is installed in the high range.

The enclosure consists of a front load horn and a bass reflex.
The exterior has a black finish.
