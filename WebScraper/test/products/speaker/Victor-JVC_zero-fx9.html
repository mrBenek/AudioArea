Commentary

A 3-way bookshelf speaker system that uses the world's first pure fine ceramics artificial jewel diaphragm.

A 33.5 cm cone type woofer using a 2 layer structure cone of fine ceramic fiber and carbon cloth is mounted in the low range.

A 6.5 cm dome type squaker using a composite fine ceramics diaphragm with added whisker materials is mounted in the middle area.

A 3 cm dome type tweeter using a pure fine ceramics polycrystal artificial jewel diaphragm is mounted in the high range.
The single-layer gemstone dome, which does not use metal base material, boasts about twice the sound speed and high-frequency reproduction range compared to aluminum, and is compatible with digital sources.

The network section uses selected high-performance elements to eliminate noise degradation factors such as interference, crosstalk, and magnetic distortion.

The front baffle is made of 30 mm thick plywood made of Karamatsu wood. It is finished into a super elliptical shape to eliminate diffraction phenomena. The unit is arranged in a G-line array by computer analysis to achieve hemispherical radiation characteristics close to the ideal.

The sound absorbing material uses 100% wool to reduce vibration noise of the sound absorbing material itself.
