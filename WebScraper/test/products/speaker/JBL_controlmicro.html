  • JBL Control Micro

  • ¥ 36,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1991)

Commentary

Control Series compact speaker system.

Newly developed 10 cm cone type full-range unit is used for the unit.
This unit not only expands the low-frequency reproduction capability but also reduces the distortion factor in the mid-range and high-frequency range.

Anti-magnetic design is applied.

There are 2 color variations, white and black.

Comes with a special mounting bracket for setting to the wall.
