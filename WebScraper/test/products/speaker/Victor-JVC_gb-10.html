Commentary

A non-directional speaker system designed to provide ideal stereo sound wherever you place it in your room.

The unit consists of eight speakers, four 7 cm cone-type tweeters and four 13 cm cone-type woofer speakers, which are arranged evenly and alternately along the spherical surface and emit sound in all directions : front-and-back, left-and-right, and up and down.

While taking advantage of the non-directional spherical form that does not feel the concentrated sound source and does not blur the sound image in the central part, it uses punching of high aperture ratio with good quality from middle and low range to high range.
We have also achieved stable sound localization by improving the network structure and introducing tuning technology.

A cone made by Kurt Mueller is used for the woofer diaphragm, and a double magnet system is used for the magnetic circuit.
In addition, Victor's proprietary silicon steel core is used in the network to achieve low distortion.

By inputting the L channel signal to System-A and the R channel signal to System-B, the stereo signal can be reproduced by one unit.
