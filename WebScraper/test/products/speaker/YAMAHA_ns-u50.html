  • YAMAHA NS-U50

  • ¥ 20,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in October 1999)

Commentary

A desktop speaker system designed for the RP-U100 multimedia amplifier.

It is equipped with a 9 cm cone type woofer by sprue scone in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter in the high range.

Comes with a dedicated slant stand for desktop use.
