  • ¥ 20,000 (1 unit, sold in February 1994)

Commentary

This 2-way speaker system is designed based on the basic concept of extending sound beyond the size and reproducing natural sound without exaggeration.

A 12 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
Bio-Croscone diaphragm made of wood pulp produced at high latitude near the Arctic Circle mixed with ascidian cellulose, which has the highest crystallinity among natural cellulose, is used.

A soft dome unit of 2.5 cm is adopted for the high range.
It uses a soft dome diaphragm with a special coating based on the same polyester fiber as the D-202A, which is a high-grade machine.

The network uses high--grade parts such as capacitors manufactured by Raderstein of Germany are used, and mutual interference is eliminated by the isolated wiring method in which low-frequency and high-frequency circuits are separately arranged.
In addition, a single unit receives a 4-octave band in which major instruments such as vocals are concentrated.

It is equipped with a large screw type terminal to which very thick speaker cable can be connected.
