Commentary

The L36 Decade's tweeter LE25-2 has been redesigned to LE25-4.
Designed for high efficiency and wide dynamic range.

125A of 25.5 cm aperture is used for the low band.
It is made with JBL's traditional technology such as the magnetic circuit using the Alnico V magnet and cone paper with the optimum mass and stiffness for low sound reproduction.

13 cm diameter LE5-6 is used for the middle area.
It has an aluminum center dome to make the connection with the tweeter smooth.

A 3.6 cm aperture LE25-4 is used for the high range.

The enclosure is 19 mm thick with high density, high compression material and an oil finish.
