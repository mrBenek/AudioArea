Commentary

This is a speaker system that condensed with thorough attention and technology to firmly express the comfortable natural sound and lively rich expression of music.

A 16 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
It uses an advanced bipolar cone diaphragm, an ultra-high-purity oxygen-free copper voice coil, and a magnetic circuit equipped with MDCT (magnetic distortion reduction technology).
In addition, the adoption of an advanced bipolar cone provides smooth high-frequency characteristics, and we have succeeded in connecting the amplifier and woofer directly without using a network.

A 2.5 cm soft dome unit with improved quality has been adopted for the high-frequency range by adopting an ultra-high-purity oxygen-free copper voice coil and applying a special coating to the diaphragm.

It incorporates the MID-4 concept, which allows one unit to receive a 4-octave band in which vocals and other major instruments are concentrated.

An all-MDF cabinet with a beautiful sound, acoustic stabilizer, and a banana plug gold-plated brass scraping terminal.
