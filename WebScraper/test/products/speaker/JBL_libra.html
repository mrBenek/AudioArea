Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system with a four sided finish.

It was designed for the LE14C, but it was compatible with other configurations and there were four variations.

The D53S11 has LE10A in the low range, LE20 in the high range, and LX4-2 in the network.
The LE10A is a 25 cm cone type woofer with a 2.7 kg magnet and a 7.5 cm voice coil made by edgewise winding of copper ribbon wire. In addition, the edge part uses Lansaloy edge.

There were Walnut oil finish and Ebony finish on the exterior.
