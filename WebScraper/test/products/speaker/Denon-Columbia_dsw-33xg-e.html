Commentary

Active subwoofer with LF DIRECT circuit.

The LF DIRECT circuit is designed to bypass the crossover circuit and level control circuit for a source bus managed by an AV amplifier, thereby eliminating phase distortion and signal degradation on the subwoofer side.

The enclosure uses a veneer glossy paint finish.

Uses large brass machined and gold-plated terminals for banana plugs.
