Commentary

A 3-way speaker system that uses a woofer with a conical dome and a panoramic horn.

A cone type woofer with a conical dome of 30 cm is used for the low range.
The conical Konica Le Dome prevents separation and parasitic vibration and further expands the pointing angle.

The mid-range is equipped with a horn type squaker that employs a cellular horn that improves directional characteristics.

In the high range, two panoramic horn tweeters with an acoustic lens for expanding the pointing angle are mounted.

The enclosure is completely sealed, and glass wool is used for the internal sound absorbing material.

Equipped with a level control attenuator that can adjust the middle and high frequencies.
