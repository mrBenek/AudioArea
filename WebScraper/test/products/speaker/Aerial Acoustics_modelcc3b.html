Commentary

Center speaker capable of controlling acoustic conditions.

The B-Series offers improved network performance and more openness in the mid-range.

The Environment switch and Program switch are mounted on the rear.
The Environment switch matches the system by adjusting the high-frequency and mid-frequency ranges. In addition to sound compensation, the Environment switch can be adjusted according to the installation method, such as installation on a stand, on a video monitor, or embedded installation.
The Program switch allows you to choose between two types of mid-range attenuation characteristics in addition to the flat characteristics.

It features an 18 cm cone woofer in the low range, a 12.7 cm cone midrange in the middle range, and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter in the high range.
These units have an anti-magnetic design that employs sealed magnets and a cast-frame frame.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
A 1 inch thick plate material is used for the front and rear, and the inside is fully reinforced to suppress unnecessary vibration.

There are 4 color variations on the exterior.

There was a speaker stand as an option sold separately.
