Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

The L99Lancer is a stereo pair of speakers with a energizer SG408S on the back of one enclosure, and the S99Athena is a single unit without energizer.

The low range is equipped with LE14A, a 35.5 cm cone woofer, and the high range is equipped with LE20-1, a 5 cm cone tweeter.

The enclosure has a hand-carved grid.
