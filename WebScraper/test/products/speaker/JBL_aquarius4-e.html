Commentary

The Aquarius series non-directional speaker system.

The Aquarius 4 adopts a 2-way configuration, and by combining each unit with a radial horn, acoustic energy is diffused in all directions of 360 ° to create a wide sound field.

The LE8T-2, a 20 cm cone unit, is installed in the low range, and the LE20-1, a 5 cm cone tweeter, is installed in the high range.

The enclosure was available in two color variations : walnut and satin white.
