Commentary

A performance series speaker system developed for sound reinforcement and musical instruments.

MI-10-1 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range, and 20 cm cone mid-range 2118J is installed in the middle range.
In addition, a horn type unit 2416H-1 using pure titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

The enclosure is a rigid multi-layer plywood coated with polyurethane to ensure high durability and water resistance.

Speaker stand MT4612 can be used.
