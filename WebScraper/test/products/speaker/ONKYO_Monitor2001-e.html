Commentary

This is a 3-way speaker system that uses an original isorayed mount system.

A 35 cm cone woofer with a pure cross carbon diaphragm is mounted in the low frequency range. This unit has a non-resonance structure with a curved cone with an elliptical cone bottom.

8 cm dome type skoker is mounted in the middle area.
A dome diaphragm made of plasma carbonite titanium is used for the diaphragm. In addition, two parts around the dome diaphragm are cut using high-precision laser processing technology. The peaks in frequency characteristics are flattened by dispersing the generation of resonance.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Plasma-carbonite titanium is used for the diaphragm, and a reinforcement structure is provided on the outer periphery of the dome to realize a strong resonance elimination effect.
In addition, an acoustic absorber, a damping material that absorbs the vibration of the frame, is used for the mounting of the tweeter to eliminate unnecessary vibration.
