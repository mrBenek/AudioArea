Commentary

This is a speaker system with a skeleton design that can be placed freely.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a paper cone for the diaphragm to obtain natural sound quality.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Uses an anti-magnetic design.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The exterior comes in 3 color variations : S (silver), MS (mint skeleton) and BS (black skeleton). The mint skeleton and black skeleton are designed so that the contents can be seen through.

Comes standard with brackets that can be attached to walls and ceilings.
