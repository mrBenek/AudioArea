  • ¥ 22,000 (a set of two cars, around 1988)

Commentary

This is a small speaker system with a full-scale design and an upgraded sound.
There are 2 types of finishes, wood-grain and gray with Tasman maple.

A 10 cm Cross Edge cone type unit is used for the low range, and a 4-layer winding is used for the winding of the drive section.
In addition, a 2.5 cm soft dome type unit with special coating is adopted for the high range.

The network is designed with a crossover frequency of 2 kHz and a slope of 12dB/oct.

The enclosure is made of high-density material with a rounded baffle finish on the front face.
