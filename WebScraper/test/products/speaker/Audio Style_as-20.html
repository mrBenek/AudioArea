  • Audio Style Premium Limited AS-20

  • ¥ 200,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in December 1989)

Commentary

Premium limited model speaker system.

An 8 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Carbon fiber cone and twin magnet system are adopted.

A 5.1 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
Titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure uses African Bubinga and has a mahogany finish.
It has a one piece structure.

The AS-20 comes with a dedicated speaker cable.
This cable has a special L/R asymmetric structure with carbon treatment of copper wire and silver wire for the hearing sense of the left and right ears.

A certificate describing the frequency characteristics is attached to each product.
