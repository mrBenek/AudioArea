  • ATC SCM10

  • ¥ 300,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1991)

Commentary

A basic model speaker system designed mainly for high-quality home use.

A 12.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit has an allowable stroke of 20 mm, reduces distortion by moving the voice coil in a uniform magnetic field at all times, and achieves input resistance of 300W.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
Also, there were 2 kinds of variations of the appearance, Yew finish and Black Ash finish.

There was a speaker stand as an option sold separately.
