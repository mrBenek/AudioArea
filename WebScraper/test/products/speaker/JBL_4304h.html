Commentary

A compact speaker system with double woofer.

The 4304H supports a wide range of playback from stereo to multi-channel, and is also ideal for center / surround multi-channel systems with large speakers equipped with compression drivers and horns.
In addition, the compact design and high sound-penetration power of the powerful screwdriver and horn enable the setting behind the sound screen.

Two 13.3 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
Pure pulp white cone is used for the diaphragm, and accordion pleated edge is used for the edge to realize crisp low-frequency reproduction.
Aluminum die-cast frame is used for the frame.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit combines a 2.5 cm pure titanium compression driver with a horn to produce a unique horn sound.

Anti-magnetic structure with cancel magnet and shield cover is adopted.

A bass-reflex system is adopted for the enclosure, and high-strength MDF is used for the plate material.
The exterior has a black ash natural wood finish.

Wall / ceiling universal brackets were sold separately as an option.
