  • JBL 4731A

Commentary

Sound Power Series 2-way speaker system.

Equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer 2226H in the low range.
In addition, a horn type unit combining a driver 2426J and a flat front biradial horn 2370A is mounted in the high range.

A debinding network is built in.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

In addition to the same enclosure size, the 4730A and 4731A are almost equivalent in efficiency, maximum allowable input power and maximum sound pressure. The difference between the two models is mainly in the directional characteristics and sound quality in the middle and high range.
