Commentary

Stage monitor speaker of MR series.

It is equipped with newly developed 30 cm cone type woofer 2022H in the low range.
A newly developed 100 ゜ x80 ゜ flat-front biradial horn 2373 and a compression front biradial horn and a compression driver 2416H-1 using pure titanium diaphragm.

The enclosure has a dual-angle design with two choices of baffle face angle (30 ° or 60 °) depending on the setting.

The corner of the enclosure is equipped with protectors.

The MR series input connectors are equipped with two standard 6.3 mm jacks.
