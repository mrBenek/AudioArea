Commentary

This 3-way speaker system uses a flat diaphragm.

It is equipped with a 12 cm planar woofer with fo = 35 hz for the low range, a 10 cm planar squaker for the middle range, and a 2.5 cm planar tweeter for the high range.

It is a handy type with a handle on the upper part of the enclosure for easy carrying.
