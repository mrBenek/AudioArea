Commentary

This is a 3-way speaker system corresponding to the unit change model of L300.

A 38 cm cone type woofer 136H is installed in the low range.
This unit uses ribbed corn paper with the optimum mass and stiffness for the diaphragm, as well as an SFG magnetic circuit and a precisely designed voice coil to enable even flat playback.

A horn type unit using a driver unit LE85 and a horn lens HL92 is mounted in the middle area to realize excellent directional characteristics.

A horn type tweeter 077 using a ring type diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

The enclosure is designed with a slanted front baffle for the best listening position as a floor type, and uses smoked glass for the top plate.

There are 4 color variations for the net grill, and you can choose from blue, brown, camel and black.
