  • Toshiba/Aurex SS-16

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, released in April 1969)

Commentary

A sealed two way speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
By adopting high-compliance free edge using newly developed material and special molding technology, low sound reproduction capability is improved.

The high-frequency band is equipped with an 8x4cm horn type tweeter that reduces the weight of the vibration section by using a special molded resin that is light and highly rigid.

Equipped with level control, you can adjust the sound quality according to the listening environment.

The enclosure uses a high-density particle board and is completely sealed with no screws on the back plate.
In addition, the front net can be removed.
