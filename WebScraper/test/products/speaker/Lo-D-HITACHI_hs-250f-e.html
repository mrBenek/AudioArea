  • HITACHI/Lo-D HS-250F

  • ¥ 25,800 (1 unit, around 1970)

Commentary

Lordi's first 3-way speaker system.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 12 cm cone squaker is installed in the middle range.

The H-54H horn type tweeter is used for the high frequency range.

Equipped with level controls for the high and middle ranges, each of which can be switched to 3 levels.

The enclosure uses the ダンプド and bass reflex method.
The exterior is finished with walnut and open boa.

Equipped with a multi-amplifier terminal with 2 ch and 3 ch switching switches on the back.
