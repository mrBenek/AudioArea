Commentary

The woofer is made of natural mica, which boasts high rigidity, mixed with natural pulp in order to increase internal loss, and coated with damping paint. The vibration range of the piston is expanded and the sound-pressure-frequency characteristics in the divided vibration range are stabilized. The voice coil is a two layer long voice coil made of polyamide fused wire that can withstand high temperatures of as high as 250 degrees.

Pure Amica is used for the tweeter.

In order to reduce resonance between both units, a slit is installed between the woofer and the tweeter, and 21 mm thick birch board is used for the baffle plate.
In addition, the enclosure has a round finish to reduce the disturbance of frequency characteristics caused by sound wraparound and diffraction phenomena, thereby further improving sound localization.

A sealed frame structure with reduced unwanted sound reflection is adopted, and viscoelastic foam material is attached to the periphery of the tweeter to reduce vibration of the baffle plate and improve sound field feeling.
