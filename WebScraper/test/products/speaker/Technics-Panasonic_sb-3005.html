  • Technics SB-3005

  • ¥ 33,200 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

Floor type 3-way speaker system.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses 50 micron hard aluminum foil with high Young's modulus for the voice coil bobbin, and is designed as a long travel voice coil.

Equipped with 12 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.
It has a back cavity filled with sound absorbing material and realizes a low fo of 300 Hz.

A 6.5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

A full-scale network has been adopted in the Network Division.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
