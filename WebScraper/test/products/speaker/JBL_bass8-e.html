Commentary

An active subwoofer that is perfect for pairing with JBL's compact speakers.

The unit is equipped with a 20 cm white corn woofer.
It uses a direct radiator system that directly radiates sound from the subwoofer in the listening direction.

It has two line-level and speaker-level inputs and a built-in 100W discrete power amplifier.
It also has a built-in protection circuit to protect the unit.
