Commentary

This speaker system pursues the W & D (Wide & Dynamic) concept in order to respond to the dynamic range of digital sources.
The maximum instantaneous input power of 300W has been obtained by thoroughly improving the response of high level and minute level, and by reviewing the fixing method and even the details of the network.

In the lower range, the diaphragm is equipped with a 33.5 cm cone-shaped woofer made of fine ceramic, which is approximately 1.8 times more rigid than paper cone.
This diaphragm is a cone developed for Zero-50Fine. It is made by adding a special adhesive to fine ceramic fiber, making it like a paper cone, and pressing it at a high sound.
In addition, an aluminum die-cast frame is used for the frame, and the structure is sandwich locked to the baffle with a total of 12 screws.

A 12 cm compound skoker with fine ceramic diaphragm is mounted in the middle area.
The complex structure of heat-resistant aluminum base with fine ceramic particles fused on the surface and the highly rigid shape obtained by computer analysis expand the range of precise pistic motion.
In addition, by strengthening the coupling with the voice coil strengthened to reduce transmission loss, and the vibration support point is made clear. In this way, we pursue a response that is faithful to both large amplitude and small amplitude.

The Dynaflat Ribbon Tweeter MkV is installed in the high range.
This is the fifth unit of the Tweeter developed with the Zero Series. A voice coil etched in a heat-resistant resin film directly drives air.
Mark V has improved its input resistance and thermal distortion characteristics by strengthening the vibration system and newly installing a copper radiator piece.

The enclosure uses a soft wood particle board with a beautiful material and is made solid and uniform by a unique high-pressure flash consolidation method.

The net part uses a caulking connection that does not use solder.
