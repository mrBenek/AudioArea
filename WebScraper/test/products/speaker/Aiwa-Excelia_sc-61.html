  • AIWA SC-61(R/S)

  • ¥ 25,400 (a set of two cars, around 1979)

Commentary

Speaker system of mini component my Pace3 series.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Lightweight, high-Young's modulus cone paper is used for the diaphragm. A special manufacturing method has been used to provide moderate internal loss, enabling playback of low and medium sound with sharp teeth.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm and the cap are integrally molded.

Equipped with an attenuator that can adjust the high-frequency level.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
Hard particle board is used for the plate material.

There are 2 kinds of variations, Rosewood type SC-61R and silver type SC-61S.
