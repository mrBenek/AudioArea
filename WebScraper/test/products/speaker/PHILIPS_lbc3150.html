  • PHILIPS LBC3150

  • ¥ 185,000 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

Professional designed 3-way 6-speaker speaker system.

It has been used in a wide range of fields such as recording studios, TV studios, theaters and discitics in various European countries.

A 12-inch (30 cm) cone woofer is installed in the low range.

The mid-range features two 3x5-inch (7.6x13cm) elliptical cone scokers and a 4-inch (10 cm) cone scoker, and the high-range features two 1-inch (2.5 cm) domed tweeters.
The elliptical squaker and tweeter are designed to face inward toward each other to improve the high-range directivity.

A metal net is used for the front grill to consider the effect on sound quality.
