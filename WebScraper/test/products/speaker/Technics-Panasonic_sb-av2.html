Commentary

An AV speaker system that uses a honeycomb flat diaphragm.

A 14 cm flat woofer is installed in the low range.
A unique honeycomb flat diaphragm is used for the diaphragm of this unit to realize low distortion and flat frequency characteristics.

A 2.8 cm flat type tweeter using a honeycomb flat diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

It uses a magnetic shielding design that does not cause color unevenness even when placed next to a cathode-ray tube television.

The enclosure is aluminum die-cast and has a screw hole at the bottom for attachment of accessories for free installation.
