Commentary

This is a speaker system based on the famous Technics1 with improved characteristics using a dome type unit.

A 12 cm cone woofer of high compliance type is installed in the low range.

A 10-cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high-frequency band to improve directional characteristics and distortion characteristics.

The network is 12dB/oct. for the woofer and 18dB/oct. for the tweeter, and is designed for each speaker.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
In addition, the exterior has an open pore finish using teak material.
