Commentary

The Super Tweeter uses a direct drive system and achieves ultra-high bandwidth playback up to 90 kHz.

In order to ensure the best listening area and obtain uniform sound pressure / phase response, a new crossover network has been developed and directivity has been improved.

The enclosure uses a veneer glossy paint finish.
It can also be used as a wall-mounted speaker by reducing the depth.

Uses large brass machined and gold-plated terminals for banana plugs.
