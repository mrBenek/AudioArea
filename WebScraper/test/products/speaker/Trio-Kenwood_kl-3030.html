  • TRIO KL-3030

  • ¥ 21,500 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

A 2-way speaker system that uses a sub-baffle in the tweeter.

A roll-free edge 25 cm cone woofer is used for the low range, and a chamber 5 cm cone type tweeter is used for the high range.

The enclosure is laid out with sub-baffles.
In addition, the material was reexamined from the hearing test, and lauan plywood was used for the baffle surface, and homogen material was used for other parts.
In addition, the bass reflex duct suppresses the boomy feeling by applying a special brake.

The tweeter is equipped with a continuously variable level control.
