  • ONKYO D-22F

  • ¥ 21,500 (1 unit, released in November 1992)

Commentary

A small 3-way speaker system with excellent cost performance.

It uses 18 cm cone type units for the low band, 8 cm cone type units for the middle band, and 2.5 cm soft dome type units for the high band.

A 24 mm thick particle board is used for the baffle plate.
