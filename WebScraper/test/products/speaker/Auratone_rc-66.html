  • AURATONE RC-66

  • ¥ 129,000 (1 unit, around 1983)

Commentary

A 2-way speaker system developed by inheriting the know-how of 5C.

Two 16.5 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
Polypropylene cone is used for the diaphragm.

A 3.4 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The exterior is finished with leather and the corners are equipped with stainless steel protectors. Metal grill is used for the front grill.
It is designed so that it can be locked for carrying in 2 sets.
