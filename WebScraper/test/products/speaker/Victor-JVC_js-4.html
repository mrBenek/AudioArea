Commentary

A low-cost speaker system designed for beginners and 4-channel rear speakers.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
KDU cone paper made by Kurt Mueller is used for the diaphragm.

A 7 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
It has a 4-sided exterior finish and can be mounted on the wall with wall mounting hardware on the back.
