  • Marantz Imperial 9

  • ¥ 189,000 (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

Marantz's large speaker system.

Two 25 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.

The mid-range has four cone mid-range and the high-range has two cone tweeters.
The middle and high range units are mounted on an air couple board to improve the sound diffusion effect while suppressing distortion.

The unit layout uses symmetrical mirror imaging to improve stereo separation by making stereo images and sound diffusion effects symmetrical.
The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

A diamond cut grill is used for the front grill to enhance the design and sound diffusion effect.

It can be placed vertically or horizontally.
