Commentary

This center / satellite speaker system is ideal for combination with S3500 and S2500.

Two 16.5 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
JBL traditional pulp cone with corrugation rib is used for the diaphragm.

A horn type tweeter using a 2.5 cm driver unit is mounted in the high range.
Pure titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm and biradial horn is used for the horn.
The horn / driver section is designed to is designed to be rotated 90 degrees and can be used not only as a center speaker but also as a vertical array satellite speaker.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The exterior has a veneer finish with cherry natural wood.
