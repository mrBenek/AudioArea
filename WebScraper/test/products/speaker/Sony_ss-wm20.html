Commentary

A left / right integrated speaker system that can be connected to the headphone port of a Walkman or other device.

Newly developed tension film diaphragm and strong samarium-cobalt magnetic circuit are adopted.

It is a carrying type that can be folded into a cassette case size that fits vertically and horizontally.
