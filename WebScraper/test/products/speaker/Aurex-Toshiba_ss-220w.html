Commentary

This is a bookshelf type speaker system with high efficiency.

A 20 cm cone woofer with a concave diaphragm is mounted in the low range.
The diaphragm is of the concave type with a concave center cap. The center cap itself is used as part of the diaphragm to minimize the swell near the crossover and to reduce the extreme resonance peak. It has good characteristics. In addition, the diaphragm is made of light-weight unbleached cone with a light rise.
A unique radial edge is adopted for the edge, and by adopting an Ω shape with two types of curves with different radius of curvature, split vibration at the edge is suppressed.

A free edge type 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a pressure balance system.
The pressure balance system is close to hermetic, and is designed to balance the internal pressure by allowing only unwanted vibrations outside the audible band to escape from the baffle port, thereby reducing the hermetic enclosure and the bus-ref-type boumey.
