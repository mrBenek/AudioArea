  • FOSTER/FOSTEX F-30

  • ¥ 29,000 (1 unit, released in 1969)

Commentary

A speaker system designed for the pleasure of listening to music.
It is the upper model of F-25.

The F-30 is equipped with two newly developed 13 cm cone skokers in the mid-range, which were manufactured through repeated trial manufacture and experiments in response to the F-30's technical team's desire for enjoyment of listening.
In addition, a 30 cm cone type woofer with roll edge is mounted in the low range, and a horn type tweeter designed for F-25/F-30 is mounted in the high range.

Equipped with 3-step level control, you can adjust the level of middle and high range according to the characteristics of the listening room.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The exterior has a four sided walnut finish.
