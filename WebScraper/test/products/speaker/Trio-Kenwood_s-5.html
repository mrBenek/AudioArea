  • TRIO S-5

  • ¥ 25,000 (1 unit, around 1979)

Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer using prescone made of special pulp is installed in the low range.
It also uses a concave roll edge for the edge.

A 3-cm soft dome type tweeter with special coating is installed in the high range.

The enclosure has slits on the baffle plate surface to prevent the woofer vibration from being transmitted to the tweeter.

Equipped with continuously variable level control.
