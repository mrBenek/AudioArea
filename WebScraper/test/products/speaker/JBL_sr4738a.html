Commentary

Large speaker system of SR series using 46 cm unit.

It is ideal as a main speaker system for small discos and dance clubs, as a stage monitor for large PA keyboards and synth drums, and as a large side fill.

The low range is equipped with 46 cm cone type woofer 2241G.
The mid-range is equipped with a 25 cm cone type mid-range 2123H, and the high-range is equipped with a horn-type unit combining a Horn 2371 with 90 ° horizontal and 40 ° vertical directional characteristics and a driver unit 2417H.

It has a built-in network.
In addition, a 2-way multi-amplifier drive changeover switch is mounted.
