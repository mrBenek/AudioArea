Commentary

A top-of-the-line speaker system that uses DENON's unique non-resonant enclosure.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The corn uses a collagen fiber that is fused with corn paper after a special chemical treatment of animal skin. By giving appropriate internal resistance, resonance of peaks and dips is suppressed.

The mid-range is equipped with a 12 cm cone type squaker that uses a collagen speaker as well as the low-range.

A horn tweeter with a light metal diaphragm is installed in the high frequency range.

DENON's unique non-resonant enclosure is used.
We developed a special material that absorbs unwanted vibrations and sandwiched it in a sandwich structure. This greatly reduces the resonance of the enclosure and greatly reduces intermodulation distortion in the mid - and high-frequency range.

Measurement data was attached to each product.
