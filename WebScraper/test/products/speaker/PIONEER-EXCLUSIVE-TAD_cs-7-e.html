Commentary

This is a bookshelf type speaker system where you can easily enjoy the impact of a 30 cm woofer.

The unit is a 3-way configuration with a 30 cm cone woofer for the low range, a 16 cm cone squaker for the middle range, and a 6.6 cm cone tweeter for the high range.

The cabinet is a natural wood walnut with an oil finish.
