Commentary

A tall boy type speaker system that is a compact version of SCIII.

Two 15 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
These two units are mounted above and below the center of the enclosure.

It has a 9 cm cone mid-range in the middle range and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter in the high range.
The tweeter unit is the same as the BNS professional speaker.

The enclosure has a unique 3-chamber configuration that prevents Woofer back-pressure from affecting the midrange.
The exterior is designed to cover 4 sides with seamless Saran.

Silver cable (van den Hul CS12) is used for internal wiring.

Anti-resonance spike was sold separately.
