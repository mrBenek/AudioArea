Commentary

Sound Power Series compact 2-way speaker system.

It can be used for a wide range of purposes, such as permanent use for small to medium-sized disks and clubs, or as a stage monitor for concerts.

It is equipped with 2123H which is a 25 cm cone type woofer in the low range.
In addition, for the high range, we have adopted Compression Driver 2416H using pure titanium diaphragm and Biradial Horn 2342 with 2.5 cm throat.

The enclosure is a wedge type.
