  • CHRYSLER CE-6aIII

  • ¥ 29,900 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

Speaker system that can be mounted on the wall.

The unit used is the same as the CE-5aII, with a 20 cm cone woofer in the low range, a 12 cm cone squaker in the middle range, and a 5 cm horn tweeter in the high range.

Level control is mounted on the front.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The front baffle uses a metal face with black finish, which is the feature of this series.

Wall mount brackets are attached to the back of the cabinet.
