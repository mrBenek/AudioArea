Commentary

A 5.1 ch speaker system consisting of five satellite speakers D-L1 and a subwoofer SKW-10.

The D-L1 uses an 8 cm full-range speaker with an OMF diaphragm and an S-line edge that reduces the reflection of propagating waves at the diaphragm and edge junction.
The enclosure is made of MDF material with 60-mm-pitch screw holes for brackets at the bottom and wall mounting holes at the back.

The subwoofer SKW-10 uses the J' DRIVE system to eliminate the time lag with the main speaker.
The unit is equipped with a 16 cm cone woofer with an OMF diaphragm and S-line edge.
MDF material is used for the enclosure.
