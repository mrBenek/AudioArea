Commentary

This is a speaker system that is small but aims for a powerful performance and the space required by a large system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In order to obtain the best constant in a closed cabinet, a cone type diaphragm with a balance weight is adopted to increase bass sound.
In addition, we have succeeded in improving input resistance by adopting polyimide bobbin which is strong for large input and 4-layer wound voice coil to increase driving force and prevent decrease of Qm.
In order to suppress harmful sharp high-frequency resonance and obtain a sense of fulfillment in the mid-range, we have adopted a dome-shaped radiator that also serves as a dust-proof cloth.

A 3-cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Using a frame with precision aluminum friction processing, it contributes to high-range transient and efficiency improvement by strengthening rigidity.

Uses an enclosure with an open pore finish made of real wood with excellent strength.
