  • Marantz Model5G MkII

  • ¥ 39,500 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

This is an all-mighty speaker system with good bandwidth balance and less distortion.

8 inch (20 cm) cone woofer is installed in the low range.
Roll edge is used for the edge, and the stroke is large.

An edgeless 1.25 inch (3 cm) cone-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.

Equipped with a high-frequency control switch, 3-position switching is possible.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
