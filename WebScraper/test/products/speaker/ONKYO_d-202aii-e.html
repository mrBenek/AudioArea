Commentary

A small 2-way speaker system created by refining the D 202 a.

A 16 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
Bicycloscone diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and it is made by non-press manufacturing method in order to increase the strength.
In addition, by applying a special coating to the outer part, smooth high-frequency attenuation characteristics can be obtained, and by adopting a direct wiring method which can be driven without using a network, higher quality reproduction is possible.
It is also equipped with an acoustic stabilizer that reduces the vibration of the woofer.

A 2.5 cm dome type unit is adopted for the high frequency band.
The diaphragm is a soft dome that uses pure silk as a fiber material that is strong and has excellent follow-up performance.
We have also applied film capacitors made by German company WIMA to the network elements.

High-purity 6N-OFC voice coil is used for both woofer and tweeter.

The interior of the cabinet is equipped with an irregularly shaped reinforcing plate, which increases rigidity and provides sound. In addition, it is also excellent in suppressing standing waves that tend to occur inside the cabinet.

The speaker terminal uses a gold-plated brass scraping terminal for banana plug.
