Commentary

This center speaker is designed with an emphasis on middle and low frequency reproduction in the range from 250 Hz to 4 kHz, which is related to the tone of human voice, and with an awareness of the important dialogue and vocals of the center speaker.

Two 12 cm PP cone type woofers that can be used as a full range are used for the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

Directional characteristics are improved by adopting a virtual coaxial arrangement in which two woofers are arranged so as to sandwich the tweeter.
In addition, it adopts a slant face design in which the baffle surface is inclined by about 10 degrees, and it can be set directly facing the listening position.
