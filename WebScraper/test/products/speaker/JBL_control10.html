Commentary

Control Series 3-way speaker system.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
A unique SFG magnetic circuit is adopted for the magnetic circuit to reduce distortion.

The mid-range is equipped with a 12.5 cm cone type mid-range.

A dome type tweeter using a pure titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high range.

It has an anti-magnetic design and can be adjacent to CRT equipment.

The mid-range and high-range attenuators allow for adjustment according to the listening environment.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
It is also designed to mount a power amplifier directly on the back of the speaker, so you can mount a power amplifier 6210 or 6211.

Separately sold options included wall brackets, ceiling brackets and adapters for the MT4612 stand.
