  • Marantz LS-50

  • ¥ 20,000 (1 unit, sold in March 1981)

Commentary

A compact speaker system.

It is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter with magnetic fluid in the high range.

There was a special stand with steel pipe finish sold separately.
