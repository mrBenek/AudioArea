Commentary

Sound Power Series large 2-way speaker system.

Two 38 cm cone type woofer 2226H are installed in the low range.
In the high range, a horn type unit is installed that combines a compression driver 2445J using a 10 cm diameter titanium diaphragm with a flat-front biradial horn 2380A.

The wedge-type enclosure can be arranged horizontally into a cluster, extending coverage by 30 degrees horizontally for each additional enclosure.

It is optimum to be used together with sub base system 4788A.

Bi-amplifier drive system is adopted.
