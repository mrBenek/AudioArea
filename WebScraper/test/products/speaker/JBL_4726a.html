Commentary

Sound Power Series 2-way speaker system.

It can be used for a wide range of purposes, such as permanent use for small to medium-sized disks and clubs, or as a stage monitor for concerts.
The 4726 ap has a single amplifier configuration with the NP4726AP network built in to the 4726A.

In the lower range, it is equipped with 2206H which is a 30 cm cone type woofer using the bent gap cooling (VGC) method.
In addition, for the high range, we have adopted a combination driver 2426J using pure titanium diaphragm and a biradial horn 2344A with 2.5 cm throat.

The enclosure is a wedge type.
