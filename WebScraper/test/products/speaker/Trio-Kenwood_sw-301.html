  • KENWOOD SW-301

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 unit, released in 1997)

Commentary

An active subwoofer developed to realize a powerful low sound reproduction.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is used in the unit and it is a bass reflex type direct radiation system.

Sigma Drive, which is well known for its single component, is used in the amplifier section.

Equipped with a 2-way surround mode switch that allows you to select the optimum sound quality according to the characteristics of each movie and music.

Equipped with auto power ON/OFF circuit.

The magnetic shielding design does not affect the magnetic field lines of the TV screen.
