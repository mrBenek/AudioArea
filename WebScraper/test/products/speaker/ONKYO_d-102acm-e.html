  • ONKYO D-102ACM

  • ¥ 38,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

This is a compact speaker system based on the D 102 a, while ensuring a smooth sound and natural sound.

A 12 cm cone type woofer by non-press bio-cross cone is mounted in the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is mounted in the high range.

A condenser made by Raderstein, Germany is used in the network section.

It uses a terminal that can use thick cables.

The speaker cable XP made by Monster is included as an accessory.
