  • ¥ 40,000 (a set of two cars, around 1995)

Commentary

This is a 2-way speaker system from the Component Series focusing on acoustic sound.

The low-frequency band is equipped with a 14 cm cone type woofer with damping treatment, and the high-frequency band is equipped with a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using a newly developed specially woven diaphragm. Both units do not rely on measurement data, but rather emphasize the sense of hearing and are completed after careful trial listening.
By mounting both units on a solid aluminum die-cast board, unwanted vibrations that cause sound nigori and noise are controlled.

The enclosure has a sealed structure.

Speaker cable with connection direction indication is included.
