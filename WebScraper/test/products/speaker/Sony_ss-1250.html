Audio Database

  • SONY SS-1250/SS-1250B

  • ¥ 39,000 (a set of two cars, around 1975)

Commentary

A speaker system equipped with 20 cm woofer and 20 cm passive radiator using Carbocon.

The woofer uses a low-distortion magnetic circuit and high-compliance special edge to achieve high efficiency and low distortion.

A 6 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

There was also a SS 1250 b with a different finish.
