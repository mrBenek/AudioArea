Commentary

A 3-way speaker system with a furniture style enclosure.

Two 30-cm cone type woofers are installed in the range.
This unit uses a newly developed foamed resin edge with high amplitude and good linearity.

The mid-range is equipped with a 20 cm cone type mid-range.
This unit is braked by a viscose.

A dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.

LC type is used for the network part.
In addition, it is equipped with balance control, and the response can be adjusted according to room acoustic.
