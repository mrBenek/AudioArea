Commentary

A speaker system that has inherited the W & D (Wide & Dynamic) philosophy and introduced the latest aircraft technology to expand the range of Skorker piste nick motion and dynamic range.

A 30 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
We have reviewed our magnetic circuits and reduced current distortion by improving magnetic efficiencies with edge wound and voice coils.
Victor Co., Ltd.'s unique alpha-corn paper has improved the impregnation agent and reinforced rib structure to further improve the synthesis.
We have also reconsidered various aspects such as the adoption of a sealed edge structure to suppress the natural sound of the edge.

A 10 cm cone type unit is used for the middle area.
We have newly completed a fine ceramic diaphragm with a composite structure of aluminum base material and adopted it as a diaphragm.
It is produced by fusing fine ceramic particles to the surface of corrosion-resistant aluminum at ultra-high temperatures of up to 10,000 ℃. At the same time, we have developed a deep drawing technology for corrosion-resistant aluminum, which was impossible in the past. We have also succeeded in realizing the shape of the diaphragm, which is close to the ideal shape obtained by computer.
Strong magnetic circuits, such as 50-mm-diameter edge wound voice coils and strontium ferrite magnets, are used.

A newly designed dynaflat ribbon tweeter is used for the high-frequency range in response to skoker.
A voice coil printed on a polyimide resin film directly drives air, realizing a wide range of up to 100 kHz.

The network uses carefully selected elements such as epoxy dipping coils, three types of low-loss metalized films, capacitors, and oxygen-free copper wiring materials with small twist pitches.

With an emphasis on the sound quality of the attenuator, a 4-step switching constant impedance L-type level controller is adopted.

The unit layout is finely adjusted by a unique phase-moire transmission pattern measuring method, and a precision bass reflex cabinet is adopted in which standing wave and reflection are suppressed by special internal processing such as reflector panel.
