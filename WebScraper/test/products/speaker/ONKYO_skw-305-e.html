  • ONKYO SKW-305

  • ¥ 30,000 (1 unit, sold on November 1, 1998)

Commentary

A subwoofer system with a built-in amplifier that employs a dual drive system.

Two 16 cm cone type woofer units are used in the unit. The dual drive system mounted on both left and right sides suppresses the vibration of the cabinet and realizes the excellent transient low sound reproduction.

Movie/Music mode switching is installed.

The cutoff frequency can be varied from 50 Hz to 200 Hz.
