  • VICTOR/JVC GB-2

  • ¥ 19,000 (1 unit, around 1971)

Commentary

A speaker system that improves cost performance while taking advantage of the non-directional characteristics of the GB-1D.

Six 8cm cone type full-range units are carried in the unit.

It has a built-in low-pass cut circuit and can be used as a medium to high range speaker by changing the way the speaker cord is inserted to cut the low pass.

The enclosure has a 14-sided design and can be hung from the ceiling using chains.
In addition, by using the stand for GB-1D, it can be used as a floor type.
