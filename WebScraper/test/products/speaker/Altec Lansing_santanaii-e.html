Commentary

A floor-type speaker system equivalent to an improved version of Santa Ana.

A 38 cm cone woofer of type 411 to 8 a is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 10 cm cone type direct radiator 427-8A is mounted in the high range.

The cabinet uses a bass reflex system.
The exterior features a black top plate with a natural slate pattern, and the overall design is a five sided wood finish including the rear panel.
