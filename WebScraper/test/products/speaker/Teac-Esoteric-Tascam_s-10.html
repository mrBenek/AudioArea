  • TEAC S-10

  • ¥ 29,800 (a set of two cars, around 1985)

Commentary

An AV speaker system specially developed for surround / BGM.

The SRSS (Sound Reflection Speaker System) developed by TEAC's original AV technology is used to ensure the wide directivity necessary for surround and BGM playback.
This product mounts a 16 cm mechanical 2-way unit in the reverse direction. Since the sound reproduced from the speaker is reflected by the reflection plate (bottom plate), it can obtain an extremely wide directivity and a soft sound without getting tired of listening.

Since the unit is a magnetic shielding type using cancel magnet, it does not disturb the image.

It is basically designed to be hung on the wall, but it can be set in various ways such as putting it directly on the floor or attaching it downward on the ceiling by utilizing the light and thin type.

There were 2 colors, black and pearl white.
