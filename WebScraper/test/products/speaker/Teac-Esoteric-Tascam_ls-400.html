Commentary

A full-scale three way speaker system with a walnut finish enclosure.

It is equipped with a 30 cm cone type woofer in the low range, two 10 cm cone type skokers in the middle range with in the middle range, and two 5 cm horn type tweeters in the high range.

The network section focuses on the mid-range and reduces the crossover frequency with the low-range to prevent interference with the mid-range and high-range of the woofer.

Independent level controls are provided for the mid and high ranges.
3-step switching by coil tap switching method is adopted for the middle range, and continuous variable by attenuator is adopted for the high range.

A push-type speaker terminal is used.
The code is color-coded white and blue and connects white to + and blue to -.
