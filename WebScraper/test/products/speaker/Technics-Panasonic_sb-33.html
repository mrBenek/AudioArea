  • National SB-33

  • ¥ 24,800 (late 1960 s)

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system with level control added to the SB-32.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In this unit, newly developed special fiber is used in the roll edge to improve the low sound reproduction capability.

A 9 cm cone type squaker is mounted in the middle area.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is a closed type using a special integrated structure method, and the fo is low by using abundant sound absorbing material inside.
