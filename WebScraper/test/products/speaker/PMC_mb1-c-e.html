Commentary

A center speaker designed for center monitoring during surround playback.

It is equipped with 31 cm radial cone type woofer in the low range, 7.5 cm dome type midrange in the middle range and 2.7 cm soft dome type tweeter in the high range.

The speaker terminal supports tri-wiring.

The enclosure uses a transmission line system.
In addition, there are 3 types of finishes, MB1-P-BA is a brush ash finish, MB1-P-AW is a walnut finish, and MB1-P-R is a rosewood finish.
