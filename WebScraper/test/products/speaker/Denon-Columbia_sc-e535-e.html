Commentary

The E-series speaker system was developed with the aim of natural harmony between music and people by reconsidering the origin of the connection between music and people.

A 17 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses an edge made of a rubber-based material with excellent pulsed transient response and a rubber-based center cap for smooth connection with the tweeter.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter of wide directional angle type is installed in the high range.
By improving the directional characteristics, a wide listening area of up / down / left / right 120 ° is secured.

The baffle plate of the enclosure is made of MDF material to ensure higher strength than particleboard, and coating with natural collagen fiber reduces unnecessary radiation.
Each unit is equipped with a front baffle made of rubber-based resin to eliminate unwanted vibration.

The terminal is equipped with a -wiring in the terminal section, interference from the woofer to the tweeter is suppressed.
