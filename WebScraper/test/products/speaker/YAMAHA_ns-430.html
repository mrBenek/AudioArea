  • YAMAHA NS-430

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

Bookshelf type speaker designed for low cost and high efficiency.

A 20 cm roll-edge type cone woofer JA-2004 is mounted in the low range.
A copper cap is attached to the center pole to reduce 3rd-harmonic distortion and enable clear low-frequency playback. In addition, the connection with the tweeter is made smooth by suppressing the rise in impedance.

The high-frequency band is equipped with a hard-dome type tweeter JA-0551 with a diaphragm made of cloth shaped with thermosetting resin.

In the network section, constant values are determined through repeated listening and measurement, and low-frequency playback is improved by using an MP capacitor with superior characteristics.

The enclosure uses a bass-reflex structure.
