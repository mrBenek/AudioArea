Commentary

A speaker system with a magnetic shielding structure by adopting a double magnet system.

A 28 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm of this unit uses a cross-carbon fine ceramic cone.

Equipped with a 8.5 cm cone type squaker in the middle area.
The diaphragm of this unit adopts a composite reinforced structure in which diamond single crystal particles and silicon carbide whiskers are added to carbon fiber, and realizes a diaphragm suitable for the service band.

A 2.5 cm hard dome type tweeter using a fine diamond ceramic diaphragm is mounted in the high range.
In this unit, a fine ceramics layer is provided between the titanium substrate and the amorphous diamond film to ensure stability and hardness.

All units have a double magnet type anti-magnetic structure.

OFC (oxygen-free copper) is used for the wiring material of the network section to achieve high input resistance and low distortion factor.

The 25 mm thick front baffle adopts a super elliptical shape that realizes a smooth radiation pattern to improve the directivity, and the 30 mm thick rear baffle adjusts the center of gravity of the front and rear.
In addition, a high-speed and high-pressure assembly method is adopted for the assembly of the cabinet, and deformation which hinders sound quality is suppressed by improving accuracy and strength.

100% wool is used for the sound absorbing material inside the cabinet to improve S/N.

The internal network uses OFC lines.
