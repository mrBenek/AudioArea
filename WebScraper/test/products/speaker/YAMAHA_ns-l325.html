  • YAMAHA NS-L325

  • ¥ 35,000 (1 unit, around 1978)

Commentary

This 3-way bass reflex type speaker system realizes linear operation of each unit.

A 25 cm cone type woofer of the NS-1000 series is mounted in the low range.
In addition to the Alnico magnet and 53 φ mm long voice coil, the completion is enhanced by attaching a copper cap.

Equipped with 12 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.
High efficiency is achieved with a large diameter voice coil, etc., and a back cavity is provided to suppress unnecessary resonance.

A 5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
A lightweight, high-transient 23 mm diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and a sound absorbing material is inserted behind the diaphragm and voice coil to suppress resonance.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex structure with a shiny oak finish.
