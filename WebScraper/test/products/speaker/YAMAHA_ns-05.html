  • YAMAHA NS-05

  • ¥ 29,000 (1 unit, released in 1988)

Commentary

A speaker system developed for vocal reproduction.

A 12 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Polypropylene cone is used for the diaphragm.

A 3 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit of each unit.
In addition, because of its magnetic shielding design, it does not adversely affect the screen even on the side of the CRT.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.
The width of the enclosure is 160 mm, almost the same as the width of same as the width of a human face, and the internal volume is designed to reflect the volume data of both lungs and head, so that vocals can be reproduced naturally as much as possible.

There were 2 kinds of variations for finishing the exterior, wood and gray.

There were 3 types of separately sold integrally tuned speaker stands.
The SPS 05 can be installed with the speakers facing up to enhance low-frequency playback or with the speakers facing sideways.
The SPS 0525 and SPS 0538 take the form of a common speaker stand, allowing you to choose the height to fit your location and preference.
The SPS 05 and SPS 0525 are designed specifically for the NS 05, while the SPS 0538 is designed for the NS 05 and NS-1.
