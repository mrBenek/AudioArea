Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system with a compact design.

The low range is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer 116A using a 1.1 kg Alnico V magnet.
In addition, it is equipped with a 3.6 cm cone type tweeter LE25-2 which adopts a 15,000 gauss magnetic circuit in the high range.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex and has a natural oak finish.
It is also equipped with a brown net grill on the front.
