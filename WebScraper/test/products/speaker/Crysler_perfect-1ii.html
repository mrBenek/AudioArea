  • CHRYSLER Perfect-1II

  • ¥ 29,900 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

The Perfect Series speaker system.

A newly developed 20 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range.
A dome-shaped tweeter is also installed in the high-frequency range.

The front baffle is equipped with level control.

The exterior finish does not use Sawood, the front baffle is made of premium camera leather, Cibo leather, and the wooden part is finished with Rosewood RUNNET.
