  • YAMAHA NS-10MT(Theater)

  • ¥ 34,000 (1 unit, released in December 1995)

Commentary

An improved speaker system for theater sound based on the NS-10M.

A 18 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Newly developed lightweight curved type white cone is used in this unit.

A 3-cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit is made of carefully selected Egyptian cotton.

The magnetic circuit of each unit adopts the A / MAG system anti-magnetic design. By magnetizing both the main magnet and the cancel magnet under the optimum environment, current distortion generated by the conventional cancel magnet system is cancelled.

While the enclosure is the same size as the previous 10M series, it offers a wide range of playback by taking the depth and adopting the bass reflex method, and is made of high-density particleboards.

Uses a gold-plated speaker terminal that supports banana plugs.

It is equipped with a screw part that can fix the SP bracket.
