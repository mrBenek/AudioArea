Commentary

This is a U-series bookshelf type speaker system that uses a horn for the middle and high range.

A 25 cm cone woofer of high compliance type is installed in the low range.

A horn type unit is installed in the middle and high frequency ranges.
These units use a spherical horn that emits spherical waves by giving a special curve to the horn shape, and the mid-range driver unit uses the equivalent of the HM-500AmkII driver unit of the Scepter series.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
