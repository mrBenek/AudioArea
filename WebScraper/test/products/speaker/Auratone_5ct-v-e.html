Commentary

A two way speaker system developed as a small monitor speaker.

A 12.5 cm cone woofer is mounted in the low range, and a 4.4 cm tweeter unit is used in the high range.
It is an anti-magnetic design using an anti-magnetic shield that covers the magnetic circuit and can be installed adjacent to a TV, etc.

It uses a cubic compact enclosure.

The exterior uses a black satin finish. There was also a walnut finish 5CT-VW.
