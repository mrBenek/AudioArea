Commentary

Status series tall boy speaker system.

The Status series is a series designed by industrial designer J. P. Klien.

It is equipped with a 20.3 cm cone type woofer and a 20.3 cm cone type passive radiator for low range.

The mid-range features a 3.8 cm dome mid-range.

A combination of a CD (constant directivity) director and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm of the dome tweeter uses Kevlar, and in combination with a CD director, excellent power response and wide bandwidth are realized.

The front baffle of the enclosure features a unique oak design.
The computer design is based on Teal & Small Theory to enhance the low-reproduction capability.
