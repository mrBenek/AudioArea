Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC SX-C7

  • ¥ 54,000 (1 unit, around 1991)

Commentary

A speaker system that employs a 2-way coaxial unit.

The unit consists of a 18.5 cm Kurt Mueller cone woofer and a 3.5 cm silk soft dome tweeter. Both units are equipped with an internal Alnico magnetic circuit.

Also, as shown in the picture below, it can be used in combination with SX-W7 of super woofer.
