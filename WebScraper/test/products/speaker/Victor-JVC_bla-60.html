Commentary

The speaker system that Victor used when he experimented with swapping between live performance and record playback. The swap was successful and became famous.

A 40 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
This unit controls the range of motion of the woofer piston by placing a Konica Le Dome on corn paper.
We have also adopted a magnetic circuit in which many small magnets are stacked and operate in the same way as an external magnetic circuit.

A pressure type horn driver and 4-division multi-cellular horn are used for the mid-range.

In the high range, directivity is emphasized, and two horn type tweeters with multi-cellular horns are adopted.

The enclosure is made of lauan plywood and is 30 mm thick.
Also, the removable front grill is made with a handmade lattice.
