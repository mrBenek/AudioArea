Commentary

This large-size floor-type speaker system was developed using Yamaha technology as a full-scale home-produced floor-type speaker.

A 38 cm cone type woofer, JA 3805, is mounted in the low range.
The cone uses a non-pre-cone with corrugation made of carefully selected materials. Both the urethane roll edge and the base fabric and impregnated material are supported by a damper that has been sufficiently selected and audibly selected.
The voice coil consists of a heat-resistant NOMEX bobbin with a 100 φ large diameter and 18 mm width wound with a pure copper ribbon wire. A zinc reinforcement ring is attached to the neck to prevent the voice coil bobbin from bending. This realizes a voice coil that can withstand a large input power of 100W continuously.
The magnetic circuit uses an internal 90 φ x40 φ x40 / 1.5 kg Alnico magnet and the magnetic flux density of the voice coil gap is 12,500 Gauss. The pot yoke uses ductile cast iron and the weight of the magnetic circuit reaches 6.7 kg.
The frame that holds the entire vibration system is made of aluminum alloy and has a weight of 2.65 kg.

The mid-range is equipped with a horn type squaker, JA 6602.
A beryllium dome diaphragm with a diameter of 66.6 φ and a dead weight of 0.7g is used for the diaphragm, and a tangential edge made of aluminum alloy is used for the edge to obtain sufficient linearity for amplitude.
The voice coil uses a 66.6 φ aluminum ribbon wire. The magnetic circuit uses a 1.34 kg alnico magnet. Both the center pole and outer pole use a pure iron to obtain a magnetic flux density of 18,500 Gauss. In addition, a silver ring is attached to the outer circumference of the center pole to suppress the generation of third strain caused by magnetostriction and the fluctuation of impedance characteristics.
The horn is equipped with an exponential horn with a cutoff of 200 Hz, a total length of 273 mm, an aperture ratio of 103 mm, and a dead weight of 2.3 kg. A slant-plate acoustic lens is used to improve the directional characteristics of the high-frequency range.
The equalizer is a 3-part zinc-machined type, and the back cover is made of cast aluminum with a thickness of 30 mm. Urethane and felt are used as sound absorbing materials.

A horn type tweeter, JA 4202, is mounted in the high range.
The diaphragm uses a beryllium ring diaphragm with a thickness of 40 μ m and a weight of 0.2g. This ring diaphragm is a V-shaped diaphragm with an outer diameter of 50 mm and an inner diameter of 30 mm and provides rigidity.
The voice coil is made of pure aluminum and is only 0.2g in spite of its 42 φ diameter. The total weight of the vibration system is less than 0.5g. In addition, the magnetic circuit is an with an Alnico magnet and has a magnetic flux density of 18,500 Gauss.
In order to improve directional characteristics by adopting a large diameter ring diaphragm, a spherical horn with a special shape is adopted and the aperture diameter is reduced (29 φ).
The horn itself is a short-cut type exponential horn with low cut-off (1440 Hz) and machined from solid aluminum.

A low-distortion inductor (distortion rate of 0.05% or less at 100W) using an E-type core made of large silicon steel sheets is used for the woofer circuit of the network.
A large MM capacitor with low dielectric loss and low distortion factor is used for the capacitor, and a non-inductive type is used for the attenuator.

The enclosure is a large-volume bass-lef-floor type, with 25 mm thick Douglas-fir plywood for the baffle plate and 25 mm thick high-density particleboards for the other parts, and each part is reinforced.
The inside is packed with carefully selected glass wool as a sound absorbing material, and the weight of the cabinet reaches 46 kg.
The exterior is finished with a crystal black polyester matte finish with rosewood accent.
