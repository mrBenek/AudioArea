Commentary

THX certified HTS System-10 center speaker system.

Two 13 cm cone type woofers are used for the low range.
An OMF (Onkyo Micro Fiber) diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and a matrix structure of natural fibrous non-woven fabric and thermosetting resin is used for the diaphragm, reducing the inherent noise generated by the diaphragm itself.
In addition, damper material with less natural vibration is used.
The unique S-line edge is used for the edge of the unit. In order to prevent the reflection caused by the mismatch of the mechanical impedance of the joint with the diaphragm from missing section information, etc., data analysis and hearing are repeated to reduce the reflection of propagation waves.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

The network section uses an independent board for each unit, and mutual interference due to electromagnetic induction is suppressed by the separate layout.
In addition, an S. G. L. (Steady Ground Level) 1-point concentrated circuit is used for the ground that is the reference of the audio signal to prevent current from flowing in the ground line.

MDF is used in the enclosure, and a reinforcing plate is incorporated inside the enclosure, so that the resonance frequency is higher than the vocal band and noise due to resonance is reduced by dispersing the frequency.
