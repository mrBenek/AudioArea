Commentary

A is a 2-way speaker system equipped with a super tweeter that can reproduce up to 80000 Hz in order to be able to reproduce up to the ultrasonic band of DVD audio.

By arranging the ultra-high frequency reproduction tweeter between two oblicones with the drive shaft facing inward, a wide range of directional characteristics in both horizontal and vertical directions is ensured, and free layout without restriction of the installation place is possible.

A 14.5 cm aluminum oblicorn unit is used for the low band.
The driving point is placed off-center to disperse mid-range resonance and realize smooth characteristics.
In addition, it uses Victor's original spider suspension to increase the following ability.

A 19 mm pure gold plated aluminum hard dome type unit is used for the high range.
To produce a more natural sound, in addition to pure gold plating, the directivity has been improved by adopting an edge equalizer, optimized frame shape, and a short horn.
In addition, it uses a neodymium magnet internal magnetic circuit that is small but has excellent drive capability, and is equipped with a three dimensional super sonic diffuser.

Strong aluminum die-cast frames are used for the frames of the low and high frequency units to increase the rigidity of the unit.

In order to improve low-frequency playback, we have adopted a twin duct and bass reflex on the back of the body.
