  • McIntosh XR290

  • ¥ 4,500,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1992)

Commentary

A total column concept 3-way speaker system with three parallel columns in an integrated enclosure.

Four 30 cm Woofers, 12 13 cm Mid-Range and 24 2.5 cm Dome Tweeters are mounted vertically in-line to form a ヴァーチカルコラム. This unique structure creates an excellent linear cylindrical wavefront in the horizontal direction and resists excessive vertical diffusion, eliminating the acoustic impact of ceilings and floors.

The woofer uses LD/HP (Low Distortion/High Performance, PAT, PEND) woofer reduced by the new magnetic circuit.

The input terminal is a 3-way independent design for bi-wiring and tri-wiring, and is connected by a gold-plated jumper plate.

The enclosure can be moved in two halves.

There were also standard Walnut / Oak finishes and custom Piano Black / Rosewood finishes for finishes.
