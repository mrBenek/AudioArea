  • ¥ 65,000 (1 unit, around late 1970 s)

Commentary

This speaker system was developed based on the theory of Electrovoice's "interface-based optimal phase inversion system".

A 20 cm cone type woofer and a 30 cm cone type radiator are installed in the middle and low range.
This radiator does not have a magnetic circuit or voice coil and works like a so-called passive radiator. However, Interface : 3 makes more active use of this radiator. The radiator receives a low frequency range of less than 57 Hz. This realizes a low sound reproduction of 34 Hz, while the 20 cm woofer realizes low distortion and high input resistance up to the middle range.

A 6 cm tweeter is installed in the high range.

The 20 cm cone woofer and 6 cm tweeter are identical to the Interface : AII and Interface : BII.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
