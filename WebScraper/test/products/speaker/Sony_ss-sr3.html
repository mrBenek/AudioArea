  • ¥ 30,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1991)

Commentary

A layout-free type speaker system that can control the sound image by changing the direction of the tweeter.

A 12 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The S. F. C. T (Sound Field Control Technology) is used for the high-frequency range. It uses a 2.5 cm dome-shaped variable directional tweeter V. D. Tweeter (Variable Directivity Tweeter). With this, the angle of the tweeter can be adjusted up to ± 20 ° according to the position of the listener. By adjusting as necessary, a variety of realistic sensations and clear sound localization can be obtained.
In addition, when the tweeter is set to the front, the time alignment configuration is adopted.

The enclosure uses a molded resin with a special foaming agent that has excellent acoustic characteristics, and a cabinet design that reduces unnecessary standing waves.

There are various types of stands sold separately, and you can set them freely.
