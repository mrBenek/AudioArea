  • ¥ 48,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

Compact Component Fill Series 3-way speaker system.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Pulp cone is used for the diaphragm, and natural sound quality is obtained by laminating PP film on press oven type cone paper.

The mid-range features a 3.5 cm dome mid-range with a full-scale magnetic circuit.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
PPTA (polyphenylene phthalamide) resin film, which is lightweight and has excellent rigidity, is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The front baffle uses a 30 mm thick round baffle, and the exterior is finished with a maple-like finish.
