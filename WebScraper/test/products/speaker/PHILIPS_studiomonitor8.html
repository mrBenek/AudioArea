Commentary

A floor-type speaker system with a full-range unit.

The unit is equipped with AD-9710M/01 which is a double cone type 20 cm full range unit.
This unit uses a 1.2 kg magnetic circuit using an Alnico V system magnet.

The enclosure has a double-bus back-load horn structure and 24 mm thick special plywood board.
The exterior is a walnut natural wood oil finish.
