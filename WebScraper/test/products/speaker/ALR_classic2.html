  • ALR/Jordan Classic2

  • ¥ 120,000 (a set of two cars, around 2003)

Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system of the CLASSIC series.

A 14 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a new metal cone with a surface coating to enhance musical expression.

Equipped with a high frequency 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter.
Polyester diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

The network section employs a fourth stage LinkWitz-Riley network.

MDF material is used for the enclosure and a bass reflex system is adopted.
Finished with natural alder material as exterior finish.

Bi-wired connection is supported.

In the Japanese catalog, it was combined with the ES-50 speaker stand made by Sona sushi Stem UK.
