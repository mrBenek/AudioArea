  • JBL 4681

  • ¥ 500,000 (1 unit, around 1976)

Commentary

This line-array-type speaker system was developed as a PA system for vocals and musical instruments in halls and concert halls.

The unit is equipped with 4 units of 25 cm cone type full range K110 which is a unit for JBL musical instrument.

The enclosure is made of solid and lightweight plastic, and the interior is made of a bass-reflex system.

The 4681 is designed to improve the middle and high range by adding the separately sold Power Pack 2901/2902. Also, the 4682 released around the same time was composed by adding 2902 to the 4681.
