  • ONKYO M50

  • ¥ 27,500 (1 unit, around 1980)

Commentary

A speaker system developed with the aim of achieving low-frequency playback that exceeds its size.

The woofer is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer. The 4-layer voice coil with a polyimide bobbin increases the driving force and prevents the Qm from decreasing, thus improving the input resistance.
In addition, in order to obtain the best constant in a closed cabinet, a cone type diaphragm with a balance weight is used to increase bass sound.

The tweeter is equipped with a 3 cm dome type diaphragm. It is equipped with an aluminum friction processed frame, and contributes to high frequency transient and efficiency improvement by strengthening rigidity.

Equipped with level control.
