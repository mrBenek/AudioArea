Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system that is an improved version of DIG.

DIG was designed and developed by Elektri.

20 cm coaxial type speaker unit 409B is used for the unit.
This unit has a coaxial structure consisting of a 20 cm cone woofer and a cone tweeter. A special diffuser is installed in front of the tweeter to improve the high-frequency directional characteristics.

The cabinet uses a bass reflex system.
