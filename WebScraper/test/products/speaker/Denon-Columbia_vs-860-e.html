  • DENON/COLUMBIA VS-860

  • Price unknown (1 unit, around 1970)

Commentary

A 3-way floor speaker system that uses a sealed enclosure with a short horn in front of the woofer.

For the lower range, a 30-cm cone-shaped unit with a voice coil with edgewise winding of copper ribbon wire is adopted.
The mid-range uses a 20 cm cone-shaped squaker and an 8-cell multi-cellar horn, and the high-range uses an acoustic lens moon horn unit with a diameter of 55 mm.

Equipped with a multi-amplifier input that can be switched to 3 stages by the attached switch.

In addition, independent continuously variable attenuators are installed in the mid-range and high-range.
