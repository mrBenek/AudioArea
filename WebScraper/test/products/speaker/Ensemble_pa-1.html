Commentary

A two way speaker system using a passive radiator system.
PA-1 was manufactured by Pawel Acoustics.

The low-frequency band features a hand-made, custom-made 13 cm cone woofer.
A unique diaphragm made of synthetic foam sandwiched between several microns of aluminum foil and paper is used for the diaphragm of the unit to realize a light and hard diaphragm. In addition, a high-speed driver is realized by taking a long stroke and driving it with a powerful magnetic circuit in order to secure a low frequency range.

The high-range is equipped with a 1.9 cm soft dome type tweeter, which is made by hand and custom-made like a woofer.

It is equipped with KEF's elliptical passive radiator SP139 in order to increase the bass reproduction capability.
This passive radiator covers most of the back of the enclosure and removes the back pressure on the woofer unit.

The front baffle is tapered and a carefully designed network is used to achieve phase matching between the two units, and the front baffle is tapered to prevent standing waves from occurring in the enclosure.

The enclosure had two finishes : Swiss cherry and satin black.
