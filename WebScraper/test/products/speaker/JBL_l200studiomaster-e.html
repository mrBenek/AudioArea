Commentary

This speaker system is based on the specifications of the established JBL professional speakers.

LE15B which is 38 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range.
A horn type unit combining a driver LE85 and a horn lens HL91 is mounted in the high range.

The network uses the LX16, a 2-way network dedicated to the L200.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and has a floor-type design with slant baffle.
There were 4 types of front grills, blue, red, gray and black.
