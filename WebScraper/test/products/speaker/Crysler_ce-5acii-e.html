  • CHRYSLER CE-5acII

  • ¥ 24,900 (1 unit, sold in August 1969)

Commentary

An improved 3-way sealed speaker system for the CE-5ac.
Mid-range units have changed.

It uses a 20 cm cone type unit for the low band and a 5 cm horn for the high band.

The enclosure is made of hard plywood with a walnut finish.

Equipped with level control and multi-amplifier terminals.
