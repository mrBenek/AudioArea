  • KENWOOD LSF-333

  • ¥ 34,000 (a set of two cars, around 1997)

Commentary

This 2-way speaker system was developed based on the point source concept.

It uses a 13 cm cone type woofer for the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter for the high range.

A conductor length compensation network is used in the network section.
The length of the conductor of the two units including the network is compensated by a conductor length compensation element (non-inductive winding resistance) to make the lengths of the signal paths identical.
This makes the connection between the two units natural and makes the sound image three dimensional.

The speaker terminal uses a gold-plated banana plug speaker terminal.
