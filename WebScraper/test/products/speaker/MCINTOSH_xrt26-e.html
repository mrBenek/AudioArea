Commentary

This 3-way speaker system uses the traditional multi-driver method.

It is equipped with 2 30 cm cone type woofers for low range, 20 cm cone type midrange for middle range, and 23 2.5 cm aluminum hard dome type tweeters for high range.

The tweeter column has precise phase control and realizes clear sound localization. In addition, the column itself supports continuous input of 250W.
The Woofer and Mid-range models use a Macintosh patented LD/HP (Low Distortion/High Performance : U.S. PAT. 5151934) magnetic circuit. The aluminum die-cast frame reduces unwanted vibration.

The Woofer and Mid-range units are mounted in a wallnut-finished enclosure of carefully selected top-of-the-line materials that carefully exclude the diffraction effects of sound waves to prevent disturbance of the radiation wave front.
You can also use the tweeter column by hanging it on the upper wall.

The input terminal is a 3-way independent type and can be bi-wired or tri-wired, and can be bi-amped or tri-amped.

It comes with an active equalizer unit so that it can be tuned to the sound condition of the room.
This equalizer functions as a compensator for the lowest frequency range and allows for a wide range of frequency response corrections.
