  • JBL J520M

  • ¥ 30,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1995)

Commentary

The J series speaker system realizes the authentic American sound and JBL traditional techniques at a reasonable price.

A 12.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a newly designed polymer-coated fiber cone that maximizes the area of the diaphragm and reduces distortion.

A 1.0 cm dome type tweeter using a titanium composite dome is mounted in the high range.

The optimum design of the network section improves the sound connection between the tweeter and woofer.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex design.
The exterior has a oak-like finish, and the back has a finish to increase the freedom of setting.
