Commentary

Active subwoofer system with microprocessor controlled MFB (motional feedback).

The unit has 2 20 cm inverted dome type woofers.
This unit can play flat low-frequency limits up to 10 Hz.

Microprocessor controlled MFB system is adopted.
In the MFB system, the amplitude of the diaphragm is detected by the accelerometer set in the speaker unit, and this is read as the acoustic radiation impedance of the feedback from the room and sent as data to the built-in microprocessor.
Based on this data, the processor uses a special algorithm to calculate and output what signal the power amplifier should output in response to the input music signal.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
In addition, there were 3 kinds of variations of the appearance, ERD, MBK and PBK.
