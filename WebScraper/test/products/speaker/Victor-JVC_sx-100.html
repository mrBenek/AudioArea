  • VICTOR/JVC SX-100

Commentary

This is a B4 size small speaker system that uses a full-range metal cone unit.

A 12.5 cm metal cone has been developed and adopted for the unit.
The magnetic circuit uses an internal magnet type Alnico magnet with high magnetic efficiency.

The cabinet adopts a newly developed cascade bass reflex structure. The cabinet interior is divided into two by a cascade board, and the low-frequency energy increased in the lower space is released from the bass reflex port.
In addition, parallel surfaces have been eliminated from the front and back of the cabinet to suppress low waves.
