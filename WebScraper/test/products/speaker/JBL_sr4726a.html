Commentary

SR series 38cm2-way speaker system.

The SR4726A can be used as a concert hall system with SR side fills or sub-woofers.
It can also be used as a main speaker system for professional musicians and semi-professionals.

In the lower range, it is equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer 2226G which adopted the JBL original VGC system.
In the high frequency range, a horn type unit using biradial horn 2381 with 90 ° horizontal and 50 ° vertical directional characteristics and driver unit 2447J is mounted.

It has a built-in network.
In addition, a 2-way multi-amplifier drive changeover switch is mounted.
