Commentary

This speaker system employs a line array system with excellent directional characteristics.

It is suitable for expanding the audio band in conference rooms, lecture halls, large and small halls, etc.

The unit is equipped with 4 13 cm cone type full range units 2105.

The thin design of the enclosure allows for installation in limited space.
The exterior is finished in gray with a black grill attached.
