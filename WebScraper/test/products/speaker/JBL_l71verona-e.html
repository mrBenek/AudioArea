Commentary

A speaker system with a calm furniture style design of North Italy.

The cabinet is made of pican material with beautiful luster and wood grain. It is elaborately carved by hand. It expresses the beauty by combining with the grill of brocade.

The speaker unit is a 3-way configuration with a 38 cm cone woofer 135A in the low range, a 12.5 cm cone squaker LE5-3 in the middle range, and a 3.6 cm direct tweeter LE25-1 in the high range.
