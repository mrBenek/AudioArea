  • TRIO S-120

  • ¥ 23,800 (1 unit, around 1965)

Commentary

A speaker system that uses a bookshelf type.

A 30 cm cone woofer with fo = 28 Hz is installed in the low range.

It is equipped with a 16 cm cone type squaker in the middle and low range.

A 7 cm cone type skoker is mounted in the middle and high area.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.

The FBL (front and back load) system is used for the enclosure.
