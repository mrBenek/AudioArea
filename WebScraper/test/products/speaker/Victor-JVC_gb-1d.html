Commentary

An all-nondirectional speaker system developed to improve directivity.

The GB-1D is designed based on the principle of the breathing sphere. When a sphere with a spherical diaphragm is vibrated as one cone, the sound spreads radially, and natural sound without directivity is obtained in any direction.
To achieve one of the speaker's ideals, the GB-1D uses a spherical cabinet with a total of eight units mounted in all directions, providing almost uniform sound pressure in all directions.

Four 13 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
Roll free edge and long voice coil resistant to large amplitude are adopted, and ferrite magnet is adopted for magnetic circuit.

Four horn type tweeter are installed in the high range.
By using a special rigid resin for the diaphragm, the weight is reduced and transient characteristics are improved. In addition, the aperture is small and has a protruding shape, so the directional characteristics are excellent. Even at 60 °, it can be heard as well as in the front.

The cabinet is hermetically sealed and is made of ABS resin. In addition, sound absorbing material is placed inside the cabinet to suppress the noise and the rise of fo.
The perforated metal cover on the front not only protects the speaker unit but also acts as an aroma diffuser to improve sound spread.

It can be hung near the ceiling or used like BGM. It can also be used as a floor type by using a metal stand sold separately.
