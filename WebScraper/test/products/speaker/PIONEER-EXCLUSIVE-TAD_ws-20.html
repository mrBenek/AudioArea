  • Pioneer WS-20

  • ¥ 2,500 (1 unit, around 1969)

Commentary

Recessed panel speakers designed to be embedded in ceilings or walls.

A 20 cm cone type full range unit is used for the unit.

It supports multiple input impedances in consideration of multiple distributed use and long distance wiring.

The design is square and synthetic resin panel is used for the panel.
