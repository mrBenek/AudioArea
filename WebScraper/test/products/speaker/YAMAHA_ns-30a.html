Audio Database

  • YAMAHA NS-30A

  • ¥ 69,000 (1 piece, around 1970)

Commentary

A speaker system that uses a unique unit for the low range.

A JA 6002 type unit is used for the woofer, a 30 cm cone type unit for the squaker, and a 5 cm horn type unit for the tweeter.
The woofer operates in the full range, the squaker cuts the low range, and the tweeter cuts the low range with an LC element.

The enclosure is open at the rear and uses 19 mm lauan plywood, and the front RUNNET is removable.

There was also a horizontal type NS-30B.
