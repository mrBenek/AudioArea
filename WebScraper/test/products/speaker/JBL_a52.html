Commentary

A compact speaker system that can be easily enjoyed in your living room or private room.

A 12.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a polymer-coated fiber cone that has been coated with a polymer polymer to provide adequate internal loss and strength. It also uses a reinforced damper and die-cast frame to reduce rolling and distortion at large amplitudes.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
A titanium composite dome is used for the diaphragm and the bobbin integrated diaphragm reduces acoustic loss. In addition, a newly designed oval shorthorn is mounted to control the directivity in both horizontal and vertical directions.

The enclosure employs an inline layout in which the woofer and tweeter are placed in a straight line in the center of the enclosure to obtain clear sound localization.

It uses an anti-magnetic design that has little effect on the television screen.
In addition, it can be mounted on walls or ceilings by using a mount Plaquette sold separately.
