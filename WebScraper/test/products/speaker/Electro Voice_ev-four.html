Commentary

A speaker system that employs an acoustic suspension system.

It is equipped with a 30 cm cone woofer for the low range, a horn mid-range with a driver with a diffuse horn for the middle range, and a 13 cm cone tweeter for the high range.

Walnut is used for the whole surface of the enclosure and poly Marcourt is applied.
