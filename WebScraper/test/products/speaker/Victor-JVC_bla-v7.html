Commentary

This is a speaker system used in the V7 series of 4-channel stereo.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
High Young's modulus cone paper is used for the cone paper. In addition, the Cross All Edge is a high compliance type using a soft damper, and combined with a 4-turn voice coil and a high-performance wet Ba-ferrite magnet, it realizes low fo and excellent transient characteristics.

Equipped with 12 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.
It uses dumped corrugation edge with less reverse resonance and high Young's modulus KP cone paper.

A 8 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Directivity is improved by KP cone paper and metal cap.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
