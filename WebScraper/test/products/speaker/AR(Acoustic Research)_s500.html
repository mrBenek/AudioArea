Commentary

Powered subwoofer with multi-channel support.

The unit is equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer.

The sun fire high power amplifier is used in the amplifier section.

Equipped with functions such as low-level input terminal, LFE on / off, high-level input / output terminal, variable adjustment of crossover frequency, phase switching, and input level adjustment.

The enclosure uses a multi-port design.
The exterior has a wood black finish.
