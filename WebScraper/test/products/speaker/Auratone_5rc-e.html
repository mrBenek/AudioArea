Commentary

A monitor speaker system designed for hard outdoor use.

The unit uses a 12.5 cm cone type full-range unit.

The enclosure is a carrying type and is designed to be locked for carrying in pairs.
The exterior is finished with black leather and the corner is equipped with a stainless steel protector.

Equipped with pin jack terminal and speaker protection fuse.
