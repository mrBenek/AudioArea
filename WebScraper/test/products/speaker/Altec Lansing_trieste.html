  • Price unknown (1 unit, released in 1971)

Commentary

A compact bookshelf speaker system.

Trieste was designed and developed by Elektri.

It has a 25 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 7.5 cm cone tweeter (direct radiator) in the high range.

A 3-dB step attenuator is mounted on the rear terminal.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
