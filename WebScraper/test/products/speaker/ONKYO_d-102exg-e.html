  • ONKYO D-102EXG

  • ¥ 64,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in March 2000)

Commentary

Based on D-102EX, this is a limited model that uses a brass scraping terminal.

A 13 cm cone type woofer with a silk OMF diaphragm is mounted in the low range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

A condenser made by German company WIMA is used in the network section.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex design with a real wood veneer finish.

The speaker terminal uses a screw-type gold-plated brass scraping terminal for banana plugs.

A speaker cable made by Monster Cable is included.
