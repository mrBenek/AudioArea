Commentary

This bookshelf speaker system is compatible with the latest analog technology and digital sources.

It is equipped with 25 cm cone type woofer LE10H in the low range.
This unit uses new cone paper and is equipped with a strong magnetic circuit with a low distortion factor achieved by the SFG structure and flux-stabilizing ring, so that it can be used for low-frequency reproduction with a sharp response.

The 13 cm cone type Scorker LE5-12 is mounted in the middle area.
By adopting die-cast frame for the frame, unnecessary vibration is suppressed.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm of this unit is coated with phenol resin with aluminum.

The enclosure uses 19 mm thick, highly dense particle board, and the joint part uses the Woodwell method to enhance the sense of unity between boards, thereby increasing the rigidity.
In addition, we have improved sound localization by adopting a vertical inline layout for the unit layout.
