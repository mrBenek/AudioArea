  • AR(Acoustic Research) 266HO

  • ¥ 108,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

HO series tall boy type speaker system.

Equipped with 2 long stroke 16.5 cm cone type woofers in the low range.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
By adopting the energy control contour around the Aeroform Tweeter which inherited the technology of the high-end model, the clear high-range reproduction is realized.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
In addition, there were 2 kinds of variations for the finish of the exterior, black and maple.

It is equipped with a gold plated terminal that is compatible with banana plugs.
