  • Pioneer S-C6

  • ¥ 22,000 (1 unit, released in September 1999)

Commentary

Center speaker with linear power system and variable tweeter.

The S-C6 is designed with the same design concept as the S-ST5, creating an ideal surround sound field.

Two 12 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
This unit uses linear power technology. This method uses an edge-less structure that eliminates the edge of the diaphragm and eliminates the problems associated with conventional edge-speaker systems. This enables extremely high linearity even for large input signals and powerful sound reproduction even with small-diameter speakers.

A 2.5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit uses a strong neodymium magnet in the magnetic circuit to achieve high-frequency playback up to 60 kHz, and can be adjusted up and down by 15 ° to achieve optimum orientation.

A bass reflex system is used for the enclosure.

Comes with Velcro to prevent the speaker cabinet from slipping.
