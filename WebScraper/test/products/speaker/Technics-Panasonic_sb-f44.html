Commentary

Linear phase design 2-way speaker system.

Equipped with a 16 cm cone woofer for the low range and a horn type tweeter for the high range.
Magnetic fluid is filled in the magnetic gap to improve input resistance.

Linear phase design is adopted to improve sound image localization.
