Commentary

A small bookshelf type speaker system that can be used for both hi-fi and AV systems.

A 12 cm cone woofer with a glass fiber diaphragm is mounted in the low range.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

It is a bass reflex design considering AV applications.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
