Commentary

The speaker system which adopted the fine ceramic diaphragm.
Rather than simply removing the super tweeter from Zero-1000, all units are newly developed while inheriting the technology.

A 32cm cone type Woofer is carried in low-pass.
The fine ceramic diaphragm which uses an aluminum cone as a base material is adopted as a diaphragm.

A 6.5cm dome unit is adopted as the mid range, and a 3.0cm dome unit is adopted as the high region.
A fine ceramic dome is adopted as the diaphragm, and the connection between units is improved by the same material.

The enclosure uses a super elliptical baffle made of new UG resin to prevent disturbance of frequency characteristics due to diffraction effects.
This baffle achieves strength that is nearly impossible with wooden materials due to its sturdy reinforcing rib structure based on modal analysis. Moreover, the iron reinforcement bar is embedded between the Woofer and the squawker attachment part.
The vibration of the box is reduced by careful modal analysis of both the baffle and the enclosure.

The level control is carried in the side panel.
The constant-impedance step switching type of a copper contact with low distortion and little sound quality deterioration is adopted.
