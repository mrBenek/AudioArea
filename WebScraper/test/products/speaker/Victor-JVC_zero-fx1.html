Commentary

A 3-way speaker system named "BodyBlow", which is small but has improved the quality and volume of the low range.

A 23 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm of this unit is made of high-quality cross-carbon laminated with carbon sheets. While increasing the response, this unit has an appropriate internal loss to extend the low-frequency limit and improve the ability to reproduce heavy low-frequency sounds.

The mid-range uses an 8 cm cone type skoker that uses fine ceramics technology.

A 5 cm dome type tweeter with fine diamond coating is mounted in the high range.
The diaphragm of this unit is manufactured by depositing amorphous diamond on a base material using a unique chemical vapor deposition (CVD) method. The film produced by CVD has high purity, high hardness, and smooth surface, thus achieving sound velocity comparable to natural diamond.

A double magnet system has been adopted for the magnetic circuits of all units to improve driving force while eliminating magnetic interference.
In addition, the vibration of each unit is reduced by adopting aluminum die-cast for the frame.

The cabinet is assembled using a high-speed and high-pressure assembly method to suppress unnecessary vibration of the unit and ensure ideal operation. In this method, accuracy and strength are improved by connecting all six surfaces instantaneously at a high pressure of approximately 300 atm. Furthermore, since no heat is applied, the adhesive surface does not become brittle, and strength and durability are improved.
In addition, in consideration of the weight balance with the front baffle on which the unit is mounted, a 25 mm thick balance board is adopted for the rear baffle. In addition, an elliptical baffle is adopted for the front baffle to improve diffraction phenomenon, and the optimum mounting position of each speaker unit is derived by computer analysis to realize a hemispherical and smooth sound radiation pattern.

Natural wool and cotton materials with low vibration noise are used for the sound absorbing material inside the cabinet.
