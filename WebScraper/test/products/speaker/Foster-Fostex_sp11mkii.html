Commentary

An improved speaker system based on SP11.

The SP11C is a model in which the input terminal is changed to a professional cannon type 3P connector.

The unit is equipped with 2 10 cm cone type full range units.

Equipped with sound quality switching function.

A bass reflex system is used for the enclosure and special reinforced synthetic resin is used.
Vertically and horizontally flexible stacking is possible.
