  • Acustik-Lab Bolero Limited

  • * Limited sale of 50 sets in Japan
    ¥ 328,000 (1 set, around 1990 s)

Commentary

A speaker system sold as a limited version of the Bolero. Only 50 sets were sold in Japan.

The basic structure is based on the Bolero, and the network and speaker terminals have been changed to by-wire.

All mounting screws of driver unit are gold-plated.

The enclosures were limited to piano black finishes only and each cabinet had a self-signed "Thanks" by Gerhard Schneider, President of the Kusu Tick Lab.
