Commentary

This is a renewed model of the Control 1 which became a hit with over 1 million copies worldwide.
While maintaining the basic design of the speaker type, size and enclosure, we aim to achieve a tighter and more energetic sound.

For the lower range, a 13 cm poly laminated corn woofer is used, which is a pulp base corn laminated with a high polymer composite material.

The high-frequency range is equipped with a balance drive type tweeter that combines the cone part with a center dome to optimize response directivity.

It is designed to protect against magnetic fields for multimedia applications.
In addition, a network with protector is installed to protect the unit from excessive input.

By using brackets sold separately, it can be attached to walls or ceiling surfaces.
