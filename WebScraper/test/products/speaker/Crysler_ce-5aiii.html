  • CHRYSLER CE-5aIII

  • ¥ 32,500 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

A relatively small bookshelf speaker system.

The CE-5aIII was selected as an excellent product by the Osaka Design Center.

It has a 20 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 12 cm cone squaker in the middle range.
In addition, a 5-cm horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
