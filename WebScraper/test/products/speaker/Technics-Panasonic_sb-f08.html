  • Technics SB-F08

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, released in 1981)

Commentary

A mini linear phase speaker equipped with an attenuator.

A 18 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The high-frequency band is equipped with a horn type tweeter in which a magnetic fluid is filled in the magnetic gap to improve input resistance.

It uses a linear phase design in which the sound sources of the tweeter and woofer are aligned.

Equipped with a resettable thermal relay protection circuit.

Equipped with hook for wall mounting and hook for joint connector.
