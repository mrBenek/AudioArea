  • AURATONE QC-66

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system developed by inheriting the know-how of 5C.

Two 16.5 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
Polypropylene cone is used for the diaphragm.

The mid-range features a 3.4 cm soft dome mid-range.

A 1.9 cm dome type tweeter using a polycarbonate dome is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method and has a walnut finish on the exterior.
