  • AR(Acoustic Research) AR-218V

  • ¥ 64,000 (a set of two cars, around 1994)

Commentary

Although it is a low-price model, this speaker system incorporates AR technology such as an acoustic suspension system.

A long stroke type 16 cm cone woofer is mounted in the low range, and a 1.9 cm dome tweeter is mounted in the high range.

The AR-218V is designed to prevent magnetic field.

The enclosure uses the AR traditional acoustic suspension system.
The exterior is finished with a wood grain finish with cherry.
