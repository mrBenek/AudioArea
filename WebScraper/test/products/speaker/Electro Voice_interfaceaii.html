Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system in the Interface Series.

A 20 cm cone woofer is installed in the middle and low range, and a 30 cm cone radiator is installed in the low range.
In addition to the passive radiator system in which a 20 cm woofer drives the radiator, Interface : BII achieves excellent low-range playback by having the radiator take over almost all of the lowest sound range.

Two 6-cm tweeters are mounted in the high-frequency range. One tweeter unit is mounted in the front and one in the rear.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

SEQ equalizer capable of 6 dB booth is attached.
