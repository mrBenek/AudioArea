  • BEVERIDGE SYSTEM 2SW-2

  • ¥ 3,000,000 (system price, 1980 release)

Commentary

A large speaker system created by Harold Vivaridge's 20-year research into electrostatic speakers.

It is composed using an electrostatic transducer and realizes uniform diffusion of all sound ranges in phase with 180 degrees.

The SYSTEM2SW-2 includes a subwoofer and a driver amplifier.
The subwoofer has an independent enclosure and the amplifier system is a full bi-amp-driven system consisting of an electrostatic main unit (tube type), a subwoofer (solid state) and an electronic crossover.
