Commentary

This is a speaker system that follows the technology of SC-A7 and improves the quality of woofer by introducing the latest technology of DENON.

A 32 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made into a corrugated rib structure by adding a special substance to polypropylene base material, large flat damper, long stroke voice coil with high heat-resistance treatment, barium ferrite magnet with 135 mm diameter, and aluminum die-cast frame are used for digital source.

The middle area is equipped with a 6.0 cm hard dome type skoker with α -Boron diaphragm.
This diaphragm is made of a material in which boron compound and alumina particles are fused in a porous state on the surface of an aluminum alloy foil. Boron compound and other materials are struck against the aluminum alloy foil at a speed close to the speed of sound in a high temperature of over 10,000 degrees Celsius by the plasma jet welding method to form a layer and physically bond the layers together. As a result, the diaphragm has approximately three times the rigidity of a titanium diaphragm and approximately ten times the internal loss of a conventional metal diaphragm.
In addition, strontium ferrite magnet, double suspension system support system and aluminum die-cast frame are adopted.

A 2.5 cm hard-dome type tweeter using α -Boron diaphragm is mounted in the high range as well as the middle range.
The aluminum die-cast frame and strontium ferrite magnet bring out the performance of the diaphragm.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure uses a rigid particle board and is a bass reflex type with a capacity of 70L.
