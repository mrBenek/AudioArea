Commentary

A compact speaker system with the essence and power of electrovoice.

The lower range features a 20 cm cone woofer that has been used in the Sentry100A and other Electro Voice professional and home applications. This unit features a strong drive and a strong cone for high input power.

The high-frequency band is equipped with a 3.5 cm soft dome type tweeter, which is also used for professionals.

The anti-magnetic design uses a canceling magnet.

The enclosure is a geared enclosure designed by computer simulation based on Teal & Small theory, and the sound quality is improved by final tuning by development engineers.
MDF is used for the material, and it is finished with Skashchyk wood-grain finish.
