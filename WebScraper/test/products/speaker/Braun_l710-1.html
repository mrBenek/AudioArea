  • BRAUN L710/1

  • ¥ 86,900 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

A sealed 3-way speaker system.

It is equipped with two 18 cm cone woofers in the low range, a 5 cm soft dome scoker in the middle range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter in the high range.

There were 3 types of exterior finish, walnut, black and white finish.
The front panel has an anodized aluminum finish and the aluminum punching metal is removable.

There was a speaker stand and a connection cord with DIN standard connector LK9C sold separately.
