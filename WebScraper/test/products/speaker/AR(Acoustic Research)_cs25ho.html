  • AR(Acoustic Research) CS25HO

  • ¥ 60,000 (1 unit, around 1998)

Commentary

A center speaker developed using HO series technology.

It uses the same focused array, energy control contour baffle and voice balanced tone matching technology as the advanced 312HO/310HO models in the HO series.

Two 13 cm cone mid-range units are installed in the low range, and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter is installed in the high range.
Each unit has an anti-magnetic design.

Comes with a rotating stand.
