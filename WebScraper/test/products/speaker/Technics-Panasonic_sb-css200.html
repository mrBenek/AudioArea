Commentary

A speaker set consisting of a center speaker and two surround speakers.

The SB-C200, developed as a center speaker, is equipped with two 6.5 cm cone type full-range units with composite mica diaphragm. The enclosure features a compact two cabinet round form with stand and adjustable horizontal opening to suit your room.

The SB-S200, developed as a surround speaker, is equipped with an 8 cm cone type full range unit.
Wall mounting is possible with the attached metal fittings, and natural surround effect is obtained by the 8-shaped dipole directional characteristic.
