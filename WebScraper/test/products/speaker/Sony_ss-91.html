  • SONY SS-91

  • ¥ 19,800 (a set of two cars, around 1978)

Commentary

Bass reflex type speaker system with anti-magnetic design.

A 16 cm cone woofer with CARBOCON is installed in the low range, and a 5 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.

It is a magnetic protection design with magnetic leakage prevention processing, and can be used on the side of CRT.
