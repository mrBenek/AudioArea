Commentary

Three way speaker system combining Lordi speaker technology of ギャザードエッジ and metal single plate cone

The low range is equipped with 25 cm metal single plate cone woofer L-253.
The metal cone expands the piston-like vibration band of the speaker by about 1 octave (2 times) in the high frequency direction by the metal cone, and by combining it with the ギャザードエッジ of Lordi original technology, excellent low frequency reproduction is realized.

The middle area is equipped with 6.5 cm metal cone Scoker M-65.

The H-40, a 4 cm cone type tweeter made of a single metal plate, is mounted in the high range.
We are conscious of the connection of sound, and we have adopted the same shape as the middle and low range.

Equipped with level control for middle and high frequencies.
