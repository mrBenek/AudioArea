Commentary

A 3-way speaker system with the same design philosophy as the SX-1000.

Newly developed link type alnico magnetic circuit is adopted in all units.
With the excellent magnetic characteristics of Alnico as it is, the back pressure in the magnetic circuit is efficiently removed by making it a ring type.

A 31.5 cm cone type woofer using fine cross carbon diaphragm is mounted in the low range.

The mid-range is equipped with a 6.5 cm domed squaker, and the high-range is equipped with a 3 cm domed tweeter.
The diaphragm of these units is made of Victor's unique fine diamond ceramic material, which realizes high start-up characteristics.

The network section is divided into three sections to eliminate sound quality degradation due to mutual interference.

The front baffle is made of 38 mm thick maple wood. It is hard and has an appropriate internal loss to improve the clarity of sound. In addition, the G-line unit using Victor's unique sound intensity method realizes a rich sound field.
The exterior has a 6-sided wood finish.

The input terminal is compatible with bi-wiring and bi-amplifier.

Comes with a special stand.
