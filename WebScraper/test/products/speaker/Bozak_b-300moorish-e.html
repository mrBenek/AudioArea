  • BOZAK B-300 Moorish ConcertoIV

  • ¥ 195,000 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

A floor-type speaker system with a 2-way configuration.

The low-range is equipped with a 30 cm cone woofer B-199A, and the high-range is equipped with two 5 cm cone tweeters B-200Y.

The cabinet is an arched frame with a matte walnut finish.
