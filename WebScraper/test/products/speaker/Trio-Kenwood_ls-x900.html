Commentary

A 4-way speaker system with a wide range.

27 cm New HR Carbon Cone Woofer is installed in the low range.
A two stage voice coil is mounted on a long voice coil bobbin and driven by two magnets to achieve powerful low sound reproduction.
In addition, Class A suspension is adopted, and more accurate diaphragm movement is obtained.

A 10-cm cone type squaker is installed in the middle and low range, and a 5-cm semi-dome type squaker is installed in the middle and high range.
Kenwood's original plasma diamond semi-dome diaphragm is used for the diaphragm of the mid-range scoker. In addition, the voice coil bobbin and dome part are integrated into a DSD structure.

A direct action diaphragm horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.

The network is a separate network and the enclosure is a round baffle to reduce diffraction.
