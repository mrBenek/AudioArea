Commentary

A floor-type speaker system that can be grounded vertically or horizontally according to the preference of the room.

There are 7 types of L45, 001, 030, S1, S4, S12, S7 and S8 in the US catalog, but they were sold in different configurations in Japan. There are 7 types of L45 (without hyphens), L45-001, L45-030, L45-S1, L45-S4, L45-S7 and L45-S8 in the catalog in 1973 when I was a Sunsui agent.
