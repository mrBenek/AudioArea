  • DENON/COLUMBIA VS-520

  • ¥ 74,800 (1 unit, sold in June 1969)

Commentary

A 3-way floor type speaker system that uses a back-load horn system.

A 16 cm double-cone unit is used for the low range, a 7.5 cm cone unit for the middle range, and a 5 cm horn unit for the high range.

The enclosure is 30 mm thick lauan plywood with rosewood surface and polyurethane open-pore finish.
The front net is removable.
