  • Pioneer CS-E400

  • ¥ 19,900 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

Two way bookshelf speaker system.

It has a 20 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter in the high range.
A FB clear cone is used for the diaphragm of the woofer unit.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
