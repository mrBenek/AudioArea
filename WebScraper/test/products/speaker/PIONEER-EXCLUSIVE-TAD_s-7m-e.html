  • Pioneer S-7MS/S-7MR

  • ¥ 29,800 (1 set of 3 blocks, around 1983)

Commentary

This is a layout-free type block speaker system in which three blocks can be combined and arranged as desired.

In the low range, two planar woofers with high-efficiency drive by small-diameter voice coils are mounted.
In addition, it is equipped with a 10 cm cone type squaker in the middle range and a 6.6 cm cone type tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure consists of three blocks, one main block and two woofer blocks.

There were 2 types, the silver type S-7MS and the red type S-7MR.
