Commentary

A two way speaker system equipped with a horn tweeter.

A 25 cm cone woofer of high compliance type is installed in the low range.

The high-frequency range is equipped with a wide-band separable horn type tweeter, which reduces the bandwidth of the woofer and enables comfortable playback.

The enclosure has a sealed structure using a hard chip board.
