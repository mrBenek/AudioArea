Commentary

V-series bookshelf type speaker system developed by inheriting G-series technology.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of a light, highly rigid CARBOCON with low resonance sharpness. The rigidity is improved by special impregnation of the cone paper. The magnetic circuit is made of a 120 mm diameter magnet, achieving 11,500 Gauss.
A voice coil with a diameter of 50 mm has been adopted to realize a tight low sound by increasing the braking effect.
In addition, the use of die-cast frames reduces split vibration and unwanted resonance.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
By making the magnetic circuit of the tweeter narrow gap and driving the voice coil at higher density, high efficiency is achieved and the transient response characteristics are also improved.

The enclosure uses an AG board to prevent interference between direct and reflected waves and sharp directional characteristics.
Also, the internal volume is 45 liters.

There were two variations.

There were 2 types of speaker stand sold separately.
