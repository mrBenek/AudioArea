  • Pioneer CS-52

  • ¥ 12,900 (1 unit, around 1964)

Commentary

Based on the compact speaker system CS-51, this is the second model developed with the aim of richer reproduction.

The unit is a 2-way configuration. It uses a 16 cm cone type woofer with a newly designed chemical roll edge cone and a horn type tweeter equivalent to PT-8.

The enclosure is sealed with a highly airtight finish made of high quality plywood as a base material, and a large amount of glass wool is packed inside to suppress the disturbance of sound waves inside.
It is finished with walnut oil and metal sash is attached to the front.
