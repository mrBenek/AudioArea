  • National SB-30

  • ¥ 21,000 (1 unit, around the late 1960 s?)

Commentary

A speaker system marketed under the National brand.
There is a model with the same model number in the Technics era, but the content is a different configuration.

It is equipped with a 20 cmMFB woofer for the low band developed exclusively for the MFB system, a 9 cm cone type squaker for the middle band, and a horn type tweeter for the high band.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
