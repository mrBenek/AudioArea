Commentary

This speaker system is slightly smaller than 873A Barcelona.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit was newly developed in compliance with 411-8A and uses a 7.5 cm diameter voice coil and a strong magnetic circuit weighing 7.8 kg.

A horn type unit using a driver 807 to 8 a and a horn 811B is mounted in the high range.

N-801-8A is used for the network.

The enclosure is sealed and a grating grill is used at the front.
The exterior has a walnut finish.

The 2875A incorporates a 875A electronic crossover bi-amplifier on the back panel of the 770A and is used by connecting the output of the preamplifier with a pin jack.
