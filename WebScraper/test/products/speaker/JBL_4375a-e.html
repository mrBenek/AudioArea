Commentary

Line-array type speaker system for sound reinforcement.

The 4375A is designed to be ideal for expanding the audio spectrum in conference rooms, lecture halls, and small halls.

The unit is equipped with 4 13 cm cone type wide range units 2105H for speech range.

The thin design of the enclosure is designed to fit into a small space.
The exterior has a gray finish and is equipped with a black grill.
