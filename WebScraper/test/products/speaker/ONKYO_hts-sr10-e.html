Commentary

THX certified HTS System-10 surround speaker system.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
An OMF (Onkyo Micro Fiber) diaphragm is used for the diaphragm. This diaphragm has a matrix structure made of natural fibrous nonwoven fabric and thermosetting resin. It suppresses the natural noise generated by the diaphragm itself. Moreover, by taking the shape into consideration, the dynamic range is expanded and distortion is reduced. In addition, a damper material with less natural vibration is used.
The unique S-line edge is used for the edge of the unit. This is designed to prevent the loss of section information due to the reflection caused by the mismatch of the mechanical impedance of the joint with the diaphragm. Data analysis and hearing are repeated to reduce the reflection of propagation waves.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

The S. G. L. (Steady Ground Level) type 1-point concentrated circuit is adopted for the ground which is the reference of the audio signal in the network part to prevent the current flow in the ground line.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
A trapezoidal shape has been adopted to ensure the internal volume while suppressing standing waves. In addition, MDF has been adopted for the plate material. By incorporating a reinforcing plate inside, the resonance frequency is made higher than the vocal band and the frequency is dispersed to reduce noise caused by resonance.

Wall mounting metal fittings are mounted on the back.

The HTS-SR10 is a two unit pair configuration and the HTS-SR10P1 is a single unit configuration.
