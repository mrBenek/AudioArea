Commentary

A speaker system designed with wood grain and black Saran geometric patterns.

It is equipped with 123A which is 30 cm cone type woofer in the low range.
This unit uses long travel cone assembly, 8 cm diameter edgewise rolled copper ribbon voice coil, and 2.7 kg magnet.
In the high-frequency range

LE20, a 5 cm cone type tweeter, is installed in the upper range in the initial stage, and LE25-1, a 3.6 cm cone type tweeter, is installed in the latter stage.
