Commentary

A floor-type speaker system that uses a large-diameter woofer.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 7 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit has a damper less structure and incorporates a newly designed special equalizer to greatly improve the sound field characteristics of middle and high sound.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The plate material is particle board, and the exterior is finished with rosewood finish.
