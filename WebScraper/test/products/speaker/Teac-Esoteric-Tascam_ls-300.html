Commentary

This speaker system was developed with the theme of rich musical quality through repeated and strict hearing tests by experts.

It uses a 30 cm cone woofer for the low range, a 10 cm cone squaker for the middle range, and a horn tweeter for the high range.
We have succeeded in reducing the crossover frequency with the woofer to as low as 700 Hz using a squaker with improved fidelity over a wide range. By reducing the bandwidth of the woofer, we have reduced the intermodulation distortion for the mid-range.

The enclosure is completely sealed and sound-absorbent with a large amount of glass wool.
In addition, the Skorker is contained in a hermetically sealed box which prevents interference with the Woofer.

It is equipped with a level control for the high frequency band. The standard value is the 10 o'clock position on the clock.
