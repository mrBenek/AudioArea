Commentary

This speaker system was born as a crystal of sound technology and woodworking technology of JBL.

As far as I checked, there are 12 combinations of speaker units, and there are variations such as full range, 2-way, coaxial 2-way, etc.
Other combinations appear to have been sold in an enclosure + unit kit configuration (see C51 Apollo page).

The enclosure was smaller than the Olympus, and there were two variations of exterior finish : a hand-carved grid grill and a cloth grill.
The difference between these models is represented by the model number, with the grid grill ending with -2 and the cloth grill ending with -1.
