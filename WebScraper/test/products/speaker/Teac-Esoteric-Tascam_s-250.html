  • TEAC S-250

  • ¥ 16,800 (a set of two cars, around 1997)

Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

It is equipped with a 10 cm cone type woofer that uses pulp cone for the low range.
In addition, a soft dome type 2.5 cm tweeter is installed in the high band.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex type.
The exterior is a European wood-grain style, and the Sa RUNNET uses a メディタレニアン blue grill.

A banana plug speaker terminal is used for the terminal.

By using the bracket sold separately, it can be installed by hanging from the ceiling.
