Commentary

The Brid series 3-way speaker system jointly developed by ALPINE and LUXMAN.

A 32 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
By using carbon graphite cone for the diaphragm and 38 cm class magnet for the magnetic circuit, a low-frequency response with effective damping is obtained.

A 32 cm carbon graphite cone type unit is installed in the low range, and 6.5 cm and 2.5 cm dome type units with α -p titanium diaphragm are installed in the middle and high range.

Each unit is equipped with an anti-magnetic processing magnet to prevent distortion and color unevenness of images when used near a TV.

The Network Division uses an Integrated Network.
In this method, a cavity (air-tight chamber) is provided in the enclosure, and a network fixed to an aluminum die-cast frame is grounded to the cavity. This completely insulates the network from the sound pressure of the speakers and eliminates the adverse effects of sound pressure and vibration of the speakers.
In addition, the position where the magnetic interaction between the network coil and the speaker magnet is minimized is selected.

The enclosure is a closed round baffle type enclosure with R-curves at the corners to prevent noise in the low range near the baffle side due to the diffraction effect of sound.
In addition, the internal reinforcement and materials of each part are carefully selected to disperse the resonance mode and eliminate the sound characteristic of the enclosure.
