  • FOSTEX GS1

  • ¥ 8,000 (a set of two cars, around 1983)

Commentary

A small-sized spherical speaker system designed as a non-directional type.

This spherical speaker has been developed in 3 sizes : large, medium, and small.

The unit is equipped with a 5 cm cone type full range unit.

The enclosure uses a spherical shape made of a special material with high rigidity.

Initially, there were four exterior finishes, gold, blue and white in addition to the standard dark gray.

By using the separately sold chain hanger, it can be used hanging from the ceiling.
