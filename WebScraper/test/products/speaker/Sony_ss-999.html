Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system with a ribbon type tweeter.

A 16 cm cone type woofer using CARBOCON is mounted in the low range.

A direct-drive ribbon type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit has a structure in which high-purity aluminum is integrally molded with special molecular film, and can reproduce up to 50 kHz or more.

It is equipped with a continuously variable level control for high frequencies.
