  • ONKYO SKR-305

  • ¥ 27,000 (a set of two cars, around 1997)

Commentary

A surround speaker developed by inheriting the concept of the flagship speaker set HTS SYSTEM-2.

One 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, two 7-cm cone type tweeters are installed in the high range, and the dipole method installed on both sides of the enclosure spreads sound evenly throughout the listening room.

The speaker terminal uses a screw-type terminal compatible with banana plug.

Wall mounting metal fittings are installed.
