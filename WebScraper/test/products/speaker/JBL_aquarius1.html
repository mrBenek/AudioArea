Commentary

The Aquarius series non-directional speaker system.

The Aquarius 1 adopts a method of diffusing from the front, and even if it is installed on a bookshelf as a bookshelf bookshelf as a bookshelf type.
Two radial diffraction slots on the front panel interact with each other to create a wide stereo sound field over the entire range.

The low range has a 25 cm cone woofer, LE20, and the middle range has a 12.7 cm cone midrange, LE5.
In addition, the LE20-1, a 5 cm cone type tweeter, is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure was available in two color variations : walnut and satin white.

The mounting base was an option sold separately.
