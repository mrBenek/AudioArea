Commentary

A powered subwoofer with a design that matches your interior.

The unit is equipped with 16 cm cone type woofer.

The enclosure features a wood grain design that matches a variety of interior designs.
Equipped with auto power on / off function.
This function automatically determines the input of audio signal and switches the power to on / standby state.

It has a line-level output jack that can connect two or more sub-woofers.
