  • AR (Acoustic Research) AR-10 π (pai)

  • ¥ 193,000 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

A speaker system that enables acoustic control of low frequencies.

A 30.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The mid-range is equipped with a 3.8 cm domed scoker and the high-range is equipped with a 1.9 cm domed tweeter.

The unique acoustic control feature ensures that the playback sound is always balanced.

The enclosure uses a traditional acoustic suspension system that improves bass playback by allowing the air inside the fully enclosed enclosure to act as a spring to support the cones.

Built-in protective fuse.
