Commentary

A typical speaker system in the early B & W era.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A condenser type tweeter of 7.5cmx55cm is installed in the middle and high area.
This unit is composed of 11 vibration films arranged horizontally, and by arranging them in a bow shape, wide directivity is obtained.

The woofer of the enclosure is sealed.

The DM70 was so original that it sold only a few in Japan.
* In the catalog of bookshelf speaker series (DM-2, DM-4, D-5), it says "only one pair was bought by a musician".
