Commentary

This 3-way speaker system is compatible with digital sources by using new materials such as polymer chemistry.

A 26 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
A corrugated cone is used for the diaphragm, and the piston motion band is expanded by the synergistic effect of new material.

A 6 cm hard dome type skoker is mounted in the middle area.

A 2-cm hard dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
By adopting a diaphragm with enhanced synthesis, a flatter and undistorted sound is realized, and input resistant characteristics are also improved.

The network section uses laminated core coils with thick wires and low DC resistance and made of carefully selected magnetic materials.
We have also adopted a low-loss MF capacitor.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed. A round baffle suppresses diffraction, and each unit is embedded in a baffle plate to suppress interference from reflected waves.
The exterior is finished in black.
