  • VICTOR/JVC JS-150

  • ¥ 14,700 (1 unit, around 1976)

Commentary

This 2-way speaker system is cost-effective.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Cone paper made by German Kurt Mueller is used for the diaphragm.

A 6 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
We have succeeded in eliminating the corrugation and damper for holding the center of the voice coil by our own precision machining, and have eliminated the squeal and nonlinear distortion of the damper.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system. The exterior is finished with metallic wood and uses a black grill net.
