Commentary

MR series large 3-way speaker system.

It is equipped with a newly developed 46 cm cone Woofer 2042H in the low range and a 25 cm cone mid-range MI10-1 in the middle range.
In the high-frequency range, a horn type unit combining a flat-front biradial horn 2373 and a compression driver 2416H-1 using a pure titanium diaphragm is mounted.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and the corner is equipped with a protector.

The MR series input connectors are equipped with two standard 6.3 mm jacks.
