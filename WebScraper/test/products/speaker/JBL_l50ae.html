Commentary

A uniquely designed speaker system that aims to bring your music to life with crisp sound and precise expression.

A 25 cm cone type woofer, 127H, is used for the low range.
This unit is covered with a glass wool shell (acoustic resistance) at the back to improve low-frequency impedance characteristics.
As for the structure, it uses cone paper with good mass balance, a 10cm-diameter center dome that extends the characteristics up to the mid-range, and a 5cm-diameter voice coil, and is designed with emphasis on basics such as reducing distortion.

LE5-10 of 13 cm cone type skoker is used for the middle area.
It is housed in a completely sealed back cavity, and aluminum is used for the center dome.

A 3.6 cm cone type tweeter LE26 is adopted for the high range, and the center dome and cone paper size are optimized to improve the directional characteristics of the high range.
The magnetic circuit uses a 1.6 cm diameter voice coil and a 15,000 Gauss magnet.
In addition, the outer circumference is wrapped in a resin material with a taper to improve high-frequency energy and directional characteristics.

The network section has level controls for the mid - and high-frequency ranges.
