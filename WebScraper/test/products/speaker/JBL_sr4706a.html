Commentary

SR series high-volume output floor monitor speaker system.

In the lower range, it is equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer 2226G which adopted the JBL original VGC system.
In the high-frequency range, a horn 2383 with 50 ° horizontal and 60 ° vertical directional characteristics and a horn type unit using a driver unit 2447J with a 10 cm diameter diaphragm are mounted.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and is designed so that two types of elevation angles, 30 ° and 60 °, can be selected for floor monitoring.

It has a built-in network.
In addition, a 2-way multi-amplifier drive changeover switch is mounted.
