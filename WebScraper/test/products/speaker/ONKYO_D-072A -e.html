Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system designed based on 02A series direct acoustic sound.

A 15 cm tall cone unit is used for the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome unit is used for the high range.

The enclosure features a sleek MDF and the speaker terminal features a threaded terminal that supports banana plugs.
