Commentary

This is a compact speaker system with a European taste that realizes rich sound field reproduction and precise expression.

A 12 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
The expressive power is enhanced by the use of the rubber-based center cap that enables smooth connection with the tweeter and the edge of the rubber-based material that has excellent pulsed transient response.

A 2.5 cm wide angle dome type tweeter is used for the high range.
We aim to secure a wide listening area and a natural sound field feeling.

MDF is used for the baffle plate of the enclosure, and this baffle is coated with natural collagen fiber to reduce unnecessary radiation.
In addition, each unit is equipped with a front sub-baffle made of rubber-based resin to suppress unwanted vibration and reduce unwanted acoustic radiation through the flash surface concept.
