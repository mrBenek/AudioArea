  • ONKYO D-40

Commentary

A compact speaker system similar in size to VHS videotapes.

In the lower range, it is equipped with a 9 cm cone type woofer using ONF (Onkyo Microfiber) diaphragm with a matrix structure of natural fiber and unsaturated resin.
The high-frequency band is equipped with a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter that employs neodymium magnets.

The enclosure uses MDF.

Equipped with screw holes on the bottom and back of the body, allowing for free setting.
It also comes with metal fittings on the back that can be hung on the wall using commercially available wood screws.
