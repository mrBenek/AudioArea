  • NIKKA CS-X60

  • Price unknown (1 unit, around mid 1960 s?)

Commentary

A floor-type speaker system that houses a National speaker unit in a specially designed bass-reflex enclosure.

20 cm coaxial 2-way unit EAS-20PX60 is used for the unit.
Cone paper with high Young's modulus and high internal loss is used for the low range of this unit, and horn type tweeter is used for the high range.
