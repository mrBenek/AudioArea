Commentary

A compact bass reflex speaker system.

The Arcam Two has been used as a monitor in the sound management department of Philips' British CD factory and in the BBC.
To ensure accurate stereo positioning, both the woofer and tweeter are tuned to match at 0.5 dB.

A 18 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Kobeck scone is used for the diaphragm.

A 2.0 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
medite is used for the front baffle and external appearance is done with a walnut finish.

Bi-wired connection is supported.
There was a special stand sold separately.
This stand is designed to fit the Arcam Two and is made of medite.
