Commentary

Based on the D-202AII, this is a limited model with reinforced Woofer magnetic circuit and vibration control technology.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
It uses a large magnet with twice the volume compared to the D-202AII, and powerfully drives the non-pressed Bio-Crocone.
In addition, the magnetic circuit uses MDCT to reduce magnetostriction.

A 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter using pure silk for the diaphragm is used for the high frequency range.

Both woofer and tweeter voice coils use 99.9999% high-purity oxygen-free copper.

Acoustic stabilizers are installed on both the woofer and the tweeter, and the enclosure has a real wood veneer finish.
In addition, we have adopted a restraint damping method in which vibration absorbing parts are mounted in two places inside the cabinet by sandwich structure of steel plate and damper.

A banana plug compatible gold-plated brass scraping terminal is adopted.

It comes with a XPHP speaker cable made by Monster Cable in the US.
