Commentary

UK series 2-way speaker system with networkless technology.

By introducing a natural phase system based on wavefront control technology and reversing the positions of the 18 cm woofer and the 2.5 cm tweeter, the sound source at the listening point is adjusted on the time axis.

The woofer uses pulp corn and a diffuser is installed in the center part to achieve clear sound quality.

Equipped with a screw-type input terminal for banana plug.
