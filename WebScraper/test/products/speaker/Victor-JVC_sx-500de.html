  • VICTOR/JVC SX-500DE Dolce Eterno

  • ¥ 175,000 (a set of two cars, around 1999)

Commentary

This is a bookshelf type speaker system for the SX 500 series that newly adopted oblidome.

A 2.5 cm oblisoftdome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is made of habutae silk oblidome. In this diaphragm, the top of the dome, which used to be at the center of the dome, is placed above the center. By this, the directivity of sound is expanded further, and rich high sound field is realized.
An Alnico magnetic circuit is used for the magnetic circuit, and the driving force is enhanced by annealing.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of a Kurt Muller cone with a special manufacturing method and a high elastic pulp cone for a rigid bass reflex. In addition, an edgewise voice coil with excellent driving force is used to make the woofer sound better. This improves the transient characteristics and provides a wide playback range. In addition, the spider suspension made of U.S. - made material with good linearity and high durability enhances the ability to reproduce minute signals.
An annealed Alnico magnetic circuit is used for the magnetic circuit.

The network section uses carefully selected components such as film capacitors with superior high-frequency characteristics, oxygen-free copper wires, air-core coils, and brass-carved input terminals for banana-plug. This improves high-purity transmission and microcurrent characteristics, and provides a sound field free from noise and distortion.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
Solid cherry wood is used as the core material of the baffle board. In addition, not only the exterior but also the inside is finished with a cherry veneer from the sound quality side, and the whole surface is finished with a mirror gloss coating.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
