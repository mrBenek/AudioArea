  • ¥ 78,800 (released in 1976)

Commentary

A speaker system developed with the goal of "reproducing music".

The Mini Monitor was developed and designed by Elektri.
According to the catalog, there are 2 kinds of descriptions, "Mini Monitor 8A" and "BELAIR Mini Monitor", and there seems to be 2 kinds of variations on the plate of the front baffle.

A 30 cm cone woofer of 411 to 8 a type is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm of this unit is made of cone paper made by a specialized factory, and the long travel voice coil is designed to piston in an effective magnetic field at all times.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter 427-8A is mounted in the high range.
The mold-molded cone supported by the die-cast frame combines with a strong magnetic field to obtain high acoustic output and wide directivity.

N-1501A is used for the network part.
The N-1501A uses a low-loss inductor with a high-purity core.

The enclosure is made of imported hard particleboards and is designed with bass reflex.
The exterior has a red oak finish.
