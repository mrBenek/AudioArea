  • ONKYO PS-500AV

  • ¥ 20,000 (1 unit, around 1985)

Commentary

A speaker system using a canceling magnet system with complete anti-magnetic measures.

The low-range is equipped with a 21.5 cm cone woofer using a delta-olefin refined diaphragm, and the mid-range is equipped with a 7 cm cone midrange.
A leaf-type tweeter is installed in the high-frequency range.

Can also be set by hanging it on the wall.
