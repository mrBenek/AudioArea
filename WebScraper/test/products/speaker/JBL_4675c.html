Commentary

A two way speaker system designed for large-scale theaters with up to 500 seats.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses VGC (Bent Gap Cooling) and SFG (Symmetrical Field Geometry) technologies to achieve both high power and low distortion.

A unique biradial horn is used for the high-frequency band to achieve the same on-axis and off-axis characteristics and precise beam width control.

Built-in crossover and constant directional horn equalizer.
