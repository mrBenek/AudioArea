Commentary

A small speaker system that can be installed freely.

A 10 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Straight cone is used for the cone paper, foamed urethane rubber is used for the edge, and heat resistant spiral is used for the voice coil bobbin.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is designed with moisture resistance, heat resistance and temperature resistance in mind, and a special damping agent is applied on the surface.

The enclosure uses an integrated cabinet made of high-density materials with low Q.
In addition, nylon is used as sound absorbing material.

There was a wall hanging bracket as an optional item sold separately.
