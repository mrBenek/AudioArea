Commentary

The D-507 series small speaker system.

In the lower range, a 12 cm cone type woofer using OMF diaphragm which realizes light weight, high rigidity and high internal loss is mounted.
This unit uses an integrally molded S-line edge that reduces the reflection of propagating waves by organically joining the diaphragm.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is an MDF based bass-reflex structure with a silver hearts real wood veneer finish.
In addition, it uses a trapezoidal form that can ensure the internal volume while suppressing standing waves.

60 mm pitch screw holes for bracket support and wall mounting metal fittings are provided on the back.
