Commentary

This 3-way all-horn type speaker system was developed as a floor type system after Patrician and Georgean.

The low-range is equipped with a 15 wk 38 cm cone woofer, the mid-range is equipped with a horn mid-range combining a T25 driver and a horn 8 hd, and the high-range is equipped with a horn tweeter T350.

The enclosure uses a back-loading type folding horn that emits a minimum sound of 45 degrees to the left and right sides.
