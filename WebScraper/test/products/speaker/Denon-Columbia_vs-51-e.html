  • COLUMBIA VS-51

  • ¥ 10,000 (1 unit, sold in November 1967)

Commentary

Small 2-way speaker system.

A 10 cm cone woofer is used for the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter is used for the high range.

The enclosure is walnut material with a semi-gloss finish in clear lacquer, and the net on the front is not removable.
