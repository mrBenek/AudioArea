  • Technics SB-2506A(Technics4A)

  • ¥ 35,000 (1 unit, around 1971)

Commentary

A late-stage speaker system for the Technics4 (EAB 2506).
It has almost the same configuration as the Technics4.

It has a 25 cm cone woofer for the low range, a 12 cm cone squaker for the middle range, and a horn type tweeter for the high range.
