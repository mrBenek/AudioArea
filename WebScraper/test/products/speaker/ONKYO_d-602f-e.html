  • ONKYO D-602F

  • ¥ 55,000 (1 unit, released in October 1997)

Commentary

A tollboy-type speaker system that achieves both audio and AV playback.

Two 16 cm units with newly developed Bio Croscone are used for the low range.
In addition, a 2.5 cm dome type unit with newly developed soft dome diaphragm and NRIC magnetic circuit is adopted for the high frequency band.

The enclosure uses natural sounding MDF material.
