Commentary

A dual-driver speaker system designed to give you more flexibility in layout.

It is in the shape of a triangular prism and can be placed vertically and horizontally freely.
The frame that supports the two speaker units is also used as a mounting bracket and can be screwed directly onto walls or ceilings.

The unit is equipped with two 10 cm speaker units using delta-olefin refined for the diaphragm, and the angle between the two units can be changed up to 120 °.

By using optional grips sold separately, you can have more freedom of layout.
