Commentary

This is a bookshelf type speaker system that incorporates the specifications used for large speakers into the main parts.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
A-OMF monocoque is used for the diaphragm, and by using tough and flexible material and integrally molding up to the center cap, the deflection of the diaphragm is suppressed.

A 3 cm ring type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Noise radiation from the duct and unnecessary resonance of the cabinet are greatly reduced by adopting the slit shape duct originally developed for the bass reflex duct and the advanced AERO ACOUSTIC DRIVE with the separation structure between the cabinet part and duct part.

The cabinet surface is finished with piano wax after 7 times of painting and polishing.

A push-type speaker terminal is used.
