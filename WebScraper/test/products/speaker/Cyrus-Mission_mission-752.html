Commentary

A toll-boy speaker system.

A 17 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using 5-layer structural material and magnetic fluid is mounted in the high range.

The network section consists of 6 elements.

The Mission752 uses an inclined baffle for the front baffle to eliminate the phase shift near the crossover frequency.
The enclosure is molded with computer-controlled machine tool TFCT (Angle-Cut Cabinet Technology) using 25 mm thick MDF. The interior is damped with a viscous and elastic material to reduce noise pollution caused by resonance energy.
The exterior has a rosewood real wood finish.

Bi-wiring is supported.
