Commentary

A floor-type speaker system designed based on Professional Studio Monitor 4333.

Equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer 136A in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of ribbed corn paper with optimum mass and stiffness. It is driven by a 10.2 cm diameter copper ribbon edgewise wound voice coil and a 5.4 kg Alnico V magnet magnetic circuit to reproduce low sound energy with a rich sense of volume.

The mid-range is equipped with a horn type mid-range using LE85 and HL92.

A ring radiator 077 is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is designed with an inclined front panel for optimal listening position as a floor type.

The exterior is designed with smoke De Grasse at the top.
Also, there were 4 variations of the grill color.
