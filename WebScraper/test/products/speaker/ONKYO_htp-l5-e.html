Commentary

A 5.1 ch speaker system consisting of five satellite speakers D-L5 and a subwoofer SL 105.

The D-L5 is equipped with an 8 cm full-range unit using an OMF diaphragm. This unit uses an S-line edge that reduces the reflection of propagation waves generated at the junction between the diaphragm and the edge.
Aluminum cabinet with wall mounting holes on the back and 60-mm-pitch screw holes for bracket support on the bottom.

The SL-105 adopts a new structure in which low sound spreads in all directions.
The unit uses a 20 cm cone woofer with S-line edge, and the cabinet uses MDF material.
