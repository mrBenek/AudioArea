  • ONKYO D-052AII

  • ¥ 21,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

It uses a 13 cm cone type woofer for the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter for the high range.

By laying out the units close to each other and using four round baffles on the top, bottom, left and right sides, we are able to reproduce the sound field taking advantage of the characteristics of a small sound source.
The enclosure is made from MDF.
