Commentary

A speaker system equipped with a unit derived from the JBL Compact Monitor.

Two 20 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
Pure pulp white cone is used for the diaphragm, and aluminum die cast frame is used for the frame.

A horn type tweeter using a 2.5 cm driver is mounted in the high range.
A pure titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, combined with an aluminum die-cast horn, and unique directional control technology prevents unwanted sound reflection on walls and ceilings.

Anti-magnetic design with cancel magnet and shield cover system is applied.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
MDF of 12 mm thickness is adopted for the plate material, and the exterior is finished with wood-like finish.
