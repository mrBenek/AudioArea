Commentary

Sound Effect Series speaker set.

The Sound Effect series was developed as a sound system for multimedia applications such as audio, TV, PC and game.
music2 consists of two two way double woofer speaker system sat2 and one sub woofer system basswave.


sat2

sat2 consists of a 1.9 cm titanium dome type tweeter and two 8.9 cm cone type woofer.
Neodymium is used for the magnetic circuit of the woofer unit.

The cabinet uses a bass reflex system.

Brackets for mounting to wall and ceiling are included.


basswave

The basswave uses JBL's unique double-chamber band-pass system and the unit is equipped with two 13 cm cone woofers.

Corner type design is adopted.

Built-in power amplifier with output of 100W.


Other

A wire manager is included to facilitate wiring to the speakers.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
