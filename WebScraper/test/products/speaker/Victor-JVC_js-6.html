Commentary

A speaker system with steady and solid design that adheres to the basics of speaker production.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Kurt Mueller's KDU cone paper is used for the diaphragm.

A 7 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
It has a structure that takes advantage of the nature of the material by using a cone shape with an emphasis on the sound connection with the woofer and without any coloring.

The silicon steel core developed by VICTOR/JVC is used for the network part, and generation of non-linear strain at large input is suppressed.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system with matching ports that are finely tuned to the unit.
20 mm thick high-density single-layer particle board is used for the front and rear baffles to provide sufficient strength.
