  • ¥ 25,000 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

This 3-way speaker system pursues sound that does not make you feel tired even when listening for a long time.

The low range features a 30 cm cone woofer, the middle range features a 12 cm cone midrange, and the high range features a 7.7 cm cone tweeter.
Pioneer's original FB cone is used for these units.

The enclosure is sealed and has a walnut oil finish on the exterior.
The net on the front is removable.

In addition to the normal input terminal, multi-amplifier terminal is also mounted on the back, and multi-amplifier connection is possible by switching the switch.
