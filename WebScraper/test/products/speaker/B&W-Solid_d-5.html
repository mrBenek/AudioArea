Commentary

This bookshelf speaker system was developed based on basic research on DM2 and DM4.

The low range is equipped with DW150/5 which is a 16 cm cone type woofer.
This unit uses a BX cone with an effective diameter of 125 mm, a voice coil with a diameter of 25 mm, and rolled rubber at the edge.

The RCH24/8, a 2.5 soft dome type tweeter, is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure is a sealed air suspension type filled with sound absorbing material.
