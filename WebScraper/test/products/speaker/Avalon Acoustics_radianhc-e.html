  • AVALON RADIAN HC

  • ¥ 2,100,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in March 1996)

Commentary

In the RADIAL variation model, HC at the end of the model name means high current (large current). This is a speaker that is supposed to be driven by a high current power amplifier that supplies large current.

The only change from RADIN is the network, with a newly designed network circuit for high-current amplifiers.
