  • Aura SP-50

  • Price unknown (1 unit, released in 1994)

Commentary

A 2-way speaker system with a simple design.

A 16 cm cone type woofer made of carbon is mounted in the low range.
In addition, a 2.6 cm metal dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.

Bi-wiring is supported.

It was recommended that the back and side should be at least 60 cm away from the wall.
