Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system using a Philips speaker unit.

A 20 cm double-cone type full-range unit, 9710M/01, is used for the mid-low range.
This unit uses an Alnico V system magnet for the magnetic circuit.

A 3-cm domed tweeter, 0160/T, is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is a bass reflex system, and a hard particle board of 21 mm thickness is used for the plate material.
