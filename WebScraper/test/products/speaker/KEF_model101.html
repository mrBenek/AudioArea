  • KEF Model101(SP1122)

  • Price unknown (1 unit, released in 1979)

Commentary

A small speaker system using the same unit as LS3/5a.

A cone-shaped woofer B110 is mounted in the low range, and a dome-shaped tweeter T27 is mounted in the high range.

The Model101 has a computer-analyzed electronic protection circuit to avoid the thermal overload problem of small speakers.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
There were 4 finishes such as walnut, cheek, cheek, rosewood, and black ash.
