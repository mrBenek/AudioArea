Commentary

This 2-way speaker system from the ARRAY series can be used in any PA scene such as concert, theater, or speech, regardless of the installation method or application.

Two newly developed 35.5 cm cone Woofer 1400 Pro are installed in the low range.
In addition to JBL's unique VGC (Bent Gap Cooling) system, which realizes high input resistance characteristics, we have put in a lot of technologies such as 10 cm edge wound, aluminum ribbon, voice coil, pole piece with copper sleeve, and powerful magnetic circuit combining neodymium magnet with 2.5 cm extra-thick top plate.

A horn type unit is installed in the high frequency range.
The driver unit uses 2450 sl, a 3.8 cm diameter driver with snoutless structure, combined with a flat front biradial horn 2376.
The 2450 sl achieves distortion reduction by connecting the phase plug and horn directly, and the 2370 is an aluminum diecast horn with H45 ° xV35 ° directional characteristics.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system, and the exterior has a 22.5 ° vent angle that matches the directional characteristics of the horn.
It is made of 19 mm thick plywood with a steel frame built-in, providing excellent strength.

Various system configurations are possible by combining with the separately sold S. A. F. E (Secure Array Flying Ergonmics) flying metal fitting.
