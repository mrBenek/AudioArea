  • ONKYO MS-1000

  • ¥ 36,000 (1 unit, around 1986)

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system condensed into an orthodox bookshelf style.

A 22 cm and 10 cm delta olefin reinforced diaphragm is used for the low and middle band, respectively, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type unit made of chemical fiber material is used for the high band.
