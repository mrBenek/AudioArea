  • SONY SS-3150/SS-3150M

  • ¥ 33,000 (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

This is a 3-way speaker system that uses Ca-O RBOCON.

A 25 cm cone woofer with CARBOCON is used for the low range.
Light and flexible edge of special shape suppresses edge breakage. In addition, large corrugation damper is used for the damper to improve linearity.
The frame uses an aluminum die-cast frame to prevent resonance.

The tweeter uses a 6 cm cone unit, and the Super Tweeter uses a 4 cm cone unit.

Equipped with continuously variable level control.

There was also a SS-3150M with a different enclosure finish.
