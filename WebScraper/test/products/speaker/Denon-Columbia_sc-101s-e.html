  • DENON SC-101S

  • ¥ 29,800 (a set of two cars, around 1996)

Commentary

This is a polished speaker system that inherits the quality of the SC 101, which gained popularity under the nickname of Super Boy.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of tough pulp with surface coating, and the voice coil bobbin is made of super-heat-resistant polyimide.

A 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit has been tuned in Europe and uses densely woven lightweight fibers for the diaphragm.

Uses an enclosure with glossy side panels.
In addition, the directivity is controlled by protectors on both sides of the front.

Uses a machined large gold plated connector for banana plugs.

Wall mounting and ceiling mounting are possible with brackets sold separately.
