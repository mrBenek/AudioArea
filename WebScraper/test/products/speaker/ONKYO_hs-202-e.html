  • ONKYO HS-202

  • ¥ 11,000 (1 unit, around mid 1960 s)

Commentary

This is a compact speaker system.

A 12 cm cone type woofer with low Q (Q = 0.36) Onkyo's unique non-pre-cone is used for the low frequency range.
A magnetic circuit with a total magnetic flux of 86,000 maxwell is used for the magnetic circuit, and a roll-edge method with less distortion at large amplitude is used for the edge to realize fo = 38 Hz.

A 8 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The use of a sealed frame prevents interference with the woofer.

A full-scale network of 12dB/oct. is used.

The enclosure has a small sealed structure and is filled with glass wool as a sound-absorbing material.
In addition, the exterior is finished with walnut oil finish.
