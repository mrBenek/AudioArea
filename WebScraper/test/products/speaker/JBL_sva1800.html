Commentary

The SVA series speaker system adopts a symmetrical vertical array layout.

The SVA Series adopts the symmetrical vertical array (vertical virtual coaxial) method adopted in the K2 Series.
This system achieves both a wide frequency range and accurate sound image localization by the layout of a biradial horn and tweeter sandwiched between two woofers.

Equipped with 2 newly designed 20 cm cone type woofers in the low range.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.
A biradial horn is used in the horn section to ensure good directional characteristics with a wide service area and a soft dome type diaphragm.

It has a magnetic shield design.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
On the exterior, the opening of the biradial horn has a Bar-like grain and a black oak-like finish.

The legs are equipped with adjustable acoustic isolation spikes made of aluminum die-cast, and vertical axis (acoustic axis) can be adjusted.

Bi-wire speaker terminal is used.
