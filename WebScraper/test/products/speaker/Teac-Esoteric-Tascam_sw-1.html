Commentary

A subwoofer that uses the D. D. AIR system.

The D. D. AIR (Double Duct Air Tuning) system is used for the enclosure system.
In this system, each unit is equipped with an independent chamber to eliminate interference between units.

The unit is equipped with 2 newly developed 13 cm cone type woofers.
This unit uses an Sherwood fabric edge and a 6-layer coil for excellent drive capability.

The enclosure dimensions are the same width as S-300, S-300R and S-300PRO, and can be fixed to the upper part by using the attached bolt.
In addition, the orientation of the S 300 mounted on the upper part can be freely adjusted.

The SW-1 has a Tasman-maple finish and the SW-1GY has a British gray finish. When combined with the S-300 series, the appearance can be matched.
