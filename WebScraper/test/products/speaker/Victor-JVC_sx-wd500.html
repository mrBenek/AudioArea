Commentary

This is a speaker system that pursues high sound quality by adopting L-series method as well as wood cone.

A 14.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of 0.4 mm thick birch material, and the center cap is also made of the same material. The diaphragm is mounted by Shinkai Hatsuka with the largest diameter of wood cone.
Uses lightweight and low-loss EPDM foam rubber edge for the edge part. Also, uses an internal magnetic circuit with 45 Φ x30mm alnico magnet equivalent to the SX-L77 woofer.

A 2.0 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
The vibration plate of the tweeter is also newly developed. It uses a 0.22 mm thin lightweight wood dome made of two sheets of 0.11 mm birch wood. The shape of the diffuser has also been examined and the high-frequency characteristics have been greatly improved.
Neodymium magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

L77 class materials are used for the elements of the network section.
In addition, in order to prevent adverse effects of coil vibration, the network board completely separates the low-pass side from the high-pass side in pursuit of high sound quality.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The side plate has the same curved structure as the SX-L77. The plate is 21 mm thick all over, and the base plate is 48 mm thick with a 30 mm thick pedestal. It is combined with a large brass machined foot to increase stability in the low frequency range.
As for the external appearance, the same white sycamore plate as the SX-L9 is used for the front baffle, and the top plate, back and both sides are finished with high quality full gloss paint finish with maple plate.

The input terminal is a brass-machined speaker terminal for large cables larger than the L-series.
The layout is designed for ease of use during biwiring, and the short wire uses four wires, the same as the internal wiring.

There was a speaker stand LS-M5 as an option sold separately.
The LS-M5 is a stand developed at the same time as the SX-WD500. It uses a new structure to control low-range reflections.
This stand can also be used for SX-WD5, SX-WD8, SX-L33mk2 and SX-LC33mk2.
