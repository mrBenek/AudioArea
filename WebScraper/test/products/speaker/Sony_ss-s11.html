  • SONY SS-S11

  • ¥ 25,000 (1 unit, around 1979)

Commentary

A speaker system created by improving the perfection and balance of individual units.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In this unit, the deflection of the voice coil bobbin is suppressed by joining a metal ring to the voice coil part, and the movement of the voice coil is accurately transmitted to the cone paper.

The mid-range is equipped with 6 cm cone type mid-range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm of this unit is made of a material ordered from Europe for the SS-G4. It realizes high sound without weariness even if you listen to it for a long time.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
