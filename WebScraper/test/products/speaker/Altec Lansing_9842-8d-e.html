Commentary

A monitor speaker system that is ideal for monitoring in the studio and control room of a recording / broadcasting station.

9842-8A has a gray finish, and 9842-8D has a walnut finish.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A horn type unit using a manta ray Lehorn is mounted in the high frequency range.
The horn used in 9842 a to 8 a is called an asymmetric manta ray Lehorn. Its directional characteristics are 90 ° horizontally, 10 ° vertically and 30 ° vertically.
The driver is equipped with a Tangerine phase plug.

The network section is equipped with a 2-band mid-high frequency equalizer, which can be adjusted like a 3-way speaker.
In addition, it is equipped with automatic power control circuit unique to Altec.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
