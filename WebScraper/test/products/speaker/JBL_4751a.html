Commentary

Sound Power Series medium 3-way speaker system.

The 4751A is based on the 4750A and equipped with two 2404H biradial ring radiators for high frequencies.

Equipped with 2 30 cm cone type woofer 2206H in the low range.
In addition, a horn type unit that combines a compression driver 2445J using a 10 cm diameter titanium diaphragm and a flat front biradial horn 2380A is installed in the middle area.

The enclosure uses a wedge-shaped design that allows multiple side-by-side clusters, extending the coverage by 30 degrees horizontally for each additional enclosure.
It can also be used with sub base systems 4745A, 4748A, 4785A, and 4788A.

The 4751A uses a tri-amp drive system.
In addition, by inserting the optional crossover network N4751AP for middle and high frequencies (sold separately), it is possible to change to a bi-amplifier drive system in which low frequencies and middle and high frequencies are separated.
