  • Pioneer S-CR22

  • ¥ 21,000 (1 set of 3, released in 1994)

Commentary

A satellite speaker system consisting of three speakers.
By adding to S-2D, you can enjoy effective surround sound such as Dolby Pro Logic Surround.

The satellite speaker is a coaxial two way type that combines a 10 cm cone type and a 2.5 cm cone type.

A base unit is included for the setting free type satellite speaker.
This base unit is designed to fit the R-shape of satellite speakers. When placed on a shelf, it is installed as it is when placed vertically, and when placed horizontally, it is installed by placing the base unit.
In addition, the accuracy can be changed such as front facing or upward facing.
When mounting the speaker on a wall, fix the speaker and base unit with the supplied screws, and then mount the base unit on the wall.
