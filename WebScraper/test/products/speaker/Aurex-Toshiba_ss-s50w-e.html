  • Aurex SS-S50W

  • ¥ 59,000 (a set of two cars, around 1983)

Commentary

Floor-type speaker system.

A 28 cm cone type woofer with a newly developed air-dry method for the diaphragm is mounted in the low range.

Equipped with 8 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.

A piezoelectric horn type tweeter is used for the high frequency range.
Unlike the conventional electrokinetic type, this horn does not have a voice coil, so the input resistance is improved and the transient characteristics are also improved.

The enclosure is a floor type of bass reflex system.
The baffle plate of this enclosure is made of four way wood to suppress unnecessary vibration such as box rumbling.
