  • ONKYO SL-605

  • ¥ 100,000 (released on November 24, 2000)

Commentary

A subwoofer system with a built-in amplifier that employs a dual drive system.

The unit has 2 25 cm cone type woofers.

The enclosure uses a dual-drive system.
This system has a structure in which a woofer unit is mounted on both right and left sides, which reduces the vibration of the cabinet and realizes low sound reproduction with excellent elasticity in transient.
In addition, MDF material is used for the enclosure, and it has a real wood veneer finish.

The speaker terminal uses a screw-type speaker terminal for banana plug.

A preset function (4 items of cut-off frequency / volume level / phase / mode) which can store 3 patterns of power on / standby, volume level, MUSIC/MOVIE mode switching, 6-stage cut-off frequency selection, phase switching, mute, custom setting state with the attached remote control (4 items of cut-off frequency / volume level / phase / mode).
