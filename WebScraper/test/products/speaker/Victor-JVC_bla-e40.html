Commentary

The H. E series speaker system pursues high efficiency.

In the H. E. series, in order to achieve higher efficiency, a special processing method is used at the stage of separating the raw material pulp so that airtightness can be secured, and a method is adopted in which apparent density, Young's modulus, propagation loss, etc. can be freely selected at the stage of Victor Vacuum Press after production according to the application of the speaker unit.

It is equipped with a 30 cm cone type woofer for the low band, a cellular horn type squaker for the middle band, a 9 cm cone type tweeter for the high band, and a 5 cm horn type super tweeter for the ultra high band.

Since the enclosure has a completely sealed structure, it is assembled by completely sealing the edges on six sides with adhesive, and the unit is fixed from the front.
This prevents air leakage, and when the unit is fixed from the inside, the duct is formed to the thickness of the baffle plate to prevent the front chamber effect.

It is equipped with level control in the middle and high range.
Also, a multi-amplifier terminal is mounted.
