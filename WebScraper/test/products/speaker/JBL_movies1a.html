Commentary

A satellite speaker system from the Sound Effect series.

The Sound Effect series was developed as a sound system for multimedia applications such as audio, TV, PC and game.

A 8.9 cm cone type woofer with a neodymium magnet in the magnetic circuit is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 1.9 cm dome type tweeter with a titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high range.

The cabinet is designed to be sealed.

Comes with freestanding stand and brackets for mounting on walls and ceilings.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
