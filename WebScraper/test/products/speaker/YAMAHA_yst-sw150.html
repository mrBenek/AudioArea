Commentary

This is a YST type super woofer (sub woofer) designed by Tall Boy.

Yamaha's proprietary YST method realizes compact size and deep bass playback.
In the YST system, distortion is greatly reduced by using an air woofer, which uses air as a diaphragm, compared with conventional units, and low-frequency playback with quick response, high speed, and sharpness is achieved.

The unit is equipped with two 20 cm cone type woofers of anti-magnetic design, and by greatly improving the maximum sound pressure, the low sound reproduction capability is enhanced.

All discrete configuration is adopted for the power amplifier section.
In addition, it is equipped with a high-cut continuously variable frequency system and a phase changeover switch, so it is possible to adjust the setting according to the condition of the room to be combined.

Equipped with an auto power ON/OFF function that turns the power ON/OFF according to the presence or absence of signal input.

Two types of input terminals are installed : a speaker input and a pin-jack input. A terminal corresponding to a banana plug is used for the speaker input.
