  • PHILIPS RH423

  • ¥ 28,000 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

Two way bookshelf speaker system.

An 8-inch cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 1-inch tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

* In the catalog, it says that the tweeter is 1 inch, but in the image on the internet, I can find one with a cone type tweeter of around 2 inch, so I don't know what is clear.
