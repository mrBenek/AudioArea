  • ONKYO SKC-305

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, around 1997)

Commentary

This center speaker was developed by inheriting the concept of the flagship speaker set HTS SYSTEM-2.

It is equipped with two 10 cm cone type woofers in the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter in the high range. The virtual coaxial type realizes accurate positioning.

The enclosure takes the form of a horizontally long cabinet that smoothly connects the left and right front speakers and improves the sound spread.
In addition, by installing a tilt mechanism to adjust the vertical angle of the baffle surface, it can be adjusted by 30 degrees upward and 15 degrees downward.

The speaker terminal uses a banana plug-compatible screw type.

Magnetic shielding design is applied so that it does not disturb the screen even if it is installed near the television.
