Commentary

A two way speaker system using a new horn type tweeter.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A newly designed 6 cm horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a walnut oil finish on the exterior.
