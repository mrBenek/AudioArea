Commentary

4-way speaker system.

Two 20 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
This unit uses a long electron spectroscopy for chemica analysis voice coil and has a power capability of 118 dB at 35 Hz.

The mid-range is equipped with a 10.6 cm cone type mid-range.
In addition, it is equipped with a 3.5 cm dome type tweeter for the high range and a 1.9 cm dome type super tweeter for the ultra high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The exterior is finished with a Danish beach finish, and has a unique design with the DALI logo engraved.
