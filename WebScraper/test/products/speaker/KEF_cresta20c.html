  • KEF Cresta20c

  • ¥ 23,400 (1 unit, released in 2002)

Commentary

Center speaker system of the Cresta series.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
Polypropylene coated cone is used for the diaphragm.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Silk dome is used for the diaphragm, and power handling is improved by cooling effect using ferrofluid.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

There are 2 variations of the exterior finish, dark apple and maple, and black finish as a custom color.

By-wire connection is supported.
