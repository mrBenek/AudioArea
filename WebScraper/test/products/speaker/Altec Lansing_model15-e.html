Commentary

A floor-type speaker system that uses a Tangerine driver.

A 30 cm cone type woofer equivalent to 414 to 8 b is installed in the low range.

A horn type unit that combines a Tangerine driver and a dedicated horn is installed in the high range.
The horn section is a wide-directional L-shaped horn used in the A-8 and other devices. It has 120 ° horizontal directional characteristics and simple transmission characteristics unique to radial horns, and is designed to be suitable for close-range hearing at home.

The network is equipped with level control so that you to control the mid and high bands independently.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
