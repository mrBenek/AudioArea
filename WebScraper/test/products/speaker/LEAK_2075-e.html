Commentary

A speaker system developed with the goal of reproducing a true concert hall at home.

A 38 cm sandwich cone woofer is used for the low range.

It uses a 17 cm sandwich cone unit for the middle and low range, a 6 cm plastic cone unit for the middle and high range, and a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter for the high range, which is the same as the 2030 and 2060. These units are installed in the upper enclosure.

A low-distortion type air-core coil is used for the network to minimize internal resistance.

The enclosure is divided into upper and lower sections and is designed for directional characteristics.
In particular, the lower enclosure is vibration-proof.
