  • TRIO SS-2

Commentary

A slim type speaker system.

The unit is equipped with an elliptical cone type full range unit of 17.5cmx12.5 cm.

It is designed for wall mounting and the rear baffle has vertical and horizontal mounting holes.
Attach two screws at a distance of 15 to 18 cm to the designated wall surface, and hook them into the holes to fix them.
