Commentary

An all-honeycomb 3-way tall boy speaker system.

It features a 22 cm honeycomb plane woofer in the low range, a 5.2 cm honeycomb plane midrange in the middle range, and a 2.8 cm honeycomb plane tweeter in the high range.

By thoroughly implementing anti-magnetic measures, it does not affect the screen even on the side of the TV.

The enclosure is a tower type and can be installed without a stand like the current tall boy type.
