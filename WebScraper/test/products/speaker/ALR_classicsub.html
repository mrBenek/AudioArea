  • ALR/Jordan Classic Sub

  • ¥ 200,000 (1 unit, around 2003)

Commentary

Active subwoofer from the CLASSIC series.

The unit is equipped with 30 cm cone type woofer.
Pulp cone is used for the diaphragm.

It has a built-in 150W power amplifier.

MDF material is used for the enclosure and the bass reflex method is adopted, and Mikage stone is used for the top plate, which realizes the beautiful appearance and the deep low sound reproduction.
Finished with natural alder material as exterior finish.

Equipped with a phase selector switch.

The crossover frequency is variable and can be adjusted according to the type of speaker you combine.
