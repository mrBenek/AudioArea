  • Pioneer IS-W5

  • ¥ 47,500 (1 unit, around 1985)

Commentary

Super woofer that uses EBD (Electronic Bass Drive) system and 50W (EIAJ) mono amplifier.

A sub-harmonic synthesizer is used to generate sounds one octave lower than the input signal, so that the sound effect of a movie can be felt more realistically.

It is equipped with an input terminal so that it can be enjoyed on a stereo amplifier or system component TV with center channel.

The crossover frequency selection switch and volume adjustment make it suitable for a wide range of medium and small speakers.
