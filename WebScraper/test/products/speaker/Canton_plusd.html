  • CANTON Plus D

  • ¥ 48,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1988)

Commentary

Speaker system designed for Cubic Super Base System / Plus Series desktops.

It has an 11 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter in the high range.

The exterior comes in two color variations, black and white.
In addition, the punching metal on the front has 3 color variations of silver brown / white / black as an option sold separately, and you can choose the color which matches the interior.

By adding the optional Woofer system Plus C, you can improve the bass playback capability.
In addition, various commercially available speaker mounting brackets and camera tripods can be used, allowing for free installation.
