Commentary

A speaker system designed specifically for horizontal positioning.

A 30 cm cone type woofer using a low-distortion SFG magnetic circuit is mounted in the low frequency range.

The middle area is equipped with a 13 cm cone type squaker using 3 d photographic image by laser beam.

A dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The unit layout consists of symmetrical mirror image pairs to improve sound localization in a horizontal position.
In addition, it is possible to adjust the attenuator for middle and high sound without removing the grill cloth.

The exterior has a walnut finish.
