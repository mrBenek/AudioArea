  • ¥ 270,000 (a set of two cars, around 2000)

Commentary

A tall boy speaker system of the High Resolution Series.

Long stroke type 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The mid-range has 2 13 cm cone mid-range units.
This unit is named MAG alloy mid-range and uses a concave diaphragm made of magnesium / aluminum alloy.

A 2.5 cm hard-domed tweeter is installed in the high range.
Diamond-coated titanium dome is used for the diaphragm.

Anti-magnetic design is applied.

A bass reflex system is used for the enclosure, and a tall boy type is realized by mounting a woofer unit on the side.
The exterior is finished with a black wood finish.
