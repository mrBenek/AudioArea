Commentary

Speaker system with FLAT-5 mounted in a dedicated enclosure.

The unit is equipped with FLAT-5 which is a 12 cm full range unit.
This unit is made from specially molded the main raw material of corn paper, mixed with chemical fiber and specially molded.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method and is made of particleboards.
In addition, a prominent frame unique to Coral is used for mounting the unit to prevent cavity resonance due to the thickness of the baffle hole.
The exterior features a walnut polyurethane finish.
