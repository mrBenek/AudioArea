Commentary

Active subwoofer with a digital amplifier.

The unit is equipped with 17 cm cone type woofer.
A non-press weight cone with surface coating is used for the diaphragm, and a powerful magnetic circuit is composed of a large diameter 6-layer voice coil equipped with a weight ring and a large magnet.

A PWM type amplifier is used in the amplifier section.

The enclosure has a unique structure that absorbs low-frequency internal noise and provides a clearer sound.

The high-cut filter is equipped with 2 modes, continuously variable and fixed.

Equipped with a phase inversion function.

Equipped with an auto power on / standby function that turns the power ON quickly at signal input position j.
