  • YAMAHA NS-F101

Commentary

A thin speaker system that can be mounted on a wall or stand.

Four 10 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
In addition, a 1.9 cm balanced dome tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
It has realized a thin body with a depth of 99 mm and can be mounted on the wall.

There were 2 types of speaker stand as an option sold separately.
You can choose the height according to the installation purpose.
