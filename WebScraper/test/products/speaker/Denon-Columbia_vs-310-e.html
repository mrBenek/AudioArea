Audio Database

  • DENON/COLUMBIA VS-310

  • ¥ 33,500 (1 unit, around 1968)

Commentary

This is a 2-way floor type speaker system of the Columbia Denon Series which was a high-class series at that time.

It is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer in the low range and a horn type tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure is fully enclosed.

A high-frequency attenuator is used to enable level control.
