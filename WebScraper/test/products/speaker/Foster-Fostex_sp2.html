Commentary

A sound pole type speaker system suitable for acoustic equipment.

SP2 and SP2S use different units.
SP2 is equipped with 4 FE103 which is 10 cm cone type full range unit.
In SP2S, it has been changed to FP103 the same 10 cm diameter.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The exterior has a gray leather paint finish.
