  • Pioneer S-X11

Commentary

A compact 2-way speaker system based on Pioneer's sound concept.

A 14 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
An aluminum die-cast frame is used for the frame, and it is fixed by the midship mounting method.
In this method, the unit is fixed to a part other than the baffle plate where vibration is unlikely to occur, and unnecessary vibration transmitted from the unit to the baffle plate is blocked.

A 2 cm soft dome tweeter is used for the high range.

Surround / Normal switches are included to adjust sound quality.
