Commentary

A four way floor speaker system using a CS tweeter.

It has a 26 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 16 cm cone midbus in the middle and low range.

A 3.8 cm hard dome type skoker is mounted in the middle area.

A 2.5 cm hard dome type tweeter using a manganese aluminum diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

We have improved impedance characteristics and efficiency by thoroughly reviewing the units used and the crossover network.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and the height of the unit from the floor surface is adjusted to improve the transient characteristics and resolution in the low range.

There were piano finish and real wood finish for the exterior.
