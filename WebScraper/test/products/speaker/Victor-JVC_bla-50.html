Commentary

A high-grade floor speaker system using newly developed speakers.

For the lower range, a 40 cm cone type unit with 7 cone-shaped Konica Le Dome, which increases the strength of the speaker cone and expands the pointing angle, is adopted.
In addition, a cellular horn type unit is used for the middle band and two panoramic horn speakers with acoustic lens are used for the high band.

The enclosure is completely sealed and glass wool is used as a sound-absorbing material for the inner surface.
In addition, it has an oil finish that makes use of the natural wood grain.
