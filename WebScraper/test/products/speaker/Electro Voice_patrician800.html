Commentary

A large-floor-type 4-way speaker system that represents Electrovoice.

There were two variations in appearance : contemporary and traditional. Contemporary is a well-known design, and traditional is a little simpler than that (the design at the back of the picture).
As for the finish of the exterior, there were 3 kinds of mahogany, satin walnut and cherry wood around 72 years. In 80's, it was changed to 3 kinds of mahogany, satin walnut and fruit wood.

It is equipped with a 76 cm cone type woofer of 30W for low band.

A 30 cm cone type midbus is installed for the middle and low band.
The midbus was equipped with SP12D and SP12M depending on the time of manufacture.

The middle and high range is equipped with a horn type mid-range combining a driver unit and 8 hd.
The driver unit was equipped with T25A around' 72 and T250 in' 80 s.

A horn type tweeter T350 is mounted in the high range.

X1835 is used for the network circuit.

It is designed as luxury furniture and features a beautiful enclosure with an elaborate woodwork finish.
The enclosure is designed to be placed in a corner of a room and is made from carefully selected 2.5 cm thick plates.
