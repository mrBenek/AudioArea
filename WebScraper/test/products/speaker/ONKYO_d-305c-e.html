  • ONKYO D-305C

  • ¥ 22,000 (1 unit, sold on May 18, 1998)

Commentary

Center speaker designed to match with D-305F and D-205F.

Two 12 cm cone type woofer using silk OMF for the diaphragm are mounted in the low range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter using a diaphragm based on polyester fiber is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure uses MDF the enclosure and a virtual coaxial layout with a tweeter in the center improves the sense of orientation.
