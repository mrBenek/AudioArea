Commentary

A speaker system that can be used in a wide range of applications.

The LM1 comes in five color variations : black, white, silver, red and green.

A 12.5 cm cone type woofer made of polypropylene cone is mounted in the low range.
In addition, a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using a polyester diaphragm is mounted in the high range.

The cabinet is weather-resistant and can be used outdoors.

LM1 is designed with an integrated stand.
By adjusting the angle of this stand, it can be installed in a wide range of ways such as horizontal and vertical, wall mounting, and on a desk.
