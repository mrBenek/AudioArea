  • DENON/COLUMBIA VS-360

  • ¥ 43,000 (1 unit, around 1971)

Commentary

A 3-way floor type speaker system with a special bass reflex structure.

A 30 cm cone type unit with edge-wise voice coil of the same type as VS860 is used for the low range.

In the middle area, two 12-cm aluminum dome skokers developed by Columbia are installed in parallel, and a large W-shaped acoustic lens is installed in front of them.

A horn tweeter with a square horn is used for the high range, and an acoustic lens is attached in front of it.

Equipped with multi-channel terminal.
