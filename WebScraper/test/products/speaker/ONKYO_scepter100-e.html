Commentary

This is a three way floor type speaker system of the Septor series.

The low-range is equipped with a high-compliance 35 cm cone woofer.
This unit uses newly developed corn paper material and foamed neoprene rubber edge to enhance low sound reproduction capability.

The middle area is equipped with a sectolhorn-type squaker using ultra-hard duralumin diaphragm.

The high-frequency band is equipped with a separable horn type tweeter.
This unit uses an ultra-hard duralumin diaphragm as well as the mid-range one, and a newly developed magnetic circuit to improve the transient.

The front panel has level controls for the middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

Equipped with a multi-amplifier terminal, multi-amplifier playback is possible by switching the switch.
