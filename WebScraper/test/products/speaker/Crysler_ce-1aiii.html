  • CHRYSLER CE-1aIII

  • ¥ 43,900 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system that employs an acoustic suspension system.

It has a 30 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 12 cm cone midrange in the middle range.
In addition, a 5 cm horn type tweeter with a diffuser is installed in the high range.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
