  • DENON USC-F07

  • Price unknown (1 unit, around 1996)

Commentary

This speaker system was announced as a high-spec speaker with the highest density in its class.

It is equipped with a 16 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter in the high range.

The front baffle has a leather finish to reduce unwanted radiation.

Speaker terminal compatible with banana plug is adopted.
