Commentary

A floor-type speaker system with high input resistance, including a large diameter voice coil.

A 32 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The mid-range is equipped with an 8 cm ring scoker, and the high-range is equipped with a 3 cm horn tweeter.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
