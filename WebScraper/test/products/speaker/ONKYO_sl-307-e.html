  • ONKYO SL-307

  • ¥ 32,000 (sold on August 9, 2001)

Commentary

A sub-woofer with a new structure that spreads in all directions at once.

The unit is equipped with 20 cm cone type woofer which adopted S line edge.

MDF material is used for the enclosure.

Equipped with a variable high-cut filter and an auto standby function that can be turned on / off.

There are 2 kinds of variations, wood grain and black.
