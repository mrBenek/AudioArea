Commentary

A high-quality 3-way speaker system with an all-horn configuration.

It has a 30 cm cone woofer in the low range and a horn in the front.

The mid-range is equipped with a horn type mid-range, and the high-range is equipped with a horn type tweeter.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Due to strict inspection and testing as a top-of-the-line product, mass production was not possible and a pre-order production system was adopted.
