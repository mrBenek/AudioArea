Commentary

This 2-way bookshelf type speaker system is also ideal as a 4-channel rear speaker.

JA 2054 of 20 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range.
The cone paper is made of a mixture of pulp and wool that has a large internal loss. In addition, a rubber type dump material is used in combination to suppress split vibration.
Phenol rubber type support material is applied to the edge to prevent the frequency characteristic dip that occurs near the edge.

In the high range, it is equipped with JA 0507, a tweeter with horn and a show with a structure close to dome type which is a mix of cone type and horn type.
A Mylar is used for the diaphragm, the bobbin is removed in order to lighten the vibration system, and the diaphragm and aluminum ribbon wire edgewise wound voice coil are directly connected.
In addition, an expanded urethane brake element is used between the diaphragm and center pole to obtain excellent high-frequency characteristics.
In addition, directivity is improved by a special cross section near the dome.

In addition to NS 410, there were 2 kinds of variations, NS 410 h of white color and NS 410 r of Rosewood.
