Commentary

This is a 4-way speaker system corresponding to the unit modification model of 4315A.

In the 4315B, 3 units other than the tweeter have been changed. The low range is equipped with a 30 cm cone type woofer 2203H, the middle range is equipped with a 20 cm cone type mid-woofer 2108H, and the middle range is equipped with a 13 cm cone type 2105H.

A horn type tweeter 2405 is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
In addition, support metal fittings are provided on the back so that it can be suspended.

The 4315B comes with a gray finish, the 4315 BWX comes with a walnut finish, and the Sa RUNNET comes with a black grill on the 4315B and a blue grill on the walnut finish 4315 BWX.
