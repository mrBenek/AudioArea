Commentary

This speaker system was developed as the most standard system in the TRIO-BOZAK lineup.

The configuration is the same as the B-312, but the volume of the enclosure increases the bass playback capability.

Equipped with a 30cm2-way coaxial speaker unit for all bands.
This unit is a combination of a 30 cm cone type woofer B-199A and a 5 cm cone type tweeter B-200Y for a total of 3 units.
The B-199A uses felted paper cone with wool mixed in pulp to achieve light weight, hardness and high compliance, as well as low distortion bass.
In addition, the vibration plate of B-200Y is made by dumping the metal diaphragm in a special method to suppress the wild behavior of the characteristics, and a smoother playback sound is obtained.

The exterior of the enclosure has a walnut oil finish.

The terminal section is equipped with a multi-amplifier selector switch and multi-amplifier terminal.
