  • TRIO KL-40Z

  • ¥ 46,000 (a set of two cars, around 1974)

Commentary

Equipped with a horn tweeter, this 2-way bookshelf speaker system was developed with an emphasis on efficiency.

A 20 cm roll-free edge and cone type woofer is mounted in the low range, and a full-fledged angular horn type tweeter is mounted in the high range.
