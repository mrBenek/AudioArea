Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system developed for small studios, adjustment rooms, and portable playback monitors.

A 30 cm cone Woofer 2212 is installed in the low range, and a 13 cm cone Squaker LE5-2 is installed in the middle range.
A 3.6 cm cone type tweeter LE25 is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure features a bass reflex design and a back cavity on the back of the Skoker unit to prevent rear interference from the woofer.

Equipped with a continuously variable control that can adjust the middle and high ranges independently.

There are 2 variations, gray finish and walnut finish.
