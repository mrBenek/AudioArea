  • INFINITY BU-120

  • ¥ 75,000 (around 2001)

Commentary

Active subwoofer with a built-in power amplifier.

The unit is equipped with 30 cm cone type woofer.

The amplifier section has a volume control function.

The crossover frequency can be continuously variable.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
Also, the exterior is finished in black.

Power supply voltage conversion transformer (100 → 120 V) is attached.
