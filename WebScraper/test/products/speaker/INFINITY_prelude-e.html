Commentary

This floor-type speaker system is part of the Composition series, a lineup of home theater speakers.

The 30 cm cone type active subwoofer for the low range allows for more profound bass playback.
In addition, it is equipped with a 12.5cmx4 cone type unit for middle and low band, a 10.0cmx2 cone type unit for middle and high band, and a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter for high band.
