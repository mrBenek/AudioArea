  • INFINITY QUADRAPOLE

  • ¥ 130,000 (a set of two cars, around 1996)

Commentary

A surround speaker system of the Composition series, a lineup of home theater speakers.

Uses 12.5 cm cone type woofer for low band and 4 9 cm cone type units for middle and high band.

In order to make more effective use of the installation space and improve the sound field reproduction capability, it is compatible with installation on the wall, built-in, and bracket installation.
