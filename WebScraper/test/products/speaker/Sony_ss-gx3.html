Commentary

This is a setting-free type speaker system for multi-use, including surround rear speakers.

It is equipped with a 12 cm cone type woofer for the low range and a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter for the wide angle type for the high range.
Magnetic shielding design with double magnet and shield cover is applied to prevent influence on TV screen, etc.

The enclosure has a 6-sided finish and can be set vertically, horizontally, or suspended from the top.
