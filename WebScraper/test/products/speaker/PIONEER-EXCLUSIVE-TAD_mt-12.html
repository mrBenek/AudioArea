  • EXCLUSIVE MT-12

  • ¥ 45,000 (1 unit, around 1978)

Commentary

Mid-high system, the mid-high part of Exclusive's Model3301 speaker system.

The unit has a 12 cm coaxial speaker.
This unit uses a 12 cm ring radiator for the middle part to improve power linearity, and a duralumin diaphragm and 120 ° wide-angle secaural horn for the high part.

The enclosure has a dual structure using lauan plywood and low-Q high-density materials.

Built-in high-cut network for the middle band and low-cut network for the high band.
