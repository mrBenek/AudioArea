Commentary

This compact speaker system was born with a thorough focus on European sound.

A 13.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The vibration plate of this unit is coated with special resin based on natural pulp to suppress harmful singing and split vibration.
In addition, the edge and dust cap are made of rubber material, which is also used in high-grade machines, to achieve more stable bass reproduction. In addition, the voice coil bobbin is made of aluminum alloy to improve input resistance.

A 2 cm balanced dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Ultra-lightweight polymer is used for the material of the vibration plate of the tweeter, and sufficient sound pressure level and directivity are obtained by making the shape 2 cm diameter balance dome type.
In addition, the voice coil accelerates propagation speed by directly driving the diaphragm without using a bobbin, and magnetic fluid is used in the magnetic circuit to improve input resistance and distortion near crossover.

It has an anti-magnetic design and is considered not to have a bad influence on the TV screen.
