  • ONKYO D-07 ESSAY

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1989)

Commentary

A speaker system designed to improve sound quality by solving problems inherent in compact speakers.

A woofer with a 12 cm pure crystal fiber diaphragm is used for the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

The enclosure is a new round form with a suede-like paint finish.
In addition, the attached stand is removable, and Onkyo blanket (TK-M50), commercially available blanket and tripod stand can be used for the joint of bottom and back.
In addition, there is a mounting hole for wall mounting on the back side.
