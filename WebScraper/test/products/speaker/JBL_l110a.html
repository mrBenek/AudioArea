Commentary

A speaker system corresponding to the minor change model of L110.

A 25 cm cone type woofer LE111H is mounted in the low range.
This unit uses a strong magnetic circuit with greatly reduced strain and cone paper coated with a unique brake agent to achieve optimum mass and rigidity.

13 cm cone type Skoka LE5-10 is mounted in the middle area.
It is composed of hard cone paper and 2.2 cm diameter voice coil, etc., enabling reproduction with less distortion even in Dainyu Chikara, and the completely sealed back cavity prevents rear interference with the woofer.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter 033 is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is made of two linen layers impregnated with phenol resin. By combining lightness and robustness, directional characteristics and transient characteristics are improved. In particular, directional characteristics of 90 ° at 20 kHz are realized.

A bass reflex system is adopted for the enclosure, and the sound image localization is improved by adopting a vertical single row unit layout.

There were 2 kinds of grill colors, black and brown.
