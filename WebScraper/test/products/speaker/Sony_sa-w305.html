Commentary

Active super woofer that adopts advanced SAW system.

The unit is equipped with 16 cm cone type woofer.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

The newly developed advanced SAW system is adopted for the cabinet.
The conventional SAW system has a structure in which the unit is mounted on a sub-baffle inside the cabinet and sound is emitted through a duct. The Advanced SAW system has been further improved based on this structure. By installing a new radiator in the front baffle, delay of emitted sound is prevented, and both good response and heavy low sound reproduction are realized.

The amplifier section is equipped with a low-frequency reproduction correction circuit.

The high-cut frequency is continuously variable so that it can be adjusted according to the combination of speakers.
