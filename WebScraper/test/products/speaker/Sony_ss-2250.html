Audio Database

  • SONY SS-2250/SS-2250M

  • ¥ 53,000 (a set of two cars, around 1975)

Commentary

This is a 3-way speaker system that uses Carbocon.

A 25 cm cone unit is used for the low range, a 6 cm cone unit for the middle range, and a horn unit for the high range.

The edge of the woofer uses a light and flexible special edge to suppress unwanted mid-range sounds caused by deformation of the edge.

The enclosure is Georgia Walnut style.
Also, there was SS-2250M with a different finish.
