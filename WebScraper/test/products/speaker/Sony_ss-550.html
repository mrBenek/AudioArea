  • SONY SS-550

  • ¥ 18,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1970)

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system developed for people who are new to stereo or want an affordable sub-speaker.

The speaker unit uses a 20 cm cone for the low range, a 10 cm cone for the middle range, and a 5 cm cone for the high range.

The enclosure uses a closed system with a walnut finish.
