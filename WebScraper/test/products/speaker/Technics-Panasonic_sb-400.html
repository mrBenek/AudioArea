Commentary

A speaker system designed to improve directional and transient characteristics under the theme of multi-seller horn.

A 25 cm cone woofer of high compliance type is installed in the low range.
The edge of this unit is non-directional and has the advantage of light weight. It is made of urethane with discontinuous bubbles. Moreover, it is formed into a convex shape opposite to the conventional concave edge to improve input resistance and linearity.

The middle area is equipped with a horn type squaker.
The horn section is an 8-cell type multi-seller horn. By dividing the inside of the horn into upper, lower, left and right cells, the directivity, which is a problem with horn types, is improved.
In addition, a hard duralumin diaphragm is used for the diaphragm to achieve low distortion and improved transient characteristics.

A horn type tweeter using a 6-cell multi-seller horn is installed in the high frequency range.

Equipped with independent attenuators for each of the mid-range and high-range regions, it can be switched to 3 stages.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a Oceania cheeks oil finish on the exterior.

Equipped with a multi-amplifier terminal, 2-way / 3-way multi-amplifier drive is possible by switching the mode switch.
