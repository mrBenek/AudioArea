Commentary

Among the R Series lineup, this is a 2-way speaker system that pursues space factor and cost performance so that anyone can use it easily.

A 20 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
The cone paper is treated with a special coating to prevent split vibration and improve transient characteristics.
In addition, by adopting the free edge method, linearity is improved, edge resonance is suppressed, and generation of distortion at the stage amplitude is suppressed.

A horn type unit made of die-cast aluminum is used for the high frequency band.
A special polyester film is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure has a structure with a sound absorbing partition in the center to prevent standing waves and to eliminate interference between speakers.

The grill net on the front has a two tone color.
