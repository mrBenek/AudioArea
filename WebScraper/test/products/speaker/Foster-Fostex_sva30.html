Commentary

Active super woofer with built-in amplifier.

The unit is equipped with a 30 cm cone type woofer using multi-layer cone.
The magnetic circuit is designed with a repartion-type magnetic shielding design to eliminate adverse effects on cathode ray tubes, etc.

Equipped with a low-pass filter that can switch the crossover frequency in three stages.

The enclosure has a 45 cm square design and uses a bass reflex system.
The woofer unit is mounted downward to minimize unwanted mid - and high-frequency radiation.

Both the high-level (RCA pin) terminal and the low-level (speaker terminal) terminal are equipped with a pair of input and output terminals. These terminals can be connected even when the input / output terminal of the amplifier is insufficient.
