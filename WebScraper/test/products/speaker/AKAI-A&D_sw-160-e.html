Audio Database

  • AKAI SW-160

  • ¥ 44,000 (1 unit, around 1970)

Commentary

A speaker equivalent to the compact version of SW 180.
At that time, Akai recommended the combination with AA-8000 made by the company.

It uses a 30 cm woofer for the low band, a 16 cm squaker and a 13 cm squaker for the middle band, two 5 cm horn tweeters for the high band, and a 4.5 cm super tweeter for the ultra high band.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type with pipe ducts.

The front net is not removable.
