  • BRAUN LV1020

  • ¥ 239,000 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

This active speaker system realizes a multi-channel system by installing a power amplifier inside.

The system is equipped with a power amplifier, and each speaker is connected directly to a specially designed power amplifier.

It is equipped with a 30 cm cone type woofer for the low range, a 5 cm soft dome type scoker for the middle range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter for the high range.

There were 2 types of exterior finish, walnut finish and white finish.
The front panel has an anodized aluminum finish and the aluminum punching metal is removable.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
