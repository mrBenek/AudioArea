  • Pioneer S-HS01

  • ¥ 31,000 (1 set, sold in September 2001)

Commentary

A 5.1 ch speaker package with satellite speakers and a subwoofer S-W11.

The S-HS01 is a combination of the DVD player DV 545 and the AV amplifier VSA C 300 as the Smart theater300.


Satellite speaker
The unit uses a 15x6cm elliptical cone type full-range speaker.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.


Subwoofer

The unit is equipped with a 14 cm cone type woofer.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
In addition, it is designed for both vertical and horizontal placement.
