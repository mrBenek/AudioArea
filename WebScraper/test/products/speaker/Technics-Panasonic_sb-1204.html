Commentary

Small 2-way speaker system.

EAS-12PL50 which is a 12 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range.
This unit is designed to show excellent low-frequency characteristics when mounted in a small cabinet. By adopting UL edge for the edge, it enables reproduction with less distortion to the mid-range.
In addition, by adopting a large magnet and a sealed yoke with low loss for the magnetic circuit, efficiency is improved and damping characteristics in the low range are also improved.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.
Lightweight and highly rigid polyester film is used for the vibration part to reduce the mass of the vibration system.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed with packing to prevent air from leaking through the joints between the baffle plate and the back plate.
