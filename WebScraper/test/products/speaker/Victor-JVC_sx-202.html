Commentary

This is an A4 size small speaker system that uses a full-range metal cone unit.

We have developed and adopted a 12.5 cm metal cone full range unit.
Aluminum die-cast frame is used for the frame and firmly fixed.

The cabinet adopts a newly developed cascade bass reflex structure. The cabinet interior is divided into two by a cascade board, and the low-frequency energy increased in the lower space is released from the bass reflex port.
In addition, parallel surfaces have been eliminated from the front and back of the cabinet to suppress low waves.
In addition, Calamatsu plywood with 18 mm thickness is used for the entire baffle.

Equipped with a wall mounting embedded nut on the back, it can also be installed on the wall surface.
