Commentary

A floor type speaker system with a modern design.

LE14A, 36 cm cone type woofer with 10 cm φ voice coil, is installed in the low range.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter, LE20-1, is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure features a smoked glass top plate design, and the front grill comes in three color variations, with a choice of coffee, mandarin or indigo.
