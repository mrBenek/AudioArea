Commentary

A floor-type speaker system using a large enclosure.

The Laboratory Series 40 cm cone type woofer L475 is mounted in the low range.

The mid-range is equipped with a horn type mid-range using a radial horn H325 made of Yonematsu wood and a driver unit D252.

A horn type tweeter T925 is installed in the high range.

3-way N31 is used for the network part.

A bass-reflex enclosure made of Yonematsu wood is used for the enclosure.
