Commentary

Sound Power Series 2-way speaker system.

It is suitable for sound reinforcement and full-scale music playback in medium-sized and larger halls.

Two 30-cm cone type woofer 2206H are installed in the low range.
In addition, a horn type unit that combines a compression driver 2445J with a 10 cm diameter titanium diaphragm and a flat front biradial horn 2380A with a 5 cm throat is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Bi-amplifier drive system is adopted.
