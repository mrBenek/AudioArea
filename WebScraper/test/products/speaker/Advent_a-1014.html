Commentary

High-efficiency 2-way speaker system with compact design.

A 16.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Aluminum voice coil is used for voice coil to improve power handling.

A 1.3 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
A polycarbonate dome diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and the ferrofluid method increases sound dispersion and expands stereo feeling.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
