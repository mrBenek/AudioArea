  • Pioneer CS-700

  • ¥ 33,000 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

3-way bookshelf speaker system.

It has a 30 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 12 cm cone midrange in the middle range.
A FB cone is used for the diaphragm of these units.

A multi-cellar horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Equipped with 3-step switching type level control.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a walnut oil finish on the exterior.

In addition to the normal input terminal, a multi-amplifier terminal is mounted on the rear, and multi-amplifier playback is possible by switching the switch.
