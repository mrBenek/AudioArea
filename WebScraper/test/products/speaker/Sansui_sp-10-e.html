Commentary

Compact full-range speaker system.
It is also designed for the rear of 4-channel stereo.

The unit is equipped with a specially designed and developed 16 cm cone type full range speaker.
We seek to improve the overall balance by reducing intermodulation distortion, which tends to be a problem.

The enclosure adopts a bass reflex system using a pipe duct.
The surface has a walnut open pore finish.
