Commentary

3-way speaker system.

Using a unique method of mixing carbon fiber evenly vertically and horizontally in the woofer, 25 cm woofer is used, which is more complete as corn paper.
In addition, non-resonance frame made of aluminum die-cast is adopted for the frame. The maximum thickness is 12 mm. Even the rib portion has a thickness of 5 mm.
In addition, a large damper is used for the damper with an emphasis on linearity at large amplitude, and a urethane coated cloth material is used for the edge.

Carbon fiber cone paper is used for the mid-range as well as the woofer.
Since the f. of the unit is 200 Hz and the high-frequency playback limit is 12000 Hz, there is enough room for the system's bandwidth of 850 Hz to 5800 Hz.

The tweeter adopts a dome type unit using ultra-hard titanium for the diaphragm, and a special filler is applied to the edge.

The enclosure combines high-density particle board and multi-layered plywood to disperse resonance modes.
In addition, various types of sound absorbing materials are combined to improve the effect, preventing standing waves and damping low frequencies without reducing the internal volume.

Metallized paper capacitor and air-core coils are used for the network.
