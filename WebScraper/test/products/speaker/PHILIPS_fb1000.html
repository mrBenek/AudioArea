Commentary

A toll-boy speaker system.

It is equipped with a cone-shaped woofer in the low range and a cone-shaped midbus unit in the middle and low range.
Alnico magnetic circuit with double short ring is adopted for these units.

ISO PHASE transducers, originally developed by Philips, are installed in the mid - and high-frequency ranges. The ultra-lightweight vibration system realizes wide-range reproduction and flat frequency characteristics that cannot be achieved with conventional systems.

The connection terminal is for bi-wiring, and LF and HF are separated to prevent mutual interference.

The enclosure uses MDF with superior vibration damping characteristics and machining accuracy, and the configuration of the enclosure is designed using computer analysis.

The cone tip of the Monitor PC is included and can be set at 4 or 3 points on the stand.
