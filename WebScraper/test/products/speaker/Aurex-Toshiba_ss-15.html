  • TOSHIBA SS-15

  • ¥ 10,000 (1 unit, around 1960 s)

Commentary

A sealed two way speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The sound quality is improved by using a newly developed material and free edge developed by special molding technology, and a newly designed long voice coil.

A 8 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The network is selected through listening tests conducted by audio consultants and the general public.
In addition, the speakers are acoustically isolated to suppress interference distortion between them.

Equipped with 2-step switching type level control on the back.
