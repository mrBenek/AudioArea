Commentary

An exclusive unit speaker system in pursuit of musical quality.

model3301 is a combination of woofer system EW-301 and mid-high system MT-12, and the orientation can be adjusted by changing the position of MT-12.

The woofer system EW-301 is equipped with a 30 cm cone woofer.
Transient is improved by cabinet shape considering diffraction effect and adoption of tough cone paper. In addition, it is equipped with a multi-channel terminal for multi-amplifier drive.
The EW-301 has a built-in low-cut network dedicated to the MT-12.

The mid-high system MT-12 is equipped with a 12 cm coaxial unit.
This unit uses a ring radiator in the middle section to improve power linearity, and a duralumin diaphragm and a 120 ° wide-angle cervical horn in the high section.
The enclosure has a dual structure using lauan plywood and low-Q high-density materials.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
By installing this stand, the diffraction effect can be processed and a clear low range can be obtained.
