Commentary

This floor-type speaker system is a column type that does not take up space and realizes 180 ° non-directional.

There are 2 high compliance type 16 cm cone type woofers in the low range.
In the Radian-III, a slit is provided in front of these units to make the wave surface spherical and improve directional characteristics.

Two 8 cm cone type tweeters are installed in the high range.
These units are mounted at an angle of 45 ° to compensate for the directional characteristics of each speaker and improve the characteristics.

It is equipped with level control for high sound.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
There are two variations of the external appearance, walnut finish and sen finish.
