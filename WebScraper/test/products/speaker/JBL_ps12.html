Commentary

The PS Series speaker system was developed with JBL's professional speaker technology in consideration of a wide range of applications.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range, and a biradial horn and soft dome type tweeter are mounted in the high range for high efficiency design.

The enclosure has a wedge-shaped design.
In addition, it uses a large carrying handle and a bunched metal front grill with the JBL logo mark.
