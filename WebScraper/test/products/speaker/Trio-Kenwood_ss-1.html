  • TRIO SS-1

  • ¥ 9,200 (1 unit, around mid 1960 s)

Commentary

Thin 2-way speaker system.

It is equipped with a 16 cm cone type woofer and a 6.5 cm cone type tweeter used as a monitor of broadcasting stations.

By adopting TRIO's unique folded duct system, low sound reproduction is improved.

There are 2 types of designs, and the attached stand can be installed vertically or horizontally freely.
