  • CANTON Karat60

  • Price unknown (1 unit, around 1989?)

Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system incorporating the latest speaker technology into the Carat series.

A 26 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range to improve the sound quality by improving the magnetic circuit and diaphragm suspension and coating.

Equipped with 12 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using a titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high range.

There were 2 variations of the exterior, mahogany and walnut.
