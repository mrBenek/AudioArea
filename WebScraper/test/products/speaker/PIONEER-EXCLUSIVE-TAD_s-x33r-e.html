  • ¥ 39,800 (a set of two cars, around 1992)

Commentary

A small speaker system developed using directional control technology to enable easy surround viewing.

3 full-range units of 8.7 cm are arranged in a row in order to obtain sufficient spaciousness.
A directional control unit is used for the center speaker in contrast to the two side speakers. The directional characteristics are controlled by a phase shift of 180 ゜, and heart-shaped directional characteristics are realized in the middle and high frequency range, which is related to the sense of direction, and a sound field with a sense of spaciousness is realized.
