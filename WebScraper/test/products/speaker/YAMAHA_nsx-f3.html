  • YAMAHA NSX-F3

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1992)

Commentary

This is a small-size effect speaker system that inherits the quality design of the NSX-F1 and reduces the size.

Equipped with a 12 cm cone type woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter in the high range, it is a 2-way bass reflex configuration.

The design is exactly the same as the NSX-F1.
Since the area of the baffle surface is small, the visual impression is small compared to the internal volume. It is harmonized with the interior by combining with the calm color tone.

Mounting brackets for various types of speaker Plaquette are mounted on the rear of the enclosure.
