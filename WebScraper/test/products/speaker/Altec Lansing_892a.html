Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system developed as part of the new bookshelf series.

A 25 cm cone woofer of 406 to 8 c is used for the low range.
In addition, a horn type unit is mounted in the high range, and a newly developed vertically long aluminum die-cast exponential horn and characteristic compression driver are used.

The cabinet uses a sealed system.
