  • Technics SB-5000

  • ¥ 32,800 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

A speaker system that achieves flattening of sound pressure frequency characteristics and phase frequency characteristics by using a wide-band, low-distortion speaker unit, a network that takes into consideration the characteristics of the unit, and an appropriate arrangement of the unit.

In addition to the high-efficiency edgeless tweeter, a 25 cm woofer is driven by a bass reflex cabinet, achieving a high efficiency of 93.5dB/W.
