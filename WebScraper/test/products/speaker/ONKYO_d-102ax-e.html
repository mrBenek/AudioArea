Audio Database

  • ONKYO D-102AX

  • ¥ 48,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

102 series speaker system with real wood finish.

It has a 13 cm duocroscone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type with MDF material.
In addition, it has a lustrous finish with real wood.

A screw-type speaker terminal is used.

The speaker cable XP made by Monster is included as an accessory.
