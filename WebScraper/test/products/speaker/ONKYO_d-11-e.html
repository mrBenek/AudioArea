  • ONKYO D-11

  • ¥ 29,800 (1 unit, around 1986)

Commentary

An AV speaker system with a 3-way unit in a space-friendly style and complete anti-magnetic measures.

It uses a 22 cm cone type unit with a delta olefin refined diaphragm with sufficient strength and less material specific sound in the low range.

A 2.5 cm dome type unit with a diaphragm made of super-hard titanium is used for the high-frequency range.
A 18,200 gauss magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

A 1.2 cm ceramic type unit is used for the ultra-high band.
