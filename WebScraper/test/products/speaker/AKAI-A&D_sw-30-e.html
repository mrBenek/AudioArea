Commentary

This is a small speaker system that uses a unique backload system called the Jet Stream System to improve the low-frequency range.

High compliance type 10 cm unit is adopted for the unit.

In order to make the most effective use of the space, four boards are installed, and the path through the bass is lengthened, and the rich bass is reproduced successfully.
