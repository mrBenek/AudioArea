Commentary

THX certified HTS System-10 dual-drive subwoofer.

The unit uses 2 25 cm cone type woofers.
The same unit is mounted opposite to each other, and a dual drive system is adopted to offset the internal vibration when the diaphragm moves.

The enclosure uses MDF.
