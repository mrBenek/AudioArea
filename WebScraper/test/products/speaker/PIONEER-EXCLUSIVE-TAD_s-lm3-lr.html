  • Pioneer S-LM3-LR

  • ¥ 40,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in September 2001)

Commentary

Slim design tall boy speaker system.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.

A 2 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

A vertical twin system is adopted for the unit layout, and by adopting a layout in which the woofer is arranged vertically symmetrically with the tweeter at the center, the sound of all band is radiated around the tweeter, so that rich sound field feeling and clear sound image localization can be obtained.
