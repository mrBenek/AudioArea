  • Pioneer S-L5

  • ¥ 23,000 (1 unit, around 1985)

Commentary

A 2-way speaker system that can be set freely.

A 20 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 7.7 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.
These units are provided with magnetic shielding to reduce the influence on cathode ray tubes, etc.

The tweeter is equipped with a rotatable movable flange, and the angle can be changed up to 45 ° to the left and right with 15 ° click stops. Moreover, it can be used for various settings such as 170 ° rotation in the circumferential direction of the tweeter.
This method can improve sound localization and live feeling.

The automatic return type breaker is equipped on the tweeter, and in case of unexpected abnormal input, the input can be cut off automatically and then return.

The enclosure has a black metallic finish.

As an optional part sold separately, it can be freely installed by using both ceiling and wall hanging speaker bracket.
