Commentary

This compact speaker system is easy to use and can be installed anywhere.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone shaped woofer.
Lightweight cone paper is used for the diaphragm in order to realize wide-band and flat characteristics. The cone shape is a カーブドコーン to remove the high-frequency peak, and a special Mylar film is used for the center cap to extend the high-frequency.
In addition, it is an AV-compatible magnetic shielding design based on EIAJ, so it does not have any effect when installed near a monitor or TV.

Uses a special polypropylene cabinet that maintains the required strength without adversely affecting sound.
A silent duct is used for the bass reflex duct to suppress the generation of unnecessary noise caused by air coming in and out.

There is a vertically and horizontally adjustable wall mounting hole in the back, and various installation is possible by using accessories sold separately.
