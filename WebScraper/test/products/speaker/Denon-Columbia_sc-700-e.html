  • DENON SC-700 Granada

  • ¥ 90,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1989)

Commentary

A speaker system developed for enjoying music freely with the theme of Renaissance in components.

An 18 cm cone woofer is mounted in the low range, and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter with a wide angle of directivity expanded by a new frame shape is adopted in the high range.
New Alpha Boron is used for the vibration plate of the tweeter, and neodymium magnet with magnetic force about 8 times that of ferrite is used for the magnet of each unit.

Walnut solid material is used in the front of the top plate of the enclosure to obtain the excellent vibration absorption effect of pure wood, and natural cork with soft texture is used for the baffle surface.

It also has a bi-wiring terminal for bi-wiring connection.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
