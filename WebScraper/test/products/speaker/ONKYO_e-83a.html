  • ONKYO E-83A

  • ¥ 41,500 (1 unit, released in 1968)

Commentary

This speaker system is the first model of the E-83A series.

At that time, E-83A, E-154A and E-22A were in one series, and appeared as an intermediate machine of the series.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a horn type unit is installed in each of the middle band and high band.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
