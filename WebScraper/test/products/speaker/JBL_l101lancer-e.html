Commentary

A floor speaker system with a marble top plate.

The LE14A 35.5 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range, and the LE14DLH horn tweeter is installed in the high range. 175
LX10 is used for the network.

By using marble for the top plate of the enclosure, we have succeeded in greatly reducing the lowest resonance frequency.
In addition, there is a hand carved lattice on the front grill.
