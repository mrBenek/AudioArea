  • DALI DALI-6

  • ¥ 240,000 (a set of two cars, around 1986)

Commentary

A toll-boy speaker system.

A 25 cm cone woofer for the low base and a 20.3 cm cone woofer for the upper base are installed in the low base.
These units perform the same operation in the low frequency band below 1.5 kHz, and the upper base woofer covers up to the follower point of the tweeter.

A 2.8 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The exterior is finished with a Danish beach finish, and has a unique design with the DALI logo engraved.
