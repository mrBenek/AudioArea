Commentary

JBL's CONTROL series speaker system.

A 16.5 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
The vibration plate is reinforced by laminating special film on SSP (super sulfite pulp), and the voice coil is made of copper-coated aluminum wire CCAW (copper-clad aluminum wire) and aluminum voice coil bobbin to improve piston motion and allowable input.

A dome type tweeter with a pure titanium diaphragm is used for the high range.
In order to obtain more pure sound quality, it is equipped with a urethane foam damper that suppresses the second harmonic distortion.

Each unit uses a cancel magnet and shield cover for thorough anti-magnetic treatment.

The enclosure is made of polypropylene with special foam material to ensure adequate internal loss while minimizing resonance.
In addition, a protection circuit is built into the network section to provide high resolution with carefully selected parts while protecting the unit from high instantaneous input.

There are 4 color variations of black, gray, medium gray and white.

Available separately for a wide range of mounting options, including adjustable wall and ceiling brackets, adapters for 19-inch racks, and adapters for setting on JBL's MT4612 stand or camera stand.
