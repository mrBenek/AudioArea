  • DENON SC-400 Firenze

  • ¥ 60,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1989)

Commentary

A speaker system of the Renaissance series that reproduces the deep taste of music with natural sound.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Natural pulp corn and neodymium magnet are used in this unit.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The new frame shape provides clear sound localization and a wide listening area of 120 ° up / down / left / right.

Natural cork is used for the front baffle of the enclosure, and natural wool is also used for the sound absorbing material to acquire natural sound.
