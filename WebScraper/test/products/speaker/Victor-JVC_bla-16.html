  • VICTOR/JVC BLA-16

  • ¥ 39,000 (1 unit, around the early 1960 s)

Commentary

A speaker system employing a non-directional unit.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The mid-range is equipped with a 16 cm skoker equipped with a wooden deflector, and the high-range is equipped with a 5 cm tweeter equipped with a metal deflector.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type.
