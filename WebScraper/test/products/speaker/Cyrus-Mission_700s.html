  • MISSION 700S

  • ¥ 128,000 (a set of two cars, around 1983)

Commentary

This speaker system is the successor to the Mission700.

A 21 cm cone woofer with a carbon fiber diaphragm is mounted in the low range.
A 1.9 cm dome-shaped tweeter with a polymer diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

The terminal part is designed to be connected by 4 mm plug or wire.

There are 2 color variations, black and walnut.

The front grille cannot be removed.
