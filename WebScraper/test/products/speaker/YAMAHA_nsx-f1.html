Commentary

This effect speaker system was developed to fit into the interior of a movie without disturbing the viewer visually.

A 16 cm cone type woofer using pulp cone is used for the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.
By adopting a bass reflex design for the enclosure, natural sound quality is achieved over all bands, and sound reproduction with good response is achieved by using a lightweight diaphragm.

The enclosure has a trapezoidal form with a small baffle surface area that gives a smaller visual impression compared to the internal volume.
In addition, there are 2 color variations of ivory white and shadow gray. The calm color tone harmonizes with the interior and reduces the presence.

Mounting part of speaker bracket mounting bracket is mounted on the rear.
