  • ONKYO D-80

  • ¥ 11,000 (1 unit, sold on November 1, 1998)

Commentary

A coaxial speaker system developed for surround sound reproduction.

Two 8 cm cone woofers are installed in the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter is installed in the high range.

All MDF cabinet is used for the enclosure.

Equipped with screw holes corresponding to 60 mm pitch mounting brackets.
