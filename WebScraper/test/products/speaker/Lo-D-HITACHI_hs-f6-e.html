Commentary

A 3-way speaker system with a passive filter.

A 22 cm woofer with SCP (Silicate Carbon blended Polymer) cone is used for the low range.
In addition, 6.5 cm and 4 cm units are used for the middle and high bands, respectively.

It has an anti-magnetic design and uses a round baffle in the enclosure.
