Commentary

This bookshelf type speaker system was developed by inheriting studio monitor technology.

A 25 cm cone type woofer LE111A is mounted in the low range.
This unit has a unique compound coating on the underside of the cone paper to provide robustness and moderate internal losses, resulting in optimum mass, high efficiency and low sound reproduction.
The voice coil uses a 7.6 cm diameter copper ribbon edgewise winding, which increases the number of windings compared to conventional voice coils, resulting in improved efficiency, transient response, and input resistance. The magnetic circuit uses an Alnico V magnet to narrow the gap and concentrate the magnetic force on the voice coil.
This woofer is responsible for frequencies below 1 kHz, but in reality, it has a smooth response even in frequencies above 3 kHz, making the connection with the Skoker natural.

13 cm cone type Skoka LE5-10 is mounted in the middle area.
The precision design 2.2 cm diameter voice coil and high magnetic flux density magnetic circuit improve the efficiency and transient characteristics in the mid-frequency range. The hard material used for the cone paper, which emits sound, prevents deformation at the maximum instantaneous input. The aluminum center dome gives the Skokcar itself the ability to reproduce up to the high frequency band and improves the connection with the tweeter.
In addition, the internal volume is calculated in consideration of the sound connection with the woofer, and mounted in a completely sealed back cavity filled with an appropriate amount of sound absorbing material to prevent interference with the woofer.

It is equipped with a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter 033 capable of high-frequency reproduction of 30 kHz or more.
For the dome part, two sheets of linen cloth impregnated with phenol resin are bonded together so that the weaves intersect at an angle of 45 ° for weight reduction and durability. In addition, the voice coil is 2.5 cm, the same diameter as the dome, and it is large for high sound, so that excellent transient response and input resistance characteristics are obtained.
In addition, the dome is securely fixed by a unique clamping ring to prevent external resonance.

The network section is equipped with state-of-the-art impedance correction and phase correction circuits, as well as high-quality parts such as air-core inductors, capacitors with superior characteristics, and resistors.

It is equipped with continuously variable level control for middle and high frequencies.

High-density compression agent with excellent plate vibration is used for the enclosure, and especially thick 25 mm plate is used for the baffle plate to prevent distortion of sound image at high output. Sufficient sound absorbing material is filled inside to absorb standing wave.
The speaker units are mounted in a straight line, avoiding the center, so that the connection between the units is smooth and sound image localization is improved.

The exterior has a walnut finish.
