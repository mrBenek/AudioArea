Commentary

This compact speaker system further enhances the sound quality achieved with Control 1.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of newly developed SuperBKP with a special reinforcement coating on a pulp base, and the voice coil is made of a long type of pure aluminum wire for high sound quality.

A dome type tweeter using a pure titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high range.

As a more thorough anti-magnetic measure, a cancel magnet and a shield cover are used for both units.

The enclosure is molded with polypropylene with special foam material to ensure adequate internal loss while suppressing resonance.

A wide range of settings is possible with one touch mounting brackets, wall / ceiling brackets, desk brackets, etc.
Also, by using a dedicated adapter, it can be set in combination with a desk stand or a commercial camera stand.

The Control3pro comes in two color variations : black and gray.
Also, there was a professional grill with a large JBL logo designed exclusively for Control1/Control3pro sold separately.
