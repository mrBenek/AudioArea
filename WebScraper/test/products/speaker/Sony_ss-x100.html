  • SONY SS-X100

  • ¥ 21,000 (1 unit, around 1981)

Commentary

A compact speaker system using a zinc die-cast enclosure.

A 12 cm balanced drive woofer is used for the low range and a ribbon type tweeter is used for the high range.

The enclosure is made of zinc die cast to reduce box rumbling.
It is the same height and depth as Sony's New York RT-66/RT-44 Receiver Deck.
