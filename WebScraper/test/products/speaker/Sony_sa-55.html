Commentary

Compact speaker system with built-in power amplifier.

The unit is equipped with a 9 cm full range cone type unit.

Built-in 5W + 5W power amplifier.
This power amplifier has a REC output terminal, so if you connect a deck to this terminal, you can record while monitoring the input signal.

The size is the same as the playback 1/2 deck TC-PB5, and the design is unified, so you can enjoy the combination use.
