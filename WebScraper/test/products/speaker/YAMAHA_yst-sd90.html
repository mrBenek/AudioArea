Commentary

A speaker system developed by YST (Yamaha Active Servo Technology).
It is a set configuration of YST-SDS90 for the speaker part and YST-A90DC for the amplifier part, and it was a limited product of 200 sets in the world.

The YST system uses a negative resistance circuit to create a powerful voice coil with virtually 0 impedance, and fully drives the Helmholtz resonator to realize an air woofer in which the air itself functions as a diaphragm.
As a result, high-quality and powerful bass playback is realized regardless of the size or design of the speaker.

In the YST-SD90, the YST system is realized in non-directional mode, and radiation is emitted in the whole circumference direction at 360 ℃.

The speaker section consists of three 10 cm PP cone type skokas and three 3 cm dome type tweeters, each of which is handmade.
