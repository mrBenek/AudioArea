  • YAMAHA AST-F100

  • ¥ 65,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1989)

Commentary

A wall-mounted thin speaker system that uses active servo technology to provide bass playback from the port.

While an air woofer with a small-diameter port, the system vibrates the air itself inside the enclosure. The air itself functions as a diaphragm and realizes low sound reproduction from 25 Hz.

Four 10 cm PP cone skokers are used for the middle range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.
The unit is anti-magnetic and does not adversely affect the screen on the TV side.

By using the special stand sold separately, it is also possible to place on the floor.

A dedicated amplifier is required for use.
