Audio Database

  • VICTOR/JVC BLA-Z6

  • ¥ 16,000 (a set of two cars, around 1971)

Commentary

A small speaker system developed for the rear of a 4-channel system.

A free edge type 16 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 7 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is sealed and has wall mounting brackets on the back.
The front connector RUNNET is removable.
