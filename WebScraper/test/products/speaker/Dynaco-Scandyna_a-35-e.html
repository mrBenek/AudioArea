Commentary

A newly developed bookshelf speaker system based on the A-25XS speaker system.

It is equipped with a 25.4 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.7 cm dome tweeter in the high range.

Equipped with 5-step switching type level control.

The enclosure uses a sealed system called Dynaco's unique dual-spectrum damping system.
In this system, the inside of the enclosure is divided into two parts by a partition plate with a small slit, and the low-frequency response is improved by applying moderate braking to the woofer while suppressing the internal standing wave.
