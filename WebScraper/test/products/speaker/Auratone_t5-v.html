Commentary

Aura Tone 2-way bookshelf speaker system.

The low range has a 12.5 cm cone woofer, and the high range has a dome tweeter.
By adopting a magnetic shielding covering the magnetic circuit, it can be set more freely.

The enclosure is sealed and has a black satin exterior finish.
