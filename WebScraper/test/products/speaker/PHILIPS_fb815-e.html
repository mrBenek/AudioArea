Commentary

Conductor Series 8 3-way speaker system.

The Conductor Series 8 is a selected model of the speaker system researched and developed at the laboratory in Eindhoven, Netherlands. It was developed with Philips advanced technology and high-quality materials.

It is equipped with a cone type unit of 20 cm in the low range and 10 cm in the middle range.
The cone is soft coated with Philips unique double doping to reduce split resonance.
The Woofer has a long voice coil and a strong magnetic circuit to achieve high linearity.

A 1.8 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is made of スプロニュル, which has excellent internal damping, and is cooled by magnetic fluid containing fine metallic particles to improve input resistance.

The enclosure improves horizontal directivity by placing all units in a straight line.
The network section is designed using CAD/CAM technology.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
