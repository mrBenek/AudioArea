Commentary

New Line Series 3-way speaker system.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The mid-range is equipped with a 16 cm cone scoker, and the high-range is equipped with a 12 cm cone tweeter.

The enclosure is finished with handmade oak oil stain finish, and the grill is a deeply carved three dimensional knitted fabric.
