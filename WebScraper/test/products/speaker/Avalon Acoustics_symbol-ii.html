  • AVALON Symbol-II

  • ¥ 619,500 (1 set of 2, released in 2003)

Commentary

A speaker system designed for two channel stereo playback.

Two 12.7 cm cone type woofers with ceramic coated cone are installed in the lower range.
In addition, a 2.5 cm inverted dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Symbol-II is designed to produce a uniform radiation pattern in the audio band, keeping in mind that the radiation pattern is an important determinant of sound stage reproducibility.
In addition, with a unique port design, one port is placed on the bottom and one port is placed for the rear high-frequency band to obtain a soft and glossy sound quality.
