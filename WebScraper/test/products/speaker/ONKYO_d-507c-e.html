Commentary

Center speaker of D-507 series.

Two 12 cm cone type two 12 cm cone type woofers are mounted using OMF diaphragm which realizes light weight, high rigidity and high internal loss.
This unit uses an integrally molded S-line edge that reduces the reflection of propagating waves by organically joining the diaphragm.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

MDF is used for the enclosure, and a virtual coaxial layout with excellent sense of localization of sound images such as dialogue is adopted.
Also, by setting it upside down, it is possible to set it upward by 6 degrees.
Finished with a silver hearts real wood veneer finish.

A screw-type terminal for banana plug is used for the input terminal.
