Commentary

The MC series speaker system was developed not only for the rich musical expression of stereo playback but also for the faithful sound of theater sound.
It uses the same units and network as the other MC series, and is configured for the center speaker.

Two 16 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
The cone contains 30% white mica, and uses PMD (Polymer Injected Mica Diaphargm) cone which realizes ideal characteristics with light weight and high rigidity.
In addition, a catenary curve reproduced by injection molding is used for the cone shape, and the material is not colored at all so that the original taste of the material can be brought out.
The magnetic circuit uses a 90 mm diameter strontium ferrite magnet, Kapton bobbin, voice coil, high linearity suspension, etc.

A 3 cm silk dome tweeter is installed in the high range.
Uncolored silk material is used for the diaphragm, and strontium ferrite magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

The enclosure is entirely made of MDF material, and the baffle surface is finished with urethane coating to give a natural sound.

The input terminal uses a gold plated terminal that can be used with a banana plug and is compatible with bi-wiring connection.

There is a special speaker stand and speaker bracket which are sold separately, so you can set up more freely.
