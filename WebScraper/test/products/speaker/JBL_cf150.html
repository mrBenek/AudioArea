Commentary

This is a CF series floor-type speaker system that allows you to enjoy high efficiency and open sound.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
High-polymer laminated cone is used for the diaphragm.

The mid-range is equipped with 16 cm cone type mid-range.
The diaphragm uses the same high polymer laminate cone as the woofer, and the connection of the sound with the low range is smooth.

A 1.4 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
A mylar laminate diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure uses a rear bass reflex system.

A binding post-type banana plug-compatible speaker terminal is used for extremely thick speaker cables.

Built-in protection fuse to protect the unit in case of excessive input.
