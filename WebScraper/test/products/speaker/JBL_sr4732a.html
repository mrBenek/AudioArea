Commentary

SR series 3-way speaker system.

It can be used in a wide range of applications such as main speaker systems in small and medium halls, side fills in PA systems, main in dance clubs, and playback monitors in rehearsal studios.

The SR4732A is a model with built-in network and equipped with bi-amp-driven changeover switch.
The SR4732A-T is a tri-amp exclusive model, and the use of channel divider M553 is recommended.

Two 30 cm cone type woofer 2206H are installed in the low range.
The middle area is equipped with a horn type unit that combines a horn 2381 with a 90 ° horizontal and 50 ° vertical pointing characteristic and a driver unit 2447J.
In addition, a horn type tweeter 2404H is installed in the high frequency range.
