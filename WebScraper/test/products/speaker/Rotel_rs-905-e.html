Commentary

This speaker system is based on the theme of high-fidelity reproduction and has been created through repeated hard listening tests.

A 25 cm cone type woofer of roll edge type is installed in the low range.

A 9 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure has a walnut finish.
