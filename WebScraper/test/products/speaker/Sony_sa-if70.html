  • SONY SA-IF70

Commentary

Wireless active speakers developed for surround rear.

Infrared transmission and an active speaker with a built-in amplifier for rear channel playback eliminates the need for cumbersome wiring.
In combination with the AV amplifier TA-V909/TA-V606, the dedicated terminal of the amplifier can be directly connected to the attached transmitter.

When combined with a normal AV amplifier, it is connected from the speaker terminal to the transmitter via the separately sold input adapter IAD-IF70.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full range speaker.

Transmitter is included.
This transmitter is equipped with a 60 mm pitch mounting screw hole.
