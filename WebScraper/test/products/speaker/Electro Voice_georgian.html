Commentary

This is a floor-type speaker system that inherits the qualities of Patrician II and is finished with a European design.

It has a 45 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 30 cm cone woofer in the middle range.

A horn type tweeter combining a 5 cm driver unit and a CD director is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is designed using computer analysis based on Teal & Small theory for high performance.
The exterior is finished with walnut material and the baffle surface is finished with beige nextel.
