Commentary

This compact speaker system incorporates the style of the JBL L88 speaker released in the 1970 s.

A 13.3 cm cone type woofer using white paper cone is mounted in the low range.
An accordion pleated edge is used for the edge to increase the amplitude linearity of the cone, and an aluminum die cast frame is used for the frame.

A 1.9 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm uses a lipless pure titanium dome diaphragm made of lightweight and highly rigid titanium molded into a dome shape with no metal stress.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.
In addition, a symmetrical design is adopted, so that the left and right pair can be used freely, such as installing the tweeter outside to obtain a wide sound field and installing the tweeter inside to concentrate sound sources.

It uses an anti-magnetic design that has little effect on the television screen.
In addition, it can be mounted on walls or ceilings by using a mount Plaquette sold separately.
