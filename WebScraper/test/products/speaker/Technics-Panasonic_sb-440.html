  • Technics SB-440

  • ¥ 42,800 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

3-way speaker system with multi-seller horn.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit achieves high efficiency by adopting EVA edge, and linearity at large input is improved by long travel voice coil.
In addition, the front of the woofer is equipped with a punching net which serves both as the defense of the unit and high-frequency braking.

The middle area is equipped with a multi-seller horn made of aluminum die-cast.

A 6.5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

It is equipped with level control for middle and high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
