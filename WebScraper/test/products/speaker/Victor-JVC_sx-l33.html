Commentary

A speaker system that employs an oblique cone unit.

Two 14.5 cm oblicorn type woofers are installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of aluminum, and the drive point is placed off-center in the shape of the dynamic balanced oblique cone system that disperses the mid-range resonance. This shape greatly improves the natural resonance generated by the aluminum diaphragm, and realizes more natural and smooth characteristics.
In addition, a spider suspension with a clear vibration reference point and good tracking performance is adopted.
Response is improved by using foam rubber edge for the edge part.

A 1.9 cm hard-domed tweeter is installed in the high range.
A pure gold-plated aluminum dome is used for the diaphragm, and directivity is improved with a short horn.
An internal magnetic circuit using neodymium magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

Aluminum die-cast frames are used for the frames of the woofer and tweeter.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

The speaker stand and wall mounting bracket were sold separately as options.
