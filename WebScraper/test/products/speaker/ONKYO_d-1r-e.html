  • ONKYO D-1R

  • ¥ 29,800 (1 unit, around 1984)

Commentary

Delta Olefin Refined is used for the diaphragm of the woofer, and the magnetic circuit and driving force are also strengthened.

The high and ultra-high frequencies are shared by two units to achieve playback up to 30000 Hz.

The Super Tweeter is made of ultra-thin polyester film with high precision, and a newly designed diffuser is installed together with the tweeter to improve the high-frequency characteristics.
