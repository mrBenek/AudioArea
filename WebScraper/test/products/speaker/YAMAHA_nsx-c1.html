Commentary

A speaker system developed and tuned specifically for surround center speakers.
It is designed considering the energy balance and texture of the whole band as well as the enhancement of middle and low band which is the centrodesmus area of the voice.

Two 18 cm cone type woofers with straight type pulp cone are installed in the lower range.
By using the bass reflex type, we focus on playing back the low frequency range around 250 Hz to 1.3 kHz, which is often found in male voices.

Soft dome type units of 6 cm and 2.5 cm respectively are adopted for middle and high areas.

It takes into consideration the setting as a center speaker, and adopts a horizontal design.
The high-density particle-particle enclosure, with a 20 mm baffle and 15 mm thickness, features a matte black exterior finish to prevent unwanted light reflections.
