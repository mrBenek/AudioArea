  • Pioneer S-33twin

  • ¥ 45,600 (1 set of 2, released in 1989)

Commentary

A speaker system that employs a vertical twin that can be installed in a variety of ways.

It uses a vertical twin system with a tweeter at the center and a woofer at the top and bottom.
Since the acoustic energy of the two woofers coincides with the axis of the tweeter, a rich sound field is reproduced and sound image localization is realized.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are used for the low range, and a 2-cm dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

Slant baffle is used to enable more aggressive use.
This is a 15 ° inclined baffle on which the speaker unit is mounted. This slant baffle allows you to enjoy a wide range of installation in addition to the normal installation.

The enclosure is sealed with a round top and base plate as well as left and right sides.
Also, the finish is 6-side finish.

The front net cannot be removed.
