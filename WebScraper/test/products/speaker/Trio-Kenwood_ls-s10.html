Commentary

A speaker system developed to easily enjoy music data from DAP or PC.

An 8 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of multi-layer coating material that reduces unnecessary vibration, and the cap is made of polyurethane material with high internal loss and excellent strain reduction, reducing strain.
In addition, a square voice coil with a high magnetic flux density is adopted for the voice coil that drives the unit to improve the tracking performance against input signals.

A 1.9 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
By placing sound-absorbing material inside the tweeter, smooth high-frequency characteristics without specific peaks are obtained.

MDF material is used for the enclosure.

Gold plated speaker terminal for banana plug is adopted.
