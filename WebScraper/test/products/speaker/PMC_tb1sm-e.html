Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system that employs a transmission line system.

Besides the normal bookshelf type, there was a model for center speaker.
The center model has -C added at the end of the model number.

A 17 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Cast magnesium alloy is used in this unit.

A dome type tweeter using aluminum alloy is mounted in the high range.

Anti-magnetic design is adopted.

Bi-wiring is supported.

The enclosure uses a transmission line system to enhance low-frequency reproduction capability.

TB1SM and TB1SM-C and TB1SM-C, walnut finish for TB1M-AW, rosewood finish for TB1M-R, and black ash finish for TB1M-BA.
