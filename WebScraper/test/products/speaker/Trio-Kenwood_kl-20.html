  • TRIO KL-20

  • ¥ 11,800 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

A roll-free edge type 16 cm cone woofer is used for the low range, and a horn type with diffuser is used for the high range.

The enclosure has been reconsidered from the hearing test, and Lauan plywood is used for the baffle surface and Homogen material is used for other parts.
It can also be mounted on the wall.
