Commentary

A floor-type speaker system dedicated to being accurate in every sense.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range, and a radial horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The tweeter is equipped with a protection circuit with an indicator.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.

A SEQ equalizer is included.
