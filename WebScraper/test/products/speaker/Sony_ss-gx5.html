  • SONY SS-GX5

  • ¥ 50,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1989)

Commentary

An AV speaker system that thoroughly selects materials, structures, and sound quality design.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The midship drive woofer system is used to improve sound quality by placing the woofer in the center of the baffle plate to reduce the influence of the stationary wave.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using a titanium dome is mounted in the high frequency range.

Anti-magnetic design is applied.

It is made of a highly rigid marble composition material and uses a G-base frame that integrates the speaker frame and baffle plate.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
