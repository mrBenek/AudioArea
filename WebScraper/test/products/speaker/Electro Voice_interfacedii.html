Commentary

Interface : A top-of-the-line speaker system of the Interface Series, an improved version of D.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is housed in the bottom of an enclosure designed based on the "optimal phase inversion" theory.

The mid-range is equipped with a 16 cm cone mid-range, Bent Mid-range.
This unit is constructed by applying the "interface type optimum phase inversion system" of the woofer division, and expands the low-frequency range. In addition, the efficiency is improved by using the huge magnetic circuit equivalent to the woofer. This improves the quality of the mid-range with the crossover frequency which is one octave lower than the usual three way system.

A radial horn type tweeter that maintains a radiation angle of more than 110 degrees at 16 kHz is installed in the high frequency range.
An electronic protection circuit is added to this unit.

Interface : The DII can be bi-amped with 350 hz crossover.

SEQ equalizer is included.
