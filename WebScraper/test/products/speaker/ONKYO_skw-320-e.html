Commentary

A subwoofer system with a built-in amplifier that employs a dual drive system.

The 20 cm woofer unit is mounted on both sides and dual drives for deep bass playback.

Equipped with a Music / Movie switch, you can choose a playback mode according to the genre of hi-fi / theater.
