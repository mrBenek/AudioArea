  • ONKYO D-062AX

  • ¥ 21,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in October 1997)

Commentary

Speaker system using OMF diaphragm.

A 13 cm cone woofer with an OMF diaphragm is mounted in the low range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

A condenser made by German company WIMA is used in the network section.

The enclosure is designed with bass reflex and MDF materials are used for all six sides.

Speaker terminal for banana plug is adopted.
