  • Pioneer S-X50

  • ¥ 29,800 (1 unit, around 1980)

Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

A 21 cm cone woofer is used for the low range and a 4.5 cm cone tweeter is used for the high range.
Uses an aluminum die-cast frame with reduced resonance.

The enclosure uses a special resin composite material that is hard to cause resonance, and since it is a special material with low Q and large specific gravity, resonance is suppressed and resolution is improved.
In addition, in pursuit of realistic sound image localization, it is designed to match the position of sound source.
