Commentary

This is a compact speaker system developed with W & D technology in pursuit of vocal and realism.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
The diaphragm uses a non-press cone developed through joint research with German Kurt Muller, a specialized diaphragm manufacturer.

A 3.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The fine ceramic diaphragm used for the diaphragm has a structure in which a fine ceramic layer is formed by a special method on the surface of the aluminum base material to increase the rigidity without increasing the weight.
In addition, the tangential edge, which is resistant to large amplitude, realizes higher quality.

The enclosure uses a conifer high density particle board.
