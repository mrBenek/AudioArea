Commentary

This is an authentic 3-way speaker system that inherits the design philosophy of the D-77RX.

It uses a unit made of 27 cm bio black cone for the low range and 12 cm for the middle range.
Magnetic distortion reduction technology (MDCT) is applied to the magnetic circuit to realize sound with low distortion and high resolution.

A 2.5 cm aluminum magnesium alloy dome type unit is used for the high frequency band.

The enclosure uses a 30 mm thick softwood MDF for the front baffle that is hard and resonant.
In addition, for better sound, the structural strength is increased by soundbars and reinforcement materials, and unnecessary vibration is suppressed.

For speaker terminals, we use gold-plated brass machined terminals for banana plugs, and for networks, we use isolated wiring networks to prevent sound degradation caused by reciprocal induction between circuit blocks.
