Commentary

Active subwoofer ideal for combination with the 125 series.

The unit is equipped with 16 cm cone type woofer.

The A-YST (Advanced Yamaha Active Servo Technology) system is adopted.
Based on the concept of an air woofer that uses air itself as a diaphragm, this system enables low-frequency playback in a small enclosure that is equivalent to that of a large speaker.

QD-Bass technology is used. In this system, the woofer unit is mounted downward and the pyramidal diffuser plate on the bottom emits low-temperature energy efficiently in four horizontal directions. This allows for smooth frequency characteristics and free setting regardless of installation location.

Built-in 50W power amplifier.

Equipped with an auto standby function.

There are 2 color variations, cherry and black.
