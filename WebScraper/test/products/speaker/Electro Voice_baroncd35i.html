Commentary

A floor-type speaker system that uses EV technology with a European design.

The Baron CD35i achieves outstanding playback by introducing new technologies and new materials everywhere.

A 30.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The mid-range is equipped with 3.8 cm dome mid-range and MFCD director, and the high-range is equipped with 3.8 cm dome tweeter and HFCD director.

Equipped with level control.

The enclosure is designed using computer analysis based on Teal & Small theory for high performance.

The exterior comes in 2 color variations, walnut material finish and mahogany finish.
The walnut finish has a beige Nextel finish on the baffle surface, and the mahogany finish has a gray Nextel finish on the baffle surface.
