  • DYNACO A-40XL

  • ¥ 59,900 (1 unit, released in 1976)

Commentary

XL series sealed speaker system.

The unit is the same as the A-25XL.

A 25.4 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit uses a newly developed special diaphragm and improves compliance to improve sound loss and improve sound image localization.

It is equipped with a level control that can adjust the high frequency in 3 levels.

The enclosure is fully enclosed for acoustic characteristics and has a walnut finish on the exterior.
