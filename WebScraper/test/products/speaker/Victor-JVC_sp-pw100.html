  • VICTOR/JVC SP-PW100

  • ¥ 25,000 (1 unit, around 2001)

Commentary

Powered subwoofer with a unique cascade bass reflex system that absorbs low-frequency noise generated inside the cabinet.

The unit adopts a 17 cm cone type unit and uses a 6-layer wound voice coil.

Built-in 60W amplifier.
