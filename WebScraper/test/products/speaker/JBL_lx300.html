  • JBL LX300

  • ¥ 66,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1993)

Commentary

A 2-way speaker system in the LX series that allows you to enjoy JBL sound easily.

A newly developed 16.5 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range, and a 2.5 cm pure titanium dome type tweeter is mounted in the high range.

The front baffle of the enclosure is equipped with damping pads to provide clear sound without turbidity.
