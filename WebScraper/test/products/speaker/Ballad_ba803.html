Commentary

Speaker system using coaxial speaker unit made by ALTEC.

The unit is equipped with ALTEC Lansing 20 cm coaxial 2-way unit CD308-8A.
This unit has an acoustically isolated dome in the center to achieve a wide range of directional characteristics while playing back to high frequencies. In addition, it uses a rust-resistant speaker frame and high-humidity resistant special cone paper.
In addition, the speaker unit used for the Ba803 is based on pair characteristic management, processed to increase the clarity of the specific frequency band masked at the time of mounting to the cabinet, and specially tuned to suppress the vibration of the magnet.

The enclosure uses perch material produced in Finland, and is determined from over 100 different combinations of wood and thickness.
A slit type is used for the bass reflex port.
