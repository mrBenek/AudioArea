  • ALPINE/LUXMAN S-102

  • ¥ 20,000 (1 unit, released in 1985)

Commentary

This is a compact speaker system of the Brid series jointly developed by ALPINE and LUXMAN.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full range unit.
This unit uses an anti-magnetic magnet to prevent adverse effects such as color Mura-ya distortion on the monitor screen.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.

By using the separately sold desk side bracket and wall hanging type bracket, free layout is possible.
