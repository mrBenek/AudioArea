  • BOSE 301VM

  • ¥ 109,600 (2 units 1 set, sold in December 1984)

Commentary

A magnetic shielding type speaker system developed to compose an AV system with sound of 301 MM II.

A 20 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range, and two 7.5 cm cone tweeters are installed in the high range.

The use of a canceling magnet in the magnetic circuit prevents magnetic flux leakage without reducing performance.
