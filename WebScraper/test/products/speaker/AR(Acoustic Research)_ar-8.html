  • AR(Acoustic Research) AR-8

  • ¥ 67,000 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

Two way bookshelf speaker system.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 3.8 cm cone tweeter is used in the high range.

It is equipped with 3-step switching type level control for high frequency.

The enclosure uses an acoustic suspension system.
In addition, the exterior finish is a walnut oil finish.
