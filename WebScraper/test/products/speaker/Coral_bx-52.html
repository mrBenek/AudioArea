  • CORAL BX-52

Commentary

This is a floor-type speaker system of the Coral High Stereo Series.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 16 cm cone squaker is installed in the middle range.
Two types of tweeters, a horn type and a cone type, are installed in the high range.

The enclosure has been designed with a tilted front baffle for ease of listening.
The interior uses a bass reflex method, and the exterior has a walnut flat finish.
