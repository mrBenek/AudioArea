Audio Database

  • LUXMAN S-5II

  • ¥ 11,000 (1 unit, released in 1980)

Commentary

Ultra-compact 2-way speaker system.

It has a 10 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and aluminum die-cast is used to completely eliminate box rumbling.

As an option sold separately, there were a tripod stand and an arm for car.
