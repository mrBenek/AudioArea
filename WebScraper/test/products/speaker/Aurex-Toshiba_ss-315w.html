  • Aurex SS-315W

  • ¥ 52,400 (a set of two cars, around 1976)

Commentary

A floor-type speaker system using a twin drive system.

Two 20 cm cone type woofers are mounted in the low range, and dynamic playback sound is obtained by driving in parallel.

A 7 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed using high-density particle board.
