Commentary

A four way speaker system using the A. S. W. (acoustic super woofer) method.

The ASW has a structure in which the inside of the enclosure is divided into two large parts and a 20 cm cone type unit is contained inside. Sound is output from a port located at the bottom of the enclosure. This structure forms a band-pass filter that selectively passes only a specific frequency range and reproduces only the low frequency range.

The remaining 3-way unit is mounted on the other part of the split.
A 20 cm cone woofer is mounted for the low range and a 12 cm cone squaker is mounted for the middle range.
The H-54H horn type tweeter is installed in the high-frequency range.

The exterior is finished with walnut oil.

Equipped with multi-amplifier terminal.
