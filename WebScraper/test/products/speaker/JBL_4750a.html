Commentary

Sound Power Series medium 2-way speaker system.

The 4750A is equipped with 2 30 cm cone type woofer 2206H in the low range.
In addition, a horn type unit combining a compression driver 2445J using a 10 cm diameter titanium diaphragm and a flat front biradial horn 2380A is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure uses a wedge-shaped design that allows multiple side-by-side clusters, extending the coverage by 30 degrees horizontally for each additional enclosure.
It can also be used with sub base systems 4745A, 4748A, 4785A, and 4788A.

The 4750A uses a bi-amplifier drive system.
