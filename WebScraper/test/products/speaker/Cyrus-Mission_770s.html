  • MISSION 770S

  • ¥ 280,000 (a set of two cars, around 1983)

Commentary

This 2-way speaker system is the successor of Mission770.

A 22.5 cm cone type woofer using reinforced homopolymer cone is mounted in the low range.
A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter with a polymer dome diaphragm is installed in the high-frequency range.

The terminal part is designed to be connected by 4 mm plug or wire.

There are 2 color variations, black and walnut.
