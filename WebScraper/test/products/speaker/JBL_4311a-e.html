Commentary

A speaker system developed for monitoring in recording studios, editing rooms, and adjustment rooms, and for outdoor recording.

A 30 cm cone Woofer 2213 is installed in the low range, and a 13 cm cone Squaker LE5-2 is installed in the middle range.
In addition, a 3.6 cm direct radiator LE25 is mounted in the high range.

Designed for hanging use, the front baffle has a layout with a woofer mounted at the top and an attenuator placed at the bottom.

There are 2 variations of the exterior finish, gray and walnut.
