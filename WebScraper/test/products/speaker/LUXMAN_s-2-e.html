  • LUXMAN S2 MINY

  • ¥ 14,400 (1 unit, sold in February 1965)

Commentary

This small speaker system was developed as a speaker that can be enjoyed at a low volume without losing to large speakers in terms of efficiency and low-frequency sound.

A 10-cm cone unit and a high-frequency unit are used for the unit, and the enclosure is sealed.

A network of 12dB/oct. is used.
