  • ¥ 98,000 (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system developed as a monitor speaker.

It is equipped with UL12 which is 30 cm cone type woofer in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a fiber cone with viscoelastic synthetic resin coating to ensure high rigidity. In addition, neoprene roll edge is used for the edge.

MC6 which is a 15 cm cone type squaker is mounted in the middle area.

The HF2000, a 2.5 cm dome tweeter, is installed in the high range.
This unit was also used as a BBC monitor speaker.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
There were also two variations in the finish of the exterior : walnut and cheek.
