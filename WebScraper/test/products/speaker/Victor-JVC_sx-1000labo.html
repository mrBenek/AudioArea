Commentary

This is a speaker from the Laboratory series that is made by gathering the best of Victor's speaker technology without compromising the hand-made process of each device.

A 31.5 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
The diaphragm of this unit is a CFRP (carbon fiber reinforced plastic) foam hybrid three layer diaphragm. This diaphragm is based on pulp corn, has an intermediate layer of resin foam, and uses polypropylene resin matrix for carbon cloth. While maintaining an appropriate internal loss, it has rigidity and sound velocity about 1.5 times that of paper, and bending rigidity 11 times that of paper.
Alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

A 8.0 cm dome type unit is adopted for the middle area.
This unit uses a diaphragm coated with pure diamond in a three layer structure of pure fine ceramics.
Alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

A 3.0 cm dome-shaped unit is used for the high range.
The diaphragm of this unit uses a pure diamond diaphragm made of 100% crystalline diamond with a thickness of 30 μ m, which realizes a sound velocity of 16,500m/s.
The magnetic circuit uses an Alnico magnet like other units.

The cabinet is made of maple wood produced in Canada, and the front baffle is 38 mm thick including the veneer.
The surface material is carefully selected Birdseye, which can be taken only a little from a maple tree, and dyed it gray in Italy.
The dedicated stand has a 4-point independent support foot (Independent Base) surrounded by pine material to reduce the influence from the floor without damaging the natural sound of the system.

The network uses a gold plated input terminal for bi-wiring, and the 3-division network prevents sound quality degradation due to interference between units.
