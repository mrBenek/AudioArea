Commentary

A typical speaker system in Auratone.

The specifications of the 5C are subject to change depending on the manufacturing time, and the improved model around 1983 is intended to improve response characteristics and power handling capability.

The unit is equipped with a 12.5 cm cone type full range unit.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a walnut finish on the exterior.
In addition, there is a wall mounting tool on the back, and it can be set on the wall.

Mounting bracket is sold separately as an option for more flexible setting.
