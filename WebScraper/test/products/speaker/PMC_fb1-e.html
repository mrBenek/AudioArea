Commentary

A speaker system designed to be used in home theaters.

The FB1 is a sibling product of the TB1/TB2, and has developed the loading line of the TB1/TB2 in a more complete direction.
FB1M is an anti-magnetic type of FB1.

A 17 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a diecast frame.

A hard dome type tweeter using aluminum alloy is mounted in the high frequency range.

By adopting a newly designed transmission line system in the enclosure, excellent low-frequency reproduction capability is obtained, and low-frequency reproduction at 25 Hz is realized.
In addition, all FB1s come with a standard speaker-based PL1.

The speaker terminal is bi-wired.

There are 3 types of exterior finishes. FB1-BA/FB1M-BA has a black ash finish, FB1-AW/FB1M-AW has a walnut finish, and FB1-R/FB1M-R has a rosewood finish.
