Commentary

A modular high-output speaker system for the concert series.

The 4850 is the base model for the 4850 speaker family.
In addition to the 4850, there are 3 variations of the 4850 speaker family, 4851, 4852 and 4853, each of which has a different unit configuration.

Two 30 cm cone type woofer 2204H are installed in the low range.
In addition, a horn type unit using a horn 2380A and a driver 2445J is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure uses high-density birch-laminated plywood with excellent strength. Surface treatment of glass-fiber-reinforced plastic ensures excellent durability. In addition, the side of the enclosure is cut into a 22.5 ° tapered shape, and by arranging multiple tubes in a fan-like shape, an extremely wide service area can be obtained.

Equipped with 8 embedded metal fittings for flying.

The Concert Series included a wide range of specialized options, including a carrying load case and various speaker mounting brackets.
