  • KENWOOD OMNI-7

  • ¥ 39,800 (1 set of 2, released in 1993)

Commentary

A small speaker system developed as an omnidirectional speaker for natural stereo sound in a wide area of the room.

The unit employs a 10 cm cone woofer and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter, each placed on top and bottom, and a sandwich structure with reflectors to diffuse sound in 360 degrees horizontally.

It can be installed freely, and by combining with the separately sold special speaker stand (the one attached in the picture), it can be attached to walls and ceilings.
