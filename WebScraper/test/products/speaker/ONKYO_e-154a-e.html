Commentary

4-way floor speaker system.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is equipped with a powerful magnetic circuit that uses eight 30 φ x30mm magnets, twice as many as conventional Woofer magnets. The lowest resonance frequency is 40 Hz, and a single fo is 20 Hz, making it possible to enjoy a powerful low sound reproduction.

The middle area is equipped with a horn type squaker.
This unit has the same design as the HM-500A sold separately.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high range, and a horn type super tweeter is installed in the ultra high range.

The enclosure is made of lauan plywood with a thickness of 15 mm and 18 mm depending on the location.
The exterior is oiled.

Equipped with multi-amplifier terminal and level control terminal.
