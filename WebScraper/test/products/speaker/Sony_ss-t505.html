  • SONY SS-T505

  • ¥ 13,000 (1 unit, released in 1997)

Commentary

A thin speaker system developed for surround sound reproduction.

Two 8 cm cone type woofers with polypropylene diaphragm are mounted in the low range.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Each unit has an anti-magnetic design.

The enclosure has a thin design with a depth of 84 mm. Ribs increase rigidity and partitions are provided inside to reduce mutual interference.
In addition, 60 mm pitch mounting screw holes are provided.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
