Commentary

This is the fifth generation speaker system of the SX 500 series.

For the low range, a 20 cm cone type unit is adopted which uses a new material of Kurt Muller cone.
While maintaining the merits of pulp such as lightness, moderate internal loss, and good transmissibility, rigidity is enhanced and toughness is improved.
An internal magnetic alnico magnetic circuit is used for the magnetic circuit that drives this diaphragm.
Magnetic properties are further improved by special heat treatment, and a solid energy balance is obtained.
Based on Victor's butterfly damper, the spider suspension originally developed by Victor has obtained lighter weight and higher rigidity by examining the material and shape.
The voice coil uses an edge-weiss voice coil with high heat dissipation.

A 3.5 cm dome type unit is adopted for the high frequency band.
It adopts a new shape of habutae silk soft dome, and this diaphragm is coated with thin film rubber in multiple layers after molding to obtain rigidity in addition to flexibility inherent in silk.
The magnetic circuit uses an alnico magnetic circuit that has undergone special heat treatment in the same way as the low-frequency circuit. In addition, the connection between the diaphragm and voice coil is strengthened.
The voice coil uses 99.9999% high-purity 6N copper wire.

Mahogany material is used for the baffle plate.
In order to make the best use of the quality of the material, it is used as solid wood of 25 mm thickness, and it has been impregnated with a newly developed resin to clear aging.
In addition, by increasing the strength of the unit mounting surface that narrows the cabinet width, the sound image density and sound field feeling have been improved.
The sound absorbing material is 100% wool.

The speaker terminal uses a gold plated banana plug terminal.
In addition, Victor's proprietary OFC (oxygen-free copper wire) is used for the network, and the woofer and tweeter are independently wired to prevent internal interference.
Film capacitors with low loss and good high-frequency characteristics are used.
