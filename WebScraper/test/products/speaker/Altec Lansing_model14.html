Commentary

A 2-way speaker system using a manta ray Lehorn.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A horn type unit is installed in the high frequency range.
This unit is a combination of a Tangerine driver and a Mantalehorn and boasts a wide range of dispersion patterns.

It is equipped with a dual-band equalizer and has a unique design that can divide the middle and high ranges for equalization even though it is two way.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
