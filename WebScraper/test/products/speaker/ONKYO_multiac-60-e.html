  • ONKYO Multiac-60

  • ¥ 21,000 (1 unit, around 1970)

Commentary

3-way bass reflex type speaker system.

A 30 cm cone type unit is used for the low range, a 12 cm cone type unit for the middle range, and a horn type unit for the high range.

The enclosure is made of walnut with a matte WATCO finish.

Also, a multi-amplifier terminal is mounted.
