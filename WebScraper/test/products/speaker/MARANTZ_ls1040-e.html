  • Marantz LS1040

  • ¥ 28,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1995)

Commentary

A two way compact speaker system with a dedicated speaker stand.

It is equipped with a 10 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter in the high range.

It is a magnetic protection design for AV.

It comes with metal fittings and fixing screws, and can be used for wall hanging setting.
