  • TOSHIBA SS-37

  • ¥ 26,900 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

A speaker system that employs a newly developed concave unit.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 12 cm cone squaker is installed in the middle range.
Cone cave technology has been incorporated into these units. This is a positive use of the part that used to be a dust-proof cap in the conventional unit. By using a concave diaphragm made of a mixture of special chemical fibers, the disturbance of the high frequency is suppressed and the muddy sound near the crossover is prevented.

A 3-cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Equipped with 3-step switching level control for mid-range and high-range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

In addition to the normal input terminal, the terminal for multi-amplifier is mounted, and 2-way or 3-way multi-amplifier drive is possible.
