Commentary

A small speaker system born from strict measurement and thorough hearing tests.

A full-range 16 cm cone type speaker is installed in the low range.
A slightly thick and hard cone paper is used, and a cloth roll mold is used for the edge.
The magnetic circuit is 11,500 gauss.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.

The enclosure uses a bus-recovery system with a pipe duct.
15 mm hard plywood is used for the material, and 20 mm acetate sound absorbing material with good sound absorbing effect is used for the inside to reduce standing waves. The exterior is walnut finished.

Wall hanging metal fittings are attached.
