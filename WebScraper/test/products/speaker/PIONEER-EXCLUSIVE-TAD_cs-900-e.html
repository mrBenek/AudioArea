Commentary

This 3-way speaker system uses a 90 ° rotating horn tweeter.

It has a 30 cm cone woofer in the low range and a cone midrange in the middle range.
These units use FB cones for the diaphragm, and the mid-range units improve directional characteristics by the effect of the central dome.

A multi-cellar horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit is a 90 ° rotary type that provides good directional characteristics regardless of whether the enclosure is installed horizontally or vertically.

The network uses thick copper wires and large ferrite cores to improve transient characteristics.

Level control is mounted on the front.

In addition to the normal input terminal, the multi-amplifier terminal is also mounted on the back.

The enclosure is fully enclosed and has a natural wood walnut oil-finish exterior.
