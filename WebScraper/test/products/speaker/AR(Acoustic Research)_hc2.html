Commentary

A set of 5.1 channel speakers with five best-selling small speakers, the Edge, and a subwoofer, the S112PS.

Edge

Speaker system with all-weather structure.

It has a 13.3 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter in the high range.
Each unit has an anti-magnetic design.

It can be used in all weather conditions by adopting ABS injection molded enclosure.

Equipped with rotating stand.

The Edge was sold as a pair, but the single pack Edge SGL was released later.

S112PS

The unit is equipped with 30 cm diameter long stroke woofer.

Built-in high-power amplifier.
The enclosure uses a slot-loading design.
The exterior has a black wood finish.

High - and low-level input terminals are mounted.
It is also equipped with a variable adjustment function for input level and crossover frequency.
