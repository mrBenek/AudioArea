  • AR(Acoustic Research) AR1

  • ¥ 580,000 (a set of two cars, around 2000)

Commentary

This speaker system is the top model of the High Resolution Series.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is driven by a Sampha ear power amplifier built into the speaker system and acts as a servo-controlled subwoofer.

The mid-range has 2 13 cm cone mid-range units.
This unit is named MAG alloy mid-range and uses a concave diaphragm made of magnesium / aluminum alloy.

A 2.5 cm hard-domed tweeter is installed in the high range.
Diamond-coated titanium dome is used for the diaphragm.

Anti-magnetic design is applied.

An acoustic suspension system is adopted for the enclosure, and a tall boy type is realized by mounting a woofer unit on the side.
There were 2 variations of the finish of the exterior, black wood grain finish and cherry finish.
