Commentary

The R Series compact 2-way speaker system.

The tweeter uses a 5 cm balanced drive titanium tweeter with an acoustic lens.
By adopting an acoustic lens, sound is radiated in a wide range and directional characteristics are improved.

The woofer uses the same urethane edge as the one used in the G9 and has succeeded in greatly reducing the low-frequency resonant frequency fo.

In addition, by controlling the radiation frequency of the enclosure, box noise is minimized.
