Commentary

This speaker system is a miniaturized version of the A7 design.

The unit is equipped with 405-8B which is a 10 cm cone type full range unit.

The enclosure is equivalent to the A7 and is a bass reflex design with front load and short horn.
The exterior has a gray metallic finish.
