Commentary

An improved speaker system for the DS 200.
While taking advantage of the advantages of highly rigid materials, we aim to realize a dynamic dynamic feeling in the middle and low range by eliminating problems such as high frequency resonance with our unique resonance dispersion technique.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
The diaphragm uses pure cross carbon, which is 15% lighter than conventional ones, and the high-frequency characteristics are improved by a unique resonance elimination structure.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is used for the high range.
The diaphragm has been changed to a plasma carbonite diaphragm to achieve 5.6 times the height of pure titanium.
In addition, a high-frequency resonance dispersion structure is adopted in which both ends of the peripheral part are cut by a laser to prevent resonance.

In order to support AV, a magnetic protection design is provided.

We have adopted a new design for the network section to improve sound quality.

A large speaker terminal is used to accommodate very thick cables.
