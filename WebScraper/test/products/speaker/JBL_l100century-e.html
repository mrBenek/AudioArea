Commentary

This is a speaker system designed for general use with JBL professional speakers that were used for monitors.

It has a 30 cm cone woofer 123A in the low range, a 13 cm cone squaker LE5-3 in the middle range, and a 4 cm cone tweeter LE25-1 in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass-reflex system and is a solid design with high-density reinforced plywood and JBL's unique rock-miter joints.

The front grille features JBL's newly developed scalp Chad Cadrex II. The grille comes in three color variations : blue, orange and brown.
