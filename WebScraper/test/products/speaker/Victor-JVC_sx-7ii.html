Commentary

This speaker system combines the spirit of thorough handcraft with the results of the latest electronics technology, and seeks performance quality suitable for recording technology.

A 30 cm cone type unit made of Kurt Mueller's KDU cone paper is used for the low range. The cone paper is a new type of cone paper that reexamines the internal loss and enhances the beautiful sound.
We pursue better sound quality by adopting Konica Le Dome to prevent split vibration and newly designed magnetic circuit with low distortion factor.
The unit is protected by a perforated metal cover.

Soft dome type units of 7.5 cm and 3 cm are used for the middle and high frequencies.
Several hundred kinds of experiments have been repeated for each of them, and all improvements have been made from the dome base fabric to the molding method and the material of the viscoelastic coating agent.
The magnetic circuit is also strengthened, and linearity and input resistance are improved.

In order to obtain clean wave front transmission without interference near the crossover frequency, more than ten different unit configurations were tested and measured. After adjusting the positional relationship in millimeters, left and right channel target configurations were adopted to improve sound field reproducibility and sound image localization.
In addition, the front baffle uses a lumber core made of coniferous forest Douglas-fur produced in North America to make the most of the sound of muku material.
A soundbar with a concave R is added to the rear baffle to apply tension to the plate and improve vibration damping characteristics.
