Commentary

The first bass reflex type speaker system in Celestion.

A 29 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, it is equipped with a 4.6 cm dome scoker for the middle band and a 1.9 cm dome tweeter for the high band.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.
The + / - 0 level control provides a balance for classical music, and can be adjusted as necessary to obtain sound quality suitable for pop music.
