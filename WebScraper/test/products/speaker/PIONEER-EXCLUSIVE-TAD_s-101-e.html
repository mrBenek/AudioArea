Commentary

A speaker system developed based on the sound concept of digital realism.
Based on the basics of speaker production, we are actively introducing new technologies.

A 18 cm cone woofer is used for the low range.
Carbon graphite is used for the diaphragm, and a midship mounting system is used in which a woofer is mounted for the inner baffle.
As a result, vibration of the woofer is not directly transmitted to the front baffle, and unnecessary vibration to the baffle plate is blocked.
In addition, since it is designed with low magnetic leakage, it can be set on the TV side.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is used for the high range.
New diaphragm ceramic carbon with carbon purity of 99.9% is used for the diaphragm.
This is held by a highly rigid aluminum die-cast flange.

The network employs a layout and fixing method that reduces crosstalk by orthogonal arrangement of magnetic flux and avoids the effect of resonance between elements.
