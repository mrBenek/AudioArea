Commentary

This is a book shelf speaker system that uses a sealed system.

It is equipped with a 30 cm cone woofer in the low range, two 12 cm cone scokers in the middle range, and two horn type tweeters in the high range.

Equipped with 3-step switching type level control for middle and high frequencies.
