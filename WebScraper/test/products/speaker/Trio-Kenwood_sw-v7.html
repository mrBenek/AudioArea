  • Discretionary price (around ¥ 18,000, 1 unit, released in September 2002)

Commentary

Active sub-woofer aiming at high sound quality and low sound exceeding class.

The unit is equipped with 16 cm cone type woofer.

Extruded aluminum is used for the front panel of the enclosure.
We have also reduced wind noise by applying a flared shape to the bass reflex duct.

The turnover frequency can be continuously variable.

It has an input terminal for the speaker cable.
