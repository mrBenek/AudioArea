  • ¥ 98,000 (a set of two cars, around the 1990 s?)

Commentary

A speaker system designed to reduce the cost as much as possible while maintaining the basics of sound reproduction so that you can easily enjoy the sound of the Kusu Lab.

It is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer low range and a 2 cm inverted dome type Tui heater for the high range.

The enclosure has a wallnut finish and is a bass-reflex structure with ports on the front.

By-wire connection is possible.
