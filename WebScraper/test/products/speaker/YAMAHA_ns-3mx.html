Commentary

This is a full-range mini monitor speaker that realizes the sound quality of NS-10M Yuzuri and a compact size smaller than B5 size.

The unit is equipped with a 12 cm cone type full range speaker.
The diaphragm uses a white cone made of the same material as NS-10M and arranged for the full range.
For the magnetic circuit, we have adopted the A ・ MAG method, a magnetic shielding circuit originally developed by Yamaha. In this method, the balance point of the magnetic flux distribution in the magnetic circuit is determined by repeating computer simulation. The canceling magnet is magnetized under the optimized magnetized magnetic flux distribution, and it is combined and adhered to the main magnet. As a result, the magnetic shielding type magnetic circuit of the conventional ferrite magnet improves the S/N feeling and poor sound loss.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
On the back side, there is a screw hole for mounting a speaker Plaquette for wall mounting, etc.

The speaker terminal uses a gold-plated banana plug speaker terminal.
