Commentary

A large speaker system with three power amplifiers and an electronic crossover network.

It uses a 30 cm cone type woofer for the low band and a 6 cm and 3 cm dome type unit for the middle band and high band respectively.
It is also equipped with a 30 cm passive radiator.
