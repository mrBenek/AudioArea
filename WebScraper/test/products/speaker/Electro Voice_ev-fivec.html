  • ¥ 65,000 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system that employs an acoustic suspension system.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a unique four layer winding structure for the voice coil, and by increasing the number of coil turns in the gap, high efficiency is ensured without sacrificing the low frequency response that is often seen in small systems.

A 6 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The tweeter unit has a structure in which the gap is filled with viscous damping material.

It is equipped with a level control for the high frequency band.

The enclosure has a walnut finish on all four sides.
