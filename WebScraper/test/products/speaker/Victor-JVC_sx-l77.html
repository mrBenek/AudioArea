Commentary

This speaker system was developed using the technologies obtained in the development of the SX-L9 and SX-LT55 Limited.

The SX-L77 uses a layout in which the tweeter is sandwiched between the upper middle and low band woofer and the lower bottom woofer.
The upper and lower units look the same, but they are completely different units. The woofer and the bottom woofer face each other at the position where the driving shaft is closest to each other, and the tweeter is arranged between them in oblique omni array arrangement.

An 18 cm oblicorn type woofer is mounted in the low range, and it mainly receives 170 hz or less.
The diaphragm is a aluminum oblicorn diaphragm plated with pure gold and dynamically balanced. The Oblicorn diaphragm is driven at a position offset from the center to improve the characteristics in the middle and high range.
The drive section is driven by a 40 mm diameter voice coil and an internal Alnico magnetic circuit with a magnetic flux density of 1,100 mT and a total magnetic flux of 825 μ Wb.
In order to enhance the effect of the oblique omni array arrangement, the center cap has also shifted the vertex and uses aluminum of recap of the same diameter with pure gold plating.

An 18 cm oblicorn type unit is installed in the middle and low range.
This unit is a full-range basic design with medium and low frequencies. The diaphragm is a dynamically balanced aluminum oblicorn diaphragm. The resonance of the material is dispersed by offsetting the driving point. This diaphragm is plated with pure gold.
The edge low loss EPDM foam rubber edge is used for the edge part, and spider suspension with excellent linearity is used for the damper.
The drive section is driven by an internal magnetic dual alnico magnetic circuit with a magnetic flux density of 900 mt (Tesla) and a total magnetic flux of 1,740 μ Wb, and a 50 mm diameter voice coil.
Aluminum die-cast frame is used for the frame.

A 2.5 cm oblidome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
It uses a oblidome diaphragm with a carbon graphite dome whose top is shifted, eliminating the coloring of the material.
An internal magnetic alnico magnetic circuit with a magnetic flux density of 1,550 mT is used for the magnetic circuit.

Carefully selected parts are used for the network part.
The crossover frequencies are determined with an emphasis on the centrodesmus range of the music, and the optimum attenuation characteristics are set for both the woofer and tweeter.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The SX-L77 uses a staggered bass reflex system. The enclosure is divided into low and mid-range sections, and the tuning frequency of the bass reflex port is shifted to achieve a clear musical scale and a sense of dynamism. The low and mid-range sections consist of an 18-liter cabinet with the tuning frequency of the port set to 42 Hz, and the bottom woofer section consists of a 33-liter cabinet with the tuning frequency set to 29 Hz. This structure realizes two bass reflex structures to prevent mutual interference. The low range section consists of twin woofer drives, and functions equivalent to a 25 cm diameter woofer.
The front baffle 30 mm, top plate 28 mm, side plate 24 mm, and bottom plate 94 mm are all different in thickness to disperse resonance and improve sound quality. In addition, maple veneer is used inside the enclosure for sound quality.
The exterior uses a White Sicmore veneer for the front baffle and a maple veneer for the other surfaces, finished with a lustrous paint.

A speaker terminal for bi-wiring or bi-amplifier drive is used.
