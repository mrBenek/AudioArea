Commentary

This is a high-end floor-type speaker system that uses a double-baffle structure for the bass part.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range and a double baffle structure is adopted.

Two 16 cm cone speakers are used for the mid-range and four static speakers for the high-range.

Equipped with level control for middle and high frequencies.
