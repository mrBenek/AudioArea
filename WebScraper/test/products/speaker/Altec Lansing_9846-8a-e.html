Commentary

2-way floor type studio monitor.

A 38 cm cone woofer of 411 to 8 a is used for the low range.
In the high-frequency range, a horn-type unit combining a driver 802 to 8 d and a horn 511B is mounted.

The network section uses N501-8A with a crossover frequency of 500 Hz.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
