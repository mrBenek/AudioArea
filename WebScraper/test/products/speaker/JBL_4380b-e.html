Commentary

A line-array-type speaker system used for sound reinforcement of speech and music playback in relatively large halls, lecture halls and public halls, or for stage monitoring.

A total of 4 20 cm cone type wide-range units 2110H are mounted in the low range, 2 in the top and 2 in the bottom.
The center is equipped with two 13 cm cone wide range units 2105H for speech range, and a special acoustic lens is used to ensure excellent directivity at 90 ° horizontal and 20 ° vertical.

The enclosure has a gray paint finish.
