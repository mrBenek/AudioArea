Commentary

A cabaret series speaker system developed for bass guitars and subwoofers.

The low range is equipped with an E155 which is a 46 cm cone type woofer.

The enclosure uses a rigid multi-layer plywood of 19 mm thickness and is externally coated with polyurethane paint for increased strength and high durability.
In addition, a front cover to protect the net and grill cloth during transportation, a polycarbonate corner guard of convenient shape when using the stack, and a carrying handle are taken into consideration.

It is listed as 4695A in the 1981 catalog.
