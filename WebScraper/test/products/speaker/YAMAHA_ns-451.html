Commentary

A 2-way speaker system that uses white cones and radial tweeters.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
A conical type straight cone is used for the diaphragm. This diaphragm realizes a diaphragm with a wide piston area by special processing of foreign coniferous wood based raw materials with old Japanese paper based technology. In addition, since it is a sheet manufacturing method, it realizes a diaphragm with less unevenness in density and thickness compared with a molded cone.
The voice coil uses a copper ribbon edgewise wound voice coil with excellent space factor, and the bobbin uses Nomex #410 made by Du Pont which is lightweight and can be used continuously even at high temperature of 220 ℃ to increase the allowable input.
The edge is flexible and high compliance type with thermosetting resin coating without temperature change to realize linear operation.
The magnetic circuit uses an internal magnet type of Alnico magnet.

A 5-cm compound tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit consists of a 5 cm cone base and a 2.3 cm dome. With this structure, vibration modes become clear by reinforcing each diaphragm with each other. The cone section enhances the middle and high range and the dome section greatly improves the directional characteristics of the high sound range.
The diaphragm uses a lightweight type of 0.13 gr, and the voice coil uses Nomex #410 bobbin with edgewise winding of CCAR (copper clad aluminum ribbon wire) for high input resistance design.
Maximum input power of 50W is realized, and instantaneous input power of 200 to 250W is also supported.

An MP capacitor is used in the network section, and the constants are finally determined after listening to the sound until there is no difference in instantaneous comparison listening with the multi-amplifier system.
It is also equipped with a continuously variable level control.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method and high-density particleboards are used for the board material.
The exterior is finished with a Century Walnut finish.
