Commentary

A 3-way speaker system developed as a successor to Sandwich300.

A 20 cm sandwich corn woofer is used for the low range.
In this unit, a sticky material is used to dump around the voice coil to prevent distortion.

It uses a 10 cm plastic cone type unit for the middle area, and the cone is coated with sticky dump material.
In addition, specially shaped holes are made in the cone paper to minimize coloring of the playback sound.

A tweeter with a 2.5 cm polyester film dome is used for the high range.
Directivity is improved because the area of the vibrator is small.
