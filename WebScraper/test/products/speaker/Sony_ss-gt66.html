  • SONY SS-GT66

  • ¥ 70,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1992)

Commentary

A tall boy type speaker system equipped with S. F. C. T (Sound Field Control Technology) which makes full use of sound field control technology.

Two 13 cm cone woofers are installed in the low range, and three 5 cm cone tweeters are installed in the high range.
The tweeter unit is mounted for S. F. C. T in left / front / right orientation respectively.

Equipped with an S. F. C. T sound field sensing function, you can switch the combination of 3 operating tweeters in each of 3 positions of narrow / normal / wide, and adjust according to the software and preference.
