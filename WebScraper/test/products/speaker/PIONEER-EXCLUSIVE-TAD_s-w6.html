Commentary

A series powered subwoofer with a natural wood finish.

The unit is equipped with 25 cm cone type woofer.
In addition, by installing a 25 cm cone type passive radiator, the generation of wind noise is suppressed and clear low sound reproduction is realized.

The amplifier section is equipped with a D-class amplifier.

Equipped with a filter-through switch, it can pass through filters such as the high-cut of the built-in amplifier.
This prevents S/N degradation and phase change, making it possible to achieve optimum deep bass playback for sources such as DVD audio.

The enclosure has a natural wood finish.
