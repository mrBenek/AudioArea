  • JBL J316PRO

  • ¥ 90,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1991)

Commentary

A compact speaker system with JBL's professional technology.

A 16.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using a pure titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high range.
This unit has a neodymium magnet in its magnetic circuit, and has a stable regenerative capacity even when Tsutomu Dainyu is in use.

Equipped with a level control, the output level of the high-frequency range can be varied in three patterns : flat, + 2 dB, and - 2 dB.

Equipped with a power indicator.
This indicator lights green for normal input and red for Riki Dainyu.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.

It can be installed on a ceiling or a wall by using an optional mounting bracket sold separately.
