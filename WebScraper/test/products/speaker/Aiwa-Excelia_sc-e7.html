  • AIWA SC-E7(R/S)

  • ¥ 48,000 (a set of two cars, around 1979)

Commentary

Mini component My Pace series speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 10 cm cone type squaker is mounted in the middle area.
Each unit was developed independently and uses a non-press type cone molded by suction system.

A 2.5 cm hard-domed tweeter is installed in the high range.
Titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm to enhance the high frequency reproduction capability.

Each unit uses a newly developed strong magnet to achieve high efficiency characteristics.

Equipped with a constant resistance continuously variable attenuator.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
A 20 mm thick plate is used for the front baffle. In addition, the directivity is improved by making the squaker and tweeter close to each other.

There were 2 kinds of variations, SC-E7R of rosewood type and SC-E7S of silver type.
