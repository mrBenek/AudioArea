Commentary

This is a super woofer system that uses a dual irradiation system.

The entire cabinet is equipped with a 30 cm woofer with Kurt Müller-Cohn Konica Le Dome, and a 38 cm passive radiator on the back. The sound from both sides is combined in space to produce ultra-low sound expansion.

The front of the woofer is equipped with a protector board that also serves as a high-pass filter. When the rear side of the cabinet is shoji or fusuma, it can be placed upside down so that it is in the same state as a hard wall.
