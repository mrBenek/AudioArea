  • SONY SS-X1

  • ¥ 17,000 (a set of two cars, around 1980)

Commentary

2-way speaker system with 8.5 cm woofer and 5 cm tweeter.

It is designed to be small and lightweight so that you can hang it from the ceiling or set it on the shelf.

Considering the combination with a monitor TV such as a profile, magnetic shielding is applied.
