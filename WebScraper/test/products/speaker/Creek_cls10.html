Commentary

This 2-way speaker system was developed in pursuit of simple beauty.

A 12 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Polypropylene cone is used for this unit, and coil with excellent heat resistance is also used for voice coil.

A 1.9 cm hard-domed tweeter is installed in the high range.
Aluminum diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

The crossover network is composed of a minimum number of parts and employs an inductor with an oversized magnetic core for transmission with less distortion.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex.
