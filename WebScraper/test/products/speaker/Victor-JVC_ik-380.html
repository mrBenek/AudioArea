Commentary

A superwoofer system developed to faithfully reproduce the fundamental sound in the lowest range.
It can also be used as a 3D center woofer or as a general stereo woofer for relatively high frequencies.

This product uses a 38 cm special woofer unit with Konica Le Dome.

The input terminal is equipped with screw terminal and 3P cannon connector.
