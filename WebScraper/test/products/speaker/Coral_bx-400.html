  • CORAL BX-400

  • ¥ 23,000 (1 unit, around 1970?)

Commentary

This bookshelf type speaker system was developed as the second series following the BX 200.

It has a 25 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 10 cm cone squaker in the middle range.

A horn type tweeter using a super duralumin diaphragm is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system with a characteristic bronze pattern on the front.
The exterior features a walnut polyurethane finish.
