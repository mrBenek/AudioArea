  • SONY SS-GT550

  • ¥ 50,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1995)

Commentary

This is a tall boy speaker system designed with an emphasis on sound balance in order to enhance the surround sound of AV systems more easily.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

An anti-magnetic design has been adopted for AV applications.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
