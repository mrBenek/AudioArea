Commentary

A separate speaker system consisting of two satellite speakers and two subwoofers.

Unlike most 3D systems, it uses a slim subwoofer with separate left and right channels, which allows for half the size of the woofer cabinet for equal performance and increased space flexibility.
In addition, two sub-woofers and two satellite speakers each have a built-in crossover network, allowing for more freedom of placement.

The satellite speaker and subwoofer crossover frequency is set to 140 Hz.

The satellite speakers have a suede touch Nextel paint gun metal gray finish.

The subwoofer uses an acoustic suspension system and the exterior is a black high-pressure laminate with a metal grill.
