Commentary

A speaker system developed for stage speakers in halls and holdbacks in studios.

The MK series is a product of ALTEC Sound Development (ASD), a brand established by Elektri, and is a Japanese systematized version of ALTEC products.
The MK-5 was developed as a more user-friendly speaker based on the achievements of the conventional A5 system.
The low range is equipped with a 38 cm cone type woofer, 3156.

A horn type unit that combines manta ray Lehorn MRII594 and driver unit 288 to 8 k is installed in the high range.

N-8500-8A is mounted in the network part.

The cabinet has a bass reflex design based on the Thiele & Small theory.
In addition, FRP and wood plywood are used as materials.
