  • AR(Acoustic Research) S20

  • ¥ 50,000 (a set of two cars, around 2000)

Commentary

Status series bookshelf speaker system.

A 16.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a coated cellulose diaphragm and the frame part is made of ABS.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
Silk dome is used for the diaphragm.
The enclosure uses a bass-reflex method, the baffle uses MDF material, and the cabinet uses a chipboard.
The exterior has a cherry finish.

Speaker terminal compatible with banana plug is adopted.
Equipped with port urethane foam, low frequency range can be adjusted.
