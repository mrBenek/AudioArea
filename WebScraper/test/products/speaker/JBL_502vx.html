Commentary

Radiance Series 2-way speaker system.

The Radiance series is designed with the concept of "from the first step of audio to JBL" and pursues cost performance. The sound is decided by JBL's Sound Quality Examination Committee called Golden Year.

It has a 20 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 7.5 cm cone tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method and has a walnut wood-grain exterior finish.
In addition, the Radiance series features a metalized Mylar finish on the front baffle.
