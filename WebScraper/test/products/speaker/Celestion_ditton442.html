Commentary

This is a book shelf speaker system that uses a sealed system.

The Ditton442 is designed for well-balanced sound and to cover sounds below fo.

The low range is equipped with a 33 cm cone woofer and the middle range is equipped with a 13 cm cone squaker.
A 1.9 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a double-sealed structure that separates the skoker section.
