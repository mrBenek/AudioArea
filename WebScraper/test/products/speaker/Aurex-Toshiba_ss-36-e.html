  • Toshiba/Aurex SS-36

  • ¥ 36,000 (1 unit, released in April 1969)

Commentary

A sealed four way speaker system.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a high-compliance free edge using newly developed materials and special molding technology.

A 16 cm cone type squaker is mounted in the middle area.

It is equipped with an 8x4cm square horn for the high range and an 8 cm cone type tweeter for the ultra high range.

The level control on the front allows you to change the sound quality continuously.

Equipped with selector knob for multi-amplifier, 2-way / 3-way / full range can be selected.

The net on the front can be removed.
