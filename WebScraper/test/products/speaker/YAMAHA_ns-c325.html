Commentary

The center speaker system of the 325 series, a middle-sized theater speaker series.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
The diaphragm uses an advanced PMD diaphragm which is a complete improvement of PMD (Polymer Injected Mica Diaphram), the edge uses foamed EPDM surround, and the voice coil uses a hybrid spiral structure to directly apply drive energy to the diaphragm.
High-rigidity steel frame is used for the frame part.

A 3-cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is made of aluminum magnesium alloy. The diaphragm and voice coil bobbin are integrated into a DC diaphragm system. By eliminating the adhesive part, the weight of the vibration system is reduced and driving loss is reduced. In addition, the diaphragm surface is coated with a special coating to enable smooth and extended high-frequency reproduction.

The network section uses carefully selected parts such as a metalized PP capacitor made by Solen and a large-capacity coil with a winding diameter of 1 mm as well as a direct connection method.
In addition, two types of internal wiring materials, one for the woofer, the other for the main wiring and the other for the tweeter, have been tuned.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The exterior has a natural wood finish of mahogany real wood.

A large speaker terminal with gold-plated brass machined processing is used for the input terminal.

The color was only cherry when it was released, but the one with black finish was added afterwards.
