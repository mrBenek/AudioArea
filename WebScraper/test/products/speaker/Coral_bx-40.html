Commentary

Bookshelf speaker system with 2 way design.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and is equipped with an aluminum grill on the front.
The exterior has a walnut oil finish.
