  • VICTOR/JVC S-C2

  • ¥ 25,000 (1 unit, around 1980)

Commentary

A small 2-way system developed with the aim of being a speaker that can listen to vocals realistically.

For the lower range, a 20 cm cone type unit using light weight high-Young's modulus cone paper developed jointly with Kurt Muller is adopted.
In addition, parasitic vibration is suppressed by an aluminum die-cast ring, and efficiency and input resistance are improved by a large magnetic circuit and voice coil design with good heat radiation effect.

The soft dome diaphragm developed jointly with Kurt Muller is used for the high frequency band as well as the low frequency band.
By matching the tone with the lower range, a more balanced natural sound is pursued.
