Commentary

This 3-way speaker system was developed based on the W & D (Wide & Dynamic) concept.

A 16 cm cone type is used for the low range, a 2.5 cm soft dome type for the middle range, and a 1.0 cm dome type with metal deposition on resin film for the high range.

The cabinet structure is suitable for both vertical and horizontal positions.
