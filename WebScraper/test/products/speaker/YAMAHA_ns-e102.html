  • YAMAHA NS-E102

  • ¥ 20,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1992)

Commentary

A slim-type speaker system that combines ease of use and sound field expression as an effect speaker.

It uses a 10 cm cone woofer that can be used in the full range for the low band and a 3 cm cone tweeter for the high band.

It is compact, lightweight and thin with a weight of 1.6 kg and a thickness of 74 mm. It can be mounted freely on a wall or on a rack.
In addition to the standard speaker stand, it is also compatible with a variety of commercially available speaker holders.
