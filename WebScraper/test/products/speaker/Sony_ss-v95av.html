  • SONY SS-V95AV

  • ¥ 11,000 (1 unit, around 1989)

Commentary

A small speaker system developed as a surround speaker.

A 10 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 2 cm dome type tweeter using titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

It is a magnetic resistant design, and even if it is placed on the side of a cathode-ray tube TV, the image will not be disturbed.

The separately sold stand allows for free setting.
