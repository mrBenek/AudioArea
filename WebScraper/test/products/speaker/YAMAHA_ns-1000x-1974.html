  • YAMAHA NS-1000X

  • Not released (around 1974)

Commentary

This is a speaker system that appears to be the prototype of the NS-1000M that appeared in the pre-release catalog of the NS-1000M.
The model number was NS-1000X at that time.

The unit configuration is a squaker / tweeter using 30 cm cone type woofer and beryllium diaphragm, and there is no difference from the NS-1000M.

Comparing the photos with the NS-1000M, there are differences such as no protective net attached to the woofer unit, no YAMAHA name plate, and the level control is attached above the NS-1000M.
