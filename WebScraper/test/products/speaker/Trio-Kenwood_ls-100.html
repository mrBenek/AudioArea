Commentary

A speaker system that reduces internal loss of the diaphragm and pursues more accurate reproduction sound.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
As the diaphragm, we have adopted a diaphragm with a rib made of the same material on a thermal shock treeed cone made by high heat molding on cone paper. This realizes a high rigidity woofer exceeding the limit strength of cone paper, and achieves more faithful music reproduction by suppressing inner loss.

A 4-cm flat tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit uses a plane radiator made of acrylic resin to improve frequency response and power response by increasing the strength of the diaphragm.

In order to reduce mutual interference in the network section, the network coil is placed remotely to reduce electrical crosstalk.

The enclosure uses baffle plates with separate structures for the tweeter and woofer sections to reduce vibration crosstalk and reduce mutual interference between units.
