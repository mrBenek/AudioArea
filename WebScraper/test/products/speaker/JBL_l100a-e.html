  • JBL L100A

  • ¥ 147,000 (1 unit, around 1978)

Commentary

A speaker system whose unit has been redesigned from the L100 Century.

A 30-cm cone type unit, 123A-3, is used for the low range.
It uses an Alnico V magnet, a heavyweight magnetic circuit, a 7.6 cm diameter voice coil, and cone paper coated with JBL's original dumping material.

13 cm diameter LE5-2 is used for the middle area.

LE25, a 3.6 cm diameter cone-shaped unit, is used for the high-frequency band.

The front grill uses a special material developed by JBL that has good transparency and directivity.
