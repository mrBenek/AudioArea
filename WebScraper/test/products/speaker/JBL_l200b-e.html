Commentary

A two way speaker system based on the specifications of professional studio monitors.

Equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer 136A in the low range.
In this unit, a mass control ring made of die cast is attached to the voice coil so that the piston motion of the cone paper is integrated with the voice coil, and stable operation can be performed even at large amplitude.

A horn-type unit combining LE85 and HL91 is installed in the high range.
The LE85 uses an ultra-thin aluminum foil diaphragm, a magnetic circuit using an Alnico V magnet, and an aluminum ribbon wire edgewise wound voice coil to obtain high efficiency and excellent transient characteristics.
By combining this with the HL91 horn lens, which has 90 ° horizontal and 45 ° vertical directional characteristics, we are able to obtain mid-range sound with a rich sense of presence.

The enclosure uses a sloped front panel design similar to the L300.
The front grille uses air foam with excellent sound transparency and comes in four color variations.
