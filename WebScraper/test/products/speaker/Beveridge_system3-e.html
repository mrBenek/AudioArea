  • BEVERIDGE SYSTEM 3

  • ¥ 1,600,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1980)

Commentary

This is an electrostatic speaker system with a vivid symmetrical sound system.

By omitting the amplifier system, it is possible to drive by an external amplifier.

The unit is equipped with an electrostatic unit with a 180-degree diffusion acoustic lens and two 10-inch cone type woofers for low frequency.
The woofer is enclosed in an internal enclosure.

In addition to the standard single amplifier method, the drive method can be a bi-amplifier method using the built-in network or a bi-amplifier method without the built-in network.
