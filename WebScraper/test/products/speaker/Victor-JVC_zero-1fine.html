Commentary

This small speaker system was developed by condensing the Zero concept of W & D (Wide & Dynamic).

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.
With the sealed edge structure unique to the Zero Series, the woofer is a dulfer cone type that cuts out the distortion component from the edge. The thickness distribution of the cone is reconsidered to correspond to the relatively wide use band of 2-way.
In addition, a large magnetic circuit of strontium ferrite magnet is adopted.

A 6 cm compound type tweeter is installed in the high range.
After deep drawing a 30 μ m thick high-purity titanium foil, a marbled porous fine ceramic material is formed on both surfaces using Victor's unique technology. This changes the surface of the titanium itself to a gold fine ceramic material with a 3-layer structure.

The enclosure is a bass reflex type with symmetrical left and right channels.
