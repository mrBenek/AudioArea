  • CANTON Karat20

Commentary

A bookshelf 2-way speaker system that inherits the know-how of Karat40.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range and a 2.5 cm titanium dome type tweeter is mounted in the high range.

6 mm thick for the speaker terminal2Large terminals are used for speaker cables.

Felt is attached to the front baffle of the enclosure to improve sound quality by absorbing the sound reflected by the tweeter.
The exterior has a mahogany finish.
