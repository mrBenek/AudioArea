Commentary

A speaker system developed by combining Infiniti's technologies.
The product name IRS stands for Infinity Reference Standard.

The IRS consists of four cabinets and is a large system with a height of 2.29m and a total weight of 544.8 kg.

The mid - and high-range enclosure contains 24 EMIT and 12 bi-directional EMIMs on the front and 12 on the back, while the low-range enclosure contains six 30 cm cone woofers.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a unique dual voice coil system to solve the problem of low resonance frequency (fo). This structure uses two voice coils with different impedance. In principle, when the first voice coil approaches fo, the second voice coil gradually takes over and flattens the frequency characteristics.
In addition, polypropylene is used for the diaphragm.

EMIM (Electromagnetic Induction Mid Range) is mounted in the middle range.
This unit is bi-directional with an ultra-lightweight plastic diaphragm sandwiched between strong magnetic materials such as samarium-cobalt.

EMIT (Electromagnetic Induction Tweeter) is installed in the high range.
Similar to the EMIM, it is a full-surface drive type made of samarium-cobalt lightweight plastic diaphragm sandwiched by samarium-cobalt, and realizes ultra-high frequency reproduction up to 32 kHz.
