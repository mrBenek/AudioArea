Commentary

A speaker system developed to meet the expectations of all music fans and audiophiles and to keep the price as low as possible.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit was newly designed by ADVENT, and by adopting a new method of outer suspension, it improves the lack of teeth at the start and the heavy low range which was considered as a problem of the conventional airtight type.

A cone-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit has its own high radiation effect in the high-frequency range and its unique free-moving suspension improves the mid-range capability, and is designed to cover a wide range with a crossover of 1 kHz.

The network section is equipped with a 3-step change-over type high-frequency balance switch, and the level can be controlled according to the condition of the program source.

The enclosure is fully enclosed.
The front grille is removable.
