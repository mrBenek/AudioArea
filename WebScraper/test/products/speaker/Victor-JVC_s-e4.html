  • VICTOR/JVC S-E4

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 unit, around 1982)

Commentary

A compact speaker system developed by inheriting the W & D philosophy of the Zero Series.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range and a 5 cm tweeter with a special film cone is used for the high range.

The enclosure has a metallic gray finish and can be installed vertically or horizontally.
