Commentary

SR series 2-way speaker system.

It can be used for a wide range of applications such as main speaker systems in small and medium halls, main PA systems, side fills, and rehearsal studios.

Two 30-cm cone Woofers 2206H are installed in the low range, and a horn-type unit is installed in the high range, combining a Horn 2381 with a 90 ° horizontal and 50 ° vertical directional characteristic and a driver unit 2447J.

It has a built-in network.

Equipped with a multi-amplifier drive switch.
