Commentary

As a speaker for the digital age, this 3-way speaker system is based on pure cross carbon woofer.

A 22 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
The diaphragm has a 2-layer structure consisting of pure cross carbon and pure carbon mat, which provides an excellent balance of strength, weight and internal loss, and improves resolution.

A 10 cm cone type unit has been adopted for the middle area.
Delta olefin refined cone is used for the diaphragm.

The high-frequency range is 2.5 cm. In order to solve the high-frequency resonance peak, which was a problem of the highly rigid tweeter, we have adopted a dome-type unit that cancels the high-frequency resonance.
Super hard titanium is used for the diaphragm.

By adopting a round baffle for the enclosure, diffraction phenomenon is suppressed, and by the technology which eliminates unnecessary vibration of the cabinet, the focus of sound image is made more vivid.
