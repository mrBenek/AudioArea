  • ONKYO D-102AXLTD

  • ¥ 58,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

A speaker system that is a limited model of the D-102AX.

It has a 13 cm duocroscone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type with MDF material.
In addition, it has a lustrous finish with real wood.

The speaker terminal uses a banana plug terminal that is gold-plated brass machined.

A speaker cable XPHP made by Monster is included as an accessory.
