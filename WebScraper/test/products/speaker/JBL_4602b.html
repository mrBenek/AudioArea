Commentary

This is a Cabaret series speaker system developed for sound reinforcement and musical instruments.

The 4602B is designed for a stage monitor.

The low range is equipped with E120 which is a 30 cm cone type woofer.
In addition, a horn type unit 2402H using a ring diaphragm is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure of the enclosure can be changed from 30 ° to 60 °.
It is made of 19 mm thick hard multi-layer plywood and has a polyurethane coating on the entire surface to ensure high strength. In addition, it is equipped with a corner guard that is useful when using the stack and a handle for transportation.

A level control is provided for the high-frequency band.

The input terminal uses an M6 phone jack.

There was a special front cover sold separately.
