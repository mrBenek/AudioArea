  • VICTOR/JVC BLA-5/BLA-Z5

  • ¥ 39,800 (a set of two cars, around 1971)

Commentary

A wide-directional speaker system developed for the rear of a 4-channel system.

The BLA-5/BLA-Z5 is designed to mount units of the same configuration on two sides of a square pillar.
As a result, it can be used in a state where it is pressed tightly against a corner, and a rich sound field is realized.

Two 12 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
Uses concave roll-free edges and KP cones.

Two 7-cm cone type tweeters are installed in the high range.

The structure of the enclosure is based on a bass reflex system.

This speaker system has two variations in finish.
BLA-5 has a walnut finish and BLA-Z5 has a rosewood finish.
