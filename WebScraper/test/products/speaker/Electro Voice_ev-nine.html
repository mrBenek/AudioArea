Commentary

3-way bookshelf speaker system.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The mid-range is equipped with a newly developed 13 cm cone mid-range.

A 9 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses an acoustic suspension system and the mid-range unit has a back chamber to eliminate mutual interference between units.
The exterior has a walnut oil finish.
