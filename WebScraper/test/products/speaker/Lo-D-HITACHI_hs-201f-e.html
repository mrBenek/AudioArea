Commentary

A speaker system developed by inheriting the design policy of HS-500.

A 20 cm free edge cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The H-54H horn type tweeter with aluminum die-cast finish is installed in the high range.

The ダンプドバスレフ system is used for the enclosure.
In addition, the exterior has a four sided walnut and open pore finish.

Equipped with a high-frequency level control for 3-step adjustment.
