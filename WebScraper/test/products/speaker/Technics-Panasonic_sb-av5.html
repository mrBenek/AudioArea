Commentary

A toll-boy speaker system developed for AV systems.

Two 16-cm cone woofers are installed in the lower range, and two 16-cm flat passive radiators are installed in the rear to achieve powerful bass playback.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using a silk dome is installed in the high range.

Each unit has a double magnet type anti-magnetic design.

The tall boy enclosure improves orientation by placing woofers above and below the tweeter.
In addition, a die-cast base is attached as a stand.
