Commentary

A speaker system based on the Model2130, an JBL professional unit.

The unit is equipped with Model2130 which is a 30 cm cone type full range unit.
The magnetic circuit uses a 102 mm φ large diameter edge-wound voice coil and a high-efficiency large magnet.

Carefully selected materials are used for the enclosure. High-density particle boards for sound are used for the side plates, top plates and bottom plates, etc., Scandinavian birch plywoods are used for the front baffle, and Yonematsu is used for the reinforcement.
In addition, sufficient reinforcement is provided by adopting a hozo structure for mounting the baffle plate and a spline structure and angle block for joining the other plates.

The exterior has a walnut oil finish, and the front grill uses handmade Shippo kumiko made of natural cypress wood for a classy finish.

It is a 2-way system with an enclosure structure that can be easily upgraded.
Examples of basic combinations of upgrades included the Model2410 Driver, Model2305 Horn and Model3120 Network Model, and the Model2405 Tweeter and Model3105 Network Model.
