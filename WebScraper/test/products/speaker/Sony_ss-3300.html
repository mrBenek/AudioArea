Commentary

A high-end speaker system designed to achieve the superior transient characteristics of a transistor amplifier.

It is composed of 3 ways, and 30 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
This woofer uses a special structure on the edge to keep the fo low, and a new treatment on the corn paper to realize a sound with good damping.
A 12-cm cone type unit is used for the middle band and a 5-cm horn type unit is used for the high band.

An LC-type 12dB/oct. network is used.
In order to realize the advantages of LC type, L with low DC resistance, air core type is used for L, and MP capacitor is used for C.
In addition, in case you need to adjust the sound quality depending on the shape of the room or the position of the speakers, the level of the tweeter and skoker can be changed to three levels with a switch.
In addition, the LC network can be removed by switching the knob, and a multi-channel system using a woofer, a squaker and a tweeter independently is possible.

The enclosure uses a unique Lumber core with a thickness of 25 mm, and the surface is finished with top-grade Eurasian cheek.
In addition, black special woven fabric is used for the net in the front.
