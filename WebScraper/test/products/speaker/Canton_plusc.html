  • CANTON Plus C

  • ¥ 80,000 (1 unit, released in 1988)

Commentary

Cubic Super Base System / Plus series sub-woofer system.

A 31 cm cone woofer with double voice coil structure is installed as a low-frequency unit.

It has built-in band division for systemization and a network function to distribute middle and high sound to left and right speakers.
The output sound pressure level is set to a value that balances the middle and low range when two Plus S or Plus D units on the left and right are combined.

The exterior comes in two color variations, black and white.
