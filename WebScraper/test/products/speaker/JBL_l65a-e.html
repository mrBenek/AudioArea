Commentary

A floor-type speaker system designed to provide more accurate reproduction from low to ultra-high frequencies.

Equipped with 30 cm cone type woofer 122A in the low range.
This unit is made of ribbed corn paper with optimum mass and stiffness. In addition, a mass control ring is attached to the voice coil to realize stable and accurate piston motion even at large amplitude.

13 cm cone type Skoka LE5-5 is mounted in the middle area.
The powerful magnetic circuit enhances the vocal band reproduction capability.

A ring radiator 077 is installed in the high range.
This unit combines a ring-shaped diaphragm with a strong magnetic circuit to achieve high-efficiency high-frequency reproduction, and a uniquely shaped horn with both exponential and dual directional characteristics to diffuse at 20 kHz with a broad directional characteristic of 110 ゜ x40 ゜.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

The exterior is designed with smoke De Grasse on the top, and the front grill comes in 3 color variations.
