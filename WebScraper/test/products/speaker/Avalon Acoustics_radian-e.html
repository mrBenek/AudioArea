  • AVALON RADIAN

  • ¥ 1,800,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in October 1993)

Commentary

A large floor type 3-way 4-speaker system with a unique style.

A 9 cm cone type unit is used for the double woofer and the squaker, the enclosure is chamfered to minimize the baffle area, and the baffle surface is a laminated baffle made of 13 cm compression material with high rigidity.
