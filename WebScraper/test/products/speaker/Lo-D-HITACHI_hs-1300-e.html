Commentary

A floor-type speaker system equipped with a large-diameter gathered edge woofer.

Equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer L-380 in the low range.
This unit has the largest diameter among units using a ギャザードエッジ, and is equipped with a Lo-D actuator to reduce distortion.

The mid-range has 2 6 cm cone mid-range units.
A sandwich cone is used for the diaphragm of this unit, and dispersion is improved by shifting the angle to the outside.

A 2.2 cm dome type tweeter using an aluminum alloy diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

Aluminum die-cast frames are used in all units to eliminate unnecessary vibration.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.
