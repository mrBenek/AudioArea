Commentary

A column-type speaker system for Musical Instruments, developed for small stages such as small halls and night clubs.

By taking advantage of the single-directional sound pressure distribution, the howling to the microphone can be kept extremely low.

The unit is equipped with 6 20 cm cone type full range units 403A.
