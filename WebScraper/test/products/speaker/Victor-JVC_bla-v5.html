Commentary

3-way bookshelf speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is a high compliance type using cross roll edge and soft damper, and low fo and excellent transient characteristics are realized by magnetic circuit using 4-layer wound voice coil and wet ba-ferrite magnet.

Equipped with 12 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.
The corrugation edge with low reverse resonance and high Young's modulus cone paper enable smooth medium range reproduction.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Directivity is improved by high Young's modulus cone paper and diffuser.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
