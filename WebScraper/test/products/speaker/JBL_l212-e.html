Commentary

A speaker system developed in pursuit of new possibilities for music playback.

The L212 is a set of three speakers and consists of two 3-way wide-range systems and an ultrabass system that plays back very low frequencies below 70 Hz.

The Ultra Bus System is equipped with 30 cm cone type woofer 121A.
The UltraBus system also has a dedicated monaural main amplifier mounted in a dedicated enclosure.

The 3-way wide range system is equipped with a 20 cm cone Woofer 112A using a 7.6 cm large diameter voice coil in the low band, a 13 cm cone Squaker LE5-9 in the middle band, and a 2.5 cm dome Tweeter 066 in the high band.
In addition, the enclosure has improved phase characteristics by arranging three units vertically in a row, and realizes more natural sound image localization.
