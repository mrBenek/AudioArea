Commentary

A speaker system that employs a woofer direct drive based on networkless technology.

A 14 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
It has an appropriate internal loss, uses a cone that is smoothly attenuated without stretching the band, and matches the in-band energy characteristics and on-axis characteristics in the full range, enabling crossover with the tweeter without using any network elements.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
It uses a high-flux density magnetic circuit with a narrow magnetic gap, and by filling it with magnetic fluid, power linearity and coupling with the filter are improved.
A catenary diaphragm made of silk is used for the diaphragm.

As for the network, the woofer side is a networkless direct drive, and only a condenser is connected to the tweeter, and loss due to network elements is adopted.

The enclosure has a 6-sided brown maple finish.
High-density MDF material is used for the baffle.
