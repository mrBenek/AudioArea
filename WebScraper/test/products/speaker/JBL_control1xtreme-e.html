  • Discretionary price (2 sets, around 2002)

Commentary

A speaker system from JBL's Control1 series of compact speakers.

For the lower range, a 10 cm cone type woofer that uses a polylaminated cone made of pulp cone laminated with a high-polymer composite material, and for the higher range, a balance drive type tweeter with laminated dome is adopted.

Due to its magnetic protection design, it can be installed on the side of TV or PC monitor.

The network is equipped with a protector to protect the unit from excessive input.

The enclosure has a bass reflex design and uses a high-strength mold cabinet.
It also uses a mesh grill with enhanced sound transparency and strength.

By using brackets sold separately, it can be attached to walls and ceiling surfaces.
