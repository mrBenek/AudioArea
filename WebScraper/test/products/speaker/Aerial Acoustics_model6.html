Commentary

A speaker system that uses high-quality parts that are also used in large systems.

A 18 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit was newly designed for the Model6 and features a large, breathable magnet, 40 mm Longs low coil and cast magnesium frame for superior bass playback.

The mid-range is equipped with the 12.7 cm cone type mid-range which is also used in the Model CC3.

In the high range, it is equipped with 10T which is an original 2.5 cm dome tweeter of AREAL.

The network section is assembled on two large glass-epoxy circuit boards with carefully selected parts and wires.

A bass-reflex system is used for the enclosure, and the front port allows installation close to the wall surface.
The overall size is compact with a limited width, and the interior is fully reinforced to reduce coloring. In addition, 2-inch thick members are used on two sides, and middle and high areas are mounted in an independent chamber filled with wool to eliminate coloring completely.

There are 5 variations in the finish of the exterior.

Bi-wired connection is supported.
