Commentary

This is a speaker system that uses the P. P. D. D. a direct drive tweeter.

The woofer uses DENON's unique P. P. D. D method. One edge of a pair of woofers is mounted in the opposite direction to achieve low-frequency playback with less distortion.

The Super Tweeter uses a direct drive system and achieves ultra-high bandwidth playback up to 90 kHz.

In order to ensure the best listening area and obtain uniform sound pressure / phase response, a new crossover network has been developed and directivity has been improved.

The enclosure uses a veneer glossy paint finish.

Uses a large brass machined gold-plated terminal for biwiring and banana plug.
