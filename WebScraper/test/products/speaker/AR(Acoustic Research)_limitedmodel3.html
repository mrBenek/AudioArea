  • AR(Acoustic Research) Limited Model 3

  • ¥ 1,300,000 (a set of two cars, around 1994)

Commentary

A speaker system of the Limited series which is the AR 40th anniversary model.

A 30 cm cone type woofer with a polypropylene laminate structure is mounted in the low range.
This unit is mounted in an enclosure using an acoustic suspension system.

The mid-range has two 7.5 cm soft domed mid-range units, and the high-range has a 2.5 cm shifted domed tweeter.
These units are mounted on the symmetrical radiation array (SRA) d-E Plain baffle to reduce the interference of irregular sound from the front baffle and achieve uniform directional characteristics.
