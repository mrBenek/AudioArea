Commentary

This speaker system is the culmination of Lordi technology at that time. It was developed with the goal of giving top priority to performance and eliminating all cost constraints.

Equipped with 30 cm cone type woofer L-301 in the low range.
The cone section is a sandwich metal cone with ギャザードエッジ on the edge. In addition, the Lo-D actuator reduces distortion and the voice coil heat sink flattens impedance characteristics.

The middle area is equipped with a composite unit M-60 using an aluminum alloy sailor cap type diaphragm.

A horn type tweeter H-35H using a cascade horn is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure is finished with American cypress.
