  • BRAUN L620/1

  • ¥ 75,500 (1 unit, around 1974)

Commentary

A sealed 3-way bookshelf speaker system.

It is equipped with a 21 cm cone type woofer for the low range, a 5 cm soft dome type scoker for the middle range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter for the high range.

There were 3 types of exterior finish, walnut, black and white finish.
The front panel has an anodized aluminum finish and the aluminum punching metal is removable.

There was a connection cord with DIN standard connector LK9C sold separately.
