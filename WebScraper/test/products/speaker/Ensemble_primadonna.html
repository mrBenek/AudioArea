Commentary

A floor-type speaker system with a new enclosure and network while using the unit used in REFERENCE.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm of this unit has a completely conical shape without a center cap, eliminating the distortion caused by the front chamber effect in the cone diaphragm.

A 1.9 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The inclined front baffle reduces the phase difference and time difference between units, and the large arc front baffle prevents acoustic reflection at the baffle diffraction point.

The enclosure had two finishes : a Swiss cherry finish and a Piano Black finish.
