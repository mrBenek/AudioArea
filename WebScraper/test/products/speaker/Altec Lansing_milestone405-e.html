Commentary

Speaker system using full-range unit 405-8H.

The 405 to 8 Hz is a 4 inch (10 cm) full-range unit of the 405 Series. The high output sound pressure of 90dB/W/m is achieved by the magnetic circuit using urethane foam edge, cast frame and large magnet in the simple configuration of single cone / full range.

In order to take advantage of the mid-range of 405 to 8 h, it is designed so that the unit and the amplifier can be connected directly only by the connector without any intervening passive network circuit for adjusting the characteristics.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex structure using 12 mm thick Itaya amicorum maple laminate, and the exterior is covered with wood and layers.
