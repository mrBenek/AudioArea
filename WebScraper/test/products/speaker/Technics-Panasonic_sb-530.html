Commentary

Fully omnidirectional speaker system.

A high compliance type 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The middle area is equipped with a 9 cm dome type skoker which is an improvement of the dome type unit of SB-500.

The EAS-5HH17, a 5 cm horn type tweeter, is installed in the high range.

It is equipped with continuously variable level control for middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure is a sealed enclosure with a unique design unique to the non-directional type.
In addition, there were two types of exterior finishes, white finish and walnut oil finish.
