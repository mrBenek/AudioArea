  • Marantz LS-60II

  • ¥ 50,000 (a set of two cars, around 1986)

Commentary

This is an anti-magnetic speaker system that uses a magnet shield system with double magnets.

A 20 cm cone woofer with a newly developed special rubber edge is installed in the low range, a 5 cm cone tweeter in the high range, and a 1.5 cm dome super tweeter in the ultra high range.

The enclosure uses a bass-reflex structure.
