Commentary

This is a floor-type speaker system that uses a multi-pipe duct system to achieve rich bass playback and mid-range playback.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range, and a 16 cm cone squaker and horn squaker are installed in the middle range.
A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure has a bass reflex design with a multi-pipe duct system, and the use of multiple ducts improves the balance between the low and middle ranges.
The exterior features a walnut polyurethane finish.

An input terminal for multi-amplifier is mounted.
