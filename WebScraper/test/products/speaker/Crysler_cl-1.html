Commentary

This is a CL series speaker system that employs a newly developed group of units.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit has a free vibration of fo = 18 Hz, an enclosure mounted fo = 50 Hz, and a frequency characteristic of 40 Hz to 7 kHz ± 3 dB.

The mid-range is equipped with a free edge inverted dome-shaped squaker using a 5 cm duralumin diaphragm.

A 5-cm horn type tweeter with a diffuser is installed in the high range.
This unit is a small-diameter horn type but a broadband type with a back cavity and diffuser.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
