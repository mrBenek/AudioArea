  • MISSION 737

  • ¥ 196,000 (a set of two cars, around 1983)

Commentary

Two way speaker system.

A 21.4 cm cone type woofer made of polypropylene cone is mounted in the low range.
In addition, a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter with a fabric dome diaphragm is installed in the high frequency range.

The terminal part is designed to be connected by 4 mm plug or wire.

There are 2 color variations, black and walnut.
