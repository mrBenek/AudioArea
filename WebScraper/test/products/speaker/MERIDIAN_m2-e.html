Audio Database

  • MERIDIAN M2

  • ¥ 298,000 (around 1985)

Commentary

Slim style speaker system with 2 power amplifiers and integrated electronic crossover network.

Two 11 cm cone type woofers are used for the low range, and a 5 cm dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

There were nine types of enclosure finishes and a stand was included.
