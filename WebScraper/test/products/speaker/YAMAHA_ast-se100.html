  • YAMAHA AST-SE100

Commentary

A speaker system exclusively for the DSP series that has been developed to be more enjoyable than the DSP 107 AST / DSP 105 AST of the Yamaha DSP series.

Yamaha Active Servo Technology enables low-frequency playback at 45 Hz from an A4-size 7.4 cm thick.

A 10 cm full range unit is adopted for the unit.
