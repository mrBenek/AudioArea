Commentary

A micro-speaker system developed with sound performance and design that matches store use.

There are 2 color variations, 161W is white and 161B is black.

The speaker unit is equipped with two 6-cm cone micro-drivers developed exclusively for small high-performance speaker technology.
By adopting a frameless structure in which a vibration system is incorporated directly into the sub-baffle, the diaphragm and magnetic circuit are expanded to the limit, and by using a powerful piston with large amplitude, the linear motion speed of the cone is increased, which makes it possible to achieve excellent low-frequency reproduction despite its small size.

Oyster shell structure is used to assemble the front baffle and the rear of the cabinet seamlessly. High-speed motion generated from high frequency perfectly joins the joint part at the molecular level to realize a completely airtight monocoque body and eliminate unnecessary resonance and box rumbling.

The unique round form is designed based on Bose's Stereo Everyware theory to create an ideal balance between indirect and direct sound. The cross firing is achieved by placing two micro-drivers at a horizontal angle of 45 ° and symmetrically facing inward.
This articulated array design creates a wide listening area in the interior space and realizes a uniform sound field regardless of where you listen.

In order to be able to use it in combination with a TV or display, it is provided with anti-magnetic specification using both cancel magnet system and shield system.

Snap-in type speaker terminal is adopted.

Equipped as standard with a dedicated wall-hanging bracket that can adjust the elevation angle in 7 stages up to a maximum of 45 degrees at a pitch interval of 15 degrees up and down.

There was a ceiling hanging bracket sold separately.
