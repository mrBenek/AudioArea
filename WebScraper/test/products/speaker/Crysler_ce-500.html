  • CHRYSLER CE-500

  • ¥ 35,000 (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

New CE series 3-way bookshelf type speaker system.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 12 cm cone squaker is installed in the middle range.
A 5 cm horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Equipped with level control.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
