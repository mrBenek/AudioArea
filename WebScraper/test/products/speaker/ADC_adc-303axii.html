Commentary

This speaker system was selected as BEST BUY along with Dynaco A-25 in the test of "Consumer Report" magazine in 1970.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 4 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.

Equipped with 2-step level control for both mid-range and high-range.

The enclosure uses an acoustic suspension system.
Also, the exterior has oil walnut finish.
