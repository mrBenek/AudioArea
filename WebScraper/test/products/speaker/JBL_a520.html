Commentary

This is a Vecchio series speaker system with a magnetic protection design.

In the Vecchio series, sound quality tuning with audibility called Voicing is thoroughly applied, and the ability to reproduce the impression and fine nuance of music is enhanced.

A 12.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a newly designed polymer-coated fiber cone, and the pulp-based diaphragm is polymer-coated to achieve both sufficient strength and moderate internal loss.
In addition, the center of the drive system is clarified by adopting a rigid centering bolt.

A 2 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Pure titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm. Neodymium magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

The enclosure uses a front bass reflex system.
In addition, the exterior has been given a real wood finish that makes use of the texture of natural wood. In the A520, only the baffle has a two tone specifications with a different wood grain.
