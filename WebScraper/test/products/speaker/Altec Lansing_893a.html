Commentary

A relatively compact bookshelf speaker system developed as part of the new bookshelf series.

A newly developed 25 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range.
A 7.5 cm cone-type tweeter (direct radiator) is installed in the high range.

The network is equipped with a 3-dB-step high-frequency attenuator.

The cabinet uses a sealed system.
