Commentary

This is a U-series bookshelf type speaker system that uses a horn in the middle and high range.

The low-range is equipped with a high-compliance type 30 cm cone woofer.
This unit uses Finnish pulp and aluminum foil bobbins.

The middle and high frequencies are equipped with a sectoral-shaped horn-type unit, which reduces the restriction of listening position and obtains broad directional characteristics.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
