Commentary

A speaker system with a 10 cm unit and power amplifier.

The unit is equipped with 2 10 cm cone type full range units.

It has a built-in power amplifier with two line and microphone inputs and volume adjustment volume.
It is also equipped with a 2-step tone quality control equalizer switch.

A bass reflex system is used for the enclosure and special reinforced synthetic resin is used for the material.

Stacking is available.
