Commentary

Speaker system with JBL professional units.

The unit is equipped with Model2120 which is a 25 cm cone type full range unit.

The enclosure is specially developed for the Model2120 and uses a bass reflex system.
The front baffle is made of Scandinavian cherry wood plywood, which has good plate vibration, to prevent unnecessary natural vibration. In addition, a reinforcing rod is used between the front and rear baffles. The connection between the side plate and top plate is made with a hollow frame structure and a spline structure. In addition, by using angle block, the structure is strong and durable enough to be used for many years.

The exterior is finished with walnut oil. In addition, the front grill uses handmade Shippo kumiko made of natural cypress wood for an elegant finish.
It is a 2-way system with an enclosure structure that can be easily upgraded. It can be converted into a 2-way speaker system by combining with JBL Pro Tweeter Model 2405 and Network Model 3105.
In addition, remove the screen for tweeter of the front baffle and the screen for network of the rear baffle before installation.
