Commentary

This speaker system is the top-of-the-line in the Interface series.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is mounted downward based on the theory of optimal phase inversion.

The mid-range is equipped with 16 cm cone type mid-range.
The magnetic circuit of this unit uses a huge 7.3 kg magnet assembly, same as the woofer.

A radial horn type tweeter with a radiation angle of 110 degrees or more in the ultra-high band of 16 kHz is mounted in the high band.
Built-in protection circuit for the tweeter.

SEQ equalizer is included.
