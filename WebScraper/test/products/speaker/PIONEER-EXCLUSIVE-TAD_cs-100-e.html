  • Pioneer CS-100

  • ¥ 96,000 (around 1971)

Commentary

Large floor type 3-way speaker system.

A 38 cm cone type woofer with a large magnetic circuit is installed in the low range.
In addition, two 16 cm cone type mid-range are used in parallel for the mid-range.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.
This unit is equivalent to the PT-7, which was sold separately. The directional characteristics are improved by the square exponential horn made of aluminum die-cast. The diaphragm is made of aluminum light alloy to reduce the weight of the vibration system.

It is equipped with 3-step switching type level control for middle and high frequencies.
This level control allows you to select from three levels : Increase, Normal, and Decrease.

The enclosure has a completely sealed structure using a 30 mm thick plate material to prevent sound quality degradation due to resonance, etc.
The exterior is finished with oil of natural wood Brazilian Rosewood.
