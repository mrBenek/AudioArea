  • ¥ 56,000 (a set of two cars, around 1981)

Commentary

A speaker system equipped with a ring-type tweeter.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
カーブドコーン is used for the diaphragm to realize high input resistance.
A 3 cm ring type tweeter is installed in the high range.
A titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and a magnetic fluid is used to achieve high input resistance, wide bandwidth and low distortion.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

There was a speaker stand as an option sold separately.
