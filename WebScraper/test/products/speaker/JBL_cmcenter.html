Commentary

Center speaker of Control CM series.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
The diaphragm is made of poly-laminated cone, which is made of pulp-based cone laminated with high-polymer composite material to realize a lightweight, highly rigid diaphragm with moderate internal loss.

A dome-shaped tweeter using a titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
