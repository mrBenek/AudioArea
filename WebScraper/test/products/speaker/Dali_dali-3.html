  • DALI DALI-3

  • ¥ 100,000 (one set of two cars, around 1986)

Commentary

Bookshelf speaker system with 2 way design.

A 20.3 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter using a polypropylene dome is mounted in the high frequency range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The plate material is 5/8 inch thick and compressed to high density, and the exterior is finished with Danish beach finish.

The setting is designed to show its ability at a place over 50 cm above the floor.
