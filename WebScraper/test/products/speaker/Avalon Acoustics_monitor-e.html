Commentary

The main feature of this system is that it is designed to fully suppress resonance and resonance of the enclosure and to fully bring out the original performance of the unit.

For example, in order to suppress reflection by the baffle surface, the width of the baffle surface is made as small as possible, and the baffle surface is made as thick as possible. As with other models, Caldas cable, which is resistant to resonance, is used for internal wiring.
