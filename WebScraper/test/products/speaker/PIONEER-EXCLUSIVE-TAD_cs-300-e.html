  • Pioneer CS-300

  • ¥ 10,000 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

Compact and easy to use 20 cm 2-way speaker system.

A 20 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 6.6 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.
Pioneer's original FB cone is used for the diaphragm of these units.

The enclosure is fully enclosed and has a walnut wood finish on the exterior.

2-way multi-amplifier drive is also possible by switching the switch on the back.
