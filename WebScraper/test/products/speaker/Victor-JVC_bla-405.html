Commentary

The KP series speaker system was developed with the aim of being a speaker that is not inferior to or superior to the world famous instruments.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The cone paper of this unit uses newly developed KP-3. This diaphragm is made with new fibers and a special material processing method using a vacuum press method. It features dense fibers and a high Young's modulus. In addition, both sides of the edge are coated with dump paint.
A 140 φ ferrite magnet is used for the magnetic circuit, and a 4-layer voice coil is used for the voice coil, eliminating the deterioration of transients caused by the reproduction of deep bass.

It is equipped with a 16 cm cone type squaker in the middle range and a 5 cm cone type tweeter in the high range.
The cone of the medium and high range unit uses KP-3 mixed with chemical fiber as a base, and gives a bright tone by giving moderate internal loss.

A multi-sellerhorn type super-tweeter is installed in the ultra-high frequency range in order to perform sufficient high-frequency diffusion.

We use conifer for the front baffle and back plate of the enclosure. Conifer has coarse and dense parts in the material, and the propagation speed of sound differs between the flat grain direction and the cross direction. Therefore, we use seven layers of conifer to compensate for the difference in speed, and combine it with a propagation rod called a sound stick (spruce material) to improve musical performance.
We have also increased the sound absorption coefficient by using a newly developed sound absorbing material that is specially processed from animal fiber (55% wool) and chemical fiber (45% ester), both of which contain complex cellulose.

Equipped with 2ch/3ch terminals for multi-amplifier.

Equipped with a level control on the front, you can adjust the tweeter and the squaker respectively.
