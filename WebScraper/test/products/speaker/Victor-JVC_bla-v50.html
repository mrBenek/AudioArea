  • VICTOR/JVC BLA-V50

  • ¥ 50,000 (a set of two cars, around 1971)

Commentary

A full-surface omnidirectional floor-type speaker system developed for 4-channel sound field reproduction.

It uses an omni-direction system ideal for 4-channel sound field reproduction.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
It uses a magnetic circuit and convex roll-free edge specially designed for the BLA-V50, and the diaphragm is made of newly developed KDU pulp cone paper made in Germany.
This unit is mounted upward in the middle of the square column and emits sound in all 360 degrees directions through a diffuser.

Four 9 cm cone type tweeters are mounted in the high range and are arranged on all four sides of the square pillar.

The enclosure has a natural cheek finish.
