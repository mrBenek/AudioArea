  • VICTOR/JVC S-E50

  • ¥ 14,000 (1 unit, around 1988)

Commentary

A bass reflex type 2-way speaker system.

It uses a 20 cm cone woofer for the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter for the high range.

The enclosure is made of wood for better sound and the unit layout is on the center line of the baffle to prevent phase disturbance.
