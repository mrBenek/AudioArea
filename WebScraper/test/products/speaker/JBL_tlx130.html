Commentary

This is the top 2-way type speaker system of the TLX series developed so that you can enjoy music with your favorite sound and the size that fits your room.

A 21 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
High-rigidity paper pulp and corn paper made of natural materials are used for the diaphragm, suppressing split vibration that causes distortion and exhibiting excellent characteristics.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm uses a titanium-laminated soft dome, which combines the characteristics of the titanium material that can be reproduced up to the high-end beyond the audible band and the good consistency with the low-frequency unit unique to the soft dome.

The enclosure has a calm design with a furniture finish.
The internal system uses a bass reflex system, and the bass reflex port is located on the back side to eliminate the muddy sound in the middle and low range.

The speaker terminals are located on the bottom of the rear baffle so that the speaker cable can be routed cleanly and with a minimum length.
