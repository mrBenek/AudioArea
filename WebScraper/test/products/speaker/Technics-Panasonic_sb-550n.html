  • National SB-550N

  • ¥ 6,000 (1 unit, released in December 1978)

Commentary

A drone cone speaker system developed for TV sound multiplex broadcasting.

The unit is equipped with a 16 cm diameter full range cone type unit.
In addition, low-frequency playback is improved by installing a 16 cm Doron Cone with the same aperture.

It is dark brown color, and the see-through net of the same color is used for the sub RUNNET.
