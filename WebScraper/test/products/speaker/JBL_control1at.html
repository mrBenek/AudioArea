  • ¥ 60,000 (a set of two cars, around 1992)

Commentary

Speaker system corresponding to the variation model of control 1.

Control 1 at has a built-in auto-transformer.
The auto transformer is a tap switching type.

Various mounting brackets for Control1 can be used.
