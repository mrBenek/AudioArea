Commentary

A three way speaker system using a multi-seller horn.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The cone paper uses FB cone produced by a unique manufacturing method, and the magnetic circuit uses large ferrite magnet to improve low-frequency characteristics with a loose design.

The mid-range has a 12 cm cone type mid-range.
This unit uses a FB cone and ferrite magnet similar to the woofer, and the rear part of the cone is completely sealed to prevent back pressure from the woofer.

A multi-celler-type horn type tweeter is installed in the high range, and the clear high range is reproduced with a wide range.

The front baffle is equipped with level control.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed, and the exterior features a walnut oil finish that takes advantage of natural wood.

Multi-amplifier terminal is also mounted on the input terminal on the back, and 2-way or 3-way multi-amplifier system is possible by switching the switch.
