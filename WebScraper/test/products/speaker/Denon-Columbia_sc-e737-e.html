Commentary

Aiming for a relaxing music space, this is a speaker system of the European Sound Series that sought materials and sound making technology from Europe.

In the lower range, it is equipped with 14.5 cmCSC (Composite Sandwich Cone) woofer laminated with two types of polypropylene materials to achieve an ideal combination of rigidity and internal loss.
In addition, a port is installed at the rear of the frame to reduce the mutual interference of back pressure. In addition, new technologies such as a newly developed flat damper and a non-reflective dust cap are introduced to achieve higher-quality bass reproduction.

A 2.5 cm constant directional WA (Wide Angle) tweeter is Wide Angle) tweeter in order to obtain more natural sound image localization and sound field feeling.
In this unit, the front plate with a slight inclination controls the balance and anti-resonance between direct and reflected sound, and is combined with the adoption of the soft dome shape to obtain flat and smooth characteristics.

It is a magnetic shielding type without magnetic leakage to the outside, and does not have a bad effect on TV image, etc.

High-quality capacitors, OFC cables and choke coils are used for the network.
The terminal uses a gold-plated brass machined screw type terminal to reduce signal transmission loss.
