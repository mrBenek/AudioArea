Commentary

This is a large speaker system of the SH series developed as a professional speaker for reproducing music and speech in high quality indoors.

This speaker is designed to have excellent directional characteristics in a 2 π (hemispherical) space and a uniform energy response up to the high frequency range. The SH30 speaker is also designed to provide a wider space than the SH20 speaker with more room in the low frequency range.

A 30 cm cone woofer is installed for the low range, and four 5 cm cone tweeters are installed for the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and the exterior is finished in ivory color.
In addition, the front grill is detachable.

SH30 can be installed on the wall by fixing the attached metal fittings to the back plate with spacers and 4 wood screws.
It can also be mounted on the ceiling by removing the woofer unit and fixing it with screws.
