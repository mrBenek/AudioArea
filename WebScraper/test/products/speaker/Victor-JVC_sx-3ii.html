Commentary

This speaker system is based on the SX-3 model and is more complete.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses KDU cone paper, which is also used for SX-3.

A 5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit is based on the SX-3 soft dome type tweeter and further improved. By adding a new back cavity, fo and Qo of the unit are kept low, and the crossover frequency is lowered by 200 Hz.
In addition, a copper cap is used for the magnetic circuit to reduce current distortion.

Both the high-cut woofer and the low-cut tweeter are designed at 12dB/oct.
In addition, a compensation circuit is built in as a countermeasure against loss before the cutoff of the woofer and resonance near the cutoff, which are likely to be caused by inductance components, to realize a smoother high-cut.
The coil is made of silicon steel plate core, which is resistant to large input power.

The enclosure uses SX-3 Yuzuri specifications, North American conifer Douglas-fir plywood for the front and rear baffles, and sound-absorbers and ester wool sound-absorbing materials for the interior.
