  • DENON SC-400S

  • ¥ 45,000 (a set of two cars, around 1996)

Commentary

D. E. G speaker system.

It uses a 16.5 cm cone type woofer for the low range and a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter for the high range.

Film condensers and air-core coils are used for the network, and large terminals are used for terminals.
