Commentary

A 12-inch subwoofer with a parametric equalizer.

The unit has a 12 inch (30 cm) cone woofer.
Butyl rubber treated aluminum diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.
The neodymium magnet used in the magnetic circuit ensures super Longs (large amplitude) by NRT technology, and realizes a sound with no distortion and damping.

Equipped with a parametric equalizer.

Equipped with a 600W power amplifier with a limiter.
