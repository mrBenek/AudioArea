Commentary

This is an APM speaker system that uses a curved top plate and base plate to further develop the Super Oval Enclosure, whose effectiveness was demonstrated with the APM-6Monitor.

530 cm in the lower range2Equipped with a flat woofer and 27 cm in the middle area.2Flat mid-range, 7 cm high range2Equipped with a planar type tweeter.

The enclosure is a super-oval enclosure to prevent sound diffraction.
In addition, the top plate and base plate of APM-4 are also curved shapes, so that diffraction can be suppressed more, and good sound field reproduction and flat sound pressure characteristics are realized.

Comes with a dedicated speaker stand that has been carefully selected in terms of sound quality and design.
