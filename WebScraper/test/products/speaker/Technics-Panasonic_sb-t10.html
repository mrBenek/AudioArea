Commentary

This is a two way tower speaker system that uses a tall boy enclosure to save space despite its large capacity.

An 18 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range, and combined with a just-tuned bass reflex enclosure realizes a voluminous low sound reproduction.
Uses a 75 mm diameter magnet for the magnetic circuit.

A unique conical horn type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Magnetic fluid is filled in the magnetic gap, and heat generated by the voice coil is dissipated through the magnetic fluid to objects with large heat capacity such as yokes and pole pieces to improve input resistance.

For mounting the unit, an original linear phase design is applied to align the sound source center of the unit to improve the phase frequency characteristics.
A high-density particle board is used for the enclosure to provide effective reinforcement through computer vibration analysis, and a three way Z-stop by advanced woodworking technology is adopted for the wood frame to realize a strong structure.

The enclosure was available in black and white models.
