  • YAMAHA NS-W2

  • ¥ 29,800 (1 unit, around 1986)

Commentary

A super-woofer system developed by Yamaha to accommodate new types of audio such as digital sources and AV mixes.

A 25 cm cone type unit has been adopted for the unit.

A 45W output low-frequency amplifier is built in.
The magnetic shielding design also allows for more freedom in installation.
