  • ONKYO D-105M

  • ¥ 8,000 (1 unit, sold on May 22, 2001)

Commentary

D-105 series speaker system.

A 9 cm cone woofer with an OMF diaphragm is mounted in the lower range. This unit uses an S-line edge that reduces the reflection of propagating waves at the junction between the diaphragm and the edge.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

MDF material is used for the enclosure.
There are 60-mm-pitch screw holes for brackets on the back and bottom, and wall mounting hardware on the back.

The input terminal uses a screw-type speaker terminal compatible with banana plug.
