  • ¥ 19,950 (1 unit, around mid 1960 s?)

Commentary

This floor-type speaker system was launched as a full-scale 3-way speaker system.

A 30 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range, a 7.5 cm cone in the middle range, and a horn in the high range.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.

In order to improve the directivity in the mid - and high-range area, the upper part of the enclosure where the mid - and high-range unit is installed is inclined.

In addition to the screw-type terminal, a unique plug jack is used for the connection terminal, and the attached cable with jack is inserted.
