Commentary

A speaker system developed for the digital & AV era while inheriting the Zero series.
Fine ceramic is used for all diaphragms.

A 25.5 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
The diaphragm uses ceramic fibers coated with carbon one by one.

A 7 cm cone type skoker is used for the middle area.
Alumina powder and silicon nitride whiskers (Si :3N4The voice coil and diaphragm are integrated with high strength and high rigidity by plastic jet coding.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is used for the high range.
By moving the sound source closer to the point, directivity is improved. At the same time, high resonance frequency is extended to 50 kHz by alumina non-crystal ion plating.

The frame of the unit uses an aluminum die-cast frame with gun black coating.
Each unit uses a double magnet system to increase driving force and at the same time eliminate adverse effects on TV screen. Each unit has a magnetic flux density of Woofer 9000 Gauss, Squaker 13,300 Gauss and Tweeter 19,000 Gauss.

It is equipped with a presence control which controls the localization of sound image according to the contents of program source.

The enclosure is a high-pressure consolidation cabinet using conifer high-density particle board.
With a matte black finish, we consider matching with visual equipment.
