  • DENON SC-V101

  • ¥ 8,000 (1 unit, around 1997)

Commentary

A compact bookshelf speaker system.

In consideration of the balance between the cabinet size and the area of the diaphragm, it is equipped with an 8 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm balanced dome tweeter in the high range.

The front baffle has been rounded to reduce the influence of diffraction effect on sound.
The enclosure is hermetically sealed. There are two variations of exterior appearance : wood finish and black pear PVC finish.

A 60 mm pitch nut is mounted on the bottom surface and a wall mounting bracket is mounted on the back surface for mounting with commercially available speaker stands and wall mounting brackets such as the ceiling mounting bracket (ASG-10), increasing the freedom of speaker setting.
In addition, the DENON emblem on the net grill can be rotated 360 degrees considering the beauty of setting.

It uses a gold plated terminal that is compatible with banana plugs and is also compatible with bi-wiring.
