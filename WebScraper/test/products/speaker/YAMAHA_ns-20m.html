  • YAMAHA NS-20M

  • ¥ 35,000 (1 unit, released in October 1983)

Commentary

A compact speaker system developed for the digital audio age.

For the low band, we have adopted a 23-cm unit made of a cone-shaped spruce material known as a material for piano soundboards.
Oxygen-free copper ribbon wire is used for the voice coil and aluminum die-cast is used for the frame.

A tweeter and a super tweeter, both of which use titanium carbonate as the diaphragm material, are used for the middle and high range.

The cabinet has a three way flow stop structure.
