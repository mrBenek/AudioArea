  • TOSHIBA SS-47

  • ¥ 36,700 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

Speaker system using newly developed cone cave cone.

A 30 cm cone type woofer using a concave cone is installed in the low range.
The Concave Cone utilizes the center part of the conventional unit, which used to be a dust-proof cap. It uses a concave diaphragm made of a mixture of special chemical fibers to achieve a smooth high-frequency attenuation, preventing the sound from becoming muddy near the crossover.

Equipped with 12 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.
A concave cone is also used for the diaphragm of this unit.

A 3-cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

It is equipped with 3-step switching type level control for middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

In addition to the normal input terminal, the terminal for multi-amplifier is mounted, and 2-way or 3-way multi-amplifier drive is possible.
