  • ALTEC LANSING 878B Santiago

  • Price unknown (1 unit, around 1975)

Commentary

This speaker system is the successor to the 878A Santiago.

A 38 cm cone woofer of 411A type is installed in the low range.
In the high-frequency range, a horn type unit combining driver unit 806-8A and horn 811B is mounted.

N801-8A is used for the network.

The cabinet features a black mesh three dimensional grill and natural slate top design.
