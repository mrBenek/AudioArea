Commentary

A large stage monitor of the Cabaret series.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A horn type unit that combines a bi-radial horn 2370A and a driver unit 2425J is installed in the high range.
Titanium diaphragm is adopted in 2425J.

The enclosure uses a 19-mm-thick rigid multi-layer plywood with a polyurethane coating on the entire surface for high strength, and is designed to be durable and easy to handle with carrying handles and corner guards.

There was a special front cover for use during transportation as an option sold separately.
