Commentary

A bookshelf bookshelf type speaker system that uses a horn type unit for the middle and high range.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

It is equipped with a horn type squaker SH35 in the middle range and a horn type tweeter TH7 in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
