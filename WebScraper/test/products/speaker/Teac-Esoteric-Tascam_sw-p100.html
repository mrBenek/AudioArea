  • TEAC SW-P100

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 unit, around 1993)

Commentary

An active subwoofer that adopts the D. D. AIRD (double duct air tuning) system.

In the D. D. AIRD system, each unit is equipped with an independent chamber to prevent interference between units.

Two 13 cm cone type woofers are used in the unit.

Equipped with a monaural power amplifier.
