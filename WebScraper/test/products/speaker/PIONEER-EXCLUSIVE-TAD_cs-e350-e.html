Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

A 20 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 7.7 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.
FB cone is used for the diaphragm of these units.

A large ferrite with a high Q coil wound around it is used for the network part, and constant loss is minimized and excellent transient characteristics are obtained.

A level control is provided for the high-frequency band.
In the Normal position, the characteristics are almost flat. In the Max position, the high-frequency range can be raised by about 6 dB. In the Min position, it can be narrowed down to 0. In addition, the range from Max to Min is continuously variable and can be freely adjusted according to your preference.

The enclosure is sealed and has a walnut oil finish on the exterior.
