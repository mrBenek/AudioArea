  • Pioneer S-X30

  • ¥ 16,500 (1 unit, around 1982)

Commentary

A two way speaker system using a planar passive radiator.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range and combined with a flat passive radiator to increase the low sound reproduction capability.

A 6.6 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
