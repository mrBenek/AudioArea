  • JBL SV400C

Commentary

SV series center / satellite speaker system.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
Pure pulp white cone is used for the diaphragm.

A horn type tweeter using a 1.9 cm semi-hard dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The rigidity of the diaphragm is increased by titanium laminate processing on the surface of the diaphragm. By combining a horn with an equalizer on the front of this unit, the directivity is accurately controlled in the listening direction.

Anti-magnetic design with cancel magnet and shield cover system is applied.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
MDF of 12 mm thickness is adopted for the plate material, and the exterior is finished with wood-like finish.
