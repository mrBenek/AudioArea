  • SONY SS-86E

  • ¥ 33,000 (a set of two cars, around 1997)

Commentary

This compact speaker system is designed and manufactured in England.

He received five stars for an article in the British audio magazine "WHAT Hi-Fi?"

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The woofer diaphragm is made of PP cone with mica, and the center cap is hand painted one by one.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

Each unit has an anti-magnetic design.

A vibration-proof capacitor and an OFC (oxygen-free copper wire) coil are used in the network section.

The enclosure uses MDF.

Uses a gold plated terminal that supports banana plugs.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
