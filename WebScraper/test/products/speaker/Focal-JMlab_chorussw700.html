Commentary

Active subwoofer from the Chorus series.

Equipped with 2 28 cm cone type woofers for low range.
These units use poly-glass diaphragms. This material has been used only in high-end models until now. It is made of cellulose fiber cone with excellent resolution and coated with hollow fine particles of extremely hard silica glass resin with a special adhesive. The combination of cellulose with high internal loss and light weight and a highly synthetic silica glass layer enables smoother and more accurate sound propagation. Butyl edge is used for the edge part.
Aluminum die-cast is used for the frame.

A 75W power amplifier is used for the amplifier section.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

The crossover is continuously variable and can be adjusted to the optimum connection.
