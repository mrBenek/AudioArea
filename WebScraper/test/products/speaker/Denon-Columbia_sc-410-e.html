  • DENON SC-410

  • ¥ 60,000 (a set of two cars, around 1992)

Commentary

This speaker system aims for a European taste in both sound quality and design.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The magnetic circuit of this unit uses a ferrite magnet with a magnetic force approximately 8 times that of ferrite.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
Natural wool, a natural material, is used for the sound absorbing material inside.
In addition, the exterior is finished with a European taste in mind and uses a camphor tree pattern.

A large speaker terminal is adopted.
