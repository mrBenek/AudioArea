  • Pioneer CS-E600

  • ¥ 30,000 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system using a FB cone.

It has a 25 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 12 cm cone midrange in the middle range.
FB system clear cone is used for these units.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using fiber with large internal loss is mounted in the high frequency range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

In addition to the normal input terminal, 2-way and 3-way multi-amplifier terminals are mounted on the rear terminal.
