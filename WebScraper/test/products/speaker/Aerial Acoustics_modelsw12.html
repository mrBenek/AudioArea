Commentary

Active subwoofer with built-in amplifiers and parametric equalizers.

The unit is equipped with 30 cm cone type woofer.
Aluminum cone diaphragm supports diaphragm motion with dual suspension, large magnetic circuit and 3-inch diameter 4-layer coil.

A high-current 400W output amplifier is used in the amplifier section.
It is a discrete configuration with full symmetry inside, and a toroidal transformer is used for the power supply.

Equipped with full-feature 2-channel crossover.

With a built-in parametric equalizer, you can adjust the characteristics.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The board is made of MDF, especially the front baffle is 2-inch thick, and the inside is carefully reinforced to eliminate adverse effects of vibration.

Remote control unit is included.

Dedicated base and display / sensor unit were optional.
