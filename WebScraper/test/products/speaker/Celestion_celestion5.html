  • Celestion Celestion5

  • ¥ 64,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1991)

Commentary

A speaker system designed by Martin Roberts, a staff member of Dr. G. Bank, a research director at Celestion and developer of the company's SL-series.

A 17 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The vibration plate uses felt fiber cone with newly developed high-tech molding to improve low-frequency characteristics.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using a titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

By using polycarbonate for the frame of all units, resonance generated in the steel frame is suppressed.

The network section has a simple design unique to Celestion, with a 5-element, second order low-pass / high-pass filter.

The enclosure is completely enclosed and constructed from 15 mm thick baffle boards and 12 mm thick particleboard.

There are 2 color variations, oak and black.

The optional speaker stand Si24STAND can be used.
The stand is all-metal used with pebbles or sand inside the center pole, and it can be firmly fixed to the floor by four spikes.
