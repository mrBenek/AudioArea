Commentary

This is a floor type speaker system with engraving.

There were 2 types of patterns for the unit configuration, D60S7R with 2-way configuration and D60S8R with 3-way configuration.

The inside of the enclosure has the same structure as that of Olympus, with a cloth grill on the front.
