Commentary

A5-sized speaker system with honeycomb flat diaphragm.

The low range has a 12 cm honeycomb plane woofer, and the high range has a 2.8 cm honeycomb plane tweeter.

The front baffle has an oval cross section to eliminate the disturbance of frequency characteristics due to diffraction effect.
In addition, by adopting die-cast cabinets, we are taking thorough measures against magnetic shielding.

Impedance 4 Ω and 8 Ω can be selected for the input terminal.
Normally, 4 Ω is used for high-efficiency reproduction, and when connecting in parallel with other speakers, 8 Ω is used to avoid excessive load on the amplifier.

Equipped with an thermal relay protection circuit to prevent unit breakage due to excessive input.
