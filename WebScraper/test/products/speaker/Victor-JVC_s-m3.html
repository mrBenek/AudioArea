  • VICTOR/JVC S-M3

  • ¥ 17,000 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

A small speaker system that can be installed freely.
It is a pocket book size and has a structure that can be carried and installed freely.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type woofer and a 3 cm soft dome type tweeter with long travel voice coil.
Aluminum voice coil bobbin with good heat radiation efficiency is used, and the performance is improved by using an Alnico magnet internal magnetic circuit for the tweeter.

The cabinet is sealed with aluminum die-cast to eliminate box squeaking, deformation, and air leakage.
In addition, ester wool with high sound absorbing effect is used for the sound absorbing material inside.
