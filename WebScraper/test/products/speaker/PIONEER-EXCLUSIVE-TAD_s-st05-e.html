Audio Database

  • Pioneer S-ST05

  • ¥ 30,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in May 1996)

Commentary

A linear power 2-way speaker system with a unique edgeless structure in pursuit of a more compact system.

The tweeter is designed with a neodymium magnet inner magnet type, and the woofer is designed with a ferrite cancel magnet.

There was also a single-pack type (S-C05) for surround center speakers.
