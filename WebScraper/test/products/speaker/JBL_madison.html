Commentary

Bookshelf enclosure designed for LE8 and S5 systems.

The D48LE8 unit is equipped with LE8, and the D48S5 unit is equipped with S5 system combining LE10A, LE30 and LX3-1.

As for the enclosure, D48LE8 is used as a bass reflex system, while D48S5 is equipped with a tweeter LE30 in the bass reflex port and has a sealed structure.

The LE8T was also listed in the 1963 catalog for the corresponding unit of the enclosure (C48Madison), but it is not known if it was sold as a speaker system.
