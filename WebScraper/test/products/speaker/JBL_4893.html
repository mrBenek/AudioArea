Commentary

A subwoofer system ideal for setting up with ARRAY series 4892 or 4894.

Equipped with 2 35.5 cm cone type woofers for low range.
This unit uses SFG magnetic circuits and JBL's unique VGC (Bent Gap Cooling).

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The material is 19 mm thick 13-layer plywood and 16 gn steel grill.

Various system configurations are possible by combining with the separately sold S. A. F. E (Secure Array Flying Ergonmics) flying metal fitting.
