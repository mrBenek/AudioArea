  • ONKYO F-600M

  • ¥ 33,400 (1 unit, around 1970)

Commentary

3-way sealed speaker system.
A multi-amp connector, which was not found in the F-600A, is added to the network.

The 30 cm cone type is used for the low range, the 12 cm cone type for the middle range, and the horn type for the high range.

The enclosure is a lion board with a thickness of 15 mm and 18 mm depending on the location.
The surface is oil-finished WATCO finish.

Equipped with level control.
