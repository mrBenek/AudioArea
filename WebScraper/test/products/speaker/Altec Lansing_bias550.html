Commentary

An active speaker system incorporating a 5-channel multi-amplifier system.

Each speaker is mounted in a separate box and the complete double structure eliminates unwanted box vibration.

Diamond-coated polyimide diaphragm is used for the tweeter and skoker, and carbon fiber diaphragm is used for midbus, woofer and subwoofer.

A total of 10 left and right amplifiers generate a power of 1400W with a distortion factor of 0.05%. It can be used for any music source.

Remote control is available so that the balance of each channel and the volume of the speakers can be adjusted by the listener position.
