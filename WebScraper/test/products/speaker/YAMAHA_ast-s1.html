  • YAMAHA AST-S1

  • ¥ 73,000 (a set of two cars, around 1989)

Commentary

This is a small A4-size speaker system that uses active servo technology to provide bass playback from the port.

Although it is an air woofer with a 3.6 cm diameter port, the air in the enclosure itself is vibrated. The air itself functions as a diaphragm and realizes low sound reproduction from 28 Hz.

It uses a 16 cm PP cone type squaker for the mid-range and a 3 cm soft dome type tweeter for the high-range.

A dedicated amplifier is required for use.
