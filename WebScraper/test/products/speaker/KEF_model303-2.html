Commentary

The speaker system is the successor to the Model303.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The exterior is finished with a walnut finish.

The stand was sold as a separately sold option.
