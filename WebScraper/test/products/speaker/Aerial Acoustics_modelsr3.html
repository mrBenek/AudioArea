Commentary

A wall-mounted speaker system.

The Model SR3 is capable of either dipole or bipole operation. The operation can be switched by the switch on the bottom, or by the wired remote control ordered.
You can also attenuate the output of the rear driver, so you can control the rear wall reflection and set the steering of the listener dipole to 0.
A 17.7 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

The mid-range features two 11.4 cm cone mid-range and the high-range features two 1.9 cm dome tweeters.

The mid-range and high-range levels can be adjusted, making it possible to optimize the design according to the sound condition of the room.

There were two variations of grills that cover the entire enclosure, white and black.

There were wall stand and wall metal fittings as options sold separately.
