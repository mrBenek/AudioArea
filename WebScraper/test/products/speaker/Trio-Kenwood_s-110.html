Commentary

This 3-way speaker system uses a bookshelf type.

It is equipped with a 25 cm cone woofer with fo = 30 hz for the low range, a 16 cm cone squaker for the middle range, and a horn type tweeter for the high range.

Equipped with level control for middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type.
