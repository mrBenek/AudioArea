Commentary

This speaker system realizes high-bandwidth playback at 120 kHz.

A unit using a Nastron diaphragm is used for the Super Tweeter to realize high-band reproduction up to 120 khz.

The enclosure has a natural wood veneer finish, a glossy paint finish, and a curved shape to reduce standing waves, which can muddy the sound quality.
In addition, the depth is reduced so that it can be used as wall hanging.

In order to increase the sweet spot of multi-channel playback, a crossover network is adopted to realize uniform directional characteristics over a wide area.

Uses a brass machined gold plated terminal that supports banana plugs.
