  • ¥ 29,800 (a set of two cars, around 1993)

Commentary

A compact speaker system that can be installed freely.

It uses a 12 cm cone type woofer made of polypropylene with mica for the low range and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter for the high range.
In addition, the crossover frequencies have been raised to 6000 Hz to achieve a full-range speaker-like sound with a Iori. Moreover, the material of the edge and the shape of the circular ring have been improved to smooth out large-stroke vibration.

The enclosure uses a special polypropylene cabinet to suppress unwanted internal resonance and to ensure appropriate internal loss.
As a result, the strength against external force is improved while suppressing the standing wave generated inside as much as possible.

The separately sold stand allows for more freedom of installation.
