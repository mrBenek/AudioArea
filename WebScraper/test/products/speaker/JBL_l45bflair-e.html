Commentary

This speaker system appeared as an improved version of the L45.

There are 5 unit variations of L45B, and the configuration is different by the character string described at the end of L45B.
Also, in the catalog, it was written in 2 ways, "L45-model number B" and "L45B-model number".

The L45B is the only 3-way system in the L45B Series and consists of 38 cm, 13 cm and 3.6 cm cone units.

The L45B-001 consists of Woofer 130A and LE175DLH.

L45B-030 is a 2-way combination of 38 cm full range units D130 and 075.

L45B-S1 is composed of LE14A and LE175DLH aiming at theater sound.

L45B-S4 consists of 38 cm woofer 130A and horn type unit combining LE175 and HL91.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
In addition, the base part is composed independently, and it can be placed vertically or horizontally.
