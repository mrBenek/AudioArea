Commentary

Double number series bookshelf type speaker system.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
A KDU cone manufactured by Kurt Muller is used for the diaphragm, and five Konica Le Dome are added to the cone to propose split resonance. A felt cap is used for the center cap to improve response and transient in the mid-frequency range.

It is equipped with a 6.5 cm soft dome type squaker in the middle range and a 3 cm soft dome type tweeter in the high range.
These units were designed by reexamining the special fabric base material of the dome using accumulated know-how, and by improving the viscoelastic impregnation agent, natural resonance is reduced, and elastic wide-directional diaphragm is formed.
In addition, a large magnetic circuit with high magnetic flux density and a high input resistant voice coil using different bobbin materials for a squaker and a tweeter are taken into consideration in order to take advantage of the soft dome performance.
In addition, an aluminum dieeach unit is equipped with an aluminum die-cast Definition Equalizer with six strong fins that also act as protectors. This adjusts the slight phase difference between the center and periphery of the dome and increases the diffusivity.

The front baffle of the enclosure is made of Douglas Fir, a North American conifer material with excellent vibration damping characteristics. This lumber core structure brings out the advantages of muku.
We have also adopted sound absorbers and sound-absorbing materials that take advantage of the unique know-how of the SX series.

The network employs low-loss capacitors and finely improved coils that enhance the laminate effect by reexamining the structure of the copper silicon plate core and reduce current distortion by selectively using air-core coils.

There was also a rosewood finish SX-55R.
