Commentary

As the laboratory ratory series and the highest point of the Zero series, this is a speaker system developed with all the technology to pursue a warm musical quality that will touch people's hearts.

It is equipped with a 39 cm cone type unit for low range and 21 cm cone type unit for middle and low range.
A composite cone of ceramic fiber and carbon cloth is used for the diaphragm, and smooth connection and flat energy are achieved by giving moderate internal loss.

A 6.5 cm dome and a 3.0 cm dome type unit using a pure fine ceramic diaphragm, which is a polycrystalline artificial jewel diaphragm, are installed in the middle and high frequency ranges.
This diaphragm was used for the first time in the world as a high-rigidity, high-speed ceramic dome with a single-layer structure without a metal base material. It has properties very close to those of ruby and sapphire and purifies the quality of the music range.

The baffle plate is 38 mm thick and adopts a super elliptical shape to improve directivity, and the units are arranged in G-line layout based on computer Kaiseki.
The sound absorbing material is made of 100% wool, which generates less noise due to vibration.

The network section is a balanced type that is resistant to external disturbances. A high grade element is selected and used. The OFC wiring material is also caulked without solder.
In addition, a gold plated terminal is used for the speaker terminal, and bi-amplifier drive is also available by removing the strapping bar.

The front baffle of the enclosure is made of 38 mm thick larch plywood and has a super-elliptical shape to obtain excellent diffraction characteristics.
Also, the arrangement of units and mutual sense are arranged in a smooth G-line based on computer analysis.
The sound absorbing material inside the enclosure is 100% wool, which produces less noise due to vibration.

There was a special speaker base sold separately.
