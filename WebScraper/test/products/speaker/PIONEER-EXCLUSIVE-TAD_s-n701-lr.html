  • Pioneer S-N701-LR

  • ¥ 17,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

Compact Component Fill Series 2-way speaker system.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of Pioneer's original pulp cone, both the rigidity required for the diaphragm and appropriate internal loss are achieved.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
PPTA (polyphenylene phthalamide) resin film, which is lightweight and has excellent rigidity, is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The front baffle uses a 20 mm thick round baffle.
