  • ONKYO D-50

  • ¥ 12,000 (1 unit, around 1989)

Commentary

This small speaker system was developed with the aim of making compact speakers with good sound quality.

The 12 cm full range cone type speaker, which is composed of special resin impregnated cone and double 75 φ x32 φ x10mm stolum magnet, is in charge of almost all band.
In addition to the cancel magnet, it is a complete anti-magnetic type covered with a frame shield case.

It is equipped with a 3.0 cm ceramic dome type super tweeter for high frequency.

Joint holes of M5, 60 mm pitch are provided on the side of the box for free layout using commercially available options.
In addition, a tripod type compact stand and brackets for hanging on the wall and ceiling are sold separately.
