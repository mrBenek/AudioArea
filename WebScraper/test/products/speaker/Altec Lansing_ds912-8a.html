Commentary

A speaker system using a coaxial unit.

A 30 cm coaxial 2-way speaker is installed as a unit.
This unit consists of a 30 cm cone woofer and a 3.8 cm dome tweeter. In particular, the tweeter has a 90 ° (horizontal) x90 ° (vertical) directional pattern and can provide on-axis sound quality service over a wide listening area.

The enclosure is a five side enclosure designed for a wide range of applications and is made of 18 mm thick poplar laminated material with 12 3/8-inch nuts embedded for flying.

The enclosure comes in two colors, charcoal gray and white.
