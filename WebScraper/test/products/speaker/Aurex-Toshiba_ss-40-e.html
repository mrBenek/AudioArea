  • Toshiba/Aurex SS-40

  • ¥ 48,000 (1 unit, sold in March 1969)

Commentary

A 3-way speaker system with a multi-channel connector.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
By adopting a free edge using a newly developed material and a special molding technology, low sound reproduction with good damping is obtained.

The middle area is equipped with a 27x9cm square horn squaker using hard duralumin diaphragm.

A 6 cm horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency band to reduce the weight of the vibration system by using special molded resin material.

An LC-type 12dB/oct network is used for the network section.

The network can be disconnected by switching the selector switch on the rear terminal board, and it can be used as a multi-amplifier type speaker.
You can also use the level control knob to change the output level of the medium and high tones to three levels.

The net on the front can be removed.
