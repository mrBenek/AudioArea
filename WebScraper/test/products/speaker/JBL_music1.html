Commentary

Sound Effect Series speaker set.

The Sound Effect series was developed as a sound system for multimedia applications such as audio, TV, PC and game.
music1 consists of two two way compact speaker system sat1 and two sub-woofer system bass1.


sat1

sat1 consists of a 1.9 cm titanium dome type tweeter and a 8.9 cm cone type woofer.
Neodymium is used for the magnetic circuit of the woofer unit.

The cabinet is sealed.

Comes with freestanding stand and brackets for mounting on walls and ceilings.


bass1

The bass1 uses JBL's unique double-chamber bandpass system and the unit is equipped with a 13 cm cone woofer.

With a corner design, it can fit neatly in the corner of a room, and can be installed in a wide range of ways such as vertical placement, horizontal placement, and vertical stacking.


Other

A wire manager is included to facilitate wiring to the speakers.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
