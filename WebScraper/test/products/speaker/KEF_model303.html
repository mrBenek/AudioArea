  • Price unknown (1 unit, released in 1979)

Commentary

A speaker system developed as an export model of the Celeste III.

The high-frequency unit is different from the Celeste III.
The Celeste III used SP1068, but the Model304 has a T33/SP1074 soft dome tweeter.

B200/SP1070 which is a 20 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and features a unique design with grill cloths on four sides.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
