  • PMC SB100

  • ¥ 320,000 (1 unit, around the early 2000 s)

Commentary

A passive type subwoofer system.

The SB100 is a modified version of the specially ordered subwoofer for Hollywood mixing engineer Dennis Sands' reference speaker PMC IB1.

The unit uses a 25 cm planar woofer unit made of carbon fiber.

Since the SB-100 is a passive subwoofer, a separate power amplifier, low-pass filter or crossover network is required.

The enclosure finishes included Walnut and Rosewood finishes as well as black ash.
