  • ¥ 9,800 (a set of two cars, around 1992)

Commentary

Setting free type small speaker system

A 10 cm full range unit is adopted for the unit.

Uses a free angle cabinet whose accuracy can be changed depending on how it is placed.

It comes with a speaker cord adapter that can convert the pin plug at the tip of the speaker cord into two parallel wires and mounting brackets for installation on walls and columns.
