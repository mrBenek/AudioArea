Commentary

This is a bookshelf speaker system that uses Sony's unique low-distortion magnetic circuit.

Sony's unique low-distortion magnetic circuit reduces non-linear distortion due to magnetic materials through two modifications : a notch in the center of the center pole and a thick copper plating or copper cap.

In cone speakers, abnormal sound is radiated at an extremely large level in a band 2 to 3 times the upper limit frequency due to abnormal vibration of the voice coil bobbin, bobbin and cone joint, etc. Therefore, we use a dust-proof cap with excellent sound insulation characteristics to prevent abnormal sound radiation.

It has a 30 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 12 cm cone midrange in the middle range.
The diaphragm uses carefully selected Northern European pulp to suppress the turbidity of the sound generated by divided vibration.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The network section uses a large air-core coil that has little DC resistance and is not distorted by large input.
In addition, it is equipped with a multi-amplifier selector switch that can drive each unit directly by removing the LC network.

The mid-range and high-range are equipped with continuously variable level control that can be adjusted according to the sound characteristics of the room.

The enclosure uses 20 mm thick hard particleboards.
