Commentary

This 3-way speaker system is a high-end model of the LX series.

It has a 25 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 12.5 cm cone midrange in the middle range.
A 2.5 cm pure titanium dome pure titanium dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The front baffle of the enclosure is equipped with damping pads to provide clear sound without turbidity, and the round baffle structure eliminates turbidity.
