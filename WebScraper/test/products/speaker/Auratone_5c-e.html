Commentary

A small speaker system adopted by recording studios and broadcasting stations around the world.

The unit is equipped with a 12.5 cm cone type full range unit.
High compliance half-roll suspension is adopted. In addition, 25 mm high heat-resistant voice coil is adopted for voice coil, and 730g strong magnet is adopted for magnetic circuit.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.

The input terminal of the early ones was a flat screw type, but it has been changed to a screw type since the late 70's.
