Commentary

A speaker system incorporating new technologies such as CD woofer and LM dome tweeter.

A 14.5 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.
In order to achieve faithful sound reproduction, this unit is a CD (casted diaphram) woofer that uses a molded diaphragm that is molded with accuracy in the order of several microns. The cone and edge are connected by a newly developed special welding method that does not use adhesive. In addition, a non-reflective dust cap that reduces distortion is used to achieve more accurate and smooth sound reproduction.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit is named LM (Laminated Metal) Tweeter, and uses a five layer structure diaphragm that combines materials with different characteristics and is heat-press-molded using unique technology.
As a result, both the flexibility of the soft dome and the compactness of the hard dome are made compatible and quick response is obtained.
In addition, the constant directional front plate improves sound image localization and sound field sensation.

The enclosure is flush surfaced to reduce irregularities on the baffle surface.
The network uses capacitors, OFC cables, choke coils, and gold-plated brass machined screw-type terminals equivalent to the high-end models.

It is a magnetic shielding type without magnetic leakage to the outside, and does not have a bad effect on the TV screen, etc.
