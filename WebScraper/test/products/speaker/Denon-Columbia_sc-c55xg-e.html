Commentary

A center speaker that adopts the P. P. D. D. method.

DENON's proprietary P. P. D. D. (Push-Pull Dual Driver) technology is used to improve low-frequency playback. The edges of a pair of woofer diaphragms are mounted in opposite directions to cancel each other's harmonic distortion.

The super tweeter uses a unit that employs a Nastron diaphragm to realize high-band reproduction up to 120 khz.

The enclosure has a natural wood veneer finish and a glossy paint finish.
In addition, a curved curve has been adopted to reduce standing waves that make sound quality muddy.

In order to increase the sweet spot of multi-channel playback, a crossover network is adopted to realize uniform directional characteristics over a wide area.

Uses a brass machined gold plated terminal that supports banana plugs.
