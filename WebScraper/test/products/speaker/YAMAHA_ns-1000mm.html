Commentary

A small speaker system developed for theater sound, inheriting design concepts such as NS-1000M and NS-10M.

A 12 cm cone type unit is used for the low range, a 2.5 cm dome type unit for the middle range, and a 1.5 cm dome type unit for the high range.
Each unit is equipped with a metal guard grill.

The magnetic circuit employs an A / MAG type anti-magnetic circuit that magnetizes both the main magnet and cancel magnet under the optimum environment and cancels the current distortion that occurs with the conventional cancel magnet system.

The enclosure is made with Yamaha's original three way flow retaining structure, which dramatically improves the durability and accuracy of the joint part of plate materials.
