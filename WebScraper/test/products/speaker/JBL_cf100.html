Commentary

This is a CF series floor-type speaker system that allows you to enjoy high efficiency and open sound.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
A fiber-cone based on pulp is used for the diaphragm.

The mid-range is equipped with a 11.5 cm cone type mid-range.
The diaphragm is made of fixed edge paper cone, and is designed with an emphasis on smooth sound connection with woofer and tweeter.

A 1.4 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
A titanium-laminated diaphragm is used as the diaphragm to achieve both the natural sound quality of the soft dome and the clear sound quality of the titanium coating.

The enclosure uses a rear bass reflex system.

A binding post-type banana plug-compatible speaker terminal is used for extremely thick speaker cables.
