Commentary

This is the smallest speaker system among the TRIO-BOZAK speaker systems.

B-199A which is 30 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range.
This unit uses felted paper cone with wool mixed in pulp to achieve light weight, hardness and high compliance as well as low distortion bass.

B-200Y which is a 5 cm cone type tweeter is mounted in the high range.
By dumping the metal diaphragm in a special way, the vibration of the diaphragm is suppressed and a smoother playback sound is obtained.

The enclosure has a walnut oil finish.

The terminal section is equipped with a multi-amplifier selector switch and multi-amplifier terminal.
