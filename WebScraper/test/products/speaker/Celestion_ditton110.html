Commentary

A bookshelf speaker equipped with a soft dome tweeter powered by a laser computer.

A 20.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
