Commentary

A small speaker system with an Italian design.

Initially, it was sold in 2 color variations of black and Italian red, and then an off-white model was released.

The speaker unit is equipped with HP 101, a newly developed 11.5 cm cone type full range unit.
This unit is designed and equipped with a special magnetic circuit that suppresses the rise of the impedance curve in the high-frequency range in order to achieve smoother high-frequency characteristics. In addition, a high-polymer material is used for the center cap to eliminate partial resonance in the high-frequency range.
In addition, the new shape of the edge that enables rich bass reproduction, and the newly designed cone paper that is partially changed in thickness and density by computer simulation are used to achieve both reproducibility of details and expressive power with a sense of scale.

An anti-magnetic design that combines a canceling magnet system and a shield system is adopted to eliminate adverse effects on cathode ray tubes, etc.

For the port part of the cabinet, we have adopted Aerodynaport which was born from Bose's original EDS theory.
The Aero Dinah Port is designed to reduce the turbulence phenomenon that occurs inside the port, and port noise is eliminated by smoothly releasing back pressure from the port.
In addition, it uses a side port structure with an opening on the side of the cabinet to maximize the baffle effect of the wall surface.
