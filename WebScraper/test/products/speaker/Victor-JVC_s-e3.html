  • VICTOR/JVC S-E3

  • ¥ 12,500 (1 unit, around 1982)

Commentary

This compact speaker system was developed for use with an R-3000 type deck receiver.

It uses a 16 cm cone type woofer for the low range and a 5 cm tweeter for the high range.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type.
