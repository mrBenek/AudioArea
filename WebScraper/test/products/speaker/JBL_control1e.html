  • JBL Control 1E

  • ¥ 50,000 (1 unit, around 1994)

Commentary

A small active speaker system in the control series with built-in amplifiers and channel dividers.

It has a 12 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm balance drive tweeter in the high range.

The Control 1E has an independent amplifier and channel divider for driving each unit.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Various mounting brackets are sold separately and can be installed freely.
