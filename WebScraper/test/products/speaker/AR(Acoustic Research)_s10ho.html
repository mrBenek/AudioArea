  • AR(Acoustic Research) S10HO

  • ¥ 88,000 (1 unit, around 1998)

Commentary

A powered subwoofer that is a lower model of the S12HO.

A long stroke type 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.

The enclosure uses a multi-port slot loading method.

Equipped with 2 types of input terminals, high level and low level.

Equipped with an autosensing function and variable adjustment function of input level and crossover frequency.
