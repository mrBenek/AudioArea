Commentary

A center speaker that adopts the P. P. D. D. method.

The woofer uses a glass fiber diaphragm and adopts DENON's unique P. P. D. D method.
By mounting one edge of a pair of woofers in the opposite direction, harmonic distortion of each other is canceled and eliminated, enabling low-frequency reproduction with less distortion.

The Super Tweeter uses a direct drive system and achieves ultra-high bandwidth playback up to 90 kHz.

In order to ensure the best listening area and obtain uniform sound pressure / phase response, a new crossover network has been developed and directivity has been improved.

The enclosure uses a veneer glossy paint finish.
The directional characteristics are also improved by tilting the baffle.

Uses large brass machined and gold-plated terminals for banana plugs.
