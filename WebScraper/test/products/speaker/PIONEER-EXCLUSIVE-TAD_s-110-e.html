  • ¥ 28,000 (1 unit, released in 1980)

Commentary

Based on sound image realism, this 2-way speaker system was developed with the aim of sound full of punch power and power feeling.

It has a simple 2-way design and uses a passive radiator instead of a woofer for ultra-low frequencies.
This passive radiator is a plane angle type that can take a large area of the diaphragm.

A 21 cm full-range unit with a lightweight diaphragm based on a commercially available PE 201 is used for the middle and low frequencies.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter equipped with an equalizing minihorn is adopted for the high frequency band to improve directional characteristics and efficiency.
