Commentary

A thin speaker system using a unit equivalent to the PIM-16A.

The unit is equipped with a 16 cm mechanical 2-way full-range unit which is equivalent to the PIM-16A.
This unit is equipped with cone papers for woofer and tweeter on the same axis of one voice coil, and is connected by a corrugation edge.

The enclosure is fully enclosed and has a walnut oil finish exterior.

As the depth is as thin as 12 cm, it can be used as wall hanging.
