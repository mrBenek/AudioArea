Commentary

Named the Ultra Doron Cone System, this is a 4-way speaker system that uses Doron Cone.

A non-press 25 cm cone woofer is used for the low range, a 12 cm cone shaker for the middle range, a 7.5 cm cone tweeter for the high range, and a horn type super tweeter for the ultra high range.

The enclosure is 12 mm thick lauan plywood with walnut material on the surface and a polyurethane open pore finish.
In addition, the input terminal and slide switch are mounted on the back plate part, and the level can be controlled in 3 levels of Bright, Normal, and Soft.
The front grill features a handmade lattice pattern that makes use of traditional crafts. This lattice pattern is removable.
