Commentary

Cabaret series speaker system developed for SR and MI.

Two 20 cm cone type woofer 2118H are installed in the low range.
This unit uses JBL's unique low-distortion SFG magnetic circuit, as well as a long voice coil and finely designed suspension mechanism to enhance linearity.

A horn type tweeter 2404H is installed in the high range.
This unit uses a biradial horn to realize a wide service area of 100 ゜ x100 ゜ and to suppress the generation of harmonic distortion.

Equipped with a continuously deformable attenuator.

The enclosure is built for durability and is made of 19 mm thick rigid multi-layer plywood with an exterior polyurethane coating for added strength.
It is also equipped with a front cover to protect the unit and grill gloss during transportation, a polycarbonate corner guard with a shape convenient for stacking when using the stack, and a carrying handle.
