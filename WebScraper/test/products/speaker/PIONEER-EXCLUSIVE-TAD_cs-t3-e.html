Commentary

Two way speaker system.

For the low range, 25 cm diameter woofer with corrugation of dry press system is adopted.
In addition, an improved roll-type Cross Edge is used for the edge of the cone paper, which has a straight line on the inner side of the cross section. This improves the boundary conditions between the cone paper and the edge, greatly improving the sound quality and characteristics of the woofer in the high sound range.
In addition, the magnetic circuit is equipped with a 100 mm diameter magnet, and the center pole is equipped with a pure copper cap that reduces strain. These features are designed to reduce strain.

A 6.6 cm aperture unit is used for the high frequency speaker. The material and shape are chosen with an emphasis on the natural connection of sound with the woofer.
In addition, a corrugation type Cross Edge with good linearity is used for the edge part, and the sharp rise of sound is important by treating with the minimum necessary amount of brake agent.
The center cap is a dome shape made of the same material as the cone paper and is directly attached to the voice coil.
There is also a large back chamber at the rear of the unit.

The network uses a metalized polypropylene film capacitor with superior frequency characteristics and ultra-low loss.

The enclosure is made of 18 mm thick, high-density particle board.
In addition, acetate fiber and felt-like material are arranged in a partition shape for the sound absorbing material inside to effectively prevent the generation of standing waves.
It also adopts a bass reflex structure with a special 24 ゜ bent pipe duct.
