Commentary

This 3D system speaker is a set of 2 satellite speakers and 1 Subwoofer.

The subwoofer is equipped with 2 20 cm cone type woofers with acoustic filter.

The satellite speaker is equipped with two upper and lower 8 cm cone type units, and a quasi-coaxial type with a 1.1 cm dome type tweeter installed at the center.

The exterior has a black finish.
