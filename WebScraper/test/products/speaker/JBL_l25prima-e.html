Commentary

A bookshelf speaker with a synthetic resin enclosure.

The low range is equipped with 125A which is a 25.5 cm cone type woofer.
In addition, the LE25-1, a 3.6 cm cone type tweeter, is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure uses Acoustiform, a synthetic resin originally developed by JBL, and is integrally molded to create a seamless enclosure.

The enclosure is available in six colors, including red, yellow, blue, brown, white and black, and comes with a net grill for each.

Shelves of the same material, size and design were sold separately and could be used in combination.
