  • ONKYO D-062A

  • ¥ 18,000 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

This is the first speaker system in its class equipped with a Bio-Croscone.

A 13 cm cone woofer with a bio-black cone diaphragm is mounted in the low range, and a 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is mounted in the high range.

The enclosure is a bass reflex type enclosure using MDF.
