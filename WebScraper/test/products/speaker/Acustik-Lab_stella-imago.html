Commentary

This is a tall boy type speaker system developed in a new form by reducing the cost of the Stella series.

Cost reduction is realized by integrating the finish of baffle board and box.

Three cones of approximately 13 cm (5 in) are mounted in the middle and low range.
Two of these units are used as a double woofer for the low range, and the remaining one is used for the full range for the middle range.

A reverse dome type tweeter of about 3.6 cm (1.4 inch) is installed in the high range.

There are 2 types of exterior variations, lacquer finish and matte finish.
