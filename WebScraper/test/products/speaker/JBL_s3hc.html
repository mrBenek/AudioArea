Commentary

This center speaker is ideal for combination with a screen or large horn speaker.

Two 16.5 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
A fiber composite cone is used for the diaphragm.

A horn type tweeter that combines a 2.5 cm diameter compression driver and a horn with a throat diameter of 16 mm is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
