Commentary

This compact speaker system is easy to use and can be installed anywhere.

A 10 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Carbon is cross-woven into the diaphragm as long fibers and combined with a special resin to improve propagation characteristics due to high elasticity and high rigidity, and at the same time, moderate internal loss is realized.

A 1.9 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Polyetherimide, which has properties intermediate between hard and soft domes, is used as a 100 μ ultra-thin plate for the vibration plate of the tweeter to improve both transient characteristics and directivity.

It is an AV-compatible magnetic protection design based on EIAJ, and does not affect when installed near a monitor or TV.

Uses a special polypropylene cabinet that maintains the required strength without adversely affecting sound.

By completely eliminating network crosstalk, more transparent sound is achieved.

There is a vertically and horizontally adjustable wall mounting hole in the back, and various installation is possible by using accessories sold separately.
