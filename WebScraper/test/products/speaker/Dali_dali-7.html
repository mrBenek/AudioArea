  • DALI DALI-7

  • ¥ 280,000 (a set of two cars, around 1986)

Commentary

A toll-boy speaker system.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
It also has a 7.5 cm dome mid-range in the mid-range and a 2.1 cm dome tweeter in the high-range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The exterior is finished with a Danish beach finish, and has a unique design with the DALI logo engraved.
