  • JBL J213PRO

  • ¥ 25,000 (1 unit, around 1986)

Commentary

This is a small speaker system that appeared as a sibling of the J216PROmkII.

A 10 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a cone with a pulp base laminated with a special new material.

A dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit uses a pure titanium deposition diaphragm similar to the J216PROmkII.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

There were metal fittings for ceiling and wall mounting sold separately.
