Commentary

This is a compact speaker system that uses a dome on the newly developed Omu.
It is designed so that sound field reproduction by DSP-1 etc. can be enjoyed smartly.

A 12 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
It uses SFCC (Super Fine Ceramic Composite) cone with improved response characteristics by combining ceramic with cone paper. It combines with the edge of polymer material to realize smooth sound rise.

A 3 cm omnidome type tweeter is used for the high range.
In order to make the most of the sound field characteristics of DSP-1, which collects reflected sound in 360 degree direction, the diaphragm is made into an omnidome shape, which is formed into almost a hemisphere shape.

Both low-pass and high-pass units are designed with a complete anti-magnetic design using a canceling magnet system to prevent adverse effects on monitors, etc.

An OFC (oxygen-free copper) air-core coil is used for the network, and the internal wiring material is also OFC to suppress signal degradation.

In consideration of sound quality and space saving, a thin cabinet made of high-density resin is adopted.
As a sealed type with a sound absorbing material inside, smooth low-frequency reproduction without sudden phase inversion has been obtained. This prevents complex interference and cancellation effects with the low-frequency range of the main speaker, and more natural spatial synthesis of the reflected sound component and the stereo sound image produced by the main speaker is performed to form the sound field.

It comes with a simple stand and hook to adapt to various mounting and installation conditions.
With the flexible stand that can change the orientation 360 degrees, it can be placed vertically or horizontally, and the degree of inclination can be adjusted. In addition, there are 2 types of hooks for vertical hook and yoko gake hook, which can be used according to the installation condition on the wall surface.

The floor tall stand (VSS-1800G/W) was sold separately. It can be easily installed by removing the VS-E1 sub RUNNET.
The speaker cord can be wired inside the stand.
Comes with a fall prevention chain.
