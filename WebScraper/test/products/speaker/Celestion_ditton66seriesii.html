  • Celestion Ditton66 Series II

  • ¥ 235,000 (1 unit, released in 1983)

Commentary

A speaker system that inherits the image of the Ditton-66 and introduces a new sound concept beyond British sound.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 30 cm cone type passive filter is mounted on the back.

The mid-range has a 5 cm domed squaker and the high-range has a 1.9 cm domed tweeter.
