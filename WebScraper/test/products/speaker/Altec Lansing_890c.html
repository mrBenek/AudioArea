  • ALTEC LANSING 890C Bolero

  • ¥ 161,000 (1 unit, released in 1970)

Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system using a phase inverter.

The 890C uses a 25 cm cone passive radiator named Phase Inverter to enhance the low range.
In addition, design changes such as a lattice grill have been added as a renewal model of 890B.

25 cm cone type woofer 406-8C with free suspension is mounted in the low range.
In addition, a horn type tweeter 3000H is installed in the high frequency range.

N-3000E is used for the network.

* The crossover frequency seems to be 2 khz, but N-3000E with crossover frequency of 3 khz was written on the network.
