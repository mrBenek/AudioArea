Commentary

Floor-based speaker system with Coral units in a rugged enclosure.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a horn type squaker is installed in the middle and high range area, which is a combination of a driver M 104 and a separable horn AH-502.

A tone control circuit is installed in the middle and high ranges to adjust the frequency characteristics.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system with a multi-slit structure.
There are 2 variations of exterior finish, walnut and black.

Multi-amplifier input terminal is mounted.
