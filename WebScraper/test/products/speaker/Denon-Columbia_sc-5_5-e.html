  • DENON SC-5.5

  • ¥ 22,000 (a set of two cars, around 1992)

Commentary

A speaker system that can be installed freely.

A 12 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range and a ceramic dome tweeter is used for the high range.

The net is detachable and comes with a vertical and horizontal wall hook.

There were ceiling hanging and wall hanging brackets and tripod stand sold separately.
