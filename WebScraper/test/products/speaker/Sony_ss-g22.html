  • SONY SS-G22

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1992)

Commentary

A compact speaker system with a compact size that does not take up much space and emphasizes the quality of sound.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of lightweight and high-strength cone paper, which is stronger than conventional paper by a special laminate manufacturing method. The bass reflex method realizes low-frequency reproduction with good relief.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

The enclosure uses a time alignment system similar to the high-end model. The sound source positions of both the woofer and tweeter units are aligned on a vertical line to eliminate the transmission time difference of sound waves, and accurate waveform synthesis is performed to reproduce more faithful sound tones.
In addition, it is a rear bass reflex system with ports on the rear baffle.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
