  • ¥ 12,000 (1 unit, sold on May 22, 2001)

Commentary

Center speaker of D-105 series.

Two 9 cm cone woofers with OMF diaphragms are mounted in the lower range. This unit uses an S-line edge that reduces the reflection of propagating waves generated at the junction between the diaphragm and the edge.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

MDF material is used for the enclosure, and a virtual coaxial layout with excellent sense of localization of sound images such as dialogue is adopted.
Also, by setting it upside down, you can set it upward by 6 degrees.

A screw-type terminal for banana plug is used for the input terminal.
