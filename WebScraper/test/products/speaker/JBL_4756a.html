Commentary

Sound Power Series 2-way speaker system.

It is suitable for sound reinforcement and full-scale music playback in medium-sized and larger halls.
The 4756A is based on the 4755A and consists of two additional biradial ring radiators 2404H as a high-frequency unit.

Two 30-cm cone type woofer 2206H are installed in the low range.
The mid-range is equipped with a horn type unit that combines a compression driver 2445J with a 10 cm diameter titanium diaphragm and a flat front biradial horn 2380A with a 5 cm throat.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Tri-amp drive system is adopted.
In addition, by inserting a crossover network N4751AP between the middle and high regions, it is possible to switch to a bi-amplifier drive system divided into low and middle regions.
