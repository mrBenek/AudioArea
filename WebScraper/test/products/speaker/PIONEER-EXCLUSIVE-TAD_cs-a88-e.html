Commentary

With 5 speakers based on 30 cm woofer and a pure furniture style design with a grid grill, the CS-A88 is a speaker designed to listen to the sound reproduced by the speaker in the atmosphere of luxury furniture.

The woofer, mid-range and three tweeters are split in a sharp network to avoid interference distortion.

Elegant cabinet with natural wood walnut and oil finish.
