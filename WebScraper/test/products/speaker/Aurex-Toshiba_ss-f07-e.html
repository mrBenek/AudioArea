  • Aurex SS-F07

Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system that uses planar speakers.

A 25 cm flat woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm of this unit uses a CR polymer flat diaphragm newly developed by Orex to realize light weight, high rigidity, and moderate internal loss.

The mid-range is equipped with an 8 cm flat skoker that uses a CR polymer flat diaphragm.

A 3-cm flat tweeter is installed in the high range.
CR polymer is used for the diaphragm of this unit, and in addition, aluminum skin material is sandwiched to increase the rigidity.

Two laminated baffle plates with increased sound and resolution are used for the enclosure CFCs and baffles.

The stand used in the picture was sold separately.
