  • Technics SB-210

Commentary

A speaker system with well-balanced playback sound.

It is equipped with a high compliance type 20 cm cone woofer with a convex roll edge for the low range.
A large 90 φ ferrite magnet is used for the magnetic circuit of this unit.

A 5 cm horn type tweeter 5HH17 is mounted in the high range.

Equipped with a continuously variable attenuator.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and the exterior is designed in a European style with Oceania cheeks.
