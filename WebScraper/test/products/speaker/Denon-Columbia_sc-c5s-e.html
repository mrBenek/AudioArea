  • ¥ 22,000 (1 unit, around 1983)

Commentary

The quality of the SC-C7 is further condensed into a compact speaker system.

A 16 cm cone type woofer that uses a newly developed polypropylene polymer cone for the diaphragm is mounted in the low range.

A 2.5 cm hard dome type tweeter using an alpha boron diaphragm is installed in the high range for wide-range playback.

The enclosure is designed and sized by measurement and listening, and is made of hard particle board.
In addition, the finish is metallic silver finish.
