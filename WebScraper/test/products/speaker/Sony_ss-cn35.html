Commentary

Center speaker with easy-to-install thin structure

Two 8 cm cone woofers are installed in the low range and one 2.5 cm dome tweeter is installed in the high range.
Each unit has a magnetic shielding design and can be placed close to a CRT.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
