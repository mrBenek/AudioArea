Commentary

This is a passive type sub-woofer designed for real theater systems.

The unit is equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer.
High-rigidity pulp cone mixed with Kevlar fiber is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The plate material is a heavy weight class design combining 25 mm thick side panels with 38 mm thick MDF material.
