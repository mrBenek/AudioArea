  • CORAL FX-10

  • ¥ 45,000 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

A speaker system equipped with the best-selling 10F-60 unit.

The unit is equipped with the F-60 series 25 cm cone type full range speaker unit 10F-60.
The diaphragm is made of Coral's original hardened cone paper, and the center cap is equipped with super duralumin dome radiator.
The voice coil adopts an edgewise winding method to realize high efficiency while suppressing temperature rise.

The enclosure is designed for a perfect match with 10F-60.
The shape of the bass reflex duct is a slit shape determined by thorough hearing test and tuning. In addition, consideration is given to materials, machining accuracy, and resonance countermeasures.
By making it a tall boy type, setting space is kept small.

Baffle plate for mounting horn tweeter H-60 is included.
The rear terminal has a hole for the level controller.
