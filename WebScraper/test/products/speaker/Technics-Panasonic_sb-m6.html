Commentary

A 3-way speaker system that uses a double magnet structure for protection against magnetic fields.

It features a 22 cm flat woofer in the low range, a 5.2 cm flat midrange in the middle range, and a 2.8 cm flat tweeter in the high range.

Equipped with level control.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex design.
