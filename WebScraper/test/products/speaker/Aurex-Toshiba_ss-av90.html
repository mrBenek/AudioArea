  • ¥ 68,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1990)

Commentary

A floor-type speaker system designed for AV.

An acoustic symmetrical method is adopted for the unit layout.
This system is a virtual coaxial system in which each unit is arranged vertically in a straight line, and reproduces the sound field unique to video AV speakers such as the localization of narration and sound spread.

Two 16 cm cone woofers are built in for ultra-low frequencies, and flat deep bass playback up to 40 hz is reproduced through upper and lower separated ducts.
This unit uses an active system driven by a dedicated amplifier, and the volume balance can be set independently of the full range.

Four 12 cm cone type full-range speakers are installed for low frequencies.
In addition, two 2.5 cm domed tweeters are installed in the high range.

Uses AV magnetic shielding compatible design.
