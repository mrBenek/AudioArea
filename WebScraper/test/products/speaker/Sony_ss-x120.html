  • SONY SS-X120

  • ¥ 10,500 (1 unit, around 1983)

Commentary

This compact speaker system was developed as the best speaker for mini components.

It is equipped with a 10 cm cone woofer for the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter for the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and is equipped with cord hooks that can wind up excess cords.
