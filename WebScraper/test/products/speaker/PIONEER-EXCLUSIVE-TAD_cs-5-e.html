  • Pioneer CS-5

  • ¥ 12,800 (1 unit, sold in March 1968)

Commentary

This bookshelf type speaker system was developed with the aim of having an ideal size that can be placed in any space and the sound quality of a large system.

The unit is a 2-way configuration with a 20 cm cone type woofer for the low band and a 6.5 cm tweeter for the high band.

Natural wood walnut is used for the enclosure, and it has an oil finish.
