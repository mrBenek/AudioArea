Commentary

Compact sealed 2-way speaker system.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
IMG (Injection Molded Graphite) cone is used for the diaphragm. This material combines polypropylene and high-rigidity graphite fiber to ensure moderate internal loss and high strength.

A 1.25 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
A newly developed polycarbonate diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The exterior has a Chatsworth Oak finish.
