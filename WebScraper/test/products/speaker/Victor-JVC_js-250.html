Commentary

A two way speaker system using cone paper manufactured by Kurt Mueller.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Cone paper made by German Kurt Mueller is used for the diaphragm.

A 6 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The center cap -deposited hard duralumin center diaphragm is used in the center cap part. In addition, the damper less structure reduces non-linear strain.

A level control is provided for the high-frequency band.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system. The front baffle is 20 mm thick, and the unit is firmly fixed by mounting it through a rubber ring.
