  • LINN TUKAN

  • ¥ 150,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1994)

Commentary

This compact 2-way speaker system is ideal not only for the main system but also for the rear of subsystems and surround systems.

A 12.5 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
It uses an aluminum die-cast frame and is magnetic resistant by double magnet.

The high-frequency range uses a 1.9 cm ceramic dome type tweeter, the same as the Celtic, the highest class speaker system of LINN at that time.
Equipped with a rear cap to prevent the effects of sound pressure changes inside the enclosure.

A strong glass fiber substrate is used for the network part.
In addition, it can be easily upgraded to a multi-amplifier system by the exclusive active module sold separately.

The speaker jacks support bi-wiring.

An enclosure made of MDF material and a grill structure without frame realizes a reproduction sound without coloring.
Low-frequency reproduction is achieved by using Caseton block with excellent sound absorption characteristics.
There are 2 types of models, walnut and black, with a veneer finish.

Bra kit with wall mounting metal fittings is sold separately and the mounting angle is adjustable.
