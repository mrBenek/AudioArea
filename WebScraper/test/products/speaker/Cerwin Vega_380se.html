Commentary

Floor-type speaker system.

A 38 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a 5.2 cm diameter voice coil and an aluminum die-cast frame.

The mid-range has 2 18 cm cone mid-range units.

The 2.5 cm horn type tweeter with a dual-phase plug design is installed in the high range.

The mid-range and high-range are equipped with level controls that can be adjusted within ± 4 dB.
The network is also equipped with a high-frequency self-reset circuit breaker and fuse as a protective mechanism.

The enclosure uses a bass-reflex design with a 3/4-inch particle board.
The exterior has a highly durable wood finish.
