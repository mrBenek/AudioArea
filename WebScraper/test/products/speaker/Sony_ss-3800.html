Commentary

This large speaker system is the top-of-the-line of the ES series.

Two 38 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
This unit adopts a strong magnetic circuit using 8 magnets of 30 φ x30.

Two 12 cm cone type skokers are mounted in the middle area.

Two 5-cm horn type tweeters are installed in the high range.
The magnetic circuit is a strong magnetic field type using Permendur.

The middle and high levels can be switched in 3 levels each, and can be adjusted according to the operating conditions.

The enclosure uses a 40 mm thick rigid homogen material.
The exterior is finished with cheek oil stain finish.

It is equipped with a network switching switch and can be switched to a multi-amplifier configuration by combining channel dividers.
