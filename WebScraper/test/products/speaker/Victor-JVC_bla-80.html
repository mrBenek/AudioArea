Commentary

This is a flagship speaker system developed by further improving the BLA-60.

A 40 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is made of cone paper with Konica Le Dome attached to it to reduce the split vibration often seen in large-diameter speakers. The magnetic circuit uses three magnets of 45x30mm in diameter, a sealed yoke with high magnetic efficiency, and a voice coil with edgewise winding of copper wire.

The mid-range is equipped with a horn-type squaker that combines a full-scale driver and a four part sectoral horn.
The driver unit uses a titanium alloy diaphragm with a tangential edge and a diameter of approximately 45 mm. In addition, although the semicircular horn is not coated with damping material, the squeal is reduced by the thickness of material and coating.

A 15-cell multi-cellular horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.
By making it a multi-cellular type, the directional characteristics in horizontal and vertical directions are improved.

The enclosure is 30 mm thick lauan plywood and the interior is sound-absorbent with glass wool.

Equipped with level control.

Equipped with 2ch/3ch multi-amplifier input.
