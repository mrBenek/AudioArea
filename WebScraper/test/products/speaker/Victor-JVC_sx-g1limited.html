Commentary

In order to maximize the attractiveness of the full range metal single cone, this is a speaker system made by the hands of craftsmen using materials such as pure gold and muku birch.

The speaker unit uses a 12.5 cm cone type full-range speaker.
The diaphragm of this unit is based on an ultra-lightweight, pure aluminum metal diaphragm. Copper plating is applied first, and gold plating is applied on the surface. This three layer structure has high rigidity and light weight. Moreover, it has moderate internal loss. The natural resonance of the metal diaphragm is eliminated, and the high transparency of pure gold is added.
In the SX-G1Limited, the unit design was carefully examined, and the butterfly damper, which had been used in the famous units in the past, is used as the damper. This allows for high linearity at the time of Oire force and high articulation even at a small volume. In addition, the Alnico magnet, which has high magnetic flux density, is used as a tube-type yoke and inner magnetic type.

The cabinet uses a cascade bass reflex system to achieve rich bass reproduction.
This is a method to obtain a natural low frequency by making a plurality of low frequency resonance points. An acoustic room of a bass reflex structure is installed inside the cabinet, and the resonance frequency is shifted from the external bass reflex duct.

The cabinet is made of birch muck.
This material is grown in Hokkaido and has been around 150 years old. It is dense, hard and heavy because it is a tree in a cold region. Victor naturally dries it for 5 years and ages it for 4-5 years to stabilize it.
These plates are precisely processed by the best of Victor's woodworking techniques, and assembled into a strong cabinet by a special assembly method using newly developed adhesive.

The cabinet surface is coated with polyester mirror coating using a special coating method.
Polyester mirror coating is also used for pianos and high-class furniture. It has undergone more than 30 processes to deep beauty, natural sound and transparent sound.

The input terminal uses gold plating with little degradation in sound quality.
In addition, no solder is used for connection, and a caulking method is used to eliminate noise pollution from the transmission system.

Comes with a speaker stand.
The stand supports are made of the same birch mulch as the cabinet, and the bottom is made of Italian mulch marble.
