Commentary

A two way speaker system.

The low band is equipped with KEF KEF original Bexstrene material.
KEF developed by KEF in the early' 60 s. Computer analysis showed that the quality characteristics of the material were stabilized. The material has adequate internal losses, uniform properties, and minimal change over time.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.

A newly developed network developed using a computer is used in the network section to minimize the fluctuation of characteristics near the crossover point and realize smooth connection.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a natural wood finish on the exterior.
