  • ¥ 20,000 (a set of two cars, around 1996)

Commentary

A compact speaker system that can be set freely according to the conditions of use, such as hanging on the wall or placing on a shelf.

Equipped with a 10 cm cone type woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter in the high range.

The enclosure is a slim design with a thickness of 10 cm for freedom of installation.

Equipped with screw holes for mounting speaker brackets and mounting holes for wall mounting.
