  • Marantz Model6G MkII

  • ¥ 49,000 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

This 2-way speaker system is almost the same size as the Model7mk2 and has high cost performance.

A 10-inch (25 cm) cone woofer is installed in the low range.
The magnetic circuit uses a 1 kg ferrite magnet assembly which is approximately twice the same class.

An edgeless 1.25 inch (3 cm) cone-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.

Equipped with a high-frequency control switch, 3-position switching is possible.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
