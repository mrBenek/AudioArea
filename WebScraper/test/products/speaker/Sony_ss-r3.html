  • SONY SS-R3

  • ¥ 32,000 (1 unit, around 1979)

Commentary

The R Series speaker system was newly developed based on the know-how of the G Series.

A 25 cm cone type woofer with a high linearity damper is installed in the low range.

A 5 cm balanced drive type tweeter using a titanium diaphragm is mounted in the high range.
The front of this unit is equipped with an acoustic lens that diffuses the high-frequency components to enrich the atmosphere and sense of presence.

In the network section, we have improved the aluminum foil electrolyte for the electrode and adopted a resin case type non-straightening electrolytic capacitor, an orient-core network coil, and an air-core network coil to improve the overall characteristics.

The enclosure calculates the radiation frequency of each plate surface by Fast Fourier Transform and controls it to suppress the box ringing.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
