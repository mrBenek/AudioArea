  • JBL A822 Vecchio

  • ¥ 45,000 (1 unit, released in 1999)

Commentary

The Vecchio series speaker system was developed as a speaker with a natural sound.

It is equipped with a 20 cm pulp corn woofer in the low range and a ribless titanium dome tweeter with an oval horn newly designed to increase resolution in the high range.

It is designed to be magnetically resistant to accommodate multimedia applications.

MDF material is used for the enclosure, and an assembly structure is adopted to eliminate unnecessary resonance.
Also, the finish is a real cabinet finish.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
