  • Toshiba/Aurex SS-26

  • ¥ 23,000 (1 unit, released in April 1969)

Commentary

A sealed three way speaker system.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses high-compliance free edges using newly developed materials and special molding technology.

Equipped with 12 cm cone type skoker in the middle area.

The high-frequency band is equipped with an 8x4cm square horn tweeter that uses a special molded resin material that is light and has high rigidity to reduce the weight of the vibrating part.

Equipped with level control, you can adjust the sound quality according to the listening environment.

The enclosure uses a high-density particle board and is completely sealed with no screws on the back plate.
In addition, the front net can be removed.
