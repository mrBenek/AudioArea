Commentary

Studio monitor speaker with an internal volume of 100 liters.

The low range is equipped with SB75-314 which is a 31.6 cm cone type woofer.
75 mm diameter aluminum bobbin and voice coil of edgecopper ribbon is adopted on a 75 mm diameter aluminum bobbin, and it is designed to be placed in a uniform magnetic flux even for high power input, so that uniform linearity and braking force are obtained.

The mid-range is equipped with SM75-150 which is 7.5 cm soft dome type mid-range.
This unit contains a magnetic circuit with a gap length of 5 mm, similar to an ordinary woofer, and an edge-wise copper ribbon voice coil with a winding width of 3.5 mm. The coil diameter is 75 mm, the same as the dome diameter. Coupled with a double suspension mechanism, this unit can withstand at least 10 times the power of a typical mid-range radio and has an extremely wide and smooth directivity.

The SH25-100, a 2 cm soft dome type tweeter, is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type with an internal volume of 100L using MDF material.
In addition, the front baffle is removable for professional maintenance.
The exterior is finished with natural wood and waxed after sealing with satin lacquer.

The final adjustment before shipment is done strictly with the stereo pair.
