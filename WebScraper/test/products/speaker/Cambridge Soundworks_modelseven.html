Commentary

Compact speaker system using pure natural oak.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter cooled by magnetic fluid is installed in the high frequency range.

The enclosure uses an acoustic suspension system that has been perfected by Henry Cross for more than 30 years. This system controls the diaphragm by utilizing the spring action of the air obtained by the enclosed structure, and achieves excellent bass reproduction.
The enclosure is made of solid natural oak veneer and is manufactured in a piece-joint construction used for traditional furniture and other items. In addition, all corners are harmful vibrations and diffraction waves.

Punching metal cover is used on the front.
