  • TRIO KLX-1

  • ¥ 32,000 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

A non-directional speaker system that provides a wide listening position, whether you hang it on the wall or place it in the center of the room.

It has a unique style. It is equipped with a 16 cm cone woofer by フィクスドエッジ in the low band and a 5 cm cone tweeter with a special chamber in the high band.

The enclosure is semi-spherical and has eight illumination lamps and is designed to create a unique atmosphere.

There are 3 colors, blue, orange and ivory.
