Commentary

Speaker system using coaxial 2-way unit 604-8L.

The unit is equipped with 604-8L which is a 38 cm coaxial 2-way unit.
In the 604-8L series, the new Tangerine phase plug is used to expand the high-frequency band and increase the sound output. In addition, the new manta ray Rayhorn made of special plastic material is used to eliminate the unique coloring of the horn.
The main damper and voice coil are made of materials with good radiation performance for the low frequency section, the coil itself is also unique and improved for powerful reproduction of the low frequency band.

The rear-mounted network section contains one volume pot for adjusting the middle and high levels.

The enclosure is a newly designed enclosure based on the old famous model 612 through repeated computer simulation and trial manufacture. This enclosure is made of Finnish birch and is finished with unique maple color by repeating sold paint several times.
