Commentary

This is a speaker system based on the D 102 a and further condensed in size.

A 12 cm cone type woofer is used for the low range.
The diaphragm uses a non-press bio-cloth cone made of high strength wood pulp mixed with natural cellulose but highly crystalline ascidian cellulose. It is also made by a non-press manufacturing method.
An engineering elastomer with excellent linearity is used for the edge material.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is used for the high range.

The network is a distributed isolated wiring network with low and high frequency interference. Components such as capacitors manufactured by Raderstein, Germany are used.

The enclosure has a wood finish and uses threaded speaker terminals for very thick cable connections.
