Commentary

The D-605 series 2-way speaker system.

A 13 cm cone woofer with an OMF diaphragm is mounted in the lower range. This unit uses an S-line edge, which reduces the reflection of propagating waves at the junction between the diaphragm and the edge.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

MDF material is used for the enclosure.

It is equipped with wall mounting metal fittings on the back and 60 mm pitch screw holes corresponding to brackets on the bottom.

The input terminal uses a screw-type speaker terminal compatible with banana plug.
