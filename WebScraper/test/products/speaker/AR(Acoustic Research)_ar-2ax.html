  • AR(Acoustic Research) AR-2ax

  • ¥ 68,000 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

An improved 3-way speaker system based on the AR-2x.

It has a 25 cm cone woofer for the low range, a 9 cm cone midrange for the middle range, and a 2 cm dome tweeter for the high range.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure uses an acoustic suspension system and has a walnut finish on the exterior.
