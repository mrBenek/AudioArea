Commentary

This small bookshelf speaker system was developed based on the know-how accumulated in the A series.

A 10 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses the same Kevlar fiber as the upper model.

A 2 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass-reflex method, and solid natural wood plates are used for the top and side and natural wood finishes for the appearance of the other surfaces.
