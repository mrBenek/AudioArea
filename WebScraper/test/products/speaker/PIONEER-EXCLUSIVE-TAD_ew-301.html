  • EXCLUSIVE EW-301

  • ¥ 45,000 (1 unit, around 1978)

Commentary

A woofer system which is the woofer part of Exclusive's Model3301 speaker system.

It is equipped with a 30m cone type woofer as a low frequency unit.

The cabinet shape considering the diffraction effect and tough cone paper have been adopted to improve the transient.

Equipped with a multi-channel terminal for multi-amplifier drive.

Built-in dedicated network for MT-12 (Low Cut)

By using a speaker stand (sold separately), the diffraction effect can be processed and a clearer low range can be obtained.
