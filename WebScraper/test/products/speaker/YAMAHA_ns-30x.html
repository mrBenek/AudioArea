Commentary

This 3-way bookshelf type speaker system was developed with the aim of high efficiency, bright and powerful sound with good sound separation.

A 20 cm unit is used for the low band.
This unit uses SFCC (Super Fine Ceramic Composite) corn, which is made by fixing fine ceramic powder on the surface of corn paper.

The mid-range uses a 2.5 cm dome unit that uses a HN titanium dome with light weight and high rigidity, with the titanium surface treated with High Nitride by CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition).

A 1.5 cm dome type unit is adopted for the tweeter in consideration of the sound balance between the middle and low frequencies. A 1.5 cm dome with aluminum evaporated on the surface of polyester film is used for the diaphragm.

The unit layout has been carefully examined in terms of sound quality and design. It is an inline center that matches the center of the baffle surface, and a round baffle with rounded edges on both sides of the baffle has been adopted.
