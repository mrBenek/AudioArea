Commentary

A four way speaker system designed to reduce the size of the PATRICIAN 800.

The low-range is equipped with a 46 cm cone woofer, 18 wk, and the mid-range is equipped with a 20 cm cone unit, SP8B.
The mid-range segment is equipped with a T25A a T25A driver and a horn 8 hd, and the high-range segment is equipped with a horn type tweeter, T350.

X25835 is used for the network part.

The enclosure is a corner type.
