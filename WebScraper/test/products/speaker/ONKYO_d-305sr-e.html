  • ONKYO D-305SR

  • ¥ 25,000 (2 units 1 set, sold on May 18, 1999)

Commentary

Surround speakers designed to match with the D-305F and D-205F.

A 12 cm cone woofer using silk OMF for the diaphragm is mounted in the low range.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter using a diaphragm based on polyester fiber is mounted in the high range.

MDF material is used for the enclosure.
It is equipped with screw holes corresponding to wall mounting bracket and 60 mm pitch mounting bracket on the back.
