Commentary

This speaker system was released as the first product of the SX series of Victor Speakers.

The woofer is equipped with 25 cm cone type woofer.
The cone paper of this unit uses KDU cone paper made by German Kurt Muller.

A 5 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the middle and high area.
The soft dome diaphragm has a highly flexible dome-like structure that realizes a spherical wave shape that could not be achieved with a hard diaphragm. The dome material is made of uniformly woven fabric so that it does not stretch.
In addition, by using a flexible rubber-based dump material, it has a feature that it does not have the natural resonance of a hard diaphragm.
The front of the tweeter is equipped with a one touch removable diffuser.

A silicon-steel core coil is used in the network to reduce non-linear strain in the Dainyu force. The DC resistance value is designed to be 1/20 or less of the speaker resistance value to prevent the loss of damping of the amplifier.

The cabinet uses North American Douglas-fir plywood for the front and rear baffles, and a soundbar to equalize the sound propagation speed in the vertical and horizontal directions of the cabinet, making it a cabinet with beautiful sound.
In addition, ester wool, which has a sound absorption coefficient of less than 1 kHz two to three times that of conventional glass wool, is used for the sound absorbing material inside.

The exterior is finished with spruce, and the high solid, flat and clear coating prevents scratches.
