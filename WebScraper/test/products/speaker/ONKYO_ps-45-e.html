  • ONKYO PS-45

  • ¥ 25,000 (1 unit, around 1982)

Commentary

A 2-way speaker system used in the Radian series stereo set.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a delta olefin diaphragm, which is also used in the D series.

A wide range horn-type ceramic tweeter is installed in the high-frequency range.
The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
