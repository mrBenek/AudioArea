  • AR(Acoustic Research) 310HO

  • ¥ 180,000 (a set of two cars, around 1997)

Commentary

A speaker system of the HO series, which is a lower model of the 312 ho.

A long stroke type 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.
The mid-range has two 13 cm cone mid-range units, and the high-range has a 2.5 cm dome tweeter.

The 310 ho has been developed with technologies such as Aeroform Tweeter, Focused Array Configuration, Energy Control Contour Baffle, and Voice Balanced Tone Matching.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

Built-in-mirror image subwoofers provide a slim design.

There are 2 variations of the exterior finish, black and maple.
