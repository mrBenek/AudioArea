  • JBL LT1

  • ¥ 120,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1983)

Commentary

A small speaker system designed for both indoor and outdoor use.

A 13.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit has an SFG magnetic circuit to achieve a low distortion factor, and LT1 has an SFG effect of up to 2 kHz to reduce distortion across the entire Woofer playback band.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is die-cast, and high rigidity is ensured by integrating the woofer frame and front baffle.

Equipped with mounting mounting bracket to increase the freedom of setting.
