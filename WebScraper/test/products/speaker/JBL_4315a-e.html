Commentary

This is a compact 4-way speaker system among studio monitors.

It is equipped with a 30 cm cone woofer 2203A for the low range, a 20 cm cone woofer 2108 for the middle and low range, a 13 cm cone scoker 2105 for the middle and high range, and a horn type tweeter 2405 for the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
In addition, it can be suspended by the support metal fittings on the back.

There are two variations in appearance : the 4331A uses a gray finish and black grill, and the 4331 awx uses a walnut finish and blue grill.
