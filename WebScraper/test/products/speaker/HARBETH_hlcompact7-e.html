Commentary

This is a speaker system that has been thoroughly reviewed in pursuit of realism and clarity by further polishing the beautiful sound unique to Havez.

A 20 cm cone woofer with a newly designed original diaphragm is mounted in the low range.
This cone uses a diaphragm manufacturing method that blends three types of polymers and arranges them from the center to the periphery, solving the problem of different cone surfaces near the center and at the periphery.
In addition, the voice coil has a unique structure with air permeability in mind to stabilize temperature characteristics over a long period of time.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit is a hard dome type evaporated on a unique T-yoke, and uses ferrofluid cooling to facilitate heat diffusion at high temperature of voice coil by applying magnetic oil.

A unique Super Tuned Structure (STS) is introduced in the enclosure design. By using a special sandwich structure in which multiple materials with different resonance and stiffness are layered between hard MDF materials, unwanted vibrations are damped while making use of the sound by combining with the physical properties of the material itself rather than weight or rigidity.
The bass reflex port of the enclosure is made into a square shape based on repeated trial listening, and important parts inside the enclosure are dumped with tar material.

The Frameless Frame method is used for the net grill. A different jersey material is used for the net grill. The frame of the grill is fitted into the groove of the baffle to improve the integration with the enclosure, thereby minimizing the influence on the high frequency range.

There was a special stand sold separately.
