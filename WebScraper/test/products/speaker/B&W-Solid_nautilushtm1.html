Commentary

This is a center speaker made with the advanced standards of the N800 series.

Two 16.5 cm cone type woofers are installed in the low range.
Paper / Kevlar cone is used for this unit.

It has a 15 cm cone type mid-range in the mid-range.
The diaphragm is an FST unit with a Kevlar cone. The FST maximizes the effect of Kevlar by improving the response time of the cone and sound transmission integrity, and the frame is designed to minimize the impedance to airflow from the rear of the driver.

A 2.5 cm metal dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure has a curved cabinet structure and uses a closed system.
In addition, a matrix structure is adopted inside.
There are 3 variations of the finish of the exterior, black ash, red cherry and cherry.

As a separately sold option, there is a special stand that can change the elevation angle in 3 stages.
This stand comes in 2 color variations, black and silver.
