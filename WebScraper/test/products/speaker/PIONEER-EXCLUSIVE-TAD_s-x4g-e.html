  • Pioneer S-X4G

  • ¥ 24,000 (1 unit, around 1982)

Commentary

This 2-way speaker system was developed by investing 15 years of know-how in making small speakers.

The S-X4G is a champagne gold color system based on the S-X4.

A 18 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses highly heat-resistant covered copper wire for the voice coil and has a large diameter of 48 mm Φ to improve heat radiation effect and input resistance characteristics.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
Titanium diaphragm is used for the diaphragm, and as with Woofer, highly heat-resistant coated copper wire is also used for this unit.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
