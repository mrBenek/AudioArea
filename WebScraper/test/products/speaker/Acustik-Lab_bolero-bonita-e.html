  • ¥ 420,000 (excluding tax, one set of two, sold in July 2007)

Commentary

A speaker system with a custom-made unit.

The low frequency range is equipped with a 5.25inch cone woofer custom-made by Peerless.
This unit is designed with a Nomex cone.

The high-range is equipped with a custom-made 1-inch tweeter developed for the Bolero Bonita by the Nordic Beifa Corporation.
This unit has a structure in which two types of diaphragms, one made of aluminum alloy and the other made of synthetic chemical products, are driven coaxially with a single coil, achieving smooth and high-frequency playback.
