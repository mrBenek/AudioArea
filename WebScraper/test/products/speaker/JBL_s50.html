  • JBL S50

  • ¥ 88,000 (a set of two cars, around 2000)

Commentary

A minimal-size horn-type speaker system incorporating traditional technology.

A 13.3 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
A paper cone is used for the diaphragm, and an extended pole piece is used for the magnetic circuit to create a vertically symmetric magnetic field.

A 2.5 cm soft dome type tweeter combined with a horn is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

A large gold-plated speaker terminal compatible with banana plug is adopted.
