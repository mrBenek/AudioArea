  • CORAL BX-800

  • ¥ 11,300 (1 unit, sold in May 1969)

Commentary

High Power Series bookshelf speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a powerful Coral that was used in the 25 cm unit class of conventional magnetic circuits. By combining a long travel voice coil and a skybar edge, large input, high efficiency, low distortion and low fo are achieved.

A 9 cm cone type witter is installed in the high range.
A powerful magnetic circuit and voice coil made of aluminum wire enable large input and high efficiency.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method and has a walnut polyurethane finish on the exterior.
There is also a hand-finished grating grill on the front, which is not removable.
