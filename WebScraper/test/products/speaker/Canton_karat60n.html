  • CANTON Karat60N

  • ¥ 115,000 (1 unit, around the early 1990 s?)

Commentary

This is a bookshelf speaker system that is the top model of the Karat-N series.

It has a 26 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 12 cm cone squaker in the middle range.

A 2.5 cm hard dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
Newly developed manganese aluminum is used for the diaphragm of this unit. By combining the characteristics of light-weight aluminum and high-rigidity titanium, band balance and transient characteristics are improved without deteriorating input resistance characteristics.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and the exterior is finished with real wood.
