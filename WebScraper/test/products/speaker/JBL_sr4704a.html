Commentary

SR Series floor monitor speaker system.

In the lower range, it is equipped with 38 cm cone type woofer 2226H which adopted the JBL original VGC system.
A horn type unit using a flat-front biradial horn 2370A with 90 ° horizontal directional characteristics and a driver unit 2426H is mounted in the high frequency range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system and is designed for floor monitoring at an elevation angle of 45 °.

It has a built-in network.
In addition, a 2-way multi-amplifier drive changeover switch is mounted.
