Commentary

A three way speaker system.

In the low range, it is equipped with 125A which is 25 cm cone type woofer.
This unit uses a voice coil of 5 cm diameter and an Alnico V magnet of 1.1 kg.

The middle area is equipped with LE5-6 which is a 13 cm cone type skoker.
In addition to the adoption of a large diameter voice coil and an aluminum die-cast frame, it also uses an aluminum dome for smooth reproduction of the middle and high sound sections.

The LE25-2, a 3.6 cm cone type tweeter, is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method, and the exterior has a natural oak finish.

There are 3 color variations of the front grill and I could choose from orange, blue and brown.

A speaker system with a net grill and patterns in three colors : orange, blue and brown.
