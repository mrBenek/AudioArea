  • ONKYO E-83AmkII

  • ¥ 54,200 (1 unit, around 1970)

Commentary

A 3-way closed-type speaker system that uses units equivalent to the Septor series.

The low-range is equipped with a unit equivalent to the 30 cm cone type woofer W-30AmkII.
This unit incorporates newly developed technologies such as an epoxy-glass fiber butterfly damper, a foamed neoprene rubber edge, and an edgewise wound voice coil.

The mid-range is equipped with a horn type squaker equivalent to the HM 500 AmkII, and the high-range is equipped with a horn type tweeter equivalent to the TW-9A. These units use duralumin diaphragms.

Equipped with level control and multi-amplifier terminals.

The enclosure is sealed with lauan plywood and has an oil finish on the exterior.
