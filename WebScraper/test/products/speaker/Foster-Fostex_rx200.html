Commentary

Coaxial 2-way speaker system using RP-type horn tweeter.

The unit is equipped with a 20 cm coaxial 2-way speaker unit.
The RP method has been adopted for the driver of the tweeter part of this unit, and we have succeeded in minimizing the phase difference by setting the diaphragm in the optimum position.
In addition, the woofer part adopts structures such as a highly rigid light alloy die-cast frame and ferrite magnet, two large and small double dampers and a long travel voice coil, and is designed with high power with a wide linearity and low distortion at input time.

The enclosure features a bass-reflex design and a natural wood walnut finish on the exterior.
