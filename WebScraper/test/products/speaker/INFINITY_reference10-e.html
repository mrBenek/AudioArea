Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

A 16.2 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range.
IMG (Injection Molded Graphite) cone is used for the diaphragm. This material combines polypropylene and high-rigidity graphite fiber to ensure moderate internal loss and high strength.

A 1.9 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm uses a polydome whose quality has been improved based on the results of the past five years. By using polypropylene on cells with small mass, wide-band reproduction and good damping characteristics are ensured.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The exterior has a Chatsworth Oak finish.
