Commentary

A speaker system developed based on the W & D (Wide & Dynamic) concept.

A 25 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
In this unit, the know-how of Zero-7 has been put into it. In order to suppress the squeal and distortion caused by the reverse resonance of the edge, the sealed edge structure which is covered to the maximum with the rib of the outer edge of the cone paper is adopted. This is because the area of the edge part occupies about 1/4 of the whole in the 25 cm cone, and the inherent sound of the edge cannot be ignored.
This structure also increases the strength of the cone paper itself and prevents split vibration.
The corn paper is made of alpha corn with the same wide pistnick motion as Zero 5.

A 7 cm composite unit is used for the middle area.
It uses a wide pistic motion diaphragm with increased strength by electroless metal plating on Phenorix cone paper developed in zero 5.
In addition, the center diaphragm made of titanium material obtained the shape with the highest composition by computer simulation, and the smooth connection with the dynaflat ribbon tweeter is obtained by completely eliminating the decomposition vibration.

A dynaflat ribbon tweeter with an aluminum voice coil printed on a polyimide film that directly drives air is installed in the high range.
In the zero 3, the response is extended to 100 kHz by reducing the mass of the vibration system, reexamining the horn aperture, and adopting the same high-flux strontium ferrite magnet as the zero 7.
