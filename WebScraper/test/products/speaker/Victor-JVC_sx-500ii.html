Commentary

This is a speaker system that was completed by further polishing the SX 500, which was made by Victor with particular attention to natural sound.

A 20 cm cone unit made by Kurt Mueller is used for the low frequency range. This cone is strongly reproduced by an Alnico magnet with a high flux and low leakage inner magnetic structure.
The low-range magnet is made of iron that has been annealed (special heat) for a long time at a high temperature of around 700 ℃ as the material of the pole piece, bringing out the good characteristics of Alnico.
Oxygen-free copper wire is used for the voice coil.

A 3.5 cm soft dome unit made of habutae silk laminated with thin rubber film is adopted for the high range.
A high-purity 6N copper wire with a purity of 99.999% is used for the voice coil, and an internal magnet type alnico magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

The front baffle, which has a significant effect on sound quality, is made of laminated maple with moderate internal loss and sound velocity. The new G-line unit layout is adopted using Victor's unique sound intensity method. The super elliptical baffle improves directivity.
In consideration of sound quality, 100% wool sound absorbing material is used inside the cabinet.
In addition, the finish is 6-all-wood finish.

Bi-wiring compatible input terminal is used to prevent back electromotive force.
The two single-channel speaker cables are connected independently to the woofer and tweeter to improve the coverage and multi-drive for upgrade.
The capacitor of the network uses a separator paper made of silk blend paper, which reduces the vibration energy generated between the electrodes in the capacitor and provides smooth sound quality.
