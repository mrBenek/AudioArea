Commentary

A line-array-type speaker system developed for use in concert halls and concert halls, as well as PA for musical instruments.

Four 25 cm cone type wide-range speakers K110 of the K series developed as a unit for musical instruments are mounted in the low range.

Two horn type tweeters 2402 are installed in the high range.

The 4682 enclosure uses lightweight, one piece plastic for strength and durability during transportation and transportation.
In addition, the four sides of the enclosure are equipped with molded hanging handles for ease of loading and unloading.

By using the separately sold front cover 46-0181, it can be stacked and transported with a grill equipped.

The enclosure and grill are orange and the cover is BlackCar and sold separately.
