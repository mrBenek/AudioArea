  • SONY SS-CN3

Commentary

Center speaker using S. F. C. T. which can adjust the direction of the tweeter.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.

A ± 20 ° movable 2.5 cm dome type V. D. tweeter with a CD horn by S. F. C. T. (Sound Field Control Technology) is installed in the high frequency range, and the directional characteristics of sound can be adjusted according to the installation location.

The enclosure is designed with a bass reflex and has a nut that can be attached to the stand.
