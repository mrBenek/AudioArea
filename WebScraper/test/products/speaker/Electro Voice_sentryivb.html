  • Electro-Voice SENTRY IVB

  • ¥ 398,000 (1 unit, around late 1970 s)

Commentary

An all-horn speaker system developed based on innovative design concepts.

Two 30-cm cone-shaped woofers are installed in the lower range, making up a folded horn with a cutoff frequency of 50 Hz.

A radial horn type mid-range with a radiation angle of 120 degrees or more is adopted for the mid-range, and 1823M is installed as a driver unit.

The ST350A, a radial horn type tweeter with a radiation angle of 120 degrees or more, is mounted in the high range.
The tweeter also has a self-resetting, purely electronic protection circuit and a 4-step switch for 1 Hz or higher response.
