Commentary

Small speaker system with round form design.
It is made with sufficient thickness using special resin molding technology, and the round form prevents sound quality degradation due to diffraction of sound.

A 12 cm full-range unit is used for the unit.
The corn paper uses Bio-Croscone, a blend of pulp and MFC (Micro-Ferri-Cellulose), which is highly crystallized among natural cellulose.
In addition, an aluminum pole phase equalizer is used to eliminate the sound quality degradation caused by air trapped behind the dust cap, improve the linearity of the low frequency range, and improve the frequency characteristic degradation caused by the hollow effect. In addition, a dust cap with good ventilation is used to effectively dissipate the heat generated by the voice coil at the time of large input.
A double magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

Since the bottom part has mounting screws for the stand at 60 mm intervals, it is compatible with various stands such as TK-M50.
