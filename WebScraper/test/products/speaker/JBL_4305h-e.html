Commentary

A 2-way speaker system that uses a horn driver.

In the lower range, it is equipped with a 20 cm (8 inch) white corn woofer that makes use of the texture of natural pulp and is uncolored and unbleached.
Low-frequency playback is improved by adopting a high-rigidity aluminum die-cast frame and installing a symmetrical bass reflex port in the enclosure.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.
It combines a 2.5 cm (1 inch) compression driver with a pure titanium diaphragm with a flat-front horn that extends to both sides of the enclosure.

Equipped with a continuously variable level attenuator that can adjust the output level of the horn driver.

The enclosure uses 19 mm thick MDF material with a black ash wood-grain finish.

A gold-plated binding post-type speaker terminal is used for connecting extremely thick speaker cables.
