Commentary

This is a speaker system for the ultra low band of the concert series.

The 4847 is a woofer system developed as part of a full-range multi-way suspended speaker array assembly.

The unit is equipped with 2225H which is a 38 cm cone type woofer.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system tuned to 40 Hz.
The top, bottom and sides have been cut into 15 ° tapers for a variety of layouts within the array.
In addition, the surface has a glass fiber coating with excellent durability and water resistance.

8 pieces of embedded metal fittings for flying are equipped as standard.

The connector is equipped with ITT Canon EP-8 type male and female (bi-amplifier specification).

The Concert Series included a wide range of specialized options, including a carrying load case and various speaker mounting brackets.
