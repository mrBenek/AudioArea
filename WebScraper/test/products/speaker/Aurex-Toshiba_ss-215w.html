Commentary

High efficiency type floor type speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer using unbleached lightweight cone is mounted in the low range.

A 6 cm cone type tweeter with a damper less structure is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed using high-density particle board.

The net grill on the front is not removable.
