  • Pioneer S-W5

  • ¥ 55,000 (1 unit, sold in August 1999)

Commentary

Powered sub-woofer with double woofer.

The unit is equipped with 2 16 cm cone type woofers.

A PWM 200W digital switching amplifier is used in the amplifier section.

Two types of input terminals are mounted : a speaker terminal and an RCA pin jack.
It also has a line-level output jack that allows you to connect two or more sub-woofers.

Equipped with auto power off function, continuously variable turnover frequency control, and phase selection switch.
