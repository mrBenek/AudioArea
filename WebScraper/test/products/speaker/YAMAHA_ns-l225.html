Commentary

This speaker system uses a 25 cm woofer to improve low sound reproduction.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a white cone manufactured by the sheet manufacturing method. Despite its 25 cm diameter, the weight is as light as 12g. The long voice coil, alnico magnet alnico magnet, roll edge, etc. reduce sound breakdown at high output.

A 5 cm radial tweeter is installed in the high range, and the combined structure of 5 cm cone type and 2.3 cm dome type makes use of both advantages.
The diaphragm is as light as 0.13g.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type with a Century Walnut finish.
