  • Pioneer S-5PG

  • ¥ 19,500 (1 unit, around 1985)

Commentary

A slim wall-mounted speaker system with integrated glass panels.
There was also a cork type (S-5PC).

The size of the glass panel is the size of a record jacket, and it is sandwiched between 4 color sheets and tempered glass.
It also comes with a metal hook to hang records.

A 14 cm cone type unit is used for the low band and a 6.6 cm cone type unit is used for the high band.

The special rib structure enhances the low-frequency reproduction capability.
