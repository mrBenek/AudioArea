Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

A 20 cm high compliance unit is installed in the low range.
The magnetic circuit uses a 90 φ ferrite magnet and a 4-layer voice coil.

A 5 cm cone type unit is installed in the high range.

The enclosure removes the cut end of the baffle and locks the rolled net into a streamlined edge.
