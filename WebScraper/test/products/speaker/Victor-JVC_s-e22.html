  • VICTOR/JVC S-E22

  • ¥ 11,500 (1 unit, around 1988)

Commentary

Compact 2-way speaker system.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Unlike ordinary cone paper, the diaphragm is molded without applying strong pressure, and the fiber structure is not damaged and natural linearity of low sound reproduction is achieved.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is used for the high range.

It comes with a hook for hanging on the wall.
