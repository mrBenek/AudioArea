  • FOSTER/FOSTEX GX-2000

Commentary

Two way speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer using SM cone as a diaphragm is mounted in the low range.
In addition, a 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure is designed with a bass-reflex design and a walnut finish on the exterior.
