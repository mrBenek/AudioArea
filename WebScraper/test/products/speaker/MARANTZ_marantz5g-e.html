  • Marantz Marantz5G

  • ¥ 29,900 (1 unit, around 1972)

Commentary

A slim style bass reflex speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type unit designed for bass reflex is used for the low range.
A 4-layer voice coil is adopted, and the lowest resonance frequency of the unit is 26 Hz to improve low-frequency reproduction capability.
The edge is made of butyl rubber.

The tweeter is a 4.5 cm cone-shaped unit, and the edge is an edgeless type that avoids mechanical noise and does not use any paper or cloth. It is directly connected to the frame with a thin film of elastic agent coated on cone paper.
