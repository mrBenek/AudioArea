  • SONY SS-9300

  • ¥ 27,000 (1 unit, around mid 1970 s?)

Commentary

A barrel type non-directional speaker system slightly smaller than the SS 9500.

The unit is equipped with 5 10 cm cone type full-range speakers.
Each unit is mounted radially and emits sound in all directions, making it a non-directional speaker that can be mounted anywhere.

The unique design of the barrel enclosure is achieved by spherical processing of the material itself, and this configuration provides high mechanical strength, low unnecessary resonance, and sound quality with effective braking.

The exterior comes in two color variations, walnut and tamo, both with an open pore finish.

It comes with a special cushion and can be used as a chair.
