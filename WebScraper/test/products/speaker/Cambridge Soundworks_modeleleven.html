Commentary

A portable speaker system that Henry Cross came up with while preparing for his vacation.

The Model Eleven consists of amplifiers, satellite speakers and sub-woofers.
The amplifier and satellite speakers can be stored in a trunk-case type subwoofer during transportation.

An acoustic suspension system is used for the subwoofer.

The amplifier section is designed with differential input, low noise and low distortion rate.
The Route 3 input is installed in this amplifier section.

The power output terminal of DC9V can supply power to portable CD players.

Equipped with tone control, balance volume, and stereo / mono switch.
