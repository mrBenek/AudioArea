Commentary

Two way speaker system using horn type unit.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The large diameter voice coil and low distortion magnetic circuit realize sharp rising sound.

A horn type unit combining a driver unit 2416H and a bi-radial horn 2371A is mounted in the high range, realizing 90 ° horizontal x 40 ° vertical directional characteristics and excellent energy response.

It is equipped with an attenuator for the high frequency band and can be adjusted according to the listening environment.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
It is also designed to mount a power amplifier directly on the back of the speaker, so you can mount a power amplifier 6210 or 6211.

The XLR and Phone type input / output terminals are installed in addition to the normal input terminal, and it can be used in a wide range of applications.

Separately sold options included wall brackets, ceiling brackets and adapters for the MT4612 stand.
