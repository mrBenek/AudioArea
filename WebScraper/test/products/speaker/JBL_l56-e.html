Commentary

A bookshelf speaker system that inherits JBL's 2-way tradition and introduces new technology.

A 25 cm cone type woofer 118H is installed in the low range, and a dome type tweeter 034 is installed in the high range.

Professional technology has been introduced into the network section to ensure a flatter frequency response.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
