  • VICTOR/JVC JS-450

  • ¥ 33,900 (1 unit, around 1976)

Commentary

Front bass reflex floor-type speaker system.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Newly developed KP5 cone paper is used for the diaphragm.

The middle area is equipped with a free edge type 12 cm cone type squaker.

A 6 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit has a damper less structure and is equipped with a center diaphragm.

Level control is provided for the middle and high frequencies.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system with a front duct.
