  • ONKYO D-11X

  • ¥ 29,800 (1 unit, around 1987)

Commentary

This is the successor to the D-11 and is an AV speaker system in the price range of the X Series.

The woofer uses pure cross carbon woofer. In addition, the anti-magnetic design of the canceling magnet system prevents leakage of magnetic field lines.

A 2.5 cm dome type unit with a carbide titanium diaphragm is used for the tweeter, and a new ceramic 1.6 cm unit is used for the super tweeter.
Like Woofer, it has a magnetic protection design.

The enclosure uses a rigid cabinet with round baffle specifications, and the front baffle uses 20 mm thick hard particleboards.
There is also a groove between the front baffle and the rear of the cabinet to reduce vibration transmitted from the unit.
