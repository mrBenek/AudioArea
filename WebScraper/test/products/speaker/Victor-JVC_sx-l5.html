Commentary

This speaker system adopts a 2-way + super-tweeter configuration to support ultra-high frequency sources such as DVD audio.

18 cm balanced oblicorn type woofer is installed in the low range.
The Balanced Oblique Cone realizes smooth characteristics by distributing the mid-range resonance by placing the driving point off-center, and uses Kurt Mueller cone for the cone paper.
This woofer covers most of the fundamental band from the low sound.

A 3 cm soft dome type tweeter is installed in the middle area.
In order to emphasize the density of the mid-range, we have adopted a soft dome diaphragm with a multi-layer coating of rubber on tough hemp, which is driven by ferrite and double magnet.
In addition, aluminum die-cast is used for the frame.

A 1.9 cm pure aluminum dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm of this unit is plated with pure gold for a more natural sound, with an edge equalizer, optimized frame shape and improved directivity with a short horn to smooth out radiation in the ultra-high frequency range, and equipped with a three dimensional supersonic diffuser.
The magnetic circuit uses a neodymium magnet internal magnetic circuit.

In order to reproduce the feeling of air by expanding the directivity of ultrasonic wave band, the tweeter unit is mounted in an independent small cabinet with reduced diffraction effect.

The top of the cabinet is tapered to give a sense of unity with the cabinet of Super Tweeter, and the front baffle is 30 mm thick conifer MDF.
A maple veneer is used for the exterior, and it is finished with a lustrous mirror surface by 25 processes of urethane coating.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
This stand is made of the same material as the main body to harmonize the sound, and the combination of solid wood and particleboard (5 layers structure) reduces the noise and vibration.
