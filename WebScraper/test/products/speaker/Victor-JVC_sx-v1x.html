Commentary

This is a bookshelf type speaker system incorporating new technologies such as Oblicorn.

A 14.5 cm oblicorn type woofer is installed in the low range.
In this unit, the driving point is placed off-center to disperse the mid-range resonance and realize smooth characteristics. In addition, the diaphragm is made of lightweight rubber edge made of Kurt Muller with high linearity and elasticity, and excellent tracking performance is obtained by spider suspension.
The magnetic circuit uses Alnico magnetic circuit which is a tradition of Victor, and is driven by edgewise voice coil of AQVC method.

A 1.9 cm hard-domed tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is made of pure aluminum plated with pure gold. It is equipped with an edge equalizer that smooths radiation in the ultra-high frequency above 40 kHz, an optimized frame shape, and a 3-D supersonic diffuser.

The network section uses carefully selected parts such as low-loss film capacitors, air-core coils, gold-plated brass machined binding posts, and oxygen-free copper wires.

Uniframe baffle structure is adopted for the front baffle.
This system requires a sound similar to that of a full-range unit. The high-range and low-range units are placed as close as possible and directly mounted on an 8-mm-thick brass baffle board. Furthermore, S/N and sound image density are improved by means of ribs for resonance dispersion and brass end pins installed at the front bottom of the baffle board.
In addition, it has a three dimensional reflective finish, so you can enjoy subtle color changes depending on the viewing angle.

Solid high-density mahogany is used for the enclosure.
In addition, new structures have been introduced to strengthen the rigidity and improve the rigidity. In addition, the external surface has been mirror-finished with full flush surface through 24 processes.

There was a special speaker stand sold separately.
This stand is also made of pure mahogany, and the height of the stand is adjusted with an emphasis on hearing.
