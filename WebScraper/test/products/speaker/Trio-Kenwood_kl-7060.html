Commentary

Speaker system for large floor type.

A 30 cm cone woofer with a roll-free edge is used for the low range, a 16 cm cone squaker with a back cavity for the middle range, two 5 cm horn type tweeters for the high range, and a 3 mc aluminum cone super tweeter for the ultra high range.

The mid-range and high-range are equipped with independent continuously variable level control.

The enclosure is a bass-reflex type.
In order to suppress the boomy low sound inherent in the bass reflex type, a damping type bass reflex duct is adopted to realize a tight low sound reproduction.
In addition, the material has been reconsidered from the hearing test. Lauan plywood is used for the baffle surface, and homogen material is used for other parts to prevent resonance completely and to reproduce bright and low sound.

A 2 - and 3-channel multi-amplifier switch is installed for the multi-channel amplifier.
