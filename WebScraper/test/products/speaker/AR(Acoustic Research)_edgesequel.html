  • AR(Acoustic Research) EDGE SEQUEL

  • ¥ 29,800 (a set of two cars, around 1998)

Commentary

A speaker system that inherits the design philosophy of EDGE and is more compact.

It has a 11.4 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 2.5 cm dome tweeter in the high range.
Each unit has an anti-magnetic design.

The enclosure uses an acoustic suspension system.
It is made of ABS resin and is injection molded.
The dedicated bracket equipped as standard is compatible with wall hanging type and self-standing type.
