  • AIWA SX-11

  • ¥ 48,000 (a set of two cars, around 1983)

Commentary

Speaker system of mini component my Pace33 series.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.

A 7 cm semi-dome type squaker is installed in the middle area.
The diaphragm uses APC film (polymer composite film for sound) developed for speakers to reduce distortion in the high frequency band.

A 4 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
APC film is used for the diaphragm in the same way as Skoka.

It is equipped with a level roll for middle band and high band.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
