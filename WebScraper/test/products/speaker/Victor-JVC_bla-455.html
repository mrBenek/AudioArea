Commentary

Based on the technology obtained with the KP series, this speaker system has been enhanced by the adoption of dome unit and thorough comparative listening.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is a high-compliance woofer using a free edge soft damper and uses DU cone paper developed in Germany for the cone paper.
The magnetic circuit uses a wet barium ferrite magnet and a large diameter 4-layer voice coil 6 cm in diameter.

Two 16 cm dome type skokers are used for the middle area.
The diaphragm is a unique dome type using KP-3 cone paper which is light and has high Young's modulus. It performs accurate piston motion up to high frequency band, and achieves low distortion and excellent directional characteristics.

In the high range, a horn type tweeter with multi-cellular and a newly developed dome tweeter using KP-3 cone with excellent sound quality are combined as a multi-speaker.
This improves the directional characteristics and input resistance characteristics.

The front baffle and back plate of the enclosure are made of Douglas-fir, a conifer widely used as a soundbar for pianos, guitars and violins.
The internal sound absorbing material uses ester wool (55% wool, 45% ester), which is specially processed with animal fiber and chemical fiber. This ester wool has a sound absorption coefficient of 2 to 3 times that of glass wool at 1000 Hz or less, and prevents entrapment.

It is equipped with level control for the tweeter and skoker.

Multi-amplifier input terminal is mounted.
