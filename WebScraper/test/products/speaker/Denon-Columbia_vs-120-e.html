  • DENON/COLUMBIA VS-120

Commentary

This is a bookshelf type speaker system with a 3-way, 3-speaker configuration equipped with Doron-Cone.

A 20 cm cone woofer cone woofer for the low range, a 7.5 cm cone squaker for the middle range, and a 5 cm horn tweeter for the high range.

The enclosure is made of 10 mm thick lauan plywood with a polyurethane open pore finish with walnut material on the surface.
In addition, the front grill uses a handmade lattice pattern that makes use of traditional crafts. This grill is removable.

Equipped with level control.
