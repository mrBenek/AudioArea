Commentary

Slim 3-way speaker system.

The unit is a 3-way configuration and uses a 20 cm cone type unit for the low band, a 7.5 cm cone type unit for the middle band, and a horn type unit for the high band.

Equipped with level control in the middle and high range, it can be adjusted according to the size and structure of the room.

The enclosure is a slim type of bass reflex structure with an oil finish.

Plug type jack is attached in addition to screw type terminal, and connection can be made easily with the included jack attached cord.
