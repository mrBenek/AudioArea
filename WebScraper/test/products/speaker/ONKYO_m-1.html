  • ONKYO M-1

  • ¥ 29,800 (1 unit, around 1977)

Commentary

A speaker system designed to reproduce human voices.

A 25 cm cone type woofer W-2530A is mounted in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a newly manufactured sheet cone with multi-corrugation, which is strong for large amplitude and has succeeded in weight reduction. This diaphragm is driven by a 50 φ long voice coil and a 120 φ x60 φ x15mm magnetic circuit, which achieves 60W input resistance and 93dB/W/m high efficiency despite being small.

TW-410A 4 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit uses a 0.15g lightweight cone because it is m designed to match the woofer.

Equipped with a 3-step mode selector.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
