Commentary

A compact speaker system using coaxial speakers.

A 12 cm laminated pulp-based cone is used in the lower part of the coaxial unit.
In addition, by strengthening the low-frequency magnetic circuits and using OFC (oxygen-free copper) wires for the voice coil, we are improving transmission characteristics.
Polyester imide is used for the tweeter part, and a highly viscous special magnetic fluid is used in the magnetic circuit gap to eliminate unnecessary vibration.
The unit has a double magnet to cancel magnetic leakage, and is equipped with a shield cap for double magnetic protection.

The network combines film capacitors and BP (electrolytic) capacitors that are carefully selected as high-pass filters to achieve even higher quality in the high-frequency range.

The enclosure uses a rear ducted ballast system with a high-density particle board to eliminate interference from units secured to the front baffle.
In addition, we thoroughly design for high sound quality in every detail such as port diameter and damper material distribution.
This product is finished with Tasman maple.
