Commentary

This speaker system is an improved version of the J216PRO.

A 16.5 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit combines excellent rigidity with moderate internal loss by coating the pulp-based diaphragm with polypropylene.

A dome type tweeter using pure titanium vapor deposition diaphragm is mounted in the high frequency range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

It can be installed on ceiling or wall surface by using metal fittings sold separately. Also, there was a speaker stand as an option sold separately.
