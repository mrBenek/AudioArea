  • DENON DSW-7.5

Commentary

A subwoofer system with external speakers and an amplifier that can drive the subwoofer.

A 16 cm cone type unit with double voice coil has been adopted.

I drive an external speaker with a 35W + 35W amplifier and a subwoofer with a 60W amplifier.
All three amplifiers can also drive a subwoofer (100w).

The cut-off frequency can be switched to three levels to allow various combinations of speakers.

It is equipped with a AVSE switch that increases the impact of the ultra-low range in AV playback.

It can be operated with a separately sold remote controller.
