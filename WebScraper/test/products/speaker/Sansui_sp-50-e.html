Commentary

A cost-performance model that brings together sound production experience and technology.

A full range type 20 cm cone type unit is installed in the low range.
By using a large yoke and magnet, the magnetic flux density of 11,500 gauss is realized, and by installing an aluminum radiator, the connection from low band to high band is improved.

A horn type tweeter is installed in the high frequency range.

The enclosure uses a bass-reflex system using a pipe duct.
15 mm thick hard plywood is used for the material, and 20 mm acetate with good sound absorption effect is used for the inside.
The exterior has a walnut finish.
