Commentary

A floor-based speaker system designed to blend in with your interior design and sound fidelity.

It is equipped with 126A which is newly developed 30 cm cone type woofer in the low range.
This unit is equipped with a powerful magnetic circuit using a 7.5 cm edgewise winding and large Alnico V magnet. In addition, a die-cast concentric ring is used for the voice coil to reduce distortion caused by sudden signal changes by making the movement of the cone accurate.

A 13 cm cone type squaker LE5-5 using a large diameter voice coil is mounted in the middle area.

A newly developed 3.6 cm horn type tweeter, 077, is mounted in the high frequency range to realize wide frequency response, high efficiency and wide directivity characteristics.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
Also, the net grill comes in 3 color variations, and I could choose from blue, brown and red.
