Commentary

A compact coaxial two way speaker system that employs a coaxial two way unit.

The unit is equipped with a coaxial 2-way unit that combines 13 cm cone type and 2.5 cm soft dome type.
The woofer purity pulp corn is used for the woofer part. Urethane coating is applied to the roll edge of the cloth material. Special damping agent is applied from the edge to the corn paper. In addition, the voice coil uses a 6-layer short coil for the BSP bobbin for smooth low-frequency reproduction.
The tweeter is equipped with a small and highly efficient ダンプド dome type using neodymium magnet which boasts strong magnetic force.
In addition, the air chamber effect of glass wool at the rear part of the magnetic circuit smoothes the connection with the woofer at the crossover point. In addition, a urethane ring is attached to the tweeter frame to prevent interference between the units.

The network emphasizes sound quality by using air-core coils, bipolar capacitors, and film capacitors, while eliminating the low-pass filter and controlling the characteristics of the woofer to establish a more natural sound balance.

The enclosure has a rear duct and bass reflex structure using a high-density particle board, and natural sound quality is obtained by minimizing sound absorbing materials.
The surface is finished with a real wood finish using a walnut thrust plate.

It is equipped with a speaker terminal for banana plug.
