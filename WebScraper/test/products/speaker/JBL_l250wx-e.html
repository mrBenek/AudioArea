Commentary

This uniquely designed speaker system was developed as a flagship product for consumer speakers.

It is equipped with 36 cm cone type woofer LE14H-1 in the low range, and 20 cm cone type mid-woofer 108H in the middle and low range.
These units have adopted a low-distortion SFG magnetic circuit. In addition, both sides of the cone are treated with Aqua Plus to increase rigidity and moderate damping.

12.5 cm cone type Skoka LE5-11 is mounted in the middle and high area.
Like other units, this unit uses a copper coil to minimize the change in power compression at the time of Dainyu force, and realizes high efficiency and wide dynamic range.

A 2.5 cm dome type tweeter 044-1 is mounted in the high range.
Phenol-based diaphragm is used for the diaphragm to increase efficiency and improve input resistance characteristics.

In the network section, all crossover points are unified to 6dB/oct. to obtain a smooth power response and enhance the effect of the slant baffle.
In addition, the bus bar method is adopted to prevent the deterioration of sound quality with the conventional continuously variable attenuator.

The enclosure features round-and-slant baffles and a trapezoidal front panel with curved edges to prevent sound diffraction and minimize peaks and dips.
In addition, the phase of each unit is matched by tilting the front baffle, and sound image localization is improved by making it symmetrical.

The exterior of the enclosure had a walnut finish.
In addition, there were 4 kinds of variations such as Black, Oak, Ebony and Rosewood as ordered items.
