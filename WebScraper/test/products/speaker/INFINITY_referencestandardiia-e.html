Commentary

In order to realize the expressive power equivalent to RS-1, the woofer part is also incorporated into the diffraction wing, and this speaker system puts the point source theory into practice.

There are two 25 cm cone units in the low range, three EMIM units in the middle range, and three EMIT units in the high range (one of which is on the back).

Special equalizer amplifier is included and bi-amplifier drive is possible.
