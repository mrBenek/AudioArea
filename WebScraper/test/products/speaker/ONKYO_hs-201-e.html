  • ONKYO HS-201

  • ¥ 12,000 (1 unit, around mid 1960 s)

Commentary

This is a 2-way speaker system that uses a bookshelf type, which was rare at that time.

Through research and development of boxes and sound-absorbing materials, we have achieved a more compact design than conventional floor-type models.

A 25 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The cone paper uses Onkyo's original non-pre-cone, and the edge part uses newly developed material and roll edge by special molding.
In addition, the long voice coil, which does not generate distortion even for large amplitude, is combined with a powerful magnetic circuit to improve the characteristics.

A 12 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure has a sealed structure using 7-ply 12-mm-thick plywood, and the interior is fully filled with glass wool to suppress the disturbance of sound waves.
