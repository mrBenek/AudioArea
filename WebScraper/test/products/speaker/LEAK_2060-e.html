Commentary

This 3-way floor type speaker system was developed with the aim of flat response and precise reproduction power.

A 30 cm sandwich cone woofer is used for the low range.
By dumping the roll edge with adhesive material, generation of strain is suppressed.

A 10 cm plastic cone type unit is used for the middle area.
It uses a perforated structure and is the same as the one used in 2030.

A plastic dome type tweeter of 2.5 cm, which is the same size as 2030, is used for the high range.

The enclosure is hand-finished and the cabinet has resonance protection, especially since the lower frequency limit of the woofer is low.
Plywood with a thickness of 18 mm is used for the material, and asphalt dump material with a thickness of 15 mm is used for the inside.
