Commentary

30 cm cone shaped woofer.

Strong magnet and strong magnetic structure made of high permeability silicon alloy steel are adopted.

Cone paper is made by mixing various fibers and forming it by heating under high pressure. It is used as a diaphragm with light effective mass.

To improve the space factor of the coil ring in the magnetic pole gap and to maximize the use of magnetic energy, the voice coil uses ribbon copper wire spread edgewise.

Roll edge is used for the edge.

A 95L or larger enclosure is recommended.
