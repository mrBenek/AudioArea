  • Maxonic HC501

  • ¥ 27,000 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

A hyperbolic expert horn that is ideal for use in theaters and large halls.
It can be used for D51 driver and D51EX excitation type driver.

In order to suppress the resonance of the horn due to the material and shape of the horn, a special light alloy is used to design it into an ideal shape.
In addition, in order to eliminate the reflection inside the horn and the fluctuation of characteristics observed in the conventional horn, HC501 is separated into each section from the horn throat tip.
