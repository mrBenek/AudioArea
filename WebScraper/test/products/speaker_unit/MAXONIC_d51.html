  • ¥ 24,000 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

Driver for middle and high sound developed for professionals and enthusiasts.

Strong magnets and high permeability silicon alloy steel are used for the magnetic structure.

The diaphragm is made of aluminum foils molded with high-pressure gas (ニュマティック draw method) and surface-treated.
In addition, ポリィミード plastic film is used for the edge, and a voice coil with a hard polyimide film bobbin and heat-resistant insulating copper-plated aluminum ribbon wire wound edgewise is adopted.
