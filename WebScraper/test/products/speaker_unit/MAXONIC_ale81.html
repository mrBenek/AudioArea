Commentary

A 2-way speaker system that combines high-grade units used in a large hall and is housed in a home-oriented enclosure.

The low band is equipped with L401 which is a 38 cm cone type woofer developed for professionals.

In the high-frequency range, the ALH45 horn with acoustic lens is combined with a driver D51.
