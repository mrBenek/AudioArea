  • Maxonic ALE71

  • ¥ 58,500 (1 unit, around 1973)

Commentary

A floor-type speaker system using a newly developed horn with built-in acoustic lens.

In the low range, it is equipped with L301 which is 30 cm cone type woofer which adopted magnetic field structure non-resonance cast frame, etc.

The ALH45 horn with an acoustic lens is combined with a compression driver D45G for the high range.
