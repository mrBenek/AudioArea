  • DIATONE VS-30A

  • ¥ 59,800 (a set of two cars, around 1985)

Commentary

A speaker system with a built-in surround amplifier that employs a wide-area sound system.

It uses a wide-area sound system that expands the listening area.
This system expands the listening area by placing one of the two units inward at 25 °, so that you can enjoy a wide range of stereo sound fields.

The VS-30A has a built-in surround amplifier, and you can perform surround playback by connecting a speaker for the RS-10 or other soundtracks.
In addition, the amplifier section contains a simulated stereo circuit, so you can enjoy stereo sound even from a source with only monaural output.
In addition, it is possible to raise the low temperature by the super bus circuit.

The unit is equipped with 2 10 cm cone type full range units.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
