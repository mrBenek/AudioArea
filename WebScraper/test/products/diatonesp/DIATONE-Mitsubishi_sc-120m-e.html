Commentary

A small speaker system of the Pro Monitor series.

The unit is equipped with a 12 cm cone full-range speaker for all bands.
It is a magnetic resistant design.

Equipped with an automatic return circuit protector to protect the speakers from excessive input.

The enclosure is designed to be small and lightweight, and can be used as a wall mount by using fittings sold separately.

There are two color variations : dark gray (SC-120M-HD) and white (SC-120M-W).
