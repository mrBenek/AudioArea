Commentary

A two way floor speaker system equipped with a passive radiator.

It is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer using a reverse カーブドコーン in the low range.
A 20 cm cone type is used for the passive radiator.

A mechanical load type 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure has an edgeless structure, and by eliminating the protruding edge around the baffle plate seen in conventional speakers, directional characteristics, sound spread, and localization are improved.
