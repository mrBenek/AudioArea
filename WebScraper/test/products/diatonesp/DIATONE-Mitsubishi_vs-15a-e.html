  • DIATONE VS-15A

  • ¥ 39,800 (a set of two cars, around 1986)

Commentary

A speaker system with a built-in surround amplifier.

The VS-15A is equipped with a D. A lens that expands the sound field by changing the propagation path length of the sound wave, and creates a realistic sensation that is enveloped by sound.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full range speaker.

With a built-in surround amplifier, you can enjoy surround sound simply by connecting it directly to the TV.
In addition, the input terminal of mini jack and pin jack is mounted, and various devices can be connected.

The bass reproduction capability is enhanced by the super bass and bass reflex method.

A simulated stereo is installed for the mono source.

There was a speaker stand sold separately.
