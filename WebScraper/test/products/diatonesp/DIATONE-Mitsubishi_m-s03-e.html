  • DIATONE M-S03(S/RD)

  • ¥ 12,000 (a set of two cars, around 1984)

Commentary

Micro-speaker system with built-in amplifier.
This is the first Diatone active speaker other than for professional speakers.

The unit is equipped with a 6.6 cm cone type full range speaker.

The M-S03 uses a flat passive radiator to expand its low-frequency reproduction capability.

There are 2 color variations, silver and red.
