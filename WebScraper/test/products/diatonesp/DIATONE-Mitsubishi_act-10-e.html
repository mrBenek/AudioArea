Commentary

In this model, an amplifier is attached to an actuator that uses the place attached to a wall or desk as a diaphragm.

Equipped with 2 actuator parts, stereo playback is possible.
The unit uses an Alnico magnet with high magnetic flux density, and is also equipped with an anti-magnetic design.
In addition, the actuator is a drip-proof design (JIS Drip-proof Type II), and can be used in kitchens, etc.

Both AC adapter and AA battery can be used for the amplifier part with 2 power source system. It can be used both indoors and outdoors.
It can be used continuously for approximately 3 hours when driven by battery.

It comes with an amplifier of output 3.5W + 3.5W. It is a 2-power supply system that can use both AC adapter and AA battery regardless of indoor or outdoor.

Since the actuator unit has a drip-proof design, it can be used in places with moisture.

The magnet uses Alnico, which boasts high magnetic flux density, making it more compact.
It also has a magnetic shielding structure that blocks the leakage flux of strong Alnico and can be installed on the television side.
