  • DIATONE DS-71P

  • ¥ 20,000 (a set of two cars, around 1997)

Commentary

An active speaker system developed for multimedia applications such as portable CDs, MDs and PCs.

In the DS-71P, 8.7 cm coaxial type unit is mounted in the unit.
This unit consists of a 8.7 cm cone type in the low range and a 2.5 cm semi-dome type in the high range. A liquid crystal polymer (LCP) diaphragm is used for the diaphragm.

Built-in 7W + 7W amplifier.
