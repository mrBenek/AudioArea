Commentary

A center speaker developed for multi-channel playback in Dolby Digital and DTS.

The DS-C1000ZV is designed as a center speaker with the same tone as the ZX series while focusing on the playback of the voicing band.

Two 16 cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
The diaphragm uses an aramid cloth diaphragm to achieve the same sound quality as the ZX Series. This diaphragm uses an organic fiber aramid, which is lightweight, highly rigid, and has an appropriate internal loss. The two layer structure eliminates the binder such as epoxy resin, which has been conventionally used for moldability and airtightness. Thus, the diaphragm is lighter in weight. It also takes advantage of the large internal loss of the aramid and improves the effect of reducing the natural sound.
In addition, by adopting a double woofer system, the vibration area is secured and excellent power response is obtained.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is made of boronized titanium, which achieves an extremely high specific elastic modulus by diffusing boron into titanium. In order to take advantage of this material's superior characteristics, a DUD structure, in which the voice coil bobbin and diaphragm are integrated, enables high-speed, clear, high-frequency reproduction.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
The front bass reflex port can be closed with the attached duct damper to adjust the sound quality in the low range.

There was a special stand with tilt mechanism sold separately.
With this stand, you can adjust the angle of the speaker in 4 stages from horizontal to 25 degrees according to the distance between the listening point and the screen.
