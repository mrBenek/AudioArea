  • DIATONE SS-100

  • ¥ 14,000 (1 unit, released in 1976)

Commentary

A speaker system that uses a special bass reflex system exclusively for floor type speakers.

A 20 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
In addition, a mechanical load type 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.

It uses a special bass reflex system exclusively for the floor type, and it has good damping and enables reproduction with less distortion up to the heavy and low sound range.
