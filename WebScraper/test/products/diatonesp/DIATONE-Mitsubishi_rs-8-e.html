Commentary

A thin speaker system with a compact design of 5.3 cm.

It can be used as a rear surround speaker or as a main TV speaker.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full range speaker.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

The cabinet uses a sealed system.

Comes with a hook for hanging on the wall and a speaker stand that can be moved vertically and horizontally.
