Commentary

A medium-sized speaker system in the Pro Monitor series.

An 18 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range, and a 60 degree horizontal and vertical directional horn unit is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Built-in self-resetting circuit protector protects the speakers from excessive input.
