  • DIATONE DS-101AM

  • ¥ 10,000 (one set of two cars, around 1996)

Commentary

An active speaker system developed for multimedia applications such as portable CDs, MDs and PCs.

The unit is equipped with a 6.4 cm cone type full range speaker.
LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) is used for the diaphragm.

Built-in 3W + 3W amplifier.

Equipped with a headphone jack and two input terminals for line output, simultaneous input is possible for mixing music.
