Commentary

A speaker system developed for NHK's in-house monitoring system.
It uses an 8 cm ultra-thin completely enclosed cabinet so that it can be used for both floor-standing and wall-hanging types.

A 16 cm woofer and a 5 cm tweeter are used for the unit.

The operation panel is equipped with a changeover switch, volume controller, and transformer.
