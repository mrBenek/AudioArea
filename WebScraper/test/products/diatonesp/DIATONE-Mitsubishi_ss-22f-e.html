  • DIATONE SS-22F

  • ¥ 26,500 (1 unit, around 1976)

Commentary

A small silver-type floor speaker system.

A 20 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
Also

The cabinet has adopted an orthodox bass reflex design.
The front baffle has an edgeless structure that eliminates the protruding edge around the baffle plate seen in conventional speakers, thereby improving directional characteristics, sound spread, and localization.

Level control is mounted on the front.
