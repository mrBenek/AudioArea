  • DIATONE SC-7205

  • Price unknown (1 unit, around 1969)

Commentary

This is a speaker system for プレレコ and solo that is used by hanging it on a baton like studio lighting equipment.

In order to improve clarity, the low-frequency range is gradually lowered. Two units, a 20 cm woofer and a 5 cm tweeter are used in the unit.

The cabinet color is BTS 0001 Association Color No. 2 (gray).
