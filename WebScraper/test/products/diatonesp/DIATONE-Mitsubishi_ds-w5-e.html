  • DIATONE DS-W5

Commentary

This is a sub-woofer with a built-in amplifier and remote control that allows you to easily enjoy low sound playback during surround playback.

The unit uses a 13 cm cone type full-range speaker for the low frequency range.

The enclosure uses the Kelton method to enhance low-frequency reproduction capability despite its small size.

Narrow or wide band width can be selected depending on the source.

Wireless remote control is included.
