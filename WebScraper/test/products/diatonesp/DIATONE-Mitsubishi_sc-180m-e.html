Commentary

A medium-sized speaker system in the Pro Monitor series.

The SC 180 m is designed as a system suitable for event halls and conference rooms.

An 18 cm cone type woofer is mounted in the low range, and a 60 degree horizontal and vertical directional horn type unit is mounted in the high range.

Built-in self-resetting circuit protector protects the speakers from excessive input.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

There are two color variations : dark gray (SC-180M-HD) and white (SC-180M-W).
