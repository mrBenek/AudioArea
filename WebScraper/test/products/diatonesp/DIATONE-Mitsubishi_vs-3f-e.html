  • DIATONE VS-3F

  • ¥ 37,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in June 1988)

Commentary

A toll-boy speaker system.

A 16 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.
It has a magnetic shielding design and eliminates adverse effects on cathode ray tubes, etc.

The stick type (tall boy type) of the enclosure ensures a large capacity while saving space.
The unit layout is designed with the center axis of 25-29 inch TV in mind.
