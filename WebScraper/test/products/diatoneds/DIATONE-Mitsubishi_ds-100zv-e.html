Commentary

A compact 2-way speaker system with flexible settings for multi-channel playback.

A 13 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is directly connected without using network elements, and through thorough sound quality tuning, it enriches the low range of speech and vocals, which are important for AV playback, and enhances reality.

A 3 cm cone type tweeter made of polyimide cone is mounted in the high range.

The cabinet is is small, it is designed to suppress box rumbling.
In addition, it is equipped with a nut on the back that can fix a wall hanging stand.
