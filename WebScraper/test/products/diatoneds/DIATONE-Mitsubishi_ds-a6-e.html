Commentary

This is a small speaker system that uses solid maple at key points to pursue the aftertaste and sound of music like an instrument.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm is lightweight, rigid, and has an appropriate internal loss, using an aramid cloth / cone diaphragm. Resin and dye are eliminated in the aramid cloth diaphragm to suppress the natural sound and achieve medium and low frequency reproduction with good S/N and high transparency.

A 4 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit is designed for the best matching with 16 cm woofer. The cone shape eliminates energy loss near the crossover frequency and provides a flat energy balance like a full range. The use of the aramid material realizes a smooth sound connection.

The network circuit uses carefully selected high-purity parts that minimize distortion during transmission.

Solid maple with beautiful sound is used for the round part on both sides of the baffle for the enclosure. The most simple potato joint is used for joining with the side plate to bring out the sound of the extracted and the beauty of the aftertaste is pursued. In addition, a leather-like ornament is arranged around the unit to reduce the cause of noise turbidity such as unnecessary vibration and reflected sound.
In addition, contributing to sound field reproduction by slimming the width, volume is secured by securing depth. By achieving the best balance between enclosure volume, bass reflex port diameter and woofer unit, low frequency reproduction of 39 Hz is possible despite being small.

Compatible with separately sold speaker stand DK-A5.
