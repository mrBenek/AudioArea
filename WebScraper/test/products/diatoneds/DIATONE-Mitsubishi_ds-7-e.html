Commentary

A speaker system that achieves both compact body size and high sound quality by using a coaxial unit.

The unit has a 13cm2-way coaxial speaker.
Aramid blend cone is used for the woofer part, making both high rigidity of aramid fiber and excellent internal loss of paper compatible.
A 2.5 cm dome type is used for the tweeter part, and a neodymium magnet with nearly 10 times the magnetic energy of ferrite magnet is used for the magnetic circuit.

The DS-7 uses an R to suppress air noise. The DS-7 uses an R to reduce air noise.

A coaxial 2-way unit is adopted for the unit.
An ultra-small 2.5 cm dome type is used for the tweeter part, and an aramid blend cone is used for the woofer part.

There were 2 types, the black model DS-7 and the beige model DS-7C.

It can be used in various applications by using the separately sold stand, etc.
