Commentary

A bookshelf type speaker system with a 3-way configuration.

PW 3016, a 30 cm cone type woofer, is installed in the low range.
This unit uses a reverse カーブドコーン, a low Q corn paper, and a strong magnet in the magnetic circuit.

The mid-range is equipped with PM-1015 which is a 10 cm cone type squaker.

A 5 cm cone tweeter, TW-5016, is mounted in the high range.
This unit uses a spider made of special synthetic rubber to enhance the reproduction capability of the high range.

A level control is provided for the high-frequency band.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a walnut finish exterior.
