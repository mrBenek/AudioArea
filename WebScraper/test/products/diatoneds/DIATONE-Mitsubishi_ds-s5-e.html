  • DIATONE DS-S5

  • ¥ 55,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in August 1994)

Commentary

A tall boy speaker system designed for wide-screen televisions.

A single-drive superbus system is used for the low band, and a 16x8cm elliptical cone woofer is installed as a unit for the low band.
In this system, the rear and front waves are utilized 100% each and integrated into two bass reflex ports, enabling high-efficiency and high-quality bass playback.

Four 9x5cm elliptical cone full-range speakers are installed for the entire band.

In the enclosure, four full-range units are arranged in-line to improve the horizontal directional property, enabling in-screen localization while maintaining the sound field.
