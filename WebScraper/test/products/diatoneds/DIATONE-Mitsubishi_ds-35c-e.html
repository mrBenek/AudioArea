Commentary

A 3-way large speaker system with Sound System Control.

Equipped with Sound System Control developed by Diatone.
This circuit further advanced the level adjustment of each conventional unit. The entire band is divided into five bands and each band can be adjusted in five levels by combining an attenuator and a network element.
This makes it possible to make 3125 adjustments, making it possible to effectively correct the sound field in the listening room and at the same time to perform delicate tone control.

A 30 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
Two 10-cm cone schokers are installed in the middle range, and two 5-cm cone tweeters are installed in the high range.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a walnut finish on the exterior.

Equipped with multi-amplifier terminal.
