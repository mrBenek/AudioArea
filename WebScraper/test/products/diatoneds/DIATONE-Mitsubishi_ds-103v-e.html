Commentary

A small speaker system using a passive radiator.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full range speaker.
In addition, a 10 cm cone type passive radiator is used to drive the unit using the rear wave of the unit, thereby reinforcing the low sound.

It is equipped with a full-scale magnetic shielding design. The magnetic flow is analyzed by a computer, a pole piece is separated, a magnetic shielding magnet and a shield cover are installed. This prevents leakage of magnetic flux, which disturbs TV images, and improves diaphragm driving performance.

The cabinet is made of olefin heat-resistant resin with reinforcement containing inorganic filler, ensuring high heat-resistant effect and rigidity that can withstand severe conditions.

By using the separately sold special stand, it can be used in various applications.
