  • DIATONE DS-16B

Commentary

A speaker system with a built-in phase shifter circuit.

Two pairs of voice coils can be used to create a four channel stereo rear signal.
Since you don't need a dedicated matrix amplifier or a main amplifier for the rear, you can enjoy four channels right away by connecting to the stereo.

It is equipped with a phase shifter circuit, and unlike the case of only difference signal, you can get a realistic sensation rich in musical quality.

Switching between R-L/L-R/Normal is possible.

The unit is equipped with P-610M which is a 16 cm cone type full range unit.

Equipped with continuously variable level control.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
In addition, the exterior has a black walnut wood grain finish.
