  • ¥ 55,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in June 1989)

Commentary

A toll-boy speaker system using the Kelton method.

The DS-10C has a structure in which the top and bottom of the enclosure are divided, and a 2-way bass reflex system is used for the upper part and a Kelton system is used for the lower part.
In the Kelton system, the enclosure is further divided into two chambers at the front and rear, and a unit is mounted inside. The unit mounted inside vibrates the air inside the enclosure to achieve rich low-frequency playback.

At the bottom of the enclosure is an 18cmx12cm oval cone woofer for the Kelton system.
The upper part of the enclosure is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full-range unit for all band and a 5 cm cone type tweeter for high band.

Full-scale anti-magnetic design is adopted.
