  • DIATONE DS-33BmkII

  • ¥ 27,000 (1 unit, sold in May 1970)

Commentary

An improved speaker system for the DS-33B.

A 30 cm cone type unit is used for the low range.
The edge of this unit is a roll edge.

An acoustic road type 10 cm cone type unit is used for the middle area.

An attenuator is installed in the middle and high frequencies, and a multi-amplifier terminal is installed.

The enclosure is made of 18 mm plywood with a walnut finish.
