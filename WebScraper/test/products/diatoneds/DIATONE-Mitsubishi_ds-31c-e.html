Commentary

This is a floor-type speaker system in the first lineup of the Diatone series.

For the low range, it is equipped with a 20 cm cone type woofer PW 2015 and a 30 cm cone type woofer PW 3012.
This increases efficiency and reduces distortion in the mid-pitch valley and near the upper limit frequency, which are common in cone-type speakers.

For 5 cm cone type tweeters, TW-23, are mounted for the high range.

The overall design, including the cabinet, simplifies the network.
The high range of the woofer is designed to attenuate at -12dB/oct. from the crossover frequency without a network.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and has a Eurasian cheek finish on the exterior.
