  • DIATONE DS-200ZA

  • ¥ 60,000 (1 set of 2, released in 1994)

Commentary

This speaker system was developed as a special tune model by reviewing all of the DS 200 z.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The diaphragm uses a new-aramid hybrid cone that is as lightweight as the pulp cone.
In addition, by adopting a woofer direct connection which directly connects the input signal to the woofer unit without using a network, coloring of the sound in the network circuit is eliminated.

A 2.5 cm dome-shaped tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm uses an HD (High Damping) alloy DUD dome, and the DUD structure that integrates the voice coil bobbin and diaphragm makes high-speed playback possible. It also uses a direct mount (DM) system that mounts the magnetic circuit directly in the enclosure, realizing a highly rigid frame structure.

The thickness of the enclosure has been increased to 18 mm and the sound quality has been improved by using a 4-side round baffle.

Uses a gold-plated speaker terminal for banana plugs.

There was a specially designed speaker stand sold separately.
