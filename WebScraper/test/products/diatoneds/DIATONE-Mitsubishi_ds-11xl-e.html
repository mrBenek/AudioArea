Commentary

This is a two way bookshelf type speaker system that focuses on the medium band, which is the fundamental band of music, and clearer high-frequency playback.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
The magnetic circuit of the Woofer uses a magnet shield system that uses a magnetic shielding type magnet to prevent magnetic flux leakage and increase the magnetic flux density to improve sound quality.

A 3-cm dome type tweeter is installed in the high range.
The diaphragm is made of a highly heat-resistant polymer film with excellent sound quality, and the Diatone's unique D. U. D structure integrates the voice coil bobbin with the diaphragm to achieve high speed.

In addition to increasing the volume of the enclosure as a small 2-way enclosure to the limit, the volume in the lower range is improved by adopting the rear bass reflex system.
In addition to the sound-absorbing material, the woofer is set at the optimum position to prevent standing waves generated inside the product, and the strong dispersion resonance type reduces resonance.

There are two color variations for the exterior finish, walnut finish and peer oak finish.

There were special speaker stand, bracket for hanging from the ceiling and bracket for hanging on the wall which were sold separately.
