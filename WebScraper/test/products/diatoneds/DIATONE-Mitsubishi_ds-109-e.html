  • DIATONE DS-109

  • ¥ 16,900 (1 unit, released in 1987)

Commentary

A small speaker system that can be set freely.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full range speaker.

The cabinet is made of highly rigid heat-resistant resin.
Also, by adopting a unique full-scale bass reflex structure, it is possible to reproduce a voluminous low sound.

Various stands were sold separately as an option could be installed for a wide range of applications.
