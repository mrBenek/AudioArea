Commentary

A speaker system based on the DS-34B with improved sound quality, ease of use and design.

A 30 cm cone type woofer with a 30 cm カーブドコーン is installed in the low range.

The mid-range is equipped with a 10 cm cone type squaker.
This unit is an acoustic load type, and the rear part of the diaphragm is completely sealed and the inside air chamber is filled with sound-absorbing material to prevent interference with the woofer and to achieve sound quality without turbidity.
In addition, a magnetic circuit of 85000 maxwell is adopted, and stable sound quality is realized by applying sufficient electromagnetic braking.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit is a mechanical load type and uses special synthetic rubber suspension to provide sufficient mechanical braking.

A level control is mounted on the front baffle, and the level of two ranges, middle and high, can be independently changed by 3 levels.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
The exterior is finished on four sides with natural walnut, the front baffle is also made with natural wood grain veneer, and the front grill is made with double shear RUNNET for a classy atmosphere.

Connection terminal for multi-amplifier is mounted.
