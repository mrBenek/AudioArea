  • DIATONE DS-S1

  • ¥ 170,000 (2 units 1 set, sold in March 1991)

Commentary

A tall boy speaker system designed for large screens larger than 80 inches.

Two 10-cm cone type woofers are installed in the lower range.
S-LCP (Super-Liquid Crystal Polymer) diaphragm is used to ensure high rigidity and moderate internal loss.

8 cm cone type full range unit is mounted for middle and high range.
LCP fiber mixed cone is used for the diaphragm.

The enclosure uses a double-drive superbus system that is two to ten times more energy efficient than speakers in a box enclosure.
A cylindrical cabinet is used to construct a spherical wave sound field.

Available in 2 colors, black and beige.
