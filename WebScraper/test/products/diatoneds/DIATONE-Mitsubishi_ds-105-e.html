  • DIATONE DS-105

  • ¥ 19,900 (1 unit, released in 1984)

Commentary

A compact anti-magnetic speaker system.

The unit uses a 10 cm cone type full range unit.
In order to prevent magnetic flux leakage that disturbs the TV screen, a magnetic shielding system is adopted in which a magnetic shielding type magnet is installed and a shield is used.

The cabinet is made of highly rigid, heat-resistant resin with inorganic filler, and is designed for heavy duty, strong against impact and high temperature. The internal system is designed by the bass reflex system.

By using the optional dedicated stand, more free setting is possible.
