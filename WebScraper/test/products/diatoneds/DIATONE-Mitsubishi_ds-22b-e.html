Commentary

Two way bookshelf speaker system.

A 20 cm cone type woofer PW 2015 b is installed in the low range.
This unit uses カーブドコーン for the diaphragm and MK-5DG for the magnet. It is designed to naturally attenuate the high-frequency band to eliminate the network circuit, which prevents sound quality degradation and flattens characteristics near the crossover frequency.

A 5 cm cone tweeter, TW-5014B, is mounted in the high range.
This unit uses a special rubber spider to reduce distortion.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex method and has a walnut finish on the exterior.
