Commentary

This is a small speaker system using a passive radiator system.

The unit uses a 10 cm cone type full range unit.
This unit has a full-scale anti-magnetic design for AV use.

A passive radiator system using a 10 cm cone type passive radiator is adopted to enhance the low-frequency reproduction capability.
In this system, a passive radiator without a drive system is driven by utilizing the back-wave energy reproduced by a full-range unit.
In addition, a 4L enclosure, which is one size larger for this class, has been adopted to further enhance the lower range.

By using the optional stand sold separately, it is possible to set freely.
