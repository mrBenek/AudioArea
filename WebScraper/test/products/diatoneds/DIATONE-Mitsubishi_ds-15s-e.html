  • DIATONE DS-15S

  • ¥ 15,800 (1 unit, released in 1969)

Commentary

A wall-mounted thin speaker system developed by improving the DS-11S.

The low range has a 16 cm cone woofer, which is almost flat cone, and the high range has a 5 cm cone tweeter.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed and is designed as thin as 5 cm for wall hanging applications.
