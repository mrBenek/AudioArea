  • DIATONE DS-5

  • ¥ 32,800 (2 units 1 set, sold in February 1991)

Commentary

A multi-use compact speaker system.

The unit is equipped with a 13 cm double cone type unit.
An aramid blend cone is used for the diaphragm, and a double cone mechanical 2-way system is used.

The enclosure uses a cylindrical style called a cylindrical cabinet.
In addition, the bass reflex port is R-shaped to reduce air noise and improve the low sound range.

There are 3 types, black color DS-5, beige color DS-5C and yellow color DS-5Y.

By using the optional stand sold separately, it can be used for various purposes.
