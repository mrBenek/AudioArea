  • DIATONE DS-103X

  • ¥ 15,000 (1 set of 2, sold on July 21, 1994)

Commentary

A small speaker system using a passive radiator system.

The unit is equipped with a 10 cm cone type full-range speaker and a 10 cm cone type passive radiator. By driving the passive radiator using the back-wave of the full-range unit, the low-frequency reproduction capability is enhanced.

It is designed to prevent magnetic field for use in AV.

It can be installed in various ways by using the separately sold stand.
