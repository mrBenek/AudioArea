Commentary

This is a two way speaker system that is compact and has excellent sound quality.

A 16 cm cone type woofer is installed in the low range.
This unit is directly connected without passing through a network circuit to realize a rich singing voice.

A 5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
This unit uses a titanium center cap to make the ultra-high frequency playback clearer.
In addition, the expanded metal grill on the front can be freely attached and removed depending on the installation location.

The Network Division takes advantage of Diatone's unique full range unit know-how and sets the crossover frequency to a higher value. This makes it possible to achieve a flatter frequency response by playing the low-to-medium range on a woofer.

The enclosure has a wood-grain finish with Linerwood grain, and the symmetrical arrangement of the units makes sound localization clearer.
The interior is a dispersion resonance type unique to Diatone to prevent the box ringing phenomenon.
