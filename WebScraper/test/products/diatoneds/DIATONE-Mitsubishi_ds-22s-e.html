  • DIATONE DS-22S

  • ¥ 26,000 (1 unit, released in 1983)

Commentary

A thin speaker system that can be hung on the wall.

An 18 cm flat woofer is installed in the low range and a 5 cm flat tweeter is installed in the high range.

Each element of the network circuit is examined. In particular, a crimping wire method using oxygen-free copper (OFC) with low DC resistance is used for connection, eliminating distortion and coloring in terms of sound quality.

Rigidity is increased by using 15 mm thick particleboards for the cabinet, and smooth directional characteristics are achieved by using round baffle.

It has a hook for hanging on the wall and is designed so that you can enjoy placing it horizontally or vertically.
