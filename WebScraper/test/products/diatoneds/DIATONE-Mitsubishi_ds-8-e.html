  • DIATONE DS-8

  • ¥ 40,000 (1 unit, released in November 1991)

Commentary

A speaker system designed to fit the high-end AV system cabinet DR-5HD released by DIATONE.

The unit is equipped with a 13 cm coaxial 2-way unit.
The woofer uses an aramid blend cone that achieves both high rigidity and moderate internal loss.
In addition, the tweeter section is equipped with a 2.5 cm dome type tweeter using ultra-miniaturized neodymium magnet to suppress unnecessary radiation from the woofer.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
