  • VICTOR/JVC XS-AV500

  • ¥ 55,000 (1 unit, around 1986)

Commentary

An AV surround speaker system with a large TV that can be placed on top of the system and can be used for stereo playback in one unit.

A total of four 12 cm full-range units are installed on the front and both sides, and the sound field is formed by 5 units including 27 cm 3D center woofer.

Equipped with Presence Control that enables you to select "Surround", "Presence" or "OFF (Normal)" by switching the switch.
