Commentary

A 5-ch speaker package designed for home theaters.

Each speaker unit contains two 6 cm cone type full-range units.
High rigidity pulp cone mixed with aramid fiber is used for the diaphragm, and it is driven by twin drive system.

It is a magnetic resistant design.

It uses a bass reflex system.
