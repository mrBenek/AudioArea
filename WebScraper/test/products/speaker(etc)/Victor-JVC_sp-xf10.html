Commentary

A 6-channel speaker package that works with an AX-F10 or active subwoofer.

The SP-XF10 consists of six speakers : two front speakers, one center speaker and three surround speakers.

The front and center speakers are equipped with two 6 cm cone type full-range units.
The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

The surround speakers are equipped with an 8 cm cone type full-range unit facing upward, and an omnidirectional directional system using a diffuse reflector realizes reproduction of sound fields close to non-directional.
