Commentary

An active speaker system that realizes the reproduction of Dolby Pro Logic with 2 speakers by 3D phonic.

In 3D Phonic, dialogue and dialogue are positioned on the screen as a virtual center. At the same time, surround components (information on sound and sound image localization) are synthesized and played back by the front speaker using digital signal processing technology. This enables clear dialogue located in the center of the screen heard from 360 °.

In addition to 3 types of 3D phonic, it is equipped with 3 types of DAP which adds the realistic feeling of the hall to the normal stereo sound, and it is possible to play the surround according to the software.

With a built-in subwoofer in the body, it realizes heavy bass playback from 33 Hz.

Space saving is realized by the integrated structure with integrated 100W amplifier and Dolby Pro Logic Decoder.

Equipped with an anti-magnetic cone type full-range speaker for all bands.

The current processor mode can be displayed in FL on the amplifier section.

Equipped with an AV remote control, all necessary operations can be performed from a remote location.
