  • YAMAHA ToP-AR

Commentary

System component composed of small components of toP-AR T-Series.

ToP-ART is an abbreviation of Total Purity Audio Reproduction Technology.
In this series, with the goal of musical expression, the characteristic values obtained by measurement are also refined from the acoustic viewpoint of musicality.

In this series, there were 2 kinds of variations, basic system and full system.
The basic system consists of a pre-main amplifier AX-10, a CD player CDX-10, and a speaker NS-5. The full system consists of a basic system plus an FM/AM tuner TX-10 and a cassette deck KX-10.
