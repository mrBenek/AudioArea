  • TRIO ST-833X

  • ¥ 105,000 (around 1966)

Commentary

Stereo components incorporating the latest electronic technology in the amplifier section.

Receiver section

It has an output of 28W to support a wide dynamic range.

The FM tuner section is equipped with a stereo monitor to identify FM multi-stereo.
We are also working to improve the quality by incorporating Trio's technologies, such as FM-multi noise reduction equipment.

Player part

The player part uses a tri-auto start system that enables smooth record performance with just one knob.

A MM type cartridge is used for the cartridge, and a hard pipe arm is used for the tone arm.

Speaker part

3-way 3-speaker configuration.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 16 cm cone squaker is installed in the middle range.
A horn type tweeter is installed in the high-frequency range.

The network is an LC type.

The enclosure is hermetically sealed.
