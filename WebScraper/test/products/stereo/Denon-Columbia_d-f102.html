Commentary

An ef series system component that inherits the technology and design philosophy of Hi-Fi audio equipment.

It follows the slim and luxurious audio component design, and uses a unified finish such as aluminum front panel, volume knob, power button, etc.

The speaker section uses European tune speakers with a natural wood finish.
