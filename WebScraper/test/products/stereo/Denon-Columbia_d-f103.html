  • DENON D-F103

Commentary

An ef series system component that inherits the technology and design philosophy of Hi-Fi audio equipment.

The D-F103 is combined with the CHR-F103 which can store CD music.
It is also equipped with an Internet radio function and a network music player function for playing music files on a PC over a LAN.

The amplifier uses a DRA-F102 with a tuner, and the speaker uses a SC-F103SG with a piano black finish.
