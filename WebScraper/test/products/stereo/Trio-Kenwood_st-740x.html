  • TRIO ST-740X

  • ¥ 79,900 (around 1966)

Commentary

Stereo component for FM multi-broadcasting.

Receiver section

Uses a receiver (tri-amp) that uses a vacuum tube.
The amplifier section is equipped with a low filter and a high filter.

The tuner section is compatible with FM multi stereo broadcast transmission.
Equipped with SCA filter and FM AFC.

Player part

A pipe arm is used for the tone arm.
The drive motor is equipped with a synchronous motor that does not require fine adjustment of speed.

DC ignition is used for the equalizer amplifier section.

The cartridge is equipped with a MM type cartridge.
Uses a hard cantilever and special damper.

Speaker part

2-way 3-speaker configuration.

Two 16 cm cone units are installed in the middle and low range, and a 6 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Other

The exterior is finished with polyester.
