  • Pioneer S-61X

  • ¥ 141,000 (around 1964)

Commentary

A separate stereo developed as a top-of-the-line model.

Receiver / player part

An aluminum die-cast turntable is used for the player.

The receiver section can receive up to three analog TV channels.
In addition, it is equipped with functions such as rumble and scratch filter.

The exterior has a walnut oil finish.

Speaker part

A 3-way speaker is used for the speaker section.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 16 cm cone squaker is installed in the middle range.
A horn type tweeter is installed in the high-frequency range.
