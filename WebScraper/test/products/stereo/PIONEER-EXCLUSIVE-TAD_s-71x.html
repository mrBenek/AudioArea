  • Pioneer S-71X

  • ¥ 106,000 (around 1964)

Commentary

This is a separate stereo with an auto mechanism in the player.

Receiver / player part

The player is equipped with functions such as auto return and auto cut.

The tuner section of the receiver has a limiter circuit, which can eliminate noise.

Speaker part

A 3-way speaker is used for the speaker section.

A 25 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 16 cm cone squaker is installed in the middle range.
In addition, a 6.5 cm cone type tweeter is installed in the high range.
