  • TRIO ST-750X

  • ¥ 85,900 (around 1966)

Commentary

A stereo component that combines a tri-amp compatible with FM multi-broadcasting and a player equipped with an auto mechanism.

Receiver section

Equipped with a terminal that can be recorded and replayed by a tape recorder.
It is also equipped with functions such as loudness control.

DC ignition is used for the equalizer circuit.

The FM tuner section is equipped with an automatic switching device between FM mono and stereo.

Player part

There are three practical auto mechanisms in the player part.
Equipped with a tri-auto start mechanism and auto-stop mechanism that automatically turn on / off the power of the motor, raise / lower the arm, and drive / pause the idler, as well as a full stop mechanism that automatically turns off the power of the amplifier at the end of the performance and puts the entire stereo into pause.

A MM type cartridge is used.
A pipe arm is used for the tone arm.

Speaker part

2-way 3-speaker configuration.

Two 16 cm cone units are installed in the low range, and a 7 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.

The network is an LC type.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.
