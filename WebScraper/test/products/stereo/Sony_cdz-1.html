Commentary

This is the first CD electric storage designed as a sound artifact.

The CDZ-1 has an acoustic design focused on CD playback, with a built-in subwoofer and satellite speakers for left and right sound (the satellite speakers are placed in the back shelf in the photo).

It has a built-in CD player for program selection and a 10-station random preset FM/AM tuner.

Equipped with auto-loudness control function and digital surround function.

The exterior is made from mahogany Tamamoku which is used for high-class house ingredients. Each item is carefully handmade.
Therefore, it seems that delivery took 2 to 3 months from the order.

It comes with a remote commander with a gorgeous finish like the main body.
