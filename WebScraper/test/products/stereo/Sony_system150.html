Audio Database

  • SONY System150

  • ¥ 49,800 (around 1971)

Commentary

A stereo system developed with the concept of "Music plus You".

System 150 is equipped with an FM mixing device that enables you to listen to both records and the sound of an FM radio microphone simultaneously.
By adding a microphone, you can enjoy participating in a song or performance.

Amplifier section

The amplifier section consists of all-silicon transistors.

Equipped with auto-stereo circuit.

The exterior has a blackface and walnut wood finish.

Player part

Equipped with auto-cut and Oatley turn functions.

The turntable part is an idler drive type, and a 25 cm turntable is used.

The cartridge uses a crystal type.

Speaker part

2-way configuration is adopted.

A 16 cm cone woofer is installed in the low range and a 5 cm cone tweeter is installed in the high range.

The Sony emblem on the speaker can be arranged vertically or horizontally.

Other

The amplifier, player, and speaker are separated into component styles so that they can be installed freely.
