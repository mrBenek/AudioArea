Commentary

This is a stereo set that uses a lattice design.

Receiver section
A silicon power transistor is used for the amplifier.

The FM tuner section is equipped with a double limiter circuit to reduce noise.
The FM multi-circuit employs a switching system.

Ferrite bar antenna is installed in the AM tuner section.

Player part
A belt drive system using a synchronous motor is adopted as the drive system.

Equipped with an auto return function that automatically returns the arm when the record performance is finished.
In addition, the auto-cut function allows you to turn the power off by moving the arm back even in the middle of a song.

Speaker part
It uses a 3-way configuration that combines a 25 cm cone woofer, a rear sealed 12 cm cone squaker, and a horn type tweeter. The network section is equipped with a full-scale LC network.

Other
The cabinet has a walnut finish and is designed to allow for separate left and right speakers and center box.

The center box is designed with a vertical amplifier section, and has a structure with a shutter door.
