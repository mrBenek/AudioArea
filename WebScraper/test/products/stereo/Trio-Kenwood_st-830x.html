Commentary

Stereo component for FM Multi (FM stereo broadcast transmission).

Receiver section
The tuner section is equipped with an FM multi-circuit, a stereo monitor to distinguish between mono and stereo, and a tuner function such as FM AFC to prevent mistuning.
The amplifier section uses a fully DC ignition circuit to prevent hum noise, and is equipped with functions such as loudness control, high filter and tape recording terminal.

Player part
The record player is equipped with a unique tri-auto system that can perform three operations by simply operating a single lever. One lever can be used to turn the power on - idler into drive position, turn the turntable - arm down and start playing.

A high-compliance magnetic cartridge is used.
Also, a static balance type pipe arm is adopted for the tone arm.

Speaker part
The speaker section is a 3-way configuration, with a 20 cm cone woofer in the low range, a 7.5 cm cone squaker in the middle range, and two 5 cm cone tweeters in the high range.

The L/C system is used for the speaker network.
