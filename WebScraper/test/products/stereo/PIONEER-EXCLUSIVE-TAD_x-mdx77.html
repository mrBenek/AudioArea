  • Pioneer X-MDX77(XR-Q700+S-P700)

  • ¥ 85,000 (released in December 1996)

Commentary

This MD mini-component is 15 cm wide and equipped with CD, MD, tape and FM/AM tuner.

Receiver / player part

3-function direct play key improves operability.

Equipped with a multi-control jog dial, you can quickly and easily input titles for MD recording / editing, select songs, tune tuners, set timers, etc.

Equipped with a multi-source A. S.E.S. function, you can easily select five recording modes by simply pressing the sync button.
This allows for flexible recording and editing using three sources : CD, MD, and tape.

Equipped with 4-mode sound control function, you can enjoy Bead/Vocal/Clear/Flat sound quality depending on the source.

The MD deck has six editing functions : move, erase, divide, program move, all erase, and combine.
It also supports katakana character input, and you can enter up to 100 characters of song information such as song name and artist name per song.

A multi-character display and aluminum front panel are used in the front part.

Speaker part

A 2-way speaker is used for the speaker section.
This speaker uses Pioneer's original linear power system to achieve deep bass and clear sound that cannot be obtained with conventional small speakers.

It has a 12 cm cone woofer in the low range and a 1.8 cm dome tweeter in the high range.

Other

Wireless remote control is included.

There was a special system case sold separately.
In addition, you can choose from 3 types of speaker RUNNET of blue / white / yellow, which is sold separately, and you can coordinate according to the atmosphere of the room.
