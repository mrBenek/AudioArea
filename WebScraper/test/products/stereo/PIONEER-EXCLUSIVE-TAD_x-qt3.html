  • Pioneer X-QT3

Commentary

Micro-component with 25 CD auto-changer.

Receiver section

The receiver section is equipped with Forte mode, and you can enjoy 3 sound qualities of F-Beat, F-Vocal and F-Wide.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal that allows recording to digital recording equipment.

It uses a CD window that displays the movement of the CD with light in pickup illumination.

Equipped with Wake-up/Rec/Sleep timer function.

Equipped with an FM wideband / AM tuner that can receive terrestrial analog TV broadcasting (1-3ch).

Equipped with a control output terminal that enables operation of the CD changer PD-F25 from the attached remote control.

Equipped with Music Search, you can skip over the cassette tape and select the music.

CD player part

A 18 cm wide file-type CD player can store 25 CDs.

Equipped with Best Selection Memory, you can store up to 25 songs from 25 CDs by pressing the Best button while playing your favorite song. After that, you can play your favorite song just by pressing the Best button.

You can play all mode with 25 CDs, single mode with only one CD, random and repeat.
It is also equipped with a program play that allows you to select up to 32 songs from 25 CDs.

A. Equipped with an Auto Synchro Editing System (S.E.S.), you can select and program a song, and simply press the A. S.E.S. button to automatically perform synchronized editing from CDs to tapes. Even if the last song on the A side of the tape is interrupted, it can be rerecorded on the B side.

With Highlight Scan, you can play one part of a song for 10 seconds at a time and quickly find a disc or song.
You can also perform various types of scans, such as a Prior Disc Scan, which plays the disc you have recently listened to in order from immediately before, a Disc Scan, which plays the first song from the disc in the changer in order, a Track Scan, which plays all songs in order, a Program Scan, and a Best Selection Memory Scan.

Speaker part

The speaker unit has a 10 cm cone type full-range unit.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Other

Wireless remote control is included.
It also comes with a 25-disc case stand that lets you know the CD title inside the changer at a glance.
