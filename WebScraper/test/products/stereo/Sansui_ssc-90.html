Commentary

High-grade compact stereo.

Receiver section
The amplifier circuit uses the complimentary service Darlington SEPP-ITL-OTL system using silicon power transistors.
In the tuner section, a silicon transistor and a 3-row varicon are used.

Player part
The record player uses a belt drive system with a synchronous motor.

Speaker part
It is a 2-way configuration and adopts a bass reflex design with a pipe duct.

Other
The free-style design allows you to position left and right speakers, amplifiers and players freely.
