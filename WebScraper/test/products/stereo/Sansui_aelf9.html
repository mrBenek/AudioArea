  • SANSUI aelf 9

Commentary

A system component composed of small components of the ALF series.

The basic configuration of the aelf9 (Alf 9) is A - α 9, CD-α 9 and S - α 9, and it is possible to add an MD deck.
In addition, there were also configurations such as aelf-spendor9 which added Spendor SP3/1 to A - α 9 and CD-α 9, and aelf-spendor PRO which combined Spendor LS3/5A.

There was a multi-stage rack as an option sold separately.
This multi-stage rack comes with a speaker insulator.
