  • Pioneer S-43X

  • ¥ 77,900 (around 1964)

Commentary

Stereo system equipped with FM multi-stereo circuit.

Receiver / player part

Equipped with an FM tuner with increased sensitivity level for FM stereo reception.
You can enjoy stereo reception even in areas with poor reception by using the attached simple antenna at short distances.

The player part is designed with 3 speeds.

The cabinet has a walnut oil finish.

Speaker part

A 2-way speaker is used for the speaker section.
