  • ¥ 43,000 (released in 1995)

Commentary

This is a micro-component that uses a slot-in mechanism in a compact size of 18 cm in width.

Receiver / player part

The CD player has a slot-in mechanism for 12 cm and 8 cm CDs.
Just by inserting a CD, the power turns on automatically and starts playing.

It is equipped with a direct play function, and if it is in a state with a CD or a tape set, just press the play button and the power is turned on and play starts immediately.

Usability is improved by considering the color, shape, and layout of the operation buttons.
In addition, a black display is used for a graphical effect. The display can be dimmed or turned off.

It is equipped with Forte mode in which you can enjoy your favorite sound according to music genre and mood.
You can choose from 3 types : F-BEAT with heavy bass sound, F-VOCAL with prominent vocals, and F-WIDE with wide sound.

Equipped with an optical digital output terminal.

Equipped with Wake-up/Rec/Sleep timer function.

Equipped with an FM wideband / AM tuner that supports terrestrial TV broadcasting (1-3ch).

Equipped with a control output terminal that enables operation of the CD changer PD-F25.

Equipped with A. S.E.S. (Auto Synchro Editing System), which is convenient for editing from CDs to tapes synchronously.

It is equipped with the music search function of the skipping selection of the cassette tape.

Speaker part

The speaker unit has a 10 cm cone type full-range unit.

The enclosure uses a bass reflex system.

Other

Wireless remote control is included.
