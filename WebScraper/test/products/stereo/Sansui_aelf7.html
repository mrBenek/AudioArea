  • SANSUI aelf 7

Commentary

A system component composed of small components of the ALF series.

The basic configuration of aelf7 (ALF 7) is A - α 7, CD-α 7, S - α 7, and tuner, cassette deck, and MD deck can be added.
There was also a composition of aelf-spendor7 which added SP3/1 of Spendor to A - α 7 and CD - α 7.

There was a multi-stage rack as an option sold separately.
This multi-stage rack comes with a speaker insulator
