  • About CREEK

A manufacturer established by Mike Creek in 1982 in Hampshire Horndeen, England.
Based on our company philosophy of "maximum perfection with minimum parts," we have a lineup of low-priced, high-quality products.
In 1991, with the retirement of Mike Creek, we moved our factory to Court Bridge, Scotland, as a brand of Tannoy's Electronics and Digital Division, and developed CD players under the support of Tannoy.
