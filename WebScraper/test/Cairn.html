This amplifier maker was established by Gilles Belot in Saint Germain, France in 1994.
Designed and manufactured by EZO's young design team, it is thoughtfully designed based on a high standard of sound production and advanced design thinking, with a high level of musicality ideal for the living room.
It is highly appreciated by more than 20 countries, mainly in Europe, and in the manufacturing process, each device is manually assembled by skilled technicians to strict standards of tolerance and various characteristics.
